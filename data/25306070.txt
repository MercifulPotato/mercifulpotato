Item(by='ghaff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607107603, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25304873, text='The argument for a shared pool from the employee side is &quot;I don&#x27;t get sick much and, as a shared pool, I almost certainly get more vacation time off than if I had a sick pool and a vacation pool.&quot;<p>However, historically, when the two were commonly separate, the sick time would often actually be a very significant chunk (maybe a month) with the understanding that actually burning through most or all of your sick time would be a rare event because of an operation or whatever.<p>Just to throw out rough numbers, you might have 4 weeks of a combined PTO pool today. 20  years ago you might have had 3 weeks of vacation time and 4 weeks of sick time.')
