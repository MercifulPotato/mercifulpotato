Item(by='dhsysusbsjsi', descendants=None, kids=[25613254], score=None, time=1609569601, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25610289, text='I find if I write down 5 tasks and cross them off one by one, I will gravitate to the short easy ones first for instant gratification.<p>Completing larger tasks gives a much deeper satisfaction yet despite a long time trying to teach my body&#x2F;brain this, it still craves the easy path instant gratification.<p>So one thing I do is split up the big task into lots of small tasks so I can cross more off and get the gratification hits. This helps my brain prioritise those over other small tasks.')
