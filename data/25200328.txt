Have a problem statement: What is wrong from a business perspective.  Example: <i>Password are unencrypted which dramatically increases risk of class-action lawsuits.</i><p>2. Summary statement: <i>Execute this, it pays for itself.</i><p>Bear in mind an executive summary is either superficial or its a lie.  You need to have a real technical report behind the executive summary so that it isn&#x27;t a lie.')
