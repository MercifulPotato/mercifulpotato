Item(by='pge', descendants=None, kids=[25724419], score=None, time=1610332447, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25720225, text='If I remember correctly, the ban against marriage came after the monasteries were well established. The monasteries were some of the largest estates in medieval Europe, and there was a fear that they would become dynasties that challenged the lords (or became poweful in their own right which could challenge the church hierarchy). The marriage prohibition kept the church in control of the monasteries and kept the relationship between the church and the lords happy.')
