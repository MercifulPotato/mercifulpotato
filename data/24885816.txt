Item(by='corty', descendants=None, kids=[24888862, 24889175], score=None, time=1603629169, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24885650, text='The design overview slides are their own critique: Wayland does almost nothing besides render buffer handling. Input? Applications job. Window decorations? Compositors job. Application talking to the Compositor? Somebody elses job. Clipboard? Maybe compositor or toolkit. Screenshots and remoting? Somebody elses job, but only after Wayland has bored the appropriate holes in its security model. This all leads to a ton of incompatibilities between compositors, toolkits and applications. And beyond Gnome, the full &quot;featureset&quot; is still not implemented, where &quot;featureset&quot; is barely adequate as an X11 replacement.<p>But the buffer handling is great, no more flickering...')
