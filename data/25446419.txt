Item(by='Taek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608143804, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446174, text='We&#x27;re using blockchain technology to build a decentralized cloud where users can store data on remote hosts without having to trust them, and without giving up ownership of the data.<p>The blockchain is a critical part of the infrastructure because the hosts need to be paid for the services they provide, but as untrusted entities we need assurances from them.
