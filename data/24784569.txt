Item(by='secalex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602732176, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24783806, text='It&#x27;s called scalable video coding. The source sends multiple streams of packets depending on their upstream, and the more streams you get the higher quality the resulting video. Each client can tell the server which streams they want to subscribe to, which are then picked apart and multiplexed per the needs of each receiving client.')
