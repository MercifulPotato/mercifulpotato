Item(by='waddlesplash', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602777352, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24784560, text='(Haiku developer here.)<p>Ultimately a lot of the things Haiku &quot;dictates&quot; are at levels that 99.9% of people, including developers, do not care about: the window manager and display server, launch interface, package manager, etc. Certainly those decisions have consequences, but ultimately (for example) do you really need the freedom to decide whether you want ALSA or OSS? People just want audio to work, and programmers want a sane API to work with it. The internals matter little to most people.')
