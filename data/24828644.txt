Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603128598, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24822223, text='I know people with severe chronic illnesses, some directly in my life. My own problems are minor by comparison, in that I can manage them to the point where they hardly affect others.  Or so I think.<p>You need to make headspace for &#x27;self care&#x27; as a category of activity, one you not only give yourself permission to pursue, but expect of yourself. One of the best comparisons I&#x27;ve heard is the oxygen mask instructions on an airplane. It&#x27;s foolish to help other people when you haven&#x27;t helped yourself. You just end up killing yourself by prioritizing expectations of others over your own health. If you do it right, you can often accomplish both.')
