Item(by='tgv', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607331461, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25328724, text='Without the container struct, you&#x27;d have to pass the pointer to the pointer of the first element, remove(&amp;head, elem), or assign the return value back to it, head = remove(head, elem). A container also allows you to maintain a counter and a tail pointer, for fast append <i>and</i> insert. Above all, a container makes it harder to return a pointer to just somewhere in the middle of the list and treat that as the head.')
