Item(by='crehn', descendants=None, kids=[24761677], score=None, time=1602555086, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24761485, text='The psychological effects of technology seem often overlooked—and they’re hard to quantify.<p>I wish there was some easier way to quantify the “soft” aspects of everything that surrounds us, e.g. the long-term impact of beautiful and usable UI design, the reduction in “ambient” psychological stress, the impacts of chaos&#x2F;consistency on how we feel, any robust measures of happiness, excitement, relaxation, etc.<p>They seem to be such important attributes, but not as easily measured as a latency. Which in turn makes reaching consensus harder—and probably why design by committee doesn&#x27;t work?')
