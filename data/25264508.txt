Item(by='adwn', descendants=None, kids=[25264639, 25265303], score=None, time=1606824751, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25263901, text='I agree with regards to lock-free not being faster than lock-based, but that:<p>&gt; <i>replace threads with separate processes that communicate via ring buffers</i><p>does not follow at all. Interprocess communication is always more work to set up and less efficient [1] than interthread communication. The only thing separate processes gain you is enforced memory protection.<p>[1] Different processes need different TLB entries, while different threads in the same process do not.')
