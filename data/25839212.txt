Item(by='kazinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611092195, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25838685, text='The tone of the article conveys agreement with the intervention.<p>For instance, the closing remark &quot;in both cases, private business had to step in when the consequences became evident&quot; could be rendered neutrally as &quot;in both cases, private businesses stepped in when the consequences became evident.&quot;<p>&quot;Had to step in&quot; has the effect of defending the actions, by insinuating that they had no alternative.')
