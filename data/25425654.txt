Item(by='webmaven', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607993713, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25412852, text='If you don&#x27;t capture the value you create, you don&#x27;t become staggeringly wealthy.<p>If you capture value you don&#x27;t create, you become staggeringly wealthy.<p>Note that I am not claiming that value isn&#x27;t being created at all in that second scenario, just that the entity capturing the value isn&#x27;t creating it.<p>If you capture value while destroying some, the picture gets more complicated, as it depends on the nature of the feedback loops involved. It isn&#x27;t too hard to see examples in the wild of organizations pursuing strategies aimed at getting a larger slice of the pie, or at getting slices of more pies, rather than strategies that are aimed at getting the size or number of pies to grow overall.')
