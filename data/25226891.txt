Item(by='bmulholland', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606468983, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25226278, text='&gt; If you want to change people, then give them positive reinforcement. If you try to &quot;change&quot; others by negative feedback, they will likely do less of everything and try not to be caught doing anything because it might be the &quot;wrong thing&quot;.<p>Which is why Manager Tools emphasizes that this form of feedback should be used first exclusive with &#x2F;positive&#x2F; feedback for quite some time (months) before using the format for negative feedback. And that, once started, positive feedback should outweigh negative feedback 3-1.')
