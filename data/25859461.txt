Meaning, statements that are true, but so piercing that they also are humorous.<p>In this vein, I can recommend the book <i>The Tao of Programming</i> by Geoffrey James.  There are also collections of funny anecdotes like “<i>COMPUTER-RELATED HORROR STORIES, FOLKLORE, AND ANECDOTES</i>”¹, “<i>Computer Stupidities</i>”² and the pre-MacOS X Macintosh-related collection of “folklore”³.
