Item(by='anilakar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601968078, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24695000, text='Using maths to either oppose or support generic duck typing is just pointless, because it&#x27;s easy to come up with counterexamples where a mathematical operation would or would not behave as expected.<p>In quite a few cases where one would overload a mathematical operation to, say, sum two objects of certain type, it&#x27;d be better to explicitly type out the operation as a functor&#x2F;lambda.')
