Item(by='sjtindell', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609630838, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25611883, text='In technology we are coming to accept that no matter how solid your security is, there are just too many attackers and someone will get in. We need to tackle our tax code and similar systems the same way. We need to design the systems to be robust to this kind of trickery from the start, not chase and plug loopholes after the fact. Particularly when the incentives are so off - the private industry accountants and lawyers getting around the rules are paid more, have more fun, and enjoy more prestige than those on defense.')
