Item(by='plasticxme', descendants=None, kids=[25623499, 25624100], score=None, time=1609689743, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25620729, text='Nginx and the like are starting to provide non-privileged versions of their container images.<p>Running as root is lazy and equals container escape, especially when running on anything other than scratch and read only file system.<p>The only reason Nginx and Traefik run as root is to bind to privileged ports (80,443). There is no reason to do that inside of a container, since you can remap exposed ports outside of the container.<p>Containers are not VMs and must be handled differently. You are always one RCE away from having your entire container platform compromised.')
