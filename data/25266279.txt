Item(by='baron_harkonnen', descendants=None, kids=[25269204], score=None, time=1606838376, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25265449, text='It&#x27;s pretty clear that the &quot;unhealthy habit&quot;, like most unhealthy habits, is a symptom not the disease.<p>In this article the problem is being overworked. The solution isn&#x27;t to remove that little bit of extra pleasure from your life so you can sleep better and be an even better, harder worker, but to find a better balance.<p>If you have this problem is very likely because you have a major part of your life you don&#x27;t feel like you have control over: maybe your jobs is awful, maybe your marriage is awful, maybe you just feel too stressed about the world.<p>Treating the symptom and not the root cause will not yield the results you are looking for even if you succeed.')
