Item(by='mdtusz', descendants=None, kids=[25173586], score=None, time=1606001351, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25158736, text='Tinycore was used at my last job to get around some frustrations of updating the embedded systems on the machines we made (which ran a full blown Linux system).<p>We mailed customers USB drives that when plugged in would mount and start tinycore Linux, then write the new system to a parallel partition, reboot the machine, then run a few health checks.<p>Some might say it&#x27;s hacky (it is&#x2F;was) but it worked pretty well and allowed for recovery of totally borked systems.')
