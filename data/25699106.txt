Item(by='edflsafoiewq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610187049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25698859, text='The original text is also completely in kana. This confuses AI models that mostly understand normal text with kanji. They don&#x27;t really &quot;know&quot; that 手に入る and てにはいる are the same thing.<p>Eg, with DeepL:<p>ダークエルフなら ほくとうのしまの  どうくつに すんでるよ！ =&gt; If you&#x27;re a Dark Elf, you&#x27;ll find me in the caves of the Far East!<p>ダークエルフなら北東の島の洞窟に住んでるよ！ =&gt; If you&#x27;re a Dark Elf, you live in a cave on the northeast island!<p>DeepL also seems to be quite sensitive to the placement of spaces.')
