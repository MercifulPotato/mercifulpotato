As the parent commenter pointed out, there is a lot more of this being published, and there is a monetary benefit for certain firms to have new types of drugs to introduce and sell. And as I also pointed out, large corporations have funded publications, news coverage, and research that help change the public perception and discussion around things previously less-accepted.<p>With all the above (more uncharitably, since actors have the <i>means, motive, and opportunity</i>), we should certainly be skeptical when we notice this happening and not hand-wave it away.')
