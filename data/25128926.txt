Item(by='pmoriarty', descendants=None, kids=[25129165, 25130054], score=None, time=1605643351, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25127371, text='My main problem with DDG is that there&#x27;s no way to be sure they actually respect their users&#x27; privacy as they claim to.<p>Ideally, services like theirs would be continuously audited by respectable, trusted organizations like the EFF.. multiple such organizations even.<p>Then I&#x27;d have at least some reason to believe their claims of not collecting data about me.<p>As it stands, I only have their word for it.. which in this day and age is pretty worthless.<p>That said, I&#x27;d still <i>much</i> rather use DDG, who at least pay lip service to privacy, than sites like Google or Facebook, who are openly contemptuous of it.<p>At the very least it sends a message to these organizations that privacy is still valued, and they&#x27;d lose out by not trying to accommodate the privacy needs of their users to some extent.')
