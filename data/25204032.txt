Item(by='SamReidHughes', descendants=None, kids=[25204477], score=None, time=1606258843, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25198187, text='For example, starting from decimal representation, you can calculate the product of five digit numbers, (&quot;abcde&quot; * &quot;fghij&quot;) with the following, where capitalized variables X and S are the magic O(1)-addition integer types:<p><pre><code>    let X = to_magic_integer(&quot;abcde&quot;)\n    let y = &quot;fghij&quot;.reverse()\n    let S = to_magic_integer(&quot;0&quot;)\n    for d = 0 to 4\n        for i = 1 to y[d]\n            S += X\n        end\n        X = X+X+X+X+X + X+X+X+X+X\n    end\n\n    return S\n</code></pre>\n(You can implement to_magic_integer with basically the same algorithm in O(n).)')
