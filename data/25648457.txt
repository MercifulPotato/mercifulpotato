Sometimes as low as 0.25% but up to 2% generally. Largely depends on credit, lender, financial situation. It also goes away once that final 10% is paid off (when the load is 80% principal). It also isn&#x27;t held against you in the sense that, it impacts your ability to get the loan. It just means you pay more out of pocket per month.<p>It&#x27;s definitely not ideal but it&#x27;s not an overall huge drag.')
