Item(by='kisamoto', descendants=None, kids=[24923062], score=None, time=1603911826, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24922207, text='There is a distinction between carbon credits and negative emissions.<p>I wouldn&#x27;t say these projects are bias towards silicon valley style there is simply a growing acceptance that nature based solutions - whole undeniably important and essential - are simply not enough.<p>They are cheap but slow. Trees can be wiped out with wild-fires releasing CO2 back into the atmosphere.<p>Negative emissions technologies such as bio-oil, direct air capture and storage, olivine and biochar, are developing to help support nature based solutions and remove quickly.<p>While expensive at the moment I believe they will become cheaper as the technologies and processes mature and the economies of scale kick in.<p>I don&#x27;t say this to convert you - nature is a great way to help combat the climate crisis - however do not dismiss this essential step for our future.')
