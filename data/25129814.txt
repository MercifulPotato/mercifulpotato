Item(by='mc32', descendants=None, kids=[25129889], score=None, time=1605647079, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25129731, text='High income earners trickle down their money to other sectors of the economy --they have more disposable income.  This is why on the one hand conspicuous consumers are good; on the other hand that means it does not get invested in longer term things like R&amp;D, infrastructure, etc.<p>They can afford the iconic $14 guacamole on toast that encourages chefs and restauranteurs to try new things and in turn those people spend and support other areas of the economy.<p>If you only have median income people, then, it&#x27;s not bad, but it&#x27;s not as vigorous an economy and things change less.')
