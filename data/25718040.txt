Item(by='PlsDontBan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610304616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25717646, text='I don&#x27;t believe alternative electoral systems are a good idea because they tend to have major unforeseen consequences. For example, proportional representation sounds great on paper but it is widely recognized as a contributig factor to the rise of Hitler.<p>Ranked choice voting would likely have the effect of eroding the two party system. Whether that is a good or bad thing is highly uncertain. I think it has a lot more potential to be bad and it isn&#x27;t worth the risk.')
