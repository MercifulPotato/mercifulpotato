Item(by='jfk13', descendants=None, kids=[25301302], score=None, time=1607081759, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25300945, text='Yes, it&#x27;s just a container for the exact same data that an OpenType font holds.<p>On the other hand, it&#x27;s possible that in the course of deployment, various things may be dropped from the original font resource: not just subsetting (reducing the repertoire of supported characters), but in some cases also OpenType features (e.g. ligatures or contextual forms), and in some cases hinting is stripped.<p>If <i>that</i> is done, the webfont version may indeed appear differently than the original &quot;desktop&quot; font, depending on local font-rendering settings.')
