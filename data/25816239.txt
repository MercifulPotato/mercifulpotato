Item(by='upofadown', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610925125, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25815341, text='The PGP thing is wildly different than the other applications in that it is a complete airgapped system on a key. The decryption and signing is actually done on key so the private key isn&#x27;t exposed. PGP is pretty much the only thing that does this and as a result can be held up as an example of where we do in fact have a unique standard.')
