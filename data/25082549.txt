What&#x27;s in dispute is whether there&#x27;s a market for these services when they reflect their true unsubsidized costs.<p>If food (or grocery) delivery companies cannot turn a profit during a pandemic, there&#x27;s no way they&#x27;re going to turn a profit when the majority of people return to in person dining or pickup.
