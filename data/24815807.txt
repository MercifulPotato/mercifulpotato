Item(by='oefrha', descendants=None, kids=[24818430, 24817158, 24816512], score=None, time=1603000680, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24808603, text='It seems lots of people&#x27;s knowledge of awk is limited to printing fields, and they&#x27;ll happily chain awk with a bunch of grep and sed when a single awk invocation would do the job without fuss. For instance, TFA uses<p><pre><code>  awk &#x27;{print $1&quot;,&quot;$2}&#x27; | sed &#x27;1i count,word&#x27;\n</code></pre>\nwhen you can just add a BEGIN block:<p><pre><code>  awk &#x27;BEGIN { print &quot;count,word&quot; } { print $1&quot;,&quot;$2 }&#x27;</code></pre>')
