I did something similar many times &amp; all it takes is to read archlinux.org news section &amp; apply .pacnew config diffs where needed. Arch is a bleeding edge distro constantly marching ahead; that&#x27;s one of its primary advantages, so it&#x27;s best to update regularly. That being said it is very much possible to not update for months, just requires a bit of extra care when you finally do due to the large number of accumulated changes.<p>I even did an online, in place switchover from SysV to systemd in 2011 and despite that being a scary amount of changes at once still got a working system.')
