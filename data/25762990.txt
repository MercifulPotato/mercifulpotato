Item(by='Jtsummers', descendants=None, kids=[25763907, 25763062], score=None, time=1610551065, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25762661, text='Thanks to the sheer number of C programs, no compiler supporting later C standards will <i>drop</i> support for prior standards unless it&#x27;s explicitly targeting the later standard <i>and that standard is fundamentally breaking with earlier ones</i>. And even then, the major players won&#x27;t because they still have to support every program ever written in C.<p>So any changes to C at this point will not break your ability to use whichever prior C standard you happen to be interested in.')
