Item(by='alyandon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606238795, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25195509, text='My biggest struggle with my Mikrotik was discovering how much consumer grade routers do behind the scenes automagically that commercial grade routers will not.<p>For instance, I wanted working NAT reflection and since my external IPv4 address is dynamic I wanted to construct a static ruleset that didn&#x27;t specifically reference it.  The solution there required me to exclude traffic destined for my LAN address range in my dnat forwarding rules to get it working properly.  Fun stuff.')
