Item(by='joemasilotti', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607544515, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25363949, text='This library is an experiment in UIKit feature-level testing, but without Apple UI Testing (XCUITest).<p>The goal is to get to ~80% feature-parity with UI Testing but be as reliable and fast as XCTest.<p>It is still a work-in-progress but supports a wide range of interactions with common UIKit controls. Pushing&#x2F;popping, presenting&#x2F;dismissing view controllers, buttons, table views, sliders, switches, and alerts are all supported.')
