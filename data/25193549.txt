Item(by='rmk', descendants=None, kids=[25194104], score=None, time=1606176343, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25193415, text='There are plenty of extenuating circumstances. For example, if you were a spouse of a corrupt law enforcement officer who&#x27;s already facing domestic violence, would it be racketeering if you did not report your spouse&#x27;s conduct? I am willing to bet it wouldn&#x27;t (you have a credible fear of being assaulted, or worse yet, losing your life if you did so).<p>This is why things like anonymous tip lines and whistleblower laws exist. You are encouraged, in general, to report misconduct. But there are very specific situations where it&#x27;s illegal to <i>not</i> report misconduct.')
