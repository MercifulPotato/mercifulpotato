Item(by='Pfhreak', descendants=None, kids=[25502920], score=None, time=1608610951, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25502548, text='I believe this is called the &quot;Gish Gallop&quot;. The idea being that I can come up with many, many more questions that are hypothetical, but that require more energy to answer than they take to ask. Often times the person will combine this with a carefully worded question that has to be unwound by the counter-debater.<p>Like, &quot;Knowing that this will cost working class Americans jobs, why do you want X?&quot;<p>Well... X doesn&#x27;t cost jobs, X might even create jobs. But now I have to explain that. And if I slow down for even one second, you have an opening to fire off another question <i>or</i> paint me as somehow waffling.')
