Item(by='Mediterraneo10', descendants=None, kids=[25822885], score=None, time=1610984412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25822357, text='As has been often emphasized, the reason that COVID-19 is considered a pandemic of such dangerous degree to require a rapid development of vaccine, is because it overloads hospitals and can deny many others (not just COVID sufferers) access to urgent treatment. Without that factor, the most vulnerable demographic for COVID-19 in terms of mortality rate is e.g. elderly people in care homes who are ordinarily a low priority for innovative medical treatments, and it is hard to imagine a vaccine would have been rushed just for their sake.')
