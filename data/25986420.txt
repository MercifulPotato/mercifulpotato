Item(by='koonsolo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612164311, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25985295, text='Computer languages are a means of communication between a human and a computer. They clearly evolved closer to human readability and understanding, and away from computer language itself.<p>When you optimize, your code needs to be closer to computer language again, and therefore further away from human readability.<p>The only way to have both, is to have a system that can optimize the code automatically. We are getting closer to that, but still sometimes this is not enough.')
