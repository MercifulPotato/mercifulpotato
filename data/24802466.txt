Item(by='simonw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602866224, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24801731, text='If you want to survive a spike of traffic from something like HN a simple way to do it is to run your site behind something like Cloudflare and set each page to cache for a short period of time.<p>Cloudflare implements a dog pile prevention mechanism where if ten simultaneous requests come in for the same page they will make only a single request to the backend, so even just a few seconds of cache time can absorb a huge amount of traffic.<p>Since my blog rarely updates I use a 15 minute cache time and manually purge the Cloudflare cache when I modify a blog entry. I&#x27;ve survived quite a few high traffic incidents that way.')
