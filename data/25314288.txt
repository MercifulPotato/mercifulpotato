If you want to construct two identical houses, you still need people to lay bricks twice. If you want two identical websites, you deploy the same files twice.<p>By the nature of software the &quot;construction&quot; part doesn&#x27;t really apply. If you only replicate already-designed pieces of software, that&#x27;s more like creating accounts in SaaS or deploying code to new servers.')
