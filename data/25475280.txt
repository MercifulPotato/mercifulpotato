There surely cannot be one.<p>Like so many policy decisions, it is simply another way for those in power to shirk the responsibility of <i>actually collecting enough taxes to pay for the things we need</i>.  To do so would mean increasing income tax, a concept completely anathema to electioneering.<p>For University in particular — a part of one’s life that should involve physical and emotional separation from the nest — it is infantilizing and disingenuous to treat undergraduates as legal adults on the one hand, and daddy’s-girls (with access to daddy’s wallet) on the other.<p>This is how it felt, for me.')
