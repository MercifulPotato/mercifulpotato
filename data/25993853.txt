And of course continue to improve our tools to properly manage more complexity.<p>Its rarely the developer who decides the size, scope, and complexity of the project.<p>Reliability&#x2F;extensibility&#x2F;dev speed&#x2F;complexity all trade off against each other, but if you&#x27;re working for someone else, you have to present those tradeoffs to the right stakeholders, not just always choose simpler.<p>There&#x27;s also the &quot;knowing the future&quot; problem - the right design for today might run into a wall when the assumptions or business goals change tomorrow.')
