A few years back, Debian changed their LTS policy to 5 years in response to Ubuntu.<p>&gt; Debian Long Term Support (LTS) is a project to extend the lifetime of all Debian stable releases to (at least) 5 years. Debian LTS is not handled by the Debian security team, but by a separate group of volunteers and companies interested in making it a success.<p>&gt; Thus the Debian LTS team takes over security maintenance of the various releases once the Debian Security team stops its work.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.debian.org&#x2F;LTS" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.debian.org&#x2F;LTS</a>')
