Item(by='scrollaway', descendants=None, kids=[25939847, 25938675, 25937038], score=None, time=1611792133, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25934497, text='PDFs can be accessible, and many of them are! Everyone who works with PDF <i>needs</i> to know about PDF&#x2F;A:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;PDF&#x2F;A" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;PDF&#x2F;A</a><p>PDF&#x2F;A is a small family of sub-formats to PDFs which amount to the accessible subset of PDF + some requirements.<p>PDF&#x2F;A and PDF&#x2F;UA are mandated by a variety of governments for PDF distribution. They&#x27;re very suitable to archival <i>and</i> are accessible (screenreaders can work with them, their text can be cleanly copied, etc).')
