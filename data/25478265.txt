Jeff Bezos isn&#x27;t &quot;worth&quot; a million times more than his warehouse workers, and he shouldn&#x27;t have that kind of money either as a matter of principle, or as a matter of democratic power. It&#x27;s not a matter of punishment, but a matter of ensuring a more just&#x2F;meritocratic and free society.<p>The fact that the state can also use some of this money to help with the funding of social programs is a secondary benefit.')
