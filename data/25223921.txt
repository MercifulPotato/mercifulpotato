Item(by='__d', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606429064, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25214649, text='The most valuable property of UTF-8 from a C-string point of view is that it guarantees there are no embedded NULs in a UTF-8 string.<p>If you naively put the bytes of UTF-16 or UTF-32 encodings into a buffer, they might contain NUL (zero) byte values.  Which, for C strings, means &quot;end of string&quot;.  UTF-8 makes sure this doesn&#x27;t happen, which makes it compatible with existing C string functions.')
