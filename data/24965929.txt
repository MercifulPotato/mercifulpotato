Item(by='AlchemistCamp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604307184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24958346, text='You&#x27;re confusing <i>is</i> with <i>ought</i>.<p>It&#x27;s not that Canada &quot;should get to&quot; pollute more than India. Canada <i>can</i> pollute more than India and still have the effects absorbed by its environment. Canada has vast forests absorbing CO2 on a scale sufficient to mostly counteract the harmful effects caused by its relatively sparse population. India doesn&#x27;t have those vast forests, and its population is enormous.<p>I&#x27;m making no claim that it <i>should</i> be this way. It <i>is</i> this way.<p>Despite emitting more CO2 <i>per capita</i>, Canada is putting a negligible strain on the planet compared to India, the US, or especially China.')
