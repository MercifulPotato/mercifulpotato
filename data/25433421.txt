Item(by='graton', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608057668, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25428621, text='For when things go wrong in your bash script, I really like the following:<p><pre><code>  # Enable verbose logging of the bash script\n  PS4=&#x27;+ ${BASH_SOURCE:-}:${FUNCNAME[0]:-}:L${LINENO:-}:   &#x27;\n  set -x\n</code></pre>\nThis improves the tracing function greatly (at the cost of much higher verbosity) in that you get the filename, function, and line number in the output.')
