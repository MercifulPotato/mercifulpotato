Item(by='pstch', descendants=None, kids=[25160974, 25162112, 25160038, 25160739, 25160090], score=None, time=1605876876, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25157491, text='I find it very unfortunate that file metadata is not encrypted : if you need this to be encrypted, you need to stack LUKS on top of ZFS, and you lose many of the benefits of ZFS (per-dataset encryption, healing ability, RAIDz, etc) while doing so.
