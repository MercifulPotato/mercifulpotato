Item(by='vetinari', descendants=None, kids=[25714843], score=None, time=1610021410, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25669793, text='The point is to use scaling; fonts at @2X are much easier to look at than at @1X.<p>Also, your DPI calculation are Windows and Linux specific - they assumed 96 dpi at @1X scale. Mac started with 72 dpi for @1X, so @2X is 144dpi. 27&quot; 4K is slightly above that, thus perfectly usable at @2X scale. For Windows and Linux, it is better to use 150-175%.')
