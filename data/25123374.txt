Item(by='vector_rotcev', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605619086, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25117112, text='I got a lifetime subscription for 90% off from a link posted on HN when brain.fm first started.<p>Anecdata: it is probably one of the best purchases I&#x27;ve ever made for workplace success&#x2F;productivity. The focus music works (light focus in the app) and has served to block out workplace sounds, improve focus, and not be distracting in and of itself - normal music seems designed to induce various emotional states that aren&#x27;t helpful for work or thinking clearly.<p>I think it&#x27;s worth giving a try, and if it works for you then it&#x27;s worth paying for. The app is certainly a lot better than it was at the start, and offers more options than the browser interface.<p>I don&#x27;t find the sleep sounds so useful but a 15 minute &#x27;chill&#x27; relax sound was often helpful when trying to get from a stressful state of mind to a calmer one, usually when going home from work.')
