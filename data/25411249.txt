If someone says <i>You can have tea OR coffee</i> they&#x27;re really expressing a NAND. You can have at most one out of the two options. Unlike with XOR, you&#x27;re permitted to have neither.<p>To express XOR, you&#x27;d need to say <i>You must have tea or coffee</i>.')
