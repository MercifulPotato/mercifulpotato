Item(by='GordonS', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604746842, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013830, text='&gt; I am certainly, in the clinical sense, addicted - eg if I miss a dose I certainly notice, and there is some degree of tolerance<p>I believe the correct term is &quot;dependent&quot;.<p>There is a lot of misinformation and stigma out there, so I think it&#x27;s really important to distinguish between <i>addiction</i> (e.g. insatiable cravings), <i>medical dependence</i> (e.g. someone taking anti-psychotics because they will otherwise have psychotic episodes, or someone taking antidepressants because they otherwise have manic depressive episodes), and <i>physiological dependence</i>, where your body&#x27;s receptors have up&#x2F;down-regulated to adjust to a &quot;new normal&quot;, and withdrawal symptoms may occur if treatment is stopped.')
