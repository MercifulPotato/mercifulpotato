Item(by='henrikschroder', descendants=None, kids=[25104310], score=None, time=1605467145, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25101510, text='I have never really understood the hydrogen hype train, because liquid hydrogen is terrible in so many ways.<p>A bucket of gasoline contains more hydrogen atoms than a bucket of liquid hydrogen, it contains more chemical energy, <i>and</i> you can actually keep it in a bucket at room temperature without anything terrible happening.<p>Sure, liquid hydrogen has a lot of energy by weight, but energy storage needs to be good by weight <i>and</i> by volume, and hydrogen is <i>terrible</i> by volume.<p>There are cases for using hydrogen as your energy storage, but if your thing is smaller than a truck, it&#x27;s probably not  one of them. There&#x27;s just so much wishful thinking around.')
