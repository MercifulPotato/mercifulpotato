Item(by='ordu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607202535, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25318261, text='A very bayesian mistake. If you know nothing about a person, then observation of a fact that he is late is an evidence that he is a kind of a person to be late. If you think about it, it is highly more likely to see this kind of person to be late, than to see a person who &quot;never late&quot; to be late.<p>If you knew your coworker better, if you&#x27;ve formed your own theory about how he is likely to be late, than if he is late you&#x27;ll probably assume that he faced some external force which became a cause of his lateness.')
