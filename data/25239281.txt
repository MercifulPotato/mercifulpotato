Today, though, the food supply chain is such that it&#x27;s both cheaper and less work to buy canned goods from the supermarket than it is to buy fresh food and preserve it yourself.<p>There&#x27;s a much more parsimonious explanation: People are are bored at home and looking for new hobbies. I&#x27;m guessing the people who are buying up jars and rings and flats now are mostly the same folks who were buying up flour and yeast back in the spring. I wouldn&#x27;t be at all surprised if yarn and knitting needles become a scarce commodity this February.')
