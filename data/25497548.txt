Item(by='RcouF1uZ4gsC', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608574023, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25492557, text='&gt; Small ISVs don&#x27;t need people like that<p>I think this becomes a lot less controversial if we realize that the author is specifically talking about a small, independent software vendor.<p>The issue has more to do with leverage. Most software companies are not able to turn a great hacker&#x27;s ability into a lot of money. As such, the baggage associated with a great hacker is totally not worth it. However, if you can turn that into a lot money, then the baggage associated with them, is not an issue.<p>Take for example John Carmack. It would be a waste of time  to hire him to develop your enterprise CRUD app. But if you want to push the limits of gaming and interaction, he is awesome.')
