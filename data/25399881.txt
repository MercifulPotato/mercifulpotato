Item(by='mauvehaus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607794127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25399705, text='Worked at Cinnabon many years ago: you can roll out dough to a rounded rectangle pretty easily. The trick is to work diagonally corner to corner. The radiused bits get cut off with the ends of the rolled, uh, &quot;log&quot; of cinnamon rolls before you cut the finished rolls off of the log.<p>There&#x27;s some waste but they&#x27;re shooting for consistent rolls. A home baker making cookies would optimize differently.')
