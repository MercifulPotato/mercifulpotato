Item(by='anonuser123456', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609983733, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25661674, text='Arnold Kling&#x27;s &quot;The Three Languages of Politics&quot; has a good answer to a piece of this puzzle.<p>In short, the major political &#x27;camps&#x27; think about events with a different dimension.  Conservatives think in the &#x27;civilization vs barbarism&#x27; axis.  Progressives think on the &#x27;oppressed vs oppressor&#x27; axis.  Libertarians think on the &#x27;authoritarian vs freedom&#x27; axis.<p>The same event, when viewed through the corresponding filter, has different interpretations.')
