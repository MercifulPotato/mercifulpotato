Item(by='psykotic', descendants=None, kids=[24903701], score=None, time=1603757499, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24897419, text='&gt; The flip side of this technique is to be careful about blowing up the size of the final executable since everything gets included.<p>Yeah, the 1970s linker model is very silly like this. The good news is that link-time optimization will handle this for you nowadays without any special per-symbol mark-up. As an experiment, put an unused &#x27;deadfunc&#x27; function definition in a file called ltotest.c and then compare<p><pre><code>    gcc -O2 ltotest.c -o ltotest &amp;&amp; (objdump -D ltotest | grep deadfunc)\n</code></pre>\nand<p><pre><code>    gcc -O2 -flto ltotest.c -o ltotest &amp;&amp; (objdump -D ltotest | grep deadfunc)</code></pre>')
