Item(by='mason55', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608586948, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25499245, text='Ah, in the context of this conversation &quot;pre-money&quot; means the value of the company before taking into account the cash raised in the current round.  It doesn&#x27;t mean a company that&#x27;s never taken any outside investment.  It&#x27;s perfectly reasonable to talk about a pre-money valuation that has a market price.
