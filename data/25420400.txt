Item(by='spamizbad', descendants=None, kids=[25420800, 25422306, 25420529], score=None, time=1607967325, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25420118, text='I think people should pay more attention to the LPDDR4X RAM that&#x27;s tightly coupled to the CPU and likely has significantly less latency and more bandwidth than anything on the Ryzen or i9. This is critical to keeping their wide design fed, and if you swapped this out with standard DDR3200 you&#x27;d see some lackluster numbers.<p>Both Intel and AMD try to overcome the memory wall with larger caches and clever prefetching, which is going to chew up more die space and power than x86 decoders.')
