Item(by='mkl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609506613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25602938, text='A prompt in this context is just the side of the flashcard that <i>prompts</i> you to remember the &quot;other side&quot;&#x2F;answer.  They can be questions, sentences with words missing, or many other things.  E.g. for learning a language a prompt could be the English word, prompting you to remember the translation.  Gwern calls them &quot;questions&quot; in his article, but &quot;prompt&quot; is more general, given that they&#x27;re often not phrased as questions.<p>There are many examples of prompts and answers in the main article, with blue backgrounds, and prompts about the article&#x27;s content with red backgrounds.')
