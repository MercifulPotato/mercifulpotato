Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602694292, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24774300, text='When metal tears, there’s a shockwave that sends most of the energy of the initial tear across the material.  Like a martial artist breaking board, most of the effort goes into the initial break, and the rest is a much smaller input.<p>It’s why structural members on planes and the feet of the Starship prototypes have holes. It arrests the fracture.<p>Satellites won’t have as much material that behaves this way, so I wouldn’t count in too much KE being converted.')
