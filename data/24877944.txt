Your copyright is protected regardless of the strength of your security measures. But it is also, separately, illegal to circumvent these measures. This latter prohibition becomes more far-reaching and has more collateral damage the weaker the security measures it applies to, which is worrying.<p>Outlawing the methods of committing a crime also (instead of just the crime) requires a balancing act between their legitimate and illegitimate uses, which seems precariously absent when talking about technically laughable “security measures” that can be circumvented by pressing F12.')
