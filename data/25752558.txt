It&#x27;s not just companies, government agencies also have similar needs due to sunshine and open-access laws.<p>The alternative to having a document retention policy is to just have employees delete things in an arbitrary manner. It&#x27;s not just CYA for leaving inconvenient doc lying around, it&#x27;s also to ensure the proper retention in the event of a preservation order or public records request.<p>Some companies go way further than others. One place I worked included &quot;notes of a personal nature (eg, birthday cards)&quot; in the retention policy and let us keep them for a week or two before destruction.')
