Item(by='wwarner', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607457223, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25348425, text='IANAL, but the GPL2 allows bundling and &quot;tivoization&quot; [0] with proprietary code, whereas the GPLv3 tries to prevent it. So, with v2, you can ship your proprietary application with an unmodified GPL&#x27;d tool along with it, but with v3 you can&#x27;t. For v2, the copyleft applies to modifications of the tool, for v3 the fear is that the copyleft applies to all the software that touches the tool.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tivoization#GNU_GPLv3" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tivoization#GNU_GPLv3</a>')
