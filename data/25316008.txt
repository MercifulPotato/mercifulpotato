For example: double-shift in IntelliJ or CMD-Shift-O in many Mac apps like Fork, Things and IA-Writer.\n- System wide search tools like Alfred and Launchbar will allow you to quickly access Files and execute Workflows\n- _Every_ Menubar in MacOS is searchable. Just press a shortcut and type what you want to do.\n- ...and let&#x27;s not forget that keyboard shortcuts exist.<p>The great thing is that you get all the power of a keyboard workflow, without losing the accessibility of a GUI.')
