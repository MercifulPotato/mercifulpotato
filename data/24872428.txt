Item(by='darkerside', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603478069, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24872078, text='I use the term to describe the introduction of unnecessary abstractions in the name of hypothetical future use cases. For example, using an enumerated collection of values where a boolean would suffice.<p>It could also refer to building resilience to load before it is necessary. Example, distributed databases with failover and recovery capabilities for a side project.<p>Poke holes all you want, those are bad examples, but they should illustrate the point. Over engineering is a real phenomenon.')
