Racism and Discrimination are different (but related) concepts.<p>Discrimination is making choices that negatively impact a group based on some criteria.<p>Racism is discrimination plus systemic power imbalances that enforce this discrimination.<p>One can be discriminatory towards the powerful group (e.g. white people in the US for instance), but not racist towards them.')
