Item(by='caballeto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606512853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25228147, text='It is absolutely possible to be not paid at all. The minimum wage law mandates salary ~$22k, but the real minimum wage is always zero. Many businesses will decide to hire less instead of hiring employees for higher rates than free market could propose. Hence the differences in unemployment levels among youth especially: ~20% in France, ~9% in US.')
