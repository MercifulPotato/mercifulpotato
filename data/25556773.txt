Item(by='konjin', descendants=None, kids=[25557931, 25556886], score=None, time=1609131051, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25555102, text='This isn&#x27;t a Lisp, this is a paren based serialization of the syntax tree of an Algol like language.<p>Even the readme says as much: The syntax of wax is inspired by WebAssembly Text Format (wat), hence the name. Though it uses S-expressions reminiscent of the Lisp family, it is actually quite imperative and most resemblant of C in its design. The idea of transpiling to many languages is inspired by Haxe.')
