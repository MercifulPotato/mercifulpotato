Item(by='antpls', descendants=None, kids=[25611513, 25611374], score=None, time=1609574902, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25605156, text='Imagine if research was like github, but deduplicated : a giant graph database of unique facts, hypothesis, assumptions, proofs and experiment results, where anyone can contribute, from fixing a typo to publishing experiment dataset.<p>You would reference to previous knowledge without having to think about citing authors, because it would be automatically generated from the graph.<p>Then we would have hundreds of &quot;knowledge verifiers&quot;, like we have bots scanning github, and ultimately bots deriving new knowledge and contributing to the graph.<p>The whole world would be building a shared database of knowledge, without any duplicated effort.<p>Most of the individual facts in this database would probably be boring (like the trivial facts in logic or mathematic), but once in a while, an interesting and important fact would be found.')
