Item(by='fennecfoxen', descendants=None, kids=[25012771], score=None, time=1604704584, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25012007, text='Roguelikes tend to have a text-based <i>map</i>, like: you have a location on an X-Y grid; some cells of the grid have walls and things. Also, time moves forward at your own pace.<p>MUDs vary widely, but <i>typically</i> operate by discretizing things into &quot;rooms&quot;, often with description in writing rather than as a map layout; the various &quot;rooms&quot; form a graph. If you&#x27;ve played Colossal Cave Adventure, like that (but with fewer puzzles and more combat). Also, time moves forward at the server&#x27;s preferred pace.')
