Item(by='neor', descendants=None, kids=[24685260, 24685339], score=None, time=1601880625, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24684091, text='The FIA has made a rule now that any new&#x2F;innovative finding on a car gets banned the next season.<p>So if a team develops something, they can run it for one year after which it&#x27;s banned from the sport.<p>All of this is to cut cost for the smaller teams; take the DAS system of Mercedes (Double Axis Steering). If the FIA doesn&#x27;t ban it all teams need to be running it next year, or they fall behind. However its a complex system and the small teams (Haas, Williams, Alfa Romeo for example) might not have the money to research and build it.')
