The comment <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;bugs.documentfoundation.org&#x2F;show_bug.cgi?id=112888#c1" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;bugs.documentfoundation.org&#x2F;show_bug.cgi?id=112888#c...</a> links to several more.')
