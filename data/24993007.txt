Item(by='eindiran', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604523595, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24990473, text='Wow, there&#x27;s a lot here:<p>&gt; Denmark will cull its mink population of up to 17 million after a mutation of the coronavirus found in the animals spread to humans, the prime minister said on Wednesday.<p>&gt; Health authorities found virus strains in humans and in mink which showed decreased sensitivity against antibodies, potentially lowering the efficacy of future vaccines, Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen said.<p>&gt; “We have been informed by Denmark of a number of persons infected with coronavirus from mink, with some genetic changes in the virus,” WHO said in a statement emailed to Reuters in Geneva. “The Danish authorities are investigating the epidemiological and virological significance of these findings.”<p>The country already had plans in place to ban mink farming, paying farmers to get out of the business, but this will accelerate things a lot.')
