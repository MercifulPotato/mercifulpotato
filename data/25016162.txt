I would guess you didn&#x27;t mean either.<p>Maybe you meant 1 2 3 4 which are SOH STX ETX EOT, which are transmission control codes for serial communications.<p>Or maybe you meant decimal 28 29 30 31 which are FS GS RS US, which are intended for the purpose of structuring data on magnetic tape.<p>The problem with those low order characters [0] is typing them and seeing them in editors. It&#x27;s not impossible to do, but just not commonly done.<p>0: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Control_character" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Control_character</a>')
