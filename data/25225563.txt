Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606451616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25225538, text='Signing let&#x27;s you prove what you did write, it doesn&#x27;t let you prove what you did not write, since all the signed emails in the world don&#x27;t prove the nonexistence of unsigned emails from the same sender. Now, if you have a regular practice of signing emails, one without a valid signature that purports to be from you is pretty strong evidence that either you didn&#x27;t send it <i>or</i> you anticipated the need for future repudiation, but it doesn&#x27;t itself prove anything about which of those applies.')
