Item(by='arc0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605705883, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25131861, text='Gödel&#x27;s proof does not apply to any single axiomatic system, it applies to any computable set of axioms that can talk about numbers, quantifiers, addition and multiplication. This isn&#x27;t arbitrary, it&#x27;s a set of properties required for just about any interesting mathematics to take place.<p>The formulae are accepted because Gödel gives us a mapping, he proves that we can convert any formula into a number without losing information.')
