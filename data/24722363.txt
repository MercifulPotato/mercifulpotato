Item(by='majormajor', descendants=None, kids=[24722407], score=None, time=1602182897, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24722194, text='If all your costs are inflating, why wouldn&#x27;t you also be inflating your prices in line?<p>This seems to be what happens in restaurants or groceries or other things where we go &quot;wow, you used to get a coke for fifty cents!&quot;<p>A business that can&#x27;t pass that cost on seems to have more fundamental &quot;loss of relative demand compared to the past&quot; issues that would squeeze it dry even without inflation.
