Item(by='8bitsrule', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602639773, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770676, text='Two styles of learning.<p>1) Something that requires expertise: learn by doing, with help from a respected author. Build on that by picking harder stuff to do. Doing makes memory stick.<p>2) Things that are mighty interesting: dig in (<i>much</i> easier with the web), make notes until &#x27;enough-for-now&#x27; ... then add-to-notes as new resources pop up. Oh, and systematize notes so adding - and finding - is easy.')
