Item(by='joiguru', descendants=None, kids=[25924662], score=None, time=1611721848, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25924096, text='It is important to acknowledge that this is a conflict between two ethical ideologies: Deontology&#x2F;Kantian ethics vs Utilitarian ethics.\nThe Deontological argument is that free speech is good, and any effort to curtail it would lead to a domino effect which will be disastrous.\nThe Utilitarian argument is that this curtailing of free speech is acceptable as the alternative is causing more harm than good.\nBoth ideologies have their limitations and criticisms, and there doesn&#x27;t seem to be any clear winner.')
