It can be done with clumsy C-style includes, or more idiomatically composed from `pub use` of items from private modules. `impl` blocks can be anywhere.<p>The point is, files and modules are decoupled. Files just happen to be a convenient default for modules, but they aren&#x27;t semantically special in Rust. There&#x27;s no syntax or privacy boundary specific to files.<p></i>) Cargo.toml with `[lib] path = &quot;&#x2F;dev&#x2F;stdin&quot;` + `echo &#x27;fn main() {}&#x27; | cargo build` happens to work :)')
