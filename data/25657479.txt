Item(by='m12k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609939407, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25657395, text='The scary thing about automation isn&#x27;t the technology itself. It&#x27;s that it breaks the tenuous balance of power between those who own and those who work - if the former can just own robots instead of hiring the latter, what will become of the latter? The truth is, what&#x27;s scary about that imbalance of power is already true, it&#x27;s just that until now, technological limitations made that imbalance incomplete - workers still had some bargaining power. That is about to go away, and what will be left is the realization that the solution to this isn&#x27;t ludditism, the solution is political. As it always was.')
