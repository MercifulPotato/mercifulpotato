Item(by='m463', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609063729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25545684, text='Yes, being on a project at the beginning is a <i>huge</i> win as far as productivity.<p>You will have a mental model from the beginning, and as decisions are made and code is added, you understand not only how everything works, but why it (currently) works that way.<p>By far the least productive I&#x27;ve been as a developer - looking back it&#x27;s a shame - was working on projects in the MIDDLE of a tech stack.<p>At the bottom, you at least get to understand the hardware.  At the top, you understand the problem and the rest of the stack abstracts stuff for you.<p>In the middle .. it&#x27;s much harder to figure out why you&#x27;re being used from above, or what is going on below you.  So you&#x27;ll be playing catchup for a long time.')
