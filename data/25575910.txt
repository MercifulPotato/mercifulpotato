Item(by='numpad0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609281337, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25573936, text='You can’t get output to be “90-95% correct” from machine translation.<p>People think of language translation as some sort of same dimension transformation but it’s more like re-projection that involve rotation in upper dimension. Simple warping goes only so far, neural networks give some uncanny slurries, human artists add a lot of their own brush strokes and it’s a lossy process both ways.<p>Speech transcription is much more straightforward because speakers are supposed to have corresponding single literal expressions for each segments of voice.')
