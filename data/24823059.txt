Item(by='dexen', descendants=None, kids=[24834348, 24823365, 24825175], score=None, time=1603077801, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24823004, text='I hear your security concerns.<p>In case of Discord, there are three key causes for preferring the desktop version - and I don&#x27;t see any easy workaround to them without adding explicit support to web standards:<p>1) custom audio codecs &amp; filters; Discord is primarily a gaming VC app and the codecs available on the desktop version are superior for purpose of cancelling echo, noise etc. at low latency,<p>2) ability to capture any keyboard &amp; mouse shortcuts for VC activation<p>3) ability to use voice &amp; keyboard shortcut while the browser window is <i>not</i> focused (in particular while in game)<p>Incidentally adding general support for those to general browsers would lead to subtle security bugs &amp; gotchas.')
