In building up a content engine the first piece is always around velocity... just practice getting more out there. Get used to publishing two posts a week, and over time it&#x27;ll come faster. The next step is quality, but using hemingway early on is a good shortcut to level up the quality. I generally recommend a grade level not above 8 for a blog post, and 6 is ideal.<p>Even beyond blog posts I find it is useful for any communication that goes out to some set of people. If you&#x27;re spending time drafting an email to your internal team, asking a favor of someone, just about anything where you really want to level up the quality hemingway is a huge positive impact for not much effort.')
