Item(by='bird_monster', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605746329, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25141066, text='I agree. Some people don&#x27;t want feedback at all. Some people don&#x27;t want feedback _from you_. The same goes with wanting feedback. Being able to decipher who&#x27;s giving feedback and why, and what their possible gain could be is important when navigating complex environments.<p>A manager&#x27;s feedback may be directly oppositional to a teammate&#x27;s feedback. Your manager&#x27;s feedback may be directly oppositional to your Director&#x27;s feedback. Sometimes someone that doesn&#x27;t know what they&#x27;re talking about offers feedback very aggressively for posterity. All feedback is not a gift. Lots of feedback is just noise.<p>I think it&#x27;s important to receive feedback from people you respect or want to be more like. Identifying these people is very important, as they&#x27;ll have tremendous impact on your work. Just because your manager was assigned to you does not mean you respect them or are interested in changing to accommodate their vision.')
