Item(by='mattkrause', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604288606, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24963481, text='Most &quot;failed&quot; pilots don&#x27;t demonstrate that an approach is fundamentally and irredeemably flawed, which could be an interesting paper.<p>Instead, they demonstrate that doing something <i>this specific way</i> is too much of a hassle, too unreliable, or too expensive to answer a particular question in a particular context: skills, budget, timeline, resources, alternative ideas, etc.<p>I don&#x27;t see how you could write pilot results up (&quot;Trust me, I&#x27;m usually pretty good at these sorts of things but this didn&#x27;t work well&quot;?). Meanwhile, disentangling these factors in a rigorous, generalizable way would turn it into an entirely different project.')
