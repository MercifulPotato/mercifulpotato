Their <i>purpose</i> is different. It is the <i>purpose</i> of an open panel to let me pick a file&#x2F;folder location. It is the <i>purpose</i> of a save panel to let me pick a folder to save a file.<p>User interfaces and user experiences go hand in hand. A user interface should be purposeful. It creates a better experience.<p>Sure, you can get used to an open&#x2F;save panel having destructive behaviour and it can be handy — but that doesn’t make it a better user experience. It puts destructive actions front-and-centre, violating the element of least surprise and potentially endangering user data.<p>&gt; Although perhaps Apple has patented it<p>Doubtful.
