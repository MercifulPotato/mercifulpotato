Item(by='mucholove', descendants=None, kids=[25129719], score=None, time=1605642943, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25121071, text='To me, it feels entirely different if you—as a user are aware of what is popular because you have chosen to see it ranked as so.<p>Will it be gamed? Yes.<p>But you might be able to as a user suspect that people are trying to game the system with fake votes.<p>What is undeniable though is that because you as a user are making a choice to see the popularity, you are much more cognizant that something might be at play. Popularity—despite its flaws—is still a good signal and a great place to begin critical thinking if you’re aware that you’re choosing to view it through the popularity lens.')
