Item(by='konjin', descendants=None, kids=[25538778], score=None, time=1608870330, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25528260, text='A rather simple improvement would be to have all the lights flash their binary encoded position for the calibration step. You go down from calibration time O(n) to O(ln(n)). Using red&#x2F;blue would be better than using on off for this since you can get a more accurate position read by averaging ln(n) shots for the location of a light.<p>I believe something similar is used by some projection mapping software.')
