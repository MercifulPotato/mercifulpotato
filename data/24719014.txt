Item(by='veddox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602164511, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24712207, text='The state of the Common Lisp library ecosystem is probably the saddest thing about the language.<p>As the author says, the inbuilt standard library is hopelessly too small for modern requirements - even basics like string manipulation are only cursorily covered. And most of the third-party libraries out there are single-person projects, out of date, undocumented, or all three...<p>The language itself is still fantastic and, IMO, still superior to most other languages out there. But the lack of decent libraries is a major productivity killer.')
