It&#x27;s also about trade within a watershed or water system, because moving large amounts of water for farming in any other way isn&#x27;t economical.<p>Cities are always going to be able to outbid farmers, in some cases by buying farmland for the water rights. The worst-case result of that is that some farmers in dry areas go out of business and more food is imported.<p>Food is a global market, water isn&#x27;t. It&#x27;s easier to import food than water since it&#x27;s much lighter for the price.')
