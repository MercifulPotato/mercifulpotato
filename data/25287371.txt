Item(by='mrslave', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606989274, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25286649, text='The Chinese I have asked who learnt Simplified Chinese characters in school attest to being able to read Traditional Chinese characters with very little additional effort; or even just on the spot (say, when shopping or dining). Those who learnt Traditional Chinese characters seem to have a harder time going in the other direction. (No studies to cite; I have asked a good 30+ people at work, social circles, etc. over the years.)')
