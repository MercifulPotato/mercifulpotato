Item(by='blululu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605071262, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25055564, text='In general I like the idea of random sampling an an efficient means of democratic process. The trouble is that democracy is better at deliberating than promulgating laws.\nI think a better approach would be to have elected representatives craft legislation in congressional committees, and then allow randomly selected citizen committees (a la jury duty) to vote and deliberate on behalf of their district.')
