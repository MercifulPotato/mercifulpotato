Item(by='shoo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611979599, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25965742, text='the next exercise might be to rank the wants from most to least important, &amp; consider if some of those wants are things you are willing to defer if they&#x27;re not immediately available now, and what your longer-term paths to attaining them could be.<p>e.g. for myself, i want autonomy, and my strategy for getting that autonomy (perhaps sub optimal) is building wealth. the wealth in itself isn&#x27;t really an end in itself, just fuel. but the path to build the wealth is grinding away at a high-paying job that happens to have relatively low autonomy and status -- so my path to achieve my longer-term goal is to roughly do the opposite of what i want in the short run.')
