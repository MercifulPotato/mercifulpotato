Item(by='jabl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607541268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25361644, text='I think CNCF, or more generally, Linux Foundation projects are better characterized as public goods. Basically a bunch of companies, many whom are competitors with each other, get together to improve the commodified base upon which they depend. Saves a lot of $$$ that would otherwise be wasted in wheel reinvention or spent on license fees.<p>For the particular case of AWS now offering Kubernetes, is that:<p>- A win for AWS, since they can leech code without contributing back<p>or<p>- A win for Kubernetes contributors and users vs AWS, since now customers have the option of moving their Kubernetes workloads from AWS to other providers.')
