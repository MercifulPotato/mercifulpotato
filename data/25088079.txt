That&#x27;s why the flu shot has to be given every year: it protects against different strains, whatever is predicted to be predominant that year.<p>Natural immunity is much more effective for viruses that don&#x27;t change much: measles, polio, chickenpox, coxsackievirus (hand-foot-mouth), even common-cold coronaviruses.')
