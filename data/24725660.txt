Item(by='mantap', descendants=None, kids=[24726240], score=None, time=1602205467, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24724787, text='I don&#x27;t agree that the steps of an implementation are copyrightable. Different versions of an algorithm are different algorithms. One merge sort algorithm may perform much better than another despite both being merge sort and having the same asymptotic complexity. You could patent a particular version of merge sort.<p>Copyright protects <i>how</i> code is written not what it does. And the <i>how</i> applies just as much to interfaces as implementations.')
