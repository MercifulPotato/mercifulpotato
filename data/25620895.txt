Such imports are wrapped into a module struct (imported modules are just regular structs). The Zig build system takes care of efficiently compiling all this with proper incremental build support (one can explore the zig-cache subdirectory which is maintained by the zig compiler for details).<p>For example, these are the imports in a top-level file:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;ed42fad79c3e9b4432ea7e1ca97d56cba48c0282&#x2F;src&#x2F;pacman.zig#L1-L7" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;ed42fad79c3e9b4432...</a><p>The sokol.zig file groups other modules under a common namespace:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;main&#x2F;src&#x2F;sokol&#x2F;sokol.zig" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;main&#x2F;src&#x2F;sokol&#x2F;sok...</a><p>...which in turn imports &quot;leaf modules&quot; like this:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;main&#x2F;src&#x2F;sokol&#x2F;audio.zig" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;floooh&#x2F;pacman.zig&#x2F;blob&#x2F;main&#x2F;src&#x2F;sokol&#x2F;aud...</a><p>...it&#x27;s one of the most straightforward module systems I&#x27;ve seen yet in any language.')
