Item(by='kepler1', descendants=None, kids=[25897393, 25896944], score=None, time=1611526647, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25896441, text='A constitution is the high level document that your citizens agree are the foundational principles governing the state.  They are a set of general points that nearly everyone can understand at that level and agree to.<p>They are not the minutiae and laws that govern in detail.<p>104 is too many principles, or at the wrong level of detail, to be at the level of a constitution for people to discuss in concept and agree to as general participants in a society.')
