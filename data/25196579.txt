Item(by='beaner', descendants=None, kids=[25197127], score=None, time=1606207977, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25196483, text='Proof-of-stake requires the majority of what is mined has to be sold on the open market. This redistributes mined coin to any user anywhere, rich or poor. If you mine and do not sell, you perpetually consume and fail in short order.<p>Proof-of-stake doesn&#x27;t require this natural, market-driven redistribution. It exchanges it for an inflationary process that further separates the rich and the poor.<p>They&#x27;re fundamentally different.')
