Item(by='acdha', descendants=None, kids=[25084899], score=None, time=1605279776, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25081460, text='The way to think about it is as an amplifier: if you have issues with latency or packet loss they’ll be notably worse over that VPN tunnel. If you have a low-latency connection or aren’t doing latency-sensitive work, it can be okay - downloads won’t be too much different, SSH will be impacted, web browsing is in between.')
