Item(by='psykotic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605064730, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25054715, text='Log-depth circuits are a useful abstraction but the constraints of laying out circuits in physical space imposes a delay scaling limit of O(n^(1&#x2F;2)) for planar circuits (with a bounded number of layers) and O(n^(1&#x2F;3)) for 3D circuits. The problem should be familiar to anyone who&#x27;s drawn a binary tree on paper.')
