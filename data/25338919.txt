Item(by='dllthomas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607381994, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25308200, text='If I find myself in a situation where, if I don&#x27;t get X, I prefer to leave, then an ultimatum is an obvious course: &quot;give me X or I will leave.&quot;<p>It can be to my benefit in negotiation for the other party to <i>think</i> that I am in such a situation, and so I may still say &quot;give me X or I will leave.&quot;<p>If I do not intend to follow through, I am clearly bluffing.<p>If I intend to follow through, but absent this gambit I have preferred to stay even in the absence of X, I think we can still call it a bluff in a weaker sense - I am not misleading about my future actions, but I am misleading about the strength of my preferences.')
