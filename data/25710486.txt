Item(by='xpe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610255337, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25707794, text='It is a naive hope that technology alone can solve major social problems.<p>&gt; It&#x27;s time to restart the cycle with new means of communication ...<p>My response is: yes, innovation can be good, exciting, powerful. But never forget to assess how human nature will respond.<p>&gt; ... with new means of communication that the new establishment cannot police.<p>Perhaps this will arrive, but I doubt it. Governments have many levers to influence behavior. Unless you are an anarchist, I suspect you support some role for goverment.<p>Even if a new communication form arises that cannot be policed -- be careful what you hope for. Free speech is not a panacea.')
