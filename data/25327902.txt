Item(by='radihuq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607299343, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25326961, text='I&#x27;ve been playing around with trying to arbitrarily quantify perceived intellect.<p>Here&#x27;s my working formula at the moment:<p>`P = A * SUM([B * C * TopicA] + [B * C * TopicB] + [B * C * TopicC] [...])`<p>Where:<p>P = Perceived intellect<p>A = Coefficient that measures the ability to connect different concepts<p>B = Depth of knowledge of a topic<p>C = Ability to break down topic into concepts<p>The people I perceive to be smart come up with logically-sound answers to questions they don&#x27;t know the answer to, based on past knowledge.<p>If I ask `what is X?`, they say `I don&#x27;t know what X is, but based on Y and Z, it might be ...`<p>So if you&#x27;re trying to identify if this person can come off as smart, perhaps a tactic could be asking them a question about a domain they are not an expert in.')
