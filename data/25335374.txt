Item(by='jskajakzkjx', descendants=None, kids=[25336272, 25336666, 25336138], score=None, time=1607366062, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25334905, text='Somebody once said that that Apple’s business model is charging a very high markup for flash memory (currently it’s $50 per 64 GB to upgrade storage on an iPhone or MacBook). The margin on other parts is also high. The user-upgradeable Mac Pro provides an escape hatch from these expensive upgrades. Therefore the entry price has to be much higher to make up for it. An affordable tower would cannibalize Mac Pro sales by providing businesses with a cost-saving opportunity that’s too good to refuse.')
