Item(by='nine_k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603508159, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24873119, text='If you want to serve private content, you have to encrypt stuff and authenticate users.<p>If you want to serve public content, you <i>still</i> need to encrypt it to avoid a MITM attack.<p>SSH solves the first problem trivially, but is slower. HTTPS solves the second problem trivially, and the first problem with some work (login flow or client certificates).<p>I only see a case for FTP for underpowered hardware like older RPi serving stuff locally. But I suspect the bottleneck would be in the USB-connected NIC, not even im the CPU.')
