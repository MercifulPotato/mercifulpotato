Opting out can be made confusing and difficult and time-consuming.<p>I&#x27;ve gone to websites that list over 300 3rd parties that they share data with. Opting out means going to every one of those 3rd parties one at a time and hunting for their opt-out link. And those come with a warning:<p><i>If you clear your cookies, you&#x27;ll have to do this again. And the opt-out only applies on this browser on this device.</i><p>Suddenly, the consumer has a part-time job that they didn&#x27;t ask for.')
