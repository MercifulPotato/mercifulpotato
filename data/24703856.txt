Everyone but Indian&#x2F;Chinese H1b holder, get their green card in 1-2 years.<p>That&#x27;s false. Maybe this was true in the past.<p>First, they cannot self-sponsor. So they generally won&#x27;t.<p>Expect the process to start a couple of years before the H1B visa is set to expire.<p>Even if started immediately, just for processing time alone you are looking at around 2 years. 2 to 6 months for the PERM process. It&#x27;s still one year on the absolute best case with no RFEs or audits whatsoever, counting from the moment the application was filed, assuming all documentation is instantly available (like employment verification letters from past workers, translations etc) and assuming lawyer time is zero (which obviously is not the case).<p>Of course, if you are Indian or Chinese, add years or decades to the above.')
