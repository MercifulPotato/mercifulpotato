Without some outward signifier like a cartoon character for an online avatar, which even then wouldn&#x27;t provide any measure of certainty, it would require mind reading.<p>&gt; People who speak genuinely but are passionate, emotional, or aren&#x27;t the best at expressing themselves can have productive conversations. Someone who&#x27;s arguing to win isn&#x27;t worth your time.<p>If you want to get along in some kind of shallow fashion then the former are better, if you want to find truth or open your mind to possibilities then the latter will outstrip the former by a long way. Both are needed in life but to dismiss either as stupid or bad intentioned seems a stretch.')
