Goldwater flipped the deep south (GA SC AL MS and LA). Nixon noticed and ran the same strategy in &#x27;68, by which point the entire south, wall-to-wall, was lined up behind the southern strategy (the states that didn&#x27;t go for Nixon went for Wallace). George Romney would have opposed AOC, but so does Gina Raimondo.)<p>At any rate, when people debate which is the &quot;racist&quot; party, they almost invariably ignore the fact that the US was a 4-party polity in the mid-century, not a 2-party polity. What&#x27;s changed since then is that we have, from 1980 through today, sorted down to 2.')
