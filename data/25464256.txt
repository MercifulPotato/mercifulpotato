Item(by='ReactiveJelly', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608270312, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25463518, text='Well, you could write a fresh SQLite DB on every Save command, and blow away the old one, thus losing all of SQLite&#x27;s nice fsync handling.<p>Or if your data structures can be modeled as vectors or ordered maps, use SQLite directly as much as possible. It _is_ a database engine, so it&#x27;s already got btrees for fast lookups.<p>Hell, one time I played back video from a SQLite file. Not HD, but it was a peculiar format where I couldn&#x27;t use any normal codec, and 30 FPS VGA recorded and played just fine.')
