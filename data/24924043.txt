Item(by='jiggawatts', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603918575, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24917818, text='A hen will lay eggs, which have a fairly direct monetary value. It doesn&#x27;t mean the farmer treats their chickens like customers.<p>In a poor town, a shopkeeper may accept barter instead of cash. Their customers might pay them with eggs, and they would be treated like valued customers even if they&#x27;re too poor to use trade with real money.<p>It&#x27;s a matter of corporate culture on how the consumers are viewed. For largely advertising-driven companies their users are products, even if they pay. For largely product and sales-driven companies their users are customers, even if they&#x27;re in a free tier or a trial account or whatever.')
