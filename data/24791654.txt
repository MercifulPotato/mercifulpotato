You don&#x27;t need consent for cookies that are &quot;strictly necessary&quot; to provide a service, but you can <i>only</i> use that exemption for users who initiate a chat session.<p>FYI this is a concrete example given by a regulatory authority of non-compliant behavior they observe in the wild, so it&#x27;s not just theoretical.')
