Item(by='ajdegol', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608297080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25464603, text='There is a technique I see none of the other comments have hit upon.<p>First, know concentration is a skill, so develop that. I can tell you how to muscle up but if you can&#x27;t pull up then knowing how won&#x27;t help.<p>You are able to convince yourself you&#x27;re interested in something by asking questions about it. Create the space in your brain that wants the knowledge to be in there. Find a question you genuinely want to know the answer to. There&#x27;s usually at least something. This is how I was able to maintain focus on reading atomic physics, funnily enough, it&#x27;s now the most prominent thing I remember from my degree.')
