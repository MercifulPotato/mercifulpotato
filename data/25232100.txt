There is simply a plugin&#x2F;component library for anything and everything you need to do, so relatively simple applications have monstrous dependency trees because it&#x27;s easier to `npm install` the whole kitchen sink and use the little bits you need, than taking some time to understand &amp; implement a smaller, focused alternative that could live in a `util.js` file in your project.<p>At least thanks to tree-shaking and bundler innovations, the end-users don&#x27;t suffer from bloated bundle downloads, but your node_modules folder is still 600MB large.')
