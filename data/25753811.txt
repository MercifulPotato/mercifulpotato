Item(by='ogogmad', descendants=None, kids=[25754389], score=None, time=1610486182, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25753397, text='Popper&#x27;s point is that if someone wants to curtail your right to free speech, you have the right to curtail theirs. On the other hand, if a political opponent respects your right to speak freely, you should do the same for them. It&#x27;s not really a paradox; it&#x27;s about symmetry.<p>By analogy, imagine if someone commits murder. Would putting them to death as punishment also be murder? No. You have the right to life as long as you respect other people&#x27;s right to the same.')
