Item(by='enriquto', descendants=None, kids=[25411779], score=None, time=1607890253, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407574, text='A cool thing that you can do with gdb is to change the values of variables in programs that are already running, or calling their functions.  For example you can update a variable on your <i>parent</i> shell, e.g., &quot;export&quot; the variable $X to your parent (!) :<p><pre><code>    gdb --batch-silent -ex &quot;attach $PPID&quot; -ex &quot;call (int)bind_variable(\\&quot;X\\&quot;,\\&quot;$X\\&quot;,0)&quot;\n</code></pre>\nI confess that I use this trick in my shell scripts more than strictly necessary, just for fun.')
