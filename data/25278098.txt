Item(by='dharmab', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606927030, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25277705, text='Horseshoe crabs have resilient immune systems; they do not have adaptive immune systems like humans that have to &quot;learn&quot; how to defend from a new pathogen. Instead, their immune systems are general purpose. This is exactly why their blood is valuable for testing for the presence of pathogens in drug and device testing.')
