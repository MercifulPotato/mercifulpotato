Lots and lots of sunny days.<p>As a counter, I moved to Vancouver which is always 10C+ essentially in the winter, often 15C days, yet <i>rain</i> and <i>cloud</i>.<p>There, I experienced SAD for the first time in my life.  And I ran away, moved back to Ontario, after 10 years of it.<p>And a day on the radio when the announcer said &quot;It&#x27;s now been 32 days since we&#x27;ve seen the sun&quot;.  The guy wasn&#x27;t kidding.  It had been more than a month since the clouds parted during daylight hours.<p>That.  Really.  Sucks.')
