Social or dominant manners are less important, and actually doing the work counts far more.<p>So it&#x27;s quite likely that people who tend towards Asperger&#x27;s or autism not only deal better with working from home, but will also be more successful in their organisation than they&#x27;d be under different circumstances.<p>So milk this while you can, I guess.')
