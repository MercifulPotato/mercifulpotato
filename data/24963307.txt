Item(by='brandmeyer', descendants=None, kids=[24963329], score=None, time=1604271578, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24963001, text='You have a typo: size_t doesn&#x27;t need <i>zero</i> extension.  RISC-V does have 32-bit signed integer arithmetic that unconditionally sign-extends the result from bit 31.  So using a signed integer as a counter (bad practice IMO, but widely recommended in style guides) doesn&#x27;t need anything extra at all.')
