Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610249364, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25707865, text='Colin Kapaernic is probably the best known activist against police brutality towards black people in the USA.<p>He defines it as abolishing the police.<p>I have come across many activists who want to abolish the police.<p>I would assume that most vaguely left leaning people wouldn’t support that as a policy, but it is certainly not a ‘fringe’ definition from the point of view of the activist community <i>with whom it is most associated.</i><p>There is absolutely nothing disingenuous about recognizing that mainstream activists <i>do</i> mean to abolish the police.<p>We have to accept that both definitions are in use.<p>What <i>is</i> disingenuous, is to dismiss or deny the idea that anyone other than opponents of it think it means abolish the police.<p>That is essentially a motte-and-bailey tactic, and all it does is stop honest discussion.')
