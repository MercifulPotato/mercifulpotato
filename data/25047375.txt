Item(by='snarfy', descendants=None, kids=[25049097, 25048836], score=None, time=1605023870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25046608, text='There is a little window on the cartridges the printer shines a light through to detect the toner.  When it can see the light you get out-of-toner errors. If you mark over it with a sharpie, it will print again.  I&#x27;ll be the judge of when it&#x27;s out of toner based on how faded the print is.  As infrequently as I print this trick can make a cartridge last months longer.')
