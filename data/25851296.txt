Item(by='uberman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611173364, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25850833, text='Just a heads up that I expected to see this discrepancy explained away via understandable mistrust on the part of Black Americans, so I did a little back of the envelope math based on an NPR survey of people indicating they would be willing to get vaccinated and this article.<p>My conclusion is that given a state like North Carolina with about 30% Black healthcare workers and their assumed willingness to be vaccinated as compared to their White, Asian, and Latinx counterparts I expected to see a 20% rate rather than a 10% rate.<p>To me it seems clear that there is much more than Black apprehension at play here.')
