The author here is fuzzy on applications. Feels like a solution looking for a problem.<p>When it comes to augmenting digital assistants like Alexa or OK Google, standalone semantic knowledge bases like Wikidata [0] seem good enough. And as natural language understanding code gets better at interpreting user queries,\nso too does it get better at ingesting unstructured knowledge, such as raw Wikipedia articles, without need to resort to structured meta-tags.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wikidata.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Wikidata:Main_Page" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wikidata.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Wikidata:Main_Page</a>')
