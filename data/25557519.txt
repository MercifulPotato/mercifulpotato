Item(by='bobthepanda', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609141835, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25556212, text='One downside of stoops is the existence of the stairs, which presents an additional burden if<p>- you are carrying anything big, bulky, or heavy, like groceries or a stroller<p>- it is snowing or raining or freezing, in which case the stairs become slick and quite dangerous<p>- you have issues with mobility; you may be old, pregnant, or have lost mobility due to either something temporary (requiring the use of crutches) or permanent<p>And some people deal with multiples of these issues. These are issues in most housing forms (most houses have some kind of steps) but NYC-style stoops can run much larger than a typical modern set of steps, where there are probably only two or three from the path to the door.')
