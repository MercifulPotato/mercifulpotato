Item(by='manfredo', descendants=None, kids=[25727699], score=None, time=1610320917, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25721357, text='I agree that the goal is to curb the spread of such views, not so much to change the minds of those who already harbor them. But I still think it&#x27;s having the opposite effect. The streisand effect is real and likely playing out here.<p>Thanks to this ban, a lot of people are wondering if the claims of electoral fraud were actually disproven or if evidence of legitimate fraud was suppressed. The bans make it very easy to appeal to uncertainty: you can&#x27;t find evidence of fraud not because fraud didn&#x27;t happen but because the evidence of fraud was purged.')
