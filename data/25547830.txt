Most Dockerfiles I come across are very much <i>not</i> written in a robustly reproducible way. The stacking filesystems model is really not very flexible (if you want to <i>compose</i> two docker images with dissimilar bases to be used together you have to resort to manually specifying which files to copy across from each base).
