Item(by='barrkel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602595134, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24764228, text='Developing UIs with toolkits like Delphi&#x27;s VCL was much faster than web development - as long as your UI was form-oriented and you had a pre-made, configurable control for everything you wanted to add.<p>But as soon as you get down to needing to do your own drawing, a complexity cliff rises up.<p>The web is the other way around. With sufficient styling you can get a UI which matches your vision without having to write a lot of imperative code, but if you want to put a simple application together quickly, there&#x27;s a real risk of analysis paralysis owing to how much control you have.')
