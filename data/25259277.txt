Item(by='dredmorbius', descendants=None, kids=[25259513], score=None, time=1606771947, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25258988, text='Yes, the Chappe optical telegraph existed, but it had limitations:<p>- Use was all but entirely limited to France.  On campaign, mesages would revert to horseback.<p>- It was stopped by inclement weather.<p>- Net throughput was low, though I don&#x27;t have a source on message traffic.<p>- Messages would have been brief ... one might say <i>telegraphic</i>.  Little detail could be conveyed.<p>- Access was limited to government business.  At least some of Napoleon&#x27;s correspondents were likely denied access.<p>As counsel to others, Napoleon&#x27;s mail-handling protocol would be subject to the long transits I describe.')
