External calls we don’t need the result of immediately get kicked out to an external queue, but we haven’t seen any issues with DB calls blocking the whole server.<p>Long or slow DB connections are an issue for individual request time and DB load though (the biggest DBs we run handle 1000-2000 concurrent connections).')
