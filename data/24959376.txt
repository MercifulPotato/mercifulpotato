Item(by='azernik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604237450, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24952673, text='You could get an equivalent of price signals by another means - rejection of products that were unfit for use. The Soviet military was able to do this, and so got equipment that was both of good quality and was of the correct mix of products. Civilian enterprises did not have this avenue available, and so ended up getting products of the minimum quality that would still skate by under the Plan&#x27;s rules.')
