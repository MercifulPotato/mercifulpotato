Item(by='ricardobeat', descendants=None, kids=[25798771, 25798955], score=None, time=1610757331, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25797624, text='As others have already pointed out, it&#x27;s a bit of a misconception about React, certainly not helped by it&#x27;s name - it is not truly reactive.<p>Think about all the ways you can trigger a re-render: either `setState`, `forceUpdate`, dispatching an event, or some other kind of update function from a state management library. It doesn&#x27;t react to any kind of assignment like would happen in Vue or Svelte. They do exactly the same as your `rerender` function here, it&#x27;s just nicely hidden. There will be no more of a mess of event listeners than you&#x27;d get using React.')
