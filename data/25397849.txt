Item(by='bswinnerton', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607776434, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25397610, text='Generally speaking you request them from a regional internet registry (RIR). In North America, the RIR is ARIN <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.arin.net&#x2F;resources&#x2F;guide&#x2F;request" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.arin.net&#x2F;resources&#x2F;guide&#x2F;request</a>. Bear in mind it’s harder and harder to get V4 addresses from an RIR these days, but it’s still possible. Once they are allocated to you, you have to find a place to announce them. Some “cloud” based solutions are Neptune Networks (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;neptunenetworks.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;neptunenetworks.org&#x2F;</a>) or Vultr (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.vultr.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.vultr.com&#x2F;</a>).')
