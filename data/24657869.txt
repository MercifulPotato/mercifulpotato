It was just a trivial example to illustrate the macro.<p>(lets (a b) (list 1 2) c (+ a 3) (d e f) (some-func a b c) (* d e f))<p>It destructures automatically. The equivalent traditional macro might look like<p>(destructuring-bind* [[(a b) (list 1 2)] [c (+ a 3)] [(d e f) (some-func a b c)]] (* d e f))<p>which is unreadable. And yeah, normally it&#x27;s indented properly and yada-yada, and people use editor plugins like paredit to make it not such a pain to type, but still. It adds up.')
