Item(by='aliceryhl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604335712, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24968610, text='The point of a newtype is that it allows you to create a wrapper around some other type for one of the following two purposes:<p>1. Marking the kind of data to prevent mixing up values of different kinds. (e.g. Email)<p>2. Outlawing some of the values of the underlying type. (e.g. OneToFive)<p>I do not think it being a tuple-struct or not is important at all when deciding whether something uses the newtype pattern, as long as it has a single field.')
