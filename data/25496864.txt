Item(by='jech', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608570487, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25496372, text='By default, we bind to &quot;:8443&quot;, which is Go&#x27;s notation for port 8443 on all IP addresses, IPv4 and IPv6.\nGalène&#x27;s WebSocket is located at &#x2F;ws, and I believe that nginx requires that you configure this specially.  See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nginx.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;websocket-nginx&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nginx.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;websocket-nginx&#x2F;</a>.<p>An alternative to reverse proxying is to use HTTP redirects: you can define a group as<p>{&quot;redirect&quot;:&quot;<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;otherserver.example.com:8443&#x2F;group&#x2F;groupname" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;otherserver.example.com:8443&#x2F;group&#x2F;groupname</a>&quot;}<p>and Galène will send an HTTP redirect whenever somebody attempts to join this group.<p>I&#x27;ll be glad to continue this conversation on the mailing list (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;lists.galene.org&#x2F;postorius&#x2F;lists&#x2F;galene.lists.galene.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;lists.galene.org&#x2F;postorius&#x2F;lists&#x2F;galene.lists.galene...</a>).')
