Item(by='posguy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607491152, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25355106, text='Not everyone has trouble with AGPLv3 being the core of their SaaS business.<p>Just because your core business software is AGPLv3 doesn&#x27;t mean any other business will be able to build a coherent business model atop it, look at Kazoo by 2600Hz or Iris by Lowes.<p>Knowing how to properly configure and maintain said code in production, creating integrations with third party vendors (that your competition likely can&#x27;t interact with on the same terms) and marketing to build mindshare among potential customers is a common way to offer your core software as AGPLv3 yet have no competitors or few competitors use your core software.')
