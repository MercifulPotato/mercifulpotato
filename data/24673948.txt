Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601751467, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24673877, text='&gt; Two photons going the same direction have no mass, but two photons in different directions do have mass.<p>This is not limited to photons, although the first part, &quot;no mass&quot; is due to the rest mass of photons being zero.<p>Two objects <i>of any kind whatsoever</i> have more mass going in different directions than going in the same direction.')
