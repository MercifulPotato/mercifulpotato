Item(by='rhn_mk1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607273998, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25319242, text='My take on it is that (most) code isn&#x27;t a picture, so drawing with it (alignment especially) is missing the point that code is a form of text instructions.<p>If anything, the editor should &quot;render&quot; the text in a form that is pleasant to the eye, divorcing the meaning from the presentation.<p>Lining up things too much is a &quot;works for me&quot; way of baking presentation into the data, where the writer assumes that because it looks good o their setup, it will look well for the reader too.<p>Similarities can be seen to building web pages on tables, sending around Word documents, or hard-wrapping emails.')
