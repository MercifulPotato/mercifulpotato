The metric within is &quot;HAQ Index&quot; which stands for &quot;Healthcare Access and Quality&quot;. I.e., access is part of the measure. In the US everybody has access, which is part of the problem that drives costs higher.<p>E.g., if you don&#x27;t have insurance you can walk into an emergency room and get treated even if it&#x27;s not an emergent situation.
