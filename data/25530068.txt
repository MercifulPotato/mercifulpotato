Item(by='EvanAnderson', descendants=None, kids=[25533519], score=None, time=1608834918, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25529688, text='Why does everybody keep data hanging around forever? It&#x27;s easier. You don&#x27;t have to think about it. Just keep kicking the files onto new media every few years &#x2F; at a new server refresh.<p>I did some IT work for a plastic surgery practice in the US many years ago. I was adding some storage to an existing server. I was shocked to see that the practice was keeping all their before &#x2F; after photos online going back years. Not encrypted. Hanging out in Windows file shares with lax permissions.<p>It certainly gave me pause.<p>Maybe some software providers in this space will think about handling this better.')
