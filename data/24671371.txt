Item(by='jka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601726141, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671215, text='Regardless of whether the contributor is paid, I think that feature requests and bug reports can be compared to feature implementations.<p>If the feature request includes, for example, technical architecture and product design analysis, and illustrates that it has considered various other options, it could be highly valuable.<p>A bug report could have involved significant debugging to identify approximate causes, affected versions, and potentially-related commits that introduced&#x2F;regressed the bug.<p>All of the above applies to documentation and support commentary too.  There is a spectrum with any contribution from &#x27;minimal&#x27; to &#x27;thorough&#x27;, and there&#x27;s not necessarily a causal link between high-value contributions and paid work.')
