Item(by='LinuxBender', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605274135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25078379, text='It might also be worth mentioning that measuring 25-hydroxy is also an approximation at best.  That still has to be converted to 1,25-dihydroxyvitamin by the kidneys.  People with high insulin resistance (diabetics, pre-diabetics) or kidney damage tend to have problems converting 25-hydroxy.  Measuring 25-hydroxy is primarily used for toxicity and even that has variables that are difficult to measure, so a statistical baseline is applied.')
