Item(by='jjav', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608089665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25426874, text='Your understanding is not correct.<p>When you buy the house your tax rate is 1% (+ local fees) of your purchase price. Every year thereafter, it goes up by at least 2%.<p>The assessed value will go up by 2% (that&#x27;s prop13) so the base tax will go up by 2%. However, local city and county can add their own fees on top of that (so they do), therefore in many years the actual dollar amount increase will be more than 2%.<p>(Source: my property tax bill over the last 23 years.)')
