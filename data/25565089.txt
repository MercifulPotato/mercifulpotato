Item(by='blackrock', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609201659, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564974, text='True, on hard drives, the controller board will eventually fail. The solders might decompose or the circuitry might fail. Then you’re left with a platter full of randomized bits.<p>But on tape, the controller mechanism is separated from the storage medium. The controller mechanism is inside the drive readers itself, which will eventually fail.<p>So for both, it’s a trade off. They’re both going to eventually fail.')
