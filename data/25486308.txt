Item(by='DerDangDerDang', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608473730, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25483815, text='The latest generation of consoles have (or at least have been slated to have for some time) very significant improvements in IO bandwidth.<p>To the extent that it may make sense to use file formats that  map very closely to in-memory formats, or to duplicate data, even at the cost of file size. If you can stream data faster than you could feasibly decompress it, and if having a flat format is faster than a normalised de-duplicated one, a lot of common sense ideas are inverted.')
