Item(by='tzs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607099205, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25302981, text='&gt; Under new Section 97.23, each license will have to show the grantee&#x27;s correct name, mailing address, and email address.<p>So mailing address will still be required?<p>Keep in mind that license information in public, so if you use your home address as your mailing address then you will be making it very easy for anyone to tie your email address to your home address.<p>If this matters to you, use a mailing address&#x2F;email address combination that you do not mind being tied together publicly.')
