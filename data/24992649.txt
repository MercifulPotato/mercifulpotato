Item(by='marcosdumay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604520984, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24990938, text='Self-reporting is a perfectly reasonable kind of measurement.<p>It has some error margin and bias that must be corrected, just like any kind of measurement.<p>It measures some things better than others, just like any kind of measurement.<p>It must be applied with a serious and conscious understanding of its limitations, just like any kind of measurement.<p>The underlying fact is that social sciences are hard. And science reporting sucks even for the easy stuff. Society would lose a lot if social scientists just placed a universal block on self-reported data, but maybe we would gain if journalists did it.')
