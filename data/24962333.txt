Item(by='renewiltord', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604263370, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24962121, text='About the only things worth correcting are when the externalities of someone&#x27;s incorrect opinion are significant. For instance, if someone mandated the ban of vaccines that&#x27;s bad for us all.<p>But if someone not at my company thinks that the best tool to write a web application in is a hand rolled web server framework in C99 then it&#x27;s advantageous to me to let him fuck those guys up.<p>About anything where loss is internalized properly to the party participating I think it&#x27;s best to let that party kill themselves over it.<p>Unless, of course, they&#x27;ll pay you lots of cash to tell them the truth.<p>The same behaviour is desirable for the opposite reason in your high-trust environments. There you don&#x27;t produce citations. You are hinting at the direction of truth. The hint is way more important than the cite.')
