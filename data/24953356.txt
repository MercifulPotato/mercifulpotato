Item(by='cle', descendants=None, kids=[24954387], score=None, time=1604165300, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24951347, text='A computer is a mutation machine, you cannot escape mutation by hand-waving it away.  If you are writing programs in which you can achieve concurrency without threads and shared memory, it’s because you’re building on the shoulders of all the engineers who didn’t hand-wave it away.  Many of us, due to product requirements, don’t have the luxury of using higher level abstractions like that.')
