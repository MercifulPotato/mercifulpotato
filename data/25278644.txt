Item(by='NoOneNew', descendants=None, kids=[25279085, 25278826], score=None, time=1606929435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25278437, text='Being &quot;well diversified&quot; is subjective. But in freelance&#x2F;contracting, the rule of thumb is to never have a customer account for more than 25% of your yearly income. You diversify your client base so when one walks away, it&#x27;s never too big of a hit.<p>Ads make up 80% of their revenue. That&#x27;s not diversified. That&#x27;s an ad company that dabbles in other things. If Shell or BP&#x27;s revenue was 80% oil and 20% solar, wouldn&#x27;t you still call them oil companies that dabble in solar?')
