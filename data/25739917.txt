Item(by='theamk', descendants=None, kids=[25740720, 25741045], score=None, time=1610408763, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25737739, text='Correction: TCP keepalives, ssh server keepalives, and ssh client keepalives are three distinct and independent mechanisms. You only need one.<p>I usually just do client keepalives as they are easiest to set up. Server keepalives are good if you are worried about “forgotten” clients. TCP keepalives are usually not worth it IMHO.')
