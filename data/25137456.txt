Item(by='chhamilton', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605711591, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25131580, text='(Chromium dev here.)<p>We don&#x27;t throttle workers (that could change, but we don&#x27;t have firm plans here yet).<p>All things being equal, if you move complex work to workers we&#x27;re happy because it frees up the main thread to handle input events, compositing, etc. Just because your tab is in the background doesn&#x27;t mean your renderer process isn&#x27;t sharing a main thread with a tab that is in the foreground, so in this case it can improve foreground tab performance.<p>Also, spreading a fixed amount of work across more cores and fewer wake-ups is actually more efficient from a power point of view (assuming the wake-ups are synchronized).')
