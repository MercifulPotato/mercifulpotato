Item(by='Measter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602355459, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24738603, text='One thing to bear in mind is that the libc and winapi crates are bindings to the libc and Windows C headers, so every single function signature in there will be marked unsafe because it&#x27;s FFI, so those will be heavily impacting your results.<p>You could also avoid some false positives in comments by searching for &quot;unsafe fn&quot; and &quot;unsafe {&quot;, rather than just the word &quot;unsafe&quot;, as those are the only tokens (to my knowledge) that can follow &quot;unsafe&quot;.')
