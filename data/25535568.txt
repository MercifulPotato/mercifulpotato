The reason is that the 8087 FP co-processor implements a stack machine with single byte instructions for common floating point operations.  Have a look at the insanely great demo Pyrit (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.pouet.net&#x2F;prod.php?which=78045" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.pouet.net&#x2F;prod.php?which=78045</a>) which includes documented source code for a simple raytracer in 256 bytes.')
