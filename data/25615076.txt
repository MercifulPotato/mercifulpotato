Item(by='SpicyLemonZest', descendants=None, kids=[25615270, 25615287, 25615126], score=None, time=1609612319, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25615038, text='Inflation is by definition an increase in prices. The amount of money in the economy is called the &quot;money supply&quot;. Some economists do argue that the money supply is the primary factor in determining inflation, but that&#x27;s very different than saying that increases in the money supply <i>are</i> inflation, because the need for money in the economy (usually expressed in terms of the &quot;velocity of money&quot;) can vary.')
