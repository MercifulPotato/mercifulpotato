Item(by='jjk166', descendants=None, kids=[25215218], score=None, time=1606336359, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25213447, text='The goal they are trying to achieve is ignition, which is the point where the fusion reactions they directly cause with the lasers release enough energy to in turn lead to more fusion reactions. It&#x27;s the same principle as using a spark plug to ignite gasoline in an engine: once some of the fuel ignites, that will cause the rest to burn. Getting that initial spark to light is hard, but going from ignition to net-power out is comparatively easy.')
