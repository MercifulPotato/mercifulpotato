It was a means to try to put other people at ease and signal &quot;I&#x27;m just plain folks and not perfect and please don&#x27;t be intimidated. Please relax.&quot;<p>Later in life it only served to convince people I was a loser and&#x2F;or had terrible self-esteem issues (or both). I&#x27;m so sure I&#x27;m better than you that I&#x27;m trying to bring myself down to your level and seem approachable!&quot;<p>0_o<p>Yeah, no. Just no.')
