Item(by='skohan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605048244, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25051977, text='I don&#x27;t know about that, I think the confident move would have been to release the new chips along with a big design update.<p>As you say, I think the average consumer doesn&#x27;t understand the difference between an Intel chip and an Apple chip, and will probably not understand what if anything has changed with these new products.<p>I would say developers would be the group which would be most anxious about an architecture change (which is probably why this announcement was very technically-oriented), and developers on average are probably going to understand that design changes and architecture changes basically orthogonal, and thus won&#x27;t be comforted that much by a familiar design.<p>On the other side, average consumers probably aren&#x27;t all that anxious due to the arch change, and <i>would</i> be more convinced that something new and exciting was happening if it actually <i>looked</i> different.')
