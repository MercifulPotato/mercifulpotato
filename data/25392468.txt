Item(by='Kliment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607726397, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25392289, text='Challenge studies get performed on non-human (animal) test subjects (this was done for the Biontech&#x2F;Pfizer vaccine), and there&#x27;s a volunteer human challenge study planned in the UK for January. A challenge study is one where you intentionally give a live virus to your subjects.<p>Arguably, releasing your subjects into an environment full of live virus, such as the United States, is a kind of challenge study, which rapidly delivers efficacy data. This is why approval could happen so much faster than usual for these vaccines.')
