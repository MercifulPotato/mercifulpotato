Item(by='forgotmypw17', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606410777, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25221611, text='In my app, I use basic auth in place of captcha.<p>Every useragent going back to IE3 and NN1 support it.<p>I then set a cookie, and users never see the prompt again.<p>Cookies are sent on the first request, before auth happens.<p>In my extensive testing, even the most &quot;non-techy&quot; people are able to complete this task, while most bots fail at it.<p>If any bots start getting through, I can change the password.<p>For visitors I haven&#x27;t met yet, I can give out guest credentials on the 401 page.')
