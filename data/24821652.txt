Item(by='est31', descendants=None, kids=[24822729, 24822517], score=None, time=1603060320, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821569, text='Despite both being conic sections, cutting up an ellipse won&#x27;t yield you parabolas. An ellipse has two focal points to which the sum of the distances is constant, while a parabola has a focal point and a directrix line to which the difference of the distances is constantly 0. Two different things.')
