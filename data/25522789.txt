Item(by='ayankovsky', descendants=0, kids=None, score=1, time=1608761002, title='Recap.dev is an open-source tracing for JavaScript backend technologies', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='Recap.dev gathers information of all the things happening when your backend handles a request.<p>That includes:<p>- Logs related to the request.<p>- External resource calls (databases, APIs) along with timings and request and response payloads.<p>- Function calls with start and end timings.<p>It represents the data in a human-friendly way, so it&#x27;s easy to understand what exactly happened. This makes it much easier to find bottlenecks or understand the root cause of an error. Recap.dev will also build statistics to help you understand the state of your application. It will also notify you of all the errors happening in your application.<p>Check the interactive demo at https:&#x2F;&#x2F;demo.recap.dev&#x2F; for more details of how recap.dev represents collected data.<p>We currently support Serverless Framework, Express, and Express-based frameworks like NestJS and Next.js.<p>We would be overjoyed if you would try it out and give us your feedback. We will also help you set it up for free, contact us at contact@recap.dev')
