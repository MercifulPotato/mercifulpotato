Item(by='protomyth', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609942885, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25655522, text='<i>A good question would be what can be done to fix healthcare in the US?</i><p>I honestly think a good first step would be to break out the concept of long term medications from insurance.  Payment support (or outright buying for low income folks) for medications that are needed long term would go a long way to making sure some very vulnerable parts of the population (diabetics, long-term cancer medication takers) are covered.<p>I don&#x27;t think there is a &quot;one big plan&quot; solution, and carving off parts of the problem would actually be a better way to go.')
