Flash used to be one of Adobe&#x27;s tent-poles!<p>Conveniently, given that any Flash content <i>published as SWF</i> was probably authored at least 10 years ago, you could very likely get away with using such an older version of Flash to view such sites.<p>Rather than tracking one down yourself, I&#x27;d suggest, for best compatibility, just using a VM of an OS that <i>ships with</i> an older version of Flash. For example, later releases of Windows XP.<p>That&#x27;s what the blog One Terabyte of Kilobyte Age (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;oneterabyteofkilobyteage.tumblr.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;oneterabyteofkilobyteage.tumblr.com&#x2F;</a>) uses to generate its screenshots, and they <i>almost</i> always work.')
