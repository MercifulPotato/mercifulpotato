Item(by='bena', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609269879, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25572939, text='Cancer shows us just how bad we are at statistics and risk management.<p>Most lung cancer is a result from smoking. That is true. However, it is also true that most smokers don&#x27;t get lung cancer.<p>So while drinking alcohol will increase your risk of cancer by a substantial amount comparatively, we&#x27;re still comparing really small numbers. An increase of 0.1% to 0.2% is a 100% increase in risk, but you&#x27;re still not likely to get it.<p>And before anyone reads this as a pro-smoking message, there are many other ailments that come with smoking that happen with greater frequency. However, smoking will not guarantee you get lung cancer.')
