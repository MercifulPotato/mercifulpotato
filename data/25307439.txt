Item(by='PeterisP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607113900, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25301890, text='A personal blog would likely qualify for that, however, many personal websites would not pass the test of &quot;no connection to a professional or commercial activity&quot; (from <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gdpr-info.eu&#x2F;recitals&#x2F;no-18&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gdpr-info.eu&#x2F;recitals&#x2F;no-18&#x2F;</a>) - if you sell something on that site, or have ad revenue, or use it as advertisement for your professional consultations, then it&#x27;s not purely household activity.<p>If you have a bootstrapped web startup project that you want to launch (e.g. collecting &quot;pre-sales&quot; signups from a minimum viable product), then it&#x27;s definitely not purely personal or household activity even before you have registered a company.')
