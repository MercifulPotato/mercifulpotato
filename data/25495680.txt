Item(by='latortuga', descendants=None, kids=[25497148], score=None, time=1608563957, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25495281, text='I believe you are misunderstanding how this new feature works in Ruby. You&#x27;ve brought up destructuring assignment in javascript which flows right-side values by key to left-side variables. In Ruby, this new syntax is &quot;rightward&quot; assignment, values flowing from left to right:<p><pre><code>    3 =&gt; x # assigns 3 to x\n    [4, 5] =&gt; [x, y] # assigns 4 to x, 5 to y</code></pre>')
