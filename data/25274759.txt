Item(by='leghifla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606900878, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25274695, text='Usually, the more competition there is in a market segment, the lesser the margin becomes.<p>Let&#x27;s say your customer often &quot;complement&quot; your offer A with B from another company. If the other company faces fierce competition on the market for B, its margin will be low. So, you may be able to capture a little bit more of the customer&#x27;s money: he wants to spend x on both stuff, the less it spends on B, the more it can spend on A.')
