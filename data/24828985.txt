Item(by='zaphar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603130595, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827249, text='I look at it with the rubrik: KISS is for coding. SOLID is for system design.<p>There is overlap obviously but SOLID becomes even more important when you have N+2 systems that interoperate. KISS is one way to help ensure you have a SOLIDly designed system when you are coding it. But it helps to understand the principles in SOLID when you are designing a monolith or a set of microservices.')
