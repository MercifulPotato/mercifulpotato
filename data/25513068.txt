Item(by='PaulDavisThe1st', descendants=None, kids=[25513760], score=None, time=1608683184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25512516, text='You&#x27;re getting very close to the much deeper question: what are these (&quot;rags-to-riches&quot;) stories for? And even more generally, what are <i>stories</i> for?<p>Suppose you drop any and all negative criticism of the story. What&#x27;s left? What&#x27;s that for? What should anyone make of this story? What lessons can they learn? What <i>true</i> lessons can they learn? What <i>invented</i> lessons can they learn (because sometimes we tell stories to convey invented lessons, rather than ones rooted in truth)?<p>What is it that you want someone to get from a story like this, and do you believe that your desired lesson is useful and&#x2F;or true?')
