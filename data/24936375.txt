Item(by='kennywinker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604007786, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24936151, text='not OP, but a superpac is a US political group separate from a candidate. They collect money from random sources in a very unaccountable way, and spend money to get candidates elected or promote specific ideas. The &quot;Super&quot; comes from the fact that after the &quot;citizens united&quot; supreme court ruling these groups have basically no restrictions on the amount of money they can raise or tracking of how they spend it - essentially allowing billionaires and the super rich to buy as much influence as they want.')
