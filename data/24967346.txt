Item(by='ChrisMarshallNY', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604319866, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24965432, text='One of the issues with Google, is that it&#x27;s a &quot;one-stop shop.&quot; There&#x27;s a lot more than just email. A Google account ties together Web services (like their various APIs and analytics), data storage, and also applications.<p>Some are &quot;business-ending&quot; serious, while others may be relatively frivolous and replaceable (like Calendar).<p>That said, some of the linkages can make &quot;frivolous&quot; services more important (like using Calendar to link business appointments, documents, and emails).<p>So, when a Google account gets nuked for a problem in one of the services, they <i>all</i> go into the toilet.')
