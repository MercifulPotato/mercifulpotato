Item(by='jdmichal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607113632, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25307041, text='Yes, this is typically how politics works.  Bills <i>should</i> pass by a very narrow margin.  Excess votes represents potential political energy.  If a bill passes with a large margin, that means that there was probably something else that could have been added that would have lost some of those votes, but not enough to still not pass.  It&#x27;s all a game of The Price Is Right, getting closest to the number but not going over.')
