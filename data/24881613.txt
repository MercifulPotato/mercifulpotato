The airmen had various ways of coping with this, my father&#x27;s way was to simply accept that he wasn&#x27;t going to make it and just do his job as best he could.<p>He told me that after the war, whenever he felt down, he&#x27;d think about what a miracle it was he survived, and would feel better.<p>He lived to be 93.')
