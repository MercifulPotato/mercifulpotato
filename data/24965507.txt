Item(by='TheChaplain', descendants=None, kids=[24965598, 24965596, 24966664, 24965613, 24966109, 24965906, 24965907, 24965551], score=None, time=1604302309, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24965432, text='Tip: Get your own domain from a non-google registrar, then connect to Gmail if you prefer. If you ever are locked out, you can just move the address to another email-provider.<p>Another trick is to use always have forwarding enabled in Gmail, to Outlook.com or another provider. All email will be forwarded despite your account being locked, so you will not miss out on important info.<p>And use Google Takeout for backing up your data, at least once a year.')
