Item(by='dane-pgp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611259433, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25863179, text='It&#x27;s true that long term adoption will likely require &quot;layer 2&quot; solutions, but it&#x27;s the worst form of &quot;premature optimisation&quot; to deliberately limit &quot;layer 1&quot; and force users into a single &quot;official&quot; proposed solution.<p>A better approach would have been to let the block size scale in line with average connection speeds and storage capacities (per dollar), and let multiple competing groups implement different &quot;layer 2&quot; approaches that users can opt in to.')
