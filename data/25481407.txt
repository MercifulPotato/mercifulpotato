As you meet new people you need to think “do they have that sense of humour”. Australia is well know for “if they take the mick it means they like you” but not everyone is like that.<p>The best thing is be yourself but add a layer of “Hope I didn’t offend you - I like to tease sometimes”. And add a tonne of common sense too!')
