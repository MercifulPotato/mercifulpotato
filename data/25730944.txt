Item(by='lbotos', descendants=None, kids=[25733450], score=None, time=1610381774, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25730658, text='The point of SMART is that it&#x27;s a framework to help you communicate your goals. They are often used in management because if someone says &quot;i wanna build x&quot; you and that person may have a different definition of what that means. If you agree on specifics and how you will measure done you&#x27;ve clearly communicated.<p>If you can&#x27;t communicate what you will deliver and by when how can someone know if you&#x27;ve done what you&#x27;ve set out to do?<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Goal#Goal_setting" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Goal#Goal_setting</a> covers some empirical research.<p>For example, &#x27;purpose&#x27; can be nebulous, but a goal should be reasonably specific.')
