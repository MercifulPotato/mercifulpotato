Item(by='bradknowles', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607147429, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25307454, text='I have helped administer the LastPass Enterprise account at my employer. It has a number of features that help make it more easily manageable and scalable.<p>I use 1Password for my personal stuff. I think they’ve got a better trust model than LastPass, and better aligned incentives to keep their stuff secure. But I’ve also tried to set up my wife with 1Password for Families, and it’s a right royal pain in the ass on that side.<p>There is the original “pass” program for *nix&#x2F;Linux type systems, and there are clients that work (more or less) on most other OSes.<p>But I have yet to find a good program that I could unequivocally recommend for teams across all platforms.')
