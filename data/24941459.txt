Item(by='rswail', descendants=None, kids=[24941577], score=None, time=1604060519, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24940485, text='The entire point of patent and copyright is (in the US): &quot;To promote the progress of science and useful arts, by securing for limited times to authors and inventors the exclusive right to their respective writings and discoveries.&quot;<p>So it&#x27;s government granting specific <i>rights</i> over intellectual items not the items themselves.<p>The name is confusing, it&#x27;s rights granted over intellectual property (&quot;writings and discoveries&quot;) to encourage the creators to share them for the common good.')
