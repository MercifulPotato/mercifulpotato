Item(by='atweiden', descendants=None, kids=[25728923], score=None, time=1610361326, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25722131, text='In his article, Vitalik claims Electrum’s cosigner model isn’t good enough, without mentioning it by name. Cosigner already provides configurable N of M multisig the countersigners of which are user selectable. These are likely to be major financial institutions in the long run. The cosigner model readily provides user-friendly, credit card style spending limits. It can put spending limits on your bitcoin, and it can get you your coins back if you lose your wallet. It does all this without forcing users to interact with “smart contracts”.<p>I have no choice but to conclude “social sharing” is the Rube Goldberg machine variant of Electrum’s cosigner model, and its primary function is to promote Ethereum.')
