Item(by='feanaro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604250836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24960356, text='In most cases you can get away with simply connecting to the application with `objection` (mentioned in a sibling post) and running `android sslpinning disable`.<p>This will fail in some cases when the application is obfuscated or uses a non-standard pinning mechanism. In this case you can decompile the application to determine the methods used to accomplish the pinning and use frida (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;frida.re" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;frida.re</a>) directly in order to manually override them.')
