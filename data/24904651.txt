Item(by='FeepingCreature', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603775705, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24897980, text='No.<p>If people don&#x27;t want to be on a site with someone, even if they don&#x27;t have to interact with them, then that&#x27;s just too bad. I will always favor local, positive rights over global, negative rights.<p>The right to &gt; The right for somebody else to not.<p>I&#x27;m a value utilitarian. I think society should be organized so that humans can follow their interests. As such, I believe it is counterproductive for people to have an interest in others not following their interests; that&#x27;s an interest that reduces net interest satisfaction rather than raises it.')
