Item(by='hntemppony', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611969062, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25959142, text='The interesting parts:<p>Capabilities describe both uniqueness and read-write ability. `iso` is fully unique and hence sendable, `trn` and `val` are write-unique (but `val` is also sendable since it isn&#x27;t writable). The `tag` capability allows identity&#x2F;message sending and is fully sendable<p>Recover blocks and automatic receiver recovery allow creation&#x2F;usage of isolated data if only sendables are used from the external environment.<p>Only sendables can be included in messages.<p>Actors have `ref` access to themselves but `tag` access to other actors.')
