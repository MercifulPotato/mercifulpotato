Item(by='Asraelite', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606955866, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25283232, text='To calculate the probability of some event with probability P coming true <i>at least once</i> out of N total tries, you do 1 - ((1 - P) ^ N), not P * N as you&#x27;ve done.<p>Doing this, your final figures should be 12.1% and 96.8%.<p>For the expected number of times it would happen though, you are right, it would be three or four times per year on average for the second case.')
