Item(by='gizmo686', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607119440, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25306498, text='&quot;Science&quot; is a very broad field.<p>In some sense, protein folding is a chemistry problem, in that it is entirely about determining the structure of (a very specific type of) molecules.<p>In another sense, protein folding is a computational problem that is a necessary input to answering higher level questions in the field of biology.<p>Put another way, this allows researchers in biology to not need to care about the science of protein folding.')
