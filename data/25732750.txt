Item(by='Nextgrid', descendants=None, kids=[25732954], score=None, time=1610387461, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25732268, text='The whole concept of plea deals is insane (but then again, so is the concept of &quot;justice&quot; in the US) and it&#x27;s sad to see it being defended here.<p>Sidestepping the issue of the severity of his crime, the concept of &quot;plead guilty to X and waive your rights to a fair trial&quot; or go to a trial which is made unfair by trumped up charges (thus increasing the corresponding sentence by orders of magnitude) almost as revenge for exercising your right to a trial doesn&#x27;t sound like justice to me.
