Item(by='julienb_sea', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603237043, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24840473, text='Google is a double edged sword. Their search engine is good enough most people, in enterprise or otherwise, will rely on it when they need answers. This results in a centralized system where it is super high value for you to work with and ensure you are taking full advantage of.<p>That does leave you at the whims of said centralized system. There is no centralized system that can avoid this. If such a centralized system did not exist and people searched on a wide landscape of competing smaller search networks, it would be dramatically more difficult for you to ensure you were well positioned to be found on all those smaller networks.')
