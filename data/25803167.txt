Item(by='nicoffeine', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610813742, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25802714, text='Make sure all your network devices &#x2F; computers are synced via NTP, or at least accurate to the second.<p>Hardwire the Pi to the network. Start a verbose traceroute and ping loop on a device on WiFi, as well as the Pi. Once it fails, observe the lights on your router&#x2F;wifi device(s). Note time to the second of the start and finish of the outage, and the behavior of the lights. Check the results of your traceroutes&#x2F;pings. Start digging through the logs of each network device.<p>Also, you can never quite rule out power, even if the lights stay on. A power conditioning UPS is useful here.')
