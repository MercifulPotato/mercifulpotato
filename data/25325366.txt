Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=[25328160], score=None, time=1607278966, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325011, text='The notion of Peak Oil is that it is a situation where crude oil supply is permanently unable to meet demand, and all of the thinking and discussion around it is about how you handle that situation, in terms of ramifications for both suppliers and consumers of oil.<p>What is instead happening is that demand for oil has peaked before supply for oil has peaked. This is the completely <i>opposite</i> situation as the scenario envisioned by Peak Oil. All of those plans you would have been making for Peak Oil are now useless, especially if you are an oil supplier.')
