Item(by='sturmeh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606967191, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25282755, text='If it&#x27;s 5 specific numbers drawn from a pool of 50 in any order, we&#x27;re talking 2118760 combinations of numbers that can be drawn, i.e. a 0.0000472% chance.<p>For 5 drawn in a specific order from a pool of 50, we&#x27;re talking about 254251200 ways 5 numbers can be drawn, with a 0.0000004% chance.<p>In this case it was actually 6 numbers, which is about 10x less likely to match a combination (orderless) and 50x less likely to match a permutation (with order).')
