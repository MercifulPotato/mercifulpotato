Item(by='Arathorn', descendants=None, kids=[25337695, 25335811, 25336078, 25337584], score=None, time=1607366971, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25334807, text='Matrix now has two flavours of threading defined and implemented as we experiment with the different approaches: label-based threading in MSC2326[1] (i.e. &quot;filter this room to only show msgs tagged #foo&quot;), and full-blown free-form HN&#x2F;Reddit&#x2F;NNTP&#x2F;SMTP&#x2F;Twitter style threading in MSC2836[2].  The former is closer to Zulip, and is implemented in Synapse.  The latter is closer to HN, and is implemented in Dendrite[3][4].<p>Clientwise, Element supports neither so far, but we&#x27;re experimenting with the HN-style threading in a dedicated playground called Cerulean[5].  The idea is to get it working well there, and then figure out how to merge it most effectively into Element.  So: it&#x27;s not ready yet, but it&#x27;s getting tantalisingly close.
