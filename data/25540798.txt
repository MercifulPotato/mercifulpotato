Item(by='jadell', descendants=None, kids=[25540807], score=None, time=1608949048, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25539855, text='Picking apart someone&#x27;s belief through explaining their logical fallacies presupposes that they got to their belief through a logical process.<p>&quot;Reasoning will never make a Man correct an ill Opinion, which by Reasoning he never acquired.&quot; -- Jonathan Swift, 1721.<p>To rephrase in a more modern way: You can&#x27;t use reason or logic to get to someone to stop believing something they didn&#x27;t use reason or logic to start believing.')
