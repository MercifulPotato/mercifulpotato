Item(by='superkuh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610557122, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25764432, text='When I was a child I&#x27;d often stare into the laser scanners at store checkouts in awe. They were so cool. Full of rotating mirrors and lasers. The modern handheld ones are a little less cool.<p>But, re: &quot;why lasers are useful&quot;, it&#x27;s in the name. They&#x27;re a way to get a spatial and phase coherent light source that&#x27;s actually high power. In the old days they&#x27;d have to take a mercury arc lamp and put a pinhole in front of it. The pinhole gave it coherence but traded away all the intensity.')
