Can be done with a combination of calcium and vitamin D. Get more calcium (maximum 2:1 Ca:Mg) than phosphorus. Serum 25(OH)D at 40(-60) ng&#x2F;mL or greater.<p>More vitamin D3 can be handled if taking higher amounts of vitamin K2 (at least 1mg MK-4 and 100 mcg MK-7 per 10,000 IU) and magnesium.<p>Could also add (sustained-release) melatonin if done growing to balance D3 in the AM.')
