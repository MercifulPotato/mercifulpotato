Item(by='ahoy', descendants=None, kids=[24945506, 24943603], score=None, time=1604071070, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24943008, text='Reintroduction and protection of large predators has diffuse benefits and concentrated damages. It affects the average rancher orders of magnitude more (and importantly, more negatively) than it affects the average non-rancher.<p>So you have a group with common interests who are ready to act as an obstruction to the greater social good because it&#x27;s going to financially harm them. You _can_ tell them to shove it, but that has political consequences and might backfire and undo your whole conservation effort. Or you can help them out financially while they transition to doing business in this new normal where wolves still get to exist.')
