Item(by='pcl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604246442, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24959784, text='Well, a nautical mile is defined relative to a fundamental constant -- the circumference of the earth at the equator. This is objectively useful when at sea, and so I, for one, prefer it to either statute miles or kilometers.<p>Here in Norway, people tend to talk about wind speed in meters per second. Happily, a knot is very close to a half a meter per second, so I&#x27;m not as lost as I&#x27;d be otherwise.')
