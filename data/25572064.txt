Item(by='ve55', descendants=None, kids=[25573473, 25573769, 25574682], score=None, time=1609262150, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25571903, text='I would go even further: Always have a local backup of anything on the Internet that is important to you, whether you made it or not, unless you know there are <i>many</i> other accessible versions of it for redundancy. You never know when a large company will delete it without notice.<p>(Is this feasible? Perhaps not, but given how cheap storage is, if this could be even semi-automated, it would easily save a lot of heartache for some at many points in their life, especially in the worst cases of things like their Google accounts being banned)')
