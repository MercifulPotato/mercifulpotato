Item(by='phire', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610142593, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25685406, text='Apple had to keep PVRTC support. Apps were shipping with PVRTC compressed textures and no fallbacks.<p>What I find interesting is that PVRTC is the very last proprietary IMG format that Apple GPUs support.<p>IMG introduced an improved PVRTC2 texture compression format in 2012. Yet no Apple device has ever exposed support for it.<p>It looks like Apple was already thinking ahead back then and wanting to avoid supporting features that would require extra royalties.')
