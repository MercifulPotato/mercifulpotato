A few decades ago, people often assumed Moore&#x27;s law would continue indefinitely, but now there&#x27;s some doubt as to whether it is physically possible to keep up the pace without some amount of marketing tricks.<p>We don&#x27;t believe AI would ever be able to solve some classes of &quot;impossible&quot; tasks, like breaking crypto.
