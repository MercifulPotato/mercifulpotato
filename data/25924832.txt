Item(by='zaarn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611727414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25923716, text='sudo can be restricted to specific commands, so you can restrict non-root users to do <i>VERY</i> specific actions (ie, the webdev can reload apache2 but not restart or stop it or take any other action).<p>This means the webdev has the least amount of access necessary for their work without giving them straight up root or using setuid on a script, which can lead to easy bugs (did you check PATH?)')
