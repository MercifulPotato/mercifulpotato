Item(by='avianlyric', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610368601, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25725214, text='I don’t think there’s much difference. If you argue that companies like Stripe and Twitter should “uphold freedom of speech”, then you’re restricting their freedom of speech.<p>My right to speak doesn’t override you’re right to ignore me, or refuse to transmit my message.<p>Now there’s a knotty issue with entities like Facebook and Twitter becoming the primary ways people communicate with large audiences, but people managed before Facebook and Twitter, so they can manage again.<p>Or the alternative is that we say Facebook and Twitter don’t deserve to have their own speech protected. At which point someone is picking a choosing who deserves free speech.<p>You can’t have it both ways.')
