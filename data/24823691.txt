Item(by='elSidCampeador', descendants=None, kids=[24823805, 24824027], score=None, time=1603087506, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24799839, text='Fun fact: before Maruti Suzuki came calling, cars were not transported on trucks or trains to the dealership. They were driven there. So it was common to buy a car new, and receive it with 500+ kilometres on the odometer because it had been driven from the factory to the dealership.<p>Maruti was the company that pioneered putting cars on a trailer and shipping them over to wherever. Even today, those trailers are called Maruti Car Carriers.')
