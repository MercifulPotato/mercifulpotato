Item(by='my123', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608132853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25443082, text='On Windows, Qualcomm uses the SCM driver to hook the Get&#x2F;SetVariable implementation and replace it with their own.<p>The UEFI standard SetVariable implementation is nonfunctional and the GetVariable one is at the same state since ExitBootServices.<p>This is due to the fact that the firmware and the OS cannot control the storage device at the same time. The issue isn&#x27;t seen on most x86 PCs since those use a separate SPI flash chip for the UEFI.')
