Item(by='ddevault', descendants=None, kids=[25104177], score=None, time=1605469477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25104018, text='Design is in the eye of the beholder - many users find SourceHut&#x27;s UI beautiful and have praised it as such. Beauty is also more than just a pretty face - SourceHut is an engineering tool, and if it elegantly and efficiently organizes information for engineers to use, that can be more important and more beautiful than an eye-catching design. In fact, an eye-catching design can often degrade the experience of engineering tools, and eye-catching elements - such as colorful buttons - are carefully and deliberately controlled for in the design of sr.ht. It optimizes getting you the information you need or helping you complete the task you came here for as efficiently as possible.<p>SourceHut has been designed deliberately and for a specific purpose. The simplicitly of its appearance is a feature.')
