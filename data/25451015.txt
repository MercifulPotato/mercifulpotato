Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608166907, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25450493, text='Google doesn&#x27;t (afaik) sell your data directly, but when they sell ads based on categories they&#x27;ve fit you to, that category information can be harvested.<p>If it&#x27;s a ad unit which includes javascript or image beacons, if your browser displays it, the ad can send the category information to the advertiser&#x2F;intermediary ad network&#x2F;partners&#x2F;whoever.
