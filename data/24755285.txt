Item(by='whoisburbansky', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602515982, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24754806, text='It might interest you to know that Popper only advocated for the right to censorship in the context of democratically established political institutions, as a last resort function of a liberal democracy with a constitutionally established rule of law, which Facebook most definitely is not. Whether or not the current US government is a better steward of censorship power is a separate question, but Popper would straightforwardly disagree with this exercise of power by private parties, imo.')
