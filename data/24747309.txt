Item(by='erichocean', descendants=None, kids=[24747461], score=None, time=1602436199, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24746883, text='The period in &quot;3.14&quot; is called a &quot;decimal.&quot; The digits after the decimal in &quot;3.14&quot; are enumerated as &quot;decimal places.&quot; What each decimal place &quot;means&quot; depends on the numeric base.<p>So, &quot;decimal places&quot; or &quot;rounding to a decimal place&quot; has nothing to do with base 10 (which is what I think you are implying): decimal and &quot;decimal places&quot; exist in all bases, including base 2, and the meaning of each &quot;decimal place&quot; depends on the numeric base (base 10, base 2, etc.).')
