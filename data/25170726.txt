Item(by='Alex3917', descendants=None, kids=[25170792], score=None, time=1605977198, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170661, text='I track all my ideas using FreeMind (<a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;freemind.sourceforge.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;freemind.sourceforge.net&#x2F;</a>), and generally recommend it.<p>It&#x27;s not really being actively maintained, but since it&#x27;s in an open data format if it ever stops working you can just export your data into whatever format org-mode uses.<p>Usually I don&#x27;t get around to doing projects until at least 5 years after having the idea, so my ideas tend to develop little by little over the years until the time is right to finally pursue them.
