Item(by='wsh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602450050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24744531, text='To show that a message existed at a particular date and time, you could use a time-stamping service operated by a trusted third party, in which they digitally sign a timestamp and the cryptographic digest of your message.<p>Several public certification authorities offer these services, described in RFC 3161, at no charge, because they’re used by code signing schemes, to prove that a digital signature on an object file was made during the validity period of the signer’s identity certificate. Here’s Microsoft’s explanation:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;docs.microsoft.com&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;windows&#x2F;win32&#x2F;seccrypto&#x2F;time-stamping-authenticode-signatures" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;docs.microsoft.com&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;windows&#x2F;win32&#x2F;seccrypto&#x2F;tim...</a><p>You don’t have to use an identity certificate, however; OpenSSL, for example, can work work with timestamps directly:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.openssl.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;manmaster&#x2F;man1&#x2F;tsget.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.openssl.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;manmaster&#x2F;man1&#x2F;tsget.html</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.openssl.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;manmaster&#x2F;man1&#x2F;openssl-ts.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.openssl.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;manmaster&#x2F;man1&#x2F;openssl-ts.html</a><p>Some examples of time-stamping servers:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;knowledge.digicert.com&#x2F;generalinformation&#x2F;INFO4231.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;knowledge.digicert.com&#x2F;generalinformation&#x2F;INFO4231.h...</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.entrust.com&#x2F;knowledgebase&#x2F;ssl&#x2F;time-stamp-url" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.entrust.com&#x2F;knowledgebase&#x2F;ssl&#x2F;time-stamp-url</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;sectigo.com&#x2F;resource-library&#x2F;time-stamping-server" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;sectigo.com&#x2F;resource-library&#x2F;time-stamping-server</a><p>Whether such a timestamp would be acceptable proof, on its own or accompanied by an explanation from an independent expert, would depend on the audience and the situation, of course.')
