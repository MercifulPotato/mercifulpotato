Item(by='pjmlp', descendants=None, kids=[25890240, 25890206], score=None, time=1611473206, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25889468, text='I am still not sold to Rust editions and think in the long run they won&#x27;t be much different from solutions in other platforms.<p>There will come the day when something will change semantics, or require different kinds of runtime support across editions, and then the headaches how to link binary crates from different editions will start.<p>Editions to me appear only to work, provided everything is compiled from source code with the same compiler, aware of all editions that came into use.')
