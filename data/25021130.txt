Item(by='laleopue', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604793492, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25017265, text='The adjective &quot;deep&quot; came from deep belief networks, which are a variation on restricted boltzmann machines. RBNs have one visible and one hidden layers, DNBs have more hidden layers - hence &quot;deep&quot;. So it&#x27;s not exactly based on a distinction between &quot;deep&quot; and &quot;shallow&quot; models.')
