Windows 7 received updates through 2019, general support was dropped on January 13, 2015 and extended support dropped on January 14, 2020. The support period for Windows 7 was mightily impressive in comparison to the competition in this case, although the upgrade from Snow Leopard to Lion was easier than Windows 7 to 10 (excluding dropping Rosetta and ending PowerPC support)')
