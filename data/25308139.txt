You only find --help contents if you already know which command you want.  Man pages are searchable (with `man -k` or `apropos`).<p>This is most relevant when it&#x27;s a tool that isn&#x27;t going to be immediately obvious anyway (if I&#x27;m trying to figure out how to do XYZ on heroku, it&#x27;s not unreasonable to expect me to look at the heroku program I installed), and especially when it&#x27;s a tool that might be installed by default or installed and forgotten about.')
