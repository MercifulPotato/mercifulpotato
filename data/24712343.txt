Item(by='shadowgovt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602102466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24712068, text='Sub-10% market share is a problem for them.<p>It&#x27;s enough of a skew that when rendering errors crop up, product managers have to decide how many eng-hours it&#x27;s worth to chase down the small implementation difference between Chromium and Gecko to snag 1 in 10 potential lost visitors, instead of throwing a UA-sniffing &quot;best viewed in Chrome&quot; banner on the page and calling it a day.<p>Being a rarely-used browser is a negative feedback loop for compatibility, regardless of what standards say (because standards are often too loose to guarantee full interoperability in all corner cases; they rarely give performance constraints and often don&#x27;t consider all possible combinations of feature use).')
