Item(by='dannyw', descendants=None, kids=[25619031, 25620161, 25618770], score=None, time=1609640616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25618679, text='App removals are serious business that affects livelihoods.<p>If T1 support sometimes has poor judgement, it should be required to internally escalate to T2 before actually making a decision.<p>For apps with more than a certain number of users, it should require another layer of escalation and approval, including a review of other apps to make sure rules are enforced clearly, fairly, and reasonably.<p>Finally, non-security removals should have a grace period of 60 to 90 days.')
