There is a hope that Bitcoin becomes &quot;useful&quot;, thus driving demand. Since supply is fixed, demand causes an increase in value.<p>So, it&#x27;s a speculative &quot;investment&quot;. I suppose whether it&#x27;s an &quot;investment&quot; depends on your definition of &quot;investment&quot;.')
