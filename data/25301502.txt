Charge for access to cover the costs of obtaining and organizing the data, but understand that clients are legally allowed to make copies and use them however they see fit, even if that impacts the supplier&#x27;s ability to charge for access.<p>If the &quot;data&quot; is prose, it&#x27;s under copyright and republishing without a license has remedies under law.
