Item(by='ptero', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607992930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25424196, text='I have no objection to your example, but it is a very different case; it is convincing vs forcing.<p>In your example the teacher is <i>arguing</i> to <i>convince</i> you, the final decision maker, that something is long term worthwhile even if near term unpleasant (and most adults who sign up for a class will usually listen to the teacher). The original example is closer to the teacher locking the door and saying &quot;you will not leave until you show me that you learned X&quot;. Which is a very different setup that, even if fruitful, few adults will find palatable. My 2c.')
