Item(by='blmsjw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603285241, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24845987, text='I think as long you keep use stored procedures as atomic operations&#x2F;transactions it both simplifies code and improves performance. In your example, a transfer money procedure should handle both the balance increment and decrement of all accounts involved in a transaction before returning. Things start to get hard to maintain if you have stored procedures that only complete a portion of the business logic and rely on code in the business logic to “understand” what still needs to be done')
