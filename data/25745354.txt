Item(by='Foivos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610450335, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25743577, text='Network slicing and Edge computing can change that.<p>It is possible to have all military traffic either terminate at the edge of the military (for example a 5G installation at an army camp) or use a dedicated slice (fully isolated from the normal user traffic) to reach a trusted cloud. Usually, they use both at the same time, so if the connection to the trusted cloud is lost, you rely on the edge cloud.<p>As far as I know, NATO has several such projects.')
