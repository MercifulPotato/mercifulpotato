Item(by='Spooky23', descendants=None, kids=[24687839], score=None, time=1601901780, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24683759, text='90% of state&#x2F;local pensions are administered at the state level.<p>Usually state plans mandate contributions to some degree -- in my state (NY) the year after a stock market crash is always a mess for schools and localities because the state requires increased contributions to make up for investment target misses.<p>Even where the state isn&#x27;t a good or responsible pension administrator, the pension system membership usually takes the obligation off of the city&#x2F;county balance sheet.
