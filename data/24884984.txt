Item(by='kwhitefoot', descendants=None, kids=[24885036], score=None, time=1603618315, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24883302, text='My dentist (in Norway) has had a machine for doing this on demand while you wait for years.  Both of my crowns were done this way:<p>- take X-rays to diagnose the problem<p>- take photographs of the existing tooth<p>- set the machine running to make the crown<p>- grind away the surface of the old tooth, drill out whatever needs removing as usual<p>- apply adhesive<p>- fit new tooth<p>- adjust tooth slightly to fit by grinding as usual for any filling.<p>From arriving at the dentist to paying the bill is less than an hour.')
