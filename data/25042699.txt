Item(by='rkagerer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604980712, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25034614, text='In addition to the onus on users to make a choice, there&#x27;s an army of developers out there who can advocate to avoid unnecessary use of exotic features.<p>Sometimes they&#x27;re appropriate (e.g. ability to drag-and-drop or CTRL+V paste a photo to an image sharing site), but as a user my personal inclination tends toward traditional interfaces (which as a happy bonus can be more responsive) and I&#x27;ve encountered an overwhelming number of sites that abuse capabilities for no good reason (e.g. those which immediately prompt for your location).<p>A little taste and restraint would be very welcome on the modern web.')
