Item(by='amenghra', descendants=None, kids=[25394738], score=None, time=1607737732, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25394402, text='Flip a penny twice, if you get heads followed by tails, write down 0. If you get tails, followed by heads, write down 1. Otherwise ignore the two flips. Rinse and repeat until you have enough bits.<p>The purpose of flipping twice is that it offsets any potential static bias (caused eg by weight difference) between the two sides.')
