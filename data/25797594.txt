Item(by='nitrogen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610751065, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25797147, text='Here&#x27;s an experiment you can try at home.  It doesn&#x27;t prove that humans or frogs won&#x27;t notice extreme temperatures, but it does show that it would be reasonable to believe that it&#x27;s <i>conceivable</i> that some animal can only detect relative temperature.<p>1. Prepare three bowls of water, one cold (but bearable), one hot (but bearable), and one lukewarm.<p>2. Place one hand in the cold water, and the other hand in the hot water.<p>3. Wait a minute or two.<p>4. Place both hands in the lukewarm water at the same time.<p>5. WTF, is this hot or is it cold?')
