Item(by='lrvick', descendants=None, kids=[25669317], score=None, time=1610013943, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25669258, text='Semantics, but worth clarifying.<p>You have to turn on untrusted sources to sideload an APK. It will verify a signature. The problem is the OS has no anchor to know if that signature is by the key of the party you expect, or that of a malicious adversary. Once you pin the wrong key it is like getting a bad HTTPs cert on first connection. All bets are off moving forward.')
