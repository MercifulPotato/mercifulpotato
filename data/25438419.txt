Item(by='cj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608086549, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25438330, text='&gt; What are the good password practices for handling passwords that several people need to be able to use?<p>The answer is typically “never share accounts” and therefore never share passwords.<p>I wouldn’t the surprised if “do not share credentials” is explicitly stated in solarwinds internal security policies.<p>Beyond the challenge of sharing strong passwords, there’s also the (important from compliance perspective) issue of losing the ability to audit who took what action if multiple people use the same credentials.')
