Item(by='barkerja', descendants=None, kids=[25443375], score=None, time=1608126988, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25442371, text='&gt; There doesn&#x27;t seem to be a good, evident third option just yet.<p>I think a good middle ground here is an opt-out approach on Apple&#x27;s end. Force the user to make a cognitive choice here with a clear understanding of what their choice means. Communication is key.<p>Some users may very well appreciate and value the hyper-relevant advertisements they see on services, and remain opted-in to the sharing of tracking data.<p>I personally know many people who have bought many things they value greatly that they discovered through advertisements on various social networks.<p>At the end of the day, it should be the choice of the user; not the system (Apple&#x2F;iOS, Google&#x2F;Android, etc.) or the relying third parties (Facebook, etc.)')
