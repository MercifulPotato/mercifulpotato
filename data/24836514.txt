Item(by='pmichaud', descendants=None, kids=[24838902], score=None, time=1603197129, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24836252, text='I think this sort of thing is not wrong, but it&#x27;s very important to bear in mind survivorship bias:<p>It&#x27;s true that you probably need to push beyond normal limits to end up at the tails. It&#x27;s also true that most people who do that end up worse off overall than if they had just done normal things. So it&#x27;s a risk necessary to end up in an usually good circumstance, but it IS a risk and should be thought of as such.')
