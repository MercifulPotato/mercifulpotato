Item(by='CharlesW', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606416507, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25207290, text='(This advice is offered in the spirit of improving your content marketing efforts.)<p>I understand the desire to &quot;reel in&quot; more readers than a post might otherwise warrant, but in my experience you&#x27;re better off not promising general advice if you&#x27;re not providing it.<p>For example, you might say, &quot;How we configure TiDB on AWS to handle XXX transactions per second for XXX&#x2F;month&quot;, or &quot;Squeezing the highest TiDB performance from AWS m5 instances&quot;. There are definitely interesting ways to title posts in a way that&#x27;s supported by the content.')
