Item(by='jsheard', descendants=None, kids=[25906977, 25911620], score=None, time=1611600131, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25906496, text='Renewing a .com for the maximum 10 years in advance is a bit of a trap, because to transfer the domain to another registrar you have to buy at least one additional year... which you can&#x27;t do if you&#x27;re already at the 10 year limit. If your registrar pulls a GoDaddy and you want to move away you might find yourself having to wait up to a year.<p>There might be similar caveats with other TLDs but I only have experience with .com')
