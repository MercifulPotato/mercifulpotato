Item(by='schoen', descendants=None, kids=[24963224], score=None, time=1604264041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24962397, text='&gt; If one is worried about nation state actors, the ownership of a device that&#x27;s secure and uncommon enough is likely to attract additional scrutiny or just be seized, legally or not.<p>The distinction between &quot;tamper evidence&quot; and &quot;tamper resistance&quot; seems to provide a good analogy here. Without secure devices, a state can spy on you in a clandestine way. With secure devices, a state can spy on you in a more overt way. You might still care about making the tampering evident!<p>(&quot;can&quot; here probably means &quot;can somewhat easily&quot;)')
