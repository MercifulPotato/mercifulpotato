In which case, minimum wages protect lower-paid workers at the expense of higher-paid workers. Which is fine in of itself.<p>But on top of that I think we have to assume that the salary envelope has some elasticity. Maybe minimum wage increases the total salary cost, and decreases returns on capital - so helping lower-paid workers at the expense of investors. Again, why not')
