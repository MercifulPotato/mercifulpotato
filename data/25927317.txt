Item(by='alias_neo', descendants=None, kids=[25928200, 25927834], score=None, time=1611749997, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25926471, text='It&#x27;s interesting because psychologically it really has an effect.<p>I find myself feeling uncomfortable at how close people are when watching Netflix shows. My conscious mind knows it&#x27;s nothing, but I had to stop and think why I was feeling uncomfortable at the lack of &quot;personal space&quot;. I paused to ask my wife about it and she felt the same.<p>We&#x27;ve got this new mental norm and intentional or not, what we see and witness is affecting us.<p>I&#x27;m in London and personal space has rarely been something you worry about here, crowded tubes with a shorter persons nose in my arm pit was not unusual before the pandemic.<p>These days just seeing people at less than arms length apart is enough to make me uncomfortable enough to need to wonder why.<p>I have no doubt plenty of media sensationalise but I&#x27;m not &quot;pro&quot; enough of a photographer to determine if this is intentional or if photographers in the media are just ignorant of the issue.')
