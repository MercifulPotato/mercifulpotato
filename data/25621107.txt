Item(by='drran', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609672361, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25619331, text='If we use third state as &quot;spacer&quot;, then we will be able to use every single line connection as serial connection, thus massively parallel, clock-less logic will be possible.<p>Instead of clocked design:<p><pre><code>  data0: 000001111111111111111111111111111000000000\n  data1: 000000000000000000000000000000000000000000\n  data2: 000111111111111111111111111111100000000000\n  ...\n  clock: 000000000001111111111100000000000000000000\n</code></pre>\nWe will be able to use clockless design:<p><pre><code>  data0: --111---\n  data1: ---000--\n  data2: ----111-\n</code></pre>\nOr even:<p><pre><code>  data: --111---000---111---\n</code></pre>\n(1 represents high signal, 0 - low signal, - - neutral signal).')
