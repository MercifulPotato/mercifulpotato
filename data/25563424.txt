Ever.  It&#x27;s rude and disrespectful, full stop.<p>Yes, I agree that the interview process can be a numbers game: candidates should apply to many opportunities that fit their skills, and expect no interest from nearly all of them.<p>But if a candidate <i>has</i> received a response, and there&#x27;s any kind of open dialog, no matter how minimal, then out of a sense of basic civility and respect, they deserve a reply, even if it&#x27;s just a canned one-liner rejection.<p>That&#x27;s the only decent thing to do.  Anything else is disrespectful.')
