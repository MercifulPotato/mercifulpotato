Item(by='nendroid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602173266, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24719525, text='Always use a monorepo.<p>There is zero difference between using repos to organize things or using folders to organize things, except for the fact that updated dependencies across repos require extra steps to update if they change. More repos = more annoyance.<p>Use folders to organize things because common sense.<p>Additionally, don&#x27;t use classes to organize your code, use combinators.<p>Folders and combinators will solve basically imo 95 percent of all organizational problems related to design.<p>Things like micoservices, multiple repos, and Gof design patterns only serve to make the organizational problem worse.')
