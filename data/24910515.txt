Item(by='chmod775', descendants=None, kids=[24910544, 24919165], score=None, time=1603824492, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24910244, text='I run a service based in Germany, and yes we don&#x27;t care about DMCAs per se.<p>However German laws are essentially: &quot;Once you are notified of copyright infringing&#x2F;illegal content, you have to remove it, lest you lose your protection as a service provider and become responsible for the content.&quot;<p>A DMCA takedown notice would be such a notification.<p>On the upside you don&#x27;t need to do anything if you don&#x27;t have reason to believe the content is illegal in any way (unlike with DMCAs where you pretty much always have to remove first).<p>On the downside it&#x27;s a bit easier to get into trouble as a provider if you chose to let something stay online.')
