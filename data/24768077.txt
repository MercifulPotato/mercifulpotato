Item(by='capableweb', descendants=None, kids=[24772553], score=None, time=1602612428, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24765007, text='If Call of Duty is anything like any other AAA game, it&#x27;s not because of the number of unique objects and textures, but the size is on purpose. Lots of video games are optimized by _duplicating_ the assets to where they are needed to be loaded, so since they are all in sequence in storage, it&#x27;ll be faster to load. But this increases the storage requirements, as lots of assets ends up being duplicated all over the place.')
