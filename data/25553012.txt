You can have bacteria without bees, but you can&#x27;t have bees without oxygen.<p>&gt; The Science Times reports that 70% of the world’s agriculture is dependent on pollination that happens exclusively by bees. Bees may do many different things but pollinating plants, allowing them to reproduce is by far their most important. Without pollination, those plants would disappear quickly.<p>Wheat, rice, maize, cassava, and potatoes don&#x27;t depend on bees for reproduction.
