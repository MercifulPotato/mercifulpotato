A good book on this is Who Owns The Future by Jaron Lanier.<p>Of course, forbidding anti-competitive acquisitions would have helped in that maybe then we&#x27;d all be using an independent Instagram under the leadership of its original founders and Facebook would be dying. But that still wouldn&#x27;t change the fact that it would be more than likely to be a quasi monopoly. That&#x27;s just the nature of social networks.')
