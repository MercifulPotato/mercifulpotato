Item(by='reaperducer', descendants=None, kids=[25037745], score=None, time=1604944332, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25037394, text='&quot;Premium&quot; is the key word here.<p>There was an article in one of the business magazines about a year ago about why so many fast food companies are adding meatless options.<p>It&#x27;s not about health.  It&#x27;s not about choice.  It&#x27;s not about the planet.  It&#x27;s about money.<p>The menus need high-priced items and low-priced items to appeal to different types of customers.  Most fast-food joints need a basic product they&#x27;re known for, and a premium product that costs the consumer more and has a larger profit margin, that the average consumer might occasionally try.<p>The fast food companies can charge more for a meatless product, and reap bigger profits.<p>A little greenwashing doesn&#x27;t hurt, either.')
