Item(by='dharmab', descendants=None, kids=[25509853], score=None, time=1608657668, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25508390, text='Games include two types of music:<p>Original music is created by the game creators and streaming rights are included in the software EULA.<p>Licensed music is created by third party artists and is usually not included in the software EULA because the music artist&#x2F;publisher does not grant that type of license to the game creator&#x2F;publisher. A gamer streaming gameplay that includes the copyrighted song will trigger automatic copyright protection on the streaming service they use and is subject to DMCA takedowns.<p>Modern games often include a &quot;streamer mode&quot; which replaces or mutes the licensed music.')
