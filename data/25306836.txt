For functions that only have a few dozen or hundred possible inputs we can exhaustively test every single case (even millions&#x2F;billions as part of a slow suite).<p>For looser languages, maybe the function takes a fuzzy notion of &quot;a number&quot; - OK, do I need to try the string &quot;12&quot;? What about &quot;012&quot;? What about &quot;12.0&quot; or &quot; 12&quot; or &quot;\\n12&quot;?')
