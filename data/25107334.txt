Item(by='aparashk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605494178, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25107220, text='It is more than likely that Geekbench will use CPUID to establish what it is running on, and then adjust its kernels to suit the host CPU architecture. Rosetta2 would be using a CPUID that excludes the AVX and any other Intel ISA extensions that it does not support, therefore, Geekbench should be running without issues on the fastest vector extension supported, most likely SSE3 or SSE4.x.')
