Item(by='wccrawford', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610999027, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25825649, text='My understanding is that for a team and project that&#x27;s been going for a while, scrum points can be roughly turned into time estimates by looking at the history of stories that were rated for that many points and averaging.<p>But that obviously won&#x27;t work from the start, and it won&#x27;t be accurate...  Just better than nothing and (hopefully) better than what a programmer will estimate in their head.<p>And IME, it&#x27;s a lot less stressful on the programmer to estimate points rather than time.')
