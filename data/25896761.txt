Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=[25897194, 25896906], score=None, time=1611526819, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25895133, text='Synergy dates back to Silicon Graphics hardware.<p>An often overlooked feature of synergy is the ability to share clipboards between machines. You can copy a URL from your email and paste it into a browser on another box. Particularly useful if the reason you&#x27;re doing synergy is to do cross-platform testing and validation.<p>The thing that made me stop using it was that they punted security to be someone else&#x27;s problem, so you had to set up some ssh tunnel and be sure to run it only over that. It&#x27;s not so bad on Linux or OS X, but that&#x27;s quite a bit of extra work on Windows.<p>Does barrier take care of authentication or session encryption?')
