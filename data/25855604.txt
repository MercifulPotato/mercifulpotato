Item(by='th0114nd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611205182, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25855416, text='It’s a short circuiting conjunction. Consider `aliceWantsADrink &amp;&amp; bobWantsADrink`. If Alice doesn’t want a drink, she knows it’s not the case that both of them want a drink. Since she says “maybe” instead of “no” she signals to Bob that she wants a drink, and so he concludes they both want a drink.')
