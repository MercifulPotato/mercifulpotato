I&#x27;ve used Azure and briefly dabbled in AWS&#x27;s web UIs, they&#x27;re not better and are worse in some cases. Critical administration tasks are going to be wrapped and reused in CLI, the web UI is usually for ad-hoc stuff.<p>This is a pretty core staple of the b2b market: customers, even small ones, respond mostly to price &amp; features that save a <i>significant</i> amount of time &amp; money; Sorry but a sluggish web UI while annoying won&#x27;t move the needle much on the bottom line.')
