Item(by='Arathorn', descendants=None, kids=[25813336], score=None, time=1610907736, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25812982, text='It&#x27;s worth noting that this sounds to be an unfederated server.<p>The reason Synapse has a reputation for being resource hungry is on publicly federated servers: if one of the users starts going and joining a bunch of large busy public rooms from their new server, then those rooms get dutifully replicated onto the server, which inevitably consumes resources.<p>The more users and the more servers in the room, the more changes of gnarly forks and merge resolution problems, and the more the risk of CPU spikes.  We&#x27;re constantly working on the memory footprint and state resolution algorithm (e.g. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;matrix-org&#x2F;synapse&#x2F;blob&#x2F;develop&#x2F;docs&#x2F;auth_chain_difference_algorithm.md" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;matrix-org&#x2F;synapse&#x2F;blob&#x2F;develop&#x2F;docs&#x2F;auth...</a> landed a few days ago), so the situation is improving, but this is the root cause.')
