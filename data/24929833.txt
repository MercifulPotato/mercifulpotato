Item(by='qayxc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603975120, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24928155, text='It would be spectacularly difficult to do for videos:<p>first there&#x27;s lossy compression, which means that there&#x27;s no guarantee your injected pixels survive the encoding pass.<p>Then there&#x27;s the additional hurdle of motion vectors, which will most likely be misaligned between the original video and the injected one.<p>This would result in hard to predict artefacts after encoding.<p>Finally, each decoder handles scaling slightly differently, so even if your embedded video trick works on one software&#x2F;hardware decoder, it might fail on another (sometimes even depending on just the version or additional settings&#x2F;filters being enabled).')
