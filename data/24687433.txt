Item(by='mumblemumble', descendants=None, kids=[24687854], score=None, time=1601903516, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24687381, text='I would say that this is a good argument for linearizing the history by rebasing, but certainly not for squashing.<p>Git bisect can only narrow you down to the scale of your commits. If you do infrequent, large commits, it&#x27;s not very useful. If you do frequent, small commits, it&#x27;s <i>great</i>. If you do frequent, small commits and then squash them into infrequent, large commits, it&#x27;s not very useful.<p>There are plenty of reasons to squash (and, personally, I generally think they outweigh the arguments against), but this is not one of them.')
