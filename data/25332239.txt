Item(by='dane-pgp', descendants=None, kids=[25332333], score=None, time=1607350415, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25332143, text='Companies offering bug bounties should allow appeals of the amounts&#x2F;severities they determine by an independent body that is qualified to make these assessments.  (Perhaps a respected team of security researchers would be happy to take on this responsibility).<p>To prevent the appeals process being abused, the appellant should have to pay for the time spent by the independent researchers verifying their complaint.  For a successful appeal, the company offering the bounty should have to pay that extra cost, encouraging them not to be stingy with the awards they give out in the first place.')
