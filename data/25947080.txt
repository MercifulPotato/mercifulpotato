Both hate speech and porn are good targets for machine learning. It needs supervision, but it can take a lot of load off human moderators.<p>Open source implementations exist, e.g.:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;t-davidson&#x2F;hate-speech-and-offensive-language" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;t-davidson&#x2F;hate-speech-and-offensive-lang...</a><p>I suspect more message board will want to start applying these sooner rather than later. Most have already figured out that they need anti-spam tools, rather than it coming as a surprise when they roll things out and it fills up with bots. The technology is similar.<p>You mention being able to share that information across boards, and I don&#x27;t know of any widespread implementation of that. You can, at least, let somebody else handle your authentication, which slightly slows their ability to create new accounts when you blacklist one. I&#x27;d like to see those sites distinguish &quot;aged&quot; accounts, so that it at least takes some effort or cost to use a new account.')
