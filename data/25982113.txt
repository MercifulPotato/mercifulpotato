Item(by='baybal2', descendants=None, kids=[25982177], score=None, time=1612123987, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25981839, text='Big asterisk there.<p>German education accreditation for Blue Card is rather fickle.<p>Germany maintains a rather short list of universities per-country which it recognises as institutes of higher learning.<p>You may have a rather ordinary uni degree in mainstream occupation, but if your uni is not on the list, you can&#x27;t do anything.<p>If you passed a university program in a recognised university, but your program is missing from a dropdown list on their website, again, you can&#x27;t do anything.<p>And if you have both, but your degree does not state in its title verbatim that it is a bachelor, or masters program regardless of years of study, again, you can&#x27;t do anything about it.')
