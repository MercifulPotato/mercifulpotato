I want a comprehensible restaurant-like menu. And we need to break up regional monopolies so that there&#x27;s actually a choice of provider. Further, agreements to pay whatever, whenever, before any diagnosis need to be banned as contracts under duress.<p>If we want the free market approach to work, we need to make it possible, easy even, to make informed decisions among competitors.<p>But fixing this is increasingly feeling intractable, and this pushes me further and further towards believing a single-payer (maybe not exactly, but that&#x27;s the buzzword) system is the better fix.')
