Item(by='nextos', descendants=None, kids=[24702753, 24703074], score=None, time=1602019934, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24702053, text='I love Julia, and in many areas both the language and standard libraries are quite polished.<p>However, simple parallelism based on threads (e.g. a parallel map) surprisingly requires a third party library. ThreadsX, the one you posted about, is also my favorite option.<p>Besides, the standard pipe operator (|&gt;) doesn&#x27;t support partial application. This has lead to several third-party reimplementations via macros, transducers, etc. Since this is a basic feature, fragmentation is a bit worrying.')
