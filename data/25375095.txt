Item(by='Macha', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607616042, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25374260, text='If it&#x27;s only to provide the stated purpose of the site (think those &quot;scan a QR code and approve this login on your other device&quot; setups), it likely falls under the legitimate interest exception. If it&#x27;s to provide cross device tracking for better advertising, then you need consent.<p>Lawyers are unlikely to take kindly to arguments of the &quot;Well technically the advertisments are an integral part of the site&quot;, that will surely be raised in response to this. This is why the law is not code and gets interpreted by judges.')
