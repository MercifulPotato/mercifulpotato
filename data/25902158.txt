Item(by='gjvc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611575645, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25901732, text='Indeed, in the original bitcoin whitepaper [1], section 3 introduces the notion of &quot;Timestamp server&quot;.  The <i>primary</i> purpose of a universal ledger (for the purposes of accounting) is to record the correct <i>ordering</i> of events, not their timestamps.  This, I believe, is what is meant by the notion of &quot;timestamp&quot; in &quot;timestamp server&quot; in the paper -- it might have been less clear had it been called &quot;event ordering server&quot;.<p>[1] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bitcoin.org&#x2F;bitcoin.pdf" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bitcoin.org&#x2F;bitcoin.pdf</a>')
