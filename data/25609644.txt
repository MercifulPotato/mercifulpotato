Item(by='mlyle', descendants=None, kids=[25609714, 25609790], score=None, time=1609554010, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25609601, text='Vaccination will create pressures selecting for variants that escape vaccine-generated antibodies.<p>The vaccine is ~95% effective.  This means you&#x27;re going to have a lot of people get infected still with the vaccine&#x27;s antibodies present; any mutation that happens that causes escape from these antibodies will prolong disease and increase transmission, even if that mutation renders the virus a bit less fit in a non-vaccinated host.')
