Item(by='t0mas88', descendants=None, kids=[25092388], score=None, time=1605355663, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25091456, text='Airplanes operated for passenger transport are on a very controlled maintenance program. Almost all parts are numbered and tracked, including their age, flight hours and cycles (basically number of flights). You cannot replace even a bolt with another that isn&#x27;t according to the same manufacturer spec and tracked in the same manner.<p>So some screws could be the same spec, but in general you don&#x27;t just pick one out of a bucket and screw it in.')
