Item(by='Const-me', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606498696, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25229366, text='Mapping pages is relatively expensive. Not the mapping itself, but as a consequence of such update CPU has to flush at least a portion of TLB cache. With overlapped I&#x2F;O the kernel has to do that for every I&#x2F;O request.<p>With RIO and now io_uring, kernels map buffers to both kernel and user addresses spaces just once on initial setup, and reuse the same buffer for many I&#x2F;O operations.')
