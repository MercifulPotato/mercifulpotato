Finding out correct IFR was a high priority. Even in April it was possible to see this IFR. New York was reporting 0.6-1% depending on the method in April.<p>I personally went through Italian data where they released deaths&#x2F;infections by municipality and I found several municipalities where they had 0.5-1% of total population dying from Covid-19.<p>IFR is about 0.5-1% in countries with older populations (western world). Early on there was a lot of wishful thinking hoping that IFR is much lower but it didn&#x27;t turn out that way.')
