This attracts more customers to businesses through increased convenience.<p>Taking cards is voluntary, there&#x27;s no &quot;forcing processing fees&quot; involved. Plenty of businesses opt not to take credit cards.<p>In fact, interchange fees (which in the US now top out around 2.7%, not 3.5%) are at an all-time low because of technology improvements. The first credit cards had a processing fee of 7% (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.businessinsider.com.au&#x2F;history-of-credit-cards-2015-2" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.businessinsider.com.au&#x2F;history-of-credit-cards-2...</a>).')
