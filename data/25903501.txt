Item(by='yarcob', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611585220, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25900128, text='Only Tesla uses a proprietary charging protocol.<p>AFAIK other car makers in the US use either CCS or ChaDeMo.<p>In Europe, all car makers (including Tesla) support CCS.<p>CCS allows DC charging and has support for load balancing, so I assume it could be feasible to create a home CCS charger that directly charges an EV with DC from solar panels.<p>However, considering how expensive power electronics are, and how difficult it would be to integrate such a charger into an existing solar installation, I have doubts that many people would buy such a charger. Also, I don&#x27;t really know how CCS works in detail, so I may be totally wrong about this.')
