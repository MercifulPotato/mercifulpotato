Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610332228, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25723436, text='Automation issues may fall into the category of manufacturer&#x2F;developer negligence; you seem to be suggesting some sort of burden shifting.<p>When you say that a manufacturer should be held liable if their system &#x27;... contributes to a problem...&#x27;, it seems like you&#x27;re suggesting adoption of the (notoriously problematic) &#x27;if not but for&#x27; test. This sort of test is used in negligence cases, but can be useless, as almost anything satisfies it (i.e. slow service at a coffee shop may have been a necessary condition for the timing of that exact accident).')
