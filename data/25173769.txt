Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[25175899, 25174504], score=None, time=1606004518, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25173601, text='horsepower is rarely the limiting factor for a sedan&#x27;s towing capacity. a sedan&#x27;s chassis is only designed to withstand accelerating and stopping its own weight (plus a margin for safety and internal cargo of a reasonable mass). you could probably get away with towing a jetski with a typical sedan, but anything much heavier than that risks bending the chassis. even if a car can tow something without deforming itself, the change in weight distribution can make for a very unsafe situation.')
