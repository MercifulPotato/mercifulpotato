You can install qemu-user-static, &quot;cp &#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin&#x2F;qemu-arm-static path&#x2F;to&#x2F;sd&#x2F;card&#x2F;mount&#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin&#x2F;&quot; and then you can chroot there from your x86 machine. It works by registering a handler in &#x2F;proc&#x2F;sys&#x2F;fs&#x2F;binfmt_misc and then it runs the binaries with qemu.')
