Item(by='adrianmonk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607488987, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25355274, text='West Texas has a lot of empty desert or near-desert land, not a lot of people, and not a lot of industry. But it does have a lot of wind.<p>Houston is a big city. Its metro population is about 7 million. And Houston has a lot of industry, probably the most industrial of the major Texas cities.<p>Texas is big, and depending on what part of West Texas you&#x27;re talking about, Houston is pretty far away from it. For example, it&#x27;s a 350 mile (~550km) drive between Abilene and Houston, and it&#x27;s a 600 mile (~950km) drive between Amarillo and Houston.<p>After lots of wind generation got built in West Texas, they had to add more power lines to bring the power to the more populated part of the state. And they did, and it worked.')
