Item(by='ObscureScience', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607768416, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25394751, text='Im not of the opinion that we&#x27;ve found the &quot;best&quot; user interface. I think the biggest hurdle is that &quot;best&quot; is so different to different people. One part is familiarity, as many power users know, your setup would be utterly useless to an unfamiliar person. I guess the current paradigm is a local maxima of the convergence of discoverability and efficiency and still working on our diverse device specifications (display sizes, input devices and performance).')
