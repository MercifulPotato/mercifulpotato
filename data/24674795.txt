Item(by='pixl97', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601760484, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24674054, text='It&#x27;s a complicated issue for a few reasons...<p>Most of it doesn&#x27;t burn every year, but many places that have been logged and now have no trees will have issues with invasive species that grow quickly and explode into flame.<p>Those invasive species will be displaced when larger tress grow over them, but in the meantime will be a substantial fire risk.<p>Having both the invasive species and high fuel loads under large trees leads to megafires.<p>At the end of the day things are going to burn. The question is can we build better to lessen some parts of the human impact, such as houses not burning to the ground. We may not be able to do anything about the air quality at all.')
