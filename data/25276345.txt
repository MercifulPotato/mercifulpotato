Item(by='gravypod', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606917527, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25276194, text='I&#x27;ve done a few different things with these types of machines. I also know a few people who run some big companies off infra like this.<p>The key for a small team is finding a colo provider with really good remote hands. Essentially you can setup your rack&#x2F;cage, leave good instructions and room for expansion. Then you buy a server from dell with financing, ship it to your DC, someone installs an OS and racks it (or you PXE boot from your rack ;) ), then you can provision anything you want. It&#x27;s essentially like cloud providers but scaling is slower.')
