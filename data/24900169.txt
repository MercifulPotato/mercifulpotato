So on the back of the envelope, a corporate culture that inclines toward hiring white men, also inclines against hiring from 65% of the population. That&#x27;s a lot of talent available for your competitors to hire.<p>And the numbers get even more dramatic the younger you look. Non-hispanic white U.S. residents under 16 made up <i>less than 50% of the population</i> at that age as of last year. And the trend direction is obvious.
