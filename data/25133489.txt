Item(by='SkyPuncher', descendants=None, kids=[25135442], score=None, time=1605677016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25131254, text='I am very adamant about following a strong scrum cycle (although, I&#x27;m loose with the details).<p>* Build a backlog<p>* Run a sprint (I like 2 week sprints at startups)<p>* Don&#x27;t put anything new in the current sprint unless absolutely necessary.<p>* Rinse and repeat<p>The simple nature of having a &quot;waiting&quot; period for new features helps put perspective on them while giving you time to make sure they&#x27;re broadly useful. If only a single person is asking for them, wait to build.')
