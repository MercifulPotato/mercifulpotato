The markets just need to be constrained and regulated. In some extents they can be <i>expanded</i>. E.g. you can force companies to buy and trade quotas for different types of externalities to cover undoing those externalities and let the markets sort out the most efficient way of addressing them.<p>There is complexity there (you need to ensure there aren&#x27;t any easily exploitable loopholes), but markets have lots of uses.')
