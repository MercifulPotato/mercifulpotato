It&#x27;s similar to many colleges: the hardest part is getting in. One coding test involved a math formula I hadn&#x27;t seen in 20+ years. It was simple, but if you &quot;forgot&quot; that, you were stuck.<p>You&#x27;re better off giving someone a more realistic take home test and have them walk you through it during the interview.
