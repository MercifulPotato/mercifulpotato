Item(by='tpmx', descendants=None, kids=[24810874, 24814220, 24810603, 24810115], score=None, time=1602943780, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24810037, text='Wild idea: Power the ship with the batteries in the 7000 cars it carries, partially (speed up) or fully.<p>One cable per car to its charging port - power can be transmitted in that direction too.<p>Edit: Quick reality check: About 500 MWh from the batteries. An oil-based ocean crossing of a similar sized vessel uses 280 tons oil, corresponding to about 3 GWh.<p>So, meh. Probably not worth all the hassle - the cabling, recharging the cars after the crossing, dealing with various extra fire risks, etc.')
