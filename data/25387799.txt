Ever since then I haven&#x27;t had any issues with hibernate, either on my Thinkpad or my multiple Dell machines.<p>I did have a fun regression recently where my ~10 year old desktop machine started randomly hanging and it seems to be a regression of some sort in the nouveau drivers since reverting to a previous kernel version fixed the issue.')
