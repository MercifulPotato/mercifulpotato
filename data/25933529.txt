Most OO involves abuse of the paradigm anyway.<p>Most people don&#x27;t use inheritance to large AND good effect. Or they use it and create a mess. Deep hierarchies of inheritance need to be carefully designed or they become a mess. All the hiding means less clarity about what is happening when things go wrong.<p>That said, its a valid approach and can yield useful results.
