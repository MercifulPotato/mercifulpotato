Item(by='liversage', descendants=None, kids=[25410945, 25412399, 25410564], score=None, time=1607893289, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25409690, text='In Denmark we have light blue lanes in many intersections to make it obvious where cars can expect bikes to cross and also guide the bicyclists.<p>Also more recently &quot;bike boxes&quot; have been drawn on the street effectively widening a bike lane to cover the entire lane right in front of the intersection to ensure that all bicyclist are in front of and not to the right of waiting cars.<p>Unfortunately bicyclists are getting injured or even killed by cars or trucks turning right without noticing the bicyclist and the blue lanes and the &quot;bike boxes&quot; are attempts to prevent this from happening.<p>Danish publication about bicycle safety in intersections with some photos: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;idekatalogforcykeltrafik.dk&#x2F;krydsloesninger&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;idekatalogforcykeltrafik.dk&#x2F;krydsloesninger&#x2F;</a>')
