Item(by='lottin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609585441, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25605644, text='Possession and ownership are two very different things.  Physical control can be transferred without loss of ownership.  This is what happens when somebody rents a car, or when somebody deposits money with a bank.   Sovereignty is a concept very specific to political science. It doesn&#x27;t make a lot of sense to use it in other contexts.  You can use it, if you want, but it&#x27;s usually a sign that the person using it doesn&#x27;t really know what they&#x27;re talking about.')
