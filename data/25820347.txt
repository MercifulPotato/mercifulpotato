Item(by='chandra381', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610966316, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817567, text='The entirety of modern economics is based on the presupposition that monetary value is the only factor in rational decision making.<p>A &quot;free market&quot; is literally defined as - &quot;a system in which the *prices* for goods and services are self-regulated by the open market and by consumers&quot; - all goods and services have a monetary price in such a system.<p>It is both inappropriate and in bad taste to call this a free market solution when it is clearly not.')
