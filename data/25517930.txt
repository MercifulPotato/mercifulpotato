Item(by='horsawlarway', descendants=None, kids=[25518229], score=None, time=1608733878, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25517541, text='Note that this working will depend heavily on what usb-3 peripherals you&#x27;re plugging in, and the exact resolution of your 4k monitors.<p>a display at 3840x2160@60hz will eat up about 19.8gbps.  Two of them together will use 39.6gpbs.<p>Thunderbolt 3 only supports 40gbps.  So if you have peripherals that need anything more than basic mouse&#x2F;keyboard (in my case, a decent webcam and a usb to ethernet adapter) this setup will fail.')
