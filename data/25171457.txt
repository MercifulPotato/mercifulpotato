Item(by='fiddlerwoaroof', descendants=None, kids=[25171641], score=None, time=1605982345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25171280, text='Part of the problem with things like &quot;implement Bubble Sort&quot; is that most actual programming tasks are &quot;here&#x27;s a problem, find the best implementation&quot;.  There aren&#x27;t many sorting tasks where Bubble Sort is an appropriate implementation: a _better_ interview question would be &quot;here&#x27;s a list of numbers, sort them without using a standard library function.&quot; Followed by &quot;what are some advantages&#x2F;disadvantages of the method you used? Can you think of a better way to do this?&quot;')
