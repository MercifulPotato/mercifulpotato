Item(by='el_nahual', descendants=None, kids=[24927166, 24927743, 24927342], score=None, time=1603938945, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24925222, text='An even bigger misconception is that indulgences were actually bought on behalf of _someone else_.<p>They were meant to address a very real problem with catholic heaven&#x2F;hell theology:<p>What if I belong in heaven, but somene I love, who I can&#x27;t possibly imagine spending eternity without, belongs in hell?<p>Indulgences were originally meant to address this: the party that belonged in heaven could, through their sacrifice, guarantee a spot in heaven for their loved one. It was only later that the &quot;sacrifice&quot; become strictly monetary.')
