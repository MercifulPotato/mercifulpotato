WhatsApp has default end-to-end encryption. Telegram does not.&quot;<p>By default, WhatsApp stores everything unencrypted (encrypted with third party keys) at third party servers, Google’s and Apple’s servers.<p>By default, Telegram stores everything unencrypted (encrypted with Telegram’s keys) at Telegram’s own servers.<p>By default, Signal stores everything encrypted at your device with your own keys.')
