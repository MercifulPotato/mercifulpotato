Item(by='bdcravens', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610046695, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25675668, text='When you mine Bitcoins, there is a reward. To constrain supply, Bitcoin&#x27;s algorithm cuts that reward in half approximately every 4 years. This reduces the <i>increase in supply</i>. Prior bubbles have correlated with these &quot;halvenings&quot;, typically about a year or so later. (2012&#x2F;2013, 2016&#x2F;2017, 2020&#x2F;2021)')
