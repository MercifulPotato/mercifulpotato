I vastly reduce temperature cycling by [disabling TurboBoost](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.archlinux.org&#x2F;index.php&#x2F;CPU_frequency_scaling#Disabling_Turbo_Boost" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.archlinux.org&#x2F;index.php&#x2F;CPU_frequency_scaling#D...</a>) and the absolute temperature by using the &quot;powersave&quot; governor.')
