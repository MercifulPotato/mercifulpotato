Item(by='tmp538394722', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606806093, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25244900, text='Isn’t “_perfect_” forward secrecy a misnomer in this context?<p>One of the big innovations of the signal protocol, (called axolotl at the time) was that perfect forward secrecy, requires a handshake between participants before _each_ message, which is not compatible with the nature of async messaging, where the recipient might be (probably is) offline at the time of sending.<p>Instead Signal protocol does an <i>eventual</i> key ratcheting as soon as messages are round tripped between participants by essentially attaching half a handshake  to each outgoing message.')
