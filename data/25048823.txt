Consumers don&#x27;t want to chase down the guy who made the screw that failed and caused a bunch of excess deaths. You put the screw in the assembly, you took most of the profit margins. So you get the lawsuit.<p>If you want to go and sue your vendor to recover damages, that&#x27;s between you and the vendor. But the class action goes to Acme Inc, not Acme Screws and Fasteners.<p>Similarly, I&#x27;m not getting a mansion. I can barely get you to buy the servers we need to make half of what you say not a blatant lie. I&#x27;m not the one who should be punished when they find out about it. I&#x27;m not the one lying to people&#x27;s faces while I pocket their checks.')
