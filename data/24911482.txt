Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603830479, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24911410, text='Electric vehicle manufacturing is almost a different business model from ICE vehicle manufacturers. If you look at the history of car manufacturing, you&#x27;ll see that the early ones would just deliver a chassis (usually with an engine and transmission). Even the body was &#x27;aftermarket&#x27;!<p>Electric vehicles have no engine, and the entire drive-train is simpler, so it&#x27;s now easier and more important to be vertically integrated (like Tesla).')
