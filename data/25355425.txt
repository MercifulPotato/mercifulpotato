Then they pay to market it and put it on the radio (which is just one big ad network these days).<p>Really it&#x27;s very similar to raising venture capital. You don&#x27;t need to do it, you can bootstrap a company with almost no money and people do it all the time. But if you have the chance to work with an expert who&#x27;s willing to put in a lot of money upfront, it can really accelerate your trajectory.')
