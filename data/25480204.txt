<i>one of</i> the primary reasons <i>some of</i> the colonists decided to declare their independence from Britain was because they wanted to protect the institution of slavery.<p>These two statements are totally different. “One of the primary things the Democrats want is to defund the police” is obviously not true. “One of the primary things <i>some of</i> the Democrats want is to defund the police” is obviously true, but means something totally different.<p>The 1619 Project’s hit piece on capitalism was so bad that Jacobin had to step in with a correction piece: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jacobinmag.com&#x2F;2019&#x2F;08&#x2F;how-slavery-shaped-american-capitalism" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jacobinmag.com&#x2F;2019&#x2F;08&#x2F;how-slavery-shaped-american-c...</a><p>&gt;
