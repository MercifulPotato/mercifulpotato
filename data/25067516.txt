Item(by='atoav', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605167590, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25066769, text='Typora is great. But I also tend to use it in &quot;source&quot; view where I can she all the markup.<p>The point of markdown is literally that things don&#x27;t hide. Wanna know if that semicolon is included in the italic styling? You will see it right away. Wanna know if a link has an old URL or is pointing to a weird domain? You see it.<p>This alone makes it worth it. Forcing the whole thing back into a WYSIWYG-editor will just mean that markdown becomes unreadable. A bit like when people used MS Word to write HTML.')
