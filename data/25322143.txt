IPv4=8.8.8.8 means &quot;this goes to the DNS server&quot;.  The DNS server helps you find all the other IP addresses you need, which is why this one is special.  One DNAT rule, one SNAT rule, and you&#x27;re done.<p>IMO IP should have had this feature from day one.')
