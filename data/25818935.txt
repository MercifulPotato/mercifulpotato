Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610952293, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25818558, text='Also, know the difference between honing and sharpening.<p>The edge of a knife is very fragile, because it is very very thin. As such, when you use the knife&#x27;s edge, it will eventually &quot;bend over&quot;.<p>A honing edge straightens the edge again. That&#x27;s not &quot;sharpening&quot;, its just pushing a stick against the knife edge to realign everything. It will cut better once you hone it.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.globeequipment.com&#x2F;learning-center&#x2F;wp-content&#x2F;uploads&#x2F;2018&#x2F;02&#x2F;Victorinox_Honing-Graphic-1.jpg" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.globeequipment.com&#x2F;learning-center&#x2F;wp-content&#x2F;up...</a><p>-----------<p>&quot;Sharpening&quot; is when you remove metal from the edge of the knife.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.shopify.com&#x2F;s&#x2F;files&#x2F;1&#x2F;0001&#x2F;8425&#x2F;4516&#x2F;files&#x2F;messen_slijpen_EN_710x710.png" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.shopify.com&#x2F;s&#x2F;files&#x2F;1&#x2F;0001&#x2F;8425&#x2F;4516&#x2F;files&#x2F;messe...</a><p>Honing should be done every time you use the knife. Sharpening should only be done every month (if you use the knife a lot) or once every year (if its a rarely used knife)')
