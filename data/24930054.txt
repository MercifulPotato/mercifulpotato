Item(by='simias', descendants=None, kids=[24930694], score=None, time=1603976767, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24929854, text='They seem to imply that the seller was actually honest and thought it was genuine, so I guess somebody did get shafted in the process (although probably by a much smaller amount).<p>It&#x27;s quite a clever hustle in a way: since the wrapped box is worth so much more than an opened one it&#x27;s an interesting paradox: if you don&#x27;t open the box, you don&#x27;t know for sure that it&#x27;s genuine but if you do open it then it loses most of its value. Therefore if you manage to make a convincing fake using a clean box and some shrink wrap-fu it will probably be hard to ascertain the authenticity of the product without damaging it.<p>It&#x27;s like Schrodinger&#x27;s Pikachu.')
