Item(by='lozf', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604273439, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24962650, text='Youtube only offer audio in either AAC (in a .m4a container) or Opus formats.<p>Since lossy to lossy transcodes always cause some degradation due to the way perceptual codecs discard data, it&#x27;s always best to use them as-is without converting.<p>AAC  is widely compatible these days and is the broadcast industry (ITU &#x2F; EBU) standard for distribution of end-user audio, the default recording format for most consumer and pro-sumer equipment, and also what Youtube&#x27;s upload giudelines suggest.  Therefore it&#x27;s better just to get the `-f140 -x`  for maximum compatibility, unless you know you can play the Opus audio and have reason to believe the upload was higher quality.')
