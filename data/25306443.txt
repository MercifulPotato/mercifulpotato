Item(by='starfallg', descendants=None, kids=[25306668, 25307071, 25306763], score=None, time=1607109393, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25306303, text='In my experience, the charities usually don&#x27;t throw those away.<p>Over here in the UK and much of Europe, the charities sell them by the ton to traders that ship them to Africa or other developing countries.<p>Some charities don&#x27;t even handle them, they enter into a contract with a third party that specialises in the trade that collects the clothes and then pays the charity a fixed amount of money per ton. The whole service is branded, so you would think that you&#x27;re donating to the charity directly when in fact they are paid by the trader by weight.')
