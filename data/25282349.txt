Item(by='davidwitt415', descendants=None, kids=[25282675], score=None, time=1606946857, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25281750, text='RTFA:<p>Batteries and electricity storage follow learning curves too\nOne of the downsides of renewable sources is their intermittent supply cycle. The sun doesn’t always shine and the wind doesn’t always blow. Technologies like batteries that store electric power are key to balance the changing supply from renewables with the inflexible demand for electricity.<p>Fortunately electricity storage technologies are also among the few technologies that are following steeply declining learning curves.')
