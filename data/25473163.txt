Item(by='Groxx', descendants=None, kids=[25473370], score=None, time=1608332545, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25469708, text='My boundary on DRYing has settled on &quot;when it&#x27;s semantically the same code&quot;.<p>Code that looks the same but is used for different purposes &#x2F; has different meaning?  Keep it separate.  They may diverge in the future.<p>Code that truly means the same thing, but may or may not look the same?  DRY it up, any variation is probably unintended and will surprise people later when A doesn&#x27;t behave like B.')
