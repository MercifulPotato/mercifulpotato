Item(by='a3n', descendants=None, kids=[24967318], score=None, time=1604318494, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24966728, text='I don&#x27;t use Gmail for personal, but a former small employer used it for business, and thus so did I for that business (only).<p>But I used Thunderbird for access, which was set up to make local copies.<p>When I was -- ahem -- fired, they soon said that I had something that was theirs. But I had given that something to another employee, who was now saying she had given it to me.<p>Although I no longer had access to the company Gmail account, I had my copy of the email thread where the other employee and I discussed the handoff of said thing <i>from</i> me to her.<p>And that was that.<p>If you use any Google or other company&#x27;s service for something important, it&#x27;s good&#x2F;essential to make backups.')
