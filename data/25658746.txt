Item(by='vntok', descendants=None, kids=[25659109], score=None, time=1609948297, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25658341, text='Unfortunately your script still calls a third party domain, which is trivial to block using a generic AdBlock&#x2F;uBlock rule. Instead, you should host the matomo script (under a different filename of course) on your own domain. That way it won&#x27;t be as easily blocked.<p>I go as far as to send all the tracking parameters through a custom server script before they are proxied to GA and Matomo. That way, I can change the script and parameter names at will, making them much more difficult to block. For example, Matomo-related blocking rules are as follows:<p>&#x2F;matomo-tracking.<p>&#x2F;matomo.js$domain=~github.com<p>&#x2F;matomo.php<p>&#x2F;matomo&#x2F;<i>$domain=~github.com|~matomo.org|~wordpress.org<p>&#x2F;piwik-$domain=~github.com|~matomo.org|~piwik.org|~piwik.pro|piwikpro.de<p>&#x2F;piwik.$image,script,domain=~matomo.org|~piwik.org|~piwik.pro|piwikpro.de<p>&#x2F;piwik.</i>&#x2F;ping?<p>&#x2F;piwik.js<p>&#x2F;piwik.php<p>&#x2F;piwik&#x2F;*$domain=~github.com|~matomo.org|~piwik.org|~piwik.pro<p>&#x2F;piwik1.<p>&#x2F;piwik2.js<p>&#x2F;piwik_<p>&#x2F;piwikapi.js<p>&#x2F;piwikC_<p>&#x2F;piwikTracker.')
