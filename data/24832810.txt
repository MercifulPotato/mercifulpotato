Item(by='jonas21', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603154586, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24832676, text='It&#x27;s not saying the law can&#x27;t be repealed by majority vote. It&#x27;s saying the <i>legislature</i> can&#x27;t change the law without a seven-eights vote. The law can be repealed by voters via another ballot initiative with a simple majority.<p>By default, laws enacted via ballot initiatives in California can&#x27;t be amended by the legislature without voter approval. California has some of the strongest protections for citizen initiatives in the country, which comes with plusses and minuses depending on your perspective.')
