Item(by='athms', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608257441, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25451391, text='In California, the two-party consent rule only applies when there is an expectation of privacy.<p>As an example, dash cams that record audio are legal in California. However, there can be cases when somebody expects to have a confidential conversation with you (on the phone, standing next to the door, or from the backseat) and you must notify them of the audio recording.')
