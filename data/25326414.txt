Item(by='baybal2', descendants=None, kids=[25326830, 25326742], score=None, time=1607287193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25326031, text='Panic is completely not a reason to not evacuate:<p>1. you do not panic in fire unless you want to die.<p>2. building may have less than an hour, or even minutes of structural integrity left after first alarm is heard.<p>3. you may have single minutes until people are incapacitated, and cannot leave on their own, or already dead from poisoning.<p>4. you may have minutes until smoke penetrates stairwells, or fire gets the emergency ventilation, or smoke gets thick enough to block emergency lighting, and people are trapped, even if they have smoke hoods.')
