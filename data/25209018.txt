Like excess of urea leading to gout or effects of trans fats on arteries.<p>Also, people who train too much (like long-distance runners), seem to be vulnerable to heart disease. [0] The theory behind this is that heart, just like other muscles, needs rest to heal microdamage resulting from vigorous exercise.
