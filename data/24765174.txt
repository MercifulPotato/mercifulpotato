Still today, the techniques used by Bootstrap are still used, altough a bit less hacky with calc(), but still we&#x27;re relying a lot on negative margins.<p>With gap, making a gutter is trivial. And while gap _can_ be used with flexbox, the thing is that it&#x27;s not supported by Safari[1], and who knows when it will be implemented.<p>1: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;caniuse.com&#x2F;flexbox-gap" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;caniuse.com&#x2F;flexbox-gap</a>')
