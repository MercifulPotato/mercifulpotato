Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605221164, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25075398, text='We all start out with the same equipment; the only automation we get is muscle memory. We add software, and multiply that. If we add automation to the software, we multiply it again.<p>There are reasons why some people can do more than other people. Some people have natural advantages, in certain directions. Nobody has them in all directions, but automation can  often compensate in the other directions.<p>If you don&#x27;t have ambition to excel, you can stay with the minimum. If you have enough natural talent, you can play to your strengths and excel only there. It&#x27;s a choice of where to invest your time and effort. It should be a conscious choice.<p>IDEs are a trap because they automate only certain things the IDE author thought was important, and do not reward impulses to improve in other directions.')
