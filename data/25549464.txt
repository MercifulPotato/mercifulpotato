Item(by='Buttons840', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609053598, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25548898, text='Sins of a Solar Empire had an interesting mechanic: You could generate trade within the area you controlled, and little non-controllable trade ships would fly around randomly within the area you were generating trade. If the enemy destroyed these trade ships they would get a small amount of resources and your trade income would drop an equal amount. It simulated logistics, because an enemy army sitting in the middle of your territory disrupted a lot, but didn&#x27;t add complexity to the game.')
