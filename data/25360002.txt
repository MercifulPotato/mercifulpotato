Item(by='segmondy', descendants=None, kids=[25361947], score=None, time=1607529690, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25359394, text='Even if it&#x27;s not a lengthy process to fire them, as someone in a leadership role I believe it&#x27;s the duty of leaders to not switch to a firing mindset, but to exactly do what this article suggests.  Mentor, Lead, Coach.   Set clear expectations especially on how your direct reports can measure their contribution.  A fast feedback cycle that any employee can tap into to see their progress is very important.<p>Hiring is not easy, so when I hire, I&#x27;m really their biggest cheerleader. I want everyone to succeed, if folks reporting to me succeed, that often means I&#x27;m doing my job, makes my life easier, means I made a good hiring decision.    I&#x27;m not saying that I&#x27;m a smart manager, but good managers don&#x27;t wish to see you fail, they really wish to see you succeed and will do everything possible to make that happen.  Just saying this because I know plenty of folks are distrustful of managers.')
