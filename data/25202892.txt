It is also a good way to &quot;win&quot; some attention and goodwill, without spending months trying to actually win the competition itself. Publishing Notebooks also helps you improve your coding&#x2F;presentation skills, for a popular notebook needs to be useful for a wide audience (or fairly competitive).<p>The best techniques, certainly coming from teams, are hardly ever published as Notebooks. But yes, many winning teams will eventually incorporate some of the information in the Notebooks, if only to hedge against the others doing the same.')
