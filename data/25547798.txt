Item(by='29athrowaway', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609030488, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25545323, text='Post-mortems start being important when users rely on your stuff and stakeholders want an explanation for a problem. They also want to understand if you understand the problem, if you care about the problem, and how is it not going to happen again.<p>If you say &quot;we fixed the issue&quot; 10 times in a row for the same issue, you lose credibility and the organization will look to replace you.<p>If a problem in your product is affecting my ability to do business, you either give me a proper explanation or I will find a replacement product.<p>The prototyping phase of a product is just an engineering honeymoon, it does not last forever.')
