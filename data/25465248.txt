Item(by='pmeunier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608282564, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25461289, text='You could certainly do that, and this is in fact one of the ways to use Pijul. However, you would completely miss out on the most important novelty of Pijul, which is <i>patch commutation</i>:<p>When we say &quot;Pijul is patch-based&quot;, we don&#x27;t only mean that it stores patches, but rather that patches that could be written independently commute, meaning that they can be applied in any order, and are guaranteed to give the same result in all cases (including when they conflict).<p>This simplifies many workflows.')
