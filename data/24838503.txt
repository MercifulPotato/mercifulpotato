[1] With the first you can create charts, timelines, and histograms, based on Vega. With the second, you can add points on maps (with images), have shapes, and outlines, from OpenStreetMap. Unfortunately, both aren&#x27;t used as much as they should because most are comfortable using other tools and baking things into PNGs, and these weren&#x27;t advertised that heavily.<p>[0]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mediawiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Extension:Graph&#x2F;Demo" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mediawiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Extension:Graph&#x2F;Demo</a><p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mediawiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Help:Extension:Kartographer" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mediawiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Help:Extension:Kartographer</a>')
