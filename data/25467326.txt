There&#x27;s also other factors like work. Cortisol levels are higher coming off of work and it takes time to wind down.<p>Think of it this way: there&#x27;s nothing special about a time zone, and yet we know that if we moved several time zones away, we&#x27;d shift. The differences are in light patterns, timing of work and social life, food intake, etc. Incidentally, many of these factors are within our control.')
