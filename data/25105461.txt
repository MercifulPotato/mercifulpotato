Item(by='bitminer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605478701, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25103911, text='Interesting.  I did some (IT) work for a company making photo plotters for the PCB industry.  The MDA Fire 9000, later Cymbolic Sciences and other companies. This was about 1985.<p>They had a minicomputer with a hardware rasterizer driving a laser writing to photosensitive film.  They had amazing throughput for the day.<p>One of the issues was temperature and humidity stability of the film.  It would change dimensions with as little as 10% change in humidity.<p>The change was more than the resolution of the laser.')
