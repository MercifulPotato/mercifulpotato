At long range the bandwidth is very very low and latencies are very very high. Meshtastic will never be a replacement for a sat phone. Think of it as more like a two directional pager. Short text messages with latencies possibly as high as 40 or 50 seconds.<p>Lots of people disappear from the forums when they realise they won’t be able to something like use this to stream YouTube to their off grid cabin...')
