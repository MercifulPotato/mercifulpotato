Item(by='willtim', descendants=None, kids=[24905553], score=None, time=1603787549, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24905351, text='AptX is lossy compression which when applied to already compressed sources (e.g. Spotify), still gives noticeably inferior audio (it&#x27;s effectively transcoding). \nMost people listen to compressed music and so the right thing to do with limited bandwidth is to send the original compressed data over Bluetooth (as Apple&#x2F;Bose do with AAC). AptX is probably fine if you have non-lossy compressed sources like FLAC.')
