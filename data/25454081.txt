To do this, simply change for example hero_version from 1 to 2 or 3. The preview is immediately updated to show you the styling of the selected version.<p>The sections are mostly brought to you by Tailblocks, some ready-to-use components built with Tailwind CSS.<p>You can find the repository here: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;jakeprins&#x2F;nextwind" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;jakeprins&#x2F;nextwind</a>')
