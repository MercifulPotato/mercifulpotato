Item(by='elihu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611721137, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25923098, text='I think you mean the reverse; equal temperament &quot;just works&quot; regardless of key and doesn&#x27;t require configuration, whereas JI you have to specify a key, and there are many possible scales depending on which of infinitely many notes you want to include.<p>It&#x27;s a tradeoff between convenience and optimally consonant harmonies.  ET makes a lot of sense for guitar because you have fixed frets (though JI guitars do exist), but violin can be played in any intonation so there&#x27;s less reason to use ET unless you&#x27;re accompanying an ET instrument like piano or guitar.')
