Item(by='cma', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608517241, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25491462, text='Maybe he means cases where a lock free queue can&#x27;t implement batching easily. Batching by making the messages themselves contain batches of messages makes them variable sized which can cause less efficient allocations etc., whereas with a lock you can hold the lock, insert N same-sized messages, release the lock.<p>If it is done as a linked list there may be good multiple insert options, but then you have a linked list and all the extra jumping around memory that involves compared to a ring buffer.')
