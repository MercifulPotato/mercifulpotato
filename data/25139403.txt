Item(by='ethbr0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605719751, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137262, text='+1<p>One key component that Voss spends a lot of time on -- if your counterparty comes out a negotiation <i>feeling</i> like they lost, everyone loses.<p>The outcome of a successful negotiation is that a fair deal is struck, and everyone feels good (or at least not bad) about it.<p>There are very few situations in the real world that are true one-offs, where you&#x27;ll never have to interact with that counterparty again. Consequently, scorched-Earth is a poor long-term approach.')
