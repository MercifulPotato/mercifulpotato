Item(by='Stupulous', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601570686, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24652580, text='If you&#x27;re right, you already have mental systems in place to produce the right answer. If you&#x27;re wrong, the feedback will help your systems self-correct.<p>If you ask for help, you&#x27;re using someone else&#x27;s systems, and whether they&#x27;re right or wrong carries very little weight about how you should be approaching things. You&#x27;re not producing a system, you&#x27;re adding an item to a lookup table that you might someday use to build a coherent approach.<p>&#x27;Fire is hot&#x27; is a good entry for a lookup table, but you also need to develop a general model of where potential dangers are and that you should approach them with caution. There is no number of entries that equal that system.')
