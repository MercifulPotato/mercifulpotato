Your mortgage absolutely lowers your savings rate.<p>Your mortgage would also affect your cash flow. If we&#x27;re talking about profit&#x2F;loss, the interest-portion of your mortgage absolutely affects your profit&#x2F;loss.<p>Only the principle portion of your mortgage statement is &quot;free&quot; from a profit&#x2F;loss perspective. Which it is: you can always leverage the principle into a HELOC or other financial instrument if you need emergency cash.')
