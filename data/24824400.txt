Item(by='heja2009', descendants=None, kids=[24830893], score=None, time=1603097160, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24823902, text='The Apple II was hardwired to run on 60Hz AC and used a limited character set. Minimal hardware design has its downsides.\nIn countries that run 50Hz AC games and other real-time software ran slower or had wrong timing. I noticed quite a number of Apple IIs in Germany being used in engineering or with measurement equipment because of the expansion slots. But software had to be modified to cope for the 50Hz.')
