Item(by='qwantim1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610418337, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25741367, text='Organized scalable social moderation is as doable with centralized services as it is with P2P.<p>The difference is that a centralized service has at least one person that must be responsible for content, whether or not they moderate it adequately, whereas, with a P2P service, each user may effectively run their own service and must be responsible for its content, whether or not they moderate it adequately.<p>P2P would seem then to have more scalable user-invested moderation. Unfortunately, some using these services think they’re in the internet equivalent of a bohemian nudist colony. That’s not how it works, though. The responsibility is heightened, not lessened.')
