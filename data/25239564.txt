Item(by='HenryKissinger', descendants=None, kids=[25239761], score=None, time=1606589485, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25239523, text='Child labour and slavery remain commonplace in many poor countries. In industrialized countries, a high level of labour productivity, technology, and automation makes it impractical or too expensive to hire a slave or a child. Put another way, advanced economies are advanced enough that there is little useful work that could be performed by a child or a slave, if child labour or slavery were still legal.<p>Data collection, on the other hand, can only take place in countries with a high level of Internet penetration and a developed digital infrastructure.')
