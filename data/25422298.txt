I&#x27;ll take cellphones over landlines <i>every single time</i>.<p>1) In the event of a major disaster, you&#x27;ve probably evacuated. You can&#x27;t take your landline with you.<p>2) Telephone poles burn.<p>3) Even if the cellular system is completely hosed, you might still be able to get on a data network.
