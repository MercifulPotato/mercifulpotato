As you&#x27;ve pointed out, the trend is for Google to pull it out of the website and display it on the results page, cutting the content creator out entirely.<p>Building a tool or service on top of the semantic web is out of reach for most developers because of inconsistencies and complexities, unless they use the tools provided by the giants – and that goes directly against the principles of the semantic web.<p>The exception here is niche use cases and academia working with huge datasets, where the semantic web seems to be incredibly useful.')
