He crucified a soldier before the Roman earthworks, both to demoralize the soldiers and to warn his fellow slaves what fate awaited them on defeat. His men breached the wall and, if I&#x27;m remembering my chronology, fought their way to the Alps (before things turned out badly for them.)')
