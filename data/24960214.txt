At this point they need to reason about almost as much as the server.<p>Search for &quot;offline first&quot; to see how people are exploring architectures like this.<p>Edit: source: I worked on a product bundling chat functionality as a plug in.<p>In fairness, I think this is not so relevant for apps which are consumption first, but if there&#x27;s any kind of collaboration, or even just writing from the client, you want to show an optimistic (at least; some systems make the client state canonical) representation regardless of sync status.')
