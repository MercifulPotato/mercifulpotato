Item(by='JoshTriplett', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606945207, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25275043, text='Instances take longer to start up; Lambda processes requests in milliseconds or less. Lambda automatically manages a pool for you.<p>If you&#x27;re running a predictable process and you know how long it&#x27;ll take in advance, an instance may make sense. If you&#x27;re running an <i>unpredictable</i> process, where you won&#x27;t know how long it&#x27;ll take until it&#x27;s done, and it might be quite fast, the low startup time and fine-granularity billing helps.')
