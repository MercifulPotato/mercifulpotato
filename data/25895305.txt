Item(by='crocodiletears', descendants=None, kids=[25896910], score=None, time=1611518522, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25895107, text='The wonderful part is also its Achilles heel. Mastodon is advertised as an alternative to social networks, but since your identity is tied to your node, its much closer to joining a web forum structured like twitter with the possibility (but not guarantee) of interacting with similar forums. From that perspective it&#x27;s great. When people offer it as an alternative to conventional social media, it comes up massively short for anyone not trying to live in a bubble.<p>Contrasted with facebook (or a twitter with groups), if you join a group of motorcyclists, there&#x27;s little to no risk that you&#x27;ll be excluded from the Scooter group of-which you&#x27;re already a member because a few bikers got into a spat with scoot-gang, nor will you be excluded from any of the groups that get along well with scoot-gang.')
