The &quot;COM loader&quot; just hands it off to NTVDM which does not exist on 64-bit Windows (there are 3rd-party implementations though)<p>And yes, the com extension is still recognized and will pop up a dialog telling you &quot;This app can&#x27;t run on your PC&quot;.<p>But actually .com and .exe files are handled equivalently, so you can just rename an .exe (i.e. PE executable) to .com and it will run - that doesn&#x27;t make it a COM executable though...')
