I am one of the maintainers of the Radicle project.<p>The main problem we encountered with similar systems that rely on blockchains &#x2F; dht for storage is the problem of &#x27;blockchain poisoning&#x27;.<p>This is when someone deliberately adds illegal content to an append-only source in hopes to make the sole act of replicating the project legally problematic, as correctly pointed out by Konstantin Ryabitsev of the Linux foundation with regards to a previous version of Radicle that was relying on IPFS. see <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;radicle.community&#x2F;t&#x2F;the-radicle-social-model&#x2F;317" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;radicle.community&#x2F;t&#x2F;the-radicle-social-model&#x2F;317</a>')
