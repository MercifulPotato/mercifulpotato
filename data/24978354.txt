Item(by='brokebroadbeat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604400345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24965343, text='I think a lot of this holds true. Our team experimented with what would happen if we stopped having meetings and sending emails, a hard shift into asynchronous working. It was really useful for helping us find the middle ground, what worked well, rather than stringently requiring everyone to work that way for ever. For example, we found that having meeting-free days really helped people focus on important tasks, getting more done.')
