Item(by='jannes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603353076, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24850528, text='You can reset your sync data for any extension in Firefox.<p>Just go to about:debugging, click on &quot;This Firefox&quot;, locate the extension and click the &quot;Inspect&quot; button. In the case of MAC this should lead you to  about:devtools-toolbox?type=extension&amp;id=%40testpilot-containers<p>In the devtools that open you need to go to the Console tab and run this command:<p><pre><code>  await browser.storage.sync.clear()\n</code></pre>\nIf you want to see the data before you delete it, you can show it with this command:<p><pre><code>  await browser.storage.sync.get()</code></pre>')
