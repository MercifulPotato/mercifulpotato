Even there, I found it very difficult to give back to the project because the maintainers of the project weren&#x27;t terribly interest in having or maintaining the enterprise-y, cloud-y features that I was implementing.<p>It seems like most OSS projects have a single key contributor &#x2F; BDFL who decides the direction of the project.  Outside of financial incentives, it&#x27;s hard to get groups of people moving in the same direction.')
