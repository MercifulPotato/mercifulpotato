Item(by='matheusmoreira', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607295452, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25327296, text='It is easy to have high availability with torrents. It&#x27;s even possible to quantify how many redundant copies are available among the swarm. Users just need to seed the data for extended periods of time.<p>Torrent trackers have come up with many mechanisms to incentivize contribution, usually new data and bandwidth. It&#x27;s definitely possible to reward redundancy instead: the less copies there are, the more users are rewarded for storing a copy on their computers.')
