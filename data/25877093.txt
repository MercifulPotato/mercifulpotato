Item(by='WorldMaker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611354505, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25873267, text='Presumptive user feedback: &quot;How come when I click this bookmark which has the icon nice and right there it sometimes takes minutes for the icon to show up on the tab?&quot;<p>Principle of least surprises for the user is probably at play here. Bookmarks and tab icons seem like reasonably similar &quot;chrome&quot; to the user.<p>Separating the caches isn&#x27;t necessarily easy either: it is just as likely to hand the trackers at that point a good signal for people who bookmarked a site based on whatever heuristic ends up being to refresh that cache if it is no longer &quot;recently accessed tabs&quot;.')
