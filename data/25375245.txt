Item(by='curiousllama', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607616976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25375131, text='Think about purpose and benefit.  A cynical view, perhaps, but the way to a good relationship is to make it part of the transaction.<p>What are this person&#x27;s priorities (outcomes they need to make happen) for the year? How can you help make that happen?<p>Come to the conversation with &quot;you&#x27;re doing X; how can I help make that happen?&quot; - and when you explain why you want to help, make it personal (&quot;I want to get to Y, and this will help me get there&quot;). You help with X, and they&#x27;ll hep with Y.')
