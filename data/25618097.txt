Item(by='AnHonestComment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609633580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25617753, text='It’s more than that:<p>“Bike 3 out of 5 days to the office” is a reflection of your goals as a human — fitness, enjoying the outdoors, whatever.<p>The problem is our natural inclination to be lazy and take the bus prevents us from accomplishing our desire to be healthier or enjoy the outdoors.<p>Having an explicit goal you measure the results of is a way to assess if you’re living the way you <i>want</i> to.<p>You’re captain here: no one is making you ride the bike — but if you <i>want</i> to ride your bike more, making that goal explicit and measured is a way to overcome your own internal conflicts preventing you from doing that.<p>Captain made a call and now we’re doing it.')
