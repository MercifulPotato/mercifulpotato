Item(by='pydry', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602249426, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24728959, text='People get very attached to their meanings and very resistant to having redefinitions &quot;imposed&quot; upon them.<p>Inventing new terms avoids all that baggage.<p>There <i>is</i> also reason to believe that a replacement term will be less misunderstood <i>if it is more precisely defined</i> than the original.<p>The words that have the most &quot;drift&quot; are ones with emotional baggage attached. &quot;Socialism&quot; or &quot;Capitalism&quot; has baggage and drift. &quot;Technical debt&quot; does too. &quot;Carbon&quot; and &quot;differential equation&quot; do not. &quot;Coupling&quot; does not.')
