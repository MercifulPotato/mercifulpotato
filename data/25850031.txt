The lobbying laws are there precisely to regulate this kind of thing. You can&#x27;t completely isolate lawmakers, and wouldn&#x27;t want to. But you do want to know who they&#x27;re talking to, and how much they&#x27;re being paid to. That&#x27;s why they have to register as lobbyists, and be audited for that. They&#x27;re not allowed to funnel money or favors, and the FEC is watching.<p>It&#x27;s far from perfect, but it&#x27;s not as nefarious as it sounds. It&#x27;s a compromise.')
