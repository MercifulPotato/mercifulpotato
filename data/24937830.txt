Item(by='csixty4', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604018050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24933010, text='You&#x27;ll see the US&#x27;s extended ZIP codes referred to as Zip+4, as they have nine digits instead of the usual five. So, 90210-1234 refers to a specific Delivery Sector and Delivery Segment within Beverly Hills. The segment can be a particular building or even just one floor of a building. Or, it can refer to all the buildings on one side of the street. It&#x27;s up to the post office.')
