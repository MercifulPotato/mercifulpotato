You can&#x27;t export it or share it to other apps because that&#x27;d be competition for the service you&#x27;re paying for. It&#x27;s online-only because it makes the entire service useless once you stop paying.<p>There&#x27;s technical solutions for this, and they&#x27;ll get no traction whatsoever so long as it&#x27;s more profitable to keep people locked into a cloud-based service.')
