Item(by='1propionyl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606305228, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25207444, text='This is tangential, but it mildly irks me how commonly &quot;elite&quot; is now used by most people to refer to a single person. &quot;Elite&quot; is a collective noun and refers to a group, not an individual.<p>An elite is a group of people who are distinguished from their peers in some way. In common parlance &quot;some way&quot; usually means influence, power, seniority, respect from peers, performance, or wealth.')
