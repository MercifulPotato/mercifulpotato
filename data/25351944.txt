Having a free gateway to your paid product is fine, but at some point you need to give potential customers a nudge they can&#x27;t refuse.<p>Pre-2014 it was fairly obvious that a full clone of a commercially supported product, with the proverbial &#x27;just a search-and-replace of the product name&#x27;, was going to attract interest &amp; action from upstream.<p>OTOH in the ~25 years I&#x27;ve been using Debian, I&#x27;ve never been (rudely) <i>surprised</i> by one of their announcements.  Their social contract [0] is clear on every point that&#x27;s relevant to users of an OS, especially users looking to commit long term to a platform.<p>[0]  <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.debian.org&#x2F;social_contract" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.debian.org&#x2F;social_contract</a>')
