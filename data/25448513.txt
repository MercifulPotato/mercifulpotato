There really wasn&#x27;t any kind of killer app, like say Pokemon Go was in the summer of that year. After a few weeks of use, the novelty wore off and I haven&#x27;t used it since.<p>Considering the massive success of group games like Jackbox.tv and Among Us, I think the killer app for VR has to have a group competition or co-op element.
