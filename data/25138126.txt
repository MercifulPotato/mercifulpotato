But for me stoicism (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;plato.stanford.edu&#x2F;entries&#x2F;stoicism&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;plato.stanford.edu&#x2F;entries&#x2F;stoicism&#x2F;</a>) as a philosophy made my life so much easier. I don&#x27;t remember last time if ever I had ever had an impostor syndrome.<p>The crux of that philosophy is that you should only worry about things that are in your control. Most things in life aren&#x27;t such as the made up comparisons of you vs other people.<p>The other solution that helped me is just to get good at one thing (for me it was keyboard optimizations). So I have at least something I can say I am better than probably anyone.')
