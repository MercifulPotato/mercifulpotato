Item(by='o_class_star', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604801783, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25021529, text='&quot;Socialism&quot; may be a word we need to retire, not because it&#x27;s an invalid concept, but because it means different things to different people.<p>Democratic socialists largely want a saner version of a market economy, where power resides with labor and government aggressively combats capital&#x27;s worst tendencies, but they aren&#x27;t calling for old-style command economies like the USSR, Communist China, or Castro&#x27;s Cuba.<p>AOC is a fantastically talented politician with a lot of good ideas, but I think she needs to replace the word socialism with something that has less baggage.<p>This is the general disadvantage socialism has relative to capitalism in terms of appealing to others. Socialism and communism have more than a hundred years of history, including a lot of mistakes we&#x27;ve had to learn from. Capitalism has no memory and therefore on its own terms has no history.')
