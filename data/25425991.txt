And even in good faith, people who don&#x27;t like the idea all have different reasons. Start with the root axiom &quot;Slavery is wrong&quot;, people will be happy to engage with you on that. Disregard them and move on to the next syllogism, you&#x27;ll have a different set of people objecting.<p>One thing that might be helpful, if I&#x27;m in favor and you&#x27;re opposed, then maybe identify one obstacle - it can be as big as you wish - where if resolved, you&#x27;d be in favor of reparations. Then maybe that is worth engaging with.')
