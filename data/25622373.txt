Item(by='chrisseaton', descendants=None, kids=[25623244, 25623351], score=None, time=1609688673, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622341, text='What do you think about the argument about it allowing you to keep your money invested for longer?<p>Say you&#x27;re ordering something large like a car. You can pay the deposit on a debit card, and lose access to the money immediately, or you can pay on a credit card, and keep your money invested for another month or so, still earning you money, until you need to settle.<p>A credit card gives a consumer liquidity. I don&#x27;t think it&#x27;s an unusual economic concept to say that liquidity is useful and helps you use your money more efficiently.')
