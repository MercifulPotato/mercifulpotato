Item(by='howlgarnish', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610534508, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25758834, text='Comparing Wikipedia versions is not terribly useful, since formal written Chinese tends towards uniformity: previously 文言, now 普通话.<p>Spoken Chinese does not, and an accurate transcription of colloquial Cantonese is quite different from colloquial Mandarin: 係唔係佢哋嘅 vs 是不是他們的, and that&#x27;s despite the grammar of the two sentences being identical (which is not normally the case either).')
