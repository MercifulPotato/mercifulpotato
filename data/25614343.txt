Item(by='fasterthanlime', descendants=None, kids=[25614528], score=None, time=1609608016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25614292, text='That&#x27;s one of the things that get me about Rust discourse: it seems that &quot;Rust is pain in exchange for performance&quot; is a common misconception. Rust is discipline in exchange for performance <i>and correctness</i>. A GC lets you be relatively worry-free as far as memory leaks go (and even then..) but it doesn&#x27;t prevent a lot of the correctness problems the borrow checker would.<p>With a checker you&#x27;re forced to think: do I really want to pass a copy&#x2F;clone of this? Or do I want to let that function borrow it? Or borrow it mutably?')
