Item(by='gerdesj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609454872, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25587855, text='A &quot;bod&quot; is a person (body.)  It is a colloquialism in the UK.  You generally only use it in this case to denote an anonymous&#x2F;generic person: &quot;A bod did stuff&quot;.<p>However, bod is also used as a formal abbreviation for body: &quot;You have a lovely bod&quot;.  In this case you should be reasonably familiar with the object or you will get slapped!<p>Sorry, bod means a person.')
