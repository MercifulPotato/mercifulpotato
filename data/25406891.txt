The owner gets the civil fine, but there are no demerit points put on anyone&#x27;s license.<p>If one is pulled over for speeding (or anything else), then the officer can identify the driver and they get points as well.<p>I think points are used as an (dis)incentive for people who would be otherwise be willing to just cut a cheque.
