Item(by='shadowgovt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610739354, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25794374, text='I once wrote a system where we chose Lua as the scripting language specifically because of its lack of batteries.<p>When embedding a language in another system, you have to worry about the abstractions the standard library enforces upon the rest of your system if those abstractions are deep. Far better, for our purposes, to use a language with an absolute handful of primitive types and build abstractions atop it.')
