Vertical scaling isn&#x27;t trendy, but it is effective, until it&#x27;s not.<p>I have yet to encounter a real situation where it <i>suddenly</i> became impossible to run a production DB on a single, high-spec server, without knowing far enough in advance to plan a careful migration to a horizontally scaled system if and when it was necessary.')
