Item(by='sudhirj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609445263, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25596026, text='This is touched on in the article, but I should probably expand on it a bit more. A queue is a transport tool that allows moving information between the stages of the workflow, but using a queue doesn&#x27;t mean you have an excellent workflow. The decoupling business advantages come mainly from having the workflow, not necessarily using a queue as a building block for it.<p>When you do use a queue as a building block in a workflow you get some technical and operational advantages, maybe those are oversold.<p>Will think about how to advocate business decoupling using a workflow as a the zeroth step before getting into queues.')
