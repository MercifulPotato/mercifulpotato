Item(by='WalterBright', descendants=None, kids=[25569149, 25572677, 25568599, 25570555, 25569442], score=None, time=1609238282, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25568186, text='I wish people (even physicists) would stop saying that the heat of reentry is from air friction. It isn&#x27;t. It&#x27;s from compression. The air cannot get out of the way fast enough, and it compresses, and compressing a gas raises its temperature.<p>That&#x27;s why the leading edge, where the air is moving the <i>slowest</i>, is the <i>hottest</i>.')
