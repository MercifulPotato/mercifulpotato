Item(by='ska', descendants=None, kids=[24898895, 24898903], score=None, time=1603734248, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24898414, text='Very few individual employee firings will have broad impact on the company the same way a CEO firing will.  The whole process is disruptive by nature, so a board needs to be sure.<p>This isn&#x27;t just &quot;elite culture&quot;, although there is probably some of that in many scenarios.<p>edit:  responding to a couple of comments - yes this is a feature of how we choose to structure these companies, it is not unavoidable.  However, given the distribution of decision making and responsibility in a typical American corporate structure, removing the CEO is inherently difficult.<p>One can imagine situations where it is less disruptive, but those companies would look and operate quite differently.')
