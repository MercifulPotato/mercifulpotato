(2017) and Glenski, Weninger &amp; Volkova (2018a), which contains 424 English news media sources categorized in unreliable (satire, propaganda, hoax, clickbait) and reliable, following Rubin, Chen &amp; Conroy (2015).<p>The list is linked in the referenced paper, but unfortunately doesn&#x27;t seem to be available anymore outside of a few examples given in the paper, such as the Drudge Report being under the hoax category. The list seems to have been compiled based on Propornot&#x27;s list[1], which doesn&#x27;t seem to have updated since 2017, (though their Twitter account is active[6]) and fakenewswatch[2] which no longer is active, based on one of the cited papers.[3] Some of the inclusions on those sites&#x27; lists I&#x27;m not completely sure about, such as putting &quot;ronpaulinstitute.org&quot; under the Russian propaganda category.
