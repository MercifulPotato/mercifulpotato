Item(by='Marsymars', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611242967, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25855753, text='My life as a developer would be much easier if every user actually upgraded their apps within a month of updates. In my experience, a substantial proportion of users either have automatic updates disabled and only manually update individual apps when they break, or don’t habitually connect their phones to Wi-Fi, and thus don’t get automatic updates.<p>It’s why apps like desktop Chrome have <i>extremely</i> aggressive automatic-update schemes which are near-impossible to disable if you’re ever connected to the internet.')
