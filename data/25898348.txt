Item(by='dade_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611541405, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25895126, text='If you give away your secret sauce recipe, no one will pay for your recipe (though some will donate), but people will still buy your fried chicken.<p>I think open source works best when it isn&#x27;t your product, but your capability.\nAn e-commerce site open sources it&#x27;s product inventory management software.  If I spin up a docker container with your software, I will be closer to building an e-commerce business, but I won&#x27;t have one without a lot more work.<p>More examples:\nA blogger makes her own open source blogging software.\nA 3D games developer - open sources their AI engine or 3D engine.<p>You get community adoption, goodwill &amp; hopefully contribution, ability to hire employees that already know your software.  Possibly it identifies new markets to pursue.')
