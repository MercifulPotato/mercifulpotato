Even the &quot;literate elite&quot; media, e.g. <i>The New Yorker</i>, has dropped in quality from a few decades ago. Just read some old issues to see what I mean.<p>Regardless, this article is hardly the first or only study of this problem. Allan Bloom wrote <i>The Closing of the American Mind</i> in 1987.
