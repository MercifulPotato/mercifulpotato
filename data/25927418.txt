Item(by='Cu3PO42', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611750753, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927355, text='I find that changing the layout creates significant mental overhead for me as well, so I just don&#x27;t do it. I use the NEO layout [1] which contains all the characters and symbols I could possibly need in reasonable locations. I barely need to leave the home row to write any of the symbols needed during programming or writing TeX.<p>As for physically different keyboards, I&#x27;ve found it to be a much lesser issue than I expected. I recently got a Moonlander [2] due to general hand and wrist pain that could only be attributed to strain from typing.<p>I took care to map the layout quite closely and the physical layout was almost immediately usable. I started at around 2&#x2F;3 my normal typing speed but was back to 100% in a matter of a week.<p>Going back to a normal keyboard takes minimal effort. I can still type on them quickly, I just loathe them more than I did before now that I know how much more comfortable it could be.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;neo-layout.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;neo-layout.org&#x2F;</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.zsa.io&#x2F;moonlander&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.zsa.io&#x2F;moonlander&#x2F;</a>')
