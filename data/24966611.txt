Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604314006, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24966214, text='The writing direction in Arabic, Hebrew and other Semitic writing systems has been inherited from the Egyptian.<p>So any hypothesis about the origin of the right-to-left direction must discuss only how the tools were used for the Egyptian hieratic (written with brushes) and hieroglyphic (carved with chisels) writing systems.<p>Maybe it was easier to carve hieroglyphs in that direction, but I am not aware of anyone testing this hypothesis in practice.')
