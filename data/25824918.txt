Item(by='antonvs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610994929, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25821098, text='The point is that js has become a de facto way of rendering content, regardless of its nature, and in some cases this impacts usability and other non-functional requirements in ways that people don&#x27;t always consider.<p>Obviously if a company wants tight control over their content then this may work to their benefit - although they may still find that their ability to render their own content requires non-trivial maintenance over time.<p>But that&#x27;s a pretty narrow view of the web, and not everyone publishing on the web fits that model.')
