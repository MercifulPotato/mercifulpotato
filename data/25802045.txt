Item(by='plaur782', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610803696, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25798925, text='Ultimately comes down to the right tool for the job. Larger organizations tend to short list a selection of databases to choose from as new applications create new data requirements. These tend to include a NoSQL option, some combination of legacy databases, a data caching or message broker tool, and an open source relational database. Postgres has a lot of momentum as the &quot;new&quot; relational option.')
