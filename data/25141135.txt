Item(by='hvac', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605727200, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25140519, text='The direction does matter, actually. The virus particles leaving an infected person will generally be inside droplets large enough to be caught by a mask before they get a chance to evaporate and atomize. Meanwhile, the virus encountered by an uninfected person nearby will naturally be either in smaller droplets or in airborne form. Much harder to defend against.')
