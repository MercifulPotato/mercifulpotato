Item(by='blackrock', descendants=None, kids=[24670989], score=None, time=1601719302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24669425, text='I had this exact idea a few years ago.<p>It was a neat idea, to offline your data to analog format. But, it would require a lot of paper printouts to back up your data.<p>The paper printout might actually last longer than CDROMs.<p>A more interesting idea, would be to encrypt your data, and back it up to the cloud.  Then, the printout is just the password-protected key to re-download the data, and decrypt it.')
