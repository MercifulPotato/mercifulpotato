I ignore private company equity offers of all stages as it&#x27;s impossible to fairly value the equity which inevitably leads to an undervalued total compensation package - even on successful exit.  I&#x27;d expect that the market would work out the generosity component if the terms were fair - but right now great startups are competing for employees with their second scarcest resource (cash) with companies that have effectively unlimited cash and great liquid equity.
