If you nerd out on preparedness, you&#x27;re just increasing the chance that the local Blood King will turn your skull into a goblet when he comes looking for the stash of canned goods you bragged about online in the Before Times.<p>That&#x27;s why meatspace social networking is part of prepping for some people: they recognize that, if there is a collapse, the way to survive isn&#x27;t just to have stuff for yourself, its to have a well-prepared <i>tribe</i>. To be (well, hopefully kinder and gentler than the title sounds, for most preppers who take this strategy) the Blood King, rather than worry about him.')
