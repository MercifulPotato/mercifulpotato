Item(by='simonw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608771228, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25524032, text='Grafana is much more focused on time series data - Datasette can handle that but it&#x27;s not nearly as good a fit, since it doesn&#x27;t do any rollups for you - you&#x27;d have to write your own SQL queries to summarize the data.<p>I imagine you could do that using SQLite window functions but you&#x27;d end up putting a lot of work in to get even a basic alternative to Grafana working.<p>The visualization features in Datasette are all provided by plugins - I&#x27;d love to people experiment with time-series visualization plugins which use SQLite (via the Datasette JSON API) on the backend.')
