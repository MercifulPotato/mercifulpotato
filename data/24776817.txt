Item(by='toyg', descendants=None, kids=[24777349, 24781851, 24779565], score=None, time=1602685304, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24776654, text='I wouldn&#x27;t think season-finale cliffhangers are a retention trick - they are just that, cliffhangers, something that any tv series does episode after episode. Usually it&#x27;s the <i>lack</i> of cliffhanger at the end of a season that signals it&#x27;s unlikely to come back. Showrunners want to wrap up stories if they can; when it doesn&#x27;t happen, it&#x27;s usually because the risk of cancellation was not clear to them until too late.')
