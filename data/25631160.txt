Item(by='ghostwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609767680, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25626010, text='Technically speaking, there are limits and you cannot compare incomparable. You can find distinctions between two phenomena, a.k.a. inherent traits that make them different-by-definition from one another, but you cannot compare (liken, collate, contrast) them in any meaningful way. Like, what&#x27;s the outcome of comparing &quot;soft&quot; with &quot;warm&quot;? And hence, comparing apples to apples are not pointless, quite contrary - they are comparable items and it makes total sense to compare them in the context of superiority by desirable value axes.')
