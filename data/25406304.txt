Item(by='kortilla', descendants=None, kids=[25416549], score=None, time=1607864744, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405551, text='I think you’re confused a bit, so let’s split apart the use cases to be clear why IPs are bad in both cases.<p>You said devs and technical staff were typing IPs into their browsers. Presumably this means the address bar, which breaks TLS.<p>SSH derives a big chunk of security from key caching. If you’re using IPs you now can’t have an IP change without triggering key warnings on the SSH clients for a new key at a minimum or (worst case) a breach.')
