(It&#x27;s complicated to explain, but in a <i>very simplified</i> nutshell if an entity is set up for the sole purpose of evading liability for criminal acts, regulatory violations, civil torts, or responsibility for financial losses, then the courts will disregard the legal entity and just go straight to the owner(s).)')
