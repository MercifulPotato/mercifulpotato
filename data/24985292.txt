Most people in the US are familiar with the Ace Hardware brand, where local stores get a branding and logistics boost from being part of the co-op, but stay entirely locally owned. That way, independent stores can function with the supply chain and purchasing power of a large multinational. IGA does a similar thing in the grocery space.')
