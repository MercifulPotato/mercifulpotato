Item(by='ternaryoperator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608277740, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25464585, text='Typically, the right to remain silent is associated with the right to refuse self-incrimination in a criminal proceeding, which is part of the fifth amendment.<p>The law can compel publication, but that power is  typically used for material arising in judicial proceedings or documents of inherent public interest (like the Watergate tapes, etc.). So, it would be unlikely to apply here.')
