Item(by='bluescrn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610645029, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25775162, text='The problem with sin taxes is that they even further increase the divide between rich and poor.<p>The wealthy won&#x27;t be concerned if they&#x27;re spending twice as much on booze, as it&#x27;s such a small fraction of their income. But the working class may be denied one of their few pleasures - even if they were perfectly capable of keeping their consumption at reasonably healthy level.<p>Even worse than taxing alcohol is trying reduce car use by taxing people off the roads (without significantly improving alternatives). That&#x27;s taking away vast numbers of opportunities from those who need them the most.')
