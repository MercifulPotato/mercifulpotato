Item(by='ethbr0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611077987, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25835932, text='The key enabler here is IAM and data (at rest and moving).<p>If we want to encourage free markets, the GDPR et al. need to be very careful to disincentivize &quot;traffic within different parts of the same entity.&quot;<p>As is, <i>if</i> my regulatory compliance is satisfied through AWS&#x27;s data and IAM handling, then <i>if</i> an Amazon-hosted service better integrates with those components, it strictly dominates competition.<p>That&#x27;s a pretty unregulatable quality, and one easily optimized by Amazon (for itself), and impossibly by everyone else (on Amazon).<p>This weaponizes data protection regulation into a moat around large everything-and-the-kitchen-sink I&#x2F;PaaS providers.<p>There needs to be balance between (a) protecting data &amp; (b) ensuring a competitive ecosystem with multiple viable solutions.')
