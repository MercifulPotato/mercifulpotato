It was a term people in Europe and USA used to define the six vilayets where there were the most Armenians. It was not an official designation.<p>Second, Nagorno-Karabakh was not one of the &quot;Armenian vilayets&quot; because it wasn&#x27;t part of the Ottoman Empire. It was part of the Russian Empire.<p>Finally, Armenians predate Azeris (and Turks) in all the areas we are speaking about and were the majority in many of them up until the Armenian Genocide. In a few, they are still the majority to this day. Nagorno-Karabakh is one of those.')
