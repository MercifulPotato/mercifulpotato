Item(by='Spivak', descendants=None, kids=[24921659, 24922660, 24922196], score=None, time=1603905490, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24921322, text='I don&#x27;t know why people are so afraid of fragmentation in this respect. There are lots of &quot;de specific&quot; protocols that are well supported everywhere like org.freedesktop.Notifications.<p>If the world can agree on Wayland then desktops can agree on some dbus interfaces for doing stuff like screenshots, screencasts, and automation.<p>Wayland is a shiny new thing and lots of people are writing compositors since it&#x27;s suddenly possible for people to write them in a way that you simply couldn&#x27;t with X11. The ecosystem will eventually mature but I think it would be a mistake to recreate the Xorg monoculture with wlroots. People seem to see the value of multiple browser implementations agreeing on standard but not display servers.')
