Item(by='likpok', descendants=None, kids=[25783968], score=None, time=1610653843, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25781069, text='One other interesting factor: BMI is a rough measure with substantial room for error. Everyone likes to criticize BMI because a lean athlete can show up as &quot;overweight&quot; due to muscle, but the opposite is also true. If you have a lot <i>less</i> muscle then increased fat can show up as &quot;normal&quot;.<p>I would hazard a guess that there are a lot more people with less lean muscle tissue than the 1970s, so BMI can understate how fat people are.')
