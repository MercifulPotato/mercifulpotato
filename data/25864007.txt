Item(by='einpoklum', descendants=None, kids=[25865644], score=None, time=1611261981, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25863529, text='I think you&#x27;re conflating correctness and degrees of validity.<p>Yes, I expect journals to have to have correct results - not in the sense that the generalizations from their findings are universally valid, but in that the statements of fact and of logical implications are valid.<p>To be more explicit:<p>* &quot;Events X, Y, Z occurred&quot; &lt;- expect this kind of sentences to be correct<p>* &quot;We did X, Y, Z&quot; &lt;- expect this kind of sentences to be correct<p>* &quot;A and A-&gt;B, so B&quot; &lt;- expect this kind of sentences to be correct<p>* &quot;We therefore conclude that X&quot; &lt;- Don&#x27;t expect X to necessarily be correct.')
