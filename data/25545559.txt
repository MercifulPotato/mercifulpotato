Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609009533, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25545324, text='The floating point control word is a register in the Intel 8087 FP coprocessor and in all later Intel&#x2F;AMD&#x2F;etc. processors that implement the 8087 instruction set.<p>That register contains bits that control various aspects of how the floating-point instructions must behave, e.g. rounding mode, precision and exception masks (also infinity type in coprocessors older than 80387).<p>For the later SSE&#x2F;AVX instruction sets there is another register with similar control bits for the SSE&#x2F;AVX instructions.')
