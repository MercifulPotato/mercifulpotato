Item(by='jschwartzi', descendants=None, kids=[25163097, 25164743], score=None, time=1605894925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25162908, text='Yeah RPi is a great toy but you should treat it like a chemistry set. Use it as a tool for learning but don’t build a product with it. The difference between a $24 board and a $200 board is often the level of support you get. The boards I’ve used in the past all have manuals down to the chip and I won’t touch anything that doesn’t.')
