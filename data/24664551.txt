Item(by='user5994461', descendants=None, kids=[24665049], score=None, time=1601657758, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24664206, text='Latency is not related to frame per seconds, yes.<p>You could feed a thousand frames per second to a TV and the TV can have 100 ms latency (delay to display a frame after it received it).<p>You could feed 30 frames per second to the same TV and it&#x27;d still have a 100 ms latency.<p>The latency is rarely advertised. It&#x27;s highly variable per TV and per image. There&#x27;s the time it takes to start displaying an image and the time until the image is fully set (it is much longer to go white to black than white to grey).')
