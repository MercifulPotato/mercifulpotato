Item(by='Jochim', descendants=None, kids=[25932703], score=None, time=1611774247, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931695, text='A 401k has the specific purpose of helping individuals fund their retirement years, something society has agreed is worth providing some benefit for. There is an element of intention to the exemption and it serves a specific purpose.<p>&gt; If anyone can do what they&#x27;re doing, then I don&#x27;t see it as particularly negative.<p>Typically not just anyone can do this, smaller firms do not have the resources set-up these schemes. Smaller firms are also much more likely to be slapped down by the government. The result of this is that larger corporations are further advantaged.<p>I think attempting to equate personal and corporate taxation is a mistake. Corporations are increasingly transnational in a way that people cannot be, and they are leveraging not just local tax codes, but often established treaties and differences between national tax codes that were introduced with different intent.')
