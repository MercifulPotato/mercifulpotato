Item(by='yamrzou', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611647744, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25911308, text='Going from a short segment of DNA randomly popping into existence, capable of nothing but self-replication to living organisms, carrying a tremendous amount of intelligence, those are two very different things.<p>To make an analogy, what you describe would be only the electronic chips or the logic gates of a computer. There are a still the CPU, RAM, and the whole software stack from the OS that runs our bodies (and mind you, there is a different one for each species) to the yet unknown abstractions from which human general intelligence emerges.<p>I think people underestimate how difficult it is for all the above to get started by accident.')
