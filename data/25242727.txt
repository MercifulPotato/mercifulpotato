As a purely non-profit corporation, having most of your revenue come from business income is a big problem.  As a for-profit, director’s fiduciary responsibilities to shareholders can get in the way of pursuing a public good mission.  And, collaboration with for profit orgs gets a little less regulatory scrutiny if a non-profit is in control.')
