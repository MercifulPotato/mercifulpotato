Item(by='bayindirh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608890167, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25535415, text='It&#x27;s not the fidelity, it&#x27;s the <i>human factor</i>.<p>An concert, a play, or any other live performance is much more than a single night, 2 hour performance. It&#x27;s countless hours of practice, errors, emotional up &amp; downs during the journey.<p>The performance one watches is the tip of the iceberg and the underlying depth is what amazes us as humans. The unmistakable performance or real-time and <i>invisible</i> improvisation during the show.<p>I used to played in a symphony orchestra. Even the slowest of classical pieces is a storm for the orchestra, if not for the spectators.')
