It&#x27;s every context switch. If the process is in the midst of using AVX registers in kernel code, but is suddenly descheduled, those registers have to be saved&#x2F;restored. You can&#x27;t know if the task is using AVX or not, so you have to either always save&#x2F;restore them, or adopt the policy that these registers are not saved&#x2F;restored.')
