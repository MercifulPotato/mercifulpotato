Item(by='tpxl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603371163, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24853961, text='&gt;I like free software and open source because of freedom.<p>The MIT license is less free than GPL in the sense that as a user of MIT software I don&#x27;t get to see the source, modify, and distribute it, whereas with the GPL I can.<p>The MIT license is more free in the sense that as a developer I don&#x27;t have to give those freedoms to my users.<p>It&#x27;s perfectly fine to prefer developer freedoms over user freedoms, but be aware of both of them.')
