Item(by='pdonis', descendants=None, kids=[25272561], score=None, time=1606864207, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25271209, text='<i>&gt; There ought to be an objective legal standard</i><p>There can&#x27;t be, because there is irremovable subjectivity in your definition of legal justice: &quot;equally and fairly&quot; are subjective, and so is &quot;wrongfully take advantage&quot;. There is no way to write an objective rule that will handle every case. You have to apply human judgment to each individual case, and human judgment will always be subjective. Written laws attempt to set down objective rules, but such attempts will never completely succeed.')
