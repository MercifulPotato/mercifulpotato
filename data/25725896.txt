Item(by='phendrenad2', descendants=None, kids=[25725957], score=None, time=1610350832, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25717390, text='Having work at both styles of companies, in my opinion you really want a mix of both. Top-down decision-making is great! It allows your engineers to be engineers, cranking out good code and not &quot;thinking up the stack&quot; and worrying about the business logic. On the other hand, when engineers see a problem area, or see a feature that could be implemented in another way, you absolutely must stop everything and listen to them. This is a rare occurrence and they usually know what they&#x27;re talking about.')
