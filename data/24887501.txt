If you have a lot of it, you’ll generally have more choices. If you don’t have it, life will probably be tougher for you. In a similar way in which being ugly or unsocial could mean some doors are closed for you. The essence is that it’s generally an asset you’re born with, and in the increasingly complex world we live in, not having it can be a liability. If you have a good amount of it, count yourself as a lucky one.')
