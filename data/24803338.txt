Often, the reputation of a person or business is based at least in part on whether they do the legal bare minimum, or if they hold themselves to some level of higher standard.<p>I also think there&#x27;s a difference between attribution because a license requires it (commonly buried several links&#x2F;pages deep in some obscure &quot;Here&#x27;s a laundry list of ALL the open source packages we used to build this&quot;), and acknowledging that a _specific_ library powers the core of a new product. I don&#x27;t know of any license that marks that line.')
