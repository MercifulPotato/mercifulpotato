Item(by='marcosdumay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605724887, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25140258, text='IT infrastructure costs scale down very badly.<p>It&#x27;s not to offset risks, if you have very little load (a site with 10k visits a day, for example), you can share the costs on the cloud and save nearly the entire bill.<p>Companies with a high revenue&#x2F;load ratio tend to stay at the cloud even after they get big. That is because even though the cloud is very expensive for their needs, it adds speed on their internal processes by saving the time to decide and buy equipment. But when that ratio is small, they just can&#x27;t afford it.')
