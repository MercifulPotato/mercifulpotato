Item(by='fastball', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608669763, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25510420, text='For people that want to learn &quot;physical engineering&quot;, I recommend the same thing I do when people ask people the best way to learn software engineering: you need a project.<p>Find something in your life that you want to automate, and automate it. Motivation is easier to maintain (&quot;I still want this problem fixed&quot;) and you&#x27;ll learn so much more from failure and iteration than you will following someone else&#x27;s tutorial about &quot;how to build X&quot;.')
