Item(by='danwills', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605605279, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25121579, text='Time most certainly is not a dimension (or even very dimension-like, in general) in my opinion. It structures the orderings of events, more like some ultimate process that all other processes are hanging off (are a part of). The complexity in the ordering could be irreducible at any given point, depending what is happening there, and that includes fully non-reversible processes. To me, this is time&#x27;s arrow.')
