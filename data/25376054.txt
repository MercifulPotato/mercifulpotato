Item(by='dvfjsdhgfv', descendants=None, kids=[25376440], score=None, time=1607621338, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25375858, text='This is an extremely delicate issue in any large organization. Skipping one level basically means: &quot;Let&#x27;s see what important things my report is not telling me&quot;. If you are asked for such a meeting, anything you say could be used against your direct manager. You need to know the office politics well before speaking anything that could have even small impact on anything. I saw people freaking out for mentioning minute stuff &quot;behind their backs.&quot;<p>With a small team, it&#x27;s very different - but usually the hierarchical structure is much more horizontally aligned so this is not much of a problem.')
