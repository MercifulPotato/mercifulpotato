Item(by='marcinzm', descendants=None, kids=[24927249], score=None, time=1603944787, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24927088, text='Codifying all expected social norms among an arbitrarily diverse group of people in a concise but fully understandable form is impossible. Attempts to do so just leave loopholes, edge cases and personal interpretations the size of jumbo jets which will be abused. This doesn&#x27;t try to do so and leaves it up to you to observe the social norms of the group. Like has been done by humans since the dawn of our species.<p>edit: A false sense of security if not actually security as the author of this article found out.')
