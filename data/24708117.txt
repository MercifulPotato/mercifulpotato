Item(by='snowwrestler', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602078423, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24707407, text='Not sure if you&#x27;re looking for site design suggestions but I would get rid of the &quot;carousel&quot; and instead have a grid of items below the fold so people can scroll for a brand they recognize.<p>The carousel is simultaneously too slow and too fast. The visitor has to sit and wait to see a brand they recognize, and then when they do, they only have a second to recognize and click.<p>Scroll works well on all modern devices and empowers the visitor. There&#x27;s a reason so many landing pages are tall.')
