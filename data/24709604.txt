Item(by='chrisco255', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602086906, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24709297, text='IPFS is a decentralized network protocol, a content-based addressing system. Filecoin is decentralized file storage, incentivized by cryptoeconomics. On their own, neither IPFS nor Filecoin have the same goals as Vapor. It seems as though Vapor&#x27;s intended purpose is to use off-chain Bitcoin transactions to transmit HTTP requests to normal APIs. The primary benefits being (optional) payments and identity.')
