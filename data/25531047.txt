Item(by='Uehreka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608841199, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25529140, text='In spite of the years of careful optimization, aggressive multithreading and 2d sprite-based graphics, Factorio still needs a pretty good computer to keep up 60 UPS (simulation updates per second) when you have a truly gigantic factory.<p>I have to imagine that if you tried to take a Factorio mega-factory (like a 1 rocket per minute factory) and load up the equivalent in Satisfactory, with its 3d graphics and off-the-shelf rigid body dynamics, it would crash to desktop immediately.<p>Don’t get me wrong, Satisfactory looks like a fun game! But nothing can match Factorio’s depth.')
