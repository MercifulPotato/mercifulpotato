Broken up:<p>I had:<p><pre><code>    a = employee(name == &quot;Jones&quot;)    a has name, salary and age\n\n    w = a[comp = salary &#x2F; (age - 18)]   w has comp\n</code></pre>\nYou want:<p><pre><code>    b = employee[name, comp = salary &#x2F; (age - 18)]   b has name and comp\n\n    w = b(name == &quot;Jones&quot; &amp;&amp; comp &gt; 500)   w has name and comp\n</code></pre>\nIn one line:<p><pre><code>    w = employee[name, comp = salary &#x2F; (age - 18)](name == &quot;Jones&quot; &amp;&amp; comp &gt; 500)</code></pre>')
