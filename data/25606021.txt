Item(by='Dylan16807', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609529681, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25603702, text='While I agree that impersonation or platforms showing up first in search results is bad, whether the restaurant gets the full margin is a separate issue and currently correlates <i>negatively</i>.<p>A restaurant that&#x27;s being impersonated without any agreement&#x2F;permission will get the full margin, because the platform has to buy at retail prices.<p>A restaurant only loses money to commissions when they have an agreement with the platform, and that agreement very often also <i>allows</i> the platform to set up those pages that show up first.<p>So a law requiring platforms to get permission to deliver might end up with <i>fewer</i> restaurants getting full margin!')
