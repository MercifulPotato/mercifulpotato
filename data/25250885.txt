Item(by='jader201', descendants=None, kids=[25254145, 25251563], score=None, time=1606712532, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25250449, text='Just like many solitaire games, you can definitely lose in Mahjong Solitaire.<p>Since there are four tiles (two pairs) of each tile set, you can play in such a way that you’re deadlocked because of a previous choice.<p>Imagine, for example, there are two matching tiles with one stacked on top of the other. If you already removed the other two matches for that same tile set, then you’re stuck, since the only way to remove the top one is to match it with another match — but you can’t get to the only remaining matching tile since it’s covered by that tile.')
