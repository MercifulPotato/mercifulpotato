Item(by='ivan_gammel', descendants=None, kids=[25327218], score=None, time=1607288603, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25326430, text='Actually there is something specific: while individual field accessors may make sense in some use cases (ActiveRecord or bidirectional UI mapping), the naming convention for them chosen in JavaBeans (“get&#x2F;setXxx”) is too verbose and doesn’t make sense at all. Compare:<p>1. a.getFoo().getBar() vs a.foo().bar() or a.foo.bar<p>2. a.setFoo(1); a.setBoo(“moo”); vs a.foo(1).boo(“moo”); or a.onSomeEvent(1, “moo”);<p>JavaBeans are often used in reflective mapping, but nothing prevents from using simpler convention the same way.')
