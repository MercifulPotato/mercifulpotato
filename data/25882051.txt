Item(by='corty', descendants=None, kids=[25882308], score=None, time=1611408595, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25881590, text='Depends on your definition of &quot;stateless&quot;.<p>A database is commonly considered state, so database queries are never stateless in some definitions. Answers to stateless queries can be cached indefinitely. Your examples can&#x27;t.<p>Your definition probably just says that one query shouldn&#x27;t influence the next one in a session&#x2F;context&#x2F;user. The other definition says that any query with the same parameters should yield the same result.')
