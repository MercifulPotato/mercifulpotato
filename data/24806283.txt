Item(by='hakfoo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602893654, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24805519, text='The other side of the coin is that creating a market for creative work optimizes for commercializability, not necessarily any other attributes we&#x27;d consciously prefer.<p>How much effort in the medical industry is devoted to me-too treatments that don&#x27;t really expand our therapeutic vocabulary?  How many writers were focusing on writing Law &amp; Order: Food Truck Inspectors Unit instead of something more distinctive?<p>I think that maybe we need to look into UBI for creatives:  we&#x27;ll pay you a comfortable living as long as you&#x27;re regularly producing some deliverables&#x2F;signs of progress, but you don&#x27;t get to drag everyone else down with patents and copyrights when you&#x27;re done.  We could probably finance a lot of singers at 50k per year for the cost of one Rolling Stones tour.')
