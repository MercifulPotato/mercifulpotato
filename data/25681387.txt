(There is the Marathon 2 source code, and efforts like M1A1, but still.)<p>Here’s the Marathon beta, with screenshots: <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;marathon.bungie.org&#x2F;story&#x2F;betasfjan94.html" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;marathon.bungie.org&#x2F;story&#x2F;betasfjan94.html</a><p>That’s also not to mention the various defunct game studios. Every once in a while some <i>incredible</i> artifact will turn up, like an SGI Indy with the source code to Turok: Dinosaur Hunter.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.pcgamesn.com&#x2F;turok&#x2F;turok-source-code-found" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.pcgamesn.com&#x2F;turok&#x2F;turok-source-code-found</a><p>Games are cultural artifacts and we should make efforts to preserve them similar to other cultural artifacts.')
