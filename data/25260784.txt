Item(by='Mediterraneo10', descendants=None, kids=[25261667], score=None, time=1606781928, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25260256, text='At least on Signal for Android, you can make all the periodic backups of your Signal messages that you want: just be sure to copy the most recent backup file from your phone to another computer, and keep the encryption key (i.e. the sequence of numbers that Signal generates for you) written down somewhere. Then, if your phone does get dropped or stolen, you will have only lost messages since your last backup.')
