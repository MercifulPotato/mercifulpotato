The idea is &quot;if you worry about X, this might be relevant&quot;.<p>The idea is we&#x27;re helping each other search the state space of the world for what&#x27;s true.<p>After all, getting someone to always convince you of things that are true is a really high bar. That means that person is effectively a coach or tutor to you. Usually you have to pay someone to do that.<p>This method allows us to exchange small messages that hint at information in a certain direction.<p>I say this as someone who has two tabs of LSD in my fridge left and about 100 g of shrooms so you know I&#x27;m not an anti-entheogen dude.')
