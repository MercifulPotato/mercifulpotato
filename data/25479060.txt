Item(by='aminozuur', descendants=None, kids=[25479207, 25479642], score=None, time=1608398549, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25479014, text='&quot;We actually cut someone open, then don&#x27;t fix him&quot;<p>Yes. There have been placebo-controlled trials whereby the control-group was just cut open, and left there for an hour. The placebo-patients believed they had undergone the actual procedure, and reported the same improvement as the actual patients. This is the placebo effect.<p>Although such trials are harder than giving fake pills, they might still be worthy of doing more often than we do now.')
