Item(by='esprehn', descendants=None, kids=[24706442], score=None, time=1602043224, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24704834, text='Blink and WebKit are not very similar anymore.<p>The fork was 7 years ago and since then Blink has been heavily changed including a whole new layout engine (LayoutNG), a new painting system, a redone compositing system, a prioritizing task scheduler and a bunch of other architectural differences.<p>Chrome has also used a different compositor backend, painting backend, and JS engine compared to WebKit since even before the fork.<p>That demos like this work on Safari is probably more about web standards, web platform tests, and where developers test than engine lineage.')
