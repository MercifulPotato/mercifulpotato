Item(by='throw93', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606934543, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25272798, text='&gt;&gt; Let&#x27;s say that their consistency model is achieved via quorum, that is writes write to a quorum of nodes while reads read from a quorum of nodes (of their metadata database) then this guarantees read after write consistency.<p>If reads are reading only from a quorum of nodes how do you guarantee they have latest data? What if the quorum of nodes don’t have the latest data yet?')
