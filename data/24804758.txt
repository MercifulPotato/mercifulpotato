Item(by='moonchild', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602880395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24801670, text='The majority of <i>all</i> GC research goes to java and (primarily the hotspot) jvm.  ZGC and kin are like the zfs of garbage collectors: insanely good, but also insanely complex and not readily replicable.  It&#x27;s not practical to expect somebody with fewer resources than oracle to create something similar.<p>Reference-counting strategies are <i>much</i> easier to optimize; so if you have fewer resources available to throw at your compiler it&#x27;s the way to go.')
