They can then post long-form stories to your inbox. That gives them the opportunity to develop a long-term relationship with you where credibility matters.<p>At any rate, that is the argument. I don&#x27;t think it will work, because buying journalism one journalist at a time is too expensive. Hell, buying it one <i>paper</i> at a time is too expensive.
