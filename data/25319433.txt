Item(by='SuoDuanDao', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607210872, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25316094, text='I came across an interesting factoid on Lex Fridman&#x27;s interview with Andrew Huberman - of the &#x27;fight, flight, freeze&#x27; trifecta, freeze is actually associated with the <i>lowest</i> level of anxiety. Fight - or any action that involves moving towards the stressor - is associated with the highest level of anxiety, though it also causes massive dopamine releases when the result is positive.<p>So thin-skinned narcissists that aggressively climb status hierarchies are probably <i>much more anxious than yourself</i>. I don&#x27;t have an opinion on what you should do with that information, but I think it&#x27;s interesting.')
