Item(by='oflannabhra', descendants=None, kids=[24768183, 24774522], score=None, time=1602611332, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24767863, text='At Apple’s scale, a decision like that has really big implications that from a single-user perspective aren’t that obvious.<p>Not only do you bifurcate your entire product line and supply chain, your customers are not just individuals, but families. Think about a family who has one new phone that now won’t work with any of the chargers the rest of the family uses.<p>On top of that, the new connector type won’t be compatible with any of the pre-existing “infrastructure”. While that might not be a big deal for some single users, it could be a major issue for others.')
