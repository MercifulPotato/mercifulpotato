Item(by='throwanem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611755870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927553, text='Pedant&#x27;s note: ITYM &#x27;disenchanted&#x27;. To be disenfranchised is to be rendered unable to vote, or more broadly to be stripped of some otherwise customary access to a measure of power or agency. To be disenchanted is to lose a fascination with something, in typical usage as a response to some event or sequence of events that places the object of disenchantment in a new and less favorable light.')
