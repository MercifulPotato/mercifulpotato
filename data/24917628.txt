Item(by='rojcyk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603885508, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24916982, text='You need to change the way you think about files with Figma.<p>In Sketch, ppl use to have everything in one huge file. Lots of variants, a&#x2F;b tests, different flows. But with Figma, you need to make a library of shared components, and split everything into smaller files so you don&#x27;t have to redownload the whole design every time you want to put a comment on something. This does require you to sub though.<p>Source: I&#x27;m helping companies setup their Figma processes and files structure so they can work effectively. This approach works the best.')
