Item(by='TazeTSchnitzel', descendants=None, kids=[24654989], score=None, time=1601576477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24653894, text='The source material doesn&#x27;t lie to you, though. The black and white you see is the true brightness of the scene, without any fake colours dreamed up by a neural network. Your mind is free to fill in the details itself without having someone else&#x27;s guess at them imposed on it.<p>It is like the difference between a film adaptation of a text and the original text. If you see the film first, it will permanently colour your reading of the text, taking away from you the opportunity to draw your own meanings from a blank slate.')
