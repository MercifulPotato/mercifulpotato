GILTI was just the latest landmine.<p>I net in the low 6 figures, via my own consultancy. This is not abnormal, in fact many experienced software people in the city work this way.<p>I also can&#x27;t just ignore it. The IRS under the Obama administration launched one of the most aggressive foreign tax ramp ups ever. With FBAR&#x2F;FACTA, they know everything you have. Plus I have retirement accounts in the US which could be affected.')
