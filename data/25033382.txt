Item(by='bjourne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604918517, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25021503, text='It would give special interest groups a lot of power. A lot of people are against subsidies in general but for most of them it is not a core issue. Now suppose there were three issues you had to vote for; subsidies to group A, subsidies to group B, and subsidies to group C. Most people won&#x27;t care and won&#x27;t show up for these votes (no one except for politicians have time to vote on hundreds of issues every year). However, a large number of people in group A will vote for subsidies for them, as will people in group B and C.')
