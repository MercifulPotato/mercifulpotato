Snoring is just a narrow air passage in sleep that makes sound by the pressure of air forcing its way through.<p>If you have the situation where your airway can become completely blocked temporarily, you have sleep apnea.  If it&#x27;s partially blocked, you snore, and you can have either one in different positions.')
