Item(by='thu2111', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609874658, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25649726, text='There&#x27;s no deal on services because there&#x27;s no single market in services for there to be a deal about.<p>One of the sad truths about the EU, and one reason the UK has left, is that the much vaunted &quot;single market&quot; was always first and foremost designed for the purposes of France and Germany. Their economies are more goods export oriented. Once goods rules were made uniform (always to the benefit of those two), the EU mysteriously lost interest in creating a single market for services. There has been very little progress for a long time in even basic things like mutual recognition.')
