Item(by='joppy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603414856, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24864168, text='The matrix inverse “undoes whatever the matrix did” is pretty much the most straightforward interpretation, if you’re thinking of a matrix as performing some kind of rotation&#x2F;shear&#x2F;scaling of space.<p>There are some other interpretations, for example if you think of a linear system Ax=b as a kind of “checking machine” (does x satisfy Ax=b? Yes&#x2F;no, try again), then the inverse of A can produce solutions, since x=(A inverse)b.')
