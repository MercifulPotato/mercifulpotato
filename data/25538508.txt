Item(by='pimlottc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608925159, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25535547, text='I would argue that, yes, making a <i>single</i> car is subtractive work. There is a specific and finite list of tasks that have to be done to make one car. It doesn’t help to make ten steering wheels for one car. If you’re working in a mass assembly plant, sure, go ahead, they’ll all get used. But otherwise, each piece of work needs to support a single clearly defined goal - a working car. More is not always better.')
