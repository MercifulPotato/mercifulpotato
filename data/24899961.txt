Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=[24901107, 24900439], score=None, time=1603739412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24896533, text='<i>It can&#x27;t be DRM, obfuscation is never a valid measure to restrict access. </i><p>From a technical (code) perspective, obfuscation may be an inadequate protection. But legally, the DMCA only requires <i>some</i> technical measure, and obfuscation satisfies that requirement. It is irrelevant that youtube-dl waits until after Youtube decrypts the content, since the law as written is agnostic to the specific technical details of how copyright protection schemes are implemented.')
