This is also prohibited by German laws, particularly with regards to apartment and employment contracts.<p>&gt; Under EU law, standard contract terms used by traders have to be fair. This doesn&#x27;t change if they&#x27;re called &quot;terms and conditions&quot; or are part of a detailed contract that you actually have to sign.
