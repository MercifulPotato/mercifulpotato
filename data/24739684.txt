Item(by='alquemist', descendants=None, kids=[24740062], score=None, time=1602344591, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24738040, text='Assuming we key a and b by y.d and generalizing b to sets, we are reaching the limits of the relational model: there is no good way to represent multiple sets associated with a given key, we need independent tables to do so. Neither cross product nor null padding is a good way to represent the schema {y, a[], b[]}.<p><pre><code>      y.d |  a   |  b\n    -------------------\n      y0  |  a0  |  b0\n      y0  |  a0  |  b1\n      y0  |  a1  |  b0\n      y0  |  a1  |  b1\n      y0  |  a2  |  b0\n      y0  |  a2  |  b1\n\n    \n      y.d |  a   |  b\n    -------------------\n      y0  |  a0  |  b0\n      y0  |  a1  |  b1\n      y0  |  a2  |  null</code></pre>')
