Item(by='hirundo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604764410, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25015218, text='We build our GraphQL schema partially by running introspection on our MySQL schema. Only fields that are indexed in MySQL are valid sort&#x2F;filter parameters in the GraphQL schema. To make a field sort&#x2F;filter-able we add an index in a MySQL migration and regenerate the GraphQL schema from the new database.')
