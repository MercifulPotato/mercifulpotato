Item(by='Droobfest', descendants=None, kids=[25802750], score=None, time=1610809992, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25789837, text='I generally agree with this, but making sure your error messages are <i>correct</i> is even more important than terseness. The last example of the article is a perfect illustration of this:<p><pre><code>  $ cat invalid\n  cat: invalid: invalid path\n</code></pre>\nAn invalid path or a file not found are not the same thing! The real danger is that being too terse and unclear is worse than unnecessary verbosity.')
