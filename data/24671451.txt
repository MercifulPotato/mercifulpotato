Item(by='mjw1007', descendants=None, kids=[24671673, 24672065], score=None, time=1601727225, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671366, text='It might be a nice to have a place where people can file low-effort bug reports, or perhaps &quot;symptom reports&quot;, where there&#x27;s no expectation that a maintainer will respond or even look at them.<p>Then they could be treated rather like automated crash reports: you could mine them to see if a problem is common, or if someone reports a strange problem you might be able to see whether anyone else had seen it and look for common factors.<p>Seems hard to make it work in practice without it turning into a place where people would just rant.
