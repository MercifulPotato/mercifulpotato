Item(by='Jtsummers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605203259, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25071954, text='Start jogging, if your knees and back can take it, and try to keep the same total time (so longer distance). I fixed my distance at the start of my fitness improvement effort (5km) and walked it (40-45 minutes initially), then started running sections until they were longer, and eventually the whole distance. Got my time consistently down to 30 minutes, and for a brief period down to 23-24 minutes.')
