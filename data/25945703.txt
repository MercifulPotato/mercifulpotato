However, the loop you were in has been abandoned, and it will not continue to execute.<p>For example, try it with this:<p><pre><code>    for N in $(seq 10); do echo $N; sleep 1; echo $N; done\n</code></pre>\nYou&#x27;ll see something like this:<p><pre><code>    $ for N in $(seq 10); do echo $N; sleep 1; echo $N; done\n    1\n    1\n    2\n    ^Z\n    [1]+  Stopped                 sleep 1\n    $ fg\n    sleep 1\n    $</code></pre>')
