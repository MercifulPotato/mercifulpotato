Item(by='rocqua', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603710314, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24891669, text='From what I read, electric engines have some pretty big limitations.<p>- Very low thrust makes it hard to use the oberth effect<p>- Low thrust to weight ratio makes the actual wet&#x2F;dry mass ratio harder to get down<p>- You are not just thrust limited, but also thermal limited. Many other rockets expel a lot of the heat they generate through the exhaust. Electrical engines do not, which means needing to get rid of that heat in other ways.')
