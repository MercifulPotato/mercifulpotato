Item(by='lookdangerous', descendants=1, kids=[24716655], score=1, time=1602136260, title='Ask HN: How many operations happen when I open Facebook?', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='From a conversation with a friend; how many operations happen when I open Facebook on my phone?<p>From battery management, to process scheduling, permissions, object instantiation, logging, authentication, requests, responses, rendering, and so on.<p>From the time I click the Facebook icon to the time the app is loaded and responsive, did a hundred things happen? A thousand? A million, or more?<p>It is a bit of a challenge to even range this question- you would reasonably consider individual processor instruction- but there are hardware behaviors, many for each instruction, to consider too. Perhaps processor instructions would be a fine place to start.')
