Item(by='pfdietz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609245472, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25568557, text='When you saying the heating is &quot;from compression&quot; do you mean (1) there&#x27;s a process in which compression occurs, and that&#x27;s where the heating is, or (2) the heating is that which necessarily occurs when a gas is compressed from pressure P1 to a higher pressure P2?<p>Because (2) is wrong.
