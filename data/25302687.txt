Item(by='greggyb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607092889, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25299979, text='AMD is colloquially referred to as &quot;Team Red&quot; in computer hardware circles. Intel is &quot;Team Blue&quot; and Nvidia is &quot;Team Green.&quot;<p>The phrase &quot;seeing red&quot; is a fairly common idiom in my experience in American English. This refers to the matador&#x27;s traditionally red sash used in bull fighting. Red is believed to draw inordinate amounts of bull attention and make them angry.<p>The use of &quot;seeing red&quot; is a bit strange, as the idiom is typically associated with anger and a pending bull charge, potentially metaphorical.<p>Thus, AMD -&gt; Team Red -&gt; seeing red -&gt; bulls.')
