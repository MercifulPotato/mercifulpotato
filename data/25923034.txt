Item(by='phkahler', descendants=None, kids=[25923189, 25923268], score=None, time=1611709814, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25922556, text='I tune my guitar by matching harmonics of adjacent strings. I dont recall which ones, but E-A, then A-D, D-G. Then take the 4x of low E and match open high E to it. Finally use harmonics to match B-E. This can leave G a bit off depending what you&#x27;re playing. The E-E is a nice way across the gap.')
