Item(by='jon-wood', descendants=None, kids=[25314665], score=None, time=1607170000, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25313434, text='If you’re doing software product work I’m totally with you on small impactful iterations. When you’re delivering a hardware product this is a lot more difficult to pull off.<p>There are definitely ways to take an agile approach to hardware development, even if you’re working with an OEM, but the outcomes look a lot more like waterfall than your typical Agile with a big A people are comfortable with. The outcome of your iterations are long specification documents (which you’ve already got feedback on from your OEM), and a lot more detail than you’d typically go into development for on the software side since it has to integrate with the actual hardware.')
