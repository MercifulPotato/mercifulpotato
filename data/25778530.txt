Item(by='otagekki', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610642655, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25777193, text='Let this be a lesson for other messaging applications: giving users an ultimatum where they have to give up their privacy in order to continue using a product is a bad idea. Damage is done for users -- who have already accepted to give their contact lists and whatnot -- and for WhatsApp although at a much lesser degree. Network effects and migration costs will retain their users, but for how long?')
