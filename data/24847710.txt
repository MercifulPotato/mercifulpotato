Item(by='couchand', descendants=None, kids=[24848561, 24848967], score=None, time=1603287253, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24845987, text='A related principle is to make illegal states unrepresentable.  If you can possibly insert a debit and fail to insert the corresponding credit, thereby making money magically appear, the schema is wrong.  This isn&#x27;t a matter of just insufficiently normalized, it simply doesn&#x27;t represent what you claim it represents.<p>The database shouldn&#x27;t be viewed as just some kind of scratch pad where you write stuff down so the application server doesn&#x27;t have to remember it.  If the database is the source of truth, then it is the model, and the application is simply a view.')
