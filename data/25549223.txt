Item(by='closeparen', descendants=None, kids=[25549711], score=None, time=1609049098, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25546043, text='I don&#x27;t buy this. Crying spells and slowed movements attract attention. Flat affect reads as ingratitude. Emotional deadening tends to leave you with the one emotion that will get you in the most trouble, anger. Lethargy and dread make you want to decline every invitation and activity, which is <i>far</i> from submissive - people feel you are rejecting them or neglecting your responsibilities.<p>Really the only thing that seems adaptive is suppressing executive function &#x2F; initiative. In every other respect, to keep your head down and avoid punishment you need to <i>conform</i>. Depressed people do sometimes manage this but they describe it as exhausting work, which was easier before they get depressed.')
