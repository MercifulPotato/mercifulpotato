Item(by='dcolkitt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611622910, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25909840, text='&gt; The average price of a new car just surpassed $40k<p>In nominal terms, the inflation rate of new cars was in aggregate 3.5%[1] since 1995 (not 3.5% per year, 3.5% total.) Over the same period, household after tax income increased by 111% in nominal terms. Therefore the affordability of a new car, for the average household, <i>doubled</i> over the period.<p>Moreover the average longevity of a car increased from 8.5[3] years to 11.9 years[4]. That means the amortized cost of one year of car ownership, in terms of household income, has fallen by a <i>factor of three</i>.<p>[1]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.in2013dollars.com&#x2F;New-cars&#x2F;price-inflation&#x2F;1995-to-2020?amount=15000" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.in2013dollars.com&#x2F;New-cars&#x2F;price-inflation&#x2F;1995-...</a>\n[2]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bls.gov&#x2F;cex&#x2F;1995&#x2F;aggregate&#x2F;age.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bls.gov&#x2F;cex&#x2F;1995&#x2F;aggregate&#x2F;age.pdf</a>\n[4]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.caranddriver.com&#x2F;news&#x2F;a33457915&#x2F;average-age-vehicles-on-road-12-years&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.caranddriver.com&#x2F;news&#x2F;a33457915&#x2F;average-age-vehi...</a>')
