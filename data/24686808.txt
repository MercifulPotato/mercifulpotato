Item(by='jsjohnst', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601898842, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24686099, text='&gt; I get 8ms even to my WiFi router, how can you have such a low response time?<p>That’s almost assuredly a sign of radio congestion on the channel you are on causing retransmits. It could also be a poor quality AP or you could have to many clients on your AP, but most likely it’s the first one (in band interference causing lots of retransmits) based on experience.')
