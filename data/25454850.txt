An attacker anywhere on the network can listen to your input devices, insert fake keypresses, view your screen, etc.\nBasically a total system compromise.<p>All without log entries.<p>The correct way to do this is to use MIT-MAGIC-COOKIE.<p>One the client host:<p><pre><code>    $ xauth -extract - $DISPLAY\n</code></pre>\nThen copy the output.\nOn the host running the X server:<p><pre><code>    $ xauth nmerge -\n</code></pre>\nthen paste the output copied previously.\nPress enter and CTRL-D (EOF).\nIf you then check the output of &quot;xhost&quot; on the client, it should only say &quot;<i>Access control enabled.</i>&quot;')
