Item(by='petercooper', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606427976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25221789, text='Newsletters have been my main business for ten years (starting in an era everyone thought they were &quot;old hat&quot; and wouldn&#x27;t work again) and baking in the flexibility to take a break that Robin talks about here is a <i>great</i> idea.<p>I&#x27;ve found the pressure of having customers (and therefore money to refund) has kept me going every week, but I&#x27;ve seen <i>so many</i> people burn out with a fixed schedule and give up on newsletters that would have thrived if they&#x27;d given themselves room to breathe.<p>This advice suits podcasting and blogging too, but seasons and irregularity respectively are more naturally baked into those formats.')
