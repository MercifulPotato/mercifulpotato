Item(by='cgrealy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606085507, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25181701, text='Atheism isn&#x27;t a religion, but it is a position on religion.<p>An atheist may not believe in god, but all of them have a position on whether religion belongs in a science class, for instance.<p>You can opt out and say &quot;I don&#x27;t care if biology is taught using creationism or evolution&quot;, but opting out means you are ok with creationism being taught.<p>And if you&#x27;re ok with that, fine (I mean, you&#x27;re wrong, but you&#x27;re entitled to an opinion). But you can&#x27;t pretend you haven&#x27;t taken a position on it.')
