Item(by='lessthanseventy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608151495, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25448075, text='I have configured i3 to be a bootleg version of PaperWM[0]. Everything tabbed by default. Alt+h &amp; Alt+l switch windows in the current workspace and Alt+j &amp; Alt+k switch workspaces. I can still flip to tiling mode if I need to see things side by side but I find myself rarely needing to. I have really poor vision so my use case is going to be different than most but this works super well for me. I&#x27;m only ever a couple keystrokes away from whatever I need to see.<p>[0]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;paperwm&#x2F;PaperWM" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;paperwm&#x2F;PaperWM</a>')
