Item(by='georgewfraser', descendants=None, kids=[25359604, 25349908], score=None, time=1607453969, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25348584, text='That post explains that there are scenarios where it makes sense to store data permanently in Kafka. &quot;Kafka is Not a Database&quot; makes a different point, which is that Kafka doesn&#x27;t solve any of the hard transaction-processing problems that database systems do, so it&#x27;s not an alternative to a traditional DBMS. This is not a straw man---Confluent advocates for &quot;turning the database inside out&quot; all over their marketing materials and conference talks.')
