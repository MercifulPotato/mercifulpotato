Item(by='nynx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608143405, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25439490, text='Honestly, I think a large barrier here is there&#x27;s no pre-existing platform yet. When developing an application for a computer with a regular screen, you have all sorts of supporting software (e.g. window managers, browsers, ui frameworks, etc). When developing for VR, you have to start over almost completely: you have to deal with controller input, different kinds of headsets, designing a UI.<p>For XR to take off, I think something akin to the <i>metaverse</i> needs to exist first, so applications can develop within its framework.')
