Item(by='GauntletWizard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605829548, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25154615, text='The problem isn’t that ZFS needs ECC more. It’s that other filesystems will silently ignore the errors that ZFS points out. With other filesystems, you data just eats some corruption. With ZFS, you’re likely to encounter errors because you notice them - Because it’s extensive checksumming points out when a bit flips.<p>I’d much prefer to know, but if you’re used to not knowing, knowing is scary.')
