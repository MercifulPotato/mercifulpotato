Item(by='Pelic4n', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606485062, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25228225, text='I have benzos as a safety net during my trips, and it occured to me that it may be a really bad idea to take them.<p>During a trip I lived what people describe as an &quot;ego death&quot;, where I saw myself disappearing and dying, and it was the most frightening experience of my life.\nNot only was I completely unable to take a benzo at this point, but it is very much a death and rebirth movement as described by Leary in the psychedelic experience. If I was pulled out from that movement without completing it, I don&#x27;t know what would have happened. I think I would have been left seriously shaken and disturbed. It was crucial perceiving myself coming back to existence in order to feel &quot;me&quot; again.<p>Sometimes &quot;bad trips&quot; aren&#x27;t &quot;bad&quot;, they&#x27;re learning experiences meant to be completed, even if it is tough.')
