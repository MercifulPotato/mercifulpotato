Item(by='ajuc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610477015, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25731699, text='A nice trick to detect anomalies in an array of arbitrary data is to make N subarrays (each with different size(array)&#x2F;N elements removed) and compress each of them.<p>The subarray that compresses the most has the anomaly removed.<p>It&#x27;s not very cheap, but it&#x27;s much more general than median or average or string comparisons. It relies on the fact that compressed size is estimate of Kolmogorov complexity.')
