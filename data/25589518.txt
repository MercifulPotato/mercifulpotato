Doctors, first responders, and other people who face extreme situations develop it to be able to do their jobs.<p>But it&#x27;s not out of reach for a person who just practices it consciously. This is not about removing your emotions, it&#x27;s about letting your mind observe the flow of emotions, and still stay and act sober.')
