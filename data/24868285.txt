Item(by='lm2s', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603452110, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24868148, text='Self-employment is a kind of legal working status. Think of an handyman (or an Uber driver), they might have a company or be self-employed.<p>Since having a company has much more legal work and expenses, many workers prefer to be self-employed. And usually self-employment seems to only happen in lower income jobs.<p>In this case bogus means that the self-employed status is being abused by a company to reduce tax payment and associated costs and benefits that an employee would have.')
