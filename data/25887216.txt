Item(by='snowwrestler', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611445233, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25886876, text='Inflation is the amount that prices rise without an increase in real value. And if there is no increase in real value, then you haven’t actually made any money.<p>Conversely, if people are making money on assets in real terms, then that is appreciation, not inflation.<p>Income inequality is a difference in real income. It’s growing because the economy is differentially allocating real value, not because of inflation.')
