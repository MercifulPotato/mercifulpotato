Item(by='Maarten88', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601934624, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24691798, text='It&#x27;s the distributed networking equivalent of programming with managed versus unmanaged memory.<p>With Akka you manage objects actively, much like memory in C, with Orleans your objects behave much like normal Dotnet&#x2F;Java objects, they are just distributed and must use async methods. Orleans adds a layer of management, where it will tear down and re-instantiate objects as required, distributes them in the cluster, etc. As a programmer you don&#x27;t have to worry about it, it&#x27;s all managed by the runtime.')
