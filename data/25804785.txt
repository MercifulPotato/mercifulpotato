Item(by='noir_lord', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610825860, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803568, text='As a lead I find the best approach is to just figure out the person on your team and use whatever approach suits their personality well.<p>On that topic, remember to say thank you when someone on your team gets you out of a jam, There are parts of the systems at work that I simply don&#x27;t understand yet&#x2F;never will because they are far outside of my team typically so if someone saves me a bunch of time because they happen to know that system, a thank you goes a long way - it&#x27;s also an admission that as a lead you <i>don&#x27;t know everything</i>.<p>I find &quot;I don&#x27;t know either, lets find out&quot; opens a more productive dialogue than pretending you know everything - on top of which seniors (and good juniors) can spot a bluff anyway and you just lose credibility.<p>Almost any management style works if you are genuine, open and authentic.')
