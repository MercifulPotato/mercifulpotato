Item(by='simiones', descendants=None, kids=[25304402], score=None, time=1607088260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25301902, text='This is mentioned in the article: the old green threads model was not blocking aware - if a green thread scheduled on an OS thread issued a blocking call (say a listen on a socket) than the whole OS thread was blocked.<p>With Project Loom, if a virtual thread executes a blocking call, the virtual thread is suspended and the OS thread is free to execute another virtual thread.')
