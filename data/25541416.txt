Item(by='neaanopri', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608957527, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25540266, text='One of the reasons liquid methane works well as a rocket propellant is that rockets need to carry their own oxidizer with them.  Because methane is a smaller molecule, less oxygen is required to oxidize a given amount of methane.<p>With an airplane, which gets its oxidizer from the atmosphere, methane&#x27;s lower oxidizer requirement is basically irrelevant.')
