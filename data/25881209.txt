Item(by='athms', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611397164, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25879886, text='There are two emission standards for California, state and federal. If the car was sold in a state that follows federal and it meets the emission standard for the model year, it can be registered in California. The state doesn&#x27;t change smog requirements; older cars remain at their original specification.')
