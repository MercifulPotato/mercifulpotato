Item(by='Cthulhu_', descendants=None, kids=[24707857], score=None, time=1602061964, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24704733, text='It&#x27;s in the name of energy ratings; using less water at a colder temperature will reduce energy usage.<p>As for the smell &quot;wet dog smell&quot; is usually mold; rinse your plates of food remains before putting it in the dishwasher, clean out your filters regularly (not as necessary if you rinse beforehand), and run a hot, empty cycle with soda crystals. Also, leave it open to air out if it&#x27;s not running (doesn&#x27;t need to be open all the way, just enough for the insides to stay dry). All of that also applies to washing machines. Basically, moisture in a closed space will cause mold and smells.')
