Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=[24888970], score=None, time=1603652706, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887827, text='As written in the Constitution, the provision granting Congress the authority to create copyright laws on its face does not include anything about fair use.<p>In fact, the Constitutional text is quite explicit about creators have &quot;exclusive&quot; rights, and at the time, fair use was not part of the (limited) intellectual property base of law in the States or the UK.<p>Reverting back to the original intent would likely eliminate fair use altogether.')
