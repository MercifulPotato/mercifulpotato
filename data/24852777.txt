Item(by='jaredklewis', descendants=None, kids=[24852961, 24853563], score=None, time=1603318177, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24852423, text='What&#x27;s your baseline? While there are theoretically more secure alternatives to using a password managers, the vast majority of people don&#x27;t have the discipline or skill to implement them effectively.<p>Password managers make security tradeoffs, providing a nice balance of convenience and defense against many of the most important attack vectors.<p>So while it of course possible to come up with basically endless possible attack vectors for password managers (and indeed all software), it is most likely not a productive exercise.<p>Also, a small tangent, but if someone compromises the play store and is able to install malicious software on your phone, there are plenty of ways for tmem to get your password that don&#x27;t involve password managers.')
