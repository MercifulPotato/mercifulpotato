Item(by='yardie', descendants=None, kids=[25945673], score=None, time=1611855990, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25945327, text='I know that France is the butt of many jokes about work, &quot;no French word for entrepreneurship,&quot; but in my experience almost every business I interacted with around socialist Paris was a small, independent business. This was the unintentional consequence of labor laws. &lt;50 employees fall under one set of labor laws, &gt;50 employees you&#x27;re in a tight spot until you&#x27;re &gt;1000 employees. Then the additional overhead of benefits for those employees is subsidized by economies of scale.')
