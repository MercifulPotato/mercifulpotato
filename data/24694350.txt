Item(by='atoav', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601952981, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24692969, text='Maybe there is no link between something being complicated and it&#x27;s complexity.<p>One is entirely subjective and the other isn&#x27;t.<p>Example: a typical CLI application might be incredibly <i>complicated</i> to my mother, while it is in fact much less <i>complex</i> than e.g. a GUI software she uses to make and order picture books, which to her is much less <i>complicated</i>.<p>Something being complicated to someone doesn&#x27;t mean it is complex. Very simple things can be complicated, very complex things can be simple.')
