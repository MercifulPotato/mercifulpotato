Item(by='masklinn', descendants=None, kids=[24841379], score=None, time=1603221368, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24841211, text='There are rather good reasons to not allow overlong encoding. For instance it allows smuggling data through unicode-unaware filters into unicode-aware applications, and while you <i>should</i> also catch the issue on the other side of the filter, overlong encoding allows trivially bypassing one line of defence for no reason. Such filters usually don&#x27;t usually care much about precomposition or decomposition.')
