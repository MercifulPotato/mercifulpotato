Item(by='astrange', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611147740, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25839547, text='Parallelism isn&#x27;t a good idea when scaling down, nor is concurrency often. Going faster is still a good idea on phones (running the CPU at higher speed uses less battery because it can turn off faster) but counting background services there is typically less than one core free, there is overhead to threading and asyncing, and your program will go faster if you take most of it out.')
