Item(by='wcoenen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609542563, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25607411, text='&gt; <i>how size improves usage and efficiency</i><p>The power of a wind turbine scales linearly with the swept area. The swept area scales as the square of the height.<p>Furthermore, wind turbine power also scales with the <i>third power</i> of wind speed, and wind speed goes up with height (though not linearly).<p>So you can see why the physics of the problem are strongly incentivizing size increases.')
