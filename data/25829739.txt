Also, people with sensitive, itchy eyes tend to avoid contact lenses: this is a two-way relationship. Nefarious eye rubbing is usually related to a daily, compulsory &quot;habit&quot;, involving a vigorous pressure on the eye using the knuckles or the side of the index, also frequently present while sleeping.')
