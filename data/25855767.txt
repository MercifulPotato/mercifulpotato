If anyone is curious, this works for <i>any</i> type in Python that implements <i>__format__</i> (<i>{now:%m&#x2F;%d&#x2F;%Y}</i> is equivalent to <i>{now.__format__(&#x27;%m&#x2F;%d&#x2F;%Y&#x27;)}</i>), and you can (ostensibly) make the format string mean whatever you want.')
