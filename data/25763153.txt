Item(by='jawns', descendants=None, kids=[25763212], score=None, time=1610551749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25763042, text='Your proposal sounds similar to the flagging mechanism on many online forums, where the person who flags the comment is asked to specify a reason so it can be triaged by a moderator.<p>In the case of downvoting, it doesn&#x27;t rise to the level of needing to be triaged by a mod, but I suppose you could argue that by giving the commenter a signal about why you downvoted, you&#x27;re allowing them to self-triage (e.g. update the post to address the issue, or at least try to do better next time).<p>Being able to choose from a short list of predefined downvote reasons -- even if it were between &quot;This breaks forum rules&quot; versus &quot;I simply disagree&quot; -- would be useful.')
