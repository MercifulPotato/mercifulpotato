Item(by='taneq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604904783, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25027126, text='This reminds me of the importance of specifying tolerances on engineering drawings - I once received an excessive quote for a part (several hundred dollars for a small, simple Aluminium widget) and it took a while to figure out that I&#x27;d not added tolerances and so they had assumed I needed it exact to the micron. I added the tolerances in and the price dropped to $70 or something.<p>Clear communication is so important!')
