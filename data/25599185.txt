Item(by='ur-whale', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609461476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25598726, text='I really dislike the way the finance industry has been conflating two notions that - while related - are actually different: that of volatility and that of risk.<p>Volatility results from the fact that the markets have a very hard time pricing an asset (because the asset&#x27;s properties are hard to understand and its future behavior is therefor hard to predict).<p>This results in the creation of noise on the price signal.<p>It does not mean the asset is inherently bad. It just reflects lack of knowledge.')
