Item(by='lexi-lambda', descendants=None, kids=[24966237, 24966154], score=None, time=1604288369, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24964562, text='As was already mentioned in another reply, what you are describing are <i>refinement types</i>. A refinement type system actually exists for Haskell: it’s called LiquidHaskell,[1] and though I have not used it for anything serious, it seems to work well for certain kinds of problems.<p>The main challenge of refinement types is that arbitrary properties are very difficult to check in general. (If that weren’t the case, software verification would be easy!)
