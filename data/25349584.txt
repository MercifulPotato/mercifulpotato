Item(by='Sophira', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607452949, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25346326, text='While individual clients may not be easily identifiable, there&#x27;s still a measure of identification that could be made, if you were to configure the public key DNS server to send a different (but persistent) public key to each IP address which asks for the DNS record.
