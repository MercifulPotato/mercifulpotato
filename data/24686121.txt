For a brief while, I worked at a startup that made software to manage vending machines and handle digital payments, from the device stuck on the front, to accept credit cards.<p>Basically, the name of the game is location location location. It&#x27;s very tough to find a good lucrative location to put your machine at. Why? Because there&#x27;s likely already a machine in such location.<p>Also, it&#x27;s a numbers game as many locations don&#x27;t make that much profit per month. Therefore you have to have many machines, and getting 50-100 machines in great locations is no easy task.')
