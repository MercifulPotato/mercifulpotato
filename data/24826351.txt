The scanners have automated and semi-automated page turners which can be used depending on the state of the book. The have &quot;beds&quot; for holding fragile books which cannot be opened fully.<p>Digitization is certainly an important part of preservation, but speedily leafing through a fragile old book with a smartphone in one hand is not the way to do it.
