Item(by='morei', descendants=None, kids=[24844463], score=None, time=1603231869, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24840836, text='I think the convergence here is toward &#x27;automatically discoverable types&#x27;. For python et al this means type annotation (because it&#x27;s extremely difficult for discover types otherwise).<p>For C++ et al this is things like the &#x27;auto&#x27; keyword that allow skipping the type specification where the compiler can work it out.<p>The end-game is specifying just enough type information that it&#x27;s still understandable to both humans and compilers.')
