Item(by='darawk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603758414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24900804, text='&quot;Ism&quot; is a suffix that implies a philosophy. An <i>ism</i> is a suite of beliefs. Anything that is unconscious is, by definition, not an <i>ism</i>. Rac<i>ism</i> refers to people with a philosophy of racial hierarchy, or ideas about race that evoke or imply such a philosophy.<p>For instance, Buddhism is a suite of beliefs about enlightenment and transcendence. We don&#x27;t refer to people as &quot;structural Buddhists&quot; simply because they have equanimity.')
