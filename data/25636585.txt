Item(by='twblalock', descendants=None, kids=[25638229, 25640232], score=None, time=1609788436, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25635967, text='It&#x27;s an argument against referendums, especially when they only require a simple majority required to pass. The Brexit referendum passed with about 52% of votes in favor -- not exactly a landslide.<p>Really big changes ought to have a higher bar than just a simple majority. A 2&#x2F;3 majority is a much higher bar to overcome, and in the case of a referendum, it would mean the result represents the consensus view of the people, not just one side in a bitterly divided close fight.')
