Item(by='tshaddox', descendants=None, kids=[25805313, 25805292], score=None, time=1610826769, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803961, text='There can be appropriate levels of imprecision. Arguably all communication necessarily requires that. This is only a problem if it leads to an unnoticed miscommunication. For example, I suspect most of the time when an engineers refers to an algorithm as being in O(n^2) they intend to preclude the possibility that the algorithm is not in O(n).')
