Item(by='nojokes', descendants=None, kids=[24773528], score=None, time=1602643853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770885, text='I think it is not as simple as that.<p>Many companies have tried to move their development to India for example (I have a second hand anecdotal evidence from one) and it is not as simple as it seems.<p>There are huge cultural differences, meaning what is considered not acceptable in the west is common practice somewhere else like getting degree for bribes etc. You have to know how to navigate in such environment.<p>You may think that because your company employs people from multiple cultural backgrounds then the environment of the company is multicultural.<p>In reality the culture of the company is most likely following the protestant work ethics (it is my generalization, there are of course variations and exceptions).')
