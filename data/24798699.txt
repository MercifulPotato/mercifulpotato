Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=[24798741, 24799317], score=None, time=1602835648, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24785175, text='Beware that the tasmota rules engine has a bunch of bugs in, and the maintainers aren&#x27;t interested in fixing (or even documenting) the bugs for fear that users now rely on the bugs.   They mostly stem from the fact the engine does no tokenizing, and all parsing is ad-hoc.<p>That makes implementing anything more complex than &quot;when this sensor goes to this state, do that&quot; very hard, because you&#x27;ll hit bug after bug.')
