Yahweh was a god and worshipped at the time. But over time, Yahweh became the single worshipped god, who became “the god”. El became a generic name for a deity much like our god vs God. In fact, other gods were acknowledged to exist for a time, until eventually they too were denied to exist.<p>I too am interested in the same question. More generally, how a polytheist belief system transforms into a monotheistic one.')
