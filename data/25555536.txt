Item(by='academi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609115618, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25554937, text='Systems should still be able to be taken offline, though, even if that means failure.<p>For example, let’s say you have a service that uses another service that raised its cost from free to $100&#x2F;hour and you call it 1000 times per hour.<p>Even though you may not have a fallback, and your service may fail, you need to be able to disable it. In this case, an admin is unavailable and the only recourse would be to lower the capacity to 0, since you have that control.<p>That doesn’t negate the benefit of validation, but don’t be too heavy-handed with validation, just as a reaction  to failure without fully thinking it through.')
