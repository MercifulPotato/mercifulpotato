Item(by='VintageCool', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610912050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25809867, text='Seven colors.<p>There is a whole spectrum of color, but there are only a handful of colors that most humans distinguish and talk about. It has always seemed odd to me that we elevate Indigo into the spectrum of named colors.<p>However, Newton was fond of the occult, and numerology, and the number seven. He wrote papers about appearances of the number seven in the Bible.<p>I think that Newton made Indigo an official color just to bring the number up to 7.')
