Item(by='abuehrle', descendants=None, kids=[25861033, 25861115], score=None, time=1611248005, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25860819, text='I have mixed feelings about pricing that scales with revenue. Most pricing ends up working this way, but at least when it&#x27;s expressed as seats or usage it&#x27;s a bit easier to justify the price tag. Revenue scaling pricing reads to me as &quot;We&#x27;ll give you the same thing, but you&#x27;re making more, so pay us more.&quot; I simultaneously understand it, and it feels dirty to me, hence the mixed feelings.')
