For example some upscaling algorithms love to draw tiny glasses. While I don’t see much harm in making some scenes more fun for people, it’s important to keep original sources, because they are ultimate truth, not algorithm outputs. And there’s danger that some people will destroy originals thinking that they are inferior.')
