I moved to a homeless camp in Tucson and rebuilt the camper. I regard living in a homeless camp a worthwhile experience.<p>In the summers I&#x27;m a camp host on the banks of a famous trout river in the midst of gold country.  I fish, prospect for gold and write independent software for college students.  As I can&#x27;t go to my home in Mexico this winter because of covid-19, I&#x27;m currently traveling around southern Arizona looking for gold in old mining district while maintaining my application and learning to draw.<p>My life has taken on a certain mellow aspect that I cherish.  At 74 I don&#x27;t feel like I&#x27;m retired nor like I&#x27;m working. I&#x27;m living.')
