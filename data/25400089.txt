Item(by='lokedhs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607795865, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25397301, text='As a native Swedish speaker, I tend to follow Swedish documentation writing rules even when I write English. In particular, one should always use passive voice, and also avoid terms such as &quot;you&quot;.<p>In other words, write &quot;How to achieve X&quot;, rather than &quot;How do I do X&quot;?<p>The point I want to make is that when I write documentation, and change the latter to the former, most people seem to agree it&#x27;s better.')
