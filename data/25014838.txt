And you have have several different objects that all have different pages.<p>With rest, you can only paginate that particular call. You cannot really paginate different results in that one call. At most you can do is say get the list of objects at this endpoint. And that is far easier to grok than graphql&#x27;s pagination in even the simple case.<p>I mean, just look at the official guide on this:\n<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;graphql.org&#x2F;learn&#x2F;pagination&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;graphql.org&#x2F;learn&#x2F;pagination&#x2F;</a><p>And that only covers a simple use case!')
