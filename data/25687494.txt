Item(by='dbrueck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610128425, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25687341, text='I don&#x27;t know how others do it, but at companies I&#x27;ve started or where I otherwise have a say in things, (1) we don&#x27;t have any titles and (b) during interviewing we weigh personality almost more than tech ability. If someone is generous, has a good sense of humor, open to feedback, is laid back but still driven, and seems to love building cool stuff for the joy it brings, odds are pretty good that they will have little interest in the contention &amp; disruption of office politics.<p>It doesn&#x27;t completely eliminate politics but it comes pretty close. When it hasn&#x27;t worked out, in most cases some coaching of people one-on-one has helped them improve, and in the other cases - where they seemed uninterested or unwilling to change, we just fired them.')
