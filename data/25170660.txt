Item(by='shawnz', descendants=None, kids=[25171867], score=None, time=1605976719, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170119, text='I think this hard separation between work areas and social areas in an office would eliminate a lot of the social advantages of the office.<p>Who wants to be seen as the one always in the social area not working while on the clock? Plus, who would consciously choose to get their socialization from work instead of their personal friends? I think office socialization has to be coincidental or it just won&#x27;t happen at all.<p>I think that model works better in university settings because the students have such disjoint schedules, they <i>need</i> those amenities to keep students occupied between classes.')
