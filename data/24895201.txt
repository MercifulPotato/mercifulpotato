Item(by='todd8', descendants=None, kids=[24895394, 24895937], score=None, time=1603713935, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24892400, text='I remember looking over \nNiklaus Wirth’s source code for a compiler for his new language Pascal. This would have been around 1975. I was stuck by the fact that it was one big single source file.<p>Later I came across a quote where he said something to the effect that what’s the point of a linker that slower than the compiler.<p>Sometimes I think what’s the point of this tangle of make files (or cmake&#x2F;conan or whatever). It’s more difficult to get right than one big single source file.')
