Item(by='pdkl95', descendants=None, kids=[25246105, 25243689], score=None, time=1606635444, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25242444, text='&gt; alias mv=&#x27;mv -i&#x27;<p>While --interactive mode is certainly useful, a better option for this problem that I don&#x27;t see mentioned often is the --no-clobber option:<p><pre><code>    -n, --no-clobber\n              do not overwrite an existing file\n</code></pre>\nI often add --no-clobber to scripts to make automated file movement safer. An alternate &quot;safe mv&quot; alias can be useful, e.g.:<p><pre><code>    alias smv=&#x27;mv --no-clobber&#x27;\n</code></pre>\n(I don&#x27;t recommend changing the behavior of normal &#x27;mv&#x27; directly; getting used to assuming &#x27;mv&#x27; means the safer &#x27;mv --no-clobber&#x27; can be dangerous when you use use a different computer without your custom environment)')
