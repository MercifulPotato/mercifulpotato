Item(by='redwood', descendants=None, kids=[25790889], score=None, time=1610712773, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25789603, text='From my perspective, Dropbox was doomed to decline ever since they made a big splash about how proud they were of moving off of public cloud in to self manage data centers.<p>While they no doubt reduced their operating costs with that move, I couldn&#x27;t help it feel that this meant that they were investing so many of their precious mental cycles,  hiring cycles, maintenance cycles, into plumbing instead of user-centered innovation which was their original sweet spot.<p>What was celebrated at the time as a great example of how cloud is a big mistake for people to me is in fact the opposite and should be used in future as a good business case study.<p>Folks never think about the opportunity cost; they get blinded by $$$ figures which are so misleading')
