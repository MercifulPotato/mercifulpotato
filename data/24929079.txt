Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603968304, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24926596, text='It is true that you need really good reasons to make virtual functions, or to do metaprogramming. I, also, usually avoid them. I have not made a more than two-level inheritance hierarchy in this millennium. There is no substitute for taste, design sense, or insistence on simplicity.<p>Stepanov referred to OO as &quot;gook&quot;, and has publicly regretted making member functions like size().<p>But putting types to work for you is a great force multiplier.')
