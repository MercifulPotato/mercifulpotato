Item(by='emilecantin', descendants=None, kids=[25582769], score=None, time=1609341845, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25575591, text='I can certainly appreciate the &quot;doesn&#x27;t surprise me&quot; aspect, but I still don&#x27;t see how AWD &#x2F; 4WD will help you when braking.<p>It&#x27;s obvious that it&#x27;ll help when accelerating, and perhaps even when turning, with a (more) even application of torque on all wheels.<p>My main gripe with it is that it often gives drivers a false sense of security (as they got going easily), and they&#x27;ll drive too fast for the conditions. Just look at some parts of this sub-thread, where people say &quot;I don&#x27;t need winter tires, I have AWD &#x2F; 4WD&quot;.<p>If you need to brake in an emergency on snow &#x2F; slush &#x2F; ice, nothing is going to help you except your tires (and perhaps some smart ABS).')
