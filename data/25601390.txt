Item(by='flobosg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609483518, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25601331, text='Spike proteins in coronaviruses are present in two structurally distinct conformations: pre- and postfusion. The conformational switch between them promotes the viral fusion to the host cell.<p>To maximize the effectiveness of the vaccine, its spike protein should stay in the prefusion state no matter what. The two mutations in the engineered spike proteins allow exactly that.')
