I just showed my kids some of the things you can do with this on the Circuit Playground Bluefruit[1] earlier today. It comes with a lot of sensors and is full of possibilities.<p>For getting started fast I can&#x27;t recommend MakeCode[2] enough. It&#x27;s a web IDE with a compiler and device simulator for programming microcontrollers with blocks or JavaScript&#x2F;Python.<p>That said, for more complex projects CircuitPython might be a better choice. There are tons of docs and lots of great examples. It re-runs your code every time you save so you get instant feedback.
