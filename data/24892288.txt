Item(by='chroem-', descendants=None, kids=[24892330], score=None, time=1603679113, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24892113, text='We already have a significant amount of legal precedent to compel a private business to serve certain groups.  Those protected groups were legally enshrined in the first place to extend them the same rights that everyone else already enjoyed by common custom, not to create a new group of people with elevated privileges.  In my opinion, it&#x27;s time that we now legally enshrine the common custom of a right to service as well.')
