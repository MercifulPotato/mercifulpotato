Item(by='kevin_thibedeau', descendants=None, kids=[25475278], score=None, time=1608354278, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25475060, text='If you wire the bits as numbered to a conventional memory device designed with LSB as bit-0, the internal address bus will induce more switching from sequential access than normal. The internal row and column decoders will be working overtime consuming more power than necessary. Reversing the bus to deal with that isn&#x27;t always straightforward on a space constrained board.')
