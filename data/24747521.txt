Item(by='harryh', descendants=None, kids=[24747751, 24747549], score=None, time=1602437737, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24747316, text='There are several problems with trading at 1 second intervals (or any other fixed interval):<p>A) What do you do when bids and asks do not match up? Who&#x27;s orders get filled and who&#x27;s do not? There are several possible answers to this problem, but not are satisfactory.<p>B) You do not eliminate the advantage of speed because everyone is still incentivized to put in their orders at the last possible moment before the end of the interval.<p>The world is continuous, not discrete. Therefore, like it or not, trading must be continuous and not discrete.')
