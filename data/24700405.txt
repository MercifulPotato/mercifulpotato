Item(by='pvh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602006010, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24700314, text='I love the analogy, but no! First, XSLT is unidirectional and although I have built some tree-like XSLT processing systems, Cambria is explicitly graph-based and bidirectional.<p>In other words, with Cambria you can build a graph of lenses that describe various data schemas and then translate from or to any of them.<p>That said, XSLT has different strengths (syntax-aside) in that it is a general purpose templating language with strong functional programming underpinnings.')
