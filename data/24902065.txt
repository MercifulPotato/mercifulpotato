Item(by='sharadov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603750027, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24901925, text='I made peace with this another way, took a remote job so that I have time for pursuits that I enjoy - working out, racquetball, reading, cooking, traveling and being there for my kids.<p>You can look for meaning elsewhere, not all professions are fulfilling, and yes medicine is one of the few professions where there is a direct tangible correlation between effort and reward. You see your patients getting better.<p>But it&#x27;s endlessly taxing to be a good doctor, you can&#x27;t really have much of a life if you are truly a good doctor.<p>My parents were doctors, ran a hospital and really passionate ones at that, but they had no time for anything else. So if you are dedicated like that more power to you. On the flip-side I&#x27;ve seen more doctors who were into it only for the money than ones who were true to the Hippocratic Oath.')
