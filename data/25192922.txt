I expect that once city amenities are available again (post vaccine), this balance will steer closer towards the pre-COVID mean.<p>That, and most folks I&#x27;ve seen online seem to be in the &quot;remote is the future!!&quot;, &quot;people will leave tech cities behind!!&quot; route. But these, like most reactions right after a major event, are probably overblown.<p>We won&#x27;t have everyone going to the office 5 days a week anymore, and satellite offices in 2nd and 3rd tier cities &#x2F; rural areas will become more common. This was already the trend before COVID. But that doesn&#x27;t mean that 50+% of engineers in top companies will be full remote in 5 years, people leave big cities permanently, and city housing price crater.')
