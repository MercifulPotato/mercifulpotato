It doesn&#x27;t work eveywhere, but I know a couple of firms who bring in cohorts of apprentices in the same way they do with graduates. They use a local company to do the first few months of training and then have a continuous development programme.<p>A friend is a senior manager at one of them and he loves it, he&#x27;s been really impressed with the calibre of the apprentices and how quickly they&#x27;ve picked things up.')
