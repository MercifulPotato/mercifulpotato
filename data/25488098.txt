ISPs either need a right of way to lay cable or a license to use RF spectrum.  Even if you could magically overcome those problems, or are willing to deal with the chaos of removing the applicable regulations, the Internet would become fragmented since the dominant players would freeze competition out of the market.  (Recall the olden days of online service providers when everyone&#x27;s network was independent of everyone else&#x27;s, and even then it was only regulation of the telephone companies that allowed those service providers to exist.)')
