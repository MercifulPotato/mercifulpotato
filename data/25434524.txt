Item(by='afarrell', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608062989, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25429140, text='Indeed!<p>&quot;What&quot; questions that calmly ask for a list are often really good because they are easy to answer if you know the information. And if someone doesn&#x27;t know the answer, it prompts them into the good habits of:<p>1. Doing some work before interrupting someone with a question.<p>2. Writing down their steps as they do so.<p>Other good ones are: &quot;What are some questions that come to mind&quot; and &quot;What are some paths forward that seem plausible?&quot; to help those who are<p>1. At a crossroads.<p>2. Having trouble articulating that they are afraid of going down a rabbit hole.<p>I&#x27;m still looking for a good question for helping those who are totally lost without a mental model that lets them put words to the domain.')
