Item(by='hannasanarion', descendants=None, kids=[25061991], score=None, time=1605123156, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061768, text='Being prepared has nothing to do with it. Nobody was prepared for the Anchorage earthquake of 1964, but within minutes people had self-organized into firefighting and excavation teams, set up emergency  response relays with a hodgepodge of walkie-talkies, police radio, and AM broadcasters, and within hours there was a centralized volunteer dispatch and food bank.<p>Relying on your neighbors in a disaster doesn&#x27;t mean hiding out in their anti-zombie bunker. It means knowing that the entire community will respond together to overcome the emergency.')
