Item(by='ashtonkem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611903869, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25953616, text='&gt; There is nothing wrong, illegal, or immoral about trying to &quot;manipulate&quot; the stock market unless you are committing fraud or have insider information.<p>Market manipulation is a crime under Section 9(a)(2) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. There is a big difference between buying or selling because you think a stock is over or under valued, and trying to manipulate a stock price to extract money from people not in on the manipulation.')
