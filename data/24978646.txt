Only a handful of mostly CAD SUN workstations stayed.<p>Main reasons were the Linux and GNU development environment slowly overtook the Solaris one in convenience - in the end even the SUNs ran mostly gcc and the whole open source stack.\nAlso getting drivers and (patch) installation was fairly easy for Linux and a major headache with Solaris.')
