Item(by='orev', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607190101, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25316325, text='Everyone is different, so it’s worth exploring different options. It’s widely recognized that a big part of smoking is not the nicotine addiction itself, but also the social aspect. Reduced nicotine cigarettes would enable people to still smoke and participate in the social part, while reducing the nicotine addiction. It’s better to address one thing at a time than to try to go cold turkey on everything at once.')
