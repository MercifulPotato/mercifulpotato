Item(by='flohofwoe', descendants=None, kids=[25301039], score=None, time=1607078944, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25300310, text='Another simple bit-twiddling-hack (it has a proper name which I unfortunately forgot) is SIMD without SIMD instructions by masking out the top-level bits (which are essentially the carry-flags), e.g. doing eight 7-bit additions at once:<p><pre><code>    uint64_t a = ((a0 &amp; 0x7F) | ((a1 &amp; 0x7F)&lt;&lt;8) ...;\n    uint64_t b = ((b0 &amp; 0x7F) | ((b1 &amp; 0x7F)&lt;&lt;8) ...;\n    uint64_t c = (a + b) &amp; 0x7F7F7F7F7F7F7F7F;</code></pre>')
