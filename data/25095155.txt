Much like you can&#x27;t describe health as a single thing, when you collapse your insight to a single variable you miss everything in your simulation of how reality works.<p>This applies to an individual level but especially applies towards a cooperative level with multiple individuals.  When we cooperate (or just act in ambiguous situations) sometimes trying to collapse your actions to &quot;mental shortcuts&quot; aka Heuristics in Judgment and Decision-Making to borrow some thinking fast and slow language further reduces insight.')
