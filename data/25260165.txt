Item(by='chmod600', descendants=None, kids=[25272988, 25271727], score=None, time=1606777426, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25258677, text='It&#x27;s easier to get people to agree to a process than a result, so processes make it a lot easier for people to work together.<p>The processes are imperfect and often take on a life of their own, but still tend to be respected, and are generally improved when the outcomes start getting too far from the spirit of fairness.<p>Transparent processes which involve specific laws are much easier to respect than opaque &quot;wise elder chooses&quot; processes. Especially when there are many wise elders who might have different opinions.')
