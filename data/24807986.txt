Item(by='presentation', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602918004, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24805969, text='I&#x27;d say Japanese is even more difficult than Chinese - at least with Chinese the majority of characters are predictably one syllable with the same pronunciation regardless of context; in Japanese you get entirely different pronunciations depending on the context, with a ton of exceptions.<p>That said the actual sounds are few and really easy to vocalize (if you ignore pitch accent). Adults do use kana for certain words and in between words, but it&#x27;s true that you do need to know a lot of kanji to read things fluently.')
