Item(by='ttul', descendants=None, kids=[25290466], score=None, time=1607011137, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25288757, text='If your company serves a smaller niche than the giants in the a16z analysis, you probably need top down sales much earlier in the growth journey. There are only so many products that get to $20M ARR without a sales team.<p>From personal experience, top down selling is very hard to get right. You suddenly have to learn how to make a sophisticated business value proposition rather than just saying, “here’s my stuff. It’s really great. Look who else is using it.”<p>It’s also hard to find good people to lead enterprise sales, particularly if you’ve been a product led founder and don’t know what good enterprise sales looks like. It’s definitely an inflection point with a lot of risk for bad execution.')
