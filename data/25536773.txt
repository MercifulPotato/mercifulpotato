The worst that can happen is that they&#x27;re transferred to another registry operator.  ICANN requires daily escrow deposits as a condition of running a TLD, which allows emergency registry operators to step in and  ensure TLD operation continuity if that becomes necessary.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.icann.org&#x2F;resources&#x2F;pages&#x2F;ebero-2013-04-02-en" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.icann.org&#x2F;resources&#x2F;pages&#x2F;ebero-2013-04-02-en</a>')
