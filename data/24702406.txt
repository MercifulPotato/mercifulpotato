Item(by='marcusverus', descendants=None, kids=[24702617, 24702556], score=None, time=1602018905, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24702077, text='The US system is far from perfect, but blaming its dysfunction on &#x27;unfettered capitalism&#x27; is absurd.<p>The US healthcare system is among the most heavily regulated industries in the US economy. Among those regulations are FDA policies that give Big Pharma monopolies on new drugs. The FDA extends these monopolies for decades, allowing companies to operate as <i>state-created</i> monopolies. Once the state-created monopoly times out, competitors face a years-long approval process before they can market a generic version of an already approved drug.')
