Item(by='andreypopp', descendants=None, kids=[24760869, 24761113, 24760273], score=None, time=1602541490, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24759891, text='Such workflows with scheduled DELETE&#x2F;INSERT often mean that the data is &quot;derived&quot;, there&#x27;s unlogged tables feature for that in PostgreSQL. Table configured with unlogged are not being written to WAL and thus generate much much less I&#x2F;O. The downside is that after a crash occurs the table might be empty (before it is repopulated by the DELETE&#x2F;INSERT workflow again).')
