Item(by='grishka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607136215, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25311078, text='Another way is to use the checkbox-and-CSS trick. You add a hidden checkbox next to the element you want to hide, add a label to trigger the checkbox, and then use a + selector to toggle the visibility based on the state of the checkbox. Like this:<p><pre><code>    &lt;label for=&quot;spoiler&quot;&gt;Toggle spoiler&lt;&#x2F;label&gt;\n    &lt;input type=&quot;checkbox&quot; id=&quot;spoiler&quot;&#x2F;&gt;\n    &lt;div id=&quot;spoilerContent&quot;&gt;Spoilery stuff goes here&lt;&#x2F;div&gt;\n    &lt;style&gt;\n    #spoiler, #spoilerContent{display: none;}\n    #spoiler:checked+#spoilerContent{display: block;}\n    &lt;&#x2F;style&gt;\n</code></pre>\nYou can also use a variation of this for tabs - just use radio buttons instead - and accordions.')
