It would shift just fine under load. My current bike has a Sturmy Archer 3 speed and doesn&#x27;t.<p>Geared hubs are great for riding in traffic because you can shift while stopped. Pulling harder with traffic up to a light and braking aggressively, and switching to the right gear while waiting for the light to change.<p>Like everything it&#x27;s a tradeoff. For me, geared hubs make a bicycle a more useful tool. Less fragile. Less maintenance. Idiot friendly static adjustment.')
