Item(by='haswell', descendants=None, kids=[24948129], score=None, time=1604101097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24947594, text='First, let me say that I fully support one’s right to take steps that allow them to feel more comfortable in their own skin. I can’t pretend to understand everything transgender people are going through, but as a man who also identifies as bi, (something that took me 20 years to acknowledge) I could not be more supportive.<p>With that said, I worry about how far the pendulum has swung for issues like this. Too many topics now fall into this category that “if you’re not 100% embracing every aspect of the thing and throwing all other viewpoints away, you’re against that thing and should not be listened to”. This is wrong and dangerous.<p>It’s generally accepted that teenagers are not fully mature, and some activities are disallowed (drinking, smoking, driving, sex) until a certain age.<p>But for some reason, on this topic, people lose their minds.<p>I don’t know what the right answer is. But we need to be having more open conversations about these things, not censoring “dissenting” views altogether.')
