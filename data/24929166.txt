Item(by='corty', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603969304, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24929036, text='Even if you had to agree to the EULA before buying the product, it might still be invalid. There are laws in the EU limiting terms of service and similar non-negotiable pre-purchase contracts to unsurprising, expected-by-the-consumer, fair and equitable terms. Meaning that if there is a &quot;buy&quot; terminology used in the contract process, small-print cannot change that to &quot;obtain a limited license&quot;, because that would be surprising and unfair.')
