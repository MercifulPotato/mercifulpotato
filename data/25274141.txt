Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606895668, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25274074, text='A useful rule of thumb: never depend on character width, unless you’re dealing with ASCII in monospace. Especially once you’re dealing with emoji, also get used to the fact that there will be very wide variation, including that some devices simply won’t have a font that can represent your text, especially newer emoji.')
