That is the purpose of ranked newsfeed. Companies just make the &quot;relevance&quot; argument, because it&#x27;s not wrong, it&#x27;s just not the whole story.<p>Specifically, if you had a passing interest in a controversial topic, the algorithm will make sure to sink its barbs into you, and exploit you emotionally.
