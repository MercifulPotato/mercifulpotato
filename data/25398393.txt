Remote file editing on other computers.<p>2. Rectangular&#x2F;columnar text edits and manipulations.<p>3. Essentially infinite kill ring : This I find is very useful, one becomes accoustomed to building blocks of text by assembling things from the kill rings. Also plays nice with point 4, easy macro record&#x2F;replay.<p>4. Easy macro record&#x2F;replay of even complicated edit sequences.<p>5.Inbuild calculator, with both rpn and algebraic modes.<p>6. The Dired mode provides a very convenient file manager.')
