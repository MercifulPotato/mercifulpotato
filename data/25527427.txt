Item(by='afavour', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608817991, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25526959, text='Because there’s a very small defined set of actions a person can take in such games: backwards, forwards, left, right, etc.<p>By comparison pages have dozens of links, form fields, menus, etc. It’s not that they can’t be navigated with the keyboard alone, it’s that it’s rarely the most efficient way of doing it. Clicking a link will always be quicker than pressing tab X number of times to select it.')
