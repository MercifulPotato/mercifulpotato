Item(by='newacct583', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604105421, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24947288, text='For clarity: no one signs a ballot in the US.  States with remote voting options generally have a ballot <i>envelope</i> which must be signed and sealed.  That is: you have to authenticate that the ballot was cast by the right person, but the vote itself can only be tallied anonymously.<p>This is sort of a dumb idea, really.  But it&#x27;s not as broken as you imagine.')
