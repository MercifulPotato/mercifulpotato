Item(by='Propaganda_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602714551, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24782322, text='If the services can&#x27;t be modified to load their config directly from env vars, write the config to an off-root scratch volume (e.g. mounted to &#x2F;tmp&#x2F;) and have them load from that.  The root volume should be mounted read-only either way to prevent modification of your services should something get RCE.')
