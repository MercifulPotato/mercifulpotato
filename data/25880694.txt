Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611391133, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25849307, text='Software <i>could</i> be a lot more like electrical engineering, if ideas like flow based programming took off.<p>The ability to completely remove side-effects from a given piece of code makes it possible to treat things as black boxes, which is an essential part of most circuit design.<p>You could compare low level Assembler&#x2F;C code to RF analog design, they&#x27;re both black arts subject to high amounts of tweaking to get things working reliably.<p>GIT for schematics would be a great improvement.  It could also come in handy for CNC G-code.<p>Git Blame to figure out why a mill crashed could save huge amounts of effort, tooling, and repair, along with making manufacturing more flexible.')
