Technically all you need is 256 bytes for EDID    and that’s it.  You can drive the panel directly from the graphics card from DVI, HDMI and DisplayPort, often with a single format conversion chip.<p>In fact, Apple’s HD Cinema Displays were built using LG panels with a DVI interface built right into them (which isn’t the norm).<p>Source:  I designed custom FPGA-based LCD panel interfaces for many years.')
