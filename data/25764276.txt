Item(by='rexreed', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610555591, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25760960, text='Remote education needs to be a supplement to in-person education. The lack of interaction with other students, especially for kids in elementary or secondary school is becoming very harmful for their overall development.<p>For adults, online education should be thought of as a supplement to either their work-learning or their life-learning. The former to help advance their pursuit of skills, the second to help advance their pursuit of enjoyment of life.<p>The needs for online education for all these realms are very different and there is no one size fits all.')
