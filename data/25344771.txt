[1]  Looking through [2], you could get a whole gentleman&#x27;s outfit for roughly $50 or so.  Even still, you&#x27;d be spending 2-3 weeks&#x27; wages on a single set of clothing - not to mention that you&#x27;d need somewhere to store all that, and you&#x27;d probably also need additional stuff to maintain it so that it looked half-presentable for your daring escape.<p>It&#x27;s much more likely that, as someone in steerage, even your &quot;finest&quot; wouldn&#x27;t have passed muster for the keenly class-conscious Brits on the upper decks.  The fabrics and cuts would be different, you&#x27;d be missing fashionable accessories and key pieces of the outfit, nothing would be tailored, and everything would be in worse wear.
