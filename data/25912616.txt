Item(by='mindslight', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611636016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25911061, text='The doctrine of laches sounds overly technical or dryly procedural, but its purpose is avoiding exactly what happened here. Election rules could have been litigated any time before the election (and many were, both successfully and unsuccessfully). But waiting until after the election has been run, when your requested remedy can only be to invalidate the entire outcome, is completely unworkable.')
