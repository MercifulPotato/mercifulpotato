Item(by='sneak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607687990, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25384734, text='You are confusing signed boot security with the activation of the T2 chip.<p>The T2&#x2F;M1 still needs activation data before it will do anything (like let you use the touchbar or touchid or secure enclave or even talk to the SSD) after a disk wipe.<p>This [online activation] is done after the recovery kernel has already booted from\nthe USB install media.
