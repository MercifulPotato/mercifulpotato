Item(by='ChrisMarshallNY', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607277484, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325098, text='Here&#x27;s one way to learn courtesy:<p>Deal with folks that are on the bad end of the stick, and consider violence to be an appropriate way to solve problems. Be someone that is trying to help them, which includes teaching them that there are other ways to resolve issues.<p>This kind of relationship means that we need to have an empathetic, respectful, authoritative aspect, but that we also need to realize that we could have a real, physical threat of violence directed our way, if we are not respectful and courteous (and sometimes, even then). We also need to <i>not</i> be doormats.<p>Teaches you to be polite, but firm, real quick.')
