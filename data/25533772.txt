Item(by='UI_at_80x24', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608864685, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25533263, text='To make the WireGuard windows app better (for non-admin users) you need to make your user(s) a member of the &quot;Network Configuration Operators&quot; group.<p>This allows you enable&#x2F;disable (or choose if you have multiple) the VPN without needing to be a member of the Administrators group.  You also need to add a line to the registry.<p>Here&#x27;s the powershell code to do that:<p><pre><code>  New-ItemProperty &quot;hklm:\\software\\wireguard&quot; -Name &quot;LimitedOperatorUI&quot; -Value 1 -PropertyType &quot;DWord&quot; -Force\n\n  Add-LocalGroupMember -Group &quot;Network Configuration Operators&quot; -Member &quot;$username&quot;</code></pre>')
