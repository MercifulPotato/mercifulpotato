Item(by='brownbat', descendants=None, kids=[24742995, 24742739], score=None, time=1602360197, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24741435, text='Another design philosophy is that there should be one and only one exit point to a function whenever possible. Maybe that&#x27;s fallen out of favor, or not important to most folks. But even if it&#x27;s just a &quot;nice-to-have,&quot; it&#x27;s still a tradeoff here.<p>If every if block includes a return statement, you&#x27;re basically writing a case switch or a series of elifs anyway. The readability of those structures is highly dependent on the language and how it expects serial conditionals to be most efficiently written.')
