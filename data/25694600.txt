Item(by='zmmmmm', descendants=None, kids=[25695336], score=None, time=1610155555, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25693895, text='Look at it this way: when private companies censor something the user has the option to go elsewhere to exercise their speech. When the government does it, they can&#x27;t go anywhere.<p>In this case, if a significant number of people are disenfranchised by this, it will naturally create the market pressure for a solution that they can use. It will spur adoption of tools like F-droid and open devices etc. It&#x27;ll force these people to embrace open web technologies instead of proprietary ecosystems. Which are all very positive and healthy things.<p>It may be very inefficient, but to some extent its a healthy process that ultimately arbitrates where acceptable standards of speech sit.')
