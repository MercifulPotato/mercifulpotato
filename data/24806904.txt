Self-aggrandizing is usually seen as a major trait of narcissistic personality disorder, whereas APD (or sociopathy) is typically more inclined towards deception and manipulation for personal gain.<p>Hence, sociopaths spend a lot of time lying to and manipulating other people. Detecting lies might very well be easier for someone who lies a lot themselves: &quot;Don&#x27;t bullshit a bullshitter.&quot;<p>With that said, from what I understand of these various personality disorders, it&#x27;s a bit of a floating spectrum of classifications, so traits and symptoms can overlap.<p>Edit: I agree, of course, that the whole text should be taken with a tablespoon of salt.')
