Minimising risk isn&#x27;t the most important goal of drivers, almost by definition. Cars are not safe in any objective sense. They are tools of convenience.<p>A fun hypothetical, you and a good friend get tested for spatial intelligence and it turns out there was a big difference in your favour - how big does the difference need to be before you tell your friend you are no longer comfortable letting them drive when you are in the car?')
