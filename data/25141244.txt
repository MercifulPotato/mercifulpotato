It does matter for all kinds of apps when they feature something, and that&#x27;s about it. Almost all developers have their own ways to drive traffic to their apps.<p>I mean, I&#x27;ve been making Android apps since 2011, and I think that the role of app stores themselves is vastly overrated too. They have their place and they&#x27;re nice to have, sure, but it&#x27;s silly that OS vendors are allowed to have their own ones and either demote or outright ban anything that doesn&#x27;t go through them. The transition to &quot;always-online&quot; was supposed to be more empowering, not more limiting.<p>&quot;But people would install malware...&quot;<p>It&#x27;s okay for new technology to require education before one can make use of it.
