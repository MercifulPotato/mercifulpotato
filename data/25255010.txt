Item(by='khuey', descendants=None, kids=[25255149], score=None, time=1606751523, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25254624, text='MSRs are called &quot;registers&quot; sure but you can&#x27;t actually perform any operations on them other than a load or a store, and you can&#x27;t use them to stash random data outside of main memory because they affect the operation of the processor. `k0` (or e.g. MIPS&#x27;s `r0`) are even worse, since you can&#x27;t even write to them.<p>That&#x27;s not what most people care about when they talk about how many registers a processor has.')
