Item(by='Blikkentrekker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610275372, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25712439, text='The &lt;k&gt; in “knee” is silent; it is pronounced &#x2F;n i&#x2F;, not &#x2F;k n i&#x2F;.<p>The reason this cluster is so difficult for Anglo-Saxon is exactly because English lacks it; it historically had it, which explains the many words that are written as such, for etymological reasons.<p>Even the actual word “gnu&quot; is pronounced &#x2F;n u&#x2F;, not &#x2F;g n u&#x2F;, but the “<i>GNU</i> operating system” is typically pronounced &#x2F;g @ &#x27;n u&#x2F;, in two syllables, with stress on the second.')
