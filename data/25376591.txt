Item(by='justicezyx', descendants=None, kids=[25376915], score=None, time=1607624254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25375588, text='A few things:<p>* Non intrusive: meaning one can snoop info of application without changing application code.<p>* Deep visibility: function level and syscall&#x2F;kernel functions reveal more context and are more accurate in a lot of cases.<p>* Low overhead: everything runs inside kernel space, no context switching compared to other kernel based&#x2F;aided tracing.<p>* Expressiveness: eBPF is fairly expressive, can do many things that usually are exclusive to high level programming languages.')
