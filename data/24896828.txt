Item(by='jvanderbot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603724290, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24889715, text='Among my many random roles in my job, I do export technical licensing review. I can assure you the industry practices on ITAR have only tightened in recent years, and the only language clarifications that have affected my review policies are over 4 years old.<p>Small arms researchers and manufacturers who are inside the US should not be affected by ITAR nearly at all. The only restriction ITAR &#x2F; EAR places on a US company is to not sell or share technical assistance (any kind of design, etc) with any foreign persons (which includes public disclosure).<p>What he may have done is change the classification of certain small arms technologies. But that does not affect the &quot;ITAR&quot; process or its &quot;looseness&quot;.')
