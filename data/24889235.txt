Item(by='robocat', descendants=None, kids=[24890078], score=None, time=1603654724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887098, text='GP poster was suggesting the victims (patients in this case) get compensation, not the company.<p>Currently cybersecurity insurance pays the company, so actually the company itself has <i>less</i> incentive to fix real security issues (albeit with more incentive to resolve the specific security-audit needs of the policy.). And expensive policies reduce the pot of money available to fix problems.<p>Government policies that make directors <i>criminally</i> liable, and ways to spread that liability for criminally negligent security faults, would probably have a better effect than monetary fines.')
