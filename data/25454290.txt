Item(by='simonh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608200809, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25454128, text='I used to work for a small company that developed a cellular radio network design application and also had a radio network engineering consultancy arm. We renamed the product and changed our job titles to include Engineer and Engineering because our sales people said it enabled them to charge more and sell up our services more easily, and I believe them. I was on some sales meetings to provide technical background so I could see they knew what they were doing.<p>It may seem silly, but words mean things and you need to clearly and effectively articulate why your product is valuable, or what value you bring as an employee. Effective communication is really important.')
