Item(by='samth', descendants=None, kids=[25059121], score=None, time=1605105821, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25057123, text='This is one of many confused articles based on Venkatesh&#x27;s research. The point of the original work is that drug gangs, despite being criminal organizations, have a ladder hierarchy and low wages at the bottom, just like many other &quot;normal&quot; jobs at conventional institutions, like mcdonalds or academia.<p>Subsequently, many people have written silly articles like this one saying that some conventional organization is like a drug gangs because it has these totally normal features. But we knew that already -- that grad students get paid less than full professors is hardly news. The inclusion of drug gangs just produces a snappy headline and reduces understanding.')
