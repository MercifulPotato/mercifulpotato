Item(by='kortex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603636771, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24875581, text='First, reproducibility is a spectrum, from a simple recipe to run `make` and get a byte-for-byte reproduction.<p>A cookie recipe is reproducible - follow these steps and get tasty cookies. But will they be the <i>tastiest</i> cookies? For that, you need more detail, but the write-ups almost never have the full story.<p>Having put dozens of academic ML algos into production, anything short of `make reproduce` which bare minimum gives you a table of results on some validation subset, is merely reproducible-ish. Not having vcs cause it&#x27;s &quot;hard&quot; is straight-up slacking (use svn if git is too hard).')
