See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;anko&#x2F;eslisp&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;doc&#x2F;basics-reference.markdown#functions" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;anko&#x2F;eslisp&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;doc&#x2F;basics-refere...</a> for examples.<p>Function declarations are statements, and you need to provide a name for the function.<p>Function expressions are expressions, and the name is optional.<p>In practice they often end up being interchangeable, but I just wanted to make sure any JavaScript is definitely representable.')
