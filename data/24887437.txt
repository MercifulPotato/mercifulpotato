The way click jacking is circumvented is two fold. Firstly, there is 2FA required for completing any UPI payment. Secondly, every payment request is mapped to a receiver who must have completed their KYC. In case of suspected fraud there is a redressal and recovery process.<p>[1]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.npci.org.in&#x2F;sites&#x2F;default&#x2F;files&#x2F;UPI%20Linking%20Specs_ver%201.6.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.npci.org.in&#x2F;sites&#x2F;default&#x2F;files&#x2F;UPI%20Linking%20...</a>')
