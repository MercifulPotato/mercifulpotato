Item(by='refurb', descendants=None, kids=[24727024], score=None, time=1602219516, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24726454, text='Big distinction there provided by the National Firearms Act.  Pipe bombs are classified as &quot;destructive devices&quot; and would require submission of Form 1 to the ATF, along with fingerprints, etc and a $200 tax stamp.<p>And that&#x27;s just federal law.  State law may have other restrictions.<p>So bringing a legal rifle to a protest might be legitimate (though not exactly prudent), but possessing an unregistered pipe bomb will get you prison time even if you don&#x27;t bring it to a protest.<p>Tannerite is exempt as it&#x27;s a binary explosive which apparently has special rules.')
