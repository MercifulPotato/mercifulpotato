Item(by='projektfu', descendants=None, kids=[24847646], score=None, time=1603280928, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846480, text='Honestly, I think cytotoxic chemotherapy will be more shocking.  Radiation oncology has made great strides in targeting the dose (cyberknife and other stereotactic approaches).  Cytotoxic therapy is still basically poisoning everything and hoping the cancer dies first.<p>Or, in the case of cancers like multiple myeloma, killing all immune cells and replacing the stem cells.<p>The future of cancer therapy is likely to be heavily reliant on customization and precise drug delivery, to give an overwhelming dose of poison to the cancer without harming the patient.')
