It&#x27;s the essence of generalization capability.<p>It&#x27;s the JOIN in Leslie Valiant LINK&#x2F;JOIN model [4, 5]:<p>&quot;The first basic function, JOIN, implements memory formation of a new item in terms of two established items: If two items A and B are already represented in the neural system,\nthe task of JOIN is to modify the circuit so that at subsequent times there is the representation of a new item C that will fire if and only if the representations of both A\nand B are firing.&quot;<p>Papadimitriou &amp; Vempala [6] extend it to &quot;predictive join&quot;  (PJOIN) model.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Chunking_(psychology)" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Chunking_(psychology)</a><p>[2] Wickelgren, Wayne A. &quot;Chunking and consolidation&quot; Psychological review (1979): <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.columbia.edu&#x2F;~nvg1&#x2F;Wickelgren&#x2F;papers&#x2F;1979cWAW.pdf" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.columbia.edu&#x2F;~nvg1&#x2F;Wickelgren&#x2F;papers&#x2F;1979cWAW.pdf</a><p>[3] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;The_Magical_Number_Seven,_Plus_or_Minus_Two" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;The_Magical_Number_Seven,_Plus...</a><p>[4] Valiant, Leslie G. &quot;Memorization and association on a realistic neural model.&quot; Neural computation (2005)<p>[5] Valiant, Leslie G.
