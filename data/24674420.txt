The tradeoff of power&#x2F;heat for the increased clock speed stopped paying off.  In fairness,  Apple&#x2F;IBM&#x2F;Motorola also learned this lesson with the G5 but they were minor players in the market and were largely ignored.  So it was roughly between 2005 and 2008 when perf&#x2F;watt became a thing. I think public awareness of it had a lot to do with Steve Jobs relentlessly pushing perf&#x2F;watt to help explain the PPC to Intel transition as Apple was just starting its public awareness rocket ride thanks to the iPod taking off.')
