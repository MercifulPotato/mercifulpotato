Item(by='ChrisLomont', descendants=None, kids=[25711070, 25714030, 25728489], score=None, time=1610246079, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25706275, text='Algorithms are not simply math, in the same way mechanical engineering is not simply math.<p>The patents are the result of engineering to create very precise and complicated tools.<p>And if it were simply math, then there would be no way to work around those patents, which plenty of open source codecs have done in various directions.<p>Unfortunately, most open source codecs are not as good at compression, mostly since the patented ones have a massively bigger pool of engineering behind them from dozens of companies banding together their work to develop best of breed solutions.<p>And eventually the patents expire, and the world gets these codecs free to use.')
