Item(by='swiley', descendants=None, kids=[25025535], score=None, time=1604839534, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25025386, text='Something a lot of people don&#x27;t seem to know about spotify is that artists are compensated based on their position in the charts, not based directly on how many times their song was played.<p>So if you only listen to obscure folk music, most (possibly all) of the $10 you&#x27;re paying goes to artists you don&#x27;t listen to. That&#x27;s why some indie artists say they prefer piracy to spotify, it&#x27;s almost the same to them.')
