Item(by='smallnamespace', descendants=None, kids=[25531155], score=None, time=1608839683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25529706, text='If you&#x27;re trying to show raw space RG (50, 50), then when you show (100, 0) and (0, 100) in adjacent pixels, there are exactly 50 * 2 units of light distributed over each pair of pixels, so your eyes will see (50, 50) if the pixels are small&#x2F;far enough.<p>Another way to put this is that dithering works because of physical blending of photons due to an insufficiently sharp eye lens <i>before</i> perceptual mechanisms in the brain.')
