Item(by='Animats', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603699280, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24890988, text='Note that the source is a press release.<p>This is very similar to the Peewee nuclear rocket of the late 1960s. A small version of the better-known Kiwi reactor, that used zirconium carbide coatings like this new plan. Those 1960s projects got far enough along that the next step was flight hardware.<p>These things are certainly buildable, but the risks are a problem. These are upper stage engines. The risk comes from a failure of the booster used to get them clear of the Earth, in which case you have a nuclear reactor re-entering in pieces.')
