Item(by='goto11', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602411131, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24744631, text='Yeah, we shouldn&#x27;t compare code to literary text or poetry, since these texts are not malleable in the way we want code to be.<p>For example Shakespeare often uses obsolete words or words which have changed meaning - but we can&#x27;t replace these with more modern words or phases, since then it would break the meter or rhyme and destroy some of the double meanings.
