I don&#x27;t know if they&#x27;ve narrowed down a major cause.  My pet theory is obesity.<p>The usual recommendation to start screening at 50 is for asymptomatic people with no increased risk.  If you have symptoms, family history of colorectal cancer, or enough other risk factors[0], bring up screening with your doctor.
