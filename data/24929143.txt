Item(by='Normille', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603969017, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24928664, text='One of those annoying words people use when trying to sound smart, but which, if you analyse it, means exactly the opposite of what they want to express.<p>Not half as annoying [mainly because so much less common to hear] as my all time favourite, that so-beloved of Americans phrase <i>&quot;I could care less about...&quot;</i><p>One that, for some reason seems to crop up a lot this side of the point is &quot;mortified&quot;, which means &quot;deeply embarrassed&quot; but which a surprisingly large number of people seem to think means &quot;very annoyed&quot;.<p>I used to have this horrible dragon of boss who would regularly berate us all at meetings and shout that she was &quot;mortified&quot; that this, that and the other hadn&#x27;t been done right. It gave me great pleasure one day to wait for her to finish one of these rants and then quietly say <i>&quot;I don&#x27;t think that word means what you think it means!&quot;</i>')
