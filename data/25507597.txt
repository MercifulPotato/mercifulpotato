Item(by='poletopole', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608652941, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25503038, text='The only book I found helpful in learning functional programming was <i>Lambda calculus and combinators</i> by Hindley. Although you can find it online for free it’s worth buying. What I discovered was that books on Haskell simply weren’t enough to understand the language, you really do need to understand lambda calculus first.')
