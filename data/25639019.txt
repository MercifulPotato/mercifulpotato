Item(by='michaelmcmillan', descendants=None, kids=[25639179], score=None, time=1609798554, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25620411, text='DNS is actually pretty nice for ACL [1]. Just make sure you use it with DNSSEC.<p>Want to check if Tom has access to &#x2F;web&#x2F;mike? Just do a lookup on the user in question with the reversed path you want to check.<p>&quot;Can Tom read &#x2F;web&#x2F;mike?&quot; =&gt; tom.mike.web.server.net<p>Advantages:<p>- Replication to multiple machines is trivial (piggybacking of DNS, after all).<p>- Lookups are fast. Can also be easily cached.<p>- All sane programming languages have built-in DNS resolution in their stdlib out-of-the-box.<p>- Manual override using &#x2F;etc&#x2F;hosts for debugging&#x2F;emergency.<p>1. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;itk.samfundet.no&#x2F;dok&#x2F;ITKACL2" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;itk.samfundet.no&#x2F;dok&#x2F;ITKACL2</a>')
