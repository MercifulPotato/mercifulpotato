Typically (but maybe not always) &quot;static build&quot; on Linux really means &quot;all libraries are statically linked except libc which is dynamically linked&quot;.<p>To make something very portable across Linux distributions, rather than statically linking libc you would dynamically link against an old version of it, as newer versions are always backwards compatible with older versions.')
