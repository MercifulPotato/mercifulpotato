Item(by='munificent', descendants=None, kids=[25338847, 25338818], score=None, time=1607379570, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25338074, text='<i>&gt; why are bicycles nonexistant on post-apocalyptic stories?</i><p>Because most post-apocalyptic media is individualism wish fulfillment porn. In most of those stories, the world has been destroyed because being that way serves to elevate the power and agency of the hero(es). Look at how bad ass they are to be able to thrive even in a world that bad!<p>Mowing down zombies in a Winnebago tricked out to be a rolling prepper bomb shelter is empowering and fun. Squeaking down a deserted highway in a rickety ill-fitting bike, knees akimbo, does not cut quite as dramatic a figure even if it is infintely more realistic.')
