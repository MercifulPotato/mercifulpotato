Item(by='zsellera', descendants=None, kids=[25033291], score=None, time=1604913156, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25032682, text='I&#x27;m kinda into tomatoes: the same variety can result different taste and nutritional value (dry matter content) depending on the growing conditions.<p>I know two greenhouse growers, growing the same variety of tomato on the same rootstock in the same medium. One of them has ~5-5.5% DMC while the other has close to 8% in the mid-spring season.<p>They taste different, but they yield different as well (higher quality means lower yields).')
