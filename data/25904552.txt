Item(by='alexpetralia', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611589800, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25901867, text='This is directionally right but practically wrong.<p>You will be a disliked product manager if you continually tell people what they want to hear, but never deliver on it.<p>Rather, tell (read: persuade) people what they <i>need</i> to hear.
