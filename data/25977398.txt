Item(by='smackay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612084990, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25977250, text='Here on the ground in Portugal I&#x27;d say the government&#x27;s response is apparently competent but it&#x27;s just not effective. Although the country is simply following what others are doing, the population is taking this very seriously. Mask wearing outside is pretty universal except if you&#x27;re exercising, everybody is working from home where possible and the schools are closed (finally). But it&#x27;s not enough. The problem is that family ties are very strong here. Relaxing the rules for 36 hours at Christmas and 24 hours at New Year was absolutely the wrong thing to do and that is why the death toll has doubled in the five weeks since then.')
