Item(by='quotemstr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610489110, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25754332, text='A corporation is an abstraction, an artificial person created to pool resources and do business at scale. It&#x27;s a good, useful abstraction --- but like all abstractions, it&#x27;s leaky, and sometimes the abstraction does more harm than good. Governments are run for the benefit of natural people, not artificial ones, and when the latter behave in ways detrimental to the interests of the former, there&#x27;s nothing wrong with setting rules.')
