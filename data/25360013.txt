Item(by='rsync', descendants=None, kids=[25360164, 25360754, 25365275, 25360718, 25361761, 25365784], score=None, time=1607529735, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25358268, text='We (rsync.net) have several PB of raidz3 deployed all over the world.<p>We use conservatively sized (12-15 drive) vdevs and typically join 3 or 4 of those together to make a pool.<p>I can see getting nervous about <i>raidz2</i> (sort of analogous to &quot;raid6&quot;) after a drive failure ... but losing 4 drives out of 12 in a single <i>raidz3</i> failure cascade is <i>extremely improbable</i>.<p>We all sleep quite well with this arrangement and have since we first migrated from UFS2 to ZFS in 2012.')
