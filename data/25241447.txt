Item(by='jacobolus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606605825, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25240694, text='<i>Nonplussed</i> means “so confused that you are completely paralyzed”. Think deer stopped in headlights.<p>Rough synonyms: confounded, flummoxed, bewildered, perplexed, stunned, shocked, dumbfounded, flabbergasted, thunderstruck.<p>Using <i>nonplussed</i> to mean “unimpressed” or “apathetic” will leave listeners both unimpressed and confused.')
