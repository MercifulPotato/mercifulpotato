In D you can simply go right-to-left. So instead of having<p><pre><code>    char *str[10];\n</code></pre>\nyou&#x27;d have<p><pre><code>    char*[10] str;\n</code></pre>\nwhich when read right-to-left trivially results in &quot;str is a array 10 of pointer to char&quot;')
