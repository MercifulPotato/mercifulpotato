Item(by='jpcooper', descendants=None, kids=[24846224], score=None, time=1603270374, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24845873, text='There are two possibilities in C++. I am not sure what the situation is in Java.<p>It is possible to define a pointer to an object whose contents may not be changed. This is done with ‘const * type object’.<p>If you want a pointer whose value must not change, but do not care about the contents of the pointed to object, you would write ‘type * const object’.')
