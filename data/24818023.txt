Item(by='beckerdo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603030542, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24808049, text='Be careful of the source of your station report. In the US, station report pressures related to aviation are in units of &quot;inches mercury&quot; (&quot;Hg) with the preceding 2 or 3 omitted. Here is a resource: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;aviationweather.gov&#x2F;metar" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;aviationweather.gov&#x2F;metar</a><p>In the grand scheme of weather, it does not make much difference, as the change in pressure in time or location is more important than the units.')
