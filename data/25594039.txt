Item(by='johnsoft', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609431810, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25593825, text='Expanding a bit on conversions: C#&#x27;s `Convert` conflates several different operations.<p>Examples:<p>Convert.ToInt32(String) – This is _parsing_. In Rust, use `parse`.<p>Convert.ToString(Int32) – This is _stringifying_. In Rust, use `to_string`.<p>Convert.ToInt64(Int32) – This is an _infallible conversion_. In Rust, use `into`.<p>Convert.ToInt32(Int64) – This is a _fallible conversion_. In Rust, use `try_into`.<p>In all these cases, Rust gives you more immediate semantic information about the conversion, and in fewer characters too!')
