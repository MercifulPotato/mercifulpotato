Item(by='heresie-dabord', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608812874, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25521787, text='Jupyter is a Rube-Goldbergian nightmare. Python is a memory hog. There are better solutions, to be sure.<p>A simple REPL is all that&#x27;s needed to both do A-type and B-type data exploration. (I won&#x27;t use the term &quot;data scientist&quot;, it&#x27;s an exaggeration in most cases.)<p>Python has a REPL, R has a REPL, Perl has PDL and both a simple REPL (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;viviparous&#x2F;preplish" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;viviparous&#x2F;preplish</a>) and a more complex one (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;metacpan.org&#x2F;pod&#x2F;Reply" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;metacpan.org&#x2F;pod&#x2F;Reply</a>).<p>Jupyter should not be used as an IDE because it is the wrong tool for development. A-type data explorers just want a painless UI and may not care much about the horrible agglutination of incomplete&#x2F;slow&#x2F;broken solutions that Jupyter represents.')
