There are other options like kickstarter, too.<p>I know more than one open source project developer who made a nice program, but it never caught on and the developer wound up discouraged and embittered. A common thread, though, is they simply put it up on a bare bones web site. They actively refused to do any sort of marketing, promotion, outreach, evangelism, read any books on sales and advertising, etc., which pretty much guaranteed failure.<p>BTW, in Apple&#x27;s early days, Steve Jobs was a master at getting attention focused on Apple&#x27;s computers. No successful outfit neglects that.')
