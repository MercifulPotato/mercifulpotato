Item(by='AnHonestComment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609890183, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25652742, text='I learn a lot of languages, but I don’t try to use them at work — just the ideas from them.<p>Example: Knowing Haskell makes it easier to understand Promises in JS&#x2F;TS.<p>I don’t think learning is a Boolean: I suggest learning a lot of things shallowly to keep practiced at learning, to expose yourself to new concepts, and to have fun playing around.<p>I always think of it as you want a wide base to draw from, but focus on deep knowledge of only a few.')
