Item(by='deviation', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604530822, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24993750, text='I don&#x27;t usually log in at work, but I feel this needs to be said since there&#x27;s a few people in my life struggling with this.<p>Psilocybin works great, and almost everyone I know with depression can attest to that- but without changing the circumstances that cause the depression it will likely always return.<p>Anecdotal example: \nMy sister is in disability care and is routinely abused by patients with severe disabilities (to no fault of their own) and she will slip into a severe depression that usually lasts a long period of time. Tripping helps her substantially deal with this, but psilocybin is for treating the effect and not for fixing the cause... If you kick a dead horse it (probably) won&#x27;t wake up.')
