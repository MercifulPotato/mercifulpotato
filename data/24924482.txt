In Maine and Nebraska, it is partially by congressional district.<p>Gerrymandering had no impact on Trump&#x27;s election. He was elected because he got the right states to pull out an Electoral College majority despite losing the national popular election.<p>Gerrymandering&#x27;s impact is on the US House of Representatives and state legislatures. Most executive offices are elected by statewide popular vote.')
