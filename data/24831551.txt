Item(by='hcrisp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603145074, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24831418, text='If you like that kind of thing, also check out &quot;Revising Business Prose&quot; by Lanham.  Avoid passive-voice sentences with long prepositional phrases, and instead use action verbs.  For example:  <i>“The history of the new regulatory provisions is that there is generally an immediate resistance to them.”</i> becomes <i>“People usually resist new regulations.”</i>')
