Item(by='shmerl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603652896, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24888942, text='Fair use should simply be a clarification of cases when copyright shouldn&#x27;t apply in the first place for those who think it might apply there.<p>To put it differently, when copyright starts being harmful to the public, fair use is a more explicit way to rein it in and keep things as they should be.<p>In theory, if the law could be defined with 100% clarity, you wouldn&#x27;t need fair use as an additional tool to prevent abuse. But things are rarely that clear.')
