If you have a continuous integration pipeline that checks out projects from scratch (as it should), every build fetches all dependencies, transitively.<p>Even ignoring download costs, a local cache (one of the functions of an artifactory) helps speed up those downloads and,with it, your builds. It probably also helps against getting blacklisted by code repositories.<p>An artifactory also automatically backs up any libraries you use. That protects against them disappearing from the internet.')
