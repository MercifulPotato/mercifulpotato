Item(by='SpicyLemonZest', descendants=None, kids=[24982507], score=None, time=1604421218, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24981055, text='The key insight you&#x27;re missing is that the concepts of emotional awareness and comfort are themselves &quot;culture fit&quot; standards. Different cultural groups have wildly different perspectives on how emotional awareness is properly expressed or which kinds of comfort people are entitled to. To pick a hopefully non-controversial example, there are some people who are most comfortable when they&#x27;re allowed to have pink hair, and others who feel that showing up to work in pink hair reflects a lack of emotional awareness.')
