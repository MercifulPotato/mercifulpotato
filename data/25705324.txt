Item(by='euthymiclabs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610229788, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25701446, text='Note that &quot;antidepressant&quot; is largely a marketing term. The drug in particular is fluvoxamine, a potent selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI). This doesn&#x27;t mean that other antidepressants will work (if fluvoxamine really does work in larger trials).<p>Believe it or not, fluvoxamine isn&#x27;t even approved by the  FDA for depression. It probably works, but it&#x27;s only labeled for OCD (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.accessdata.fda.gov&#x2F;drugsatfda_docs&#x2F;label&#x2F;2017&#x2F;021519s009lbl.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.accessdata.fda.gov&#x2F;drugsatfda_docs&#x2F;label&#x2F;2017&#x2F;02...</a>). Usually, we&#x27;ll preserve it for more refractory cases of OCD since it tends to have more side effects and interactions than other SSRIs.')
