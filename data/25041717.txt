Item(by='tenebrisalietum', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604968910, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25039045, text='Thought experiment.<p>An entity is conscious if:<p>- it has external inputs and a facility for creating a symbolized account of them,<p>- it has a facility that stores these accounts,<p>- it can order those accounts roughly by time,<p>- it can place or relate a symbol representing itself in these accounts.<p>The quality of consciousness varies widely, a program that has an object that references itself and can do the above things is very technically conscious but likely nowhere near to the depth of a human being.')
