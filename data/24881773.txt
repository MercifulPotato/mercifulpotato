So you&#x27;ll lose one threshold voltage, not two. (You can see these bootstrap load capacitors in the die photo near the pads.)<p>Inconveniently, the 8008 datasheet doesn&#x27;t specify how low the low outputs are; it just says they are at most 0.4 volts.')
