(I used firefox as an example but you can change it)<p><pre><code>  busctl call --user org.gnome.Shell &#x2F;org&#x2F;gnome&#x2F;Shell \\\n    org.gnome.Shell Eval s \\\n    &#x27;global.get_window_actors().forEach(w =&gt;\n       w.meta_window.wm_class == &quot;firefox&quot;\n       &amp;&amp; w.meta_window.delete(global.get_current_time()))&#x27;</code></pre>')
