Item(by='Gaelan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606141734, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25185940, text='musl is a Linux libc, so you can&#x27;t use in on any other OS (because it wouldn&#x27;t know how to send syscalls to the kernel).<p>macOS handles libc a little differently from Linux; whereas the Linux kernel publishes its syscall API, and allows programs to make syscalls however they want (e.g. with glibc or musl, or with go, which does syscalls itself), macOS&#x27;s syscall API is subject to change at any time, and the only (supported) way to make syscalls is to dynamically link to the system libc.')
