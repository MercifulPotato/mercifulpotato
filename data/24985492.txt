Item(by='jonny383', descendants=None, kids=[24985818, 24985891, 24985746], score=None, time=1604448664, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979258, text='Termux has saved one of our projects literally tens of thousands of dollars in SMS fees from someone like Twilio.<p>We have a few areas where non urgent SMS reminders need to be sent to users. We literally just use a couple of cheap ($100) Android phones with termux on an unlimited message plan with a local provider (who approved us for commercial usage).<p>The actual process is a glorified bash script that pulls API jobs down with curl and then uses termux-sms to actually send the message.')
