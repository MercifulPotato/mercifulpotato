Item(by='megous', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604583430, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24997445, text='It&#x27;s not that hard in Arch Linux. You get the PKGBUILD for original package, and run makepkg once with an option to just prepare the src&#x2F;, then you can modify it and then you run makepkg with a flag to skip the src&#x2F; preparation and finish the build.<p>Just to change the build&#x2F;configure flags, you can simply edit the PKGBUILD.<p>Nothing fancy, I&#x27;ve been building firefox this way to disable signing requirements for extensions, until I gave up on firefox extensions completely, because of the general unfriendliness to developers with the signing requirements that can&#x27;t be disabled without wasting a shitton of electricity.')
