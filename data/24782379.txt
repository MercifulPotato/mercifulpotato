Item(by='umanwizard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602714135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24782071, text='Courts often need to employ _chains_ of interpreters: for example, one to translate from English to Spanish, one to translate from Spanish to one Mayan language, and one to translate from that Mayan language to a different, more obscure Mayan language spoken by a monolingual defendant.[1]<p>Chinese and Swahili would give them no trouble at all.<p>1: Real example from my friend who represented such a person in an immigration hearing.')
