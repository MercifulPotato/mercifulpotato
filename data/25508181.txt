Item(by='Thorrez', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608655466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25504525, text='For 2, if it&#x27;s a password that the user chose, the site should never email it, because the user likely reused that password across many sites, and someone who snoops on the user&#x27;s email (say a housemate) can get the password to a ton of sites.<p>If it&#x27;s a password generated by the site, then it&#x27;s actually fine to email it. Although you likely don&#x27;t want it too early in the email that it would show up in a phone notification or in a body summary in gmail.')
