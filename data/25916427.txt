Item(by='dralley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611673137, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25915478, text='Free software is doing well, yes. Businesses built-around free software, not so much. The list of successful companies based on fully FOSS software can practically be counted on  one hand. Most of the time, the product may be very successful, but the company is really struggling.<p>A lot of OSS development is now done by companies who don&#x27;t care that much about the software, because their revenue is totally independent.  Netflix, Uber, Microsoft, etc.<p>Or it&#x27;s open-core like Redis and Gitlab, with paid proprietary bits. Or &quot;open source&quot; with anti-service-provider licenses like MongoDB, Elastic, Redis.')
