Item(by='ryathal', descendants=None, kids=[25455489], score=None, time=1608211200, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446021, text='Financing cars is a poverty trap. There are cars in the 1-2k range that work perfectly fine and will reliably work with minimal maintenance, and there are sub 1k cars that run ok enough that you can use as a bridge to save enough for a slightly better car.<p>Financing a car is a double whammy because you pay interest and have a deprecating asset. At the low end cars really don&#x27;t deprecate much. The act of not having a car payment is a great first step to building wealth.')
