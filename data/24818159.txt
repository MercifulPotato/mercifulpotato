Item(by='michaelt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603031962, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24816684, text='Applying a vastly simplified model, every job has a salary range possible:<p>From the company&#x27;s side: At most the amount of money you make the company, and at most the cost of hiring a replacement who could do the same job equally well.<p>From your side: At least the second-best job offer you&#x27;ve received.<p>Raising productivity is often a <i>prerequisite</i> for raising salaries - but you <i>also</i> need a tough hiring market, if you want the business to give the money to the workers instead of the bosses, investors or customers.')
