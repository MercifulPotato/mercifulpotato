Item(by='bacon_blood', descendants=None, kids=[25410322, 25410696], score=None, time=1607893578, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25410159, text='Both x86 and arm clang can output for the other architecture, or universal.<p>Pass `-arch arm64` not `-march`<p>You can also `clang -arch x86_64 -arch arm64` to build for both at once.<p>You can even go a step further and run your clang as native from bazel, via `arch -arm64 clang`.<p>Put it all together and you have: `arch -arm64 clang -arch x86_64 -arch arm64`.<p>It may seem like I&#x27;m joking but I&#x27;m not.')
