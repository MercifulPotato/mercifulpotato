They held weekly all-hands Q&amp;A sessions with Larry and Sergey, built an internal tool for sharing company-related image memes (including those critical of leadership), and hosted high-traffic internal mailing lists on a wide range of political and non-work related subjects.<p>Some of this has been curtailed in recent years, as leaks to the press increased and activist employees became increasingly critical of management.')
