Item(by='Veen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605691872, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25133286, text='With some of my clients, I go even further. Where possible, I figure out what the possible answers to my question might be and write them in a  concise numbered list (with a “something else” option at the end”). Ideally, the response is a number with a little context. At the very least, it prompts a response that challenges my assumptions, which is useful too.<p>I started doing this because I received so many short and incomprehensible or long and incomprehensible responses that rarely included the information I asked for.')
