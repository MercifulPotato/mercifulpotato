These aren’t artisan goods that someone can easily try offering on a whim, these magnificently complicated machines are only affordable because of economies of scale&#x2F;mass manufacturing. You can probably find someone to custom build what you want, but it’s not going to be anywhere near cost competitive with a standard model. Maybe you can get close by using stock parts from other cars.<p>Or maybe this is a market opportunity, and you should set up a manufacturing line for stripped down jeep-like vehicles.<p>Probably won’t be road legal, though, because they’re horrifically unsafe in a collision compared to modern cars.')
