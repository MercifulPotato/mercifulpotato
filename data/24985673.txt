Item(by='toomanyrichies', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604450543, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24985499, text='I think a middle ground would be that it&#x27;s fine to <i>start</i> with a belief about how something <i>might</i> work (also known as a hypothesis), after all that&#x27;s the first step in the Scientific Method.  But crafting a hypothesis without a subsequent experiment to prove or disprove it is just wasted breath.  As the adage goes, claims that are made without evidence can be dismissed without evidence.')
