Think of them as analogous to standard blues licks and riffs.<p>Also, if you are not a native speaker of Indian languages, especially with instrumental music you aren&#x27;t hearing the words to the songs. A native listener would however recognize the fixed compositions and themes because they often know words that go along with them.
