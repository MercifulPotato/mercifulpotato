The word &quot;safe&quot; is used by almost everyone to mean a feeling they get. Something is safe to them if it feels safe. Flying is &quot;safe&quot; if you don&#x27;t fear flying. It is not if one is afraid of getting on an airplane. Is driving a car &quot;safe&quot;? It is if you don&#x27;t feel scared when driving in a car. Any other use of the word would need to have some statistical evaluation of the situation. Saying <i></i>the risks of the treatment are outweighed by the benefit<i></i> is a great rational way of saying drug is safe enough to use, but most people don&#x27;t use the word safe in that way.<p>To me a chainsaw is a safe tool to use. Dangerous, but safe. To others it is not. Is a chainsaw a &quot;safe&quot; tool?')
