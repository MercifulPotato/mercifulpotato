Item(by='trykondev', descendants=None, kids=[25467194], score=None, time=1608299806, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25466759, text='The parent comment is correct -- &quot;offering a pawn&quot; means allowing the other player to capture a pawn without yourself having an immediate way to regain it, usually banking on some kind of other piece activity as compensation for the material.<p>On move 9 it is a simple exchange of pawns and since the material remains equal after the resulting sequence of exchanges we would not refer to that as offering a pawn.')
