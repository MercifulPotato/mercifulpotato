Item(by='dmayle', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603647010, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887521, text='More and more, when looking for (open source) software solutions to adopt for my startup, Go as a development language is a deciding factor.<p>Go-based software is smaller, faster, easier to deploy, easier to update, easier to contribute to.<p>I find that the installation footprint of a Go-based service is usually in the 10s of megabytes or less, where the installation footprint of pretty much anything else starts in the hundreds of megabytes.<p>In the classic world of one or two servers for a small company, sure, the disk space is meaningless, but with containerized workloads, small images are great for quick deploy, and quick iteration.')
