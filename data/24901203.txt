Item(by='DoingIsLearning', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603745277, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24900382, text='This is easier said than done, specially in more junior positions when you don&#x27;t carry that much weight.<p>As an anecdotal example I remember some years back I was interviewing with Philips Respironics for what would have been my 2nd job out of university.<p>On my 1st round interview (HR call) I was asked about my current salary, I chose the polite non-answer of &quot;I don&#x27;t feel it would be respectful for my current colleagues to disclose my salary or package conditions&quot;.<p>The interviewer immediately went into passive agressive mode and told me that she would not be able to progress the application forward without an answer to that question. I ended up moving to a better role but that was a valuable lesson.<p>I agree with the points made but this only really works once you sort of build a name for yourself and you carry some weight in the value that you bring.')
