We should all just give up and pick the least bad centralized service.<p>With that thinking we would all be using AOL.<p>Making a robust flexible protocol that can support a bunch of different client and service implementations is hard, but that is how we ended up avoiding email and web browsing being controlled by a single entity.<p>Matrix is solving the hard problem of providing the core functionality of tools like Slack and Whatsapp without sacrificing user freedom or asking you to trust any one entity.<p>This is what ethical engineering looks like, and I don&#x27;t mind tolerating occasional growing pains in exchange for freedom.')
