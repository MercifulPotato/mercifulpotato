Item(by='CapriciousCptl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610656504, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25780812, text='Another way to look at wealth is as total spending power between now and eternity. Taken that way, it doesn&#x27;t matter as much if your wealth is tied up in unsaleable stock-- stock afterall represents your claim on the discounted future earnings of a company, so those earnings will be available given enough time.<p>Estimating the true future earnings of businesses, and the amount available to current shareholders of course, isn&#x27;t easy. But taking the stock market&#x27;s approximation of that <i>intrinsic value</i> is quick and dirty way to get it. Of course, the caveat is that stocks aren&#x27;t always efficiently priced.')
