As the train will get closer to the less rural place, it will start running under the wire. At the rural end of the line there can be a dedicated charging station for layovers. This way, there are plenty of opportunities to charge the trains.<p>In Germany, a very high percentage of Diesel Services could be replaced by trains having about 50km of autonomy - and for the rest it would be possible to fill the gaps using only a relatively small amount of electrification.')
