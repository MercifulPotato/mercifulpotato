If software has been around for a long time, people can gather experience about how to use it. Longstanding software in the hands of someone very familiar with it, will use it like a simple, comfortable, reliable tool. That has huge value to the individuals who use it.<p>They know they are blind to potentially better products, but they no longer have the time to gather the experience and confidence in the new products.')
