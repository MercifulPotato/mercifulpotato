Item(by='alistaira', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607438083, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25344915, text='If a person has no symptoms, I think it is reasonable to say they are &quot;asymptomatic&quot;.<p>There is absolutely a difference between &#x27;yet to have&#x27; and &#x27;never will have&#x27; but I think it is potentially confusing to attempt to delineate between asymptomatic and &quot;pre-symptomatic&quot;.<p>The main concern with messaging that causes confusion in this regard is that a superficial reading of &quot;asymptomatic doesn&#x27;t spread virus&quot; would imply that if you aren&#x27;t symptomatic, you aren&#x27;t a danger.')
