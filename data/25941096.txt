As a result it may clash with other efforts underway, or our future roadmap. Also, not having discussed it prior to the PR increases the suspicion that you have not followed our coding guidelines. Consequently this PR is closed. Please propose the change on the mailing list and take part in the discussion before revising this patch and resubmitting the PR.&quot;<p>Because of this I think an auto-close is fine.  I also do think Github should allow disabling the PR tab.')
