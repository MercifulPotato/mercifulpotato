Item(by='yokaze', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611219026, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25856775, text='I wonder if a law enforcing more transparency couldn&#x27;t help here.<p>It is in the interest of the music labels and the streaming companies to maximise their profit, which means minimise the pay-out to the artists.<p>Customers have to be very motivated to be able to make an informed decision. And most often they probably aren&#x27;t really motivated and happily chose the cheaper option.<p>But I have hope, if the impact of the customers choice on their favourite artists is directly visible, it will create more pressure to increase the payout.<p>E.g. each time you listen the song on A, the artists gets X, if you buy the song the artists Y.')
