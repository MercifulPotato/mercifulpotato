Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=[24981321], score=None, time=1604409840, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979023, text='Although true, that seems moot because <i>all</i> tax on business (whether VAT or not) is, one way or another, passed along to the end customer (or not passed on according to business pricing decisions).<p>No matter which company in the supply chain pays it!<p>With VAT, it&#x27;s raised prices and shown on the receipt.<p>With other business taxes, it&#x27;s raised prices and not shown on the receipt.<p>Customer pays either way.<p>Of course, competition and other factors also govern prices.  It&#x27;s not a given that changing business taxes always causes a business to change its prices by a related amount.<p>But that is the same for VAT and other business taxes too.')
