Item(by='currymj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609880466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25648186, text='i tend to think that the approach here described as &quot;incompetent&quot; isn&#x27;t necessarily bad as long as you maintain honesty about it.<p>for instance, it might be more effective to just donate money but there&#x27;s nothing wrong with wanting to volunteer hands on, as long as you don&#x27;t fool yourself.<p>of course from a very, very strict utilitarian perspective, making a somewhat less effective choice is in fact morally evil. but I don&#x27;t think that viewpoint actually holds up to scrutiny -- few people would agree that volunteering at a food bank rather than donating money is morally equivalent to taking food away from hungry children.')
