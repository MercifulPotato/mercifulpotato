Item(by='aminozuur', descendants=None, kids=[25241431], score=None, time=1606604265, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25239278, text='In a nutshell, &#x27;taarof&#x27; is the social code whereby people fight for the lowest rank, as to show respect for the other person. It affects everything from the seat you take when entering a friends house, to how much you pay (you fight to pay the full bill, while your friend insists on him paying).<p>As a Persian aspie I feel this wastes time, and I prefer the blunt and direct communication style of the Dutch.')
