Item(by='Doctor_Fegg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609455321, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25597756, text='Anecdata:<p>We recently instituted a 20mph limit across our town (I’m the chair of the town council and also a keen cyclist). We do regular speed surveys.<p>Not everyone sticks to 20mph. But no one drives above 30mph any more. When we had a 30mph limit, we’d regularly get people driving up to 40mph.<p>So it’s not perfect, but it does make a difference, here at least.')
