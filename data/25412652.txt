Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607910988, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405875, text='Every pre-, post-, and invariant condition violation represents a programming error, at an indeterminate point in the past, rendering the state of the computation unknown, and likely undefined, and definitely unrecoverable.<p>An exception, by definition, is to enable recovery from externally imposed events in a well-defined program state.<p>The only useful, meaningful response to discovering a contract violation is termination. Throwing an exception is the worst imaginable response.')
