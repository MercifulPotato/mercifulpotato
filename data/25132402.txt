And if you look at the historic willingness of the Tories to run ethnic and female candidates for very important roles, it’s considerably better than Labour: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Great_Offices_of_State" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Great_Offices_of_State</a><p>I guess all I’m saying is that it’s worth factoring in proximity to power rather than just raw numbers')
