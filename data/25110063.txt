Item(by='totalZero', descendants=None, kids=[25110071], score=None, time=1605521314, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25109928, text='Opt-out is not appropriate for privacy concerns, IMO, for two reasons.<p>1. Opt-out is not necessarily sticky. I don&#x27;t have confidence that when I opt out, my preference will get rolled forward every time there&#x27;s an update to the way the feature works. I have noticed this in the past with Facebook and Google.<p>2. What&#x27;s good for the goose is good for the gander. If Apple needs to implement opt-out to placate sophisticated users who actually understand what the problem is, then what about the poor saps who don&#x27;t really know why this is a bad thing in the first place?<p>If this is truly a &quot;feature,&quot; let people decide for themselves at time of device setup whether they want to opt in.')
