Item(by='dagw', descendants=None, kids=[25914512, 25914241], score=None, time=1611651124, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25913562, text='<i>Say, your program is an editor of png files</i><p>Say you are writing a program to edit images that can potentially read lots of different formats. However most people don&#x27;t need support for .sgi or .ecw files and won&#x27;t have the libraries to read those formats installed. Your program is still perfectly usable without those libraries and so you should still be able to compile and run it, just without support for those formats.')
