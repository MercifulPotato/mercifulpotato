Item(by='fennecfoxen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601687831, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24666583, text='There is very much ideology around these issues. One particularly relevant article of ideology suggests that people who are not trans, and whose names would not ordinarily cause confusion, ought nevertheless adopt a practice of specifying pronouns, in service of the idea that those who are trans (or nonbinary) might not stand out when they do so.<p>While I am agree that there are human rights issues involved in the issues generally, I would say that is <i>not</i> specifically a human right to have others around you adopt this specific practice to support such a person as he, she, or they assert an identity.')
