Item(by='hombre_fatal', descendants=None, kids=[24819230, 24818838], score=None, time=1603034377, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24817952, text='The article makes it sound like the alternative to white noise is luxurious, dead silence.<p>But, especially in the city, the alternative is usually just patches of semi-silence interrupted by loud or sharp things. Dogs barking, a police siren, the neighbor coming home at 3am, your partner flushing the toilet, inexplicable noises that start and then stop before you realize why you&#x27;re up.<p>I listen to white noise like a fan because it smooths over those noises. I&#x27;d pick dead silence if I could.')
