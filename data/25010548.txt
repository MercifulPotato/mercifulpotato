Item(by='wegs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604692278, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25006297, text='Yes, and no. Management teams have different specialties. You can&#x27;t be an expert at everything. A big part of doing management well is understanding what to do yourself, what to delegate internally, and what to outsource.<p>There are plenty of companies which didn&#x27;t do so well when the management team went from a domain-specific background (e.g. having an electronic engineering run an IC firm) to people with an MBA background.<p>Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, Jeff Bezos, and many other founders were exceptionally competent managers, but at the time, they had never run a major firm before. Everyone has their first major restructuring at some point.')
