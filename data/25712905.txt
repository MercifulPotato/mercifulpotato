Item(by='devbug', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610271884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25707552, text='Windows bundles software decoders (for H.264 and H.265) that are available through MFT and DXVA.[1][2] Chromium leverages both already.[3] It&#x27;s also a low effort way to support hardware-accelerated decoding as vendors (Intel, AMD, NVIDIA) register MFTs that wrap their propriety SDKs (Quick Sync, AMF, NVDEC).<p>These provided MFTs have to meet certain minimum requirements for certification by Microsoft, which amounts to &quot;good enough.&quot; Results vary in practice and can be incredibly frustrating (especially encode).
