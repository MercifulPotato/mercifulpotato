And you have to go trough the firewall...<p>I prefer something you can generate yourself, like encryption keys. That&#x27;s the approach taken by yggdrasil (and cjdns before): generate an encryption key, map the public part to an IP address (there&#x27;s almost enough bits in v6). Plus, it can easily be end-to-end encrypted.<p>Another plus is that you can generate as many as desired.<p>As for the protocol, Matrix is experimenting a bit with going p2p.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yggdrasil-network.github.io&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yggdrasil-network.github.io&#x2F;</a>')
