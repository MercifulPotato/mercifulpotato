Item(by='royjacobs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601640856, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24661412, text='Because this will break in situations where you have multiple consumers or if there&#x27;s any sort of redelivery&#x2F;failure scenario.<p>Ordering only works if there&#x27;s a single-threaded consumer somewhere that processes exactly one message at a time and blocks processing as soon as there is an error of any kind. The amount of scenarios that this works for is probably very small.<p>The footgun is that people don&#x27;t realize the above constraints and assume that ordering will magically fix a lot of ordering issues (some more examples of this in the rest of this comments section).')
