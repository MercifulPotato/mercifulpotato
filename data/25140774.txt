Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=[25140836], score=None, time=1605725657, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25140007, text='&#x27;Market failure&#x27; is a term of art, and it&#x27;s generally best to avoid redefining terms of art unless there&#x27;s a very compelling reason to do so.<p>Failing to meet people&#x27;s needs is definitely a problem, but not a uniquely market-driven one. If there is a specific phenomenon which causes markets to fail in the delivery of socially desirable goods, it would be good to come up with a specific description and corresponding term of art.')
