This is because the owner not doing something else with their time (called opportunity cost) is accounted for as a cost. What you&#x27;re describing would be called &quot;accounting profit&quot; instead of &quot;economic profit&quot; by an economist to specify it only takes into account costs on the books, not opportunity costs.<p>Edit: See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Zero-profit_condition" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Zero-profit_condition</a>')
