Item(by='amalter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611773784, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931684, text='I think you&#x27;re arguing for a market with only direct investments and no secondary derivatives. If that was all the equity market was, we could be in the same zip code.<p>However, we live in this hyper-securitized world, where every part of the economic fabric has bets for and against, with insurance, leverage and information asymmetry baked in.<p>The only way to &quot;discover&quot; price is to provide instruments that provide &quot;gravity&quot; for both upward and downward price movements. The lack of supply on it&#x27;s own is not enough, especially when malicious actors are pushing on the supply and demand levers.')
