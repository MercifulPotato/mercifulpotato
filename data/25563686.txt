Item(by='comex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609192504, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25562599, text='Accelerate.framework is a shared library distributed with the OS.  Like most other system libraries, it’s not available as a static library, and though you could theoretically ship a copy of it in an app bundle, you’re not supposed to.  Instead the ABI is kept backwards-compatible.<p>There’s no publicly available <i>compiler</i>, proprietary or not, that produces binaries that use the instructions.')
