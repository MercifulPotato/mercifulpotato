Do you stay focused on one subject, iterating multiple times over the result, in order to achieve incremental improvements? Do you churn out mindless crap just for the sake of it?<p>It&#x27;s a rather fuzzy and abstract philosophical notion and any attempts for an absolute framing of &quot;the one single truth&quot; are flawed.')
