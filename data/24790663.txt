This is leverage, the opposite of hedging.<p>It has always seemed strange to me that Wallstreet has convinced American&#x27;s to treat their retirement as leverage rather than a hedge. If the market is doing fantastic, then it&#x27;s not as important to have savings, and if the market is doing poorly it&#x27;s important have other means of economic stability.')
