Item(by='leadingthenet', descendants=None, kids=[24959589], score=None, time=1604211640, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24957553, text='Because the platform conventions are different.<p>Think of it this way: if you have a system full of apps that conform to platform conventions, then you only need to learn the platform conventions and you’re suddenly more or less an expert in every new app you encounter.<p>If you need to learn every. single. apps. stupid rules and UI all over again, then sure, you can transfer skills in that one app over to another platform, but that’s it. You want an email app? Good luck learning every keyboard shortcut and ridiculous UI decision all over again.<p>Let’s just say this paradigm shift has not been driven by people with OCD (or good taste, for that matter).')
