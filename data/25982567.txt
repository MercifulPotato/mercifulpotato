It&#x27;s a bit confusing to say federation does imply decentralization point blank.<p>For a long time, federated architecture was seen as special case of decentralized, for example in designing stuff for internal use. It still makes sense in that context.<p>In the context of larger external facing systems, I&#x27;ve found it useful to think of federated as &quot;partly decentralized&quot;, and &quot;decentralized&quot; is short-hand for &quot;fully decentralized&quot;.')
