Item(by='stan_rogers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605428742, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25097995, text='Sines and cosines <i>really are</i> fundamental. Pass a triangular, sawtooth or square wave through a narrow-band bandpass filter (an actual physical filter) centred on the fundamental frequency and you&#x27;ll get a sine wave out the other end. Anything that <i>isn&#x27;t</i> sinusoidal has components that aren&#x27;t at the fundamental frequency.')
