Item(by='aksss', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606796034, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25261764, text='I&#x27;m far more risk tolerant than that. &quot;Safe&quot; is a watchword for me that reflexively makes me doubt the argument. I have no problem with the idea of disclaimers, ratings, whatever, but give adults a choice to engage the open information dream that the Internet was&#x2F;is.<p>At least when the government tries to police something we can grab onto it, debate it as a matter of policy, and there&#x27;s a process to change that policy. With private companies trying to do the work of the government (if that really is the case, and whatever their reasons - fear of formal regulation, etc), it&#x27;s far harder to manage. I&#x27;d rather have a clear conversation about the government&#x27;s ability to censor speech than have a few oligarchs working as shadow censors on behalf of the government.')
