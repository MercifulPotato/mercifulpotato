Item(by='vkou', descendants=None, kids=[24770556, 24770490, 24770438], score=None, time=1602623619, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770321, text='You can fight for what you want more effectively when you fight collectively.<p>There also seems to be a popular misconception that unions prevent you from negotiating for yourself. Unions set salary and benefit <i>floors</i>, not ceilings. If you think you are a superstar negotiator, you&#x27;re free to demand a higher wage than that floor.<p>Actors and football players have unions, yet also have an incredible wage disparity between the top and bottom end of the pay scale.')
