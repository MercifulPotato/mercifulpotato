Item(by='kgwgk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606678965, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25247233, text='&gt; If someone says they&#x27;re &quot;37% sure&quot; tomorrow will rain, what does that mean exactly?<p>When someone says they&#x27;re &quot;37% sure&quot; tomorrow will rain they mean that they assign the same probability to &quot;tomorrow will rain&quot; that they do to &quot;if you throw three dice you&#x27;ll get 9 or less&quot; or &quot;if you threw three dice you got 9 or less&quot; (in the second case the event is either true or false, their probability assignment is just their best guess with the information they have).')
