Maybe this depends on whether you think renewables will become cheaper than coal&#x2F;gas, and maybe you&#x27;re pessimistic about that. I&#x27;m not.<p>Robust because the phenomenon can begin detached from the energy grid by profit-seeking entities with capital to invest.
