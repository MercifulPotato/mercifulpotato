But this paper on [1] <i>&quot;Ultrasonic Device Tracking&quot;</i>, has convinced me that the technology is definitely possible.<p>Apparently it&#x27;s known as ultrasonic malware and is a type of side-channel attack to leak information to&#x2F;from devices. So high-frequency audio is transmitted to devices that are <i>listening</i> and can even respond.<p>The scary thing is that researchers have already found this type of software embedded into [2] many applications.<p>Now this isn&#x27;t the same as listening to &quot;human conversations&quot;, but it&#x27;s not hard to believe that some apps are waiting for any audio and just sending it to servers somewhere...<p>What apps do you have installed on your phone? Any games?
