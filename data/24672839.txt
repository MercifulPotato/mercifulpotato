Item(by='Mathnerd314', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601740671, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24672654, text='There&#x27;s another step at the beginning: after you have a problem, you search for similar projects that might have already solved your problem.<p>And in fact this step seems to generate most of the bug reports - you get &quot;please implement feature X&quot; and &quot;does this have feature Y?&quot; and so on, from people who have used your software for all of 5 minutes (or not at all).<p>It&#x27;s only after a lot of time is wasted that a new project is started. Alternatively, it could be that the people who try to use&#x2F;reuse software and the people who write new software are completely separate, so that no amount of bug tracker work will lead to new source contributions.')
