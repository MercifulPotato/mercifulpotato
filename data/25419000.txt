Item(by='cesarb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607961539, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25418820, text='If that codebase is older than C++0x, many of these std::shared_ptr could be taking the place of std::unique_ptr (which didn&#x27;t exist back then; std::auto_ptr was a footgun). If you&#x27;re in the mood for a refactoring, you could take a look at each one and see whether then can be replaced by std::unique_ptr, or whether they really have shared ownership semantics (which is what std::shared_ptr should be used for).')
