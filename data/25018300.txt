Item(by='Rizz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604778283, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25006895, text='In European law it specifically states:<p><pre><code>  Technological measures shall be deemed &#x27;effective&#x27; where the use of a protected work or other subject matter is controlled by the rights holders through application of an access control or protection process, such as encryption, scrambling or other transformation of the work or other subject-matter or a copy control mechanism, which achieves the protection objective.\n</code></pre>\nThere is no provision for effectiveness in any other sense than what I claimed.')
