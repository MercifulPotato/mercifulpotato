Item(by='choeger', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609409274, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25591252, text='Think about what growth means: You do something today that you did yesterday and you do it better now than you did then. And generally, you do it better than the person that did it before you. You either do it faster, or better, or cheaper.<p>Whatever it is you do, the learning effect is a human constant. To say there is no growth is to say humanity does not learn anymore or that something prevents us from applying what we learnt.')
