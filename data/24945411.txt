In our 1st KS we definitely did significant discounts. Thankfully we remained profitable, but what we didn&#x27;t really considered well enough was the amount of money you need to build inventory AFTER the campaign.<p>Rule of thumb now: 3x your production cost. 1x for manufacturing, 1x for various expenses including discounts, 1x for next batch.')
