I aim to trade rarely and discuss trading even less, so I have simply picked out new issues that have a remotely credible whitepaper, placed a bet, and waited. The ones that pan out become the seed for the next round a few years later. My biggest worry is simply in losing the wallets or fatfingering a trade.<p>The industry as a whole doesn&#x27;t have to be showing profits or cashflow so long as capital keeps coming in -- and it does, because it&#x27;s sufficiently disruptive to finance that everyone looks for an angle, and because it&#x27;s very easy to create new tokens, the money is being made, in some sense, simply by putting out the hat and asking: &quot;is this idea good?&quot;')
