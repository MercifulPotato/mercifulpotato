Item(by='dTal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606869844, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25271564, text='If you want more technically accurate terms, you might call raytracers and polygon-pushers &quot;image space&quot; and &quot;geometric space&quot; renderers, respectively.<p>Worth noting that neither method is particularly physically based, and really just boils down to whether you approach the rasterization task &quot;forwards&quot; or &quot;backwards&quot;. Which method ends up faster is essentially a function of how much geometry and how many pixels you have.')
