Item(by='pmontra', descendants=None, kids=[25814680], score=None, time=1610913521, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25812815, text='You shouldn&#x27;t share one single company account across the team. Everybody should have their own account, of course on the company domain, and use that one.<p>Advantages<p>1. You know who does what.<p>2. If Google bans an account the others keep working.<p>3. Permissions per employee, because not everyone need to to everything.<p>4. If an account is compromised, you ban the account.<p>5. When an employee leaves, you ban the account instead of changing the password.<p>6. N people sharing a common password on an account nobody has particular responsibility for, ouch.')
