Item(by='jagged-chisel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605278866, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25082069, text='Technically: one must have the private key to perform decryption. There are many schemes that attempt to protect the original key and derive a decryption key, or do various other things to prevent disclosure of the private side of a key pair.<p>Practically: the point is not to &quot;secure&quot; anything, but to make it obvious that any infringement is due to &quot;circumventing a protection device.&quot; Circumvention is also not allowed under DMCA, because it was obvious to the authors of the law that to view content one must indeed decrypt it.')
