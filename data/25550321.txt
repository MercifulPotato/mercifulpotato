Item(by='dempseye', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609068509, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25550195, text='I never noticed this, but after thinking about it for a few minutes it seems that in standard usage there is a difference between attributive adjectives and predicative adjectives.<p>As an attributive adjective, &quot;foot&quot; is indeed correct, e.g. &quot;a 130 foot yacht.&quot;<p>As a predicative adjective, &quot;feet&quot; is correct, e.g. &quot;the yacht was 130 feet long.&quot;<p>Occasionally, in certain regional variations, you may hear &quot;foot&quot; used in the second case: &quot;the yacht was 130 foot long.&quot;')
