However, using ethics to try to guide behavior is a waste of time. Basically, every ethical system has axioms. By changing your axioms, you can get your ethical system to justify or condemn any behavior you want.<p>So people end up doing the same thing, they just have more &quot;logical&quot; explanations of why their behavior is justified.')
