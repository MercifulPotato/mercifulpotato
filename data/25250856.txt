Item(by='throwaway201103', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606712088, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25249410, text='Your mention of OneDrive made me remember that pass creates a cleartext copy of your password file, while you are editing it.<p>pass will try to create the file in &#x2F;dev&#x2F;shm but if this fails it will use $TMPDIR.<p>If you&#x27;re on a platform without &#x2F;dev&#x2F;shm you&#x27;ll want to use some other equivalent such as a small ramdisk, or at the very least be sure your $TMPDIR is not synced into the cloud.')
