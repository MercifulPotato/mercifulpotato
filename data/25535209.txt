Even Logic started as a Windows-first DAW.<p>Indeed, the whole point of Apple buying Logic (and discontinuing the Windows releases) was that they needed a DAW on MacOS to get audio professionals to consider the OS. People don&#x27;t remember this any more, but the Apple versions of Logic were very much inferior to the Windows versions.')
