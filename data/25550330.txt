Item(by='astrobe_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609068690, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25548015, text='Total annihilation took a completely different approach of having factories instead of races. I only know TA-like games through Zero-K, but factories introduce a lot of diversity and perhaps less imbalance risks because players always have the option to go for a mirror match-up (i.e. choosing what is considered the best factory for the map being played).<p>Some game designers claim that Blizzard&#x27;s uses races to keep players interested by <i>creating</i> imbalances. The idea being that you keep players interested by making slight changes all the time to respond to alleged imbalances from the players.')
