Item(by='jimmyswimmy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611773166, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931498, text='It had never occurred to me to wait for a class to teach me something. Just go out and learn it! If there are no references (books, wiki, blog, training resources) it&#x27;s great fun to go your own way and figure it out from scratch. Usually there are some shoulders to stand on and reading a quick paper is fine well spent, but in every niche area it&#x27;s best not to spend too long searching for that paper. Just do. Learn along the way.<p>I suppose in less niche areas it might be better to spend more time finding good tutorials, but even then, we learn much more when we do, not when we listen.')
