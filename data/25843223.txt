Item(by='otterley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611124831, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25838462, text='IPv6 doesn&#x27;t solve the difficulties of container networking. Containers have private network namespaces, which means that a container can&#x27;t communicate with other hosts without (1) a dedicated interface or (2) a bridge (basically a software switch) for host-to-container communication.<p>These problems are Layer 2 problems, while IPv4 and IPv6 are at layer 3.')
