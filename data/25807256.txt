Item(by='tomnipotent', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610847528, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25804786, text='&gt; Cite the past but look for future changes in behaviour.<p>I&#x27;ve heard this referred to as &quot;feedforward&quot; before (as opposed to feedback). Instead of saying &quot;last time you did X, you made a mistake and should instead do Y&quot; you phrase it &quot;next time you do X, make sure to do Y&quot;. It&#x27;s subtle, with the goal of avoiding defensiveness.')
