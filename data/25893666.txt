Item(by='tyingq', descendants=None, kids=[25893878], score=None, time=1611508395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25880414, text='One non-obvious reason dd is often used in examples is because of device permissions.<p>You can&#x27;t (easily&#x2F;legibly) present a one-liner that combines cat and sudo because sudo doesn&#x27;t give the shell redirection (sudo cat &gt;&gt; &#x2F;dev&#x2F;whatever) permissions.  But &quot;sudo dd of=&#x2F;dev&#x2F;whatever&quot; works fine.<p>Edit: Yes, you can make a one liner via sh -c, tee, etc, but depending on cut&#x2F;paste with quotes is tricky, or the one-liner gets long.')
