Item(by='leppr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607814049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25402399, text='Dark mode is not a fad, it&#x27;s simply a good option to have in the valid cases you describe[1], and has been available in many products for decades.<p>The reasons we&#x27;re seeing more of it are that developers are switching their bad hard-coded UIs to more flexible systems which allow it, and now that even MacOS&#x2F;iOS supports it, there&#x27;s an expectation for more and more apps to conform.<p>[1]: another valid case is apps with a strong emphasis on graphics: Photoshop, YouTube, Instagram, etc. A dark UI mitigates visual interference with the content.')
