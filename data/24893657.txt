Rust, too, if it survives.<p>You have to be arrogant to design a language and expect it to go anywhere, so you also tend to think you know better than everybody else, and that nobody has anything to teach you. It&#x27;s an occupational hazard. Designing a language you don&#x27;t mean to use yourself manufactures special failings.<p>Rust&#x27;s designers started with unusual humility, and have kept it.
