Item(by='fossuser', descendants=None, kids=[25877994, 25878024], score=None, time=1611359619, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25877715, text='Pseudonymity and anonymity are not the same thing.<p>Pseudonyms that are not trivially created and discarded can build reputation and will generally behave themselves (see Urbit’s approach to this problem).<p>Anonymous accounts incentivize awful behavior (see 4chan).<p>Real life is different because you’re not truly anonymous in person - you’re recognizable most of the time and identifiable. There are also more in person norms and social constraints.<p>The exception to this in real life might be mobs, where the chaos and confusion can provide some anonymity and well - people in mobs don’t behave that well either.')
