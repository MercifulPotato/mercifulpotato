Item(by='tshaddox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611178066, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25851504, text='When people say “anyone could do that” in reference to art, they’re usually not just saying that anyone could <i>copy</i> that after having seen it. Of course most people could take a famous novel and type out all the same words. They’re saying (regardless of whether they’re correct) that the actual conception of the new work doesn’t seem like it required significant creative effort.')
