Item(by='amluto', descendants=None, kids=[25491484, 25490992], score=None, time=1608506335, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25490438, text='It’s not just the lack of sandboxing — iMessage uses language-level serialization of object graphs. This design is <i>never</i> suitable for sending across privilege boundaries.  Apple should replace the format with a reasonable wire format. If this requires updates to apps that integrate with iMessage or breaks interoperability with older iOS versions, so be it.')
