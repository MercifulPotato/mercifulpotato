Item(by='caseysoftware', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610395390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25734545, text='I&#x27;ve been kicking around how to do something like that because you need to consider all the layers.<p>On the app side, you need neutral phones&#x2F;devices and the app distribution to get apps out there.<p>On the hosting side, you need the hosting itself (cloud or colo), the DNS and registrars, the SSL to secure it, SMS or emails for verification &amp; resets, and probably a few others.<p>On the financial side, you need payment processing and the banking system behind it. That&#x27;s one of the hardest because even if <i>you</i> can handle it, your customers have to be able to send you money from their bank.<p>All of those (and other components) have been cut off&#x2F;disrupted for various organizations in the last year or two.')
