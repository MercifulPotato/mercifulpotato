It’s great.  But the dc-&gt;ac converter needs the grid to be active to do its conversion, so when the power goes out no solar. (I suspect it syncs with the grids 60hz...)<p>If you include a battery backup in your system, you can run without grid power.<p>When I worked at a power monitoring start up we had a demo house that was charging an electric rav4, so it’s possible.')
