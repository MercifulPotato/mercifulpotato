Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=[24870628, 24871130], score=None, time=1603466193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24869772, text='Most people are only aware of the SaaS products used by their team or department. If you were to list out every single tool or service used across many 100-1000 person companies, it wouldn’t be much shorter than this list.<p>If you’ve never worked in sales, you’d be surprised at how many different SaaS products and services can go into running a successful sales department.<p>Sales people tend to be more sticky to their preferred SaaS products than engineers. You may end up with a lot of duplicated functionality across sales tools just because the VP of sales has always used certain tools for certain operations and has no desire to change that.')
