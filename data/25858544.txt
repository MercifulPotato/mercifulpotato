Grayscale, a BTC ETF launched a &quot;drop gold&quot; marketing campaign. Gold would be an example of exactly the kind of investment people buy and hold.<p>There are also dividend investors and critics of today&#x27;s high valuations, buybacks, et cetera. Stocks are an interest in a company that in ideal terms should be creating value. Bitcoin is a medium of exchange at best. Even if you accept that it is a store of value, that is distinct from a company that turns profits.<p>I don&#x27;t disagree that there are plenty of shenanigans going around, but nor do I accept that as excusing the problems with BTC.')
