Item(by='edflsafoiewq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602783820, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24785429, text='Being too easy to predict may break the blackbox nature of the PRNG (you might &quot;predict&quot; it by accident).<p>For example you can increase the quality of a RNG sequence r(n) by adding a multiplicative hash, r&#x27;(n) = A r(n) for some good constant A. A consumer treating r&#x27; as a blackbox expects B r&#x27;(n) = AB r(n) to be a good random sequence, but if B is close to the inverse of A, the constant AB may no longer be &quot;good&quot;.')
