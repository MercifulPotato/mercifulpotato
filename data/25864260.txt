On Intel CPUs in particular there are only 2 slots for filling cache lines, and filling them randomly from main memory takes many cycles, so two threads can easily get starved.<p>If thread X is chasing a linked list and thread Y is compressing an MPEG then it&#x27;s brilliant.')
