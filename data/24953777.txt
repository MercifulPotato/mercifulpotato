Item(by='Yetanfou', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604167751, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24950376, text='I mostly use LineageOS (formerly Cyanogenmod) as a starting point, sometimes with a few customisations but often just straight off the &#x27;net.<p>As to app stores, I use F-Droid and Aurora Store (an alternative front end to the Play Store) for those few proprietary apps (mostly the Swedish BankID electronic ID app) I need to have access to. Aurora Store works with anonymous Google accounts, you do not need - and are advised not to use - a real Google account for this.<p>I use MicroG (free software implementation of the Google Services Framework) on a few devices but mostly just do without it.')
