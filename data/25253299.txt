Item(by='DanBC', descendants=None, kids=[25255162], score=None, time=1606741483, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25250941, text='Do they all count suicide using the same definition? Are they age-standardised or not?<p>In the UK suicide stats come from ONS, and they count:<p>&gt; all deaths from intentional self-harm for persons aged 10 years and over and deaths caused by injury or poisoning where the intent was undetermined for those aged 15 years and over.<p>The US uses<p>&gt;  Death caused by self-directed injurious behavior with an intent to die as a result of the behavior.<p>(but not for people under 9 years old).  Including &quot;intent&quot; means the numbers end up being pretty different.')
