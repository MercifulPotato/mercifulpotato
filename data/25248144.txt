Item(by='pontifier', descendants=None, kids=[25248197, 25249089], score=None, time=1606684476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25247499, text='In my robotics experience I&#x27;ve come to the conclusion that some problems just need to get back to the physics, and trying to put labels on things like &quot;grasp&quot; is fraught with problems that constrain things in unhelpful ways.<p>It&#x27;s more useful to think in terms of what forces are needed to be applied to the environment to accomplish a task. Then you can build a solver to develop innovative physics based solutions that are unconstrained by the semantics.')
