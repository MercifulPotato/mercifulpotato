Item(by='Wowfunhappy', descendants=None, kids=[25246602], score=None, time=1606665366, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25240968, text='My first thought was, if they wanted an arrow why wouldn’t they go with:<p><pre><code>    x &lt;= 5\n</code></pre>\nBut of course, that means “less than or equal to”.<p>You could do:<p><pre><code>    x &lt;- 5\n</code></pre>\nBut I suppose the mere presence of the “less than” symbol is weird in context. (Although, maybe not <i>inherently</i> weirder than using the equals sign for something other than equality?)<p>Perhaps we would have been better off if we’d all just agreed early on, that @, #, or ~ should represent assignment.')
