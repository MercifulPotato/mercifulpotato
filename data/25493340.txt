For what it&#x27;s worth, this correlation should hold for <i>every</i> mental health diagnosis.<p>Pretty much all diagnosis criteria include some form of &quot;and these things cause some sort of problems in the patient&#x27;s life&quot;. Even if it doesn&#x27;t, if someone&#x27;s school&#x2F;life&#x2F;career is going OK, they don&#x27;t seek out a mental health diagnosis. And IQ is one thing that is positively correlated with all <i>sorts</i> of desirable life outcomes.<p>Like, one notable anecdotal example of this is Bill Gross, the &quot;Bond King&quot;.
