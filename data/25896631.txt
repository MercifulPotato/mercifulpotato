Item(by='ahepp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611525913, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25895126, text='When I was a broke college student, I cared a lot more about open source. Now that I have more resources, more experience, less time, and a personal interest in getting paid for software, I have stronger feelings about the interface.<p>Whether or not the box is black isn&#x27;t really on my mind these days. I rarely have the skill or time to fix them when they break, or don&#x27;t do what I want. I&#x27;d rather the box be easier to swap out or build around when requirements change. I&#x27;d rather have a richer ecosystem of blacker boxes, than a bunch of broken transparent boxes laying around.<p>A problem, of course, is that businesses don&#x27;t want their boxes to be interchangable. Transparent boxes do tend to help.')
