Avoiding things and avoiding running toward things are different risk classes for humans, and we aren&#x27;t always rational in these cases (see also: Thinking Fast and Slow).<p>I&#x27;ve known a couple of people with anemia, and many of those conversations have lead to discussion of its opposite - hemochromatosis. The iron and vitamin C link is very temporal. If you&#x27;re trying to increase your iron absorption from food, adding it to the dish is much more effective than taking a vitamin C pill in the morning. If you&#x27;re trying to lower it, you try to avoid ever getting vitamin C with food.<p>A routine blood test can show elevated iron levels (mine came back elevated but not dangerous, which is why I stopped using cast iron for cooking).')
