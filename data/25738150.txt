Item(by='ohazi', descendants=None, kids=[25738865], score=None, time=1610403117, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25737502, text='There aren&#x27;t a lot of advantages to VHF&#x2F;UHF over the direct microwave links that they&#x27;re already using (and a handful of disadvantages), so it&#x27;s probably not likely.<p>The big perceived advantage to HF is eliminating all of the hops, despite the dramatically reduced bandwidth. With VHF&#x2F;UHF, you&#x27;d still need the hops, but you also have reduced bandwidth. If you need to hop, might as well make use of the bandwidth that you can get with microwave.')
