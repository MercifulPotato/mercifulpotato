Part of it is that it&#x27;s complicated due to copy-on-write and snapshots. Other part is manpower, it has not been a high-priority issue for most production pools it seems. Google for &quot;block pointer rewrite&quot;.<p>Assuming your free space fragmentation is not too bad (check output of zpool list, &quot;frag&quot; column is free space fragmentation level), you could just move files back and forth between the two. Assuming you don&#x27;t have snapshots holding on to the files, this can help reduce the fragmentation.<p>Otherwise you&#x27;re stuck with the send&#x2F;receive.<p>Though I&#x27;d try the mailing list[1] to see if any of the gurus can help identify what&#x27;s going wrong before attempting random ailments.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;zfsonlinux.topicbox.com&#x2F;groups&#x2F;zfs-discuss&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;zfsonlinux.topicbox.com&#x2F;groups&#x2F;zfs-discuss&#x2F;</a>')
