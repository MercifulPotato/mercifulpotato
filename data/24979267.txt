Item(by='alexmingoia', descendants=None, kids=[24979431], score=None, time=1604408983, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979079, text='Housing <i>is</i> included in CPI. Housing is included as rent, and owner-adjusted rent (what rent would be if a house was rented).<p>Housing purchase prices aren’t included because a house isn’t consumed within a year. The price of a house represents both current and <i>future</i> housing consumption. It’s not some conspiracy to hide inflation.')
