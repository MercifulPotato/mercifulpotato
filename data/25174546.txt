Item(by='magicalhippo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606013774, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25173635, text='I forgot to mention an interesting point here.<p>When using a physically based, photorealistic renderer and you model an indoor or studio scene as realistically as possible, you often find that it looks flat, dull or just bad.<p>And to fix it, you have to employ the similar tricks that professional photographers and cinematographers do, adding extra light sources to reduce harsh shadows, add highlights etc.<p>So by making the renderer more realistic you&#x27;ve got to fake it like a photographer.')
