Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=[24789265], score=None, time=1602771517, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24788358, text='Asylum is a human right, that <i>doesn&#x27;t mean</i> everyone can successfully claim it!<p>It means everyone has a right to put in a claim to asylum and have it assessed properly by the authorities.<p>&gt; There is no right to freely cross borders.<p>In some circumstances, <i>yes there is</i>.<p>If there&#x27;s a war in country A, and non-combatants flee to neighbouring country B out of genuine necessity for safety, yes that border crossing is widely regarded as a human right.<p>It derives from the basic right to life.<p>Doesn&#x27;t mean country B will respect it.')
