Item(by='strgcmc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605540528, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25108611, text='The trick is to know the bounded context of the &quot;iffy-ness&quot;.  Iffy code that might occasionally lead to latency or a timeout for some users using your service, is different from iffy code that charges the user the wrong dollar amount at checkout, or iffy code that could brick a user&#x27;s device. Great engineers know which things are safe (enough) to take what types of shortcuts on.')
