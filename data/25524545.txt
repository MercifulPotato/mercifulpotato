Item(by='LinuxBender', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608777099, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25524123, text='Depends on codec used and the latency&#x2F;bufferbloat [1] between the clients and the server.  If you and your friend are each sub-30ms from the server and using Opus [2], you should not even notice the delay.  This also assumes your chat client is not running on a highly loaded CPU core.  If either of you has latency and&#x2F;or bufferbloat, you will have bad lag.  You can also reduce the lag by lowering the bitrate in the client.<p>[1] - <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dslreports.com&#x2F;speedtest" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dslreports.com&#x2F;speedtest</a><p>[2] - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;opus-codec.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;opus-codec.org&#x2F;</a>')
