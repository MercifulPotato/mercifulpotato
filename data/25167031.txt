Item(by='coreblocks', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605920860, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25164868, text='This article gives the wrong impression. High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) is a specific term where DRAM and main chip is packaged on a silicon interposer. This allows for a 1024-bit bus to be routed within the silicon interposer. This is not possible with a regular substrate. The DRAM on the M1 are just LPDDR4X-4266.')
