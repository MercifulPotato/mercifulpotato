Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[25884685], score=None, time=1611424925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25884201, text='I don&#x27;t think they&#x27;re any less &quot;sharp&quot; in general; it&#x27;s just a lazier communication style that offloads (perhaps unintentionally) some of the work onto the other person.<p>to be effective at async communication, you have to not only ask your question, but also anticipate all the context that person will need to give a good answer. then you do your best to condense that into a clear and concise initial message.<p>in synchronous communication, I find that much more often the asker just asks their question and expects the recipient to come up with all the pertinent followup questions themselves. the asker might even feel they are more productive in this mode, since they don&#x27;t have to account for the time&#x2F;effort needed to gather context.')
