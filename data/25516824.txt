I’ll admit, I didn’t expect ad-based revenue to be the solution. It makes sense in theory, but at the same time it might be a hard sell for existing users. Most people I know that use Telegram use it because it’s not WhatsApp. They appreciate privacy. While Telegram promises to protect privacy while serving ads, that’s become harder and harder for companies to stick to.<p>Then again, network effects.
