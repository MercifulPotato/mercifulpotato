Item(by='topherhaddad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612207524, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25990928, text='Great points! The GPS analogy is definitely not 1-to-1. Our main point with the analogy is this: when GPS technology was developed, there was no foresight of applications like Tinder, Uber, Pokemon Go, which all would not be possible without GPS. So in that sense, we expect that if we are able to push satellite imagery in the direction of becoming a commodity, this will unlock applications we have no foresight of today. SVB &amp; Space Capital released a great report on this idea called The GPS Playbook')
