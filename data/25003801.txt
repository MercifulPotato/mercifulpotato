Item(by='EdwardDiego', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604624237, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25001617, text='Sure IDEA is primarily aimed at the JVM market, but I use IDEA&#x27;s refactorings in Python, Go, Bash etc.<p>And I&#x27;m unsure about your point about meaningful names - IDEA typically won&#x27;t suggest a name when renaming an existing language artifact - type, class, object, method, function etc.<p>It will when extracting an expression into a variable, based on the context it was derived from, and that&#x27;s actually a pretty good heuristic - e.g., if the expression being extracted is<p><pre><code>    &lt;some_var&gt; = dependency.get_campaign_results()\n</code></pre>\nIt&#x27;ll suggest things like<p><pre><code>    campaign_results\n    campaigns\n    results\n</code></pre>\nMost of which are what a developer would usually use in that situation.')
