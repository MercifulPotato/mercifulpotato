Bush won with a minority of votes. But if they used Ranked Choice, and Nader voters are generally assumed to have the preference order (Nader &gt; Gore &gt; Bush) then Gore would have won.<p>We can assume that politically, Nader is to the left of Gore, and Bush is to the right.<p>So in fact, ranked choice voting helps the candidate that is in the political center!')
