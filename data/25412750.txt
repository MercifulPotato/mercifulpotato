Item(by='7thaccount', descendants=None, kids=[25415648], score=None, time=1607911930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25406835, text='Ada and Pascal have some syntactic similarities, but I&#x27;m not sure they cover the same kind of niche enough for someone to say &quot;I&#x27;d learn Ada prior to Pascal&quot;.<p>Today, Delphi is used in some shops the same way C# or Java is used in others. Free Pascal with Lazarus is kind of like a free version of that. You can quickly build desktop apps.<p>Ada is nothing like that. It&#x27;s almost exclusively used in the defense industry and others like aviation and infrastructure where the code is likely to be embedded and can NOT fail.<p>One is for standard business and one for critical infrastructure.')
