Item(by='ben509', descendants=None, kids=[25868562], score=None, time=1611286185, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25866630, text='The entire premise of a republic is that power is checked and balanced. The aim is not to represent the &quot;will of the people&quot;, because the will of a majority is frequently to oppress a minority.<p>The EC was more than a compromise, it was an integral part of a system that prevents too much power being handed to any one institution or demographic. And forcing national candidates to appeal to a multitude of states rather than a handful of cities has a moderating effect.<p>If anything, what&#x27;s outdated about the EC is we need <i>more</i> checks on the political influence that is centralized in cities because the founders didn&#x27;t anticipate urbanization.')
