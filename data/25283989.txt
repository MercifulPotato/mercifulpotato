Item(by='frankmcsherry', descendants=None, kids=[25284346], score=None, time=1606957525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25283708, text='It&#x27;s easier to describe the things that cannot be materialized.<p>The only rule at the moment is that you cannot currently maintain queries that use the functions `current_time()`, `now()`, and `mz_logical_timestamp()`. These are quantities that change automatically without data changing, and shaking out what maintaining them should mean is still open.<p>Other than that, any SELECT query you can write can be materialized and incrementally maintained.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;materialize.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;sql&#x2F;select&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;materialize.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;sql&#x2F;select&#x2F;</a>')
