That story blew up, and people started reporting &quot;flying saucers&quot; everywhere.<p>It even got into the heads of some government officials. For example, the initial press release given <i>by the airforce</i> for the Roswell incident was that they had captured a &quot;flying disc&quot;. They later made a correction that it was a weather balloon, but you can see how the government making such statements would cause confusion and add to the mania.<p>On the Wikipedia page for the incident you can see the original news articles: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Roswell_UFO_incident" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Roswell_UFO_incident</a>')
