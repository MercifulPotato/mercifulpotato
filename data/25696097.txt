Item(by='geofft', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610162190, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25695975, text='The ability to use Twitter (or the newspaper) is not a <i>presidential</i> power.<p>Certainly it is true that it is hard to run a campaign without a media presence. That has been true for hundreds of years. Competent candidates have entire teams, if not the vast majority of their campaign, figuring out how to effectively contract with private media entities (whose freedom is protected by the First Amendment) to get their message out. That incompetent  candidates exist is not a reason to change this system.')
