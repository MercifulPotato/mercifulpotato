Item(by='bdcravens', descendants=None, kids=[24846442], score=None, time=1603271893, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24845587, text='Working on a Ruby application that uses a database that predates the application (it was originally written in another language), there are simply some things that can&#x27;t be written in AR, or when they are, the performance just isn&#x27;t there.<p>Additionally, we often have to create ad-hoc queries to answer questions from stakeholder. It&#x27;s easy enough to just pop up DataGrip and write the query, and copy the tabular results into a doc in Google Sheets for sharing.<p>I think the best approach is ORM-first, not ORM-only.')
