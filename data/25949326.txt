Item(by='PaulDavisThe1st', descendants=None, kids=[25950728], score=None, time=1611868495, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25939422, text='Forward contracts and futures contracts have a number of important differences. Most notably, futures contracts involve assets that already exist, but <i>will</i> be traded in the future. Forward contracts involve assets that do not (typically) exist at the time of the contract, and will not be traded if they do not exist at the contract&#x27;s specified date.<p>They also serve quite different purposes for a society&#x2F;economy.')
