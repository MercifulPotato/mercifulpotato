Item(by='afandian', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610915506, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25814416, text='We&#x27;re talking about mostly-text-pages which have no sensible reason to rely on JavaScript to render, not rich webapps. In these cases I consider the text to be a meaningful representation for the purposes of indexing or archive.<p>I don&#x27;t agree with this:<p>&gt; Search engines and archival tools can handle all this very well today.<p>I think most developers have been in a situation where they had to explain to users that a moon on a stick isn&#x27;t easy just because Google can do it. It may be commonplace, but it&#x27;s not easy or cheap.<p>The difference between real HTML and JS execution is orders of magnitude of time, expense, maintenance, and stuff to break. That really can&#x27;t be underestimated. It&#x27;s the difference between a 500 millisecond call to curl and a whole headless browser and a 5 second wait.<p>I hope you don&#x27;t think I&#x27;m putting this too strongly, but this raised bar is a force in the monopolisation and de-diversification of the web.')
