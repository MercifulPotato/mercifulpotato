Item(by='improbable22', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611150333, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25846107, text='Time is a continuous variable, and kids know this pretty early on: they will happily tell you they are 5 and a half years old. Their second year starts when they are aged 1.0.<p>English in general makes this distinction: &quot;He is fewer than 6 years old&quot; is simply a mistake, while &quot;less than 6 years old&quot; is fine. (I think some dialects use &quot;fewer&quot; less often, in general, but would be pretty surprised if any prefer it here.)')
