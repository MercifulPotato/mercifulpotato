Item(by='curo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610624650, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25775134, text='The original post discusses harm as a boundary condition.<p>Things like libel, slander, false statements of fact, false advertising, etc have long been considered beneficial as a limitation on free speech (e.g., not protected by the First Amendment)<p>The term &quot;misinformation&quot; contains an argument that there is a broader set of statements (not yet considered libelous or false in the eyes of the law), which should be regulated by an appointed group or new set of laws.<p>The backlash is against this new zeitgeist: a campaign against misinformation is certainly one that dials back the liberal view on freedom of speech, more so than the current boundary conditions already established.')
