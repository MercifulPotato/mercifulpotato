Item(by='ripe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611709403, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25920852, text='This discussion confused me until I looked up &quot;torus&quot; and found there are really two different definitions of this word:<p>(a) a SURFACE generated by a circle rotated about a coplanar axis outside the circle. Examples: an inner tube or a swimming tube. (This is what&#x27;s meant by &quot;a hollow torus&quot; in the description).<p>(b) a SOLID generated by a circular lamina rotated about a coplanar axis outside the lamina. Example: a bagel or an annular gasket. I was thinking of this one, and the descriptions made no sense at all.<p>Now it all makes sense.<p>Hmm. I thought before this that I knew what a torus was...')
