Item(by='IshKebab', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607467638, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25350358, text='Because Linux isn&#x27;t perfect. It can be improved upon.<p>* All code in the kernel including all drivers is 100% trusted. This is very bad for security.<p>* No stable driver ABI, so writing out-of-tree device drivers is very difficult.<p>* GUI is an afterthought, which means it is very difficult to do things like graphical secure attention keys, and frankly graphics&#x2F;display support on Linux is very janky as a result.<p>* Many of its features are firmly stuck in the 80s.')
