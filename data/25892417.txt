I repeat what I said previously: these stats are collected in order to provide insights into important issues such as health, employment, housing and yes, inequality.<p>For example, the COVID-19 pandemic has revealed that deaths from COVID-19 are higher among black and South Asian groups. Without this data we would not have been able to identify that these communities are at higher risk.<p>Link to my previous comment on this thread: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25891543" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25891543</a>')
