Item(by='Doxin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606203903, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25190883, text='Double counting an axle is a critical failure for an axle counter. It could keep a track section blocked until someone comes to fix the axle counter. Even worse it could unblock a track section while there&#x27;s still (part of) a train on it.<p>If you&#x27;re worried about double-counting an axle you probably need to design your axle counters better.')
