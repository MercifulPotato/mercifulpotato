Item(by='TeaDrunk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603304822, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24849485, text='A tip to me, to write better as an exercise- Rewrite something (a short story, a paragraph, a chapter, etc) you love, word by word, to understand the craft of it. The act of writing something vs reading it can best be reflected in this way.<p>Then attempt to rewrite that thing from memory, and see what you came up with and how that changes the story.<p>If you practice this repeatedly, you will inevitably write better. This is copying the brush-strokes of the previous greats in painting, but in prose.')
