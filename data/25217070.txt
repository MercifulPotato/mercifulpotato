How you achieve it doesn&#x27;t matter to being eventually consistent, just that it is.<p>[1] - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Eventual_consistency" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Eventual_consistency</a>')
