Item(by='mark_and_sweep', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607622056, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25374086, text='If Deno was only &quot;Add TypeScript support&quot;, &quot;Add security capabilities&quot;, and &quot;Add URL imports&quot;, etc. it would simply be a new version (or multiple new versions) of Node.<p>These (and other) disruptive breaking changes are about fixing mistakes that cannot be fixed (or at least, are hard to fix) in Node.<p>Maybe, in a few years, you&#x27;ll say something like &quot;Oh, I wish legacy Node would be more Deno compatible&quot; because Deno will be the de-facto server-side JS scripting runtime. Equally, it&#x27;s possible that Deno will fail, but that many good ideas will be incorporated into Node as breaking changes.<p>Node and Deno as well as their environments can grow further together or further apart. I think it&#x27;s too early to tell which future is more likely.')
