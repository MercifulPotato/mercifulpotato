Item(by='reitzensteinm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604056493, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24939863, text='I have used refs for a multiplayer server. Five years in to Clojure, there was finally a reason to break them out.<p>Players were in rooms, which were updated ten times a second. All of the rooms are largely independent, meaning the best strategy to do this is to use a threadpool to update them in parallel.<p>However, occasionally operations would require synchronization between rooms (eg, move one player from one to another).<p>It worked pretty well.')
