Item(by='prionassembly', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607696551, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25385351, text='There are many consumer headphones with an “open air” design that let street noise in.<p>I live in a busy hipster neighborhood that’s also a major traffic hub... and I like it. I’m a city rat. The music is supposed to come <i>with</i> the roar of cars at a green light and the dozens of half heard conversations on busy sidewalks.<p>I have the-cheapest-model Sennheiser open airs and a with-mic-for-Zoom-calls JBL. The JBL absolutely isolates the environment mechanically. This is good for a meeting (except for the fact that you hear your voice from your skull, but heck.)')
