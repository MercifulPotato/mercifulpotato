Item(by='dr_orpheus', descendants=None, kids=[24841561], score=None, time=1603221207, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24841067, text='Delta T in this case is probably referring to the change between the minimum and maximum temperatures experienced on the surface of the moon. In other words, the delta between something getting really hot spending 14 days in direct sunlight with no convection to cool it down and then getting really cold spending 14 days in darkness.<p>Delta T (for temperature) can also refer to the delta in temperature across an object as well. One side is very hot and one side is very cold. Often used when describing problems of relative thermal expansion.')
