Item(by='jcims', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603554900, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24879135, text='Kafka and SNS only conceptually intersect, SNS is more for intra-service and endpoint notification at huge scale (millions of subscribers) and is directly integrated into a variety of AWS services (e.g. S3 event notifications).  You can set up a topic, subscribe a customer via SMS and send a message in just a few lines of python:<p><pre><code>    $ python3\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; import boto3\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; sns=boto3.client(&#x27;sns&#x27;)\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; sns.create_topic(Name=&#x27;DopeService&#x27;)\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; sns.subscribe(TopicArn=&#x27;arn:aws:sns:us-east-1:xxxxxxxxxxxx:DopeService&#x27;,Protocol=&#x27;sms&#x27;,Endpoint=&#x27;+18008675309&#x27;)\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; ...&lt;repeat for other SMS #&#x27;s&gt;...\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; sns.publish(TopicArn=&#x27;arn:aws:sns:us-east-1:xxxxxxxxxxxx:DopeService&#x27;,Message=&#x27;sup&#x27;)\n</code></pre>\nIf you&#x27;re a masochist you can have Amazon notify everyone on the topic every time someone uploads to your bucket<p><pre><code>    &gt;&gt;&gt; sns.set_topic_attributes(TopicArn=&#x27;arn:aws:sns:us-east-1:xxxxxxxxxxxx:DopeService&#x27;,AttributeName=&#x27;Policy&#x27;,AttributeValue=&#x27;&lt;policy&gt;&#x27;)\n    &gt;&gt;&gt; s3.put_bucket_notification_configuration(Bucket=&#x27;dopebucket&#x27;,NotificationConfiguration={&lt;notification config&gt;})\n</code></pre>\nYou send directly to HTTP&#x2F;HTTPS service endpoints, other SNS topics, Lambdas, SQS queues and mobile app push notifications.  SNS is more about signaling than a raw message pipe.  Relative to Kafka it&#x27;s basically apples and oranges.')
