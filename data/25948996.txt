Item(by='sudosysgen', descendants=None, kids=[25949319], score=None, time=1611867040, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25948442, text='The Régie des logements in Québec absolutely does cap price increases, or even forbid them outright some years.<p>It also does extra controls that many countries don&#x27;t do, such as capping price increases even if the tenant change (the tenant needs to request the former rent, though).<p>Allowing rent increases in the case of renovations is unusual, but not that rare either. That said, it&#x27;s absolutely not &quot;just a dispute resolution board&quot;. It 100% is traditional rent control with capped price increases, on top of the rest.')
