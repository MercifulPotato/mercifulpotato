Item(by='ARandomerDude', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603725919, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887268, text='&gt; No fancy beekeeper suit necessary<p>Professionals wear PPE because if you incur a low-probability risk every time you do X, and you do X often enough (because it&#x27;s your profession), X will happen to you.<p>For example, if you have a 99% chance of nothing going wrong, and you do that thing 500 times, it&#x27;s essentially guaranteed to happen (0.99^500 = 0.0066).<p>The stinging insect removal guys <i>will</i> be stung if they are in the profession for decades without PPE.')
