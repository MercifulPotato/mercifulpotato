Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611252009, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25861202, text='Sure, but accountants will not know the likelyhood of success of a new product or service, how the features will affect sales, the likelyhood of making large sales, the state of strategic relationships, etc. etc.<p>Yes, they may have models and estimates, but the real information will be in the hands of those involved directly.<p>Even things like refinancing and the options for doing so can be affected by things like letters of intent from potential large clients, industry validation, etc.<p>Just knowing numbers and dates isn’t enough.<p>I’m not saying accounting isn’t important, but it just isn’t the only source of truth.')
