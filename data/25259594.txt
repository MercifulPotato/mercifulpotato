Item(by='simonh', descendants=None, kids=[25261626], score=None, time=1606773671, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25259150, text='Qualcomm could come out with an M1 class chip, they have the engineering capability, but if Microsoft or Google don’t adopt it with a custom tuned OS and dev tooling customised for that specific architecture ready from day one, they’d lose a fortune.<p>The same goes in the other direction. If MS does all the work on ARM windows optimised for one vendor’s experimental chip and the chip vendor messes up or pulls out, they’d be shived. It’s too much risk for a company on either side of the table to wear on their own.')
