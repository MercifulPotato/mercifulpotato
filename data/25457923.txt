Item(by='RcouF1uZ4gsC', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608226877, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25442120, text='&gt; When the Khoisan hunter-gatherers of sub-Saharan Africa gazed upon the meandering trail of stars and dust that split the night sky, they saw the embers of a campfire. Polynesian sailors perceived a cloud-eating shark. The ancient Greeks saw a stream of milk, gala, which would eventually give rise to the modern term “galaxy.”<p>This is one of the cases where wonder and grandeur and awesomeness of reality, makes every myth and imaginative explanation pale in comparison. Our ancient ancestor’s wildest dreams were not wild enough.')
