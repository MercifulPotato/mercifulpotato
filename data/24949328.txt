We have food stamps to cover those who can&#x27;t afford food.  But that doesn&#x27;t mean we need a nationalized canned soup manufacturer.  Or to nationalize Wonder Bread.<p>With proper money allocation and regulation, there&#x27;s no reason that &quot;basic rights&quot; can&#x27;t be provided for by a competitive albeit monitored market.')
