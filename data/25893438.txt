Item(by='erikpukinskis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611506896, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25892279, text='My rule for major new abstract components is: “if you’re going to do that, you have to get it right”.<p>It’s fine to have a major abstraction, but it needs to actually make a problem space disappear, for some definition of “entirely”.<p>That means:\n - great code\n - great documentation\n - tested\n - engineering resources assigned to maintain it full time<p>... for as long as it takes. Until you reach that level of “makes the problem space disappear entirely”.<p>So when someone suggests a new abstraction, I just ask if we’re going to invest all of that. And if not I suggest we stick to just using the tools we already have.')
