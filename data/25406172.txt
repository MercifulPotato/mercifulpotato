Item(by='rswail', descendants=None, kids=[25406341], score=None, time=1607862893, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405960, text='Frictionless, permissionless large payments are illegal. The problem is that no one has worked out how to make micropayments costless, and that has nothing to do with AML&#x2F;KYC and everything to do with the costs of running a payment system.<p>Bitcoin has turned out to be expensive to mine. Most other distributed ledger &quot;currencies&quot; have been launched to make money for the creators of the artificial scarcity.<p>Governments cannot give up control of the money supply but they are the only ones that can establish a form of electronic currency that works the same as cash does now.<p>Whether they&#x27;re willing to make it as anonymous as cash or allow it to be exchanged for other government&#x27;s electronic currency is part of the problem that needs to be solved.')
