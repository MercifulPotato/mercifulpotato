Item(by='howlin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609091832, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25551023, text='One thing to keep in mind about direct (learn the policy&#x2F;behavior) versus indirect (learn the model and then simulate behaviors on the model to choose the best) is that sometimes it&#x27;s much easier to find a good enough policy than it is to learn an accurate enough model for simulation.  Driving is a good example of this.  Most of the time all you need to do is stay in your lane and obey the rules for intersections.  A simulation of a driving environment, on the other hand, is quite difficult.')
