Item(by='jedberg', descendants=None, kids=[25886078, 25878347], score=None, time=1611359052, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25874039, text='We really need to get the cube root rule passed.[0]  Gerrymandering wouldn&#x27;t really be useful if each district were smaller, and then we&#x27;d get Representatives that have a similar breakdown to the people they represent.<p>For example in the US each representative would represent ~475K people instead of ~755K.  So California would have 84 representatives instead of 53.<p>It&#x27;s a lot harder to gerrymander when the districts are much smaller.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Cube_root_rule" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Cube_root_rule</a>')
