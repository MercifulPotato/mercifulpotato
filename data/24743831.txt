Item(by='dodobirdlord', descendants=None, kids=[24744559], score=None, time=1602392178, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24742391, text='In an interesting demonstration of how all ergonomics is personal, my use of a Mac laptop and the repeated wrist rotation from the trackpad to they keyboard and back led to RSI.<p>That said, I love the Mac trackpad and it has great ergonomic potential, I just personally have to use it with a split keyboard with the halves on either side of the laptop. That way the motion of moving from trackpad to keyboard and back is a bend at the elbow, while the wrist stays straight.')
