Item(by='my123', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606507149, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25232042, text='XTAJIT, which is the x86(_64) application compatibility layer on Arm Windows is far from garbage, and got a ton of engineering work.<p>The problem is that having a 30% performance penalty on a Snapdragon 8cx (because Arm only started really caring about fast CPUs starting from the Cortex-A76, which is the first generation stemming from that effort, resulting in not-ideal perf characteristics for x86 emulation) results in not very good performance. This can only be fixed through better, smarter Arm hardware. (which is coming)')
