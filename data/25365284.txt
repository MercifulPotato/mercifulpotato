Talk to the Adguard devs about whether Safari&#x27;s API is sufficient on its own to make an effective adblocker.<p>The only reason why Apple&#x27;s move got less criticism than Google&#x27;s is because comparatively not many people use Safari, and most of us don&#x27;t really care what the extension ecosystem looks like on Safari.')
