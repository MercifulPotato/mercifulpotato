Item(by='Rury', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605211256, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25072689, text='&gt;what are possible trade-offs when there is substantial constrains on monopolies, platforms, and aggregators ?<p>The benefits to centralization&#x2F;aggregation is that things can be made uniform everywhere, which can make things convenient. (e.g. if language A had a monopoly on languages, it would be easy to communicate with others no matter where you go)<p>It then follows that - the downsides to limiting centralization (or encouraging decentralization) is that things are not necessarily uniform everywhere. (e.g. might be difficult to communicate when everyone&#x27;s speaking different languages everywhere; difficult to get brand X in market Y)')
