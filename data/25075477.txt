Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=[25075748], score=None, time=1605217375, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25075415, text='The problem is not that your premise is too complicated for others to understand, it&#x27;s that the &#x27;walled garden&#x27; is a trade-off which people disagree about.<p>Walled gardens are safer, and usually have some implicit or explicit quality guarantee (like the Nintendo seal of quality).<p>Open platforms allow for more flexibility and innovation by new entrants.<p>On top of this disagreement, we have the meta-issue of whether business model innovation should be allowed; the USA generally permits new business models, whereas European governments are more restrictive.')
