Item(by='derefr', descendants=None, kids=[24819346], score=None, time=1603038980, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24817158, text='When you have such a large awk script, you may as well also demonstrate that Awk is a “real scripting language” by putting it into its own script with a `#!&#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin&#x2F;awk -f` shebang line. Much easier to edit than trying to get your shell to do it. (Unless you’re using a fancy editing shell like Emacs’ shell mode; but at that point, why would you need Awk? You probably know far better how to use your own shell-runtime’s text-manipulation primitives.)')
