Item(by='cassianoleal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610733497, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25793608, text='I know quite a few people who have either not looked for jobs because it would have meant less income overall, or refused promotions &#x2F; higher salary for the same reasons.<p>This is a well known phenomenon known as the welfare trap [0].<p>This may not be the case if you have the opportunity to go from unemployed directly to a higher salary but most unemployed people have minimal wage as a best prospect, many times not even that.<p>UBI evens it out in that if you do get a job you&#x27;ll make more money instead of downright trading your free time for busy time with no financial gain.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Welfare_trap" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Welfare_trap</a>')
