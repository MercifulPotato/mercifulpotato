Item(by='rendx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609499186, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25602260, text='The article suggests to use torify, which is a thin &quot;best effort&quot; wrapper to route traffic through Tor. If the more secure torsocks is available, it will use it. However, it will fall back to less secure tunneling methods that can leak DNS and UDP in cases where torsocks is not available.<p>It is recommended to install and use torsocks directly instead, because this will prevent leakage.')
