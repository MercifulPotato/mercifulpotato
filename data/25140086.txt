These color keywords have been deprecated, however, as they are insufficient for their original purpose (making website elements look like their native OS counterparts), represent a security risk by making it easier for a webpage to “spoof” a native OS dialog, and increase fingerprinting surface, compromising user privacy.</i><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.w3.org&#x2F;TR&#x2F;css-color-4&#x2F;#deprecated-system-colors" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.w3.org&#x2F;TR&#x2F;css-color-4&#x2F;#deprecated-system-colors</a>')
