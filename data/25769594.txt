Item(by='1vuio0pswjnm7', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610581289, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25769164, text='I disable swap.  If I run out of RAM, thrashing may occur but it is not fatal.  Some process that needs to write to &quot;disk&quot;  might fail, but the system does not.  I just delete some file(s) to free up the needed RAM, restart the process and continue working.  I don&#x27;t think NetBSD has anything like &quot;OOM killer&quot;.<p>Note that one drawback with &quot;disk-less&quot; via read-only SD card or USB stick is how to have a good source of entropy at boot time.')
