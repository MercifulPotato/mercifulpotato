Item(by='ptmcc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609357790, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25583408, text='I&#x27;ve come to similar conclusions as you.<p>&gt; the anticipation and research heightens the joy of actually getting the thing<p>I&#x27;ve found that the research and thinking process is often the most fun part, and that I can replace that little dopamine hit from buying a bunch of cheap crap by window shopping for nicer, more expensive things for the future.<p>Then when I am finally ready to buy something nice, I&#x27;ve already done most of the research to know exactly what I want&#x2F;need.<p>It also helps filter out stuff I don&#x27;t really need or care about, since I tend to forget about that junk if I don&#x27;t impulse buy it. Put junk in your cart but don&#x27;t buy it until you sleep on it, and 9 times out of 10 you realize you don&#x27;t really need it or even care about it once the novelty is gone.<p>I end up buying less throwaway crap, and I end up mostly very pleased with the nicer things I do buy.')
