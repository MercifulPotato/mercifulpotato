Item(by='joezydeco', descendants=None, kids=[24802302], score=None, time=1602861480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24800681, text='The part not discussed is how many PCB layers (signal, ground, more signal, more ground, etc) you need when laying down DDR.<p>If you are playing with a 2x2&quot; EVK then 8 or 10 layers isn&#x27;t a budgetary problem.  When the board starts getting larger it&#x27;s now it&#x27;s a huge cost adder as opposed to low speed 2-layer PCB.<p>This is why designers switch to doing it with SOMs.  The SOM can be a high-speed part and the carrier PCB is cheap and slow.')
