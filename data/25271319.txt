Item(by='dragonsky', descendants=None, kids=[25271667], score=None, time=1606864455, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25270701, text='Then you make sure that the person who is the janitor actually wants to be doing that and is given the resources to be able to be the best janitor he can be.<p>There seems to be an assumption that everybody wants to be the captain of the ship.  Nothing could be further from the case. Most people just want a job they can grow in and be allowed to manage to do their best at.  I know a lot of technical people who die inside when they have to make the shift to management path just to get a little more money. Almost inevitably they make fairly poor managers because what they really want to be doing is the technical work and they see their subordinates having all the fun.')
