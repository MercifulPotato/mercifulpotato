Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611951813, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25961367, text='There&#x27;s nothing horrible at all about shorting, it&#x27;s just about investing in the other direction.<p>It&#x27;s horrible if funds try to push a company down on that basis, but otherwise it&#x27;s normal.<p>It&#x27;s &#x27;good&#x27; because you want people betting on the other side of irrational hubris - if the market is way-overvaluing a stock, you want them to &#x27;lose&#x27; and for the stock to come to something within reasons.<p>Shorting helps prick mini bubbles, or stabilize them, before they start to get way out of hand.')
