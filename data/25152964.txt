Item(by='EricE', descendants=None, kids=[25153738, 25153430], score=None, time=1605812015, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25152531, text='Just remember that if your machine needs to swap because of low RAM you are now writing to your SSD.<p>The permanently soldered in SSD that has a finite amount of writes.<p>Saving $200 on RAM up front could get wiped out if you kill your SSD prematurely with constant, heavy swapping.<p>Here I see the fast SSD speed as a negative - making it painless to do something inherently dangerous towards the lifespan of your SSD.')
