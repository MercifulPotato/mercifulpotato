Item(by='kace91', descendants=None, kids=[25457081, 25457127, 25460110], score=None, time=1608220466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25455651, text='There&#x27;s a lot of generational change due to the use of computers in writing, in general.<p>For example, in Spanish, when I was a kid it was a given fact that exclamations and questions went between marks (¿? and ¡! respectively).  Nowadays it&#x27;s <i>extremely</i> rare to see opening marks - they exist in very formal contexts like books or newspapers, but even emails in the workplace got rid of them, and using them on social media or messaging apps would be just as weird as signing the message at the end.')
