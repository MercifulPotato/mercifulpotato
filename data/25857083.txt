Item(by='29athrowaway', descendants=None, kids=[25857208], score=None, time=1611221444, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25856572, text='In 2008, Benn Jordan (aka The Flashbulb and other pseudonyms), got tired of iTunes making money from his music and receiving no revenue for it, that he personally shared his new album &quot;Soundtrack To A Vacant Life&quot; via BitTorrent.<p>He attached a text file to the release, explaining how he makes essentially no money from iTunes and other platforms distributing his music and invited people to buy the album from his website directly. And that strategy seems to have worked.<p>Radiohead released their album &quot;In Rainbows&quot; exclusively online and invited people to pay what they want. As a result they made much more money than they usually made with that release.')
