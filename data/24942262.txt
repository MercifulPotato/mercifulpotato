Item(by='kqr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604066542, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24941746, text='You can also trivially come across data designed for consumption with AWK. Whenever I write a script or utility to dump some data for later analysis, I take into consideration if I can shape the output in a way that will make it easy to work with in AWK.<p>Because I won&#x27;t know ahead of time what all the interesting values or summaries will be, but if I know I can get it through AWK it will be cheap to extract or compute those values and summaries later, once I know what they are.')
