Item(by='joppy', descendants=None, kids=[25977673], score=None, time=1612087544, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25977549, text='“Self-made”: if you come from a well-off (not necessarily “wealthy” family), you’re more likely to attend university, get a well-paying job, etc. If you come from a worse-off family you’re less likely to do those things. Upbringing and socioeconomic class are still a huge factor in how wealthy one ends up (even excluding inheritance entirely), showing that in the vast majority of cases people tend not to be self-made, but products of their environment.')
