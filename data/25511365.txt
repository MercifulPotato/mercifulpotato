Even in their role as &quot;company&#x27;s lawyer&quot;, they could talk to you both about how the existing agreements affect the resignation, firing, or mutually-agreed exit of a founder, and discuss ideas that help the company survive &amp; thrive past that event.<p>Though, to actually negotiate (&amp; legally execute) a durable exit agreement, you&#x27;d likely want a separate lawyer on your side. (And, given the values implied by the outside investor&#x27;s price-paid, &amp; your estimation of future revenues, spending a few $thousand here could save $tens-of-thousands or more later.)')
