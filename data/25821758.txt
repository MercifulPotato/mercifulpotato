Item(by='jokethrowaway', descendants=None, kids=[25822597, 25822264], score=None, time=1610978030, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25820955, text='The majority of &quot;fake news&quot; are factual news described from a partial point of view and with a political spin.<p>Even fact checkers are not immune to this and brand other news as true or false not based on facts but based on the political spin they favour.<p>Fake news is a vastly overstated problem.\nThanks to internet, we now have a wider breadth of political news and opinions and it&#x27;s easy to label everything-but-your-side as fake news.<p>There are a few patently false lies on the internet which are taken as examples of fake news - but they have very few supporters.')
