Item(by='smt88', descendants=None, kids=[24919222], score=None, time=1603894768, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24918846, text='In defense of anyone managing WordPress, its database schema is not well-normalized or organized (last I checked, it still had something like 6 tables).<p>That scenario (in general, not referring specifically to WP anymore) makes database indexing&#x2F;optimization much more expensive at webhost scale, because you potentially have columns that range in size from empty to 1MB.<p>That&#x27;s why most WP users have gone deep down the rabbithole of view-level caching, because optimizing an uncached result is so much harder in that environment.')
