Item(by='fastball', descendants=None, kids=[24981962], score=None, time=1604424253, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24981623, text='The goal of the experiment is not to get a certain arbitrary number (the one Millikan came up with), the goal is to get the approximate value of the elementary charge.<p>Reproducible means that if you follow the method outlined by Millikan, you should get an approximate value for <i>e</i>. This is indeed the case, so the experiment is reproducible.<p>A non-reproducible experiment would be one where you follow Millikan&#x27;s method but get something that is nowhere close to being a value for <i>e</i>.')
