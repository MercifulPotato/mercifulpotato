Item(by='falcolas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603894671, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24918635, text='MySQL (and PostgreSQL, for that matter) isn&#x27;t bad at handling queries, when the tables are tuned for those queries. People don&#x27;t do this well (if at all), and few databases are capable of automatically tuning tables (creating indices), since they require resources, and can have tradeoffs between read and write performance.<p>Properly tuned, a database is able to handle millions of requests per <i>second</i>.')
