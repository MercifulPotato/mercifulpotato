It cannot <i>prevent</i> parties from illegitimate sharing of my data, but it does give them the <i>option</i> to hand over control to me. There are lots of companies that currently hold data on us but for whom that data is not their primary competency, and they only need a small nudge (like GDPR) to make having the customer responsible for that data an attractive proposition.')
