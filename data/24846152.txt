Item(by='sjwright', descendants=None, kids=[24847687, 24846270], score=None, time=1603272865, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846116, text='The problem comes when anyone thinks the ORM vs No ORM debate has a single answer. For me it&#x27;s a simple test:<p>• Your data makes sense as objects;<p>• Your data is small enough to fit in memory;<p>• Most of your operations are CRUD.<p>If you can answer all three with yes, then use an ORM or some other kind of SQL abstraction layer. Otherwise don&#x27;t.')
