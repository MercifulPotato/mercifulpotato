Item(by='candiodari', descendants=None, kids=[25256023], score=None, time=1606755514, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25255540, text='The full chain is DNA -&gt; mRNA -&gt; Ribosome -&gt; tRNA combinations -&gt; amino acid chain -&gt; protein.<p>It&#x27;s true that in nature there are many steps between DNA and proteins (this list doesn&#x27;t even include the steps that mediate the translation, ie. start it, stop it, slow it down, ...), but the structure of a protein is fully determined by the DNA code.<p>Protein folding is about you start from the DNA code that is fed into the ribosome ignoring all the meta information, and come up with an atomic model (VERY long list like &quot;H atom at 3.27,2.17,12.18, C atom at 2.87, 2.19, 12.33, ...&quot;). Now there&#x27;s a million niceties we&#x27;ve discovered to make this problem simpler and nicer looking, but that&#x27;s what it boils down to.')
