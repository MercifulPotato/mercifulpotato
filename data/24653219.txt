Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=[24656643], score=None, time=1601571077, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24648698, text='Yes, really only D and Rust, nothing else.<p>If you think other language X supports RAII, you don&#x27;t really understand it. It&#x27;s not really compatible with obligate-GC. Finalizers, in particular, are not it.<p>The thing about destructors is that reclaiming memory is only their most trivial use. In C++, as in D and Rust, and Haskell, too, catching errors is the type system&#x27;s most trivial use. It does the heavy lifting.<p>If your program isn&#x27;t putting the type system to work, you&#x27;re toiling away with a screwdriver while people around you are using impact drivers and forklifts.')
