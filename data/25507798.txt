Item(by='hansvm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608653918, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25504311, text='Lots of other factors too, but look closely at your word choices -- &quot;wealth&quot; vs &quot;money.&quot; Wealth is mostly tied up in non-money assets (not that it necessarily has to be, but that&#x27;s the state of things), whereas poor people have some small amount of cash, wages which don&#x27;t track with inflation, and rent and expenses which do. As money is devalued, non-money assets continue to hold their value (increasing when denominated in dollars), and every financial instrument used by anyone at or below the low&#x2F;middle middle class is royally shafted in comparison.')
