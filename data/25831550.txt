Item(by='Lio', descendants=None, kids=[25831630], score=None, time=1611048544, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25809846, text='In my favourite sport, Cycling, suffering has long been venerated.<p>The imagery includes stories of Hinault riding through a snow-storm with frostbite.<p>Or Pantani fighting his inner daemons (a battle he sadly later lost) to summit a mountain with his arms held wide like “Christ on the cross”.<p>The great Irish rider Sean Kelly once pulled down his shorts to show a journalist a saddle sore the size of an egg when asked why he wasn’t as good as expected.<p>I often speculated if it’s a coincidence that Cycling as a sport is traditionally most popular in catholic countries such as Italy, Spain, France and Belgium.')
