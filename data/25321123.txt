Item(by='dreamcompiler', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607230171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25320241, text='The fully-general continuations that call&#x2F;cc creates require copying chunks of the stack to the heap for storage and back again when they&#x27;re used. (Or pointing the stack pointer at the heap, which I find even more cringe-inducing.) They&#x27;re memory hogs whose lifetimes are hard to reason about and they provide relatively little benefit to most programs, which is why Common Lisp doesn&#x27;t provide them.')
