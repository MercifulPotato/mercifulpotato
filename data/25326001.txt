Item(by='phkahler', descendants=None, kids=[25326056], score=None, time=1607283367, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325426, text='Very interesting, but in the end this violates many good programming practices. The author is going to end up with a single type that can represent points, lines, rotations, planes, and probably more. The same operations will do different things based on which of those things you give them. It&#x27;s important that the compiler can figure it all out because the programmer won&#x27;t be able to tell what a*b does by looking at their code.<p>Not sure if I should be impressed or horrified.  ;-)')
