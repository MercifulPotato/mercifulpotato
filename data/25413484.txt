Item(by='uuoc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607919385, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25411824, text='In many jurisdictions there is also a legal minimum speed stated within the statutes, often based on some value below the posted limit.  They are often unknown to most drivers (and cyclists) because the minimum speeds are almost always not placed on signage on the roadway.  But legally, there is often a minimum speed as well as the more well known and visible maximum speed.')
