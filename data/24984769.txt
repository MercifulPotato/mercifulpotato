Item(by='analog31', descendants=None, kids=[24986367], score=None, time=1604442658, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24984688, text='While their use is hopefully dwindling, I expect there will be a long tail.<p>In my household, the printer gets used for a couple of things. The first is sheet music. Musicians are starting to use a big tablet screen, with a foot pedal to turn the pages, but adoption will be slow. Musicians seem innately impatient with technologies that don&#x27;t just work instantly when you pull them out of the box.<p>The other thing is documents to read for school, which is still more ergonomic when done with paper.<p>At the office, I suspect nobody needs to print any more, because nobody reads any more. I can&#x27;t count the number of times I&#x27;ve hit &quot;send&quot; on a report that I was supposed to write, and a manager will be in my cube a few minutes later asking me if I could just summarize it for them. The first paragraph in the report is a summary.')
