Item(by='vharuck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605027046, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25047160, text='From TFA:<p>&gt;The study, published in The Lancet Psychiatry journal, analysed electronic health records of 69 million people in the United States, including more than 62,000 cases of COVID-19. The findings are likely to be the same for those afflicted by COVID-19 worldwide, the researchers said<p>&gt;In the three months following testing positive for COVID-19, 1 in 5 survivors were recorded as having a first time diagnosis of anxiety, depression or insomnia. This was about twice as likely as for other groups of patients in the same period, the researchers said.<p>So still a doubling when compared to other people living in a pandemic society.')
