Item(by='donw', descendants=None, kids=[25174346], score=None, time=1606009733, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25174126, text='Inconsistent definitions or methods render statistical comparisons meaningless.<p>You need that historical baseline to assess the current situation.<p>To provide an extreme example, as extremes are often useful in illustrating failure cases, it would be trivial to say that the murder rate in the US has dropped by nigh-on 100%. Simply redefine &quot;murder&quot; to mean &quot;persons killed by a woman wearing a blue hat on the third Tuesday of February&quot;.')
