Item(by='darkerside', descendants=None, kids=[24935436, 24935563, 24934951], score=None, time=1604001248, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24934856, text='Just like in real life, I think the answer is not prevention but accountability. The only thing stopping a random person from bashing in my head on the sidewalk (besides moral decency) is their understanding of the consequences. They can do it, but they&#x27;ll go to jail for a long, long time.<p>Similarly, the answer on the net is not to stop bad actors from being bad. It&#x27;s to punish and hold accountable those that do bad things. That avoids the &quot;thoughtcrime&quot; problem, and makes space for actual freedom.<p>The only thing we have to lose is privacy for domain owners. Seems like a trade-off worth considering.')
