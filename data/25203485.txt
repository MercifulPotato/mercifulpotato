Item(by='bhaak', descendants=None, kids=[25204684, 25215818], score=None, time=1606255476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25201444, text='I wouldn&#x27;t call Bitcoin&#x27;s POW as &quot;trust in algorithm runtime on modern hardware&quot;.<p>Just think about it. The algorithm can be changed by anybody. What does prevent the miners from changing the source code to allow them double spending or even extra coins?<p>But if they do that and don&#x27;t have a significant hashrate that do the same, they are on a blockchain fork that is worthless to anybody else.<p>So we trust in the economic game theory that miners don&#x27;t tamper with the code.')
