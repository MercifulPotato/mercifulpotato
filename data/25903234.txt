Ships benefit in this regard from going much slower. They also benefit from floating by natural buoyancy rather than by burning fuel. Containers in particular are cost-effective because they are packed and stacked very densely -- a passenger ship would have to be uncomfortably cramped indeed to even approach that efficiency.')
