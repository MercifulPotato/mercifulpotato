Item(by='Reelin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601679320, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24667338, text='I think tortious interference (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tortious_interference" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tortious_interference</a>) requires having the specific intention of causing the violation to occur.
