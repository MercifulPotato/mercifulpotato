If your company is profitable, every dollar of infrastructure costs cut is a dollar more profit. For a company still working on a runway, every dollar cut from your infrastructure is more time you can spend working on a product.<p>It&#x27;s also important to note that those efficiencies <i>don&#x27;t go away</i> on their own. It&#x27;s more profit&#x2F;runway until you retire that code.<p>Opportunity costs exist, but so does spending your money wisely.')
