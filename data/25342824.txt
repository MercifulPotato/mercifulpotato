The original idea was to get travel to orbit almost on level of airliners, reasonably priced and with short turnaround times, a mission every week or so.<p>The final design of the space shuttle was not able to support this kind of use. Very expensive and refurbishment demanded a lot of human work. So it sapped a lot of money and workforce from the space program.')
