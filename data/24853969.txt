There&#x27;s a reason that all tv show episodes aren&#x27;t just one long episode. So why not push the boundaries of that and break up a normal tv show episode into smaller ones?<p>It seems there are 3 different types of videos with decreasing segment (or episode) lengths: movies, tv shows, bite-sized shows. Each has its own place for user needs. I think sometimes people don&#x27;t want to (or don&#x27;t have time) watch a full movie, so they opt for a tv episode. Similarly, sometimes they don&#x27;t want to watch a full tv episode.')
