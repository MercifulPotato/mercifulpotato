Item(by='colejohnson66', descendants=None, kids=[25509467, 25511822], score=None, time=1608650913, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25506868, text='Also, QR codes don’t work too well with damaged or faded paper. Think receipts. 1D barcodes work much better there as there’s redundancy in the second direction. Basically, QR codes do have the advantage in the density department, but are more complicated (relatively) to parse and more susceptible to damage. Tradeoffs that depend on your situation.')
