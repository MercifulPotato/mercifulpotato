Item(by='Schiendelman', descendants=None, kids=[24979799], score=None, time=1604405761, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24977283, text='Comparing US infrastructure to infrastructure in the developing world requires a pile of caveats.<p>The biggest reason for the difference you’re pointing out is safety. A mezzanine creates  safety in two directions. First, as a gathering area with likely positive pressure ventilation in case of a train fire. Second, a refuge for incidents on the surface.<p>The developing world generally isn’t planning for either in their infrastructure.')
