Item(by='mullingitover', descendants=None, kids=[24769616], score=None, time=1602618105, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24769318, text='&gt; I always understood that for a telephoto lens you wanted the f-stop number to be higher, so that the image is clearer since the subject is at a greater distance.<p>You want the lowest f-stop number possible for telephoto, so you have more available light hitting the sensor. This allows you to get a higher shutter speed. The higher shutter speeds compensate for the exaggerated shake you get from the longer lens.')
