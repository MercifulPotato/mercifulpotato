Item(by='kamilner', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603577371, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24881174, text='&gt; Am I also reading it right that the author is saying the axiom of choice is equivalent to the statement that every vector space has a finite basis?<p>Not quite. The axiom of choice is equivalent to saying every vector space has a Hamel basis, which is to say every element can be represented as a finite combination of elements of the Hamel basis. It doesn&#x27;t imply that the Hamel basis is finite itself.')
