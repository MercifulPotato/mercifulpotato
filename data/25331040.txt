AMD&#x27;s Duron platform was excellent for this. Not suitable for gaming, but fine for daily computing. Big heat sinks with heat pipes work pretty well. Big, slow fans when there&#x27;s no way around them. Plastic rivets instead of screws and rubber washers manage to keep vibration noise down (fans, spinning disks).')
