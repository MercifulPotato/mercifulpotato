Item(by='moonchild', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610091722, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25681362, text='&gt; After executing the nop instruction the PC will be advanced by 1, because that&#x27;s the size of nop and advancing it by 1 causes it to point to the next instruction<p>Nit: the IP will be advanced by 1 <i>before</i> the nop is executed.
