However, this amount is proportional to the value of Government revenue received from Crown Estates.<p>This is in keeping with the agreement between King George III and Parliament to give up revenue from the then unprofitable Crown Lands (now more precisely termed The Crown Estate[1]) in return for a Government stipend.
