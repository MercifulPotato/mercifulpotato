Item(by='learnstats2', descendants=None, kids=[24856318, 24853701, 24853651], score=None, time=1603315550, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24851141, text='When I was working in research, we had a journal group which each week critiqued the statistics done in scientific journals - most often contemporary articles from Science or Nature.<p>As I recall, every single one of the articles we looked at would have failed our &quot;Red Team&quot; standards.<p>I don&#x27;t (only) mean that they failed by making trivial errors of statistics - I mean a small team of researchers could find fundamental errors that potentially or likely undermined or contradicted the whole article. It&#x27;s not surprising that something like 50% of the conclusions are not reproducible.')
