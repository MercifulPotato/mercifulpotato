Item(by='petertodd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604159071, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24952273, text='If you want to try Lightning out - and see for yourself whether or not it is vaporware - the easiest way to do that is to get a mobile Lightning wallet such as Eclair (fairly easy) or BlueWallet (easiest) from your appstore. As I mentioned, ManyVids accepts Lightning payments for a real product, as does <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bitrefill.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bitrefill.com&#x2F;</a> (phone refills, gift cards, etc). For testing with smaller amounts, <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yalls.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yalls.org&#x2F;</a> will let you buy access to blog posts for trivial amounts of money (eg one cent).')
