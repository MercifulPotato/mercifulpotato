The rebate scheme was really central to the issue.<p>Then, they didn&#x27;t clearly say &quot;you can&#x27;t install software&quot; or &quot;everyone who wants to install software has to do X&quot; they just made installing Netscape or removing IE functionally broken.<p>That&#x27;s really different than &quot;everyone who wants to make money through my platform has to pay me 30%&quot;.<p>The place Apple might actually have trouble would be Spotify - where Apple is competing with their product, allowing subscriptions in-app for their own platform, but not for Spotify unless they cough up 30% or whatever.')
