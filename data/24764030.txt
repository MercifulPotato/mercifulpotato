Item(by='selfhoster11', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602583962, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24755614, text='Here&#x27;s a point that I don&#x27;t see raised anywhere in the comments: short-term rented items are stateless. Wear, tear and failure is undone by the rental company, but so are any customisation, decoration, or adjustments to make them better<p>If you need a car adjusted for the needs of disabled persons (whether it&#x27;s yourself or a member of your family), or if you want to put up a picture frame or a poster in a short-term rented room&#x2F;office, then those adjustments will be treated as deviations from the norm, and &quot;corrected&quot; at the end of your rental term if you leave them in place.<p>Part of human condition is the need to self-express, and you cannot really do that with short-term rented items (and to some extent, even long-term rented items). This can discourage personalisation and customising your environment altogether, which may feel like being in a prison.')
