Item(by='Arubis', descendants=None, kids=[24949336], score=None, time=1604108356, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24948247, text='At the risk of being too reductive, it strikes me that it’s most appropriate to let the public determine the strategy: the broad strokes and goals they want (clean, safe, low&#x2F;no carbon energy sources), but to defer to actual trained experts on the tactics: the generation mix and transmission tech.<p>This isn’t to say there should be no supervision or accountability; more that the elites are called so for a reason (a better word would be “experts”), and that there’s otherwise a risk of micromanagement and all that it entails.')
