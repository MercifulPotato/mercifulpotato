Item(by='Jedd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603154116, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24832123, text='I know that historically, newspaper headlines were lengthier, and quite likely to be a grammatically valid sentence.<p>However, periods were never used to end them, as absent a period it invited the reader to actually engage with the rest of the story.  (This story may be apocryphal, but it sounds reasonable.)<p>Perhaps there&#x27;s a little of that going on with people&#x27;s communications where they wish to imply there exists more than they are saying (which may or may not ultimately be delivered to the reader).<p>For my part - as an over-30 non-American - I&#x27;ll stick with standard punctuation.')
