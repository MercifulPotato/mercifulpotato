Item(by='nextos', descendants=None, kids=[25198347], score=None, time=1606224800, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25197269, text='In my opinion, a promising avenue for programming would be DSLs with restricted semantics + SAT&#x2F;SMT solving to deliver lightweight formal methods.<p>So, instead of programming in Turing-complete languages, one creates many specialized DSLs with just enough expressiveness. And, in parallel, some automated formal methods. The combination of a DSL and SAT&#x2F;SMT makes this feasible, which contrasts with automated theorem proving on Turing-complete languages.<p>Racket seems like a good platform to do this.')
