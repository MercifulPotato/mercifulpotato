Item(by='combatentropy', descendants=None, kids=[25564770], score=None, time=1609198984, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564629, text='But this highlights the difference between the two devices, stopwatches and timers.<p>A stopwatch counts up. Therefore, it makes sense to use the floor (4.99 is still &quot;4&quot;, not yet &quot;5&quot;).<p>A timer counts down. Therefore, it makes sense to use the ceiling (3.01 is still &quot;4&quot;, not yet &quot;3&quot;).<p>In fact this was a turning point in the original article: &quot;rounding down . . . makes a lot of sense when counting up. . . . But for a countdown timer, this is counterintuitive.&quot;')
