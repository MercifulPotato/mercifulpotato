Item(by='riverdroid', descendants=None, kids=[25545311], score=None, time=1609004807, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25544472, text='There might be some software issues at play with some modern AWD systems that I am not aware of, but it&#x27;s traditionally a mechanical issue with 4WD and AWD. 4WD has a physical link, so having two different diameters linked stresses the links and tears the rubber. With AWD there is a differential, and different diameters cause mechanical stress and wear in a mechanical diff.')
