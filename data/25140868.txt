Item(by='pja', descendants=None, kids=[25144549], score=None, time=1605725993, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137585, text='My personal comparison is between the HumbleBundle store and the HumbleBundle payments widget.<p>The rake on the former, where HumbleBundle brings the customer? 25%<p>The rake on the latter, where the game dev embeds the widget on their own website &amp; HB handles the payments infrastructure for them? 5%<p>For App devs able to drive customer engagement directly themselves, Apple is effectively taking an extra ~20% of profit for themselves. Apple is treating every sale as if they were the ones delivering the customer. For $1 shovelware Apps this seems fair enough. For franchises where the Dev has an already established customer base through their own marketing efforts it seems completely egregious.')
