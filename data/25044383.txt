Item(by='jonnypotty', descendants=None, kids=[25044447], score=None, time=1605003736, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25036036, text='IDEs are one of the biggest barriers to programmer development, flexibility and knowledge. They try to insulate you from your actual environment and encourage lazy practices and thinking.<p>I learnt java in eclipse and it was dreadful. Code completion stops you learning, IDE errors and auto fix stops you understanding compiler errors. build shortcuts stop you understanding how javac and maven builds work.<p>In short they take all of the available deep knowledge  your programming language and environment, hide it from you and then pretend it&#x27;s a good idea to replace this with a load of superficial knowledge about how to click the right buttons in some bad software.')
