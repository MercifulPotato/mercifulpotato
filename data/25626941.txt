My first computer was an Acorn Electron. IIRC the user guide included things about IRQs, 6502 assembly language, and even details like using &#x27;two pass assembly&#x27; to assemble a program which referred to a something before that thing had been defined.<p>My first PC came with instructions on how to connect the keyboard, mouse and monitor. I recall thinking it was really strange that something could be sold without instructions on all its functions. Until then every electronic device I&#x27;d bought came with instructions that were &#x27;complete&#x27; in the sense that everything you could do in normal use was documented.')
