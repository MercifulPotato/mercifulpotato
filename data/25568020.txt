Item(by='ddalex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609232532, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25557985, text='Portability was a long desired attribute of computer software up until it turns out that it doesn&#x27;t matter at all.<p>When software was deployed on floppy disks and there were 10k architectures going around, portability was critical to expand the market reach of any distributed software.<p>But in the modern age when the software runs &quot;in the cloud&quot; and there are dominant platforms out there, turns out that portability doesn&#x27;t matter - nobody is changing platforms when they don&#x27;t need to.<p>So this makes this achievement a pretty cool demo without any industry impact, since people don&#x27;t distribute C binaries anymore.<p>The idea behind it and the technology developed is pretty cool :)')
