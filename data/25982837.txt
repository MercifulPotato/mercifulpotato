Just the number of products on the market is huge and changes all the time.<p>The law therefore specifies things in general terms and uses &#x27;reasonableness&#x27;, or &quot;fitness for purpose&quot;, or what would be expected by an &quot;average bystander&quot; (in English law), etc. in the same way this piece of legislation does.<p>This allows courts and juries to then make a judgement on the infinite number of particular cases that get before them.')
