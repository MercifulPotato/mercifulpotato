Item(by='cesarb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610570345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25767132, text='In Java it&#x27;s worse, for SimpleDateFormat (the most often used date formatter in Java) YYYY (uppercase) is &quot;week year&quot; (strftime %G), the correct way to get the year (strftime %Y) is yyyy (lowercase). It&#x27;s very easy to get it wrong; the correct way to get the full ISO timestamp is &quot;yyyy-MM-dd&#x27;T&#x27;HH:mm:ss.SSSXXX&quot;.')
