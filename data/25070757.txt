Tell departments that at least XX% of their hires are expected to be woman, or older than 50, or racial minorities, or whatever else. Set the percentage based on the pool of applicants, minus a bit to allow wiggle room. (And if there are legal blockers, let&#x27;s change the law!)<p>It <i>feels</i> wrong to me too, I know it does! But if hiring happens based on gut, and if our gut is biased, then we need a way to <i>systematically</i> counteract our biases.')
