Item(by='onecommentman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609466887, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25599703, text='May I also recommend burning a copy or two on CD or DVD, as well as keeping a copy on memory card.  Even if many computers drop their optical drives, a lot will still be around, DVD players can display picture files, etc.<p>If they are family pictures, send a copy to all other family members with a file documenting what the picture files are, who sent them, when.<p>Get a photo printer and print out those valuable enough that you would be willing to put them in a physical album and document them.<p>Ridiculous overkill?  No, not at the cost of storage media.  Someone 50 years from now will be very glad you did.  Once the pictures are gone, they are gone...')
