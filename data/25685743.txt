And that causes a bullet item to appear.<p>That&#x27;s not really how it works.<p>The same type of data can appear in multiple categories, and there are exceptions that mean you don&#x27;t have to list certain usages.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;developer.apple.com&#x2F;app-store&#x2F;app-privacy-details&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;developer.apple.com&#x2F;app-store&#x2F;app-privacy-details&#x2F;</a><p>For example, if your app doesn&#x27;t ordinarily collect email addresses, but you have a contact form for support purposes that does collect them, you don&#x27;t need to list email address as being collected.<p>The privacy label doesn&#x27;t really cover the app - it covers what the services that the app connects to do. An entirely offline app shouldn&#x27;t need to list anything on the label, even if it does use APIs like location.')
