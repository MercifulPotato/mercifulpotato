Item(by='alexchamberlain', descendants=None, kids=[24805581], score=None, time=1602885532, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24803687, text='CTEs effectively act as optimisation barriers; up to loading all the rows, there is essentially no push down of conditions into the CTE. This is contrasted with a sub query that will respect pushed down conditions. That being said, a CTE may be beneficial if it produces a small number of rows that are used a lot, or if you&#x27;re simply using it for readability purposes.')
