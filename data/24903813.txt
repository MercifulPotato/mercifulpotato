Item(by='rmrfstar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603763929, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24903672, text='I can&#x27;t stress this enough: talk to a lawyer.  Your local bar association can put you in touch with one anonymously.  You won&#x27;t regret it.<p>One of the things the lawyer will tell you is how to identify and react to any prospective retaliation.  Should things go sideways, they will maximize your severance and preserve your references.<p>You can also check with your lawyer to see if you live in a &quot;one party consent&quot; state that allows you to tape record conversations you are a party to.  There are cheap pen recorders on Amazon.')
