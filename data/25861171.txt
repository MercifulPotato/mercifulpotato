I helped an urologist typeset his PhD thesis about prostate cancer treatment in TeX (he used some math equations within) and he told me the same.<p>Some cancers are aggressive and some are slooooow. In younger patients, cancers tend to move fast and kill fast. But a slow prostate cancer in a 70 y.o. may be better left as it is, because the risks of the operation may actually be higher.<p>This, of course, is a difficult judgment call and belongs to the experts only.')
