One month later I was on medication. A few more months and I am in weekly counselling. For me personally, the positive changes from medication and counseling have been dramatic and immediate.<p>After only 4 months, my coworkers impression of me is starting to shift for the better.<p>ADHD is a serious mental illness. I&#x27;m so thankful that the help I&#x27;m getting is working.<p>&quot;Taking Charge of Adult ADHD&quot; by Barkley, helped me understand ADHD and how I should deal with it.')
