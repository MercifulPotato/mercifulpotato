But they are. They attain positions of power.<p>Through reflection you may come to realize they are  among people you consider friends or family.<p>You can’t “fix” them, but you can learn how to handle or avoid them.<p>Sometimes, if a person looks inward and into their past they will find a pattern of people who have taken advantage of them.<p>If that’s the case there may be work to do, like:<p>- confront these past abuses<p>- recognize those that are ongoing and how to navigate them<p>- build and practice skills in recognizing and dealing with new toxic people going forward<p>Life is hard and we get taught things unevenly.<p>It is not a wrong to be ignorant of an important life skill.<p>And even when you have read all of the evidence and logic needed to recognize and change your circumstances, some wait far too long, or never do.<p>I do not blame folks in these positions, my heart goes out to them. Because I have been there.')
