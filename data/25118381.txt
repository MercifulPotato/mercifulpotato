Item(by='jchook', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605568948, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25114048, text='DKIM was not designed to authenticate emails far into the future.<p>In fact, it does not authenticate any emails without a corresponding public key <i>currently</i> published to DNS. It provides specifically for &quot;empty&quot; or revoked keys to avoid such retro-validation.<p>Seems the central thesis is that because these messages are patently no longer authenticated by DKIM, we should eliminate any remaining hope of them being construed as authenticated by DKIM.')
