Item(by='jude-', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610730471, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25787678, text='The system selects one of a set of competing miners to produce the next block via a VRF whose state is recorded to Bitcoin OP_RETURNs.  There is at most one Stacks block per Bitcoin block, and the probability of winning is proportional to the relative amount of BTC burnt or transferred.<p>The system&#x27;s design is well-documented via the SIP process.  See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;stacksgov&#x2F;sips" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;stacksgov&#x2F;sips</a> (you&#x27;ll want to read SIP-001 and SIP-007 in particular).')
