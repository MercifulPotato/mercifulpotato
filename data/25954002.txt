Item(by='dcolkitt', descendants=None, kids=[25954684, 25954580], score=None, time=1611895193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25953688, text='Toxicity is a well-defined technical term in market microstructure literature. It basically means whether the direction someone trades in predicts the short-term direction of the market.<p>It&#x27;s not a moral judgement. So to answer your question, hedge fund order flow is considered highly toxic. Which is why Citadel Securities pays to interact with non-toxic retail flow, instead of toxic hedge fund flow.')
