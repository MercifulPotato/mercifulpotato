Item(by='notfbi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608228191, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25457342, text='The 34 million calculation is only for &quot;certain&quot; orders. I think this means some trades would be advantageous under the RH model (small trades where a $5 fixed commission would overwhelm any percentage), some trades would be disadvantageous under the RH model (very large value trades), and they only summed the comparative fees on the disadvantageous trades.<p>This might make sense: if users were knowledgeable and had multiple brokers, they may execute at the better trade-specific broker for each trade, so adding up just the potential bads gives some perspective. But on the other hand, it&#x27;s not a perfect indicator for Robinhood&#x27;s net gain as its omitting trades where Robinhood&#x27;s structure leads to lower commission.')
