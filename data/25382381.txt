Item(by='chrisco255', descendants=None, kids=[25382661], score=None, time=1607661613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25380782, text='Your body and brain are a collection of cells. None of those cells are what we would call intelligent on their own. Intelligence is an emergent property of a system of cells. Ant and bee colonies act as superorganisms (since they all depend on their queen for reproduction) and therefore the apparent intelligence emerges at that social level.')
