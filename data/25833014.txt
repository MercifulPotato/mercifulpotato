Item(by='pleeb', descendants=None, kids=[25833827], score=None, time=1611061067, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25832765, text='After extensive research, this was ultimately my conclusion: Going iOS. My rationale was that it is significantly easier to de-Google an iPhone then it is to de-Google and android phone. I can also lock down an iPhone more easily (Disabling iCloud, using DNSCloak, etc).<p>There are plenty of projects that aim to remove Google from an android, but I question their security; even if the privacy is good, there could be exploits or unpatched vulnerabilities.')
