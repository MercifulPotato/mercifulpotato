Item(by='red_admiral', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603008620, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24815896, text='In crypto, eprint.iacr.org is where you want your paper to be - it&#x27;s their own version of arXiv, so I&#x27;m almost a bit surprised this isn&#x27;t published there.<p>If your paper is good, on eprint and you spread the word a bit, then it will get citations - often more than if you publish in a 2nd tier conference - and when it&#x27;s popular enough then eventually one of the main conferences (journals are less popular in crypto) will accept it in some form. This happened among other things to several of Dan Bernstein&#x27;s papers (the guy who invented curve25519 among other things).')
