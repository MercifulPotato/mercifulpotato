Item(by='qznc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605985769, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170546, text='You have to distinguish between the incentives for the company and individuals.<p>For example, it makes little sense for a company to use a different web framework every year. It makes sense for individual developers to accumulate a large list for their resume.<p>Likewise, it makes not sense for a company to optimize for a large headcount. It does make sense for an individual manager because leading 100 people looks better than 20 on his resume.')
