You end up with a winner takes all market not too dissimilar to the music industry on streaming services. Instead of it being something that adventurous people do to finance a fun life in their early twenties, it&#x27;s going to be just another McJob soon.<p>All of this being said, I still don&#x27;t understand on an emotional level what could bring so many people to pay as much $15&#x2F;month for such subscriptions.
