Item(by='boplicity', descendants=None, kids=[25928065, 25928059], score=None, time=1611754093, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25922953, text='The term &quot;cancel culture&quot; is extremely ironic. The implication of the term is that people should not be &quot;cancelled&quot; for expressing their views. And yet, backing out of a book deal, for example, <i>is</i> an expression of a view.<p>In practice, the term is used to de-legitimize some opinions, while upholding others.<p>The term &quot;cancel culture&quot; <i>is</i> cancel culture.')
