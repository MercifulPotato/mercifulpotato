If users have already attempted to tip you within the past 90 days, you may find that rewards arrive shortly after you sign up.<p>Tips only leave user wallets when their intended recipient is verified. They will remain in the user&#x27;s wallet for up to 90 days awaiting the verification of their intended recipient. After that time, they are folded back into the user&#x27;s balance, and able to be given to another creators.')
