Item(by='aeyes', descendants=None, kids=[24713135], score=None, time=1602104480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24712573, text='There is another reason: People understand you, interrupting with corrections hurts the conversation. I often ask for corrections but nobody ever does it. When I&#x27;m unsure how to say something I sometimes ask to get some feedback. Sometimes I only understand 70% of what a person is saying (mostly due to dialects), I rarely need to ask them to repeat it because the brain is capable of piecing it together.<p>Another problem is that unlearning something you always did wrong is extremely hard. You need to get it right from the beginning.<p>That&#x27;s the beauty of language school, they point out your mistakes early and you can learn from the mistakes of other students in your group. If you take the time and take real classes, you&#x27;ll have that tight feedback loop you need.')
