Item(by='chriswarbo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603709092, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24893762, text='Structural editing can be used in many languages, not just Lispy ones (although it&#x27;s most powerful in Lispy languages).<p>I use Emacs to write Haskell, Scala, Python, Bash, etc. and use at least some structural editing operations all the time, e.g. ctrl-right turns &#x27;(a, b), c&#x27; into &#x27;(a, b, c)&#x27; and ctrl-left goes the other way.')
