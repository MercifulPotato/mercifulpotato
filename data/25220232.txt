Item(by='dTal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606400625, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25220146, text='There&#x27;s nothing wrong with solving legal, cultural, and regulatory problems with technical solutions. It happens all the time.<p>British colonists solved the legal, cultural, and regulatory problem of unfair taxation with the technical solution of firearms.<p>The legal, cultural, and regulatory problem of a literate elite dominating culture was very satisfyingly solved by the technical solution of the printing press.<p>Technology is socially disruptive. Governments and corporations are more than willing to take advantage of this, with CCTV, Great Firewalls, adtech. But they aren&#x27;t omnipotent - why should we give up on fighting back?')
