Item(by='nradov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610574752, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25768298, text='Antibody tests only give us <i>estimates</i> of the number of people who have had COVID-19. Some patients, especially the asymptomatic ones, either never produce detectible levels of antibodies or have them fade away quickly. We can get somewhat more accurate numbers by also checking immune system memory cells, but that&#x27;s a more complex assay and difficult to do in large studies.')
