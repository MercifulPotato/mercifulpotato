Item(by='mumblemumble', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608247698, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25459939, text='It sounds like you have misunderstood the purpose of a privacy policy. It is very rare that I encounter one that is designed to protect the user&#x27;s privacy. Far more often, it&#x27;s there to protect the company. &quot;I have read and agree to the privacy policy,&quot; is a coded way of saying, &quot;I have read and agree to waive my claims to privacy, as outlined in the privacy policy.&quot;')
