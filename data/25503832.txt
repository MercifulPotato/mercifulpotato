Item(by='steve_adams_86', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608621698, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25502337, text='What many neglect as well is that in many cases, software is regulated. I’ve worked on software where there were real laws about how and what I could produce, how the data must be managed and where, and what degree of quality and testing must be maintained.<p>I had to write an oath to my province to execute on that faithfully. I was working with private health records. I know lives weren’t at risk, but it took a lot of knowledge and resources to execute on that properly, and maintaining quality and privacy around those systems is genuinely very important.<p>I don’t call myself an engineer, but I don’t think my job is a joke either.')
