Item(by='zinekeller', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605797338, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25149855, text='Windows: Network Connectivity Status Indicator (NCSI) will ping www.msftncsi.com on older versions (8.1 and lower) or www.msftconnecttest.com on newer versions (10), and then prompts to open the web browser. Since IE&#x2F;Edge will be always there, it works.<p>macOS: same as iOS (a dedicated window pinging www.apple.com).<p>Firefox: Detects detectportal.firefox.com and asks the user if the want to access the captive portal.<p>Android: Detects connectivitycheck.android.com on Android 5 and connectivitycheck.gstatic.com on Android 6+. I don&#x27;t know if it applies to all phones, but I&#x27;m sure (that except for phones on Android One or Nexus&#x2F;Pixel) Samsung also does this for phones outside of China.')
