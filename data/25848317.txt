Item(by='tzs', descendants=None, kids=[25848435], score=None, time=1611160039, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25845919, text='Although doing it &quot;to keep numbers low&quot; doesn&#x27;t seem to me to be a good reason to do it, it is generally a good idea to have some hard stuff early in a field.<p>The idea is that if the hard stuff comes too late, people might not find out that they aren&#x27;t really suited to that field as a career until it is too late to change majors without having to take an extra year or two of college.<p>The intro class is too early for that, though, because CS is useful enough for other fields that many people (perhaps even a majority of people) taking intro CS don&#x27;t intended to actually go into CS. Better is to put it in the first class in a field that will mostly only be taken by people who are intending to go into that field.')
