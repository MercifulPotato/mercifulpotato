Item(by='ethbr0', descendants=None, kids=[24652565, 24651617], score=None, time=1601562516, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24650867, text='This is a simple problem, and there&#x27;s a simple solution.<p>Problem: Physical connectors previous encoded information about data&#x2F;power transfer mode support. When USB C unified the physical connector, that information was lost.<p>Solution: Mandate visually encoded supported standards on the USB cable in colored ring form, a la resistors.<p>Make them plastic, so you can snip them off if the aesthetic bothers. But I should be able to reach into a bin of different USB C cables, and determine what each cable can and can&#x27;t do.<p>The law here for specifications teams should be: assume the consumer is never going to read your spec. Their ability to use the technology shouldn&#x27;t be contingent on their doing so.')
