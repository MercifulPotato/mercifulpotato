Item(by='jedberg', descendants=None, kids=[25838781, 25838458, 25838467], score=None, time=1611087625, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25837378, text='&gt; Reddit content ranks really well on Google<p>The most public contribution I made to reddit&#x27;s codebase when I was there was the SEO features.  I did all the usual stuff like cleaning up title and meta tags, and adding a sitemap.  But the change that had the largest effect, by far, was adding the title of the story into the URL.  As soon as we launched that, our Google traffic shot up.<p>The way you know you have truly mastered SEO is when Google takes away your control of the crawl rate on your SEO control panel. Soon after that we had lunch a special set of servers that just serve requests for Google, because they were killing us by crawling years of old posts.')
