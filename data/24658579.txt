Item(by='cloverich', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601609851, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24658032, text='I&#x27;d counter: the right level of abstraction. If you have complex views that rely on a variety of properties, and you want performant updates, mutable change tracking automates the process of making it work at precisely the right level. The problem space that goes away is, in redux terminology, the need to make custom selectors as a performance optimization. Instead, you access the properties you need in your render functions, using vanilla js syntax, and mobx tracks it for you.')
