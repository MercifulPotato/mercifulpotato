Item(by='troydavis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611414338, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25882704, text='&gt; Based  on  15,000  survey  responses from working age Americans, carried out between May and October 2020, we estimate that  one  half  of  paid  hours  were  provided  from  home  during  this  period.  After  the  pandemic,  employers are planning for about 23 percent of paid days to be spent working from home, a four-fold  increase  relative  to  before  the  pandemic.  This  works  out  as  roughly  2  days  a  week  for  the  roughly 50% of employees than can work from home.')
