Item(by='d_silin', descendants=None, kids=[24716522, 24716419], score=None, time=1602135209, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24715786, text='A better solution is to have an <i>even</i> number of people, but have a tie-breaker vote token that is passed around between everyone in round-robin fashion.<p>In the event of the tie, the tie-breaker vote owner resolves it and passes the to token to the next person.<p>In this manner, the decision bias is averaged out between all of the board members.')
