Item(by='mytailorisrich', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607000564, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25286733, text='I think the somewhat confusing thing here is that Japanese can pronounce the same kanji in different ways, from &#x27;purely local Japanese&#x27; to variants of old Chinese pronunciation.<p>米国 &quot;beikoku&quot; sounds like a strange choice for &quot;America&quot;. But then in 亜米利加 it&#x27;s pronounced &quot;mei&quot;, which does indeed explain the choice better.<p>Chinese use 美 &#x27;mei&#x27; for America and so 美国 &#x27;meiguo&#x27; is the US.')
