Item(by='alextheparrot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611720380, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25922975, text='&gt; Learning from messing up does NOT come for free.<p>This is a bit further down in the thread, but really is the core point new engineers need to hear.<p>Many of us have sat through retrospectives after spending hours writing up what went wrong and even more hours fixing a problem.  Sometimes the problem has arisen because the person making changes should’ve asked more questions or looked for more answers regarding the system they were changing.<p>The only problem I’ve ever had with questions is when the same question is asked too many times.  That can mean a few different things, maybe that the team’s documentation is poor, but most of all it means that the person asking the question isn’t learning from the answer.')
