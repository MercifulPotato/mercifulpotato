Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608581464, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25498938, text='No, DMCA <i>weakens</i> the incentive for Twitch to implement a policy like this compared to copyright law outside of the DMCA. With the DMCA, serial offenders are just a bureaucratic headache as Twitch has to deal with takedowns. Without the DMCA, Twitch would be potentially liable for copyright infringement at the first offense by a streamer, and facing much greater risk of large damages for contributory infringement for continuing to fail to prevent repeat violations by known serial offenders.<p>The copyright law motivating the ban policy is the basic exclusive rights of copyright, not the DMCA.')
