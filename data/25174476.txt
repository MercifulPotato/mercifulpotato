Item(by='WalterBright', descendants=None, kids=[25174704], score=None, time=1606013103, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25174457, text='It&#x27;s a more meta observation that &quot;poverty&quot; has many subjective definitions. Certainly many (not all) people we would consider living in poverty today would be considered well off even 100 years ago.<p>For example, owning a functioning automobile was a huge luxury only for the rich 100 years ago.<p>For another example, the terms of the debate have shifted. It used to be about the objective state of the poor people. Today it is less about that and more about the subjective state of poor people relative to the state of wealthy people, i.e. &quot;inequality&quot;.')
