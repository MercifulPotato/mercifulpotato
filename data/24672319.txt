Item(by='thundergolfer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601735700, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671368, text='Enjoying the best of the past makes the best of the latest more enjoyable too.<p>Consumption of the best-ofs in a media category is an exercise in building media literacy. By listening to Pink Floyd you can get more out of Tame Impala.<p>Big media companies make more money from new than old, so are quite happy to have a consumer go through their life paying for potboiler ephemera. The consumer shouldn’t be happy with that though, as they’d have paid dearly in time and money for media that’s painfully beige compared to the best old stuff and also the best new stuff that they’d only have known to look for if they had built the media literacy.')
