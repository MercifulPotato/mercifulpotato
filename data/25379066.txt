Item(by='asciimike', descendants=None, kids=[25379376, 25379128], score=None, time=1607636241, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25378833, text='Moreover, once API calls are locked, what next? You can&#x27;t delete files, and even if you can delete them, you aren&#x27;t able to retrieve them before deletion... If a platform allows you to do those actions, then it&#x27;s rife for abuse, and at public cloud scale that ends up being a far, far bigger problem than the occasional blog post that ends up as a refund (because the other blog post is &quot;I got free storage forever with this one weird trick&quot;).<p>It&#x27;s really not a simple problem because the next action depends on the choice the developer wants to make: do they increase the budget or decrease usage, and no cloud provider wants to make this choice because no matter what the choice is it will be viewed as wrong. The best they can do is provide developers the best insight and tooling to make this choice themselves.')
