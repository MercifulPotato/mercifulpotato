Item(by='EvanWard97', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609970548, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25658642, text='Regardless of where anyone thinks the maximas are in software trade-off space, developers are going to experiment shipping  software at new points anyway, as markets at known points become saturated and exploration again becomes worthwhile in expectation.<p>The aspect of this collective optimization process that seems particularly helpful and tractable to focus on is ensuring that users know the risks and benefits of their various options.<p>I&#x27;m more interested in seeing web platforms point users to excellent, impartial 3rd party analyses of their options and their associated risks&#x2F;benefits, rather than stomp out innovation that some people clearly think is worth trying.')
