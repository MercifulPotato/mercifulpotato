Item(by='germinalphrase', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602184269, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24720951, text='Just to add on: the wavy hamon pattern is created by applying clay to the steel. When the blade is then quenched the clay slows the temperature change beneath it keeping that part of steel blade softer then the rapidly quenched cutting edge. The advantage of this was the soft spine would be tougher (less brittle) while the harder edge could be made sharper.')
