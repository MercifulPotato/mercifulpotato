Item(by='wtallis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608988354, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25543165, text='&gt; With this explanation it seems that if one wheel gets slightly stuck, the other wheel gets all the force, causing a dangerous spinning.<p>This <i>is</i> how a (open) differential behaves, though a spinning wheel isn&#x27;t so much dangerous as ineffective.
