Item(by='zarkov99', descendants=None, kids=[25182508], score=None, time=1606083154, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25180808, text='I think that perhaps we have not yet adjusted to the full implications of the democratization of speech. Extreme opinions, held by the tiniest, most unhinged minorities, get amplified precisely because they are so unusual. In other words, its not so much that violent, racist ideologies are on the rise, as much as we have access to the ramblings of the entire country and the ideas that most easily stand out in the noise are the horrific ones.')
