Item(by='peterhunt', descendants=None, kids=[25215272, 25215958], score=None, time=1606343719, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25214391, text='proof of work really doesn&#x27;t work well in practice. spammers have huge farms of compute, often on residential ips, and legit users are accessing the service from a device that is often power-constrained (like a phone). you end up either hugely penalizing legitimate users, or having to employ many of the standard antispam techniques (IP&#x2F;ISP reputation, captcha, rate limiting etc) on top, so the proof of work adds a lot less incremental value.')
