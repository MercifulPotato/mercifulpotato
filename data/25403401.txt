Item(by='tptacek', descendants=None, kids=[25403810, 25404612], score=None, time=1607822230, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25402360, text='Respectfully, I feel like you all are making up a taxonomy that feels right to you, but that is definitely not accepted by the vuln research field.<p>Further: this idea that &quot;research&quot; is something we have to valorize, and that you have to meet a public interest threshold to be worthy of it, is itself a standard to which the real world does not adhere. There are lots of different kinds of &quot;research&quot; out there; there are researchers who look for cures for cancer, and there are &quot;researchers&quot; who maintain stacks of index cards full of competitive market intelligence gleaned from press releases. The term &quot;researcher&quot; is about the kind of work you do, not the use to which it&#x27;s put.<p>Maybe I&#x27;m just old and not keeping up with what the kids these days are saying, but I don&#x27;t think &quot;bug prospector&quot; is a thing.')
