Item(by='moonchild', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605599016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25120987, text='Depends on what you mean by &#x27;classical disassembler&#x27;.  In many circles, disassembler refers not to something like objdump, but to a complete reverse-engineering framework that has more advanced tools for examining and reversing binaries (which may or may not include a decompiler).<p>Examples include ida, ghidra, binary ninja, hopper, and x64dbg; cutter is an opensource program with similar functionality to those.')
