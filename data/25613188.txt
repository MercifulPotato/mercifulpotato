I hand-edit each entry.<p>Sometimes, I copy and paste the changelog entry into the last commit of the release (but I make sure to have a short, succinct initial sentence, as that is what shows up in Git browser history).<p>The changelog may end up being a lot more &quot;generalized&quot; than the commit comments, though, as it&#x27;s meant to be read by users.<p>I appreciate the kinds of changelogs that you usually get with developer tools, where they are like encyclopedias.')
