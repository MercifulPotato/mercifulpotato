Item(by='garaetjjte', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601747572, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24673395, text='There are some normal trichromats that also better resolve luminance and S-cone signals, so it&#x27;s not necessarily mutually exclusive.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jov.arvojournals.org&#x2F;article.aspx?articleid=2135713" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jov.arvojournals.org&#x2F;article.aspx?articleid=2135713</a><p><i>Normal trichromats read Ishihara plates using their L- and M-cones. Red-green color vision defective observers rely on S-cone and luminance signals in reading the plates.
