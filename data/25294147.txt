Item(by='thrusong', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607026646, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25293981, text='Film rental has actually gone down a lot since the studios forced all the chains to go digital from 35mm film.<p>It wasn&#x27;t uncommon for 80-90 per cent of ticket sales to go to the studios in the first week of a blockbuster&#x27;s release, which would drop down to about 50 per cent in the fourth week or so. It was around 35 per cent for second run.<p>The average rental since the switch to digital has been about 50 per cent, although it&#x27;s been a few years now since I was last running projectors in a real cinema.')
