Item(by='fouc', descendants=None, kids=[25286987], score=None, time=1606967408, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25284803, text='For those that aren&#x27;t aware, he&#x27;s referring to the strategy known as &quot;Embrace, Extend, and Extinguish&quot;, likely coined at Microsoft in the early-90s.<p>First Chrome started out as a technically amazing, open source, standards compliant browser. Developers everywhere adopted it and promoted it to their friends and family.  Rapid adoption ensued.<p>Development tools became amazing, further spurring on developers to build websites for Chrome primarily.  Creating a vicious cycle, now others need Chrome to enjoy these websites.<p>Then gradually Chrome starts stripping away user freedom in the name of security and privacy.  Google added auto-update to force Chrome browser upgrades on every user.  This pushes the web into a monoculture where Google can further centralize and establish control.')
