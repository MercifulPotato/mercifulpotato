Item(by='tobr', descendants=None, kids=[24661136], score=None, time=1601634580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24661007, text='There seems to be confusion about “systemic” vs “systematic” here. Systematic racism would imply a system that is <i>designed to</i> achieve racism. The more common term is systemic racism, which implies racism more or less as a “side effect” of a structure or system that arguably has a different, benign purpose.')
