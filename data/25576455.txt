Item(by='rrrrrrrrrrrryan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609285188, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25573905, text='We tend to think that the first widely deployed military drones are going to be like the movies: big bipedal humanoid robots kicking down doors, armed with machine guns and flamethrowers.<p>In reality, it&#x27;ll probably be swarms of tiny quadcopter drones, each with a gun and only a few bullets (and&#x2F;or some sort of self-detonation ability), shooting out the glass windows and flooding into a house.<p>There&#x27;s obviously some AI involved in this scenario, (navigation, mapping, facial recognition), but you definitely don&#x27;t need to balance a huge machine on two feet to wage an effective drone attack.')
