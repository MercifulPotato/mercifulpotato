Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=[24690332], score=None, time=1601913599, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24685792, text='“Entropy source” is a phrase that has a specific meaning.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Entropy_(computing)" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Entropy_(computing)</a><p>PRNGs, even CSPRNGs, are not an entropy source.
