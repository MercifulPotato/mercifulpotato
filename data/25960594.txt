Item(by='gritzko', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611944949, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25960525, text='Thanks for the question. \n&quot;No arbitrary nesting&quot; applies to inline formatting. Higher precedence formatting can be nested within lower. So, nothing can be nested within a code span, for example, as it is the highest. \nJust a way to order things.<p>Container blocks are nested arbitrarily, up to 16 levels. Quote &gt; list &gt; quote &gt; code, for example, is OK.')
