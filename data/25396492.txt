Item(by='rayiner', descendants=None, kids=[25396517], score=None, time=1607758829, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25396369, text='Censorship by definition includes not only banning and deletion, but “suppression,” which fits denying monetization to particular content on a site otherwise designed for it.<p>For example, it wouldn’t get around the first amendment for the government to say “we’re not banning this book, you just can’t charge money for it.” (I understand that Google as a private actor is permitted to engage in censorship while the government is not. My point is that demonetization is clearly censorship because of the government did it it would be illegal.)')
