Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604913614, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25032740, text='If you want to grow crops in a low CO2 environment, just grow them in a greenhouse with near zero ventilation.   The CO2 levels in the greenhouse will drop rapidly, and the plants will grow much slower.    Don&#x27;t add mulch or fresh topsoil, because that gives off a lot of carbon, especially when disturbed.')
