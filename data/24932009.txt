The number of times I read &quot;utilize&quot; or &quot;leverage&quot; seemingly because the author doesn&#x27;t want to say &quot;use&quot; is very high.<p>&quot;leverage&quot; in particular seems to get over-used, even in cases where there is nothing approaching <i>actual</i> leverage.<p>Make the reader think about <i>your actual point</i>; anytime you make them instead think about your word choice (in the form of &quot;why did they use <i>that</i> word?!&quot;), you&#x27;re reducing the information transfer and effectiveness of your writing.')
