Item(by='mikepurvis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601586749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24654014, text='It matters in the case of a malfunctioning device, as others have pointed out. If you&#x27;re going to have an adapter than a allows a 15A device (which would normally only need to fail safely in the presence of 15A, or perhaps a very brief lighting surge) into a plug that can supply 25 or 30A, the adapter should almost certainly contain its own 15A fuse.<p>The reverse of this is plugging big loads like power tools or vacuum cleaners into extension cords intended for use with desk lamps, but I think most people understand that that&#x27;s a bad idea.')
