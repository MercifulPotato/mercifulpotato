Item(by='gene91', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601920317, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24690094, text='Auto manufacturers are approaching Adaptive Cruise Control in a manner that intermingles the two: GM &quot;Super Cruise&quot;, Ford&#x27;s upcoming &quot;CoPilot 360 Active&quot;, etc.<p>In my opinion, these two features are very related. In order to have a reasonably low false positive, the sleep detection must be reasonably lenient. In turn, this means the vehicle must be able to (most of the time) tolerate a few seconds of inattentive driving.')
