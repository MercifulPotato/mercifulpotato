Item(by='NotPavlovsDog', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609543604, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25608237, text='Sociophysics has not been met with a wide welcome in management studies, perhaps due to its approach to proof. It requires a lot more work than popular hand-waving. \nIt will be interesting to see whether physics-derived tools and approaches will lead to improvement in social studies in regards to current critical concerns.<p>It is unfortunately (or fortunately, depending on how you look at it) a far-away in the future area for me, as just applying viewpoints outside of the mainstream to experimental research in management has a lot of low-hanging fruit with potentially significant impact.<p>That could be one of the benefits of the humanities aspect of management studies, it is well justified (though not popular) to take a humanitarian (and thus biased) stance towards improving the well-being of employees.')
