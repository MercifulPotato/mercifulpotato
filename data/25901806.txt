Item(by='mrkeen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611572160, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25898522, text='You might expect your service to only receive a particular JSON format, but it could really receive any JSON, or any String, or any bytes, etc.<p>At some point in the program you want to rely on your data already having been validated.  HashMaps are not a good fit for this.<p>For me the neatest way to address both of these is to have a very narrow conversion window.  Transform the data from bytes&#x2F;string&#x2F;hashmaps into well-typed objects only within the controller.')
