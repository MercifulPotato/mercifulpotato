Item(by='kangnkodos', descendants=None, kids=[25943405, 25951176], score=None, time=1611785276, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25930444, text='There&#x27;s always about the same amount of money bidding above and below the current price. The market maker keeps it in balance, by matching buy bids and sell bids which are close to the middle of the range. This matching process sets the price and makes sure that there is always liquidity.<p>The price may shoot way up, or plunge, but this process almost always works, so there&#x27;s almost always liquidity.')
