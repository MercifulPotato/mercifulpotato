Item(by='matthewdgreen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608601533, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25501061, text='There is research that shows that subjective passage of time depends on physiological changes to the brain. The most compelling example is that certain brain tumors make the passage of time seem extremely fast or slow to patients with them. The fact that time “seems to go faster” as you age is related to a normal aging related change (though natural, not caused by tumors.)')
