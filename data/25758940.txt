Item(by='jlos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610519801, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25757559, text='For me the <i>contrarian impulse</i> is that if Im arriving at the same conclusion as a large number of people, did I actually reason my way to that conclusion or am I letting unconscious social forces guide me? So, I play the devil&#x27;s advocate. Then, after having challenged the conventional notions, I have more confidence in my conclusion. At the very least, I will usually have a more nuanced view.<p>Ironically, being contrarian can itself be an unconscious social force to differentiate yourself; sometimes the herd is right.')
