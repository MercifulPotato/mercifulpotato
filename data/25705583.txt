PRs should be without emojis, you&#x27;re not there to play emotional games. If you make a statement, the receiver of that statement should consider that to be objective, straight forward and well meaning.<p>If you need to use emojis in code reviews, you have distrust, or perhaps other bigger problems in your team.<p>Ask yourself this question &quot;Why do I need to add an emoji?&quot; and introspect the reasons.
