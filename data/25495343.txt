Item(by='loeg', descendants=None, kids=[25495577], score=None, time=1608561899, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25495096, text='Sure, it&#x27;s better for the planet to have less USB C chargers in some abstract sense.  To accomplish that goal, Apple could have included lightning-USB A charger cables, which everyone has a brick for.  Instead, lightning-USB C.  Either USB-C chargers are better than USB-A (and in several respects, sure) and the planet should have more of them, or just provide USB-A compatible charging.<p>This choice was dumb <i>even if</i> their expressed environmental intent is authentic and it wasn&#x27;t a marginal revenue grab.')
