Item(by='shawnz', descendants=None, kids=[25317095, 25317618], score=None, time=1607187935, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25316034, text='There are plenty of other alternatives. The simplest (but least flexible) would be hardcoding an IP of the final server.<p>Somewhat more sophisticated would be hardcoding an IP to a server with a REST endpoint that returns the real final IP. (Basically just like what DoH does, but without calling it DoH).<p>Even more sophisticated would be hiding the final IP on some kind of public web service like Twitter or Github.')
