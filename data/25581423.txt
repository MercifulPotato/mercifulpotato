Item(by='Joeri', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609334615, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25572411, text='In many places landlords cannot suddenly jack up the price, but can only increase it in small amounts year by year. One could similarly imagine rent controls on digital services, where sudden price hikes are simply not allowed, and terminating the rental agreement one-sided is similarly restricted. The practical downsides of that kind of legislation would probably end up quite similar to rent controls.')
