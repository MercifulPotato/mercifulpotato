Item(by='closed', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611896660, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25952196, text='One critical piece methods miss is they can&#x27;t decentralize contribution. For example, the gganimate package in R gives user new ggplot functions. With a `+` users can use functions from any package, so the gganimate approach works.<p>With method chaining the gganimate author would have to mutate some class, and users would have to load all methods (vs importing what you need).')
