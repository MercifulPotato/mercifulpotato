Item(by='ineedasername', descendants=None, kids=[25674007], score=None, time=1610039540, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25673637, text='The nature of each &quot;public good&quot; could be evaluated to determine what was appropriate to ensure all citizens equal access to a public good.<p>FD just required a media outlet to make sure the media basically gave another candidate the opportunity to access, it didn&#x27;t actually require equal time, not unless that candidate wanted to, or in the case of advertisements was willing to buy them.<p>Certainly parkland should be equally available to members of different political parties, but it&#x27;s not like you have to use the same standard either: the nature of a &quot;public good&quot; would determine the nature of what fair equal access meant.
