Item(by='tptacek', descendants=None, kids=[25152645, 25152701, 25153553, 25153317], score=None, time=1605808235, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25151131, text='In case you&#x27;re just wondering what the changes to the rule are, they are roughly:<p>* The SEC can designate professional certifications from accredited institutions as a surrogate for wealth --- to begin with, FINRA Series 7, 65, and 82.<p>* Employees of investment funds can be accredited for purpose of investing in their employers fund.<p>* Firms that are SEC-registered investment advisors may now be accredited.<p>* To meet the individual standards of accreditation, households can pool wealth between spouses, regardless of whether both spouses are acquiring a security.<p>* LLCs with 5MM in assets are accredited, as are family offices with 5MM in assets.')
