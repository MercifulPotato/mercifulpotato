Item(by='crispyambulance', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606838924, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25263868, text='I&#x27;ve found it helpful to wake up at the same (early) time everyday no matter what and then rely on my body to &quot;tell me&quot; when it&#x27;s time for sleep. This keeps me lined up on a steady circadian rhythm. If it gets disrupted, that&#x27;s OK, it snaps back in day or two as long as I stick to the same wake-up time.<p>It&#x27;s much harder to do this with a prescribed &quot;bed time&quot; if you&#x27;re not ready, it&#x27;s really hard to sleep.')
