Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606078036, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25180731, text='A middleman refers to someone whose role in the transaction is to bring the actual transacting parties together.<p>While credit card processors are involved in the transaction between the customer and restaurant, they are <i>not</i> a middleman to the transaction because they don&#x27;t bring the parties together; the parties or a third party does that.<p>Additionally, credit card processors provide services to both sides of the transaction: they provide non-cash payment collection services to restaurants, and they provide customers a means to pay for things without immediately exchanging cash or other assets.')
