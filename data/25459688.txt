Item(by='LeifCarrotson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608235459, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25458718, text='The satellite is looking at the of flight and intensity of the reflected signal, not &#x27;color&#x27;. It&#x27;s basically monochrome. You can make your ceiling either very massive or very conductive to reduce the signal reflected, or install a corner reflector in the attic to overwhelm the sensor with its own high-intensity signals (like taking a photo in a mirror with the flash on).')
