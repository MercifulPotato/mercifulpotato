Item(by='nickcw', descendants=None, kids=[24987732, 24987681], score=None, time=1604475967, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24986132, text='&gt; In Go, you import packages by URL. If the URL points to, say, GitHub, then go get downloads HEAD of master and uses that. There is no way to specify a version, unless you have separate URLs for each version of your library.<p>Go modules has solved this problem.<p>I find that in practice Go modules work very well. Certainly better than the alternatives and I&#x27;ve been through most of them.<p>Incrementing the major version of your module is very clunky though.')
