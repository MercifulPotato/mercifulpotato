I didn&#x27;t notice the same amount of improvement in other network bound stuff but, Firefox went from &quot;meh&quot; to &quot;youjustdidwhat!?&quot; after DNSMasq installation and modem change.<p>Same thing also happened when I changed my modem 7-8 years ago after my venerable Alcatel Speedtouch died.<p>So, in retrospect, modem chipset really plays a central role in your home network performance.')
