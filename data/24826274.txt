Item(by='Retric', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603115007, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24825955, text='IKEA has different product lines at different price points.  They give the option of going with particle board, but spend more money and you get much longer lasting furniture.<p>Realistically, for people that move around a lot having a very cheap option to fit each living space is just efficient. Particleboard is effectively a form of wood recycling so using it is surprisingly environmentally friendly even if doesn’t last very long.  However, once you buy a place it’s time to get something that lasts.')
