At that early stage, anything beyond a few lines of YAML seems like definite overkill.<p>Eventually, it becomes clear that it&#x27;s very hard to maintain, but by then there&#x27;s thousands of lines of hard-knocks battle-tested, working production config to try and basically recreate from scratch, without breaking anything.<p>Until you&#x27;ve gone through this process once (or maybe a couple times..) it&#x27;s hard to see why you should make that initial leap to a much more complex, tools-required workflow. &quot;But eventually, we will probably need...&quot; is a tough sell against someone arguing to do the simplest thing.')
