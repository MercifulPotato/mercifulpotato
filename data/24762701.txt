The idea has been tried many times in many European cities and it has been proven to be an excellent idea almost every time.<p>Making city centers into pedestrian zones with bike paths and a few bus lanes passing through does wonders for sound levels, safety, makes getting around easier, improves air quality and make the city centers more alive and pleasant.<p>[Here is a report by the European Commission](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ec.europa.eu&#x2F;environment&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pdf&#x2F;streets_people.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ec.europa.eu&#x2F;environment&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pdf&#x2F;streets_people.pdf</a>) that analyses the effects of reclaiming streets from cats in eight different cities.')
