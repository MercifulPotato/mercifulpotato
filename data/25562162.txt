Item(by='ericcholis', descendants=None, kids=[25562394], score=None, time=1609182646, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25562066, text='Developers who don&#x27;t have regular physical access to a Mac.<p>My use case might be too specific. But, at home I have a Windows Desktop and Laptop. In the office I use a Mac Mini. Currently, I need to test Apple Pay for Safari. This requires test iCloud credentials. On a mac, this is difficult or prohibitive to swap between live iCloud and test iCloud logins. On Windows, testing via browser emulation is shaky at best.<p>Also, easily sharing access to a &quot;cloud&quot; (read: remote) instance of macOS with other developer staff is beneficial.')
