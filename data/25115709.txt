Item(by='theli0nheart', descendants=None, kids=[25117608], score=None, time=1605553909, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25112498, text='This is a little meta, but you can use the the -I &#x2F; --head argument to tell curl to download headers only. This will ignore the rest of the response and means you can eliminate all of those other flags. E.g.:<p><pre><code>    $ curl -I https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtube-dl.org&#x2F;downloads&#x2F;latest&#x2F;youtube-dl-2020.11.12.tar.gz\n    HTTP&#x2F;1.1 302 Found\n    Date: Mon, 16 Nov 2020 19:10:37 GMT\n    Server: Apache&#x2F;2.2.15 (CentOS)\n    Location: https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtube-dl.org&#x2F;downloads&#x2F;2020.11.12&#x2F;youtube-dl-2020.11.12.tar.gz\n    Connection: close\n    Content-Type: text&#x2F;html; charset=iso-8859-1</code></pre>')
