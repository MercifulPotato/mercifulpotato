Item(by='sudosteph', descendants=None, kids=[24806185], score=None, time=1602887972, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24801706, text='I&#x27;m in a similar boat, and agree completely that it&#x27;s super fun to learn this way. Coming from playing guitar, I was already used to starting from chords and listening for the rest, so it wasn&#x27;t a big change. I&#x27;m a big believer in proper timing and solid rhythms being more important than notes, and learning by ear makes those aspects more intuitive, in my experience at least.<p>One thing I did find was the very helpful, was putting those stickers with note names on each note on the keyboard. They help me keep track of what I&#x27;m playing, and when I compose something I rely on the stickers to help me transcribe.')
