Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607104853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25304526, text='Abstraction should not be a response to imagined future requirements (though there is a fuzzy boundary between imagined and reasonably-foreseen near-future requirements: you don&#x27;t want to spend effort solving problems from the hazy future, but you also don&#x27;t want to be doing major rework in a month because you didn&#x27;t take reasonable steps in anticipation of something clearly anticipated but not fully solidified), but code duplication isn&#x27;t the only trigger. Problem decomposition often results in reusable abstraction as a natural consequence.')
