It has more in common with a bookie&#x27;s ledger than a warehouse of goods.<p>Why this is often harmful and dangerous is that the financial viability of businesses and individuals are often backing these bets. A person or company expecting financing to be predictable and stable may suddenly find it not so because their lender is desperate to cover their gambling losses.')
