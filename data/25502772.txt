Studies on the effects of banning hate speech communities suggest that the mechanics don&#x27;t work that way. For example, when reddit banned &#x2F;r&#x2F;fatpeoplehate and other subreddits centered around hate groups, other subreddits did not inherit the hate speech problem, and former hate group users reduced the amount of hateful content they posted.<p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;comp.social.gatech.edu&#x2F;papers&#x2F;cscw18-chand-hate.pdf" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;comp.social.gatech.edu&#x2F;papers&#x2F;cscw18-chand-hate.pdf</a>')
