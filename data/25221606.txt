Item(by='carapace', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606409706, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25220139, text='In re: E and capabilities, see also &quot;macaroons&quot;:<p>&gt; This paper introduces macaroons: flexible authorization credentials for Cloud services that support decentralized delegation between principals. Macaroons are based on a construction that uses nested, chained MACs (e.g., HMACs) in a manner that is highly efficient, easy to deploy, and widely applicable.<p>&gt; Although macaroons are bearer credentials, like Web cookies, macaroons embed caveats that attenuate and contextually confine when, where, by who, and for what purpose a target service should authorize requests.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;research.google&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pub41892&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;research.google&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pub41892&#x2F;</a>')
