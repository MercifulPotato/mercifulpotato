Take a look at its terms of service, information collected section:<p>&quot;We also save searches, but again, not in a personally identifiable way, as we do not store IP addresses or unique User agent strings. We use aggregate, non-personal search data to improve things like misspellings.&quot;<p>So they save your web searches and claim that they do so in an non-personally identifiable way.
