Item(by='jacobolus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606554393, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25235262, text='Doing integer&#x2F;rational arithmetic gives you a choice: either never do any rounding and require exponentially growing precision that makes even the simplest algorithms impractically expensive (not to mention giving up entirely on the many common computations which cannot be represented whatsoever in an exact rational arithmetic system), or allow rounding&#x2F;approximation of some kind and end up with roughly the same problems floats have.')
