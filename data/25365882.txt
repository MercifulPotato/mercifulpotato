Item(by='doonesbury', descendants=None, kids=[25368345], score=None, time=1607551310, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25363650, text='Consider the case of a trader (trading firm) with a custodian bank. What&#x27;s the best way to reconcile the custodian&#x27;s view with one&#x27;s own trading system and its backend? Recall this is important because, as the custodian does settlement, the trader doesn&#x27;t want to purchase instruments with cash the custodian doesn&#x27;t think it has nor purchase shares the custodian bank thinks it already has. Keeping these two matched is a real, pressing, daily problem.<p>Blockchain I think is an impractical, over-hyped solution that has no purchase in Fintech or even classical finance.')
