Item(by='nerdponx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610018658, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25652503, text='There is obviously a need here that is not met by current tools.<p>I believe that <i>Conda</i> is the closest thing to an answer to this need.<p>It works on Windows, Mac and Linux. It supports packaging arbitrary binaries. It lets you specify dependencies that can be arbitrary libraries for arbitrary languages. And it has a relatively simple package format, build process, and repository format.<p>It&#x27;s not the best piece of software when it comes to user experience, and its niche is still very much data science. But it is a glimpse of what <i>should</i> be possible, but is not currently available.')
