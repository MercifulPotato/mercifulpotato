You get smacked in the face with the request for your phone, once you&#x27;ve followed some interesting people, interacted with the site a bit and will form some attachment. Then you are more likely to enter your phone to keep using it.<p>There&#x27;s a psychological effect of feeling more strongly about <i>retaining</i> a given amount of already owned value compared to <i>gaining</i> an equivalent amount of stuff you don&#x27;t have yet.<p>You wouldn&#x27;t pay with your phone number to get a Twitter account, but you would pay with it to keep an account you already have and started using.<p>Dark patterns.')
