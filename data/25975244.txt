Item(by='rufius', descendants=None, kids=[25975549, 25975555, 25975542], score=None, time=1612060808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25974780, text='Honestly, frameworks like Rails or Django are an anti-pattern for Golang.<p>The standard library makes it very easy to build web services and there’s a couple or popular packages for making routes and dealing with HTTP requests&#x2F;responses simple.<p>There’s projects like Micro that try to do a little more batteries included but I wouldn’t consider them to be pervasive in the way Django or Rails are.<p>Beyond that, it’s just app logic and the typical Golang boiler plate.<p>Source: I’ve built a few big Golang services for a couple companies.')
