Item(by='leereeves', descendants=None, kids=[25722392], score=None, time=1610324234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25722114, text='My objection to that argument is that the companies claim that the content on the website <i>isn&#x27;t</i> their speech when invoking Section 230, then claim that it <i>is</i> their speech in order to invoke the First Amendment.<p>Section 230 says:<p>&gt; No provider or user of an interactive computer service shall be treated as the publisher or speaker of any information provided by another information content provider.<p>So how can a company have free speech rights regarding content if they aren&#x27;t the speaker?')
