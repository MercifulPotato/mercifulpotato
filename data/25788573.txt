Item(by='cryptica', descendants=None, kids=[25789065], score=None, time=1610701221, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25788493, text='My main problem with declarative&#x2F;functional programming languages is that they force developers to separate the logic from the state (they are not co-located based on concerns) and this often causes developers to neglect the separation of concerns principle altogether; which is by far the most important principle in my experience.<p>Most developers never learned proper blackboxing&#x2F;state encapsulation.<p>It&#x27;s impossible to create truly modular code without colocating related logic and state.')
