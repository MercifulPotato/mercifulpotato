Item(by='winrid', descendants=None, kids=[25995626], score=None, time=1612214441, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25990585, text='Bad deployment system. You should do an A&#x2F;B deployment and warm up the services&#x2F;caches before the switch, so that the architecture handles the problem and not the programming language&#x2F;VM.<p>At FastComments we run our E2E tests on the new instance to JIT the app. Before the Jit API calls can take 100ms, and after 10-30ms. Still, that is fast enough that most people wouldn&#x27;t notice...<p>Also, the problem is not Java. Your application probably has way too many abstractions for a simple login page.')
