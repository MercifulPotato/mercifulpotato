Item(by='MatthewMcDonald', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604327742, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24967908, text='The only way to get around your dependence on OAuth connections is to migrate off of them, one at a time.<p>My suggestion would be to get your own domain, get an email service that supports wildcard email addresses, and get a password manager. Start migrating your accounts one by one, until you no longer depend on your gmail account for OAuth.<p>It will be tedious, but for most websites you can update your email address in under a minute. Put a movie on and knock them out in an afternoon.')
