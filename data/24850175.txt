Xyratex did that in the near-line storage units (e.g. RS-4835), as did Hitachi and others.  Once you&#x27;ve seen them, it&#x27;s the obvious choice to maximize the number of 3.5&quot; drives in a rack (~48drives in a 4U chassis).  Heavy beasts though -- you want a lift to mount them.<p>Don&#x27;t know about relative reliability to horizontal mounts, but afair the drive manufacturer recommends mounting either vertical or horizontal and we didn&#x27;t observe any obvious issues.')
