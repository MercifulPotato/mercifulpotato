Item(by='michaelt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610979758, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25821692, text='<i>&gt; It&#x27;s not clear to me what you think Facebook should do differently.</i><p>The traditional trick to stopping the government regulating you is to figure out roughly what problems the government is considering regulation to solve, form an industry body with your competitors, then come up with a code of conduct that addresses the problems but without draconian enforcement.<p>That way you don&#x27;t have to follow rules written by politicians, get your house 100% in order, and have a regulator breathing down your neck: You&#x27;ve just got to follow rules you&#x27;ve written yourself, and get your house well enough in order that legislators have more pressing things to focus on than you.<p>That might sound difficult - but if it was easy to run a $700 billion company, everyone would be doing it.')
