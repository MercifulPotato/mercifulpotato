Item(by='lhorie', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612218477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25993338, text='I like fusion cuisine: it tends to be endemic of places with lots of immigration and it&#x27;s part of the character of these cities.<p>What I think you&#x27;re referring to is the idea of authenticity, which I think is illustrated well by looking at how different cultures barbecue. The &quot;classic&quot; american barbecue features harburgers and hotdog sausages in bread, brazilian barbecue offers a large variety of cuts of meats along with an extensive salad buffet, and chinese barbecue might get you duck to go with rice.')
