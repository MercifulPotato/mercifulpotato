Item(by='abootstrapper', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608708941, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25514580, text='Mine was my freshman year in college. I had a craving for chocolate cake one evening. My epiphany was that I could just hop in my car and go buy one. I didn’t have to answer to or explain myself to anyone. So off I went and bought a cake! My dorm roommate seemed just as enlightened at the notion and he helped me eat it.<p>I’m not sure I mark this as “adulthood,” but it was a strangely significant milestone in my budding independence.')
