Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611908161, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25951159, text='&quot;treating your users as incompetent&quot;<p>Actually, this is good practice.<p>We are all busy and distracted. Dumbing down an experience to it&#x27;s essential aspects is a great way to make something easier to use.<p>If a &#x27;fool could use it while trying to juggle&#x27; then that&#x27;s good.<p>Making it dumb means speaking to intuition, not thought.<p>I should add, there are some good comments here about &#x27;command line&#x27; etc. - I suggest that that makes sense but in an entirely different realm of usability.<p>Designing for something that&#x27;s going to be used by competent people all day for their jobs, is different than designing for the proles standing in line at the bank, looking for 3 minutes of respite from whatever, or even trying to squeeze in that little update.')
