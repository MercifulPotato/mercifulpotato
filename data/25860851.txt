Item(by='im3w1l', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611247435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25860440, text='Imagine a curve. On the x-axis is amount of censorship. On the y-axis is opposition. A slippery slope corresponds to a flat part of the curve, where a large increase in censorship causes a small increase in opposition.<p>Any policy in the middle of the slippery-slope is not stable. Political alliances are easiest to form advocating either side of it.<p>In this case there are few people who want Trump banned but think banning Venezuela&#x27;s National Assembly is a step too far.')
