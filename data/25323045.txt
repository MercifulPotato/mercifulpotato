Item(by='amzans', descendants=None, kids=[25323123], score=None, time=1607257212, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25322651, text='I tend to prefer tools that have: a) withstood the test of time, or b) I feel comfortable debugging it if my business depended on it.<p>My argument for battle-tested tools is, if they are still being used by a significant community even if no longer &quot;trendy&quot;, they probably solve a problem well, there&#x27;s lots of help online, and the ways in which it can fail are well known.<p>That said, I do try some lesser known tools if they help me ship the product. I just look at them as a risk factor until I understand them better.')
