Doesn&#x27;t fly because the rule that it should be equally easy to opt out.<p>- Cookie banners with no real opt out? No way.<p>- Cookie banners where you have to deselect 927 &quot;partners&quot;? Also no way.<p>The only ones that seems legal are those who either uses a pure minimum of cookies for preserving state and those allow one to opt out directly but inform you that ads might become less relevant.')
