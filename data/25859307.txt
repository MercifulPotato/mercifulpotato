Item(by='ubermonkey', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611240279, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25852962, text='Folio Society editions are amazingly gorgeous. I have a few, but none that cost $345.<p>Two things about the linked article:<p>1. The lede is just bad. Why would dense writing be in tension with winning a Nobel?<p>2. That the Folio Society consulted Noel Polk on this is an excellent sign. Polk, who sadly passed away in 2012, was one of the foremost Faulkner scholars. I had the pleasure of knowing him as I was growing up; his kids were a couple years behind me in school, and we attended the same church. I knew he was an English professor, but I had no idea of his stature until I picked up a copy of _As I Lay Dying_ for class, and saw it had a forward by Dr Polk.<p>Sadly, this edition of TSATF is long since sold out.')
