Item(by='drewg123', descendants=None, kids=[25271841], score=None, time=1606845776, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25266626, text='I highly recommend zfs with beinstall (&#x2F;usr&#x2F;src&#x2F;tools&#x2F;build&#x2F;beinstall.sh ).  It automatically creates a new ZFS bootenv, installs world &amp; kernel to it, and upgrades your packages.   If there is an issue, you can select the old bootenv from the boot loader prompt and be back in your old, pre-updated OS with nothing at all changed.')
