Item(by='identity0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607436162, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25342350, text='<p><pre><code>    String s = null;\n</code></pre>\nThat&#x27;s not possible with nullable types. The whole point of nullable types is to mark types that can be null. So this is possible:<p><pre><code>    String? s = null;\n</code></pre>\nBut now it&#x27;s not much different than this:<p><pre><code>    Option&lt;String&gt; s = None;\n</code></pre>\nMy point is that these are fundamentally the same thing.
