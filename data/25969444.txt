Item(by='cesarb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612017803, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25968672, text='That documentation says &quot;[EINTR] A signal was caught during open().&quot;, and the documentation there for sigaction with SA_RESTART says &quot;If set, and a function specified as interruptible is interrupted by this signal, the function shall restart and shall not fail with [EINTR] unless otherwise specified.&quot;, so as long as all signals were installed with SA_RESTART (which is probably the most common case since nobody likes dealing with EINTR), open() should never fail with EINTR.')
