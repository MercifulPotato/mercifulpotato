Item(by='ashtonkem', descendants=None, kids=[25909850], score=None, time=1611600005, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25906076, text='Jurisprudence includes the concept of a &quot;Limited Purpose Public Figure&quot;, which typically includes anyone or anything that is part of a prominent matter of public interest. It is not a requirement that this person or group be prominent before hand. Unfortunately this occasionally has the effect of making some plaintiffs a LPPF by the very nature of the allegedly defamatory statements made against them, but that&#x27;s how the law works.')
