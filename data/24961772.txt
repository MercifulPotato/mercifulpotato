Item(by='MaxBarraclough', descendants=None, kids=[24961796, 24964696], score=None, time=1604258471, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24961643, text='There&#x27;s very little harm in making the conversion explicit. It&#x27;s more verbose, but it&#x27;s also more explicit, which is a good thing.<p>There is harm in implicit conversions catching the programmer unawares. This is a fairly significant cause of bugs in C code, and it&#x27;s why C++ uses stricter rules, requiring you to always make these conversions explicit, even when using malloc.')
