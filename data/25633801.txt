Item(by='gen220', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609778653, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25632616, text='There are plenty of UX reasons (learning new interfaces, etc). The burden here is generally distributed and diffuse.<p>The really big one, for companies of a certain size &#x2F; cash flow, is compliance. Companies spend a lot of time developing compliant work flows around a service like Slack.<p>Migrating to another service requires rewriting the compliance narrative. The current compliance people might not have the confidence or willpower to do that effectively, and can raise legal objections to any such migration indefinitely.')
