They don&#x27;t exist or aren&#x27;t central in a whole lot of modern businesses. How do you analyze FB in terms of the CWS model? It&#x27;s like trying to examine the health of a rose bush by taking its temperature.<p>It&#x27;s a pretty good example of intellectual failure in economics, compounded by legislative&#x2F;industry agendas. Defining reality using a model rather than the other way around. The model is good at measuring market power of something like a centralized buyer of agricultural commodities. The more unlike an agricultural cartel the industry, the lower the &quot;evidence&quot; for monopoly.')
