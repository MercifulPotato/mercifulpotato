Item(by='himinlomax', descendants=None, kids=[24967801], score=None, time=1604316316, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24965293, text='While you&#x27;re at it, always display a vu-meter. It gives feedback on what is transmitted and thus can alert a user whether they are being heard or not. It&#x27;s the most basic of sound recording tools, and was a standard part of recording equipment for over half a century for good reason.<p>And if you need minimalism, offer a toggle for that. But I think most people should have it forced on them, would save anyone a lot of trouble -- just think about all the aggregate time lost talking into a muted mike by all users.')
