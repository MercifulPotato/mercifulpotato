Item(by='unanswered', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611601515, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25903518, text='Unpopular opinion: anything that outputs an AST is, by definition, not a parser but a recognizer with extra steps. The reason for this is that an <i>abstract</i> tree has not yet been parsed. The input is only parsed once the AST has been traversed, presumably by a much-maligned recursive descent method.')
