Item(by='auganov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607162879, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25312854, text='On a purely technical note, something being &quot;fully decentralized&quot; would usually imply low reach. To amplify voices a platform has to be conceptually centralized enough to allow for a common experience. Which comes with the ability to impose rules. People may disagree on what the rules should be, but the great majority do want some.<p>The notion of huge swaths of people being sucked into some totally anonymous decentralized platform with no rules and nobody in charge sounds implausible.')
