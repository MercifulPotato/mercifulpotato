Item(by='gumby', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602258929, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24730204, text='Actually they can, though it is not recommended as URLs appear in running text, which may break lines at space characters.  Just look at appendices A and B of RFC 3986.<p>Although it&#x27;s recommended that you encode them in user representation (for this reason) you can certainly should be able to send a space character through at the protocol level and if not it&#x27;s a bug.<p>Note that a space is not valid in the host name portion if you are using DNS (which most URLs do, of course).')
