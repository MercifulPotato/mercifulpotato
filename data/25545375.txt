Item(by='jakear', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609007917, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25544232, text='One more vote against “nobody orders because of the app”, back in college ordering Dominoes (or Daaahhmmss, as we were more likely to refer to it at the time), was the go-to purely because of the app. It saved each of our favorite orders, our payment methods, and our locations. Thus it was trivial to make complicated purchases even N drinks into the night. No other pizza provider came close to that level of simplicity. Hell, barely any software in general receives the esteemed “can you use this while totally blasted” UX certification.')
