Item(by='crehn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603176556, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24830399, text='My attempt at explaining why periods at the end feel uncomfortably stiff in a Slack-like environment:<p>Social conversations are fluid and open-ended, with participants organically shifting between listening and speaking. A period however conveys finality, which is off-putting in such a human setting.<p>For example, “No.” sounds like the very stern “No” of someone who’s not willing to listen or discuss any further.')
