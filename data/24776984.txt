Item(by='bluGill', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602686195, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770954, text='There is another advantage of good fakes: they often can be shipped in production as your demo mode. I&#x27;ve seen cases where when of the most important new features to marketing was just make some part of the fake more useful in production. Sometimes because there is a show coming up and the demo mode is what customers will see.  Sometimes because the trainers want a better simulation of some issue.<p>In some cases the fake is a lot more complex than the real implementation. (I worked on a diagnostic tool, the real implementation &quot;just&quot; needed to send&#x2F;receive bytes, the fake needed to understand the bytes and return responses)')
