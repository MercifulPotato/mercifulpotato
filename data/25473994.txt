You could describe multiple different workflows in your application as different state machines, save them to a data store, and load them up dynamically depending on the situation&#x2F;user.<p>I’m going off topic, but this approach goes beyond state machines. Focusing on a data driven approach allows you to do some pretty interesting stuff, like designing whole APIs and UIs using data only: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;BNkYYYyfF48" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;BNkYYYyfF48</a>')
