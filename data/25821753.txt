Item(by='cheph', descendants=None, kids=[25822803, 25822336, 25821917], score=None, time=1610978020, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25821344, text='I am not sure by what criteria you can say these are billion year old living organisms.<p>If you meant they have been around that long, sure, they have, but a stromatolite is not a living organism by most (if not all?) definitions. It is a layered sedimentary formation. It is about as much an organism as an ant heap without ants is an organism.<p>And I&#x27;m pretty sure that none of the individual organisms that create stromatolites are even close to a 100 years old, never mind a billion years old.')
