Blind optimism is perfectly fine for making progress, in the cases where the action taken turns out to work.<p>Plenty of people started businesses on what may be termed blind optimism and succeeded.<p>I think it even helps a little to underestimate the obstacles in innovation - otherwise one might not attempt it in the first place.
