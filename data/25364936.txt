The point is, co-owning the company you work for has some fundamental advantages. By the way, this wouldn&#x27;t be true in a perfectly competitive market, it&#x27;s only true thanks to market imperfections.<p>For example, in the company I work for, sometimes I slip into the mode of feeling like I &quot;own&quot; the product we&#x27;re making, making an extra effort, being emotionally invested, etc. But then I remember, I would just be a fool who works for his employer for free. Also, achieving change requires a lot more effort and often fails, which can be demotivating. In general, I feel like I could run things substantially better if I had the power. Seeing the mediocrity at fairly successful companies makes me really think about working at a startup.')
