Item(by='solidsnack9000', descendants=None, kids=[25608232, 25607949], score=None, time=1609539997, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25607735, text='If (a) there is no definite speed limit, just an imprecise policy statement (like &quot;don&#x27;t drive too fast&quot;) and (b) a person drives by the same cops for years at the same speed and gets clocked and doesn&#x27;t get ticketed, and then (c) is one day pulled over and told that they&#x27;re driving imprudently, then they indeed have a basis for complaint.
