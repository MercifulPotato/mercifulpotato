Putting the punctuation on the next line, looking at it displeased, moving it back, questioning the purpose of rules if they&#x27;re not followed; racking my brain over the world&#x27;s least consequential conundrum. Ultimately I decided to adhere to my limit, but to treat punctuation and the word that precedes it as a single unit to be wrapped together.')
