Item(by='jamesponddotco', descendants=None, kids=[25168301], score=None, time=1605924179, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25167161, text='The T models without the S usually have no soldered RAM, or only one soldered slot. The ThinkPad T480, for example, has two upgradable RAM slots and one or two NVMe slots. Heck, even the Wi-Fi card is removable.<p>When talking about ThinkPads, I always recommend going with the T line and skipping anything with S at the end.')
