Item(by='neilwilson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604412548, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24978968, text='Take a tax rate of 1%<p>Now spend $100. Tax it at 1%. Then the next person gets $99 income. Spend it all again. Tax that at 1%. Then the next person gets $99.01. Tax that at 1%. And so on like a stone skipping across a pond.<p>When you get to the end of the sequence and total up the tax take, what is the value?<p>You&#x27;ll be surprised.<p>Now work out why that doesn&#x27;t happen in the actual world. The answer is that somebody didn&#x27;t spend everything they earned straight away. And that&#x27;s what a deficit is.')
