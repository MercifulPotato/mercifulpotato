On the other hand, do we have any sort of obligation to elevate the standards of living of people in non-developed countries, especially when it may impact the standards of people living in our borders?<p>There is a moral question to be answered, especially given our purported system of values and the way in which the wealthy and wealthy countries came to be as such.<p>I&#x27;m not staking out a position or trying to shame anyone.  I&#x27;m just saying I think it is the underlying question we should be thinking about to decide how we judge globalization and its effects.')
