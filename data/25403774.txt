Item(by='desdiv', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607826304, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403532, text='What you are talking about is a salary renegotiation, i.e. the employer offering the employee a new contract at a lower salary for work <i>to be performed in the future</i>.<p>What had alleged happened in the story is salary theft, i.e. the employer stealing part of the employee&#x27;s remuneration for work <i>already performed in the past</i>.<p>I can&#x27;t speak to India&#x27;s legal system, but if this had happened in the US then it would be a federal crime under the Fair Labor Standards Act.')
