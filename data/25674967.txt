Item(by='jedberg', descendants=None, kids=[25675847, 25675267], score=None, time=1610043468, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25673275, text='In regards to the health insurance thing, there is a good middle option.  I&#x27;m not affiliated but I use Savvy (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gosavvy.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gosavvy.com&#x2F;</a>).<p>They take advantage of a law that just started in 2020 that lets a company owner offer tax free insurance payments without having a health plan.<p>Basically you set a budget, and then the employee chooses any health plan they want from any provider and pays with tax free money.  If they spend more than your budget it just comes out of their paycheck, so you could theoretically set the budget to $0 and at least let them have tax free health care payments.')
