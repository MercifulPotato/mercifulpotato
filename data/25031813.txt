Item(by='Swizec', descendants=None, kids=[25031989, 25031905], score=None, time=1604900911, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25031611, text='Deadlifts are great for running. They give you more explosive power and increase speed because you get a bit more distance with each stride. Over a marathon (40,000 steps) that extra half-inch per stride adds up.<p>They’re also great for boxing. Help you generate more power on your punche. Uppercuts especially.<p>The trick is to <i>not</i> lift for hypertrophy. Lift for power and speed and neuromuscular activation. If you’re sore the next day you went too hard.')
