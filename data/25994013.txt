Item(by='teraflop', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612213895, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25993866, text='By default, VLC tries to seek exactly to the timestamp you tell it to, which may requiring reading and decompressing a significant number of I-frames.<p>Various other players, including mpv and MPC-HC, will instead just snap to the nearest keyframe and display that instead.<p>Try turning on &quot;Fast seek&quot; in VLC&#x27;s Input&#x2F;Codecs preferences tab. It&#x27;s still not <i>quite</i> as fast as other players, in my experience, but it&#x27;s significantly better.')
