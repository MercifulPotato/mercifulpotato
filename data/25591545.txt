Item(by='the_why_of_y', descendants=None, kids=[25594098], score=None, time=1609411742, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25590791, text='The FSF <i>does</i> address version incompatibility, the problem is that some popular projects flat out refuse to follow their guidance.<p><i>The FSF uses the approach of asking people to release programs under “GNU GPL version N or any later version.” This licensing is compatible with version N, and also with N+1 (because it offers version N+1 as an option). When you combine code under “GPL 3 or later” with code under “GPL 2 or later”, the license of the combination is their intersection, which is “GPL 3 or later”.</i><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gnu.org&#x2F;licenses&#x2F;license-compatibility.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gnu.org&#x2F;licenses&#x2F;license-compatibility.html</a>')
