Item(by='WalterBright', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607328794, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25329108, text='One reason not mentioned (or maybe it&#x27;s only me):<p>If I&#x27;ve got bunch of problems, I&#x27;ll work on the easiest ones first, saving the hardest for last. This clears my mind from the drag from the easier problems, and as a side effect I wind up committing more.<p>There&#x27;s no particular correlation between how hard a problem is to solve vs how much time it takes to solve them. So hit the easy ones first and make your users happy!')
