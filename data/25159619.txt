Item(by='xorcist', descendants=None, kids=[25160582], score=None, time=1605873553, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25158631, text='md is the normal tool for doing RAID in software on Linux. It exposes a number of physical devices as one block device, which looks just like a normal hard disk.<p>btrfs is also a volume manager with RAID-like functionality of its own. But you can also use btrfs on an md device, just like you would with any other filesystem.<p>Software RAID is something I have only used for personal use, and with the enormous consumer hard drives available now, striping them seems less necessary than before.')
