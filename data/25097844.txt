Item(by='lostcolony', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605409313, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25097723, text='Look for a rescue that will let you give it a try, as it were, for a month, and if it doesn&#x27;t work out you can return the animal without issue (so you won&#x27;t feel like you have to keep the cat if it isn&#x27;t working out).<p>I did that back when I first was on my own and wanted a pet...and then found my allergies couldn&#x27;t handle it. Was so thankful to be able to return her to a good home. Her personality changed completely, too (which wasn&#x27;t a problem, except she went from being very standoffish, to becoming a complete cuddle bum, which exacerbated my allergies even more).')
