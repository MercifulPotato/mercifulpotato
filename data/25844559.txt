Except if you work with the discrete Fourier transform.  Then 1-based indexing is extremely cumbersome and annoying.  More generally, if your indices are to be understood &quot;modulo N&quot;, it makes sense that they are all strictly smaller than N (or even signed, and centered around 0).')
