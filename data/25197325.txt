Item(by='frankenst1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606215555, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25193585, text='The issue here is that for some reason I often struggle to remember which function sorts in ascending order [(b-a) or (a-b)].<p>In fact, your example suffers from the same problem: It sorts in descending order (20,10,2,1) instead of ascending (1,2,10,20).<p>Telling JS to use a TypedArray [Int8,...,BigInt64] instead can solve this without remembering and providing your own comparator:<p><pre><code>    Float64Array.from([1,2,10,20]).sort(); &#x2F;&#x2F;[1,2,10,20]</code></pre>')
