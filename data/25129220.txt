Item(by='Cactus2018', descendants=None, kids=[25130678], score=None, time=1605644395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25127419, text='&gt; We investigated how background tabs use system resources and found that JavaScript Timers represent &gt;40% of the work in background tabs. ... Beginning in M87, we’re throttling JavaScript timer wake-ups in background tabs to once per minute. This reduces CPU usage by up to 5x, and extends battery life up to 1.25 hours in our internal testing. We’ve done this without sacrificing the background features that users care about, like playing music and getting notifications.')
