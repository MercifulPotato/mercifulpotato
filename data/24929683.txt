Item(by='agbell', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603973915, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24927225, text='I think it depends what your goal is.  The &#x27;How to take smart notes&#x27; book is specifically about taking research notes for academia or at the very least non-fiction writing.<p>Zettelkasten Method can make a lot of sense in contexts like that.  If you are writing a weekly blog, keep building notes up and polishing parts of them for the blog.  Then your note taking process is in fact your blog writing process, but a bit more procedurally free form.')
