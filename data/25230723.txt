Item(by='mjw1007', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606498736, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25221840, text='Nowadays an approach that I find often worth trying is:<p>- download a project in a suitable language that claims to do what you need<p>- read its list of direct dependencies<p>- implement your own thing using those same libraries<p>- write your own replacement for any of those libraries that seem more trouble than they&#x27;re worth.<p>Indications for when this is likely to go well:<p>- the top-level piece of software has grown its own configuration language<p>- the the top-level piece of software has dozens of options for tuning its behaviour<p>- the maintainers of the top-level piece of software have spun parts of it out as libraries, and they&#x27;re being used by other people.')
