Item(by='mlthoughts2018', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610334733, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25716652, text='Just a reminder to all the product managers and budding executives who will read this:   <i>you are not Apple</i>.<p>You don’t have a world class consumer computer business, mobile device business, entertainment products business, app store business, etc. etc. to fall back on when you decide to do the bare minimum.<p>The difference between average &amp; bare minimum <i>for you</i> is the difference between going bust vs. holding steady, or the difference between mass layoffs vs. hiring freeze, or the difference between giving raises and not.<p>Don’t go off think you are a maverick tastemaking Steve Jobs who can toss out “do the bare minimum” as some savant-like wisdom.')
