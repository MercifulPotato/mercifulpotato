Item(by='ska', descendants=None, kids=[24860921], score=None, time=1603382508, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24857988, text='Perhaps &quot;fiction&quot; is the wrong way to look at it, because the exercise has value.<p>I think of it this way: every business&#x27;s financial model is a collection of numbers, some empirical (and hopefully correct) and some estimated.<p>The thing about a startup is that there are few empirical numbers (but they are critical, you have to understand your burn rate and what affects it) and the estimated numbers <i>are poor estimates</i>.   The evolution of the size of the error bars on those estimates is probably more important than the size itself.  If you are very early on, you should be using these to help you focus information gathering on the most impactful areas.')
