Item(by='ineedasername', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609476682, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25597932, text='Yes, and it&#x27;s important to remember with any lock, safe or other security measures is that the goal is not for it to <i>stay</i> locked, but to <i>open when certain criteria are met</i>.<p>Designers of such mechanisms intend those criteria to be met a certain way, but that isn&#x27;t required. Like standard locks, which are intended to open with a key, but the design simply requires the pins to form a certain configuration-- key optional.')
