Item(by='o_class_star', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602261171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24731462, text='Capitalism has replaced the community with the office. You don&#x27;t know your neighbors, you see your extended family once a year if that, and it&#x27;s almost impossible to make friends after age 30 because we live in a stratified world built to keep us apart from each other. But the people who share the (mis?)fortune of selling their time to the same employer, they&#x27;re the ones you spend all your time with, and they become your community or your small town.<p>This pushes you to invest yourself emotionally into a community that a wealthier, more powerful person can take away in an instant.')
