Item(by='EForEndeavour', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601827829, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24677551, text='These are great Finder tips, but they all involve the mouse, which I find mildly infuriating.<p>Command-Option-C: copy the full path of the selected file or folder as plaintext, with space characters <i>not</i> escaped with backslashes.<p>Command-C, then Command-V into a terminal prompt: paste the full path of the copied file or folder, with space characters backslash-escaped. Tested in Terminal and iTerm2.')
