Item(by='nine_k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604464350, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24985764, text='Frankly, color laser  printers that would print a decent photo cost a lot, and the quality is but decent, not perfect.<p>An inkjet which can do justice to a photo is also usually not as cheap, its inks cost like French perfume (and sometimes there are 6 of them, not 4), and you also need to use expensive photo-quality paper. This allows you to have really great photos. Using such a device for printing business documents or school handouts is wasting it.<p>So yes, go with a laser, a color laser if you need, and just take your photo printing to a good lab, they usually have one of these inkjet beasts anyway.')
