The users of the recipes are the cooks, not the customers. Cooks are free to change and distribute recipes. There is no recipe that a cook can&#x27;t make, because in this case, the cook is the executant, not some computer. There are no compiled recipes.<p>- If there were cooking robots that would cook compiled recipes that would not be free, he would be against it, as he is with proprietary software.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;jUibaPTXSHk?t=914" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;jUibaPTXSHk?t=914</a>')
