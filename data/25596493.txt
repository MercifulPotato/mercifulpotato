Item(by='colejohnson66', descendants=None, kids=[25596863], score=None, time=1609444902, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25596396, text='By definition, a timer just counts time (either down or up). So a clock is a kind of timer. But colloquially, a “clock” is a timer that shows the time while a “timer” is one that counts down from a preset amount (for cooking or whatever). Then there’s a “stopwatch” which is a timer that counts up from 0.')
