Remember, video effects and graphics editing software uses the 16-core Neural Engine, which the software professionals use—FinalCut Pro, Logic, Affinity Photo, etc.—take advantage of. Adobe&#x27;s apps aren&#x27;t far behind.<p>And that&#x27;s on top of the 8 core CPU and 8 core GPU which rivals dedicated graphics cards.<p>A college student now has access to the kind of video, graphics and sound production and editing capability that required a $4000 or $5000 workstation a few years ago.<p>However you want to phrase it, these Macs are in the process of changing the game.')
