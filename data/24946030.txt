Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604086547, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24944081, text='I think you’ve just mixed up wait-free with lock-free.<p>- Lock-free: at least one thread will make progress<p>- Wait-free: all threads will make progress<p>The difference between locks and lock-free is very noticeable if a thread holding a lock is suspended (which can happen on a modern system just by accessing memory that is paged out). On real-time systems it’s also possible to guarantee that high-priority tasks will make progress, regardless of how a low-priority task behaves. With locks, a low-priority task can easily prevent a high-priority task from running at all.')
