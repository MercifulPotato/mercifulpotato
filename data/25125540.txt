For example, time.h calls that may touch the TZ env var aren&#x27;t thread-safe, because getenv is unsafe (it gives a pointer to its internal globally mutable data structure without locks).<p>Because C is so bare-bones, it usually leans on POSIX as its extended standard library, and that is also full of old cruft.')
