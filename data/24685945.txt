Item(by='pron', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601889653, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24682111, text='I use the word parallelism to talk about multiple processors simultaneously <i>cooperating</i> on solving a <i>single</i> task for the purpose of making the time to complete it shorter (i.e. its latency is reduced).<p>I use concurrency to describe the problem of simultaneously managing <i>multiple</i> mostly-independent tasks that are <i>competing</i> for hardware resources and performed on one or more processors. The performance we care about is throughput, not latency, and increasing concurrency helps throughput due to Little&#x27;s law (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;inside.java&#x2F;2020&#x2F;08&#x2F;07&#x2F;loom-performance&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;inside.java&#x2F;2020&#x2F;08&#x2F;07&#x2F;loom-performance&#x2F;</a>).')
