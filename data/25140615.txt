Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=[25140849], score=None, time=1605724966, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25140407, text='&gt; What kind of explanation could you possibly want?<p>Something that isn’t a tautology. “Why do you pay $4 for a cappuccino?” “Because that is the market price for a cappuccino.” “What does ‘market price’ mean?” “It means the amount of money people pay in the given market.”<p>You can see that nothing at all has been explained.<p>So when someone says that people are paying a certain price in a certain market “because it is market price”, that is getting things backwards, and not explaining anything at all. It’s like the surly teenager whose parents ask “Where are you going?” and he says, “Out.”')
