Item(by='CuriousSkeptic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610828875, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25804930, text='Perhaps it would be better to express a genuine feeling instead.<p>Praise implies being in a position to praise at all. Having some authority or mastery with regard to what is praised.<p>Often enough, I am that authority. I don’t expect praise from my clients. I’m getting payed precisely because they lack the mastery I offer.<p>Instead I would be happy to hear about their true feelings. Stories of gratitude, or relief, or whatever else happened in their life that I might have played a role in.')
