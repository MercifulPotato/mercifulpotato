Item(by='PaulDavisThe1st', descendants=None, kids=[24762358], score=None, time=1602547697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24760743, text='Zoning allows people that do not own land to decide what can be done with it.<p>That has <i>some</i> positive purposes ... it may indeed be a good idea not to put a toxic manufacturing facility next door to residential lots or a school. It might possibly make sense to focus retail development in a certain area within a community rather than haphazardly throughout it.<p>But single-dwelling has almost nothing to do with such high minded purposes, and exists almost entirely to keep higher income home owners away from lower income renters or mixed-income housing. It is, quite simply, rich people telling poor people &quot;we don&#x27;t want you here&quot;.')
