Item(by='dingaling', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611218901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25852802, text='The problem is that society expects everyone to have a pre-conceived judgement about every topic.<p>I encounter this frequently. &quot;What do you think about &lt;some news event&gt;?&quot; . &quot;Do you think &lt;some music genre&gt; sucks?&quot;. &quot;What&#x27;s your position on &lt;divisive topic&gt;?&quot;.<p>Maybe I just haven&#x27;t arrived at any opinion because it&#x27;s not relevant enough to my life.<p>And this insistence in finding someone&#x27;s position on every topic has a negative effect on conversation, because someone&#x27;s always ready to take offence.<p>TLDR: as few opinions as possible, only relevant to your immediate circumstances')
