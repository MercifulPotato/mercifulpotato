That’s harder.<p>Do it ten times each for ten people at once. That’s a lot harder.<p>Do it ten times each for ten people each across ten different cities in ten different states in ten different countries. That’s much, much harder.<p>It’s the “Wheat and Chessboard” problem all over again (see <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Wheat_and_chessboard_problem" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Wheat_and_chessboard_problem</a> ).<p>The fundamental problem here is one of scale.')
