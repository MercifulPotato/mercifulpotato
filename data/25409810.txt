Item(by='thaumasiotes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607890643, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407546, text='Why is that surprising? They have exactly the same meaning; the difference is that &#x27;but&#x27; additionally carries information about the subjective point of view of the speaker. (Where &#x27;and&#x27; indicates that two things are true, &#x27;but&#x27; indicates that (1) two things are true; and (2) the speaker considers this surprising or anticipates that the listener might find it surprising.)')
