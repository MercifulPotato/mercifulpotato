Here agreeableness is divided into compassion and politeness. Compassion corresponds to your idea of what empathy means, and politeness corresponds to being generally nice and cooperative, respecting authority and so on. From these <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ipip.ori.org&#x2F;newAB5CKey.htm" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ipip.ori.org&#x2F;newAB5CKey.htm</a>, the primary ones for compassion are Warmth, Sympathy and Understanding, and the primary ones for politeness are Nurturance, Cooperation, Pleasantness.')
