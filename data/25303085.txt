Item(by='MaxBarraclough', descendants=None, kids=[25303191], score=None, time=1607094786, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25302828, text='Seems like roughly the right solution. Sick days should exist to protect people who are unfortunate enough to get sick. Bad luck shouldn&#x27;t cost you your pay, your job, or your holiday days.<p>Sick days should not be a simple pool of days off that you get to treat as equivalent to holiday days. This approach has the effect of (further) rewarding people who are lucky with their health, and incentivises not taking sick days when you should.')
