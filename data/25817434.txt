Item(by='1vuio0pswjnm7', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610936193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25816910, text='Sooner or later we may have something like &quot;Content Centric Networking&quot;<p>It does not matter where (the address) we download the content from.  We only need a hash find the content and to verify the content comes from the &quot;original&quot; source and is therefore authentic.<p>If we are retrieving public information, then the only data that actually needs to travel over a secure channel is the signed hash of the content.<p>Content publishers could publish their public keys out of band, e.g., in the newspaper.')
