Item(by='karim', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606609465, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25240961, text='One reason I think ebikes are great is because their main target isn&#x27;t hardcore cyclists but everyday people who need to get from one point to another easily.<p>Ebikes require a bit more maintenance than a regular bike for sure, but a lot less than a car. They also let you transport more cargo than a regular bike and very often get you there quicker than a car.<p>I also know a couple people who own ebikes and use them exclusively for running errands (e.g because they don&#x27;t want to break a sweat when getting groceries).')
