Item(by='bwood', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610667919, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25784281, text='The trick is to not give advice. Structure the software so that it&#x27;s entirely self-directed and you don&#x27;t push users towards a specific investment.<p>You&#x27;ll probably still attract attention from regulatory bodies (we did!), but it&#x27;s fine if you can show that you&#x27;re not telling people what to do with their money. It&#x27;s okay to help them do the things they want to do, but they have to be in control.<p>In our case, we showed how our software is functionally equivalent to a rebalancing spreadsheet, just with a wicked UX and flexible feature set.')
