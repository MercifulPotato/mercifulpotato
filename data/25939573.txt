Item(by='maest', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611822830, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25939201, text='No.<p>Naked shorting is when you sell the underlying stock without borrowing it first. You have 2-3 days from transaction to settlement (depending on the market), so you can enter a transaction at T and then only locate borrow at T+2. Obviously, you&#x27;re running the risk that you can&#x27;t find borrow in time.<p>In most cases, however, you short by checking for available borrow first, and _then_ shorting.')
