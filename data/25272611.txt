Item(by='pushrax', descendants=None, kids=[25273745, 25272854, 25272845], score=None, time=1606875975, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25272577, text='If your primary operation is inserting at a random location in the list, linked lists are faster than arrays at large sizes. You avoid having to move all the memory after the index you are modifying (to make space for the inserted element).<p>A linked list also avoids copying the entire array when you need to insert more elements than you have allocated space for.')
