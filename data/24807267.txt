Go to cell properties and you will find there is a &quot;locked cell&quot; property that is on by default.<p>When you Protect a spreadsheet, all Locked Cells become... locked.<p>So a typical use case is to only un-lock the cells you wish people to enter variables into, (color the cell to make it clear), and then protect the sheet so nothing else can be tampered with.')
