Item(by='wolfgke', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603222807, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24841227, text='&gt; It&#x27;s the combination of a really wide range of skill and a really big difficulty assessing that skill from just the resume.<p>If you know what skills you are looking for (hint: lots of companies and recruiters don&#x27;t), this is not actually that hard.<p>For every skill, there are questions that should be really easy to answer for everybody with expertise, but are suprising&#x2F;hard to answer for everybody who pretends to have knowledge. \nThis enables you to check quite quickly whether the claimed skills are actually there or not.<p>For example, the easier sections in<p>&gt; <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;satwikkansal&#x2F;wtfpython" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;satwikkansal&#x2F;wtfpython</a><p>might give you a hint for topics that most seasoned Python programmers should know blindfolded while they are probably rather surprising for anybody with shallow Python knowledge. Let the applicant <i>explain</i> how they came up with their solution for, say, some of the problems, and you will see rather fast whether the claimed skills are actually there or not.')
