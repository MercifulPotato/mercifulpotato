Item(by='harshreality', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604029135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24936983, text='The moment you xor your one time pad (OTP) with the song to generate a ciphertext C, both OTP and C become, you could say, <i>conditionally-colored</i>.<p>You&#x27;re not restricted in redistributing one, so long as you don&#x27;t redistribute the other.  If you redistribute both, even in different channels, logically that&#x27;s a copyright violation unless you have sufficient controls or restrictions to prevent further redistribution until someone downstream from ending up with both and being able to recombine them.<p>Realistically, the only reason you&#x27;d generate C and distribute both OTP and C is so that someone eventually gets them both and can reconstruct the song.  Trying to claim you didn&#x27;t have that intent wouldn&#x27;t work in a civil context, and might not even work in a criminal context.')
