Item(by='bityard', descendants=None, kids=[25085551, 25086105], score=None, time=1605291221, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25085202, text='Calories in&#x2F;calories out ignores that fact that the _type_ of calorie matters a great deal. It also ignores the fact that the body is capable of moderating its metabolic rate in response to energy requirements and supply.<p>If calories in and out were _really_ all that mattered, I would be able to calculate my energy needs precisely and then eat exactly the right number of cupcakes (and only cupcakes) every day for the rest of my life. And yet we intuitively know that would be extremely unhealthy. Why is that?')
