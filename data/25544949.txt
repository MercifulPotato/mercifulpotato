Item(by='GuB-42', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609004553, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25543640, text='When you do design you need to know about typography, when you do finance, you need to know about accounting, when you are making a game, you need to know about game design, when you are doing weather forecasting, you need to know about meteorology.<p>None of them are skills that a programmer needs unless he works in the field. Maths is the same. You need very little maths to code unless your field requires it.<p>And even then, sometimes you don&#x27;t even need to. I worked on a software that computed material fatigue with absolutely no idea about the maths involved, the mechanical engineers just told me the formulas and constants to use as well as the expected results and I implemented them. (not entirely true, but it was just personal curiosity, absolutely not required for the job).')
