I don&#x27;t think we&#x27;re going to grow out of that behavior anytime in the next 1000 years. But the author makes a leap from there to &quot;nationalism is inevitable.&quot; There is an entire spectrum of organizational bodies and humans need not (and probably cannot) abolish all of them to form some sort of universal civilization.
