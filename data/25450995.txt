Item(by='tikhonj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608166748, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446929, text='People say this, but it seems like a false dichotomy. I use Emacs <i>exactly</i> because I choose productivity!<p>Emacs might take more time to configure, but that&#x27;s time measured in hours or, at most, days—a tiny blip compared to the total amount of time you spend programming in the environment! On the other hand, you get a system that you can quickly adapt to your specific needs in a way that other editors do not really support. It&#x27;s the perfect environment for &quot;situated software&quot;[1], and environment that can <i>grow</i>[2] with your needs and workflow.<p>Emacs&#x27;s learning curve and configuration is an O(1) cost for an O(n) benefit. As long as you&#x27;re planning to use it a lot—and we&#x27;re programmers, we&#x27;re going to use it a lot—even a small increase in the benefit is worth a substantially larger up-front cost.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.drmaciver.com&#x2F;2018&#x2F;11&#x2F;situated-software&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.drmaciver.com&#x2F;2018&#x2F;11&#x2F;situated-software&#x2F;</a><p>[2]: &quot;Grow&quot; in a similar sense to Guy Steele&#x27;s &quot;Growing a Language&quot; talk: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.youtube.com&#x2F;watch?v=_ahvzDzKdB0" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.youtube.com&#x2F;watch?v=_ahvzDzKdB0</a>')
