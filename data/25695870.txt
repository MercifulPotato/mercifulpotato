Item(by='caconym_', descendants=None, kids=[25697855], score=None, time=1610161117, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25695708, text='Lots of lies can&#x27;t easily be proven false, but many can, as long as we agree on some really basic principles.<p>For instance, if you tell me that two plus two equals five, I can count out two sets of two coins, then put them all together, count them again, and see that the answer is 4. So in that case I will be confident in calling you a liar.<p>You could, of course, say I&#x27;m wrong.')
