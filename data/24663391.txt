Item(by='marcthe12', descendants=None, kids=[24663744, 24664844], score=None, time=1601651184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24663074, text='Certain libs use dlopen() which needs dynamic linking. Mesa, Pam, Qt (static is possible but a lot of stuff will break), Glibc(static is possible but nsswitch.conf will break), Alsa. So most graphical apps will fail to be pure static(Prob possible but will hardware&#x2F;config specific). So we need to tackle and provide alternatives to dlopen.')
