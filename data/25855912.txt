Item(by='zrm', descendants=None, kids=[25855989], score=None, time=1611208130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25855811, text='The effect of not including non-free firmware is that the user is inconvenienced when using that hardware, but not hardware with free firmware. It also causes the user to be aware of whether the hardware they bought has non-free firmware.<p>One way to resolve the inconvenience is to install the firmware. Another is to buy different hardware. The latter puts pressure on vendors to provide free firmware.<p>The Linux desktop market is not a huge percentage of the PC market but it&#x27;s still millions of people.')
