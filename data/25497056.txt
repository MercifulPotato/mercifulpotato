Item(by='mattkrause', descendants=None, kids=[25498168], score=None, time=1608571451, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25496213, text='I asked my new dentist why her recommendations were so different from her predecessor.<p>She said that there&#x27;s not always one &quot;correct&quot; treatment. Some dentists will try to nip potential problems in the bud, lest they get worse. Others have more of a wait-and-see attitude which can avoid some procedures but make others worse.<p>Doctors, plumbers, builders, and mechanics all do the same thing. Do you replace the brake pads now, while the wheels are off anyway, or do you squeeze another season out of them since there&#x27;s still a bit left? Rebuild the whole deck, or maybe just replace the more banged up boards and wobbly railing?')
