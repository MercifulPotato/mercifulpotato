Item(by='cm2187', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611858628, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25942443, text='People conspiring to push prices in one direction is a clear cut market manipulation and I believe a criminal offence in most jurisdictions.<p>It&#x27;s fine to have lots of people buying something, but if they discuss how they can coordinate to hurt short sellers or something like that, particularly on a public forum, despite not being a lawyer I am fairly confident this is prosecutable.<p>Market trading regulations are full of fine lines where the wrong side can land you in jail. For instance the difference between front-running and pre-hedging &#x2F; anticipating market liquidity is basically the intent and the information the trader has.  The actions are indistinguishable.')
