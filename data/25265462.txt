Item(by='51stpage', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606833133, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25265042, text='I too don&#x27;t need any of the features that cryptocurrency brings.  However, cryptocurrencies are making substantial headway on solving the hardest class of problems that humans face: coordination problems. Common examples include the Tragedy of the Commons and collusion [1].<p>Vitalik has written some surprisingly approachable pieces on the various problems that cryptocurrencies face.  It&#x27;s not too much of a leap to see how these problems appear in non-cryptocurrency contexts.  The value I see in cryptocurrencies is that they <i>must</i> develop robust&#x2F;antifragile solutions to the coordination problems that have plagued society <i>forever</i>.  Even if some problems can&#x27;t be solved, then there are mental tools for making sense of what&#x27;s going on.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vitalik.ca&#x2F;general&#x2F;2019&#x2F;04&#x2F;03&#x2F;collusion.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vitalik.ca&#x2F;general&#x2F;2019&#x2F;04&#x2F;03&#x2F;collusion.html</a>\n[2]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vitalik.ca&#x2F;general&#x2F;2020&#x2F;08&#x2F;20&#x2F;trust.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vitalik.ca&#x2F;general&#x2F;2020&#x2F;08&#x2F;20&#x2F;trust.html</a>')
