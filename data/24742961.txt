Item(by='dmacvicar', descendants=None, kids=[24743035, 24743285], score=None, time=1602376224, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24742797, text='I never saw the point of getting a real tube amp unless you play exclusively in a studio or rehearsal room.<p>The volume required to crank and get the best of it sounds it makes it too loud for almost any residential area.<p>One of the best features of modeling amps is that you can get the sound of a cranked amp set at a high volume, with low&#x2F;moderate real volume or even with head-phones.<p>Even in a rehearsal room, real amps are too loud and make the band members start competing for volume. Low stage volume and in-ear-monitoring allows me to play better.')
