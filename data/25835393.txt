Item(by='Bakary', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611075136, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25827430, text='While I agree in principle, the easiest way to fall for propaganda is to believe yourself immune to it.<p>I would advocate for a two-track solution where you keep a healthy skepticism but try to be aware of your own pareidolia and possible lack of understanding of certain topics, and accept the idea that we are just jumping from one bubble to another (notice how &quot;question the narrative&quot; people tend to speak and think along the same tropes?)<p>I don&#x27;t mean total skepticism of everything for its own sake, but the idea that a person ought to pick their battles carefully and keep reminding themselves that they can&#x27;t understand the world as easily as they think they do. It&#x27;s important not to confuse skepticism for insight or critical thought.')
