Item(by='cjohnson318', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602171111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24718510, text='I am not trying to mislead anyone, this is my reasoning for using the language that I did. A subsidy is a sum of money given by the government to a corporation, so it is analogous to government spending on social welfare programs. A tax cut reduces the government&#x27;s income, which on a balance sheet, has the same effect as government spending. Sure, a tax cutting policy is not the same as a spending policy, but their net effect on cash flow are both negative.')
