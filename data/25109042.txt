Item(by='AnHonestComment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605511298, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25108760, text='Most people aren’t free trade absolutists — they want a mixture of free trade and national security.<p>One example is US ITAR restrictions, which prevent certain technologies being exported.<p>Another is looking to protect local food supplies against foreign competition — eg, dairy co-ops from foreign mega farms.<p>There’s lots of empirical evidence that a mixed system is more stable with little to no loss to innovation.<p>Most recently, COVID demonstrated the problem with foreign supply chains.')
