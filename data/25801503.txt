Item(by='skohan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610794905, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801376, text='This was one of the most redeeming things about Objective-C.  It is a superset of C, and when I was working with it, I found myself writing the high-level stuff in ObjC, but with a lot of pure-C functions sprinkled in.  It was really nice for working with C libraries like OpenGL, because you could find an example online and directly apply it without going through any kind of translation step.  It was a really cool feature to have.')
