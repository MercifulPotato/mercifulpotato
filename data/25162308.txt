Item(by='nkohari', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605891585, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25161022, text='With respect to the last paragraph, vaccination is a sticky topic, because the vaccine protects both you <i>and</i> those around you.<p>Vaccines are not 100% effective, don&#x27;t convey permanent immunity, and not everyone can be vaccinated due to various health restrictions. As a result, if you voluntarily choose not to be immunized when you could otherwise have been, you are voluntarily exposing some people you come into contact with to potential harm.<p>It&#x27;s up to you whether that falls within your definition of &quot;violence&quot;, but some would say so.')
