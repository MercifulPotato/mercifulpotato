Sadly I never quite got enough users to be worth continuing with.  These days I pay for gsuite instead, and use offline IMAP only to keep a backup of the remote mails.<p>It was an interesting thing to work on, and I&#x27;ve been following the new aerc client to see what happens there.  I&#x27;m sure there is still room for new console based mail-clients, perhaps mine was just a bit too unfamiliar!')
