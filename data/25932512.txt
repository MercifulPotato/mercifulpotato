Item(by='zamalek', descendants=None, kids=[25935265], score=None, time=1611774851, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25930053, text='Microsoft found that compressed pages were always faster, because added by de-&#x2F;compression was less than the latency to disk (given a sufficiently fast compression algorithm). As a bonus, it&#x27;s also faster to read and write compressed pages to disk (if that absolutely has to happen). Zswap is therefore enabled by default on Windows.<p>I configure my own kernel on Arch and Zswap is enabled by default there, too.')
