Item(by='clircle', descendants=None, kids=[25020666], score=None, time=1604786686, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25019919, text='N=45 is relatively high power for simple analyses, like a t-test (not sure if that&#x27;s what the paper did).<p>You can detect an effect size of 0.6 with 80% power when N is 45. You can also detect a effect of 0.7 with 90% power at N=45.<p>There are other things that can make results unreliable, like p-hacking, but a sample size of 45 is not a sufficient condition for bad science.')
