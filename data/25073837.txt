Item(by='zeteo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605209351, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25072064, text='There&#x27;s also some potential for selection bias in the drop-out rates:<p>&gt;The numbers of drop-outs after one, three, and five years of follow-up were 76 (19%), 104 (26%), and 132 (33%) in the HIIT group, 58 (15%), 76 (20%) and 101 (26%) in the MICT group, and 61 (8%), 95 (12%), and 156 (20%) in the control group.<p>In other words, the entire observed effect may be due to HIIT being better at making unhealthy subjects drop out of the study.')
