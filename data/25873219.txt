The SQLite docs gives developers the okay to write &quot;n+1&quot; style queries (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sqlite.org&#x2F;np1queryprob.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sqlite.org&#x2F;np1queryprob.html</a>).
