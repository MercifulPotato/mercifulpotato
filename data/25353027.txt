Item(by='1vuio0pswjnm7', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607470108, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25347307, text='&quot;I can&#x27;t snoop on the network traffic going over https but somehow I can get a list of all names queried.&quot;<p>SSL&#x2F;TLS&#x27;s servername extension puts those names in plaintext just like DNS.  The popular browsers all include this extension even when the website does not require it.<p>As such, one can get a list of names by snooping on HTTPS traffic, instead of DNS traffic:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;kontaxis&#x2F;snidump" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;kontaxis&#x2F;snidump</a><p>ESNI-enabled software and Clouflare&#x27;s ESNI-enabled CDN is an option, but you have to keep making DNS queries to get a key that changes every hour.')
