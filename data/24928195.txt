Item(by='GeneralMayhem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603958091, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24927999, text='I think the problem arises when people want to have it both ways. If you want to say &quot;we don&#x27;t like you, please leave&quot;, you&#x27;re probably [1] within your rights to do so, but then you don&#x27;t also get to claim to have a fair and impartial process.<p>[1] This gets nastier when &quot;you&quot; is a corporation or the host of a generally public event. Legally and ethically, your latitude to exclude people from a private party is much greater than is your latitude to exclude people from a public-ish gathering that any person could show up to.')
