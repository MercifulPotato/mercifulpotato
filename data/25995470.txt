Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=[25995708], score=None, time=1612221467, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25995160, text='Regular forms can also become irregularized; it&#x27;s not a one-way street. Speakers of American English will be familiar with the past tense of &#x27;dive&#x27; as &#x27;dove&#x27; instead of &#x27;dived&#x27;. Similarly, the plural of &#x27;octopus&#x27; has tended towards the irregular &#x27;octopi&#x27; instead of the regular &#x27;octopuses&#x27; (yay hypercorrection).')
