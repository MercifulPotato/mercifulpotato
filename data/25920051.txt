Item(by='dan-robertson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611689215, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25917519, text='I wouldn’t really call the intermission an ad break as they’re not really trying to sell you something. The main purpose is as a buffer between shows with slightly different timings or with live shows that won’t finish at an exact time (or may overrun like sports matches). You get the same on the radio too though they often have a short news briefing in the gap as well. Very occasionally they will have too large a gap to fill and read a poem.')
