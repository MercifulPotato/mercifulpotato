Item(by='lukifer', descendants=None, kids=[25164372], score=None, time=1605899387, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25162458, text='It&#x27;s a convoluted setup I&#x27;m not sure I&#x27;d recommend to anyone else! :) Still tinkering on it, but current rig is a Respeaker Core [0] running voice2json [1], which sends MQTT commands to a Mac Mini running mopidy [2], and the audio is routed through Airfoil [3] to multiple AirPlay speakers. (In theory, a Pi could drive all that directly, using shairport [4] rather than Airfoil.)<p>The major shortcoming is that Airfoil doesn&#x27;t support Airplay 2, so it doesn&#x27;t take full advantage of HomePod stereo pairing; and while the sync is perfect, it comes at the cost of massive lag (5+ seconds).
