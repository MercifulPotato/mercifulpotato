Item(by='bzbarsky', descendants=None, kids=[25091065], score=None, time=1605335340, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25089615, text='You have to keep in mind that the way tax collection worked in that area at the time is that it was contracted out by the state.  The contractor needed to deliver some amount of money to the state, and empowered to gather money from people; the difference between what they gathered and what they delivered to the state was their profit.<p>Tax collectors operating within this incentive structure were not likely to be very likable.')
