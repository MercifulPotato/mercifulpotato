Item(by='analog31', descendants=None, kids=[25938908], score=None, time=1611809927, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931398, text='I remember learning about HTML for the first time, maybe in Byte Magazine. It was before I actually had a computer with a browser. But the article talked about a benefit of HTML, which was that the tags could be interpreted by custom browsers for accessibility, including for the blind or sight-impaired. It seemed like a pretty cool idea to me.<p>Later on, when people started treating web design as a general purpose graphical and GUI framework, many of the accessibility benefits probably vanished.')
