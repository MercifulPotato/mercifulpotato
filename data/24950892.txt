Item(by='tremon', descendants=None, kids=[24951878], score=None, time=1604147663, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24950658, text='<i>I think there’s a difference between “stable” and “evolving”.</i><p>No, that&#x27;s pretty much the Debian definition of &quot;stable&quot;: software that doesn&#x27;t need changing for multiple years.
