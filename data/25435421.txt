You are not just paying for bandwidth. You are paying for the rest of the infrastructure. So, for example, in Azure&#x27;s case (which is what we use in $dayjob) we get VNETs, NSGs (basic firewalls), Routes, DNS, IP Addressing, etc, plus then bandwidth. Yes, i would like the bandwidth bill to be cheaper, but you also need to look, again, in the case of Azure, of the rest of what they are offering in a global network and number of POPs around the world...')
