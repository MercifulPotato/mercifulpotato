Item(by='dartharva', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610041711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25669864, text='Here in India everyone uses WhatsApp, from grandmas and children to teachers, office workers and even Police officers. It serves versatile purposes, of hosting both a chat platform and a social network for groups and has been so much ingrained in India&#x27;s societies that &quot;texting&quot; literally means sending a WhatsApp message here. In state elections, WhatsApp is the main platform the ruling party (BJP) focused most of its campaigns on. With such a setup it is extremely difficult to move to another platform.<p>The next popular platform is Telegram, but it is used mainly to share and access pirated movies and apps from shady channels than for everyday communication.')
