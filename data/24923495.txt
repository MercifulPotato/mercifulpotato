Unfortunately, in plenty more cases, you can&#x27;t. That goes for games, creative software, business admin tools, and just about anything else.<p>And while LibreOffice isn&#x27;t bad as an office suite in its own right, MS Office is the still <i>de facto</i> standard in many lines of work, and being 95% compatible when you export as an Excel spreadsheet or Word document just doesn&#x27;t cut it for professional use.<p>Until not just Windows <i>but also the major software packages that run on it</i> are no longer the dominant standards, it&#x27;s always going to be an uphill battle moving normal people onto an alternative platform like Linux, and one that often can&#x27;t be won today.')
