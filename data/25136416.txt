I do think that aesthetics could be improved in the tech field (I wuz an artist, way back, when).<p>But accessibility is critical. There are tools to do things like desaturate pages and apps, and measure contrast.<p>I write Apple apps, and I make sure that I provide (sometimes, quite verbose) accessibility labels to all my UI elements. There&#x27;s actually a ton more that can be done, but acc. labels, and respect for dark mode, reduced transparency, and increased contrast, as well as haptics, is usually sufficient.<p>One thing about providing accessibility strings: They should be translated, as well, if you are localizing.<p>Here&#x27;s a really cool tool, that I use: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;michelf.ca&#x2F;projects&#x2F;sim-daltonism&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;michelf.ca&#x2F;projects&#x2F;sim-daltonism&#x2F;</a>')
