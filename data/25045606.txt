Item(by='zxcmx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605014420, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25043662, text='I agree that &quot;lies&quot; and &quot;bullshit&quot; seem to be among the defining problems of our time.<p>What seems to have broken down is trust. Any program to restore some ground truth to common discourse will have to operate by building (or rebuilding) broad societal trust in one or more public institutions.<p>It&#x27;s a hard, long-term problem and I don&#x27;t have any good ideas for solving it.<p>The problem, transitively, seems to be of similar importance to climate change or public health. Meaningful action in these areas depends on the public arriving at a broad common understanding of the issues.')
