Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603645269, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887656, text='Australian fuel prices are listed in cents to one decimal place. &gt;80% of the time the last digit is a nine, the rest of the time it’s most commonly a 5. I’ve seen other odd digits rarely, with even numbers just about unheard of. But what you actually <i>pay</i> will be rounded to the cent, or to the nearest 5¢ if you pay in cash.<p>The whole concept involved here is called psychological pricing (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Psychological_pricing" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Psychological_pricing</a>). I wish it was illegal.')
