Item(by='naniwaduni', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609367523, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25586924, text='No. Not only because what&#x27;s &quot;basic&quot; varies between languages, but because it&#x27;s precisely the most &quot;basic&quot;-seeming lemmas that map <i>most poorly</i> between languages.<p>Take the first ten &quot;operations&quot; of the Ogden list: come, get, give, go, keep, let, make, put, seem, take. <i>Every one</i> of these is the kind of word that has multiple non-interchangeable &quot;synonyms&quot; that you could use, depending on context. Expressing the &quot;same&quot; &quot;concept&quot; in another language is kind of like being forced to choose between them, when there&#x27;s generally no 1:1 mapping.')
