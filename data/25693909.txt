Item(by='arrrg', descendants=None, kids=[25694401, 25695695], score=None, time=1610153970, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25693111, text='A typical argument pattern is to use free speech as a full stop in any discussion. Engaging with the content of something is rejected straight away, it’s always the principle of the thing.<p>That’s a tiring argument and describing that as a very American way of fetishising free speech is entirely appropriate.<p>Free speech is hard. And there are many difficult questions around it where you have to actually engage with the content of something.<p>So I get why people want to take shortcuts, but that is just so tiring.
