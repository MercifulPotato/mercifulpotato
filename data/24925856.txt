That and partial page changes will be possible with Turblinks frames which is a huge upgrade.<p>A while back I made a video on the benefits of using Turbolinks and how to set it up if anyone is interested <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nickjanetakis.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;improving-your-page-load-speeds-on-any-site-with-turbolinks" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nickjanetakis.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;improving-your-page-load-spee...</a>.<p>Just be mindful that Turbolinks alone isn&#x27;t enough to replace a SPA. It&#x27;s typically paired with Stimulus but you can pair it up with jQuery, Alpine or whatever you want when you want to do more than page transitions, form submissions and swapping content in a specific area of a page.')
