Item(by='sputr', descendants=0, kids=None, score=2, time=1606480056, title="Mobile developers: don't only depend on the phone language setting", item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='Dear mobile app developers: If you support more than just English, but you do not support ALL languages ... don&#x27;t only depend on phone language settings!<p>PLEASE add a language select dropdown to your settings page! Many of your users can speak multiple languages, but English isn&#x27;t necessarily one of them!<p>You may not support Slovenian, but if you support Croatian, Serbian or German your Slovenian speaking, but not English speaking, users might still be able to use your app.<p>Don&#x27;t force them to change their entire phone&#x27;s language just to use your app.')
