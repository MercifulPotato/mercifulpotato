Item(by='conradev', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603189804, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24830801, text='Comparing Slack to email, in an email I generally provide a feedback sandwich: start with a positive note or warm introduction, transition into a call to action with explanatory details, and again end on a positive note.<p>In Slack I find that I can use emoji in lieu of some of the positivity, if that makes sense.<p>If I have any takeaway from this conversation it is that I should use _more_ emoji simply because a lot of emotional context is being lost through the internet. I feel like this is especially important because I manage people and my tone can really matter.')
