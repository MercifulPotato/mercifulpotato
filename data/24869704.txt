That paid off for them (and it&#x27;s arguable whether or not it was a good thing for the industry in general -it sank a number of companies, but also put Seattle firmly into the center of the tech world).<p>Then they became a blue-chip company, and all that startup-y stuff wasn&#x27;t working anymore. Also, the consumer market changed a lot, with mobile overtaking desktop systems. For many, many folks, smartphones are the only computers that they will ever use.<p>He did have the right vision for a new corporate direction.')
