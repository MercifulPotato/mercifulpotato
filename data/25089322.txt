Item(by='AnthonyMouse', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605315401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25088273, text='Preventing something from being copyable is only possible by not distributing it. If you can see it you can copy it.<p>The copyright holder could prevent you from copying it by never distributing it, but that doesn&#x27;t mean you don&#x27;t have a right to fair use, it only means you don&#x27;t have the ability to exercise that right. Much the same as you can&#x27;t exercise freedom of the press if you can&#x27;t afford a printing press (or any modern equivalent).<p>It might be physically impossible for you to have an abortion, e.g. because you&#x27;re infertile, but that doesn&#x27;t mean you don&#x27;t have a right to one under existing precedent.')
