They’d rather eat stolen food from garbage rather than have to work for a restaurant meal.<p>My mother was a primary school teacher in a Gipsy village and saw this first hand: small kids being encouraged to beg or steal instead of study by their own parents, abandoning school for the street lifestyle their family lived.<p>There are exceptions, of course, but the dark attraction of the gipsy culture should not be understated.')
