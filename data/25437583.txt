I don&#x27;t agree with the categorization of the present as &quot;peak big tech&quot;. Most tech firms have long roadmaps and global expansion plans, we are hardly at the peak.<p>Every social network that has gained any significant popularity (&gt;100M users) is built on a closed system. Email is the only open protocol system that is relevant to bring up, and its flaws and shortcomings are well documented. Most enterprises use a closed system on top of email (Office 365 or G suite primarily) and communication is migrating to entirely closed systems (Slack or Teams).<p>Closed systems offer huge advantages for innovation, providing excellent user experience and strong privacy controls. As such, closed systems will continue to be the dominant paradigm.')
