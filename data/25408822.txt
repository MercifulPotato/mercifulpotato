Item(by='IgorPartola', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607884266, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25402841, text='I am curious, is it possible to implement a headerless allocator? Basically keep an index of free blocks and an index of used blocks using a B-tree or a skip list, and allocate and free them by simply moving from one index to the other. Presumably this would make it more cache friendly.<p>Toying with allocators is one of my favorite programming pastimes for some reason.')
