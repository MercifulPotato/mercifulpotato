Item(by='ggreer', descendants=None, kids=[25103105], score=None, time=1605462263, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25102837, text='It’s not anti-EV sentiment. It’s anti-blanket-ban sentiment. Even a decade from now there will be some use cases where gas vehicles are a better fit than EVs. Instead of a blanket ban on new gas cars, it’s less coercive and more economically efficient to tax their externalities.<p>I’m not a fan of gas vehicles. The only car I own is a Tesla. Still, I’m aware that gasoline cars do have certain advantages and banning them outright seems more detrimental than taxing them.')
