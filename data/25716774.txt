If you&#x27;re controlling your calorie intake then that&#x27;s irrelevant.<p>It&#x27;s relevant because controlling caloric intake without relying on the bodies natural hunger signals seems to be close to impossible for most people. Everyone else eats until they are satiated and will gain weight.<p>There&#x27;s an obesity epidemic in the US and discounting mounting evidence of a substance modifying our eating habits in a potentially unhealthy way as &quot;unsubstantiated woo&quot; strikes me as counterproductive.
