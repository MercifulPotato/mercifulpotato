So I looked into the RFC&#x27;s author, and noted his position as one of the champions of QUIC as HTTP&#x2F;3, and his promotion of encrypted DNS (specifically DoH), and then an interesting article that on the surface sounds like it advocates for ensuring end users retain control of their DNS requests, but which actually advocates for burying the implementation of DNS in the OS and only exposing a control layer to the end user (e.g. an on&#x2F;off toggle).
