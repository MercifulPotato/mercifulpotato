Item(by='Edmond', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607282620, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325633, text='A &quot;trusted MITM&quot; is just that, a trusted MITM...You kind of accept the compromises that come with such a setup.<p>A CA that can issue a certificate to a third-party without the consent of the domain owner is not a trusted CA by definition.<p>In other words the issue in Kazakhstan should probably not be viewed as a technical issue...authoritarian governments will always get their way if the only recourse against them is a technical solution. In the case of Kazakhstan, they are forcing their citizens to accept the compromised CA, that is not a technical problem to solve.')
