Item(by='colechristensen', descendants=None, kids=[25262599, 25262459], score=None, time=1606795955, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25262147, text='In my engineering education my favorite and most productive learning came from the professor who assigned graded homework and provided fully worked solutions to the given problems before when the problems were assigned.<p>You had to do them, you were completely able to just copy the solution, and when you were working on something you got immediate feedback as to whether you were doing it well. If you wanted to learn you could, if you didn’t you wouldn’t.  The lack of a week or two of lag between solving a problem and figuring out if you had done it correctly really made a difference.')
