Item(by='ninjha', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608660693, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25508101, text='Kubernetes is a multi-physical-machine operating system. In a typical OS you have CPU, RAM, etc. and the kernel decides how much of each to allocate to each running task. Kubernetes works the same way, but across multiple computers by default- and it can additionally manage any other arbitrary resource through the use of CustomResourceDefinitions (IPs, higher level concepts like “Minecraft Servers”, etc.)')
