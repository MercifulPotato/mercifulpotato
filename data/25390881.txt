Item(by='tromp', descendants=None, kids=[25391037], score=None, time=1607720211, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25390281, text='&gt; if it&#x27;s going to halt we don&#x27;t really care what it does immediately before.<p>The final move does not matter, but the symbol written matters to the extent that BB(n) is defined as the number of 1s on the tape upon halting.<p>So, while redundant, the 4(n+1) count is chosen for parsimony. If one is willing to specialize the transition to halt to have no move and a mandatory write of 1, then a count of 4n+1 still agrees upon the same value of BB(n).')
