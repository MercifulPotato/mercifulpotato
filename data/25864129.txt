If you&#x27;re a blind reader, you&#x27;d prefer not to have that experience when you&#x27;re using a piece of software, you&#x27;d prefer something that just works reliably.<p>It&#x27;s the same reason you probably used a keyboard to type this comment instead of speech to text.
