Item(by='shash7', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608774229, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25521487, text='I&#x27;ve been building complex UIs since early this year and one thing that&#x27;s been interesting is that whenever I add a feature which performs an action automatically for the user(let&#x27;s call this a smart action), it almost always backfires.<p>Generally speaking, people like their actions to be predictible, reproductive and deterministic. Whenever an app trys to add smart actions in the hopes of making the UI more responsive, it has the opposite reaction.<p>Notion seems like one of those tools that relies on a lot of background smart actions. Not that I mind too much, I&#x27;m a big fan actually.')
