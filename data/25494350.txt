Item(by='roflc0ptic', descendants=None, kids=[25495270, 25494497, 25494444, 25494595, 25494878], score=None, time=1608554271, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25493646, text='I just started working somewhere that does a different equity scheme called “profit interest.” The gist is, they issue you equity whose worth is based on growth in valuation from when you joined. So if you’re granted 1% shares and the company grows from 100m to 200m on liquidity, you’re entitled to 1m. It avoids you having to front money for stock options, and it also avoids the tax burden b&#x2F;c when issued, the shares are worth zero dollars')
