Item(by='yazaddaruvala', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607101884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25303485, text='I’ll add a controversial opinion, specific to  programming an application:<p>Premature Abstraction is the root of all evil.<p>Don’t panic! The code will be a mess. Let is be a mess. Don’t abstract too early, or the eventual mess that regenerates will be even worse than without an abstraction.<p>If a part of your application or assumptions in a piece of code haven’t changed for 12 months, then abstract that part of your application. However, only abstract the bare minimum.')
