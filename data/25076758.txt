Item(by='LinuxBender', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605223796, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25074959, text='A temporary work around that helped me was to use<p><pre><code>  sudo route add -net 17.253.17.207 255.255.255.255 -blackhole;\n  sudo route add -net 17.253.17.202 255.255.255.255 -blackhole;\n</code></pre>\nbased on<p><pre><code>  dig +short ocsp.apple.com |grep -E ^[1-9]\n  17.253.17.207\n  17.253.17.202\n</code></pre>\nThat shortens the delay.  Others here found adding ocsp.apple.com to &#x2F;etc&#x2F;hosts using a private address also helps.  Whichever is easiest for you.  To remove:<p><pre><code>  sudo route delete -net 17.253.17.207 255.255.255.255;\n  sudo route delete -net 17.253.17.202 255.255.255.255;\n</code></pre>\nor reboot.')
