Item(by='ainar-g', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606979363, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25286263, text='Niklaus Wirth is a visionary, and his legacy is honestly severely\nunderappreciated.  <i>Pascal</i> was a reasonably well designed language for its time\nand purpose.  The <i>Oberon</i> ecosystem has in many ways inspired <i>Plan 9 From Bell\nLabs</i> and, transitively, the whole post-<i>Unix</i> movement.  His book “Compiler\nConstruction” is still one of the most accessible introductions into the world\nof compilers, which I recommend to anyone interested in the topic.')
