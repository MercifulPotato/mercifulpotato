Item(by='nudgeee', descendants=None, kids=[24759421], score=None, time=1602514730, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24754611, text='In order to carry the USB logo, chargers&#x2F;cables&#x2F;connectors&#x2F;etc must pass a suite of compliance testing to get certification. Specifically for USB-C, you can find details of the USB Power Delivery 2.0&#x2F;3.0 Compliance Plans at <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.usb.org&#x2F;usbc" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.usb.org&#x2F;usbc</a>. This of course does not stop cheap knock-offs being made without proper compliance testing.<p>I think as long as these hacks stay in &#x27;prototype&#x27; mode, and is clearly called out as such, then it is fine. Once it goes beyond that (ie for sale without proper testing, etc), then it gets worrisome.')
