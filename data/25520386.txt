Item(by='oarsinsync', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608747576, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25518985, text='The regulation doesn’t have to prohibit the existence of the product, but it can ensure that consumers are made explicitly aware before &#x2F; during purchase: this product requires an internet connection, and may not function without one.<p>There’s nothing obvious at purchase time that this new breed of Apple laptops require an internet connection to reinstall.
