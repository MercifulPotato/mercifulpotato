Item(by='Nextgrid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603624103, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24883168, text='The free software “industry” and its enthusiasts should realise and accept that IT is a <i>tool</i> and not a lifestyle for many people. This includes me despite being a developer and somewhat of a tech enthusiast - a decade ago I had plenty of time to tinker around with a custom Linux installation, nowadays I just run macOS and call it a day.<p>Software that doesn’t infringe on your freedoms is great, but functionality and user experience is important. A big reason why proprietary and increasingly user-hostile software won is because it <i>works</i> well enough (despite the hostility) for the majority of people.')
