It’s a pragmatic simple container and now VM management platform and can help companies to build vendor neutral cluster and high availability system leveraging the knowledge of HPC and Linux cluster community.<p>Kubernetes is good for google size companies, for startups it’s an additional overhead and tie their products to vendors specific kubernetes’ distributions given the complexity and moving parts.<p>Try the above setup and than see the simplicity. Also give a try to kubernetes and see which one is easier and better able to help. In my view for installing database and  blob storage infrastructure as part of the application VM’s are still better than containers.')
