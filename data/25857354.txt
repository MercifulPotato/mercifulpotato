Item(by='EE84M3i', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611224048, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25856211, text='Note that you still can use the the iro&#x2F;color suffix for aka (red), ao (blue), midori (green), you just don&#x27;t have to and they also function as standalone adjectives.<p>E.g. akai (赤い) -- red (adjective) vs akairo (赤色) -- the color red (noun)<p>compare: chaiiroi (茶色い) -- light brown (adjective) vs chaiiro (茶色) the color light brown (noun) -- literally &quot;tea color&quot;')
