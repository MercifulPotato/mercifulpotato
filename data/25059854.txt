That&#x27;s why I created quicktemplate [1]. Usually it is much easier and natural to write templates in quicktemplate comparing to built-in templating library. As an additional benefit, quicktemplate templates work much faster (10x and more) than standard Go templates. The only downside that quicktemplate templates must be pre-compiled to Go code before building the app.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;valyala&#x2F;quicktemplate" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;valyala&#x2F;quicktemplate</a>')
