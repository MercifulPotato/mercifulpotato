Item(by='patentatt', descendants=None, kids=[25400033, 25400073], score=None, time=1607789390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25398722, text='Just because it ‘felt natural’ doesn’t imply that it’s a discovery rather than invention. Generally in IP the word discovery is in regard to a natural phenomenon, something found in nature. Natural laws are discovered, everything else is invented. So what you describe sounds like an invention rather than a discovery.')
