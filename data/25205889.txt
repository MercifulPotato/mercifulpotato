Item(by='sliken', descendants=None, kids=[25206654], score=None, time=1606276494, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25205180, text='Typical desktops have 2 64 bit dimm into 2 channels (64 bits wide each) or 1 channel (128 bits wide).<p>The M1 Mac&#x27;s seem to be 8 channels x 16 bits, which is the same bandwidth as a desktop (although running the ram at 4266 MHz is much higher than usual).  The big win is you can have 8 cache misses in flight instead of 2.  With 8 cores, 16 GPU cores, and 16 ML cores I suspect the M1 has more in flight cache misses than most.')
