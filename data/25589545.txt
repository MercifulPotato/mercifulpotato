Item(by='AnHonestComment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609388342, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25588483, text='&gt; We can only do our best.<p>Maximizing QALY is the standard medical tool to decide what “do our best” means.<p>If something is QALY negative, it means we were better off doing nothing — “do nothing” is the zero line for QALY analysis.<p>The classic example is stopping chemo for terminal cancer patients or deciding which of two patients receives an organ.<p>“Not lockdown; do nothing” is the zero line for evaluating responses to the pandemic — so yes, if our current plan is QALY negative, we should have done nothing. The current plan has an expected net loss of quality years of life.')
