Item(by='jorams', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612180366, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25983630, text='Rather than keeping a lower-quality copy of my music on my phone, I stream my own music library by running MPD[1] in a satellite setup. All files are served by a simple webserver[2] that converts them to Opus when requested, to save bandwidth. Music I never listen to never gets converted. I wrote about my setup here[3].<p>This won&#x27;t work on iPhone, since MPD hasn&#x27;t been ported, but it works great for Android.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.musicpd.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.musicpd.org&#x2F;</a><p>[2]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~joram&#x2F;transcoding-music-server" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~joram&#x2F;transcoding-music-server</a><p>[3]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.joram.io&#x2F;blog&#x2F;android-streaming-mpd&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.joram.io&#x2F;blog&#x2F;android-streaming-mpd&#x2F;</a>')
