Both are trying to integrate the best of the other operating system. WSL -&gt; Give Windows users a good modern web app development experience. Wine -&gt; Give Linux users Office and games. To that end, both have their measure of success, and shortcomings. As someone who ran Linux on the desktop for 19 years, I tried every incarnation of Wine I could get my hands on.
