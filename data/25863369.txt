Item(by='mhh__', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611258929, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25863002, text='When I am writing or choosing (or bindings to) an API I always go and look at other languages (especially what the functional people do) to avoid repeating non-obvious mistakes and seeing where the impedance mismatches are.<p>Most recently, I&#x27;ve been playing with eBPF on Linux: The system call API is terse and pretty impenetrable, whereas the high-level APIs either mean writing gadgets in C with a nearly-blind debugging experience or relying on a library to retain the power of the featureset the kernel exposes.')
