The ideas and processes are the goals or blueprints that need to be implemented.<p>Today I watched four guys pour the foundation of a house using very conventional methods. Those four guys weren&#x27;t along for the ride. They were building the house. True they didn&#x27;t come up with the method, nor the concrete, but they did build part of the house.<p>Likewise, the person who made my taco last night might not have invented the taco but she did make it.<p>Very often the most impressive part of building something isn&#x27;t the novelty, it&#x27;s the raw effort.')
