Item(by='nicbou', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609491905, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25595280, text='Googling isn&#x27;t a standalone skill. It comes with the ability to ask the right questions, and that&#x27;s only possible with some diagnosing skills.<p>The author&#x27;s dry throat issue is an excellent example of this. If you just look for &quot;sore throat&quot;, you likely won&#x27;t find much. You first need to form a few hypotheses and test those with better-formed searches.<p>This is also where broad technical knowledge comes useful. It&#x27;s easier to form an hypothesis if you understand what you&#x27;re working with. This is how you can quickly identify DNS problems, frayed cables, bad fuses, lack of an ingredient, and various other common culprits by their symptoms.')
