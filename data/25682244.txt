That means that if ESNI is used the domain is never transmitted in plaintext. However, it has the signfificant downside that if you can&#x27;t or don&#x27;t want to use Firefox&#x27;s DoH, and your system DNS is already encrypted (DoH, DoT, VPN, etc.), then you can&#x27;t get ESNI.<p>See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;bugzilla.mozilla.org&#x2F;show_bug.cgi?id=1500289" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;bugzilla.mozilla.org&#x2F;show_bug.cgi?id=1500289</a>')
