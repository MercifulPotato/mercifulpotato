Item(by='KaiserPro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603175725, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24826628, text='Modern farming assumes that the soil is a mostly sterile medium to dump both fertiliser and seed.<p>Basically sand with a tiny bit of organic fluff.<p>Firstly sand blows away, secondly it doesn&#x27;t hold water anywhere near as well. This means that you need more drainage channels, which means you get more runoff, which means you need more fertiliser.<p>_proper_ soil, with plenty of organic matter is both more porous _and_ able to absorb more water.')
