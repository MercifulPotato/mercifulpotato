Item(by='physicles', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610846919, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801005, text='The book Designing Data-Intensive Applications talks about “schema on write” vs “schema on read”. In order to interpret your data, you must apply a schema, so your choice is whether you do that explicitly when the data is written, or implicitly when it’s read.<p>Or as Yoda would say, Schema read or schema write, there is no “no schema”.')
