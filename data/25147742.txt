Item(by='kranner', descendants=None, kids=[25147821], score=None, time=1605778271, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25147250, text='&gt; You know, sometimes less is more, if the game world is just about detailed enough that you can make out what things are supposed to be, you have to let the creative part of your brain do much more work to fill in the blanks, which ultimately gives the final product that your consciousness consumes a more personal note, since it&#x27;s filled with your fantasy.<p>Scott McCloud made the same point while discussing Abstraction in comics in his book &#x27;Understanding Comics&#x27;. The more abstract the representation of a character is, the more easily a reader can project themselves onto that character and identify with it.')
