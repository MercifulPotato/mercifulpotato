Item(by='nine_k', descendants=None, kids=[25321452], score=None, time=1607226868, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25318363, text='British customs used to have a simple mechanism to bind declared value to market value.<p>A captain could declare the value of the cargo whatever he sees fit, and pay the tax on that amount.<p>The customs reserved the right to buy the entire cargo at the declared price, at their discretion.<p>It&#x27;s a bit harder to implement with large parcels of land, but should work well with home-sized lots.')
