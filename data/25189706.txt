Item(by='tomxor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606155178, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25189522, text='Clarification for those unfamiliar with NPM:<p>The weekly downloads on NPM do not indicate individual manual project installs. They include <i>every</i> download including automatically through `npm install` from package.json, and often further automation via CI tools (which is where the vast majority of download numbers come from on all npm packages). These amplify the numbers significantly.<p>i.e While they are a proxy for popularity, it can only be used relatively.')
