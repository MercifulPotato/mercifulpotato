And they did some contractual tying of Office to Windows too iirc.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.justice.gov&#x2F;atr&#x2F;us-v-microsoft-courts-findings-fact" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.justice.gov&#x2F;atr&#x2F;us-v-microsoft-courts-findings-f...</a><p>So it should be no surprise that DoJ may have problems with attempts to tie Occulus to Facebook’s legal monopoly product.')
