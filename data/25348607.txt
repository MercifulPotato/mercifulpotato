Item(by='miahi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607449081, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25343422, text='Deleting the queues is an operational decision that I made to be able to put the system back online after the network device was replaced (the important part was the uptime&#x2F;SLA). From a quick analysis of the logs the percentage of bad messages was ~90% (there was a ~50% chance that the original &quot;touched&quot; bit was 0 so no change was done, but the messages had multiple &quot;touched&quot; bits at fixed intervals).<p>There was an escape hatch, but the conditions to hit it were a bit complex. Implementing new message filtering of this kind at 2AM while the system was down was not feasible.')
