Item(by='ralmidani', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610387309, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25730949, text='Releasing people convicted of non-violent drug offenses is not enough; it’s important that we provide a smooth ramp to re-join society.<p>We should wipe their records clean, train them in useful, employable skills, and help them find decent jobs. (Edit: this is the bare minimum; I don’t want to get into direct monetary compensation, even though I think it’s worth considering.)<p>I would imagine a lot of people formerly-incarcerated for non-violent drug offenses are (rightfully) resentful of society, and we should give them an alternative to committing crimes in order to survive.')
