Item(by='ISL', descendants=None, kids=[24766094], score=None, time=1602598314, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24765616, text='Yes.<p>When an airplane hits the ground, it leaves a crater. &quot;To Crater&quot; is to fall precipitously or crash. In this context, it is in the age-old tradition of financial journalism of selecting exaggerated descriptors of market movement.<p>&quot;Pork bellies fry in early-market trading, fall 5%.&quot;<p>It is generally helpful to completely remove the descriptors and replace them with &quot;increase&quot; or &quot;decrease&quot;.<p>&quot;San Francisco Apartment Rents Decrease 31%&quot;<p>That way, you can decide for yourself whether or not it is a big deal.')
