Item(by='zelly', descendants=None, kids=[24940266], score=None, time=1604019308, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24933845, text='The success of Medium has proven that lowering the barrier to write content is extremely valuable. If you&#x27;re asking people to &quot;work for free&quot; then it should be as easy as possible and not feel like an errand. When you&#x27;re inspired to write something, you just want to write it and not have the computer get in your way, or else you&#x27;ll understandably give up. Wikipedia also proved this a couple decades ago.<p>Also rolling your own wiki is beyond stupid in this day and age. Just import all the MDN articles to MediaWiki. Making a script for this is a one-person weekend project. As I understand it though, Mozilla owns the copyright to all the content and they might shut you down for doing so.')
