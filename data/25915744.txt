Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611669203, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25911915, text='This comes back to what I mentioned earlier in the thread that it “requires a shocking amount of initiative on the part of the patient.”<p>There are also easy and hard paths through the system. If you are motivated to get a diagnosis, you can talk to a psychologist or psychiatrist and get a diagnosis. You can look for a therapist who specializes in those issues. You can take this diagnosis to a psychiatrist and say “I have diagnosis X and symptoms Y.”<p>If you go into the hospital with a broken leg, you can just check into the hospital and you will come out with a cast and an appointment to have it removed. If you have mental health issues, unless they are severe enough to require hospitalization, you are in charge of your own treatment.')
