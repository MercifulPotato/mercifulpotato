Item(by='hkarthik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603438558, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24856897, text='Yes, I started with a massive Windows 8 Media center PC in 2003 which evolved to an Xbox1 with XBMC, followed by a couple of all in one PCs which could load standalone XBMC Linux based kernels by 2010.<p>Around 2015, I switched to having a Synology NAS hold the content with small streaming devices around the house with Plex running on both ends.<p>But these days 99% of the content we want is on Netflix, HBO Max, or Disney+. We occasionally rent movies via AppleTV. I can get all the content I want more conveniently through these services. And I am rarely debugging the setup during a family movie night while my wife and daughters groan about how my stuff never works.')
