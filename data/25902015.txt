Item(by='user-the-name', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611574223, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25899572, text='The Amiga had a large part of its regular OS libraries in ROM, and the base OS was available directly to the bootloader on the floppy.<p>You could ignore it and access the hardware directly, if you wanted, and many games did, but you could also use the OS if you wanted to.<p>The default bootloader would basically just say &quot;launch the default shell and tell it to load the startup shell script file&quot;.')
