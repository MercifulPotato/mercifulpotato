Item(by='PaulHoule', descendants=None, kids=[24652524], score=None, time=1601564955, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24651565, text='I am skeptical when a business says &quot;we really make money on A,  but B is just a hobby&quot;.<p>Often you will hear &quot;we really make money on B and A is just a hobby&quot; a few years later or maybe a few hours later to a different audience.<p>If a company is serving you as a hobby then it is not listening to you through price signals,  you are going to get bad service,  and the company will be out of touch for the market and heading for bankruptcy in the long term unless it is counting on a bail-out.')
