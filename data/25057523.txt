Item(by='0xdeadb00f', descendants=None, kids=[25057781], score=None, time=1605094814, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25053190, text='&gt; - The &quot;virtual home&quot; specified with --private=&#x2F;path&#x2F;to&#x2F;folder runs the app with the specified folder as a home folder. I use this for all the apps I sandbox to make sure my real home does not get polluted by tens of config files, cache, etc.<p>I understand firejail does a lot more but FYI, you can do this simply by typing `HOME=&#x2F;path&#x2F;to&#x2F;folder &lt;app&gt;` in your shell.<p>Or do this in a wrapper script, for example:<p><pre><code>    #!&#x2F;bin&#x2F;sh\n    export HOME=&#x2F;path&#x2F;to&#x2F;dir\n    firefox</code></pre>')
