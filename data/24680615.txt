Item(by='mhh__', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601831936, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24679265, text='I would look at the LLVM tutorials, and maybe the insides of your favourite languages compiler (other than clang)<p>When it comes to both parsing and emitting IR, keep it simple (stupid!) - the difficult bit (on a day to day basis) with the compiler is almost always the language semantics, the backend is more theory-dense but to just implement a language you can use LLVM as a black-box.<p>If you minimise mutable state and utilise lowering (you can implement foreach by lowering the AST to a for loop, for example) your compiler will be much easier to debug.')
