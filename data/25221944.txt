I find this is more convincing initially for mechanically sympathetic programmers (including myself) than any taxonomy of endofunctors.<p>[0] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;elementsofprogramming.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;elementsofprogramming.com&#x2F;</a>')
