Item(by='pupppet', descendants=None, kids=[24652110, 24652355, 24653664, 24651990], score=None, time=1601565624, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24650832, text='The problem with being a slave to these Lighthouse scores is the test is only concerned with the one page you are testing.  If you assume your visitor will only visit a single page on your site that&#x27;s fine, but even if they visit a second page, nearly everything it suggests you do will make that second page load <i>slower</i> than if you had developed the page using the traditional methods- external CSS&#x2F;JS files that apply to your entire site and can be cached.')
