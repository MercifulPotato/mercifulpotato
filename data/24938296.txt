Item(by='xvedejas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604022944, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24933239, text='A nitpick on technical language: what you describe is a _positive_ feedback loop. A negative feedback loop is one that is able to calibrate its output by applying corrections to fluctuations. On the other hand, a positive feedback is one which can grow out of control, because it does the opposite. I think that&#x27;s what you meant when describing how failing media outlets will double-down on the wrong strategy.')
