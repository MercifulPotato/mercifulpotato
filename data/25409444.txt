Borrow lots of money at a low fixed rate to buy property and you might even make a profit.\nUnexpected inflation is a wealth tax on people who loan out their wealth at fixed rates or store most of it in cash, and an sales tax on people and organisations unable to renegotiate salary or contracts. Exactly who is negatively (and positively) affected is complicated, but it certainly isn&#x27;t directly proportional to how well off people are.')
