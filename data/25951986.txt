Item(by='bigiain', descendants=None, kids=[25952529], score=None, time=1611881260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25942029, text='I think there should totally be a commonly agreed philosophy of developers being able to say:<p>&quot;Here&#x27;s my hobby project, that I write for my own satisfaction. I&#x27;m happy for others to use it, and to license it under my choice of open source license. I am not though, going to feel obliged to change anything about my work on it in response to demands, requests, ideas, advice, or pull requests - or even acknowledge or read any of those. This repo will continue to be my hobby project to accomplish my goals in my timeframes for my personal amusement. If you find it useful? Great! If you want something different? I hope you make it yourself and maybe share it to, or at least find what you are looking for somewhere else, good luck.&quot;<p>There&#x27;s a fundamental difference between &quot;scratching your own itch&quot; and &quot;becoming a software project manager and community leader&quot;. I&#x27;d much rather people who only ever want to do the first of those, don&#x27;t feel the need to keep all their code hidden out of fear of being pushed into the second of those.')
