The maximum loss is infinite and so it is hard to get a brokerage to allow you to do this, but it is done. Indeed if the price goes up you are on the hook to buy the shares and deliver them.<p>Calls are commonly either covered by shares you own or in a spread where you buy and sell the same ticker at different prices or dates.')
