Otherwise it&#x27;s basically curl | sh with all its associated problems <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;docs.monadical.com&#x2F;s&#x2F;against-curl-sh" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;docs.monadical.com&#x2F;s&#x2F;against-curl-sh</a><p>Using URLs means there are no rules enforced, the code hosted at that URL can change out from under you without any warning.
