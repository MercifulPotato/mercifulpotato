Item(by='DoreenMichele', descendants=None, kids=[24889795], score=None, time=1603582879, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24882734, text='I live in a small town. America used to have a lot of thriving small towns with lively downtown areas. I am interested in seeing more support for that sort of thing.<p>Not everyone lives in the big city. Not everyone needs to. Even within the big city, there is lots of room for improvement.<p>And making it possible for more people to run some of their errands on foot doesn&#x27;t mean &quot;You aren&#x27;t allowed to leave this 5 square kilometer area and can never drive or use public transit.&quot; It just means some of the things you do normally can be reached on foot (for a greater percentage of people than is true currently).')
