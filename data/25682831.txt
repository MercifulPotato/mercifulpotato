This ended when we got got off the gold standard in the 70&#x27;s.<p>As bitcoin becomes the dominant non-sovereign store of value, we’ll slowly start to reprice things in terms of satoshis (sats), the smallest denomination of bitcoin [3].<p>Sats are already the standard on Cash App and other apps and services that exchange fiat for BTC.
