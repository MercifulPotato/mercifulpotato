Item(by='refurb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603363754, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24852333, text='Reminds me of a chart I saw on the incremental gain of various therapies for colorectal cancer.  You’d see a lot of discussion around “this drug increased median overall survival by 4 months, is that really a worthwhile advancement?”.<p>But you track it over 15 years, median overall survival had gone from 6 months to 10 years.  All small incremental gains.<p>That’s the difference between a death sentence and manageable disease.')
