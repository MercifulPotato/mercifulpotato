Item(by='bluGill', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611975350, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25965125, text='The job of negotiation is to get a good deal for all. Price is only one part of that - if you only focus on price bad things happen.  too low a price and the supplier goes out of business before you get all the product.  Too low a price and your supplier doesn&#x27;t have enough profit to remain in business for the next time you need something.  Too low a price and the supplier won&#x27;t negotiate with you at all next time you need something.<p>Of course too high a price has issues as well. It is important to pay attention to price. Sometimes you need to break away from a deal because it will never work out.<p>Long term the best deals are win-win.')
