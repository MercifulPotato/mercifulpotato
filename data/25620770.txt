Item(by='jzwinck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609667239, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25617393, text='zipfile uses zlib which is C. But it&#x27;s even better than that: it releases the GIL, so it gives linear speedup with multiple threads. If you need to (de)compress a bunch of files, you can do them all at once quite easily using e.g. concurrent.futures.<p>If you want that speedup on the command line without Python, check out pigz. It&#x27;s gzip with parallelism. Easy 10-20x speedup for some jobs.')
