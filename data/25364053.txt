Item(by='jonheller', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607544753, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25361439, text='The survivor &#x2F; confirmation bias is my biggest issue with a lot of these books.<p>I listen to a lot of &quot;How I Built This&quot; and have noted that the vast, vast majority of the founders on that program will answer the &quot;what percent is luck vs skill&quot; question with 90 (or above) for luck.<p>This rarely seems addressed, specifically in the entrepreneurship segment of these books. Which is understandable as &quot;get lucky&quot; is not a pitch that is going to sell a lot of books. But it&#x27;s unfortunate when some of these folks use blanket advice like quit school or quit your stable day job because it worked for me.')
