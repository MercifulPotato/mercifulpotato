Mac clones were a threat to Apple’s hardware business. Running alternative operating systems on Apple hardware is not a threat to Apple’s hardware business. Apple has been willing to support non-Apple operating systems on their hardware before - Windows via Boot Camp, MkLinux.<p>(Mobile is a different case, because allowing third party operating systems might threaten their control of software distribution and its tie-in with the hardware; that isn’t the case on Macs since Apple does not mandate their App Store on macOS.)')
