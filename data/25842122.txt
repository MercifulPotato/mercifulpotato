Item(by='jonathansampson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611111734, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25840900, text='You don&#x27;t have to participate in the advertising bits of Brave; those are optional. But, they&#x27;re a privacy-centric way to generate rewards which can be passively contributed to your favorite sites, sustaining their content creation.<p>I would love to hear more about your privacy concerns; we have excised quite a bit from Chromium. You can read about those changes [on GitHub](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;brave&#x2F;brave-browser&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Deviations-from-Chromium-(features-we-disable-or-remove)" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;brave&#x2F;brave-browser&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Deviations-from-...</a>). We also audit our network activity to make sure nothing ever slips out. You can read about our [desktop](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;brave-tops-browser-first-run-network-traffic-results&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;brave-tops-browser-first-run-network-traff...</a>) and [iOS](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;ios-browser-first-run&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;ios-browser-first-run&#x2F;</a>) audits on our blog.<p>Perhaps more of interest would be an independent, third-party review and comparison. Trinity College&#x27;s School of Computer Science and Statistics did a comparative review of Chrome, Brave, Edge, Firefox, Yandex, and Safari. They found that Brave was the &quot;most private&quot; in terms of phoning home. You can [read their review online](<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.scss.tcd.ie&#x2F;Doug.Leith&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;browser_privacy.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.scss.tcd.ie&#x2F;Doug.Leith&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;browser_privacy.pdf</a>).<p>Always happy to discuss any specific concerns you may have.')
