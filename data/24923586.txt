Some perspective on this is the story of Alves dos Reis (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Alves_dos_Reis" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Alves_dos_Reis</a>) who in 1924 stole ~1% of Portugal&#x27;s GDP in bank notes printed under forged orders.
