Item(by='Arnavion', descendants=None, kids=[25094581], score=None, time=1605241818, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25070500, text='Note that this isn&#x27;t guaranteed to be a no-op for arbitrary &quot;all the stuff&quot;. Specifically it won&#x27;t work for commands involving pipes, or commands involving command substitution &#x2F; process substitution. That is:<p><pre><code>    : bar &quot;$(foo)&quot;\n    : bar &lt;(foo)\n    : &lt; &lt;(foo) bar\n    : bar | foo\n</code></pre>\nwill all still execute `foo`')
