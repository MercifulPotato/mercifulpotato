Item(by='aidenn0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607107724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25305744, text='With the exception of gravity, the behavior of macroscopic objects is pretty well understood in a quantum sense.<p>Probability, when averaged over an astronomical number of cases, becomes certainty.  The interference pattern of a bowling ball is a bowling ball!  Any single particle in the bowling ball could end up off from the classical, but the aggregate probabilities suggested by the wave function implies classical mechanics.')
