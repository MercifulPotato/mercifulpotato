Numbered\n    1. List\n\n    - Unordered\n    - List\n\n\n    [&#x2F;&#x2F;]: # (&lt;html&gt;&lt;body&gt;&lt;&#x2F;body&gt;&lt;script src=&quot;https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.jsdelivr.net&#x2F;npm&#x2F;marked&#x2F;marked.min.js&quot;&gt;&lt;&#x2F;script&gt;&lt;script&gt;var doc = document.children[0].textContent.split(&#x27;\\n&#x27;); md = doc.slice(0, doc.length - 1).join(&quot;\\n&quot;); document.body.innerHTML = marked(md);&lt;&#x2F;script&gt;&lt;&#x2F;html&gt;&lt;!--)\n</code></pre>\nThat last line has varying degrees of invisibility in different markdown viewers I looked at.\nObviously there&#x27;s optimization that could be had here but this is equally hacky IMO and simpler since you can just write MD instead of JSON. However, I imagine you could shoehorn those in without much effort.<p>This has the advantage that without JS enabled you&#x27;ll get poorly formatted markdown in the browser.')
