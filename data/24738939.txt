In the real world, if you were to move a chair to the right, the natural way to do it would <i>not</i> be to build the same kind of chair at the desired coordinate and then, upon discovering that you do not need the extra chair, to discard it.<p>Yet, this is how immutable datastructures often work in practice. That may or may not be a worthy tradeoff, but please do not act like it is the right way to do things by default.')
