Item(by='indymike', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602346579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24738760, text='It is easy to try to blame a problem caused by people consuming on the people that own the means of harvesting. The demand for harvesting animals from the commons (i.e. seas) or mining oil exists regardless of who owns it. Someone else will step in to provide the food and the energy. The problem remains demand, and regardless of economic status, people contribute to that. The whale in the room isn&#x27;t the whale. It&#x27;s the low cost of harvesting fish, or pumping oil compared to the alternatives.')
