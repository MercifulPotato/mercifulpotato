Item(by='PeterisP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611848279, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25942899, text='While the business dying means maximum profit for the shorters, the act of shorting isn&#x27;t killing the business in any way; the price of shares does not affect the revenues of GME nor their workers jobs, it has some impact on  the bonuses of the top managers and that&#x27;s it.<p>The shorters are <i>predicting</i> that the business will fail (and hoping for their predictions to come true), but they aren&#x27;t <i>causing</i> the failure, so it&#x27;s misleading to say that they are trying to kill the business as long as the only thing they&#x27;re doing is shorting the stock. There&#x27;s a very big gap between hoping that a rich uncle dies because you&#x27;d get some inheritance and actually trying to kill him.')
