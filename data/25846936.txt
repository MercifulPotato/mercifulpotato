Item(by='slightwinder', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611153639, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25835397, text='The Streamdeck is just a fancy macro-pad&#x2F;programmable keyboard, and those are around in several areas since decades. Especially video- and music-makers have a pretty sophisticated experience there. But<p>But for software-developers I don&#x27;t really see the value. We alrady do have our tools for managing macros and shortcuts on regular keyboards. Macropads don&#x27;t really have much value there. Usually the trend is to reduce key-estate and build elaborated layer-interfaces, not expand them and waste desk-estate.<p>Though, I could maybe see some value if there would be support for animations on the streamdeck, and more scriptability.')
