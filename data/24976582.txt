The probability isn&#x27;t &quot;realistically much closer to 50%&quot; -- that&#x27;s not how uncertainties in statistics work. It is possible that (if the uncertainty in the model was smaller) that the &quot;real&quot; probability is 50% but there&#x27;s no way of knowing if that is the case.')
