Item(by='jonstaab', descendants=None, kids=[25947410, 25951345, 25952617], score=None, time=1611856916, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25942864, text='Maybe this would work for simple websites. But for web apps, it&#x27;s a hard pass for me. For context, I write a web-based POS application, and waiting for a network request on every micro interaction is a no-go.<p>There are lots of other problems with this approach that could be solved, but aren&#x27;t immediately obvious, such as (similar to React) over-rendering. If someone has their focus in an input within the frame that gets swapped out, they&#x27;re going to lose their focus. This has implications for accessibility as well as just regular usability.')
