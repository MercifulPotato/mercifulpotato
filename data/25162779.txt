Tried it with tab-indented Clojure code too.<p>However, 1-2 space indented code becomes just too ambiguous when rendered with proportional fonts, because the spaces are way too thin.\nI wish editors would render em or en-sized spaces instead, at least for the line-leading spaces.\nThat way we would get the same indentation as intended with monospaced fonts, but still save horizontal space, while maintaining readability...')
