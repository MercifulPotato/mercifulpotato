Its format is ancient and not well documented. Its behavior is not consistent across different implementations. Worst of all, no proper i18n support.<p>There&#x27;s a tool like roff2html, but again it&#x27;s pretty sketchy in terms of reliability and i18n support. I wrote my own converter when I was translating OpenBSD manpages [1], but I hope more people recognize this issue. \n[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;euske&#x2F;openssh-jman&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;roff2html.py" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;euske&#x2F;openssh-jman&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;roff2html....</a>')
