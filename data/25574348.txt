Item(by='ip26', descendants=None, kids=[25580421], score=None, time=1609273135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25568003, text='My definition of &quot;winning&quot; is making a useful product that sells &amp; turns a profit. But if the liability is unlimited &amp; unknowable, your new chemical might turn a profit &amp; do some good - or you might be sued into bankruptcy, and you can&#x27;t know for sure which it will be, no matter how much study you do. It&#x27;s just gambling.<p>With liability limits, you can do the math on whether a chemical is worth the risk because while some things are unknowable, your maximum liability is already established.<p>That&#x27;s why I favor a &quot;due diligence&quot; regulatory arrangement.')
