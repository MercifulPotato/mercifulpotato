With a covered call your potential profit is capped, but you aren&#x27;t exposed to the infinite risk of buying the stock (since you already own it) at a bananas price if it exceeds your strike.<p>Selling covered calls can be a fine substitute for limit sells if used carefully. Assuming one is comfortable selling at the strike price, the covered call trickles a little profit in the meantime.')
