Item(by='Dylanlacey', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607310901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25309337, text='It&#x27;s interesting to me that no-one is talking about steel frames, which are becoming something of a standard in Australia, mainly because they share some of the advantages of both constructions styles.<p>A Steel framed, clad house can be constructed relatively quickly, but is also durable.  Alterations aren&#x27;t as easy as with wood, but are significantly easier then concrete.<p>With modern insulations and soundproofing, you can have a lot of the benefits of concrete or stone.  Plus, there&#x27;s less thermal mass in the structure, which is a big advantage when building in Australia; Your primary challenge is keeping the inside cool in Summer.')
