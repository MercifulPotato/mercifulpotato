Item(by='crazygringo', descendants=None, kids=[25994757], score=None, time=1612214096, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25993681, text='They&#x27;re different concepts.<p>Eating habits are, well, just habits. They&#x27;re what you usually do. But they don&#x27;t embody notions of right and wrong.<p>Grammars embody notions of right and wrong. Americans don&#x27;t avoid eating horses and dogs because it&#x27;s not part of their habit, they do it because they think it&#x27;s wrong.<p>Habits are relatively easy to change. Grammars are not. I think food grammar is a great term.')
