Item(by='closeparen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611786759, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25933056, text='Once people started building with cars in mind here, they were building single family detached houses. Most apartments, duplexes, 4-plexes, etc. were built in the era of streetcars.<p>We do have <i>some</i> multifamily from the postwar era, but it tends to be either very luxe (contemporary gentrification) or very crappy (from when cities were for poor people). The stately, comfortable, once-grand-but-now-middle-class stock is all pre-automobile.')
