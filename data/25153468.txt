Item(by='samatman', descendants=None, kids=[25154064, 25153637], score=None, time=1605814617, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25152893, text='The interesting question, to me, is whether we will see a visible spike in all-cause mortality for, let&#x27;s say, 2020-2025.<p>COVID-19 is particularly hard on the elderly, which is by no means an inevitable fact about epidemics. Every person who dies of this disease would have lived longer, which makes their death a tragedy: but how much longer?<p>Cards on the table: my suspicion is that we won&#x27;t. I could be wrong, particularly if recovering from the disease shortens lifespans for an appreciable number of patients. We&#x27;ll know when we know.')
