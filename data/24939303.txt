Item(by='dodobirdlord', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604033711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24937123, text='A few areas of the world that are unusually low-lying and situated over permeable rock like limestone will be flooded. For the rest of the world, dikes are a well-understood technology, and quite cheap compared to sacrificing large land areas or moving cities. Even worst-case shortest-timeline estimates of sea level rise are not an existential crisis.')
