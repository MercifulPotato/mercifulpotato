If we want to pursue a replacement for palm oil, we need to solve for that specific use case on a technical level.<p>Cooking oil (simplifying greatly) is just a heat transfer medium from the stove to all surfaces of the food being fried. Like a catalyst, ideally it should not be consumed in the process.<p>If we can develop a benign heat transfer medium that is infinitely recyclable in cooking, that should be a real replacement for palm oil.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;scroll.in&#x2F;article&#x2F;967186&#x2F;as-worlds-largest-importer-of-palm-oil-india-has-a-duty-to-push-for-ethical-production-practices" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;scroll.in&#x2F;article&#x2F;967186&#x2F;as-worlds-largest-importer-...</a>')
