Item(by='derekp7', descendants=1, kids=[25996836], score=4, time=1612232113, title='Ask HN: Can we get a Who is Hiring but for open source projects?', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='Given the number of open source foundations that many of us depend on, and how thinly stretched the resources are to maintain many of them, would such a monthly thread be a good idea?  The thread would be open for anyone who is involved in a project to post details about the project, what areas are in need of volunteers at the moment (feature development, testing, documentation, project management, marketing, languages used...), and would be a great resource for people getting their feet wet in any of these areas.  Also it can be an avenue for struggling projects to semi-promote themselves without feeling like they are spamming.')
