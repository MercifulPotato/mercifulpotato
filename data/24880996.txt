Item(by='banana_giraffe', descendants=None, kids=[24881081], score=None, time=1603564050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24879740, text='One thing many people are not aware of:  youtube-dl actually does it&#x27;s thing for a whole bunch of sites that aren&#x27;t youtube.<p>Generally, it pulls down the HTML page for a site, parses it, and uses the results to pull down the video.  The extractor for youtube itself is the most complex one, but there are plenty of others.<p>Last time I checked it had extractors for something like 700 different sites, along with a generic one that works on a bunch of sites.  In general, you can just point it at any video page you see online and have a fairly high chance of it doing the right thing.')
