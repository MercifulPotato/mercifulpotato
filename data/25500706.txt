Seatbelts are another form. A car that does various things to avoid driver distraction is another form of safety. Just because you can think of a more immediate safety system does not make other increases to safety in some way &quot;not practical&quot; or &quot;not real increases&quot;.<p>There are many things, small and large, that go into practical safety of a car')
