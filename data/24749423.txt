Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602453214, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24746204, text='Private ownership of nuclear weapons has a slightly different problem that isn&#x27;t usually brought up in the &quot;nuclear garden gnome&quot; discussions: it has a capability to break the MAD doctrine. If your country allows anyone to own a warhead (and perhaps also a delivery system), then from the outside perspective, you&#x27;re an unpredictable first strike threat. Which means suddenly the best option is to preemptively glass you, in order to not have to deal with you anymore.')
