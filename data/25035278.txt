Item(by='SuperCuber', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604933062, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25035276, text='Dotter is a dotfile manager and templater.\nIt is aimed at users who have their configuration files in one central folder (often a git repository) for easy uploading and synchronization.<p>Dotter provides a flexible configuration, intended to make it easy to deploy the same files on different machines while adapting the files to each specific machine.\nIt is possible to deploy your vimrc to <i>different locations</i> on your Windows and Linux machines, without needing separate git branches or any other similar awkward solution.\nIt is even possible to have the <i>contents</i> of the files changed on a per-machine basis, for example hiding the battery meter from your bar on your desktop machine while still showing it on your laptop.<p>For more information, check out the wiki - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;SuperCuber&#x2F;dotter&#x2F;wiki" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;SuperCuber&#x2F;dotter&#x2F;wiki</a><p>Feel free to leave feedback on anything in the form of comments or issues, especially regarding documentation and additional features you&#x27;d want to see, as well as questions you may have.')
