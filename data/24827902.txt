Item(by='ssivark', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603124860, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827295, text='The best form of communication is ultimately based on the amount of shared context (Eg: acronyms), so it’s silly to create rules per the communication medium — slack, email, HN comments, etc. Even within a medium, a slack DM to a friend has very different shared context from a message to a company-wide channel. Being mindful of that, and communicating respectfully, regardless of the actual medium, is likely to be far more useful. Nothing special about Slack.<p>For me, respectful written communication also includes proper punctuation &amp; capitalization — since it makes reading easier.')
