Item(by='gregdoesit', descendants=None, kids=[24785689, 24782181], score=None, time=1602712317, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24781631, text='Most companies use an ATS to organize inbound applications and ask job seekers to upload their resume. Here, recruiters&#x2F;hiring managers later view this resume.<p>You need a PDF or Word doc here (PDF preferred). When applying for larger tech companies, this is the use case 90% of the time.<p>As a hiring manager, I want to see details quickly. The less &quot;fanciness&quot;, the better. I can scan traditional resumes much easier than this one. So my view is the same as when choosing a fancy template: you make the job of the hiring manager more difficult, thus lowering your chances.<p>With software developers, boring and simple format is good. Your resume content should sell you, not the presentation.')
