Item(by='powerapple', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605776891, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25124333, text='As Chinese you don&#x27;t need to register when you move. There is Hukou system which determine which city&#x2F;province you belong to (mostly used for welfare system), the system basically require you to have a permit to stay and work in a different province. It was quite strict 30 years ago because of the imbalance between provinces, many migrate workers. Residence address is not important as in western countries (where your bank account links to your residence address). That said, hotels (and netcafes) do need to register customers&#x27; ID.')
