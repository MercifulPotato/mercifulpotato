Item(by='sokoloff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612227643, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25989010, text='&gt; profit margins are 50-200% per sale<p>Given that it’s over 100%, that’s almost surely a <i>markup</i> calculation, not a <i>margin</i> calculation.<p>Margin is (unit profit&#x2F;unit revenue). Markup is (unit profit&#x2F;unit cost).<p>A $25 COGS widget sold for $100 is a 75% margin product ($75 &#x2F; $100 revenue) and a 300% markup product ($75 &#x2F; $25 COGS).')
