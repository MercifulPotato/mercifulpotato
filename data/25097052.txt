I ran the mail servers for AOL in the mid-90s. I’ve done that same job for plenty of other places. It’s hard work to do it right.<p>It’s hard enough to do this job right, even when you have all the money and equipment that you could potentially do it right. It’s almost impossible to do this job right when you’re paying for everything out of your own pocket.<p>That’s why I’ve outsourced the e-mail administration for my vanity domain since 1995.')
