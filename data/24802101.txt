Item(by='perl4ever', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602864032, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24800918, text='Among people who would pretend to be a given well known person, surely more of them know that person&#x27;s real name than something that would make a plausible pseudonym. Or in any case, more of them would choose it.<p>And if someone thinks a pseudonym is a particular famous person, they must have a reason, which is unlikely to be weaker than just assuming a normal name is accurate.<p>I&#x27;m not up to doing a Bayesian analysis right now, but I feel like one could show it makes more sense to doubt an unverified name.')
