Item(by='karmakaze', descendants=None, kids=[25347808], score=None, time=1607445851, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25344358, text='In a nutshell: client encrypts to proxy, which decrypts &amp; removes client info, then asks resolver.<p>&gt; “What ODoH is meant to do is separate the information about who is making the query and what the query is,” said Nick Sullivan, Cloudflare’s head of research.<p>&gt; In other words, ODoH ensures that only the proxy knows the identity of the internet user and that the DNS resolver only knows the website being requested. Sullivan said that page loading times on ODoH are “practically indistinguishable” from DoH and shouldn’t cause any significant changes to browsing speed.')
