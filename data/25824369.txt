Item(by='cuspycode', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610992125, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25824027, text='And in the old days people were accustomed to be able to resolve local hostnames, without having to supply any domain part. The &quot;search&quot; keyword in resolv.conf is a remnant of this. By adding a final dot, you ensured that the domain part you wrote referred to the global DNS root, not any local one. Unless of course someone had changed &quot;ndots&quot; which controls how many dots are needed to disable the search feature.')
