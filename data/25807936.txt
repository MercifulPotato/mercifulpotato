Item(by='taliesinb', descendants=None, kids=[25808814, 25808360], score=None, time=1610855574, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801063, text='Is anyone aware of a resource that collects examples of what complex numbers “encode” in various settings in which they naturally appear?<p>For example, complex numbers in the eigendecomposition of a real-valued square matrix encode permutations among dimensions in a particular eigenspace via the roots of unity — which amount to rotations. So they’re a bookkeeping device that allows the eigendecomposition of an automorphism of a real vector space to actually work in all cases.')
