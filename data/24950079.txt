Item(by='anticristi', descendants=None, kids=[24952409], score=None, time=1604137318, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24949285, text='Ownership in capitalism has nothing to do with usage. If I rent a car, I am using it, but I definitely don&#x27;t own it. Ownership is about a right, the right to decide what to do with a property. Ownership is also not exclusive: Several people can own a house or a car simultaneously.<p>Communist countries tried to reconcile ownership with usage by enforcing that everyone owns one flat at maximum. The results are mixed.<p>To sum up, I still think that ownership is a social construct, be it for a physical item or an intellectual artefact.')
