If the last 20 years has taught us nothing else it has taught us that protocols won the whole <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.c2.com&#x2F;?ApiVsProtocol" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.c2.com&#x2F;?ApiVsProtocol</a> debate.
