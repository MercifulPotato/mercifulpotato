Item(by='dredmorbius', descendants=None, kids=[24895943], score=None, time=1603717899, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24895636, text='Fair use copying, in any amount so deemed, &quot;is not an infringement of copyright&quot;.  The statute lists four tests.  Any one of these, or other conditions, may be sufficient.<p>The third test is &quot;the amount and substantiality of the portion used in relation to the copyrighted work as a whole&quot;.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.law.cornell.edu&#x2F;uscode&#x2F;text&#x2F;17&#x2F;107" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.law.cornell.edu&#x2F;uscode&#x2F;text&#x2F;17&#x2F;107</a><p>Again, Hagemeister establishes that a bare minimum copying is performed, sufficient to verify code function.  A fact which would overwhelmingly tend to a fair use finding.')
