Item(by='im3w1l', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608388082, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25477124, text='The issue with ruminating on social issues is that there is a very high risk that you go down the wrong path, and that it makes your life more difficult. As a society, our written down understanding of human behavior is still <i>vastly</i> inferior to the tacit understanding you get from lived experience.<p>Maybe if you are happy with your life and friendships, philosophizing a bit can make it even better. But if you are unhappy it can be a trap.')
