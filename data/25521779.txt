Item(by='natrik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608755154, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25521487, text='Notion has had some great marketing, and has some useful features, but I don&#x27;t think its as great as the hype around it suggests. YAWT, Yet Another Writing Tool. To be <i>forgotten?</i> in a few years.<p>When I used it, it seemed like a Jack of All&#x2F;Master of None type of tool. Seemed like it was focused more on those who enjoy and find value in the act of taking notes rather the value of the note itself. I&#x27;m probably not the intended user, but for a lot of daily uses (Meeting notes, TODO lists, etc.) I found a barebones GDocs&#x2F;Word&#x2F;Pen and Paper to be more efficient and less taxing to pick up.')
