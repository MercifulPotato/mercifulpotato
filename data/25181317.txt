Item(by='mikequinlan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606080863, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25177360, text='<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tablespoon" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Tablespoon</a><p>The unit of measurement varies by region: a United States tablespoon is approximately 14.8 mL (0.50 US fl oz), a United Kingdom and Canadian tablespoon is exactly 15 mL (0.51 US fl oz), and an Australian tablespoon is 20 mL (0.68 US fl oz).
