Item(by='andrewla', descendants=None, kids=[25573966, 25572392], score=None, time=1609260436, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25571163, text='Yes, I think that confirmation bias is very strong here.<p>My rule for analyzing social science articles is pretty simple: If the study contradicts your intuition or understanding of human behavior, it is likely that it does not support the conclusion because of extensive methodological errors or statistical skullduggery.<p>If, on the other hand, the study confirms your intuition or understanding of human behavior, it is likely that it does not support the conclusion because of extensive methodological errors or statistical skullduggery.')
