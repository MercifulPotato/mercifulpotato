Item(by='anemoiac', descendants=None, kids=[25700590], score=None, time=1610163579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25695088, text='With regard to those questioning the general integrity of such a large, heterogeneous system as the US electoral process, I don’t think the relevant question is <i>“Is there any reason to believe that some amount of voter fraud may have occurred.”</i> Instead, we should be asking, <i>“If voter fraud did occur, is there any reason to believe that it would disproportionately favor one party over the other?”</i> It’s a big leap from “the system’s latent vulnerabilities likely resulted in at least some failure” to “the system’s vulnerabilities were systematically exploited to the benefit of one party.” I haven’t encountered any convincing evidence in support of the latter conclusion.')
