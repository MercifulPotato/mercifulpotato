Item(by='pembrook', descendants=None, kids=[25813737], score=None, time=1610906830, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25812779, text='I&#x27;m not saying the alternative to frugality is to spend your way to death.<p>But it&#x27;s important to remember, if generating revenue is so difficult, you don&#x27;t have product market fit or even a business then.<p>Pinching pennies is only valuable in the sense that it can extend your runaway long enough so you can find product-market fit.<p>It&#x27;s not a means to an end by itself.<p>Irrational frugality is pretty much the default impulse of all American software engineers (and the entire HN crowd), so I don&#x27;t think HN needs any more encouragement to be frugal.')
