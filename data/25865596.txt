Item(by='dmitriid', descendants=None, kids=[25866677, 25866050], score=None, time=1611269091, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25862943, text='In the USSR central museums (think The Hermitage or The Pushkin Museum) would distribute a lot of art from their archives to regional museums, even to museums in small villages. So you wouldn&#x27;t be surprised to see good quality art (sometimes by famous artists) even in small towns.<p>This is something that can be encouraged more, especially across borders. For example, British museums definitely don&#x27;t know what to do with their vast caverns filled with plunder .... ahem, vast archives of art, but even a small fraction of a fraction of that would be a great addition to a museum in, say, Moldova (my country), or Serbia, or ...')
