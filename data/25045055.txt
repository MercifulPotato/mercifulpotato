Item(by='nl', descendants=None, kids=[25045172], score=None, time=1605010164, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25045010, text='In case anyone doesn&#x27;t realize, the author of this erronously assumed that all mail-in ballots are mixed in the  mail and processed.<p>Instead (in most states), they are actually sent to the district counting centers and counted with other votes from that district.<p>That&#x27;s why you see higher Democratic votes from ones from later countered ones: they are counted along with the larger districts, which are in cities, and <i>have more Democratic voters</i>.<p>Previously discussed here: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25031344" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25031344</a><p>TL;DR: good analysis, one critical assumption incorrect, no fraud.')
