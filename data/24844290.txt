Item(by='jart', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603247937, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24843480, text='madvise is more about good citizenship since kernels are usually tuned with a default strategy of salting the earth to get the best possible score on simple benchmarks:<p><pre><code>    madvise(mapped_addr, mapped_size, MADV_WILLNEED | MADV_SEQUENTIAL);\n</code></pre>\nPlease call that function before consuming a large file. If you ingest more data than is stored in RAM, then without madvise, you&#x27;re going to make everything else on the system go really slow afterwards, since every other program that has hot pages in cache waiting to be re-used will become cold and have to hit disk again.')
