Item(by='majewsky', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603273231, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24842301, text='(Context: I&#x27;m from Germany.)<p>In retrospect, with the 20&#x2F;20 vision of hindsight, the one thing all Western governments should have done differently early on is not to focus on &quot;don&#x27;t buy surgical masks (because we need them for hospital staff)&quot;. Instead, they should have said: &quot;Everyone get out your sewing machines and make face masks for your friends and family.&quot; Publish sewing patterns and instructions for free on a government website. Maybe even video tutorials.<p>My mother happened to have all the materials at hand, so I got a dozen high-quality reusable cloth masks early on.')
