Item(by='0bit', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612213525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25991500, text='Go to Settings, scroll down to find the app, and select it. Then you get the &quot;Allow &lt;app&gt; to access&quot; and a list of entitlements, one of which is &quot;Photos&quot;. Select &quot;Photos&quot; and you get three options &quot;Selected Photos&quot; (which is what you likely have), &quot;All Photos&quot;, and &quot;None&quot;. Push &quot;None&quot; and you should never see that dialog again.')
