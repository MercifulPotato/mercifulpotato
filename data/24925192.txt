Item(by='osrec', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603926359, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24917721, text='I read somewhere that before the advent of paper and books, ancient civilisations would keep epic stories in their heads. After we started writing things down in larger volumes, committing entire &quot;books&quot; to memory was not needed, so it became less prevalent and eventually stopped altogether.<p>I guess smartphones may further reduce what we need to commit to memory, which will probably have some implications in terms of neuro-plasticity - i.e. it may well shrink (or at least change) parts of our brains.')
