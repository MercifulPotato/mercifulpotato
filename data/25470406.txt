Item(by='indymike', descendants=None, kids=[25470506], score=None, time=1608317130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25459506, text='A lot of programming maxims like &quot;stay DRY&quot; are rules of thumb, and often are dangerous, or at least lead to unexpected results when treated like laws of nature. I had a developer who drank functional flavored Koolaid and  refactored any single line of code that appeared more than once in an application. Was about 38K lines of code. When he was done, it was still about 38K lines of code. Was it functionally pure? yes. Was it very difficult to debug? Yes... you had to step into sometimes five or six functions to get to a single line of logic.')
