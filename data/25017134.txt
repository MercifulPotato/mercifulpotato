Pursuing programming is a practical vocation. Biomedical engineering is an interesting field.<p>But they are separate. Programming in python is orthogonal to changing the economy. Orthogonal to biomedical engineering. And so on.<p>It&#x27;s ok to pursue a vocation. It&#x27;s ok to pursue an intellectually interesting academic field.
