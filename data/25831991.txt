Item(by='galvin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611052718, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25831796, text='Democracy requires an informed electorate and publicly funded broadcasters are a means to achieve that. The government must have as little control as possible over their funding to ensure their journalistic independence.<p>That&#x27;s the guiding theory at resulted in most European countries having some variation of a public broadcaster funded by TV (formally radio) licences.<p>There are many implementation differences between countries and none of them are perfect but I would argue that those I&#x27;m familiar with serve their purpose.')
