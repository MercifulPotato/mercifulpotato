But English has neat rules for creating words from other words. Nouns to verbs, adverbs etc.<p>They are &#x27;wrong&#x27; in that they aren&#x27;t in the dictionary (yet). They are &#x27;right&#x27; because they are correctly constructed by the rules.<p>I say that as a &#x27;programmer&#x27; or &#x27;coder&#x27; who does &#x27;computing&#x27; on a &#x27;computer&#x27;. I could be a &#x27;painter&#x27; who does &#x27;painting&#x27; (and produces a &#x27;painting&#x27;!) Or a doctor who does doctoring. And on and on.')
