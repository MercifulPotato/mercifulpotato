Item(by='wcoenen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610748468, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25795275, text='The Chapwood index looks a bit weird. It&#x27;s based on only 500 items (compare to CPI which uses 94,000 according to [1]). And those 500 items[2] include things like &quot;cat grooming&quot;, &quot;horseback riding lessons&quot; and &quot;first class airfare&quot; (but not economy class airfare?). Tennis is apparently very important because there&#x27;s &quot;tennis lessons&quot;, &quot;tennis racquet&quot; and &quot;tennis ball&quot; in there separately. Something similar is going on for golf: we find &quot;golf shoe&quot;, &quot;round of golf&quot; and &quot;golf lessons&quot;. I could not find information on how these items are weighted.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bls.gov&#x2F;opub&#x2F;hom&#x2F;cpi&#x2F;pdf&#x2F;cpi.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bls.gov&#x2F;opub&#x2F;hom&#x2F;cpi&#x2F;pdf&#x2F;cpi.pdf</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;chapwoodindex.com&#x2F;chapwood-index-items&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;chapwoodindex.com&#x2F;chapwood-index-items&#x2F;</a>')
