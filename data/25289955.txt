Item(by='ambivalents', descendants=None, kids=[25311089, 25311345], score=None, time=1607010368, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25288133, text='As a PM I sometimes get asked a question, typically by Engineering, and I think the ability to answer this question well is an embodiment of the role. It can take different forms but it&#x27;s basically: &quot;Why?&quot; Why build X over Y? Why this feature, this way, instead of another way? Why now, instead of later? Why should I build it, and not her?<p>Answering this well requires a) ability to communicate convincingly, b) evidence, typically through user data, research, competitor analysis, etc., c) authority in your space, d) ability to translate business requirements into technical speak, and vice versa, and e) respect from the team you&#x27;re working with.')
