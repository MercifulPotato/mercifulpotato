Item(by='fairity', descendants=None, kids=[25141891, 25141451], score=None, time=1605726884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25138934, text='&gt; People don&#x27;t bother giving feedback to those who they don&#x27;t care about or have to work with again.<p>This mindset is basically trying to cast good intentions on the criticizer to reduce the sting of the critique.<p>I don&#x27;t like these mindsets because they aren&#x27;t reliably true.<p>While some managers or teammates will give you feedback with good intentions in mind, others will critique you out of frustration and negative emotions.<p>Instead of tricking yourself into thinking that everyone is acting with your best interests in mind, why not accept reality.<p>The path forward is to embrace the fact that you can&#x27;t please everyone.  Instead, have a deep understanding of what your goals and values are.  And, act accordingly.  How others react or judge your actions are only important insofar as they help you achieve your goals or align your actions with your values.')
