With Beyond Compare I sync all files that I need from this drive with my machine.<p>This harddrive is backed up a few times in a month to another encrypted harddrive via a Raspberry Pi Server in the LAN. On that Raspberry Pi also Nextcloud is installed to allow syncing specific files with other members in the house.<p>The first harddrive that is connected to my machine directly is also backed up to another drive once or twice a year.')
