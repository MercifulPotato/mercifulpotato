Item(by='bananaface', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606264469, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25204294, text='Weirdness is good. If the market is weird, it means people don&#x27;t know how to exploit it, i.e. you actually have a chance of finding an edge that hasn&#x27;t been exhausted by someone else.<p>There isn&#x27;t a compendium of standard tactics that &quot;work&quot; in normal markets. If there were, they would no longer be profitable. Anything that works is secret &amp; novel, and finding a novel approach to an old market is harder than finding a novel approach to a weird one.')
