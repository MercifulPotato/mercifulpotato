So any laws related to approved medicine, devices, vaccines, etc do not apply automatically to EUA products.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.fda.gov&#x2F;vaccines-blood-biologics&#x2F;vaccines&#x2F;emergency-use-authorization-vaccines-explained" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.fda.gov&#x2F;vaccines-blood-biologics&#x2F;vaccines&#x2F;emerge...</a>')
