Item(by='avian', descendants=None, kids=[25623917], score=None, time=1609698600, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25623315, text='As someone who also ran a similar test myself and haven&#x27;t seen a bit flip, I&#x27;m also skeptical of the 96% figure.<p>I&#x27;m too lazy to run the exact numbers right now, but with &quot;4 GB, 96% percent chance, three days&quot; as the hypothesis, I think you&#x27;ll find that an experimental result of &quot;8 GB, 0% chance, 14 days&quot; is highly statistically significant.<p>Edit: rough back of napkin estimate - you&#x27;re not seeing an event in roughly 10x trials (2x number of bits and ~5x number of days).
