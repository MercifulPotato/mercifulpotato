Item(by='georgeoliver', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602564589, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24761793, text='I would strongly encourage you to think less about systems and more about performance goals.<p><pre><code>  * How much energy use? \n  * How much carbon do you want to burn?\n  * How much money do you want to spend year over year? \n  * What kind of indoor air quality do you want to achieve?\n  * What is your comfort zone temperature wise?\n</code></pre>\nThen design your system around that. This also encourages thinking about how these goals interact with site conditions: glazing, solar orientation, climate, etc.<p>I don&#x27;t know the scope of your project but consider getting a mechanical plan drawn up. Most residential projects don&#x27;t have them and in my opinion (I&#x27;ve been building since 1999) it&#x27;s a lost opportunity for a better, more efficient installation.')
