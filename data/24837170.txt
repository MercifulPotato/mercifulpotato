The upside is consistency - they all use the same number.  The downside is this figure may not be fully representative of the economy.<p>I would argue that U-5 or U-6 better represent the state of the economy (as it relates to employment), as both included some set of under-employed and recently-given-up would-be workers.<p>But, we&#x27;d have to make a concerted effort to cut over to those numbers to avoid confusion.')
