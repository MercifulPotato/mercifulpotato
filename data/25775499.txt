It only limits one&#x27;s power, not freedom in forming it.<p>Having the freedom and the power to do things are two entirely different things. A mute and deaf man is considerably hampered in the power to express his opinion, but not in his freedom to.<p>Similarly, freedom to own property is an entirely different matter to whether a citizen have the capital to purchase it.')
