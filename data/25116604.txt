Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605558519, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25113511, text='Counterpoint for some situations.<p>Sometimes it seems necessary to use words like &quot;obviously&quot; or &quot;of course&quot;, otherwise a setting-up part of my explanation will be rebutted as condescending with &quot;but that&#x27;s obvious&quot;, and the rest, my actual point that follows, will be ignored.<p>If I acknowledge that something is obvious to both of us, there&#x27;s a chance that the listener won&#x27;t think I&#x27;m being condescending and will instead give enough benefit of the doubt to keep listening until the conclusion.')
