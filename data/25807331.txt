Item(by='karpierz', descendants=None, kids=[25808588], score=None, time=1610848497, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25806724, text='The point isn&#x27;t to lower your expectations, the point is acknowledge that the way you feel is under your control and it&#x27;s up to you to do something about it. So if your coworkers let you down, they don&#x27;t make you feel let down, your own expectations make you feel let down. How you fix that is your responsibility.<p>You can try and help your colleagues, you can rely on them less, or you can quit your job. Blaming your colleagues and making them responsible for your feelings means feeling shitty and feeling powerless to feel better.')
