Item(by='joshvm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603029801, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24817585, text='If your pipeline depends on running inference on a single frame at a time, for example some kind of control loop, then you need to be a bit careful about how you measure speed; you have to use the effective time per batch (ie batch size 1), not the amortised frames per second using as big a batch as will fit. You can still interleave processing though.')
