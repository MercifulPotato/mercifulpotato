Item(by='BlackFly', descendants=None, kids=[24719468, 24716666, 24716806], score=None, time=1602137522, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24716239, text='Anonymity is not free speech, but anonymity is absolutely required for some free speech. Consider the case of free elections, most people consider it an absolute given that these need to be anonymous.<p>The potential for reprisal in expressing your choice or opinion in some circumstances is very high and anonymity is sometimes the only thing that can eliminate the chill of that risk. It is in this way a free speech issue.<p>On the other hand, if a crime or tort was committed, well that isn&#x27;t considered free.')
