Item(by='bob1029', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610745795, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25793743, text='One trick you can use if you are super lazy is to just use a config.json file that always resides next to your exe. JSON files are very easy to edit&#x2F;share, and can be trivially serialized in and out of your models for convenience (1 liner with either Newtonsoft or built-in serializer). JSON is also a good interchange format between systems, so it can quickly integrate into a bigger system without much hassle.')
