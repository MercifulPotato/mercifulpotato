Item(by='colejohnson66', descendants=None, kids=[24971206, 24971228], score=None, time=1604340921, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24970855, text='Regardless of the understanding when ticking the box, the courts (in the US) have upheld shrink wrap contracts <i>and</i> rejected the “I didn’t actually read the TOS, so I can’t be bound by them” argument.[a] Basically, you’re SOL.<p>[a]: The reason being: you checked a box <i>acknowledging</i> that you read them so you can’t claim you didn’t because then someone could read them, then claim they didn’t. It’s a terrible abuse of contract law, but it is unfortunately the world we live in if the legislature won’t fix it.')
