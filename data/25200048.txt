Item(by='ssully', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606235951, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25199844, text='The games are usually compressed, but you can very easily find yourself downloading over 100 gigs for a single game in a month.<p>A big trend in games these days what is called &quot;live&quot; or &quot;service&quot; games. These are games that are having new content added on a regular schedule (monthly, or sometimes weekly). This means its not unusual to get updates that range in size from 20-60 gigs for a single game in a month. If you play a lot of games or have kids that play different games, you can very easily be burning 200-400 GBs of data a month just keeping your games up to date.')
