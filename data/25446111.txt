Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608142734, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25444286, text='Injecting HTML is a <i>terrible</i> idea. It has extreme overhead compared to just evaluating some JavaScript in the page context, and it will break the occasional page that expects certain things of its DOM, and it’s fairly inevitably broken when you have CSP things. The DOM belongs to the document. You shouldn’t touch it unless you actually <i>have</i> to to provide your functionality, and this isn’t such an extension. There’s a a proper mechanism for executing scripts in the document context, which should be used.')
