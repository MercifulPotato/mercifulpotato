Item(by='FreeTrade', descendants=None, kids=[25317567, 25313473, 25315422], score=None, time=1607164502, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25313277, text='Transitive trust &#x2F; Web-of-Trust is the solution I think. You do need to find one trustworthy entry point to such a web of trust, maybe a journalist or a news source that you trust. Once you trust one node in a network that is providing trust ratings of other nodes, you start to have some footing to judge content and authors.<p>Within this network, you may have a pocket of users who espouse Flat earth theory, but I believe if you were to rely on such users for trust ratings, the picture of the world created would be so incoherent, it would simply be unbearable.')
