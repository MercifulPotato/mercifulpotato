Only when communication between two willing parties is unhindered is it even <i>possible</i> to build a just world.  The power to clamp down on voluntary communication is a power no authority should ever be trusted with, because there are no examples in the history of mankind where that power is only used for good - arguably there <i>can be</i> no such examples, what with power being a vector for corruption.')
