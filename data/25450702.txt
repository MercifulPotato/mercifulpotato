Item(by='reissbaker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608164678, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25447555, text='If you can be trusted to do the right thing — that is, report a positive result to relevant health authorities so they can do contact tracing, and self-quarantine for the duration of your illness — then having it automatically reported for you is a useful convenience.<p>If you can&#x27;t be trusted to do the right thing — that is, if you&#x27;re not going to report your positive test result — then automatic reporting is useful for society to handle that case, too.<p>I&#x27;m generally against mass data collection, especially from the government. But not in this case. Hiding a positive result is ethically indefensible. Even if you&#x27;re feeling fine, interacting with you while you&#x27;re positive may be a death sentence for others.')
