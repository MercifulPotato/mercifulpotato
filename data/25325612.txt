Another would be collaboration between well-credentialed investigators. Yet another is having the backing of a large-scale professional firm or institution.<p>Peer review is lauded by people who either (A) don&#x27;t know anything about research, or (B) do research without stellar credentials or the imprimatur of a large institution.
