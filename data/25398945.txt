Item(by='a3n', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607788153, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25398606, text='If you&#x27;re having trouble organizing around classes, objects and inheritance, classes might not be a good fit for your project.<p>Consider modules as your main organizing scheme. That&#x27;s also a decent guide for code file organization.<p>What goes in a module? The tool(s) that you need to implement your project. Think about what off the shelf tools you wish you had and build those tools&#x2F;modules.<p>Use doc strings per module and function, written to your future self, explaining <i>what</i> is being done. The how should be in descriptive variable names and function signatures, with comments where necessary.<p>Don&#x27;t over comment. Out of date comments will confuse.')
