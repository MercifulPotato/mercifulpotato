Item(by='Mediterraneo10', descendants=None, kids=[25654286], score=None, time=1609891804, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25651965, text='Even if Severian has become a better man by the end of the BotNS, in the coda to the sequence <i>The Urth of the New Sun</i> he is still keen on bedding the women he comes across. From reviews and fora discussions, it is this constant interest of the male protagonist in casual sex that has turned off many female readers. Sure, the encounters are now consensual, but Severian remains a horndog, and it leads those readers to assume that Wolfe was indulging in puerile fantasies.<p>Compare the similar &quot;eww&quot; reaction to Larry Niven&#x27;s <i>The Ringworld Throne</i> where the author kept describing the characters&#x27; casual sex with the locals of the places they passed through.')
