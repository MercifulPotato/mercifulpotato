Item(by='rienbdj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607204836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25312681, text='I would skip the source texts because they are often hard to read. Unfortunately ground breaking ideas and a clear expository style rarely go hand in hand.<p>Philosophy Hour with Brian McGee was a great BBC show from the 70s where they interviewed top philosophers in an accessible way. Definitely check it out.<p>There is also the Stanford Encyclopaedia of Philosophy, which is a top level resource but also very accessible.')
