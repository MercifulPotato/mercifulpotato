Item(by='dehrmann', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604994031, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25024901, text='For all running threads, imagine that every operation can interleave in any possible way. Since the permutations grows in factorial time, the only way to manage concurrency is through well-defined interaction points with no more than a few interesting parts of execution. Are there only 4 possible sequences of significant instructions? Cool. Almost any more and it gets hard to reason about because there are so many paths that aren&#x27;t obviously enumerable.')
