Item(by='jl6', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601931591, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24691330, text='I implemented a [private] genealogical data entry system based on the GenTech data model, which has a &quot;surety scheme&quot; entity, designed to capture perceived uncertainty. I also added a &quot;Fuzzy Date&quot;, where every date-like value was decomposed into all its constituent components (year, month, day, hour, ...), all optional. It could also capture a range of such fuzzy dates, so it was possible to enter a &quot;date&quot; such as &quot;The first of a month no earlier than 1950 and before June 1990&quot;. There was a loooong list of validation constraints to attempt to prevent contradictions being entered, and I <i>think</i> I caught all the cases, but...')
