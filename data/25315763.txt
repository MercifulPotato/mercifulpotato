What is a fire-and-forget guided missile but an autonomous suicide drone?  Some missiles, particularly anti-ship missiles, even use computer vision in the air to identify objects and pick targets.  Mark 60 CAPTOR naval mines sit underwater listening to nearby ships.  When a noise is classified as an enemy ship, the mine releases a guided torpedo to kill it.  Of course autonomy in naval mines is nothing new; the most basic sort &#x27;decide&#x27; to kill any object that bumps them too hard.  The innovation is making them discriminate between enemy ships and everybody else.')
