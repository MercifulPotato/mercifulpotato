You may agree?<p>To accomplish big things, sometimes you have to change many minds just a little bit. Sometimes you start by asking people to notice something. Then maybe you ask them to wonder if it <i>could</i> be different. Then maybe you convince people that it <i>should</i> be different. Then maybe you convince people that it <i>must</i> be different; that the status quo is not ok. This can be hard work; think about the organizing for civil rights in the US.<p>This process involves big ideas. Sure, asking people to give $5 for the cause can help, <i></i>and<i></i> you also need people willing to do more than give. You need them to agree deeply, because you need their support over time.')
