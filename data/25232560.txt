Item(by='ip26', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606511095, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25231959, text='I don&#x27;t think it&#x27;s actually a <i>unique</i> form of hubris. This is a common discussion point in the politics of class. The wealthy quickly come to convince themselves of their own greatness &amp; deservedness. Elements of luck, failures, and aid from others are all removed from the personal narrative. You wind up with a tautology; <i>I made it because I am great, and because I am standing here, I must be great.</i><p>It&#x27;s hard &amp; uncomfortable to think that you are just as lucky as you are good, and that others equally deserving (but less lucky) have nothing.')
