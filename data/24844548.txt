We will soon add vertical&#x2F;horizontal scaling (early next year). For concurrent connections, we use PostgREST[1] for RESTful calls, and an Elixir&#x2F;Phoenix server [2] for realtime data. This reduces the number of connections to your DB, handling concurrency in the middleware. We are building the benchmarks now, but &quot;enterprise scale&quot; is top of mind with everything we are doing.<p>&gt; Do you have a built in authentication solution?<p>Yes, we use GoTrue [3] for authentication, and we use Postgres Row Level Security for authorization.
