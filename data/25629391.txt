Item(by='gnicholas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609754414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25629047, text='When someone is considering signing up, they&#x27;re weighing the possible upside (of whatever you&#x27;re offering them) and the possible downside (SPAM!). You need to make sure that the upside clearly outweighs the downside.<p>You might also want to make the upside bigger for people who share with others. Some startups let folks jump the line if they refer friends. Don&#x27;t turn people into annoying spammers, but consider why&#x2F;how people would spread the word about your new thing. At the very least, give them easy share buttons.')
