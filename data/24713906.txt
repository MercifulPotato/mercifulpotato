Item(by='pvaldes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602112312, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24707096, text='The fastest way to eat an orange is to treat it like a melon. Cut it in &quot;watermelon portions&quot; and just tear off the good parts with your teeth. Is not so messy for nails this way (but some juice can still drop).<p>The procedure is much more convenient if done with navel oranges (that are always seedless).')
