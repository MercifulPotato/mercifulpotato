In Germany there&#x27;s the term &quot;vollgasfest&quot; (full throttle proof) for cars and it means ability to e.g. empty the gas tank in the pedal-down position in one go without damage. Especially some of the foreign brands from the 70&#x27;&#x2F;80&#x27;s are known to overheat in such conditions and also some of the earlier turbos. Wear is of course increased under more load but i wouldn&#x27;t regard it unhealthy for engine&#x2F;transmission and co per se.')
