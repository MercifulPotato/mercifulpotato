All aspects of the interface need to be considered.<p>Localization is hard. This is one of the big reasons why Europe is not able to re-create Silicon Valley. If a startup in Silicon Valley creates a US only version of a service, they have access to a massive, wealthy market without having to do any localization. While the EU as a whole is larger than the US market, it is split into a lot of different localities with different languages.')
