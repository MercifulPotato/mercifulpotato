Item(by='gnicholas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606892215, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25272533, text='I can imagine that there is something better than email for many people, but the problem is that people have optimized their lives and companies have optimized their processes around the fact that email is a key mode of communication.<p>So even if you knew what would be better for people than email in the long-run, it&#x27;s not clear that it would be better for them in the short-run. And even if it is actually better for people in the short-run, you still have to convince them of this in order for them to try your new thing.<p>So to disrupt email you not only have to &#x27;skate to where the puck is going&#x27;, you also have to convince a bunch of other people to skate away from where the puck is now.')
