Item(by='Jon_Lowtek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610453248, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25744760, text='Many people trust this &quot;word of mouth&quot; way of telling stories, but for those who know rhetorics this pattern should raise a warning. <i>(it is not my intention to accuse, just doing some rhetoric deconstruction)</i> The &quot;so the teardown is correct&quot; part is backwards. Calling the billionaire ceo by his first name tries to create an aura of personal knowledge. At the core it is an <i>argumentum ab auctoritate</i>. Honestly this is worse than &quot;amazon said so&quot; because it is veiled.')
