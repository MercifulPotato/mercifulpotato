Item(by='CPLX', descendants=None, kids=[25652913, 25653032], score=None, time=1609888876, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25649596, text='&gt; most productive uses<p>The problem comes when you try to define this phrase in a way that’s not a tautology.<p>Our current economic system defines economic productivity to be the thing that generates the highest economic returns <i>according to the rules of the system</i>.<p>Without an independently derived set of values the phrase doesn’t have any descriptive value.<p>You could just as well be describing Bostrom’s paperclip maximizer, and I would suggest, in fact, that this is exactly what you’re doing.')
