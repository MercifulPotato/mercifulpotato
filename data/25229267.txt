Item(by='danenania', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606489917, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25229098, text='&quot;Questioning someone&#x27;s beliefs&quot; doesn&#x27;t have to mean &quot;tell the idiot why I&#x27;m right and they&#x27;re wrong in the form of a series of questions&quot;.<p>If you ask with sincere interest and an open mind, and avoid passing judgment on the answers you get (including with nonverbal cues), the person you&#x27;re questioning will generally feel flattered, not attacked.
