Item(by='mst', descendants=None, kids=[24979486], score=None, time=1604393821, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24977314, text='My use of vi-like editors is sufficiently minimalist that I use a locally built <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;ex-vi.sf.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;ex-vi.sf.net&#x2F;</a> and still don&#x27;t really need more than about half the features of <i>that</i>.<p>I -personally- find it way more comfortable than even a minimal vim install because when I typo a command it beeps at me, whereas in vim I generally accidentally activate a command I don&#x27;t understand and get broken out of flow while I figure out wtf just happened.<p>I don&#x27;t at all judge people who&#x27;re IDE wizards, I&#x27;ve not seen any correspondence between maximalist versus minimalist taste in tooling and competence, but this approach works way better for me.')
