Item(by='theptip', descendants=None, kids=[25854020, 25856159], score=None, time=1611185177, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25835212, text='F-strings are great. The only downside I’ve found is they are incompatible with the stdlib logging framework’s deferred string interpolation feature (using %s style string interpolation), which can give you a nice performance boost if you make lots of expensive debug logs for tests, but run at info level in production.<p>That said most apps probably won’t see much of a difference in perf between the two approaches.')
