Once you have that, the &quot;Search Problem&quot; of finding new fiction is a bit easier.<p>Try finding interviews of - or reviews&#x2F;letters written by - the authors you admire. They&#x27;ll usually reference some influential authors you can expand into. If a publisher&#x2F;editor is sufficiently focused, you can recurse in that direction, too.<p>You can recursively apply this algorithm, adjusting priority by how much you enjoy the authors you&#x27;ve found so far.<p>This requires a little bit more up-front work, and you have to be willing to tolerate&#x2F;toss out the occasional dud. But it&#x27;s worked well for me so far.<p>The same trick works for movies, and art more generally.<p>---<p>For example, see this interview of Karl Ove Knausgaard [1].
