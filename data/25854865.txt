Item(by='mindcrime', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611198548, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25854267, text='Surveys probably aren&#x27;t your best bet, for various reasons. People don&#x27;t want to answer them, or they don&#x27;t answer honestly, or they don&#x27;t offer the opportunity to drill into the whitespace between your pre-selected answers, etc.<p>Direct, one-on-one conversations with individuals will probably give you more &quot;bang for your buck&quot; in this regard.<p>Also, I personally would not recommend outsourcing something this important to a 3rd party firm. Make the calls and do the interviews yourself if at all possible.<p>My suggestion is to read <i>The Four Steps To The Epiphany</i> and follow the advice contained within.')
