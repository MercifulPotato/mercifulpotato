You don&#x27;t just write perfect code every single time you sit down, right? Sometimes you write something and test whether it&#x27;ll work. Sometimes you find it works, other times it doesn&#x27;t.<p>I encourage people to try to do the same with their life.<p>I was always of the mind &quot;Meditation is some kind of stupid mumbo jumbo and it&#x27;s dumb and a waste of time&quot;<p>I now meditate, because I tried it and realized it was helping me.<p>You don&#x27;t have to go into some idea thinking this is the rest of your life, it&#x27;s not, you can try something, see if it helps you or you enjoy it. If you aren&#x27;t enjoying it or you don&#x27;t see it actually helping you then stop and do something different.<p>I read a lot, over 20 books this year, most of it fiction. It has replaced watching tv in 2020 and I set a goal to read at least 30 minutes a day.')
