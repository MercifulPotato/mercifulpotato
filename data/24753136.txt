Item(by='dogma1138', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602499640, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24753071, text='IPA can ruin the surface of some phone screens, it likely won’t damage the screen itself but the layer that protects the screens from fingerprints and other residue can be washed off with alcohol.<p>That said it would probably still be better than UV as the UV might actually damage the screen especially if it’s an OLED one.<p>Overall if your phone is IP rated some dish soap and a moist cloth is probably the best option.')
