Most engineers I&#x27;ve been around care about making a good product, and get excited about using better technology or finding a better design.<p>However there&#x27;s a constant care about making things that are certifiable, and make business sense (otherwise it&#x27;s just not going to happen). It seems to me that it&#x27;s difficult for people outside this type of industry to appreciate how certification impacts the decisions.<p>We strive to make systems that are reliable, easy to use and maintain, and efficient; but they have to make their way to the customer, through the authorities and business constraints.')
