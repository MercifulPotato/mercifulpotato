Item(by='shadowgovt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609777030, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25630456, text='The misperception that unions are only for blue-collar workers is one of the causes of the decline of quality of life for the average American worker.<p>America made a massive shift from labor-backed economy to service-sector-backed economy, and in doing so, the percentage of workers in unions dropped drastically. Unions aren&#x27;t for only labor; they&#x27;re for any situation where there&#x27;s an asymmetry in negotiating power between the company owners and the employees (which is, basically, every company).')
