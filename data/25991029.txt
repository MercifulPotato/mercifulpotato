Item(by='Chris2048', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612201475, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25987004, text='&gt; When I purchase a stock, I do it in the express belief that I will get a physical share of that company<p>When you put money in a bank, acquiring interest, you no longer control that money, the bank is free to invest it, though obligated to return it. This is part of your agreement with the bank.<p>When a broker buys stock on you behalf, there are often similar arrangements in the T&amp;Cs. Your stocks therefor, cannot be borrowed, or sold, without your consent; but you need to read the terms to see what you are consenting to.')
