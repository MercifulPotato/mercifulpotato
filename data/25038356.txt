Item(by='telotortium', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604948579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25037331, text='Scanning the Wikipedia page for 5-HTR1A, it looks like most of the studies were done in the first decade of the 21st century, when the candidate gene (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Candidate_gene#Criticisms" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Candidate_gene#Criticisms</a>) approach was at its height, before it became known that many of these studies were statistically underpowered and thus likely detecting noise. So I&#x27;d be skeptical of any associations between 5-HTR1A and personality unless there&#x27;s been recent follow-up.')
