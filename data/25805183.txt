Item(by='stncls', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610828784, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25804592, text='There is a standard protocol for USB webcams. It is called &quot;USB video device class&quot; and usually referred to as UVC or UVCvideo in the Linux kernel:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;USB_video_device_class" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;USB_video_device_class</a><p>Unfortunately not all USB webcams use it yet, but its adoption is growing. Webcams that do use UVCvideo are usually well supported by the Linux kernel. Explicit compatibility list here:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.ideasonboard.org&#x2F;uvc&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.ideasonboard.org&#x2F;uvc&#x2F;</a>')
