Item(by='mkasu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608910435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25535298, text='While I think general awareness of ethics concerns is needed, I think it might also sometimes bias research directions in itself.<p>I.e., dealing with ethics concerns and&#x2F;or ethics committees becomes a huge additional workload in itself, so the research is prioritized to minimize dealing with it.<p>For example, one might stop a research which might help to treat cancer but dealing with the necessary approvals for patient data makes it unfeasible. Instead you switch to a general purpose target domain where it suddenly (unintentionally) could be used for war instead, but being general purpose it does not need to be approved by the ethics committee..')
