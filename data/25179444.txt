Item(by='gwern', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606067945, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25176488, text='To be fair to TWDNE, just generating random faces is among the least interesting use-cases, and one could do better than TWDNE with some more work.<p>For example, here is a demonstration of how the new model averaging&#x2F;transfer tricks let you convert a face from human to anime to fox to pony to furry in any direction by any percentage (summing to 100%): <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;twitter.com&#x2F;arfafax&#x2F;status&#x2F;1296084902928986113" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;twitter.com&#x2F;arfafax&#x2F;status&#x2F;1296084902928986113</a>\n(And since you can encode any pre-existing photograph into the human face model, you can do things like &quot;what if Barack Obama but only 60% human and 20% fox and 10% anime and 10% My Little Pony?&quot; Impossible-sounding, yet, there you have it.)<p>The pony&#x2F;furry models are the same ones used for <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;thisfursonadoesnotexist.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;thisfursonadoesnotexist.com&#x2F;</a> and <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;thisponydoesnotexist.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;thisponydoesnotexist.net&#x2F;</a> , incidentally. (TFDNE&#x2F;TPDNE came out much nicer than TWDNE because of better cropping&#x2F;aligning, longer training runs on entire TPU pods rather than a few GPUs, and transfer learning.)<p>We&#x27;re also still working on BigGAN for generating entire anime images. An older model: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gwern.net&#x2F;Faces#danbooru2019e621-256px-biggan" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gwern.net&#x2F;Faces#danbooru2019e621-256px-biggan</a> Samples from a current training run: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.discordapp.com&#x2F;attachments&#x2F;693736350561861633&#x2F;780096939261821008&#x2F;individualImage.png" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.discordapp.com&#x2F;attachments&#x2F;693736350561861633&#x2F;78...</a> <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.discordapp.com&#x2F;attachments&#x2F;693736350561861633&#x2F;780096682230022184&#x2F;individualImage.png" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;cdn.discordapp.com&#x2F;attachments&#x2F;693736350561861633&#x2F;78...</a> We had mostly given up, but recently discovered that random cropping was the reason our runs were taking forever and were pretty bad; fixed cropping like top cropping works much better.')
