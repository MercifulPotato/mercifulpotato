Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=[25495965], score=None, time=1608556975, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25494043, text='Most sales jobs offer some kind of revenue share.  Sometimes revenue share is the <i>only</i> compensation.<p>When cosco buys apples from a farmer for $1 and resells them for $2, they are effectively taking a 50% revenue share.<p>A referral bonus for bringing in a new customer is also a kind of revenue share for the existing customer, and plenty of startups do those.<p>I think the key is to compensate based on a revenue share of what <i>you</i> bring in, rather than of the total revenue.')
