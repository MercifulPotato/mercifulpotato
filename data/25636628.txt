An employee-backed check bolsters this fiduciary duty.<p>&gt; If I had a company, I would like to have the right to make bad decisions. And who defines good and bad decisions.<p>Feel free to do that in a company of 1. As soon as your company exceeds 1 person, you lose the absolute right. You lose the right when your decisions impact the livelihood of those around you. It doesn&#x27;t drop to zero instantly but it is attenuated as the company grows.')
