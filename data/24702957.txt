Item(by='frompdx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602022413, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24701912, text='In my experience pair programming is good when it happens organically and bad when it is prescribed.  The times I have seen it prescribed have usually been the result organizational or planning issue.  &quot;This feature isn&#x27;t getting done fast enough, you two pair on it.&quot;  Or, &quot;I don&#x27;t want to bring anything else into the sprint, you two pair on this story.&quot;  In those cases it seems like leadership is looking at pair programming as an avenue for gaining some form of efficiency, but most of the time this encumbers things further.')
