Item(by='krylon', descendants=None, kids=[25920665], score=None, time=1611690596, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25919187, text='I really dislike titles of this form (&quot;I love to do X, and so should you&quot;). It might not be the most rational response, but I intuitively refuse to let some article in some magazine tell me what I should or should not do, let alone &quot;love&quot;.<p>Just to be clear, I find the history of computing fascinating, and I have a big soft spot for old-school hardware. My heart still skips a beat when I hear a modem&#x2F;fax screeching, and every time I visit my doctor who still uses a dot matrix printer, I smile when it does its thing.')
