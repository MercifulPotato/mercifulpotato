Item(by='pferde', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611924230, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25955939, text='The &quot;manipulative&quot; here refers to the UI actively or passively lying to you, and slightly nudging you towards doing something you do not want.<p>Ads, or the way ads are presented can be manipulative too - e.g. articles which pose as informative, but in fact are written just to push a certain product. But if the ads are presented overtly, they are not manipulative in the sense that is discussed here.')
