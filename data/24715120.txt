Item(by='aphroz', descendants=None, kids=[24716718], score=None, time=1602125017, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24714361, text='I have worked on projects where I needed to extract firstnames and lastnames and if you want to use this dataset to extract names, here are some caveats:\n- firstnames can be lastnames as well\n- common words can be names as well\n- some stop words can be names\n- the order can change, you can write firstname, lastname or lastname, firstname\n- Some names are as short as one letter<p>Using ML can be useful if you can separate people by origin or in more homogeneous population.')
