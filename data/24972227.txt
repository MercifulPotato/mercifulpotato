Item(by='paulgb', descendants=None, kids=[24973554, 24973274, 24972442, 24974874], score=None, time=1604346172, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24958215, text='The article mentions having a long history of data, but I want to stress a corollary of this: if you think you might eventually want to sell data, either put your data in an append-only data format <i>now</i> so that history is preserved, or at least take regular snapshots.<p>Otherwise, any time you update the data in place, you make it impossible to reconstruct what the data &quot;looked like at the time&quot; for a backtest.')
