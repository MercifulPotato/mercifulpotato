Item(by='jpm_sd', descendants=None, kids=[25876982], score=None, time=1611320919, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25869122, text='I worked on Loon for about 3 years. We had a team of over 200 people and launched thousands of balloons. Resource constraints were never the issue. In fact I think the lack of constraints contributed to some bad engineering decisions.<p>The technology worked. It was, in fact, totally possible to build solar-powered, balloon-borne LTE base stations that provided internet access directly to handheld phones.<p>But in the end, it was just a fundamentally flawed idea. Satellites don&#x27;t pop and fall out of the sky. Balloons do, frequently.')
