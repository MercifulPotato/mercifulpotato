Item(by='acd', descendants=None, kids=[25490087, 25490430], score=None, time=1608501695, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25489840, text='Different countries have different purchase power so subscriptions may be different expensive depending where you live. That said, paying for news is essential for our democracy. Paying for news allows news sites to pay journalist to research and write news stories.<p>If we do not pay for news, news has to be financed through online advertising which is basically selling personal data for profit. Without paying revenue local news sites cannot afford to hire journalist. If there is not local journalist, there will not be controls, checks and writings on what elected officials do. Local news papers feed national news feed with articles and national news.<p>So its essential for our democracy that we are willing to pay for news services which pays journalists.')
