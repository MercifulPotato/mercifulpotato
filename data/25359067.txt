Item(by='yvdriess', descendants=None, kids=[25373961], score=None, time=1607525721, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25358586, text='I personally would love to see a frontend architecture that more closely resembles the backend: dataflow execution.\nIt&#x27;s been researched in the 80s&#x2F;90s, with some hybrids popping up in literature from time to time (TRIPS, ADAM, etc)<p>Counterpoint: processors have gotten so good at extracting ILP from a sequential ISA that I wonder how much benefit you would gain from a dataflow ISA. The downside is that it would require to completely rethink and redo the software stack. (efficient par to seq is just as hard as efficient seq to par)')
