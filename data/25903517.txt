Item(by='BelenusMordred', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611585293, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25903189, text='SHA2 hashing which secures the chain itself faces no known threats from quantum-computing.<p>ECDSA wallet signatures can easily be broken with a sufficiently sized QC and now are essentially a billion dollar bounty on whether a country or corp has gotten there.<p>Switching over to post-quantum signatures is a simple matter but the coins will have to be moved to secure them.<p>So, someone with access to such a computer can learn the private key for any wallet on the planet and steal the contents, but they can&#x27;t magically mine with 100% hashpower or undo history and all prior tx&#x27;s.')
