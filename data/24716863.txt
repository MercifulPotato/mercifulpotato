Lisp macros do overlap with what you can achieve with Haskell things like typeclasses, laziness, monads and template Haskell, but the overlap is far from 100%.<p>The author of Hackett has a video about it: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;5QQdI3P7MdY" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;5QQdI3P7MdY</a>')
