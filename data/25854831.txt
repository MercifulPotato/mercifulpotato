Item(by='naikrovek', descendants=None, kids=[25855025, 25854943, 25855011], score=None, time=1611198128, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25853813, text='Pollution capable of completely killing the dominant species of a planet would last a long time after the creation of that pollution ended.<p>The pollution would vastly outlive the polluters, so it would be detectable for some time after their demise, especially if anything they automated also polluted.
