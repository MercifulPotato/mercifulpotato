Item(by='a_imho', descendants=4, kids=[24719472, 24719369, 24719303], score=2, time=1602166605, title='Ask HN: Pushback on Interviews?', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='Interviews are becoming an ever increasing time sink. Multiple rounds of phone screens, quizzes, take home tests, online assessments and whiteboarding. It is ridiculous that companies who want to hire professional knowledge workers are expecting candidates to put in tens of hours of work (not counting any prep time) for free without having any skin in the game themselves. Where to draw the line? How to push back on ridiculous requirements? Is it fair to ask for compensation of your time? How to cut back on interview bloat?')
