Usually they just assume that if you have more money you&#x27;ll be happier.<p>Maybe a fancy car will increase your happiness, but there is no unit of measure you can divide the purchase price of that mid-life crisis car by.<p>The value of a life is roughly how much money they would have made if they had lived until some arbitrary end date.
