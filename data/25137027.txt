Item(by='ukoki', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605709376, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25134220, text='However employers decide to compensate employees in our post-Covid future, it doesn&#x27;t matter because they must still participate in the market.<p>If an employer&#x27;s particular remote-salary algorithm spits out a number that is less then what equally-qualified employees can get elsewhere, they will go elsewhere. If not, they won&#x27;t. The end effect is that any employer participating in a market will must pay what that market is demanding, regardless of what the algorithm says.')
