Item(by='abrowne2', descendants=None, kids=[25995927], score=None, time=1612221060, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25992390, text='I was diagnosed with ADHD around age 12 in early school. For about 10 years, I was on a lot of stimulant medication; Vyvanse, Adderall, Concerta, and tried many others. Only in the last couple years did I stop entirely. You get used to the sleep impairment after a while. It affected my ability to socialize. I was making excuses for my behavior and largely, I could summarize it as &quot;feeling like a zombie, entirely reliant on a pill.&quot; My opinion on it now is that it can help you, but now I prefer to be in my default mode, unencumbered by meds.')
