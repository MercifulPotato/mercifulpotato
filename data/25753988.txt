Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610486834, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25753223, text='There are no single criteria that can be used to judge how democratic a country is.<p>Many European countries have more restrictions on free speech than USA, so yes, they are less democratic by this criterion.<p>By other criteria, e.g. by evaluating how many abusive laws they have that favor a few rich individuals that own some large companies against the majority of the citizens, most European countries are more democratic than USA.<p>The same conclusion comes from other criteria, like how easy is for most citizens to access education or health services.')
