Item(by='shash', descendants=None, kids=[24671730, 24670823], score=None, time=1601716277, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24663048, text='The thing is, cogeneration (incinerators producing power) is a bit of a red herring; usually our waste doesn&#x27;t have the calorific value to actually produce usable power, so it&#x27;s mixed with fuel, which is not exactly ideal. Also, unsegregated garbage, when burnt, will produce stuff like dioxins and furans which you <i>don&#x27;t</i> want in the atmosphere.<p>It&#x27;s necessary for some types of waste (medical, for example), but it&#x27;s not a power generation method.')
