Item(by='Foivos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602622452, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24768873, text='Both LTE-M and NB-IoT are considered technologies of the 5G family. Technically they are way simpler versions of LTE, but they can coexist with &quot;normal&quot; 4G and 5G. A 4G &#x2F; 5G base station can allocate part of its radio resources to these mobile technologies. In the future, with slicing this allocation can even be dynamic.<p>Most of the times, when people discuss how 5G will serve &quot;massive machine type communications&quot; they refer to LTE-M &#x2F; NB-IoT.')
