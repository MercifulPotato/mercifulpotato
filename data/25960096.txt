Most common indices (by AUM) are market-cap weighted. If a total market ETF contains 0.25% of it in GME, and GME doubles in value, it&#x27;s GME it holds is now 0.50%, and that&#x27;s how much it should hold now, according to market-cap weight. No buying or selling.<p>Of course there&#x27;s many ETF&#x27;s that aren&#x27;t market-cap weighted, but the majority of funds&#x2F;value is.')
