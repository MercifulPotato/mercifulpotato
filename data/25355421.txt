Item(by='timoth3y', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607488716, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25352088, text='I put myself through college largely by playing in clubs and bars on the east coast.<p>Because the change happened so slowly, many people don&#x27;t appreciate exactly how streaming killed musician&#x27;s incomes.<p>For the most part, they were cover bands, and we never made much money at the door. Most of our income came from selling $15 CDs to drunk patrons who were having a great time. Live music isn&#x27;t as popular as it used to be, but the move to MP3 and streaming took away the impulse purchase that made so many local bands profitable.')
