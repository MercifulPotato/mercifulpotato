Loss is mentioned a lot but not mentioned is linking the asymmetric movement of trust to the cognitive bias of loss aversion.<p>2. Aside from Putnam, another take worth mentioning is Steven M. R. Covey&#x27;s <i>The Speed of Trust</i> which speaks to the link of trust to performance within organizations.<p>3. The interface, boundary or contract is a common method of encapsulating trust. For example, since ancient times people have used &quot;silent trade&quot; in which trade goods are placed in the open and left behind.
