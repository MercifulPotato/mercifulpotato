Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[25278837, 25279581, 25279225], score=None, time=1606926013, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25277413, text='I guess I can&#x27;t speak for the movement as a whole, but the vegan&#x2F;vegetarian activists I know aren&#x27;t very pragmatic. they tend to take a very all-or-nothing approach to convincing people.<p>I guess this is a personal moral failing, but I just can&#x27;t commit to never ever eating meat, cooking with butter, etc. what I can do is stuff like saving meat for special occasions (or when I&#x27;m served it at someone else&#x27;s place), and substituting olive oil for butter. the two things I have a really hard time cutting back on eggs (such a great way to get protein in the morning) and milk for my coffee.<p>anyways, I feel like &quot;consume less&quot; is a much easier message to sell than &quot;consume none&quot;. I wonder if it wouldn&#x27;t be more impactful to convince a large group of people to consume less than to persuade a small group of die-hards to abstain altogether.')
