Item(by='gregjor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603131511, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827269, text='Tracking what you spend time doing is not measuring or optimizing productivity.<p>In software development the 3rd definition of “productive” seems most applicable: “yielding results, benefits, or profits” (Webster’s). That’s how you measure productivity, not by how much time you spent on it.<p>If you need an app to break down your time like this you probably have a bigger problem with time management and focus.')
