Item(by='Certhas', descendants=None, kids=[24990256], score=None, time=1604505757, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24989774, text='Just to give you a feel of what is actually possible in a well run election: Germany (80 Million people) has a fully manual (all paper ballots, no voting machines or automatic tallying) and fully witnessed official result on election night. This count is then checked and certified as final a few days later (discrepancies are rare and small). People don&#x27;t have to wait in line to vote. And voting happens on Sunday, which is a mandatory day off for the vast majority of people.')
