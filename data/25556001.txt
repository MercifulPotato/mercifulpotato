Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609120474, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25555946, text='I’m arguing that the examples by themselves don’t tell us anything about <i>how</i> the US could be managed better, or even <i>if</i> there is anything the US could apply that they are doing.<p>I don’t preclude the possibility of learning from other countries, but I think it’s meaningless to say that just because a small country happens to be running better right now, there must be something it is doing that the US could be doing that would help.<p>It’s possible that there is, but there is no prima facie evidence of that.  If someone wants to make that argument, they need to explain <i>what</i> is being done better and <i>how</i> the US could adopt it.  Otherwise it’s just nationalism.')
