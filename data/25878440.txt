Item(by='asveikau', descendants=None, kids=[25879022], score=None, time=1611363664, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25878340, text='I think it becomes an application specific or domain specific thing.<p>For example, I&#x27;ve done some work with audio or video.  <i>Nobody</i> working on that goes straight to malloc on every packet or frame.  It&#x27;d just be asking for pain.<p>But a <i>general purpose</i> allocator doing its own free-list on the assumption that libc is going to suck?  I think that&#x27;s outdated.  If you do want to support it, I think it&#x27;s better to allow the caller to replace the allocator through a function pointer, rather than just do it by default in a library.')
