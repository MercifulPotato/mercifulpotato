Item(by='dreamcompiler', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601779456, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24669138, text='The numbers being sorted in Bead Sort are base 1, which means it&#x27;s never faster than O[n] where n is not the number of numbers being sorted, but the <i>largest</i> number being sorted.<p>That means it won&#x27;t scale well and is impractical for sorting realistic numbers (barring a lot of special-purpose parallel hardware).')
