Item(by='julianlam', descendants=None, kids=[25170553], score=None, time=1605964805, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25168166, text='I have an orthogonal approach to rice cookers. I find that the extra features are pointless and were added purely to differentiate themselves from competitors (or even between models themselves).<p>Of the people I know who use rice cookers, they all use it to make rice, but usage of the other features drops significantly afterwards. After all, do you feel like using your rice cooker to make oatmeal?<p>My ideal rice cooker has one button: a physical switch, and no timer.')
