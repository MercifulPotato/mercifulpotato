Item(by='westwing', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606136654, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25185958, text='The PCR test amplifies RNA fragments. If after a certain amount of replication cycles the amount of detected RNA crosses a threshold, it is considered a positive.<p>A positive PCR test does not imply infectiousness. Many people who have a positive test are not just asymptomatic, they are not really infected to begin with.<p>A negative PCR test does not rule out disease. Some people who have a clinical diagnosis of COVID may produce a negative PCR result.')
