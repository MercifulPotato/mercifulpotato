Item(by='mkl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606303728, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25205018, text='Say you have a $100 debt, so your net worth is -$100.  If I take that $100 debt away from you, your net worth goes up by $100.  -$100×-1 = +$100: a negative times a negative is a positive.<p>A debt is a negative quantity, and me taking something from you changes your quantity of whatever it was by a negative amount (if I take one apple from you, you gain -1 apples), so a negative times a negative is a positive.<p>If you have 3 $100 debts.  If I take two of those $100 debts away from you, your net worth goes up by -$100×-2 = $200.')
