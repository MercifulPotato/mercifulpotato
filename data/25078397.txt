Item(by='PeterisP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605237188, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25077913, text='We&#x27;ve dug out almost all the easily accessible minerals - we&#x27;re currently mining minerals that are cheaply accessible to <i>us</i> with modern technology, but the ones that were cheaply accessible to pre-industrial civilizations are long gone.<p>For some minerals (e.g. metals) that might possible even be a bonus, as we&#x27;ve brought it up to the surface and it might be more accessible than before (a scrapyard that&#x27;s buried under some soil could be an excellent place to mine iron), but there won&#x27;t be shallow coal or shallow oil that a second  industrialization could use as a cheap power source.')
