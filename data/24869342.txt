Item(by='the_other', descendants=None, kids=[24869888], score=None, time=1603460490, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24867240, text='I’m pretty sure this is right.<p>However the kind of work you’re discussing doesn’t match my understanding of “busywork”. Busywork means unnecessary work done to give the appearance of actual work.  Busywork doesn’t produce much value beyond appearances.<p>The work you’re discussing is “the work”. It’s the underpinnings, the research, the practice, the artist’s sketchbook, the exploration, the learning. None of that is “busywork”. If anything, it’s the opposite. Much of it will look like busywork (i.e. bringing little value), yet actually producing the highest value in the long run.')
