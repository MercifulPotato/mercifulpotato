Item(by='Silhouette', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602659882, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24773555, text='This often feels like a meme: &quot;I had a scalability problem, so I made everything distributed and asynchronous. Now I have three problems!&quot;<p>It&#x27;s not that the idea is fundamentally broken, but I suspect a lot of people dramatically misjudge the trade-offs. Never underestimate the power and efficiency of one big, fast server, nor the added complexity that is introduced by taking loose coupling to extreme levels.')
