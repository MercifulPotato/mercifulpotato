Item(by='Anechoic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601564600, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24651474, text='If you travel a lot already (say, for work), loyalty programs can provide worthwhile perks. I had a 10-day vacation in Japan a couple of years ago, complete with first-class flights and luxury hotel stays and only paid ~$500 out of pocket (and $300 of that was for a Japan Rail pass). The rest was paid for out of a combination of airlines mies (three different FF programs) and hotel points (2 chains).<p>If you don&#x27;t travel a lot, it&#x27;s probably not worth it, but if you&#x27;re already going through the motions, why wouldn&#x27;t you?')
