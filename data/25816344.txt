Item(by='dfox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610925949, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25815179, text='It is somewhat natural to just refer to 802.X standards as 802.X  when implementing them as that is the correct name of the standard and you need to read most of it in order to implement it. If you want to talk about distinct added features it makes sense to use the name of TG (ie. 802.[number][letter]) that drafted the specification of said  feature, but it is mostly only an handy shorthand.')
