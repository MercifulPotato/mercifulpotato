If the power level is high enough to do damage, detecting it would be trivially easy.<p>Just a wire dipole, a diode, and a simple meter will do the job. At most  it would require a set of dipoles to cover each band of interest.<p>Most radio hobbyists could make a set of dipoles from an a old coat-hanger in a few minutes.')
