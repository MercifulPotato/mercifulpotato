Item(by='jiggawatts', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604807413, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25019441, text='What I&#x27;ve been looking for is a not-outrageously priced home theatre system that supports all the latest standards, like DTS-X, Dolby Atmos, etc... and has proper, decent speakers, not tiny little tin cans or a soundbar or something.<p>However, nobody seems to make the &quot;proper&quot; speakers that have the up-firing part needed for Dolby Atmos <i>and</i> are also wireless so I don&#x27;t have to run cables from the TV to the rear speakers.<p>The few that I&#x27;ve found so far are crazy expensive.<p>I think home audio needs an IKEA-type manufacturer to upend the traditional economics of the products.<p>The expensive part of home theatre audio is that proper wood speakers are big and expensive to ship.<p>Someone needs to figure out a way to flat-pack full size speakers that consumers can assemble at home, but without requiring soldering irons and power tools.')
