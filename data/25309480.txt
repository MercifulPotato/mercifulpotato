Item(by='linknoid', descendants=None, kids=[25309657, 25310481], score=None, time=1607122876, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25309252, text='The simple trick with modulo 7 is take all the 3 bit sequences: (x &amp; 111), (x &amp; 111000) &gt;&gt; 3, (x &amp; 111000000) &gt;&gt; 6, etc. and add them all together.  It means you just have to do bitshift + add instead of all the complexity of division.  Same applies to higher powers of 2^n-1 as well.')
