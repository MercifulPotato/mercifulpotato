Item(by='bserge', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609860125, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25642381, text='Pure caffeine is quite different - it removes many of the (negative) effects, notably stomach upset, bloating, frequent urination.<p>For me, powder in capsules works best, pressed tablets are slower to take effect, and the brand also matters, although there&#x27;s so many of them you&#x27;d have to try and see for yourself - either part of the extraction process or the source of caffeine is different, but some brands are noticeably and consistently different in effects.<p>Some have that brain fog and jitteriness that is common with coffee, some are just a clean alertness with clean withdrawal.<p>Quitting coffee using pure caffeine might work for some, and not for others.')
