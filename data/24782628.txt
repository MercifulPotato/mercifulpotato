Item(by='lumost', descendants=None, kids=[24782685], score=None, time=1602715754, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24782473, text='I don&#x27;t believe social harmony is a sufficient reason for censorship <i>or</i> that censorship is the correct approach.  There are many potential remedies including.<p>- Mandatory content identifiers tags on content&#x2F;claims for unfactual or unverified reporting reaching more than X people.<p>- Identifying frequent reporters of misinformation and identifying them as such when presenting&#x2F;resharing information.<p>- Reducing the spread of misinformation in algorithmic sources such as newsfeeds&#x2F;search rankings.<p>None of these approaches removes or censors the content, but they do identify it and help place it in the appropriate context.  In the 90s I had to go looking for claims that the president had dinner with aliens every Tuesday, today such news articles are pushed via feeds.')
