Those went extinct in about 1905. The High Plains Locusts went extinct in about 1930. [1]<p>There are various theories about why they went extinct -- something to do with what the settlers and farmers were doing to the land. But it wasn&#x27;t an obvious culprit like pesticide, which wasn&#x27;t really in widespread use until about the 40s.<p>1. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Rocky_Mountain_locust" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Rocky_Mountain_locust</a>')
