Item(by='fartcannon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607217785, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25313776, text='I recently bought a dumb TV (Proscan something or other, 4K) where I watch Netflix, Prime Video, Youtube and Crave TV in a modified version of Chromium (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.vpetkov.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.vpetkov.net&#x2F;</a>) on a Raspberry Pi 4. \nAdditionally I stream my Steam Library using the Steam Link deb in the raspbian repos and a set of Steam Controllers via the dongle (the SC bluetooth connection kinda sucks on the RPI4)<p>For best perforance, use a wired ethernet connection, and a bluetooth dongle for your non-Steam controllers and bluetooth headset.<p>Works flawlessly and it&#x27;s a delight to have a full Linux distro on my TV.')
