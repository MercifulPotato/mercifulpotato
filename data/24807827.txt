Item(by='junon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602915636, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24806704, text='By modularization, I mean being able to configure the sorts of terms you want in your license, perhaps via UI.<p>Instead of 6 or so fully-fleshed-out licenses for 6 unique purposes, you&#x27;d instead have a selection of &quot;terms&quot; or whatever that each add a limitation or a grant to the license.<p>I&#x27;d be able to check something like &quot;limited liability&quot;, &quot;free to use&quot;, &quot;free to modify&quot; but perhaps leave &quot;used commercially&quot; and &quot;must re-contribute modifications&quot; unchecked.<p>The generated result would be a license in a similar format to what PolyForm does now (which I really like, by the way) but with the customized terms.<p>You could then retrofit the existing v1 contracts into the new system as presets.')
