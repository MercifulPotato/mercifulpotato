Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611314784, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25868715, text='A strong lion may seemingly indulge such a code of honor for a while - not because of honor, but because healthy antelopes taste better and the lion is fast enough to catch them. But these conditions can change at any moment.<p>This mirrors how, in general, markets approach human values. They&#x27;re a nice thing to have if you can afford them, perhaps you can mention them in your marketing - but the moment competition threatens your margins, you&#x27;ll drop these values to stay competitive. Ethics is a market inefficiency, and expecting companies to behave ethically <i>over time</i> if not explicitly forced to is, indeed, naive.')
