Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605740311, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25143673, text='I have spent a bit of time thinking about this, and can&#x27;t really come up with a way of calculating a &#x27;justifiable cut&#x27; for Apple.<p>Would it be their cost per average app? Should it be a fixed fee with a variable rate on top? Should they license access like the videogame companies used to? Won&#x27;t these smaller profits further incentivize Apple to offer competing apps in every category, and dis-incentivize quality control of third party apps?<p>My conclusion (so far) is that it&#x27;s best to let each platform developer choose their business model, and see which one works best. Business models can compete along with hardware and software.')
