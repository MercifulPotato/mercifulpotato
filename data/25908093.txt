Item(by='jccooper', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611605345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25907497, text='EINs don&#x27;t accumulate Social Security, but when you file taxes you&#x27;ll pay &quot;self-employment tax&quot; on earnings from that &quot;business&quot; and those go to your personal SS account.<p>When you use an EIN you&#x27;re basically claiming to act as a business. For some cases, you can do that just fine. But a lot of SSN requests for identification or credit checks it won&#x27;t work. And anyone who cares that it&#x27;s a SSN vs a TIN can figure that out easily.')
