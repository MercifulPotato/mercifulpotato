Item(by='legerdemain', descendants=None, kids=[25573179], score=None, time=1609265993, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25568628, text='Another fun one: the word for &quot;marker&quot; (i.e., a felt-tipped pen for drawing) is &quot;фломастер,&quot; from the brand Flo-Master.<p>Although, given the way things are going, it wouldn&#x27;t surprise me if the word was now &quot;шарпи.&quot;<p>Surprisingly, the art supply brand Caran d&#x27;Ache is from the Russian &quot;карандаш&quot; and not vice versa.<p>The Ukrainian for &quot;pencil&quot; simply comes from the word for the metal tin, which, like lead metal, was in use for styluses used in drawing before the discovery of graphite.')
