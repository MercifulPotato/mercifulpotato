Item(by='statenjason', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603926236, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24924997, text='Displaying a loading spinner in under 50ms while waiting for a 500ms request to complete doesn&#x27;t necessarily build a strong relationship.<p>SPAs can start off with the good intention of being low-latency and responsive. But when you&#x27;re backed by a web server, you still have to account for it though optimistic rendering, prefetching, and loading states.
