Item(by='bostik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602862519, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24799658, text='If you&#x27;re debugging weird latency problems, I&#x27;d recommend to start from relative timestamps. Then filter to just one or two TCP streams that exhibit the problem and go from there.<p>Inspect packet contents. The packet dissectors are doing a lot of heavy lifting, so <i>look</i> at the deconstructed data. I realise this may not be a welcome suggestion, but a sharp pencil, an A3 (or larger) scratch pad and a good ruler go a long way.<p>I&#x27;ve dug to the bottom of quite a few network and traffic problems in my life by drawing the observed traffic patterns into sequence diagrams. Once you have the diagram visualised, it&#x27;s easier to spot the places where something funky either happens or looks to be missing.')
