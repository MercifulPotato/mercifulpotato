Item(by='Veen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609265090, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25571206, text='Essays historically originated---with Montaigne&#x27;s Essais---as discursive, digressive, long-form prose. They are a prose artform that attempts to achieve more than a straightforward information dump.<p>You might not like the form, and there are certainly many bad examples, but a long essay is no more a failure to write a short blog article than a poem is a failure to write a news report.')
