Item(by='rini17', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609854252, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25645269, text='Looking forward to these!<p>Just that supercaps aren&#x27;t going to be universal drop-in replacement  for batteries since capacitors&#x27; voltage drops linearly with discharge. I.e. when half-discharged as measured in Ah, the voltage drops to half, too. But by that point, only a quarter of <i>energy</i> measured in Wh is left in capacitors, so loads that aren&#x27;t sensitive to voltage (or have a regulator&#x2F;dc-dc converter) will be fine.')
