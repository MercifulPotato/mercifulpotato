Item(by='dcolkitt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610585102, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25769391, text='On the technical merits, I think Bitcoin is actually rather behind other blockchains. But, remember that money is a Schelling point. There&#x27;s a huge first mover advantage.<p>Money, in any form, is only valuable because we expect others to continue to value it into the future. The reason that gold is used as a monetary asset, whereas rhodium is not despite being as rare, is because gold arrived earlier in history.<p>One could also that the Bitcoin community&#x27;s conservative nature also lends it credence as a long term store of value. Ethereum as a protocol much more amenable to technical innovation. But if you&#x27;re in the &quot;stay rich&quot; business, instead of the &quot;get rich&quot; business, than that can be a bug not a feature.')
