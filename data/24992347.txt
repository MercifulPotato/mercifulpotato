Item(by='throwaway894345', descendants=None, kids=[24992376], score=None, time=1604518894, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24992272, text='It’s not a micro-optimization, and you really shouldn’t naively optimize for lines of code. The explicit error handling is to encourage programmers to think about errors and to make it easier for readers to trace the error path through the call stack. Making it harder to punt on error handling is the whole point, just like static typing is tedious for the programmer who only cares if their code <i>appears to be</i> correct.')
