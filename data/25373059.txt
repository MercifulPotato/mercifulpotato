Item(by='imtringued', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607604041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25371446, text='Water futures are just a promise to deliver water at a certain price. You can sell the promise for money even if you don&#x27;t have the water today but you know you will have the water tomorrow. If you sell your water on the day it is extracted then you are vulnerable to daily market swings. Sometimes there will be gluts, sometimes droughts.')
