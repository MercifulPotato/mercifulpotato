Item(by='jka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601729281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671451, text='It&#x27;s a bit of an unusual idea, but I&#x27;d suggest that making symptom reports more public would help -- as long as they are anonymized and opt-in.<p>That&#x27;s a really tricky technical problem to achieve, since many bug reports depend on the user&#x27;s local environment in order to reproduce the problem, and that could conflict with anonymization.<p>Making problems public creates incentives for the project to fix issues (since they can&#x27;t hide their existence) and also provides opportunities and information for contributors to  help resolve them (including for purely selfish reasons, to fix issues that affect their own systems too).')
