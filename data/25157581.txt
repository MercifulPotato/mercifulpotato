Item(by='hapless', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605850303, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25157196, text='SVR4 was released in 1990, before win32 or NT even existed. By the time Interix came along, the whole world was ELF — there was even an Intel standard that unified all x86 UNIX ABIs, iBCS.<p>AIX is the only Unix to retain COFF.<p>It’s deeply weird, unsupported by standard tools, and shipped with a not quite 100% compatible shim for the gnu linker.<p>Trust me when I say it is not a fun combination.')
