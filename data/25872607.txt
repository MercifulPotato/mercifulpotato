Item(by='Terretta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611330345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25870620, text='Here’s a list of password managers:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;List_of_password_managers" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;List_of_password_managers</a><p>It has a column for Secure Sharing, but not one to show granularity.<p>Ones that make organization easy seem to choose to offer persistent sharing at the vault level (multiple vaults shared to nobody or to different sets of people), easy ways to move items between vaults, and flagging if you have multiple or OOS copies of items.<p>Careful, most seem to offer per-item share-as-a-copy that the recipient should store, which I wouldn’t consider as counting as the kind of sharing needed for this thread.')
