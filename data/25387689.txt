Item(by='marpstar', descendants=None, kids=[25387916, 25387920], score=None, time=1607704985, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25387635, text='I&#x27;ve had this problem on Mac. In my case a pair of AirPods sounded really muddy and low quality. macOS was setting them up as the output AND input audio device. The two-way Bluetooth communication eats more bandwidth, hence the lower quality. The solution was to set the audio input to something other than the AirPods.<p>Try setting your audio input to something other than the headset on the Windows machine.')
