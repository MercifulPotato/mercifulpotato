Item(by='p_l', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603630171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24885860, text='Part of it is toolkits throwing sometimes multiple megabyte bitmaps to draw, which starts to have problems the moment you don&#x27;t have a zerocopy, gpu accelerated method to draw them.<p>The old core protocol approach extensively used optimized graphic operations on the server side, with clients sending things like &quot;draw me a rectangle&#x2F;fill a rectangle&#x2F;draw a bunch of lines&quot; etc. - today you&#x27;re going to get pretty big bitmap (especially with high dpi).<p>It&#x27;s the same problem that mobile devices faced, and is related to a lot of issues on how android devices were &quot;janky&quot; (and related to the various hacks that Apple did to make sure your application wasn&#x27;t capable of overstressing the early iPhones - cause just displaying basic UI was close to doing that.)')
