Item(by='salawat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602109675, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24712248, text='The issue is that in engineering there are certain patterns of implementation that are simply converged on.<p>Upholding the API copyright would be like giving Craftsman grounds to sue Ryobi for manufacturing circular saws because Ryobi combined a power cord, an electrical motor, a toothed circular blade and a housing; a state of affairs that only seems even remotely plausible to endorse because source code is inherently a read product.<p>You look at it and see an authored work. I look at it and I see an n-dimensional turing-space relationship diagram as expressed through transform functions.<p>And last I checked, you could patent some math(RSA), but not not copyright it. Eventually it&#x27;s just prior art.')
