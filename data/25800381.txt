Item(by='dmichulke', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610776062, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25799368, text='If you&#x27;re trying to learn a language, use the Assimil books.<p>They have the target language on the left side and usually both a literal and an easy-to-understand translation on the same height of the page on the right side.<p>Sometimes with explanations as to where the expression comes from.<p>Also: very little grammar (only to the extended needed, nothing upfront)<p>Best books I ever found for learning languages.<p>I have no affiliation, but learnt 2 languages to B2&#x2F;C1 level with their help.')
