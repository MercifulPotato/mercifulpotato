Item(by='hodgesrm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603338403, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24845420, text='Your view is the original design idea behind stored procedures.<p>Stored procedures as far as I know were first implemented in Sybase SQL Server. We tended to think of them as enabling RPC (remote procedure call) access to the DBMS. Sybase was also the first major relational database to offer client access through the network. Our connectivity was influenced by distributed system concepts like RPCs that were top-of-mind in the 1980s.')
