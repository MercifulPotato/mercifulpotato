Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608748456, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25519421, text='&gt; Generally, when one says average, they mean average.<p>“Average” is an ambiguous term. The mode, median, and the various (harmonic, geometric, arithmetic) means are all averages.<p>In income statistics specifically, the <i>usual</i> average referenced is the median of the appropriate measure.<p>In context-neutral informal conversation, the arithmetic mean is the most common average.<p>If you want to be clear, its a good idea to avoid “average” and use the specific term for a measure.')
