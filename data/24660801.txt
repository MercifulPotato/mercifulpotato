Item(by='DaiPlusPlus', descendants=None, kids=[24666910], score=None, time=1601631986, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24660208, text='I think it&#x27;s worth distinguishing between &quot;shell&quot; browsers that simply wrap an encapsulated rendering engine (such as those IE6&#x2F;MSHTML-based browsers from decades ago) and other browsers like Chromium&#x2F;Edge&#x2F;Opera which have a different level of integration with the rendering engine - because those browsers are able to tweak and extend - or disable - the engine&#x27;s features on a granular scale.<p>It&#x27;s better to compare Chrome&#x2F;Edge&#x2F;Opera as &quot;distributions and customizations&quot; of Chromium&#x2F;Blink instead of mere as wrappers - the same way that Linux distributions aren&#x27;t reduced to just &quot;GNU&#x2F;Linux kernel wrappers&quot;.')
