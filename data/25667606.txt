Large fleets buy modern, streamlined trucks from Volvo and Freightliner (Mercedes) to save on fuel, while self-employed truckers buy Peterbilt and Kenworth that look like they haven&#x27;t changed since the 70s.<p>Though the latter have the added benefit of fewer breakdowns, due to older designs having beefier parts, but also weigh more and burn more diesel.')
