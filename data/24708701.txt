Item(by='djrogers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602081567, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24708509, text='That’s not at all what the T2 is.  It’s a multi-purpose processor that handles some security related services (like the Secure Enclave), acts as the SSD controller, handles the camera light, touchID, and other stuff.<p>It’s not there as a ‘bridge’ - plenty of macs without T2 processors run macOS 11 and Siri with full feature parity, and neither of those things relies on the T2.')
