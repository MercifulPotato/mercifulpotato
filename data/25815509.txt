Humans live in a lot of places where you need heating to survive anyway. Currently we use &#x27;dumb&#x27; heating, where heat is generated without any useful computation. This can be painlessly replaced with &#x27;smart&#x27; heating, when heat is a result of mining. Any home appliance like iron or heater can do some useful work (and even earn some spare change while at it).')
