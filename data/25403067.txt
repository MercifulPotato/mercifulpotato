Item(by='mewse', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607818470, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25401168, text='I&#x27;ve built something very similar as well.<p>For me, I found that the display I&#x27;m using works reasonably well for photos, but you need to slightly boost the contrast of the image before updating the display, to compensate for the darkness of the display.  If you&#x27;re using PIL in python, this is just something like &quot;image = PIL.ImageEnhance.Contrast(image).enhance(1.3)&quot;<p>I do that immediately before dithering into 16-greys, and get entirely acceptable photo displays that way.')
