Item(by='ItalyPaleAle', descendants=None, kids=[25867183], score=None, time=1611245613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25860370, text='Speaking for myself, containers are started and destroyed faster. When using VMs, the tendency is to keep updating the software within the VM, making changes to their state, etc: this eventually leads to drifts. When using containers, if you need to make a change, you destroy the container and re-build it, and the state is always consistent with what you (and possibly your teammates) use')
