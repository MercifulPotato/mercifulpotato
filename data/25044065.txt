A lot of people come into e.g. the Go slack channel and ask &quot;What is the best web application framework?&quot;, because that&#x27;s the go-to thing when you do e.g. Java; the common answer is &quot;YAGNI&quot;, because the standard library is good enough. If not, there are some really lightweight libraries on top of the stdlib that do things like adding route matching, or slight conveniences to do SQL.')
