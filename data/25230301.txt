Between the forest and the meadow is where lies the life that may be desirable, whether fauna or flora.<p>I had labeled the two distinctions as destroyer&#x2F;creators versus maintainers.  I tear things down to create better replacements, whereas my partner is an excellent maintainer - As long as my new system is not too onerous, she is usually willing to keep it going, as I move on to the next process.  And it seems that the transition from a small company to a larger one is also the transition from the independent to the more conventional minded, even though it contains its own traps (like having independents forced out - even from their own companies, or being strangled into oblivion by bean-counters, as it were).<p>Those who don&#x27;t&#x2F;did not do well in school may be in the independent group, more often than not.
