Item(by='bognition', descendants=None, kids=[25906130], score=None, time=1611595476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25905662, text='The signals you pick up between 0.5 Hz and 40Hz are mostly likely &quot;population&quot; level signals or put another way the average activity of millions of neurons spread across large swaths of the brain.  A good analogy re: trying to decode the brain from EEG would be akin to trying to decode what is going on in a soccer match but only using the noise of the crowds.')
