The CA Waste Burner must be able to automatically shut down before any human can react to an alarm and choose what to do. If human action were ever required for operation, other than during startup procedures, then we would consider it a design failure...</i><p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;doi.org&#x2F;10.1016&#x2F;B978-0-08-101126-3.00023-3" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;doi.org&#x2F;10.1016&#x2F;B978-0-08-101126-3.00023-3</a>')
