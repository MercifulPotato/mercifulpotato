Item(by='thaumasiotes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606974491, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25284811, text='&gt; I&#x27;m sure many readers implicitly converted 775% to the expectation in their heads.<p>Most people cannot do this correctly; the most obvious interpretation of a &quot;775% chance&quot; is that it represents a 25% chance of seven occurrences and a 75% chance of eight occurrences, with no other possibilities.<p>The problem gets even worse when you have expectations less than one. If the expected number of occurrences is 80%, what are the odds of getting any occurrences at all? They&#x27;re less than 80% as long as it&#x27;s possible to have more than one occurrence.')
