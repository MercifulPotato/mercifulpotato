Item(by='raiph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611000031, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25787043, text='For single placeholders I prefer the look of either the `_` syntax or the alternative some PLs have of `.`.<p>But otoh, Raku nicely stretches the approach to both zero and multiple placeholders:<p>* The zero placeholder `.methodname` in Raku is the same as Scala&#x27;s `_.methodname`<p>* Any number of placeholders are allowed, so one can write eg `* + *` as an anonymous binary add function.')
