Item(by='pfdietz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609245741, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25566412, text='Geothermal is a lot like nuclear: it&#x27;s a capital intensive baseload source.  As such, it suffers like nuclear in an environment with increasing intermittent penetration.<p>One might have imagined an environment in which geothermal replaced (say) diesel at remote locations, because diesel fuel is expensive.   But add some wind&#x2F;solar&#x2F;batteries to that diesel and you save most of the fuel.  The benefit of replacing it with geothermal is reduced.')
