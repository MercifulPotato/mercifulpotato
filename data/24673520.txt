Item(by='riskable', descendants=None, kids=[24675273], score=None, time=1601746856, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24672678, text='Yeah, magnetic levitation is the exact opposite of magnetic separation.  Magnetic levitation switches are basically just linear switches that get harder to press the farther down they get.<p>Magnetic separation switches are the opposite:  The force is strongest at the start but then gets weaker the farther down the key travel you go.  Here&#x27;s a force curve that I&#x27;ve measured for my 3D printable magnetic separation switch:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;qHrgo4s" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;qHrgo4s</a>')
