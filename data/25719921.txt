Item(by='arcturus17', descendants=None, kids=[25720294], score=None, time=1610313174, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25719205, text='Let’s not kid ourselves, most people use Code because it’s free as in free beer, and that’s it.<p>I personally feel lucky that unlike for categories like web search, social networking or mobile messaging, the IDE market features some reasonably-priced <i>paid</i> alternatives of extremely high quality.<p>But many people - including some truly outstanding developers - will continue to use VSCode because they believe that free as in free beer is always better, no matter what. There may be privacy violations, a desecration of FOSS ideals, shady business practices - you name it - and they will continue to use it as long as it’s free and the negative externalities are not <i>too bad</i>. I wouldn’t agree, but I do empathize.')
