A simple minimax implementation that searches the possible moves <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Minimax" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Minimax</a><p>From there you start optimizing your both by adding additional features to the evaluation function and improving your search using pruning and caching')
