Item(by='monkeydust', descendants=None, kids=[25182843, 25182195], score=None, time=1606083884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25179296, text='Having done &#x27;Product&#x27; for a long time now I think the term is ripe for a reboot.<p>I personally don&#x27;t look for Product Managers but Business Managers.<p>Difference?<p>All the traits discussed here plus an understanding that your responsible for delivering products that need to generate $. It may sound obvious but I have seen many PMs who are obsessed about crafting beautiful applications when sometimes a cheaper and dirty approach is what is called for to win the business. This requires deep commercial awareness and an understanding of client incentives along with ability to properly cost up your business to form an opinion on the profitability of each client.')
