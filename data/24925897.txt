These are two different things.<p>Just about any object can facilitate the act of killing. A car, a bowling ball, a swimming pool. Do these things cause murders too? That&#x27;s not how causation works.<p>A car can facilitate going to the store. Does the car <i>cause</i> you to go to the store? Of course not. You go to buy the thing you needed. The car is just a means.')
