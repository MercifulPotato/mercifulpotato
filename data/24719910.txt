I’ll add: If your company does feature freezes or general code freezes around holidays or corporate earnings, it can be harder to get “run the business” changes approved during these periods in a monorepo. So even in monorepo companies it’s common to have a separate “infra” repo for network and cloud configuration, sometimes for auth too. Makes the politics easier.')
