Item(by='wenc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605633692, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25126213, text='In your example, the primary difference for me wasn&#x27;t so much active-vs-passive, but attribution. I&#x27;ve seen the passive voice used to great effect for softening statements and avoiding the assignment of blame&#x2F;other undesirable attribute.<p>Instead of &quot;Chris and Jack disagreed during the discussion&quot;, one could say &quot;No agreement was reached during the discussion&quot;. One may say this is mealy-mouthed, but it seems to me that managing emotions is a supremely important part of being a good communicator and being a consensus-builder. Avoiding the use of language that triggers &quot;fight-or-flight&quot; responses is very useful in many situations.')
