Item(by='dabbledash', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608838931, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25530470, text='If you are a member of a species with 3 females to every 1 male, that means the average male of your species has 3 times as many offspring as the average female.<p>Under those circumstances, if you have a mutation that causes you to produce all male offspring, you will have many more grandchildren than average.<p>This pressure will always favor producing whatever sex is in short supply, pushing sex ratios to 50%.')
