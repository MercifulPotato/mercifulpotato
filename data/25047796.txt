Item(by='anonunivgrad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605025859, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25046157, text='If you have a psychologist examine people, they will find a “mental illness” in some huge percent. People don’t understand what “mental illness” means. In fact, the definition is a hot topic of debate among psychologists and philosophers alike. But it basically boils down to some sufficiently undesirable behavior or thought pattern that we can measure and theorize about. Most of us could frame our flaws and anxieties as “mental illnesses” if we wanted to.')
