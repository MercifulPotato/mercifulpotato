There are books and courses about how the english sentence &quot;we might have to reconsider&quot; means &quot;no&quot;. How you would offer sweets twice, because of course the first answer will be &quot;no&quot; and the other party will want to be &quot;convinced&quot; to say &quot;Yes&quot; on the second round.
