Item(by='avian', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601892846, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24684872, text='The issue with cheap (&lt;1000 EUR) color laser printers seem to be that you will throw them out once you use up the supplied cartridges.<p>Either 1) you won&#x27;t be able get new cartridges because of model churn 2) new cartridges will cost more than a new printer or 3) even with new cartridges your printouts will be unusable because the transfer drum&#x2F;fuser&#x2F;paper feeder will be worn out at that point.<p>In my opinion, for that kind of money you&#x27;re better off buying a high-end inkjet if you need color printing.')
