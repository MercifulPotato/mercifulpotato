Item(by='corty', descendants=None, kids=[25594025, 25592373, 25592668], score=None, time=1609419028, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25591724, text='You can&#x27;t typically start the firewall before the eth link goes up, since rules that contain network interfaces might not work before those are created (e.g. in case of bridge or bonding interfaces). Systemd&#x27;s &quot;start everything at once, let the dependencies sort out automatically&quot; is a major regression for server systems. With sysv-init you could just pick the right ordering and be done, everything was stable for every boot after that.')
