That&#x27;s one of the primary <i>purposes</i> of governments, to ensure the quality and safety of what citizens consume and how it is delivered.<p>Otherwise, why are there regulators for advertising or for food or government standards for anything?<p>I&#x27;m not talking about censorship or somehow evaluating the quality of <i>content</i>, I&#x27;m talking about if a company is delivering a service and I am paying for it, then the conditions under which they deliver that service should be regulated to ensure a fair and competitive marketplace.<p>Apple shouldn&#x27;t be getting plaudits for making privacy a unique selling proposition.<p>All the other companies should be getting told that their business is unfair and exploitative. Privacy should be a right, the control of my personal information should be mine and consent to have it should be able to be withdrawn at any time.')
