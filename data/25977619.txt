Item(by='bitwize', descendants=None, kids=[25977760], score=None, time=1612087240, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25976909, text='It&#x27;s probably a better idea to throw BadWindow or BadDrawable when an untrusted client queries about windows or pixmaps it doesn&#x27;t own.<p>As for dropping events... the idea is to isolate clients, such that it&#x27;s as if X resources not owned by the client do not exist to the client. If the UX of the client depends on violations of that rule, then it&#x27;s either a program like a window manager that should go on a trusted whitelist, or it&#x27;s up to something nasty.<p>Note that Firejail does this by using Xpra as a proxy to the real X server.')
