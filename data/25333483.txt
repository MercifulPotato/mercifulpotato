Item(by='leonim', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607357460, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325329, text='Yes, vd does a nice job on JSON data. To open nested data you can use the expand-col (keyboard shortcut &quot;(&quot;) command, and the unfurl-col &quot;zM&quot; command.<p>You can also use the pyobj-cell &quot;z^Y&quot; command to explore single cell as a table.  A single cell can be a whole nested part of your JSON data.<p>Also, the transpose &quot;t&quot; command is useful when working with JSON that is an object with a lot of keys.')
