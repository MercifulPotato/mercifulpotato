Item(by='natch', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611558379, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25899610, text='Free chargers are scattered around in a lot of places.<p>We have three Teslas out of six parking spots in our apartment building with no charging on site and we do fine.<p>I rarely use superchargers because the free options are so good, and free. And most of my supercharger visits are around 5-10 minutes which is not bad.<p>To be fair this is in a big city with a lot of resources.<p>I’d be the first to agree that not every place is like this, but it’s worth doing a careful check of your area because there are sometimes better options than you think.')
