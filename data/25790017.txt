Item(by='praptak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610712914, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25789743, text='The principle is true and follows from the rule that no resource should be idle if there are requests that need it to be processed.<p>Memory, however is seldom the bottleneck. This is because most apps are not designed to queue requests if they don&#x27;t fit in the available memory - they just allocate until they fail.<p>So, unless your apps are designed to queue requests in order to fit them into memory, this principle is probably not the explanation for the high memory usage.')
