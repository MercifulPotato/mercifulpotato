But when you want information about a topic that you don&#x27;t know anything about and for which you have absolutely no will to invest time in they are  all you need to know.<p>Or take reddit. You want to learn about a topic. Go to its subreddit. Scientific topics excluded).<p>I could go on and on. It becomes hard to find a hidden gem on the internet becomes gems are not hidden anymore but exposed.')
