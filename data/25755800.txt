Item(by='three_seagrass', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610495546, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25753328, text='&gt;I think a lot of confusion comes from people who only acknowledge free-speech-as-legal-right encountering people who support free-speech-as-moral-principle.<p>I think we can agree that there is confusion but not on the type of confusion.<p>Even free-speech-as-moral-principle has limits, as defined by John Stuart Mill, who said that free speech can be curtailed when the expression causes harm.  People get confused about this because they think free speech means all speech is free of consequence.<p>Inciting violence, coordinating death threats against individuals, and other similar expression causes harm.')
