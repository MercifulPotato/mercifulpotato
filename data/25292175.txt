Item(by='Youden', descendants=None, kids=[25293722], score=None, time=1607018693, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25291585, text='They&#x27;re entirely different things.<p>A Workers&#x27; Council is a legally recognized body representing employees of a single employer. It has very limited rights and duties, like being informed in the case of mass layoffs.<p>A union is a body that represents workers in an entire industry. It can have whatever duties the employees feel like giving it.')
