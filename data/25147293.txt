Item(by='marmaduke', descendants=None, kids=[25147372], score=None, time=1605773488, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25143407, text='The problem is that it’s called a recommendation, as if your close friend, who knows you, told Amazon, hey I think this person might also want to buy XYZ.  Or a jazz connoisseur says you might like this album if you liked that one.<p>But it’s not, it’s a tractable similarity based solution to the question of, which of millions of ads to show, in order to increase CTR.<p>The biggest difference is motive: a good recommendation is made in good faith for the benefit of the recipient while ads are businesses trying to turn a profit.  Maybe that’s why this meme never dies.')
