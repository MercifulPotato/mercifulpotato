These requests are counted towards the limits in Docker&#x27;s new rules.<p>Systems which built on top of the GGCR library[0] are switching to using HEAD requests instead[1]. These don&#x27;t fetch the entire manifest, instead relying on just headers to detect that a change has occurred.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;google&#x2F;go-containerregistry" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;google&#x2F;go-containerregistry</a><p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;concourse&#x2F;concourse&#x2F;releases&#x2F;tag&#x2F;v6.7.0" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;concourse&#x2F;concourse&#x2F;releases&#x2F;tag&#x2F;v6.7.0</a>')
