Item(by='relaunched', descendants=None, kids=[25050667, 25050511], score=None, time=1605035339, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25048980, text='It&#x27;s hard to understand, when taken at face value. But, when you add a little context, VCs can do much worse. They can refuse to sell (through approval rights) and let company die on a the vine. They can force out existing leadership and bring in new leadership. They can force an acquisition. They can kill a company in a million different ways.<p>For a fund to realize that the company can live on, even if it&#x27;s not the 10-100x they were hoping for, shows class. Listen, it&#x27;s not curing cancer - but, it shows a level of maturity and understand that we should praise and not take for granted.<p>The way that fund economics work, they can write off a lot of small bets. However, the way that partnerships work, there are big egos at play. It&#x27;s a dirty game, but when people do the right thing, it&#x27;s worth praising.')
