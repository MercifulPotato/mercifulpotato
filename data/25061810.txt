No. After a divorce, total costs across both parties go up because resources can no longer be shared: one party needs new housing, possibly a new car, possibly needs to get their own health insurance rather than being on the other&#x27;s plan, etc. Not to mention both parties have to buy anew whatever goods (cookware, television, etc.) the other person got.<p>Even completely amicable divorce destroys a tremendous amount of wealth.')
