Item(by='iambvk', descendants=None, kids=[24786720], score=None, time=1602708623, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24775435, text='Here is what the paper says:<p>Phase 1.<p>(a) A proposer selects a proposal number n and sends a prepare request with number n to a majority of acceptors.<p>(b) If an acceptor receives a prepare request with number n greater than that of any prepare request to which it has already responded, then it responds to the request with a promise not to accept any more proposals numbered less than n and with the highest-numbered proposal (if any) that it has accepted.<p>Important part of the statement is<p><pre><code>  ...with a promise not to accept any more proposals numbered less than n...\n</code></pre>\nSo, Phase-1 promise is not on the proposer-identity, but on the proposal-number.')
