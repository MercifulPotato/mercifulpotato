Item(by='burlesona', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602992268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24814054, text='I agree with you that it’s worthwhile to read what everyone has to say.<p>One problem is that Facebook and Twitter, not to mention the NYT and the rest of the media, are not that.<p>Social media places you in a filter bubble, and whatever you click on first shapes everything you’ll see forever, such that your entire worldview is shifted.<p>The media does essentially the same thing, tailoring it’s articles to match a particular target audience and then feeding them more and more of what they want to hear.<p>I don’t really know how we break out of this, but I think some level of decentralization - breaking up the social media networks so none of them have more reach than say, CNN, would be a good start.')
