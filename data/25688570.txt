Item(by='ekidd', descendants=None, kids=[25689236, 25688654], score=None, time=1610132399, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25688178, text='I&#x27;ve been on the Internet since 1994, and it has <i>always</i> been the case that if you made a sufficiently odious pest of yourself over a long enough period of time, that someone would contact your university, employer or ISP, and you would lose your account, or at least your access to Usenet.<p>You were always perfectly free to go find a new account elsewhere if anyone would have you. But there was always a point where if you passed far enough beyond socially acceptable behavior, your Internet provider might choose to stop doing business with you.<p>This was especially true in the case of harassment, attacks on network infrastructure, and criminal behavior.')
