Item(by='acabal', descendants=None, kids=[25402554], score=None, time=1607798835, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25400409, text='That&#x27;s what I do on the command line (though I went so far as to just alias `grep` to `grep -E`), but when writing scripts I prefer to use expanded flags for clarity. When going back to a script one wrote a year ago, it&#x27;s way easier to grok expanded flags instead of a bunch of single letters that have to be re-deciphered. (Especially with something like `grep` or `sed` that have 1,000 flags each.)')
