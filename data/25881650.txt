In small doses it’s good, but too much of a good thing becomes a bad thing pretty quickly.<p>Actually, I think this is a wrong rule of thumb. In my experience, there is more of a &quot;barbell dynamics&quot; (Taleb):<p>I can take a lot of stress at once, but then I need some time to recover. I find this perfectly healthy and it grows me as a human being. On the other hand, when I&#x27;m under constant stress with no relief in sight, that&#x27;s the real killer.')
