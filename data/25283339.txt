Item(by='thaumasiotes', descendants=None, kids=[25284811], score=None, time=1606952405, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25283173, text='There are two very different questions:<p>1. How often does this happen? This is a question about expected value, and the answer could be anything zero or above.<p>2. What are the chances that this will happen within a year? This is a question about probability, and the answer must lie between zero and one. There is no such thing as &quot;a 775% chance it would happen yearly&quot;.')
