Item(by='falcolas', descendants=None, kids=[24964065, 24964830], score=None, time=1604274166, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24963454, text='One of my favorite fountain pens is the Pilot Varsity - it’s a disposable fountain pen. Lots of ink combined with one of the best nibs out there, no matter the price. There’s an entire set of users out there dedicated to figuring out ways to refill these so they can re-use the nib. I haven’t had a single one leak on me, either.<p>They go for around $2 each.')
