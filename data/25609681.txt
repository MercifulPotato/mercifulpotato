LEB128 is a representation of arbitrary-size integers: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;LEB128" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;LEB128</a><p>As I understand it, this library is optimized to avoid branching (which incur an overhead) and take advantage of SIMD instructions (which process data in parallel).')
