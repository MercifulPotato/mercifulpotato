You might still have an appreciable amount of stuff hosted on AWS, Azure, GCP or similar.<p>Depending on your scale might even be able to peer with them directly if you&#x27;re big enough.<p>Or, if you are big enough to justify it you can order direct 10Gbps cross connects within a number of facilities to AWS.')
