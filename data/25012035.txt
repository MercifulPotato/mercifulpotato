Item(by='btwillard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604704520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25011773, text='I&#x27;m only one person involved, but my primary reason for choosing Theano over TensorFlow has to do with the ability to manipulate and reason symbolically about models&#x2F;graphs.<p>In order to improve the performance and usability of PyMC, I believe we need to automate things at the graph level, and Theano is by far the most suitable for this--between the two, at least.<p>You can find some work along these lines in the Symbolic PyMC project (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;pymc-devs&#x2F;symbolic-pymc);" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;pymc-devs&#x2F;symbolic-pymc);</a> it contains symbolic work done in both Theano and TensorFlow.')
