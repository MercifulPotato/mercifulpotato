Item(by='lopmotr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602887032, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24801378, text='While I agree in principle, I don&#x27;t think many humans are capable of what you want. Without a desperate need to work, most people don&#x27;t seem to bother doing much even it that would give them great satisfaction. Just look at almost every unemployed person who has a place to live and food to eat but nothing to do all day. Why aren&#x27;t they spending their plentiful time on high quality of life activities?<p>This might be hard to imagine if you&#x27;re well educated, already working, and in your 20&#x27;s and the world seems full of excitement and opportunity but you&#x27;re held back by the annoying need to pay your bills. If you didn&#x27;t have to work, you might start a business or spend all day on your side project or whatever. But most people aren&#x27;t in that position. Often, their only sense of fulfillment comes from their work no matter how unpleasant it is, and they only work because they have to to survive.')
