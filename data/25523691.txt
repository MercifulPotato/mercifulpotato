Item(by='UweSchmidt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608767719, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25523115, text='I think we tend to underestimate the effect of environment and organization on the individual.<p>A city (or office...) can be &#x27;democratic&#x27; if it has a variety of places to meet with different degrees of formality or organization, be it in a park, a public place or a side street.<p>Or it could be &#x27;authoritarian&#x27; if it&#x27;s centered around institutions, makes the public easy to surveil and discourages deviation from the task at hand.<p>It&#x27;s easy to see how through a systematic understanding of these factors (or by instinct) leadership would want to build a city (or design an office) a certain way and it&#x27;s worth paying close attention to what&#x27;s going on in this regard.')
