Item(by='whatatita', descendants=None, kids=[25449380], score=None, time=1608155912, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25449294, text='Another solution that comes to mind is to remove the ability to resell newly released items within a period after their initial release.<p>The first few weeks after initially going on sale is the time where an item is still highly coveted and the manufacturer doesn&#x27;t have the capacity to create more. In that same window, bulk-purchasers cannot often afford to sit on the item for weeks on end.<p>If they made it illegal to - for example - resell an item fro more than 10% higher than the retail price within the first 3 months, most price gougers wouldn&#x27;t be able to afford it.')
