Item(by='barrkel', descendants=None, kids=[25800533], score=None, time=1610769155, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25799534, text='Recursive CTEs are an excellent way to write a very slow query.<p>You save the cost of network round trips, parsing the query, optimizing the join order etc., but your recursive CTE is otherwise executed once per iteration. For trees, that&#x27;s typically the depth; for linked lists, it&#x27;ll be the length of the list. In practice they both suck, and will be trivially outperformed by almost any other technique.')
