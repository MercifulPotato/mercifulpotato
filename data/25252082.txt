Item(by='fisf', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606727925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25251942, text='Predicting the economic future 20-30 years down the line is very ambitious, even more so because markets are changing faster then ever.<p>I don&#x27;t think that trying to dig a place over this timescale, given this uncertainty, should be a justification to sacrificing a big chunk of your life -- very few companies can expect to survive so long.<p>So, basically, if you are not seriously rich by that time, the additional effort might not make a difference.<p>There are reasons to invest one&#x27;s life into a business (loving what you do, belief in doing the right thing, adding to the world, etc.), but market forces should not be on this list.')
