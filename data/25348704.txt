Item(by='reader_mode', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607449489, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25347797, text='It&#x27;s funny, sometimes better reproduction quality ruins the experience - for example I rewatched some sci-fi movies on my 4K TV in full resolution and the extra detail just brings attention to flaws in effects, it shifts the experience from people in a magical place to actors in front of a green screen.<p>But everything is objectively better and the experience is better in 95% of the cases.<p>Likewise for music. When I listen to some music on my studio headphones I get precise sound and more detail, but in the end some music sounds worse than on cheap but loud bass heavy headphones.')
