Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609410806, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25578074, text='Except that <i>really</i> doesn’t pan out well here: it’s not just one or two things that need patching, it’s a <i>lot</i>, and the patching will slow some extremely common operations down quite a bit (sometimes immensely), and it’ll still be incomplete because there are syntax elements to it (e.g. for..of) that can’t be polyfilled.<p>Thus, for as long as you want to support a certain vintage of browser, you’re very commonly better to just write things in the old way—which here means using Map.prototype.forEach instead of Map.prototype.{entries, keys, values} and&#x2F;or for..of loops.')
