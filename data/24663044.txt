This isn&#x27;t a case of users knowingly using a tool to scrape data for their own use, the users installing the extension are, at most, mules, for the extension makers.<p>Alternately, maybe copyright infringement. Some of the data are considered facts, that are not copyrightable (even in aggregate, in the U.S.) but at minimum, the actual content of ads scraped is copyrightable. There might be a question of whether Facebook has standing to sue over infringement of ads for other businesses but their ToS may cover some non-exclusive copyright to shared')
