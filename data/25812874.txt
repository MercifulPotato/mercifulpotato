Item(by='dhruvkar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610905568, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25809852, text='It depends based on the type of business you&#x27;re in.<p>I help run a physical goods business.\nMargins on these types of businesses (for the most part) 10-15%.<p>If we find a way to reduce expenses by 0.5% (of revenue) by spending 10 hours, it&#x27;s worth it. On the face of it, it seem trivial and not worth it. But at the end of the year, these cost saving tactics add up and contribute to profitability. Sometimes it&#x27;s the difference between giving our team a bonus or not.<p>Whereas in a SaaS business, the margins are far higher and your time is better spent (probably) on increasing revenue, rather than shaving expenses.<p>As someone already said, there&#x27;s only so many expenses you can cut.')
