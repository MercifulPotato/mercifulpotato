With hidden scrollbars or, as I believe you&#x27;re describing, simply not even looking at the scrollbar, there&#x27;s no indication of where you are in the content or how large the content is.  I would argue this is one of the chief grievances I have when consuming content on a mobile device (among an armada of other grievances with mobile consumption, at least on the web).<p>Those of us who like knowing contextual information such as document size at a glance are sad to see (proportional) scrollbars go.')
