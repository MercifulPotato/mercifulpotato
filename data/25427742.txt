Item(by='jfim', descendants=None, kids=[25429926, 25427973], score=None, time=1608015375, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25426281, text='&gt; Air travel is easily replaceable by low-carbon alternatives that exist today.<p>Intercontinental and cross continental travel are a bit harder to replace with low carbon alternatives.<p>Europe has a functional high speed rail system due to its high population density, but the distances between some city pairs in the are hard to replace with rail or other low carbon alternatives. Think of Los Angeles to NYC, or San Francisco to Paris.<p>Many of these flights could be replaced by video conferencing, but if one really has to travel that distance, rail is a hard sell. It&#x27;s not unpleasant to take Amtrak across the continent, but it&#x27;s not fast either.')
