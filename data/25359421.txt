Item(by='klmadfejno', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607527477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25355580, text='General logic - If there&#x27;s 4 points on a blob, you want to break it into 2|2, not 1|3, because each move can only split a group of 3 into 1|2, but can often split 2 groups of 2|2 if they line up. The exception to this is if you can draw a line between two points that crosses a gap, then you can break a group of 3 in one move. At higher levels, you might need to create these gaps yourself with your extra moves. Idk, didn&#x27;t play much beyond level 20ish')
