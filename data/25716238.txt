As far as tooling goes I&#x27;d take a look at the &quot;usb-tools&quot; project [2] on Github. The GreatFET One hardware [3] is good and works with the Facedancer software hosted by the usb-tools project.<p>I enjoyed working on USBQ and learned a lot by doing it but the kernel loadable module it depends on wasn&#x27;t written by me and probably needs some love. The usb-tools project and the related hardware has advanced quickly. Were I to do the same project over again (hacking Zwift for fun) I&#x27;d probably start there.<p>There is so much to USB... even some surprising things!
