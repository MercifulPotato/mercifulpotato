Item(by='octoberfranklin', descendants=None, kids=[25150390, 25149757], score=None, time=1605795145, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25149461, text='Never send DNS queries into a VPN.  They are all logged along with the originating client IP.<p>If your VPN provider claims they &quot;don&#x27;t log&quot; it just means they&#x27;re tagging the query with your IP as it leaves their network and letting their upstream ISP do the logging.<p>Never, ever, send UDP&#x2F;53 traffic into a VPN.')
