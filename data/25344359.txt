But not every single time somebody wants to ship a container. They work it out once and that&#x27;s it. Even if the lawyer costs 100 million dollars, if they ship millions of containers every year, they can scale the cost down to one dollar per container in the end.<p>The system to only ask for money in case of accidents doesn&#x27;t actually make it cheaper or less complicated. There simply is the inherent &quot;cost&quot; (a ka expected loss) of accidents, that you have to pay one way or another. There may also be people who don&#x27;t want to pay, and then you still need your lawyer.<p>My travel insurance is very cheap, by the way.<p>Insurance is an old and established business, many learnings already exists. The industry presumably is quite good at estimating and pricing risks.')
