It&#x27;s an extraordinarily difficult issue which would require a very precise implementation.<p>Once you start assessing peoples property in such a wholesale fashion, you almost end up in a classic &quot;managed economy&quot; situation.  &quot;From each according to his ability, to each according to his needs&quot; failed due to the combined difficulty of assessment + corruption of the assessors.  LVT is the same principle applied to land.<p>I think that LVT will end up as one of those ideas that is great in theory but doesn&#x27;t work in practice.')
