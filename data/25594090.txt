Item(by='j-pb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609432068, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25593165, text='The last time ULID was mentioned in a thread it got some flack for its &quot;weird&quot; generator local monotonicity property.<p>But it&#x27;s actually pretty useful, when you&#x27;re building database indices, because it reduces the amount of entropy that your index has, and is therefore a big help for succinct data structures, which occupy space proportional to the information theoretic lower bound up to a log factor.\nThis can make the difference between a multi mb and multi gb index.<p>Obscure feature, big gain.')
