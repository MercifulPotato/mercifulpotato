Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=[24998764], score=None, time=1604587411, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24998606, text='&gt; Car air-conditioners are not powered by heat from the combustion engine. They are powered mechanically by a belt (&quot;serpentine belt&quot;) connected to the engine.<p>But that belt is powered by the expansion of gas that takes place inside of a piston, due largely to the increase in heat from combusting gasoline. Ultimately, an ICE engine is a heat-engine (like a steam engine or sterling engine, but different).')
