Item(by='tsimionescu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608237249, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25459045, text='Git internal delta compression has nothing to do with why Git commits are most usefully thought of as diffs. All git operations start by computing diffs between commits, and then trying to apply those diffs before conceptually taking a snapshot. It is quite likely that the textual diff used in the operation and the internal diff produced by the delta compression are entirely different.')
