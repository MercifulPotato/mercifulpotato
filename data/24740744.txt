Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602353731, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24740324, text='No.  It&#x27;s not &quot;at least they get a tax write off&quot;, because that  implies a benefit, such as when paying profits to a charity you want to support anyway.<p>With these returned items, the seller just loses money and has a lower income than if they could resell them.<p>Their tax goes down because of returns, but that&#x27;s just normal income tax because their income goes down.<p>If they didn&#x27;t donate the goods, just threw them away, they would pay exactly the <i>same</i> tax.  So there is no tax benefit gained from donating.')
