Item(by='dmurray', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604275099, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24963554, text='Cardio mainly exercises your lungs and heart, which is built differently from most of your muscles (it&#x27;s designed for constant use). Also if you eat too much, it&#x27;s good for burning up excess calories.<p>It has mixed benefits for the rest of your body. You&#x27;ll improve your muscle tone a bit through running or cycling or swimming, but repetitive strain injuries are also a risk.<p>For most people, the benefits of cardio far outweigh the downsides, but on balance it&#x27;s not necessarily good for your joints and ligaments.')
