Item(by='feanaro', descendants=None, kids=[25692619], score=None, time=1610148729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25690530, text='&gt; Just protect your private key.<p>This is what PFS is all about: it is about noticing that keeping your private key secret is really hard and admitting you&#x27;re probably not going to be able to do it.<p>But even so, when it leaks, the attacker won&#x27;t be able to use it to unlock the ciphertexts of all your previous conversations that he meticulously recorded.
