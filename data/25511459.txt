Item(by='kube-system', descendants=None, kids=[25511831], score=None, time=1608672518, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25511325, text='A job that <i>supports</i> the core business is definitely not the same thing as a job <i>unique</i> to the product offering or business model.<p>e.g. food safety is something that is important to PepsiCo&#x27;s business, yet they are far from the only business that needs food safety expertise.  In contrast, they may also need potholes filled in the parking lot, but they may choose to hire outside help for this, because they do not do it enough to be good or efficient at it.  Paying a middleman is often cheaper than hiring labor directly.  Labor is the largest single cost for most businesses.')
