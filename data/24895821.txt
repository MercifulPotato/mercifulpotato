It&#x27;s a symbolic calculus app, GPL, cross platform, GUI and TUI based on notebooks, there&#x27;s also a Maxima kernel available for Jupyter.<p>Maxima includes a complete programming language with ALGOL-like syntax but Lisp-like semantics. It is written in Common Lisp and can be accessed programmatically and extended, as the underlying Lisp can be called from Maxima. It uses gnuplot for drawing.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Maxima_%28software%29" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Maxima_%28software%29</a>')
