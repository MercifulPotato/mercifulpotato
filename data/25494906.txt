Item(by='varjag', descendants=None, kids=[25495289], score=None, time=1608558738, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25494804, text='Dangling parens are the loose closing parens on separate lines. I.e. this:<p><pre><code>  (defun plus (x y)\n    (+ x y)\n  )\n</code></pre>\ninstead of this:<p><pre><code>  (defun plus (x y)\n    (+ x y))\n</code></pre>\nIt suggests the author tires to balance parens manually&#x2F;visually rather than using a proper editor mode. An understandable and common mistake of a newbie, but looks bad when you are writing a tutorial.')
