Item(by='sjwright', descendants=None, kids=[25099243], score=None, time=1605408277, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25097516, text='There are numerous excellent reasons why that will never happen. And why it’s not in Apple’s interest to do so.<p>The most obvious reason is that it would utterly destroy the Mac among influencer communities and developers.<p>But perhaps the most underrated reason is that Apple already has a managed computing platform in the iPad. Rather than the Mac becoming more locked down, I expect the iPad will become ever-more desktop-like and take over more and more market share from traditional computers.<p>I’d contend that an iMac-like desktop iPad is a more likely future product than a fully locked down Mac.')
