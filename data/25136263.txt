However, Arm macOS assumes an Armv8.3-A architecture baseline. Patching at runtime from Armv8.3-A to v8.2-A is quite feasible, to 8.0-A like an RPi4 is another matter.<p>Of course, this will apply to VMs only (for now at least). <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;alephsecurity.com&#x2F;2019&#x2F;06&#x2F;17&#x2F;xnu-qemu-arm64-1&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;alephsecurity.com&#x2F;2019&#x2F;06&#x2F;17&#x2F;xnu-qemu-arm64-1&#x2F;</a> was a good starting point for me.')
