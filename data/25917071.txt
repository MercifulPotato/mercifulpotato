Naturally I was a bit worried about performance and scaling if the not launched yet app becomes a major success.<p>I began simulating a heavy use scenario. 100k users creating 10 records daily for three years straight.<p>100000 x 10 x 365 x 3 ~= 1 billion rows or about 200 GB with a record&#x27;s length of 200 bytes. This is peanuts for modern databases and hardware.<p>Seems like a single node can support it for a long way before I have to worry about performance...')
