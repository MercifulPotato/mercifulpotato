Item(by='rsfairman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602428068, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24742518, text='A big problem with systems like KaTeX and MathJAX is that they are not compatible with LaTeX. They are superficially similar, but not the same. LaTeX has a huge number of packages that serious math people heavily rely on.<p>What&#x27;s needed is version of the underlying TeX engine that is HTML-aware. This way, a multipage document could be complied as usual, say to be printed or displayed as a static PDF, or it could be compiled to a web-friendly format for display in a browser, with links, etc.')
