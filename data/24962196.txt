The second argument can be optionally incremented and&#x2F;or complemented. A few interesting ones include:<p><pre><code>   y = cond ? 0 : -1;\n   y = cond ? x : -x;\n   x = cond ? 0 : x+1;  &#x2F;&#x2F;&lt; look ma, circular addressing!</code></pre>')
