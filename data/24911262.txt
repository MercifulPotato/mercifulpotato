Why spend your time studying obscure insects and animals when you could be doing something worth while? Why study pure math or chase strange theorems that often have no application to anything relevant what so ever?<p>Because these endeavors all lead to novel tools and learnings that are at times applicable in surprising ways to help solve &quot;whatever important problem.&quot;<p>For example, number theory, on its face, seems completely useless, and hundreds of years were &quot;wasted&quot; by scholars, and then we got cryptography.')
