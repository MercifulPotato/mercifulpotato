Item(by='jefftk', descendants=None, kids=[24985074], score=None, time=1604444283, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24984892, text='It&#x27;s not a system that works by minimizing latency, instead it works by making the latency absolutely predictable and adapting to it. While you don&#x27;t want to have your server in a completely random part of the world, you also don&#x27;t need to locate it for minimal ping.<p>The bucket brigade aspect of the setup is that each person sings a bit and then that bit of singing is passed on to the next person to sing on top of. Each person hears everyone ahead of them, and is heard by everyone after them.')
