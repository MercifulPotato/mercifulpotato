Item(by='spopejoy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605293282, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25084698, text='One thing the example shows is the classic problem with OO&#x27;s mechanism, namely binary functions. For a given class, something like `+` is impossible to implement elegantly, it has to bias one operand over the other -- after any syntactic sugar, it&#x27;s always `arg1.op(arg2)`. Module functors resemble true ADTs more, in that they do not dispatch from a live instance of the datatype.')
