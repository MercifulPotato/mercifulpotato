Item(by='paxys', descendants=None, kids=[25513211], score=None, time=1608682848, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25512975, text='The lockup period for an IPO is usually 6 months. That means that institutional investors have the opportunity to realize their gains any time post IPO, while employees have to count on the price to hold for that long before that can get any liquidity. Two or more earnings calls in the period add to the risk.<p>In a direct listing employees can sell on day 1, since there are no obligations to underwriters.')
