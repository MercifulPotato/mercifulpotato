I couldn&#x27;t stand it anymore and it became a source of boredom and stress for me.<p>About a decade ago, I discovered a passion for writing. I knew I wanted to stay in the corporate world. I didn&#x27;t do anything about it because I was salivating from the six digit salary.<p>Eventually, I gave up, and switched to a technical writing gig. I&#x27;ve been enjoying so much more than my previous career. I make way less money than before, but at least I&#x27;m enjoying my work.
