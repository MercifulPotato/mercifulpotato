Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=[25619927], score=None, time=1609652079, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25617810, text='Do you remember life before the Internet?<p>Because it reads as though maybe you don&#x27;t remember.<p>It&#x27;s not that big of a deal, just put it away.<p>You need your phone far less than you think you do, just put it away for some occasions, maybe don&#x27;t take to lunch (or to work).<p>Being &#x27;homebound&#x27; is a great excuse to get rid of it: other than SMS, everything else can be had via the web, and the notebook form factor is not nearly so mobile.<p>The &#x27;problem&#x27; is real but the intensity I think is overblown.<p>We haven&#x27;t had phones for the last 10 000 years of civil evolution and 50% of people alive today remember quite clearly a time when we didn&#x27;t have them and it was just fine.<p>Finally: &quot;The eventual goal is a rich life&quot; it never was that, that rich. Reading, practising music, walking, watching TV, listening to music - it&#x27;s not necessarily very &#x27;rich&#x27; it&#x27;s just different for the most part. The romanticized notion of what a &#x27;rich life&#x27; is might be a little bit of the problem.')
