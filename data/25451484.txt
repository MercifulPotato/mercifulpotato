A Honda Civic costs $25k new [1], an 8-seater Honda Odyssey costs $31k new [2]. A car lasts 10+ years so conservatively that&#x27;s $12k extra in transportation costs for your fourth child. Maybe you can argue it&#x27;s $20k with fuel.<p>That...doesn&#x27;t seem unreasonable?
