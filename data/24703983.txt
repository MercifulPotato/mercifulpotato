Item(by='guenthert', descendants=None, kids=[24706733], score=None, time=1602029683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24695421, text='Cooperative multithreading might have lower overhead, as it saves the trips from and to kernelspace, if handled by the application and by that can be assumed to be more efficient, which might be significant when there is a high rate of thread scheduling events.  I don&#x27;t see how it improves on latency, i.e. the delay between an external stimuli and the expected reaction to it.  The maximum latency for cooperative multi-threading is in the general case unbounded and hence unsuitable to low-latency applications, much less for real time application.')
