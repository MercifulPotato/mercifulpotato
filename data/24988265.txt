Feel how you may, that is an extraordinary claim and requires stronger evidence than &quot;I have a really professional website&quot; and &quot;it makes sense to me&quot;.<p>I personally think the idea that the M2 growth we&#x27;re seeing is OK is absurd and I&#x27;ve been very happy with my gold holdings. If it isn&#x27;t doing a great deal of damage, empires would have been using this tactic to great effect for centuries. But nobody has come up with a watertight case linking monetary recklessness to anything, so it really is still a personal judgement call.')
