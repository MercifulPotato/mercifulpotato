Item(by='cbanek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602945441, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24809530, text='I feel like FOMO is one other way to waste your career.  The article talks about how they missed out on Uber&#x27;s hyper-growth phase, but really, that&#x27;s 20&#x2F;20 vision after the fact.  What if Uber went down instead?  You never know the growth trajectory of any company really, even ones you work for.<p>Getting a job and then doing it (and moving, if that is required) takes a lot of time.  Every time you move, you might as well kiss away 3 months of time from your life, between moving, packing, new friends and routines, ramp up at work, etc.  Moving careers just because of a feeling isn&#x27;t something you can do twice a year just because.<p>This also totally misses out on doing things you want to further your career that aren&#x27;t in your job time, which I think is a lot more flexible and amenable to quick change to pursue growth and passion.  Things like hobbies, side projects, etc.')
