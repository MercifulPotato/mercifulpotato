Item(by='slg', descendants=None, kids=[25295710], score=None, time=1607031192, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25294123, text='I did feel a little corny using a comic book movie as an example because there are plenty of others movies that come to mind that I could point to and earn some film nerd cred.  However there is nothing that symbolizes what the loss of theaters would do to populist filmmaking better than that moment.  It was the zenith of a series of over 20 blockbuster movies that spanned a decade.  It was truly an unprecedented moment in the history of cinema.
