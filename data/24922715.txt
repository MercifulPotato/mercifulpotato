Item(by='andjd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603911409, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24906634, text='As a player, not a developer, the usability of TTS for boardgames is heavily dependent on how much effort the developer put into the mod.  Good ones have scripts and snap-points that minimize the difficulty of playing in a 3d sandbox environment.  More minimal mods can be very difficult to enjoy.  If you&#x27;re having issues, I would recommend starting with a well scripted game.')
