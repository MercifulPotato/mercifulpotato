Item(by='wait_a_minute', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603985809, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24931352, text='The investments we make into space exploration technology improve technology we use on earth too. It also employs a lot of people in good-paying jobs with benefits. And there is no version of a post-capitalist future that does not involve space travel - we will need more resources and more space as the species grows.<p>It&#x27;s also a fraction of a percentage of total spending to invest into space. Even if it was 1% of our total spending, it wouldn&#x27;t meaningfully move the needle on improving and protecting Earth. Earth will always need to be our home base, but there are virtually no negatives to moving onto the moon and then to Mars and beyond.')
