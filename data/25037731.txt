Item(by='jacinabox', descendants=None, kids=[25039866], score=None, time=1604945744, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25035994, text='Calling skeptics as &quot;denialists&quot; is a rejection of scientific discourse. The skeptic could reasonably be defined as someone who doubts a proposition and gives reasons for doubting it: if truth is given as a condition of skepticism, it preempts the frame of discussion in which truths are settled, and makes discourse into a game of who can define the other out of existence more effectively.')
