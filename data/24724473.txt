There is a ton of self-censorship and coercion by these activists asserting their supposed moral authority, which is not a good road to go down.<p>In an industry full of data scientists and engineers, it amazes me how people go along with this narrative that disparate outcomes among members of arbitrary group identities is evidence of discrimination.
