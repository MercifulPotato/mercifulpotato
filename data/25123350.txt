Item(by='thrwyoilarticle', descendants=None, kids=[25123752], score=None, time=1605618912, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25123189, text='Then you&#x27;d need centralised protections to decide who can delete and you instantly lose the distributed features.<p>If I&#x27;m a user and I want to save, I do it with my text editor. If I want to share, I can do it with Github, Gerrit, Gitlab, email, pigeon.<p>I never email people things I didn&#x27;t mean to and, if I did, I wouldn&#x27;t expect my email software to let me delete it. The centralised services allow it to some degree but even they don&#x27;t allow information to be un-disseminated.<p>VCS is necessary to deal with changes that overlap and conflict. It&#x27;s not for backup and it&#x27;s not a method of communication. If all you need to do is save and share, you don&#x27;t need VCS.')
