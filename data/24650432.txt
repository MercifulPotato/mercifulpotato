Item(by='yellowstuff', descendants=None, kids=[24650472], score=None, time=1601557928, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24650230, text='I don&#x27;t like the term &quot;programming language&quot; and your comment illustrates why.<p>We would expect that an aspiring architect needs to learn a little bit of notation, and a lot about the tradeoffs and constraints of making a building. We <i>should</i> expect that programmers learn a little bit of notation, and a lot about the tradeoffs and constraints of making software.<p>Knowing your tools well is important, but not sufficient. Focusing on &quot;programming languages&quot; makes people think that the important part is the notation. It is not.')
