The Steve Jobs biography actually latched onto this as a key narrative element—a way to construct Jobs&#x27;s personal arch—and I do believe it&#x27;s genuine based on everything else I&#x27;ve read about the guy.<p>And it&#x27;s notable that Jobs only really reached his zenith in these later years. My admiration of Jobs is really for the person he was in his last decade.
