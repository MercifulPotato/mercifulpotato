Item(by='simonw', descendants=None, kids=[24809894], score=None, time=1602690962, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24776761, text='Little-known Homebrew tip: Python applications are installed in their own Homebrew-managed virtual environment, which means you can run pip in them to upgrade to the latest version of packages even when the tap hasn&#x27;t been updated yet.<p>I just tried this and it worked - now &quot;vd&quot; gives my Visidata 2.0 on my system:<p><pre><code>    &#x2F;usr&#x2F;local&#x2F;Cellar&#x2F;visidata&#x2F;1.5.2&#x2F;libexec&#x2F;bin&#x2F;pip install -U visidata</code></pre>')
