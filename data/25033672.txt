This is why putting an apple and an avocado in a plastic bag will cause the avocado to ripen faster. It is also the origin of the saying &quot;one bad apple spoils the whole basket&quot;, because bad apples produce a lot of ethylene and causes the rest of the basket to quickly overripe.
