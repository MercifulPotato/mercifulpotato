Item(by='im3w1l', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606142203, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25185535, text='Well there are two parts to it really.<p>Take a number say 123. Write it as 1 * 10^2 + 2 * 10 + 3.<p>In the first step we multiply parts of this expression by 20. This doesn&#x27;t change the residue since 20 = 1 mod 19<p><pre><code>    1 * 10^2 + 2 * 10 + 3\n    1 * 10^2 + (2+3*2) * 10\n    1 * 10^2 + 8 * 10\n    (1+8*2) * 10^2\n    17 * 10^2\n</code></pre>\nNow heres the part where we <i>do</i> change the residue. We note that 10 and 19 are coprime. So 17 * 10^2 is divisible by 19 if and only if 17 is. So replacing 17 * 10^2 with 17 might change residue, but wont change divisibility.')
