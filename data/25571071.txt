Item(by='kgwxd', descendants=None, kids=[25571658, 25589486], score=None, time=1609257774, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25570308, text='Touch screens are the worst possible input for anything productive. Maybe if there was far less latency, you could make some decent music but, in my experience, anything beyond basic, unexpressive rhythm, chords, and melodies are the best you can achieve. Nuance can be added in post, but that&#x27;s tedious and sucks the life out the process.<p>I don&#x27;t draw, but I know doing it on a touch screen is far more painful than paper.<p>I think alternative input devices are a must, at which point you might as well just use a laptop. That might not be practical if you&#x27;re on-the-go e.g. riding the bus. If you&#x27;re not, then why use a phone?')
