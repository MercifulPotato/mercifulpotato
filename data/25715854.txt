Item(by='thinkingemote', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610293870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25713816, text='reading the comments and the links, I&#x27;d like to think about this a bit differently.<p>Instead of &quot;working hard is Good&quot; it appears that it&#x27;s more like &quot;Because I have worked hard and been successful it means that I probably have Gods favour compared to this beggar over there&quot;.<p>So working hard is the result from predestination (its already been decided who will get into heaven so we better do our best to look like we are blessed already and working hard and being successful looks like that ), and also a result from a kind of protestantism that advocates spending time intently working as a kind of devotion, and the kind of protestantism that pushes asceticism;  saving money, not spending much, not giving cash to the poor but instead donating time and work to charities..<p>To say Catholics don&#x27;t believe in hard work being good and only believe in belief isn&#x27;t correct therefore.  I suppose I would clarify by saying that they believe in doing good work but don&#x27;t view being rich and successful (the end results of working hard) as a way to get into God&#x27;s good books.')
