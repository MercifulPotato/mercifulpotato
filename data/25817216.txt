Item(by='thought_alarm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610933382, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817101, text='Yes, on the early models.<p>Flashing text is a feature of the video hardware, and on early Apple II models the cursor is just a space character with the Flash bit enabled.<p>You can turn on flashing text with the FLASH command. E.g.:<p><pre><code>    FLASH:?&quot;HELLO WORLD&quot;\n</code></pre>\nLater models will produce the flashing cursor in software rather than hardware, so you can&#x27;t inadvertently end up with multiple flashing cursors on screen.')
