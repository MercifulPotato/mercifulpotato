Item(by='tomhoward', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603846426, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24912746, text='One important negative effect of Vit D supplementation is that it reduces magnesium absorption (due to increasing calcium absorption, which is a magnesium antagonist).<p>It’s no big deal if you have sufficient magnesium, but for people who have low magnesium (which is common) it can make the problem worse.<p>This may explain the fatigue&#x2F;lethargy at least one commenter here reported after taking fairly high-dose Vit. D supplements.<p>IMO this doesn’t mean you shouldn’t take them, but they should be taken with caution and ideally with nutritional profile testing so magnesium or other deficiencies can be addressed.')
