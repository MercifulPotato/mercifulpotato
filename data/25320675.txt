Item(by='UncleMeat', descendants=None, kids=[25321043], score=None, time=1607224124, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25318603, text='Defining signed overflow requires sticking more instructions for every integer addition, since the platform is not guaranteed to <i>actually</i> overflow when you want it to so the program will have to detect this and force the overflow to happen. Major slowdowns.<p>Lots of loop stuff also depends on non-overflowing signed integers. If integers can shrink on addition then you cannot safely do a lot of unrolling or fancier stuff based on affine loop transformations.')
