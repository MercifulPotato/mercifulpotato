Item(by='FroshKiller', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607293941, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25327073, text='The way I use them, &quot;pixelated&quot; refers to images rendered to a target resolution in pixels. &quot;Pixelized&quot; refers to an image that is transformed to resemble pixel art of lower quality (in terms of resolution and color depth).<p>A screen from Super Mario Bros is pixelated. That&#x27;s what the art is supposed to look like.<p>A screen from Broken Age running in Retro Mode is pixelized. The art isn&#x27;t actually intended to look like that, and it&#x27;s just for nostalgic effect.')
