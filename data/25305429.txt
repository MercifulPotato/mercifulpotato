If you&#x27;re the only individual in your company who protects their private time, then you&#x27;ll feel pressure to fall in line. Even if the boss doesn&#x27;t give you any grief, your coworkers will: &quot;I respond to emails after hours, so why doesn&#x27;t he?&quot;<p>The bosses will likely favor those who work more than they should. And often working longer is seen more favorably than working better. This is mainly because it&#x27;s more visible.<p>I tend to think that salaried positions and unpaid overtime shouldn&#x27;t exist.
