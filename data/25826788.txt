Item(by='kylecordes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611004960, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25825244, text='Sometimes a request for an “estimate” is really a request for a promise, quotation, a guarantee that something will be delivered by X time or cost.<p>It&#x27;s easy to detect this:<p>Gently begin a discussion of how much uncertainty is tolerable, do they want to know the number we are 50% likely to hit? 80%?<p>If you get emotional pushback to discussing uncertainty, they are looking for a promise.')
