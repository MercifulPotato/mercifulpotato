Society exists because it is a structure that reinforces cohesion and cohesion means reducing animosity between groups. So within society there is a desire and the mechanisms to address imblances such as these. Where one group begins to dominate it can be in society&#x27;s best interest to level the playing field. Diversity is a good thing for systems that whish to stay away from local minima.')
