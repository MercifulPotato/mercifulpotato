Item(by='RobertoG', descendants=None, kids=[24773456], score=None, time=1602627097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770749, text='So you agree that creating reserves is not necessarily inflationary.<p>Also, a bank is not limited by reserves to lend. In fact, in practice, banks first lend and then search for the reserves in the inter-bank market. Those demand-offer dynamics between banks determine the interest rate. If the Central Bank doesn&#x27;t want to loss control of the interest rate, it has to increase reserves in the system when there is demand.<p>Central Banks have to choose, or they control the quantity or reserves or they control the interest rate. They target the later. The quantity is kind of irrelevant.')
