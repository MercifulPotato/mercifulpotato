Item(by='superasn', descendants=None, kids=[24750832], score=None, time=1602468461, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24749040, text='I think creating a side project just because all the cool kids are making money doing it can make you exasperated.<p>It looks like when the reason for doing it is not to solve your own or anybody else&#x27;s problem, your focus becomes more and more about technology, competition, process, etc.<p>Otoh when you&#x27;re alleviating the pain, your focus is always to get it done as quickly as possible no matter how many corners you need to cut.<p>So yes it&#x27;s time to take a step back and figure out what your goals are and if they align with whatever it is you&#x27;re doing with your time.')
