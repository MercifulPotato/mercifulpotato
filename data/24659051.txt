Item(by='triangleman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601614888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24658677, text='The Latin <i>idiota</i> is borrowed from Greek ἰδιώτης <i>idiōtēs</i> which is from ἴδιος <i>idios</i> meaning &quot;private&quot; or &quot;peculiar&quot;.<p><i>Idiot</i> has historically meant &quot;retarded&quot; so if you wanted to say &quot;ignorantly&quot; you could have just said that, or &quot;idiotically&quot; is fine but don&#x27;t pretend it has such wholesome roots.')
