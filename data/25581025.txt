Item(by='ptaipale', descendants=None, kids=[25581123, 25581392], score=None, time=1609330602, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25580931, text='Regarding the &quot;lack of information for those caring for the elderly&quot;, that sounds fairly implausible, because information about the epidemic has been abundant.<p>The proportion of old people among the dead is not much different in Sweden from other comparable countries; it seems to me that any strategy with &quot;protect the elderly and let the younger people take the hit&quot; is doomed to fail. The more infections there are in the general population, the more infections there will be among the old and weak. And of course even the young and fit will also have a proportion among them who get seriously ill.')
