So when someone uses it, perf isn&#x27;t scaling linearly.<p>With GraphQL, you can have a frontend person <i>add</i> comments without anyone from the backend team modifying anything. They say &quot;yay it works, GraphQL is amazing&quot;, and nobody realizes this is causing scaling problems because nobody on backend actually thought about it.')
