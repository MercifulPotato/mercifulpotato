Item(by='colinwilson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606011213, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25172789, text='Systems like Hadoop and Spark will run multiple versions of the same task writing out to a FS at once but to a temporary directory and when the first finishes the output data is just moved to the final place. It&#x27;s not uncommon for a job to &quot;complete&quot; writing data to S3 and just sit there and hang as the FS move commands run copying the data and deleting the old version. It is just assumed a rename&#x2F;move is a no-op in some systems.')
