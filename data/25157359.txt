You will start getting &quot;Access Denied&quot; type messages. If this happens, just disable it for the site.<p>There are very very sites which get triggered by Dark Reader, but Red Hat sites are one of them.<p>Sadly this extension has a slight negative impact on loading performance, but I don&#x27;t mind. It&#x27;s otherwise great and makes reading much easier.')
