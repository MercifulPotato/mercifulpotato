As a result, it seems to have better name recognition, especially outside of programmer circles, and it&#x27;s nice to be able to use a single legal text for software that also contains images, audio, or similar assets. (Slapping software licenses onto media files impedes their re-use outside the context of that particular software project.)<p>Is there an alternative you&#x27;d recommend?')
