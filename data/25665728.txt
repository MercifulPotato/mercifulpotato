Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=[25665778], score=None, time=1609981281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25665580, text='&gt; Freedom of speech would involve delivering all messages, not just the ones you like.<p>No, my freedom of speech and the press means I don’t relay speech or use my press to reproduce messages I don’t think deserve to be relayed.<p>That’s pretty well established to be the meaning of that freedom.<p>&gt; Requirement to deliver messages != direction of the media.<p>Yes, the state mandating that I use my resources (such as a printing press, or its digital analog) to reproduce and disseminate messages I disagree with relaying is direction of the media and (outside the usual strict scrutiny terms for restrictions on essential liberty) a violation of my First Amendment rights.')
