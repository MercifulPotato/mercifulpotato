Item(by='lorenzhs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602158850, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24717901, text='Solving the Knapsack problem is pretty easy, it&#x27;s probably <i>the</i> textbook example for explaining branch-and-bound algorithms. What you probably had in mind is that it&#x27;s NP-complete, meaning that we don&#x27;t know any polynomial-time algorithms and most experts think that none can be found, although nobody has been able to prove that (this is the famous P=NP? problem).')
