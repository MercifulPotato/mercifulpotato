Paying a commission doesn’t protect you from anyone maximizing profits.<p>All the discount brokers sell order flow.<p>&gt; Schwab earned 1.4% of revenue from payment for order flow, TD Ameritrade about 8.4%, and E<i>TRADE about 6.1%.<p>They all make half or a lot less of their revenue from commissions.<p>&gt; The contribution to revenue across the discount brokerages is minimal: 6.8% at Schwab, 28% at TD Ameritrade, and 17% at E</i>TRADE.<p>&gt; Interactive Brokers is the one standout, largely because it caters to high-volume professional and semi-professional traders; it makes 49% of revenues on commissions.<p>All RH did was run a tight ship on costs and cut commissions to zero.
