Item(by='eru', descendants=None, kids=[24978693], score=None, time=1604384318, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24976409, text='Primes are very common.  So as long as you have a few bits to fiddle with in your file, you can probably make it a prime very quickly.<p>Given an arbitrary stopping point x, you have approximately x &#x2F; log x primes between 1 and x.<p>In other words, for a file of length n, you have to try about O(n) slight variations to find a prime number.')
