Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=[25123261], score=None, time=1605611490, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25121558, text='Git is I think objectively more complicated any other popular VCS, simply due to the fact it has much more in the design model.<p>It&#x27;s also more powerful.<p>But the tradeoff is ugly within the context of the nature of the UI.<p>Git is like C++, it has a lot of features, the UI is not well thought out and it creates countless corner cases.<p>Git was not designed to focus on the core cases, making things simple.<p>Git is one of the great &#x27;litmus tests&#x27; for product design thinkers. People who don&#x27;t understand why git is problematic (even if they are really good at it) I think would have trouble with product design.')
