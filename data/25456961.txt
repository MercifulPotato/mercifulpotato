Item(by='ploika', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608222043, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25456544, text='Irish lost the overdot diacritic in the move away from traditional Irish type around the 1940s, but it will never lose the acute accent (síne fada) because the meaning of a word is completely different if you drop it.<p>Eg:<p>Féar - grass. Fear - man.\nCás - case. Cas - turn.\nLóndubh - (nonsense) black lunch. Londubh - blackbird.')
