Item(by='jasode', descendants=None, kids=[24970096, 24970182], score=None, time=1604329567, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24968264, text='<i>&gt;The fault lies in the assumed importance of the list.</i><p>The &quot;importance&quot; of the mailing list I&#x27;m trying to emphasize is the <i>Schelling Point</i>.  This is the <i>social discovery</i> aspect you&#x27;re not addressing.<p>Let&#x27;s dissect your following example:<p><i>&gt;When I send a kernel patch, I send it to the maintainer, then CC anyone I myself think should be involved, and finally CC the relevant lists (plural). The list acts as feed for lurkers, and as an archive.</i><p>The list <i>also</i> acts as a Schelling Point to <i>coordinate and consolidate public discussions</i>.<p>And consider the seemingly trivial action of <i>&quot;I send it to the maintainer&quot;</i>.  We have to unpack some hidden steps in that.   How does a new contributor --  or a new non-contributing non-programmer enduser who just wants to file a bug request -- get a list of maintainers&#x27; email addresses <i>ex nihilo</i>[1]?  A mailing list also has a <i>Schelling Point</i> aspect where people can <i>discover</i> email addresses of maintainers.<p>Right now, the yt-dl.org About[2] webpage is broken and therefore doesn&#x27;t show the maintainers.  I don&#x27;t know the email address of the current youtube-dl maintainer and Google search results[3] don&#x27;t quickly reveal it.<p>- &quot;github.com&#x2F;ytdl-org&#x2F;youtube-dl&quot; acted as a Schelling Point<p>- &quot;yt-dl.org&quot; also acted as Schelling Point<p>How would new people easily <i>discover</i> the maintainers&#x27; email addresses without any Schelling Points?<p><i>&gt;It&#x27;s a minor inconvenience at best, and mostly to those that were not part of the development flow </i><p>I disagree.  Today, if I notice a bug where youtube-dl is broken, I don&#x27;t have a canonical place to report the issue.  Somebody said the official maintainer moved the repo to Gitlab -- but when I search Google for &quot;youtube-dl gitlab&quot; and click the top link[4], the repo owner says <i>&quot;I&#x27;ll wait to see what happens with the GitHub repository and the current maintainer before I do anything with this clone.&quot;</i><p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Ex_nihilo" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Ex_nihilo</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yt-dl.org&#x2F;about.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;yt-dl.org&#x2F;about.html</a><p>[3] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.google.com&#x2F;search?q=youtube-dl+maintainer+email+address" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.google.com&#x2F;search?q=youtube-dl+maintainer+email+...</a><p>[4] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gitlab.com&#x2F;ytdl-org&#x2F;youtube-dl" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gitlab.com&#x2F;ytdl-org&#x2F;youtube-dl</a>')
