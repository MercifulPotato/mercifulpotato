Item(by='gt565k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604629902, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24999793, text='It&#x27;s time to hire product owners who understand the domain and use cases and are also UX experts.<p>Problem is, I&#x27;ve seen UX&#x2F;UI designers botch an entire UI and miss the point because they don&#x27;t understand the domain, and are just trying to apply principles without really understanding that what users want is not necessarily what they need.<p>Until a person becomes an expert user of their product, they really shouldn&#x27;t be designing it. That&#x27;s why the saying goes that when a founder is a user of their product or build the product to fit their needs, it&#x27;s usually the most successful.<p>You have to be the expert user and be trained or knowledgable in design and product development to really build a top of the line product that resonates with users (one of whom is yourself).')
