Item(by='ebiester', descendants=None, kids=[24841187, 24841132, 24841209], score=None, time=1603219977, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24840325, text='The fence is there because the risk of doing something new is hard, especially because testing hypotheses in hiring is expensive.<p>I am a hiring manager. I&#x27;ve also put significant thought into how we can change the hiring process, if only to get better candidates. However, it comes down to this: I only get 2-3 hires a quarter. Trying to get a meaningful result on changes I make is difficult. If one doesn&#x27;t work out, did it not work out in a way that my original process would fix?<p>And so, we get conservative because even though it rejects a lot of qualified candidates, it also gets good candidates.')
