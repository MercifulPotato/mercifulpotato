If someone is selling you a HIPAA certification they are scamming you.<p>HIPAA “compliant” simply means you’ve hired a 3rd party to audit your business. Essentially they ensure you do what you say you do in your policies and procedures.<p>There are certifications that can effectively illuminate the need for these types of questionnaires. HITRUST as an example in Healthcare and SOC 2 for broader SaaS providers.')
