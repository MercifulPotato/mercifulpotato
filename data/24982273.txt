Item(by='lisper', descendants=None, kids=[24983456, 24982597], score=None, time=1604427177, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24982060, text='The real lesson here is that &quot;number of parameters&quot; is not a useful measure of information content.  The only useful measure of information content is information entropy, which is the logarithm of the number of distinguishable states.  The base of the logarithm is arbitrary, but by modern convention is invariably taken to be 2.  The resulting unit is the bit.')
