Item(by='underseacables', descendants=None, kids=[25533842, 25534135], score=None, time=1608865179, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25533787, text='I reason that filling loneliness should not be the primary focus of a relationship. If you’re lonely, you’re making decisions because you’re lonely. However if you’re not lonely - meaning you’ve found happiness in being alone - then you would seem to be more free to choose partners based on other things than filling your loneliness. I guess in short, loneliness seems to be a really shortsighted reason to date someone.')
