Item(by='_ph_', descendants=None, kids=[25218902], score=None, time=1606386455, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25218497, text='Software developers are essential for a healthy eco system. Without them, there are no applications which utilize the device. Yes, there is a lot of software development for the iPad, but it is always limited because you have to write applications on a completely different system than you are targetting for. And it is limited by what applications you are allowed to write.<p>But even graver are the consequences for first time computer users. When I grew up, I learned computing with machines which would have at least a basic when delivered. I had the ability to do programming and understand what a &quot;computer&quot; is. Children, which today grow up with iPads are actively prevented of having that great learning experience. The Apple ad &quot;What is a computer?&quot; on the one side is brilliant marketing, on the other side is outright scary.')
