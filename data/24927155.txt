Item(by='Nextgrid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603944980, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24926924, text='Ember has somewhat solved that problem though.<p>You have the concept of data adapters which would be clients for your API (you can make a custom one if extending the existing ones isn&#x27;t an option) and the rest of the application just interacts with the equivalent of database models without ever having to worry about fetching the data.
