Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[25190385], score=None, time=1606156863, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25186708, text='you need to have someone who is very comfortable saying &quot;no&quot; to be in charge of maintaining the interface. otherwise, and especially if the plugin devs have access to the core code, they will say stuff like &quot;hey, I see you have a very convenient function in the core, can you expose it for my plugin?&quot;. once you open the door to this, your encapsulation suffers death by a thousand cuts. you can end up with a de facto monolithic codebase that also has a complicated plugin interface that doesn&#x27;t really encapsulate anything.')
