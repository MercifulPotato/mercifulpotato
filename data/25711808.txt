It just means that you write a statement in such a way that everyone agrees it is true.<p>&quot;the sky is green&quot; , is obviously not likely to be accepted by many people.<p>&#x27;In his science fiction story foobarbaz written in 2002, John Smith asked: What if people said &quot;The Sky Is Green&quot;?&#x27; \nIn this case, we can agree that the story is written, we can look it up in the library. (I made up the title in this case).<p>NPOV and consensus intersect here: Consensus is reached when no-one disagrees, and NPOV is defined as a point of view that no one could possibly disagree with.')
