Not just to deal with ADHD, but life in general.<p>For anyone interested in getting started, I can absolutely recommend Waking Up by Sam Harris. (The idea behind the app&#x27;s name is just what you do - starting the day with a mindfulness meditation.)<p>The App has a great 28 day introductionary course (easily digestible 10 minute sessions), among others, and is expanding its content right now.
