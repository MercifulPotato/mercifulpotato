Certbot will, for many common web servers, verify your servers&#x27; domain address and install a cert that will work for ~3 months. It&#x27;s free and mostly automated.<p>I&#x27;ve been getting certs for all of my side projects and it takes about ~10 minutes each. Highly recommend.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;certbot.eff.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;certbot.eff.org&#x2F;</a>')
