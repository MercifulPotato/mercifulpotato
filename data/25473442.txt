Item(by='lmilcin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608334428, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25459506, text='I am always wary of any extreme opinions.<p>DRY is yet another tool. Skilled hands will be able to judge when to use it and when to keep a little duplication to preserve simplicity and legibility.<p>I see frequently developers going to great lengths to avoid duplication of couple of lines of code. In the process, they create much more new code, use half a dozen patterns and make the code more difficult to understand. That&#x27;s not the ultimate goal.<p>The ultimate goal is always to keep the code produce more value and this <i>usually</i> is best served by simpler and more maintainable code.')
