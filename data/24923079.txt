Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603913268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24923024, text='&gt; As I understand if you repeal Section 230, and these companies become regulated as publishers, they will be required to censor ALL content.<p>If you just repeal Section 230, then if they don&#x27;t moderate at all, they will become (at least, based on the legal precedent which motivated 230) <i>distributors</i>, which generally have liability only when they have actual notice of unlawful content. If they actively engaged in preemptive moderation <i>at all</i>, then they will become publishers and fully liable, with no notice requirement, for all content.<p>So, self-initiated moderation becomes all-or-nothing.<p>Section 230 was passed to encourage the middle ground.')
