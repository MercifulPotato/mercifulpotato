Unlike Japan, Sweden keeps track, not of &quot;race&quot; but country of origin for migrants or children of migrants.<p>For Sweden, 36.5% of men aged 15-44 are either foreign born, or has at least one parent foreign born. This data is from the Statistics Sweden (SCB) for 2019.<p>In this cohort, 62.5% are from, or have a parent from, Africa&#x2F;Asia (Middle East), 9.3% from Western Europe&#x2F;USA, 3.5% from the other Nordic countries, an 24.7% are from &quot;Other&quot; countries.<p>I&#x27;m not sure about the data for the other Scandinavian countries.')
