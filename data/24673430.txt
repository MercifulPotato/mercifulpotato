Item(by='jameshart', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601745908, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24672983, text='No, it really is that collecting the data is bad! GDPR is not about making breaches less harmful (though that is a benefit) - it is about preventing businesses from secretly gathering data about you, without your knowledge, and using it in ways that harm you.<p>One of the ways in which a business can harm you through abusing the data they have about you is certainly allowing it to leak out - but that is very much not the only intention of data protection law.')
