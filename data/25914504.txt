There are 3 levels: frequency control (for example dams), primary reverse (fast acting power, in less than 10s), and secondary reserve (slow acting: &lt; 15min).<p>Participants can either be positive (they consume more energy, for example PHES systems), or negative (they inject or consume less energy).<p>All these ancillary services are paid (annual auction + per case). Nowadays, it&#x27;s getting bigger with VPP: virtual power plants, which aggregate small loads (i.e. small ~1MW generators) in order to propose a bigger load to TSOs.<p>It&#x27;s all automatic<p>If you&#x27;re interested: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.swissgrid.ch&#x2F;en&#x2F;home&#x2F;customers&#x2F;ancillary-services.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.swissgrid.ch&#x2F;en&#x2F;home&#x2F;customers&#x2F;ancillary-service...</a>')
