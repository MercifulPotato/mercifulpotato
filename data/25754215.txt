Item(by='adamnew123456', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610487676, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25749441, text='Maybe your bank is better about this and offers OFX, but ultimately I gave up on this approach due to my bank adding more aggressive anti-scraping measures over time. The credit card sites were even worse about this.<p>Now I have a Postfix server setup which accepts the daily transaction summaries they send and makes CSVs out of those. There&#x27;s still some HTML munging involved but I&#x27;ve found it much easier to let the data come to me than to go out and try to fetch it myself. In my experience financial institutions simply don&#x27;t have a lot of reliable automation options accessible to the normal consumer.')
