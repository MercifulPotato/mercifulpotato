Item(by='secalex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602727697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24783713, text='The default mode is not E2EE. The same key is used by all participants and is distributed by Zoom&#x27;s servers. In this mode, all features are available, including the ones that require a cloud service (like 1-800 number support).<p>If a meeting is set to E2EE, then a bunch of features are turned off and the keys come from the host* and are sent to the other attendees enveloped with their public keys. Zoom&#x27;s infrastructure never sees the keys, only the encrypted content packets that are relayed to all the participants.')
