Item(by='rbecker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601651864, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24663193, text='&gt; I keep seeing people who argue that TVs would be notably &quot;more expensive&quot; if they didn&#x27;t come with ads.<p>Even if that were true, it ignores a very important question - where does that ad money come from?<p>Companies buy ads (to get you to spend money on something you don&#x27;t need, or due to an arms race with their competitors, fighting over a limited market) with money the get from consumers, that pay more for their products.<p>All ads &quot;contribute&quot; to society is change in consumer behavior, that is ultimately paid by those same consumers. Even ignoring that behavior change, consumers are, on the whole, financially hurt, not helped, by ads.')
