Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610176930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25698128, text='Section 230 protects websites from liability arising from content that a user created. It also protects users from liability arising from content another user created that they shared.<p>It protects sites from liability from moderating or not moderating content.<p>Some liabilities are not exempted. Federal criminal law, intellectual property, privacy, and sex trafficing law are not affected. Its effect on state law is complex (as described in the article).')
