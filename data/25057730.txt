Item(by='u801e', descendants=None, kids=[25058135], score=None, time=1605097114, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25057372, text='It seems that the boundaries of what a unit test should cover have never been clearly defined.  In my opinion, unit testing should only cover logic that does not have side effects (like writing to disk or communicating over a network).  If you want to test external side effects, you should write functional or integration tests for them.')
