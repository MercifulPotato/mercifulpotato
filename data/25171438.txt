And therein lies the answer.<p>SE Asian countries have a pattern of widely adopting technologies before others do. Despite not having any obvious technological edge. They just seem to catch on easier. It would seem to me people are simply more willing or more able to deal with them.<p>Patterns that may be a huge usability issue in one place might not be elsewhere.<p>Wechat in China is a good present-day example. It&#x27;s not impressive in its tech or design. Would probably be considered a mess in most places. What&#x27;s impressive is the adoption.')
