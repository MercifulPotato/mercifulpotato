Item(by='caylus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612029554, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25971137, text='You can deal with that issue by defining things in a submodule, then importing and re-exporting them in the top-level (e.g. `type Foo = subdir.Foo`). That even lets you move things into the submodule without breaking callers. Other submodules can then import from the submodule rather than the top-level.')
