Item(by='vbuterin', descendants=None, kids=[25987916, 25987514], score=None, time=1612156920, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25985015, text='When I last looked at Ouroboros it still had the property that there&#x27;s only &quot;one confirmation per slot&quot;, so you need to wait ~log(n) slots before an attacker has a &lt;1&#x2F;n probability of reversing the chain. I personally think that a good PoS system should strive to have hundreds of confirmations per slot the way eth2&#x27;s LMD GHOST does. That said, it&#x27;s very possible that there&#x27;s an improved version of Ouroboros that already does this and I just haven&#x27;t caught up to it yet.')
