Item(by='mariodiana', descendants=None, kids=[25250575], score=None, time=1606497863, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25229799, text='The difference between true genius and your regular old smart person is that it takes about all the brainpower the smart person has to understand something that&#x27;s pretty complicated, and their thinking then becomes locked in. The effort not only cements their conceptual understanding; it cements their emotional attachment to their understanding. By contrast, the genius has the brainpower to understand a thing, understand alternative explanations just as well, and then innovate beyond all that.')
