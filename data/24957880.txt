Item(by='yourqiwi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604217204, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24957735, text='I collect interesting facts and tidbits I find in books, podcasts, and YouTube, and turn them into flashcards. It helps me remember various insights that I would otherwise forget. For example, I made cards for different Russell conjugates[0], definitions of certain words (eg culture, knowledge, optics, reify, ancillary, falsifiable), and quotes from books.<p>You can program yourself with flashcards[1]. EG I have a card that says &quot;What should you do when someone is explaining something and you don&#x27;t understand?&quot; (Ask questions until you understand).
