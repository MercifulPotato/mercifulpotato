Item(by='grayclhn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606866193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25267847, text='I&#x27;ve thought about this a lot, and the easiest rule of thumb is, &quot;buy something consumable.&quot; Like chocolate, notebooks, wine, etc., that&#x27;s one or two steps nicer than they&#x27;d buy for themselves. (Or than you&#x27;d by for yourself.)<p>They&#x27;ll probably like it. It doesn&#x27;t take up space. And, worst case scenario, it&#x27;s trivially easy for them to share or regift if they don&#x27;t like it.<p>Also, statements like, &quot;Good Gift Giving Is Incredibly Important,&quot; are unhelpful and untrue. People already feel too much anxiety about gifts. The worst case scenario is that they don&#x27;t like it. As long as your present isn&#x27;t offensive, nbd.')
