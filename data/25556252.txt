Item(by='cmyr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609123808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25556208, text='This is correct, the hyperbezier positions auto points to maintain curvature, so anything that impacts curvature of a segment can cause adjustment of the handles of neighbouring segments. In addition, auto points do not need to be smooth; you can have a corner with two auto points, which will be adjusted automatically in response to changes in segments to their &#x27;side&#x27; of the corner (see the heart demo on the page for an example of this.)')
