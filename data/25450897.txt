Item(by='userbinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608166122, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25450451, text='I agree with the general sentiment about size, but page size isn&#x27;t everything; it&#x27;s really more what that size is contributing towards. If a multi-megabyte page consists of many high-resolution images, that clearly gives far more value to the reader than if the same amount of data was spent on tons of JS of which next to none actually gets executed. Likewise, the <i>huge</i> amount of video data on a YouTube page is of definite value to the viewer (perhaps excepting the ads).')
