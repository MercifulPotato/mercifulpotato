Item(by='sbarre', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604321007, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24967273, text='The problem with the DMCA is that the anti-circumvention clauses are vague at best, and unfortunately it&#x27;s easy for well-funded corporations to make claims against it.<p>There is nothing in the law that talks about the effectiveness of the anti-circumvention tech, so even if the tech is trivial to bypass, if that bypass is done via &quot;unintended usage&quot; (according to the rights holder, not you) then they can claim you are doing something illegal.<p>It&#x27;s a shitty law.')
