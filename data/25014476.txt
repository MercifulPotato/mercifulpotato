Item(by='nestorD', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604748302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25014415, text='JAX does JIT compilation which is not done by Cupy so you get better performance by fusing operations and compiling them cleverly ([0] is a good benchmark to get a feeling for the performance of the various options).<p>However, it is a trade-off, the jitting time will increase your run time (you can expect at least 10 seconds of compiling time) and JAX does not let you do in-place operations (however, it has workarounds).<p>If you can afford the time needed to jit your code then I highly recommend JAX.<p>[0]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;dionhaefner&#x2F;pyhpc-benchmarks" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;dionhaefner&#x2F;pyhpc-benchmarks</a>')
