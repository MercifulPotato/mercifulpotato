Item(by='vecinu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606503246, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25231376, text='I may be able to share my experience here since I went through multiple TN1s.<p>You&#x27;ll satisfy the citizenship requirements but will have difficulty proving significant ties to Canada if you don&#x27;t have a bank account, car, home or family still in Canada.<p>Typically speaking you&#x27;d have some of these ties if you were a resident of Canada but it doesn&#x27;t really follow as a hard requirement.<p>As long as you satisfy the border officer that you have the intent to return home to Canada after finishing your contract, you will most likely receive a TN1. It becomes easier if you&#x27;re a current resident.')
