Quoting from (1):<p><pre><code>    quicksort1 :: (Ord a) =&gt; [a] -&gt; [a]\n    quicksort1 [] = []\n    quicksort1 (x:xs) =\n      let smallerSorted = quicksort1 [a | a &lt;- xs, a &lt;= x]\n          biggerSorted = quicksort1 [a | a &lt;- xs, a &gt; x]\n      in  smallerSorted ++ [x] ++ biggerSorted\n</code></pre>\n1: <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;learnyouahaskell.com&#x2F;recursion" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;learnyouahaskell.com&#x2F;recursion</a>')
