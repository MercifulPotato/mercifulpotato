Item(by='ufo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611240020, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25857845, text='My 2 cents:<p>Algebraic data types (tagged unions) are very useful for representing syntax trees and other things of the sort.<p>I&#x27;m not sold on the idea of going 100% purely functional (a-la haskell) though. Even a compiler written in a nonfunctional language is already going to be architected as a series of passes. The thing that makes it hard is not the presence of mutable global state. It&#x27;s more the actual algorithms and optimizations.')
