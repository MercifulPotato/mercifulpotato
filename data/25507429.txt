Item(by='tgb', descendants=None, kids=[25509050], score=None, time=1608652191, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25504669, text='I think these concepts are significantly different - as different as salts and peppers at least. Peppering helps protect against database access revealing password. Horcrux protects against password manager access. Peppering is stored on the server, but outside the database. Horcruxes are stored in the user&#x27;s head. You could do both, one, or neither. Client-side peppering would be having part of your password outside of the password manager but still on your computer. If anything it&#x27;s brain-side peppering.')
