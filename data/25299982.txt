Ideally, your employer or client will respect you as a human being who has something valuable to bring to the table.<p>When I find &quot;good jobs&quot;, they almost always crop up from serendipity in areas that I am genuinely interested in at the time. Looking back it feels like dumb luck, but the sort of dumb luck that is helped along by trying to make your own opportunities.<p>If you ever have a chance to talk candidly with an executive or entrepreneur about a subject that you are passionate about...well, at least take the meeting.
