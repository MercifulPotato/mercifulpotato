Item(by='chillacy', descendants=None, kids=[25639606], score=None, time=1609801125, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25634858, text='Etherium seems quite promising. They recently moved to Proof of Stake, which gets around many issues with Bitcoin like energy consumption, miner consolidation, and 51% attacks (it would by definition be more expensive to pull off than you could possibly gain by faking a transaction). Add to that the possibility of staking Etherium and you have an asset that generates returns.')
