Up until then, what I was supposed to do next was always clear. I was building up towards being an adult, and then suddenly there I was.<p>That shift from planning for the future to realizing that future was now was tricky. It took me a couple of years of aimless drifting to finally come to terms with it, and convince myself that it was time to start actually doing the things I had always planned for doing in the future.')
