Item(by='kbenson', descendants=None, kids=[24660397, 24661044, 24660768, 24667981, 24660659, 24660516, 24660420, 24660743, 24660457, 24663254, 24661053, 24665301], score=None, time=1601626433, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24659282, text='If only more languages supported quote operators like Perl.  They really do make certain things like this so much easier.<p>Non interpolating quotes:<p><pre><code>  q&#x2F;I wasn&#x27;t surprised when he said &quot;boo&quot; and game me $5&#x2F;;\n  q@I wasn&#x27;t surprised when he said &quot;boo&quot; and game me $5@;\n  q!I wasn&#x27;t surprised when he said &quot;boo&quot; and game me $5!;\n  q(I wasn&#x27;t surprised when he said &quot;boo&quot; and game me $5);\n  q[I wasn&#x27;t surprised when he said &quot;boo&quot; and game me $5];\n</code></pre>\nInterpolating quotes:<p><pre><code>  qq&#x2F;Hello, $name!&#x2F;;\n  qq!Hello, $name\\!!;\n  qq@Hello, $name!@;\n  qq(Hello, $name!);\n  qq[Hello, $name!];\n  qq&#x27;Hello, $name!&#x27;;\n</code></pre>\nRule of thumb, single q for single quote string (non-interpolating normally), and two q&#x27;s for a double quote string (normally interpolates).')
