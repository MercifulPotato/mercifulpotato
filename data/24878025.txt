Item(by='disgruntledphd2', descendants=None, kids=[24892880], score=None, time=1603532049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24877054, text='Just as an FYI, micro-targeting is very difficult to make work (i.e. measure) on Facebook, or indeed any digital ads platform.<p>FB&#x2F;Google&#x2F;Twitter etc work by measuring some kind of conversion (pixel hit, app install etc). Micro-targeting in terms of voting doesn&#x27;t have this signal, and as such any of these ads will be difficult to make work on FB.<p>You can optimise for engagement, but I suspect an ad targeting black voters saying about the 94 crime bill is unlikely to elicit much engagement.')
