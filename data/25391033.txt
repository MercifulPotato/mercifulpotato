Item(by='ska', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607721022, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25390952, text='Basically ROS is a collection of libraries, mostly for and by researchers for interfacing with and controlling robots.<p>Libraries exist at different levels of abstraction, but it is for the most part not exactly plug-and-play.<p>It&#x27;s super useful if you are for example building robots in an academic lab, say, or re purposing existing hardware &amp; software.  The further you get from that environment, the less obvious the fit will be.  But if you are playing around with robotics, ROS can help you avoid reinventing a whole bunch of wheels just to get started.')
