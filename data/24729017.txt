Item(by='tom_mellior', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602244924, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24728892, text='Clang, like GCC, has -C and -CC flags to preserve comments during preprocessing. However, these are really flags for the underlying preprocessor. Your parent might be thinking of some application of the Clang frontend that does <i>not</i> run preprocessing. For example, clang-format will probably not want to preprocess the code nor strip out comments.')
