Then, the iphone 6s plus scored 535, and the oneplus 3 (using the 820) scores 306 - i.e. 57% of apples performance. On multicore it achieved 77% of the performance (also a larger difference than today).<p>Qualcomm <i>has</i> been catching up lately on single-thread and multithread perf, just not very quickly (i mean at this rate it&#x27;ll be well over a decade before they&#x27;re equal in single thread perf...).<p>Sources: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;browser.geekbench.com&#x2F;android-benchmarks" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;browser.geekbench.com&#x2F;android-benchmarks</a> and <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;browser.geekbench.com&#x2F;ios-benchmarks" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;browser.geekbench.com&#x2F;ios-benchmarks</a>, search for oneplus 3 and iphone 6s plus.')
