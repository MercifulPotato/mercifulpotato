Item(by='sanitycheck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610487237, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25749265, text='I had this on a laptop, the caused seemed to be an old touchpad driver that wasn&#x27;t compatible with S0 &quot;low power mode&quot; which Windows defaults to now. If you know your device supports S3 sleep, you can force Windows to use it. My identical problem went away.<p>reg #### add HKLM\\System\\CurrentControlSet\\Control\\Power &#x2F;v PlatformAoAcOverride &#x2F;t REG_DWORD &#x2F;d 0<p>(By removing the #### and pasting the above readers agree that I am not liable for breakages)')
