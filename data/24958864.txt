Item(by='jasoneckert', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604231750, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24957783, text='It&#x27;s important to note that the actual slice names in this article may vary, depending on whether you have a HDD or SSD, and whether you&#x27;ve upgraded macOS through several versions.<p>For example, if you upgraded to Catalina from a pre-APFS version of macOS on an SSD, &quot;Macintosh HD&quot; will likely be disk1s5 in the APFS container scheme. In this case, &quot;Macintosh HD - Data&quot; will be disk1s1, &quot;Preboot&quot; will be disk1s2, &quot;Recovery&quot; will be disk1s3 and &quot;VM&quot; will be disk1s4.')
