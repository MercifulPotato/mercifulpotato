The idea is to represent all major cloud storage through a single simple frontend API. You have to implement each provider&#x27;s OAuth flow, but once you have the files it&#x27;s pretty much the same.<p>Dropbox, Google, and my own protocol[0] will come first, but Solid is planned eventually.<p>What I&#x27;ve learned so far:<p>* Google is extremely draconian unless you use their JavaScript picker. To really integrate nicely you would have to spend at least $15000-$75000 getting your app audited by a 3rd party.<p>* Dropbox picker integration is very easy and slick.<p>[0]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;gemdrive" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;gemdrive</a>')
