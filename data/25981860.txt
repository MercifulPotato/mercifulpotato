Item(by='avaika', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612122238, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25977419, text='That&#x27;s default behavior. Docker tracks the main process. If it dies, and your database is not the main process, then the entire container will be restarted (depends on configuration). Means your database will be forcefully killed. At some point you might run into data corruption incident.<p>If you make database your main process, then docker will be blind for your application death.')
