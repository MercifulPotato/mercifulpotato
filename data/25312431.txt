Item(by='smcameron', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607151111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25306716, text='I think distros should disallow binaries that do not have man pages.  I remember on SunOS 4.x, for example, pretty much everything in &#x2F;bin and &#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin had man pages.  You wonder what something does?  Read the man page.  If you produce a binary and fail to produce an accompanying man page, you have committed a crime against humanity.')
