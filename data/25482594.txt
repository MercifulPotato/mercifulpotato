Item(by='spion', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608423162, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25475947, text='I would omit most information that can be inferred from context so long as context locality is good.<p>Single-letter names are OK for context smaller than 1 line (like lambdas), type information can be omitted for context up to 10 lines (lists etc) and generic functions (filter, concat, inner_join) are better over custom domain functions when the audience consists of other programmers.<p>Abbreviations are best avoided unless they are extremely common and familiar in the specific domain &#x2F; industry. If they are only ever used within that team and company, that&#x27;s probably bad.<p>Example here: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25477495" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25477495</a>')
