Item(by='eslaught', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603002163, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24815617, text='I&#x27;ve heard that unsalted butter makes it easier to measure the salt to put in a recipe, since you don&#x27;t need to reverse engineer the salt content of the butter. But in practice I don&#x27;t find this very compelling because (in my experience) the salt is pretty standardized. I generally convert my own recipes back to using purely salted butter so I don&#x27;t have to stock two kinds of butter.')
