The flaw is not fatal; that is, it does not render the software nonfree. But it does cause practical problems, including incompatibility with the GNU GPL.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gnu.org&#x2F;licenses&#x2F;license-list.html#OriginalBSD" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gnu.org&#x2F;licenses&#x2F;license-list.html#OriginalBSD</a>')
