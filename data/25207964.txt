Item(by='gchadwick', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606302118, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25207876, text='One issue with OpenGL is it was originally designed in the 90s when modern GPUs didn&#x27;t exist. Whilst the API has evolved since then there was a reticence to chunk it all away and start again with a clean slate, or at least have a radical resdesign (something DirectX didn&#x27;t shy away from).<p>Vulkan is built based on modern GPU architecture as well as being lower level then OpenGL. This makes it easier to get the best out of the hardware.')
