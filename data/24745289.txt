And yet, the needs of the children are the same. &quot;Coding&quot; is not the literacy we are teaching to children. We are teaching &quot;computational thought&quot;. Logic, loops, algorithms. Everyone needs an understanding of what computing is, what it can do, how to approach it, and how to blend it into other subjects as a tool. Not everyone needs to be a coder or pursue technology for its own sake.')
