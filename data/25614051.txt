Item(by='adrianmsmith', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609606108, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25612033, text='It might be worth considering that if you start too much, you might finish nothing. If the payoffs only come after finishing something, that would be a lot of effort for not achieving much.<p>It might be better to put the same (total) effort into fewer things. If those things get finished, leading to payoff, there might be more payoff in total for the same total effort invested.<p>This is my interpretation of Warren Buffet’s idea of “focus” as described here: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jamesclear.com&#x2F;buffett-focus" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;jamesclear.com&#x2F;buffett-focus</a>')
