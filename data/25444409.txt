Item(by='pimeys', descendants=None, kids=[25445458], score=None, time=1608135862, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25444215, text='It has been much better for the past 5+ years, but what I did with my workstation is I installed Arch into a btrfs file system. Now when I `pacman` whatever, it&#x27;ll create a snapshot first and then run the updates. If anything breaks, I go to the grub menu, boot into the previous snapshot and rollback into that state.<p>Never needed it yet, but it&#x27;s good to have. I remember the problems in the past...<p>You can also do it with zfs, but I wanted to not have the filesystem as DKMS.')
