Item(by='a1369209993', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603132231, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24828086, text='The word &quot;density&quot; means <i>mass</i> per unit <i>volume</i>. It&#x27;s sometimes qualified like &quot;charge density&quot; for (assumed, in turn, to be electric) charge per unit volume, but &quot;density of charge&quot; would mean mass per unit volume of charge, like &quot;density of iron&quot;, means mass per unit volume of iron.<p>So density of space is mass per unit volume of space, not space per unit volume.')
