Item(by='rsynnott', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606587949, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25239017, text='Micro-architectural differences. For instance, from the 386 to Pentium 3 (and on Athlon, I believe), shifting was cheap, due to the presence of a barrel shifter. This was dropped in the P4, and suddenly shifting was quite expensive (I think, though I’m not sure, that it may actually have come back in later P4s). That’s the sort of thing compiler writers need to know about.')
