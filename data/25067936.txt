Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=[25067965], score=None, time=1605172108, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25067719, text='As expected, the Apple M1 is a little faster than Inter Tiger Lake in single-thread applications, but it is a little slower than AMD Renoir in multi-threaded applications.<p>So for things like software development where you compile frequently your projects, the new Apple computers are a little slower than similar computers with AMD CPUs.<p>So even when taking only CPU performance into consideration, there are reasons to choose other computers than those with Apple Silicon, depending on what you want to do.<p>Of course, nobody will decide to buy or not buy products with &quot;Apple Silicon&quot; based on their performance.<p>Those who want to use Apple software will buy Apple products, those who do not want to use Apple software will not buy Apple products, like until now, regardless which have better performance.')
