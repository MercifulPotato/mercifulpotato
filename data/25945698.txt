Item(by='rirarobo', descendants=None, kids=[25946370], score=None, time=1611856383, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25943209, text='Because sexual abuse is a visceral trauma real to many people, by some estimates 1 in 3 women and 1 in 6 men have experienced sexual abuse. Those who have experienced sexual abuse also face higher risks of PTSD, drug abuse, suicide, and many other challenges. They also often never report their abuse, never discuss it with others, and suffer lifelong emotional distress at higher rates than for any other violent crime.<p>Consequently, sexual violence is not treated lightly in most public discourse. In that sense, it is simply a common courtesy.')
