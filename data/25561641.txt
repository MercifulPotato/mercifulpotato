Item(by='eyelidlessness', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609179999, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25558089, text='For what it’s worth, having gone the opposite direction (dynamic imperative langs -&gt; clojure -&gt; typescript), I highly recommend sticking with it for at least a little while. The dynamic type system must surely be painful if you’ve already enjoyed a good static type system, but getting a good instinct for how rarely code needs to be stateful and how easily maintainable even complex FP can be... really pays off when you come back to a language like TS.')
