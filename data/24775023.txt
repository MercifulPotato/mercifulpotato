Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602670578, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24774984, text='The Bayesian view of probability is that the 50% comes from the information you have. If the <i>only</i> thing you know is that there are two mutually-exclusive possibilities, then you&#x27;ll give both a 50% estimate of being true. The more you learn about the world, the more refined your estimate becomes.<p>But it&#x27;s important to remember that the &quot;I don&#x27;t know therefore 50-50&quot; describes your state of mind, not reality.')
