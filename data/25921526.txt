That should be multiplied in to any analysis of friction.<p>&gt; I think a service that allows for website usage based payments, a Spotify&#x2F;Apple News for websites would be interesting.<p>There have been <i>many</i> attempts to do that, none of which have succeeded. One major problem: they tend to track all your web activity, and the kinds of people interested in services like this are very much the kinds of people who don&#x27;t want to be tracked.
