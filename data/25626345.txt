From my experience, everyone is ultimately able to reach a higher level of understanding for the topic or problem which allows for better decision making. The more diverse the participants&#x27; backgrounds the larger the network of knowledge one is able to tap into.<p>Unfortunately, you are right that there are people who are extremely threatened by the Socratic Method.
