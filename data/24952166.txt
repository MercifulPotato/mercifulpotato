Item(by='imglorp', descendants=None, kids=[24954793], score=None, time=1604157934, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24951958, text='Not against zones; I was hoping to shine a light on all the complexity around the TZDB, which is a mapping of zones to switchover times. Eg,<p><pre><code>    $ zdump -v US&#x2F;Eastern | grep 2020\n    US&#x2F;Eastern  Sun Mar  8 06:59:59 2020 UT = Sun Mar  8 01:59:59 2020 EST isdst=0 gmtoff=-18000\n    US&#x2F;Eastern  Sun Mar  8 07:00:00 2020 UT = Sun Mar  8 03:00:00 2020 EDT isdst=1 gmtoff=-14400\n    US&#x2F;Eastern  Sun Nov  1 05:59:59 2020 UT = Sun Nov  1 01:59:59 2020 EDT isdst=1 gmtoff=-14400\n    US&#x2F;Eastern  Sun Nov  1 06:00:00 2020 UT = Sun Nov  1 01:00:00 2020 EST isdst=0 gmtoff=-18000\n</code></pre>\nIf everyone simply picked a zone and stayed there, without the switching schedule above, you&#x27;d only need a mapping of zone to GMT offset and be done with it.')
