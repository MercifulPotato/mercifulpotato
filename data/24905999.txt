Item(by='jeromenerf', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603794278, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24905310, text='Org mode supports adding metadata to the whole document (a la pandoc, frontmatter) and any node. There are a lot of properties (dates, settings, user defined ...).<p>With pandoc, the frontmatter metadata are only used when exporting the document. With org mode, the metadata are also used during editing, executing code, etc.<p>The headings themselves can embed tags.')
