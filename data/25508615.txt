Item(by='ceras', descendants=None, kids=[25509834], score=None, time=1608657435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25508455, text='Setting aside whether it&#x27;s a good idea to encourage this: in practice today, combining laws is a reliable way for different groups to compromise.<p>If group A wants a policy that B doesn&#x27;t want, and B wants a policy that A doesn&#x27;t want, then combining those policies into one law is an easy and reliable way to compromise. You know that the whole bill will either pass, or it won&#x27;t -- Congress votes on entire bills rather than parts of it, and the president doesn&#x27;t have line-item veto power.')
