That doesn’t mean hopping on every latest JS fad, but it does mean continually looking for more information, and learning how others in the field are solving problems.<p>Physicians have to keep up their board certifications&#x2F;licenses by undergoing yearly(?) retraining, in order to continue practicing medicine.<p>It’s not that hard to find what other software engineers are doing, to start, Read hacker news!
