Item(by='andrewprock', descendants=None, kids=[25937026], score=None, time=1611787651, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25935122, text='Stop loss is really the wrong term mathematically. In truth, a &quot;stop loss&quot; is really better thought of as a &quot;max price&quot;. If you set it up to sell when the prices drops to $320, you are guaranteed that it will sell at a price below $320, not that it will sell at $320.<p>In truth, you are not even guaranteed to be able to find a buyer.')
