Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605043150, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25051483, text='It&#x27;s funny how blindingly obvious these things sound, until you drop into an actual company and find entire teams and departments who are so far off in the weeds that everyone has forgotten why or how their work is important for the company&#x27;s success.<p>It&#x27;s a particularly difficult growing pain for leaders who go too deep into delegating everything. Delegation is important for scaling, but delegating something doesn&#x27;t mean it&#x27;s completely hands-off. Leaders still need to deep dive into teams every once in a while and make sure everyone understands what the goals are and what&#x27;s being done to reach those goals.')
