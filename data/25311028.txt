Item(by='zelly', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607134611, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25304705, text='The business case for native apps (whether on desktop or mobile) is that it&#x27;s not just another tab people can X out of. Installing a native app feels like a commitment. The icon is there. It might be sitting in the tray&#x2F;taskbar. It commands attention. It can push notifications that most users wouldn&#x27;t turn off.<p>Most users do not know what a PWA is and won&#x27;t install it unless it was done for them. Even then a PWA is at the mercy of the host browser&#x27;s configuration and addons which can degrade the experience. (And on Apple devices it won&#x27;t even work.)')
