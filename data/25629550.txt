Item(by='Veen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609755914, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25629178, text='&gt; why be different<p>Because, although everyone understands passwords, they have security and customer experience issues:<p>- Users choose easily guessed passwords and get hacked.<p>- They use the same passwords on multiple services and get hacked.<p>- They forget passwords, requiring an email reset, so they need to access their email anyway.<p>- The service doesn&#x27;t need to store and manage passwords.<p>Every authentication method involves tradeoffs, and the service has to decide which set of tradeoffs they prefer. Personally, I prefer passwordless logins, but I understand why they&#x27;re annoying for some.')
