Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611704426, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25918088, text='&gt; <i>An advertisement that is not within the user&#x27;s interest is wasted, rather than going to the competitor.</i><p>Not completely, if there is anything to the concept of &quot;brand awareness&quot; (and I believe there is, and a good chunk of advertisers do too). An ad that is not within user&#x27;s interest still makes an imprint of the brand, product and vendor in their head. Later on, where user&#x27;s interest change, that brand&#x2F;product&#x2F;vendor is a bit more likely to be recalled by them. A mistargeted ad is better than no ad at all.')
