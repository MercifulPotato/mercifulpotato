Chrome and other browsers are starting to scope their caches by domain, to mitigate tracking techniques that used 304 request timing to identify if the client had visited arbitrary URLs.<p>Yes, I&#x27;m aware that &quot;it will be cached&quot; lost most of its glory when bundling became mainstream, but I still hear it as an argument when pulling things from common CDNs.')
