Item(by='nickelcitymario', descendants=None, kids=[25874240, 25875240, 25875697, 25874569], score=None, time=1611337267, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25873856, text='I don&#x27;t understand the &quot;free rider&quot; problem.<p>I pay my ISP for bandwidth. WHAT I choose to download with that bandwidth has no impact on their bottom line, unless they were counting on me not actually using it.<p>Websites and web apps pay for bandwidth. Anyone who has paid an AWS bill knows there&#x27;s nothing free about it.<p>The only group of companies that are guaranteed to make money in any internet venture are the ISPs. The rest of us are gambling, hoping that our investments will be worth it. On top of that, we&#x27;re all competing with a global market, whiles ISPs tend to only compete with a handful of local providers at a time, allowing them a lot more control over prices.<p>Meanwhile, they&#x27;re selling pick axes in a gold rush and crying victim?<p>Doesn&#x27;t make a lick of sense to me.')
