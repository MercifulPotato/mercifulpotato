Item(by='Symbiote', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611750531, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25923276, text='Standard touch-typing advice is to use opposing hands to type combinations like &quot;Shift+A&quot;.<p>On an ergonomic, split keyboard this might be less important: the modifier keys are often under the thumbs, and on both sides.<p>Also, it&#x27;s fairly easy to program the keys however you like, e.g. make a &quot;Fn&quot; key, so Fn+DvorakQ sends Ctrl+X.')
