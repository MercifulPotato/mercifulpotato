At the turn of the 90s, millions tuned in to televised hypnosis sessions with Anatoly Kashpirovsky. Many others sought services to get &quot;programmed&quot; to stop drinking alcohol. The faith in life-extending properties of ginseng and especially miraculous cave tar <i>shilajit</i> is overwhelming.<p>This is the same thing you see with people called &quot;undecided voters&quot; in the US. By turning away from the mainstream and claiming that &quot;both sides are wrong,&quot; they show great willingness to buy into exotic and elaborate conspiracies that give them an illusion of control over their lives. Belief in magic takes many forms.')
