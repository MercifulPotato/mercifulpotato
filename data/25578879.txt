Item(by='monocasa', descendants=None, kids=[25582735], score=None, time=1609306175, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25577986, text='It&#x27;s way less Windows than Android is Linux.<p>They took out the idea of processes for instance.  A single executable runs at a time, and it&#x27;s dynamically linked against the kernel and running in kernel space.  There are no system calls instructions used, just normal call instructions that get fixed up on load.<p>USB, network, and graphics stacks all live in the executable, not the kernel provided by the box (despite everything running in kernel space).<p>I&#x27;ve been writing a Rust based devkit for the Original Xbox recently and it feels more like uCOS than Windows.')
