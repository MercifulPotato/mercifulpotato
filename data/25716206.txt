However, a lot of software and service vendors have a vested interest in convincing people otherwise. And, historically, they&#x27;ve had all sorts of great platforms for doing so. Who doesn&#x27;t enjoy a free day out of the office, with lunch provided?<p>It&#x27;s also much easier to hide complexity than it is to remove it. One can be accomplished with (relative) turnkey solutions, generally without ever having to leave the comfort of your computer. Whereas the other generally requires long hours standing in front of a chalkboard and scratching your head.')
