Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[24822217, 24827118, 24822789], score=None, time=1603063488, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821355, text='I would also question just how valuable concurrent editing of a text document is. it&#x27;s cool that it&#x27;s technically possible, but it doesn&#x27;t seem that useful for multiple people to be editing the same sentence or paragraph in real time. in practice, I find that people divvy up the document into sections and each work on their assigned section independently. this makes google docs &#x2F; office 365 a moderately lower friction version of everybody working in their own documents and copying changes to a master doc with version history, which has been a supported workflow in desktop word for quite a long time.<p>similarly, slack can be pretty useful, but I doubt it feels revolutionary to anyone who grew up using IRC.')
