It correctly states the behavior of two-factor auth systems but incorrectly understands their <i>value</i>.<p>Yes, 2FA won&#x27;t protect you from being phished by a site if you aren&#x27;t careful.  2FA absolutely will protect you from a phishing site using the password it stole.<p>So no, when someone says &quot;Use 2FA if you might be phished&quot; you shouldn&#x27;t be replying &quot;That&#x27;s not how 2FA works&quot;, you should reply &quot;That&#x27;s right!  But let me explain why you are still at risk of password theft and should still be careful.&quot;')
