There&#x27;s already a 100 TB SSD that&#x27;s the size of a 3.5&quot; HDD.[0] 10000 times the Library of Congress would fit onto 2100 of these drives.<p>The dimensions for this 3.5&quot; drive are 26.1 x 147 x 101.8 mm.[1] That&#x27;s a volume of roughly 391 cm^3. 2100 of them take up 821,100 cm^3.<p>A Fractal Design Define 7 has the dimensions of 547 x 240 x 475 mm. That&#x27;s a volume of 62,358 cm^3.[2]<p>You could fit 2100 of those SSDs into the same volume as about 13.2 Fractal Design Define 7 cases. That&#x27;s roughly the size of a bookshelf. You could fit the books of 10000 Libraries of Congress onto one bookshelf.<p>Now imagine using tape for this.
