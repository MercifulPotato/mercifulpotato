Item(by='nemothekid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610562511, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25758529, text='If you are using something like Netlify, then you would need them to serve your S3 content from their CDN (which I don&#x27;t think they support). For my specific use case, we host our  application on our own domain (on our PaaS), then use a CDN for static assets. If we needed to also serve s3 assets from the same domain then it would mean routing routes in that node application to serve from s3 (effectively a server side proxy).')
