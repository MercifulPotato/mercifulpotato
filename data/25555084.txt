Item(by='PaulDavisThe1st', descendants=None, kids=[25555284], score=None, time=1609110968, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25554999, text='My understanding of the term &quot;micro-inverter&quot; is that it describes a small, unsophisticated, lower power inverter. Such a device could not match grid phase and could not handle high voltages. They are useful for other reasons, but by themselves don&#x27;t connect to the grid.<p>My Solar Edge system <i>uses</i> micro-inverters on each panel, but still has &quot;an inverter&quot; before the grid interconnect.')
