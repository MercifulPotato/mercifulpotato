Item(by='QuadrupleA', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603639348, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24886609, text='Just realizing now, &quot;CRUD app&quot; is almost meaninglessly vague - just means an app that reads &amp; writes data, which is almost every app. Calendar, contacts, email client, image editor, blog, comment section, forum - all CRUD apps, as they focus on reading &amp; writing specific types of data.<p>But obviously there&#x27;s a world of difference in interface, usability, aesthetics, performance, good writing, security etc. The CRUD part is the simple part.<p>I guess there&#x27;s some spectrum between a dumb unspecialized list of table editors ala phpMyAdmin, versus hyper specialized interfaces like game engines that manipulate scene data, triangle data, player inventory, physics entities etc.')
