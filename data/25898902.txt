Item(by='lolinder', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611547116, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25898893, text='This essay has been on my mind a lot in recent weeks. This idea seems relevant now more than ever:<p>&gt; The man who lives in a small community lives in a much larger world. He knows much more of the fierce varieties and uncompromising divergences of men. The reason is obvious. In a large community we can choose our companions. In a small community our companions are chosen for us. Thus in all extensive and highly civilized societies groups come into existence founded upon what is called sympathy, and shut out the real world more sharply than the gates of a monastery.')
