Drug crimes account for about half of Federal prisoners.<p>But percentage-wise, when you add up federal, state, and local prisons and jails, drugs are not as large a percentage as you&#x27;d think. Another half million people in prisons or jails haven&#x27;t been convicted of any crime at all, either because they can&#x27;t afford bail or because they&#x27;re just being processed today. If you had to guess what someone went to jail for, and you only knew they were in a State penitentiary, violent crime would be your best guess.<p>Here&#x27;s a bit pie chart: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.prisonpolicy.org&#x2F;reports&#x2F;pie2020.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.prisonpolicy.org&#x2F;reports&#x2F;pie2020.html</a>')
