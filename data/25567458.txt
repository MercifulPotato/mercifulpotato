Item(by='wrs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609225269, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564666, text='The class name “Map” is a noun (because it’s a class), the method name “map” is a verb (because it’s a method), and the conceptual relationship between that noun and verb is exactly the conceptual relationship between the class and the method. There’s nothing “unfortunate” about it at all. It’s elegant, in fact. (And if you know some math, it’s even familiar.)')
