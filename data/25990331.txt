Item(by='Vinnl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612198110, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25989922, text='You can choose whether to self-host or whether to have someone else handle that for you.<p>As for also hosting your apps: I think the answer to that is the same as for why you might still choose to use an email client even if you&#x27;re hosting your email server.<p>However, I would expect the majority of people not to self-host, but to have that handled by a party they trust. They would then not be limited by that host also having to support the particular photo-viewing layer they are interested in, but instead are able to choose one independently.<p>(Disclosure: I work for Inrupt, but opinions are my own.)')
