Item(by='madaxe_again', descendants=None, kids=[25683526], score=None, time=1610102713, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25682307, text='This is how tithings used to work. Ten men would be grouped into a tithing, and if any of those men were suspected of a crime, then the rest of the group either had to prove their innocence or had to condemn the guilty party. If they did neither, all were equally punished for the crime.<p>In principal, it strikes me as a good idea - it promotes collective good behaviour, and responsibility for ones peers.')
