Item(by='altacc', descendants=None, kids=[24822846], score=None, time=1603042330, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24818407, text='Also known as sustaining innovation vs disruptive innovation.<p>When you’re embedded in a technology or business it’s harder to come up with something totally new, your perspective is often limited by your situation (everything is a nail when you have a hammer). As something brand new can also be damaging to the current business model, it feels uncomfortable.<p>Often you need somebody with the right mix of experience and independence (plus enough capital) to create something truly disruptive.')
