To say that a natually-occurring physical process or phenomenon was invented is nonsensical.<p>Electricity is not the same as electric power, just as nuclear fission is not the same as a nuclear weapon, just the same as fire is not the same as a coal fired power plant.<p>Electricity and nuclear fission were <i>discovered</i>, not invented.')
