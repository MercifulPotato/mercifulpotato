Item(by='renedet', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610047536, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25671463, text='A new &quot;learning&quot; collaborates with the improvement of &quot;work&quot;, but the problem is having &quot;time&quot; to study, and still studying something that will really bring a return on investment. We have a basic triad where we spend our &quot;time&quot;: &quot;studying&quot;, &quot;working&quot; or &quot;resting&quot; (other activities).\nIf I dedicate time to work, my study and rest will suffer. This occurs with the other factors: dedicating to learning harms work and rest ...\nThe point is: choose something important to learn that can change your life to have better quality work and more time to rest.')
