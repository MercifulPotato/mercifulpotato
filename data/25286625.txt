Item(by='cosmotic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606982030, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25285862, text='New shiny libraries, frameworks, and platforms draw in developers. Those new things are typically not well polished or optimized. By the time they get optimized, developers move on to newer and shinier things.<p>Java, PHP, .NET, and Javascript were all slow until they got fast (although with Javascript, things got really complicated and slow again).<p>I say this as a developer that uses all those now-fast things and avoids the shiny and new for these reasons (and more, including lack of tooling).')
