Item(by='Xylakant', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611410142, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25882174, text='I have kids and deal with inclement weather - it’s been a mix of rain and snow for most of the last couple of days. Granted, I’m a knowledge worker, but shops are open until midnight in the neighborhood and worst case, both of the large supermarket chains here deliver until late with prearranged slots.<p>For the majority of (urban) people it’s definitely manageable. More than 50% of all households in central Berlin own no car, numbers rising.<p>I used to own a car, and the tipping point from “I can’t do that” to “no problem to do that” was selling the car. It’s an acquired habit for most of us. I now own an umbrella and a good waterproof jacket instead.')
