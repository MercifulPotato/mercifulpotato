Item(by='WorldMaker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609962520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25654155, text='The article mentions KSUID which has a very similar spec, but with a wider random segment.<p>For comparison: KSUID is 160-bit wide versus ULID sticks to 128-bit wide (which is somewhat more compatible with UUID-like database column types, for instance, so long as they don&#x27;t try things like UUID version checks). KSUID uses Base62 encoding versus ULID uses a much simpler to (lexicographicly) sort Base32 encoding.<p>(I&#x27;ve found ULID useful in some of my own projects.)')
