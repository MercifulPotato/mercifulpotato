Throughout the course, you have a project to build a columnar relational datastore with C.<p>I don&#x27;t think the lecture videos are all publicly available, but you can learn a lot from the slides and readings.<p>[1]: <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;daslab.seas.harvard.edu&#x2F;classes&#x2F;cs165&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;daslab.seas.harvard.edu&#x2F;classes&#x2F;cs165&#x2F;</a>')
