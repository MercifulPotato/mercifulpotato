Have another site handle signups. (admitedly, setting up something with LDAP is often a massive pain. I duct-tape around it by using LdapJS on top of a CMS)<p>I&#x27;ve had a handful of projects where human spammers will bother to create an account and jump through the loops, but in the 2-3 years of running a Gitlab instance, which has 1300 users, I only had 2-3 incidents (we keep an eye on recent projects, snippets, etc).')
