Item(by='crote', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603913531, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24923058, text='If you mean ISO 8601 and its &quot;2007-04-05T12:30-02:00&quot; timezone thingy, not really.<p>It doesn&#x27;t store a timezone, but a UTC offset. Which is a problem because the UTC offset of a timezone may change.<p>This happens rarely enough that people do indeed use &quot;ISO 8601 + offset&quot; to store &quot;timezoned&quot; timestamps, but often enough that it&#x27;ll probably end up corrupting your data sooner or later.')
