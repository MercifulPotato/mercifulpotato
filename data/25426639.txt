Item(by='sbinthree', descendants=None, kids=[25426926], score=None, time=1608003011, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25426582, text='I think it&#x27;s interesting that people forget anything network based tends to unwind just as fast as it winds up. Decorrelation (e.g. low viral coefficient) can be antifragile. If everyone moves to your city for a <i>different</i> reason, it is more resilient to downturns than if everyone moves to your city for the <i>same</i> reason and conditions change. Facebook may unwind for the same reason it was successful: the lack of privacy.')
