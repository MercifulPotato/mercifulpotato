Imagine, if you will, a software engineer; probably based in the US, that writes a backdoor into equipment used to manage a banking transaction network. This is a fairly natural place to have it, as &quot;follow the money&quot; is a classic forensic technique.<p>Of course, access to this network could net nefarious (probably non-state) actors a <i>lot</i> of money.<p>Said software engineer suddenly quits and buys a Bugatti.<p>The back door is now a front door, and it&#x27;s baked into some hardware that can&#x27;t easily be changed, as no one trusts the patches, now.')
