There&#x27;s a degree of practicality that comes with a school that can be met with common resources, but it&#x27;s still apparent communities don&#x27;t want modular schools (regardless of individual opinions).<p>There&#x27;s a long list of reasons schools aren&#x27;t just a cleared field with a bunch of mobile-unit classrooms thrown out there despite that being the most cost-effective way to house classes.')
