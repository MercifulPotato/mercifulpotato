Item(by='barumi', descendants=None, kids=[24866749, 24867124], score=None, time=1603432755, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24865605, text='&gt; Pedigrees are dumb.<p>&quot;Pedigree&quot;, and other terms used to refer to the same concept, are nothing more than the reflection of the track record of an institution regarding scientific quality and training.<p>&quot;Pedigree&quot; also reflects a pre-screening of candidates with regards to academic and technical potential.<p>It might be true that I won&#x27;t become the next Niels Bohr if I attend the same universities he attended or lectured in, but let&#x27;s not fool ourselves into assuming that you&#x27;ll learn the same from a course taught by amateur underperforming lecturers than what you would from a course taught by nobel laureates to a room of top-performers.')
