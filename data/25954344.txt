Item(by='NineStarPoint', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611898098, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25944486, text='Higher taxes on income, dividends, and realized gains incentivize money being reinvested in the business instead of distributed to shareholders. If taking money out of a company creates taxable income, stockholders have an extra reason to press companies to find ways they can use that money to increase value instead.<p>Basically, an extra incentive for companies to hire more people and do more research instead of do things like\nstock buybacks.')
