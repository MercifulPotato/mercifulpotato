Item(by='yorwba', descendants=None, kids=[25434129, 25430496], score=None, time=1608030123, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25428961, text='The law applies to any service <i>targeted at</i> EU citizens. It does not apply to services that any EU citizen happens to be using even though they&#x27;re primarily intended for citizens of other countries.<p>A &quot;local&quot; Bavarian news site hosted in the US would be in scope, but a local Nebraskan news site hosted in the US wouldn&#x27;t.<p>Edit: Recital 23: Applicable to processors not established in the Union if data subjects within the Union are targeted:  <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gdpr.eu&#x2F;Recital-23-Applicable-to-processors-not-established-in-the-Union-if-data-subjects-within-the-Union-are-targeted" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gdpr.eu&#x2F;Recital-23-Applicable-to-processors-not-esta...</a><p><i>In order to determine whether such a controller or processor is offering goods or services to data subjects who are in the Union, it should be ascertained whether it is apparent that the controller or processor envisages offering services to data subjects in one or more Member States in the Union.</i><p><i>Whereas the mere accessibility of the controller’s, processor’s or an intermediary’s website in the Union, of an email address or of other contact details, or the use of a language generally used in the third country where the controller is established, is insufficient to ascertain such intention, factors such as the use of a language or a currency generally used in one or more Member States with the possibility of ordering goods and services in that other language, or the mentioning of customers or users who are in the Union, may make it apparent that the controller envisages offering goods or services to data subjects in the Union.</i>')
