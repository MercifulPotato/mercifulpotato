Item(by='Rygian', descendants=None, kids=[25226612, 25227320, 25227796, 25228474, 25227395], score=None, time=1606464974, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25219036, text='I am impressed that UX and Usability can reach such lofty philosophical considerations, yet it&#x27;s 2020 and I am still trapped by UIs that keep shifting around the content I&#x27;m about to interact with and trick me into clicking or tapping where I didn&#x27;t intend to.<p>Not a day passes by that a partially-loaded screen moves that button or textarea just a couple milliseconds before I click on it (so now my click goes elsewhere), both on web apps, phone apps, and to a lesser extent, native apps.<p>There should be a hardcoded Rule #0 of User Experience: don&#x27;t move anything on screen unless the user directly initiated an explicit action for the specific thing to move.')
