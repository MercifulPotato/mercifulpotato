Item(by='TaurenHunter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606581379, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25237356, text='I like to think that probability is the ratio unknown&#x2F;known, information available divided by all possibilities.<p>You know that a coin has 2 faces (known=2) and that if you toss it, 1 face will be up (unknown which=1).<p>The ratio is more about something in the mind than intrinsic to the objects.<p>That helped me understand why in the Monty Hall problem, when you switch doors, the probability of getting the prize increases.')
