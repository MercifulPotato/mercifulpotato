Item(by='jerf', descendants=None, kids=[25141425], score=None, time=1605719218, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137835, text='Something everybody involved ought to understand before going too far down this road; you&#x27;re not going to spread Silicon Valley prices all around the US and internationally. If you force the companies to pay a flat wage, you&#x27;re going to get an averaging out at best, and <i>nobody&#x27;s</i> going to be paid Silicon Valley&#x27;s current wages.<p>Whether this is good or bad is beside my point, as are what other second-order effects may arise. I&#x27;m simply pointing out, &quot;everybody in the world gets paid SV rates&quot; simply isn&#x27;t on the table. So don&#x27;t go into this process expecting that outcome.')
