Item(by='lxgr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609526940, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25604039, text='Many countries have (or used to have) similar stored-value cards. They are really useful for some use cases: Given that they are very cash-like (transferable, anonymous unless registered), they are usually available to foreigners and minors without any formalities.<p>Japan‘s version could at one point even be used to purchase games in Nintendo‘s eStore by tapping a transit card on a game console – genius, given that a big part of their target audience doesn‘t have a credit or debit card.')
