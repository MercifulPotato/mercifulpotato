Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605168616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25067088, text='I think the benefits are where some automated system writes your &quot;preload&quot; entries for you.<p>For example, a webpack stage could render inside a sandbox each page of your site, detect which resources get loaded, and add all of those as preload&#x2F;server push entries.  The server itself can keep records of which resources have been pushed to a specific client, and not push them again.<p>Writing preload lists by hand is never going to scale with today&#x27;s web apps with hundreds or thousands of requests for a typical page.')
