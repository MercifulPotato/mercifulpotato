I&#x27;d love to see some sort of automatic regulation for situations of market failure. E.g., If the 2 largest players in a market have more than an 80% market share, the presumption should be regulation.<p>In SF, I have multiple ISP options. I get gigabit fiber for $40&#x2F;month and no nonsense. But my girlfriend lives in an area with Comcast only, where the price is much higher, the service is much worse, and they had loaded her bill down with absurd equipment rental charges.<p>When sufficient competition doesn&#x27;t exist, we shouldn&#x27;t just abandon people to predatory companies.')
