Equity isn&#x27;t really intended to &quot;reward&quot; early employees. Living organisms have inputs and outputs and that&#x27;s part of being alive.<p>In this case, one &quot;output&quot; is that people leave sometimes. The fact that it happens is natural and normal. But you don&#x27;t want them to leave in a way that&#x27;s like cutting out your heart without anesthesia.')
