Item(by='fefe23', descendants=None, kids=[25198500, 25197056], score=None, time=1606204424, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25193349, text='If you mean malloc() by that: malloc will return aligned memory so that you can store all data types, i.e. it is 64-bit aligned at least (so you can store double) but probably has even higher granularity.<p>It&#x27;s the downside of malloc not knowing the type you want and returning void*. It has to assume the worst alignment requirements.<p>Which actually happen to be 128 bytes for SSE instructions.')
