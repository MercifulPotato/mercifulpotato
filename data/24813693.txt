Item(by='davidajackson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602971471, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24810399, text='I did a consulting gig for a new YC company this year and it went well. I started by reaching out to them on Work At A Startup, which led to an informal chat. I provided some background on previous experience and projects that used similar tech stacks and were in the same space.<p>Instead of an algorithmic interview it was really just us sitting down at a whiteboard for a few hours mapping out how the backend would work and what external APIs would be needed. I think this is the best approach -- be super transparent about what you&#x27;re specialized in&#x2F;and what you&#x27;re not, and make sure that you&#x27;re the right person for it. Providing a small amount of value upfront and for free can go a long way.')
