Item(by='mmgutz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611096720, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25839695, text='Docker&#x27;s custom iptables chains, upon restart of network or docker, can and most likely WILL clobber rules that used to work. Docker adds the DOCKER-USER user chain for the purpose of readding rules back <i>after</i> docker has set up its rules. Most engineers never have to deal with it unless they are deploying to production boxes.')
