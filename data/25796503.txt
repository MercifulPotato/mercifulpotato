Item(by='manfredo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610744877, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25795752, text='There&#x27;s a distinction between collective action and government-sanctioned monopolies on labor. It&#x27;s one thing for workers to bargain collectively. It&#x27;s another thing when the government prohibits the company from finding another labor pool, and effectively gives the union a monopoly on labor. The latter is when corruption runs rampant, and unions have no competition.')
