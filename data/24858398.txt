Item(by='bonestamp2', descendants=None, kids=[24858921], score=None, time=1603376998, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24857761, text='For those who are curious, it&#x27;s called &quot;Box Breathing&quot; because, if it&#x27;s helpful for you, you can picture a box and then trace the edges of the box in your mind as you&#x27;re counting for each of the 4 steps (inhale, hold, exhale, hold).
