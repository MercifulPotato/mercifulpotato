It might be &quot;private sector&quot;, but it sure isn&#x27;t subject to market forces.<p>It&#x27;s no accident that those industries with the most anti-competitive regulations are those with the highest costs and poorest service (which ironically begets more anti-competitive regulation).<p>Tensions between supplier and consumer are profit opportunities for extant and would-be competitors.
