Item(by='danhak', descendants=None, kids=[25399426], score=None, time=1607790558, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25398429, text='In my view, there is a uniquely American fiction that there exists for everyone some pursuit that both (a) will satisfy their passion and (b) be highly remunerative.  Your job is to find that pursuit.  And if you fail, I guess it&#x27;s because you weren&#x27;t earnest enough.<p>I don&#x27;t doubt that successful founders are highly motivated by the challenges of their work.  But the suggestion that <i>the point</i> isn&#x27;t to build a business and make money is, I&#x27;m sorry to say, typical of the self-serving retconning we are starting to see from those at the top.  And that we have always seen from Silicon Valley: &quot;Don&#x27;t Be Evil,&quot; &quot;We&#x27;re Changing the World,&quot; &quot;Facebook&#x27;s mission is to make the world more open and connected.&quot;<p>*Thanks to my mom, barber, third grade teacher, roommate, dog and barista for reading drafts of this comment.')
