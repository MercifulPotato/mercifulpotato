Item(by='jakeprins', descendants=0, kids=None, score=1, time=1608125969, title='NextWind: A free starter-kit for building landing pages and blog', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='I made a starter-kit for building a blog or landing pages quickly with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and Netlify CMS.<p>It allows you to switch between different versions of page sections from within the CMS. The preview is immediately updated to show you the styling of the selected version.<p>The sections are mostly brought to you by Tailblocks, some ready-to-use components built with Tailwind CSS.<p>You can find the repository here: https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;jakeprins&#x2F;nextwind')
