Item(by='mdavis6890', descendants=None, kids=[25448640], score=None, time=1608147026, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446747, text='I agree we need more cash payments to humans. But we have to be careful not to confuse money with stuff (the goods and services produced by the economy). Giving money does not produce any more stuff, it only increases the competition for and price of the stuff that is being produced.<p>We are producing less stuff because of the pandemic, which means there is less to go around (however it gets allocated), and no amount of money will change that.')
