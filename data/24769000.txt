Item(by='jjuhl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602615943, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24765405, text='I once worked on an application where we (in one common situation) had to sort an array that was always &lt;= 5 elements and in 90+% of cases was already completely (or mostly) sorted. I got a lot of heat from co-workers when I used bubble sort for that task, since &quot;bubble sort is terribly inefficient&quot; (and it <i>is</i> for large random datasets). But, given the actual data in question, no-one could actually make any other sort perform faster.<p>Know your data.')
