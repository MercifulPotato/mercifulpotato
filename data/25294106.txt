They actually have some positive impact on people’s mental health, eg those with PTSD. I do take issue with abuse of the regulations. Some beneficial medications can be used recreationally, and governments give abundant clarity on legal and illegal uses. It would be harmful for people to start calling those medicines “ridiculous” because some people use them to get high or improve their performance.<p>I think a solution is for the government to place restrictions on emotional support animal use, eg the same way prescriptions are handled.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;journalistsresource.org&#x2F;studies&#x2F;society&#x2F;culture&#x2F;emotional-support-animals-research&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;journalistsresource.org&#x2F;studies&#x2F;society&#x2F;culture&#x2F;emot...</a>')
