Item(by='curryst', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601848770, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671182, text='It&#x27;s the incentive structure.  It&#x27;s effectively Goodhart&#x27;s Law in action: When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure.  In this case, the measure is &quot;number of pull requests&quot;, and the result of making it a measure is that people created an insane number of PRs that simply adjusted white space in a README or slightly reworded phrases but kept the exact same meaning.  Because they wanted the T shirt, because they felt that it validated them as a developer (ignoring that good contributions would have been a much better validation).')
