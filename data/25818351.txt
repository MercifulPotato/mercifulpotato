Item(by='SamBam', descendants=None, kids=[25818796, 25827780], score=None, time=1610946144, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25818161, text='I always hate that frequent retort, though, because people are always saying &quot;this recipe says caramelize the onion for 20 minutes, and that&#x27;s impossible, this recipe will take an hour!&quot; but they&#x27;re attacking the wrong thing: <i>cook</i> the onions for 20 minutes, then quibble about whether &quot;caramelizing&quot; is the scientifically-correct word.<p>The intent of the recipe is almost always to cook the onions for the time stated.
