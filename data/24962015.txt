Item(by='ansible', descendants=None, kids=[24963419], score=None, time=1604260613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24960407, text='Yes, the old, old way of having a single condition code register or the like (which dates back 40+ years) doesn&#x27;t work well these days.<p>I like the Mill CPU approach, where every &quot;register&quot; (it doesn&#x27;t have named registers actually) has the full set of status bits associated with it, and not just for overflow.
