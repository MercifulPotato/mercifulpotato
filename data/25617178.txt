Item(by='krapp', descendants=None, kids=[25617348], score=None, time=1609626471, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25615810, text='I would change the definition - the main characteristic of a conspiracy theory is that it is believed despite a lack of evidence, or even evidence to the contrary.<p>Believing Epstein could have been killed, because the circumstances around his death seem extremely suspicious, isn&#x27;t a conspiracy theory.<p>Believing that the Clintons sent a hit squad to kill him in order to cover up Pizzagate <i>is</i> a conspiracy theory.<p>People who believe the former also likely hold some doubt, because the actual evidence leaves room for doubt. It is after all <i>possible</i> that he did kill himself, stranger things have happened.<p>Whereas, no one who believes the latter will likely be convinced otherwise, because belief is obviously a matter of faith in that case, not reason.')
