Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612055197, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25972662, text='Current versions of Chrome still use gQUIC for most instances when talking with gQUIC-capable servers such as Google and Youtube.<p>Just checked, mine used version Q050 just now.<p>Of course, there are many versions of gQUIC, some more like IETF QUIC than others as they are transitioning parts of the protocol in stages.
