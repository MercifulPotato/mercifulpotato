If you are sweating a microsecond, 100 nanoseconds is significant chunk of your budget. For this and possibly other reasons, a many-core CPU isn&#x27;t always a great choice for hosted storage. If your goal is to export NVMe blocks over a network interface, you might be better off with an easier-to-program 4- or 8-core CPU. I don&#x27;t like seeing 128 cores and a bunch of NVMe devices in the same box because it just causes trouble.')
