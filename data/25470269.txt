Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608316368, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25469886, text='The &quot;IBM PC architecture&quot; tells you where to find the PCI config space (either the well known i&#x2F;o address 0x0CF8 and friends, or memory mapped address found in ACPI tables)<p>BIOS provided tables allow you to enumerate devices that aren&#x27;t on the PCI bus, and BIOS provides methods to interact with storage devices. UEFI does all the same things, but differently.<p>A lot of ARM64 boards intended towards general purpose use include UEFI these days.')
