They would never share the pricing advice they gave to a competitor.<p>I don&#x27;t know if what Patrick does is problematic. It wouldn&#x27;t surprise me if he was trying to be glib and his actual advice is more nuanced.<p>If one interprets it literally: Someone who receives advice from him to raise prices can look down Stripe&#x27;s customer list and find its competition and feel confident that the competition will receive the same advice.<p>Again, I don&#x27;t know what he does.
