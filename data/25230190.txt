Everyone incorrectly assumes each branch acts in perfect harmony with the rest of the org, when it is anything but.<p>You would need to remove the incentives of selfish actions and backstabbing to see any of the efficiencies with a formal hierarchy.  Otherwise each branch becomes a metaphorical tumor.')
