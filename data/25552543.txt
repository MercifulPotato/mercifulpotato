Item(by='wiredfool', descendants=None, kids=[25552855, 25553587], score=None, time=1609090019, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25551976, text='There are actually two exclusions for countries on the treaty list, one is the first ~100k of income, and the other is a tax credit for taxes paid in the other country.<p>So, if you happen to be an expat in a higher tax country (which is certainly Europe), then you won&#x27;t owe taxes in the US. You still have to file, which can cost $500 for an accountant to make sure everything is correct, but owing taxes isn&#x27;t really an issue.')
