Item(by='avian', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601877314, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24684640, text='One role of product churn is to reduce the impact of unbiased reviews and make comparisons with the competition harder. If the model you&#x27;re buying is only available in your specific region for a month or two it&#x27;s impossible to know beforehand what kind of lemon you&#x27;re buying.<p>My recent example: local consumer association published a comprehensive test of some 250 printer models in July. Out of those I could find 6 models I could actually buy, none of which met my needs.')
