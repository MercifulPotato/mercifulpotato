Item(by='balabaster', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607630376, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25374782, text='Finding peace in the day-to-day is a great way to find your way back to a good place. Allow yourself to do anything or nothing at all and not feel guilty about not being productive or even checking stuff off your to-do list. In fact, look at your to-do list and ask yourself if it&#x27;s critical to your existence or happiness. If it doesn&#x27;t bring you joy or ensure your continued existence - i.e. pays the bills, delete it from the list.')
