Item(by='bleepblorp', descendants=None, kids=[25216416], score=None, time=1606357883, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25215842, text='Suppose, hypothetically, that someone with malicious intent installed an advertising billboard, next to your home, that falsely accused you of having committed a horrific crime. Suppose, further, that you lost your job and your family because of the false allegations on that billboard. Would you work to get that billboard taken down or would you put up your own billboard to deny the allegations and call it a day?<p>Proving disinformation wrong does not make it go away and the answer to malicious speech is not more speech.')
