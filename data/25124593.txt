If we&#x27;re going to take a sober look at where to focus our efforts, this:<p><i>&quot;Global aviation’s contribution to the climate crisis was growing fast before the Covid-19 pandemic, with emissions jumping by 32% from 2013-18&quot;</i><p>Should be:<p><i>&quot;Global aviation’s contribution to the climate crisis was growing fast before the Covid-19 pandemic. Aviation&#x27;s contribution to global CO2 emissions rose from 1.3% to 1.9% from 2013-18&quot;</i><p>But the latter doesn&#x27;t attract clicks so much. Transportation overall accounts for 28% of greenhouse gas emissions [1]. That&#x27;s the story that matters.<p>There&#x27;s certainly a point here about wealthy nations and wealthy people being responsible for a lion&#x27;s share of the problem, but really, everyone knows that.
