Item(by='ocdtrekkie', descendants=None, kids=[25093739], score=None, time=1605374168, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25093675, text='Ultimately, your first and foremost need is to move to a different email address: It&#x27;s the hardest thing to change, it&#x27;ll take you the longest. Get your own domain name, and set up an email address on that domain name. (I recommend Fastmail, but you can usually just do some rudimentary forwarding of it to your Gmail if you want too.)<p>Everything else you can mostly download from Google in advance, but it&#x27;s key to update all of your accounts to not refer to a Gmail address. In the event of losing your Gmail address, you might lose any related account that requires access to it to verify your identity or login.')
