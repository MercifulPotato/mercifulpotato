Item(by='Spivak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601644731, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24662059, text='The Flatpak model is good for &quot;pet apps&quot; where you have a company, team, project whose entire job is to make this one app. Relying on upstream to fix security vulns is less of an issue because these companies have incentive to fix them. Spotify, Teams, Skype, Bitwarden, Slack are all pet apps.<p>The Flatpak model is terrible for distros who are already strained for people and who benefit from factoring as much of the work as possible out of each application so you only have to update one place and push out a fix everywhere.')
