Item(by='betamaxthetape', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605643541, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25127194, text='Take a look at the Creative Commons licenses[1]. All but one of them (CC0 - the Public Domain one) require any copies &#x2F; remixes of the work to display attribution. As the creator, you can specify how you want the attribution, such as with a link back to the website. (There&#x27;s a fall-back in the license to a default attribution if you don&#x27;t specify how you want the attribution).<p>Creative Commons have a license chooser[2] to help people find the right license for them. It asks two simple questions:<p>* Do you want people to be able to share adapted versions of your work?<p>* Do you want people to be able to use your work commercially?<p>And from your answers to those, recommends a suitable Creative Commons license.<p>Of course, there are plenty of other licenses out there, but Creative Commons is good because it is widely known and used.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;creativecommons.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;creativecommons.org&#x2F;</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;creativecommons.org&#x2F;choose&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;creativecommons.org&#x2F;choose&#x2F;</a>')
