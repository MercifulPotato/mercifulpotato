The entire Orwell canon was banned, of course, but you could still hear it on BBC broadcast from abroad (the authorities tried to jam the signal, with various levels of success).<p>We liked 1984. Forbidden fruit tastes the best, but it also spoke to us. Those empty slogans and lingering fear of the Secret Police did translate fairly well into real life behind the Curtain.')
