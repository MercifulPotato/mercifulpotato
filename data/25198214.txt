Item(by='apetersonBFI', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606224427, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25196820, text='Any expression or series of gates of only ORs has the non-decreasing property, changing an input from 0 to 1 can never change the result from 1 to 0. So you can&#x27;t implement any gate that needs that using ORs.<p>Similarly, any collection of XOR gates is simply a summation mod 2.')
