Item(by='jiggawatts', descendants=None, kids=[24668067, 24668831, 24670750], score=None, time=1601679358, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24666751, text='Just to be pedantic: You can never smell the alcohol (ethanol) in any alcoholic drink. It&#x27;s a tasteless and odourless chemical. What you taste or smell are the <i>congeners</i>, which are other organic molecules that are the byproducts of fermentation. In the case of whisky, you also have the additional flavour and odour chemicals leached from wood barrels.<p>You&#x27;re basically smelling &quot;charred wood extract&quot; when smelling Whiskey.')
