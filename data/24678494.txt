Item(by='sebmellen', descendants=None, kids=[24678631, 24678643], score=None, time=1601814484, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24678402, text='The line where free speech ends is pretty clearly delineated, and that&#x27;s when it crosses over into criminal or violent action in the &quot;world of atoms&quot;.<p>Almost all of your examples can be prosecuted based on their <i>direct effect on the real world</i> (or representation of a crime, such as with child abuse videos), not the <i>content of their speech</i>. The difference is crucial.<p>To take things to an extreme, if I say &quot;all XXXX should be killed&quot;, that&#x27;s very different from actually killing all members of the XXXX group, or even plotting to do so.')
