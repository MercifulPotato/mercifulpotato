I was hooked. Espionage after reading him would never be the same again. I can attribute my Cold War &#x27;philia&#x27; just to him, I reckon. I am yet to read his autobiography.<p>Other than some well-made new TV works based on his work like The Night Manager and The Little Drummer Girl, you might want to check out the excellent works on TV in the 80s and 90s; usually done by the BBC.<p>He was the master of unhurried, ruminating, and unostentatious spy craft writing. Tasteful.')
