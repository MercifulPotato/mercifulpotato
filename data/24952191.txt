Item(by='_ph_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604158099, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24951677, text='That is mostly an illusion. Over time, the day cycle aligns with the sun, not what the clock says. People in places which are on the west side of the corresponding timezone tend to do things later than those on the east side. And since introduction of the DST, a lot of countries shifted their typical timepoints towards &quot;later&quot; to compensate for it.')
