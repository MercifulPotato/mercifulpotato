Item(by='CJefferson', descendants=None, kids=[25607254], score=None, time=1609533943, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25605473, text='I think the problem is requiring excessive polish in academic papers.<p>This causes two problems:<p>1) It&#x27;s easier to polish something which is incremental.<p>2) It&#x27;s easier to achieve the look of polish by purposefully hiding issues and smoothing over edges.<p>I&#x27;d personally like every paper about a technique or algorithm to include as many problems where it does terribly as where it does well -- this is annoyingly uncommon. Also, people shouldn&#x27;t be afraid to discuss all the nasty unfinished bits but in practice if you mention them reviews say &quot;well, go fix that before acceptance&quot;.')
