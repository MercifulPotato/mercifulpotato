Item(by='MaxBarraclough', descendants=None, kids=[25291508], score=None, time=1606997976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25278781, text='As an aside, I really dislike this pattern of deliberately misleading headline.<p>Make it appear that you&#x27;re asserting a point of fact, <i>Google illegally spied on workers before firing them</i>, knowing that&#x27;s how the reader will read it, then modify it at the last minute to dial down your commitment to the claim, with <i>US labor board alleges</i>.<p>You&#x27;d never use this structure in conversation, you&#x27;d just say it the natural way, <i>US labor board alleges Google illegally spied on workers before firing them</i>.<p>Unfortunately even the BBC uses this style sometimes.')
