An established hardware company has to sell what it has, and shouldn’t try to sell what it doesn’t have (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Osborne_effect" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Osborne_effect</a> is the classical example of why not to sell what you don’t have (yet))<p>(if you don’t have anything to sell yet, selling what you don’t have is fine.
