Item(by='dylan604', descendants=None, kids=[25284141], score=None, time=1606957023, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25281919, text='The original discovery request may be something along the lines of &quot;please submit all communications that occurred in an electronic format via printed copies, blah blah leaglese&quot;. You then wait until the last day allowed in the discovery request, and then send over what ever print outs you have decided to release. If opposing counsel feels you&#x27;ve left something out (intentionally or otherwise), they can&#x2F;will go to a judge to get a warrant. That warrant will not state &quot;collect print outs of communications from personal devices&quot;. It will state &quot;collect any personal electronic devices&quot;.')
