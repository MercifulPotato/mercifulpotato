Item(by='andrew_', descendants=None, kids=[24849063, 24849344], score=None, time=1603294690, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24848841, text='When working to maintain a large open source project with hundreds of monthly contributors, in which those forks&#x2F;branches contain a significant number of commits themselves, squashing is preferable when merging to master. We only really care about the end result of a Pull Request (in the context of Github), and we use that to assemble changelogs and release notes.<p>There isn&#x27;t a one-size-fits-all doctrine here. It&#x27;s subjective based on the goals, size, contributor base, etc of an individual project and the leadership of the project.')
