The number of people passing by a location is anonymous in itself.<p>It seems however that in the case of the Canadian mall implementation (or more precisely their solution provider) they stored more than this number, losing the anonymous property of a system they sold as an &quot;Anonymous Video Analytics&quot;.<p>In my opinion, something should be called anonymous if and only if you cannot go back from the stored data to a personally identifiable information.')
