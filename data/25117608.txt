Item(by='jefftk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605564295, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25115709, text='Not quite: when you pass &quot;-I&quot; curl makes a HEAD request instead of a GET request:<p><pre><code>    $ curl -sS -v -I \\\n        https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.jefftk.com 2&gt;&amp;1 | grep &#x27;^&gt;&#x27;\n    &gt; HEAD &#x2F; HTTP&#x2F;1.1\n    &gt; Host: www.jefftk.com\n    &gt; User-Agent: curl&#x2F;7.58.0\n    &gt; Accept: *&#x2F;*\n    \n    $ curl -sS -v -D- -o&#x2F;dev&#x2F;null \\\n        https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.jefftk.com 2&gt;&amp;1 | grep &#x27;^&gt;&#x27;\n    &gt; GET &#x2F; HTTP&#x2F;1.1\n    &gt; Host: www.jefftk.com\n    &gt; User-Agent: curl&#x2F;7.58.0\n    &gt; Accept: *&#x2F;*\n    &gt;\n</code></pre>\nIt turns out that, often enough to be worth worrying about, servers do not return the same headers in response to a HEAD request as a GET request, so I always send a GET request when debugging strange behavior.')
