Item(by='dsr_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611575367, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25897956, text='The charitable explanation is that you don&#x27;t know what a killfile is. It is a personally created and maintained list of subject lines and&#x2F;or poster addresses that you don&#x27;t want to see. They can be limited to specific groups or be universal; they can be used to remove entire threads or just the messages that match.<p>In other words, it&#x27;s a personal filter on incoming information. It&#x27;s Joo Janta Peril-Sensitive Sunglasses. If it is censorship, it is a censor who works for you, obeys your commands, and never imposes your opinions on anyone else.<p>And it&#x27;s not particularly good for spam, nor does anyone think that is its purpose. A killfile works against persistent trolls and idiots.')
