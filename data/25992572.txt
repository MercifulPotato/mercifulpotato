Item(by='cgrealy', descendants=None, kids=[25996770, 25993123, 25993954, 25993019, 25993480, 25992954], score=None, time=1612207744, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25990929, text='As a general rule: you (and your company) are not special.<p>If you look at an industry way of doing something and decide it’s wrong, you should have solid reasoning to back it up.<p>I’ve seen so many companies decide that they have some unique requirement to do X that every other company out there doing X doesn’t have. It leads to reinventing the wheel, but generally in a worse way.<p>All that said, the most dangerous words in engineering are “because we’ve always done it that way”.')
