Item(by='boublepop', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603874127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24911312, text='Another good way to get through the mental lock is to tell yourself your going to intentionally do a bad job.<p>Finding it har to start up writing that article because you can’t quite find an angle or conjoint up motivation? Just write a really bad version first. That gets you going and builds momentum, and then only on your second version try to improve quality.<p>The equivalent of “I’m going to put my running shoes on and walk around the block”. It puts you in the situation without worrying about performance.')
