Item(by='stale2002', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609470420, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25600125, text='Holding enough currency to have a small amount of hedged safety against risk of that currency changing in value is not really a good enough reason to say that a country would be able to &quot;force&quot; &quot;taxpapers to be subject to its currency&quot;.<p>Even if we say that people would hold some of that original currency, there could still be a significant benefit to transferring most of your wealth to a deflationary currency.
