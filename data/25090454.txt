Which is why the ballots tend to be long.<p>Granted, I don&#x27;t think this makes them very complicated; my ballot had plenty of offices this year, and it was essentially a one-page double sided bubble sheet, with accompanying voter guides sent in the mail. It&#x27;s not very hard.')
