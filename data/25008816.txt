Conversions can pretty easily be done for a very reasonable cost. There have been 500 eur and the like conversions incl batteries. Toyota&#x2F;Lexus hybrid drivetrains are very cheap and plentiful and make great conversions. Same goes for Nissan Leaf and Tesla motors. Lots of options for batteries as well for much more reasonable prices if you’re willing to scour.<p>My own project is a Lexus GS450h hybrid that I bought with a broken ICE for very cheap. The two electric motors in the hybrid transmission are capable of about 220kW. And there’s little to be modified at the car besides de-ice-ing and adding batteries.<p>[0] - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openinverter.org&#x2F;forum&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openinverter.org&#x2F;forum&#x2F;</a>')
