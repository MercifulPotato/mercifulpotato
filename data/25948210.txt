Item(by='nostromo', descendants=None, kids=[25948408], score=None, time=1611864203, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25947824, text='This is a pet peeve of mine about star ratings.<p>Visually, allowing 1 to 5 stars inflates the way ratings look, because the first star is always present, even for the worst products.<p>Imagine if it was pie chart or a bar chart: the lowest rating would look like 20%, not 0%.<p>Or imagine a product where 50% of people gave it the lowest rating (1 star) and the rest gave it the highest rating (5 stars).  This would be presented as a 3&#x2F;5 star item, which visually appears quite a bit better than its real rating (50%).')
