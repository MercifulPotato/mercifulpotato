Item(by='ljm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609205162, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564882, text='If all your configs follow the XDG spec then you can turn ~&#x2F;.config itself into the repo,and then use .gitignore as a whitelist:<p><pre><code>    &#x2F;*\n    !git&#x2F;config\n    !tmux&#x2F;tmux.conf\n</code></pre>\nI do this and clone my repo directly to .config on a new machine. The only hold out was tmux but that&#x27;s solved by installing or compiling one of the recent versions released this year (3b&#x2F;3c). I&#x27;ll still use manual symlinks for things like bash, but they&#x27;re outliers.<p>Works great for me.')
