Item(by='eecc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608488757, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25487916, text='Well, there’s something to humans - testers in particular, who seem to be wired like hackers rather that programmers - that is superior to any fuzzing or “QuickCheck”  approach. It’s the intuition, the art of second-guessing the mental model of the programmer that authored the code under test. That’s why it’s such a shameful tragedy to waste human capacity on scripted tests, the human factor should be authoring the automation.')
