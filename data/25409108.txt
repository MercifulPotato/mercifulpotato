Item(by='Someone1234', descendants=None, kids=[25410113, 25410812, 25411733, 25420870, 25414762, 25416806, 25412927], score=None, time=1607886219, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25408587, text='Simply make text <i>bigger</i>.<p>125% DPI or 125% &quot;zoom&quot; depending on the OS&#x2F;application help with eye strain massively, and it surprises me how few colleagues are willing to <i>try</i> it (e.g. &quot;I can see perfectly, why would I increase text size?!&quot;).<p>Between that and lowering a monitor&#x27;s brightness to below 50% (often around 35% depending on brand), are my biggest tips. Windows now has a blue light filter built right in (Settings -&gt; System -&gt; Display -&gt; Night Light).')
