Item(by='open-source-ux', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611688379, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25917198, text='Not exactly jaw-dropping but...when I tried an iPad with an Apple Pen and the Procreate app for the first time, I was hugely impressed by the drawing experience. The pen was smooth, responsive and sensitive to pressure and tilt. This felt like the most natural experience created so far to mimicking the pencil and other mediums from real life.<p>I never imagined that the iPad would overtake traditional digital drawing tablet screens in the popularity stakes. But today, you would be hard pressed to find an illustrator who doesn&#x27;t have an iPad and Apple Pen (and a copy of Procreate).')
