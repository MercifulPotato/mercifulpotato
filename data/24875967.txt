MD5 is sufficiently broken that finding such messages is easy: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;thereal1024&#x2F;python-md5-collision" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;thereal1024&#x2F;python-md5-collision</a><p>Two different hashing schemes applied in <i>parallel</i> is better.
