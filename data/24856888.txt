Item(by='beagle3', descendants=None, kids=[24857418], score=None, time=1603366057, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24856237, text='Were you exercising on these 3 days?<p>My experience (from longer fasts - never felt anything like that after 3 days) was that muscles that aren&#x27;t in heavy use do indeed get metabolized for energy (which makes a lot of sense - maintaining muscles costs energy; if you&#x27;re not using them and running on fat reserves, why keep them?)<p>However, if I exercise while fasting, I hardly lose any muscle at all.<p>One interesting thing is that unaerobic exercise, such as boxing or rope skipping becomes harder for me while fasted; but aerobic exercise, specifically, running - becomes easier and it feels like I can keep running forever (whereas unfasted I usually feel my ability degrading near the end of a 10km run)')
