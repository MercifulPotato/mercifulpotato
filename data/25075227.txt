Item(by='scrozier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605216105, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25073155, text='Scientific studies are validly subject to inspection and comment. Just because the authors state something doesn&#x27;t mean they&#x27;ve reached the correct conclusion, that the study was well designed, that there are not alternate explanations, etc. IOW, it&#x27;s perfectly acceptable, and useful, to critique a scientific study.
