Item(by='bananaface', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603477411, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24872254, text='I would say: technical debt is anything that makes the system more expensive to maintain (and grow) over time, and becomes more expensive to fix the longer you leave it. If it&#x27;s just a bad or incomplete aspect of the system, that&#x27;s not sufficient. It has to become more expensive over time.<p>So e.g. lack of functionality isn&#x27;t tech debt, but a bad abstraction is usually tech debt.')
