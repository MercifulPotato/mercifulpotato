Item(by='arghwhat', descendants=None, kids=[25974638], score=None, time=1612051212, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25971395, text='The position of the Wayland project is that security isolation requires sandboxing. Otherwise, applications can do whatever you can do, including changing all your personal config files and scripts.<p>The security considerations in Wayland are primarily aimed at not being a weak link undermining sandboxing efforts, which is the case with X11.<p>Without sandboxing, writing to  e.g. ~&#x2F;.profile would in most cases be enough for a malicious application to take over the machine. Equivalents apply to all platforms.')
