Item(by='DrScump', descendants=None, kids=[24869703], score=None, time=1603438892, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24862515, text='<p><pre><code>  In the good old days the soft serve and shake machines were taken apart every night and every piece was washed and bleach-sanitized by hand. \n</code></pre>\nI was a McD manager in my youth.<p>Not only were the internals fully disassembled and sanitized, they were left to soak in the solution overnight. All that was left behind was the cylindrical plastic chamber, sanitized and left to air dry overnight.<p>The messiest part was lubricating the O-rings with a petroleum jelly. Failure to remove all that when cleaning seemed like a good vector for microbes.<p>The worst job of all was cleaning the fryer vats.')
