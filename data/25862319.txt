Item(by='stu2010', descendants=None, kids=[25864649, 25862672], score=None, time=1611254370, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25862264, text='Relational databases enable some very flexible data access patterns. Once you shard, you lose a lot of that flexibility. If you move away from a relational model, you lose even more flexibility and start having to do much more work in your application layer, and usually start having to use more resources and developer time every step of the way.<p>The productivity enabled by having one master RDBMS is a big deal, and if they can buy commodity servers that satisfy their requirement, this seems like a fine way to operate.')
