Item(by='sbergot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605775512, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25135760, text='It is not whether you need a database or not. It is &quot;how configurable&#x2F;dynamic things should be&quot;. More configurability usually means more power to your users and more pain to maintain the software. So I usually go to the least amount of configurability and then add things when required.\nThe same goes for abstractions (you often need more of them when things becomes more configurable)')
