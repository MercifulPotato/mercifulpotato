The completion, SMS, had the highest cost of data transmission on the planet, so not hard to see the reason for its growth.<p>WhatsApp squandered their early lead with slow development and so competitors like Line, Viber, Wechat and Telegram all came later and added features and took market share.  If you are an international business person, you have all these messengers installed, and more. The switching cost is low, and the only real trouble is agreeing with someone on how to connect.')
