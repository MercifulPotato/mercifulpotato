Most of the engineering classes I took had weekly problem sets that, yes, took at least a solid evening to do.<p>The way my undergraduate did credits was the (theoretical) number of hours a week they took between lectures, lab, recitation, and study&#x2F;problem sets. Typical classes added up to 12 with usually 4 hours of the first 2 categories and 8 hours of the last. Classes varied but that wasn&#x27;t <i>too</i> far off for the typical class. (Though there were some real time sink outliers.)')
