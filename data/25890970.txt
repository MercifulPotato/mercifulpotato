Item(by='mbeex', descendants=None, kids=[25893781, 25891711], score=None, time=1611485220, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25888757, text='Not this simple. Refactoring might mean invariance, but the more comprehensive your tests are, the more they also test the moving parts. That means, they have to be changed too. Many people forget the maintenance costs (and this has a much broader meaning than the refactoring example). Tests themselves have to be considered as a software (or more general) artifact with a cost.')
