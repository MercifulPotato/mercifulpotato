Item(by='pabue', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607686858, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25384550, text='Haxplore is a little web app I created because I didn&#x27;t like to click around the HN interface to read submission and discussions. I also wanted to experiment with different ways of implementing keyboard control in web apps and HN seemed like the perfect test subject. So maybe it&#x27;s useful for some of you too.<p>The most important features are:<p>- Link detection in comments (Use your number keys to open them directly)<p>- Better readbility and formatting of comments (Like highlighted quotes)<p>- Hotkeys to perform common actions<p>- Ability to open comments in a separate modal for reading long texts')
