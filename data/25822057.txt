Item(by='lmkg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610979926, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817913, text='In GA4, with Google Signals enabled, the &quot;identity signal&quot; is a psuedonymous identifier unique to your Google Account. I don&#x27;t know exactly if it&#x27;s a hash or a join key or what, but the point is it&#x27;s stable across devices and domains.<p>It does not directly contain PII. Google can join it to PII, website owners cannot.<p>It is <i>not</i> aggregate. You can pull the hit-level data from GA, including the identifiers being used.')
