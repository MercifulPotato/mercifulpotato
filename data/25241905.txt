Item(by='segfaultbuserr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606610468, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25238804, text='&gt; <i>Curious why specifically a Pi?</i><p>Not the OP, but I guess the power of Raspberry Pi is that everyone already has one - really useful if you want to do something in a hurry without anything extra. I&#x27;ve used a Pi as an emergency programmer on multiple occasions, as an SPI flash burner, as an SWD debugger, as an AVR programmer. You can even use the Pi as an USB-FDD bootdisk or emulate an IDE driver via its parallel interface.')
