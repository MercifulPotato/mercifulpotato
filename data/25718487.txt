Item(by='ethbr0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610306369, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25718177, text='The biggest difference I&#x27;ve found (moreso on stainless steel than cast iron) is food weight: which is to say, time food is left without moving.<p>Which makes sense. Essentially burnt-to-pan bits are food-pan interface, as opposed to food-oil-pan interface.<p>Give enough time, heavy food displaces oil and comes in direct contact with pan. Given enough movement, oil is able to reimpose itself between the two.')
