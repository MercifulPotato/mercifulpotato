Item(by='sangnoir', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611950080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25960740, text='Short-sellers <i>are part of the free market.</i> They are how the equation balances itself when trying to find the &quot;true value&quot;  of a concern, or at least an approximation.<p>Obviously the person who owns stock, or is set to earn billions when the share price reaches a certain level is going to be adversarial to someone whose actions result in the share price being depressed - even if that is the fair value.<p>&gt; Large funds making public bets against a company have a material impact on that company&#x27;s liquidity.<p>There are always bigger fish - and if the public bet is wrong, someone can, and will earn money at the funds&#x27; cost.')
