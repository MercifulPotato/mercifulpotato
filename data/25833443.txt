Item(by='imtringued', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611064861, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25829291, text='The fundamental problem with estimates is that the recipient believes that the estimate will always turn out to be true, which is obviously not correct.<p>So, why can&#x27;t we express the upper bound in combination with uncertainty?<p>Example: &quot;There is a 50% chance that the feature will be delivered within 1 month.&quot;<p>As time progresses confidence starts increasing and we get closer and closer to how much time it actually takes (be that 5 days, 5 months or 5 years). On the day the feature is completed, confidence is 100% because the real delivery date has become known.')
