Moderation doesn&#x27;t scale.  It&#x27;s NP-Hard or whatever.<p>Dynamical systems theory tells us that when you have a large densely-interconnected system (like YT) you get dramatic behavior.  In other words, YouTube is always going to create memetic storms and we can predict that (even though we can&#x27;t predict the exact content of the storms.)<p>Adding regulators won&#x27;t help.<p>Cybernetics revealed the <i>law of requisite variety</i>, to wit: &quot;the variety in the regulator must be equal to or greater than the variety in the system being regulated&quot;<p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;pespmc1.vub.ac.be&#x2F;ASC&#x2F;LAW_VARIE.html" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;pespmc1.vub.ac.be&#x2F;ASC&#x2F;LAW_VARIE.html</a><p>YouTube is doomed, the question is do we let it take us down with it?')
