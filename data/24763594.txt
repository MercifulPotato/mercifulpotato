Item(by='numlock86', descendants=None, kids=[24763781, 24763836, 24765725], score=None, time=1602579076, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24763470, text='That&#x27;s exactly the argument why you should go serverless. If all you do is keep a vanilla Linux distro running in a VM with occasional updates and some initial config-magic (webserver, certs, ip tables, ssh etc.) why even bother? The serverless isn&#x27;t going to be any different, other than it just runs. No need to make a cron for updates, no iptables, no certs or webserver-stuff ... just put your app on it and let it go. On the other hand, if you actually need to tinker with your OS, roll your own. But what you described is the prime example of serverless target audience.')
