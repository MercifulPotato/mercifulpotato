Where as only 10% of all plastic produced has been recycled.<p>The shift will be back towards reusables. Coke was once served in glass bottles - but centralized for increased profit due to invention — plastic was cheaper than glass to produce and transport.<p>Now, if the consumer provides their reusable, a company like Coke only needs to transport concentrate, which is far lighter than shipping a bottle of mostly water. That will also decrease the cost of the beverage to the end consumer.
