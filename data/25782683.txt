Item(by='Sharlin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610658972, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25781240, text='The D7000, like all system cameras, has a mechanical shutter it uses when taking photos. Even though technically ”rolling” as well, it is <i>much</i> faster than a typical electronic shutter due to the way a CMOS sensor must be read out. Phone cameras have no physical shutters and use e-shutter all the time, but your D7000 only resorts to it when shooting video.')
