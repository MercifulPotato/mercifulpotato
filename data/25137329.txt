Item(by='chhamilton', descendants=None, kids=[25139528], score=None, time=1605710942, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25130678, text='(Chromium dev here)<p>Yes, lots of sites are written with tight setInterval&#x2F;setTimeout loops, checking for modifications to the DOM, visibility, doing animations, etc. In most cases there are modern web APIs that do these things for you that don&#x27;t require polling (rAF, MutationObserver, IntersectionObserver, etc). We are not trying to penalize well-written sites, and if you have valid use cases that you feel are being unfairly penalized we&#x27;re more than happy to accept feedback!')
