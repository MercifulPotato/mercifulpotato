Item(by='sliken', descendants=None, kids=[25259265], score=None, time=1606771319, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25258967, text='I&#x27;ve written a benchmark to measure such thins and from what I can tell.<p>Each fast core has a L1D of 128KB.<p>The fast cores have a cluster with 12MB, cache misses to to main memory.<p>The slow cores have a 4MB L2.<p>The cache misses from the fast L2 can&#x27;t quite saturate the main memory systems (I believe it&#x27;s 8 channels of 16 bits).  So when all cores are busy you keep 12MB of L2 for fast, 4MB of L2 for the slow cores, and end up getting better throughput from the memory system since you are keeping all 8 channels busy.')
