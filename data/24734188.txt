Item(by='jon37', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602275868, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24734077, text='Consumers want a lot of things with negative externalities - goods that cost less because they&#x27;re produced with slave labor, transportation that emits greenhouse gases, etc. Their preference shouldn&#x27;t trump the obligation not to harm third parties.<p>Automated recommendations of a human-curated set of content - e.g. Netflix recommendations for its suite of programming - are much less objectionable, because they can&#x27;t amplify anything the organization has not intentionally decided to present.  It&#x27;s the combination of UGC and ML recommendations that presents problems.')
