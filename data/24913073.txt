Item(by='reificator', descendants=None, kids=[24913215], score=None, time=1603839749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24911312, text='This is less about the length of the task and more to do with simply identifying the first step to take.  You can see that by the example of starting to run by putting on your running shoes.<p>Advice like this frames procrastination as laziness that can be overcome by committing to less work, but that&#x27;s misleading.  If this helps you stop procrastinating I think it&#x27;s much more likely that you&#x27;re simply overwhelmed with choices.<p>Try listing out the things you want to do, and for each of those things list out just the first step, even if it&#x27;s more than 2 minutes long.  No more than that, don&#x27;t start writing down instructions for yourself, just stick to &quot;what do I do next here?&quot;<p>You might find out that your problem isn&#x27;t laziness, it&#x27;s unharnessed motivation that&#x27;s just pulling you in too many directions at once.')
