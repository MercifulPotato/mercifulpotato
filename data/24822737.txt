Item(by='pdonis', descendants=None, kids=[24822875], score=None, time=1603072606, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24822552, text='<i>&gt; I think the shell theorem says that the gravitational forces cancel if you are on the inside of a hollow sphere and all mass is on the surface.</i><p>No, it&#x27;s stronger than that.
