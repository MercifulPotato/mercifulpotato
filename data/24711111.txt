Item(by='jk700', descendants=None, kids=[24711982, 24711353, 24711273], score=None, time=1602095050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24710867, text='MITM like that still works for most https websites because of the automatic domain validation by ACME-based certificate authorities. The only caveat is that now an attacker has to get a valid certificate, so first he has to do MITM on the route from the datacenters where CAs run validators to the datacenter where the website is hosted, which for most websites today is likely a long route crossing many countries, after that an attacker gets the exact capabilities as with MITMing http.')
