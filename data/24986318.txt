Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=[24987048], score=None, time=1604461386, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24984950, text='Water, sewer, trash pickup, and power have very easy to understand quality metrics, and can be easily regulated.<p>An internet connection is not so easy to measure. You could have a very well provisioned and maintained local loop, and poor connectivity from there. I strongly support local loop unbundling --- you can regulate the local loop in simple terms, and let competition manage the messy network interconnection bit.')
