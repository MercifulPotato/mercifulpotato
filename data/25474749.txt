Item(by='kazinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608348808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25474630, text='When you&#x27;re screen sharing, you could receive company notifications that you don&#x27;t want everyone.<p>Firstly, you don&#x27;t necessarily coworkers to see some notifications. A manager might not want underlings to see their notifications, for instance, like that such and such is going to be laid off.<p>Secondly, who says the screencast is always for coworkers? You don&#x27;t want a client or investor to see internal company notifications.<p>So, yes, there is a use case for suppressing notifications even if you have no personal logins.')
