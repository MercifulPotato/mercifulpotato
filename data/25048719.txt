But she can&#x27;t do the same for online voting.  She&#x27;s not going to audit the software used.  She&#x27;s not going to verify network integrity, or authenticate packets.<p>The manual processes are much better for <i>transparency</i> and <i>verifiability</i>.  And we really badly need those qualities right now, when you have to be able to <i>prove</i> to the other side that the vote was legit, and be able to <i>prove</i> that every claim otherwise is false.')
