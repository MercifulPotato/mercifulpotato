Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=[24895170], score=None, time=1603643260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887100, text='I think you&#x27;ve confused the US system with the UK system.<p>The US has always had lawyers; a great deal of the founding fathers were themselves lawyers.<p>Fees were never fixed by legislation, generally cases were simpler back then so they didn&#x27;t have or need time-based billing (that was a relatively recent development).<p>Individuals already can represent themselves in small claims courts.
