The license drafter had their own goals in mind, but their terms get repurposed by others.<p>Even very popular <i>permissive</i> licenses have been written with contribution front-and-center in mind. See, for example, the contribution-related terms of Apache 2. The definition of contribution. The built-in CLA. The compromise on scope of the patent grant.')
