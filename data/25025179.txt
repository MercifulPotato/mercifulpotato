Item(by='JosephRedfern', descendants=None, kids=[25025223], score=None, time=1604836740, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25025066, text='Fun fact: 5&#x2F;6 years ago, a bunch of Huawei phones came with world readable &#x2F;dev&#x2F;fb0 (framebuffer) device files.<p>This made it trivial for any application to read the display, totally bypassing any Android screenshot&#x2F;screen recording API (not that one existed at the time).
