Item(by='anonypla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607510268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25344358, text='One should also note that, even if you use ODoH, eSNI and even Tor (or any VPN service), your ISP could still reliably fingerprint your web access activity at the source using deep learning with over 96% accuracy as shown in this study (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;distrinet.cs.kuleuven.be&#x2F;software&#x2F;tor-wf-dl&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;distrinet.cs.kuleuven.be&#x2F;software&#x2F;tor-wf-dl&#x2F;</a>).<p>So while ODoH is a good thing (and also recommended in this study which has shown the weaknesses of DoH&#x2F;DoT <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.esat.kuleuven.be&#x2F;cosic&#x2F;publications&#x2F;article-3153.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.esat.kuleuven.be&#x2F;cosic&#x2F;publications&#x2F;article-3153...</a>) and is very similar to DNS over Tor with a DNS hidden service resolver (which Cloudflare also provides). It won&#x27;t prevent a skilled and motivated adversary from determining your activity and possibly apply censorship.<p>I would guess that a solution to mitigate these would be to use an hybrid solution of VPN over Tor (or Tor over VPN) while also using DNS over Tor or ODoH and eSNI.')
