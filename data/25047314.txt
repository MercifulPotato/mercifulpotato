If you&#x27;ve flown on an MD-11, you&#x27;ve flown on an &#x27;aerodynamically flawed&#x27; aircraft. Most real systems are dynamically unstable without some kind of controller (implying software) in the loop.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Relaxed_stability" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Relaxed_stability</a>')
