Item(by='cle', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607798904, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25400170, text='I think a misunderstood part of Emacs is that this is exactly what it is—-it’s just glue, but instead of being in a command-oriented environment, it’s in an editor-oriented environment. You run the same Unix tools but glue their results together into a text buffer with a full programming language that then lets you programmatically edit the results.<p>People joke about Emacs being an OS but it’s really just a different kind of shell, oriented towards text editing instead of issuing commands.')
