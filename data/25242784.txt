I feel like if you don&#x27;t have your content in a relatively straightforward form like plaintext or markdown or html, you&#x27;re gonna lose it eventually. \nI was considering going raw html, but Jekyll ultimately does have some convenient features while still keeping my content accessible to me forever in markdown.\nIt is also very convenient to serve from a github.io.<p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.philipzucker.com&#x2F;i-moved-my-blog-from-wordpress&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.philipzucker.com&#x2F;i-moved-my-blog-from-wordpress&#x2F;</a>')
