Item(by='rodw', descendants=None, kids=[25537191], score=None, time=1608866755, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25522969, text='On second thought I do think Paul Westerberg has a strong melancholy streak.<p>At first I had stuff &quot;Waitress in the Sky&quot; or &quot;Tommy Gets His Tonsils Out&quot; or &quot;Alex Chilton&quot; or whatever in mind, but now that I think about it there&#x27;s a dark undercurrent to plenty of the songs in the core Replacements canon: &quot;Hold my Life&quot;, &quot;Unsatisified&quot;, &quot;I Will Dare&quot;, &quot;Bastards of Young&quot;, &quot;I&#x27;ll Be You&quot;, etc. - not to mention of a handful of slow tempo tracks with obviously dark themes.<p>I don&#x27;t normally associate the Replacements with mopey music but in retrospect  I&#x27;m not sure why. It&#x27;s kinda all over the place, under a very thin veneer of drunken cynicism.')
