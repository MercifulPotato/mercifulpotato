Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=[25241181, 25241213], score=None, time=1606603278, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25241103, text='The trick is to avoid trusting parties that have incentives to abuse that trust and means to do so. Free market working the way it does, sooner or later one of such entities will abuse that trust.<p>So, baked beans are probably OK in terms of SIGINT. Depending on how well food regulations are enforced in your area, I might or might not worry about the edibility of them, though. But on-line services are definitely suspect with respect to data handling. Doubly so, if they pop up where they shouldn&#x27;t be in the first place - like e.g. IoT - as that&#x27;s already evidence of a business model built on abusive relationship.')
