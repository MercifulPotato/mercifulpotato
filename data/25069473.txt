Item(by='spyke112', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605186930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25069131, text='Regarding observables, specifically the ones from HttpClient, i always just do a .toPromise(), and use async&#x2F;await. My rule of thumb is that if i expect a stream of events use observables, but if it&#x27;s a one of call, like a http request really is, then i use promises. If i need to memoize the data from the request, i do it in a service, and call through that.')
