But with YAML!<p>FCGI really is a &quot;serverless&quot; environment. Launches an application when called for. Starts more copies of the application if there are enough requests. Shuts down idle copies when not needed. Starts a fresh copy if one crashes. You can even scale with load balancers. Really, that&#x27;s most of what you need to get work done.')
