Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=[25982637], score=None, time=1612116028, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25980236, text='There’s a very specific legal definition of what a margin account is.<p>Robinhood accounts are Regulation T (aka Reg T) margin accounts by default. They have limits, yes, but they are very much margin accounts. That’s how they make their UX work.<p>The point is that typical user activity will be performed on margin, transparently. The flurry of synchronized activity caused a larger than normal draw on their margin, which further strained their working capital.')
