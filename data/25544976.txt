Item(by='Natales', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609004737, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25543955, text='After years of being beaten by customers with stories like these, I learnt to treat InfoSec and Compliance teams as <i>finite state machines</i>, particularly at banks and other financial institutions. Learn not to question the sacred spreadsheet, or debate the merits of a request. It&#x27;s pointless, and you keep rolling your eyes will only end up with you at the optometrist.<p>Instead, treat compliance like part of your API. Ensure your product delivers on the expected answer, while continuously improving the security of your products in the parts that are not directly visible.')
