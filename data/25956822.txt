U.S. FDA Title 21 CFR Part 11 is very strict as to what a medical device company needs to do, and it&#x27;s not easy to meet those requirements. Most people do not realize that even in the early startup stage, if you build something that is a medical device, there are rules to follow (mostly about traceability: you have to know what you&#x27;ve built, using what parts, and where those came from).<p>Shameless plug: I am the solo founder of PartsBox (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;partsbox.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;partsbox.com&#x2F;</a>), which helps a lot with managing traceability when building electronics.')
