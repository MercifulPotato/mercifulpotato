Item(by='ortusdux', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611167301, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25832980, text='I had not thought about the possibility of a connection between locust and the increasing popularity of no-till farming.  As I understand it, tilling is one of the primary causes of the decrease in locust swarm population and frequency over the last 100 years.  No-till is on the rise, reaching 21% in 2017.  I wonder if we will start to see more swarms in the future.')
