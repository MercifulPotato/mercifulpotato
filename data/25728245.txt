Item(by='Kototama', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610369678, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25726588, text='Biggest problem I had with Haskell was once you know the language you also need to learn a pile of extensions that any serious project is using. Also there is a tendency in the community to always look for the &quot;best&quot; (abstract) solution. It makes the whole ecosystem fast changing.<p>I would prefer a more stable platform designed for engineers , something like Clojure but with types. Ocaml has a small community and Scala brings unnecessary complexity with its support for OOP.')
