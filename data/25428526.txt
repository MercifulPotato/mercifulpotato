Item(by='mangecoeur', descendants=None, kids=[25428841], score=None, time=1608024432, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25428426, text='This point was also made in David Attenborough’s recent climate documentary: the point is not about palm oil but about rainforest protection. If there is no protection, and if people can’t make a living another way, the forest will be cut for whatever makes money. The most tragic example i’ve seen is rainforest turned into those wood chips they put around potted plants. Is that a high value commodity? No. But it makes &gt;0 money and if the value given to the wild forest is zero, then it will happen.')
