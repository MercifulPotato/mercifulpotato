Item(by='playingchanges', descendants=None, kids=[24682306], score=None, time=1601823612, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24679019, text='The ‘more of what you already are’ idea seems very reductionist and unhelpful to me.<p>There are definitely real risks that need to be made known around pre existing mental conditions and psychedelic use. Those are appreciated.<p>But your thesis suggests that transformative experiences are unlikely to happen which from personal experience I can say is false. The impact of a breakthrough psychedelic experience can be profoundly positive especially for those starting from a less enlightened place.<p>BUT everyone knows that guy who has the “I’m Jesus” moment instead of “we’re all Jesus” and it’s usually easy to see it coming in retrospect.')
