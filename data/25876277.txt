Item(by='anigbrowl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611349105, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25876214, text='When you&#x27;re driving, there are two kinds of accidents; the ones that happen suddenly with no warning, and the ones where you realize things are going wrong but you still have some control over your vehicle. In the latter case, going off the road or being involved in a collision is still very unpleasant, but you can mitigate a lot of the damage as long as you don&#x27;t panic. In many cases you can even get back on the road and resume your journey safely.')
