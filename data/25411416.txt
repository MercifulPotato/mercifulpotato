Item(by='combatentropy', descendants=None, kids=[25415844, 25411971], score=None, time=1607901056, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407905, text='&gt; What is wrong with a yes response to &quot;do you want to eat pizza or pasta?&quot;<p>It doesn&#x27;t answer the question. The questioner is asking for your help in making a decision. If you are completely neutral about the two, then most people answer with the phrase, &quot;Either one is fine&quot;. Saying just &quot;yes&quot; sounds like you misunderstood the question --- unless you say it with a wry smile, showing that you mean to be funny in your answer.<p>(I suppose occasionally someone could ask the question with a different inflection, with the last word at a higher pitch, meaning, &quot;Are either of these fine with you?&quot; rather than, &quot;Please pick one.&quot;)<p>&gt; Similarly with the boy or girl question, &quot;no&quot; may be stillborn.<p>The sex is set at conception.')
