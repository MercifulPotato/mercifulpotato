Item(by='dmurray', descendants=None, kids=[24966281], score=None, time=1604310421, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24966165, text='You could store the hash of the downcased address plus a capitalization mask which tells you which letters to capitalize.<p>This works from a technical perspective, as letters with ambiguous capitalization (Turkish i, etc) aren&#x27;t allowed in emails. It&#x27;s a very minor privacy compromise: if a user has a very rare pattern of capitalization then an attacker with access to the database could identify their account. Negligible compared to the current standard.')
