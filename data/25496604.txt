Item(by='vardump', descendants=None, kids=[25506362], score=None, time=1608569168, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25495813, text='It&#x27;s true whenever the function call overhead for the comparison operation dominates. Inlining a trivial operation (often just one instruction) is one extreme.<p>At the other extreme L1C cache (typically just 32 kB) gets constantly trashed by numerous type specialized functions. Individually fast when microbenchmarked, but collectively slow.<p>That&#x27;s why profiling is a must when high performance is required. Microbenchmarks can be <i>very</i> misleading.')
