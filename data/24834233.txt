Item(by='efitz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603172434, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24834125, text='Without corporate personhood, you wouldn’t lose any of your rights as an owner (stockholder), employee, or officer of a corporation- you just wouldn’t get to magnify your individual rights by using the power of the corporation.  As an individual, you’d still get to speak your mind on whatever.  In my opinion, you just shouldn’t be allowed to take the enormous financial power of a corporation and turn it towards your own ends and call it “individual rights”.')
