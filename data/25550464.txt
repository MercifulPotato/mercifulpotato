Item(by='joshgel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609070602, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25550428, text='This is the beauty of randomization and blinding. Because both the participants and the doctors (the ‘double’ in double blind) don’t know who got the vaccine or placebo, anything you tell them to do is told equally to both sides. You can’t alter the trajectory of one group over the other because you don’t know the groups.')
