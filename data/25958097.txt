Item(by='contravariant', descendants=None, kids=[25959741], score=None, time=1611933664, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25957479, text='The problem there is that (assuming a source of <i>true</i> noise) X_b is a random variable, in fact if b is sufficiently high its distribution approximates your target distribution pretty well. So if you&#x27;re arguing &quot;instead of burn-in you can also use an approximate sample of your target distribution&quot; then that&#x27;s technically correct.<p>However <i>generating an approximate sample</i> is the exact problem that a MCMC process is trying to solve.')
