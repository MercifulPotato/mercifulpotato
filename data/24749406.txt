Item(by='AnthonyMouse', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602453127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24748871, text='&gt; Meanwhile people say capitalism drives innovation.<p>The saying is really that free market <i>competition</i> drives innovation.<p>Obviously patents and copyrights are government-issued monopolies, and monopolies are by definition lacking in competition.<p>The theory is that by granting the monopolies we get more innovation. Often the theory is wrong.<p>Especially when we allow the company to leverage the monopoly on the thing they actually invented into a monopoly on ancillary things that are only used in combination with that class of product.')
