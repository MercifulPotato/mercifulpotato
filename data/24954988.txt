Item(by='bachmeier', descendants=None, kids=[24955681], score=None, time=1604176534, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24949882, text='Requiring a functional programming approach when you teach makes your life <i>much</i> easier. I teach a computationally-intensive course for advanced economics PhD students. These days I have them think carefully through their problem, set up a recursion, and make one call to reduce. The vast majority of problems fit into this framework. I no longer have to work my way through 500-line monsters that look like entries in an obfuscated Perl competition. I read a few lines of code, I know exactly what it does, and I get on with my life.')
