Item(by='wittyreference', descendants=None, kids=[25908090, 25907925], score=None, time=1611604390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25907501, text='Viruses are believed to become less deadly over time when being less deadly offers an evolutionary advantage in ability to <i>spread</i>. If being more deadly and more spreadable have the same mechanism (e.g., avoiding spike protein-inhibiting antibodies), you can expect that old adage not to hold true over the short-term.')
