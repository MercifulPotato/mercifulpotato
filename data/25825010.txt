The makeup of a 30-checkout supermarket here (Sweden) is probably 20 self-checkout, 5-no-cash manual ones and 5 that can handle cash.<p>Many if not all small businesses like hotdog vendors, flea markets etc have completely dropped cash because it&#x27;s expensive and inconvenient.<p>Once the costs of cash management is higher than that of cards or digital transactions, there is no incentive.<p>It&#x27;s easy to say &quot;we need cash for privacy&quot; but if people  are doing better business without cash, it&#x27;s hard to convince them.')
