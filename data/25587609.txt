Item(by='d-sc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609371057, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25586883, text='Leadership is about getting people from point a to point b. Generally to do that you need trust, a good idea of where point a,b are, and what the path between them looks like.<p>Age is a component of trust, but only a small one and very dependent on the situation.<p>If you want to be leading someone who has more experience than you, best be sure you have trust and a rock solid understanding of the path you need to be on.')
