USB-C is of course reversible, and USB-A also is.<p>There’s no constrain on the number of keys. You can use 1 device with unlimited sites, both v1 and v2, because keys are generated on-the-fly and not stored.<p>(There’s a limit on number of resident keys, and we supported 50 in v1, while for example yubikeys support 25.
