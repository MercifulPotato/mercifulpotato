Item(by='KineticLensman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607891370, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25409687, text='All of the above.<p>Also, if you wear glasses, check your prescription. A while ago, I switched to what in the UK are called Occupational lenses. The bottom of the frame is for close-up (less than 30cm) and the upper half is objects that are about an arms length away - my screen position. This helped me sit further back from my monitor but still have good visibility. Before changing, the position where I could see the screen the best wasn&#x27;t ergonomically good and if I sat with good posture I couldn&#x27;t see fine detail so well. It only took a few days to get used to the vari-focus.')
