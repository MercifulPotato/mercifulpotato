Item(by='williamstein', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609350564, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25583819, text='Moreover, if you use leverage to make an investment in stock (say) and the stock market temporarily goes down enough, you can  get hit by a margin call and go broke.  With a house, when the housing market goes down, it is an inconvenience (e.g., it can be harder to get a home equity line of credit or refinance your mortgage), but there&#x27;s NO automatic &quot;margin call&quot; as long as you can pay your monthly payments.   For people with limited capital, a leveraged investment without any possibility of a margin call is very valuable.   I&#x27;m glad I put my money into buying a house.')
