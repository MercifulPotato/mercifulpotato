Item(by='martinald', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601945272, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24693156, text='Not entirely sure of that exact setup but usually the &quot;monopoly&quot; ISP will terminate the connection to the &quot;resselling ISP&quot; in some sort of IP tunnel. The underlying physical connection is basically invisible to the reselling ISP and it just looks like a normal IP tunnel they can route to and from. The ISP providing the physical connection bills the reselling ISP a wholesale rate for the connection, which then bills the end customer.')
