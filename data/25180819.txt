Item(by='quantified', descendants=None, kids=[25185527, 25184564], score=None, time=1606077358, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25178343, text='The purpose of the signature is to create an artifact that demonstrates the signatory’s acceptance. In a legal proceeding, the artifact can be produced as evidence. That is the purpose of all contract signatures.<p>If you have a contract that obliges something from me, and it’s not my signature, you may be attempting fraud on me. See bankers and robo-signing. That is why I need to see, on your copy, my signature and any other personalizing marks that my original document contains.')
