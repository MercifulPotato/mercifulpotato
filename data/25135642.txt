Item(by='detaro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605699210, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25135471, text='&quot;roaming&quot; as a technical term just refers to any case where you move between networks and something maintains a connection. mobile phone network roaming is just one case of that, but e.g. moving to a different WLAN access point is the same. In the case of <i>mosh</i>, you can change your IP but it will still continue the existing session with the server (unlike SSH, which will fail the connection)')
