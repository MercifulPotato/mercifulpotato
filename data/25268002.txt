Item(by='forgotmypw17', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606846780, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25265674, text='HTTPS has its own downsides, and I disagree that you should always use it.<p>When accessibility is paramount, HTTP is still better, because the risk of MITM is overtaken by all the things that can break with HTTPS, such as:<p>Wrong SSL version<p>Client date is off<p>Client doesn&#x27;t have proper certs installed<p>Server cert is broken or misconfigured<p>SSL takes too long to set up over slow connection<p>No one&#x27;s going to MITM my blog, while using HTTPS often impairs my own ability to use it.<p>Also, if you think HTTPS can&#x27;t be intercepted and spoofed by anyone, you&#x27;re kidding yourself.')
