Item(by='bleepblorp', descendants=None, kids=[24877015], score=None, time=1603510885, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24876248, text='The United States was founded on rejecting a system that vested lawful power with the British government and denied power to the colonists. Were the founding fathers wrong to declare independence to ensure that their &#x27;side&#x27; was not denied power?<p>The entire principle of popular sovereignty, as exercised through representative democracy, is that leaders are selected according to the wishes of the the majority of the people. Minority rule--no matter how it is codified or how long it has been in effect--is incompatible with this principle.<p>America can be either a country ruled by a Republican party answerable accountable only to rural interests or it can be a democracy. It cannot be both.')
