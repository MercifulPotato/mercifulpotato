Item(by='bobthepanda', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605478067, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25103667, text='The not-so-great news is that common characters like spaces and semicolons have various similar-looking characters defined in Unicode, which would not be very noticeable to a human but would be noticeable to a machine.<p>So you just need to do random substitutions that uniquely identify the document and you&#x27;ll have a fingerprint. It wouldn&#x27;t be very challenging to do and it wouldn&#x27;t be very challenging of a record to maintain.<p>You also don&#x27;t need to uniquely identify it to a person; you just need to narrow the search space and then apply other techniques that would narrow it down. If it&#x27;s a version of a document that leaked through an email chain then you&#x27;ve just limited the search space to the recipients, which is still plenty useful.')
