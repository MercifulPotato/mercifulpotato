Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603201750, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24833842, text='&gt; <i>The burden of evidence to introduce chat logs in legal proceedings is much lower than you would think. Often screenshots of Facebook messages are used, or SMS records (which are also easily spoofed). A judge or jury will likely not entertain the possibility the messages were fabricated unless you have substantial evidence to the contrary.</i><p>This is true for now.  It seems likely that fabrication of screenshots etc will start to appear in legal cases, and as soon as it does, the burden of proof will shift.')
