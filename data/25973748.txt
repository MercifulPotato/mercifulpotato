Item(by='Tagbert', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612046420, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25971716, text='For almost all normal use cases, the Volt gen 2 is a series hybrid. The engine only runs when the battery is depleted and it operates in a non-stressed, constant rpm mode where it is supplying charge to the EV motor that is buffered by the battery. There is a parallel hybrid mode but it is only triggered under extreme conditions.')
