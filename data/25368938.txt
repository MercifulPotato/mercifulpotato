Item(by='coldtea', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607567771, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25365309, text='&gt;<i>&quot;Should&quot; is not a principle of law or of reality, for that matter.</i><p>It can very well be a principle of law. In certain legal codes, it actually is.<p>As for reality, that&#x27;s a pretty low bar.<p>We have many &quot;shoulds&quot; (e.g. you should not cross a red light lest you get a ticket &#x2F; prison) that are not some inherent property of reality or physical law, but things we&#x27;ve decided upon.<p>In fact, those are the only things that makes sense to consider as &quot;shoulds&quot;. The constraints of reality are not negotiable, so should there is superfluous.')
