Item(by='mkl', descendants=None, kids=[24662236, 24677043], score=None, time=1601638821, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24660443, text='Those strings also support things like \\0 to get a null byte, and \\uxxxx to get a Unicode character.  This is useful for working with filenames and other things with spaces, quotes, and so on using find, xargs, etc.  E.g.<p><pre><code>  find ... -print0 | while read -d $&#x27;\\0&#x27; f; do ...; done</code></pre>')
