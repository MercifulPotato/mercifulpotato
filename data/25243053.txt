Without proper opportunity you can achieve nothing.<p>I wish we keep survivor bias in mind when reading success stories; we should ask &quot;out of those who work hard and gritty, how many are successful and how many are actually not?&quot; And not &quot;out of those who are successful, how many are hardworking?&quot;<p>Most of the time, hardwork is just the minimum prerequisite to success that every aspirants have, de-emphasizing the role of luck in life is simply unwise.')
