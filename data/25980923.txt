The deposits are the Brokers saying, &quot;Hey, trust me, I&#x27;ve got everything I need to complete the settlement, here&#x27;s my collateral&quot;.<p>As such, if a Broker doesn&#x27;t have the funds to complete the transaction, you can pull it from their deposits.<p>Your money doesn&#x27;t really play a part in this, and indeed the illusion of &quot;give $X for Y stock&quot; is just an illusion.')
