I eventually got backed into a spot of not enough &quot;professional&quot; dev experience to leave, and old enough to not be desired to start over as a junior.<p>If you&#x27;re dead set, though, I&#x27;d start with HVAC - I think it&#x27;s the best balance of interesting with a little manual effort to it.
