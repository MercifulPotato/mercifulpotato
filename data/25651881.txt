When you have a gigantic spinning turbine, the amount of power it takes to change its speed is immense. This has huge implications for grid stability when we are talking about renewables.<p>The resolution for this is something called a synchronverter: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Synchronverter" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Synchronverter</a>')
