Item(by='freeqaz', descendants=None, kids=[25410989], score=None, time=1607889923, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25409537, text='I don&#x27;t really have any distance issues seeing. I&#x27;m technically slightly farsighted.<p>What I do have is an astigmatism. Getting glasses (it was free with my work insurance) made a huge difference in my ability to stare at the screen all day.<p>If you find yourself _ever_ squinting at the screen, go get glasses. Or if you feel your eyes get &quot;dry&quot;. They&#x27;re both likely symptoms of an astigmatism.<p>I can use my computer and phone without glasses fine, but after ~4 hours I start to feel fatigue. And text is _slightly_ crisper with glasses on.')
