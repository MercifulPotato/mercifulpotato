Item(by='newsbinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606931084, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25278680, text='HN has institutional memory against the dangers of Eternal September.<p>Humor is easy because it barely needs to engage with the post or comment it&#x27;s under to be considered a valid &quot;joke comment&quot;.<p>An insightful or deeply curious comment is harder, because it more strongly needs to engage with the content in order to be a valid &quot;insightful comment&quot; or &quot;insightful question&quot;.<p>Sometimes HN upvotes humor, when the humor offers insight or a clever summation of something.<p>Refugees from Reddit learn quickly that going for the funny quip or the easy jab is just as likely to get downvoted as upvoted.')
