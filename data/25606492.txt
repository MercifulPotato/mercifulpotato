It&#x27;s common to engage freelancers to fill in the gaps and accelerate schedules.<p>However, it&#x27;s usually not a good idea to outsource your entire core technology development to freelancers. Building, launching, running, and maintaining a technology product is far more expensive than most non-tech people estimate. You would need very deep pockets to commit to outsourcing the entire development.<p>If you&#x27;re trying to raise investment money, the process will likely grind to a halt when your investors realize you don&#x27;t have any in-house technology people.')
