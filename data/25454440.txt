Item(by='user-the-name', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608202631, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25450061, text='You will find disagreement only if you deliberately choose words like &quot;brittle&quot;. The word you want is &quot;simple&quot;. Crypto protocols need to be simple, to reduce attack surfaces.<p>&quot;Flexible&quot; protocols are the ones that are actually &quot;brittle&quot; -this is where the use of metaphorical language breaks down. &quot;Flexibility&quot; actually just introduces more places to break.<p>It is by now well accepted that modern crypto protocols should, in fact, be simple, and you will NOT find much disagreement with this.')
