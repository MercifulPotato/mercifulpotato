All of these are dialects of BASIC very much geared towards games and multimedia.<p>Don&#x27;t think any of the same people are involved but the similarities are striking, and I&#x27;m somewhat heartened to see there&#x27;s still a market for these kinds of programming environments. They&#x27;re certainly great for teaching and getting kids interested in computing because of the almost instant results you can achieve.')
