Item(by='elgfare', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610153706, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25692377, text='Group A fails to address the fact that emotionally loaded speech severely dulls the rationality of most listeners.<p>Group B requires someone to take a stand on what the different types of speech are, how we recognize them, and how each should be handled, and also what the threshold of proof for untrue statements should be.<p>I think group A is ideal as long as the speech holds a certain level of rationality, but that limitation sort of moves it into group B since it&#x27;s now subjective.<p>I think it boils down to: If we are unable to agree on even the simplest rules for our discourse there&#x27;s no way to prevent the discourse from collapsing.')
