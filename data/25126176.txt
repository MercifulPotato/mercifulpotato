If they write a fact check that turns out to be libelous or illegal in some way, they are liable for that specific piece of content. I think the law is clear about that.<p>GP was saying something else entirely. They were contending that if FB moderates or fact-checks even a single user post, they are now the publisher of <i>everything</i> that users post on their platform. Which is not at all how the law works.<p>Platforms are still liable for content that they author and post themselves.
