Item(by='cableshaft', descendants=None, kids=[25905908], score=None, time=1611591022, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25902010, text='My policy is ignore them unless my wife complains (she hates big bugs for some reason) or they can cause harm to us or our dogs (yellow jackets, centipedes).<p>For the former I&#x27;ll make an attempt to capture and transport outside (and if not I&#x27;ll leave it alone), if it&#x27;s the latter I&#x27;ll either try to shoo it outside or kill it, with killing being the last resort.<p>I don&#x27;t use pesticides or weed killer and I also actively leave a couple patches of wild flowers and clover in the back yard unmowed for bees and butterflies and other insects to use. If my dogs didn&#x27;t like trying to eat bees I would consider doing some sort of beekeeping.')
