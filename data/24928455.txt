Item(by='ScottBurson', descendants=None, kids=[24929110], score=None, time=1603961353, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24927999, text='It&#x27;s not &quot;every group of people&quot; -- it&#x27;s organizations that invite the participation of the public, or at least of others they don&#x27;t know well, as long as they adhere to the mores of the community.<p>More importantly, I take the parent&#x27;s point to be, not that such an organization &quot;owes&quot; anything to anyone, but simply that anyone formalizing a Code of Conduct and a committee to enforce it would do well to understand why the protections of due process exist.  If their intention is truly to create a safe space for interaction, setting up a process that can be easily abused is not going to help with that.')
