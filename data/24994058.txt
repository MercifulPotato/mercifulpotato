Item(by='rland', descendants=None, kids=[24994126, 24995175], score=None, time=1604531721, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24993945, text='I completely agree. The structure of our society actively deprives people from human connection which is essential to happiness. Individuals do suffer from depression at different rates, but it pops up the same way popcorn pops when you turn the heat on. The direct cause is the atomization of modern life, not some chemical imbalance in the brain.<p>The reason people treat depression as an individual problem to be addressed by individual means (medication) is that we are totally unwilling to acknowledge the complete emptiness of modern life. And also because modern life teaches us to treat everything as an individual problem. The symptom is itself the mechanism.')
