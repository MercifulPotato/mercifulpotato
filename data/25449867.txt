Item(by='contingencies', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608159104, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25448608, text='I&#x27;m approaching 40 and in some ways have never felt <i>more</i> in crisis. Partly this is because having assets means exposure to new forms of risk. Major business or life decisions require days of effort over periods of weeks because the complexity and stakes now cross scope and potentiality boundaries that are unsettling at best and downright scary if not tackled in a structured fashion. In short, while I now have better tools, resources, and experience there is nobody to delegate the most critical decision making to, the stakes are vastly increased and the results don&#x27;t just affect me anymore, therefore any execution what Bezos calls a &#x27;type 1 decision&#x27; (effectively irreversible) must now be extremely well considered. Things that help: supportive investors, good lawyers, good doctors, the maintenance of long term friendships completely outside of work and family circles, family, exercise, nature.')
