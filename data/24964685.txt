E.g. we can prove (or just assume since the case is simple) that construction of Norm3 is defined for any triple except (0, 0, 0), and that it is preserved under rotation and under reflection, but guaranteed to be broken under addition.<p>I suspect that if we factor out such basics, the amount of proof for a reasonable function may become manageable.')
