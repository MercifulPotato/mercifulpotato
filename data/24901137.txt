Item(by='p1necone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603744944, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24897279, text='I think trying to replace regular GUIs and mechanical inputs in <i>every</i> use case is naive, it seems pretty obvious to me that clicking on a button or pressing a keyboard shortcut is pretty much <i>always</i> going to be faster than uttering a voice command.<p>But there&#x27;s a huge swathe of use cases where it <i>does</i> make sense, and I think we should be focusing on those - situations when you can&#x27;t use your hands. Voice assistant technology doesn&#x27;t even need to be great for this, just good enough that you can look up unit conversions with your hands covered in bread dough, or navigate while driving or whatever.')
