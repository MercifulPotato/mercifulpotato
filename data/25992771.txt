Item(by='notJim', descendants=None, kids=[25992842], score=None, time=1612208666, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25992546, text='It actually seems really problematic for drug trafficking to be a crime while consumption is decriminalized. It creates an easier market for organized crime to sell into, which reduces their risk. IMO instead we should do something along the lines of allowing doctors to prescribe pure versions of illegal drugs to people who are addicted. Then the drugs can be made by legitimate firms that can be regulated heavily.')
