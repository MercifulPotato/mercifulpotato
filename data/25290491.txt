Item(by='PretzelPirate', descendants=None, kids=[25290711, 25290548], score=None, time=1607012641, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25290313, text='I think part of the confusion is the word “currency”, which implies it’s something like USD.<p>Ethereum’s ETH token, for example, is an asset that will appreciate if demand of the Ethereum network increases for real-world business applications (not just financial payments). That makes it much more like investing in a limited resource (ex: oil) that will go up in value.<p>It’s still speculating, but most stock investments are close enough to speculation that you can’t really tell the difference.')
