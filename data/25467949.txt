Item(by='tolien', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608305193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25467554, text='In the case of Zen&#x2F;Zen2, some of the dies didn&#x27;t have direct access to main memory but went through an adjacent die&#x27;s memory controller and channel. This had a performance and latency effect, and required some extra work on the part of OS schedulers to make sure that tasks weren&#x27;t being scheduled on related cores.<p>It&#x27;s (relatively) easy to have lots of cores on one die, it&#x27;s not so easy to keep them fed.')
