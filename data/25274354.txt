Item(by='gpderetta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606897667, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25274173, text='Inline in c++ doesn&#x27;t do what many people think it does, i.e the keyword is neither necessary nor sufficient for performing the inlining optimization (although the compiler might treat it as a weak hint).<p>Instead is a directive to the compiler to ignore the One Definition Rule for the function and  it is an artefact of the header inclusion model of compilation.')
