Works on SQL Server and I think is more performant?:<p><pre><code>  SELECT department, first_name, salary\n  FROM salary AS s\n  WHERE s.[salary] = (\n    SELECT MAX(ex.[salary])\n    FROM salary AS ex\n    WHERE s.[department] = ex.[department])</code></pre>')
