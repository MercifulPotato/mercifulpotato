More is not:<p>&gt; Dietary protein supplementation significantly enhanced changes in muscle strength and size during prolonged RET [resistance exercise training] in healthy adults. Increasing age reduces and training experience increases the efficacy of protein supplementation during RET.<p>&gt; With protein supplementation, protein intakes at amounts greater than ~1.6 g&#x2F;kg&#x2F;day do not further contribute RET-induced gains in FFM [fat-free mass].')
