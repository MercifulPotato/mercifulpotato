Item(by='ashtonkem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607439569, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25343334, text='There are also some stoves that can run Isopro through the same heating cycle that Kerosene and Naptha go through, theoretically allowing you to use Isopro below its vaporization point. My MSR Whisperlite Universal can do that by simply inverting the Isopro canister (it also runs right-side up in warmer weather), but personally I’d opt for white gas if I was going anywhere near Isopro’s failure temperatures, just to be safe.')
