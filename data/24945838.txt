Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604085533, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24945596, text='The Supreme Court can overturn precedent. For example, Plessy v Ferguson (which held that segregation was legal) was overturned by Brown v Board of Education (which held that it was not). More recently, South Dakota v Wayfair directly overturned Quill v North Dakota in holding that states can directly charge sales taxes on internet purchases from out-of-state businesses.')
