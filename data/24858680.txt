Item(by='majewsky', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603378634, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24854928, text='The problem with these surveillance requests is that only rejections need to be argued. When the court says yes, it&#x27;s mostly just a rubberstamp. When the court says no, they have to write about 4 pages of text. Given the overload of the court system, judges tend to say yes except in the most egregious cases because they need to move on to the next case.<p>(Disclosure: I&#x27;m occasionally involved in public relations for a local chapter of the CCC, a German NGO that deals among other things with surveillance policy.)')
