There also wasn&#x27;t a great way to update stored procs at the time - we built this into an RPM that just ran a postinstall script to do an atomic swap.<p>I wouldn&#x27;t build this into the database were I to build it today.<p>[1] blocket.se&#x2F;leboncoin.fr&#x2F;segundamano.(es,mx)')
