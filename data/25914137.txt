It&#x27;s effectively a private tax. Comparing like-for-like, one should really include the health premiums paid by employers in the tax total when comparing to Canada.<p>Even with that in mind, in a number of recent years, the median Canadian tax bill has been lower than the median American tax bill [1]. And to your point, it varies by state&#x2F;province. The most expensive state OR province for high-income folks is California (51.9%) and the lowest is Alberta (39%).
