Item(by='kenhwang', descendants=None, kids=[25631144], score=None, time=1609767263, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25630946, text='I think it&#x27;s the exact opposite problem the tech industry has.<p>The film industry has way more talent supply than the employment demands, which naturally suppresses pay. The guilds&#x2F;unions are a way to increase scarcity to maintain pay and ease hiring.<p>The tech industry has much less talent supply and much much much more employment demand. Tech might be better modeled after high skilled trade unions (which have a labor shortage) than the comparatively lower skilled film&#x2F;auto&#x2F;factory unions (which have a labor surplus).')
