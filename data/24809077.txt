Item(by='mehrdadn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602931901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24808272, text='In addition to the other comments (which are the primary reasons), beware that you can actually get different behavior due to something called &quot;argument-dependent lookup&quot;. The most well-known example being <i>std::swap(a, b)</i>. In general it is not equivalent to <i>using namespace std; swap(a, b)</i>, though in this case, you actually want to say something else (<i>using std::swap; swap(a, b)</i>) to avoid importing the entire std namespace while also ensuring you call the ADL version of swap that may have been provided.')
