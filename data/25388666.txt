Item(by='coldcode', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607709918, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25386764, text='Some things are incredibly important but not directly related to the customer; like services called by service called by services called by clients. It&#x27;s not always easy to connect the dots especially in a micro service world. But without the ultimate service the customer can&#x27;t do anything.<p>Making good tickets across a dozen organizations and 100&#x27;s of people is hard to ever get right. Which is why counting tickets is sort of pointless, you might have a ticket to add a single value to a database and without it, the whole product doesn&#x27;t work, but you have no idea since there are 10 layers between you and the real customer.')
