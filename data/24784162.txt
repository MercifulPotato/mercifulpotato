Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602728556, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24769847, text='Folks need to grasp the distinction between the company requiring employees to be careful about using specific terms like &#x27;market dominance&#x27; vs. speaking up.<p>Using terms like &#x27;market dominance&#x27; may have legal implications, they surely will have populist implications as anti-trust issues are as much political as anything else.<p>So instructing staff to &#x27;not speak about Google&#x27;s market position&#x27; is very reasonable, because words have power and implications beyond what most employees probably believe when they&#x27;re using them.<p>This is somewhat different from asking people to &#x27;not have opinions&#x27; and a totally different league from trying to suppress whistle-blowers etc..')
