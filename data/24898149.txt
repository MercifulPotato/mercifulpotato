Item(by='ocdtrekkie', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603730680, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24895805, text='Distributing your website on a CDN is fine, but then, all of your fonts and JS belong on that same CDN.<p>Basically, you should never have a production website which calls out to cdnjs.cloudflare.com or ajax.googleapis.com or fonts.googleapis.com, you should be hosting all of your site&#x27;s dependencies in the same place or set of places.<p>As a side perk, your site also will stop looking like trash for users who use browser extensions to block such external calls. ;)')
