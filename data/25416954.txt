Very short summary: It&#x27;s like C in that it&#x27;s plain imperative code that compiles down to efficient machine-code pretty naturally. It&#x27;s unlike C in that it&#x27;s much safer, with excellent support for strong optional runtime checks. Ada has excellent interoperability with C. Syntactically it&#x27;s pretty different from C.')
