Receiving mutually anonymous tzedakah takes much of the sting out of being on the receiving end.<p>The highest level is helping someone become self-sufficient.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.chabad.org&#x2F;library&#x2F;article_cdo&#x2F;aid&#x2F;256321&#x2F;jewish&#x2F;Eight-Degrees-of-Giving.htm" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.chabad.org&#x2F;library&#x2F;article_cdo&#x2F;aid&#x2F;256321&#x2F;jewish...</a>')
