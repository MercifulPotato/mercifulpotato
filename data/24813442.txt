Some games worked even better on Wine than native Windows. The thing is that you&#x27;re never 100% sure it will be 100% compatible.<p>If you have two computers in your home, one Windows, one Linux and you, for example, share your Steam libraries with other gamers in your family, then it&#x27;s great.
