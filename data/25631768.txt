Item(by='est31', descendants=None, kids=[25634422, 25631984], score=None, time=1609770724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25631595, text='&gt; But for a fun fact, if you see many embedded systems that say they can only support an SD card up to 32 GB, it is a soft limitation due to the fact that media over 32 GB are pre-formatted to exFAT (and the system lacks support for exFAT).<p>There might also be the issue of the card reading hardware only supprting SDHC instead of SDXC (or SDUC).')
