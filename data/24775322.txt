Item(by='technothrasher', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602674268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24773775, text='Yes, a ballot question can be a referendum or an initiative.  Basically, at least in MA, if you as a citizen want a law passed or repealed and you don&#x27;t think the legislature will take it up, you can collect signatures of people who agree with you.  If you get enough, it will be added to the next state election as a question for the voters to yea or nay.<p>In other words, it&#x27;s a direct democracy end run around the usual representative democracy system.')
