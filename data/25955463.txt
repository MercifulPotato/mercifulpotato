Item(by='rswail', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611911198, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25955391, text='The systemic risk is because the intermediaries have to use their liquidity to keep the system moving, not because the short sellers might go bankrupt.<p>Trading in securities with daily settlement, while movement of money is not operating on the same frequency, means that there is a requirement for someone to take that risk.<p>That&#x27;s the systemic risk, not the trading itself. If the execution, settlement, and clearing of a trade were simultaneous and instantaneous, then there&#x27;s no risk to the market itself.')
