Item(by='grey_earthling', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610294256, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25714581, text='It&#x27;s common for one person to have multiple feeds&#x2F;identities, so this is less of a problem than you might expect.<p>There&#x27;s no structured way to indicate that 2 feeds are the same person (aka #sameAs), but you can easily link to the other feed and just say “this is also me” (because your audience is a bunch of humans that mostly pass the Turing test).<p>Because of this, we get the bonus feature that you can easily follow a different set of feeds on different devices — e.g. perhaps on your phone you want feeds you&#x27;re less likely to reply to, because you prefer typing on a physical keyboard.')
