Item(by='fulafel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604296988, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24960079, text='Good list.<p>Observation re list of ISAs that evolved conditional move vs ISAs that omit conditional move: MIPS, POWER, x86, SPARC all targeted high power &quot;fat core&quot; applications at the point where it got added. AVR, Hitachi SH, PowerPC didn&#x27;t add it while being driven more by low power &#x2F; embedded applications. And many ISAs continued to see wide use in the pre-cmov versions of the ISA in embedded space (eg MIPS) after the additions. (PowerPC even removed it when being modeled after POWER)')
