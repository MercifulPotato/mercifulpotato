Item(by='jiggawatts', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607772319, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25396671, text='Don&#x27;t confuse long-range www traffic with the total number of endpoints. Serving a single page might take dozens or even hundreds of interacting services.<p>Not one of our enterprise customers has IPv6 enabled.<p>Not one of the public clouds we manage have IPv6 addresses on their virtual networks.<p>Meanwhile, putting a CDN in front of an otherwise 100% IPv4 web server will add an IPv6 address whether you like it or not, and <i>that</i> traffic will contribute to those stats you mentioned.<p>This article is about public cloud providers hoarding IPv4, which applies to things like the PaaS and SaaS services, internal APIs, etc... which are nearly 100% IPv4 in all three of the big public cloud providers.')
