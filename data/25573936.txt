Item(by='cbhl', descendants=None, kids=[25574236, 25574146], score=None, time=1609271155, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25571638, text='I think the sweet spot is machine-assisted translation.<p>If you can get the output to be 90-95% correct, you can then display the raw and the machine output side-by-side, and have a human make corrections inline. Instead of a team of four working around the clock for a day or two, maybe you could have a translation as fast as it takes to proofread it three or four times end-to-end.<p>Rev is the same idea in the speech transcription space -- they have humans listen to the audio and fix up a machine-generated transcription.')
