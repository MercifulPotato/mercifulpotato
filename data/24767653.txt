Item(by='danans', descendants=None, kids=[24767811], score=None, time=1602609828, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24767554, text='I think the latest thinking is that all people of Japan to varying degrees are descended from the Jomon people along with other populations migrating from mainland Asia over millennia.<p>This pattern - early hunter gatherer populations forming the substratum that later mixed with larger migrations of agricultural populations - is not unique to Japan.<p>It&#x27;s quite similar to what you find in the paleogenetics and history of most regions of the world, including Europe and South Asia.')
