How do we know solipsism isn&#x27;t true? Because of two reasons, (1) the physiology of others is similar to our physiology, and (2) we can see evidence of inner state reflected in behaviour.<p>For dogs we can make a similar argument using (1) and (2). It is slightly weaker than applying it to other humans but not by much. Searle&#x27;s comment pertains to (2).')
