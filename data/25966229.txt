Obviously that fails when involving small companies with high short interest and hordes of &quot;4chan found a Bloomberg Terminal&quot; investors.<p>I looked a little at GME during the week and I saw that the option market makers had significantly reduced their participation. I bet that they&#x27;re also big losers. But that&#x27;s what they do for a living, it&#x27;s their own fault if they blew up selling calls naked. They knew the risks. Delta hedging doesn&#x27;t work when a stock can move 100% in a single day.')
