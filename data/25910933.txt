Item(by='azinman2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611621389, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25910033, text='My issue with tarsnap vs Dropbox is entirely workflow. Right now I can treat my Dropbox effectively as an external drive that happens to live on my file system by making everything selectively synced. If I want something backed up, I just drag it into that folder. But once it’s backed up I can turn on selective sync and free up my disk space.<p>Backup of personal data is often a 1-way endeavor — here’s a dump of photos I scanned that I don’t need to look at anytime soon. But with tarsnap I can’t do that, nor can I then have the ease of browsing to the file and just opening it while it transparently downloads on demand.')
