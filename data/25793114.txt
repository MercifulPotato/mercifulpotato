An even bigger challenge could be convincing industries and utilities to forgo cheap natural gas.<p>Off-the-grid remote locations is the obvious market for electricity generation currently addressed with diesel fuel. Combined electricity&#x2F;heat for large campuses and remote communities seems like another popular use case.<p>This article makes the case for high temperature industrial processes like the manufacture of Portland cement.
