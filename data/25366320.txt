Item(by='gnicholas', descendants=None, kids=[25366645], score=None, time=1607553580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25366229, text='Libel is a statement that damages the reputation of a person. Libelous statements are not protected under the First Amendment.<p>Ideas about election fraud do not fall into this exception to the First Amendment unless they refer to individuals and make defamatory statements about them.<p>For the record, I&#x27;m not saying there couldn&#x27;t be a reason to ban these statements, just that a libelous statement is not a good comparison because it is one of the established exceptions to the First Amendment.')
