It&#x27;s easier for people to throw away the plastic bottle, green wash it because it gets &quot;recycled&quot;, then buy another one.<p>On an individual level, one can reduce their plastic consumption by using things that are not packaged in plastic (eg. powdered laundry detergent, bar soap, powdered shampoo, and so on).
