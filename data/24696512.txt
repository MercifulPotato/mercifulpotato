Item(by='eCa', descendants=None, kids=[24696626], score=None, time=1601982536, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24695551, text='The old ISBN10 standard could get X (meaning 10) as the last character (the last number is a check digit), but ISBN13 (which prefixes 978 or 979 to ) is all numbers. The check digit is calculated differently. One obvious advantage is that the ISBN13 is barcode compatible.<p>I would guess that most modern actors in the book business has been primarily using ISBN13 for at least the last decade.')
