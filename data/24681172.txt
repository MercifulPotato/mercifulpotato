Item(by='Xylakant', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601836309, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24680706, text='It’s also a statement that sometimes requirements conflict. As a business owner, I’m required to keep proper books. That means every invoice needs to be on the book for at least 10 years. But invoices contain personal info - a name and address at least, possibly other data. You can’t require me to delete those invoices on GDPR grounds and violate the bookkeeping requirements.<p>And unlike data collection by random companies, data collection required by law is subject to public and judicial review. Laws are known - what data companies collect not necessarily.')
