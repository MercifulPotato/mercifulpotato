Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602788848, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24790377, text='The quickest way to resolve political standoffs in remote companies is to fly people out to an office and stick them in a conference room for a day.<p>The agenda barely matters. People just tend to behave better when they&#x27;re dealing with a real human being that they&#x27;ve met instead of a screen name in Slack that they argue with every day.<p>This is why I never promise anyone 100% WFH or 100% remote any more. I tell everyone to plan for 0-3 weeks travel, giving some leeway for flying people out for face-to-face planning.')
