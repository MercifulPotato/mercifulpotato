Item(by='dmos62', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603974853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24926979, text='termdown is worth a mention <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;trehn&#x2F;termdown" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;trehn&#x2F;termdown</a><p>It&#x27;s a countdown timer that uses figlet to render the countdown. Here&#x27;s a pomodoro timer script I use fairly often (`bup` is another script that emits a low pitched beep (a bup)):<p><pre><code>    #!&#x2F;bin&#x2F;bash\n\n    if [ $# -ne 1 ]; then\n      echo $0: usage: pomodoro time_expr\n      exit 1\n    fi\n\n    TIME_EXPR=$1\n\n    get_time ()\n    {\n      date &quot;+%H:%M&quot;\n    }\n\n    START=$(get_time)\n    termdown --font big --critical 0 $TIME_EXPR \\\n      &amp;&amp; bup\n    END=$(get_time)\n    echo &quot;$START - $END&quot;</code></pre>')
