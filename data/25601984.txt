Item(by='TheOtherHobbes', descendants=None, kids=[25602267, 25602134], score=None, time=1609493476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25601579, text='I think you need to revise your understanding of what aliasing is, because you are - to be blunt - wrong.<p>Any process that generates or distorts a waveform at any sample rate <i>will generate aliasing in the digital domain</i> -  unless it has no &gt;Nyquist components.<p>And most waveforms do have &gt;Nyquist components. Sine waves &lt;Nyquist don&#x27;t, but ramps, steps, and virtually all forms of distortion do.<p>If you don&#x27;t understand this go back and reread the basics, because hundreds of DSP engineers have spent countless hours devising ways to handle this problem.')
