Item(by='bmelton', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605026184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25047649, text='bdcravens is correct. m3o is a numeronym for &#x27;micro&#x27; and does not relate to the version number.<p>The format of a numeronym is to take the first letter of a string (micro) + x + the last letter of the string, and replace x with the number of characters between both. So, Andreesen Horowitz (the most popular one I&#x27;m aware of) becomes a16z. Micro becomes m3o.')
