One or more if your projects generates more than 1 binary)<p><pre><code>   |__&#x2F;bin_name1\n\n   |__&#x2F;bin_name2\n</code></pre>\n|<p>|__&#x2F;internal (folder for your sources packages)<p><pre><code>   |__&#x2F;config (package config)\n\n   |__&#x2F;http (package HTTPServer, api, etc)\n\n      |__&#x2F;api\n\n      |__&#x2F;models\n\n   |__&#x2F;database (package database)\n\n   |__...\n</code></pre>\n|<p>|__&#x2F;api (swagger and such)<p>|<p>|__&#x2F;docs (godoc)<p>|<p>|__&#x2F;.golangci.yml<p>|<p>|__...<p>Something like this.')
