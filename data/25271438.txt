Item(by='xfitm3', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606865260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25267651, text='Having previously performed an extensive TCO calculation (2010) it was substantially cheaper for on-prem vs AWS. We had &lt;10000 machines, mostly cheap&#x2F;supermicro. We had our own parts inventory and staff dedicated to maintaining equipment. I also ran facility operations for things like the generator, fuel testing, UPS PMs, air filters, professional cleaning, etc.<p>You have to know your business and you need people with facilities skills. Investing in cold aisle containment reduced our PUE quite a bit. You also have to become an expert in logistics to run your own facility, where just almost anyone can click away in AWS and spend money.')
