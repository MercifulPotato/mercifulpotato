Item(by='kazinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605033924, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25046691, text='You need documentation not just to get your happy case working (how do I call this?), but to know things like:<p>1. Know whether a good behavior you&#x27;re getting is a fluke, or required.<p>2. If something stops working with a new release of the API middleware, is it due to their bug, or because you misused it?<p>3. What limitations of the API will leak through to your program? What input ranges to watch out for?<p>4. What unexpected situations could occur that must be handled? These won&#x27;t happen if you get the happy case working, or not all of them.')
