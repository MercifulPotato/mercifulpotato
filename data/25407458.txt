Item(by='meekrohprocess', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607875482, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25406415, text='IMO, you could highlight the differences between similar titles.<p>It&#x27;s easy for recommendations to get stuck on a local maxima if they only look at one metric at a time, like &quot;similarity&quot; weights. But if you have a lot of metadata about each title, you can break out of those &quot;loops&quot; by sprinkling in metrics like ratings&#x2F;genres&#x2F;release date&#x2F;popularity&#x2F;etc. This doesn&#x27;t have to hurt from a performance perspective, either; you can filter on the same single metric, but request more recommendations than you need and pluck out a pseudo-random set in the application logic.<p>That also lets you provide context for the recommendation. &quot;It&#x27;s like this, but [older&#x2F;more obscure&#x2F;with vampires].&quot;')
