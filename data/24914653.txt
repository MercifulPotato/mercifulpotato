Item(by='morrvs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603854464, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24910116, text='I&#x27;ve heard the Assembly vs. C analogy a couple times now, and find it hard to apply:<p>Assembly languages come in many flavors whereas C is a single, standard abstraction over these.<p>DOM is a single, standard API whereas frameworks&#x2F;libraries come in many, ever-changing flavors and provide different abstractions over the DOM.<p>It&#x27;s a very different situation. The level of abstraction that React provides over the DOM is comparably low (they are based on the same programming language).')
