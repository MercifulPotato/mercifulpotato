Item(by='ghaff', descendants=None, kids=[25060842], score=None, time=1605117309, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25060419, text='A lot depends on what failure modes you&#x27;re preparing for and protecting against.<p>At the time of the original virus shutdown in the US, I saw a ton of essentially panic buying of things (like canned goods and rice) and bottled water that seemed to be aimed at a complete infrastructure breakdown. Yet, I&#x27;m guessing that most of the people preparing to hole up for months lacked many of the other things they&#x27;d need if they actually lost electricity and water for an expended period of time.<p>While of course they would need many other things as well, if you actually believe that there&#x27;s going to be widespread societal breakdown, you probably do want a gun and ammo in addition to food, large quantities of water, and so forth.')
