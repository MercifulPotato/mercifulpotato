Item(by='jessaustin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605313055, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25088663, text='Ordinarily one would say that one who understands {less, more} is {less, more} literate. The base case is Babel: complete mutual incomprehension, like unto that of the beasts. Any who leave this state, in no matter which direction they leave, have read and understood. So, I can&#x27;t in good conscience answer your question. Language is just exactly what we make of it.<p>Irony, on the other hand, is a single message for multiple audiences. It <i>requires</i> a degree of mutual incomprehension.')
