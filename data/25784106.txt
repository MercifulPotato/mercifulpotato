Item(by='gregoryl', descendants=None, kids=[25785012, 25784146, 25786649, 25789002, 25786725, 25785000], score=None, time=1610665039, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25782861, text='My time to shine!<p>Hazelnut Choc Chip cookies, designed for Aquafaba.<p>NB. You can switch out more of the hazelnut&#x2F;almond meal to adjust the taste, but don&#x27;t go much below 1&#x2F;2 cup of almond!<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;a&#x2F;G2M1QoE" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;a&#x2F;G2M1QoE</a><p><pre><code>    1 &amp; 1&#x2F;4 cup almond meal\n    1 &amp; 1&#x2F;4 cup hazelnut meal\n    1&#x2F;2 cup dark chocolate chips\n    2&#x2F;3 cup flour (gluten free blend works fine)\n    2&#x2F;3 cup raw sugar\n    1 tsp baking powder\n    1&#x2F;2 tsp salt (ideally fine)\n    \n    Mix above together, set aside.\n    \n    1&#x2F;2 cup aquafaba\n    \n    Separately, whisk aquafaba until fluffy\n    \n    6 tbsp coconut oil (liquid)\n    1 tsp vanilla extract\n    \n    Add to aquafaba, whisk until combined\n    Add dry ingredients from above\n    Mix until tacky dough forms\n    Place in fridge for 30 minutes\n    \n    Preheat oven to 190C\n    \n    Form a ball from about 1.5 tablespoons of dough.\n    Place ball onto baking paper lined tray.\n    Bake in oven for about 15 minutes.</code></pre>')
