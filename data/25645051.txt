Item(by='DiogenesKynikos', descendants=None, kids=[25645663], score=None, time=1609851888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25644445, text='The treaty was ratified after the passage of the act, and the act only applies to the US through the treaty. This is not some normal case of, &quot;We&#x27;re required by this treaty to tax pickles at 25%, but Parliament has only imposed a tax of 20%.&quot; Extradition to the US is enabled by this treaty. Without the treaty, there&#x27;s no extradition in the first place. The treaty was ratified after the passage of the Extradition Act, and the government apparently felt that the terms of the treaty, including the provision on political offenses, could be implemented without further legislation.')
