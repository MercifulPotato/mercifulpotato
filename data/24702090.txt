Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602016702, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24698976, text='Note: while Walmart has potential antitrust concerns, it&#x27;s due to how they impose pricing demands on suppliers, and possibly for their online marketplace.<p>The fact that they sell store brands in their retail locations is immaterial, as that has been a part of retail since before third party brands existed.<p>See, e.g., the Sears case, in which it was Sears&#x27; ability to fix prices due to its market dominance that concerned antitrust regulators. They didn&#x27;t care that Sears sold store brands alongside competing products.')
