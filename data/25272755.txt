If data is hosted on personal devices, it’s not available all the time at high-bandwidth. If data is hosted on a dedicated server it costs money and time to maintain. That means users will need to pay a fee, or see ads. Open platforms are a means to circumvent ads, so that leaves paying a fee. But it seems users prefer free with ads, so those businesses will attract more users and capital.<p>The one thing that could change this is a major change in internet connectivity, such that it’s feasible to host on your own devices. As personal connectivity and bandwidth improves, P2P becomes more viable.')
