Item(by='jawns', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608057408, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25433132, text='By that I mean not just the means of production but the means of consumption.  It used to be that the means of production was the hard problem (capital costs to create a new print publication were high).  But now perhaps the more difficult problem is disrupting the way that people are used to consuming news and persuading them that quality journalism is worth paying for (or at least persuading them that if nobody pays for journalism, quality is going to suffer).')
