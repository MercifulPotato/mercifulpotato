It will give you less strength if you measure how much you can lift in an 8-rep set.<p>The key false dichotomy here is strength vs. hypertrophy. They are not in opposition, bigger muscle is a stronger muscle. To be as strong as you can, you need to do two things:<p>1) Get the biggest muscles you can.<p>2) Get practice in the rep range you are interested in being tested.<p>As good as Mark&#x27;s books are, a lot has been researched and discovered since.
