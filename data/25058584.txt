Item(by='pjc50', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605104437, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25057629, text='Lots of the popular languages have &quot;eval&quot;, which can&#x27;t be compiled ahead of time.<p>Even if you did compile Javascript ahead, before delivery, what would you compile _to_? It would have been a huge uphill struggle for the smartphone era if all websites had been x86-only.<p>AOT compilation forces you to pick your customer hardware. JIT leaves that choice to the customer.<p>Mind you, there are situations where I&#x27;d quite like AOT for Python, or at least advance _linking_, as it would solve a lot of the distribution problems and virtualenv hassles.')
