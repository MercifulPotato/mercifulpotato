Item(by='jonahx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607301317, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25326552, text='Good article.  The key insight was a bit buried though:<p>By using the pointer to the current element, you lose information (that element&#x27;s ancestor), forcing you to introduce a &quot;prev&quot;, and to track and update 2 variables.<p>By using a pointer to the pointer of the current element, you have access to all the information you need -- the &quot;prev&quot; and the &quot;cur&quot; -- just by following the pointer trail one or two steps.')
