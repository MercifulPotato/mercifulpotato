Item(by='umvi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611203799, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25855225, text='Pro tip: if you want to get someone a birthday card, just get them a card that says &quot;10 years old!&quot; on the front. Then use a sharpie to put an asterisk after the &quot;10&quot; and then on the inside of the card put: &quot;* (in base X)&quot;, where X is how many years they are turning. Worked well for my 2 year old daughter as well as my 57 year old dad.')
