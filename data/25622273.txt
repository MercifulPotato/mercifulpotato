Item(by='robjan', descendants=None, kids=[25624420], score=None, time=1609687704, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622235, text='In the UK, at least, if a transaction paid with a credit card is between £100 - £30k and the merchant becomes unable &#x2F; unwilling to render the goods &#x2F; services, you get the full amount back under Section 75 of the Consumer Credit Act. This is to prevent people from being in debt for things they never receive. You don&#x27;t get that protection with a debit card.<p>I&#x27;m sure other countries have similar protection.')
