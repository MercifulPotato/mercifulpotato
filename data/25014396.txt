Item(by='neuronic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604746960, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013336, text='Despite the overly inflated ego of many neurosurgeons, neurologists and neuroscientists we have <i>no conclusive picture</i> about the brain as a working system.<p>We just have a few pieces of a puzzle and no idea what the overall image actually is. Neuroplasticity and adaptivity make this even harder because often you can&#x27;t just take a part away like in a car and pinpoint issues due to the absence or disturbance of these parts.<p>If you knockout an area and a patient loses smell, will you say that you found the &quot;center&quot; of smell? We are still poking at the brain and figuring out its responses.')
