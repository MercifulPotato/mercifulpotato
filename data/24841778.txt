Item(by='Kluny', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603224051, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24839810, text='In Denmark it&#x27;s so prevalent that it&#x27;s codified as Jante&#x27;s Law. It&#x27;s not a prescriptive law, despite the way it&#x27;s phrased. It&#x27;s more descriptive, a satirical summary of the way Danes think of ambitious people.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you are anything special.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you are as good as we are.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you are smarter than we are.<p>- You&#x27;re not to imagine yourself better than we are.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you know more than we do.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you are more important than we are.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you are good at anything.<p>- You&#x27;re not to laugh at us.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think anyone cares about you.<p>- You&#x27;re not to think you can teach us anything.')
