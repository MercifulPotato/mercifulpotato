Item(by='asdff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601673010, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24664738, text='I think there are some stimulant properties with psychedelics that I wouldn&#x27;t be surprised if reaction times were improved. One thing I noticed from my experiences with LSD was that I could run seemingly indefinitely without tiring, and drink quite a lot of beer as if it were water and not feel drunk, when that same dosage while otherwise sober would make me struggle to walk. I think it would be too tough to stay focused on an empirical task to truly measure this, however. My mind might be running quite fast, but is easily distracted in this state.<p>Historically, Dock Ellis threw a no hitter while tripping on LSD. He could scarcely see the home plate through all the visualizations, but he did it.')
