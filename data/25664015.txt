Item(by='chordalkeyboard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609974644, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25663014, text='&gt; I think a lot of HN readers who are fairly sophisticated forget that half of the world is not. Diverse information diets are not a good thing, because most people are not equipped to understand what is reasonable.<p>Sophisticated people are just as prone to bias and cognitive error as unsophisticated people. The danger with limiting the spread of information is that the task of deciding what to censor is vulnerable to corruption by the interests of the censor.')
