Item(by='ppf', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610700138, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25783593, text='According to this paper[1] (and using an online calculator to roughly convert GJ to CO2e), manufacturing and assembly of a Li-ion pack adds around <i>300Kg</i> CO2e&#x2F;kWh. This number is actaully a little high, compared to other ways I&#x27;ve calculated it, but is definitely in the right ballpark.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S0007850617301099" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S00078...</a>')
