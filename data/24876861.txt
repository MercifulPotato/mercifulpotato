Item(by='petertodd', descendants=None, kids=[24877342], score=None, time=1603512415, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24876044, text='By &quot;in parallel&quot; I don&#x27;t mean XOR. I mean concatenation. So in my example sha256+md5 would concatenate sha256&#x27;s 256 bit digest, and md5&#x27;s 128 bit digest, to form a 384 bit digest.<p>The intuitive explanation for why XOR instead of concatenation doesn&#x27;t work, is it lets the output of the broken hash function influence the output of the remaining good hash function. That is obviously cause for concern!')
