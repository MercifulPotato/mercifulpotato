Item(by='welterde', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605818435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25152350, text='UTC is defined to be within 1s of mean solar time at 0deg longitude (= UT1).<p>If leap seconds are a problem for the intended application then UTC is the wrong thing to use. There are other scales that are defined differently that can be used in it&#x27;s place (such as TAI).<p>However if exact frequency is not critical one could also directly use UT1 instead of UTC (people that use leap second smearing for instance should have no issue with that..).')
