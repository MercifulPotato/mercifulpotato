Item(by='bedobi', descendants=None, kids=[24975513], score=None, time=1604366349, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24975290, text='If most of the callers did need both pieces of data it would have made sense to combine them into a single service. But, overwhelmingly, callers are interested in one piece of data or the other, and the load profile, tolerance for staleness in caching etc etc for each of the two services are vastly, vastly different. This is why we chose to split the two into different services.<p>The few callers that do need to obtain both pieces of data just make concurrent calls to both and them zip them into a single result.')
