Item(by='ivanbakel', descendants=None, kids=[25468387], score=None, time=1608306329, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25467996, text='&gt;I actually don&#x27;t think it&#x27;s that bad of a practice as no browser will ever stop supporting it<p>It&#x27;s not a bad practice to use center because it&#x27;s not HTML5-compliant (though that&#x27;s not a bad reason to avoid it). It&#x27;s bad practice because putting purely visual info in your semantic markup is a bad idea - mixing responsibilities between different layers creates pain points for later work.<p>In this sense, it&#x27;s like a GOTO. It&#x27;ll work well enough for one person on a small project, and you might thumb your nose at the experts who tell you it&#x27;s a bad idea - but it&#x27;s a source of technical debt, and the better approach is to just avoid it from the start.')
