Item(by='chii', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610868146, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25808244, text='The difference between speculating (in the stock market&#x2F;shares&#x2F;commodities) and a casino is that casinos&#x27; house-odd games (i.e., odds that are always in favour of the house) are guaranteed long term losing propositions by definition.<p>Speculating on the markets is not gambling in the same way as in a casino - there&#x27;s some element of skill in speculating in the markets, and there&#x27;s not house-odds on the markets. I would compare speculating on the markets to be similar to a game of poker (which is not a house-odds game).')
