Item(by='uyt', descendants=None, kids=[25818929, 25819080], score=None, time=1610951948, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817605, text='This seems way too interesting to not have more scientific studies on it.<p>It reminds me of <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Split-brain" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Split-brain</a> where after cutting the connection between the two hemispheres of the brain:<p>&gt; It is known that when the corpus callosum is severed during an experimental procedure, the experimenter can ask each side of the brain the same question and receive two different answers. When the experimenter asks the right visual field&#x2F;left hemisphere what they see the participant will respond verbally, whereas if the experimenter asks the left visual field&#x2F;right hemisphere what they see the participant will not be able to respond verbally but will pick up the appropriate object with their left hand.<p>So it&#x27;s definitely possible to have two consciousness occupying the same brain.')
