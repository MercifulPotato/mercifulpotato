The total cost per unit depends on how many units are produced.<p>If you process the waste from few units, the disposal fee per unit is very expensive. If you sell thousands of units, you can plan for very large scale standardize disposal procedure that drives down price to fraction what it for small number of units.<p>Customers don&#x27;t want to pay for startup risk. So first 100 - 500 units sold could be too expensive.<p>You could solve this problem by selling the company to someone  big, like Berkshire Hathaway Energy. They have shoulders to take the risk and money to do the capital investments when the time comes.')
