Item(by='betwixthewires', descendants=None, kids=[25519432], score=None, time=1608718743, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25514494, text='&gt;no negative consequences<p>Meanwhile politicians tell us that double digit unemployment is the new normal.<p>There <i>are</i> people that are not skilled enough to warrant certain minimum wage. There <i>are</i> jobs that are not feasible at certain wage levels. When you put a floor on what people can agree between themselves that their work is worth, you price people and jobs out of the market. It is self evident when you look at it.<p>Now, the problems we try to solve with minimum wages are real. Cartel formation of labor demand, coercive bargaining, these are real problems. Minimum wages solve them to a degree, hut not without a new set of problems.<p>Did you know Norway, Denmark, Sweden, Iceland and Switzerland have no minimum wage at all?')
