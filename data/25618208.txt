Item(by='jasonv', descendants=None, kids=[25618235], score=None, time=1609634608, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25616472, text='I’ll repeat a recommendation I’ve made before: reinstall your smartphone’s OS, don’t restore from a backup, and install as few apps as possible. Start bare. Then get on with your life and see how little you can get away with on your phone.<p>A lot of apps I still had on my phone were installed in my “enthusiast” stage of iPhone living, and they never went away. Once I started over, I was just fine minimal-ing my iPhone life.')
