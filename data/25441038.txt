It helps in setting up SLIME, Paredit, etc. in Emacs quickly. The section &quot;Line-by-Line Explanation&quot; of the README at <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;susam&#x2F;emacs4cl#line-by-line-explanation" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;susam&#x2F;emacs4cl#line-by-line-explanation</a> explains every line of the .emacs file.<p>It provides a middle ground between manually installing SLIME, Paredit, etc. yourself with M-x package-install commands and installing Portacle that hides away the underlying details of what goes into making Emacs ready for Common Lisp development.
