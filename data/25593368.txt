Item(by='steveklabnik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609427694, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25593324, text='The difference is not &quot;testing&quot; in a broad sense, the difference is &quot;tests guaranteed to pass&quot; vs &quot;tests may run if they exist but we don&#x27;t gate on them passing.&quot;<p>Being able to guarantee that they pass means having the expertise to fix any issues, and with a timeliness to be able to fix them and not block a release. That requires people around to support the target, with a higher burden than tier 2.<p>(Technically, it&#x27;s even more than &quot;not block a release&quot; it&#x27;s &quot;not block any PR that may start failing on that target,&quot; since all tests <i>must</i> pass before a PR is landed.)')
