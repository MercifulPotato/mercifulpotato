Item(by='dumpsterdiver', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606349400, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25215014, text='A topic of conversation doesn&#x27;t have to be toxic to cause reasonable people to avoid engaging with it. There is nuance in every subject, and pursuing those unknown details requires energy. Sometimes people don&#x27;t have energy available to commit to a highly nuanced subject because they have other obligations.<p>One way of excusing yourself from such a conversation in a (hopefully) unoffensive way would be to make an honest statement like, &quot;Gosh, my brain is so overworked right now that I can&#x27;t even begin to delve into the nuance of that subject, but I hear where you&#x27;re coming from.&quot;')
