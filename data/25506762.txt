In the latter case it&#x27;s right there in the name and it has a direct bearing on employment prospects.<p>Should schools be reorienting evaluations in a way that isn&#x27;t as cheatable? Probably. Use take home open book exams and just use ongoing take home assignments instead of proctored finals as much as possible.<p>Maybe universities could have made classes in the current year pass&#x2F;fail. But that has its own set of problems as many would just coast and be ill-prepared for future classes.<p>Getting rid of most controls just isn&#x27;t always possible. And as soon as you start proctoring there&#x27;s going to be a tradeoff between effectiveness and invasiveness.')
