Item(by='mappu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604186209, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24954181, text='Precomp works by brute-forcing the zlib&#x2F;...&#x2F; parameters used to get byte-identical input; then decompressing; and (optionally) recompressing with a stronger compressor.<p>It was closed-source for a long time and AntiX was an open source version.<p>One interesting use case is for archiving Android ROMs for projects such as LineageOS. Each ROM is a zip file, they differ in bytes almost entirely and storing a long amount of history takes a lot of space. But under precomp, the differences between two nightly builds are quite small, and they can be packed together into a solid archive for a significant (90%+) space saving.')
