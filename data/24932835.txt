Larger companies want specialists<p>This is sometimes true. I&#x27;ve worked at startups though that only wanted to hire people that had familiarity in their chosen stack, since they have short runway and don&#x27;t have time to invest in people&#x27;s ramp up. In the current large company I work for we generally favor &quot;fungible&quot; software engineers (but, in one&#x27;s day to day work, maybe a particular engineer will wear fewer hats at a large company).<p>When you interview at a company, you should interview your interviewers to find out whether this is true in the particular situation you find yourself in.')
