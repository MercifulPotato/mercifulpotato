The &quot;Vector shift protection&quot; and &quot;Rate of Change of Frequency&quot; in embedded generation (Home solar etc.) means that if it senses anything &quot;funny&quot;, it&#x27;ll shut off.<p>Which in turn means if anything else goes wrong with the grid and there isn&#x27;t enough generation, then all home solar countrywide cuts out, making the problem 10x worse.<p>Lesson learned:  &quot;I detected something weird, lets turn off&quot; is not a good algorithm for everyone to use on a system requiring high uptime!<p>It was obvious from the start really...')
