Item(by='mlthoughts2018', descendants=None, kids=[24847812], score=None, time=1603284621, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846967, text='You can use a tree for multiple dispatch (visitor pattern is not required, not sure why you mention that) or you can use a plain vtable approach where you take all the function operands and place them in a tuple. The type of that tuple (as a single object) then determines the multi-operand dispatch in the vtable approach. Some languages do make it harder to construct these tuple types, but in principle vrable is fine for multiple dispatch even in eg C++.')
