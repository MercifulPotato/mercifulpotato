Item(by='jakelazaroff', descendants=None, kids=[24801104], score=None, time=1602854572, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24800256, text='<i>&gt; But the fact remains that Twitter is a viable product only because it is granted special immunity from legal consequences for publishing, immunity that you or I would not enjoy if we were to publish the very same content on a personal website, or hand out pamphlets on the street.</i><p>That’s not quite true — Section 230 protects <i>all</i> platforms from liability for content produced by <i>third parties</i>. You enjoy that exact same immunity for comments others post on your personal website. And conversely, Twitter does <i>not</i> enjoy immunity for content that they themselves produce, such as descriptions of trending topics.')
