Item(by='pkroll', descendants=None, kids=[24845141], score=None, time=1603253711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24839848, text='I&#x27;ve done a fair amount of capturing VHS video, losslessly, and then rendering it down afterwards. You can get rid of a fair amount of noise, using a median filter in AviSynth, after capturing multiple copies of the video. It&#x27;s tedious, and every multiple has diminishing effects, but a median filter with five copies of the video, is going to look noticeably better than a single capture.')
