Item(by='teodorlu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609698758, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25623411, text='As someone who tried out GHCJS, then Purescript, then Elm, then Clojurescript:<p>- GHCJS and purescript are powerful, but the learning experience might be steep[1]<p>- Elm is an excellent entrypoint into ML programming in the browser. Solid story for new users, and a great standard library for interactive web applications.<p>- ClojureScript differs from Elm in that it embraces its host, with all its power and all its wrinkles. Writing Elm is mostly a smooth experience. Read the guide[2], then you can actually build a web app.<p>I&#x27;ve spent the most time with Elm. Other people might have different experiences.<p>[1]: a few years since I tried, might be better now.\n[2]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;guide.elm-lang.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;guide.elm-lang.org&#x2F;</a>')
