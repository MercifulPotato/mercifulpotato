Item(by='stickfigure', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606371539, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25217079, text='Mostly we program in languages that read like a kind of shorthand english, and mature programmers are expected to be able to read the text fluently. WHAT comments tend to be superfluous.<p>Nobody sane reads 100+ character regexes fluently, so WHAT comments are totally appropriate. Any time I write a nontrivial regular expression, I always try leave an example of what it would match in a comment. I&#x27;ve thanked last-year me for this more times than I can count.')
