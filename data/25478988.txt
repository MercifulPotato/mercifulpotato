It&#x27;s a series of six music albums exploring and depicting memory loss, Alzheimer&#x27;s disease and dementia. I wish I had the writing skills necessary to convey the emotional turmoil I went through by listening to the six stages in one sleepless night. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.youtube.com&#x2F;watch?v=wJWksPWDKOc" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.youtube.com&#x2F;watch?v=wJWksPWDKOc</a> I also appreciated the attention to the details from the track titles to the choice of album covers.
