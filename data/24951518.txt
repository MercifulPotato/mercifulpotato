Item(by='nicoburns', descendants=None, kids=[24953850], score=None, time=1604152684, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24948848, text='Nuclear has problems with the load being intermittent because it&#x27;s too inflexible (you can&#x27;t realistically run a nuclear plant for 12 hours and then turn it off, you really want it to be running the whole time). If you have nuclear capacity for peak load, then what do you do with that extra energy at off peak times? The solutions for this end up looking a lot like the solutions for dealing with the intermitency of solar&#x2F;wind.')
