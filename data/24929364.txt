Item(by='thinkingemote', descendants=None, kids=[24934921], score=None, time=1603971133, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24929338, text='Code of conducts are untested unlike democratic processes.<p>They are too new and too different. Some can be written benign and others not. They are tools.<p>Most political structures have rules in them which work regardless of who weilds them, codes of conduct don&#x27;t. Codes of conduct assume they will be used by good people (and most of them are!) real law or poltical processes assume they might be used by bad people.<p>There&#x27;s a real good reason why we think in law that the accused should be treated as if they are innocent. Most codes assume the accused is guilty.')
