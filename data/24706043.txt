Item(by='numpad0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602055677, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24704829, text='On title: correct notation is “AN&#x2F;TVS-3” in ALL CAPS, as used in the original article. This “AN&#x2F;“ prefixed system is called JETDS, used to identify electronic gears in US military.<p>Examples include AN&#x2F;PVS-14(nightvision goggles), AN&#x2F;ALR-67(V)3 (airplane RWR), AN&#x2F;PRC-152(walkie talkie radio).')
