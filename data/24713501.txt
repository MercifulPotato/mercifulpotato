Item(by='gpm', descendants=None, kids=[24714527], score=None, time=1602109393, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24713428, text='The current law <i>does</i> exempt APIs from copyright via sensible general principles (you can&#x27;t copyright functional things, which as applied in this case is known as the merger doctrine).<p>If the court gets it wrong congress should fix it, but ideally we don&#x27;t want a law that is a patchwork of tiny little exceptions because that fails to generalize to new things, so we would rather that the court just properly understands how to apply general principles.<p>Even if the court gets it wrong, my favored way of fixing it would be to modify the general principle to be less ambiguous to this case, not to explicitly make an api exception.')
