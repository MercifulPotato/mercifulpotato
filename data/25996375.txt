Item(by='kab63', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612228373, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25993512, text='Our Honeywell humidifier has been working perfectly for years. One trick I haven&#x27;t seen mentioned here is filling the tank exclusively with RO (reverse-osmosis) water. This keeps the mineral deposits down to piratically nothing, meaning that things (pet hair, dust, etc.) don&#x27;t really stick to the fan blades. After each winter season I spray the fan with compressed air and throw the filter away. We usually go through two filters per winter season.')
