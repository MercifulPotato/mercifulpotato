Item(by='theluketaylor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611615565, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25909846, text='Canadian here, and yes, it&#x27;s basically impossible to mark your ballot wrong for a commonwealth parliamentary election for the simple reason you only vote for a single thing: who should be member of parliament for your riding.<p>Since there is only a single question there is tons of whitespace between options and you can&#x27;t accidentally mark selections for one question in the space asking a different one.<p>I&#x27;ve never had to vote in a referendum like brexit, but it wouldn&#x27;t surprise me to learn the ballot was more complex that the usual parliamentary election one.  Looking at images of the brexit ballot it appears pretty similar to what I&#x27;m used to, but with far less white space between options.')
