Item(by='Olipro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605033665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25048736, text='Well, there is.<p>Say I&#x27;m an ISP with X million subscribers.<p>Given a peering with Y GBit of throughput to someone like say, Twitch or Facebook; with multicast, I now only end up with a single load of data for each livestream coming over the pipe.<p>However, this does require direct peerings. Multicast over the Internet is just not a thing. This could technically be built out with IPv6, but nobody&#x27;s realistically going to arrange for globally-unique multicast space allocations and convince every AS in the world to start peering multicast over BGP for general public use.')
