Item(by='e1g', descendants=None, kids=[25368660], score=None, time=1607536896, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25361297, text='&gt; Some people just want a place to put their thoughts out, and don&#x27;t really care what anyone else is doing.<p>Not being snarky: this is what personal journals are for.<p>It&#x27;s difficult for me to reconcile &quot;I want to put my thoughts out, but for as many people as possible to see them, and without caring about impact&quot;. At best, it feels like void navel-gazing, and at worst it might be a source of so much thoughtless&#x2F;marginalizing content on Twitter: because the author did not care what their words will do to the reader.
