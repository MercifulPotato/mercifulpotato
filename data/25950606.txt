Item(by='eeZah7Ux', descendants=None, kids=[25957154], score=None, time=1611873984, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25947329, text='There are thousands of building regulations.<p>Here are few examples among some EU countries: setting a minimum density and number of floors in urban areas to prevent &quot;mcmansions&quot;.<p>Mandated commercial areas (aka shops) on the ground floor on new buildings.<p>Requirements on walkability applied to new road design to prevent sprawls e.g. limit cul-de-sacs.<p>Minimum and maximum percentage of buildings for industrial &#x2F; office &#x2F; shop &#x2F; domestic use.<p>Minimum and maximum apartment size.<p>Tons of limitations on renovation of historical buildings.<p>A lot of this stuff makes it very difficult to quickly transform an already urbanized area into a bunch of super-luxury villas or similar.')
