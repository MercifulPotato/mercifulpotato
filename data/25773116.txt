The conclusion was that it&#x27;s simply not possible in most of Europe unless one manages to find a Sami spouse, and even then, most Sami seem to live fairly modern lives. As a Nordic citizen the Greenland option is there as well, but the situation is similar to the Sami one.<p>And the above presumes the existing population would accept you, and it&#x27;s quite likely they won&#x27;t.<p>Deciding to live off the land without special privileges enjoyed by native populations is simply not possible; regular financial transactions between you and society is a requirement, and it&#x27;s pretty much impossible to raise children without access to modernities and without having the authorities go after you for child abuse.')
