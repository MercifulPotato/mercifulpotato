Item(by='mattkrause', descendants=None, kids=[25098408], score=None, time=1605407390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25097467, text='You don’t need CRISPR for sex selection—-it can already be done as part of the preimplantation screening for IVF.<p>For the other things, the best editing technique in the world is useless without a known and well-defined target. Intelligence is certainly partly heritable, but it’s not like there’s a single switch that needs to be toggled: genome-wide association studies find hundreds or thousands of linked genes—-and many of them likely have multiple functions. I would bet over-expressing any single one of them leads to many, many more problems than it solves.')
