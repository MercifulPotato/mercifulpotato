Item(by='krapp', descendants=None, kids=[25407685], score=None, time=1607858808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405619, text='Governments arguably have a public safety interest in aggregating traffic data and being able to identify potential criminal suspects by vehicle. Yes, they <i>could</i> abuse that in some nebulous Orwellian future, but data collection and analysis isn&#x27;t evil in and of itself.<p>Also, this is public data. Anyone sitting behind you in traffic has your license plate number, your employer and anyone you schedule appointments with can predict your behavior to a degree. Your movements were never that private to begin with.<p>If you want to be afraid of someone, be afraid of businesses, and what they can know based on analyzing customer data.')
