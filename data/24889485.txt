Item(by='ThrustVectoring', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603656674, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887975, text='GitHub&#x27;s actions are not <i>required</i> per se, they just need to accept being civilly liable for any alleged infringement if they want to ignore the takedown notice. This requires a high-level decisionmaker, and there are a lot of takedown notices, so only relatively small sites can get away with making decisions on a case-by-case basis rather than doing blanket takedowns of infringing content.<p>Like, there&#x27;s nothing stopping you from responding to DMCA takedown requests with &quot;this is clearly non-infringing and&#x2F;or fair use, if you want to sue us send servicing to the following address&quot;. You just have to be a particular kind of ornery and make the judgment call that you&#x27;re willing to risk being sued over protecting third-party speech.')
