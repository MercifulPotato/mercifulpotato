Item(by='ternaryoperator', descendants=None, kids=[25550269, 25549548], score=None, time=1609053372, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25548498, text='In my parents&#x27; generation, there was a <i>lot</i> of pressure to have children before 30. There was a widespread belief that giving birth after 30 risked the health of the mother and the child. As medicine advanced and neonatal care greatly improved, that deadline was pushed back and women started to seriously entertain the idea of careers before having children.<p>There are other cultural and societal factors at play, but the greater longevity and better medical care have opened up the time period for the expectations that previous generations had for their 20s.')
