I&#x27;m not sure the use cases for spinning disks these days justify that kind of investment - they aren&#x27;t (or shouldn&#x27;t be) used in random-write-heavy workloads (as in frequently updated databases), but more for archival and, often, they sit behind a flash disk acting as a cache (I do that for my home server - a lot of write traffic never hits the disk because it&#x27;s overwritten before being evicted from the flash)')
