Item(by='klmr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607092044, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25300758, text='&gt; <i>Massive confounding factor here is that starting around April seasonality … was putting massive downward pressure on the epidemic.</i><p>That’s wrong, the opposite is the case. The study controls for this by looking at a synthetic control over the same time period, which consists of other populations without mandatory mask-wearing. It’s an observational study so it can’t control for everything but it <i>does</i> control for seasonality, even if “seasonality” isn’t specifically mentioned in the paper.')
