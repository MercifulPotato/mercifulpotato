Item(by='superice', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605111932, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25059131, text='REST usually has the same content in your request replacing the entire resource on a PUT, or returning an identical thing on a POST. There is no difference in the schema of the JSON (or XML) you send and receive.<p>As a sibling comment said: if you implement GraphQL with DTOs that look exactly the same for reading and writing, sure, you won&#x27;t have CQRS, but it seems like the structure encourages you not to do that since you explicitly CANNOT use the output types as your input types in a GraphQL schema. Besides, real world usages seem to mostly treat it in a CQRS-style way instead of just CRUD operations.')
