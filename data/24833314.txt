Item(by='Exmoor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603159579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24831441, text='I was homeschooled k-10th grade, after which I entered community college.<p>Certainly social contact and learning is something you want to encourage, but I would not let that hold you back from homeschooling your son if you feel it&#x27;s working very well. It&#x27;s complicated to do in the current situation, but playdates, sports, field trips with other homeschool kids, etc. go a long way. There are likely also co-op classes where your kid can meet other kids.<p>I&#x27;m still friends with dozens of kids I was homeschooled with. Most have had very good outcomes. I don&#x27;t feel that the few who haven&#x27;t would have done much, if any, better in a traditional school environment, but that&#x27;s an educated guess.')
