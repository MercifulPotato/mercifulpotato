I made the SVG conversion  thinking people may want to use the clipart on the Web and because more programs support SVG than WMF.<p>After trying several conversion tools I settled on <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;hidekatsu-izuno&#x2F;wmf2svg" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;hidekatsu-izuno&#x2F;wmf2svg</a>.  It was the best in terms of quality (the backgrounds were properly transparent when they should be, for example) and targeted a vector format.')
