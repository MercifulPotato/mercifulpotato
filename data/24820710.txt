Item(by='non-cotton', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603051727, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24813749, text='I think this has a lot to do with the platforms vs. publishers issue, and the Section 230 dustup we&#x27;re about to see.<p>IMO any platform whose owners have enough editorial control for these engagment-hacking techniques to be useful (the ability to decide what gets seen, what doesn&#x27;t, and who sees what) should be treated as a publisher, not a platform.<p>This doesn&#x27;t have to kill online communities generally. As long as we can distinguish between editorial control and freedom of association (i.e. the ability to ban rule-breakers and people we don&#x27;t like) I don&#x27;t see why effective moderation wouldn&#x27;t be possible.')
