This is why applicants get frustrated or angry.<p>I heard a recruiter once (socially) tell me that they ignore all resumes with foreign names for sales positions because on average their yield on such calls is too low to bother.<p>Fair -- absolutely not.<p>Optimal to recruiter -- probably.<p>Good for the organization - no.<p>Good for society - no.<p>I&#x27;d love if there was something company <i>owners</i> and operators did to prevent things like this which are local maximums but globally inefficient (as well as unethical and unfair)')
