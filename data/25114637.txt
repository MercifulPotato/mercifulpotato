The OWID data is a good data source for our use case because it collects many data characteristics from all over the world and is very trustworthy at the same time. On the other hand, you can&#x27;t break down this data further than on a country level.<p>Other data sources differentiate more in terms of geographical characteristics, but include a smaller amount of other information, e.g. the New York Times data used by Google (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.google.com&#x2F;search?q=covid+data+usa+county+state" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.google.com&#x2F;search?q=covid+data+usa+county+state</a>).')
