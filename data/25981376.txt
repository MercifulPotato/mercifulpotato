Item(by='anonisko', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612119041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25977751, text='Musk didn&#x27;t inflate Doge. He tweeted about it and a bunch of get rich quick folks scrambled to buy, driving the price up like a penny stock.<p>A share of stock represents a tiny percentage share in a company&#x27;s assets and profits. For simplicity, let&#x27;s say a company has 100 shares, and you own 1 share or 1%. If they then issue 100 new shares and sell them on the market to raise cash, your ownership in the company has been diluted from 1% to 0.5%.<p>That&#x27;s why company shares will never be safe as an arbitrary store of value like bitcoin is. No one can arbitrarily dilute your share of the bitcoin pie.')
