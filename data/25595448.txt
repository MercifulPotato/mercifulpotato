Charisma is kind of paradoxical because you&#x27;d need to change so many things at the same time. Changing them one by one leaves you often worse and even less charismatic. Something that boosts the charisma of the charismatic guy may actively work against the non-charismatic one.<p>Charisma is kind of like the anti-fragility concept of Taleb. It&#x27;s when things that would normally harm people will paradoxically strengthen you.')
