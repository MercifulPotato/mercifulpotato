Item(by='genidoi', descendants=None, kids=[25414514], score=None, time=1607869808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405997, text='If all that’s changing on a site is the presentation&#x2F;ordering of elements, then you just need to update some XPath selectors every now and then and have a notification system for knowing when those selectors aren’t working.<p>You can even automate this process by having a known input (eg, URL to a book on goodreads + its known [and hopefully unchanging] book title) and have a script that periodically checks that the xpath string matches the known pages text &#x2F; generates a new one to point to the title. This is harder for values that do change but there are always workarounds')
