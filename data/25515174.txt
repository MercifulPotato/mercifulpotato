Item(by='mewse', descendants=None, kids=[25515330], score=None, time=1608706698, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25515128, text='In that case, you can use:  `git config -global pull.rebase true`<p>After that, `git pull` will automatically attempt to rebase your local commits on top of new commits pulled from a remote, so you don’t even have to do it manually.<p>(I still do it manually, though, because I’m a little bit paranoid about my version control and prefer to do my merges&#x2F;rebases explicitly, with a lot of extra testing and inspection.  Though obviously that won’t work for everyone in every codebase!)')
