Item(by='Blikkentrekker', descendants=None, kids=[25400814], score=None, time=1607801072, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25400556, text='The label would not own the performance, no, but he would still not be allowed to give a performance of any material he does not own without the permission of the rightsholder.<p>There are some “reasonable fair use” exceptions such as simply singing a certain tune, but the point is that the fact that he wrote the original composition is irrelevant; it only matters that he owns the copyright.<p>If the copyright indeed were transferred to the label, then he enjoys no more rights to perform it than anyone else.')
