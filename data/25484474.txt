Item(by='enriquto', descendants=None, kids=[25485879], score=None, time=1608447222, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25482356, text='Besides seeing through clouds and at night, you can often correlate images taken at different times and detect tiny movements (of fractions of a millimeter).  This technique is called &quot;interferometry&quot;.  You can observe minute movements of large swaths of terrain; this is impossible with optical images.')
