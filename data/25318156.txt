Item(by='AnHonestComment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607201103, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25317926, text='Early isn’t necessarily premature.<p>I always took the quote to mean you should focus on the right optimizations at the right time — starting with architectures and algorithms that alleviate as much work as possible (big savings) and only then get into code level optimizations (small savings). Architecture and algorithm design is where you get your “big O” wins, while code optimizations get you that last scaling factor of efficiency.<p>It’s a retort to people who micro-optimize an HTTP response... while having a process that generates 10x the API calls it needs to by not caching or joining data.')
