Item(by='pmoriarty', descendants=None, kids=[24828314], score=None, time=1603063972, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821683, text='There is good work done in GNU Emacs, but in other respects I have to disagree.<p>Having Emacs be &quot;Lisp all the way down&quot; is my ideal, and the ideal of many other Emacs users.<p>While rewriting the C portions of Emacs in Rust would be a step forward, because of its non-Lispy nature it would still fall far short of an Emacs fully written in Lisp or Scheme.<p>Ideally, Emacs would be fully written in and scripted in the same language, and that language would be a Lisp.')
