Item(by='necubi', descendants=None, kids=[24873532, 24873574, 24873811], score=None, time=1603483422, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24873243, text='You&#x27;re talking about relative pitch, not perfect (absolute) pitch. Any competent musician will have excellent relative pitch, which is the ability to recognize intervals between pitches. This is necessary for transcription, learning music by ear, jamming, etc.<p>Perfect pitch means that, if someone plays a sine wave with no context, you can tell what pitch it is.')
