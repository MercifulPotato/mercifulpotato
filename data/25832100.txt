Item(by='hypertele-Xii', descendants=None, kids=[25833051, 25832664], score=None, time=1611053476, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25831864, text='The central philosophy in capitalism is <i>competition</i>. The world is currently very acutely seeing the second and third order effects of such &quot;winner takes all&quot; philosophy, ranging from consumer-harming monopolies to environment-destroying profit-seeking. If we set socialism as an opposition to capitalism, it rises naturally to represent <i>cooperation</i>; The idea that everyone is entitled to a base level of creature comfort regardless of accumulated capital, and that in coming together to solve problems, rather than trying to one-up each other, problems can be solved more effectively and with <i>less harm</i>.')
