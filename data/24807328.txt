Item(by='danielheath', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602906374, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24803615, text='That’s a “utilisation” view, and it’s a terrible model.<p>People who already have work to do are not responsive to new input.<p>By ensuring everyone is working all the time you also ensure nobody can respond to the unexpected.<p>The counterpoint to utilisation thinking is latency thinking (“how fast can our team respond to requests”).<p>An instance of utilisation thinking: “Our roads are not always full of traffic, how can we get more cars onto them?”. This is clearly absurd; why is “developers have work to do” a goal when “roads have cars on them” is not?')
