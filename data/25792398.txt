Item(by='johnvanommen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610726840, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25791211, text='According to the research done by Harman&#x2F;Samsung, listeners generally prefer a curve that&#x27;s gently sloping by one decibel per octave.<p>IE, if you want a pleasing sound, a loudspeaker that&#x27;s playing 90dB at 100Hz should be playing 83dB at 16,800Hz. And the response curve should be consistent from point A to point B.<p>This recommendation can be impacted by the power response of the speaker, but that&#x27;s another subject...')
