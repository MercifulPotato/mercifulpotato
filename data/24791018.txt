Item(by='ip26', descendants=None, kids=[24793687], score=None, time=1602782033, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24790663, text='I see it as a hedge against inflation. In boom times with soaring markets, there is (usually) considerable inflation. If you ride the market, you probably won&#x27;t see outsize returns but you won&#x27;t lose purchasing power to inflation either.<p>For this reason I frequently wonder whether I should invest HSA funds exclusively in medical stocks. If healthcare gets more expensive, my HSA has probably grown. If healthcare gets cheaper, my HSA might shrink, but that&#x27;s fine because healthcare is cheap.')
