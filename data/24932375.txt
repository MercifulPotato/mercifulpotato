Columbus mistranslated units and ended up with a world about half the circumference of Earth, putting the far east in easy reach. NASA actually has an article on this [0].<p>Had the Americas not existed, Columbus would have had had to make his way back with no fresh water or food, and a high risk of scurvy.<p>0: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;pwg.gsfc.nasa.gov&#x2F;stargaze&#x2F;Scolumb.htm" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;pwg.gsfc.nasa.gov&#x2F;stargaze&#x2F;Scolumb.htm</a>')
