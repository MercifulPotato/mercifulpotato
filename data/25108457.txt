It was not user-hostile back then and a decent Unix env. and Apple seemed to be headed down the right path, promising to support FOSS.<p>Switched from MacOS to Linux circa 2008 when it became abundantly clear that Apple&#x27;s &quot;backing&quot; of OpenSource was a complete hoax designed to attract FOSS hackers to the platform and lock them in (the only parts of the OS that were FOSS were the lower layers, the entire stack above it was closed source - a tiger simply can&#x27;t change its stripes).<p>Never had to look back, and when I look at the locked-in walled garden privacy hell that OSX has become, boy am I glad I switched.')
