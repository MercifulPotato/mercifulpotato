Item(by='EugeneOZ', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604780666, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25014582, text='GraphQL should die in the same hell with SOAP.<p>For me 3 reasons &quot;why not GraphQL&quot; are:\n1) Access control: rights, permissions, roles - they are just a fog when client can write own query to DB.<p>2) Knowledge sharing: when your client knows too much about DB, you have a very tight coupling.<p>3) Complexity of the service layer. If there is no tool for the language you love - write your own GraphQL library. And there is no guarantee that your other client will be able to reuse this API.')
