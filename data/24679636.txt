Item(by='Nextgrid', descendants=None, kids=[24679821], score=None, time=1601824644, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24679612, text='SIMs are out of the question for public wifi hotspots - to authenticate a SIM (via EAP-SIM or EAP-AKA) you need an existing relationship with the mobile carrier.<p>When it comes to MAC addresses, 1) there isn’t a central registry of addresses anyway so having the MAC address alone won’t help much in an investigation, 2) it can be set by the user so malicious users will fake their MAC address and 3) for privacy reasons most devices nowadays will generate unique MAC addresses for each network.')
