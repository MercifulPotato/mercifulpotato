Item(by='sriku', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611099301, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25835866, text='One purpose is to enable you to concentrate on work, but I am don&#x27;t think that&#x27;s the biggest win. The core idea here is to use physical space mapped to mental space so you can move between activities easily. It can be a specific corner of a room too. Like - &quot;when I sit here I&#x27;m ideating, when I go there I&#x27;m designing, when I sit at that desk I&#x27;m coding&quot; sort of split. You don&#x27;t need to be fine grained, but it is similar to &quot;when I sit at the dining table, I eat&quot;. Quite possible to design this even in small spaces.')
