Item(by='stickfigure', descendants=None, kids=[25431967, 25431860, 25434803, 25433570, 25433975, 25431796], score=None, time=1608049403, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25428767, text='There is absolutely no limit to this. Putting on a suit for a financial job interview exploits the cognitive biases of interviewers. Tattoos exploit the cognitive biases of hipsters.  Putting hockey-stick growth projections in pitch decks exploits the cognitive biases of VCs. Equity grants (pretty much always) exploit the cognitive biases of startup employees. Driving a fancy car exploits the cognitive biases of (a very large portion of) the dating market.<p>Human beings are not computers. No aspect of human behavior is perfectly logical or rational. You simply cannot ban emotional appeals in principle, because all appeals have an emotional component. This way lies a dystopia far more terrible than &quot;grandpa shared some nonsense on facebook&quot;.')
