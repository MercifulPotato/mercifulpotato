It was named so because the gatherings were happing at Delos, a Greek island. Many state-cities had contributed to a fund that was to be used for guarding against future Persian attacks. Shortly after the league&#x27;s creation, Pericles moved its base to Athens, and the story goes that part of that fund (the equivalent of today&#x27;s $4 billion) was used to build Parthenon.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Delian_League" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Delian_League</a>')
