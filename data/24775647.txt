Item(by='Retric', descendants=None, kids=[24777154], score=None, time=1602677901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24773831, text='Solid state devices can still fail from mechanical stress.  Heat cycling and strong magnetic fields could be a problem for wireless charging even if they don’t need to be.  The core issue is generally trying to use the cheapest solution, not just mechanical stress.<p>Ethernet connectors are probably the best example of this, for servers that might be moved every few years their cheap and maintain a solid connection.  It’s really desktops and especially laptops where they become such a major issue.')
