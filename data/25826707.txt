If these companies were willing participants, Google would not have started encrypting it&#x27;s datacenter linkages which were subject to NSA fiber taps, Apple wouldn&#x27;t have continually sought to improve device encryption to defeat law enforcement, etc.<p>It <i>is</i> true most web services are impossible to audit the privacy of, as always, storing things yourself on hardware you control is the safest, but I&#x27;d argue that Google and Apple have very clearly different stances, if you follow the money.')
