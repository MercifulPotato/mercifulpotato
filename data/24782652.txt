Item(by='pvtmert', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602715925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24779787, text='I&#x27;m bit aganist having env-secrets inside container.<p>Because PID 1 has that env, all processes spawned from that can read all of those.<p>I prefer mounting them to &#x2F;run&#x2F;secrets via tmpfs. Which can also have selinux policy attached.<p>This way, someone else cannot read them by spawning shell inside container')
