Item(by='sandworm101', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607352845, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25332332, text='I&#x27;m all for electrification, but it is not the full answer when it comes to indoor air quality.  Too often &quot;electrification&quot; is cover for just not ventilating a room.  Rather than air ducts which waste valuable space, a developer would rather throw in an electric baseboard heater and a tiny window.  Net result: a sealed room with no real airflow.  Dust, mold ... name any indoor pollutant and it will be worse without ventilation.')
