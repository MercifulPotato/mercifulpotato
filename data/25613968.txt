Item(by='goto11', descendants=None, kids=[25614405, 25614035, 25615527], score=None, time=1609605510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25613786, text='Movie scripts follow a very strict format. Always courier 12pt, no bold or italic. Every scene have a standardized header. Characters names and certain other keywords are ALL CAPS. It probably originated in the age of mechanical typewriters, but the format has become completely entrenched and everyone in the industry knows how to read it.<p>Lots of script guidelines like (&quot;1 page is one minute of screen time&quot;, &quot;the first turning point should happen before page 20&quot;) assumes a standard script format. So you cannot just change to Times New Roman if you think it is more legible.')
