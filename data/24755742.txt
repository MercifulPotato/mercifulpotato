Item(by='NovemberWhiskey', descendants=None, kids=[24786434], score=None, time=1602518163, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24755283, text='&gt;The concept of free speech has nothing to do with governments or private institutions.<p>But the manner in which governments can affect free speech is very much broader, because they can criminalize and regulate it in a way that is generally binding on all societal participants.<p>Saying that free speech has nothing to do with governments is like saying making contracts is nothing to do with governments: except that the government includes the judicial branch that decides disputes over contracts.')
