Item(by='jeffschofield', descendants=None, kids=[25337095], score=None, time=1607372354, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25336173, text='Tailwind is a system for design. It constrains what options the designer has to work with. This means both less chances to get things wrong and a smaller domain to wrap your head around.<p>Tailwind is configured out of the box by some very excellent designers. The color palette, fonts, spacing, etc. are all crafted to work together harmoniously. You have to go out of your way to configure it poorly.')
