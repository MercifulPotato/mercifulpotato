Item(by='petra', descendants=None, kids=[25211861, 25213094, 25209856, 25210043, 25209577], score=None, time=1606314616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25209246, text='What&#x27;s missing from this system(and P2P systems in general), is automatic pinning - i.e. the system automatically choses which files to pin on which user, to ensure every file is available on the net.<p>It&#x27;s a bit of tricky problem, because people may not like unknown, random files on their pc, and probably, because such algorithm is distributed and will need to resist attacks.<p>But i think that the payoff is big: what Bitorrent did to popular files, such an algorithm could do to rare files.')
