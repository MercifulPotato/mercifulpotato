Item(by='LukeShu', descendants=None, kids=[25076860], score=None, time=1605222994, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25076504, text='Not just what &#x27;aaronbrethorst said; Cairo isn&#x27;t just <i>a</i> 2D graphics library, but it is <i>the foundational</i> 2D graphics library that underpins most of the modern GNU&#x2F;Linux desktop: It&#x27;s what&#x27;s used by gstreamer, GTK+ 2, GTK+ 3, Java... and if an app manages to avoid using any infrastructure that uses Cairo (Qt doesn&#x27;t), if the app does 2D graphics things then it&#x27;s likely that the app uses Cairo directly.')
