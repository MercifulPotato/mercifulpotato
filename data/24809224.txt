Item(by='2ion', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602934158, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24808511, text='In any case, yes:<p>If it&#x27;s a userspace-based model with kernel modules (like nvidia) - yes, you can insert&#x2F;remove kernel modules without rebooting and exchange userspace libs freely.<p>If it&#x27;s an in-kernel driver part like the i915 module that comes bundled with your distro&#x27;s kernel, you can kexec the new kernel, which is simple with systemctl kexec.<p>It will likely require to exit and restart the graphical session though.')
