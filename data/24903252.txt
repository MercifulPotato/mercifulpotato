Which means that it being able to infringe alone isn&#x27;t enough for it to violate 1201, but also that because it can be used for non-infringing purposes doesn&#x27;t mean it doesn&#x27;t violate 1201. As with many many laws intent matters and deciding intent is why we have judges and juries.')
