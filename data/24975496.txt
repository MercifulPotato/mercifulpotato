Item(by='mc10', descendants=None, kids=[24975574], score=None, time=1604367612, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24975351, text='OCaml functors are not the same as Haskell&#x2F;F# functors&#x2F;applicatives (which are essentially one and the same in OCaml if you use named arguments).<p>Instead, they&#x27;re roughly &quot;functions from modules to modules&quot;: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;dev.realworldocaml.org&#x2F;functors.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;dev.realworldocaml.org&#x2F;functors.html</a>.
