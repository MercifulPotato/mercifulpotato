Item(by='greg-m', descendants=None, kids=[25087177], score=None, time=1605294863, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25085960, text='Hey, I&#x27;m the PM at Cloudflare for WebSockets on Workers.  Support is still in beta, so we&#x27;re still working through the timeout details here.<p>With the Workers Bundled plan (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;developers.cloudflare.com&#x2F;workers&#x2F;platform&#x2F;pricing#bundled" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;developers.cloudflare.com&#x2F;workers&#x2F;platform&#x2F;pricing#b...</a>), your WebSocket connection will stay open until your 50ms of CPU time expires.  On Unbound (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.cloudflare.com&#x2F;introducing-workers-unbound&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.cloudflare.com&#x2F;introducing-workers-unbound&#x2F;</a>), which does not have a CPU time limit, your WebSocket connection will stay alive as long as it remains active and your Worker doesn&#x27;t exceed its memory limits.  If the connection goes idle, it may be terminated.  We&#x27;re currently considering an idle timeout on the order of 1-10 minutes.')
