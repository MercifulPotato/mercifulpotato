Item(by='JoshTriplett', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607456895, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25345375, text='The vast majority of users do not have a &quot;trusted network administrator&quot;; they have a hostile upstream network. That&#x27;s where the defaults come from. Trusting the network (or anything outside of the device itself) should always be a non-default setting, and nothing from the network should be able to change that setting. You should have the <i>option</i> of configuring a device you own and control to make its traffic inspectable, but that should never be the default.')
