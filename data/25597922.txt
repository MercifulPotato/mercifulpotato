Item(by='roelschroeven', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609453817, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25597446, text='It has the same (approximate) field of view on your D3400 as a 160.65 mm lens would have on a full frame camera, yes. 157,5 mm come to think of it actually: as far as I know the crop factor on Nikon crop cameras is 1.5. On a medium format camera you would need an even longer lens to get the same field of view.<p>I&#x27;m not sure about the aperture equivalent. Aperture influences both exposure and depth-of-field, and I don&#x27;t think they vary in the same way between crop cameras and full frame cameras.')
