Item(by='dmurray', descendants=None, kids=[25334103], score=None, time=1607359596, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25333501, text='The main strategy is the tremendously complicated scheme of &quot;buy as many tickets as you can physically key in, any time the prize pool goes above $x.&quot; Everyone who played the Michigan or Massachussets lottery that week was &quot;hacking&quot; the lottery the exact same way!<p>That&#x27;s not quite the optimum solution - what if you accidentally played the same numbers a million times, instead of a million different sets of numbers? So you should make sure you spread out your plays. However, I believe spreading them randomly is &quot;good enough&quot;: the interesting maths only improves your edge a tiny amount.')
