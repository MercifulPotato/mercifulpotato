Item(by='u801e', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607146428, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25309129, text='I think the solution is to rely on hanging indents rather than trying to align with the column of the previous opening parenthesis, bracket, etc.<p>So instead of this:<p><pre><code>  def some_method(param1\n                  param2):\n      statement1\n</code></pre>\nyou do this:<p><pre><code>  def some_method(\n          param1,\n          param2):\n      statement1\n</code></pre>\nHanging indents work regardless of whether you use tabs or spaces for indentation.')
