Item(by='kordlessagain', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604592315, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24998741, text='Emotions just are. Thoughts come and go, but we may or may not pick the ones on which we loiter. If undesirable emotions ebe when we loiter on certain thoughts, repeated behaviors may emerge. Let&#x27;s do this to get rid of that emotion, your brain might say to itself.<p>Missing something, like your sunglasses, makes you wonder where they are, look for them, call people asking if you left them over, etc. This is worry, which is a light version of fear.<p>&quot;Missing out&quot; isn&#x27;t like looking your sunglasses but still makes you wonder if where you are isn&#x27;t where you should be, which is still a type of anxious behavior which will lead to repetition if the emotional response is allowed to go on unchecked.<p>Check out &quot;Plutchik&#x27;s wheel of emotions&quot; for emotion groupings.')
