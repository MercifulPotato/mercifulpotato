Item(by='danielrhodes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607029446, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25291034, text='It&#x27;s not about lock in or getting access to the side-show features available in other clouds. For a large company there is significant business risk in having all your infrastructure in one place. A billing error or some other misunderstanding could have catastrophic consequences. I&#x27;ve heard of absurdly close calls here. I know that one public company dependent on AWS maintains multiple accounts to lower the risk of this happening. Multi-cloud is another way to mitigate that risk.')
