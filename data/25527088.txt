Item(by='darkerside', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608815024, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25521522, text='I think one of the biggest problems we have today is that we use the same word for estimating or evaluating a characteristic or ability of a person, as we do for evaluating the person themselves. I am evaluating your ability to perform this complex task in this situation, and it has nothing to do with my value for you as a person.<p>You may not care whether I value you as a person, but at that point, that&#x27;s your problem and not mine.<p>I&#x27;m not even exaggerating, I believe this is a major source of strife in modern societies.')
