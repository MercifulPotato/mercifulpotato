Item(by='m463', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604016244, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24937356, text='I had a subscription for soylent for years.<p>I remember someone asking me if I could go 100%.  I said NO WAY.  maybe 10% of your diet, more is not great.<p>I treated it as a snack replacement because -- hey, it&#x27;s better than reaching for something really bad, right?<p>In the end I think it IS a snack.  It was better than eating donuts, or hotdogs all day, but is only by a matter of degree.  It is a highly processed food, which is not great.<p>I see it a little like the emergency room - it will prevent you from dying today.  But it will not help you with a healthy and thriving lifestyle.')
