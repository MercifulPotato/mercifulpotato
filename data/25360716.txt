Item(by='willis936', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607533080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25360520, text='The lack of RAMDACs in new GPUs is a real problem.  The nicest DP-VGA adapters I can find need to be imported from Germany and even they top out at around 200 MHz pixel clocks  before there are sync drops.  When the blanking time is factored in, 1600x1200 at 75 Hz is unstable.  Very disappointing when I have a P1130 (rebranded G520, 1200 TVL).')
