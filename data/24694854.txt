While Stephenson&#x27;s practice of inventing alternative names met with some derision (like <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xkcd.com&#x2F;483&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xkcd.com&#x2F;483&#x2F;</a>), it adds something to the work that wouldn&#x27;t come through the same way with familiar names.')
