Item(by='lmm', descendants=None, kids=[25937560], score=None, time=1611797584, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25935766, text='In an OO program all state is global, because you can&#x27;t understand any given object&#x27;s behaviour without knowing what its state is and what the behaviours of all the objects it communicates with are, and you can&#x27;t understand those without knowing their states either.<p>State can and should be hierarchical - and you can do that easily in FP - but the idea that OO lets you make it somehow non-global is a myth.')
