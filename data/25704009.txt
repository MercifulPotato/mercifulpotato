First 20 years free. Ten years more costs $10,000. Another ten $20,000. Etc.<p>Under this model, it would cost about $2.5M to maintain a copyright for a full century. If a work still remains valuable, you’re looking at spending millions annually on renewals, with the cost doubling every decade. It’s an incentive to innovate rather than collect royalties on the old stuff forever.')
