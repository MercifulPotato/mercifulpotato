Item(by='labawi', descendants=None, kids=[25648534, 25645942], score=None, time=1609853365, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25644207, text='If you want physically accurate averaging (resize, blur etc), then RGB is fine, as long as you use linear values (or do long-winded transformed math). AFAIU it is by definition 100% physically accurate. As was said, sRGB uses gamma values, where typical math creates ill effects, as in many if not most typical programs.<p>If you want to do perceptually uniform averaging of colors, color mixing &#x2F; generating &#x2F; artistic effects, that&#x27;s something else entirely.')
