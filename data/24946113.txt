Item(by='wk_end', descendants=None, kids=[24946322], score=None, time=1604086949, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24945980, text='One of the irritating things  about these CoC discussions is that the codes, really, are acting in a metonymic fashion. Codes of behaviour are, of course, common place all throughout the world. When people complain about Codes of Conduct in software, they’re broadly complaining about a particular movement that’s pushed codes of particular conduct, enforced with particular mechanisms (and they would often argue biases). The HN guidelines don’t fall under that umbrella.')
