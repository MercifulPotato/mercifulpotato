Item(by='panic', descendants=None, kids=[25505718], score=None, time=1608628657, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25476842, text='Apple&#x27;s Frameworks teams tend to ship two kinds of APIs -- APIs designed around the needs of first-party apps and APIs designed specifically for third-parties to use.  Core Data is characteristic of the second kind of API.  It sounds cool in theory, but when you try to use it, it never really seems to do what you want it to.  Hence the need to &quot;use an abstraction layer&quot; over an API which is itself an abstraction layer (sqlite) over an abstraction layer (file I&#x2F;O).')
