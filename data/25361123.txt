It&#x27;s up to the jury to decide what weight, if any, to place on the evidence.<p>Now, true, the judge is probably not going to let the defense attorney, in closing arguments, say &quot;That evidence came from a hack of the defendant&#x27;s phone, which is a really invalid thing to do, so you should ignore that part of the evidence.&quot;  Judges get <i>really</i> unpleasant with attorneys who cross the judge&#x27;s lines on what they can and can&#x27;t say in front of the jury.<p>So this would probably be left to the jury to figure out on their own.  I&#x27;m not sure how likely that is...')
