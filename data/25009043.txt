You will be free, and you will live a life truly worth living. We all fall short of this ideal, of course. Yet trying to live up to it really works.<p>Naturally, the obvious criticism is that while you may not be unhappy, it may an indication that you are not actually living. At best, you exist without allowing yourself to &#x27;feel&#x27;. To live is to feel pain.')
