Item(by='jacobolus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611973687, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25965360, text='For output of SDR content in a typical context on recent displays with small pixels (e.g. &quot;retina&quot; displays), a high-quality-dithered 8-bit image is visually indistinguishable from a dithered 12-bit image.<p>But for image editing, 16-bit integers or single-precision floats are certainly helpful.<p>And for HDR and wide-gamut images, 8 bits gets a bit limiting.')
