Item(by='CloselyChunky', descendants=None, kids=[25972267], score=None, time=1612030371, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25970111, text='Small nitpick: The existence of one-way functions has not been proven, yet. Actually proving this would also prove `P != NP` so this would be a big deal (interestingly enough, proving that one-way functions do _NOT_ exist, would _NOT_ prove that `P = NP`). Currently we can only assume and hope, they exist.')
