Small businesses, like small anything, are less efficient than larger versions. The inefficiencies inevitably bother customers.<p>It just so happens that in business, much of that inefficiency is the number of employees required per unit of economic activity. Ultimately all employees are an unwanted cost to the consumer. Small businesses have fewer options to avoid that cost, so they account for more employment.')
