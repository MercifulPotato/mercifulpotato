In the age of social media, it is extremely risky to go against the accepted narrative in different parts of the world.<p>For example, many large entertainment firms display pro LGBT messages in the States. However, they choose to selectively remove those messages in Asia.<p>It&#x27;s been established that knowing your audience, their political leanings, so that you can shape your narratives correctly is key.
