Item(by='breakfastduck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604432199, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24952436, text='I have significant professional experience in audio engineering so let’s skip the petty rhetorical questions.<p>Windows audio drivers are so poor that they’re bypassed completely and basically all major hardware producers offer either ASIO drivers on windows or native CoreAudio drivers.<p>Using the same high quality external sound card, I get better performance on macOS with latency and buffer than I do on a windows machine with significantly ‘better’ hardware.<p>I know full well sound hardware makes a difference, and macOS and CoreAudio interfaces with said hardware significantly better than windows.
