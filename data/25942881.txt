It is typical to put clauses into standard contracts that would never hold up in court, but that people will follow anyway out of fear.<p>I believe that everyone in the corporate world, or even wider, should go through a &quot;Law 101&quot; course just to learn the basics of contract law and how our legal system works, so people can recognize such behaviors.')
