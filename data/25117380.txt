Item(by='AntiImperialist', descendants=None, kids=[25117989], score=None, time=1605563270, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25116655, text='I would highly advise against rolling release distribution of Linux. Please hear me out.<p>The idea behind having a rolling release is that your upgrades happen in small chunks and therefore the breakages are small and manageable. The problem? The can come in anytime.<p>When you do LTS release upgrades for example, you know when you have to do that and can do proper preparations and set aside time to do it.<p>While the rolling distro upgrades work fine most of the time, the odd time it doesn&#x27;t takes away all the supposed benefits... and it can happen at a time you&#x27;re not prepared for.')
