I had ridden in taxi cab number 1729 and remarked that the number seemed to me rather a dull one, and that I hoped it was not an unfavourable omen. &quot;No,&quot; he replied, &quot;it is a very interesting number; it is the smallest number expressible as the sum of two cubes in two different ways.&quot;')
