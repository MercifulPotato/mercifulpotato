Stokely Carmichael is credited for coining the phrase institutional racism in the late 1960s. He defined the term as &quot;the collective failure of an organization to provide an appropriate and professional service to people because of their colour, culture or ethnic origin&quot;.<p>Racism isn&#x27;t purely about the belief that one race is superior to another.
