Item(by='cassianoleal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611013603, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25827395, text='&gt;  Mechanical engineering has specifications for &quot;bug&quot; tolerance.<p>That&#x27;s what these days is referred to as an SLO.<p>Golden signals, USE, RED are ways of measuring them on a high level.<p>You program your systems to be resilient to a certain level of failure (retries, graceful failure), and you program them to be able to cope without a malfunctioning part by isolating it from the rest of the system (circuit breakers, bulkheads).')
