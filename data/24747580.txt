Item(by='seanwilson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602438217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24745194, text='The pattern I don&#x27;t like is the one where people load JS like jQuery from a CDN with a local fallback if that doesn&#x27;t work:<p>- realistically, nobody is going to be testing if the fallback functionality works as the site grows so you&#x27;re going to get weird breakages if the fallback misbehaves (e.g. your local file is a different version).<p>- big CDNs go down so rarely it&#x27;s not worth thinking about (and if it is serve it from your own site)<p>- it&#x27;s easy nowadays to put your whole site behind a CDN so there&#x27;s little point (the article debunks the caching advantages)<p>I think people are just copy&#x2F;pasting snippets that do this from somewhere without thinking.')
