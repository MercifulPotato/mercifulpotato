Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=[25407140], score=None, time=1607869893, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25406611, text='And “<i>or</i> else” has long been perfectly normal (“if a then do b, or else do c”).<p>See also “elsewise”, equivalent to “otherwise”; use in this construct “if a, b; elsewise, c” is a good deal rarer.<p>I would commonly speak sentences like “I was not hungry; else would I have eaten it” among family and friends, but not typically among the general public. It definitely feels a tad archaic, or at least highfalutin.')
