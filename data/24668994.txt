Item(by='thristian', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601691973, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24668772, text='Often, when a database entry is geotagged with a country, province, or city, trying to plot that entry on a map will result in a marker being drawn at the exact geometric centre of the region, implying more precision than actually exists. When the database entry represents something important to people, like stolen property or a missing person, and something happens to be at the place the map marker lands, desperate people can do desperate things.')
