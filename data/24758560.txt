Item(by='Animats', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602531588, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24757173, text='<i>So much of the internet is gathered under Cloudflare, a single failure point.</i><p>It&#x27;s worrisome that Cloudflare has a DNS provider, a domain registrar, a certificate authority, and a Border Gateway Protocol system.
