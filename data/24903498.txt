Everyone gets this instinctively when it comes to &quot;Sir, this is a Wendy&#x27;s.&quot; Sometimes mundane things like lunch take precedence over great moral conflicts: not because the conflicts are unimportant, but because we shouldn&#x27;t hold up the drive-thru line until the great conflicts are resolved.
