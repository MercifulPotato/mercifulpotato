That said, cyanoacrylate (?) does seem to have sudden fracture failure, e.g. cracking.<p>When in doubt, I&#x27;m a firm believer in two part epoxy [0]. Fiberglass impregnated when necessary. Also stores easier!<p>And where water is involved (e.g. fiberglass shower tubs), marine epoxy like PC-11 [1].
