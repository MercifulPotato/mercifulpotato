Item(by='simonblack', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607835268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403333, text='This was quite common back when. When a single-sided floppy cost about $5, it was a no-brainer to see if you could convert it to read the other side as well.<p>You only needed a piece of cardboard to act as a template and a one of those leather punches. My 20 floppies instantly became 40, saving me around $100 bucks.<p>A couple of years later, double-sided floppy drives arrived and we didn&#x27;t need to make our &#x27;flippies&#x27; any more.')
