For example, at least in my mind, Noncommercial and Free Trial kind of want to work together, for a pretty common use case.<p>Folks involved in drafting certainly thought about multi-licensing. But I not so secretly hope the licenses are easy enough to read, without legal help, for diligent folks to work through implications themselves.')
