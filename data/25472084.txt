Item(by='andyjpb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608325883, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25471730, text='Mutually Assured Destruction relies on the victim knowing that they&#x27;ve been destroyed.<p>Lots of these so-called &quot;Cyber weapons&quot; are operated by actors who are very effective at leaving no trace.<p>It&#x27;s been in the background before but in the reports about this SolarWinds issue the &quot;leaves no trace&quot; angle is starting to be emphasised much more.<p>Not knowing if or what has been compromised means that the attacker can choose when or how to use the information they obtained and the victim will be surprised, even a long time down the line.')
