Item(by='bob33212', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607182860, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25311095, text='Projects fail because you have misaligned incentives. The CEO gets a pat on the back when he announces a Technology Modernization project, The Executive team gets a pat on the back when they negotiate 20% off the Modernization Project with IBM. The IBM salesman gets his commission as soon as the deal is signed.<p>Then when the project fails everyone blames someone else. If you want the project to succeed tell all those people that they will get their reward at the end of a successful project not before it has even started.')
