Item(by='pengaru', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606594210, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25239728, text='Unless I&#x27;m mistaken, the efficiency gain is from shortening the length of the pivoting pins to just what&#x27;s required for connecting the conventional outside chain links to the saddles.<p>In a conventional chain the pins cross the sprocket teeth and must rotate across that entire width; more frictional loss.<p>In this design the saddle seats on the tooth, and just the relatively short pins linking the saddle to the outside chain must pivot.')
