Item(by='layer8', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608679363, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25512151, text='I’m aware of the trade-offs. :) I’d rather use a digraph operator for mutating assignment, again with the Huffman coding argument. One design I’m thinking of is using “@” as the mutable qualifier (e.g. “@int” is a mutable int), and correspondingly use “@=” for mutating assignment. (The swirl in the “@” glyph is meant to allude to mutation, and it’s a character that visually stands out in code, as mutation should.)')
