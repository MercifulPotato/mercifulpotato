Item(by='titanix88', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607663749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25373831, text='Clojure is a great language for small creative teams to achieve great(relatively) things. It&#x27;s difficult to understand that from a startup mindset of hacker news where money is no problem and peace of mind is an after thought.<p>If you are a budget constrained individual living in a third world country with nothing to invest but your intellect and time, Clojure is a very good language. It lets you to -\n1. Create relatively large systems without pulling your hair out.\n2. Juggle between different projects with relative ease. Great for managing a projects in maintenance mode. Great for contractors with long term support contracts.\n3. Realize your vision of a complete product with relatively low time and money.<p>Overall, it&#x27;s a way of doing effective programming. It&#x27;s probably not a good way of making money if the focus is to siphon investor money with a large team and millions of lines to show for.')
