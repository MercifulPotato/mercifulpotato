Item(by='rswail', descendants=None, kids=[25767268], score=None, time=1610548041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25760175, text='DNS search in MacOS is managed by their DNS infrastructure which is documented under resolver(5).<p>That lets you route the resolution of particular domains to specified nameservers. The files live in &#x2F;etc&#x2F;resolver but can also be manipulated with scutil.<p>So it&#x27;s not an OSX deficiency, it&#x27;s an OSX difference, similar to launchd vs systemd.')
