Item(by='biztos', descendants=None, kids=[25680767], score=None, time=1610066557, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25675173, text='I don&#x27;t think that&#x27;s a quirk.  In Germany the public health insurance is based on your salary, but private insurance is not -- and independent contractors (such as everyone working for Gumroad) are required to use the private option.<p>From the private insurer&#x27;s point of view, it would be crazy to charge more or less based on how much someone works or earns.  The public insurance does so out of a solidarity imperative coming from the government, and is subsidized as needed to make that possible.<p>In the US, there is no public option.  Feature working as designed, for better or worse.')
