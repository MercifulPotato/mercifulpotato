Item(by='brudgers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604345899, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24969176, text='I&#x27;m sometimes a villain. I try to avoid it. I try to accept and acknowledge it when I am. Hard as it is, I have even apologized for what I have done to people who did not expect, let alone demand, an apology.<p>There are people who don&#x27;t act in good faith. People who will harm those who love them because those who love them deserve it. Thinking about it as agreeing to disagree is a categorical error.<p>This is orthogonal to familial obligations one might observe. It is healthy to recognize that difference. To recognize that observance does not require assuming good will. By not making one&#x27;s self vulnerable in the way that such an assumption makes one vulnerable.<p>There are times for &quot;my colleagues on the other side of the aisle.&quot; And there times to accept that some people are harmful to one&#x27;s well being.')
