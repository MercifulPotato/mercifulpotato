Item(by='brendangregg', descendants=None, kids=[24657428], score=None, time=1601593021, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24648569, text='Please stop using bonnie (I know, it&#x27;s a 2008 post, but in case it inspires anyone). It was useful at the time, but written in a different era, and nowadays produces misleading results on multi-threaded I&#x2F;O stacks (along with other problems). I spoke to Tim about this issue back at Sun, and he did write about it.<p>Today I usually use fio for disk&#x2F;FS benchmarks, as it&#x27;s frequently updated by Jens Axboe, who is also responsible for the modern Linux multiqueue block I&#x2F;O implementation.')
