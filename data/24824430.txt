Item(by='dmurray', descendants=None, kids=[24830932, 24824479], score=None, time=1603097414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24824363, text='I would think of it as closer to an equity investment: they provide some of the capital and share in some of the upside. And they likely provide some of the things equity investors do: strategic advice, domain knowledge, access to their network, all of which may or may not have value.<p>In some jurisdictions they may need to structure and describe it as a loan. But a non-recourse high-interest variable-payback-schedule loan is really blurring the lines between equity and debt no matter what you call it.')
