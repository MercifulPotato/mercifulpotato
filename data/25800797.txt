There you see a list of your alias names. And you can create a new one with a click. Underneath the alias name you select which email address to forward to (I manage several email addresses for my family). That‘s it. You can even setup aliasses in a way that you can send messages from them, so they do not serve for receiving emails alone.<p>Their service is excellent. You get answers from real human beings within 24 hours (unlike trying to get help for Gmail; won‘t work).')
