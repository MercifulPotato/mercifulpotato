Item(by='vorpalhex', descendants=None, kids=[25347826, 25349556, 25347583], score=None, time=1607444943, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25346460, text='I&#x27;m a software architect and how I handle this between my teams is:<p>+ Set an expectation for teams to raise difficult problems they don&#x27;t know how to solve for guidance<p>+ Help teams meet and determine standards and contracts together<p>+ Make sure teams have the power to make bold choices within their own domains<p>Sometimes these go a bit off the rails or a less optimal choice gets made and frankly... that&#x27;s fine. Mistakes are where we learn and letting engineers make mistakes and recover from them is part of letting engineers grow.<p>Being an effective manager often means buying cover for your team to do their work and evolve it organically.')
