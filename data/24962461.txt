Abolishing timezones would be more practical now than at any point in the past, but it&#x27;s also less beneficial.<p>It&#x27;s more practical because you can almost instantly learn what hours the sun is up anywhere in the world, which lets you make an educated guess about whether people are awake or in the office (and this would work without timezones). It&#x27;s less beneficial because you can almost instantly learn what time it is anywhere in the world (with timezones).')
