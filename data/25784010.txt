Item(by='gradys', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610664589, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25783152, text='I made a tool for visualizing text datasets in 2D or 3D: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nebulate.ai" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nebulate.ai</a><p>It runs a machine learning model in your browser to convert the text into points in a high dimensional space, and then it projects those points down to 2&#x2F;3D.<p>Right now you can tell it to visualize post titles or comments from any subreddit or load an hourly updating snapshot of Twitter.<p>You can also view your own data in it by selecting the New Nebula option.
