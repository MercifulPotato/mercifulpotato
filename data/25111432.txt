Item(by='rich_sasha', descendants=None, kids=[25111446], score=None, time=1605533836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25111284, text='It would seem at least reasonable to hope that one follows from the other. If our bodies fight the virus effectively, the viral loads we would shed ought to be lower. The period where high virus loads are found in our bodies will be shorter, requiring less hospitalisations, and thus chances to give it to other vulnerable people.<p>There are of course lots of hypotheticals here, and things to be concerned about, but a priori surely we should be hopeful it reduces infections too?')
