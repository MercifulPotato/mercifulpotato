At least not in hireable numbers.<p>And those that do are buried deep in the guts of consultancies, who can afford to pay them way more than customers can.<p>It&#x27;s a shame, because it results in suboptimal software. And suboptimal tooling available to the devs that do work in that space.<p>The best solution I&#x27;ve seen are products that lower the knowledge barrier of entry (at least for creating a proof of concept) + designing for non-programmers as your primary users.')
