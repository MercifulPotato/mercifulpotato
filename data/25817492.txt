Item(by='sircastor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610936942, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25815091, text='We’ve been a part of our local buy nothing group for a couple of years. It’s been really great. We had some neighbors we met in 2019 to our Christmas Party that year.<p>I’ve also been able to get rid of a bunch of stuff I don’t use, and we’ve received all kinds of things, including a Laptop and power tools.<p>I’ve found one of the most empowering things about giving stuff away is I’m free from trying to figure out if I’m getting a fair price for selling something, or if it’s going to “the right person”. No strings giving is really nice.')
