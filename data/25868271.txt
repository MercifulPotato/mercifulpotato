Item(by='ahepp', descendants=None, kids=[25869184], score=None, time=1611289179, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25866879, text='I think if you&#x27;re writing a widely read, critically acclaimed, publicly available blog and feel that anonymity is crucial for your safety then yes, it would be appropriate to practice opsec.<p>Journalists should be hostile, in the sense that they should not collude with subjects on how to present a story to the public. The crisis in journalism we see today is that there is <i>too much</i> collusion between subjects and journalists.<p>There&#x27;s a clear public interest in knowing the identity of a subject. Ideally there would be no journalistic discretion at all. Reveal the information, unless there&#x27;s a clear, concise, and open policy rationalizing not doing so.')
