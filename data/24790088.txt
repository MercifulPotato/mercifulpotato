Item(by='mdorazio', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602777933, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24789156, text='No.  Microjet printers use a binder (like a glue&#x2F;epoxy) with powders (usually ceramic) and sometimes add colors as well to achieve the end product.  SLS uses a laser to melt together (sinter) the powder (in this case nylon, but could also be metal).  They are both powder-based, but the method of turning the powder into the end result is quite different.')
