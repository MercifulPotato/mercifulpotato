(e^i^x) =  (e^(ix)) therefore is periodic as well. And the most natural periodic cycle is a circle (which is of course, just sin&#x2F;cos).<p>To make this &quot;period 4&quot; into a proper circle, multiply by pi&#x2F;2.<p>i^(pi&#x2F;2)^x == period 3.141592.... or the &quot;circumference&quot; of the unit circle.<p>e^i^(pi&#x2F;2)^x == e^(i * pi&#x2F;2 * x).
