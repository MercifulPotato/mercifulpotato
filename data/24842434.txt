Palo Alto Networks(California) and Orca Security(Israel) would not, however there could be made a case that the video in question resides on servers(youtube) in New York.<p>The argument for the application of 15 U.S. Code § 45b appears to only apply to &quot;form contracts&quot;.<p>&gt; means a contract with standardized terms— (i) used by a person in the course of selling or leasing the person’s goods or services; and (ii) imposed on an individual without a meaningful opportunity for such individual to negotiate the standardized terms.<p>It appears as though the EULA is a form contract and Orca indeed falls under the protections of the Consumer Reviews Fairness Act.<p>EULA: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.paloaltonetworks.com&#x2F;content&#x2F;dam&#x2F;pan&#x2F;en_US&#x2F;assets&#x2F;pdf&#x2F;legal&#x2F;palo-alto-networks-end-user-license-agreement-eula.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.paloaltonetworks.com&#x2F;content&#x2F;dam&#x2F;pan&#x2F;en_US&#x2F;asset...</a>')
