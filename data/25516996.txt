The content rarely engages you, and that&#x27;s part of the feature that&#x27;s trapping you.  Partial reinforcement is extremely addicting.  If you gave somebody a pile of dimes and said &quot;put these in this slot machine, pull the lever and get 11 cents every time&quot; nobody would play that game.  But the randomness of a slot machine means that when it hits, your brain lights up.<p>Youtube&#x27;s &quot;recommended for you&quot; section is a slot machine for whatever will engage you.  For me it&#x27;s novelty, for a lot of people it&#x27;s an irresistible clickbait title.  Nobody is immune to it, although I feel the hacker news crowd might be suspect to clickbait much more specific than whatever youtube chums the waters with on their front page when you&#x27;re not logged in and have no history (open Youtube in an incognito window to see what I&#x27;m talking about, it&#x27;s terrible).')
