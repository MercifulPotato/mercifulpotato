Item(by='pmoriarty', descendants=None, kids=[24700212], score=None, time=1602001103, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24697832, text='I got a physical ballot last time I voted, but when I went to submit it, it was placed in to... an electronic vote counting machine.<p>This also defeats the point of democracy.<p>Machines can not and should not be trusted for voting, no matter what fancy schemes are dreamt up for to make them supposedly verifiable.<p>Voting can not be legitimate unless machines are taken completely out of the loop and humans are the ones doing all the vote counting by hand from end-to-end.<p>Yes, there can still be fraud, but it will of necessity be quite limited and relatively easy to detect than if voting and&#x2F;or vote counting was done by electronic means.')
