I bumped into this first, way back in the heyday of CD burners, deep diving on all the info and comparisons I could find. A real aha moment in my life.<p>With cars, there are often just superficial differences to create a much larger perceived value difference between an Audi or VW or a Lexus or Toyota (on some models).<p>I find this topic quite fascinating, as it relates to human psychology.')
