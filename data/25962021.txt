Item(by='jjk166', descendants=None, kids=[25962791], score=None, time=1611952188, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25957759, text='Censorship is not and never can be a solution to the issue of misinformation. At best it can hide the problem as people are forced to take their wrongthink underground, but there it festers and no one can effectively root it out.<p>No the only way to deal with incorrect information is correct information. You can&#x27;t suppress the argument, you need to win the argument. People need to be taught how to think critically and evaluate various conflicting sources of information, we must go out of our way to present information showing our positions to be true and call out even those we want to agree with when they don&#x27;t, and we must take the high road and maintain composure even when others do not act in good faith. Yes, it&#x27;s exhausting, but no one ever said doing the right thing is always easy.')
