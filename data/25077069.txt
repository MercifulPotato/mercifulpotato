This is reinforced by most of the dotnet templates and documentation, which make it feel like if you ask for a banana you&#x27;re given a gorilla and the entire forest whether you want it or not.<p>In comparison, if you look at Go you&#x27;ll see the &quot;net&#x2F;http&quot; package.
