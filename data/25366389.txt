Item(by='OpieCunningham', descendants=None, kids=[25367461], score=None, time=1607553928, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25366016, text='Supermarkets do not pay for product as you describe. Manufacturers and&#x2F;or distributors pay the supermarkets slotting fees to get their products on the shelves. Additionally, slotting fees are higher for ideal shelf location, and in some cases proximity to competitor products. For example, the bottom shelves are undesirable. And then you have end caps which are highly desirable.')
