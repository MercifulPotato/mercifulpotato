Item(by='aeyes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602633239, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24771612, text='Using the NNTP protocol (which is very similar to SMTP) each message has two parts: Headers and content. Your client would usually only download message headers which contains information like author, date, title and message size.<p>Back when UUCP was still around there wasn&#x27;t much binary data in groups. It took a while to develop algorithms to encode binary data (uuencode, base64), the protocol was text only.')
