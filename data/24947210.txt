Item(by='m463', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604094563, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24930615, text='I&#x27;ve written a lot of stuff in pygame on the pi.<p>The major issue I ran into was no hardware acceleration, so performance came from (software) blits of only the updated part of the screen and similar techniques.<p>But I found it very fun to program in an environment with integrated graphics and sound and fonts and video and input devices and a event loop with timer ticks.<p>A lot of linux folks are thinking x vs wayland, but I&#x27;ll bet if we replaced everything with an integrated environment that had everything necessary to support a game, we would have much better linux user interfaces.')
