Item(by='vmlinuz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605153673, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25065963, text='Of course there are specific niches, but it&#x27;s pretty easy to answer in general: &#x27;actual professionals&#x27; need some combination of two criteria - capacity and reliability&#x2F;durability&#x2F;usability. A device which can handle &#x27;large&#x27; loads - be it CPU-bound, memory&#x2F;storage-bound, or even screen size bound, and can be worked hard for 6-8-10-12 hours per day, 5-6-7 days per week without either the machine or the user breaking.<p>The point is not a professional using a computer, it&#x27;s a professional computer user - someone who doesn&#x27;t just use a computer to do their work, but someone for whom the limits of the computer are the limits of the work they can do.')
