Item(by='a_humean', descendants=None, kids=[25063239], score=None, time=1605128520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25062791, text='Its actually quite rare to see a library without either first or third party support these days. Many new libraries are either written in typescript, or the authors have taken care to create declaration files or contribute to definately-typed. If a library reaches any degree of popularity it usually isn&#x27;t long before a third party declaration appears.<p>Older libraries that are long abandoned by their maintainers usually don&#x27;t have anything. At work we pretty much ignore all libraries that don&#x27;t have types from somewhere these days, but that doesn&#x27;t eliminate many things.')
