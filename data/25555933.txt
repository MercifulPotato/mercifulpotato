Item(by='Macha', descendants=None, kids=[25557121], score=None, time=1609119642, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25554935, text='Syncthing (and SaaS equivalents like Dropbox) don&#x27;t really qualify as backup tools because as soon as the file is updated on one device it&#x27;s updated on all devices. Paid services usually give you a certain amount of rollback to cover the &quot;oops didn&#x27;t mean to save&#x2F;delete that&quot; case but even that won&#x27;t help for something like ransomware trying to keep you out of files.
