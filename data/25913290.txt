Linear transformations in logit space don&#x27;t preserve the sum = 1 requirement of the corresponding probability so you can&#x27;t just take the sigmoid of each element individually. This is just a slick way of writing it, you can write it all out without any logits &#x2F; sigmoids, but it&#x27;s easier to do the algebra in logit space.')
