Item(by='Enginerrrd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609435805, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25593865, text='I agree with the spirit of this, but there are some deeply broken aspects to this idea.  First, you need to define what a society is.  A large component of that definition is going to come down to some semblance of shared expectations.  Those shared expectations are your duty.<p>At the risk of being guilty of the same hyperbole I&#x27;m criticizing: While it&#x27;s true that a society saying you shouldn&#x27;t murder your neighbor is ostensibly &quot;less free&quot; than one that allows you to determine for yourself whether or not that&#x27;s a commitment you want to endorse, the resulting society is in many ways much more free in a pragmatic sense.')
