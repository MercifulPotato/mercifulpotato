Item(by='strogonoff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604451901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24985499, text='In English, “I believe X is Y” is often used to mean exactly the same as “I think X is Y”. If you believe “Scientist thinks X is Y” is equivalent to bullshitting, your loss—chances are they arrived at their hypothesis through a line of reasoning worth hearing. “Scientist knows X is Y” would be a more suspect turn of phrase.')
