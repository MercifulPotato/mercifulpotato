Item(by='thu2111', descendants=None, kids=[24690778, 24690328], score=None, time=1601911665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24686059, text='For a very long time, it did.<p>Weaknesses of Access in no particular order: very poor multi-user&#x2F;multi-device-access features. No real concept of servers. Buggy, had a nasty habit of corrupting its custom file format causing data loss (Excel never does this). Does expect you to understand SQL, data normalisation, etc. VBA just about serviceable for a spreadsheet but not good enough for a &#x27;real&#x27; database-backed app.<p>Probably someone could try and make a better Access. There are surely lots of startups doing that already, along with hosting. All the &quot;no code&quot; tools that are out there, etc. Access had the advantage of coming with Office so lots of people had it already, whereas today&#x27;s subscription based services that host your data have incremental cost.')
