Item(by='AlanYx', descendants=None, kids=[25291969], score=None, time=1607014783, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25290065, text='I can&#x27;t speak for California law, but where I live (which has a very generous wrongful termination regime), an employee resignation ultimatum (i.e., &quot;if you don&#x27;t agree to X, I will resign&quot;) is one of the classic employee blunders. It&#x27;s not even really &quot;hardball&quot; for an employer to run with it, since being able to characterize it as a resignation rather than a termination is one of the few things that can dramatically affect what they may have to pay out.')
