Item(by='incrudible', descendants=None, kids=[25918589, 25918739], score=None, time=1611680577, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25917698, text='If there&#x27;s more money to be made from keeping people sick than outright curing them, that logically is a disincentive from curing them.<p>It&#x27;s not conspiracy thinking to look at the incentive structures within systems to predict possible negative outcomes.<p>Of course, there&#x27;s a <i>moral</i> incentive to not keep people sick, but history shows that we can not rely on people acting morally. The pessimism is justified.<p>If we expect companies to provide cures over treatments, we at least must allow them to profit as much or more from a cure as they would from a treatment.')
