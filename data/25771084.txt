Item(by='davidwsilva', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610591457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25728504, text='The Solid Project uses OpenID authentication (which is built on top of the OAuth 2.0 protocol). The claim is that Solid uses OpenID to uniquely identify every single shared object in a Pod. OpenID is then responsible for the access control to Pod&#x27;s resources. It is an interesting and intriguing enough of an idea which makes it worth investigating (even for curiosity-only purposes).')
