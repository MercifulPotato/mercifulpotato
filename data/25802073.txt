A missing piece cannot roll by itself.”\n”Have you ever tried?” asked the Big O.\n“But I have sharp corners,” said the missing piece. “I am not shaped for rolling.”\n“Corners wear off,” said the Big O, “and shapes change. Anyhow, I must say good-bye. Perhaps we will meet again…”<p>Often times we forget that the first step in getting what we want is making steps and effort to move toward it. I think you can read this like - &quot;No one can love you until you love yourself&quot; because of the missing pieces attempts to find a partner.<p>The other read is that you can trick yourself into thinking you are making progress by focusing on all these other things around you and looking for someone &#x2F; something outside of yourself to make the progress for you.
