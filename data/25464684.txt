From CSS 2.1 section 10.3.3:<p>&quot;If both &#x27;margin-left&#x27; and &#x27;margin-right&#x27; are &#x27;auto&#x27;, their used values are equal. This horizontally centers the element with respect to the edges of the containing block.&quot;<p>i.e. horizontal centering is easy because that&#x27;s a natural thing to want to do in a document, but vertical centering seems to make less sense and is thus correspondingly more difficult.')
