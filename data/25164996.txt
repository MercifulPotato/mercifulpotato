Item(by='konjin', descendants=None, kids=[25165080], score=None, time=1605906642, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25162803, text='This is not literate programming.<p>Literate programming involves having a meta language that is extended into the target source code through nested macros.<p>The killer feature was the ability to see everywhere a piece of code was used on dead paper by looking at the auto-generated index, with the chunks being <i>logical</i> rather than language driven.
