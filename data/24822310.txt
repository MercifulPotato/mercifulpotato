Item(by='jph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603067213, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821883, text='How to run well on a wide range of phones and networks is a big challenge. The PRPL approach of preloading wastes bandwidth, and is a hard pain point for people who pay by bandwidth. Yet the load-on-demand approach is perceptually slower.<p>Svelte and Sapper get pretty close to what PRPL is describing, including much of the guidance of Next.js &amp; Nuxt.js.<p>What can help? Browser hinting is one approach. Browser settings that make it easy to accept&#x2F;reject irrelevant content are another approach. Browser proxies with compression and filters are another.<p>IMHO success looks like the user&#x27;s agent&#x2F;browser&#x2F;phone&#x2F;desktop saying &quot;Please use this kind of quality of service profile for now, and send what&#x27;s most relevant.&quot;')
