Item(by='WhyNotHugo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606780285, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25255536, text='&gt; Is there a reason to look at poetry if you&#x27;ve got the pip&#x2F;virtualenv combination working fine?<p>You probably have a bunch of scripts that do what poetry does (either that, or you repeat the same commands over and over A LOT).<p>Switching to poetry might have some initial overhead, but a big upside is that you stop using custom, internal tooling, and use something industry-standard.
