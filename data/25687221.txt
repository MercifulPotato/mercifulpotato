Item(by='sean_pedersen', descendants=None, kids=[25691635, 25687346], score=None, time=1610127234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25686978, text='Thanks for the reference. But this misses out on another important tool already in use against spamming: White lists.<p>Yes, in theory PoW makes mass mailing very expensive and thus directly hurts spammers that are not on the common anti-spam white lists. Organizations that need to mass mail users for legitimate reasons are however most likely already on anti-spam white lists and thus need not to do a very hard PoW hash or none at all as they are already proven to not be spamming.<p>So this paper&#x27;s conclusion is not the real deal.')
