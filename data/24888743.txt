Item(by='mierle', descendants=None, kids=[24888806], score=None, time=1603651435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24867643, text='My team works on Pigweed [1], which offers a more terminal based approach to embedded development. Instead of working with Make, we use GN [3] as our primary build system (though we also support CMake, and plan to support Bazel).<p>The &quot;pw watch&quot; command is an integrated watcher that can detect file changes from e.g. vim, then re-build, re-flash your device, and re-run tests according to the dependency graph.
