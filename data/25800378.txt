Item(by='KirillPanov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610775971, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25799877, text='You&#x27;re hand-waving past a critical detail when you say &quot;tied to&quot;.<p>Signing and encrypting are different processes.  Verifying a signature uses the public key, which everybody has.  Decryption uses a secret key, which by definition isn&#x27;t publicly available.<p>Most of the schemes you&#x27;re thinking of (Intel SGX, etc) boil down to surrendering control to the CPU manufacturer and trusting them to manage your keys for you, and do the right thing.  Even when pressured by nation-states.')
