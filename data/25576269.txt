On HN earlier this year someone posted a link [1] which allows one to peruse property taxes anywhere in the state.<p>For this reply I picked a location in Daly City [2] to have a look. The black represent the highest property taxes and the green the lowest - there are few green bubbles. Take a look around, I&#x27;m sure there are areas like Huntington Beach [3] with absurdly low taxes, but I&#x27;ve not seen the actual analysis that favors the claims made either way.<p>Proposition 19 effectively eliminates the Parent to Child reassessment exemption, but it wasn&#x27;t done for altruistic purposes. It was done to put more houses on the market for the real estate profession who funded the campaign to pass the proposition.
