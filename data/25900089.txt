Item(by='trabant00', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611557240, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25897736, text='We can see from past examples that market share has big inertia. Even in cases where the top choice was under performing by a large margin it took years for majority of users to make the switch. Think about examples like Internet Explorer, Yahoo Mail, Windows on servers, etc.<p>We can also see from those past experiences that at first a very small minority of - let&#x27;s call them power users - made the switch when otherwise the underdog seemed hopeless. But from them the infection spread. I think power users are also &quot;influencers&quot; and are worth a lot more that one single normal user as far as market share goes.<p>Now, functionally, WhatsApp is still good. So it&#x27;s not clear if this will lead to anything. But something happened, there&#x27;s no denying that.')
