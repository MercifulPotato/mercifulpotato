I also use it to put a &#x27;float&#x27; of CO2 in bottles that I want to last longer.<p>Simply, I put a bottle under the SodaStream nozzle and give a few puffs of CO2 to be confident that it&#x27;s displaced most of the air.<p>I do this with normal drinking wine sometimes, but more often things like vermouth that I don&#x27;t open daily.')
