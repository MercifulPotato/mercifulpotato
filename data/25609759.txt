Item(by='alsetmusic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609554888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25609319, text='See if you can get involved in a community outreach program or similar.<p>A friend was in a therapy theater program that operated at San Quentin. They helped the prisoners write and act in theater pieces. At the end of the program, one of the prisoners, a lifer, told her that it had given his life meaning for the first time since sentencing. He&#x27;d been in for something like 15 years. I can&#x27;t imagine how it would feel to find out you made such a difference for someone.<p>The US prison system is a terrible thing. That our society derives jokes about the atrocities within really shows the depravity of its citizenry.')
