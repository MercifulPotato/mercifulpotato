Item(by='dleslie', descendants=None, kids=[24943550], score=None, time=1604070525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24942849, text='IIRC, Steam still does not allow a developer to release a Linux-only title.<p>It may seem like a minor or fair limitation, but the ability to release Windows-only titles is a key component in maintaining the dominance of Windows as a gaming platform. Letting developers release on Linux first might go a long way to increasing adoption.<p>I can imagine releasing on Linux a few months early, dealing with the issues not caught during development, then launching on Windows.')
