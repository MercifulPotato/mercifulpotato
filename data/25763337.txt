Item(by='tlb', descendants=None, kids=[25765408], score=None, time=1610552443, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25762971, text='You may have a postmodern version in mind, but the actual Gresham’s law doesn’t involve any hard decisions. It says that when using coins made of precious metals, people will always spend the lightest coins they have. A perfectly rational and simple behavior that causes all the circulating coins to be underweight.')
