Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605470695, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25103972, text='&gt; What on earth does it mean to &quot;out&quot; a trans person?<p>Typically, it means to publicly reveal, when this is not welcome by the subject, the gender-socially-ascribed-at-birth (usually, on the basis of external genitalia) of a person whose gender identity and public gender presentation are not consistent with that ascribed-at-birth gender. (Though in the case of the legislation at issue, it generally goes beyond that kind of outing to also include forcibly ascribing the gender-ascribed-at-birth and regulating substantive behavior and options on the basis of that ascribed gender.)')
