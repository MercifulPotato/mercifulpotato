Measuring raw entropy basically  assumes that the only one non-random thing a user can do is repeat a character.<p>for example, by the p*log(p) measure<p>`1234567890abcdef` and `936a0ce28b1d74f5` \nboth have log(16!) bits of entropy, yet one of them is much easier to guess than the other.')
