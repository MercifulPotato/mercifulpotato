Item(by='coddle-hark', descendants=None, kids=[24713291, 24711238], score=None, time=1602095139, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24710813, text='Yes, but in reality you don’t want your page to load until all above the fold assets are loaded anyway. We went through this a couple of years ago with increasingly complicated techniques to avoid the dreaded “flash of unstyled content”.<p>Head-of-line blocking is only a problem if you’re multiplexing connections. Just open one TCP socket per request and you’ll never have head of line blocking. There are issues with opening multiple TCP sockets of course (mainly Slow Start) but these aren’t insurmountable.')
