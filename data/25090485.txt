No surprises when it comes to pronunciation.<p>It&#x27;s modern. It&#x27;s one of the youngest languages in the world.<p>The language emerged from contact between French settlers and enslaved Africans during the Atlantic slave trade in the French colony of Saint-Domingue.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Haitian_Creole" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Haitian_Creole</a>')
