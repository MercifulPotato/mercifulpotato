Item(by='simonh', descendants=None, kids=[24967220], score=None, time=1604306867, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24961791, text='I was simply responding to the claim that the only reason to revoke a cert is to disable malware, and that such requests were very rare, and gave a counter example showing this is not true.<p>One of the uses of certs is to sign test builds with a different cert from the production release, so that you can ask Apple to disable them after release. So Apple gets requests to revoke certs for this, and other reasons all the time. In this case HP had used a different cert for these drivers as against other drivers, showing that they used different certs for different purposes, that&#x27;s all.')
