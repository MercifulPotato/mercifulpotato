I recommend reading his books back to front because he&#x27;s clearly difficult to edit and starting at the end means you don&#x27;t have to go through the process of listening to him sound his ideas out. If you only read his &quot;Bed of Procrustes,&quot; and a selection of his technical papers, you&#x27;d be both better off, and following most of his advice.<p>A hagiography is a story that exalts the life of a saint. An &quot;autohagiography,&quot; is what some writers call a shamelessly self-aggrandizing memoir. Motohagiography was a play on writing about motorcycles in a self-aggrandizing way, but could be extended to other technology. But thank you for giving it some thought!')
