Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603161171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24830567, text='Don’t take on straightforward tasks in the name of expediency. That’s the hole you’re already in and you have to stop digging.<p>A little pair programming or watching someone use the new thing you wrote can go a long way toward informing you on how to close the gap. The tricky bits may not be that tricky for others, and the obvious bits often aren’t.<p>It’s not all roses, of course. The person who doesn’t understand that you built a ladder for them to climb may complain loudly about how long it took you to get to the top of it.  It takes a somewhat special team to be able to do the right thing and not get punished for doing so.<p>Somewhere in between that and Lord of the Flies is most teams.')
