Search engines and archival tools can handle all this very well today.<p>Yes, if a site loads a ton of external content it has the potential to break. But that doesn&#x27;t apply to just JS but also images and all other static assets. If a JS-heavy site is hosted completely from its own domain there&#x27;s nothing stopping easy archival of it.')
