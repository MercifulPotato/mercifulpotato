Item(by='legulere', descendants=None, kids=[25689238], score=None, time=1610134190, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25688589, text='No right is absolute, because there are areas where exercising one right might infringe on another right.<p>Everyone has a right to their life, which is one of the most sacrosanct rights, but in self-defence you might still rightfully kill someone else. Those intersections of rights are always a fine line to walk on and you have to be careful so it is not abused. Just putting one right above the other can never be the solution.<p>Also freedom of speech has its limitations. Defamation is one case where I guess most people can get behind.<p>I think one could also make the case here, where there is a real danger that democracy can be lost.')
