I&#x27;ve noticed that coworkers who don&#x27;t keep their cameras on seem less human.  Without a face to the name, it&#x27;s harder to conceptualize them as a person.<p>So I keep mine on.  I think video presentation is going to become the new &quot;dress for the job you want&quot; over the next decade or so, so it&#x27;s worth it to put a little bit of effort in.  Now that remote is becoming normalized, do you want to be the person on Zoom with a video that&#x27;s grainy from trying to compensate for the lack of light, or the person with lights that make them look like they just came from the spa all the time?<p>It shouldn&#x27;t make a difference, but it does.')
