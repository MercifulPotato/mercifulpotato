Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=[25392884], score=None, time=1607722362, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25390861, text='No, you don&#x27;t get a 100% credit for VAT against your income taxes; the credit only exists as an offset against your VAT payable liability.<p>If for some reason you can&#x27;t offset your input VAT, then you get the same deduction against your income as you would for a sales tax, because it&#x27;s part of your expenses. BUT NOTE: that if the acquired good is depreciated, then in both cases (VAT or sales tax) the tax-related deduction is spread over several years rather than all at once.')
