Item(by='tgbugs', descendants=None, kids=[24742066, 24741890], score=None, time=1602363029, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24741475, text='I work with a consortium that is trying to map the anatomy of the peripheral nervous system. My key take home has been that our knowledge of peripheral anatomy at population scale is woefully inadequate. People look at Vesalius and think &quot;oh, that was settled 500 years ago,&quot; but as it turns out it seems that we know far more about the peripheral anatomy of our pets and domestic animals than we do about our own.')
