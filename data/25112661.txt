Item(by='scythe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605539759, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25111269, text='Fructose is almost always absorbed; fructose malabsorption causes SIBO. Fiber reduces the glycemic index and makes fruit more satiating. Glucose aids the absorption of fructose preventing SIBO.<p>But in the case of fruit w.r.t. gout, the missing variable here is that while fructose increases the production of uric acid, potassium promotes the excretion of uric acid [1], and fruits are generally an excellent source of potassium while soft drinks contain little to none. The net effect of fruit on gout risk seems to be inconclusive [2] but it&#x27;s clear that fruits are much less concerning than foods with added fructose. Since a significant fraction of Westerners do not consume enough potassium [3], and potassium is key to preventing hypertension and stroke [4,5], it is bad advice to suggest reducing fruit consumption for most people, unless you&#x27;re already eating like Steve Jobs.<p>1: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S0085253815337844" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S008525381...</a><p>2: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;academic.oup.com&#x2F;rheumatology&#x2F;article-abstract&#x2F;58&#x2F;7&#x2F;1133&#x2F;5475481" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;academic.oup.com&#x2F;rheumatology&#x2F;article-abstract&#x2F;58&#x2F;7&#x2F;...</a><p>3: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S0270929506001434" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S02709...</a><p>4: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;link.springer.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;10.1007%252Fs11906-011-0197-8" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;link.springer.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;10.1007%252Fs11906-011-019...</a><p>5: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.thieme-connect.com&#x2F;products&#x2F;ejournals&#x2F;abstract&#x2F;10.1055&#x2F;s-2006-933305" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.thieme-connect.com&#x2F;products&#x2F;ejournals&#x2F;abstract&#x2F;1...</a>')
