Recently I purchased an RTX 3090, and between my Ryzen with 24 threads, and the 64 GB of memory I bought for a few hundred dollars it was really occuring to me how much power my PC has for really not that much money.  I don&#x27;t need to be spending so much cash on cloud services when my local machine has more than enough horses to do everything I need.<p>I think the M1 is one more force towards the pendulum swinging back.
