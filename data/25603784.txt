Item(by='NovemberWhiskey', descendants=None, kids=[25604496], score=None, time=1609514273, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25603401, text='We used to use distcc (2007 time period) for the daily builds of one of our large, in-house C++ products - order of 10M LOC.<p>In principle, it was a good use-case with a highly modular structure and a clearly defined but chunky build graph.<p>In practice, it did work, but throwing more hardware at the problem on a single host turned out to be faster than the existing distcc setup and had much reduced operational complexity.<p>We could probably have tuned it further with distcc plus the new hardware, but we achieved the performance target we were looking for.')
