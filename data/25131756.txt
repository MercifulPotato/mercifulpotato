Item(by='johngalt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605659711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25127049, text='Paraphrasing:<p>&quot;People don&#x27;t want a drill, they want the hole&quot;<p>Talk to your intended audience to figure out what they want to accomplish, not just the tools they ask for.<p>This is a useful way of looking at things. Yet, there is a limited distance we can expect the audience to traverse before using what they are familiar with. The number of fax machines in existence shows the staying power of habits.<p>As a startup, look at the decison&#x2F;control points specific to the &#x27;job to be done&#x27;. This is a game of revealed preferences and principle-agent problems.')
