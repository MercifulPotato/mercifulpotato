The Open PKM Catalogue is meant to be a reference to all things personal knowledge management.<p>I run a Discord server (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;discord.gg&#x2F;HzkFcs" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;discord.gg&#x2F;HzkFcs</a>) where we talk about PKM. Most of the questions we get are about the meta process of how to take notes - &quot;regardless of what tool i&#x27;m using, what are practical workflows and patterns I can adapt to effectively manage information?&quot;<p>The open pkm catalogue is our attempt to collect and curate what we know on the topic.<p>It currently has the following sections<p>- methods: different ways of working with notes\n- tools: different tools used to work with notes\n- ref: a glossary of terms and features related to pkm<p>The catalogue is very sparse. like swiss cheese sparse. there&#x27;s a lot that&#x27;s missing. if there&#x27;s a tool or workflow that you love, please contribute it to the catalogue')
