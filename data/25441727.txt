Item(by='tsimionescu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608121477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25439516, text='Wrong syntax is more like using the wrong words in human languages than it is like what we normally call bad grammar.<p>If you write in C &#x27;foo(a;&#x27;, that&#x27;s equivalent to saying &#x27;ball foo with a&#x27; - the compiler really can&#x27;t understand what you mean.<p>The equivalent of &#x27;I go store&#x27; is something more like writing &#x27;int p; printf(&quot;%d&quot;, p);&#x27; - the compiler understands what you mean, but it&#x27;s bad style, and possibly wrong (you&#x27;re supposed to initialize a variable before you read it).')
