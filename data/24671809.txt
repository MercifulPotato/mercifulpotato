Item(by='tenebrisalietum', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601730659, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24671771, text='Sometimes the market chooses the best of various bad options, if the only options available are those bad options, or information to avoid the bad options is not readily available (e.g. you should be able to know which apps are undeletable before buying the phone).<p>So the best product, that violates the ideas of ownership in this case (if I own a device I should decide what apps run on it) might win in a free and open markets.  But it&#x27;s still OK to, for example, enforce normal expectations of device ownership, or enforce disclosure of information to ensure the consumer choice is genuine.')
