Item(by='fastball', descendants=None, kids=[25687174], score=None, time=1610126385, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25681791, text='Nobody proposed growth at all costs, but I don&#x27;t think we have the same understanding of what growth means. To wit: how does your standard of living &quot;reach a certain point&quot; without growth? How does your SoL get higher without <i>growing</i> your manufacturing capabilities; without <i>growing</i> your power generation capacity?<p>We&#x27;re neither a virus nor a cancer. We&#x27;re the only thing that matters. The earth doesn&#x27;t care if it dies. It doesn&#x27;t have feelings. The last [insert endangered species here] doesn&#x27;t know that it is the last of its kind, it doesn&#x27;t care either. The <i>only</i> reason these things matter is because <i>people</i> care. The solution is therefore never going to be &quot;make people&#x27;s lives worse&quot;.')
