Item(by='gfodor', descendants=None, kids=[25580706, 25581125, 25579045], score=None, time=1609304031, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25577258, text='The argument isn&#x27;t for freedom of speech per se, it&#x27;s that if you remove the ability for people to persuade others through discourse, you remove the ability for progress to occur (other than through violence or death) on the same issues the former group is concerned about. And it is eminently falsifiable and has been proven to be the case - one does not have to look very far back in recent history to see free speech (and other liberal approaches to resolving differences) overturning prejudices and ending injustices that have lasted for centuries.')
