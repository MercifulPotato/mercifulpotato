Item(by='petschge', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607021034, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25291953, text='While maximum resolution is limited by the diameter or the largest baseline in an array, there is one advantage to single dishes: There is no lack of small baselines. Which implies that you can only really get images of large, dim structures with a single dish.<p>Also sensitivity is given by total collecting area, which is very competitive for a single huge dish compared to a limited number of small dishes.')
