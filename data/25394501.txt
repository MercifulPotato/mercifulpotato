The dense tech community here espouses a lot of ideas around meritocracy and capitalism that make it reasonably center&#x2F;right-leaning.<p>This is of course not to suggest right wing <i>extremism</i> is present here, but merely a general lean. A lot of folks mistake being “socially liberal” as sufficient to be “left-leaning” when in reality the fiscal part of the equation is inextricably linked as well.<p>The big government and regulation one often sees as part of implementing an equitable society is not supported here. You know that as well as I do. This is a community with a lot of hackers and startup minded folks who see regulation &#x2F; government involvement as an impediment rather than a platform to lift up the disenfranchised.<p>Disruptors don’t like the forces that squash disruption. Fairly unremarkable.')
