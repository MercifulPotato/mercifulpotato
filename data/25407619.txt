Item(by='cgriswald', descendants=None, kids=[25408007], score=None, time=1607876545, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407226, text='&gt; &quot;or&quot; is almost always exclusive, i.e &quot;do you want to eat pizza or pasta?&quot; does not imply that eating both is an option.<p>English is way too ambiguous for this example and often relies heavily on context. Wanting to eat pizza does not preclude also wanting to eat pasta. If it was exclusive or, and you wanted to eat both pizza and pasta, the honest answer to this question would be &quot;no,&quot; but what you should say is &quot;yes.&quot;')
