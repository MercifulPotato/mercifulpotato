Item(by='clintonb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611087244, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25837662, text='I&#x27;ve played Jackbox games monthly with my family via Zoom. I have a gigabit fiber connection, and they have slower cable connections. The Jackbox timer can be configured to a longer amount, but we&#x27;ve never had an issue with this. Most timer issues have been due to someone being distracted by something else, not the game itself.<p>Generally, if a group can use any form of videoconferencing, they can use Jackbox. It&#x27;s no different from one person sharing a presentation. I&#x27;ve only encountered one game that required realtime interaction, and we know to avoid it.<p>It&#x27;s pretty easy to get around corporate machine lockdown by using a personal machine to host. Participants are just using the web browser.')
