Item(by='hliyan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609336798, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25577821, text='&quot;If you want to be happy, live two notches below your means&quot; was a piece of advice I received at age 22 and have been doing my best to follow ever since. Applied mainly to vehicles (which can cost as much as 1&#x2F;2 the value of a person&#x27;s home where I live), electronic devices and things that are considered luxuries. I do not, however, apply this to basics: food, clothing and housing. This strategy requires you to turn a blind eye to what your peers spend on, though. That&#x27;s not easy for most people.')
