Item(by='upofadown', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611770747, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25929194, text='Forward secrecy normally requires a handshake where both endpoints are online. Signal is obsessively forward secret. The Signal Protocol&#x27;s claim to fame is that it can do offline messaging while still providing forward secrecy. It very much requires a server to store the required cryptographic state to do so.<p>I guess the restriction could be considered some sort of downside that comes with protocol complexity.')
