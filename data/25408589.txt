However, there were some things in CC that I didn&#x27;t find elsewhere. As an example, it focuses on how a lot of people don&#x27;t realize they&#x27;re in a poor&#x2F;tense conversation until it&#x27;s too late, and it emphasizes the need to develop the skill to become aware of this.<p>OTOH, CC attempts to prescribe how to behave are pretty poor. I would not focus too much effort on emulating those.<p>At first read, all 3 sound like fairly different books.
