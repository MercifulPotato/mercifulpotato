In many industries, that &quot;investment&quot; would be accounted for as capex, and in many countries you don&#x27;t get an immediate write off against tax for capex but you get it over some approximation of useful life of the assets you&#x27;ve invested in.<p>There is a strong case for allowing immediate, or faster, write-offs for capex spending in preference to lowering corporate tax rates because of this effect.')
