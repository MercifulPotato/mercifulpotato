We have a vague idea that mandarins, tangerines, satsumas, clementines, nadorcotts are all different varieties, but the typical catchall is &quot;mandarin&quot; or increasingly in supermarkets, &quot;easy peeler&quot;.<p>You wouldn&#x27;t be corrected over here.')
