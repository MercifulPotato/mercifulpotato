Item(by='Hnrobert42', descendants=None, kids=[25069245], score=None, time=1605185120, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25069124, text='It is very much not anonymized. It is trivially easy (albeit not cheap - around $10k&#x2F;month) to buy access to mobile ad id data, draw a polygon around your house, and get all the “anonymous” ids. From there, you can see all other locations of those ids.<p>When they anonymize you, that just means they give you an is not linked to your phone number or account. It doesn’t mean someone can’t link it for you.<p>The best defense to this is to reset your ad id regularly. Or on iPhones, turn off ad personalization. I don’t know about Android.')
