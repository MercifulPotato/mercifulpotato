Why bother? They have used their product; they see it as &quot;good enough&quot;.<p>But very often, end users get frustrated by things like input latency, and can&#x27;t express what is making them frustrated in specific terms. So they tell their IT department that &quot;it&#x27;s slow&quot;, and IT goes back and starts hammering on the __ team (networking, server farm,  whatever).<p>It&#x27;s remarkably powerful if you can help you users develop vocabulary to recognize and report what they experience. (it&#x27;s also flipping hard)')
