There is also the reason that perl is closer to C than a lot of other languages in this regard.<p>Copying (often happens when passing an arg to a function &#x2F; class) a reference is much quicker (only a 4 or 8 byte copy depending on arch) than doing either a single level shallow copy (4 <i>x</i> length(array) or 8 <i>x</i> length(array) bytes).
