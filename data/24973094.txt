Item(by='moultano', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604350045, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972024, text='There are lots of positive feedbacks, but they are very difficult to predict the timing and magnitude of with any degree of certainty.<p>Some examples include:<p>- increased fires increasing CO2<p>- melting permafrost releasing CO2 and methane<p>- less ice decreasing ocean and land albedo and absorbing more sunlight<p>- greater temperature and salinity gradient reducing the mixing of the ocean and reducing the amount of CO2 it can absorb<p>- warm water absorbs less CO2<p>- and lots more.')
