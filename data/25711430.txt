Item(by='cgriswald', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610260339, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25711132, text='I pronounce etc in my head as “ets” as if you’re about to say “et cetera” but stopped short. I read <i>etc.</i> in print the same way and have since I was a kid.<p>I suspect that split pronunciation between head and mouth is very common. I do the same thing with some words, like giblet, which in my head has a hard g, but when I say it I say it with a soft g.')
