Item(by='hgibbs', descendants=None, kids=[25738929, 25739524], score=None, time=1610404602, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25738093, text='The reason not to trade something that cannot be hedged is because it is impossible to replicate. Of course if the future cash flows of the security are known in advance this point is moot, but for options the idea is that you can replicate the option position by holding a dynamic hedge (this is basically what the black-scholes pde says).')
