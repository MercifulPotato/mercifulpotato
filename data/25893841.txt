Because some people think their choice to use hard tabs and a given tabstop setting are right and refuse to allow autoformatting tools or even just a hard tab replacement. Or they might argue: sure, the formatting is a mess, but if we reformat the code it will make tracing version control blame logs more difficult.<p>35 years ago, a friend told me the he and the other guy working on a piece of code would play tug of war with their shared files. My friend would replace tabs with spaces whenever he had to edit a given file, then the other guy would replace leading spaces with tabs whenever he edited a file.')
