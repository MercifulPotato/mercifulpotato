If you&#x27;re worried about arguments with your spouse becoming fatal, please talk to a counselor ASAP.<p>As for negligent discharges, which are probably the most legitimate concern, you should always follow the four rules of gun safety:<p>1. Treat all guns as if they are always loaded.<p>2. Never let the muzzle point at anything that you are not willing to destroy.<p>3. Keep your finger off the trigger until your sights are on target and you have made the decision to shoot.<p>4. Be sure of your target and what is behind it.<p>In my experience, these are hammered into civilian students who take firearm training classes, but soldiers in the military often haven&#x27;t heard of the four rules and generally are far more casual with their guns than civilians.')
