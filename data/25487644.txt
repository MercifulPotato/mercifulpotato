Item(by='zippy5', descendants=None, kids=[25489466, 25487851], score=None, time=1608483749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25487530, text='I think it’s worth noting that popularity isn’t always deterministic or meritocratic.<p>I once read about an experiment where users would listen to music and and the platform counted the number of listens and showed users. When the experiment was rerun, a different set of songs from the first experiment were popular. I believe I read this in Invisible Influence by Jonah Berger.<p>The point is that most content platforms select for content that is sufficiently good (or clickable) but not necessarily the best.')
