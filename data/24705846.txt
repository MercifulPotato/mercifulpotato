Item(by='BeetleB', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602052696, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24705488, text='If you look at most advice given on using spaced repetition well, the standard advice is &quot;Don&#x27;t learn random stuff&quot; and usually some other advice on ensuring what you put in your cards is meaningful to you.<p>For me, there is a bit of effort required in creating the card. That alone makes me only put in things I think are meaningful to me. Lots of times I learn something interesting and think to myself &quot;I should make a card out of that&quot;, but it&#x27;s too inconvenient. As such, most of my cards are for things I am <i>intentionally</i> studying.')
