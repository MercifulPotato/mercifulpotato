Item(by='qubex', descendants=None, kids=[24804528], score=None, time=1602868711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24802515, text='Variabilising all a company’s costs is every CFO’s dream: it makes all projections perfectly linear and totally eliminates any risk as zero revenue doesn’t create a loss because all costs also go to zero.<p>At least, in the start-up phase of operations.<p>Later, when economies of scale kick in and break-even has been far overcome, one seeks to negotiate smaller unit charges, primarily to capture more of the added value for oneself, and partially to reduce visibility of one’s revenue.')
