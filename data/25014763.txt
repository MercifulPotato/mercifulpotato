Item(by='jl2718', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604753177, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25011757, text='It’s not a majority, it’s a hierarchy. Humans self-organize into hierarchy amongst members of a network. The nuance is the size of the network.<p>Just a few people meme a hopeful idea into existence. Then you get the idealists, maybe 0.1%. Then the 1% of angry people latch onto it as enforcers. Then the 10% of people looking for acceptance send virtue signals of agreement. Half the rest agree out of apathy, and the remains keep quiet out of fear.<p>This is a game of telephone. The final form may be nothing like the ideal.')
