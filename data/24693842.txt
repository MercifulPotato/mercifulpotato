Item(by='dredmorbius', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601946587, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24693606, text='On a visit a decade or two back, there was a rather sobering history of fire on the mountain (Tamalpias) at a popular stop.<p>From the about 1875 to 1930 there was a major burn every few years.  There has now <i>not</i> been on <i>since</i> the 1930s, a few grassfires excepted.<p>Houses extend far up the mountain.  Streets are narrow, steep, and twisted.  Trees grow two, and sometimes <i>within</i> structures.  Wood and cedar construction are common.<p>Yes, the fog helps.  But it won&#x27;t protect forever.')
