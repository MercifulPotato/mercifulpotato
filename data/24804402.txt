Item(by='jtbayly', descendants=None, kids=[24804466, 24804470], score=None, time=1602878047, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24804326, text='For the people downvoting my comment, please note the following from Section 230:<p>&quot;No provider or user of an interactive computer service shall be treated as the publisher...&quot;<p>FB and Twitter are interactive computer services in this case, and we call them &quot;platforms.&quot; The law says they are <i>not</i> to be treated as the <i>publisher</i> of the content that users post on their site. Thus, there is a big difference between a platform and a publisher. That&#x27;s the whole <i>point</i> of the law.<p>Downvote all you want, but... that&#x27;s the law.')
