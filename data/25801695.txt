Item(by='totalZero', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610798217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801520, text='I agree with your desire to draw conclusions only on an evidentiary basis, but technically there is a logical flaw in your way of thinking:<p>If I isolate a virus from a bat in a laboratory, and then that virus escapes my laboratory (could be due to poor disposal of specimens, inadequate disinfection procedures for employees, etc -- not necessarily intentional), it would be both true that the virus came from bats and that the virus came from my laboratory.<p>I&#x27;m not saying that the virus came from the Wuhan lab. I&#x27;m just saying that &quot;it clearly came from bats&quot; does not preclude the possibility that the virus was stored in (or spread from) a particular laboratory.')
