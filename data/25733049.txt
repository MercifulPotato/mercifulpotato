Item(by='gruez', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610388454, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25731352, text='&gt;Common things that existed 20, 30, or even 40 years [...] often lasted 10 or 20 years in some cases<p>It&#x27;s worth mentioning that the common problem with these anecdotes is that it doesn&#x27;t account for survivorship bias. That 30 year old refrigerator at your parent&#x27;s house might lead you to conclude all refrigerators back in the day lasted 30 years, but in reality you&#x27;re only seeing the refrigerators that lasted 30 years, not the ones that broke down and were replaced.')
