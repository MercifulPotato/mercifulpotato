Item(by='meowzero', descendants=None, kids=[25537436], score=None, time=1608910805, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25530700, text='Maybe not A book, but some books that helped me change my behavior just a little bit.<p>Brain Rules for Baby: How to Raise a Smart and Happy Child from Zero to Five - if you&#x27;re thinking of having children or have young children already. This book gives some good insight on possible, data-driven ways to raise a happy and smart kid. I currently have a newborn and have altered some ways I behave around her. It also helps to think about how you were raised, and if that impacted the way you are.<p>The Mind Illuminated: A Complete Meditation Guide Integrating Buddhist Wisdom and Brain Science for Greater Mindfulness - if you&#x27;re into meditation, I think this is the book to get. I started out with Headspace and this book takes it to the next level.<p>How to Fail at Almost Everything and Still Win Big: Kind of the Story of My Life - This was an entertaining book on how Scott Adams failed and succeeded. He gives some practical tips on what he did to get to where he is. Of course, there is survivorship bias, but I found some of it helpful.<p>The Charisma Myth: How Anyone Can Master the Art and Science of Personal Magnetism - People aren&#x27;t born Charismatic. You can practice it.<p>Never Split the Difference: Negotiating As If Your Life Depended On It - A book about negotiation. I used to hate negotiating. Heck I still do. While I might not use the exact tactics outlined in the book, it helped me realize that I should try to negotiate more. And it might have helped me to get good deals or better salary just by asking.')
