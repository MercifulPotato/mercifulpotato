In the public setting? HTML comes with things missing from Markdown that you want, sure, but it also comes with a full scripting environment, the ability to arbitrarily inject code and so on.<p>If you can drop to HTML, you have to have a sanitiser process. So then you&#x27;re dropping to some-unknown-subset of HTML if the process is automatic - or you&#x27;ve failed to reduce the amount of effort being put on the editors if it&#x27;s a human one.')
