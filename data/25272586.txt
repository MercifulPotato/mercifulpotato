Item(by='pushrax', descendants=None, kids=[25273569], score=None, time=1606875797, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25271996, text='Expanding on this: usually linked lists allocate each element with a separate malloc() on the heap, so they end up all over the place in memory.<p>You could, in principle, allocate your linked list out of contiguous memory in such a way that sequentially reading the list doesn&#x27;t cause so many page accesses. That would itself be a complicated data structure to maintain, so simply using a linear array is often the right tradeoff.')
