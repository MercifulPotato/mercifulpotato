Item(by='sam0x17', descendants=None, kids=[25851714], score=None, time=1611154696, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25846770, text='I look at it this way: Which is a better world to live in? A world where a number of commercial entities make their software &quot;open&quot; but with commercial restrictions like Mongo, etc., or where these companies simply close source their products completely to avoid the consternation of the open source community for being &quot;open&quot; but not &quot;free&quot; for all use cases. Clearly the former is the better world to live in.<p>If we only allow a super narrow definition of open source to be considered open source, we are doing a disservice to the software community at large.')
