Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602011498, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24696886, text='It occurs to me that even with a self-hosted solution, project maintainers could benefit from having a broader corpus of sample data from various use cases.<p>One of the complaints about European armaments in the middle ages was that the guys making the plate also made the arrowheads and crossbow bolts so there was never the same arms race you had in Japan, as a common counterexample.<p>I think it would be cool to see a tool like this one, along with a data anonymizer, playing cat and mouse trying to scrub data versus inferring PID from it. I think that might entice more white hat security folks to investigate this problem space and it feels like we could use more of that sort of attention on this domain.')
