Item(by='Erwin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607017738, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25289461, text='An interesting feature if you want to just play Windows games once in a while is &quot;PCI passthrough&quot; aka VFIO. With that you can assign a PCI device to be used directly by Windows emulated within a VM, which lets you play game in Windows in emulation with almost no performance penalty.<p>But then you need another graphics card for your main Linux system.')
