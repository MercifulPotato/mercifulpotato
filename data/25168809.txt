Item(by='ivegotnoaccount', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605953463, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25168294, text='If you keep track of how many &quot;virtual servers&quot; your cluster has, and take into account the fact that the text obviously implies &quot;per server&quot; (let&#x27;s not play dumb simply to be able to spit on the article), the math works correctly:<p>- I have x servers. I optimize to 1&#x2F;10th of the time. I still have x servers but now have (1+9)x = 10x virtual servers.<p>- Now, I optimize again to 1&#x2F;10th of the time. I have 10x virtual servers, so (1+9)(10x) = (1+99)x = 100x virtual servers.')
