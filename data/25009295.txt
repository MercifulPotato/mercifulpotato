Item(by='dheera', descendants=None, kids=[25009433, 25010731, 25011725], score=None, time=1604683473, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25009120, text='In my experience technical debt almost always comes from management&#x27;s misconception that:<p>(a) A product that works now will always continue to work, even after you accumulate 10X the number of users.<p>(b) Adding new features only involves adding code, not rewriting stuff that already works.<p>(c) For a product A, delivering milestone A1 by deadline A1 is independent of delivering A2 by deadline A2. In reality the existence of A2 affects the timeline and architecture decisions of A1.<p>All of these are rampant massive fallacies in technical management.')
