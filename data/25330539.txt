Nothing beats the feeling of your first commit in the new project- be it a bug fix, a small feature enhancement, documentation improvements, etc.<p>I think this one is huge and helps to have a reinforcing effect on a lot of the other concerns. As long as you feel like you&#x27;re actively contributing something, even minor documentation fixes, it can stave off a lot of imposter syndrome as you mentally accumulate &quot;achievement capital&quot; and confidence.
