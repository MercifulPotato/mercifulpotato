Item(by='mleonhard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603240657, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24843144, text='I did a similar thing with some old VHS.  I used an SVHS VCR that had component output, a firewire digitizer, a firewire PCI card, a PC, and some Windows software to capture the raw video from the firewire card.  There were zero audio sync issues.<p>Tearing at the top and bottom of the image is unavoidable.  You just have to crop the top &amp; bottom of the video.  If you look carefully at the author&#x27;s example video, you can see that the professional video was zoomed in slightly, indicating that it was cropped.')
