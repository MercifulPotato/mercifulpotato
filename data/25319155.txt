Item(by='strokirk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607208349, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25311114, text='I like emojis quite a bit, and I suspect many other people do to. I&#x27;d be sad if the CLI tools I use today stopped outputting them, and I feel their output would lose a lot of clarity.<p>Many people are more visually oriented, and are greatly aided by images and color. A standard `NO_EMOJIS` environment variable could perhaps be used to help both camps, just like `NO_COLOR` is available today.')
