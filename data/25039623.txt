Item(by='falcolas', descendants=None, kids=[25043461], score=None, time=1604954535, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25039368, text='For English, it&#x27;s always &#x27;?!&#x27;. The question mark gets priority over all other punctuation but an ellipsis (which is pretty rare).<p>Other countries and languages (Japanese, in particular) give the exclamation point the priority.<p>As a side note, I can&#x27;t recall ever seeing the ligature of ?! before today. It feels like one of those typesetting relics we&#x27;re better off not using.')
