That sucks&quot; - Some pundit from the 1500s.<p>There is nothing inherently wrong with talented, ambitious people chasing opportunities where there is the greatest profit. A lot of discoveries can be made in that pursuit that benefit other domains.<p>This is a similar parallel to how space exploration led to discoveries that were used in other domains. \nLook at all the knowledge we as a society have acquired about making secure, reliable digital products due to engineers trying to make Google ads get more eyeballs (not to mention products like Google Translate, Google Maps, etc).')
