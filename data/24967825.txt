Do you see losing weight as a purely physical phenomenon, or do you see it as one that&#x27;s heavily influenced by psychology and other such factors? The actual physical act of losing weight i.e. reducing one&#x27;s mass needs to be distinguished, in my opinion, from all the factors that actually go into that. If it turns out that in response to hunger that your brain secretes a hormone that makes you <i>much more</i> hungry than the average, the psychological aspect of losing weight has been compromised, even while the physical laws stay the same.')
