Item(by='reificator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604217229, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24957031, text='Undefined <i>should</i> mean in most cases that you&#x27;ve made a programming mistake, be that a typo or an invalid assumption about the type of thing you&#x27;re getting back.<p>Null <i>should</i> mean in most cases that the data you wanted to access was invalid, but you can expect it to exist there in other circumstances.<p>Neither are a guarantee in JS, and you can find counterexamples for the counterexamples of the counterexamples, but that&#x27;s the intention behind each.')
