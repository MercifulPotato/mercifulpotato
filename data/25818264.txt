Item(by='codetrotter', descendants=None, kids=[25825896], score=None, time=1610945337, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817804, text='Seems like there are extensions for threaded discussions.<p>For example, the following XEP (XMPP Extension) talks about best practices for message threads:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xmpp.org&#x2F;extensions&#x2F;xep-0201.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xmpp.org&#x2F;extensions&#x2F;xep-0201.html</a><p>And XEP-0085 about chat state notifications has a section about threads.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xmpp.org&#x2F;extensions&#x2F;xep-0085.html#bizrules-threads" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;xmpp.org&#x2F;extensions&#x2F;xep-0085.html#bizrules-threads</a><p>So probably your best bet is to look at the list of supported features of all XMPP clients and see which ones support XEP-0085, and then between any clients that support that see if they support threading.')
