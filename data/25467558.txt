Item(by='chongli', descendants=None, kids=[25468878, 25476307], score=None, time=1608302790, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25467410, text='It’s a radio emission, not a radio transmission. That’s a key distinction that may be lost on a lay reader (or anyone not paying enough attention).<p>Astronomers, amateur astronomers, and astronomy enthusiasts all tend to be aware of the fact that radio is just another part of the spectrum we can use to look at the universe. It’s a strange quirk of our culture that the word radio carries connotations of technology and thus connotes intelligence.')
