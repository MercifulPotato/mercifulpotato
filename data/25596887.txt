Item(by='frombody', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609447331, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25594451, text='Jira is typically not slow.  It&#x27;s usually the plugins you have installed.<p>Some plugins like big picture even have their own  databases that need to be updated separately from the plugin, and can cause performance issues.<p>Start jira in safe mode and check the performance.  Then check your plugins one at a time until you find the culprit.<p>They also have some nice built in tools that often help you find the source of these problems.<p>It&#x27;s a product, but it&#x27;s also a platform, and sometimes the stuff that is built on the platform causes the platform to buckle.')
