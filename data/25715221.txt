Item(by='gonzo41', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610290964, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25714987, text='It&#x27;d be better to start off project with a depreciation schedule on software. Such as discussing plans for ROI after 5 years etc. Past that point, maintenance needs to be considered new work, and billed as a new project.<p>We&#x27;ve got to stop thinking about software in agile sprints once a product exists. I&#x27;m not saying don&#x27;t use agile for the build. But we&#x27;ve got to learn as software professionals to think like professions that maintain other expensive assets, such as aircraft, ships, or public works. There&#x27;s a recognition, that a 50 year dam, needs a tear down and replacement in 50 years.')
