Item(by='4rt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609597941, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25611609, text='I&#x27;ve used pulumi for work in a relatively large scale set of deployments, and have been using it since an early beta - but for Azure.<p>There are significant issues with it - terrible stability and reliability between version upgrades, its constant desire to tear down entire sets of resources and regenerate them when no change has occurred etc.<p>I assume these issues stem from the fact that pulumi&#x27;s azure providers are generated directly from the terraform providers and practically nobody ever uses those.<p>The only reason I&#x27;m mentioning it is that I only ever see pulumi described in glowing reports of success whereas for us it&#x27;s beginning to seem like a mistake ever using it in the first place.')
