Item(by='rglullis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610832633, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25805476, text='The <i>instance</i> dies, not the whole system.<p>Synapse (Matrix reference server implementation) had for a long time severe performance issues when dealing with large rooms. Clients would try to enter a room and would take literally forever to be able to get messages <i>for that room</i>.<p>Also, the natural reaction from people upon seeing how some rooms from bigger instances were causing problem was to <i>create</i> their own instances and their own rooms.<p>Federated protocols are anti-fragile.')
