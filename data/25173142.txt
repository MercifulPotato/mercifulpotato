Item(by='pugworthy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605996827, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170091, text='When I was a kid, my dad was a HAM and we used to spend hours printing TTY art and even making some now and then.<p>He&#x27;d get word that someone would be transmitting a picture at a certain time and frequency, then he&#x27;d set up the paper punch to record the transmission so we could replay and reprint at a later date.  Local HAMs would also loan and copy the tape reels so others could have copies.<p>Playboy centerfolds seemed the most popular thing back then with HAMs.')
