Item(by='aprdm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603122836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827309, text='An artist is mostly about the art and doing things that result in beautiful images approved by their supervisors!<p>Knowing programming can make them more effective but it is not the job, you want something that your CG Supervisor approves and that looks beautiful in the screen.<p>There are usually Technical Directors (TD) that work close to the departments and know programming more to help the artists.<p>That said, some artists can be quite technical and know programming. The tool Houdini which produces FX (simulations for fluid, debris, fire, etc.) usually has a couple of programming wizards. They can be quite good in algorithms &#x2F; hacking stuff together to make it work well in a scene, but not necessarily software design.')
