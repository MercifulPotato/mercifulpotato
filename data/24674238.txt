Item(by='jjeaff', descendants=None, kids=[24674606], score=None, time=1601754534, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24673374, text='I&#x27;m not sure that the affects of stimulants for ADD are across the board, as you describe. That is, for lots of people, me included, add meds seem to boost my executive functions.<p>Without them, I actually have no problem focusing on certain things, but it&#x27;s usually some unimportant problem or project that I become distracted by.<p>With meds, I find it much easier to step back and get a clear picture of not just my work, but my whole life. I become more attentive to my family, less irritated by distractions. And more a able to get important things done, like my taxes, that before would plague me.<p>I know some people seem to react to the meds by turning into a worker drone, But it doesn&#x27;t do that to me. It does make me more excited to make progress and get things done right.')
