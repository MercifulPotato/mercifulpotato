Item(by='crazygringo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608165151, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25449170, text='I&#x27;ve gone back and forth between Apple Books and Acrobat Reader a lot.<p>Ultimately I currently use Books because I do a lot of multicolored highlighting which is far more consistent in Books, and because Books lets you set bookmarks (to go back and forth between main text and endnotes) which Acrobat inexplicably still does not support.<p>Ultimately every PDF app is good at some things and terrible at others, and you&#x27;re just going to have to find the app that has the best set of features for you. It is pretty sad that all the apps have significant drawbacks for certain common workflows.')
