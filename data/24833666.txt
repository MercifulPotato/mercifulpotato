Item(by='bitwize', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603164292, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24831641, text='There&#x27;s one place where quicksand (or quicksand-like substances) is alive and well: as a hazard in video games. Even in recent Mario games, if Mario dips his toe in sand that appears to be moving, he will sink and eventually die. Earlier games gave you an out by mashing the jump button frantically to get Mario free, but the 3D ones don&#x27;t even seem to afford you that. The same goes for the bubbling purple muck in a jungle-themed level.')
