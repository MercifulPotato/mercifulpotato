Item(by='wongarsu', descendants=None, kids=[25306851, 25304742], score=None, time=1607100998, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25304385, text='There is a reason why fizzbuzz is a famous interview question. There are plenty of people who can&#x27;t code (to any reasonable standard) but still apply to programming jobs. They might be fresh out of college, or they might come from a job where they were doing glorified IT or making Excel spreadsheets.<p>&quot;super complex algorithm whiteboarding&quot; is obviously not the answer. You want something so simple no qualified person could fail even under stress, but hard enough that you know that the person is confident in writing if-statements. &quot;Iterate a B-Tree in-order&quot; filters too many people, &quot;compute the area of a circle&quot; is maybe a bit on the easy side.')
