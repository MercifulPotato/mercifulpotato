Item(by='YeGoblynQueenne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602091174, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24684865, text='The thing to keep in mind is that &quot;organic&quot; refers to the production of milk - how animals are grown, what they are fed, whether they are allowed to roam freely etc.<p>The rest, whether the milk is raw, pasteurised, UHT, or whatever, that&#x27;s not to do with production anymore, but distribution and storage. So it&#x27;s perfectly possible for milk to be both produced organically (so &quot;organic&quot;) and also Ultra-Heat Treated (so UHT).')
