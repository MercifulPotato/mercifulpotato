Item(by='arghwhat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607035553, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25295758, text='Also note that alacritty&#x27;s benchmark are throughput oriented, not latency oriented. The benchmarks measure how fast it can tear through data, not how quickly pixels change on screen.<p>But, rendering the entire terminal buffer is slightly overkill when only a few pixels changed, and the time it takes can end up significant on weaker devices driving high resolution displays.<p>As a result, Alacritty doesn&#x27;t have the best &quot;time to light&quot; performance, and there have been long debates in the issues about it.')
