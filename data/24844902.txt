Item(by='jojobas', descendants=None, kids=[24845380, 24846460, 24845288], score=None, time=1603256824, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24834716, text='Fun fact: a cone open on one end (saxophone) supports all harmonics, as does a cylinder open on both ends (flute).<p>A cylinder open on one end (clarinet) only supports odd harmonics, which is why a clarinet sounds &quot;rounded&quot; and overblows into a twelfth, rather than an octave (as on former two).')
