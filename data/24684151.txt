Item(by='ribs', descendants=None, kids=[24685977], score=None, time=1601866637, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24682703, text='The real problem with these terms - if there can be said to be a real problem - is that they give the appearance of there being a naturalistic process by which the English language evolves a great deal of flowery diversity. One assumes that, because there are all these apparently used terms, there must be a reason of sorts as to why they evolved.<p>But there isn’t. They’re just silly things people made up for the purpose of making them up and having funny names for things.')
