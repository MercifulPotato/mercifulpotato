Item(by='Nextgrid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611786291, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25934737, text='In my case, searching for my network name in Keychain Access brings up 2 items, one in my System keychain and one in the iCloud one.<p>Both of them end up displaying the raw password on the detail view now, though I do indeed remember that a year ago I saw some wireless passwords being in hex instead.<p>I&#x27;m not denying they might be <i>reversibly</i>-encrypted (although this doesn&#x27;t happen to me anymore for some reason), but in any case for a WPA(2)-PSK network the key cannot be <i>hashed</i> using a one-way irreversible hash because it is needed as-is to actually complete the authentication. What you&#x27;re seeing is <i>encryption</i> and not hashing.')
