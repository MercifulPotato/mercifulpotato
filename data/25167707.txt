Item(by='xemdetia', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605929897, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25166504, text='I think one of the forgotten blessings of PHP&#x2F;CGI was that requests had to finish sometime so as to free the PHP process for the next request. This alone I feel ended up making decisions easy around when it felt right to offload stuff when slow requests started to affect index.php from rendering in a timely fashion but now that&#x27;s often masked (rightly) by caches and other tricks.<p>Right now in a microservice model often you have to remind developers that it just isn&#x27;t ok to have a ~1ms response in a basic case and a 1 second to infinitely blocking case during errors or slow remote services.')
