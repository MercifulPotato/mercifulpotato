So e.g. maybe your Google account. But this key does not exist when you receive the Titan in its packaging, it&#x27;s created (randomly) when you enroll the key for your Google account.<p>These devices create entirely random ECDSA private keys for every single enrollment, and this attack recovers one key, using a real challenge from the relying party for that key.
