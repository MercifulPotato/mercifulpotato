Item(by='mceachen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605144218, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25064581, text='Although there is an official API, your photos and videos pulled via the API will have most of their metadata stripped, including GPS location tags.<p>The best way is via Google Takeout.<p>If you have a large library, your archive may fail to be generated by Google: you may have to resort to creating several large albums, and restricting your Google Takeout to one album, and creating several Takeouts.<p>You can only create 3 Takeouts at any given time.<p>Yes, it&#x27;s a pain. I&#x27;m sure they know.<p>Know that &quot;high quality&quot; photos and videos frequently have garbled tags, as well. If you&#x27;ve kept backups of your original photos and videos, they&#x27;ll be much better than what you&#x27;ll get from Takeout.<p>(Source: me doing research to support reading Google Takeout metadata sidecars that are then imported by PhotoStructure).')
