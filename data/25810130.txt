Item(by='fenja', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610885863, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25804507, text='&gt; To-do lists are for things that aren&#x27;t a real priority, otherwise you would be giving them your full attention and the tracking would be unnecessary. At most, it&#x27;s helpful to track administrative minutiae. If you give something your full attention, tracking will seem like a non-sequitur.<p>This is not true for humans with deficits in memory or attention, which is a common problem in a world of constant distraction.\nEven when the majority of the articles and techniques is geared towards people with underlying problems that should be fixed first, it doesn&#x27;t mean the gained knowledge and functionality is useless to everyone.')
