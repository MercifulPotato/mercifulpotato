I think that&#x27;s why none of these languages work very well.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;hitchdev.com&#x2F;strictyaml&#x2F;why-not&#x2F;toml&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;hitchdev.com&#x2F;strictyaml&#x2F;why-not&#x2F;toml&#x2F;</a>')
