Item(by='marcus_holmes', descendants=None, kids=[25178622], score=None, time=1606058697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25177886, text='The point of a physical signature is not that anyone else can prove you signed something. It&#x27;s that if you go to court, and the court asks if you have signed the document, you&#x27;re committing perjury if you say &quot;no&quot; (assuming you did sign it). It&#x27;s the act that&#x27;s important, rather than the resulting scribble.')
