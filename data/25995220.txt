Item(by='OldHand2018', descendants=None, kids=[25996690, 25996741], score=None, time=1612219865, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25995014, text='Winter in a house with a forced-air heating system just makes everything far worse.  If you have the opportunity and the means, you want to go with a boiler, radiant in-floor heat, etc.  Anything but forced-air.<p>By the way, in the winter we almost never use the dryer when washing clothes - always hang them up and they dry quickly and add their moisture to the air.  We also open the dishwasher door as soon as it finishes and let the moisture evaporate into the air.  Etc.')
