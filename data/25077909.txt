This will let you move faster with confidence your services will survive when they (inevitably) fail. Generally a short outage is preferable to slow development velocity.<p>Examples:<p>- Bad deploy: rollback to previous version<p>- Launching features: guard new behavior with a flag you can control and quickly disable')
