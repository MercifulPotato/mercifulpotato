I&#x27;ve been told by several teachers that the process of entering meditation is what you initially want to practice the most, because the rest of your practice relies on being able to do that.<p>I set different &quot;reminders&quot; for myself to meditate. In the beginning I used &quot;being annoyed&quot; and &quot;waiting for something&quot; as two &quot;triggers&quot; for me to begin meditating immediately, and do it for at least 10 breaths.')
