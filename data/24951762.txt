Item(by='dahart', descendants=None, kids=[24952062], score=None, time=1604154631, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24951730, text='If you stay on DST through the winter, most places in the US will have a morning commute in the dark. The reason we switch is to keep the sunrise just <i>slightly</i> more even. The annual swing in sunrise time is just under 3 hours. With a DST switch, the swing becomes less than 2 hours, at the cost of switching twice.')
