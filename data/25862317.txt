Item(by='guenthert', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611254365, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25860545, text='The Parallax Propeller has an on-board boot ROM, but no E(E)PROM.  Not much an issue for the intended audience (hobby and educational sector it seems), but the delay between reset and program start (while it is being loaded into internal RAM via I2C from external memory) would be for some hard real-time use cases.  Something to be aware of.')
