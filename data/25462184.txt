Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608249806, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25460802, text='&gt; - Unrequited -&gt; requited.<p>The verb “requite” and the adjective “requited” are quite normal English words, and “unrequited” is derived from them in the normal way.<p>“Unrequited” may be the form most commonly encountered (“mutual” is most common in many places “requited” would naturally fit), but the other forms are not neologisms created by backformation.')
