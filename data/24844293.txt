Item(by='DoreenMichele', descendants=None, kids=[24844904, 24844680, 24845984], score=None, time=1603247960, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24842894, text='Your genes are like blue prints. Your diet and lifestyle are like the building blocks feed into a machine trying to follow the blue prints.<p>In vernacular architecture, local materials are used. An adobe house in the Southwest of the US looks very different from, say, an igloo.<p>That&#x27;s kind of the difference the diet and lifestyle can make and it&#x27;s something you can engineer yourself with enough reading, keeping a journal and so forth.<p>It won&#x27;t fix everything, but study after study after study shows that &quot;diet and lifestyle&quot; are major factors in every deadly disease from cancer to diabetes to heart disease, yet people basically point and laugh if you suggest these are a legitimate means to address a serious condition with a genetic component.<p>The reality is that much medical research is aimed at the ability to look impressive in headlines and far too little of it is aimed at genuinely improving human health and quality of life. And noting that gets far too much flak from far too many people.')
