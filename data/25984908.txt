Item(by='PaulDavisThe1st', descendants=None, kids=[25991895], score=None, time=1612146431, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25968122, text='&quot;The universe is made of stories, not atoms&quot;<p>When I first came across this quote from the poem &quot;The Speed of Darkness&quot; by Muriel Rukeyser I thought it was all too typical glib and hand-wavy poetic license.
