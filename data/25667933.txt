Item(by='rramadass', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610001191, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25659036, text='From personal experience, i can suggest;<p>* RFCs on IPSec.<p>* ITU Recommendation on H.323 Protocol for Packet Based Multimedia Systems.<p>* The NIST documentations on FIPS and the various Crypto algorithms.<p>The first two are an &quot;umbrella&quot; of protocols and thus the specifications go from overview to extremely detailed in a very nice step-by-step manner. The third one shows you how to specify detailed and complicated algorithms.<p>Writing good Specifications is extremely demanding! But this is the <i>only</i> way to really understand the Domain.')
