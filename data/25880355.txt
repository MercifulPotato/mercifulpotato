Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=[25880757], score=None, time=1611386134, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25857773, text='The best way to turn off old API&#x27;s is to not need to.<p>If your API is simple enough that it can be reimplemented as a shim to the new API, and that shim layer needs no state or special permissions, then it becomes effectively maintanance free.   Your shim cannot have security issues (since it has no special permissions).  It likely won&#x27;t have scaling issues (no state makes scaling easy).   You can leave it sitting there forever if necessary, and the total cost will be well below the risks of turning off an external API.')
