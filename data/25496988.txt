Item(by='jech', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608571036, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25496804, text='Duplicating a packet in the network is called <i>multicast</i>, and it isn&#x27;t implemented in the real Internet.<p>Most large-scale broadcasts use technologies such as HTTP Live Streaming (HLS) or MPEG-DASH, which carve a video into 3-second intervals that are then distributed using ordinary HTTP over a CDN.  The problem with these technologies is that they have a latency of a few seconds, which means that you cannot have a meaningful conversation over them.')
