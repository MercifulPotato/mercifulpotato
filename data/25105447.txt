Item(by='mschuster91', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605478583, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25104656, text='All power grid infra is already hardened against lightning strikes because power lines are, trees aside, usually the highest point and thus the best target for lightning.<p>Usually there is one or two lines at the top that are grounded at each pole to dissipate lightning, and in the event that lightning does hit a power-carrying line there are arc &quot;spikes&quot; at the isolators so that the lightning energy can again dissipate over the masts to the ground safely.')
