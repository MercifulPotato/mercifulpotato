Item(by='zeroimpl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604031148, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24931136, text='I discovered this in my late teens and thought it was super cool and made me want to understand more about repeating decimals. After playing around for a bit I realized you could form arbitrary repeating decimals by dividing by 9, 99, 999 etc.  So for example, 1&#x2F;7 = 142857&#x2F;999999. Or written another way, 999999&#x2F;7=142857.')
