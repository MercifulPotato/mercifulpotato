These days it&#x27;s not that hard to determine what a competitive rate for a job is. Then ask for the top end of that, or even a bit more. It shows that you know what you are getting into, what you are worth, and quickly surfaces that there&#x27;s been a misunderstanding about the job, if there has been one.<p>Sure maybe push back a little to see if they&#x27;ll throw out the first number.
