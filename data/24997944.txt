Item(by='alexmingoia', descendants=None, kids=[24998559], score=None, time=1604582187, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24995141, text='A simpler variation on this system is for the ballot keypair to be generated by the voter, and then the public key given to the registrar to &quot;register&quot; for the election. The registrar publishes all the registered ballots signed by their key. All the registrar does is ensure eligible voters submit a public key to register. This would require a voter to register in person or a national ID card that could be used to sign the ballot public key.')
