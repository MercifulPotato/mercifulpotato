Item(by='slaman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604511705, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24990747, text='In Canada, mail in ballots have a separate, earlier deadline.<p>If you do not mail your ballot on time, there is another simple solution, you go to the (lineless) polls on election day.<p>When they check everyone in they confirm that they have not already received a mail-in-ballot from that individual before allowing you to the booth.<p>With enough time allocated for mail-in-ballots, I feel that it doesn&#x27;t matter that the deadline is earlier.\nDeadlines are fairly arbitrary either way and at some point you need to select a winner.<p>If you have plenty of opportunity to vote and you do not vote by the deadline, your vote is not counted regardless of the method you have chosen.')
