Item(by='quickthrower2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604392651, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24976686, text='There is nothing inherently bad about storing unrelated things in the same db, in general.<p>There might be security issues but good DBs offer security grants that are granular enough to deal with this.<p>Data sovereignty might be an issue forcing you to split, that depends on the domain and application requirements.<p>But in general two things being unrelated isn’t a reason to split the DB and splitting the DB isn’t a reason to split into services.<p>I’m not for or against microservices. I’m definitely for good reasoning though!')
