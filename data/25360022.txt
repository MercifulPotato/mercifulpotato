Item(by='ISL', descendants=None, kids=[25360093], score=None, time=1607529787, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25359903, text='More-dangerously, it is taxing theoretical money that you haven&#x27;t yet made.<p>There is no guarantee that the current market-clearing price for a given object is equivalent to that at which a larger inventory can be sold.<p>Taxing capital gains at the time of sale makes sense -- there is actual revenue available for taxation.  Taxing unrealized gains can be a path to really-negative outcomes. This is particularly so if the government doesn&#x27;t give matching credits for unrealized capital losses.')
