Item(by='stevewodil', descendants=None, kids=[25827440, 25830850, 25827381], score=None, time=1611006096, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25826835, text='It&#x27;s actually a very good sales question, I don&#x27;t find it demeaning at all.<p>If you&#x27;re on a sales call selling a product that increases user retention and someone says &quot;no we don&#x27;t need that&quot;, you would often reply with &quot;So you have perfect user retention then?&quot; to probe them and re-open the conversation.<p>It could come off as standoffish but when used correctly it&#x27;s very effective because it gets the person on the other end to open up more and you try to get to the bottom of their objections.')
