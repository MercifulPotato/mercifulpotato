Item(by='kube-system', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604947742, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25036924, text='Literally speaking, a 3d printer becomes a 2d printer if you unplug an axis.  You can get open source kits that are basically this, called plotters.<p>The issue is that hobbyists can&#x27;t achieve the resolution necessary for printing texts or images, no matter how many axes the machine has.  A $30 inkjet printer at Walmart has easily 10x or 20x the resolution of a desktop 3d printer.  That kind of precision is hard to do without specialized machinery.  The people with that ability are, well, printer manufacturers.')
