The automotive industry may have more in common with personal electronics than it did thirty years ago, but forced comparisons belie the unique and complex challenges in shipping a road-legal car.<p>And yet, that massive gulf is the whole point here.<p>Apple is treading in 95% unfamiliar waters, <i>but the company has proven time and again this is its secret power.</i> They&#x27;d never made portable music players before; they&#x27;d never made phones before; they&#x27;d never made watches before.
