Item(by='Hnrobert42', descendants=None, kids=[25279655], score=None, time=1606933302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25278855, text='I don’t use Siri for much, and I certainly don’t have it always listening. But I did hack out a useful Shortcut to record my blood pressure and heart rate to Apple Health.<p>I say, “Add blood pressure &lt;pause&gt; 120 over 80 plus 60.” Then I use the shortcut to parse the string on the &#x2F; and +, and record it to Health.<p>The hard part was finding delimiters that Siri would consistently record as a single character. That and realizing I needed a manual review step to make sure Siri didn’t happily pump garbage into my logs.')
