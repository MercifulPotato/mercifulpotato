Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=[25804433], score=None, time=1610819710, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803702, text='Strassen algorithm is 7-multiplications for a 2x2 matrix. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Strassen_algorithm" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Strassen_algorithm</a><p>A matrix can always be split into groups of sub-matrix, and the groups of sub-matrix is itself a matrix.
