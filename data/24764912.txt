Item(by='choeger', descendants=None, kids=[24770111], score=None, time=1602591968, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24763713, text='The term &quot;dynamically typed&quot; is meaningless in the context of type systems. As you say yourself, a type system proves the absence of certain errors. A &quot;dynamic&quot; type system tells you precisely where and when such an error occurred. As such, these languages are untyped (or uni-typed which is just a more fancy way of saying that they are untyped).')
