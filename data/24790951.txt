Item(by='praptak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602781724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24789865, text='There&#x27;s nothing like a network of people you actually know to find out whether something is shitty.<p>Be it a company, a product, or a service - ask people you know personally. As a fallback, most online communities do have an &quot;unrelated stuff&quot; subforums or chats. Asking there lowers a chance of getting an answer from a marketer. I do sometimes get (and provide) good advice from a local gaming community.<p>The key is to ask a community that is unrelated to what you are considering to buy, i.e. skip those groups which an unscrupulous company would swarm with fake advice.')
