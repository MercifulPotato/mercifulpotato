Item(by='bleepblorp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606344693, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25214985, text='Part of the point the author makes is that the tech sector--as a cluster of monopolies with disproportionate socioeconomic power--is part of the economic problem.<p>Like it or not, the tech sector does not operate in a vacuum. What the tech sector enables, and how it concentrates wealth, has consequences for all of society. Anyone who is connected to the tech industry, as an investor or employee, has a social obligation to be aware of the externalities created by this industry.<p>No one gets to mix paint thinner into the office coffee and then cry &#x27;stop playing office politics&#x27; when they get called on it.')
