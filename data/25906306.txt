Item(by='jkingsbery', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611597122, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25905162, text='If <i>I</i> was hiring an agency to build a mobile app, and one of them came back with &quot;195 days,&quot; I would have pretty high confidence that they&#x27;ve never built anything of noteworthy complexity before, and that they think that software projects can be planned to the day months in advance.<p>On the other hand, for operational promises (e.g., a 24-hour SLA) means something more specific than a 1-day SLA: 24 hours is clear, whereas anything based on days requires definitions (Literally the next day? After one full calendar day has passed?<p>So, no, smaller units are not more credible... smaller units make a more specific claim which in some cases is more credible, but in some cases is not credible.')
