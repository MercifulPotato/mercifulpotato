We tokenize all secrets and then store the tokens in our database. The raw secrets are stored with our tokenization provider, VGS. When you fetch your secrets, either via our dashboard or CLI, we exchange the token for the raw secret value and then relay that value in our response. This ensures that our infrastructure never persists raw secret values.
