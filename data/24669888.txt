Item(by='gumby', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601704423, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24669692, text='Being consistent is almost as good — essentially as good in fact.<p>I have a glucose meter that’s quite wrong, but the shape of its curve isn’t bad (the error isn’t linear, but is consistent— around 100 it measures about 82; at 150 it measures 130) but now I’m used to it it tells me what I need to know.  And the strips are cheap.<p>I don’t need the watch to tell me if my heart rate is too high but it’s useful if it tells me my heart rate was about the same the whole run.')
