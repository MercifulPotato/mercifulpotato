Item(by='lacker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608151488, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25447489, text='I found openings to be very useful for players rated 1000-1500 when working with my high school chess team. The main benefit is that by understanding the typical moves of an opening, you make it a lot easier to avoid dropping a piece in the first 10 or 15 moves, or ending up massively behind in development because something tricked you. You avoid a decent amount of losses that way.')
