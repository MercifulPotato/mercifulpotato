It&#x27;s full of oddities and minilanguages and special cases. For the main body of the work it was no quicker or easier than Markdown. For anything complex I wound up spending hours poring over the docs, stackoverflow, ancient forum posts, github issues trying to learn the magic incantations.<p>I would have greatly preferred LaTeX or DocBook, but the offered alternatives were Word or Google Docs.')
