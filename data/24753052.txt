That vote may affect the actions on climate change, but it may not be in the way you want. And it has lots and lots of related side affects.<p>Let us say that the president who would take more steps to curb global warming would also cause growth in a non US country that is environmentally destructive. Or that they themselves would make so many deals and compromises that the deal that gets passed is actually a long term net loss.<p>I am totally not saying that will happen, but making things too black and white can lead to surprises.')
