Item(by='compressedgas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602737588, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24784773, text='I think it is due to motion processing attempting to remove the blurring of moving objects. It processes a window of frames and tries to produce an image that has the object without the motion blur that would have been present. This can result in artifacts which some people find objectionable and might be the cause of the artifacts you have observed.')
