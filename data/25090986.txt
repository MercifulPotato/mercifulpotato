Item(by='sradman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605341786, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25090218, text='Dogsheep Beta is a pun on Wolfram Alpha [1] (dog&#x2F;sheep&#x2F;beta are humble alternatives to wolf&#x2F;ram&#x2F;alpha). Both are queryable knowledge bases.<p>Dogsheep Alpha leverages SQLite FTS (Full Text Search) adding a search interface to Datasette’s SQL, accessed via a Python powered WebUI. All self-hosted.<p>The “personal” part leverages Datasette plug-ins for services that export user data (e.g. Twitter, Hacker News, GitHub, Pocket, Evernote, and Apple HealthKit).<p>More a poor-man’s ElasticSearch for SQLite&#x2F;Datasette than a Data Warehouse, in my opinion. Simon Wilson is on a roll delivering simple yet powerful tools.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;WolframAlpha" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;WolframAlpha</a>')
