I coded something which pretented to be a web browser with the NSPlugin interface. Then I loaded the Linux Flash plugin in that, and iterated frame by frame, and encoded the frames with ffmpeg to mp4.<p>This way we didn&#x27;t have to support Flash on the Digital Signage player hardware, but could still accept Flash animations from the ad agencies.')
