Item(by='gpderetta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609514976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25602333, text='Be careful that &#x27;trivial&#x27; is a term if art in the context of C++ initialization. Having to do work is different than not having to do it. Constructting a vector of N optionals is O(N) as opposed of a vector of a truly trivially constructible type, which is O(1) (although I&#x27;m not 100% sure if the standard guarantees it or it is just a QoI).')
