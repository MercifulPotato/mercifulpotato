Item(by='jacobolus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611656003, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25910048, text='It is not actually &quot;logarithmic&quot; per se.<p>At a given level of adaptation the relation between luminance and perceived lightness is closer to a square root.<p>But over the course of about 30 minutes there are several different kinds of adaptations (some faster than that) which the eye&#x2F;brain can make to the current light level, which has an effect of shifting that curve up or down by up to several orders of magnitude.')
