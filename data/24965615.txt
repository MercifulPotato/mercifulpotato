They&#x27;d have been copied the instant they gained a little success and been diluted away.<p>People proposing no copyrights always seem to focus on exploiting existing work, not on how new work will be created. It&#x27;s expensive to create and promote it so why would anyone invest that money if they can&#x27;t get it back? Are you counting on passionate independently wealthy hobbyists to create everything copyable for free?<p>Almost all successful open source software is protected by copyright and license terms that restrict its use.
