Item(by='xaedes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606149800, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25188433, text='My mnemonic for lose vs loose:\nA &quot;loser&quot; is someone who loses, i.e. does not win&#x2F;gain. \nI think this is easier to remember, because we are exposed to this word &quot;loser&quot; quite a lot.\nSo &quot;lose&quot; is the word for &quot;not winning&quot; (or similar worded).<p>&quot;Looser&quot; on the other hand we don&#x27;t see very often. The meaning is &quot;more loose&quot;.')
