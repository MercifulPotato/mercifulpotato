Item(by='macintux', descendants=None, kids=[25010647], score=None, time=1604681237, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25008395, text='The scoping punctuation is very easy if you think about it. I begrudgingly accept “I don’t like the syntax” as a reason to not use Erlang, but there’s nothing challenging about it. It’s a much smaller syntax than Elixir.<p>The punctuation rules are very similar to English: comma means you’re not through with your thought yet, semi-colon means you’re introducing a new clause, period means you’re done with the entire thought.')
