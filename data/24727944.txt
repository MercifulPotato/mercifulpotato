Item(by='helsinkiandrew', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602232169, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24725940, text='Eventually companies become so large they can&#x27;t innovate - doing something new is too big a risk (it will eat into existing revenue and may fail anyway) and too expensive (its hard to change the structure and direction of a 10&#x27;s and 100&#x27;s of thousands of employees).<p>The only way they continue operating is to outsource R&amp;D - buy companies that have proven a new business model or product, or replicate what they&#x27;ve done.<p>This has been the case since VisiCalc, Wordperfect, Netscape, IBM etc. - is there an alternative for large companies?')
