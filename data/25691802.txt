Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610147840, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25687604, text='As it is I know of at least three completely different smearing regimes, all incompatible with one another and extremely complicated to implement.<p>One thing is certain: anything complicated and difficult to implement will, in a large fraction of systems, <i>not</i> be implemented, or be implemented incorrectly, leading to needless hundreds-of-milliseconds disagreements between systems about what time it is.<p>Drift is absolutely harmless almost everywhere, and easy to correct for in the rare, isolated cases where it is not. The only cases I know of where it matters involve spacecraft, and those certainly are not using UTC internally.')
