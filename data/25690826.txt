Something like 1&#x2F;3 of the weight is evaporated away during dry-aging, so that 16oz steak was originally 24oz.  There&#x27;s also loss to trimming, which is required due to both a desiccated crust and fungal and mold growth on the surface.  And it is typically a high-grade beef to start with.
