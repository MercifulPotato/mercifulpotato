Item(by='metiscus', descendants=None, kids=[25061386], score=None, time=1605118847, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25060995, text='While I suspect a certain level of sarcasm was intended, a valid point for discussion does exist within your comment - The idea that preparing is merely forwarding supplies to your local warlord. This basically assumes a TEOTWAWKI scenario, which should be very far down the list of any preparation. It is always recommended in preparation circles to get to know your neighbors and integrate yourself into your local community. Under any reasonable set of circumstances, a well functioning local community should be able to stand up to any war lord types and would be the atoms from which a new society is formed.<p>The classic advice goes something like this - in a survival situation you will be killed by the first critical problem you can&#x27;t solve yourself. Communities form for a reason and knowing the geography of your operational area includes the social geography.')
