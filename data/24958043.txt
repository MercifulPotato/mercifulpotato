Item(by='sneak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604219510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24956367, text='There is an argument for judging programming languages based on the worst code possible within the language, due to the fact that with a wide enough ecosystem, you will regularly encounter lots of stuff in the median between that and your “just don’t do that” standard.<p>This is one of the reasons I love Go: the worst code possible in it is still pretty readable.<p>The worst code possible in PHP is scattered across thousands of files corresponding to individual routes, and isn’t indented at all.')
