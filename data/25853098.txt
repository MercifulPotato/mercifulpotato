I get enough partisanship and parroting in the news already; we don&#x27;t need more people trying to convince others that they are right and only succeeding in either breeding echo chambers or talking past each other. In my opinion, the &quot;art of debating&quot; is merely a means to an end: the goal is to arrive at a deeper level of understanding about a topic.<p>What I really want is for more people to make good faith efforts to make substantive arguments against their own opinions, and learn to recognize the usage of fallacies (ad hominems, etc) and other forms of &quot;pop politics&quot; (&quot;X said Y, which implies some character flaw!&quot;)<p>Argument maps are certainly a useful tool to have, but IMHO, one does not arrive at good charts through arguing in debates (especially heated ones!), but through dispassionate impartial curation. &lt;&#x2F;two-cents&gt;')
