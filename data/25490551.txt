Item(by='wpietri', descendants=None, kids=[25491507], score=None, time=1608506409, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25490385, text='Humans are also naturally violent, but me saying &quot;Hey, I&#x27;m going to stab you because I&#x27;m just human&quot; is probably not something you&#x27;d shrug off.<p>In particular, what you&#x27;re indulging here is the naturalistic fallacy, or the is&#x2F;ought fallacy. What&#x27;s natural tells us nothing about what&#x27;s good. Tooth decay is natural and your toothbrush is artificial. But I&#x27;d bet you&#x27;re not going to stop brushing your teeth just to stay philosophically consistent.')
