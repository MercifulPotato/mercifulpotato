Spent almost 3 years at Google and my view is that it is an intended side benefit of how they do development.<p>If something that relies on say, one of their log services, and that log service is getting end-of-lifed then all the dependent services that aren&#x27;t really critical will also get shutdown. It&#x27;s a forcing mechanism to keep the amount of cruft down.  If that service that relies on the old service is that important they&#x27;ll put the resources on the project long enough to port it to the new service.')
