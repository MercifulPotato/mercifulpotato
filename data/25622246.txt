Item(by='mprovost', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609687395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25618878, text='At Weta we divided up the NFS servers into &quot;src&quot; and &quot;dat&quot; - &quot;src&quot; was everything made by artists, and &quot;dat&quot; was the output from the renderwall. We backed up &quot;src&quot; every night, but &quot;dat&quot; was never backed up. Every once in a while there would be some mass deletion event but it was always faster to re-render the lost data than to restore from backups.<p>Also none of the high end commercial filers are single node - they&#x27;re all clusters of varying sizes.')
