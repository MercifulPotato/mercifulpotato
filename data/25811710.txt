Item(by='bonoboTP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610899058, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25811580, text='Or perhaps it&#x27;s more about synchronizing, like a musical orchestra under a conductor&#x27;s lead. Whichever piece they play, it matters most that they play the same piece to the same beat and pulse, instead of everyone trying to play their own favorite melody.<p>Often among roughly equally talented people what matters is to have a clear vision, any vision and to orient a whole group towards the same direction. It&#x27;s really hard to judge in advance who is better in long term vision and strategy. Experts don&#x27;t have a great track record in this and CEOs also don&#x27;t seem to have a great sense of direction (ie their track record quickly regresses to the mean).<p>Leadership is to a large degree about crafting a convincing narrative and fostering acting in unison. &quot;To make people long for the sea&quot; etc.')
