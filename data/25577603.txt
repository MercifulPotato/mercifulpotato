Item(by='chiefalchemist', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609294683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25576557, text='Neuro-plasticity means what it says: the brain is moldable. It&#x27;s not static. It&#x27;s not set in stone. Prior to neuro-plasticity the brain was perceived as being set in stone, so to speak.<p>The brain is a result of how you &quot;exercise&quot; it, or not. It&#x27;s the result of what you feed it, or not. It&#x27;s not static.<p>Neuro-plasticity enables stroke victims to recover. And the depth and breadth of that recovery is a function of how much or not the brain is forced to rewire.')
