Item(by='blunte', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609346145, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25581282, text='Two spaces, like the Oxford Comma, reduce ambiguity and aid in visually parsing text information.<p>As a side benefit, that extra space can help software identify a sentence vs a line which contains something like Mr. Bob.  With a two-space system, Mr. Bob would be part of one sentence.  In a one-space system, the software cannot tell if the sentence ends at Mr. with the next sentence beginning as &quot;Bob ...&quot;')
