Item(by='mindslight', descendants=None, kids=[25240347], score=None, time=1606576089, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25237430, text='They probably do have nothing better to do, and that&#x27;s a <i>good thing</i>. Basic queuing theory says that a system needs excess capacity in order to be able to respond to peak demand - you can&#x27;t just tell a second fire to wait for an hour while fighting the first one. If three engines respond to a fender bender and a more pressing call comes in, they will leave and go there.<p>I had an immediate family member in the hospital when that &quot;nurses playing cards&quot; ignorance was going around. I wish nurses did have some time to play cards, because it would mean they&#x27;d answer when they were needed, rather than being busy with other patients for an hour and then forgetting. We truly are optimizing ourselves to death.')
