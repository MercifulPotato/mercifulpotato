Item(by='kmeisthax', descendants=None, kids=[25355971], score=None, time=1607493375, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25355106, text='AGPL isn&#x27;t pro- or anti-SaaS, though. It just says that you have to provide sources to anyone you grant access to the software over a network. So if you host an AGPL database for someone else, they need access to the source code for that database somehow. But it doesn&#x27;t prohibit you from hosting the database at all.<p>It&#x27;s the perfect license for MongoDB and all these other companies trying to keep Amazon from taking their software and proprietarizing it... but instead they make weirdly restrictive one-off licenses nobody are going to contribute to.')
