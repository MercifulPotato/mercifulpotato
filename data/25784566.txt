Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610666936, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25781845, text='Phones aren’t a utility merely because of their general importance, but because of the specific importance to the public interest of having a single, universal telephone network.<p>I don’t think there is a great public interest in a single curated (algorithmically and&#x2F;or manually) user-generated-content newsfeed system. In fact, insofar as such a category of product is desirable, I think that the public interest is in competition and freedom to differentiate, including in terms of content focus and preferences within the bounds of the law, just as is the case with, say, news outlets that <i>aren’t</i> driven by UGC.')
