Item(by='mrob', descendants=None, kids=[25180770], score=None, time=1606075822, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25176765, text='The clavichord plays like a piano with aftertouch. Instead of hammers bouncing off the strings with no control except speed, the strings are hit with &quot;tangents&quot; that remain in contact with the string like tapping on a guitar. It was not often used for performance historically because it&#x27;s too quiet, but modern amplification solves that problem.')
