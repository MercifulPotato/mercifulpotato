Item(by='jfk13', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612025993, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25970330, text='Whether it&#x27;ll work or not for the various non-Latin scripts will depend what fallback fonts the browser happens to pick, so people&#x27;s results will vary.<p>Your jsfiddle also fails even for English in most browsers, because you didn&#x27;t load both regular and bold weights of Recursive. As a result, you get a browser-generated synthetic bold effect, which varies between browsers and in most cases (but not Chrome, IIRC) will alter the width.<p>(To fix that aspect, you need something like &quot;family=Recursive:wght@400;700&quot; in your Google Fonts link.)')
