Item(by='sfvisser', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609188109, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25562731, text='Most CRUD apps and UI still has IO on the outside pure&#x2F;domain stuff on the inside.<p>Most CRUD server handlers basically boil down to fetch and parse incoming HTTP request, decide to fetch some external resources (db&#x2F;cache&#x2F;service), prepare a response, and reply to the client. Fits the IO-on-the-outside model perfectly.<p>React and friends have shown that UI views are mostly pure functions. Some local state and external resource can be fetched in some runtime hook and passed into your pure renderer.')
