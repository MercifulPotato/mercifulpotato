Item(by='missblit', descendants=None, kids=[25356293], score=None, time=1607493323, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25354993, text='The reason it works locally is because charset detection works different when loading from a local file than when loading from a webserver.<p>In particular browsers tend to be a lot more enthusiastic about treating local files as unicode than they are for responses from web servers.<p>The reason the meta charset doesn&#x27;t pick up is you&#x27;re missing quotes around `utf-8` (also it may be past the 1024 byte mark, hard to say without running wc).')
