Item(by='hashmush', descendants=None, kids=[25802172, 25801764], score=None, time=1610798811, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801619, text='A Chinese guy I knew was studying for the TOEFL test and was memorizing all kinds of medical terms I&#x27;d never heard about, and yet, when he said goodbye he&#x27;d also say &quot;I go first&quot;. At that time I had no idea why he did it, but as I learned more Mandarin it became clear that he just translated the phrase 我先走 (lit. I first go) which is very common when parting ways.<p>Point being, many study for tests but can&#x27;t <i>speak</i> English.')
