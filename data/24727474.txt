Item(by='anw', descendants=None, kids=[24727553, 24734361, 24727604, 24727666], score=None, time=1602226181, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24718227, text='I often wish that the schema around domain names was reversed, to reflect something closer to Usenet.<p>Instead of news.ycombinator.com we would use com.ycombinator.news, similar to how a news group might be rec.arts.punkrock or comp.os.linux. This makes more logical sense to me, as you start from the broadest on the left (tld) and work your way to the most granular part (the path, query, or fragment of the resource).')
