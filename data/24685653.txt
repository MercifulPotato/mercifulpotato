Item(by='beagle3', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601885994, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24685500, text='Some places still observe the &quot;first sale doctrine&quot; which means that if you legally got a copy of a piece of software, you have a right to use it whichever way you please -- so, in such a jurisdiction, as long as you obtained it <i>legally</i>, you have the right to use it.<p>(Analogy: You buy a music CD; You do not need a specific license to listen to it, and you&#x27;re not violating anything even if it has a sticker that says &quot;by opening this sticker, you hereby agree to never play this using a Sony CD player, and to never play it at faster than 1.25x on any non-Sony CD player)')
