It doesn&#x27;t prevent non-Free competitors from existing.<p>If you don&#x27;t want to be part of the copyleft Free and Open Source software world, then you always have the option to not be a part of it. You can make your own competing product under a non-Free licence, or base your work on Free Software that uses a permissive &#x27;copycenter&#x27; licence. This isn&#x27;t a point against copyleft.')
