Item(by='lobsang', descendants=None, kids=[25291761, 25296396], score=None, time=1607012358, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25288166, text='I think some of the comments so far here and in the artice might be missing the point - not helped by the use of word UX or the common interpreation as meaning UI.<p>I think the author is advocating that you start with understanding the expectations of the product, what should the user be able to do, what do they need to do, what does the market expect. From that you then work out what tradeoffs need to be made in different areas and how to make them.<p>In that sense I completely agree - starting with anything else is just going to impose restrictions and limitations that are likely to go against what you want to acheive.')
