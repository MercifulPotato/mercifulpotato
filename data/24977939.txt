Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604395504, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24977404, text='&gt; <i>If the gouging is happening at the penultimate step of the supply chain and the profits mostly don&#x27;t make it back to the manufacturer, then price gouging can&#x27;t do much to incentivize higher production.</i><p>And that was, in fact, the problem with price gouging of PPEs in this pandemic. The manufacturers didn&#x27;t raise prices, the stores didn&#x27;t rise prices - it&#x27;s the middle-men who swooped in, bought all the stock at retailers and wholesalers, and resold at inflated prices. None of the pricing signal made it back to manufacturers.')
