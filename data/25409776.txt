But in my experience, the simplest and best way is to force people to demo <i>something</i> at a pre-specified time. If you&#x27;re doing it &quot;hackathon&quot; style, then it should be at the end of the hackathon. If you&#x27;re doing it 20% style, then probably end of the quarter? You could do it with zero accountability, but having tried to run these programs (see my other top-level reply here), I think you&#x27;ll mostly get a lot of people not doing much if you don&#x27;t have any accountability.')
