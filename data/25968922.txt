Item(by='GuB-42', descendants=None, kids=[25969011], score=None, time=1612013709, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25961420, text='An interesting approach would be try to code something with only the official documentation <i>and source code</i> of the libraries your are using. No internet except for the git clone.<p>I like doing that as an exercise. It trains you well for a very common situation for professional developers. You have to work on a project, poorly documented, the original developers left long ago and no one really knows how it works, except maybe for one guy who don&#x27;t have time to answer your questions. Of course it is internal stuff, so the internet is no help. It is a valuable skill, literally, companies are ready to pay good money to people who can do that.')
