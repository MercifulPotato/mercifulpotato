Item(by='overlordalex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611248708, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25858623, text='My go-to example is &quot;awful&quot;. The original meaning is literally to be full of awe. I think you would be hard-pressed to find a speaker today that adheres to that meaning over simply meaning &quot;very bad&quot;, which is almost an antonym.<p>Languages change and are influenced by their use, so you need to pick your battles. If something can be unambiguously understood then you&#x27;re going to be fighting an uphill battle and it&#x27;s only a matter of time')
