Item(by='cookiengineer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610560217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25743970, text='&gt; You can collect Arbeitslosengeld II (&quot;Hartz IV&quot;) in cash at the local job center, no bank account required.<p>First off, that&#x27;s not the default. So statistically this isn&#x27;t true.<p>Second: The Finanzamt and the GwG would have a different opinion about that, see [1] and [2] (pp. 10 and pp. 11) regarding that.<p>I personally know somebody who did collect it in cash in the past, got sued by the Finanzamt, and lost in front of the court (ironically with a Bargeldstrafe).<p>Also note that even the SGB 2 and 3 make this very clear, without room for interpretation, that you need a bank account [3] (pp. 43). The only theoretical exception would be if the costs for a bank account would require more than 100 bucks that you are legible to get in advance per month [4] (pp. 43a) but that&#x27;s pretty much impossible to prove without having a bank account in the first place.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;gwg_2017&#x2F;__10.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;gwg_2017&#x2F;__10.html</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;gwg_2017&#x2F;__11.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;gwg_2017&#x2F;__11.html</a><p>[3] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;sgb_2&#x2F;__42.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;sgb_2&#x2F;__42.html</a><p>[4] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;sgb_2&#x2F;__43a.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gesetze-im-internet.de&#x2F;sgb_2&#x2F;__43a.html</a>')
