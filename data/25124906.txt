Or is it just that most engineers don&#x27;t want to spend time interviewing so that&#x27;s what we&#x27;ve optimized for?<p>Some of the absolute best engineers that I&#x27;ve worked with take their time to wrap their heads completely around a problem before diving in.  They aren&#x27;t slow thinkers, but they aren&#x27;t people who excel at these kinds of interviews either.<p>I&#x27;ve been doing interviews for a long time now and I find it more effective to surface strong opinions about things they&#x27;ve worked on -- good and bad.<p>I&#x27;m not hiring into a feature factory -- I don&#x27;t care about fast cogs. I&#x27;m hiring people who care about what they do and giving them an environment to thrive in.')
