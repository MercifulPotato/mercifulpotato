Item(by='ardy42', descendants=None, kids=[24901101, 24905127, 24901958], score=None, time=1603737898, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24896654, text='&gt; One of the absolute laziest ways to write a &quot;news&quot; article is the &quot;here are a list of things people tweeted about this topic&quot; compilation.<p>It&#x27;s definitely less work that investigative journalism, but to the extent that twitter is influential, it&#x27;s a topic that should be covered.<p>IMHO, the media has (at least) two important jobs: 1) conduct novel investigations and 2) summarize the firehose of events and ideas into a form concise enough for someone to read on a daily&#x2F;weekly basis.  &quot;What people are saying on social media&quot; falls squarely into the second category.')
