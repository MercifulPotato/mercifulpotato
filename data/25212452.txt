Who wants to work on them?<p>It&#x27;s always tempting to complicate something simple because in part &quot;ooh shiny&quot;, and you can always find reasons to justify why.  It takes some strong engineering leadership to effectively argue against complicating things, and not be just a constant pain in the arse to everyone and every thing.<p>The kinds of people that are that good, tend to be people that aren&#x27;t going to want to do something so boring as build and maintain the infrastructure for hosting status pages.')
