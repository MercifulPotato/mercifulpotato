Item(by='jarofgreen', descendants=None, kids=[24766536], score=None, time=1602591446, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24764449, text='The copyright owner can switch license on any future versions of the software at any point.<p>But the complication is that if the software project is made up of contributions from 100 different people, it&#x27;s not immediately clear who the copyright owner is. In the worst case you might have to get permission from all 100 people before you can switch.<p>This is the problem contributor agreements were designed to solve.<p>Of course, some people call this a feature and not a bug.')
