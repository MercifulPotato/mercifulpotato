Item(by='Plantlover69', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609227024, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25566810, text='Ceramic pots are heavier, especially at larger sizes, and can be difficult to lift, upturn, generally handle for some people.\nAlso the thin plastic pots have a bit of give allowing one to squeeze them, loosening the soil and root ball, which can make it easier to remove the plant. Otherwise tools might be needed, like a scoop, that can damage to roots.')
