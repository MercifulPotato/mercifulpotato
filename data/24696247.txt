Item(by='barrkel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601979122, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24695372, text='Multi-cursor is actually a better version of search-and-play-macro, not search-and-replace.<p>The advantage of it is that you can execute the &quot;macro&quot; incrementally and spot early when you&#x27;ve made a mistake, rather than recording a macro then letting rip and discovering, too late, that there was a mistake 80% of the way though, and now you need to undo the whole thing and and try again.<p>(I use multiple-cursors in Emacs for multiple selections, iedit-mode for live substitution (sometimes in combination with wgrep), as well replace-regexp for plain search &amp; replace, and while they all overlap, none are poor versions of any of the others. They each have their strengths and weaknesses. However it&#x27;s vanishingly rare that I use macros any more.)')
