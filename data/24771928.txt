Item(by='garbagetime', descendants=None, kids=[24774285, 24778227], score=None, time=1602635435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24771271, text='I don&#x27;t think begging the question is necessarily fallacious. If one is asked to prove that P from premise P one would do it like this:<p>1 (1) P A [1]<p>i.e. by assuming what is in question, i.e. by begging the question. It&#x27;s not at all fallacious.<p>[1] Or, in English: based on step one, step one asserts that P, by the logical rule of assumption')
