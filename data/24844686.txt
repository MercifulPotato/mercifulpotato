Item(by='remarkEon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603253729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24833063, text='Right, but that&#x27;s sort of the point of this protocol statement, no?<p>If a founder of a lunch restaurant starts out by saying &quot;hello everyone, I&#x27;m here to make the best lunches possible but only for southern white people&quot; I suspect, given my experience with lunches on three continents and in 37 states in the US, that he is going to have a hard time finding people who would actually be able to make good lunches.<p>Which is the point.<p>This does two things: it focuses people on what the actual mission of their company is or should be, and it can expose the kinds of missions that are nuts, or counter-productive, or just plain bad.')
