Item(by='smoyer', descendants=None, kids=[24661214], score=None, time=1601635040, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24657288, text='My 15 year-old cousin had undiagnosed hypertropic cardiomyopathy and collapsed during half-time at a high-school basketball game.  Since AEDs were rare then (and still are in rural, less affluent areas), his heart rhythm couldn&#x27;t be restored and he died.<p>There are two important points: 1)  you don&#x27;t want to be exercising with many different heart conditions whether temporary or permanent and 2)  when you&#x27;re out and about in your community, keep an eye out for AEDs ... if you don&#x27;t see them at a reasonable frequency, speak up and help get them in place.<p>Donations are accepted and funding may be available at <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gregaed.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;gregaed.com&#x2F;</a>')
