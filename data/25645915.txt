Item(by='jjoonathan', descendants=None, kids=[25646104, 25650378], score=None, time=1609857425, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25645733, text='Desktop UIs are hardly mature, but for the last 15 years UI innovation has been about putting lipstick on a pig rather than deep-diving subsystems and figuring out how to graphically represent and control them.<p>Designers aren&#x27;t doing subsystem deep-dives, they&#x27;re just implementing subsets of existing wholly inadequate GUIs using new widgets. Conversely, systems people who understand the un-GUIed subsystems aren&#x27;t doing design work to better expose them, or expose them at all. It&#x27;s an unproductive and frustrating stalemate.')
