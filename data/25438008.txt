Item(by='charrondev', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608082900, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25436778, text='At work almost every endpoint supports an “expand” parameter that will do various expansions of referenced resources in the returned records. This can:<p>- cause additional sql joins\n- or pull individual records from a cache (if it’s a small enough dataset)\n- or cause one additional DB query and save a network round trip.<p>Example:<p>&#x2F;api&#x2F;v2&#x2F;discussions?sort=recent&amp;expand=insertUser,updateUser,lastCommentUser,parentCategory<p>This would be a pretty extensive case but is very useful.')
