Item(by='shadowgovt', descendants=None, kids=[24726448, 24724887], score=None, time=1602197915, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24723337, text='If the goal of this hypothetical is to show how technological methods are a societal risk, I would suggest couching the hypothetical in something other than &quot;a bomber that is otherwise un-findable.&quot; Technology making it possible to find them through data correlation---even if the technology also has some false-positive risk---seems like an argument in favor of these methods.<p>It&#x27;s probably also worth noting that these technologies can be used on the opposite end of the story too. &quot;but officer, I can&#x27;t possibly have been at the site of the bombing, here&#x27;s my alibi data trail&quot; is also enabled by these mechanisms.')
