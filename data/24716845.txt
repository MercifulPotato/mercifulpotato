Item(by='Doxin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602142900, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24707612, text='A mirror doesn&#x27;t mirror left-to-right, it mirrors front to back. The clue being that you&#x27;re looking at a reflection of your face in a mirror, not a reflection of the back of your head. Of course since nothing <i>but</i> a mirror can do that in day-to-day life most people expect a mirror to do a 180 degree rotation instead, leading to this confusion.')
