So the <i>primary</i> cause is the incentive system and the political environment which makes it possible for them to do that.<p>It&#x27;s all about how the reward functions are designed. So of course if you reward scarcity and profiteering that&#x27;s what you get - as a secondary outcome, not a primary cause.')
