Item(by='nickbauman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608661858, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25501286, text='This can be summarized as<p>&quot;It&#x27;s better to repeat yourself than use the wrong abstraction.&quot;<p>It happens often in the attempt to be DRY we add a parameter or some condition to handle a new variation to what seems like a universal logical construct in the code. Do this enough times and the code is no longer comprehensible to any of the people who wrote each variation, let alone a newcommer. We mistake some commonalities with a universality. We become zealots.')
