Item(by='amadeuspagel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603310215, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24850102, text='There&#x27;s a general sense in the article and the comments here that how much a company invests depends on how much money they have. This is wrong. If there&#x27;s a profitable investment to make, the company is going to make it, if they don&#x27;t have the money, they&#x27;re going to loan it. If there&#x27;s no profitable investment to make, the company isn&#x27;t going to make any investments, if they have money sitting around, they&#x27;ll return it to their shareholders. Yes, there are some frictions involved here. If a company has some crazy genius idea, it might not be that simple to loan money. But for infrastructure investments, it should be.')
