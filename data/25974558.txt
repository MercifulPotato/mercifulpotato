Item(by='nichos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612053656, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25966045, text='This is the only thing that worked for me, been running like this with no issues for about 2 years:<p>Browse to C:\\Windows\\SoftwareDistribution, right click on the Downloads folder, click properties. Security Tab, Advanced, Change Permissions, Disable Inheritance, Remove all inherited permissions from this object, Apply.<p>To enable windows updates, reverse this process. You may have to stop the service or go to safe mode to this, though I don&#x27;t remember doing that.')
