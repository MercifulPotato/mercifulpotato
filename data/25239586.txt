Item(by='iguy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606589664, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25237932, text='Having things available right now, in small quantities, near to you, is precisely the service local hardware stores have <i>always</i> provided. For a small fee.<p>Of course they can be had cheaper, and in a wider variety, from some huge warehouse further away. But then you can&#x27;t get the job done this afternoon.
