I don&#x27;t get the polarised nature of the debate to be honest - there is room for all approaches in a &quot;best tool for the job&quot; way. For everything else there is Dapper.<p>I think a large part of the problem here is that developers who learn to leverage a database only through an ORM are missing out, and really they should also learn SQL (literally the only part of the article that is still objectively correct is the author&#x27;s advice to learn SQL) to gain a better understanding of when (and when not) to use an ORM.
