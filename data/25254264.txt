Item(by='wxnx', descendants=None, kids=[25257876, 25254318], score=None, time=1606747787, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25254205, text='Antisense drugs are the same biological material, but have a very different mechanism of effect.<p>Specifically, mRNA vaccines require an immune response to be effective. The mRNA (vaccine) encodes a viral protein, and the host (human) immune system still has to learn to respond to that protein.<p>Antisense drugs are much simpler, with no immune response required. The mRNA (the drug) itself simply binds to the target mRNA.')
