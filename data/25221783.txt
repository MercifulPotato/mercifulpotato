Item(by='chmod775', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606410692, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25221593, text='You can get a much more powerful alternative for &quot;free&quot; if you support de-structuring assignments (in arguments).<p>JavaScript Example:<p><pre><code>    function test({a,b,c}) {\n        console.log(c,b,a)\n    };\n    \n    test({\n        a: 1,\n        b: 2,\n        c: 3\n    });\n</code></pre>\nIf you have this there&#x27;s really no need for named arguments anymore. If you add features to your de-structuring, your &quot;named arguments&quot; will have them too (things missing for JS specifically: optional objects, TS support to specify types for the destructured objects inline instead of repeating the entire thing).')
