Item(by='Tainnor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606761908, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25254337, text='&quot;I have an accent&quot; as a kind of shorthand for &quot;I have a non-standard accent&quot; is in theory fine, since most people know what you mean.<p>The reason I pointed it out, though, is because the reverse direction - &quot;you have an accent, which is why I don&#x27;t understand you&quot; - especially when directed at actual native speakers implies (even if not intended) some sort of deficiency on the part of the interlocutor.')
