Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608045009, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25430688, text='I&#x27;m having a hard time thinking of a developer documentation use case with any substantial structure that Markdown <i>does</i> support well (by itself).<p>Any form of structured information, for example API functions and their argument types, properties of a class, or tables of historical compatibility, is already stepping outside Markdown.<p>You&#x27;re left with either writing everything out in text and having error-prone parsers cross-check pages against each other, or badly cross-referenced information that goes undetected, or using something that is Markdown <i>plus</i> additional markup for semantic data.')
