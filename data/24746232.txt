Item(by='liquidise', descendants=None, kids=[24746451, 24747649], score=None, time=1602428520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24745564, text='As a former epileptic, the strobing that comes with CRTs is something i’m happy to be rid of. It wasn’t something I could <i>see</i> so much as <i>perceive</i>, but it’s a feeling my body still remembers.<p>That said modern monitor tech still has big problems. Quantum dot has a chance of shifting the field in the next 10 years but right now any monitor effectively excels at only 2-3 of: color gamut, color accuracy, pixel density, refresh rate, response time. HDR is a whole different topic that the industry hasn’t figured out.')
