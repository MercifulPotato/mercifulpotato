Item(by='gradschool', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608737074, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25497328, text='The advantages of C++ over C that account for its popularity boil down to three main things.<p>1. It&#x27;s easier for managers to hire C++ programmers because there are\nmore of them than C programmers.<p>2. C is designed to trust the programmer more than C++ is. In some\norganizations, this difference might be advantageous for containing\nthe damage caused by the dumbest team member.<p>3. It&#x27;s easier for a developer to appear productive to a manager using\nC++, especially a non-technical manager, because it requires writing a\nlot of boilerplate.')
