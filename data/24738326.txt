Item(by='exdsq', descendants=None, kids=[24738391], score=None, time=1602330888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24738280, text='EXIT is trapped in the same way as 0, it&#x27;s something that happens when your shell exits. Ctrl+C sends the SIGINT signal but you can catch it with INT too. You want to do the latter because your gracefully exiting the script, if you want to have some cleanup after that you could trap EXIT (for deleting tmp files or something).')
