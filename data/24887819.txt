It is well-studied, well-established, and hopefully uncontroversial that adult men are several inches taller on average than adult women.<p>I can use that information to predict a wide variety of things with very high confidence, but I can’t use it to tell you how tall any individual human will become. That doesn’t make it wrong nor useless.')
