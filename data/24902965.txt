That&#x27;s it. The DMCA takedown process is emphatically not the avenue to remedy an alleged circumvention tool as defined by the DMCA. The DMCA allows relief through the courts in that case, a route the RIAA chose not to persue because it is easier to misuse the takedown process. The DMCA takedown process is intended <i>only</i> to remedy the situation where your or your clients&#x27; copyrighted content is hosted without permission - any other use is perjury.
