Item(by='romanoderoma', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605905289, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25164056, text='I&#x27;ve been using Linux on and off since 1996, but I settled on it for the past 10 years and the most traumatic thing I had to do since has been changing gear.<p>And even then, moving your entire setup from a machine to another have never been easier, when you know how to do it.<p>Corporate OSs are more polished, that&#x27;s undeniable, Apple especially provides the best out of the box experience, but it&#x27;s nothing comprared to the flexibility I can experience using Linux.<p>I can have a beefed up work laptop with Plasma and all the effects enabled and the same exact setup on the cheap low end laptop with XFCE.<p>Everything, really, just works.<p>And when it doesn&#x27;t, I can somehow make it work.<p>There have been times I spent days trying to make the Nvidia card work, but it was because I was looking for the perfect setup that I usually don&#x27;t experience on Windows or MacOS, but there&#x27;s nothing I can do about it, no matter how much time I spend trying to fix it.<p>It&#x27;s simply out of reach.<p>I&#x27;m aware that that&#x27;s not what the average user is willing to go through, and that&#x27;s perfectly fine, I don&#x27;t feel better than them because I use a more complicated system, but I cannot go back to being limited in what I can do because I am not allowed to.<p>Also, modern distros are really really stable out of the box.<p>The laptop I use as a replacement for media server is an Ubuntu 20, it was an Ubuntu 18 and 16 before, I simply upgraded it to the new version and it worked, every time better than before.<p>If there&#x27;s something I&#x27;ve learned in the past 25 years is that freedom do comes at a cost, but as of today, that cost is actually negligible.<p>P.s. forgot to mention that I do not have to care anymore about bloatware, automatic updates, things calling home, software you rely on going out of business and, most of all, lack of support or documentation')
