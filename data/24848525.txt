Item(by='mekoka', descendants=None, kids=[24848848], score=None, time=1603292580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24848336, text='The double negative has its use beyond language sophistication. It can serve to more specifically mark a neutral stance in a vocabulary that may seem to only offer a dichotomy. e.g. what I have available is <i>I like her</i> and  <i>I don&#x27;t like her</i>, but what I mean to say is <i>I don&#x27;t dislike her</i>.')
