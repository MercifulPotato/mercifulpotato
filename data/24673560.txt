Item(by='geophile', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601747302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24670700, text='&quot;The best programs are the ones written when the programmer is supposed to be working on something else.&quot; - Melinda Varian.<p>This is missing one important variable -- the age of the project. Sure, if I have to fix a bug at work, in some crufty old software, that I probably didn&#x27;t write, that&#x27;s a lot less appealing than writing brand new, clean, perfect code on my own project.<p>On the other hand, if my new project gets to be a few years old, and develops some cruft of its own, and I have to fix a bug  in it, that might look a lot less appealing than a brand new work project, in which I am writing new, clean, perfect code.')
