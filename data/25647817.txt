Item(by='nostrademons', descendants=None, kids=[25647918, 25647986], score=None, time=1609867001, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25647063, text='Making DST permanent effectively shifts all of the U.S. one timezone eastward (so EDT = UTC+4, PDT = UTC+7, instead of +5 and +8 for their standard time equivalents).  People seem to prefer the earlier timezone (which gives extra daylight in the evening at the expense of the morning), and it also makes collaboration with Europe a bit easier.')
