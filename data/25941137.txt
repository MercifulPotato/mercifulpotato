Item(by='j1elo', descendants=None, kids=[25951833], score=None, time=1611838271, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25941050, text='&gt; When you know what the project scope is, tight enforcement of that scope is critical to prevent the complexity of the project from running away and ultimately killing the project entirely.<p>Which is why I believe a &quot;benevolent dictatorship&quot; style of governance has more chances (nitpick prevention: <i>more</i>, not <i>all</i> the chances) to become successful and thrive.<p>If you let too many community members decide on what is in and out of scope, the project ends up being a conglomerate of the most random favorite features from the most vocal participants.')
