Item(by='fh973', descendants=None, kids=[25014023], score=None, time=1604731899, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013140, text='One important aspect that should be mentioned is that OpenEBS is essentially a block storage system (think virtual hard drives, which need to be formatted with dfs&#x2F;ext4) and by its nature can&#x27;t expose the same files to multiple pods.<p>Ceph(FS) and other distributed file systems allow you to access your data from any number of machines. Also they provide faster and more robust failover as they don&#x27;t have to unplug the virtual hard drive and repair the local file system.')
