Item(by='dessant', descendants=None, kids=[25764908], score=None, time=1610556872, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25762560, text='Having a link to an external web page to receive donations is not considered a violation on the App Store, this is a mistake by a reviewer.<p>Collecting funds &quot;within the app&quot; means that the payment flow is completed without leaving the app. They explicitly list two ways for any app to accept donations, by redirecting the user to an external web service opened in Safari, or by collecting payments using a text message.<p>You obviously have to somehow communicate to the user that donations can be made, and that is allowed to happen by showing an external link.')
