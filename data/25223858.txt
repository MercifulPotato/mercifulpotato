Item(by='the8472', descendants=None, kids=[25226352], score=None, time=1606428355, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25223568, text='Any async or event-loop runtime can be almost entirely powered by io_uring. Timers, waiting for work when you&#x27;re out of CPU-bound tasks, most IO syscalls, it all can go through io_uring.<p>You&#x27;ll still need a few worker threads for blocking syscalls that haven&#x27;t been ported to io_uring yet but that need is greatly reduced compared to the previous state of things.<p>So even if you&#x27;re not using io_uring yourself the language standard libraries or server frameworks will.<p>There are WIPs for netty, libuv, nginx. Other projects are exploring it or have announced intent to use it.')
