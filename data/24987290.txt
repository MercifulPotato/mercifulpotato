Item(by='43920', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604478473, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24987267, text='I had one of those too, and it worked great for about 6 months, until one of the print nozzles became clogged. It turns out that on most printers, the print heads are part of the cartridges (=replaceable), but on the ecotank printers they&#x27;re built-in to the printer - so if the printer&#x27;s built-in cleaning system doesn&#x27;t work, it&#x27;s almost impossible to unclog.')
