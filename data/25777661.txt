Item(by='mytailorisrich', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610638925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25776620, text='Moderation is not upvoting&#x2F;downvoting.<p>For example, when you moderate a debate you do not silence opinions you disagree with, you simply ensure that people express themselves within &#x27;acceptable&#x27; boundaries, which usually means civility.<p>To me this means that &#x27;decentralised content moderation&#x27; is largely an utopia: Whilst the rules may be defined by the community, letting everyone moderate will, in my view, always end up being similar to upvoting&#x2F;downvoting which is a vote of agreement&#x2F;disagreement.')
