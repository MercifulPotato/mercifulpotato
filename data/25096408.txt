Item(by='uberman', descendants=None, kids=[25097074], score=None, time=1605394207, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25096096, text='The notion that something as simple as:<p>AB&#x2F;CD<p>Could have multiple &quot;valid&quot; interpretations boggles my mind.
