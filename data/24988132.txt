Item(by='pessimizer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604489477, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24985147, text='I&#x27;ve been a fan for a very long time of getting to all of these sites through <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;used.addall.com" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;used.addall.com</a><p>It&#x27;s a very good used book meta-search engine that sorts by price and condition, and includes specific notes from the merchant <i>in the results page.</i> I went through computer science&#x2F;engineering in college using it, and one semester managed to get one-off editions of all of my books for less than $20 <i>total.</i><p>Using it also ends up getting you acquainted with a lot of online used booksellers. It also includes Amazon, but you start to notice that a lot of the specific copies of books on Amazon are actually also being sold by the same sellers on friendlier or their own platforms, and when they&#x27;re on Amazon they&#x27;re often marked up to cover the Amazon tax.')
