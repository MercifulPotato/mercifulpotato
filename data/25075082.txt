Vim is not an IDE.<p>If you take a step back and look at software development as a whole then IDEs like VSCode and PyCharm take the top down approach.<p>Whereas Vim is one of the solutions which do it bottom up.<p>With both approaches you can theoretically arrive at the same functionality but in reality, there will always be something missing.<p>Some people prefer the bottom up approach as that gives them the illusion of knowing their tool completely whereas people with the top down preference think everything is curated for them.<p>Over time, both kinds of people notice this isn&#x27;t quite true.')
