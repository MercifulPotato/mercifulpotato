Item(by='Karunamon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601907906, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24686888, text='Wouldn&#x27;t it be preferable, then, to avoid squashing your commits, and then tag your releases? This gets you the best of both worlds, you can still back out to a known good version in case of issues, and you can still bisect to narrow down the exact commit that caused your problem.<p>Bisect is <i>the</i> killer feature of git, for me. Squashing releases takes that superpower away.')
