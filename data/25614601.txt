Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609609580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25614106, text='Listening to your customers doesn’t mean “give them what they <i>think</i> they want”.<p>Customers typically just mention a solution that’s a bit better than what they have, and won’t even mention the root problem they want to solve (they’ll ask for faster ways to fill in paper forms, for example, without questioning the existence of those forms)<p>Good designers will ask follow-up questions to unearth the problem to be solved, then think about the best (given the state of technology, available budget, political and social constraints) solution. That <i>may</i> be a faster horse, but need not be.')
