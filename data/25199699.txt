Item(by='saalweachter', descendants=None, kids=[25199824], score=None, time=1606234202, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25198826, text='Square mil, and square mil-e[quivalent], for the battery-only efficiency, not miles.<p>When you have a volume-per-distance measurement, such as liters&#x2F;km, you can cancel units to get an area.<p>The square mil -- a mil is 1&#x2F;1000 of an inch, usually used in machining parts -- is the US customary unit which is best suited to the magnitude; 62 square mil is so much nicer than 0.04 square millimeters or 40000 square micrometers.')
