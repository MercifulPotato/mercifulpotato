Item(by='YeGoblynQueenne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609857254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25643950, text='Well, one&#x27;s horoscope is also a set of &quot;determined and pretty precise&quot; values but that&#x27;s not to say that we have learned anything useful from looking at a person&#x27;s horoscope, except from what can be informed by a belief that it influences a person&#x27;s behaviour.<p>That is to say, that the value of a variable can be precisely known doesn&#x27;t say anything about the utility of knowing that value. Accordingly, the talk in the linked video is not arguing that the values of random variables cannot be determined with precision, rather that doing so is not sufficiently informative for the purposes of understanding physical reality.')
