Buy heirloom varieties.<p>Organic isn’t a magic word that means better, and as it has become popular and more industrialized, it means less. There are plenty of questionably healthy practices which count as “organic”.<p>Something being grown nearby doesn’t make it automatically any better.<p>Finding local producers that can deliver seasonal heirloom varieties would be maximizing your chances.  Growing your own getting your seed from somewhere like the seed savers exchange would have a bit better chances.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.seedsavers.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.seedsavers.org&#x2F;</a>')
