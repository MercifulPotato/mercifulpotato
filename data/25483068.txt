Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608428226, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25482109, text='It &#x27;stands out&#x27; for it&#x27;s dystopian naivete.<p>&#x27;Work&#x27; is an essential ingredient in the continued creation (and improvement) of the machine form which we develop our standard of living.<p>While we can strive for &#x27;free as in liberty&#x27; - nothing material in this universe is &#x27;free as in beer&#x27; - we still must make do with &#x27;work&#x27; - which means an <i>intelligent and conscientious choice</i> to do it.<p>We actually are &#x27;free as in liberty&#x27; to chose to live in material poverty, or to make the effort to improve our condition.<p>Let us radically simplify:<p>1) Your household will become &#x27;messy&#x27; as you live in it.\n2) Nobody is going to &#x27;clean it&#x27; but you.\n3) &#x27;Cleaning&#x27; is &#x27;work&#x27;.<p>So you can chose to A) live in an ever dirtier and dysfunctional household or B) clean it (i.e. work) and enjoy the fruits of your labour.<p>In other words, there is basically no &#x27;magical cleaning robot, that doesn&#x27;t require fuel or maintenance&#x27;.')
