Item(by='woodruffw', descendants=None, kids=[25976787, 25974196], score=None, time=1612046150, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25972923, text='As the phrase &quot;dots per inch&quot; implies, it&#x27;s a ratio. Specifically, it&#x27;s the ratio between your resolution (say, 4K) and the size of your screen.<p>What is &quot;not true&quot; is that every screen&#x27;s pixel density is the same. Different screens have different sizes and resolutions, which means they have different pixel densities.')
