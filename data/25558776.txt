Item(by='magicalhippo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609159498, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25555878, text='Just to be pedantic, low-voltage transport is wasteful (expensive) compared to high-voltage transport of the same power (energy).<p>That is because transport (cable) loss is a function of the current squared, while the transported power (energy) is a function of both current and voltage.<p>Of course most appliances that want DC usually do so at a lower voltage (typically 5-24V), hence why they often go hand in hand.')
