While it won’t completely cancel sharp, sudden, very loud sounds, it will significant reduce their intensity.<p>It’s easy to miss someone talking to you, or knocking on your door, for example, when wearing NC headphones.<p>The Sony headphone software has settings to adjust the intensity of blocking for different kinds of sounds (such as voices, or traffic sounds).')
