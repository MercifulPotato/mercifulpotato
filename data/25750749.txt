Item(by='runako', descendants=None, kids=[25751489], score=None, time=1610474728, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25742585, text='&gt; Many people spend upwards of years before going to trial.<p>Slight hijack, but this is a key reason behind the movement to abolish cash bail. The thrust of the argument is essentially that people with more financial resources can pay bail. Those without those resources cannot, so the pre-trial period is often a de facto prison sentence whose length is unknown in advance. This setup obviously results in a tiered justice system.<p>There are ~500k people in jail who are innocent under the law. The elimination of cash bail is intended to eliminate this disparity.')
