Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610556526, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25764401, text='You can formally verify a finite state machine by simply testing all state-transitions.<p>You don&#x27;t need to do an exhaustive check of the 2^8^15 all 15-byte combinations. You just check all state-transitions of the state machine.<p>Or to put it another way: you don&#x27;t need to test all 2^8^15 byte combinations. You just need to check all invalid-instructions that have ONE invalid byte, to prove the attributes of the finite-state-machine.')
