Item(by='pmoriarty', descendants=None, kids=[25018972], score=None, time=1604778375, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25017663, text='This reminds me of a story about when Kurt Gödel, who had fled Nazi persecution of Jews in Austria, went to apply for US Citizenship.<p>In the taxi, on the way to the citizenship hearing, Gödel excitedly told his friend Albert Einstein that he intended to talk at the hearing of a flaw he had discovered in the US Constitution that could allow a dictatorship to take over.<p>Einstein managed to convince Gödel not to talk about that at the citizenship hearing, and just answer to questions.<p>But perhaps Gödel wasn&#x27;t so wrong.')
