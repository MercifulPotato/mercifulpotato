You can license your works, but you can&#x27;t relinquish your author rights (&quot;Urheberrecht&quot;).<p>I&#x27;m only familiar with Germany because I live here, but from talking to a friend about this recently, the situation is apparently similar in other countries as well. They were looking into using OSS and ran into that issue because they&#x27;d also need to look into the (at the time of contribution) home-countries of contributors to make sure that the license would &quot;stick&quot;.')
