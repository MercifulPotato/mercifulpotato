Item(by='boulos', descendants=None, kids=[25555954], score=None, time=1609095613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25551679, text='The “recipe” itself (the ingredients, the steps) isn’t copyrightable but the <i>surrounding text</i> of a “recipe” can be considered copyrightable.<p>So you can happily reproduce the statements of facts and the process. You can’t include someone’s anecdotes about how their grandfather liked to make the dish for New Year’s Eve.')
