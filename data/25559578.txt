Item(by='viktorcode', descendants=None, kids=[25561871], score=None, time=1609167109, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25557354, text='I think the author conflates simplification of the whole codebase of a 3D rendering engine with the simplification of its architecture. Of course, complex image effects and arcane optimisation techniques will bloat the code, but real time ray tracing will allow throwing away whole series of hacks and tricks used now to produce believable image. The rendering pipeline will become much simpler in terms of stages.')
