Item(by='nyanpasu64', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603097129, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24820016, text='Years ago, Windows Firefox switched to DirectWrite (Chrome-style) font rendering. Due to user complaints, Firefox now uses GDI-style font rendering for a specific list of fonts (Arial,Consolas,Courier New,Microsoft Sans Serif,Segoe UI,Tahoma,Trebuchet MS,Verdana). The font in the screenshot is Arial, being rendered using GDI. And GDI-style font rendering cannot rotate text well (possibly due to the fractional vertical positioning needed).')
