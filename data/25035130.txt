Item(by='lmilcin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604932154, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25029974, text='I think it is naive this is any kind of proof of anything.<p>The fraud can happen in many ways. It might be a person leaning one way or another thinking they are working for a good cause. Actually, I think making it difficult for people from opposing party to vote is a kind of fraud, made in broad daylight.<p>Proving absence of fraud is much more difficult than showing there aren&#x27;t obvious signs of it.<p><i>BUT</i>, and here is important thing, this is really a question of burden of proof, and in this case it is on the accuser. In absence of proof it is assumed voting was legitimate. Also, extraordinary claims require extraordinary proofs.')
