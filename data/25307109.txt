Item(by='M2Ys4U', descendants=None, kids=[25310712], score=None, time=1607112599, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25306205, text='Parliament can&#x27;t <i>propose</i> new legislation (except in one or two very narrow areas provided for in the treaties), but it <i>is</i> a legislature.<p>In nearly all cases (except again in narrow areas defined in the treaties) Parliament <i>must</i> pass the legislation first, before it goes to the Council. They then <i>must</i> agree any changes made by the Council.<p>And yes, Parliament can - and almost always does - amend legislation as it goes through the legislative process.')
