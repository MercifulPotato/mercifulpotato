For example, Prettier will take this JavaScript line:<p><pre><code>  document.addEventListener(&#x27;keydown&#x27;, e =&gt; { console.log(e) });\n</code></pre>\nand convert it to this, which changes both whitespace and syntax:<p><pre><code>  document.addEventListener(&quot;keydown&quot;, (e) =&gt; {\n    console.log(e);\n  });\n</code></pre>\nI don&#x27;t think there are any diff tools that can hide the differences there, even though they&#x27;re superficial.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;prettier.io&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;prettier.io&#x2F;</a>')
