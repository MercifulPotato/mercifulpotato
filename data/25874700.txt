Item(by='coliveira', descendants=None, kids=[25884209], score=None, time=1611341198, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25871787, text='The reason desktops were customizable in the 90s is that they followed strict GUI rules and used the same toolkits. Nowadays, apps want to have control of their UI to a higher degree, which makes it difficult to provide customization. The same applies to the web: since every app wants to have it own UI, there is no space for user-level customization.')
