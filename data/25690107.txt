If she wishes to meet people in less-secure rooms it&#x27;s entirely possible to create meeting rooms with fewer or even no significant entry or security controls if you wish, but your personal office, places where you store sensitive data etc... can&#x27;t just have em behind a few wooden doors.<p>Of course some countries opted for the benefit of a modern building.
