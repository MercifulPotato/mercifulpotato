In many cases performance can be achieved with stateful, imperative yet efficiently executed code that economizes on the use of resources on a single node. In many cases, such as for mobile, desktop, or on-prem deployments, this is the only way to &quot;scale&quot; since you do not have the luxury of increasing number of nodes on demand.')
