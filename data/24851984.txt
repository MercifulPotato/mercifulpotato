Marsupials have an advantage on dry land.  But if a marsupial mother with babies in her pouch swam, she&#x27;d drown them.  So monotremes have an advantage in the water.<p>Placentals, of course, usually outcompete both.  With occasional exceptions, such as the success of opossums in North America.')
