Item(by='UnpossibleJim', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605635594, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25125296, text='Not to be a naysayer, but when you get into legal terms, you have to be careful with terms like &quot;addictive&quot;. Addictive implies a component of physicality (by the legal&#x2F;medical definition, not the common definition). It&#x27;s horribly habitual, which is as bad as, and hard to differentiate except in the physical symptomology of withdrawal.<p>I know this sounds like splitting hairs on message boards, but I can guarantee you that all of these people who have been called to testify have been briefed heavily by squads of lawyers.')
