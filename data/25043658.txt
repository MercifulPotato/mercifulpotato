Item(by='duskwuff', descendants=None, kids=[25045510, 25047624, 25044938], score=None, time=1604994468, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25042932, text='The IIgs came out in 1988, four years <i>after</i> the Macintosh. As such, GS&#x2F;OS was pretty heavily influenced by the Mac user interface; it was practically a demake. It still had some unique quirks, though -- for instance, it used proportional scrollbars, which wouldn&#x27;t show up on the Macintosh until 1997 (in Mac OS 8).')
