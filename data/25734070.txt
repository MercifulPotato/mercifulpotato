Item(by='el_nahual', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610391044, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25733305, text='Most antidepressants actually have it right there on the tin as a potential side effect.<p>Paradoxically, starting an antidepressant regimen can <i>increase</i> the odds of suicide for some people. The mechanism seems to be something like &quot;doesn&#x27;t completely cure your depression, but makes you un-depressed enough to actually act on suicidal ideation.&quot;<p>This is why any competent psychiatrist will pair getting started on antidepressants with an increase in vigilance of the patient.')
