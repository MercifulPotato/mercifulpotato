Item(by='gitgud', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606267173, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25203038, text='Most progress is determined by a multitude of factors, network speed, device speed, RAM size etc... And even some things that are unknown to the process; server load, problem complexity, problem size...etc. It&#x27;s just easier to lie!<p>A good solution is to have a progress bar that goes for a certain amount of time 0-&gt;10 seconds. But if it finishes earlier, then it quickly jumps to 100%.<p>Still a lie, but it&#x27;s satisfying to watch and accurately <i>finishes</i> every time (so it&#x27;s harder to spot the lie).')
