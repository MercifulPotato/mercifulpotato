Item(by='taneq', descendants=None, kids=[24745963], score=None, time=1602424602, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24745564, text='Gaming LCDs these days boast response times in the low single digit millisecond range. Even at 144Hz that’s less than a frame.<p>If there’s framebuffer-to-photons lag, I’d point my finger at DVI decoding before the physical movement of liquid crystals.<p>Also, for all the focus on “physical changes” there’s no mention of phosphor fade rate, which is a physical property of the CRT screen with a built-in trade off between latency and flicker.<p>Please let’s not have CRTs become the new Monster Cables.')
