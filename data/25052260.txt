How well do you remember MSDN? In the mid-2000s, Microsoft documentation lived on three or four separate sites, and I don&#x27;t remember the names of all of them. The two that spring immediately to mind are MSDN and TechNet. MSDN had the infuriating issue of broken links. You&#x27;d be deep-diving on an issue, find a link to an article that looked like it might perfectly address your question, and... 404.<p>It got so bad that a VP ended up making a pronouncement, declaring a war on broken links. He announced a multi-year plan to reduce and eventually fix all the broken links on MSDN. This was the vacuum that was partially responsible for the birth of Stack Overflow.')
