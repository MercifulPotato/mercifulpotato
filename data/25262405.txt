Item(by='yarg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606797950, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25262235, text='Unfortunately, the part left out of the analogy - that which is different - tends to be the more complex part.<p>So although we may be able to establish a baseline along analogous terms - and may be able to use that understanding adequately well in explaining the deeper concepts (or underlying truth) we do need to get to a point of understanding where whatever analogies we used to scaffold out explanation can be discarded.<p>Which leaves me wondering the depths to which we must be expected to delve to explain a deeply technical subject matter in a university context - and what the scaffold actually achieves.')
