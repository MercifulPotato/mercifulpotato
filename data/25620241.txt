Item(by='polm23', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609659170, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25615883, text='Several reasons come to mind.<p>1. The original word2vec is considered a reference implementation, used for benchmarks, so some people might not want it to change much.<p>2. The original hasn&#x27;t been updated since 2017, and development was never very interactive.<p>3. word2vec has been widely reimplemented, and some reimplementations may be more widely used than the original (particularly Gensim).<p>4. The original had a paper from the very start (rather than being an open source project where a paper came later), so other papers reference it. For future papers that use the koan form, having a paper makes it easy to use in a similar way.')
