Item(by='ssivark', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610310950, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25719275, text='As has been pointed out, Microsoft has been very heavily pushing for the language server approach with the argument that it simplifies the integration between {languages, editors} from M*N to M+N.<p>That they would forbid their holy language server implementation from integrating with other editors exposes the perfidy at the heart of their advocacy.<p>If they want to keep the extension proprietary and charge for the language server, that’s fine!
