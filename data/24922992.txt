Item(by='ATsch', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603912786, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24920612, text='While it&#x27;s true that originally X was network transparent, this hasn&#x27;t truly been the case for a long time now. All modern applications (including the ones you&#x27;ve listed) render the whole window locally for performance reasons. This does not work over the network. Instead, xorg will transmit a picture of the window, giving you an in many ways worse version of VNC. The only difference is the level of integration with other programs like SSH.')
