Item(by='Baeocystin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606861625, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25270243, text='&gt;I adore my two but the planet is likely to be worse off, ecologically at least, for their existence.<p>Our planet is already on the tail end of its lifespan, in terms of how long it will be able to support advanced life before the sun gets too hot and cooks everything.<p>Ours is, as best we can tell, the first and only species to arise that has enough intelligence to be able to do something about it.<p>If we expired as a species tomorrow, the chance of another one rising to the challenge before things wind down is (probably) very low.<p>We need smart, motivated people to work on the problem, and we need these people to have kids and pass on the same pro-education, pro-problem-solving cultural habits to the next generation.<p>In my opinion, that&#x27;s worth far, far more than the extra eco burden one additional human life adds.')
