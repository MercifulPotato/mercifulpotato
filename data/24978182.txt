Item(by='regularfry', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604398447, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24977643, text='The dictionary definition of &quot;need&quot; is instructive.<p>The first definition (in Collins 2018) is &quot;to be in want of&quot;, so in a sense you&#x27;re right.  However, one of the subsequent definitions is &quot;distress or extremity&quot;, followed by &quot;extreme poverty or destitution&quot;. &quot;Need&quot;, then, is a want that&#x27;s extreme enough to cause suffering if it&#x27;s unfulfilled.<p>In that sense, people did <i>need</i> antibiotics. They might not have known it, but they did. In the same way, today we <i>need</i> treatments that we haven&#x27;t discovered yet. &quot;Being able to survive&quot; isn&#x27;t the bar. &quot;Not suffering for the lack&quot; is.')
