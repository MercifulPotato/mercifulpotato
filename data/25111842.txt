Item(by='LocalH', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605536180, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25110680, text='There are two different types of value, simultaneously existing. The content is valuable, certainly. But the actual physical artifact is <i>also</i> valuable, for different reasons.<p>I liken it to prototype&#x2F;prerelease software - the data is certainly valuable, but at the same time a physical artifact with actual provenance can be worth a pretty penny too. It also irks me when people say &quot;oh, it devalues the physical artifact if you preserve the data&quot;. No, because the physical artifact itself <i>cannot be duplicated</i>.')
