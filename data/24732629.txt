Item(by='czzr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602266683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24731934, text='Not OP but basically a red queens race is a competition where everyone is running to stay in the same place. So in this case tracking gets increasingly invasive as as companies struggle to outcompete each other, although the end result is not necessarily any better for any one company, and certainly worse for privacy.<p>One way out is to ban targeted advertising - then no one has to invest in building better profiles, they can still serve ads based on the content being served.<p>The “red queen” concept comes from evolutionary biology by the way, to describe evolutionary arms races. The actual name “red queen” is an Alice in wonderland reference.')
