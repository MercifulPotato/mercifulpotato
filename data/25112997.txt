Item(by='praptak', descendants=None, kids=[25113129], score=None, time=1605541255, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25112484, text='&gt; Unless you have a respirator, there isn&#x27;t anything you can take to resolve &quot;difficulty breathing&quot;.<p>As a private person you can realistically get an oxygen concentrator. These are simpler and less expensive than ventilators (I am not even sure if an untrained person should  operate a ventilator).<p>I was involved in getting a concentrator for my friend who got a very bad case of covid though not bad enough to get into a hospital.
