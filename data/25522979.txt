Item(by='acdha', descendants=None, kids=[25523796], score=None, time=1608762257, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25522617, text='You have an interesting definition of “non functional” describing a UI component seen daily by billions of people which is, well, an iconic part of your site&#x27;s branding.<p>As for the rest, welcome to distributed systems. Any time you&#x27;re working on something which involves multiple codebases on different schedules you&#x27;re going to have a crossover period. For the web that period is measured in years since it has many clients and billions of users.<p>The article covers this but if you look at the data (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;caniuse.com&#x2F;link-icon-svg" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;caniuse.com&#x2F;link-icon-svg</a>) there are really only two things you need:<p>1. An SVG icon for modern browsers<p>2. favicon.ico for everyone else<p>If you want to support higher resolution icons for Safari&#x27;s pinned tabs or iOS&#x2F;Android you can add those as well but that&#x27;s an optimization for new way to interact with web apps which those device makers added and isn&#x27;t necessary unless you expect lots of people to use that feature and your existing icon color scheme clashes with the system UI.')
