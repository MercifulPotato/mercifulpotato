This is great for quickly dicing veg into really tiny pieces (grain of rice size).<p>IMO: If you&#x27;re going that small, get a food processor.<p>Food processors are very useful in a number of recipes: homemade cuts of ground beef (ever try Rib-eye burgers?), breadcrumbs, casserole?<p>----------------<p>Similarly, if you&#x27;re julienne  (aka: french cut) a lot, consider a mandoline slicer.
