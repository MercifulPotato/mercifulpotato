Item(by='Kednicma', descendants=None, kids=[24805823, 24805850], score=None, time=1602887261, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24805334, text='It is essential when we look at deep time, or any sufficiently high-dimensional and detailed time series, to remember that we are only looking at a tiny slice. We always like to talk of &quot;the tree of life&quot;, which might mislead folks into thinking that we get a clean cross-section of every branch of some high-dimensional tree. But, in truth, what we get is more like a tiny wedge cut out from a beanstalk with many central vines; we have only small leaves and cuttings from a mighty thick overgrowth of life.')
