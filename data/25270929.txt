Item(by='lostcolony', descendants=None, kids=[25272217], score=None, time=1606861969, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25270283, text='Once a company realizes that it&#x27;s not magical unicorns, they&#x27;re too deeply embedded.<p>Is it better to spend X every year to just make the Salesforce solution work (thereby not having to admit to a mistake, and mitigating personal risk), or spend 3X in one year to write something in house (that requires maybe 1&#x2F;10th X to maintain&#x2F;extend from then on, but which is riskier, and unlikely to show up on future evals such that they can claim credit and get a raise)?<p>Corporations seem especially vulnerable to sunk cost fallacies.')
