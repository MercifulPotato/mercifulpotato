Item(by='FiddlerClamp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608409542, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25478791, text='I visited my father in a nursing home for years when his mild cognitive impairment had turned into full-blown dementia.<p>There was always at least one patient screaming to be let out, to go home, or that they were suffering in some way. Screaming the same thing, every ten seconds. Hour after hour.<p>One shouted &quot;Please!&quot; &quot;Please!&quot; over and over again, in a piteous voice.<p>Those are the ones who still have some speech faculty left. While I know that many were either unaware of anything (like my father at the end), or lived in some pleasant accommodation, there were several who went through agony.')
