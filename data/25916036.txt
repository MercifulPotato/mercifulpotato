Item(by='WJW', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611671021, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25915818, text='Making investments in future scalability at the cost of dev speed and performance is exactly the wrong tradeoff given that 90% of startups fail. At the start, when you have very little income, you want to allow for as much speed and flexibility as possible so that you can get to product&#x2F;market fit ASAP. By the time your company gets big enough that plain MySQL&#x2F;Postgres can&#x27;t handle the load any longer, you will have more than enough money to afford a few experts that can help you migrate.')
