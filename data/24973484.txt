Item(by='abeppu', descendants=None, kids=[24975092], score=None, time=1604351892, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972669, text='Some time ago, I switched to google fi. The experience has been pretty positive (monthly bills are low, speeds are decent, the idea of seamlessly switching between networks is appealing).<p>But when I read horror stories of people getting their google accounts terminated, I realize that losing my google account would also  remove my ability to receive SMS 2FA codes, and recovering all my accounts would be even more difficult than for a person who is merely dependent on gmail.<p>This would be less of a concern if google had a way to associate separate accounts with each service, users could have confidence that google would actually treat them as distinct, even if they&#x27;re continually used from the same devices, etc.')
