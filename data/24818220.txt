Item(by='tom_', descendants=None, kids=[24818793], score=None, time=1603032549, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24815966, text='Names of locations in assembly language are typically constants, similar to global arrays in C. There&#x27;s no my_ptr, as such; there&#x27;s an address, and my_ptr is its name. When you watch my_ptr, you want to monitor what&#x27;s at that address - ``watch *my_ptr&#x27;&#x27;, in gdb terms.<p>Not the end of the world, just annoying.')
