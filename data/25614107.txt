Item(by='cheese_van', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609606451, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25613009, text='Not an answer to your question, but rather an observation regarding budgeting:<p>I daily document my account balance to the penny, which unexpectedly acts (for me) as a brake on superfluous spending.<p>Simply the act of penny-wise documentation compels the need to be aware of spending.  Having merely a spreadsheet and a budget app, that framework encouraged me to compare my current day balance with the previous month&#x27;s day, and provided a visual reward when my balance was higher.<p>In sum, maintaining granular data about my account led to good things.')
