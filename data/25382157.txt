We live in a low density neighborhood with right-of-ways along property lines, but no sidewalks, curbs, or below-ground storm drains. The roads are designed to discourage high speeds, and it&#x27;s a pleasant walking environment, so the non-vehicular traffic along the roadside is higher than most suburban neighborhoods.')
