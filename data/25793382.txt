Item(by='jude-', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610731463, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25785471, text='Not really.  The key difference is the degree of involvement Bitcoin miners have in Stacks mining.  In side-chains, Bitcoin miners would need to opt-in to mining the side-chain and validate its blocks.  In Stacks, Bitcoin miners only need to include the Stacks miners&#x27; block-commitment transactions into Bitcoin blocks.  Since block-commitment transactions look like normal BTC transactions, no Bitcoin miner involvement is necessary beyond what they&#x27;re already doing.<p>The full protocol description can be found in <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;stacksgov&#x2F;sips" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;stacksgov&#x2F;sips</a> (see SIP-001 and SIP-007 in particular).')
