Item(by='jayparth', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603421269, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24865193, text='True, it doesn&#x27;t have to tell you when you work. But it does have to pay you a minimum wage. If a staffing company said: &quot;work whenever and we&#x27;ll pay you a minimum wage&quot;, then drivers would probably routinely drive at times they found pleasant, not profitable times for the staffing company. In this case the staffing company would start up restrict the hours a driver could drive.<p>A staffing company wants to reduce the amount of unprofitable hours driven. To do that they have to restrict working hours. It&#x27;s not guaranteed but a highly likely outcome of the set up.')
