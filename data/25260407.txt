Item(by='wrp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606778999, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25260253, text='I have used both Octave and Scilab as a substitute for MATLAB. Scilab is a much more polished system, but it has multiple gotchas if you are moving back and forth between it and MATLAB. Octave aims for full MATLAB compatibility, but I found the system pretty rough all around.')
