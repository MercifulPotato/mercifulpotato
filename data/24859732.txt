I&#x27;ve only done bootstrapping on x86 and 8-bit processors (which is just set your reset vector), but x86 has a lot of isoterric setup stuff, and learning about the magic is interesting, if not totally useful.<p>Also, some runtime environments have a lot of manageability within; probably less than an OS, but maybe enough.
