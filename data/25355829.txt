Item(by='pabs3', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607494116, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25351258, text='Most of the complaints about AWS are not about license compliance and source code release, but about money and competition.<p>AGPL doesn&#x27;t solve the AWS problem, which is that Amazon will take the project your open source company wrote and soak up all the money to be had offering that project as a service and they will do a better job of it, while still complying with the license.<p>A related problem is that Amazon have enough engineers they can just look at your proprietary or source available project and just clone your programming interfaces on top of a new project (and if you are unlucky, open source that, killing your market entirely).')
