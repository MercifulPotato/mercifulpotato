Item(by='jmiskovic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607681066, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25377390, text='More actionable, the choice of colors and their combinations is the key ingredient for cohesive and tight art direction. Pixel art, vector art, low-poly 3D - they all rely more on good colors than on shapes.<p>It&#x27;s quite hard to pick each color from color picker wheel and ending up with something decent. Much better approach is to use pre-assembled palettes like ones on lospec.com&#x2F;palette-list collection. My favorite versatile palette is edg32, but others will work better for certain moods. Working with more than 30-is colors requires experience and patience.<p>These guidelines can help to build something that catches attention, and assets tend to be simpler and easier to iterate on.')
