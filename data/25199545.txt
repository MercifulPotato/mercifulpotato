Item(by='singlow', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606233358, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25199302, text='It is in fact traditional formal style, at least in the U.S., when writing the name of a married couple with a shared last name, to put the _woman&#x27;s_ name first: &quot;Jane and John Doe&quot;. In contrast, when using the titles it is the man&#x27;s title first: &quot;Mr. and Mrs. John Doe&quot;.')
