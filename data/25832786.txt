But if life started through some extremely lucky event like lightning repeatedly hitting just the right collection of particles in an early planet&#x27;s ocean, then panspermia gives you a plausible mechanism how this happening just once in a galaxy could still lead to multiple planets having life (and maybe one of them developing into intelligent life that can ask itself how this happened).
