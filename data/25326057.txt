This usually means the first release containing new features, excluding beta releases. It is intended for mainstream consumption and more stable than beta. Maintenance releases come next. They fix bugs in features but generally don’t release new features.<p>Of course, such nomenclature and how strictly it’s adhered to varies from project to project.')
