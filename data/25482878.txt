Item(by='onli', descendants=None, kids=[25482986, 25484225], score=None, time=1608425873, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25482668, text='I use IceWM for well over a decade now. It&#x27;s a nice WM. It is close enough to Win 95&#x2F;98&#x2F;2000&#x2F;XP to not feel too new in the beginning, with its start menu and regular window decorations. But it also has the features of a real Linux WM, with virtual desktops, window rules, mouse focus settings, configurable shortcuts, integrated command runner etc. I don&#x27;t use use the taskbar anymore (replaced it with a combination of conky&#x2F;simdock&#x2F;trayer, but the desktop still works with IceWM at the core), but the integrated network and load indicator was very useful to get additional feedback, that a program is starting or stuck, or a download finished or paused.<p>It&#x27;s a complete package, but at the same time a very lightweight WM. It&#x27;s great.<p>Just back then the default theme was ugly. Coming from Ubuntu I&#x27;m still using <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.box-look.org&#x2F;p&#x2F;1018090&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.box-look.org&#x2F;p&#x2F;1018090&#x2F;</a> - doesn&#x27;t look any worse than a modern DE to me now.')
