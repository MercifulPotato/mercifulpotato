A lot of knowledge is sociological in nature. Failures and tricks of the trade are discussed at the bar after 5. I&#x27;ve experienced this first hand.<p>Academic publications and lectures, in their (rightful) pursuit of rigor, aren&#x27;t usually the right space for conversations around hunches and experiences.
