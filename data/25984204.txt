Item(by='olah_1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612139624, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25982860, text='The point about simplicity is important and often overlooked.<p>&gt; Complex software that can’t be developed by a different group of people creates dependence, step one of user domestication. That alone is enough to open the door to problematic developments.<p>This was one of the goals of Urbit[1]. Reduce complexity to increase freedom.<p>It’s true that Urbit uses esoteric naming for Hoon, but there are many contributors at this point and from what I understand, it is possible for one person to maintain the whole thing. Assuming that’s true, it’s an amazing accomplishment in simplification of software.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;urbit.org&#x2F;blog&#x2F;precepts-discussion&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;urbit.org&#x2F;blog&#x2F;precepts-discussion&#x2F;</a>')
