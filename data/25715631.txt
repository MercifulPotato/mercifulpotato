Item(by='pembrook', descendants=None, kids=[25716588, 25715665], score=None, time=1610292867, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25715497, text='Again, all options are a zero sum game. No value is created or destroyed (outside of trading fees), just transferred between parties.<p>I think you might be confused by the name &quot;binary options.&quot;<p>The word &quot;binary&quot; doesn&#x27;t refer to the fact that they are specifically zero sum. This doesn&#x27;t need to be called out, because again, all options and futures are zero sum.<p>The &quot;binary&quot; qualifier refers to the fact that they result in &quot;yes&#x2F;no&quot; outcome. Did the thing you bet on happen? Yes? You win. No? you lose everything.')
