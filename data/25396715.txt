Item(by='lifthrasiir', descendants=None, kids=[25396979, 25397062], score=None, time=1607761411, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25393181, text='I don&#x27;t think Python&#x27;s approach is particularly more elegant than JavaScript, as they both distinguish property-as-value and property-as-function. It&#x27;s just a matter of where to put property-as-function (and unlike Python, JavaScript can have non-configurable properties which can be optimized). As property-as-function can subsume property-as-value, no built-in property-as-value would be truly elegant (Ruby is close, but not exact).')
