Item(by='sneak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604354643, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24973174, text='Not everyone is perpetually stationary.  IP geolocation amounts to city-level coarse location, and accessing a service over time basically shares with them your travel habits: at which dates and times are you in which places?<p>Using a VPN is a good way to obscure that information.  Not every website you visit or app you use needs to know that you&#x27;re in city X on Monday and Tuesday evenings, and city Y the rest of the week.  That&#x27;s pretty sensitive stuff for a lot of people.')
