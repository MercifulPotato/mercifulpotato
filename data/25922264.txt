Item(by='bsimpson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611703551, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25922065, text='It&#x27;s the other edge of the monorepo&#x2F;standardized tooling sword.  You get nearly unlimited access to the work of people who have come before you, but separating your work from the internal commons is no small task.<p>I suspect most Google projects would be non-trivial to open source (even without legal concerns).  Building software with Google&#x27;s internal tools is very different than what&#x27;s popular in the open source world.<p>It cuts both ways: Many open source tools would be difficult to use at Google, because they presume open source tools and package managers that don&#x27;t have equivalents in the Google codebase.')
