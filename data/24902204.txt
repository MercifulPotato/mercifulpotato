Item(by='jeff-davis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603750879, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24897633, text='The nanoprocess concept seems like a big deal.<p>I remember reading about a vulnerability in the unix utility &quot;strings&quot;. The code is incredibly simple at first glance, but it had a dependency to detect the filetype, and that dependency was not safe on untrusted input.<p>At that moment I realized that unix security was fundamentally flawed. A utility that does nothing but read its input and write the output shouldn&#x27;t have permission to do anything else.')
