But really, it&#x27;s the &#x27;emotion&#x2F;novelty&#x27; of utility, not actual utility.<p>At least 1&#x2F;2 of the tech industry is driven by this, it&#x27;s hilarious. We HNers, so neurotically passionate about whatever it happens to be ... are the most guilty.<p>Of course a lot comes out of purely speculative and creative use of technology, it&#x27;s just that we should be better at discerning.<p>Slack, if it&#x27;s bad, is probably because it&#x27;s a noisy channel, not because it&#x27;s Salesforce.')
