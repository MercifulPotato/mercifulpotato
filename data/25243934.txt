Item(by='chrismorgan', descendants=None, kids=[25243965], score=None, time=1606640729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25243881, text='So long as the &lt;picture&gt; and &lt;source&gt; tags and the media attribute are whitelisted, you can do this:<p><pre><code>  &lt;picture&gt;\n    &lt;source src=screenshot-dark.png media=&quot;(prefers-color-scheme: dark)&quot;&gt;\n    &lt;img src=screenshot-light.png&gt;\n  &lt;&#x2F;picture&gt;\n</code></pre>\nAnd then if the site is doing dark mode toggling by some means other than `@media (prefers-color-scheme: dark)`, it can go through and rewrite the media queries in all user-generated content, e.g. to change that one to media=&quot;all&quot; in dark mode or media=&quot;not all&quot; in light mode.')
