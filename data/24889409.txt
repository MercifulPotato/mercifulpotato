Item(by='mattashii', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603656004, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24885193, text='Looking at the RFC, the grammar is not well-defined:<p>&#x27;opt-value&#x27; is specified as any amount of &#x27;pchar&#x27;[0], which includes both &#x27;&amp;&#x27; and &#x27;=&#x27; (the delimiters between opts, and between opt-key and opt-values, respectively).<p>So in order to parse a payto-link, you either go eager and have only one option in the string, or lazy and my valid opts &#x27;description=Food &amp; drinks&#x27; will error out on a missing value for the &#x27; drinks&#x27; authority-specific option, or go nuts and have parser-specific behaviour.<p>[0] pchar as defined in <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tools.ietf.org&#x2F;html&#x2F;rfc3986#appendix-A" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tools.ietf.org&#x2F;html&#x2F;rfc3986#appendix-A</a>')
