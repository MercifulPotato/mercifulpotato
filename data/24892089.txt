Item(by='geerlingguy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603677295, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24891633, text='Both is best, but typically a 6&quot; deep wall with offset 2x4 studs, insulation in the wall, resilient channel, 5&#x2F;8&quot; drywall (which is also fire resistant usually, with fiberglass in the core) and the right caulk and electrical install will stop all but very loud low rumbles.<p>The key is the installation and design of the system, though.
