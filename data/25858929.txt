Item(by='choeger', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611238186, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25858354, text='GDP is an <i>excellent</i> example, I think. Basically, it measures the total flow of wealth inside a country. If everyone behaves rationally this is a good proxy for wealth as a whole. But what happens if the government would setup a group of companies that trade immaterial or trivial products amongst each other at absurd prices? The faster that group trades, the higher the GDP. A metric has become a goal.')
