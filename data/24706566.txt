Item(by='bleepblorp', descendants=None, kids=[24706728, 24706717], score=None, time=1602063067, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24706064, text='Probably not possible.<p>Searchlights work by focusing light from a source to infinity. Physics dictates that small light sources focus better. Getting searchlight-level brightness from an LED source will require an LED array, which will be too big to focus effectively.<p>LEDs make excellent floodlights, but if you want reach, xenon discharge lamps are still the best option.')
