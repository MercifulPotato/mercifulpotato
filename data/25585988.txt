Item(by='chongli', descendants=None, kids=[25586925, 25586379], score=None, time=1609361743, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25585777, text='I&#x27;m not the GP, but if I had to guess: the negative externalities are reflected in all of the small, traditional retail businesses that have shrunk or failed outright as Amazon has grown to capture their markets. All the people who either owned or worked for those small businesses are now out of work and have to find some other way to earn a living. If they are not able to do so, then society suffers as a result.<p>Similar situations have happened in the past, such as when big grocery store chains drove mom-and-pop general stores out of business. It&#x27;s the price we pay for efficiency.')
