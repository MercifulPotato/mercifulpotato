Most\nCLI tools are not interactive, they just produce some output and\nthe user expects that output to be easy to parse and compatible\nwith as many terminals as possible.<p>You can easily output tables, bullet lists and many other things\njust with basic symbols supported everywhere. If your CLI\nprogram requires installing fontawesome or breaks in a terminal\nmultiplexer etc. I&#x27;m probably not going to use it.')
