Item(by='JamesBarney', descendants=None, kids=[25699635], score=None, time=1610171584, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25697028, text='Just a reminder that greater infectiousness is worse than greater lethality.<p>Say you have two variants, variant S-spreader and variant L-lethal.\nS kills 1 in 100 people and has a doubling time of 3.5 days\nL kills 2 in 100 people and has a doubling time of a week.<p>First week L kills twice as many people of S.  \nWeek 2 they kill the same number of people.\nWeek 3 S kills twice as many people as L\nWeek 4 S kills 4x as many people as L.')
