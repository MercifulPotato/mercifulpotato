Item(by='Joeri', descendants=None, kids=[25034436], score=None, time=1604922670, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25033604, text='What I find troubling is that we don’t own our goods in the digital space. When you “buy” a kindle book, you don’t own that book, you own a license to read that cannot be transferred or inherited. That means that parents no longer can pass on cultural artifacts to their children and grandchildren. This cannot be a healthy evolution for humanity.')
