Item(by='znpy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605696150, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25135192, text='Virtual addresses are a thing already.<p>It&#x27;s used by business to pretend to have an office in city X (expensive) while actually working in city Y (or in a completely different country). It can also be used to provide an address coming from a fashionable&#x2F;trendy part of the city.<p>There are similar services that offer re-mailing services. Basically you pay a fee and have a fund for postages&#x2F;stamps. Whenever mail arrives it gets re-packaged and re-mailed to your real address.<p>It&#x27;s basically proxying for real mail.<p>I used reship.com multiple times to appear to be located in Oregon and overcome &quot;we don&#x27;t ship to your location&quot; problems.')
