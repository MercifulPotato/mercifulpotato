Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611638948, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25910708, text='Satellites don&#x27;t land. Boosters do. They have lots of very high-spec mechanical parts--turbines and valves exposed to high-temperature, oscillating high-pressure, often oxidizing fluids--that must be inspected for damage and wear, so as not to cause explosion on the next flight. In addition, high-stress non-moving parts, e.g. nozzle neck, can erode under high-temperature matter flows.<p>Exploding rockets ruin their day, disappoint customers, and endanger any people onboard.')
