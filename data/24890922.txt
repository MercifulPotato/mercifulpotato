Item(by='my123', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603667126, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24889170, text='On Arm, the possible granule (base page size) sizes are 4KB, 16KB and 64KB.<p>On iOS and macOS on Apple Silicon, the used page size is 16KB across the board.<p>On RHEL&#x2F;CentOS on Arm64, the used page size is 64KB.<p>In other cases, it&#x27;s generally 4KB (and is always 4KB on Android or Windows&#x2F;arm64).')
