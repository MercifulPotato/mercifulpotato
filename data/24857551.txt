Item(by='fyfy18', descendants=None, kids=[24857628], score=None, time=1603371895, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24856559, text='What you are talking about is OpenID Connect, not OAuth. It does use OAuth 2.0 under the hood, but they are two separate protocols.<p>OAuth is an authorization protocol. It can be used (for example) to give Facebook access to your Flickr photos without having to give out your Flickr username and password to Facebook or share API tokens, and have a standardized way to revoke access when you realise Facebook scraped all your private photos.')
