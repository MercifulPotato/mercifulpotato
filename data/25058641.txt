Item(by='ThePadawan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605104819, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25050348, text='I recently learned that the German polls also rely on random calls - but worse than that, they only use the publicly registered phone numbers, i.e. the ones in the phone book.<p>That list mostly includes landlines, and biases towards older landlines as well (since IIRC phone book registration switched from opt-out to opt-in at some point).<p>So the polls strongly overemphasize older voters (because no one else has a landline phone anymore).')
