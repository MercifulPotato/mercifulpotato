Item(by='twic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603905744, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24921281, text='Your city is very spread out because everyone has a car. This is why this is such a difficult problem - transport networks, whether road or rail - are a fundamental part of the anatomy of a city. You can&#x27;t simply take a city which grew up around driving and replace that with taking the train, any more that you can transplant a crab&#x27;s exoskeleton into a rabbit.<p>Nonetheless, our cities must eventually become crabs. So we need to have a plan for how we&#x27;re going to make that transformation gradually, as the cities grow and evolve.')
