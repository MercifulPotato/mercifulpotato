Item(by='GhostVII', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603166756, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827476, text='Whenever I read a sentence with a period in my head, I read it in a very flat tone, since the period explicitly excludes any exclamation point, question mark, emoji, etc. So that makes it sound weird when someone uses a period in casual conversation, because if I was talking to them on person they would usually have some kind of emotion.<p>For example, I would read &quot;Sounds good&quot; in a generally positive way just like when someone is saying that in person, but when I read &quot;Sounds good.&quot; I read it without any emotion in my head which sounds wierd.')
