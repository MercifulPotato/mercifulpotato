Item(by='mandelbrotwurst', descendants=None, kids=[24703157], score=None, time=1602004647, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24698691, text='I think about this in terms of how much clock time has elapsed versus how much working time I spend on something - i.e. I am very often able to trade an increase in clock time elapsed (taking more breaks) for a decrease in working time, and vice versa.<p>As with most things, this comes with diminishing returns as you push toward minimizing one over the other.<p>Sometimes, even negative returns, e.g. because of increased context switching costs, or confusion and general malaise caused by staring at something for too long.')
