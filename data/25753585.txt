Item(by='SassyGrapefruit', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610485281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25752804, text='They are materially different. A pardon reflects forgiveness and seeks to redress civil disability typically as a result of a systemic injustice.<p>A commutation is a lessening of the penalty. It implies that the act was wrong and the sentence was deserved but perhaps it was heavy handed. There is no implication of innocence in a commutation.')
