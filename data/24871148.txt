Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=[24871886, 24871295, 24871870], score=None, time=1603470309, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24871100, text='You&#x27;re not thinking like a pollster. Florida is currently projected to be Biden&#x27;s win. So what happens if Trump wins Florida?<p>It means the polls were wrong. Which means, you&#x27;d have to reconsider the state-polls in all the other states with that new information: that the country is slightly redder than polls today suggest.<p>If Trump wins Florida, then there&#x27;s a &quot;hidden vote&quot;, so to speak, that&#x27;s pushing Trump&#x27;s victory. Once you add that hypothetical hidden vote to the rest of the states, it becomes a closer race.<p>---------<p>The question is whether or not you think there&#x27;s a hidden vote for Trump. For all we know, there&#x27;s a hidden vote for Biden this year: but we won&#x27;t know that until the polls close and the votes are tallied up.<p>In the absence of knowledge, the best we can do is find indications of cross-correlations and model those.')
