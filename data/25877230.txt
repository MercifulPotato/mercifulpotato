Item(by='brundolf', descendants=None, kids=[25878650, 25885479], score=None, time=1611355457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25876554, text='The only solution I think is to not engage. Focus on long-lived relationships with people who actually have gotten&#x2F;will get to know you. Save your complex and nuanced thoughts and feelings for those people; random strangers will always see you through a cartoonized lens. When you do have micro-interactions with strangers - which, never forget, includes all public social media posts whether you want it to or not - keep things inoffensive. If someone else inflames the situation, quietly and diplomatically withdraw from it.<p>Beyond just being a strategy for individuals, doing this contributes in a tiny way to lowering the temperature of the whole room.')
