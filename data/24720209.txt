Item(by='tptacek', descendants=None, kids=[24723028], score=None, time=1602171908, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24720100, text='The difference between {Rust, Go} and Ruby is almost entirely about rigor.<p>The difference between Rust and Go, on the other hand, is mostly pragmatic: Rust is &quot;strict&quot; about design and implementation details Go&#x27;s runtime handles for the programmer; there&#x27;s nothing for Go to be &quot;strict&quot; about.<p>Rust is more rigorous than Go, but to a much smaller degree than Go is over Ruby.')
