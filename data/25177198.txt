Prefix is increment pre (increment before getting the value). Postfix is increment post (increment after getting the value). Both have side effects as does x+=1.<p>consider the call f(x+=1,x+=1) when x=5; what is the call? f(6,7) or f(7,6)? Iirc even f(6,6) and f(7,7) were valid behavior in the past.')
