The round was led by Lightspeed, with support from Diagram Ventures and strategic angel investors including Zachary Perret, CEO of Plaid; Max Levchin, founder and general partner at SciFi VC; Alexa Von Tobel, founder of LearnVest, and Inspired Capital; Henry Ward, CEO at Carta; and more. The platform is simple: Synctera connects community banks and FinTechs, making an otherwise complicated partnership easier and more streamlined.
