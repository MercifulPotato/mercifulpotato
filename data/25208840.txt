Item(by='xadhominemx', descendants=None, kids=[25214587], score=None, time=1606309925, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25208658, text='Putting the CPU and RAM on the same die is absolutely not the future. Not only is it impossible to mix and match process technologies in that way, it would be extremely wasteful given the far greater numbers of metal layers on a CPU and the performance improvement would be marginal. The same is even more true with integrating CPU and NAND flash.')
