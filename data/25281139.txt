This made it really easy and expected to ask for more feedback and to have users give it proactively.<p>Community channels are key as you grow. Frankly this is an area where we&#x27;ve underinvested as we&#x27;ve been heads down building&#x2F;shipping product and is going to be a huge area of focus for us in 2021.
