It&#x27;s relying on everyone to obey the two design rules that: 1) only the writing side should close the channel and 2) only one side should write.<p>In this sense, it&#x27;s as safe as mutexes: everything works as long as everyone (including any 3rd party library) does exactly what they&#x27;re supposed to. When compared to what modern and robust concurrent programming looks like (e..g Erlang), it seems like a compromise to me')
