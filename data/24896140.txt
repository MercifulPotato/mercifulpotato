Item(by='dathinab', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603720382, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24895700, text='Given that cache hits only work with a specific URL the results are in practice anything between pointless to only slightly good (with maybe one or two exceptions).<p>I mean to have a cache hit you need:<p>- Same CDN<p>- Same library<p>- Same library uploader&#x2F;name<p>- Exact Same library version down to every byte of js<p>- Exact same way to refer to given version (e.g. if latest is 1.3.2 then foobar-1.3.2 and foobar-latest are <i>not</i> the same, except if foobar-lastest is a temporary redirect to foobar-1.3.2. But that would induce a further round trip).<p>If we furthermore consider that most people most times visit a small number of domains it&#x27;s not to hard to reason that the value gained from caching doesn&#x27;t outweigh the cost for the majority of users.')
