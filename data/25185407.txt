Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606128158, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25185352, text='The main idea behind it is that adding 19 to a number is the same as adding 20, then subtracting one.<p>So, most of the time, it adds 2 to the ‘tens’ column, and subtracts 1 from the units column. Twice that -1 plus that 2 equals zero, which is divisible by 19.<p>When it doesn’t, it adds 1 to the tens column and 9 to the ones column. Twice that 9 plus that 1 equals 19, which also is divisible by 19.')
