Item(by='ashtonkem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608660058, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25504771, text='If you want really good sound quality, you should be using separate audio gear. No on-camera mic is going to perform well due to various cost and physics based limitations. Look to what podcasters use, and buy that for the audio half. You can put a better mic into a webcam, but it&#x27;ll always have background noise issues compared to a mic that&#x27;s closer to your mouth.')
