Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=[25564227], score=None, time=1609193756, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25563575, text='The term &quot;red flag&quot; usually refers to a dealbreaker, like if the company tells you to expect working 7 days a week or that you need to answer e-mails urgently on weekends and holidays for no good reason.<p>I wouldn&#x27;t rush to dismiss a company just because they let the HR person spruce up the job description with some boilerplate. You really need to talk to the team you&#x27;ll be working with and the future manager you&#x27;ll be working under.<p>Unfortunately, it&#x27;s just not possible to tell what a job will be like by reading a job description. You have to talk to the teams.')
