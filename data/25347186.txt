Item(by='phlakaton', descendants=None, kids=[25347653], score=None, time=1607444049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25346479, text='Avant-garde does not precisely mean “unlistenable.” There’s plenty of music that’s listenable but still way “out there.”<p>Glass’s music is maybe not consistently on the edge, but his avant-garde reputation comes from works like the “opera” Einstein On the Beach, which is a formidable listening and performing experience, even if it’s heavily diatonic and based on familiar major&#x2F;minor modalities. It is a sui generis approach to musical structure and musical theater.')
