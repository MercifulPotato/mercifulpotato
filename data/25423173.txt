Item(by='bird_monster', descendants=None, kids=[25423610, 25423808], score=None, time=1607979388, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25423108, text='I have moved into thinking that mocks don&#x27;t really have much value. Testing real things is the only way to verify real things.<p>If you can, set up a real DB, cache, whatever, and run your services in tests with real instances of their dependencies. Obviously this falls through for external APIs and things that are very expensive, but your savings in &quot;But the tests passed&quot; confusion time will be huge.')
