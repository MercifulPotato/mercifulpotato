Item(by='bob1029', descendants=None, kids=[24912602], score=None, time=1603836574, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24908105, text='The best I have been able to achieve is a solution which initially requires fully-custom code, and then evolves into something where the most maintenance-heavy parts are data-driven and managed via a web interface by the business.<p>Trying to make 100% of your solution no-code is a fool&#x27;s errand at best, and a tragic whale hunt on average. Especially, if you set out to achieve a no-code solution from day 1.<p>There are certainly trivial problem domains where no-code solutions are good answers (e.g. IFTTT). But, there isn&#x27;t much money or glory in solving trivial problems.')
