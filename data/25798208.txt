Item(by='sobeston', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610754401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25797965, text='The concept of the heap is in the userspace of the language. This terminology is confusing to someone thinking of usermode&#x2F;kernelmode - this is instead talking of userspace versus language-space. As in, users implement code to reason about heap memory; the language does not know or care what heap is.<p>At a language level, zig does not have a &quot;new&quot; or a &quot;malloc&quot; or anything of the sort.')
