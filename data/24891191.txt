Item(by='nine_k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603669035, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24885053, text='&gt; <i>only gets developed and supported if customers pay for it</i><p>Not exactly. A feature gets developed if it brings in more money from customers, but not always because the customers are happy to pay.<p>Consider putting ads to the software.<p>Consider turning software to a subscription service.<p>Consider preventing other software from interoperating with yours without a license, or at all, and locking users down on your platform.<p>I agree that when a user votes with their money, it is a great incentive. But the way it currently works with commercial \nsoftware is a bit far from a meritocratic ideal. Other mechanisms are possible (see sponsorship, Kickstarter, bounties), and I hope they will grow in popularity.')
