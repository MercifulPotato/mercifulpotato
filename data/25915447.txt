Punctuation is usually a hassle on mobile, as is capitalization (when not done automatically). Accuracy is not top priority in real time chatting since the reader can quickly ask something they didn&#x27;t understand. Regarding this last point I often find myself using proper grammar when writing a message that I don&#x27;t expect the reader to read immediately, or if I&#x27;m initiating a conversation, then switch to a more informal tone.')
