Item(by='fpoling', descendants=None, kids=[25887157], score=None, time=1611438064, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25886180, text='If one only allows container to run as a non-root user (no user namespace either) with all privileges dropped with strong mount isolation and some form of syscall filtering, then the attack surface is similar to that of hypervisors if not smaller while the performance is significantly better.<p>But yes, quite a few services assumes they have root privileges and do not work as is in such containers, like recent OpenSSH. For those cases VM isolation makes for much smaller attack surface.')
