Item(by='anticristi', descendants=None, kids=[25926131], score=None, time=1611682097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25916442, text='You don&#x27;t always need consent. There are 6 legal reasons to collect data. &quot;Public interest&quot; as with tax returns is one.<p>As another example, the post office is allowed to collect and process your address, since otherwise they could not fulfill their contract to deliver your parcel (contractual obligations).<p>Similarly, paramedics don&#x27;t need to worry about asking for consent from a patient that is unconscious: They can look into their medical records based on &quot;vital interests&quot;.')
