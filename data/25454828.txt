Item(by='Johnythree', descendants=None, kids=[25455058], score=None, time=1608206770, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25454521, text='Faraday cages are a bit of a myth. Unless the metal sheet is continuous with no gaps, and there are no wires passing through the sheet, they will be ineffective.<p>It means that all joints must be screwed or soldered, all doors to have tight joints, and low-pass filters fitted to all power leads, comms leads, etc.<p>And often you need two completely independent layers (connected at only one point) to achieve sufficient attenuation.')
