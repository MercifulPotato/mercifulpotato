Item(by='deathanatos', descendants=None, kids=[25205639], score=None, time=1606270735, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25204981, text='UI toolkits include &quot;indeterminate&quot; progress bars. Back before AJAX spinners (really, a simplified form of indeterminate progress bars) took over, they were a blob that bounced back and forth, or scrolled left to right repeatedly, within the progress bar&#x27;s trough. Sometimes, a barber pole. The good ones would only advance the animation so long as actual progress was occurring, so that a hung system was indicated by a hung animation.<p>There&#x27;s no reason to lie.')
