Item(by='sanedigital', descendants=None, kids=[25218993], score=None, time=1606385836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25218380, text='It&#x27;s super trendy right now.<p>Especially on LinkedIn.<p>These single sentence paragraphs.<p>Provocative.<p>At least that&#x27;s what they think they are.<p>I find them annoying.<p>Trite.<p>Overdone.<p>In all seriousness: this article reads more like an outline than an actual piece of writing. Good writing is all about pacing. Some of your sentences should be long and winding. While others are short.<p>That variance in rhythm keeps your reader involved in the piece. It&#x27;s not necessary to convey a point-information is just as easily digested in bullet-point form-but it&#x27;s necessary if you want to maintain your audience&#x27;s attention over time.')
