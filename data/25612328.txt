Item(by='pornel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609590246, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25611376, text='The missing information here is that lifetimes <i>don&#x27;t apply at all</i> to types that don&#x27;t contain any borrowed references (such as `i32`, or self-contained `String`).<p>So `T: &#x27;static` doesn&#x27;t require types to live for the entire duration of the program. It requires borrows <i>if there are any</i> to be valid for that long. If there are no borrows involved, then &#x27;static is ignored.<p>In practice `T: &#x27;static` should be understood as &quot;all temporary references are forbidden here&quot;.')
