Modern high-speed trains are more than twice as fast as the fastest WWII-era trains.<p>* The American Morning Hiawatha ran at a top speed of 160 km&#x2F;h in 1939.<p>* The Japanese Shinkansen ran at a top speed of 210 km&#x2F;h in 1961.<p>* The French TGV reached 300 km&#x2F;h in 1995.<p>* The Chinese Fuxing trains have been running commercial service at up to 350 km&#x2F;h since 2018.')
