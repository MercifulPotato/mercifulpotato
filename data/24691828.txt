Meanwhile most other popular languages will happily wrap your fixed-size integers around on overflow, or silently perform integer division with truncation when you don&#x27;t append a decimal fraction part manually to any of the numbers.<p>Point being, all tools come with strange caveats that one needs to be familiar with.
