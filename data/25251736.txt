Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606724241, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25251664, text='The thing is it&#x27;s hard to measure the inputs and outputs of your body. And with the same inputs, different bodies will produce different outputs.<p>The thing about scientific studies is they&#x27;re generally looking for something that works for everyone. If a small number of people in a small study have good results from a fad diet, and they can stick to it, that&#x27;s good for them, but the study is going to show no significant difference between control and test.<p>The key is finding something you can stick to, has good results <i>for you</i>, and doesn&#x27;t cause other problems. For a lot of people, that&#x27;s easy, and for some people it&#x27;s hard.')
