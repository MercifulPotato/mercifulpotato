It is hyperbole to argue otherwise. Yes, many forms of speech are distasteful, or downright grotesque (hate speech, for example), but it is absolutely not the same as an act of terrorism.<p>[The idea that the government may restrict] speech expressing ideas that offend … strikes at the heart of the First Amendment.
