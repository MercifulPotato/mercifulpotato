Item(by='dleslie', descendants=None, kids=[24751897, 24751966], score=None, time=1602471378, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24750565, text='The most important thing about Unity is to know that the Editor is not the same as the Client.<p>Assets load differently, in surprising ways. Materials may work fine in Editor but not in Client.<p>Code executes differently. Timings are different, the Editor shares managed Heap with the Client, and so garbage collection is different.<p>And so on.<p>Rule 0 with Unity: _do not rely on the Editor for testing_.')
