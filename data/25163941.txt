Item(by='bitwize', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605900564, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25159704, text='RISC typically means &quot;load-store architecture&quot;: load operands from memory to regs, perform operations in regs only, store results back to memory.<p>CISC refers to old-school, programmer-friendly, addressing-mode-laden ISAs. Add  D0 to the address pointed to by A0 plus an immediate offset and store the result back to memory, that sort of thing.')
