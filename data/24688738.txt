These posts have led to two public radio stations in Seattle terminating his segments (KUOW and KNKX).<p>He obviously has the right to these kinds of posts, but his skill in delivering these largely poorly-researched messages to a general public audience is incredibly amateurish, especially given the trust he&#x27;s garnered as a scientist.<p>As a scientist myself, I take particular issue with the way he presents topics regarding climate change. These posts are picked up by climate deniers and suddenly &quot;UW Scientist Says Climate Change is a Hoax!&quot;.
