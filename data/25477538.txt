Item(by='andor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608387613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25475942, text='It&#x27;s trivial to observe runtime behavior with a debugger.<p>If you use constructor injection as it is recommended, just add a breakpoint in the constructor and start the app in the debugger.<p><pre><code>  @Component\n  public class Foo {\n    public Foo(Bar bar) {\n      &#x2F;&#x2F; breakpoint here\n    }\n  }\n</code></pre>\nEdit:<p>In most cases it should be obvious what is getting injected, e.g. in the example Bar is another component&#x2F;bean that was manually created. If you have two beans of the same type, use a qualifier to specify which one you want.<p>If you are having issues with unexpected things being injected, I&#x27;d recommend setting up a few tests that establish that your app is properly wired up.')
