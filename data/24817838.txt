Item(by='lllr_finger', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603028814, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24817396, text='As a follow-up, if you&#x27;re wondering why, 500mb is roughly 5700m&#x2F;18kft above ground level in the US - not quite jet stream level (200-300mb) - but high enough to give insight into large scale patterns that drive surface patterns.<p>Where surface features are more localized and near-term, 500mb analysis gives you insight into whether there is broad zonal flow, a deepening upper trough, and all sorts of important information about how things on a larger scale can play out in the longer term. They&#x27;re sometimes called &quot;steering winds&quot;, because they&#x27;re the primary component that dictates direction and speed of storms.')
