Item(by='mslot', descendants=None, kids=[25405360, 25405510], score=None, time=1607849720, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25402430, text='The important thing to remember is to use COPY .. FROM STDIN, not insert, for bulk loading into PostgreSQL. Most PostgreSQL drivers support COPY from the client, though it&#x27;s not always available through generic database APIs.<p>COPY commands typically write hundreds of thousands of rows per second on a large server&#x2F;cluster. It&#x27;s useful to write over multiple connections, but rarely more than ~16.')
