Item(by='bipson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604926980, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25034202, text='Maybe there is a slight semantic misunderstanding here, but in fact a &quot;re-infection&quot; is defined as such:<p>A patient must present a infection and the particular virus must be <i>sequenced</i>, and then present with an infection later on where the virus is again <i>sequenced</i> and identified as sufficiently different from the first sample. Otherwise it cannot be deemed a re-infection.<p>If these small differences in the genomic sequence are already a different strand, I don&#x27;t know, not an expert.\nBut all 5 (?) cases known so far had a different sequence identified the second time.')
