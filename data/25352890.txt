Item(by='lrossi', descendants=None, kids=[25352965], score=None, time=1607469284, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25347915, text='I don’t care much about location, but I would use a notification service that is provably end-to-end encrypted. Possible use cases:<p>* Long build finishes on my PC. Send me a message on the phone.<p>* Log watcher detects an issue on a server. Send me a message.<p>* CI job fails. Same.<p>* Some notification pops up in my laptop’s browser. Forward it to the phone.<p>Last time I looked at apps doing this, they either did not appear particularly trustworthy, or were too limited.')
