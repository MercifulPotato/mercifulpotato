Item(by='einpoklum', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606493975, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25226464, text='&gt; Do we really need leaders?<p>Well, the thing is that commercial companies - which are owned by a party other than their employees [1] - &quot;need&quot; agents of owners&#x27; control. Whether they &quot;lead&quot; or not is not the main point. The point is that they answer directly to the owners, and through this they have power and control. Without them, the workers would &quot;do what they want&quot; - which may be a good thing or a bad thing, but it won&#x27;t be what the owners decide.<p>So, in that sense, &quot;we&quot; &quot;need&quot; &quot;leaders&quot;, yes.<p>[1] - If workers also own company stock that doesn&#x27;t changed the structural nature of the ownership relations.')
