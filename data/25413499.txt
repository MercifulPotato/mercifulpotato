Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607919579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25412671, text='&gt; You may not realize this, but when someone says &quot;I am reasonably certain that X,&quot; they are not, in fact, generally asserting their certainty. They are, in fact, asserting X to be true.<p>No, they are claiming that they have a belief (implicitly, often, a <i>justified</i> belief) that X has a high probability of being true, unlike stating merely “X” which denotatively is a claim that X is true without qualification.')
