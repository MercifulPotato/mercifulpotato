Item(by='omginternets', descendants=None, kids=[24926111], score=None, time=1603933665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24925897, text='Facilitation is literally a mediator or moderator in an effect. It is not a <i>root</i> cause, but it is part of the causal chain. As such, acting upon it can reduce the effect.<p>Simply put: an effective crackdown on guns, cars, snowballs, scissors, and Bitcoin can absolutely reduce the associated downsides. The question isn’t “is it effective”.  The question is “is it worth it”.')
