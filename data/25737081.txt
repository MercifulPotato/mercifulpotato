Item(by='chipsa', descendants=None, kids=[25744054], score=None, time=1610399530, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25722290, text='Homemade soap is soap. The only way to make soap is the saponification process. Which involves lye. The oil used can vary, but a strong alkali is required, and any strong alkali you use is going to do the saponification process on your seasoning.<p>Commercial dish soap can be soap, or it could be detergent. Detergent is made differently, and doesn&#x27;t necessarily involve lye.')
