Item(by='Retric', descendants=None, kids=[25811792], score=None, time=1610898275, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25811473, text='USB-C can’t charge nearly as quickly as a high power proprietary charger.  In use USB-C needs to supply power for both charging and operating the laptop, so what might take 3 hours to charge with USB-C might take 20 minutes to charge with a dedicated connector.<p>That’s not to say every laptop’s proprietary charger will supply noticeably more than 100W, but many examples exist.')
