It&#x27;s still defamatory, and that&#x27;s how US law treats such statements (and how it has <i>always</i> treated defamatory statements).<p><i>Truth</i> is the defense to a defamation lawsuit, not &quot;a little bird told me.&quot;<p>TLDR: unless you actually know that Bob is a pedo because you witnessed his crimes or he has been convicted, don&#x27;t defame Bob.')
