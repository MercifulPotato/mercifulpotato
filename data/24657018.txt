Item(by='shard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601592891, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24654402, text='I&#x27;ve always had the thought that religious texts should be updated with modern prose and sensibilities. Not for the believers of the religion, but for the non-believers. People are not incentivized to slog through some text that was written centuries ago, using phrasings that they&#x27;ve hardly ever encountered. But if a modern twist was put on it, perhaps it would be better accepted as a good yarn by the general population, and widen the readership.')
