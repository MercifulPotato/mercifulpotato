Item(by='dilatedmind', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607880361, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403448, text='The best use case I&#x27;ve had for partitioning tables is when there is a lot of churn that would otherwise result in significant vacuuming. For example, a table holding the past 7 days of data would require deleting 1&#x2F;7 of its rows every day.<p>Swapping out a child table and dropping the old one is free in that sense, but does require an exclusive lock which can cause issues if you have long running queries.')
