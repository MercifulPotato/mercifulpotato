Item(by='radford-neal', descendants=None, kids=[25170570, 25173079, 25173780], score=None, time=1605975170, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170396, text='No, frequentist and Bayesian statistics are not equivalent.<p>There are some special cases in which a frequentist 95% confidence interval and a Bayesian 95% credible interval based on some sort of default prior are numerically the same, but that doesn&#x27;t happen in general.<p>Statisticians would hardly have been vigorously debating the issue for two centuries if it didn&#x27;t really matter.')
