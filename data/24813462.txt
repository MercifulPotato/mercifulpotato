Item(by='klelatti', descendants=None, kids=[24816225], score=None, time=1602969487, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24813346, text='When RISC first appeared they really were simpler than competing designs.<p>But today I think it&#x27;s hard to argue that modern pipelined, out of order processors with hundreds of millions of transistors are in any sense &#x27;simple&#x27;.<p>If there is a general lesson to be learned it&#x27;s that the processor is often best placed to optimise on the fly rather than have the compiler try to do it (VLIW) or trying to fit a complex ISA to match the high level language you&#x27;re running.')
