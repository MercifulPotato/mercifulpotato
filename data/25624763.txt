Item(by='LASR', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609704471, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25624631, text='The difference is in the programming model. The library itself is probably quite easy to support both models.<p>If you have some UI in retained mode, chances are that rendering it live every frame will lead to unacceptable performance.<p>But if you have UI in immediate mode, you can probably switch to retained - essentially lazy rendering until something else happens. But you&#x27;ll have to be very careful that your render calls are indeed pure functions, and cannot produce different outputs unless some input has indeed changed.')
