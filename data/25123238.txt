Item(by='kweks', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605618204, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25122816, text='There is a nice counter-balance to the &quot;I&#x27;ve got nothing to hide&quot; idiom, which is &quot;I&#x27;ve got nothing to hide, but I&#x27;ve got nothing to share&quot;.<p>Changing the focus from &quot;hiding&quot;, which insinuates underhandedness or immorality, to &quot;sharing&quot; - often allows the conversation to extend to &quot;What would you be comfortable sharing with (the government | the police | your neighbors) etc.<p>In my experience, this angle helps people grasp more quickly that they&#x27;d be uncomfortable sharing many things with most people.')
