Item(by='bonestamp2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607221911, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25319893, text='A lot of automakers seem to be making electric cars because they have to instead of embracing it as a gift. I understand it&#x27;s more expensive (batteries) and there is potentially lower margin, but it&#x27;s also an opportunity to create a second tier in a strong brand (pricing, experience, etc), or re-invent a failing brand (new dealers, new design language, etc).<p>For companies like Aston Martin, the sound of the engine&#x2F;exhaust are a large and visceral part of the experience. But again, this is an opportunity to create something different. Audi took the opportunity to create a really cool sound for their e-tron:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;MUcbXplAvSc?t=74" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;MUcbXplAvSc?t=74</a><p>Admittedly, it doesn&#x27;t sound that great in the video, but I heard one drive past me as I was walking and it sounded very natural, like it was the noise the motors were making. It&#x27;s not annoyingly loud, it&#x27;s very subtle, and it really adds the same thing a great exhaust note can.')
