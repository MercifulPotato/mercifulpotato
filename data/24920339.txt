Item(by='megous', descendants=None, kids=[24921014, 24920379, 24920442], score=None, time=1603900823, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24918927, text='Something I read in the RFC for email message format (or maybe elsewhere, but I have it associated with email for some reason) is: to be strict in output that you produce, and lenient in the input you accept.<p>I think it makes some sense for data formats, if you want max compatibility of independently developed software that processes the data.
