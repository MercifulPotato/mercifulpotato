Item(by='lovehashbrowns', descendants=None, kids=[25329704], score=None, time=1607317161, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25329356, text='There are so many things. First, the network effect. Going from an established platform to a new one means you lose a lot of viewers that aren&#x27;t going to switch and the potential to gain new ones since the other platform is new.<p>Secondly, well-established payment methods such as Twitch Prime which provide a lot of revenue for streamers on Twitch.<p>Third, existing contracts with Twitch.<p>Fourth, streaming platforms are incredibly expensive to create, maintain, and promote. They will not be profitable for a long, long time, on top of requiring mountains of cash to start.<p>And finally, users &quot;get used&quot; to a specific platform, e.g. Twitch chat, and it&#x27;s difficult to break these emotional attachments.')
