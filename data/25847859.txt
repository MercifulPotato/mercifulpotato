This is the same reason that stocks get halted for impending news intraday, and earnings reports are released outside of regular trading hours (except perhaps for ADRs and other multi-market securities). Scheduled injections of information make it easier for market participants of varying sizes and geographies to receive and digest information at parity with one another.')
