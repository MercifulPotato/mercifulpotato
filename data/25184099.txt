Item(by='sirtaj', descendants=None, kids=[25189734, 25184567, 25186200], score=None, time=1606112850, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25184024, text='&gt; Society is best when people think about how their worst actions effect other people.<p>In our day to day lives, I use this as a basic character test of both individuals and societies. People fail this inductive-thinking test - &quot;If everyone did what I am about to do, would I like to live in the society that would result?&quot; - all the time. Signs of failure range from lack of civic sense (litter, rash driving etc) all the way to climate change and social collapse.')
