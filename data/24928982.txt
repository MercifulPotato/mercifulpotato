All modern gyros have a prerotator - without one you&#x27;d need a very long take off roll to get the rotor up to speed. The prerotator is <i>definitely</i> not capable of flight though, it&#x27;s only good to get the rotor up to about 2&#x2F;3 the speed needed to take off.')
