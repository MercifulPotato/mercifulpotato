They show that it isn&#x27;t a surprise that we are alone in the universe if we consider the uncertainty inherent to parameters of the Drake equation. Their analysis estimate that there is (at least) a 39% chance that we are alone in the observable universe.<p>[0]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arxiv.org&#x2F;abs&#x2F;1806.02404" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arxiv.org&#x2F;abs&#x2F;1806.02404</a>')
