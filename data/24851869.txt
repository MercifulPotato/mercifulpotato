Item(by='wenc', descendants=None, kids=[24860250, 24852626], score=None, time=1603312494, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24847752, text='Comma or dot separators caught me out once.<p>In the Anglo-Saxon world, we write 3,000.00.<p>In many continental European countries and countries like Brazil, the same number is written 3.000,00.<p>I once generated a CSV in my program which could not be read correctly in Excel in Brazil. I later figured out that I had to use another delimiter than a comma, because the comma was the decimal separator.')
