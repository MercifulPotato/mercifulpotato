Properly sized steppers have more than enough margin for rapid movement on hobby machines without losing steps ( Video example at 1000ipm: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.instagram.com&#x2F;p&#x2F;B1wSmXfnm6C&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.instagram.com&#x2F;p&#x2F;B1wSmXfnm6C&#x2F;</a> ).<p>Skipped steps are only a problem if the machine is tuned wrong, like you said, or the steppers are too undersized to keep up.<p>People tend to underestimate the strength of common NEMA23 steppers while overestimating the cutting forces they need. Most hobby machines don&#x27;t have enough rigidity or spindle power to require more than a few pounds of lateral cutting force.')
