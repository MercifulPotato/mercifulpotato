Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609356955, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25563625, text='There’s a lot you get for free from things like SwiftUI, such as accessibility, support for right to left locales, portability all the way from the Apple Watch to the TV, etc.<p>I am also currently writing an app using SDL on Linux, which I have previously written in SwiftUI and it’s a lot of fun, but it’s just not comparable.<p>My SwiftUI version runs on the watch, the iPhone the TV and even in widgets with barely any modification. (And with much better fonts!)<p>The Linux version is fun, but it’s just not comparable in terms of the work the platform is doing for me.')
