It usually goes &quot;Yada yada presentation but bigger picture (usually about how the fate of the world is at stake and only the hero can save it)&quot;.<p>It&#x27;s fine, that&#x27;s what I expect from a fantasy book, but what&#x27;s missing is what&#x27;s your USP, why should I read your book instead of the countless others.
