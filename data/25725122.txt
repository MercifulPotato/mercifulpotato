You can look back on projects you’ve done or projects you’re working on and try to see how they fit with your work.<p>2. Trim the list down and add the remaining items to a list or documented ordered by what you work on. Keep the remaining items in a handy place you can reference when you’re in appropriate steps of your project.<p>For example, the  rules in the article can possibly be grouped as follows: 1. Project timelines, 2. project accountabilities, 3. Engineering requirements and design. I would then refer to these in the corresponding phase of a project.<p>It would be nice if there was a store attached to each law. Knowing the source of the law would be helpful in knowing how to apply it.')
