Item(by='ratherbefuddled', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606323990, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25210423, text='I think there were lots of good reasons to have more layers of abstraction between apps and OSes - portability, security, ease of deployment to name the obvious ones.<p>Browsers were the wrong thing to target but they were the only thing getting even vaguely standardised across the proliferation of devices and OSes.<p>The problem I think was the pace.  Nobody really had a chance to say &quot;hang on folks, if we&#x27;re going to do lots of this we need to talk about javascript and the DOM&quot;.  Now we&#x27;re stuck with it for good.<p>At this point the best we can hope for is to put a better layer on top and treat it as machine code.')
