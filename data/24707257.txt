You would have been able to reach your savings goal without living <i>so</i> frugally. Your employer at the time was comfortable paying you a relatively small amount because you couldn’t go anywhere else.<p>They’re not asking immigrants to live more lavishly. They’re asking for immigrants to be paid more so there’s less downward pressure on wages.')
