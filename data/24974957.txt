Item(by='inopinatus', descendants=None, kids=[24975568], score=None, time=1604362414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972783, text='Indeed, but this remains a nominal explanation. It validates the possibility of one hyphen placement but does not explain why any of the equally available alternatives are not chosen. Why not ZK-S-N-ARK, and other combinations besides? Heck, what kind of diseased mind thinks hyphens even belong in initialisms and acronyms? Don’t even get me started on the use of arbitary case variants (“zk-SNARK”).<p>My thesis being, justifying any one particular placement of a hyphen is a <i>necessary</i> but not <i>sufficient</i> part of an adequate explanation.  Just as Alice was vexed by Humpty Dumpty.')
