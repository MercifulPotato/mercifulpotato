Item(by='zozbot234', descendants=None, kids=[25247460, 25246868], score=None, time=1606670502, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25245206, text='One unique feature of the Amiga that I&#x27;ve not seen referenced here is that programs, scripts etc. could reference removable media by label and if the media was not in the drive when needed, a Kickstart-managed requester would pop up asking for it to be inserted promptly.  This made it very easy to manage even a single-floppy-drive system. Linux could support this even now, as all the required pieces are there (e.g. automount support) and it would be quite useful for a number of things; however it doesn&#x27;t, AFAICT.')
