And those dynamic components are somehow essential to the content of the email? A receipt, for example.<p>There&#x27;s some precedent for this kind of regulation. For example, the government requires medicare advantage insurance carriers to send <i>physical mail</i> to insured members under many circumstances, regardless of the member&#x27;s digital-accessibility constraints or lack thereof.')
