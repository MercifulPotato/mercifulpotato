Though that finding resembled a positivity bias, this interpretation was ruled out by trials that demonstrated participants could also learn from negative outcomes.</i>&quot;<p>So, a positivity bias implies that one <i>cannot</i> learn from negative outcomes?<p>&quot;<i>But there was more to it.
