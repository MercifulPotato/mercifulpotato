US citizens take the First Amendment as a virtue without a lot of exploration into its origins or practical utility.<p>It has a LOT of practical utility... But it&#x27;s really important, I think, to know what that utility is and what its limits are.<p>And the limits exist and are quite noteworthy. Restricting someone from saying something defamatory is legally challenging relative to, say, the UK. But repeat someone&#x27;s story verbatim and claim it as your own? Oops, you violated copyright, and no amount of bleating about your First Amendment rights will get you off the hook for the money you owe them, even if you didn&#x27;t charge for your copy.')
