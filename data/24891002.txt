Item(by='simonblack', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603667818, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24889027, text='If your system depends on the Web always being available, then you&#x27;re going to have times when you can&#x27;t do anything because you can&#x27;t access your data. When the Web isn&#x27;t there, your data does not exist.<p>I&#x27;m usually an early adopter, but I had some severe problems with the &#x27;Cloud&#x27; very soon after I stated using it. I then went the other way, making it my policy to store as little as possible on the Web. My mail is still stored on the Web, but if I could work out how to <i>easily</i> back that up or store it totally locally, I would.')
