You can be taught the &#x27;science&#x27; but not the &#x27;art&#x27;.<p>Similarly, a painter can be taught the techniques of painting and putting paint on a surface, but classes and tutorials will never be able to to teach him to be an &#x27;Artist&#x27;.')
