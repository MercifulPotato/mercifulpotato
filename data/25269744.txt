Item(by='ChuckNorris89', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606855956, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25269631, text='Another clarification, the display of the Bip(and Pebble, Garmin and others) is not a transreflective type but a <i>SHARP Memory LCD</i> where pixels only need power to change their state but need almost no power keeping their current state, like e-ink, but better, so it looks like transreflective in the sunlight.<p>The transreflective LCD type of the Gameboy would absolutely nuke your battery life as it needs to be actively refreshed even on stationary images.<p>I&#x27;m deeply saddened this type of display is not more popular among wearables vs OLED. Who doesn&#x27;t want 30 day+ battery life and always-on sunlight visibility?')
