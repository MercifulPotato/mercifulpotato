Item(by='saalweachter', descendants=None, kids=[25184337], score=None, time=1606099777, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25178497, text='I wonder what the largest checksummed file you could manually (well, programmatically) test every single bitflip against, rechecksumming to see if you found the bad bit, in a reasonable amount of time is.<p>1 kilobit of data seems pretty doable, only a thousand variants to check.
