Item(by='paulnechifor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606512294, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25229492, text='In middle school I often had to reset my plugged-in 12-hour alarm clock at night because I would switch plugs for some reason.<p>I noticed a pattern that sometimes my alarm would refuse to ring in the morning but would ring at 8 at night.<p>I eventually noticed that it only happened when I stayed up late and reset the time&#x2F;alarm at midnight and figured out that my clock was broken because it thought that 11 PM is followed by 12 AM.<p>It was more than a decade until I found out that it was correct.')
