Item(by='yboris', descendants=None, kids=[25087355, 25087415], score=None, time=1605292307, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25085130, text='Given that 99% of animals come from factory farms, the safest choice is to abstain from consuming meat.<p>Given that the incentives are massively aligned with cutting corners to save money, even farms that aim to offer better treatment of animals, will be pushed to cut corners to compete. They will have more incentive to <i>appear</i> to treat animals well, not to <i>actually</i> treat animals well.<p>Given that there are sensible alternatives to meat, the question for a meat eater isn&#x27;t between meat and not eating food, but instead eating a slighly-differently-tasting dish.')
