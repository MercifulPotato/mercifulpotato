Item(by='monocasa', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604346508, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972022, text='We know how it works, and it&#x27;s a simple version of a Fourier transform.  The cochlea has hairs of different lengths that vibrate according to the contribution of the sine wave that corresponds to the resonant frequency of that hair&#x27;s length.  By the time neurons get involved at all, it&#x27;s already a signal of the frequency domain over time.')
