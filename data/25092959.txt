Item(by='Geminidog', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605368751, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25091988, text='There are problems outside of cognitive burden. More expressiveness actually leads to a higher error surface area.<p>The more power you have the more opportunities you also have to shoot yourself in the foot.<p>Removing mutability as a feature from a language also removes all mutable logic errors from a language. Removing null values from the language removes all null related runtime errors from a language.')
