Item(by='ip26', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604249236, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24959109, text='Turns out faster clocks don&#x27;t help much if your logic isn&#x27;t getting faster too. You do less work per cycle, while your cycle overhead remains fixed, resulting in <i>less</i> work done per unit time. If it wasn&#x27;t for branch misprediction, you&#x27;d be better off with very deep pipes and slower clocks - see GPUs.')
