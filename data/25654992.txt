Item(by='tkgally', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609912457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25654458, text='&gt; anyone knows how best to refer to anime art style in Japanese?<p>The term <i>mangachikku</i> (漫画チック, マンガチック, &quot;manga-tic&quot;) is sometimes used to refer to the art style typical of manga and anime; it can also refer to exaggerated, caricatured depictions in general. Perhaps <i>anime fū irasuto</i> (アニメ風イラスト, anime-style illustration), while a less colorful expression, would be closer to what you&#x27;re looking for.')
