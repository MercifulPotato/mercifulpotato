Item(by='open-source-ux', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607598131, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25359897, text='There never was an open-source business model.<p>Among the thousands of open-source projects, the ones championed as a successful business make a tiny proportion i.e. the <i>exception</i> not the norm.<p>Meanwhile the SaaS business model continues to eat the software market. A model that gives users less control than the desktop software model.<p>Open-source software has been critical to the success of the SaaS model. You could argue SaaS is the true success of the &#x27;open-source business model&#x27;. Just not the way open-source advocates expected it to be.')
