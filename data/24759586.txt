Item(by='wonnage', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602537762, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24755614, text='This is kind of related to the lean supply concept that&#x27;s been waylaid by COVID-19. You think you can just pay for capacity when you need it, until it turns out everyone needs it at the same time. I can&#x27;t imagine anyone who lives near wildfires in California not owning a car. Lots of people don&#x27;t live near parks, or the parks are poorly maintained. A friend might lose their job and need a place to stay for a few months.<p>There are lots of what-if scenarios which are individually unlikely but cumulatively probable. If you have the resources, why not prepare for them?')
