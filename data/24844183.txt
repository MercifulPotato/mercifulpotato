Item(by='asveikau', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603245892, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24842866, text='&gt; There&#x27;s at least some argument for the existence of UCS-4.<p>I think advocacy for 4 bytes per codepoint misses the mark.  People tend to erroneously assume that this takes away all complexity and brings us back to an ASCII-like reality of random access and 1 integer per glyph.  However Unicode is full of complexities that mean that is not attainable.  (Joiners, combining characters, and bi-di are a few.)')
