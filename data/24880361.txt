Item(by='stretchwithme', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603558340, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24879986, text='I would be happier if kids learned fundamental principles, like the law of supply and demand, rather than some interpretation of historical events.<p>If history is going to be discussed, it should be more just one view.  The Great Depression, for example, is often described as a failure of economic freedom, rather than the failure of the government&#x27;s myriad interventions.<p>Knowing only ONE side of the story doesn&#x27;t necessarily leave kids better prepared to learn, unless it&#x27;s essentially true (good luck reaching that consensus).')
