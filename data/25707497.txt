Item(by='kccqzy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610242473, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25707052, text='It&#x27;s both. Making video codecs is hard. Making your own video codec and then convincing video sites and browsers and hardware vendors to adopt it is even harder. Video sites won&#x27;t adopt your custom codec because people can&#x27;t play it; browsers aren&#x27;t interested in supporting it because (a) there&#x27;s no content (b) there&#x27;s no hardware acceleration. That&#x27;s why the AOMedia consortium (behind AV1) includes semiconductor firms, browser vendors, video producers, and video distributors.')
