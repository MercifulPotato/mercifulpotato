Item(by='elliekelly', descendants=None, kids=[25524267], score=None, time=1608760683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25520439, text='You are conflating Reg NMS NBBO with a broker-dealer’s duty of best execution. RH has a fiduciary duty to customers to (on balance) achieve best ex. And a fiduciary duty means they must act in the best interest of their customers — including putting customer interests ahead of their own. Here RH was clearly prioritizing their own interests ahead of their customers.')
