Item(by='jph', descendants=None, kids=[25034647], score=None, time=1604922753, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25032956, text='Git could learn from Pijul, IMHO, in the sense of better emphasis for newcomers for how to think in terms of patches.<p>For example Pijul aims to focus on first creating a patch, whereas git tutorials IME tend to focus on first creating a branch, then editing files, and only later discussing patch oriented areas such as `git add --patch` and `git stash`. I see many newcomers finally get their &quot;AHA&quot; insights about git&#x27;s power when they learn `--patch`.')
