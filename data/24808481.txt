Item(by='aksr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602923878, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24793546, text='Leonardo is a polymath by his time standards—he <i>just</i> didn&#x27;t know everything.<p>I think this quote is problematic:<p>&gt; If someone is good at computer science, epidemiology and finance, surely we can trust their opinion on politics as well?<p>This quote could be used to <i>justify</i> a politician&#x27;s stance &quot;I know better than the people&quot;.<p>Just because we have a word for it (&quot;politics&quot;) doesn&#x27;t mean it something &quot;special&quot;.\nThe real question is &quot;what is politics?&quot;—a way of living, a mish-mash of multiple disciplines and one doesn&#x27;t have to be a politician to have an option.\nMost politicians are not politicians by profession.<p>Politics <i>should</i> be excluded from other (so to speak, more objective) disciplines, and everyone <i>should</i> have an opinion about politics.')
