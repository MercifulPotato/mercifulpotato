Item(by='KittenInABox', descendants=None, kids=[25138876], score=None, time=1605717382, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25138000, text='I&#x27;m also a tech worker and I know plenty of things we can agitate for:<p>* Better treatment against the rampant ageism in our industry (eg. access to lawyers when IBM decides to do mass layoffs of people above a certain age...)<p>* Guaranteeing reimbursement for self-education materials, time off for self-education purposes like conventions and hacker spaces<p>* Better treatment for H1B visa holders, a political push to stop the abuse of workers who cannot leave employers or risk losing immigration status<p>* Support for and communal funding for OSS<p>* A group pushback against flagrantly unethical, insecure, or straight up illegal behavior on the part of companies (eg. that non-poaching agreement back in the day that kept people&#x27;s wages from being as high as they should&#x27;ve been)<p>* Standard establishment of making sure remote workers are paid for the value they bring to the company and aren&#x27;t stiffed purely because of where they are geographically located at the time.')
