Item(by='im3w1l', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607037268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25296329, text='Basically companies have an optimal cash reserve. Below that optimum a dollar in the company increases future discounted cashflow of company by more than a dollar -&gt; They should raise money. Above that optimum a dollar in the company increases the future discounted cashflows of the company by less than a dollar -&gt; They should hand out money.<p>Because of transaction costs there is in practice a zone where they should neither raise nor give dividends.<p>Close to the optimum a dollar in dividend will change the value of the company by approximately one dollar.')
