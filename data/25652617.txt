Item(by='yazaddaruvala', descendants=None, kids=[25652776], score=None, time=1609888683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25651362, text='I&#x27;m sure most of you are well aware, but for anyone reading that isn&#x27;t aware of the nuance:<p>&gt; is monthly expense of renting vs monthly expense of buying<p>Note: &quot;monthly expense of buying&quot; is very different than &quot;monthly cash flow of buying&quot;.<p>The &quot;monthly expense of buying&quot; is the monthly interest paid, taxes, and maintenance.<p>The &quot;monthly cash flow of buying&quot; is the monthly mortgage paid (principal and interest), taxes, and maintenance.<p>When deciding to buy a space, you need to use the &quot;monthly cash flow&quot; to ensure you don&#x27;t default on the loan. When comparing buying vs renting a space, you need to use the &quot;monthly expense&quot;.')
