Item(by='tobinfricke', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610595994, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25771457, text='No, it yields a Gini coefficient very close to zero.<p>The Gini coefficient is defined as 1&#x2F;2 the mean absolute difference considering all pairs of households:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Gini_coefficient" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Gini_coefficient</a><p>A group of people where everyone has the same income as a Gini coefficient of exactly zero.
