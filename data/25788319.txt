Both in media and IRL. Knowing some people who work at a local one, all I can say, is that this quite certainly is the road to the future.<p>For one, because unions and govts are pushing down to delivery services that pretend to have &quot;freelancers&quot; working for them, but really just offload costs (social fees, insurance, taxes) to underpayed workers.
