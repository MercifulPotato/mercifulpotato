Item(by='stevesearer', descendants=None, kids=[25118370, 25118191], score=None, time=1605564251, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25117394, text='I was on a jury a number of years ago on a suspected DUI case where the police officer for the case failed to complete the proper paperwork and record of events at the time of arrest.<p>As a jury we ended up deciding that the defendant was Not Guilty because without that filed paperwork, it was just two people&#x27;s recollection of the events and we didn&#x27;t feel the officer was more trustworthy.<p>I wouldn&#x27;t have thought much about it after the fact, except that the prosecution was visibly upset at the decision which made me realize that that was likely not what normally would have happened.')
