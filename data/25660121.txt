Item(by='lxgr', descendants=None, kids=[25661447], score=None, time=1609955021, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25659617, text='Multiparty conferencing without a media relay is difficult, as bandwidth requirements scale quadratically with the number of participants for a full mesh topology.<p>You can also have the participant with the beefiest connection act as such a relay, but if every participant is on a metered or upload limited connection, that&#x27;s not an option.<p>It&#x27;s pretty sad actually: We seem to be caught in a perpetual catch-22 of &quot;nobody needs public IP reachability and symmetric bandwidth for home connection, since everyone uses beefy cloud servers anyway&quot; and &quot;we need beefy cloud servers because home connections are asymmetric and NATs are horrible&quot;...')
