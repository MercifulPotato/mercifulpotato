Item(by='simiones', descendants=None, kids=[24705647], score=None, time=1601997697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24696605, text='You shouldn&#x27;t forget that, since the mathematical  equations have a singularity at the center of a black hole, we can be reasonably sure that they are in fact incomplete and can&#x27;t accurately describe the internal structure of a black hole.<p>So, take any such description with a grain of salt. It&#x27;s much more correct to say that our current models can&#x27;t accurately predict what is happening past the event horizon of a black hole.')
