Item(by='nickff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604626838, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25004009, text='Air-launched torpedoes are different from submarine-launched ones. The former are usually lighter, battery-powered, and almost exclusively employed against submarines. The latter are larger, often chemically powered, and used against both submerged and surface targets.<p>Super-cavitation just isn&#x27;t very helpful for air-launched torpedoes.')
