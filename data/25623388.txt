Item(by='simonw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609696182, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622149, text='We implemented a RFC-style process at a previous employer. It worked pretty well.<p>One thing that was particularly valuable was ensuring every proposal came with a &quot;alternatives&quot; section (called &quot;competitors&quot; in the NABC model). We also made sure that every proposal included &quot;do nothing&quot; as one of the alternatives, to provide an anchor for a discussion about what would happen if we simply didn&#x27;t take on the problem at all (or stuck with whatever imperfect solution we were currently using).')
