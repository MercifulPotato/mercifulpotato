Item(by='imglorp', descendants=None, kids=[25178019, 25178007], score=None, time=1606057253, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25177863, text='A tablespoon of sugar--a common amount in a recipe--is 12.5 grams. As the other guy said, a kitchen scale can easily do this: throw your bowl on there, hit the &quot;tare&quot; button to zero the scale, and then any sugar you add will be the reading on the scale.  No spoons or cups needed; it&#x27;s simpler!  Tare again and add the next ingredient.')
