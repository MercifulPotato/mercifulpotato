Item(by='yarcob', descendants=None, kids=[25178957, 25177659], score=None, time=1606050102, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25176633, text='If the machines are on the same network I usually just do this on the receiving machine:<p><pre><code>    nc -l 1234 | pbcopy\n</code></pre>\nAnd on the sending machine<p><pre><code>    pbpaste | nc receiving-machine.local 1234\n</code></pre>\n(where &quot;receiving-machine.local&quot; is the hostname or IP address of the machine you want to copy data to)<p>nc (netcat) is installed on macOS by default, so this works everywhere without any configuration. It&#x27;s unencrypted, but on my private LAN I don&#x27;t worry about people snooping.')
