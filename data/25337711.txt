Item(by='ManuelKiessling', descendants=None, kids=[25338952], score=None, time=1607376027, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25337441, text='&gt; ...a full 95 percent of respondents were male<p>Well, that’s exactly the problem I see with surveys in general: people read the above statement and automatically parse it as „95 percent of GitHub users are male“.<p>No, they aren’t.
