You might argue &quot;$3.50 per month is nothing&quot;. Well, you&#x27;re not going to be using a wiki and only have one person able to make changes. Otherwise you&#x27;d create a page some other way. You&#x27;re going to have to pay on a per-person basis for a wiki that they might edit only a couple times over the course of the month.\nCarry that out to five years, and the math doesn&#x27;t work.<p>2. You have to give your data to a company you don&#x27;t know and storing it in an alternative location.<p>3. You&#x27;re dealing with a second, small company you&#x27;ve never heard of.')
