Item(by='FerretFred', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601907550, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24677407, text='I tried something similar after looking at what I really <i>needed</i> a NAS for: serving music and video to my home network, and via Wireguard VPN if needed. When I actually looked at the requirements in detail I set up a Raspberry Pi 4B with 2Gb memory, a 1Tb SSD connected via USB3&#x2F;SATA and just installed Samba and MiniDLNA. Careful tweaking of the Samba config file ensured that it played nicely with all devices here, including Roku and Apple. It&#x27;s simple, works well and is backed up to another, offline disk.')
