Item(by='gnusty_gnurc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602527060, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24757318, text='I’m glad I’ve dealt with anxiety for most of my life before all this happened.<p>It’s interesting to see the general population deal with high anxiety for the first time.<p>I think the only way to live healthily with anxiety, outside of medicine, is to develop metacognition about your emotions and thoughts.<p>Recognize that life is inherently risky and you have little control over it outside of your reactions.<p>The hardest test is dealing with somatic symptoms and navigating the catastrophic thinking. It’s the ultimate challenge to talk yourself down when you’re convinced you’re having a heart attack or unable to breath.<p>Covid is a risk but for most people not a significant threat.')
