Item(by='ajuc', descendants=None, kids=[24846083], score=None, time=1603270676, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24834248, text='In some EU countries (for example Poland) copyright is separated into &quot;personal&quot; and &quot;economic&quot; rights.<p>You can give up the economic rights and these are inherited and you can trade them.<p>Personal rights cannot be traded or inherited and remain with the author till their death. So you cannot change the text without author&#x27;s permission, you cannot lie about who wrote something, and author can prevent you from using his art for a different purpose (for example if you wrote a song and somebody used it as a military recruitment ad and you&#x27;re a pacifist - that sort of thing).')
