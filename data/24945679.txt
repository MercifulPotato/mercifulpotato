I use Cargo mode and an after-save hook so that each save runs `cargo check` and, on success, also runs `cargo fmt`. A nice side effect is that I don&#x27;t need to care about formatting any more.<p><pre><code>  (defun cargo-process-check-sentinel (process event)\n    (when (string= &quot;finished\\n&quot; event)\n      (rust-format-buffer)))\n\n  (defun my-after-save-hook ()\n    (when (eq major-mode &#x27;rust-mode)\n      (set-process-sentinel (cargo-process-check) &#x27;cargo-process-check-sentinel)))\n\n  (add-hook &#x27;after-save-hook #&#x27;my-after-save-hook)</code></pre>')
