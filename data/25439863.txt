I never went back to my 12&quot; laptop screen after I discovered larger monitors with big font.<p>Specific to day time use:<p>-Try and avoid glare. You can place your screen in front of a large source of light and turn up the screens brightness.<p>Specific to night time use:<p>-Use applications like Redshift or f.lux to reduce blue light as much as you can.<p>-Reduce the brightness of your screen.<p>-Try and get ambiant light, such as a difuse lamp or something, behind your screen to illuminate the area surrounding your work space.<p>These usually work for me.
