Maybe the best way of rolling out universal healthcare is to take a refactoring approach. Get more and more people onto medicare, lowering the age limit and raising the maximum income threshold.  Eventually, it would only be the wealthy who were still on the private insurance only system. Then employers can gradually replace health coverage with other benefits.')
