One, antitrust includes more than monopoly, including oligopoly and monopsony. Two, there is no generic advertising market; there are a variety of specific ones. This came up during the Stop Hate For Profit campaign; large companies joined easily, but many small businesses couldn&#x27;t. Having zero effective choice is a clear sign of anti-trust concerns.')
