If someone makes a call to violence that is not intended to be taken seriously and is not at risk of being taken seriously that is different from making a call to violence in the hope that someone picks it up at a time when people are likely to act on it.<p>There is a test for this decided at the supreme court level: inciteful speech can only be prohibited if the speech is &quot;directed to inciting or producing imminent lawless action.&quot;')
