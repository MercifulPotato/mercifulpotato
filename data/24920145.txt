Item(by='franze', descendants=None, kids=[24920410, 24921214, 24920634], score=None, time=1603900010, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24917721, text='Whenever I do a SEO workshop I ask 3 questions to my audience:<p><pre><code>  * How often did you Google this week?\n  * What was the last thing before the last thing that you Googled?\n</code></pre>\nFor the first question, I get laughs. For the second one blank stares.<p>Later, I shoot another question:<p><pre><code>  * What is the last thing you read, that you googled before, top to bottom?\n</code></pre>\nFor the last question everybody looks at their feet.<p>The use case I optimize websites for is:<p><pre><code>  * Users do not know when they Google.\n  * Users do not know what they Google.\n  * Google is an extension of their thinking.\n  * They do not read what they find. \n  * It just need to move them forward in a way.\n</code></pre>\nWe are all users.')
