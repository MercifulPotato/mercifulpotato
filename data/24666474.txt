Item(by='pkolaczk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601668806, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24665874, text='The worst case for a hashtable degenerating to a linked list can be often exploited to form a DOS attack. It is enough that user controls the keys inserted to the hashtable and the hash function is not cryptographically secure (typically they are not for performance reasons). That&#x27;s why it is much wiser to use a binary tree for resolving collisions.<p>So in this case - the worst case analysis is useful.')
