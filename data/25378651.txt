Item(by='submagr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607634281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25378650, text='Can you teach me is a slack extension inspired from donut (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.donut.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.donut.com&#x2F;</a>). A user can have a list of things she would like to learn about. Other users can see the list and see if they can give her a head-start.<p>Idea:<p>- A user can have a list of things she would like to know.<p>- Other users can see them and tag them if they happen to have worked with it in past.<p>- Every week the app will pair up two people who can teach each other one thing.<p>- Both of them will meet on a video call for 1 hr.\nThey will try to learn the thing themselves by googling around, but the expert user can help them finding good resources and solving quick doubts.')
