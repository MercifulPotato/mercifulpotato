Item(by='koonsolo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607244777, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25316328, text='I&#x27;m 40 and most of my time is spent sitting in front of a computer.<p>I try to be healthy, but read somewhere that sitting for long periods is about the same as smoking.<p>So after seeing Wolfram walking on a treadmill while working on the computer, it convinced me to do the same.<p>I have a ikea standing desk, and a movable walk pad underneath. I try to walk about 30 to 60 min a day. It actually works really great. You can still read properly while walking, and if you can rest your wrists, typing and mouse are no problem.<p>Highly recommended!')
