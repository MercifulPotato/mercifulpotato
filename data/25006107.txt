Item(by='sebmellen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604652649, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25006038, text='I&#x27;m not sure I agree with that.<p>Of course there are many moonshot projects where money is of vital importance. But for most things, the market is the best signalling mechanism available. Having little money forces you to be very attuned to the signals of the market, giving you an advantage over those who might insulate themselves from that feedback mechanism with a buffer of money.<p>It&#x27;s hard to keep yourself attuned enough while also having money in reserve, though perhaps it is possible to have enough discipline to do so.')
