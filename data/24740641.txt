For example, when you depend on the &#x27;set -e&#x27; behavior in a function and <i>call</i> the function from within a condition, the &#x27;set -e&#x27; has no effect at all. So you better don&#x27;t count on &#x27;set -e&#x27;.<p>But don&#x27;t expect me to follow my own advice, as not using &#x27;set -e&#x27; isn&#x27;t a good option either...<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.in-ulm.de&#x2F;~mascheck&#x2F;various&#x2F;set-e&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.in-ulm.de&#x2F;~mascheck&#x2F;various&#x2F;set-e&#x2F;</a>')
