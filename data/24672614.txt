Item(by='dahart', descendants=None, kids=[24673130, 24672754], score=None, time=1601738632, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24661231, text='&gt; Donald Knuth famously wrote that premature optimization is the root of all evil.<p>It’s funny how often Knuth’s actual point is left out of the re-telling of this quote. The actual point he was making was “we should not pass up our opportunities [to optimize] in that critical 3%.“ He wasn’t suggesting the avoidance of optimization, he was suggesting the avoidance of <i>premature</i> optimization.')
