Item(by='mumblemumble', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605627456, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25124821, text='It seems to me that Ansible is best for deploying servers, and containers are best for deploying applications.<p>You can absolutely use Ansible to deploy applications, but, if there is any essential complexity in your run-time environment or configuration needs, it seems that Ansible has a tendency to make that complexity compound multiplicatively instead of additively.<p>Disclaimer: I say this, not as an ops person, but as a developer who often uses containers and occasionally watches people wrestling with Ansible from the sidelines.')
