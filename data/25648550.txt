Item(by='csharptwdec19', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609869570, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25647221, text='MSSQL will lock on Reads by default, and can(will) escalate locks to entire pages for reads.<p>Making MVCC Mode &#x27;Opt In&#x27; requires enabling Snapshot Isolation, and making it default requires another change. It&#x27;s also worth noting the way MSSQL does MVCC is far more TempDB&#x2F;Memory hungry than Oracle&#x2F;Postgres (due to the nature of using snapshots). I&#x27;ve been in more than one shop that did not want to enable it due to the memory requirements.')
