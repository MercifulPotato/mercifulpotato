For me, pasta is in the &quot;normal&quot; range.<p>&quot;Fast&quot; -- candy, soda, etc. Hits in 20 mins and is gone in 2.5 hrs.<p>&quot;Normal&quot; -- pasta, a sandwich, rice, etc. Hits in 30-45 mins and is gone in 4&#x2F;5 hrs<p>&quot;Slow&quot; -- high fat foods, peanut butter, meats, etc. hits in ~2h and is gone in 7-9<p>Made a little chart (†).  The area under those curves is the same for the same g of carbohydrate but the shape is different.<p>The thing is, pasta is heavy. Compared to most meals it&#x27;s dense and almost pure carbohydrate. So if you have even a little bit of insulin resistance you will definitely feel it.<p>Then there&#x27;s alcohol, that lives in the complete opposite corner: a shot of vodka hits in the &quot;fast&quot; category but then actually lowers the BG later on.<p>† <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;i.postimg.cc&#x2F;5tqc24zn&#x2F;fast-slow.png" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;i.postimg.cc&#x2F;5tqc24zn&#x2F;fast-slow.png</a>')
