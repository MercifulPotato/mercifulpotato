At least you&#x27;ve diversified risk.  Mind that these may (and likely <i>will</i>) consolidate with time.  Skype, WebMeeting, Instagram, YouTube, GitHub, and Blogger were once freestanding companies.  They no longer are.<p>- Self-hosted solutions.  NextCloud, FreedomBox, etc., or DIY service bundles on your home, office, and&#x2F;or a hosted service can avoid the problem entirely at least for highly stateful services (email, contacts, files, documents)<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;duckduckgo.com&#x2F;?q=google+alternatives" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;duckduckgo.com&#x2F;?q=google+alternatives</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Nextcloud" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Nextcloud</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nomoregoogle.com" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nomoregoogle.com</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;alternativeto.net" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;alternativeto.net</a>')
