Item(by='justin_oaks', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606242796, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25199167, text='While I was in a single-person department, I used a Jira project where I put my tasks and commented clearly about progress.  Then I realized that I was the only one using it. Neither my boss nor anybody else cared whether the information was in Jira, a spreadsheet, a Word doc, or if I just verbally explained what I had done.<p>So I stopped tracking things meticulously in Jira and only kept basic notes of what I had done. Worked out fine.')
