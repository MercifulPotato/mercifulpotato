She explained that bundling up during the winter is taken so seriously that babies and young children wear thick insulating hats pretty much 24&#x2F;7 during every non summer month. The idea that being cold causes colds (infections generally) is taken for granted by Russians. Oddly, taking a dip in ice cold water is considered good for your health.')
