Downturns are the best years.  They flush out all the folks who are hoping to get rich fast by peddling snake oil, and leave behind the folks who are building, who are inventing, who are a little bit weird, or who just love the beautiful weather.  The next tech boom starts when somebody finds a crazy invention useful; that&#x27;s a lot easier when people are living ordinary lives than when everybody&#x27;s chasing a hot fad.')
