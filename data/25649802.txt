Item(by='useerup', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609874695, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25649439, text='There&#x27;s a property tax of 1% for home values up to around DKK 3M, and 3% for the value above 3M.<p>That is the based on the &quot;official estimated value&quot;, however, which is typically conservative and lagging and therefore somewhat less (like e.g. 60%) of what  the property actually sells for.<p>The 3M is set so that the vast majority of <i>homes</i> will not exceed this value.<p>In addition to that we pay a &quot;lot tax&quot; which varies by municipality between 1.6% -3.4% of the value of the <i>lot</i> without the buildings.')
