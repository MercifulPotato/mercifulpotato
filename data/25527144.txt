Item(by='dkarp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608815622, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25527101, text='I began learning piano at age 27.<p>Piano fingering technique is very important. That&#x27;s not just which finger hits which key, but also the posture of your finger. That could be what&#x27;s straining your fingers and giving you trouble reaching keys.<p>I found a local piano teacher and took weekly lessons. The piano teacher caught these bad habits like finger technique, but also how hard to hit the keys and other things that I wouldn&#x27;t have noticed on my own.<p>If you are set on self-learning, getting a few lessons every once in a while will help you stay on track.')
