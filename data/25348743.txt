Item(by='vorpalhex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607449635, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25348443, text='Communism still has currency.<p>I don&#x27;t know why this is a popular misconception. You may have ration points or communism bucks instead of dollars, but you still need a hard currency to allow interpersonal trade. You may have resources that aren&#x27;t obtainable through currency (housing), but you need currency because you need to settle preference&#x2F;availability&#x2F;tradeoffs. You probably also even still have some taxes for consumption.<p>The price of all goods is not equivalent on a fundamental labor level and the preference for goods is not flat.')
