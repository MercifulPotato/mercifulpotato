Item(by='jorangreef', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611414737, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25882695, text='I have found that planning for DIO from the start makes for a better, simpler design when designing storage systems, because it keeps the focus on logical&#x2F;physical sector alignment, latent sector error handling, and caching from the beginning. And even better to design data layouts to work with block devices.<p>Retrofitting DIO onto a non-DIO design and doing this cross-platform is going to be more work, but I don&#x27;t think that&#x27;s the fault of DIO (when you&#x27;re already building a database that is).')
