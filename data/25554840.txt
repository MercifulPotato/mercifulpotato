Item(by='jessriedel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609108297, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25551469, text='Isn&#x27;t he assuming a steady exponential improvement in solar power costs but taking the cost of long-distance power transmission (which during the fossil fuel era has had little reason to improve) to be fixed? Like, <i>obviously</i> that means solar will eventually become local. But as solar displaces coal, it will be more and more profitable to transport power further distances, which will probably put significant downward pressure on long-distance transmission costs.')
