A strawman is when you take your opponent&#x27;s argument and instead recast it as a much weaker version so it&#x27;s easy to tear down.<p>In this case the actual argument I&#x27;ve heard made is &quot;Experts on TV (in addition to, I might add, Bill Barr, the current US Attorney General) are saying that the hack was from the Russians <i>because investigation insiders said it had hallmarks of known Russian hacking groups</i>&quot; which is being recast as the much weaker argument, which I&#x27;ve never heard any serious expert actually make, of &quot;Experts on TV are saying that the hack was from the Russians <i>just because it&#x27;s such a complicated hack</i>&quot;.')
