Item(by='munificent', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602544044, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24756179, text='&quot;Friction&quot; is a great word.<p>The author is essentially advocating a low-margin lifestyle. There is little spare capacity or other tolerances engineered into their choices. They may have <i>cash</i> in the bank, but they don&#x27;t have any reserves of <i>interior space</i>, <i>exterior space</i>, or <i>transit capacity</i>. Instead, they rely entirely on being able to acquire that on-demand.<p>That works out <i>most</i> of the time, but it&#x27;s a risk. And, like any other risk, it needs to be planned for, and it carries its own emotional and experiential weight.')
