Item(by='Closi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604358003, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972433, text='There are plenty of companies that treat content that was created while you were a premium user differently when you revert back to a free user.<p>I’m not saying that anyone is entitled to it, I’m just saying that I think the model of ‘content created when I was a premium subscriber can retain some limited premium features’ is a much more graceful and customer-friendly downgrade path.<p>Forcing all your documents into either public, or a downloadable format you will never be able to edit again even if you resubscribe, doesn’t seem very consumer friendly.')
