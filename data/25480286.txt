More generally, it refers to the process by which money or resources obtained through ethically questionable means is passed through layers of other activities so as to obscure its original source.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Money_laundering" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Money_laundering</a><p>So the idea is, you have some bias in some process (racial, religious, whatever). You set up some algorithm that relies on the bias to make predictions or classifications. Now you can say it&#x27;s not you that&#x27;s biased, it&#x27;s just the algorithm. The algorithm is some process by which your bias is &quot;made legitimate&quot;.')
