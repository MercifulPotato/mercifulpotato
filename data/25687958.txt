Item(by='hrolfgar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610130611, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25687167, text='Sort of like the prisoners&#x27; dilemma; at the end of the day, the selfish actors will choose to locally optimize.<p>Btw, this argument about the sex ratio stabilizing at 1:1 is known as Fisher&#x27;s principle. Fisher made another really interesting observation about how extremely exaggerated male ornamentation, like in peacocks, reaches its equilibrium when the aesthetic advantages from a large, attractive tail becomes offset by the practical costs of being slower, needing more food to produce said tail, being highly visible, etc. This one&#x27;s called &quot;Fisherian Runaway&quot;. These purely aesthetic traits will &quot;run away&quot; all the way up until they becoming detrimental to survival.')
