Item(by='hairtuq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607079506, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25300983, text='The name SWAR is sometimes used (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;SWAR" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;SWAR</a>).<p>Indeed, even 8-bit fields can be added in parallel, using the fact that ^ is like a + that does not produce a carry:<p><pre><code>    uint64_t signmask = 0x8080808080808080;\n    uint64_t sum_without_sign_bits = ((x &amp; ~signmask) + (y &amp; ~signmask));\n    uint64_t sum_of_sign_bits = (x ^ y) &amp; signmask;\n    return sum_without_sign_bits ^ sum_of_sign_bits;</code></pre>')
