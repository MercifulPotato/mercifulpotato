Item(by='zwayhowder', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609102048, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25553770, text='If you have a lot of meetings with external parties (it&#x27;s not about scheduling between the 3 internal) a good calendar scheduling tool is worth it in my opinion. When I was a sales engineer at a software startup we probably spent 10-15 minutes per lead sorting calendar times out. Considering only 1 in 10 leads turn into sales this was 2.5-3 hours per converted lead.<p>By having a self service scheduling tool we already filter for the non serious people &quot;I&#x27;m not wasting my time finding time in your calendar&quot; and improve our lead quality while reducing the back and forth nonsense that no one enjoys.')
