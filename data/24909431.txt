Item(by='bo1024', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603818520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24908728, text='Your example is a reactive event. Something happened in your environment.<p>This idea is more useful for situations that you are initiating, and where feedback is not immediately obvious.<p>An example could be turning your car’s lights on at night. Before starting the car, you force yourself to point to the switch, say “lights on”, and do it.<p>I use this with keys. When leaving my office, house, or car, I hold up the key in my hand and establish sight (I don’t say anything out loud). Then I lock the door.')
