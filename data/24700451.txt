Item(by='supernova87a', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602006387, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24699510, text='And I hate to be the bringer of discouraging news, but the reason tech companies are succeeding at selling us our own data is that <i>we want it</i>.<p>In every click we make to like something, buy something targeted at us, listen to media we like, we teach companies or algorithms that we want what they have.  And so yelling at the company for delivering what we have shown we want is a bit like yelling at yourself for eating so much ice cream.<p>Until we put down our phones and stop reinforcing the outcome, we&#x27;re a big source of our own problems.  You might as well try to change human nature.')
