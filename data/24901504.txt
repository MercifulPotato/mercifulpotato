If you omit the &quot;no&quot; and soften the &quot;never&quot; by substituting &quot;don&#x27;t&quot;, you achieve the same thing without jeopardising the flow of interaction.<p>Just say &quot;I don&#x27;t share that.&quot; in a good natured, confident smiling tone.   Then be absolutely silent and wait for them to continue the conversation.<p>If they rephrase their enquiry, don&#x27;t revisit your statement, which has already been clearly delivered and doesn&#x27;t require repeating.  Instead, take the opportunity to move the conversation along:<p>&quot;What salary and conditions are you offering for this position?&quot;')
