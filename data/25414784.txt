That is, to modify an example from the website: You have &quot;14.01 1601 Timesheet&quot;. But you have a new ID for every pay period. So you have &quot;14.02 1602 Timesheet&quot;.<p>Better would just be have &quot;14.01 Timesheets&quot;, and each timesheet gets the year and week prepended to it.<p>He discusses this a bit in the exceptions page[1].
