Item(by='wbillingsley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608346832, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25473356, text='&gt; In Australia we usually have to pick our major before we finish high school.<p>On paper. In practice, it&#x27;s not very hard to begin one degree but then switch to another.<p>&gt; I start a degree in Computer Science, in most of my classes all my peers are also CS students<p>It depends. In our CS degree, you stand a very good chance of meeting (at least virtually) students from other disciplines, degrees, and backgrounds in some subject - from the statistics, not just in mathematics classes but also in Python classes and anything that looks like data science. It does depend on how your degree and how <i>their</i> degree was designed, however - some disciplines prescribe the entire programme while others make more space for including subjects from other fields.')
