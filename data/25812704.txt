Item(by='yarcob', descendants=None, kids=[25824656, 25813550], score=None, time=1610904688, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25811769, text='Importantly, HDMI will be around for projectors. Almost every meeting room and every class room everywhere in the world has a beamer and an HDMI cable on the desk. I don&#x27;t think this is going to change in the next 5 years. It took more than 10 years for most of the world to upgrade from VGA to HDMI cables, and HDMI works fine technically, so there&#x27;s less pressure to upgrade to USB-C.<p>So for the foreseeable future, if you ever do a presentation, and you have a Mac, you are going to bring a dongle.<p>If you&#x27;re a teacher and you don&#x27;t want to deal with an unreliable dongle 5 times a day, the most convenient Mac for you is currently the 2015 Macbook Pro with its built-in HDMI port.')
