Item(by='pavlov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603662926, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24868316, text='His paintings feel extremely nostalgic to me because they remind me of Amiga graphics in the 1987-1993 era.<p>It&#x27;s probably due to three factors: 1) Elson&#x27;s bright colors with limited color blending evokes the Amiga&#x27;s limited 32-color palette; 2) the texture detail is often similar in granularity to a low-resolution hand-pixelated image; 3) scifi and fantasy paintings were often recreated by young Amiga artists.')
