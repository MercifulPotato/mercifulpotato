It’s difficult for interviewers to assess potential so many rely on experience as the key indicator.<p>It’s difficult for candidates from disadvantaged to show the same level of experience as candidates from non-disadvantaged backgrounds.  Therefore candidates from disadvantaged backgrounds are systematically segmented out from the hiring pool.<p>Well-run diversity programs aim to better assess the potential of disadvantaged candidates and there are a variety of indicators for success.')
