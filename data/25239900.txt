My father got me into programming on a TI-99&#x2F;4A [0] machine, so the BASIC language always has a place in my heart.<p>However, I recently also came across a nice game development course from Harvard, CS-50 [1] (an <i>excellent</i> course, much recommended!). And for CS-50 the Lua language is used in combination with the LÖVE [2] framework.<p>After previously dabbling with SpriteKit [3] and Swift I have to admit LÖVE has been extremely pleasurable and fun to use. It&#x27;s really nice to get some quick results.
