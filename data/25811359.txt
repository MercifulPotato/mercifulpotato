Item(by='shiftingleft', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610896870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25810867, text='From the website: &quot;The Great Silence implies that one or more of these steps are very improbable; there is a &quot;Great Filter&quot;&quot;<p>The paper argues against this in that you don&#x27;t need a few <i>very</i> improbable.\nIn fact, there are many parameter choices where none of the probabilities in the Drake equation are particularly low, yet the resulting number of intelligences is still staggeringly small.<p>tl;dr: All parameters can be a bit lower.
