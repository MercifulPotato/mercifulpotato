Item(by='krapp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605694418, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25134421, text='It&#x27;s not that the definitions are broad, it&#x27;s that many sites have been incrementally integration social media features over the years in the belief that doing so drives engagement, so the lines between what is and isn&#x27;t social media (or what should and shouldn&#x27;t be) have been intentionally blurred. Just as with literary or media genres, &quot;social media&quot; as a classification mostly exists as a marketing term, not science.<p>And I do believe forums count as social media, albeit a primitive version of it. Hacker News is social media. It has a culture, it has memes, it has gamification and dopamine loops.')
