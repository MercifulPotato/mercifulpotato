Item(by='sib', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607036650, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25293789, text='It&#x27;s not just about bragfests. The box office revenue generated is a key input variable in downstream licensing prices (that is, for premium Pay TV, cable channels, syndication, etc.) So, the bigger the box office, the more the movie generates &quot;automatically&quot; during the rest of its lifecycle. In some cases, you can think of the massive investments in marketing around the time of the theatrical opening as a loss leader for greater lifetime revenue.')
