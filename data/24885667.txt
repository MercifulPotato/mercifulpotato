Item(by='dijksterhuis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603627662, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24885593, text='Services like GitHub, Gmail, Apple etc allow you to use recovery keys in place of 2FA for this exact circumstance.<p>The keys are generated by the service and you must make a note of them ahead of time. And keep them secure obviously. You get about 10 possible keys from GitHub iirc.<p>Also, some services (Google, apple) allow you to perform 2FA over multiple options like one of TOTP, phone call, SMS or other device interaction (in apple&#x27;s case).<p>So if the device gets bricked, find a cheap replacement and Bob&#x27;s your auntie.')
