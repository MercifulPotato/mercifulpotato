Item(by='oatmealsnap', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602714480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24781876, text='Code for the developer that comes after you, not for yourself.<p>If you’re going to implement a coding style, make it easy to follow. Provide a linter that works with multiple editors, and that can automatically fix common problems or design choices.<p>If the project is intended to be short-lived or you know for a fact your won’t update it after two years, then who cares. Try something new or get it done quick and dirty.<p>Pick the right tools and methods for the job, and make sure those methods decrease friction with your team (communicate and get feedback often).')
