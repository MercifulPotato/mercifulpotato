Item(by='qppo', descendants=None, kids=[25064279], score=None, time=1605136701, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25064109, text='I don&#x27;t have access to Ableton&#x27;s source code so I can&#x27;t benchmark it to tell you if it&#x27;s doing better or worse than the OS scheduler, but I would be shocked if it was worse. The penalty for even thread synchronization is quite high, let alone interprocess sync&#x27;ing. Particularly when it comes to latency.<p>Sand boxing isn&#x27;t done for performance reasons, and it&#x27;s why you can disable it in bitwig. The sole purpose of sand boxing a plugin in its own process is because it is the only way to catch a segfault and prevent a shared library from crashing a host.')
