Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=[24973441], score=None, time=1604348768, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24971579, text='We know that intact ecosystem spreads out from the edges. They did studies in South America decades ago comparing recovery rates in rectangular versus square clearings, and the rectangular ones always won.<p>So if we&#x27;re talking about clearcutting, we ideally want to back up a step and make rules about the topology of a clearcut (not just shape, but holes as well).<p>If it&#x27;s a forest fire, we may want to know how far natural reforestation can be &#x27;stretched&#x27;, and replant areas that are outside of that range, accounting for how fast the artificial forest will spread.')
