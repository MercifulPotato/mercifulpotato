Item(by='Turbots', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611669874, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25915762, text='For one application, kubernetes is obviously giant overkill. Companies with hundreds and thousands and applications need a platform that can offer standardised deployment pattern, runtime pattern and scaling pattern for those apps. Kubernetes is a great start for managing all those containers in a consistent, secure, multi tenant environment. I know customers with literally 10000s of VMs that are moving to more cloud like environments, either on premise in their own datacenter or in the public cloud. They need these kind of platforms to automate away the repetitive stuff and have a secure posture, throughout the company.')
