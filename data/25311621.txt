Item(by='bombastry', descendants=None, kids=[25311848], score=None, time=1607140902, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25310889, text='In Emacs, an org-capture template[1] can be used to quickly and easily append new items to logs. It can prompt the user to enter text in multiple locations, eliminating the need to navigate around boilerplate to write entries. It can also prompt for tags to help make organization and searching easier. Additionally, it has the benefit of being callable anytime and anywhere within Emacs.<p>I&#x27;ve only recently gotten around to using them, but they&#x27;re really nice for quickly logging things that I was too lazy to do otherwise (e.g. recipes I have tried).<p>[1]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;orgmode.org&#x2F;manual&#x2F;Capture-templates.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;orgmode.org&#x2F;manual&#x2F;Capture-templates.html</a>')
