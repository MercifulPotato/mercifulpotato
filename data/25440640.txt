The point was that fishing for results, by trying everything, is guaranteed to find something.<p>So if, in a study like this, they looked at differences in morality rate between: 1) men and women, 2) younger and older people, 3) younger and older surgeons, 4) surgeries in summer vs winter 4) surgeries in morning vs evening 5) etc etc.... And it came down all the way to n) surgeries on birthday - to find an effect. Then it would be almost guaranteed that such a finding is spurious.')
