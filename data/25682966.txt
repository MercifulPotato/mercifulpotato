There&#x27;s no way that just moving to predefined limb positions would be stable.<p>You can&#x27;t script an unstable system without feedback. Any tiny perturbation would cause the robot to fall over. You need to actively control the system to counteract the instability.<p>So there will be some (scripted) goals, and a very complicated control algorithm that tries to reach the goals without falling over.')
