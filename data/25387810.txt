Item(by='mhh__', descendants=None, kids=[25387932], score=None, time=1607705600, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25387670, text='Native means you have access to the hardware through the kernel of your choice, it&#x27;s not about performance.<p>If there is a hypervisor in between you and your CPU - let alone emulation - you lose direct access to things like performance counters (which I need to be able to do a lot of things I enjoy programming).
