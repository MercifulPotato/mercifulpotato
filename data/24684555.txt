Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=[24687076], score=None, time=1601871915, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24683768, text='The last time I saw someone talking about LCGs, they warned that some people might be tempted to use them as an RNG but they are rarely fit for that purpose.<p>For one, they tend to behave more like shuffle than like random. With two dice rolls there is a 1&#x2F;6 chance of getting the same output twice in a row, and so games lacking a good RNG would truncate the output of an LCG to get reasonable output.<p>If the seed is PRNG, and regenerated every time, then I suppose that avoids this problem.')
