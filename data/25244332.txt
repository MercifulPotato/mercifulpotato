If you could make 100% predictions then your knowledge of the balls would be equivalent to the balls&#x27; description.<p>Why do you even use &quot;describe the balls themselves&quot; to describe this situation? From the perspective of probability you just set an upper bound to how much knowledge you could possibly have about an object, it&#x27;s still knowledge.')
