Item(by='listenallyall', descendants=None, kids=[25023007], score=None, time=1604805231, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25021181, text='Sampling errors are random, and expected. Other types of misses are not simple &quot;errors&quot; but polling flaws, like sampling a non-representative group, ignoring non-responders or assuming they break the same as the responders, asking poorly-worded questions, etc.<p>Occasional flaws in polling is understandable and tolerated. But when those misses repeatedly line up the same way, and are rather sizeable, that&#x27;s evidence of either systematic flaws, or outright bias.')
