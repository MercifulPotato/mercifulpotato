Item(by='thayne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604723616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25009931, text='This problem isn&#x27;t unique to Let&#x27;s Encrypt.  Let&#x27;s Encrypt is not the only entity with certificates signed by DST Root X3. And as these devices get older, more root certificates will expire. What happens when all of them expire?<p>What is unique about Let&#x27;s Encrypt, is they may have a harder time getting cross-signed by a CA that will still have a valid root cert on these devices for a significant amount of time, because, as has been pointed out in other comments, Let&#x27;s Encrypt is disrupting the CA industry.')
