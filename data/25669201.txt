Item(by='MayeulC', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610012959, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25669041, text='The good thing is that matrix can be bridged to Signal[1], to allow for a smoother transition period.<p>This is also true with Whatsapp[2], but against their terms of service, so you risk getting banned, and built on reverse-engineering, plus you need an android VM of some sort.<p>I&#x27;ve been personally moving my family to Signal, since that provides the best UX and easier transition from Whatsapp. Once I&#x27;m comfortable enough with it, we&#x27;ll likely transition to matrix.<p>What Matrix is missing is in my view:<p>- Client with simple UI, polished UX, and not just a smoking pot of features: FluffyChat[3] is mostly there.<p>- Server of which I can guarantee the uptime. Dendrite should lower the resource usage for a ~5-100 accounts server, and decentralised identities[4] would allow falling back to another server (such as a friend&#x27;s).<p>We&#x27;re mostly there, so I&#x27;m starting to prepare the switch, starting with my more technical friends, by setting a bridge up.
