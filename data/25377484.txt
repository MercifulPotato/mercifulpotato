There are at least two other reasons you write short paragraphs. The first is the most likely explanation for this post:<p>Because you don&#x27;t want to spend time writing a good paragraph that mixes multiple ideas together.<p>As a substitute for tldr, either before or after a long block of text. I frequently use this approach. I don&#x27;t want to spend a lot of time writing an internet comment, time being valuable and all, but I do want the reader to know the point I&#x27;m trying to get across even if the writing isn&#x27;t 100% professional. I picked it up on Reddit long ago, as a reaction to someone always giving a lengthy comment the exact opposite interpretation to what was intended.')
