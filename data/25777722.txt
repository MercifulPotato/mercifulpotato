Item(by='neartheplain', descendants=None, kids=[25778790, 25779709, 25778254], score=None, time=1610639189, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25777030, text='&gt; Guns are mostly used to kill people.<p>That’s an incorrect statement. Let’s look at the evidence.<p>More than 50 million Americans engaged in some kind of recreational shooting in 2018 [0]. Compare that to 14,000 gun-related homicides across the country in the same period [1], mostly in certain high-crime communities [2].<p>The CDC estimates there are between 500,000 and 3 million instances of defensive gun use in the US each year [3]. They also found that cases of self-defense where the victim used a gun resulted in less injury to the victim, on average, than in cases where other self-defense strategies where employed.<p>There are also obvious downsides to a society with permissive gun ownership, but overstating the case for gun control doesn’t move the discussion in a positive direction.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nssf.org&#x2F;research&#x2F;nsga-participation-reports&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nssf.org&#x2F;research&#x2F;nsga-participation-reports&#x2F;</a><p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.thetrace.org&#x2F;2019&#x2F;01&#x2F;gun-deaths-2018-america-mass-shootings-suicide&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.thetrace.org&#x2F;2019&#x2F;01&#x2F;gun-deaths-2018-america-mas...</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.theguardian.com&#x2F;us-news&#x2F;ng-interactive&#x2F;2017&#x2F;jan&#x2F;09&#x2F;special-report-fixing-gun-violence-in-america" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.theguardian.com&#x2F;us-news&#x2F;ng-interactive&#x2F;2017&#x2F;jan&#x2F;...</a><p>[3] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.ncdsv.org&#x2F;images&#x2F;IOM-NRC_Priorities-for-Research-to-reduce-the-threat-of-firearm-related-violence_2013.pdf" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.ncdsv.org&#x2F;images&#x2F;IOM-NRC_Priorities-for-Research-...</a>')
