Item(by='ColinWright', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610697705, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25786717, text='Yes, the Voronoi Diagram can be visualised as growing spheres from the given points until they reach each other ... absolutely true.<p>However, the final result is not &quot;the top view of a set of spheres centered at the points&quot;.  If you arrange spheres centered at the points, no matter what their sizes, there is no view where what you see is the Voronoi Diagram.<p>So your comment gives a useful way to think about it, but is not actually literally true.')
