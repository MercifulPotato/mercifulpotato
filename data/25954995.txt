Item(by='danidiaz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611905664, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25954383, text='The same &quot;final state&quot; of a fake can potentially be achieved by many different patterns of interaction between the system under test and the fake.<p>We don&#x27;t really care about those patterns of interaction as long as they end up doing the right thing: allowing the SUT to affect his &quot;outside world&quot; as intended.<p>Making assertions about the exact pattern of interaction (that is: using mocks) is too high granularity, and will make the tests break on valid refactorings. Because we don&#x27;t care about the pattern itself—it&#x27;s just an implementation detail—but about what it achieves.')
