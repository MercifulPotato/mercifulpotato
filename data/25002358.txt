Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604612761, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25002306, text='The tax is based on entities &quot;engaging in business within the City as an administrative office&quot; as defined elsewhere in city law (for the payroll tax component) or just plain &quot;engaging in business in the City&quot; (for the tax on gross receipts attributable to the City portion), not by place of incorporation, so, yes, a business can be incorporated anywhere else on the planet, and do basically all of its activity in San Francisco, but that&#x27;s not going to limit its exposure to the tax.')
