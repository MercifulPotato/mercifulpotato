Item(by='larkeith', descendants=None, kids=[25590644], score=None, time=1609397090, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25588888, text='&gt; &quot;Water is blue because it mirrors the sky.&quot;<p>Seeing that people believe this was quite unexpected, at first - it&#x27;s such an obvious phenomenon looking down into any slightly deep body of water. But on reflection, it makes sense: many (most?) people in the world might live most or all of their lives without going beyond the shorelines of a sea, ocean, or larger lake&#x2F;river.<p>It&#x27;s a good reminder to always be critical of the assumptions you make, and how much they depend on your own context.<p>(If you ever get the opportunity, visit Crater Lake. The drop from blue to midnight to pitch is beautiful.)')
