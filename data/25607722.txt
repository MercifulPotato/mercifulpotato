Item(by='8sleeff9', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609538911, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25607404, text='This was a personal project over the Christmas Holiday period. Got the idea after watching The Crown and not finding anything else like it already online.<p>Used Geni.com for raw genealogy data, Python for data gathering&#x2F;cleaning&#x2F;processing, and D3.js for charting.<p>Each path in the chart corresponds to a person. The path height ordering is the line of succession at that point in time (lowest is monarch). Filled circles represent birth&#x2F;death. Unfilled circles represent legitimate&#x2F;illegitimate dates (e.g. the abdication of Edward VIII).<p>Interact with the chart at <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;british-succession.co.uk" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;british-succession.co.uk</a> (zoom&#x2F;pan&#x2F;hover).<p>Visit <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;jdclarke5&#x2F;british-succession" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;jdclarke5&#x2F;british-succession</a> to see all the code, open source and licensed under GPLv3.')
