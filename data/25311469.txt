Some call it Financially Independent Retire Early abbreviated FIRE.  There&#x27;s a whole community talking about how to get to FIRE and what getting there means for them.  Some have tried the retire early part and it wasn&#x27;t for them, so they have found some work that is fulfilling to them.<p>Bogleheads[0] is a good community, and Reddit has &#x2F;r&#x2F;financialindependence and &#x2F;r&#x2F;fatFIRE  If you search these communities, you&#x27;ll find others asking for similar situations with different numbers.<p>You can now control what you do in your time.  You can use it to improve your health, learn something new, spend time with family, or work on a passion project of yours.<p>If you have questions about how to invest and live off your windfall, Bogleheads is a good place to learn about that as well.<p>[0] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;bogleheads.org" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;bogleheads.org</a>')
