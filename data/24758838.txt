The car&#x27;s in state where is is unusable without intervention from the manufacturer. There&#x27;s no technical difference between this an a &#x27;bricked&#x27; motherboard, cell phone, or other piece of consumer electronics: the hardware is not destroyed, but it&#x27;s unusable unless the manufacturer is willing to fix it.<p>The only difference between this bricked Ferrari and a phone bricked by a bad firmware update is that the Ferrari is worth enough that it&#x27;s worth while for the manufacturer to fix it.')
