Item(by='gwern', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604533356, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24993389, text='But, a fitting homage to Emacs then:<p>&#x27;A cocky novice once said to Stallman: “I can guess why the editor is called Emacs, but why is the justifier called Bolio?”. Stallman replied forcefully, “Names are but names, ‘Emack &amp; Bolio&#x27;s’ is the name of a popular ice cream shop in Boston-town. Neither of these men had anything to do with the software.”<p>His question answered, yet unanswered, the novice turned to go, but Stallman called to him, “Neither Emack nor Bolio had anything to do with the ice cream shop, either.&#x27;')
