When a robinhood user buys a call, a market maker is selling it to them. If the stock runs up or down too much before they can balance their book to be delta neutral, they&#x27;ll lose a lot of money.<p>Rather than risk losing a ton of money, market makers have told the discount brokers that they won&#x27;t be opening any new orders.
