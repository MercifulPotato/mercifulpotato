Item(by='mfontani', descendants=None, kids=[25430574], score=None, time=1608039460, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25429911, text='On Vim, &quot;:set mouse&quot; enables the use of the rodent inside the application. See &quot;:help mouse&quot; for more information.<p>If it&#x27;s set to &quot;n&quot; (for &quot;normal mode&quot;) or &quot;a&quot; (for &quot;all modes&quot;), it pretty much does what you&#x27;re mentioning: one can instantly move the cursor to a given spot; use the scroll wheel to scroll the document, and (via netrw or any other file explorer or side-bar plugin) double-click to open a different file.')
