Most web site content these days are served from the web site owner’s own domain.<p>It’s only if a.com and b.com have (for example) the exact same image URL (c.com&#x2F;img123.jpg) embedded, and you visit both sites, that this cache partitioning will make a difference.<p>In essence, there’s very little legitimate Internet traffic that would be effected by this change, but lots and lots of creepy spyware behaviour will be prevented.')
