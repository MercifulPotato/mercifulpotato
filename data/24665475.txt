It might give you more confidence to pursure your dream to move to Hawaii!<p>Before 1991, Cuban agriculture was heavily based on cash crops and relied on subsidized Soviet oil.  The collapse had severe effects and caused a famine, made worse by state interference.  Despite all this, the ability of the people to quickly adapt away from industrial agriculture to organic and urban farming saved them from mass starvation.  It’s an inspiring story of human adaptability through the most difficult of times.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Special_Period" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.m.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Special_Period</a>')
