They seem to hunt them and would need to live in the beekeeping box as I understand.<p>There is a tiny bit of information on them in german Wikipedia [0] (automatic translation [1], let me know if I can help with translation). Wikipedia says that they can be found worldwide, so maybe they will be of help.<p>(Please note that under _no circumstances_ this was meant as encouragement to introduce new invasive species to your area!)<p>Here is more information: the page of the beekeeper researching pseudoscorpions and bee interactions is at [2], [3] is about a book he wrote, called &quot;Instruction manual for species-appropriate husbandry of bees and pseudoscorpions&quot;.
