Item(by='corty', descendants=None, kids=[25467244, 25467273], score=None, time=1608298581, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25466586, text='Reasonable is relative to what you want to do: Cloud instances are only cheap if your machines are usually idle, and you overprovisioned your datacenter massively. Cloud instances are only cheap if there is no great amount of output&#x2F;input data to transfer like simulation results or large model input datasets. Cloud instances are only cheap if you don&#x27;t need assurances about locality or security for compliance. Cloud instances are only cheap if you can live with a slow network between the instances, as soon as you want Infiniband or at least dedicated Ethernet links, you are either out of luck or in &quot;really expensive&quot; territory again.')
