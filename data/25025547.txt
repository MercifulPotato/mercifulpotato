How much of the drawing&#x27;s ui should be shown vs word processor&#x27;s. It was also a nightmare because unless every user had OpenDoc and the exact same components on the exact same version nothing worked.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;OpenDoc" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;OpenDoc</a>')
