Item(by='toast0', descendants=None, kids=[25889221], score=None, time=1611439619, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25885406, text='It&#x27;s hard to have realtime communications be censorship resistant if there is defacto authority to block them.<p>Terrestrial wired and wireless telecom is out; government can order them to turn off.<p>Broadcast RF is hard, because jamming and locating the source are relatively simple. And taking actions to stop jammers is probably more illegal than the protest.<p>Line of sight optical signaling is probably easy to locate the source of, but could work; otoh, easy to disrupt with smoke, etc.<p>If you can do something with peer to peer radio, that could work, but wifi or bluetooth frequencies are jammable.')
