The problem as given does not describe the general rules by which Monty operates. It describes only a single round of playing the game.<p>Thefefore, Monty could be using the strategy of &quot;if the player chose the car door, open a goat door and give the option to switch. Otherwise don&#x27;t give the option to switch and the player wins the goat.&quot; In that case switching is a losing strategy.')
