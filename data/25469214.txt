Item(by='theamk', descendants=None, kids=[25469916], score=None, time=1608311367, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25468551, text='I disagree with the author when they dismiss Message-ID as unreliable.\nI believe that rather than trying to get everyone to fix POP3, the right way is to download the message again on failure, and de-duplicated based on Message ID.<p>In practice, it is pretty reliable -- even when it is not set by the sender, most MTAs will add it if missing. So your origin &quot;sendmail&quot; will generate one, and failing that, the smart host will, and failing that, recipient&#x27;s MTA will. And the common format often includes GUID or high-precision timestamp, so the collisions are pretty unlikely.')
