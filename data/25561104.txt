Item(by='nostrademons', descendants=None, kids=[25561237, 25561244], score=None, time=1609176808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25561023, text='LVT is a tax only on the <i>unimproved</i> value of the land.  Property improvements are not covered: the land owner gets to keep (or pass onto the renter) 100% of the value of such improvements.  No tax credit is necessary; it&#x27;s a natural consequence of how the tax is structured.')
