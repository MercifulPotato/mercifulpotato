Item(by='inciampati', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602076097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24707486, text='There are many large-scale projects in biology that benefit from open collaboration. In general, these require lots of work and produce useful resources used by a whole field. Insights that could revolutionize a field can sometimes be described in a sentence or two, and reproduced in a few days or weeks, so scientists are more uneasy sharing them.')
