Item(by='Too', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610872891, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25808216, text='Scons is another example of Python as the config language. In fact most of the times you don&#x27;t need a comlicated DSL, or any language at all. Look at Webpack, npm, Android Blueprint or Bazel which more or less is just JSON-flavor, or Rusts Cargo.toml which is just toml markup.<p>You can get very very far with just a list of files and some config properties, if there is something complicated needed in the configuration the markup can refer to an external script.<p>In fact not accidentally mixing the configuration step with the execution step, as Gradle often does, can be considered a feature.')
