Item(by='steerablesafe', descendants=None, kids=[25790691, 25789300], score=None, time=1610706665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25789021, text='I&#x27;m not a filesystem developer, but assuming the corruption is caused by a race condition in the filesystem driver the difference can come down to many aspects:<p>* Different timings accessing a raw device compared to a virtual drive.<p>* The presence of the host scheduler can also alter timings.<p>* CPU configuration on the guest that&#x27;s not typical on a bare metal (single CPU VM?)<p>It&#x27;s not at all obvious that a bug must manifest the same way on a VM than on bare metal, although probably more often than not that&#x27;s the case. If filesystem testing is also done on VMs then that would explain how this slipped through QC.')
