Item(by='jka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605977131, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25169737, text='It seems feasible - the technology components to build a platform like this exist, and the sub-problems (community-building, trust&#x2F;spam, peer review and consensus, supply chain analysis, and so on) have been researched and developed into large-scale production systems.<p>Bringing the pieces together in a robust and scalable way that results in an effective, simple and collaborative product is the challenge.  It&#x27;s potentially more a question of motivation, persistence and focus than technology.<p>As for whether the timing is right for it to succeed - I don&#x27;t know:<p>Some people might not want or find it easy to follow even the best-intended guidance a platform like this could provide, for example if they&#x27;re bound by local economic or cultural habits and constraints.<p>That could lead to social rifts (&quot;we can&#x27;t make these changes&quot; vs &quot;those people won&#x27;t make the changes&quot;) which risks creating undesirable conflict.')
