Item(by='dllthomas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607795520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25399058, text='I agree with the thesis but if you&#x27;re taking this approach with vim it&#x27;s very much worth being aware of how to use the quickfix and&#x2F;or location buffers so you can easily step between the things that you&#x27;ve found.<p>If you put a bunch of location-prefixed lines in a vim buffer (either from running a command within vim or by piping to `vim -`) you can get them in your quickfix buffer with `:cbuffer!` and then step between locations with `:cnext` and `:cprev` (one of the few things my top-level .vimrc does is bind these to tab and shift-tab, respectively).')
