They almost say it in the FAQ:<p>&gt; 2.5 Why not just use a subset of HTTP and HTML?<p>&gt; [...] The problem is that deciding upon a strictly limited subset of HTTP and HTML, slapping a label on it and calling it a day would do almost nothing to create a clearly demarcated space where people can go to consume only that kind of content in only that kind of way.
