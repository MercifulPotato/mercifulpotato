Item(by='caseyscottmckay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602775050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24760038, text='Vary your sentence length. Don&#x27;t follow strict length rules for each sentence, as good writing employs a mix of short, medium, and long sentences. Incorporating variety avoids monotonous writing, creates emphasis where needed, and helps the reader understand the relationship between different points.<p>Write in different environments&#x2F;context (e.g., write drunk, edit sober).<p>Refer to the &quot;The Elements of Style&quot; when in doubt concerning style.')
