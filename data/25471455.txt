Item(by='tomjen3', descendants=None, kids=[25471770], score=None, time=1608323051, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25467548, text='Java is fast enough for most things, but try working with an array of a million objects in C++ vs Java and you will see the difference.<p>In Java you will have to deal with an array with a million pointers to a million objects and in C++ you will deal with a sequential segment of memory that is a million times the size of your object - and mostly in your CPUs cache.
