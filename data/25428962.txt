Item(by='anthony_r', descendants=None, kids=[25434071], score=None, time=1608028808, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25427919, text='In particular you cannot provide an internal SLA on write requests to low-level storage solutions if you can&#x27;t guarantee that you actually have the spare disks.<p>The SLA would have to be something like &quot;we guarantee 99.999% write availability unless team A or team B or team C makes a mistake&quot;, so now you have to look up what team A and B and C are up to and what do they guarantee.
