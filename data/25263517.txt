A section of the podcast is about &quot;Quench Protection&quot;, which relates to what occurs when the superconductor looses its superconductivity and the energy needs to be removed from the system as fast as possible before it melts.<p>From the abstract of &quot;Capacitor bank for Fast Discharge Unit of iter facility&quot; [2]:<p>&gt; In case of failure, in particular quench in a superconducting coil, rather fast and safe energy discharge from the magnetic system is provided by FDU, which breaks the coil supply loop and provides energy dissipation in high energy resistors.
