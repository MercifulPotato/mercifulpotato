Item(by='brandmeyer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602775325, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24789449, text='Not all instructions are re-startable.  ldm&#x2F;stm that writes back to the base register is only re-startable if the write-back and the update to PC are last.<p>The microcontroller-scale cores have a few extra bits in the control&#x2F;status register that govern restart of an interrupted ldm&#x2F;stm or IT (if-then, a form of hammock predication).')
