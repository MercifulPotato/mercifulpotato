Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=[25133100, 25134020], score=None, time=1605671576, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25120207, text='It&#x27;s not really about &#x27;writing skills&#x27;, it&#x27;s &#x27;communication skills&#x27;.<p>&#x27;Writing&#x27; usually implies the ability to be creatively articulate, for example, like writing a long form news article. People can be great writers and poor communicators.<p>For coherent team communications you even don&#x27;t need proper grammar - what you need the ability to be clear, succinct, appropriate, provide context, timely etc..<p>It&#x27;s not about &#x27;writing&#x27; so much as it is &#x27;social&#x27; and &#x27;structured&#x27; communicating.<p>People with strong sensibility for others in a social context will know what needs to be said and not.<p>For example, some people, when reading something ambiguous in an e-mail, will take it the wrong way. Some will not. Some will know when not to write something and when to.<p>Again, this is not a &#x27;writing skill&#x27; per sey more like communication.')
