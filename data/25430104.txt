Item(by='Blikkentrekker', descendants=None, kids=[25431682, 25435410], score=None, time=1608039201, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25429885, text='<i>Kate</i> isn&#x27;t a particularly good editor and it&#x27;s scripting language is quite lacking.<p>The reasons both <i>Emacs</i> and <i>Vim</i> have a <i>Qt</i> version as well instead of purely relying on a terminal protocol are obvious:<p>- ability to have fonts of different sizes<p>- ability to listen to more complicated keyboard shortcuts<p>- cleaner ways of opening popups<p>- ability to combine multiple fonts<p>- support for more colors<p>The downside of such a more complex protocol is that it&#x27;s more involved to tunnel it over a network and doing attaching to <i>X11</i> is also quite a bit more complicated than doing it to a terminal program.')
