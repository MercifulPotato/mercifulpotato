Item(by='detaro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601666972, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24666120, text='A hash function has a limited range of outputs (e.g. for a hashtable it might be a number only a few bits large), whereas the space of possible inputs is larger or even unrestricted - e.g. could be arbitrary text.<p><i>If</i> you have a space of possible inputs that isn&#x27;t larger thant the outputs, then you can indeed design hash functions that do not collide.')
