Item(by='balabaster', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605105711, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25057693, text='The end user being the programmer?<p>I find it humorous that so many programmers still can&#x27;t see past the end of their nose when it comes to designing code for application testing.<p>An application is deliverable. That means testable and deployable with a consistent, repeatable process. Anything short of that is not an application, it&#x27;s a prototype.<p>Yes, it takes longer to deliver up front, but it removes the long tail of maintenance. Getting a low quality product out of the door makes for a bad user experience and garners you a bad reputation.<p>The test suite _should_ be a first class citizen.')
