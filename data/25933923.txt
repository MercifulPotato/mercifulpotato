Here I use it to report mitigations in an old FWTK component that I use for an internal legacy system.<p><pre><code>    $ hardening-check &#x2F;home&#x2F;fwjail&#x2F;usr&#x2F;local&#x2F;etc&#x2F;ftp-gw\n    &#x2F;home&#x2F;fwjail&#x2F;usr&#x2F;local&#x2F;etc&#x2F;ftp-gw:\n    Position Independent Executable: yes\n    Stack protected: yes\n    Fortify Source functions: yes (some protected functions found)\n    Read-only relocations: yes\n    Immediate binding: yes\n\n    $ rpm -qi hardening-check | grep ^URL\n    URL : http:&#x2F;&#x2F;packages.debian.org&#x2F;hardening-wrapper</code></pre>')
