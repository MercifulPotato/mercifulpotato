The human eye has about 500 Mpixels. In a healthy individual, the central yellow spot of maybe 100 Mpixels can be zoomed in with perfect focus on a spot no larger than a few square cm. Sequentially looking at the painting in small patches from very close, you have a similar order of magnitude for the resolution.')
