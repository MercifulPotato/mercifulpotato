Item(by='spooneybarger', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611938064, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25959065, text='It&#x27;s in our tag line. But what OO means varies from person to person. There&#x27;s no set definition of it.<p>Pony features classes and actors are like classes that can receive asynchronous messages.<p>However, unlike some OO, there&#x27;s no inheritance.\nUnlike a lot of other OO there&#x27;s a heavy emphasis on asynchronous messaging.<p>I wouldn&#x27;t use &quot;OO background&quot; as an indicator of what might make Pony harder to learn. I think the key parts are experience with rigorous type systems and experience with concurrency. Without a background in those, your path to learning Pony will be harder than the path for someone who is already familiar with them.')
