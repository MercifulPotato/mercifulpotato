Unfortunately, in modern times that just paints a bigger target on your back.<p>This is why I got out of cybersecurity. And if you are disclosing vulns you found when not actually hired to do so, forget about it - the best you can hope for is a thank you and a bounty that doesn&#x27;t make it worth the time it took. The sky&#x27;s the limit on the worst you can get.<p>The prospects of folks probing systems for the fun of it and disclosing how to secure them better to the owners in order to make the world a better place have become too grim. If you really want to do this, then I recommend you join a security company and do it as an employee to get that protection.<p>Every decade since 1989 I have marveled at how much closer our world has gotten to the world of Shadowrun.
