Item(by='AmericanChopper', descendants=None, kids=[24669605], score=None, time=1601685401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24668395, text='Aspiring writers aspire to make a living off their work. It might be an entirely misguided career path, but they’re not aspiring to be destitute their entire life. Remove ownership of their own work, and the illusion that they’ll ever be able to earn a living from it disappears.<p>The only way to get to the point where you earn a living as a writer, or actor, or film maker, or painter... is to start off by doing it without getting paid. The fact that most people ultimately fail isn’t evidence that they’re not interested in succeeding.')
