Item(by='dsr_', descendants=None, kids=[25765651], score=None, time=1610558096, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25764797, text='Employers give it for one of three reasons:<p>1. It was negotiated as part of your job offer. (It is too late to try to negotiate it at any other time.)<p>2. It provides a lever for future behavior. &quot;By accepting this severance payment, you agree not to sue THE COMPANY for any reason, and not to disparage THE COMPANY, and ...&quot;<p>3. It makes the employer look reasonable to current and future employees. &quot;Laid off 11% of the workplace due to economic conditions; 3 months of severance and various benefits&quot; is much more conducive to hiring than &quot;Laid off 11% of the workforce with no notice, no severance, no nothing&quot;.')
