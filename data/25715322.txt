Item(by='Blikkentrekker', descendants=None, kids=[25715460], score=None, time=1610291395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25715135, text='I&#x27;d rather fancy a system where such platforms can choose between being a publisher and a platform.<p>If they be a publisher, they may take editorial control but the also become liable for anything that escaped their control and can no longer claim “The views expressed here are not our own.”.<p>In the alternative, they can be a platform, and may not take editorial control and only be required and allowed to remove content they are legally required to remove.<p>The current situation is that they want their cake and eat it too.')
