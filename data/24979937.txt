Item(by='volument', descendants=None, kids=[24980171], score=None, time=1604413962, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979167, text='Cookies are not an issue for GDPR, it&#x27;s all about respecting users&#x27; privacy. In fact you can freely store anonymous data to cookies, localStorage, and sessionStorage without issues. The problem comes when you are dealing with personally identifiable information such as permanent identifiers.<p>You definitely need a &quot;cookie banner&quot; when using Simple Analytics, Fathom, or Plausible. Any service that accesses the device information such as the URL needs a permission from the user according the ePrivacy directive.<p>We have consulted EU law specialists when building our upcoming analytics service that is as privacy-friendly as Simple Analytics, while still measuring important things like retention and conversions.
