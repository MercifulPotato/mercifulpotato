Item(by='pron', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607098435, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25303792, text='It answers the question with a &quot;hello, world.&quot; To do more sophisticated stuff, like what you want, you&#x27;ll need to replace or wrap the standard single-thread Executor with your own Executor. There is exactly one method you need to implement. For example:<p><pre><code>    Executor ste = Executors.newSingleThreadExecutor();\n    Executor myExecutor = task -&gt; {\n      if (task instanceof Thread.VirtualThreadTask vtt) System.out.println(&quot;Scheduling &quot; + vtt.thread() + &quot; on &quot; + Thread.currentThread());\n      ste.execute(task);\n      if (task instanceof Thread.VirtualThreadTask vtt) System.out.println(&quot;Descheduled &quot; + vtt.thread() + &quot; from &quot; + Thread.currentThread());\n    }\n    var myThreadFactory = Thread.builder().virtual(myExecutor).factory();</code></pre>')
