Item(by='rcoc', descendants=None, kids=[25702279], score=None, time=1610211170, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25700135, text='I am working on a tool called Playbook (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;useplaybook.app" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;useplaybook.app</a>). Playbook is an application that allows anyone who presents product demos to tell a more complete, integrated story.<p>One of the primary features of many SaaS products is how they integrate smoothly with other platforms customers are already using, like Salesforce or Slack. However, in a demo environment, it can be difficult to show that workflow in a way that doesn&#x27;t require flipping between persona accounts and taking precious time to generate the activity. Playbook hooks into a demoer&#x27;s demo accounts and facilitates creating &quot;plays&quot;, automated workflows between any number of SaaS apps that show off your product&#x27;s integrations. It also supports working with multiple personas to create lifelike collaboration instantly.<p>There are still many kinks to work out but this is the first step in building Demo Engineering-as-a-service.')
