Item(by='blensor', descendants=None, kids=[25449564], score=None, time=1608156288, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25449079, text='We are developing an open source VR fitness game and after almost a year of exercising im VR I can tell you that in my case the sweat inside of your headset is not a problem but you should use the right VR cover. The stock ones of the Quest are very hard to keep clean.<p>The sweat on the rest of your body is a completely different issue. After about 40 minutes of VRWorkout I usually look like coming fresh out of the shower. Without VR I could never build up the willpower to &quot;grind on&quot; for so long.<p>Our game does not try to put the game aspect first but the workout. The game elements are subordinate to the movements if your body and the muscles you are using not the other way around.<p>That&#x27;s something we have can&#x27;t compromise on since the movements that are expected from the player (jumping, pushups, crunches, burpees) should not be compromised by trying to achieve some arbitrary goal like hitting an enemy.<p>Here is a video of this crazy idea <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;mFYzaQzsUI4" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;mFYzaQzsUI4</a><p>[1] VRWorkout <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vrworkout.at" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;vrworkout.at</a>')
