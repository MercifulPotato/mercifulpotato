Item(by='runarberg', descendants=None, kids=[25611169], score=None, time=1609563480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25609948, text='In behavioral science it is well documented that punishment is an ineffective driver for behavioral modification. In fact, for punishment to work at all it has to be consistent and immediate. Prison is neither.<p>So our best theory of behavioral modification states that prisons is not a deterrent for crime, nor is there any evidence for it in the records. All the while prisons are an inhumane and terrible way to treat people. Can we just please abolish prisons.')
