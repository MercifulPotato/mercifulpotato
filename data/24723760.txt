Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602189890, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24723318, text='&gt; The Supreme Court is only the last word on constitutional cases.<p>No more so than statutory or any other cases; just as regulation can be changed by the executive and statute can be changed by the Congress, the Constitution can be amended by the process set out in the document itself.<p>The Supreme Court is the ultimate arbiter of the <i>current state</i> of the law, but no law that it applies, including the Constitution, is immutable.')
