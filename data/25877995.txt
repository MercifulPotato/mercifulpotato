Item(by='maxerickson', descendants=None, kids=[25878401, 25878821], score=None, time=1611360013, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25877925, text='These discussions about materials are often difficult, because there are meaningfully different processes that are lumped together in everyday language.<p>Mechanical stresses leading to failure at a particular point are certainly a form of degradation, but I believe they mean degradation in the sense where the entire body of the material is undergoing some change (like a plastic bottle fogging or such).<p>I think seeking clear descriptions is fine, treating it as a &quot;gotcha&quot; game is tedious and unnecessary.')
