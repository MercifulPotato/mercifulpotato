Item(by='redis_mlc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607919262, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25412128, text='The SR-71 used a different jet fuel than normal, special engines, and likely required a lot of maintenance.<p>The SR-71 program was cancelled because satellites were good enough, and the above maintenance plus over a dozen dedicated tanker airplanes (for the special fuel) made them a headache to operate.<p>One of the most impressive things to me about the program was that after a certain date, all flights were done in radio silence, including multiple refuelings. A TLA told them that the radio calls were being tracked by Russian trawlers around the world.')
