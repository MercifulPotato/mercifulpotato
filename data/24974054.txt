Item(by='zaroth', descendants=None, kids=[24974465], score=None, time=1604354978, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24972489, text='It is logically impossible to prove a negative. The burden of proof can only possibly be on the people claiming there <i>is</i> an effect.<p>To put it technically, it is &quot;inappropriate to draw substantive conclusions on the basis of a lack of statistically significant effects.&quot; You are not proving the null-hypothesis, you are failing to demonstrate the alternative hypothesis.<p>Or to put it another way, lack of evidence is not evidence that a thing doesn&#x27;t exist.')
