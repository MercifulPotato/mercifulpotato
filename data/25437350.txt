Item(by='telotortium', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608078072, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25432890, text='The exceptions are easier to get now with the passage of Prop 19 this year. The summary on Ballotpedia (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ballotpedia.org&#x2F;California_Proposition_19,_Property_Tax_Transfers,_Exemptions,_and_Revenue_for_Wildfire_Agencies_and_Counties_Amendment_(2020)" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ballotpedia.org&#x2F;California_Proposition_19,_Property_...</a>) is pretty good:<p>- allow eligible homeowners to transfer their tax assessments anywhere within the state and allow tax assessments to be transferred to a more expensive home with an upward adjustment;<p>- increase the number of times that persons over 55 years old or with severe disabilities can transfer their tax assessments from one to three;<p>- require that inherited homes that are not used as principal residences, such as second homes or rentals, be reassessed at market value when transferred; and<p>- allocate additional revenue or net savings resulting from the ballot measure to wildfire agencies and counties.')
