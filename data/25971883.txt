Every one of them has come to that opinion via hard won experience dealing with real world problems they encountered.</i><p>They may not realize they have them - at runtime, in the object graph, possibly indirectly. There&#x27;s little difference in principle between cycles in &quot;static&quot; code vs. runtime state, but we often can&#x27;t express them in the former, because our languages don&#x27;t specify the concept of dependency at enough granularity (and some rely on a linear compilation pass).')
