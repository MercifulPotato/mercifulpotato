Item(by='Viliam1234', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605658724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25117100, text='A friend of mine who tried different working schedules described it like this: With five-day workweek, the weekend is not long enough to recover. With four-day workweek, the weekend is enough to fully recover. With three-day workweek, you have enough energy during the weekend to start working on your own project. With two-day workweek, the work no longer makes you tired, it is as if you are on a perpetual vacation.')
