I think we should make it easier for people to come and work in the US.<p>The existing H1B system works pretty well for employers and employees.  When I hear people describe H1B immigrants as somehow taken advantage of, their experience seems far different than mine (I have never seen people mistreated by the system).<p>The idea that this system makes it harder for native software engineers seems crazy to me.
