Item(by='nextweek2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604687970, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25000403, text='A better approach is to have a reverse proxy in a difference account&#x2F;hosting provider. That way the take-down is of the proxy which should be minimal config and no data.<p>Setting up another reverse proxy in the event of a take-down should be minimal effort.<p>It then becomes a game of whack a mole if anybody wants to take down a service. I assume this is how The Pirate Bay stayed up for so long.<p>Downside is doubling bandwidth costs.')
