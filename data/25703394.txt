Item(by='baybal2', descendants=None, kids=[25703499], score=None, time=1610218975, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25701959, text='There is a third option on the table! Using DMA controller.<p>People say what&#x27;s the difference in between copying memory by CPU, or by DMA controller? The difference is exactly that.<p>You rarely need that, but in some cases you have:<p>1. You do very long copies, and want to have full CPU performance.<p>2. You do very long copies, and want to have caches to not be flushed during it.<p>3. You care about power consumption, as DMA controller may let the CPU core to enter low power mode quicker.<p>4. On some CPU architectures, you can get wildly spread out pages quicker than with CPU&#x2F;software.')
