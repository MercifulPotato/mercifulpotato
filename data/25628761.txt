Item(by='gabriel0r', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609747615, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622233, text='Even though there are hundreds of note-taking and to-do apps, I could not find one that combines notes and to-dos properly. I ended up using separate tools for notes and to-do&#x27;s. Therefore, I decided to create a product that combines the best of both worlds.<p>A few things make Organizedly stand out:<p>1. Notes, tasks, and a calendar are tied closely together\n2. Drag-and-drop tasks from your todo-list directly to your calendar.\n3. You can write todo&#x27;s inside a note, in a todo-list, or the calendar and it is automatically synced across the board.\n4. Queries that show notes dynamically based on their content.\n5. Graph view that shows how notes are related to each other. You can traverse the graph by clicking on the notes.')
