Item(by='musicale', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607492858, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25328375, text='Yeah, I don&#x27;t know why companies do it because it is so counterproductive and damaging: it reduces quality and increases time to completion while harming employee health.<p>Very brief crunches can maybe push you over a deadline but will (in my experience) result in lower overall productivity per hour (and higher defect rates) for the entire crunch + recovery interval.<p>Which seems to mean do a two week crunch and then fire your developers, but replacing them is usually more costly than recovery time.<p>It probably isn&#x27;t a coincidence that universities often have two week final exam&#x2F;final project periods followed by two (or more) weeks of vacation.')
