It&#x27;s a codename for the evolution of their CPU architecture.<p>Zen, Zen+, Zen 2 and Zen 3 are the generations of CPU architecture.<p>Ryzen is the consumer brand of CPUs. Geared towards anyone who doesn&#x27;t specifically need Threadripper.<p>Threadripper is the enthusiast&#x2F;workstation brand of (rather) high core count CPUs. If you run software that uses a lot of cores, you should know whether this is the right line of CPUs for you.')
