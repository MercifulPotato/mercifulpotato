There was a lawsuit in 2019 but I couldn&#x27;t find any updates since then.<p>Two separate accidents in 2018-2019, Model S crashed, fire started, owner (and passenger in the second case) survived the crash but couldn&#x27;t get out of the car because the flush electronic door handles didn&#x27;t unlock and present themselves out. The police officer on scene couldn&#x27;t open it to get them out. Both cases everyone inside the car burnt to death [1][2][3].<p>In all modern cars, including the Model S [4] the doors are supposed to automatically unlock on a crash or airbag deployment.
