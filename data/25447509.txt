Item(by='mrich', descendants=None, kids=[25448336], score=None, time=1608147870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25445493, text='The most important thing on your path to becoming a software engineer is building stuff, improving it, releasing it, and thinking how you can do it better. Having been through the lifecycle of a piece of software from design to maintenance will also provide a wealth of experience.<p>I understand that it&#x27;s popular to let people solve toy problems during interviews, and think that&#x27;s ok for people coming out of university. However for more senior roles I would rather hand out a coding assignment. You can judge the experience of a developer much better by the way she&#x2F;he chooses algorithms, considers corner cases, supportability etc.')
