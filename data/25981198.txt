Item(by='RickS', descendants=None, kids=[25990305], score=None, time=1612117797, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25980267, text='The weaponization of detail.<p>Beyond a certain breadth, an overload of specificity becomes too vague to engage with. Claims that cover such wide ground are impossible to refute or dissect.<p>The people who do it think they&#x27;re well-informed and providing a service, but it&#x27;s poor form. It forces readers into a poison choice: abandon nuance to pile on in blanket agreement, or bash their heads against each other bickering in 50 threads about 50 tiny points for eternity.<p>No good comes from that.')
