Item(by='hedgehog', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605030973, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25047802, text='Four parts: 1) the bulk of returns are the few best performing investments, 2) there are material fixed costs in carrying an investment (partner attention, conflict management, admin overhead), 3) it&#x27;s a way to get proprietary insight into a new space, and 4) there&#x27;s value in founder relationships (deal referrals, recruiting). Large funds write small checks to get an early view into promising companies that are too early for bigger investments. They are aware that many won&#x27;t out but they believe they are better served getting in early &amp; divesting most vs waiting and having less information or access.')
