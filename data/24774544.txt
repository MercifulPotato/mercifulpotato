Item(by='Cthulhu_', descendants=None, kids=[24774954, 24777970], score=None, time=1602665457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24773294, text='I think it&#x27;s not really microservices you pine for, but it&#x27;s being able to untangle said ball of mud into discrete and - more importantly - manageable codebases.<p>100K LOC is hard to work with, but 10K is manageable.<p>I do think that&#x27;s why developers idealize the microservices architecture; they don&#x27;t want to have to keep 100K LOC in their head, or work with 10 other developers; they want to tend to their own 10K LOC garden, they want to isolate a problem space and focus on that, instead of the big picture which is too much for any one person to deal with.')
