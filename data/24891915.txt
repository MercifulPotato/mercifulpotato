Item(by='thespoonbends', descendants=None, kids=[24892098], score=None, time=1603675484, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887521, text='After disliking it for 6-7 years, because it doesn&#x27;t offer the amount of abstraction that others languages offer (Scala, Haskell, Java), Go has grown on me.<p>I got too caught up in designing elaborate abstractions in those languages. I couldn&#x27;t avoid it either, since other libraries would also use elaborate abstractions.<p>With Go, I just write plain dull code, against a suite of good dull libraries. Ultimately I spend less time writing programs (typically internet services) in Go than other languages.')
