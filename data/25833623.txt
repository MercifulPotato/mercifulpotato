When an old product is deprecated, it&#x27;s often cheaper for a big manufacturer to get rid of the equipment and buy new for the new product line, find out where obsolete equipment is going. It&#x27;s not worth paying $100&#x2F;hr for an integrator to repurpose with no warranty, but at hobbyist $100&#x2F;week budgets that scrapyard is full of treasure. Some manufacturers and integrators will have &quot;boneyards&quot; of old equipment that they&#x27;ll purge on occasion.<p>The article said that Teknik Clearpath servos are considered a great value, and they are, but even their smallest fractional HP 24V motors cost more than some name brand Allen Bradley (unofficial slogan &quot;you can get better but you can&#x27;t pay more!&quot;) 3-phase servos at my local supplier: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.surplusindustrialsupply.com&#x2F;motors-mot.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.surplusindustrialsupply.com&#x2F;motors-mot.html</a><p>These places are like a garage sale for industrial lego.')
