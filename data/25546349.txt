Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609015750, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25545635, text='The question of nominal versus PPP GDP is a bit complicated, but a basic rule of thumb is that nominal GDP is better if you&#x27;re measuring trade (since the nominal rate is the actual value you&#x27;ll get during international exchange transactions), whereas PPP works better for individual cost-of-living or quality-of-life metrics.<p>For measuring gross economy size, I would go with nominal GDP.')
