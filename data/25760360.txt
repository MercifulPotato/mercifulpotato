Item(by='w_t_payne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610534334, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25760097, text='The problem is that land is not subject to the same laws of supply and demand as other goods and services. It&#x27;s very hard to make new land (unless you&#x27;re Dutch), and very hard to move residential capacity around in response to fluctuating demand. The alternative isn&#x27;t necessarily rent control. The smart people in this forum could probably come up with some market-oriented system which would dramatically improve the flexibility and efficiency of residential real estate supply.')
