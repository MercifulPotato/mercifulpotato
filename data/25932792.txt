Item(by='luma', descendants=None, kids=[25932845, 25933002, 25932857], score=None, time=1611776049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25932721, text='A free market involves a customer that has the option to simply not buy what is on offer.  Healthcare can never be a free market in this sense, because the customer is faced with a choice of &quot;buy service and&#x2F;or product&quot; or &quot;be dead&quot;.<p>That is entirely unlike the choice involved in buying something like a refrigerator and seriously skews the applicability of traditional free market thinking to the problem.')
