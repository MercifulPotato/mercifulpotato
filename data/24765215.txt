Intentionally taking someone else&#x27;s work and passing it off as your own is bad juju through and through.<p>And even if I kept my code under copyright with an attribution license, not all forms of plagiarism would necessarily violate the copyright. For example, ideas and facts generally can&#x27;t be copyrighted, but one could imagine cases where they can be plagiarized.<p>So even if I did use a license that requires attribution, that only works insomuch as plagiarism is a violation of copyright. And as we all know, there is a ton of grey area there.
