Honestly the main point of knowing about Hegel anyway is because he deeply influenced Marx, Kierkegaard, Sartre, and many other philosophers that you might actually <i>want</i> to read and apply to your life and worldview. Peter Singer’s <i>Hegel: A Very Short Introduction</i> is short, clear, and has all the information you’d need (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.amazon.com&#x2F;Hegel-Short-Introduction-Peter-Singer&#x2F;dp&#x2F;019280197X&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.amazon.com&#x2F;Hegel-Short-Introduction-Peter-Singer...</a>).')
