Item(by='imiric', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608146749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25441213, text='My favorite WM! I&#x27;ve been using it for years now, after trying i3, herbstluft, xmonad, qtile and a bunch of others.<p>It&#x27;s very lightweight and highly customizable. The configuration file is just a shell script of bspc (client) commands. It doesn&#x27;t handle keybindings on its own, but leverages another awesome tool by the same author: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;baskerville&#x2F;sxhkd" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;baskerville&#x2F;sxhkd</a>\nWhich also simply calls bspc, but can be used for generic hotkeys as well.<p>It&#x27;s just an awesomely simple pair of tools that work great, stay out of my way, are rock solid and well maintained. Pure gems of the open source world. Please consider donating to support the author: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;baskerville&#x2F;bspwm&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;doc&#x2F;CONTRIBUTING.md#donations" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;baskerville&#x2F;bspwm&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;doc&#x2F;CONTRIB...</a> (I have no affiliation, just a very happy user.)<p>This WM is the only thing besides habit keeping me from switching to Wayland. Though I guess I could get used to Sway.')
