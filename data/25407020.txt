Item(by='wanderingstan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607871921, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403240, text='Related, I’ve often thought that good listening involves <i>becoming</i>—in some small way—the person you are listening to. That is, your brain and your “self” are temporarily loaned to the person.<p>Such empathy is a sort of self-expansion, as you learn to see the world from their point of view. And the speaker, in turn, can learn from your observations of what it’s like to inhabit their life for a time.')
