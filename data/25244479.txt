Item(by='lotsofpulp', descendants=None, kids=[25247572], score=None, time=1606648862, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25243930, text='Are markups and margins not the same thing?<p>If they aren’t, what’s the difference?<p>If they are, then a markup isn’t a function of cost, it’s more accurately a function of pricing power, or how much your customers are willing to pay you over a competitor.<p>Best Buy can’t markup more because people will buy from Amazon or Walmart or Costco or Target or Aliexpress.  Apple can markup more because people can’t get a similar product elsewhere for cheaper.')
