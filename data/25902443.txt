Item(by='alohapersona', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611578254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25902294, text='Normally, when the deadline is extended, this is correctly reported on the Companies House website.<p>One has to apply for deadline extension before the deadline. Between 25 March 2020 and 25 June 2020, every application that mentions COVID-19 as a reason was accepted. Additionally, all companies with accounts due date between 26 March 2020 and 29 September 2020 automatically got an extension under the Corporate Insolvency and Governance Bill.<p>There are also all kinds of temporary changes to deadlines, the one that applies here is 1.4 from <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gov.uk&#x2F;government&#x2F;publications&#x2F;the-companies-etc-filing-requirements-temporary-modifications-regulations-2020&#x2F;temporary-changes-to-companies-house-filing-requirements" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.gov.uk&#x2F;government&#x2F;publications&#x2F;the-companies-etc...</a> under which is exactly the 29 October 2020 listed on Companies House website.')
