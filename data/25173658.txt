That includes direct taxation and inflation (under a monopolistic currency dictated by the government).<p>You can only justify taxation under an authoritarian standpoint (eg. &quot;we <i>must</i> tax people so that...&quot;). Going that way, whoever has more guns wins the argument.<p>Making a relative assessment of which taxation scheme is more &quot;fair&quot; seems to be pointless to me, in the same way it would be pointless to discuss what crime would be preferable given the choice of rape or murder.')
