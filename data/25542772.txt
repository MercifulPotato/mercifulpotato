Item(by='iggldiggl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608982122, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25540250, text='I have no opinion on the merits of Git-style branches vs. Mercurial-style branches (mostly because I haven&#x27;t really used the latter, either), but Mercurial has supported Git-style branching (termed &quot;bookmarks&quot;) for quite some time now, too.<p>Other than that, I like not having to deal with the additional complication of Git&#x27;s staging area in Mercurial (anything I personally did with the staging area I can replicate much better with Mercurial interactive commit feature), plus Mercurial&#x27;s evolve extension, which turns commit amending, rebasing and all other kinds of history editing into non-destructive operations.')
