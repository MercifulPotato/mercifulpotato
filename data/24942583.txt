Item(by='enriquepablo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604068234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24941960, text='I would say that you are mixing up &quot;parsing&quot; and &quot;calendaring (or something of the sort)&quot;. As far as I understand parsing is syntactic analysis, i.e. going from a linear structure to a more complex structure (usually a tree); it should not add to the tree anything that was not in the linear structure. It shouldn&#x27;t consider a <i>semantic</i> context (such as the current date) to produce an ast.')
