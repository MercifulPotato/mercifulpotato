But why not just use signed binaries?   Have a user editable keystore that includes the accepted signatures.  The default would be apple, installing chrome would require accepting the Google signature, photoshop would require the adobe key.  Then users could add their own for brew, firefox, or whatever community software they would use.<p>This would give good protection against hackers, and not require uploading a signature for every binary run.')
