On advice of my employer&#x27;s IT department I went from &quot;spinning up new VMs in VirtualBox reliably leads to thermal excess, CPU throttling, and total system shutdown&quot; to a system that <i>actually works</i> -- just by moving the dock connection to the right side.<p>It&#x27;s a little funny because the advice used to be you should use the left-side USB-C ports first because they were faster (both for data and charge, IIRC?)')
