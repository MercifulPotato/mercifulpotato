Item(by='totalZero', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611199264, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25853544, text='Unfortunately, gatekeeping is essential to academia. Ask anyone who wants to publish a finding independently, or read a paper for free without being associated with an institution. Heck, ask the undergrads who paid tens of thousands of dollars for Zoom classes that have MOOC alternatives alternatives.<p>&gt; Too little gatekeeping means trouble finding the signal through the noise and time wasted on dead ends.<p>Much time is supposed to be spent on dead ends anyway. That&#x27;s the way the corpus matures, not to mention an inevitable result of peer review (no need to review anything if all the research product is perfect). Research is a <i>search</i>; imagine running BFS without expecting to encounter dead ends.')
