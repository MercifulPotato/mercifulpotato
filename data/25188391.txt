But Panzer General, with its eagerness to embed me in the role of a German general goose-stepping and kowtowing to his Führer, is a bridge too far for me.<p>The &quot;ethics&quot; in the title seemed to me to be referring less to the personal ethics involved in playing the game than to the ethics of reinforcing the &quot;clean Wehrmacht&quot; myth.')
