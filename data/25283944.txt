Item(by='brandmeyer', descendants=None, kids=[25284172], score=None, time=1606957188, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25283845, text='Balls drawn in any order, without replacement.<p>The probability of guessing all 6 balls in a single lottery is 1 in (50 ncr 6).  So, the probability of losing is 1 - 1&#x2F;(50 ncr 6).  The probability of losing every time is (1 - 1&#x2F;(50 ncr 6)^(n_games), where n_games = 365 * 1000.  Therefore, the probability of winning at least one game is (1 - (1 - 1&#x2F;(50 ncr 6)^(n_games)).')
