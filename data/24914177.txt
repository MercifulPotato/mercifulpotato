Item(by='emptybits', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603850146, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24913498, text='This may help you picture it.<p>&quot;In the A600 the CPU is soldered directly to the mainboard and cannot be removed. Thus the only way to install it is to clip it over the CPU, using a PLCC socket placed upside-down. PLCC sockets are not meant to be used this way, so this is really a bit of a hack and might not even work in all cases. Space around the CPU is quite limited, hence the odd shape of this board.&quot;<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;SukkoPera&#x2F;OpenAmiga600FastRamExpansion" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;SukkoPera&#x2F;OpenAmiga600FastRamExpansion</a><p>Definitely not contemplated during the design&#x2F;spec of the PLCC socket, I&#x27;m sure!')
