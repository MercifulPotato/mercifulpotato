Item(by='Context_free', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607226079, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25319789, text='It takes some tricky math to get where military spending is only 12% of federal spending.<p>First, a soldier wounded in Afghanistan and being treated in a VA hospital would have to have that cost excluded from the budget.<p>The department of energy dealing with nuclear weapon waste is excluded from that budget.<p>The office of a foreign US embassy serving as cover for a CIA case officer is excluded from that budget.<p>Interest on military deficit spending from last year is excluded from that budget.<p>In fact, add all these things up which don&#x27;t fall within this theoretical 12% and it comes to hundreds of billions of dollars.')
