You will sit on data and code until you are ready to publish, sometimes for years.<p>Datasets and analytical code must be regarded as a scientific achievement on its on, albeit not as substitute for the &quot;full&quot; substantive analysis in a paper.<p>A social science PhD in Denmark, for example, must write two to four peer reviewed papers at the moment. The option to substitute one of the articles with a data set?<p>Things get better though: People nowadays at least understand my citations better, since I cite at least the explicitly included R-packages used in my work.')
