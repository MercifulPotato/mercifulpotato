Don&#x27;t forget that a lot of C (and increasingly a lot of Rust) is written for embedded targets where that is usually not true (sometimes address 0 is the start of flash).<p>The only things you can rely on are what the C Spec says you can rely on, and that does not include the behaviour of address 0.')
