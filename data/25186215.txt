Item(by='pjc50', descendants=None, kids=[25186857], score=None, time=1606135923, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25185827, text='We could do with different words for these. You&#x27;re right that from the perspective of both electronics engineers and sociologists there are all sorts of &quot;control systems&quot; that affect behaviour. It&#x27;s the great achievement of Weberist &quot;modern&quot; societies to take control away from individual chains of authority (feudalism) and into impersonal systems (bureaucracy, police, capitalism). But the individual lived experience of being controlled can still be oppressive.<p>We swing backwards and forwards between trying to hold individuals accountable for system-caused behaviour vs trying to change &quot;the system&quot; which is both huge and thwarted by individual actions.')
