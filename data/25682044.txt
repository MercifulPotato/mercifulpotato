So you are paying a lot of money for insurance you hope to never use, and in the event you need some kind of basic care you are still paying out - and have no meaningful negotiating power. I’m under the impression in most countries with single-payer &#x2F; government run health schemes, you might also pay a great deal for your coverage, but that it’s all inclusive.')
