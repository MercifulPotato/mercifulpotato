Item(by='pc86', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611148824, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25845570, text='Whether an individual pardon is good or bad certainly depends on how you feel about the crime committed by the pardoned, and whether we want to admit it or not, at least a little bit on the party of the President who pardons them. Even if you could objectively measure the damage done by any individual pardon (you can&#x27;t), the good in aggregate outweighs the bad, especially of any one case that we disagree with but 10,000 equally reasonable and intelligent people may think is great.')
