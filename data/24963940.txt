Item(by='pmiller2', descendants=None, kids=[24964166], score=None, time=1604278263, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24962627, text='LaTeX works great for just writing words, too.<p><pre><code>    \\documentclass{article} \n    \\begin{document} \n        &lt;your words here&gt; \n    \\end {document}\n</code></pre>\nis pretty lightweight and distraction-free.  Substitute the appropriate documentclass for whatever thing you&#x27;re working on (book, report, whatever), and it will just work, provided you&#x27;re willing to accept a lot of default formatting.<p>Once you get into twiddling formatting, then you&#x27;re back in the realm of non-distraction-free writing, but that&#x27;s not the tool&#x27;s fault.')
