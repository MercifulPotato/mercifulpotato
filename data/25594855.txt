Item(by='mceachen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609436299, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25590623, text='That article addresses how to do a &quot;scrubbable&quot; infinite scroll for their timeline view.<p>Rendering correctly-scaled images is a different issue: Google Photos renders the image to the precise viewport dimensions (which is why it takes sometimes a couple seconds to render).<p>When I designed PhotoStructure, I knew I wanted everything to show instantly, so resizing is done to several common viewport sizes at import time (and these sizes are customizable). When you&#x27;re browsing your library, your browser fetches the smallest variant that will still show with high quality based on native display resolution.')
