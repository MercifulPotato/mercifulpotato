It always seemed to be one of those weird things that keeps happening despite nobody being willing to stand up for it. I think I see now, that to the people who consider themselves the self-appointed guardians of Wikipedia&#x27;s quality, each extra page spreads the work of guarding a little thinner.<p>The counter to which is, of course, that Wikipedia doesn&#x27;t have to be run by a relatively-tiny number of highly-dedicated editors.
