Item(by='wott', descendants=None, kids=[25782016, 25782044], score=None, time=1610655256, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25781275, text='Yep. Languages with a simplistic syntax (and a simplistic paradigm) make writing compilers for those languages easy, but they do not make creating programs in the languages easy. We can think of Lisp, or even more of Forth. It is also a common characteristic of &quot;esoteric&quot; languages: providing very little syntax, very few keywords, instructions and ready-built features, is an ingredient of their recipe for obfuscation.')
