Item(by='scott_s', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603293197, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24847188, text='Agreed. But! Not all architectures implement atomic operations by locking the bus, as x86 does. The Power architecture uses load-linked, stored-conditional (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Load-link&#x2F;store-conditional" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Load-link&#x2F;store-conditional</a>) where when you load a value, you can say &quot;pay attention to who modifies this value,&quot; and when you go to store the value, you can say &quot;only actually store this value if no one else modified it.&quot;')
