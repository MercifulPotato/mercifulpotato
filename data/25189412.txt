Item(by='legitster', descendants=None, kids=[25192709], score=None, time=1606153683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25189059, text='A lot of it is second-mover advantage.<p>Microsoft spent the better part of a decade trying to make tablets work. They poured a ton of money and resources into getting manufacturers on board, developing drivers, refining UIs. Most of it was terrible and clunky, and they had to include increasingly niche features to make them work for the enterprise clients they could land.<p>The whole time, Apple could afford to wait when doing their own R&amp;D. They could jump in when the hardware was available and affordable without compromises.<p>Nearly every major product they develop follows this model.')
