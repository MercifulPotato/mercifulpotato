There are probably other esoteric cases that I can&#x27;t think of off the top of my head. &quot;Qty&quot; is often prefixed, for instance. It&#x27;s a complicated problem for a natural language calculator, to be sure. Here are a couple examples of ways to represent four thousand Canadian dollars that you might encounter and have no trouble interpreting as a human. This could be a good target for an ML tokenizer...<p>-$4000CAD<p>CA$-4,000.00<p>4.000,00CAD$')
