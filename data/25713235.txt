Item(by='cforrester', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610275023, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25712066, text='It seems misguided to suggest that deplatforming is not a constructive approach. Platforms which are tolerant of extremism enable extremists to more-easily connect with each other, develop more-unified messaging, and reach non-radicalized people at much lower social and financial costs. In effect, providing a platform for extremism <i>is</i> a divisive act.')
