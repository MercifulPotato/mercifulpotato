Item(by='koyote', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608153111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25429287, text='Best way between London and Amsterdam is the ferry!<p>It&#x27;s not quite as seamless as a direct train but still very enjoyable:<p>You take a train in the evening from London to the ferry terminal in Harwich, then overnight ferry in a private cabin (it&#x27;s a big ferry with restaurant and bar etc.) to a port in southern Holland. Then a train from there to Amsterdam.<p>The train tickets are part of the ferry price (rail and sail) and you get into Amsterdam nice and early.')
