Item(by='ddingus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607845635, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25397379, text='Current death rate is computed:<p>Dead people &#x2F; Recovered people and that is around 3 percent, down from 5 to 7 percent a few months ago.<p>Infections &#x2F; deaths is not accurate as cases in progress are added to those with definitive outcomes.<p>That can also be seen as a best case, given nobody currently infected does not die.<p>A worst case can be seen assuming everyone infected will die.<p>But if we want to know the percentage of people who get sick and die, we have to compute that percentage with the people who got sick and did not die and their case ran its course to a definitive outcome.<p>Infections lead to outcomes.<p>Outcomes are where the dead vs not dead become known.<p>Everyone infected will eventually arrive at an outcome.')
