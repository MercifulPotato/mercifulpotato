Item(by='permo-w', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604512881, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24988089, text='I think Asimov explored this idea a little in one of his short stories.<p>I think there should be scientists and science writers. These writers should be employed by research institutions. Their job should be to understand the concepts and explain them in simple words.<p>I feel this would give the science world a lot more accessibility.<p>My problems with science papers have always been that the statistics have been written incomprehensibly (no layman knows what a p-number is) and that they repeat themselves so much that half the paper is just nothing')
