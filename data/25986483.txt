Do you have any case law to support that position?<p><i>See</i> Ford Motor Co. v. Summit Motor Products, Inc., 930 F.2d 277, 291 (3d Cir. 1991), <i>cert. denied</i> 502 U.S. 939 (1991); Madrid v. Chronicle Books, Pixar, 209 F. Supp. 2d 1227, 1237 n. 5 (D. Wyo. 2002); Micro Consulting, Inc. v. Zubeldia, 813 F. Supp. 1514, 1531 (W.D.Okla. 1990)(&quot;a derivative work does not [implicate the underlying copyright] unless it has been substantially <i>copied</i> from the preexisting work&quot; (emphasis added)).')
