You can find benzene rings as a substructure of some amino acids all over your proteins doesn&#x27;t mean eating protein will poison you with benzene...<p>Sometimes just as little as a change in the spatial configuration of a compound (cis&#x2F;trans isomers for example) or the addition of a carbon can dramatically change the effect of a molecule on a living body...')
