Item(by='nradov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604421637, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24981128, text='There is no real evidence of a significant herd immunity effect in Sweden yet. Swedish government policy has consistently focused on sustainable pandemic control measures based on the assumption that the virus will be with us for a long time to come. Their low death rate over the past 3 months is probably mostly a result of effective measures to protect the elderly. And the harvesting effect: many of the frailest people already died during the first wave.')
