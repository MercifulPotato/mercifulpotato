Home sizes increasing doesn’t mean home prices, relative to purchasing power and technology changes, have stayed consistent with real wages.<p>And it’s likely not purely true. Home sizes being larger is also a function of REITs and corporate ownership of more consumer real estate, as well as boomers using soaring retirement fund profits to finance remodels, etc.<p>It’s way more complex than to draw any conclusion that this means purchasing power of homes has stayed close to wages. It hasn’t.')
