Item(by='maest', descendants=None, kids=[25740325, 25740091], score=None, time=1610403487, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25737762, text='This may dissapoint you but, with very few exceptions, most of the time nobody knows why a stock is going one way or another on any given day.<p>Unless there&#x27;s some significant news or M&amp;A deal that you can tie to price action (and even then, you&#x27;re most likely explaining only part of the price movement), you won&#x27;t know why a price is moving.<p>There is a whole industry of people adding post-hoc rationalizations to price movements (e.g. CNBC, stock gurus and others).
