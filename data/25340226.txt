Item(by='colechristensen', descendants=None, kids=[25340407, 25342661], score=None, time=1607389697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25339713, text='It’s the fundamentally different kind of danger from machines. They make mistakes we can’t understand.<p>I can understand a person being distracted, reacting slowly, not seeing me, etc.  I can also mitigate this risk. Crossing the street I can make eye contact with a person and be really confident that they aren’t going to run me over.<p>Walking in front of a robot?  I have no internal model for the kind of mistakes they make.  It seems equally likely in any circumstance that they’ll make the mistake of running me down, and there is nothing i can do to avoid it.')
