Item(by='ridaj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611072940, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25833755, text='There are more reasons than just cost to do just for signed in users on a given domain.<p>- Privacy (expected for consumers, debatable for domains).<p>- Willingness to pay (if your stuff is worth protecting, might as well charge for it).<p>- Decoding the watermarks: I assume this is only done on demand by authenticated users at a very low rate (otherwise if it&#x27;s freely available to anyone, you give leakers an oracle that lets them determine whether their media scrubbing &#x2F; fuzzing has indeed removed the watermarks, and these systems are more effective when they have no way to check &#x2F; must live in fear that they haven&#x27;t scrubbed it right)')
