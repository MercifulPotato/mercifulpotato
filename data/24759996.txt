Item(by='ashtonkem', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602540457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24758772, text='Serverless has been a clear case of a solution looking for a problem, IMO. The vast majority of the software I’ve deployed in my career gets called a lot; deploying them onto a serverless platform would’ve increased our cost and maintenance burden significantly above simply dockerizing some web app.<p>There are certainly use cases for the occasional script run, but that feels like more of job for some cron-like software, and also feels like a far cry from what serverless promised.')
