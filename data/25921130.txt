Item(by='jandrese', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611695616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25919236, text='I suspect that 1 in 96 figure is mostly people who have uMatrix or NoScript installed, not people who have disabled JavaScript entirely.  A little judicious blocking can greatly improve browsing experience at the cost of having to set up rules for sites when you first visit them sometimes.  It can be a real hassle when someone has embedded a video you want to watch and there are like 8 layers of embedded scripts from different domains necessary before you get to the actual video content.')
