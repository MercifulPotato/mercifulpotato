Item(by='jodrellblank', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610572739, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25756628, text='Maybe we&#x27;re using different definitions of &quot;strong case&quot;, I&#x27;m imagining it in a court of law, a case for going one way is &quot;billions of dollars and market dominance&quot; - that&#x27;s a strong case. The case for changing direction is &quot;in 30 years, giving your stuff away will be less legal trouble&quot;.
