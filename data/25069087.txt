For some rough equivalency (ignoring seek times, but also ignoring tcp overheads etc, total spherical cow territory)<p>- An 8x cdrom narrowly beats 10meg ethernet.<p>- 1x dvdrom narrowly beats 100meg ethernet.<p>- ATA133 narrowly beats 1gbit ethernet.<p>Original SATA is 1.5gbit, so 1Gbit ether bottlenecks us to 1999 storage speeds.')
