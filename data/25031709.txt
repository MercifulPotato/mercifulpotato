Item(by='zo1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604899355, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25030882, text='Just because it&#x27;s distributed or handled individually by each state doesn&#x27;t necessarily mean it can&#x27;t be compromised or interfered with.<p>If anything, I would argue that because it&#x27;s distributed and handled by individual smaller units, each individual one is susceptible to interference within its own area. On a side note: There is a reason why people recommend &quot;don&#x27;t roll your own encryption&quot;. Some things such as security are hard problems, and just because you want to be &quot;independent&quot; doesn&#x27;t mean you should come up with your own half-baked solution to the problem instead of using a secure and standardized one.')
