Item(by='betterunix2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610202956, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25698099, text='&quot;the impetus for the passing of Section 230 was to protect free speech by removing liability concerns for what others say online.&quot;<p>No, it was not meant to protect free speech.  It was meant to protect the market for Internet services, from ISPs up to user-facing services.  The Internet ecosystem would be crushed either by trolls&#x2F;nazis or by waves of lawsuits against companies that dared to moderate such things without some form of liability protection for services that perform some degree of moderation.')
