Libraries and frameworks comes and goes, it&#x27;s your base knowledge you need to improve upon, not specific APIs offered by easy-to-consume libraries and frameworks. Learn them, adopt their best ideas, throw away the rest, pick the right tool for the job, probably the best tool is not the tool you&#x27;re currently most familiar with, if you rank your choices based on GitHub stars anyway. People use stars for all kinds of purposes, don&#x27;t extract &quot;It&#x27;s valuable&quot; because of that.')
