My first NAS was my old desktop computer, using the motherboard SATA ports.<p>That said, ZFS should work on the Raspberry Pi 4 at least in 64bit mode. You probably will want to use Ubuntu as it has ZFS support.<p>If you have a spare drive you can test with, give a whirl. Just keep in mind this[1] if you have poor performance from the USB-SATA.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.raspberrypi.org&#x2F;forums&#x2F;viewtopic.php?t=245931" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.raspberrypi.org&#x2F;forums&#x2F;viewtopic.php?t=245931</a>')
