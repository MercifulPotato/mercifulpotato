Item(by='LeoPanthera', descendants=None, kids=[25279018], score=None, time=1606928589, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25277843, text='If you hold an Amateur Radio license it would be entirely legal for you to repurpose this hardware for transmitting in one of the many allocated amateur bands, as long as the standard rules were followed. (No encryption, always identify yourself with your callsign.)<p>There is a long tradition of radio amateurs re-purposing other radio hardware. CB radios can often be retuned into the 10 meter band, wifi transceivers can work inside the 13cm band, for example.')
