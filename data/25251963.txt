Item(by='5cents', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606726659, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25227181, text='TiddlyWiki (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tiddlywiki.com" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tiddlywiki.com</a>), which I think is amazing both in simplicity and flexibility, also offers export to static sites. There are two versions: either the wiki as a single html+js-file or on node.js, but export to static html exists in both. You can test it from the home page (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tiddlywiki.com" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tiddlywiki.com</a>), just go to Tools in the sidebar -&gt; Export all -&gt; Static HTML. Amazingly simple.<p>Not mine, but a (nice) demo: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mentalnodes.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.mentalnodes.com&#x2F;</a> and more advanced guide: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nesslabs.com&#x2F;tiddlywiki-static-website-generator" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;nesslabs.com&#x2F;tiddlywiki-static-website-generator</a>')
