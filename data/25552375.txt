Item(by='tmikaeld', descendants=None, kids=[25552485, 25557056], score=None, time=1609088702, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25551950, text='There&#x27;s a lot of advantages to API-first.<p>1. You&#x27;re not bound to one supplier, you can integrate multiple.<p>2. You don&#x27;t need to manage the security for a large application, instead, the responsibility is distributed to each API.<p>3. Scaling becomes a non-issue (unless you abuse the rate-limits&#x2F;grow too fast).<p>4. The cost of sysops and development goes waaay down.<p>The dis-advantages: it might be way more expensive, but at least you&#x27;ll have shipped! (And of course GDPR&#x2F;data control might be an issue)')
