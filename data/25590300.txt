Item(by='8bitsrule', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609398153, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25574180, text='I&#x27;d never bet my life on any map. If it&#x27;s really cold, after dark, or a remote location ... and if you don&#x27;t have provisioning for everyone in case of vehicle failure ... it&#x27;s a good idea to stay on well-travelled routes. Even then ... you <i>never know</i> ....<p>To get to the in-laws place for Xmas, I used to drive my family on a good highway, but it went through a 50-mile-wide frozen swamp with little traffic on it. Year after year. So risky ... suppose I hit a deer. Now I wonder why in the hell I did that.<p><i>All</i> maps are going to have limits - computer maps just moreso - overtrust is always unwarranted.')
