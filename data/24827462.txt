Item(by='dunham', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603122601, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24823813, text='Chrome has something called Desktop PWA that allows a PWA (that meets some additional requirements) to be installed as a native app. On macos it has its own .app bundle that can be launched by user and participates in the app switching as a separate app, but underneath shares process tree as the user&#x27;s chrome instance.<p>So it&#x27;s like having an Electron app with stricter sandboxing, less memory usage, and an easier installation &#x2F; upgrade process.')
