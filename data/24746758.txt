Item(by='s5300', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602432672, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24746158, text='This is a little bit away from the topic at hand - but a useful experiment I think kids should see...<p>Once a kid is at the age where he general has an idea of what metals are, and has the slightest knowledge of what iron metal is<p>Take some cereal, like bran flakes or something else with iron in it, put a serving or two in some water, blend it pretty well, then take a magnet to the blender cup to show him the non-negligible amount of iron that can be seen attracted to the magnet.<p>Then kinda be like... yeah - that metal you know about? Totally part of what makes your body work day to day, kid. Literally in your food and such.')
