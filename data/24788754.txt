There’s a process in place where your unreleased manuscript is sent to other researchers in your field for review. After making corrections based on their feedback, you can be approved for publication, at which point the general public gets a chance to evaluate it.<p>Having done it, I don’t necessarily love the process and would love for the process to be more open and accessible, but it generally works ok.')
