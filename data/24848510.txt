Just check the query for localizable keywords ( :{KEYWORD} ), and replace that with the word it represents.<p>So if the query is &quot;in:bin&quot; just convert that to &quot;in:trash&quot; and use that for searching, without the user knowing. Then you also have no problem with switching languages.')
