Item(by='nrclark', descendants=None, kids=[25132181], score=None, time=1605642438, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25125989, text='Fwiw, the Gettysburg address would have been pretty bad as prose. It was written to be delivered as a speech.<p>A speaker can control inflection and intonation, which add their own meaning and interact with the actual word choices. Written word has no such luxury.<p>Also, the Gettysburg Address was written 150 years ago. Language norms change over time.')
