At the same time, Earth pursues a straightforward military strategy of building a large space fleet to stop the Trisolarians, but are unable to anticipate the aliens&#x27; superior technology makes their efforts futile.<p>Ultimately, Earth sends the MAD signal, and ends up destroyed.
