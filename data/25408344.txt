Item(by='TheOtherHobbes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607881306, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405741, text='Magnetic cards appeared in later HP calculators - starting with the HP-65 - and were copied by TI.<p>These calculators look a bit clunky and retro today compared to modern phones, but they were unbelievably futuristic in the mid-70s.<p>The idea that you could have a programmable microcomputer in your pocket which could load applications from plastic cards was jaw-dropping.')
