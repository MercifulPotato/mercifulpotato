Item(by='tomtomtom777', descendants=None, kids=[25774974], score=None, time=1610620797, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25772607, text='There is an additional cost to such a dependency that isn&#x27;t expressed in bytes.<p>Javascript&#x2F;CSS&#x2F;HTML forms the common basis of web development. You may assume every reader of your code is familiar with it.<p>If you use a specific library, you are restricting (easy) readability to those that know the specific library.<p>This has merit if the library provides sufficiently useful abstractions or shortcuts, but it&#x27;s only a drawback if the library provides merely an alternative way to write something already possible in the base layer.')
