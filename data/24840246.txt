Item(by='kwillets', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603215710, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24839743, text='There have apparently been some injection attacks where an overlength &quot;;&quot; or something has been missed by a string sanitizer.  Overlength means eg a 7-bit ASCII character is encoded in two or more bytes, neither of which would be noticed by an 8-bit delimiter checker. The only flag in this case is that bitfields in the two bytes have 0&#x27;s in the high bits beyond 7.')
