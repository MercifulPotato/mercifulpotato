We&#x27;re worried about black boxes and long for formal proofs of our programs because they are useful only when we know <i>what</i> they do.<p>If I hand you a black box with a button and two LEDs that blink in response to input, it would be initially useless to you precisely because you do not know <i>what</i> it does.
