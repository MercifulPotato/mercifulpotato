A lot of people get thrown into the top layer of the abstraction itself and then move down from there.<p>What instead should happens first is that these people should be shown the problems React or other frameworks for that matter are trying to solve. It is way more motivating to gradually move up the abstraction layer when learning.<p>Get the know the problem first and then find the solution to that problem. Not the other way around.')
