I&#x27;d add to your list, the source code, future plans, etc.<p>I do some basic steps to harden my dev machines:<p>- A Chrome profile without extensions for developing for the most part.<p>- The localhost is not 127.0.0.1 but a random IP under 127&#x2F;8, with a random port. See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=20028108" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=20028108</a><p>- When creating SSH keys, add extra rounds (e.g. `ssh-keygen -a 64`) to make it harder to brute-force the passphrase.')
