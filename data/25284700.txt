Item(by='dimitrios1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606963777, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25279993, text='Most likely, everyone you know, especially outside of tech, when referencing a search engine, says to &quot;google it&quot;.<p>There are, unfortunately, millions of ways we form relationships with brands. A brand&#x27;s reason for existing is to live somewhere in our psyche either subconsciously or consciously. We say &quot;Q-tip&quot; instead of cotton swab, &quot;band-aid&quot; instead of bandage, &quot;Advil&quot; when we need ibuprofen, or &quot;Tylenol&quot; when we want acetaminophen. When we see white polar bears around Christmas time, we think of Coca-Cola, and so on and so forth.<p>All that to say, kudos for you for taking a principled approach, but I am not sure it&#x27;s going to achieve much.')
