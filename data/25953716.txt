Buying stocks with high short interest is, over the long run, a terrible investment strategy. Mountains of academic research has consistently found the most heavily shorted stocks to significantly underperform the market[1] (to the order of 10% per year).<p>Heavily shorted stocks tend to be the companies with the most negative cash flow shocks[2], low quality earnings statements[3], negative earnings revisions, and future bad news[4]. In other words, when a company is heavily shorted it&#x27;s almost always for a good reason. (Like being a brick and mortar video game retailer, that&#x27;s losing mountains of cash with no turnaround plan.) High short interest is a tell-tale warning of a bad investment.<p>This shouldn&#x27;t be surprising. Research has consistently found that short-sellers are the most sophisticated and well-informed investors in the market[5]. There activity strongly predicts soon-to-be released negative public announcements[6]. Short sellers are exactly the type of trader, you do not want to be on the other side of.<p>[1]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;0022-1082.00495" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;0022-1082.00...</a>\n[2]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S0304405X16300320" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.sciencedirect.com&#x2F;science&#x2F;article&#x2F;abs&#x2F;pii&#x2F;S03044...</a>\n[3]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;link.springer.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;10.1007&#x2F;s11142-006-6396-x" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;link.springer.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;10.1007&#x2F;s11142-006-6396-x</a>\n[4]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;fima.12144" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;fima.12144</a>\n[6]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.newyorkfed.org&#x2F;medialibrary&#x2F;media&#x2F;research&#x2F;conference&#x2F;2010&#x2F;cb&#x2F;Boehmer_Jones_Zhang1.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.newyorkfed.org&#x2F;medialibrary&#x2F;media&#x2F;research&#x2F;confe...</a>\n[5]<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;jfir.12121" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;onlinelibrary.wiley.com&#x2F;doi&#x2F;abs&#x2F;10.1111&#x2F;jfir.12121</a>')
