Item(by='tchalla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611228627, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25857315, text='I have noticed that labelling decisions as binary (right vs wrong, good vs bad) tends to create factions. I recommend turning decisions from binary into options&#x2F;implications. A &quot;bad&quot; decision is an option with an implication assigned to it. If stakeholders are willing to agree with the implications, that&#x27;s how it is.')
