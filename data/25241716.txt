Item(by='pmoriarty', descendants=None, kids=[25241982], score=None, time=1606608315, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25241658, text='The problem with javascript is not just abuse, it&#x27;s overuse.<p>There are countless websites which have no dynamic content and yet choose to require javascript to function.<p>The other major problem is the vulnerabilities you open yourself up to by allowing javascript to run in your browser.<p>99.9% of the time it&#x27;s simply not worth it for me, and I wish I could say &quot;no thank you&quot; to all those sites that use it but don&#x27;t actually need it to deliver all their static content.<p>Even many of the sites that deliver dynamic content via javascript could probably have been designed to work just fine without it, but no, companies just have to have their tracking and serve up all their ads via JS.')
