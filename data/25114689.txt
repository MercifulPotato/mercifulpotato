Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605548936, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25111352, text='Using straight edge and compass:<p><pre><code>  - draw a line\n  - pick two points on the line\n  - label them 1 and 10\n  - bisect the segment to get √10\n  - bisect the halves to get 10^¼ and 10^¾\n  - etc.\n</code></pre>\nYou can’t do much better, as the logarithms of rational numbers tend to be transcendental.')
