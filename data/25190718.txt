Item(by='magicalhippo', descendants=None, kids=[25190831], score=None, time=1606160127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25189733, text='I&#x27;m running several Pi&#x27;s from SD card, some for years, and the only issue I had was with one that randomly crashed.<p>Measured the resistance of the &quot;USB charging cable&quot; I had used for it and found it was 1 Ohm! So when the Pi peaked at 1A current draw, the cable dropped 1V.<p>Swapped it out for something proper (&lt;0.1 Ohm), and haven&#x27;t had issues since.')
