Right tool for the right job.<p>Whilst I agree that the idea of putting differing lines of work into different branches is great, that&#x27;s not what stash is for. That&#x27;s what checkout is for.<p>Stash is used to record the current state of the working directory and index while also returning to a clean working state.<p>I guess for the author, stash is used to preserve changes when pulling in upstream changes you missed. It can, but it does waaaay more.<p>What happens if, like many people, you&#x27;re working on a few branches at a time. Could be a monorepo, could be a couple long living branches, w&#x2F;e.
