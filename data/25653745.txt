If two access points are close enough to hear each other&#x27;s transmissions and they are operating on the same channel they will share that channel&#x27;s bandwidth. Wifi was designed this way for exactly this reason, and it&#x27;s probably why wifi still kind-of works even in large apartment buildings.')
