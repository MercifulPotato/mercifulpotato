Item(by='ogogmad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611402433, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25881327, text='I don&#x27;t think many people believe (even crypto advocates) that a large number of transactions are going to happen directly on the blockchain. There seem to be two possible futures for Bitcoin: Either most people will transact their BTC on an energy efficient network separate from the blockchain (like the Lightning network) or they will use BTC as a long-term store of value like gold*, and not use it to buy everyday things like coffee.<p>* Of course, at the moment it&#x27;s not a very good store of value because of the price volatility. But advocates argue that these are just growing pains.')
