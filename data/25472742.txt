Item(by='briefcomment', descendants=None, kids=[25473346, 25472957, 25473078], score=None, time=1608329713, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25471348, text='We need more writing where people are the best they can be.  Imagine the good Shakespeare could have done by making his characters the best role models.  People imitate, and Shakespeare has incredible reach.<p>As I get older, I have little patience for flawed characters, and love it when I see someone act good all the time in fiction.<p>Edit: Tintin, for example, is one of my favorite fictional characters of all time.  He is smart, athletic, capable, dependable, and, most importantly, always acts in good faith.')
