It is fundamental to basic success in life.  You have to know how to form ideas, express those ideas, and then act on those ideas.<p>I had a very little bit of U.S. Army training.  (Nice people, turned out not to be my thing.)  One of the things that we were taught is that a big reason some armies are more successful than others is because some train their soldiers to think, others train them to blindly follow orders.<p>Another arena where we see this is education.  There is no shortage of people graduating from high school and college who can pass written exams through memorization.  But there is a massive shortage of people graduating from high school and college who can think.')
