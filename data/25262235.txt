Item(by='troupe', descendants=None, kids=[25262298, 25262405], score=None, time=1606796044, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25257087, text='It appears to me that the problem with reasoning by analogy is that we often focus on how x is analogous to y, but skip over the part where we think about how x is not like y. Without intentionally making that step, we bring a lot of unwanted assumptions into our understanding of x because we haven&#x27;t focused on the ways it is different.')
