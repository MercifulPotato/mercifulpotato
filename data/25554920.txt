With overloaded [], an accidental use of indexOf would have been obvious.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bleepingcomputer.com&#x2F;news&#x2F;security&#x2F;bouncy-castle-crypto-authentication-bypass-vulnerability-revealed&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bleepingcomputer.com&#x2F;news&#x2F;security&#x2F;bouncy-castle...</a><p>So I think [] has a place for containers. Overloading * and -&gt; for smart pointers also makes things look a lot nicer. I think the more advanced indexing modes of numpy (and by extension tensorflow) were a mistake though. Those create weird edge cases and would have been much better of as named methods.')
