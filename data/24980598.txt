Item(by='u10', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604417995, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24980489, text='Different groups have different issues that must be addressed. There are few &#x27;one size fits all&#x27; solutions.  People do want to be treated as peers and equals, but unfortunately there are intrinsic issues that inhibit that.<p>For example, a person with a black sounding name will, on average, will face more difficulty in getting a job compared to a person with a white sounding name, all else equal.  It is not particularly productive to say &#x27;if we treated everyone the same they wouldn&#x27;t have this issue&#x27;.')
