Item(by='tmikaeld', descendants=None, kids=[25631930], score=None, time=1609771050, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25631338, text='The team i worked with hosted simple solutions on VMs at first. But after a few years, the client requirements and features grew until our biggest problem became maintenance and upgrades.<p>The sysop costs became higher than our earnings, so we weighted our options of re-building or partner with a larger API-first solution.<p>Eventually we choose to partner up and in the end, we cut costs to a fraction, while still keeping the customer base and increasing performance.<p>Which is another point I see constantly, founders desperately want to keep control by not outsourcing when it _should_ be, and by doing that, the costs continue to increase and the already over-worked team can no longer push features, competition gain even more ground, margins fall even more and eventually layoffs and inevitable shut-down.')
