Item(by='runjake', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611177365, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25848536, text='My advice having gone through this: buy a low end, but well-reviewed DSLR with the kit lens, to start.<p>Learn the fundamentals of a DSLR (aperture, f-stop, and all that jazz).<p>Gradually grow from there as your abilities improve.<p>I thought I&#x27;d be buy a DSLR and take awesome photos and while I did by accident produce some stunning shots, there&#x27;s a learning curve.<p>Also, if you have any interest in space, try astrophotography. It really helped me learn the challenges of ISO, focus, aperture, etc.')
