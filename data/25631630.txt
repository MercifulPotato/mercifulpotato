I&#x27;m not going to install some random SaaS vendor&#x27;s app on my phone just so I can log in<p>* requires internet access on phone: sucks if your phone doesn&#x27;t data, cell reception is spotty, or wifi isn&#x27;t set up<p>* less phishing resistance: one time sign in links are impossible to phish, and passwords have mitigations that protect against phishing (eg. password managers that only auto-fill on the correct domain) and users are generally aware to &quot;check the address bar before entering password&quot;. scanning a QR code has neither of these.')
