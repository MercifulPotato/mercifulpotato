Item(by='depingus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609951838, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25655508, text='I use OneDrive for work. The desktop app has &quot;Files-On-Demand&quot; (enabled by default). It keeps everything in the cloud until you click on a file. Then it downloads a local copy and keeps it in sync with the cloud. You can also manually designate files to &quot;Always keep on this device&quot; or &quot;Free up space&quot;. You can take space saving even further with Storage Sense in Windows 10. It can delete local copies of files that haven&#x27;t been used for a designated amount of time (Never by default).<p>Is Microsoft better than Google? Probably not. I don&#x27;t know of any open source or self hosted solution this robust. You might want to consider getting a NAS; which lets you mount a network share on your local PC and use your normal file manager.')
