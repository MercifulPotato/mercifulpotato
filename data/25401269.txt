Item(by='arcticbull', descendants=None, kids=[25401281], score=None, time=1607804111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25401218, text='Create a new profiling environment based on the template you&#x27;re interested in, making sure &quot;Choose a profiling template for:&quot; is set to &quot;Your Computer &gt; All Processes.&quot; Next, in the top-left of the window, where it says &quot;Your Computer &gt; All Processes&quot;, click on All Processes. Select Choose Target... and navigate to your binary and hit Choose. Then hit the red circle to begin recording. This will launch a new instance of the specified binary and profile it.<p>This may not work for binaries that set PT_DENY_ATTACH, though.')
