Item(by='Amorymeltzer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601663319, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24665350, text='Preferred gender pronoun:<p>&gt;Preferred gender pronouns or personal gender pronouns (often abbreviated as PGP) refer to the set of third-person pronouns that an individual prefers that others use in order to identify that person&#x27;s gender (or lack thereof). In English, when declaring one&#x27;s preferred pronouns, a person will often state the subject and object pronouns along with the possessive adjectives—for example, &quot;she, her, hers&quot;, &quot;he, him, his&quot;, or &quot;they, them, theirs&quot;—although sometimes, only the subject and object pronouns are stated (&quot;he, him&quot;, &quot;she, her&quot;, &quot;they, them&quot;).<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Preferred_gender_pronoun" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Preferred_gender_pronoun</a>')
