Item(by='matthewdgreen', descendants=None, kids=[24799972, 24800269], score=None, time=1602849413, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24799531, text='The CAN bus is shared by a number of devices, each with its own microprocessor and thus (in principle) potentially subject to exploits that cause it to run malicious code. Some of the devices on the bus interact with the outside world via cellular, Wifi, Bluetooth and USB. The disturbing idea here is that your car is basically an internally-unfirewalled network of computers that can kill you if they malfunction.')
