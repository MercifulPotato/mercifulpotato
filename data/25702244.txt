As expected, I went through several rounds of editing with them.  This was <i>after</i> several of my own &quot;look again on a fresh morning&quot; rounds of private editing.  The process was quite instructive.<p>I benefited from a range of &quot;bug fixes&quot; from the fine editors—suggestions to make the lede paragraph more robust; small rephrasings that greatly improve readability; and seemingly minor edits that contribute to the overall structure and coherency.  The value of an experienced and judicious editor is hard to describe well.  (Thanks Jake, et al!)<p>I relished the back-and-forth review rounds, good-natured arguments, and &quot;nitpicks&quot; that greatly benefited the end product.
