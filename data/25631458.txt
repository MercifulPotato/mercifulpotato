Item(by='anonunivgrad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609769162, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25628628, text='Normal payments are not debt. If you want to buy a tv at a store, they can decline cash as a form of payment. You don’t <i>owe</i> them. The tv in your cart at the checkout aisle is still theirs. There is no <i>debt</i>.<p>But if you bought that same tv on an installment plan, they would have to accept payments in cash.<p>The first one does not involve debt, the second does. That’s the difference.')
