Item(by='freitasm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610826575, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25802714, text='Are you on a xDSL service of fibre?<p>On xDSL services, some monitored alarms may cause line problems when they &quot;check in&quot;. In these cases an in-line DSL filter should do the trick.<p>Another possibility is that some modem&#x2F;routers have a &quot;self healing feature&quot; that is just really a restart. See if there&#x27;s anything like this in the admin&#x2F;system part of the configuration settings.')
