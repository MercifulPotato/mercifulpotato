It often is done when people are trying to be too &quot;dry&quot; and trying to remove as much duplication as possible.  But duplication is just an &quot;indicator&quot; that something might have bad modularity. However code should have good design principles guiding it and must drive modularity decisions like ensuring modular parts have good cohesion, it&#x27;s not simply just because there is duplication.')
