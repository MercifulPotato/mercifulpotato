I think it is more effective to think about this from business perspective. RAII has people on payroll whose job is to hurt piracy by all means. Making life hard for everyone including developers who develop youtube-dl is their _job_. DMCA requires good faith belief that there some kind of infringement and authorization to file a claim. Many scream perjury but note that the perjury part in the notice only applies to &quot;under the penalty of perjury, that I am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right&quot;. With all the references to copyrighted works, proving this claim was made explicitly in bad faith is next to impossible. Even if this is reversed, they will still feel good about a day&#x27;s worth of work.')
