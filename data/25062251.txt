Item(by='brobdingnagians', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605124613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25060054, text='It is more about intent. Being married is an explicit commitment to one another for life (nominally). That provides a foundation where a bad day, month, or year is okay because you are committed, and that is a place of security for children who know their parents are similarly committed to them. Cohabitating couples are less likely to stay together even if they eventually marry. There&#x27;s something about commitment to marriage that leads to better outcomes in relationships. The feeling of permanence for the future improves the experience of the now.')
