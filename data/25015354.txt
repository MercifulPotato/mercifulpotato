Item(by='lmilcin', descendants=None, kids=[25015417], score=None, time=1604760568, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013550, text='Intermittent fasting gets easier with time for many different reasons.<p>Your body learns to differentiate hunger from the feeling of not being sated.<p>You learn to exercise willpower which is good not just for fasting but in general.<p>Your body trains metabolic pathways that are normally dormant or active for a very short time of day.<p>Your body learns to recognize and switch metabolism faster, causing lesser and shorter &quot;hangover&quot; effect.<p>I am on&#x2F;off intermittent fasting (ie. sometimes stop, mainly due to social demands), but I noticed it became easier to start intermittent fasting.')
