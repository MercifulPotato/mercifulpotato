Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=[25801455], score=None, time=1610793412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801278, text='I wouldn’t have picked 65,536, too, but there’s a trade-off between the increment and the number of items you can append without running into problems.<p>If he had picked 2^63, it wouldn’t be possible to create an initial list of even 2 items.<p>Depending on the use case, I probably would have picked 2^54 or so (allows for appending about a thousand items)<p>If your lists are small it doesn’t matter much what you do, but for large lists, I don’t think there’s a clean solution for this, if one keeps the key size constant.')
