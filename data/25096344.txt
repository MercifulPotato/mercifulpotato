Item(by='musicale', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605393679, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25095608, text='I think the &quot;microprogramming&quot; bit can&#x2F;should simply be a multi-cycle, non-pipelined implementation.<p>Non-pipelined is easier to write and debug, especially if you are used to regular sequential programming; once you have that working then it isn&#x27;t too hard to turn it into a pipelined implementation.<p>A basic RISC style design is probably easier than a Z-80 because of the simpler addressing modes and the simpler decode.')
