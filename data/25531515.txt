Item(by='jlgaddis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608844682, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25528762, text='&gt; <i>Ideally there would be one or two private tlds codified just as there are private ip ranges ...</i><p>There are several, in fact.<p>RFC8375 [0] states:<p>&gt; <i>This document registers the domain &#x27;home.arpa.&#x27; as a special-use domain name [RFC6761] [1] ... &#x27;home.arpa.&#x27; is intended to be the correct domain for uses like the one described for &#x27;.home&#x27; in [RFC7788] [2]: local name service in residential homenets.</i><p>In addition to &quot;home.arpa.&quot;, there are several other domain names listed in IANA&#x27;s &quot;Special-Use Domain Names&quot; registry [3] that &quot;users are free to use ... as they would any other domain names&quot; -- even if they are technically intended&#x2F;reserved for other uses.<p>For as long as I can remember, I&#x27;ve used a subdomain of one of my registered domain names for everything in my home network.
