Item(by='rincebrain', descendants=None, kids=[25548001], score=None, time=1609031724, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25547896, text='It appears the decision was made to hardwire AVX support to &quot;on&quot; recently as all the hardware for Big Sur supposedly supported AVX, not noticing that Rosetta 2 violated this, and then the bug report(s) started coming in about precompiled binaries being broken under Rosetta 2 since that change.<p>So I personally find it interesting because I would not have expected Rosetta 2 to be missing AVX instructions if all the supported Big Sur hardware possessed them, and this is an interesting failure mode to watch and see how the Brew developers handle.')
