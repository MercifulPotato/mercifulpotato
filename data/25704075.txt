Item(by='vlovich123', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610222312, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25704015, text='I think Skylark (the language for Buck&#x2F;Basel) represents an interesting compromise here that I like better, even though in the past I was a huge Cmake advocate.<p>The majority of rules are declarative and easily understood. Python (or at least a dialect extremely close to Python) is how you extend and write new rules. The two live in very different files (declarative rules are in BUCK&#x2F;BUILD files, .bzl files contain the imperative definitions of rules, and various other conventions control how you deal with platforms (ie select rules).')
