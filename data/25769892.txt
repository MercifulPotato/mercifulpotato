Item(by='Xylakant', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610583032, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25769781, text='It’s definitely possible to have non-competes in some cases in germany, but they come with a catch: the employer needs to pay the employee the (usually full) salary for the entire period, potentially reduced by any income from a job. And it needs to be negotiated in the work contract and cannot be unilaterally enforced after the fact.<p>So generally speaking, they’re only worthwhile for people that have important company secrets.')
