Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610126151, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25685818, text='When you have a deadline, you get a bright line between &quot;fast enough&quot; and &quot;too slow&quot;, and no immediately apparent benefit from being faster than just enough.<p>In such cases, if it is known more work <i>could</i> be done, often more work can be found to do, but it is hard to know without actually trying to do more. If it is not certain the extra work could be finished in time, it might be wrong to start it instead of doing something else simpler and more certain.')
