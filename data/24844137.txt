Item(by='musingsole', descendants=None, kids=[24844211], score=None, time=1603245146, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24841811, text='As the other answers note, multiple dispatch is largely a performance improvement. When writing Python, performance has usually been sidelined as a priority in the first place (within some constraints).<p>Personally, I hate this presentation. Multiple dispatch (to me) is cognitively heavier and not what I would default to. If it&#x27;s a performance gain and I need performance, then sure. But too often people hold it up as a virtue because the need for speed is assumed.')
