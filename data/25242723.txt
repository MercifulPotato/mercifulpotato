Item(by='bigbubba', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606620580, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25241863, text='He may have insulted you, but not all insults constitute logical fallacies.<p>Ad hominem: <i>You&#x27;re wrong because you&#x27;re an idiot.</i><p>Just an insult: <i>You&#x27;re an idiot because you&#x27;re wrong.</i><p>Furthermore, concluding that somebody is wrong because they used a logical fallacy is itself a logical fallacy.  If I said <i>&quot;2+2=4 because you&#x27;re an idiot&quot;</i> my reasoning would be fallacious, but to conclude that the answer must therefore not be four is also fallacious.')
