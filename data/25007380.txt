Item(by='enriquto', descendants=None, kids=[25008518, 25007692], score=None, time=1604668689, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25005567, text='As a unix purist, the &quot;scp&quot; program seems kludgy and in-elegant.  We already have &quot;cp&quot; for copying files, why do we need another program?<p>The appropriate thing to do is to mount the remote disk, and then use regular cp as God intended.  Fortunately, this is possible today using &quot;sshfs&quot; (but there&#x27;s still some ugliness in the fusermount implementation).')
