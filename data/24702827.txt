The core of the problem is that the premise of the H1B visa itself – extraordinary skill that cannot be found in the US – is complete bullshit. Everyone participating in the program (including the government) knows this, and yet they have to do the entire song and dance of years long approval processes, fake job listings, RFEs and whatnot.<p>Fix the real problem, and salaries will take care of themselves.<p>Another problem with simply raising the salary bar is that there are actually professions (especially in healthcare) that genuinely have a talent&#x2F;supply gap. A small hospital in the midwest, however, isn&#x27;t going to be able to compete with a hotshot SF startup with unlimited venture funding to throw at foreign hires.')
