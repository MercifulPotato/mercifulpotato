Item(by='baumgarn', descendants=None, kids=[25192968, 25192853], score=None, time=1606171132, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25192653, text='Zoomquilt 1+2 was done by a bunch of illustrators. A person would paint a single frame, which would be scaled down and given to another person to continue painting around while blending into the previous frame. The picture would develop much like the Cadavre Exquis surrealist drawing game, and part of the fun is to continue and transform what the person before you left. Arkadia I painted together with my friend Sophia Schomberg.')
