[1] You wouldn&#x27;t want all the client tasks to recalculate their subset every time, so anything that requires each client task to know the total client task count is a dead end. Likewise when the backend task count changes, client tasks shouldn&#x27;t change whether backend tasks that existed before and still exist are in their chosen subset.<p>[1] Autopilot: Workload Autoscaling at Google Scale, <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;research.google&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pub49174&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;research.google&#x2F;pubs&#x2F;pub49174&#x2F;</a>')
