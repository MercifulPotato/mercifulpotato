Item(by='hardtke', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609184331, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25559843, text='The best land for high density construction in SF are the smaller rent controlled buildings, not single family homes. These smaller rent controlled buildings are near lively neighborhoods and transit, and are also often at the outer edge of their useful lifespan. I have yet to see a policy proposal to open up this land for development because nobody wants to give up rent controlled units.')
