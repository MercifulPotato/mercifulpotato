The most important is that you should put users before advertisers, even though the advertisers are paying and users aren&#x27;t. One of my favorite bumper stickers reads &quot;if the people lead, the leaders will follow.&quot; Paraphrased for the Web, this becomes &quot;get all the users, and the advertisers will follow.&quot; More generally, design your product to please users first, and then think about how to make money from it. If you don&#x27;t put users first, you leave a gap for competitors who do.<p>That aged well! Reading these timeless stuff many years after their original writing shows you how the disruptors are starting to become the disruptee.')
