Item(by='minipci1321', descendants=None, kids=[25219331], score=None, time=1606391173, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25218951, text='I used to work on drivers and firmware for consumer electronics remote controls, and the empirical threshold we used at the time, was that an average human cannot press a button two times in a row much faster than with a 200 ms delay. (So, if the driver was getting same key presses spaced by less than that, it could safely assume those come from auto-repeat in the RC.)')
