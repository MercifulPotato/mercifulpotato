A fresher mind, due to more leisure time, will solve problems faster and reach insights quicker.<p>I&#x27;ve seen friends ramp up their work time and the thing I notice that goes downhill the fastest is their decision making ability.  When you work longer hours, you have less time to think about harder problems so you push off making those decisions which may take 30 or 60 minutes of focused thinking about.  With more free time, employees can have more energy to make decisions faster and better [1]<p>We already know that working overtime leads to poorer results [2].
