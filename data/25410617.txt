Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607895884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25410310, text='The case fatality rate was considerably lower in the second wave in almost all cases, and the total number of deaths generally lower as well.<p>The &#x27;lag&#x27; is accounted for in that most &#x27;second wave&#x27; places have past the peak over their waves.<p>Deaths were &#x27;higher&#x27; in the second wave in places that had a small first wave, like Austria.<p>But for places that had a &#x27;real&#x27; first wave, the second wave was generally milder in terms of overall deaths, and much milder in terms of CFR.<p>We can&#x27;t ignore the real risk of &#x27;waves&#x27; but it&#x27;s also important not to deny the nuanced reality of them. In particular, the &#x27;second wave&#x27; had many more younger people, probably accounting for the lower case fatality rate.')
