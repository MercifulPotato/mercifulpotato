Of course it can be faked, but why bother?<p>I understood devices in the comment as personal devices for end users rather than servers. In that case discovery is really not external. Same with NAT - it&#x27;s a difference between &quot;kind of works sometimes&quot; and &quot;is designed to work with it&quot;. See Skype&#x27;s initial success. Or how SIP documents NAT behaviour extensively <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tools.ietf.org&#x2F;html&#x2F;rfc6314" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;tools.ietf.org&#x2F;html&#x2F;rfc6314</a>')
