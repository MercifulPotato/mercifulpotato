The security page only shows that they use Google for Speech to Text.<p>Instead, Text to Speech is done using technology developed by Lyrebird.ai, which Descript has bought over. Descript rebranded it as Overdub. Note that style transfer learning of voices is a hard problem and Overdub seems to have nailed it perfectly. I speculate that the underlying technology of Overdub is based on sv2tts (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arxiv.org&#x2F;abs&#x2F;1806.04558" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arxiv.org&#x2F;abs&#x2F;1806.04558</a>).<p>The closest comparision to Overdub would be <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.resemble.ai&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.resemble.ai&#x2F;</a>.')
