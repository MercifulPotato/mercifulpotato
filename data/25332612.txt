Item(by='bharathsnvs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607352731, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25329311, text='This reminds me of the way exodus parties across deserts used to use a simple risk-sharing mechanism against potential theft&#x2F;pirates. Put simply, it was agreed upon that those on the extreme ends of a migrating party were more vulnerable to attacks from desert pirates. So, they would be compensated by the rest of the group (in the case of any such events) for shouldering the riskier positions in their migration.')
