Item(by='mlthoughts2018', descendants=None, kids=[24748395], score=None, time=1602444608, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24748324, text='A typical user will rarely ever use the built in CSV library in Python, and would choose among the many third party options for much greater performance, ease of use, parity with rectangular data structure tools like pandas, and so forth.<p>Comparing anything against the built-in CSV reader in Python is pretty much an exercise in nothing.<p>In 20 years of programming Python professionally, I have used the built in CSV parser about 5 total times, and all were use cases where 10-20x improvement of CSV parser performance did not matter whatsoever to the application.')
