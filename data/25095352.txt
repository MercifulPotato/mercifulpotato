Item(by='libraryofbabel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605385646, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25094848, text='I thought it was great, a wild journey through the 17th century. But maybe not to everyone’s taste - massive sprawling scope and there is indeed a huge cast of characters; it’s packed with various (mostly super interesting!) digressions into things like coinage, banking, alchemy, court politics. It will take you a while to get through.<p>If you’re not sure, you could read his <i>Cryptonomicon</i> first, and if you like that, and are good with <i>even more so, and in the 17th century</i>, then Baroque Cycle is probably your thing.')
