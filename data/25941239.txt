Most PRs change something that requires new tests to be written, so I have a rule that any PRs must add unit tests verifying the new behaviour. If your feature really matters to you then it also matters that it’s correct. I don’t want to spend my time triaging bugs in a feature I didn’t write and don’t use.<p>For some reason this rule weeds out about 80% of contributions - including almost all low quality PRs. It’s delightful.')
