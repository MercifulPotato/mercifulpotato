The number of times I&#x27;ve been burned in my own (or more likely shared) spreadsheet by things like copying down a formula that got modified in one instance but not all and related issues is staggering.<p>Being able to have some cells where core logic lives makes it easier for less technical people to understand what&#x27;s going on, and makes formulae a lot more reusable.')
