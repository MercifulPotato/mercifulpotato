METARs [1] are standardized weather reports that pilots use to evaluate current conditions. They&#x27;re often reported from stations located at airports, and include (among other things) wind direction, cloud bases, temperature&#x2F;dewpoint, current altimeter setting, etc. They look like this in raw text form:<p>KMDS 150935Z AUTO 30009KT 10SM CLR M02&#x2F;M06 A3007 RMK AO2<p>This app parses METARs into a visual representation, and displays it in a widget on your home screen. Useful for keeping tabs on current conditions!<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;METAR" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;METAR</a>')
