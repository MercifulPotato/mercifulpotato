Item(by='monoideism', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607684230, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25384133, text='Many Haskell and functional Scala books will give you a good understanding of type-driven development, but I&#x27;d recommend &quot;Functional Scala&quot; by Chisano&#x2F;Bjarnson (and do the exercises).<p>Alternately, &quot;Learn You a Haskell&quot; will provide a decent base.<p>Either of these will give you the base needed to explore higher-level concepts that can easily be used in Haskell, Scala, Purescript, OCaml, a few statically-typed functional languages, and to some extent, Rust.')
