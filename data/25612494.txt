Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609591847, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25611558, text='The problem with robotics, is the difference between a world of atoms and a world of bits.<p>When the plague hit, I was sucked into an effort to build a ventilator, I still have a pile of parts that were purchased for it, but the chassis fabrication never happened, so I had nothing to write code for, thankfully the need evaporated. I learned that it takes MONTHS to get motors and gear trains supplied if they don&#x27;t happen to be in stock.<p>If you thought the 1960s world of waiting a day for your output deck to see if your program compiled and ran was bad... it&#x27;s got nothing on the world of supply chains.<p>You can buy off the shelf components from Motion Industries, but they are out of the price range of hobbyists.')
