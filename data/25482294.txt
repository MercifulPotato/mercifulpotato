Item(by='kabirgoel', descendants=None, kids=[25484457], score=None, time=1608420480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25481406, text='Fantastic article. As an Indian American, I’ve seen people go to great lengths in order to fit in with white America and distance themselves from other minorities. Like the author says, in the early years of Indian immigration, these efforts were necessary for survival. Today, though, I think a lot of Indian Americans don’t really have a reason to continue. Yet we have, and now we seem to think that it’s a virtue. The article is an interesting peek behind the curtain.')
