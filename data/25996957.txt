Item(by='throwaway09223', descendants=None, kids=[25997187], score=None, time=1612234401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25996178, text='I have an under-sink RO&#x2F;DI system, the whole system was about $200. It&#x27;s a great investment for a place with hard water. I use it to get demineralized water for my house plants, fish tank, and drinking.<p>I use the RODI for expensive steam appliances like my Wagner, but I don&#x27;t use RODI for regular old vegetable steamers or humidifiers. It&#x27;s just not worth it. The scale buildup isn&#x27;t a big deal and it&#x27;s very easy to dissolve with an acid or just brush off.')
