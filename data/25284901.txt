Or cordless phones? Or chicken pox vaccinations? Or cars designed to protect childrens&#x27; lives and health? What did almond milk cost in Duluth? How much were encyclopedias?<p>Some things are absolutely more expensive, but we definitely have improvements since then, and automation (like modern logistics) contribute to almost all of them.<p>To put it another way, I would rather have the median income today while doing day-to-day activities now than any past decade.
