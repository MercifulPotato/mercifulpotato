If you are rewriting history, you are already ahead of the curve and you can use the command line. Commit, push, pull, branch and merge are all 90% of users need, and those last two are already an advanced topic.<p>For a good example of &quot;user friendly git&quot; I suggest taking a look at mercurial.')
