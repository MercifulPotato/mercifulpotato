Item(by='Izkata', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607214036, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25319179, text='Assuming sibling comment is right and it was baobab, mouse over any part of the circular layout and a tooltip will tell you that directory and how much it uses, and other markers will point at and label the child directories that use the most space.<p>The center circle is the directory you&#x27;ve chosen to analyze, the next ring out is the immediate subdirectories of it, the ring after that is the next level of subdirectories, etc.<p>Baobab also has a toggle below the sunburst that switches to a nested-rectangle view that might be a lot more intuitive for you.  It&#x27;s definitely more useful for me when trying to see relative usage across the whole drive, but I like the sunburst when looking for the largest sibling subdirectories.')
