Item(by='akamaka', descendants=None, kids=[25417533], score=None, time=1607917111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25411214, text='As a teenager, I picked out a copy of <i>The Little Drummer Girl</i> from a used book store quite by chance.  I expected a simple spy action story, but what I read was a deep and anguishing examination of the personal impact of geopolitics on a someone’s identity.<p>If you’re new to Le Carré and want to tip your toe in, I’d recommend watching <i>The Night Manager</i> mini-series.<p>For someone who already enjoys Le Carré, it would be a shame to overlook <i>A Perfect Spy</i>, which is his most autobiographical and soul-wrenching work.  Both the book and 1987 mini-series are excellent.')
