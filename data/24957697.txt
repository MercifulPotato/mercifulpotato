Item(by='dingaling', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604214115, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24955389, text='&gt; The difference between having 1,000 and 10,000 nuclear weapons is fairly meaningless from a MAD standpoint, but the second still costs ~10 times as much.<p>Forget about MAD, that was an interim doctrine that faded out in the late 1960s.<p>Once delivery systems demonstrated sufficient accuracy to hit point targets the doctrine changed to counterforce.<p>For counterforce, targeting individual weapons and enablers, you need lots of lower-yield warheads. The total yield of the nuclear aresenals actually declined as a result.')
