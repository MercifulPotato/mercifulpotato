Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611211734, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25854802, text='Actually it is vice-versa.\nBrown is a color whose hue is orange and which has low brightness.<p>You can use &quot;orange&quot; to describe a saturated or even a monochromatic color, at any brightness, so orange can be used to describe a color of light.<p>You cannot use &quot;brown&quot; for any color with high brightness, like you cannot use &quot;black&quot; or &quot;gray&quot; for colors with high brightness.<p>Given a brown color, if you increase its brightness, at a certain level it will no longer look brown.')
