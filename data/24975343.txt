Item(by='recursive', descendants=None, kids=[24975830], score=None, time=1604366061, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24971226, text='There&#x27;s no need to notify the server that your user-agent is opting out of cookies.  Cookies only work because your user agent remembers them.  You can opt out of cookies <i>today</i> by turning off cookies in your user agent.  As a bonus, this even works for shady servers that don&#x27;t respect earnest requests.')
