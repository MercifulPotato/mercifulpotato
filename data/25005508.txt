Item(by='hyko', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604644820, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24999145, text='In a world without a level playing field, the solution to poor quality industrial policy is better industrial policy.<p>Foreign subsidies don’t result in a net transfer of wealth if they undercut your otherwise competitive domestic industry, for obvious reasons. It results in a misallocation of capital and the erosion of opportunities that should have otherwise existed domestically.<p>All countries engage in free market distortion, to varying degrees. That may well be a suboptimal use of their resources, but unfortunately it also hurts countries that don’t respond in kind and who have bought into some simplified economic policy slogans.')
