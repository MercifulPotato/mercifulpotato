Item(by='GistNoesis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610969554, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25817605, text='It doesn&#x27;t seem like a good idea.<p>When you are lonely and sad, conjuring up entities could probably lock-you up in a mental hellish prison of bad thought cycles, where lack of imagination feeds into itself.<p>Imagination is a very powerful tool that is very under-utilized in our modern western societies. There would be a lot to gain by teaching the basics, providing some guidance and a safe-environment to let it run.<p>Once you have enough imagination so that your mental world feeds itself plenty of interesting novelties, you probably haven&#x27;t time to be lonely anymore.<p>Growing an interesting mental world, probably benefits a lot from experiences in the real world from other real people and an escape from reality probably isn&#x27;t the solution.<p>Specifically confronting reality is a great way to discover holes in your mental models.')
