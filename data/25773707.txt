Item(by='barnacled', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610613116, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25769570, text='I honestly think this is a completely wrong-headed way of looking at things. You are paid what you&#x27;re paid not because the company is determining what is &#x27;fair&#x27; given all of your circumstances, but rather the minimum they feel they can get away with.<p>Nearly all employees would accept more pay if they were offered it, nearly all employers would pay less if they could - so like any market it is always an equilibrium between the two. And frankly in some job markets the salary is negotiated better than others.<p>There is a sort of learned helplessness to &#x27;I don&#x27;t deserve more for this job&#x27; etc. in the eternal words of Unforgiven - deserve&#x27;s got nothing to do with it.')
