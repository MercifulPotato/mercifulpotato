That can be incredibly confusing if you don’t introspect about it. Some tasks are difficult or pointless to try to master all new again, but if you find the right task to focus on you there’s a chance you’ll love these strange new connections being drawn. But it is not easy and not for everyone. Most notably for me personally is that it can let you forget habits, those habits that you are not even aware of')
