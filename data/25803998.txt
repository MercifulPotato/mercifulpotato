Item(by='xg15', descendants=None, kids=[25804063, 25808298], score=None, time=1610820519, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803740, text='There are legitimate reasons to set minimum prices: It can prevent price dumping, where a company with deep pockets starves out competition and the hikes the prices once competition is gone (or otherwise abuses its now dominant status).<p>Rebates can also be used to covertly raise prices: Just declare your current price a rebate, then at some point &quot;return&quot; to the regular price which is now higher. Also great for discriminating against certain groups or forcing a certain behavior: Just tailor the rebate to everyone <i>except</i> the group you&#x27;re targeting.')
