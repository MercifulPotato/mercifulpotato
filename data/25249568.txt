Item(by='pritovido', descendants=None, kids=[25250722], score=None, time=1606696792, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25247715, text='A very important thing with software is that the most painful work can be done by the computer itself.<p>Tools like Lisp are essential in your toolbox for whatever language you use for coding. If you can script and automate and test everything life is much better.<p>Most of the pain in software development is self inflicted. The most boring something is for a human, the easiest is for the computer to make it.<p>Lots of people coming from the &quot;code must be efficient&quot; front, like assembly, c and c++ programmers simply ignored the interpreted, functional and painfully slow &quot;you don&#x27;t know how things are implemented&quot; world and viceversa.<p>But both worlds are complimentary.<p>When I was a kid I discovered Numega SoftIce. That was an incredible debugger and you could automate everything.<p>Turns out you can do the same with gdm and lldb today and bugs just pop up from automatic tests.')
