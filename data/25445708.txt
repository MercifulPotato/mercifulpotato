Item(by='mplewis', descendants=None, kids=[25445943], score=None, time=1608140957, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25445606, text='In Bitcoin, reusing an address gives folks who watch the blockchain information about whose money this is and what they use it for.<p>Let&#x27;s say you have $20 in your address, and you want to spend $10 on candy bars. A common pattern of use is to spend all $20: $10 goes to the candy bar company, and $10 goes to a new address you just created. This helps obfuscate how much money you actually have.')
