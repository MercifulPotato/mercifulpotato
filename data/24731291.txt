Item(by='vorpalhex', descendants=None, kids=[24732499, 24731471], score=None, time=1602259723, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24731105, text='Do you believe the general public is better at filtering out misinformation than they were 100 years ago? What about 500 years ago?<p>It takes time but people do seem to get better at filtering out mis-information. I believe (and we have some evidence) that we can speed this up by helping teach critical dissection of articles and content and build the skill set to weigh sources.<p>The problem will never go away entirely. It&#x27;s an arms race between people who want to get closer to understanding reality, and the people selling a story to some end (profit, power, etc). Like all arms races, sometimes one side lags behind but they usually adapt and leap ahead.')
