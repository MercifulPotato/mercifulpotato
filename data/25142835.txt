Item(by='touristtam', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605735892, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25138808, text='There have been cases in other western language where the word nigger (or its local variation) was used to describe an actual object or an activity. Since then it has often been scrubbed out. Was it the right thing to do? Was it wrong?
