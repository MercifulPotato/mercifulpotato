So the factor of 2.4 times isn&#x27;t quite as high as it might first appear.<p>The size of a median single family house has also grown. Americans on average are getting older, sicker, and fatter. New treatments have been developed which can extend the lives of some of the sickest patients but at an extremely high cost.
