Item(by='DoreenMichele', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606414682, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25219924, text='Average pay for writing isn&#x27;t that high.<p>I think an angle you should focus on is that technical blogs are a means to develop technical careers. Most blogs don&#x27;t make money or don&#x27;t make much money.<p>But if you have a skill, like programming, that can command an excellent wage or salary, a blog can help you learn your craft, show your expertise and network. However, you will need to back that up with real world examples, not just hypotheticals.<p>That&#x27;s the way to avoid being thought of as &quot;Someone getting rich telling people to buy my book about how to get rich&quot; when that&#x27;s your entire schtick.')
