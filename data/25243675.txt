A milestone that marks when you have done &quot;enough&quot; and can quit without feeling like you failed.<p>2. A metric that reinforces the idea that &quot;I need to keep doing this to avoid bad things or to get good things. When I stop, things I don&#x27;t desire happen instead.&quot;<p>Without one of those, you will tend to have some internal, subconscious thing go either &quot;Yup, I&#x27;ve gotten what I needed and can&#x27;t be bothered with this anymore.&quot; or &quot;This is pointless drivel and doing nothing and wasting my time, and I can&#x27;t be bothered with this anymore.&quot;')
