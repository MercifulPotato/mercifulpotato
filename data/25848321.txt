The equality stand is harmonic with this goal, and completely antagonistic to open core business models.<p>That&#x27;s why people can move between companies and continue working on the same code, because it is solving a internal problems on many different companies. And the companies don&#x27;t block sharing the improvements because the problems the software solves is internal.<p>Anyway, that business model does not lead to creating companies. It&#x27;s more prone to creating foundations.')
