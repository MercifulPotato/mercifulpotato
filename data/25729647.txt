Item(by='chaoxu', descendants=None, kids=[25730002], score=None, time=1610377080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25723633, text='There are a few misconceptions that some other commenters are already making.<p>This min-cut is different from the max-flow min-cut theorem&#x27;s min-cut. This is what referred to as global min-cut, which does not fix the terminals. In max-flow min-cut theorem, it is about disconnecting two fixed vertices s and t. In the global min-cut, it is about disconnecting two arbitrary vertices.<p>Source: thesis was on hypergraph min-cut')
