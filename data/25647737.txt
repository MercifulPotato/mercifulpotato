Item(by='franky47', descendants=None, kids=[25648704, 25654119], score=None, time=1609866661, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25646262, text='No need to go through the speakers, you could detect keystrokes, map them to transients in the recorded audio stream and use a noise gate to reduce their volume (at the cost of a little added latency).<p>Bonus point: this would allow you to speak and type at the same time with minimal reduction of speech comprehension, especially if using a multi-band noise gate (that acts on various frequency bands independently).
