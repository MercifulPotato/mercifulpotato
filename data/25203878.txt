Item(by='dkersten', descendants=None, kids=[25204692, 25203947], score=None, time=1606257780, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25203744, text='It reminds me of the old (maybe it still does it, I dunno) windows install progress bars vs ones where multiple things are wrapped into one.<p>With multiple things wrapped into one, you often end up in situations where 0-99% (or whatever) might happen super fast but that last 1% takes ages, making the progress bar useless as a &quot;something&#x27;s happening&quot; indicator.<p>Those old windows installers used two bars: the top one was basically a steps indicator (file N of M, for example) -- just like the single bar for multiple tasks. But the second bar was a per-file bar. So if step 99 took longer, you had a second bar showing you that steps&#x2F;files progress.')
