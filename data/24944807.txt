Item(by='timonoko', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604079563, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24944053, text='In 1972 the Nova 1200  Jump-to-Subroutine wrote the Program Counter contents above the subroutine address and thus Subroutine-Return was a short jump via that address. It was possible at compile-time to check if this mechanism was enough. Otherwise the subroutine call was three words, because it was possible to make one word Pushs and Pops in that advanced architecture.<p>I made lisp-interpreter and toyed with compiler.')
