Item(by='TheOtherHobbes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608321106, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25469581, text='Just because it&#x27;s the official definition of a start-up doesn&#x27;t mean it&#x27;s a <i>good</i> definition.<p>There&#x27;s been a steady stream of bootstrapped companies providing legally and morally uncontroversial, high quality products to satisfied customers, and growing steadily until they become much bigger than &quot;small businesses&quot;.<p>IMO there&#x27;s something deranged about the need to operate on the basis of explosive growth from day zero.
