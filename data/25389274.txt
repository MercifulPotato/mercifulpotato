Item(by='riggsdk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607712994, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25389137, text='Check your USB devices - they can wake up your PC. You can disable this on a per-device level in the device manager. Find the device, open it&#x27;s properties and find the setting under the &quot;Power management&quot; tab. A windows PC can also be woken up by &quot;Wake-on-LAN&quot; magic packets sent to your network card. Go to your network adapters and open it&#x27;s properties. Press the &#x27;Configure&#x27; button. Go to the Advanced tab and find &quot;Wake on Magic Packet&quot; and turn it off.')
