Item(by='chkaloon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604330422, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24965432, text='After a bit of a scare a few weeks ago, I&#x27;ve started doing regular Google Takeout data downloads of email in addition to my normal backup procedures. I also verify that I can load the resulting mbox files into Thunderbird or another client.<p>I don&#x27;t use Google Photos, I have my own local storage, backed up in triplicate by three different processes.<p>Finally, I&#x27;m a heavy user of Google Drive, but that is synced locally on several machines, and backed up in triplicate using the same processes as the photos.<p>Never rely solely on a 3rd party for your critical data. Always have an exit plan. And test it regularly.')
