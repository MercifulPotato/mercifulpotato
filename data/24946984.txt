Like they always give an answer, and their failure modes are so different to ours that that it can be hard for non-experts to interpret the outputs.<p>In some of the less harmless applications of computer vision and machine learning, sometimes it will have very severe consequences for real people that a computer says yes or no when it really doesn&#x27;t have the information to say either or.
