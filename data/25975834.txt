Item(by='pickpuck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612066197, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25975110, text='These are mostly front-end issues.<p>Software is often judged based on these UI&#x2F;UX nuances, but the complexity of building a competent front-end system remains an undervalued part of the process.<p>The front-end team is usually (1) brought in too late in the game to provide effective feedback and (2) not given enough time to build.<p>When there&#x27;s not front-end representation at early design reviews or at initial discussions on how APIs should work, then it&#x27;s easy to forget things like form error handling.<p>Designs don&#x27;t account for all possible states, services&#x2F;APIs are in flux, until the very end.<p>Everything else is late, and then all of it has to get slapped together with duct tape and shipped in a few weeks. Performance and browser support be damned.')
