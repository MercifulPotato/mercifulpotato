Item(by='LegitShady', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603468368, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24867128, text='If you learn classical methods of drawing techniques you often do poses in different time frames - 1 minutes, then 2 minute, then 5 and 15 minute poses.<p>The shorter poses don&#x27;t let you put in any detail but you learn how to quickly express gesture and delineate important masses and shadow.<p>For 5 minutes poses you&#x27;re essentially practicing the first 5 minutes of a drawing intentionally without intending to finish it.<p>It&#x27;s essentially a similar idea.')
