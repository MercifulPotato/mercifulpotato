Item(by='souprock', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606892640, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25256961, text='In the US, &quot;college&quot; means narrow subject matter, and &quot;university&quot; means a wide variety of colleges all together on one campus.<p>For example, there may be a &quot;College of Engineering&quot; and a &quot;College of Arts and Science&quot; that are part of one university.<p>It&#x27;s possible to have a stand-alone college that isn&#x27;t in a university.
