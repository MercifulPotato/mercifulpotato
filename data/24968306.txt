Item(by='ikeboy', descendants=None, kids=[24970980, 24969663, 24970405], score=None, time=1604326672, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24968267, text='RIAA made a claim that some content violates their rights under DMCA, which in this hypothetical causes my account to be blocked. I can bring a case under the declaratory judgement act against RIAA, asking a court to decide whether the content is actually infringing. See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Declaratory_judgment" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Declaratory_judgment</a><p>I may also have a tortious interference claim, although damages are likely negligible so it&#x27;s probably not worth bringing any claims other than that declaratory judgement one.')
