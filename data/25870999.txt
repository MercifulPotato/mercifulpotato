Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=[25871956, 25871397], score=None, time=1611317712, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25870327, text='Strongly this.<p>Privacy is not a technology problem. It&#x27;s a business problem. As long as the adtech industry is allowed to thrive, as long as people build companies with ad-based or data-resale-based business models, this will be an endless game of whac-a-mole, with the browsers only ever growing in complexity, and building anything on the web only becoming more difficult.<p>We have to address the root cause: advertising as a business model. My suggestion: let&#x27;s apply regulatory measures to kill this business model entirely.')
