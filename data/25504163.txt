The environment of the D.C. area is corrosive to American democracy IMO. But it is an environment. It&#x27;s not &quot;dinner that one time&quot;. It&#x27;s your kids going to the same schools, sharing gyms and frequent meals, friends in common even family.<p>Lobbyists work for foundations and politicians before lobbying and sometimes return to their roots after a time. They even ping pong back and forth. Just like tech workers bouncing through the industry.<p>Those relationships and networks are powerful. This is part of why, until the new conservatism arose there was a good deal of cooperation and agreement among legislators. The professionalization of the government and it&#x27;s connections to elite academy and industry created a singular cultural entity.')
