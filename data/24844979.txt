Item(by='otterley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603257829, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24841536, text='A good rule of thumb is that when an attorney sends you a cease and desist letter, you should hire an attorney to read it and give you advice on what to do and&#x2F;or how to respond -- <i>especially</i> if you are inclined not to assent to the demands made.<p>I know this, not only as an attorney today, but as someone who (before I got my law degree) did not do this and paid a very high price for my immaturity. Hiring an attorney could have saved me many thousands of dollars.')
