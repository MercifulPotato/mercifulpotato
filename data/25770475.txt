Item(by='tzs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610587017, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25767523, text='The whole point of a stable release is to be, well, stable.<p>People want to be able to develop their things they need, such as their organization&#x27;s website, online store, internal email system, support system, and things like that, deploy them, and then get on with doing whatever the organization was organized to do.<p>They don&#x27;t want to have to be constantly fiddling with all those things to keep them working. They want to build them, deploy them, and then not have to spend much effort on them until they want to add new features.')
