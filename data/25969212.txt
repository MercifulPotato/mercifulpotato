Item(by='darkerside', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612016005, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25969173, text='You&#x27;re falling into a common trap of misassociating logic with truth. They are related, but not 1:1. A logical fallacy does not price something is false, it just demonstrates that an argument is not logically airtight.<p>A statement based on a logical fallacy can turn out to be true. A prediction based on nothing at all can turn out to be true as well. All a logical fallacy means is that <i>the statement will not NECESSARILY turn out to be true</i>.')
