Item(by='cerved', descendants=None, kids=[25501978], score=None, time=1608598748, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25501537, text='My beef with mutable data is that it&#x27;s often very hard to see when it&#x27;s supposed to be modified and by what.<p>I don&#x27;t get upset by a local var i in a while loop, although I often don&#x27;t see the point.<p>The problem is the dark mutable data. All those objects being passed around, are they just being read?, are they being modified by this method call?<p>So if mutable data is used responsibly, it&#x27;s not a problem. But there&#x27;s no guarantee that it&#x27;s used responsibly. Unless all data is immutable. Then it&#x27;s guaranteed')
