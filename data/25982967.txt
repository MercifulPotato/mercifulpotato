Item(by='admax88q', descendants=None, kids=[25983300], score=None, time=1612130417, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25980057, text='It feels like Lisp scales vertically and Java scales horizontally.<p>Java scales for number of developers, its idioms and ecosystem make it easier to have tons of developers on a single project.<p>Lisp scales on the size of problem that a small close knit team can solve.<p>Things like the macro system in Lisp encourage defining your own problem specific DSL, which is great for individual developer productivity, but bad for involving lots of developers.')
