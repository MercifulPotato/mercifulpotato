Item(by='skye-adaire', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611935690, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25954964, text='&gt;<i>In offline rendering we usually just have some primitive (sphere, cube etc) that acts as the boundary</i><p>Right, using a SDF as a boundary condition is to discretely check it as an indicator function (if negative do x else do y). You can use basically any SDF to bound your procedural medium, including a procedural boundary (via domain distortion).<p>You can also apply a continuous blend instead of the discrete check (smooth step the level curve). This is preferred since discrete checks will often result in popping.')
