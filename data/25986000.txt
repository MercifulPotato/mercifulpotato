Item(by='emrah', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612159147, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25979502, text='That&#x27;s a nice quote but I wonder if something was lost in translation. &quot;Preparation&quot; seems too &quot;point-in-time&quot;, like you do X, Y, Z and you are ready.. which doesn&#x27;t seem right.<p>Opportunities in life are moving targets, there is no way to be &quot;prepared&quot; at any point in time. I think a better way to say this would be &quot;Luck is what happens when momentum meets opportunity&quot;.<p>So you are engaged with something (like running a SaaS company), something happens (like one of your large competitors goes out of business) and your activities up to that point put you in a spot that makes it possible to benefit from that event.')
