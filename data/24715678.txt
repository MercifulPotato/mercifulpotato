If you simulate it, 1&#x2F;3 of all games go to &#x27;stick&#x27; regardless.  Either 1&#x2F;3 or 2&#x2F;3 go to &#x27;switch&#x27;, and either 1&#x2F;3 or 0&#x2F;3 (respectively) end up with Monty revealing the car.')
