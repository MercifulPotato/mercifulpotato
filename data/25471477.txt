Item(by='twh270', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608323135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25466763, text='We have crap software because the (perceived) consequences of crap software are generally low: wasted time, rework, correctable errors. As the consequences of crap software become more serious, the tolerance for it goes down.<p>The reason there are so many terrible software developers is simple: the world tolerates the software they write.<p>This feature isn&#x27;t limited to software. As Theodore Sturgeon put it, &quot;90 percent of everything is crap&quot;.')
