Item(by='foopod', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611775321, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931912, text='I love examples like this, I hope you don&#x27;t mind me tagging on my own.<p>Our team was doing in home interviews for a TV product. One of the customers subscribed to an expensive sports service and yet barely ever watched it.<p>When we probed it was because her two sons had left home in the last year and she knew they would come home when a game was on if she had the service. She said that she wish she knew when big&#x2F;popular games would be coming up so she could invite them around and see them more often.<p>We trialed a fortnightly email newsletter for people that were subscribed but not very active. And saw a decent uptake in viewing almost immediately.')
