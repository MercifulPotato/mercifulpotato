What are the legitimate use cases for being allowed to speak to other humans without your voice being recorded in case the authorities want to know what you said later?<p>Fundamentally financial privacy is just privacy. People desire financial privacy for all the reasons they desire any other kind of privacy. It&#x27;s certainly fine to make the collectivist argument that individual privacy is not worth the social cost of opacity, but the idea that this is true <i>only</i> in finance seems odd and difficult to justify.')
