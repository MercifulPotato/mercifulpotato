Item(by='krapp', descendants=None, kids=[25948949, 25948945], score=None, time=1611866452, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25948724, text='A slippery slope argument assumes the maximum degenerate outcome of sliding down the slope as a certainty, that no one will be able to correct for, mitigate or reverse the slide when it starts. That&#x27;s what makes it a fallacy - it&#x27;s a thought terminating cliche meant to eliminate the possibility of nuance, as any middle ground <i>is implicitly part of the slope.</i><p>Otherwise, it&#x27;s just an argument that risk exists, which is obvious and uninteresting.')
