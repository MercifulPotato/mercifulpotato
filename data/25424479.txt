It was added to C++11 with the intent that it be used by garbage-collected implementations, but I&#x27;m not sure if anyone has ever properly implemented it.  At least, the big three STL implementations have get_pointer_safety() always return pointer_safety::relaxed (&quot;All pointers are considered valid and may be dereferenced or deallocated&quot;), and there has been a proposal to remove pointer safety from future versions of the standard.
