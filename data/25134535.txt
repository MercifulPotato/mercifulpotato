Item(by='xenadu02', descendants=None, kids=[25136522], score=None, time=1605689565, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25132679, text='Stickiness of architecture is a change from how Rosetta worked in the PowerPC to Intel transition: in macOS Big Sur child processes will prefer the arch of the parent process if one is available to maximize compatibility. So for example launching x86_64 sh will cause a python invocation from that shell to also be x86_64.<p>The &quot;arch&quot; utility is your escape hatch to switch the preferred arch for the spawned process and all of its children.')
