Item(by='yorwba', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603545902, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24877969, text='A nice story, although some parts make it obvious that the author heavily relied on Google Translate to make sense of the Chinese sorces:<p>&gt; Once the Ministry of Public Security gave the order the teams swooped in and made the arrests of the five key suspect targets: Ma Mou, Ma Mosong, Wen Mou, Lu Mou, and a technician with the surname Liu (no first name)<p>Actually, the only person in that list whose given name is partially revealed is Ma Mosong, whose name apparently ends with -song. Mou (spelled Mo in parts of the article) is a wildcard used in Chinese to partially hide someone&#x27;s name. So Ma Mosong is actually Ma Something-song (and Ma is his family name).')
