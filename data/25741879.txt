Item(by='Alupis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610422041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25741844, text='More explicitly, you own the shape within a certain, limited in scope, industry.<p>Most trademarks that contain everyday words must explicitly state no ownership claim of such words, but instead claim ownership of the shape of the word (and sometimes color) within a specific context, ie. &quot;Apple&quot;.<p>For most trademarks, this usually means shape of the logo is what is protected, not the word used inside the logo.')
