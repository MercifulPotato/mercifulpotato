Item(by='scrollaway', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607049105, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25296282, text='For those who actually want to do this on Google, there is a tool called the Inactive Account Manager (not available for GSuite&#x2F;Google Workspace):<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;myaccount.google.com&#x2F;inactive" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;myaccount.google.com&#x2F;inactive</a><p>(More info: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.google.com&#x2F;accounts&#x2F;answer&#x2F;3036546" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.google.com&#x2F;accounts&#x2F;answer&#x2F;3036546</a>)<p>This utility allows you to give access to a variety of data to another user when your Google account becomes inactive for a configurable long period of time. It also allows you to send certain emails or whatever.<p>I recommend everyone who uses a Google account checks it out at the very least. It&#x27;s a hell of a tool.')
