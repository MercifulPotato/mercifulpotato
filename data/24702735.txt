For instance, lay out your desk a certain way and you&#x27;ll finish your work earlier and have more time to spend gaming.  Acknowledging productive tasks that allow for dealing with pent up energy is also important.<p>My parents for some reason thought hanging negatives over my head always mentioning &quot;think of what would go wrong if you don&#x27;t do the right thing&quot;.
