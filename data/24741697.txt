It also turns out that many complex tasks can be reduced to a collection of simple tasks, otherwise known as &quot;a bunch of crappy scripts&quot;.<p>I encourage all my teams to avoid built-in CI&#x2F;CD features and plugins and just script what they want in a Docker container.
