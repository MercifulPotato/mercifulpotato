Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611780771, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927172, text='Those people can still be useful:<p>When there is an open question, with important consequences but unclear resolution, it is hard to know the right answer. Somehow, it is easier to know the <i>wrong</i> answer, and that person will reach for it immediately. So, watch him and choose the opposite.<p>In any group there is such a person, called the Oracle of Wrong, and almost anybody can tell you who it is. He is the one most likely to wear a trilby, and no wrong choice he has made has ever caused him any personal discomfort.')
