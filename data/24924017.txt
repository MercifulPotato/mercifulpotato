Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603918438, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24923873, text='&gt; We only allow those of a certain age to vote because they are able to understand and weigh the issues fairly.<p>Yes, but we draw a simple, arbitrary rule at a fixed age because everyone grows older, so it isn&#x27;t manipulable by anyone who is motivated to do so.<p>If the vote permission line is drawn by more complex means that gives a different answer for different people, it becomes a primary target for manipulation, abuse, and group selectivity.<p>For example if it was based on IQ tests or even general knowledge tests, it is already well understood that these are heavily biased tests which test for social background, culture and upbringing, rather than general intelligence.<p>If there&#x27;s going to be a voter test, it needs to be extremely robust, something just about everyone has confidence in to be fair and appropriate.')
