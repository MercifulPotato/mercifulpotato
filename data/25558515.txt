Then he pauses, stares at the blackboard, says &quot;one moment please&quot; and runs out of the door. Five minutes later he comes back in, with a wad of scratch paper in his hand, picks up the chalk and says &quot;it is <i>indeed</i> obvious that ...&quot; and continues the lecture.')
