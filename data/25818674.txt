Item(by='Johnythree', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610949704, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25816204, text='I think the main reason that phones don&#x27;t have broadcast AM and FM enabled (even though the hardware is present in the chips), is because the digital chips in the phone (micro-processor etc) would generate so much interference (at these relatively low frequencies) that it would be unworkable. To sufficiently shield the main PCB would require a huge effort, which means weight, size and cost.<p>To get an idea, you only need to hold your AM&#x2F;FM radio near your PC or Laptop.')
