Item(by='gen220', descendants=None, kids=[24864039, 24863627], score=None, time=1603402402, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24863389, text='I feel like pandoc is kind of like a secret club.<p>Once you know about pandoc, you orient all of your prose around it, because it&#x27;s so powerful and useful.<p>But before you use it, the common opinion is &quot;oh that sounds nice&quot;, not &quot;oh let me build all of my workflows around it&quot;.<p>It is incredible how far markdown, sed and pandoc (with filters) can take you!<p>I&#x27;ve made websites with breadcrumbs, validated inter-document links and images, on and on; mostly pipelines with sed and pandoc.<p>All of my non-source-code homework in college went through pandoc, and was persisted as markdown source. It makes for clean diffs, and great version history.<p>Anyway. Give pandoc a try if you haven&#x27;t already.')
