Item(by='sylvain_kerkour', descendants=None, kids=[25595537], score=None, time=1609435673, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25594449, text='Is there any reasons to prefer BIGINT instead of UUID (other than the very few % of performance, which may never be a problem)?<p>I just find instagram&#x27;s ID complex (custom epoch, mandatory use of RETURNING) while Ulid is elegant (easy to generate and use).<p>For me the biggest selling point was the interoperability with other kinds of UIDs, if later your requirements change.<p>Also, note that (at least in Rust) Ulid are implemented as u128, so are pretty fast.')
