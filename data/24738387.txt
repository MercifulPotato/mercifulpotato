Item(by='mattivc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602331685, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24734159, text='The experience is very close. Much more so than with an iPad and Apple Pencil.<p>I guess what I love about it is that in many ways combines the best of both physical and digital writing. It feels very much like writing with a real piece of paper, and it does not have any distracting apps like social media or a web browser. It also brings the benefits of being a digital device, in that you can edit things after the fact. Such as rearranging pages. Selecting portion of a text or drawing and then move it within the page, or to another page. Do copy and paste, and things like that.')
