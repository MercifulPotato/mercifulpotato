The networking is done by, say, nginx rather than a server that is part of the application.<p>fastcgi applications can &quot;scale to 0&quot; and cost nothing when they are not used. An application server must always have at least one open socket on a running machine.<p>fastcgi would probably do better over http&#x2F;2 than other protocols not designed for multiplexing.')
