To efficiently automate trading, AI4Finance provides this educational resource and makes it easier to learn about deep reinforcement learning (DRL) in quantitative finance.<p>In quantitative finance, automated trading is essentially making dynamic decisions, namely to decide where to trade, at what price, and what quantity, over a highlystochastic and complex stock market. Taking many complex financialfactors into account, DRL trading agents build a multi-factor model and provide algorithmic trading strategies, which are difficult for human traders<p>FinRL provides a framework that supports various markets, SOTA DRL algorithms, benchmarks of many quant finance tasks, live trading, etc.')
