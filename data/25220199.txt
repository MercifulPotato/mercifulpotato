You think you&#x27;re going to be left alone from those in positions of power (whether it be the mainstream media or government bodies)?<p>The idea is to envision an internet where there is no specific target to leave alone or not leave alone.<p>If publishing and consuming content are low-friction events, and censoring that content is impossible, then the internet will have achieved a substantial milestone in solving the legal, cultural and regulatory problems you correctly identify as underlying causes.<p>Is it a complete solution?
