Item(by='ddingus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604868035, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25028580, text='One last thing:<p>Conflating secrecy with security lies at the heart of many election problems in the US today.<p>Secure vote records mean those vote records, once cast, are kept consistent and not lost, so that confidence in a publicly visible count remains high.<p>Not identifying votes to voters is the basis for secrecy being conflated into reasons why the public is denied visibility into the process.')
