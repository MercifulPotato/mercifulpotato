Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604352254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24973401, text='I’m not sure if you were asking a question or if you’re trying to lead the discussion in a particular direction.<p>Cardio doesn’t solely use cardio muscle, true, but your cardiovascular system is what you are targeting with the exercise and it <i>does not</i> need a recovery period, unlike other parts of your body you would exercise.<p>You can do intense cardio every single day, or at least nearly every day, because the cardiovascular system works differently from your skeletal muscles. You should <i>not</i> do an intense deadlift session every day, because your skeletal muscles need time to heal.<p>So I would in general not try to draw many comparisons between cardiovascular exercise and other types of exercise.')
