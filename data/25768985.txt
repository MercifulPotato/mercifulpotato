Item(by='r1ch', descendants=None, kids=[25770600], score=None, time=1610577659, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25764718, text='If you can live with only 802.11ac, I&#x27;ve had great results flashing OpenWRT onto Mikrotik wAP AC boards. Performance peaks at about ~400mbps TCP throughput at 2x2 MCS-9. WPA3 works without problems. For multi-ap, setting up 802.11r is fairly straightforward, k&#x2F;v requires some custom scripting to generate the neighbor reports. Be careful not to get the new revision with the two chain radio as the chipset is different and not yet supported by OpenWRT.')
