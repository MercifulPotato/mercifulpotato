Item(by='roenxi', descendants=None, kids=[24661644, 24661535], score=None, time=1601636861, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24661234, text='&gt; There is no such thing in general as &quot;too many people for the economy&quot;.<p>Sure there is. If nothing else, at some point you have more people than can be physically fed with the available land.<p>Each additional person uses up a certain amount of resources. If the marginal resource gain of adding another worker is smaller than that; then economically there are too many people. There is no principle saying that cannot happen.')
