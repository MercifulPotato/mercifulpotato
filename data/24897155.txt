What&#x27;s new is the motivating control (choice) moved from the advertiser&#x27;s intent to the algorithmic recommenders.<p>#2<p>Third parties learned to effectively manipulate the algorithmic recommenders. So whereas before the gatekeepers acted as a great filter, third parties are now able to command attention and drive narratives.<p>--<p>Forgive me for stumbling over my descriptions. I&#x27;m just now trying to write out my notions. And I don&#x27;t think any of this is &quot;new&quot;.
