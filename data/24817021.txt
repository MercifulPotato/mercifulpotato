Item(by='Slartie', descendants=None, kids=[24817273], score=None, time=1603019962, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24816565, text='Your critique would be a lot more valid if you would admit that nuclear power proponents also usually &quot;forget&quot; about the systemic costs of nuclear power, such as the incredible costs of deconstructing old plants, the costs of storing the waste in a safe place for thousands of years, and the costs of insuring against catastrophic failures in plants that might make wide swaths of land around them uninhabitable...oh wait, that insurance is so expensive that effectively nobody provides it at any cost.
