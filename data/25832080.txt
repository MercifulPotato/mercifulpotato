I think partly because of an influx of new devs who weren&#x27;t in the position of having little choice but to learn at least some of the non-GUI way, particularly in the web sphere. As things have matured (arguably) and become more complex, and those devs have gained experience, they&#x27;ve started seeing the benefits of other methodologies.<p>I just hope it doesn&#x27;t swing back too far the other way as it has before (I&#x27;ve been around long enough to have seen the cycle previously!). I&#x27;m a big fan for command line, scriptable, tools and plain text pipe-able output, and have been since being introduced to pipes &amp; redirection all those decades ago, but we need to avoid the holier-than-thou thing that seems to develop when our old timer ways get the lime-light for a while. It only serves to put people off learning that they are great tools for many jobs and definitively the right ones for a some.')
