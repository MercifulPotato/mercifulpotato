Item(by='djrobstep', descendants=None, kids=[25122915, 25122580, 25122761], score=None, time=1605612584, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25122103, text='I have used and followed Ansible for a long time (iirc I was one of the first hundred people to star it on GitHub in the early days) and while I love certain aspects of it (agentless, strong emphasis on idempotency), I found the yaml format to eventually be limiting and inflexible, and have shifted to a more roll-your-own approach to infra with vanilla python and libraries like paramiko and boto3.<p>I wish there was a convenient way of using the modules (which are robust and powerful) without the yaml format and boilerplate.')
