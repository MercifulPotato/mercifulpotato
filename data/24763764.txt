Item(by='JulianMorrison', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602581111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24762980, text='The midway point between &quot;maintain your own servers&quot; and &quot;wholly in the cloud&quot; is &quot;configuration as code&quot;, using chef and terraform, or similar tools.<p>You <i>don&#x27;t</i> patch your OS, or your apps, you define their versions and configuration in code and it gets built for you. And typically snapshotted at that point and made into a restartable instance image that can be simply thrown away if it&#x27;s misbehaving, and rerun from the known good image.')
