AWS will assign 2 static IPs (not EIPs, but they will never change until the Global Accelerator is  deleted) or you can use your own block of IP (BYOIP).\nThen resolve the DNS to the Global Accelerator static IPs. And forward your traffic from AWS Global Accelerator to your ALBs, EC2 instances or NLBs.')
