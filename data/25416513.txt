Item(by='BaronSamedi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607948450, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25415315, text='One rarely discussed effect of drug legalization is that pharmaceutical companies would start developing recreational drugs. I expect they would create milder versions of current drugs specifically for the recreational market. That market would almost certainly be huge. Because of this, Big Pharma ought to be one of the biggest proponents of drug legalization. Whether the net effect on society would be positive or negative I do not know.')
