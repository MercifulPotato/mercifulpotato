Item(by='jszymborski', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602362237, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24741657, text='FWIW, Mathjax doesn&#x27;t have much to do with AJAX calls.<p>AJAX is just ubiquitous and it&#x27;s easier to use without frameworks nowadays. React is a library that solves different problems from the frameworks that made AJAX easier like Prototype and jQuery.<p>React helps folk build UIs through interesting data-models and the ShadowDOM. AJAX is required by anything but the most trivial React projects to make server-side requests.<p>Hope that helps :)')
