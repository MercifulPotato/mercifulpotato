Item(by='roblabla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604147372, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24949716, text='First, piracy is not a breach of EULA, it&#x27;s copyright infringement, which are entirely different beasts. Second, breaching the EULA of a hardware product might be breaking the law in the US, but in the EU, hardware products cannot be bound by contracts. You buy a good, not a service, so no EULA can apply. I think even in the US, it depends on the state you&#x27;re in...<p>So, no, legally, they have almost nothing in common.')
