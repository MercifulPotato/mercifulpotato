Item(by='lastofthemojito', descendants=None, kids=[25933268, 25933234], score=None, time=1611777414, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25931398, text='One of the great things about accessibility is that it often doesn&#x27;t just benefit people with disabilities.<p>My wife and I have watched a lot of TV in the past year with the volume down and captioning on, while we enjoy some down time while our baby is sleeping.<p>A ramped entrance to a building allows wheelchair-bound folks access, but it also helps able-bodied people using delivery dollies.<p>Making simple, lightweight web pages makes them accessible to those with older browsers and devices, but it also saves power consumption, time, and frustration for folks on M1 MacBook Pros.')
