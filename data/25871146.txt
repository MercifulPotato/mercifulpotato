Item(by='pas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611318937, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25870865, text='You&#x27;d run into a bigger problem than collisions a lot sooner (IMHO): time ordering. &quot;ms&quot; precision is very misleading, because keeping clocks really in sync while processing this much data is a real challenge.<p>Sure, maybe it doesn&#x27;t matter that one server&#x2F;emitter and thus a bunch of IDs are out of order, but if this is really just for helping indexes, then a simple 0.1 second precision would make more sense. (Or even simply truncating the timestamp to however many bits to get ~2-30 sec precision, and using a data store with LevelDB-like staged compaction. Which is virtually a requirement at this scale.)')
