Item(by='tomaskafka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605642200, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25124389, text='Having the experience (or, love&#x2F;hate relationship - so awesomely thin and quiet, so underpowered) with 12&quot; Macbook, one surprise is that throttle time really depends on environment temperature and GPU use.<p>In a cool room it can last few minutes before throttling, while outside on a warm day it throttles almost instantly.<p>Also, a thermal budget is shared with GPU, so once you plug-in the external display, or start Sidecar, you run out of thermal headspace pretty much instantly.<p>I&#x27;d love to see these two factors tested.')
