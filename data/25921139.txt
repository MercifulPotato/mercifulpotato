That alone is manipulation I believe.<p>You&#x27;re meant to buy(sell) because you like the idea of (not) owning something. That means you don&#x27;t want to move the price. A false price is a great false piece of info to manufacture.<p>I&#x27;m watching with interest for when WSB attracts the interest of the regulators.
