Item(by='daveoc64', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609754021, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25629232, text='The link in the email may or may not work that way - that would be down to how the authentication system has been designed.<p>Some of them recognise that users aren&#x27;t always signing in on the same device that their email account is set up on, so the link in the email just confirms that the login attempt is genuine.<p>This is similar to how Google&#x2F;Apple&#x2F;Microsoft&#x2F;Blizzard&#x2F;Steam handle login requests with their respective authenticator apps.
