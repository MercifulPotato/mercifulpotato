Item(by='Cd00d', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607629878, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25373780, text='I worked in remote sensing 2012-2014 and we had an agriculture customer base in California.<p>The problem I saw with nut trees, is during the drought nuts became far more profitable, largely because they are <i>not</i> drought resistant. So, a lot of farms were replacing the more traditional, regional, drought-resistant plants (hay, alfalfa, soy?) with nut trees that would take 5-8 years to mature and use way more water.')
