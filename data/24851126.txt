I just ran through this process to ensure I didn&#x27;t miss anything:<p>1)  Visit the site.<p>2) Click the container button or use the shortcut.<p>3) Click &#x27;Manage Containers&#x27;<p>4) Click &#x27;New Container&#x27;<p>5) Name the container, click &#x27;Ok&#x27;<p>6) Open the multi-account containers panel again.<p>7) Click &#x27;Always Open This Site in...&#x27;<p>8) Select your new container.<p>But wait, <i>you&#x27;re still not done!</i><p>9) Open the site again in a new tab.<p>10) Check the &#x27;Remember my decision for this site&#x27; box. (Didn&#x27;t I already tell it to <i>ALWAYS</i> open the site in that container, in step 7?)<p>11) Click &#x27;Open in [your container] Container&#x27;<p>That&#x27;s how you create a new container to always open a site in with the Multi-Account Containers extension.')
