But they&#x27;re not intended to. They&#x27;re intended to restrict the behaviour of people who, by and large, respect the law.<p>It&#x27;s practically unthinkable that a company like Apple&#x2F;Amazon&#x2F;etc is going to create an expensive, complicated international legal structure to conceal wilful infringement of some open source licence.<p>It&#x27;s perfectly achievable to write a licence that forbids use or incorporation in products, servers or services from particular companies.')
