You were hunting or eventually farming near your clan&#x2F;family.<p>One of my favorite pieces of trivia is how social greetings changed post-industrialization.<p>Now when you meet people you ask: “What do you do?” which implies a utilitarian view of ‘what is your role in the economy’<p>Pre-industrialization, one would ask: “Who are you?” e.g. where is your family from, how are you related, who do you know, where do you live, etc.')
