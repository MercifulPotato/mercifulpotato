Item(by='giantrobot', descendants=None, kids=[24691766], score=None, time=1601924861, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24690019, text='The environment for CSS isn&#x27;t nearly as &quot;standard&quot; as you might think. Even though WebKit and Blink are the majority engines on mobile there&#x27;s tons of combinations of versions, screen sizes, and aspect ratios. Trivial layouts are easy but more complicated layouts can be very difficult to get working right, let alone <i>looking</i> right, across browsers&#x2F;platforms.<p>The problems are often solvable but not necessarily easy. The difficulty is compounded by the heterogeneity of the Android space.<p>It&#x27;s great the majority browser engines follow the CSS standards but a lot of problems aren&#x27;t bounded by the accuracy of the implementation.')
