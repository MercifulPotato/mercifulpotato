Item(by='fwip', descendants=None, kids=[25501840], score=None, time=1608590340, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25497897, text='One way I deal with this problem in an alternative workflow manager (Nextflow), is by calculating the memory requirement for the ~95th percentile of a job, and submitting with a rule &quot;If this crashes from going OOM, re-submit with memory*N&quot; (up to some max number of retries&#x2F;RAM). This lets most jobs sail through with a relatively low amount of RAM, and the bigger jobs end up taking a bit more time and resources.<p>The better your estimator function, of course, the tighter constraints you can use.')
