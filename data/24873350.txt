Item(by='mindslight', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603483260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24873213, text='This is not a valid DMCA takedown request, as the claimant is not asserting copyright ownership of youtube-dl.<p>This notice is a mishmash between two <i>separate</i> concepts in the DMCA, the copyright takedown process and technological circumvention devices. If the RIAA wishes to claim that youtube-dl is a circumvention device, the proper route is to sue the authors of youtube-dl. This notice is an abuse of process, and highlights the need for a penalty for fraudulent DMCA requests.')
