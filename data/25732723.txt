Item(by='cgriswald', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610387359, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25731657, text='&#x27;Free speech platform&#x27; is a meaningless phrase, because it means different things to different people.<p>Almost anyone would agree that a platform which allows any speech at all is a &#x27;free speech platform&#x27; even if they don&#x27;t agree with allowing certain kinds of speech.<p>Most people would agree a platform that allows any speech outside of socially accepted exceptions (<i>e.g.</i> threats of violence, slander) is a &#x27;free speech platform&#x27;.<p>But for people who are largely not allowed to share their views (rightly or wrongly) a platform which is heavily moderated for ideological conformity can <i>also</i> be a &#x27;free speech platform&#x27; if they agree with the ideology, because they are free to speak all the speech they want to speak and are prevented from doing on other platforms. It&#x27;s pretty clear that Parler falls into this category.')
