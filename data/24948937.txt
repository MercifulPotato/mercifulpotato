Item(by='reitzensteinm', descendants=None, kids=[24951046, 24950567], score=None, time=1604114888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24948513, text='Maybe a decade ago, I read a story here on HN where somebody was analyzing a singer&#x27;s audio to detect how close their pitch was, coming to the conclusion that the root was spot on but other notes were slightly sharp or flat.<p>Someone in the comments pointed out that the analysis was done assuming even temperament, and the singer was actually totally nailing just intonation.<p>It&#x27;s one of those anecdotes that has stuck with me since as a reminder to try to not get tunnel vision on a specific metric.')
