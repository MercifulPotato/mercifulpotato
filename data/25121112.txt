Item(by='jcampbell1', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605598645, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25120075, text='It is pretty important to do mid-shift counts randomly as well.  A common scam is for the cashier to ring no sale if the total is an easy price, say $2 for a coffee.  Then mid shift the register will have extra money which would have been pocketed at the end of the shift.  If there is too much money in the register, the employee is skimming.')
