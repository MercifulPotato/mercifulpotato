Item(by='BeetleB', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607883086, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407203, text='What is missing is the information on why they choose to believe one camp and not the other. What led a particular person down that path?<p>Ask yourself this: When an anti-vaxxer speaks to another anti-vaxxer, what is different compared to when they speak to you? Are they more guarded with you? Why?<p>The other point, which is often not emphasized enough: It&#x27;s not enough to <i>be</i> something, you have to <i>show</i> it. You may know everything about why the person is an anti-vaxxer, but he doesn&#x27;t know that. Asking open ended, nonconfrontational and nonjudgmental questions often signals to others that your goal is to listen, and not to change their mind.<p>Of course, if your goal is to change their mind, you&#x27;ll make little progress.')
