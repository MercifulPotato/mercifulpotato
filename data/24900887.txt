Item(by='vorpalhex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603743591, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24899261, text='Foam is a Roam-like that is open source, easily modifiable and assembled on top of VSCode but leaves you with plain jane markdown. I use a vanilla markdown editor on my phone for quick notes, and then fire up VScode when I need tools.<p>It&#x27;s less an &quot;app&quot; and more a set of recommended extensions that work well together. At the end of the day you have markdown files in a directory, however you want to manage and sync them (git, dropbox, rsync, whatever).<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;foambubble.github.io&#x2F;foam" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;foambubble.github.io&#x2F;foam</a><p>My personal setup also integrates heavily with my Zotero toolkit for tracking and storing papers and other materials.')
