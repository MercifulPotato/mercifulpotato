Item(by='dwheeler', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609446169, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25596543, text='Open design is basically a generalization of Kerckhoffs&#x27;s principle.<p>Kerckhoffs&#x27;s principle is usually stated as &quot;A cryptosystem should be secure even if everything about the system, except the key, is public knowledge.&quot; Note that Kerckhoffs&#x27;s principle only refers to cryptosystems. The open design principle is a generalization that applies to all systems, whether or not they are cryptosystems.')
