Item(by='lordnacho', descendants=None, kids=[24944437], score=None, time=1604065814, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24935680, text='Just to be clear here, burden of proof is on whoever makes a claim. Strawmanning is refuting a claim that isn&#x27;t quite what someone else claimed.<p>Of you claim something exceptional, you need exceptional proof. Such as the mentioned world-will-end claims.<p>If you are merely saying &quot;there&#x27;s a guy who says...&quot; it&#x27;s a whole lot easier, you just have to point at where he said it.<p>I&#x27;m not making any claims, just pointing out that if someone indeed says that the world will end soon, they gotta do the heavy lifting.')
