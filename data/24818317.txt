Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[24818819], score=None, time=1603033350, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24817709, text='&gt; Cult (worship) is the root of culture<p>&quot;cult&quot; is indeed a substring of &quot;culture&quot;, but the etymology is likely the other way around. both come from the latin verb &quot;colo&quot;, which concretely means something like &quot;till&quot; or &quot;care for&quot; (in the sense of agriculture). it&#x27;s used more abstractly to mean &quot;worship&quot;, but this likely comes from the idea of caring for &#x2F; tending to an actual effigy or temple of a deity.')
