Item(by='waterhouse', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601597318, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24656790, text='How about: an insurance policy such that, if the employee <i>does</i> end up stealing, then the employer is made whole.  The former-thief employee could pay for such a policy, and then it would seem the employer would be indifferent between the former thief and the average applicant.  The policy could be paid for out of the thief&#x27;s salary; he would effectively be accepting a lower wage.<p>If the job is minimum wage, of course, then it&#x27;s not possible to lower the wage further.<p>Also, discovering the theft and proving it was that employee might be difficult.')
