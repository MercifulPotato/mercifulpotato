Item(by='ctrager', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611696374, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25921178, text='I think what you are referring to is &quot;temperment&quot;, and a TLDR version of that topic would be that for tuning a piano so that it can sound in tune no matter what key, it is tuned to be evenly  out of tune everywhere, aka, &quot;equal temperment&quot;. This little app follows equal temperment. A string player playing unaccompanied solo might tend to play in a way that sounds better, aka, &quot;just temperment&quot;, and so would seem to be out of tune according to this tool.')
