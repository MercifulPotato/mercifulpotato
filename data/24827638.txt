Item(by='raztogt21', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603123412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24822223, text='Last year I suddenly had severe atypical urticaria plus arthritis out of nowhere. It looked way worse than how it felt, but it was a little uncomfortable.<p>Today is more manageable. I improved my sleep schedule, diet, alternatives exercises, sun exposure, etc. All these actions reduced my stress levels. The funny story was that all doctors were saying &quot;your body is extremely stressed&quot;. I didn&#x27;t felt any mental stress at all.<p>In my opinion daily stress is strongly related to how quickly and how strong the chronic illness develops. I know it sounds cliche, but optimize to a low-stress life.')
