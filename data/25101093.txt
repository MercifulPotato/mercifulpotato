Item(by='sneak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605447715, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25100337, text='A positive on on the bloom filter is just an indicator that you do the bigger, more expensive (and privacy-reducing) check, like an encrypted OCSP query for that specific certificate.  It&#x27;s not the final verdict, specifically because of the risk of false positives.  Bloom filters are a way of making it so that you don&#x27;t have to do that bigger, privacy-leaking query <i>every time</i>.')
