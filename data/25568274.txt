Item(by='bjeds', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609235370, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25568028, text='Perfect opportunity to clear up a common misconception:<p>PII is more or less an American term. In the context of European legislation such as the GDPR, there is no such thing as &quot;PII&quot;.<p>Within Europe and within the context of the GDPR, you do however talk about &quot;Personal Data&quot;.<p>Personal Data and PII are both about data that can identify a person, but Personal Data (Europe) is much broader than PII (US) from a regulatory perspective.<p>This was a pet peeve of the best GDPR lawyer I ever worked with, and now it&#x27;s a pet peeve of mine as well. :-)')
