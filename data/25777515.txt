Item(by='JulianMorrison', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610638333, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25777358, text='The way it works in Mastodon is that (1) not everyone runs a node, but there are many nodes, and they each have their own policies and can kick users off, and (2) nodes can blacklist other nodes they federate content from.<p>This two level split allows node operators to think of most other users at the node level, which means dealing with far fewer entities. It provides users with a choice of hosts, but means that their choice has consequences.')
