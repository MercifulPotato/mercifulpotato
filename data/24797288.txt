Item(by='acadien', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602820468, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24796785, text='This is a common misperception. You cannot estimate the shape of simple things in low light. Even humans are susceptible to optical illusions and are effectively blind during certain times of day (try driving in the direction of the sun during sun rise&#x2F;set). Lidar is not susceptible to any of these things.\nSimilarly ML will never solve low light or direct light situations, that is a physical limitation of cameras.')
