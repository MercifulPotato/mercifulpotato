Item(by='coldpie', descendants=None, kids=[24806008, 24806449], score=None, time=1602878440, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24804402, text='You have to read the whole sentence:<p>&gt; No provider or user of an interactive computer service shall be treated as the publisher or speaker of any information provided by another information content provider.<p>The law doesn&#x27;t establish two categories, called &quot;publisher&quot; and &quot;platform.&quot; It says, if User writes something on Website, then Website will not be treated as the publisher of whatever User wrote. Instead, User will be treated as the publisher. It defines who is responsible for the content that is served by Website: the User who wrote it, not the Website that served it. At no point does it create a category called &quot;publisher&quot; who is subject to different rules from a category called &quot;platform.&quot;')
