Item(by='Smaug123', descendants=None, kids=[24878749], score=None, time=1603539957, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24878562, text='I think there&#x27;s a false dichotomy here. Memorising a dictionary is <i>easy</i>, because memorisation is a choice (with spaced repetition). Reading a book in an unfamiliar language takes lots of time and mental effort. Both will contribute to your understanding of the language; perhaps memorising a dictionary contributes less, but then there&#x27;s a lower activation energy involved in it too: whenever you have five minutes spare, you can just sit down and learn a couple of cards.')
