It is uniquely and specifically a basic “101” concept of statistical science and ML &#x2F; data science, where the custody and tracking of data are specifically tied to iterations of experiments, prototypes and research, for the sake of reproducibility.<p>If I was interviewing an experienced statistical researcher and they didn’t at least have a working knowledge of the core concepts, that would be a huge red flag.')
