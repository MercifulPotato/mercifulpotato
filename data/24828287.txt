Item(by='pochamago', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603126749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24828028, text='It&#x27;s not that it&#x27;s nefarious, but because human language often relies so much on the tone of voice used, we&#x27;ve begun creating informal norms for how to get that across in text form. A period at the end of a short phrase gives it a flat intonation in many circles. Communication isn&#x27;t as simple as proscribing by fiat how people ought to use a given medium, and pragmatism requires that we adapt to what the group does for clear communication.')
