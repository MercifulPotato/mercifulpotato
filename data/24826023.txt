You can imagine it as a &#x27;normal&#x27; lens, without unnecessary bulk of the material, just keeping the curved surface and a flat backside here. Since this isn&#x27;t possible while retaining the smooth curvature of a normal lens, it is stepped and has concentric rings of different curvature instead.<p>You can find one in front of the flash of your smartphone for example.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Fresnel_lens" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Fresnel_lens</a>')
