Item(by='gwright', descendants=None, kids=[24831221, 24834359, 24831926, 24831374], score=None, time=1603142571, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24830882, text='It isn&#x27;t clear to me that we&#x27;ve got a good technical, never mind social solution, to the problem of anonymous actors.<p>I suspect that &quot;be careful what you wish for&quot; is applicable to the idea of a communication medium that &quot;nobody can ever control or censor&quot;. We already have numerous problems caused by anonymity with our existing infrastructure (DDOS, ransomware, threats, defamation, etc.).<p>I think neither extreme, a completely anonymous infrastructure or a completely non-anonymous infrastructure, is desirable.')
