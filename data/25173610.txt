Item(by='AtlasBarfed', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606002529, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25161504, text='&quot;My advanced, fast, high-performance, and powerful language&#x2F;system&#x2F;framework is so efficient, flexible, concise, usable, readable, extensible, structured, and simple.&quot;<p>In general if I read any of those descriptors without the associated comparative object, I categorize them as advertising weasel word for whatever X is being described as having them.<p>Those words are comparison words.
