Item(by='Wowfunhappy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602277676, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24733509, text='Enabling 2FA by default is probably a good idea, but I do believe quite strongly that users should be able to turn it off. You don&#x27;t know the user&#x27;s situation—maybe they don&#x27;t have consistent access to a single phone, for instance—and if they actually use a strong and unique password, that should be sufficient.<p>Additional factors are of course always safer, but everything is a trade-off between security and usability, and users should have control.')
