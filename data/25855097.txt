Item(by='ploxiln', descendants=None, kids=[25855280, 25855204], score=None, time=1611200442, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25854711, text='Also, remember that the first argument to the logging functions is considered to be a format string. So using f-strings is not just less efficient, it is also not robust, unless you do:<p><pre><code>    logging.debug(&quot;%s&quot;, f&quot;Unexpected, got {got}&quot;)\n</code></pre>\nbecause otherwise, if the format string is the first&#x2F;only argument, and `got` unexpectedly contain `%`, an exception will be raised due to a missing format parameter.<p>(Also, for the logging functions, you don&#x27;t pass a tuple, just a normal series of arguments.)')
