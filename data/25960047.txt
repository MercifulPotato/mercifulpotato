Item(by='wtallis', descendants=None, kids=[25960193], score=None, time=1611942184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25959940, text='I think on recent kernels, using the hipri option doesn&#x27;t get you interrupt-free polled IO unless you&#x27;ve configured the nvme driver to allocate some queues specifically for polled IO. Since these Samsung drives support 128 queues and you&#x27;re only using a 16C&#x2F;32T processor, you have more than enough for each drive to have one poll queue and one regular IO queue allocated to each (virtual) CPU core.')
