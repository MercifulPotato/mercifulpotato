Item(by='greggyb', descendants=None, kids=[25230350], score=None, time=1606489454, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25228658, text='Infinitesimals and infinities are nice if you&#x27;re doing some sort of bucketing logic.<p><pre><code>    tiny = infinitesimal\n    huge = infinity\n    N = number to be bucketed\n\n    tiny &lt;= N &lt; 10  --&gt; bucket 1\n    10 &lt;= N &lt; 20    --&gt; bucket 2\n    ...\n    X &lt;= N &lt; huge   --&gt; last bucket\n\n</code></pre>\nThis removes edge cases you need to test for if you&#x27;re trying to bucket positive values. This may not be something you&#x27;ve had to do, but I&#x27;ve had reason to want this before on a few occasions.')
