Item(by='marcus_holmes', descendants=None, kids=[24776606], score=None, time=1602681600, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24775730, text='Because &quot;raise the question&quot; doesn&#x27;t have the same connotation as the commonly-understood meaning of &quot;beg the question&quot;.<p>Begging the question means (to most people, I think) that there is an obvious question in response to the statement. The statement itself is &quot;begging&quot; for this question to be asked.<p>Raising the question means that one&#x27;s response to the statement is to ask a question. It may not be obvious, and it&#x27;s not an attribute of the statement itself that implies this response.<p>I think that&#x27;s a useful distinction.')
