Item(by='bhupy', descendants=None, kids=[24861941], score=None, time=1603392957, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24861833, text='I generally agree with the opposition to wealth taxes (and no, I&#x27;m not wealthy), but I&#x27;ll try to engage with this by providing what I believe is the strongest good-faith counterargument:<p>&gt; a wealth tax creates a perverse incentive to not invest back in the company.<p>This is arguably a &quot;good thing&quot; because one could argue that it&#x27;s &quot;unfair&quot; that large, high revenue &#x2F; high income corporations allocate so much capital in our economy. It would arguably be better for them to pay the taxes and instead have that capital be allocated by a democratically accountable body: the government.')
