Item(by='Taek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610269400, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25712497, text='It&#x27;s not really correct to say that Skynet stores all files &#x27;by default in cleartext&#x27;. Skynet is a platform for building applications. Whether data is encrypted or cleartext is up to the app developer.<p>The files uploaded via <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;siasky.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;siasky.net&#x2F;</a> are cleartext, but the files uploaded via <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;skysend.hns.siasky.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;skysend.hns.siasky.net&#x2F;</a> are encrypted. There are also entire dropbox-like apps that are encrypted by default, for example <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;marstorage.hns.siasky.net&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;marstorage.hns.siasky.net&#x2F;</a><p>Most Skynet usage is app-driven, and therefore whether or not things are &quot;encrypted by default&quot; will depend on the apps that are used.')
