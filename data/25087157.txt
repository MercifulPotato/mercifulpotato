Item(by='johnday', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605299801, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25087094, text='LaTeX does report this as a problem, of course - the refrain of &quot;overfull hbox&quot; is near universal (and ignoring the warning is just as universal).<p>Where it fails is in reporting the problem in a meaningful way. This is, of course, very difficult to accomplish, since there is no nice mapping from line-of-TeX to visual error. What&#x27;s more, any tool which <i>could</i> provide such an error would be so constrained as to be useless for typesetting.')
