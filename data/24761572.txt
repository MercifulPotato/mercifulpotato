I routinely copy parts of various code projects, I’ve used before because Im too lazy to write it again. To the point where I don’t remember how it works anymore. Does anyone else do this? I feel like I’ve been coding for months sometimes when its been more half a decade.<p>Suppose my advice to any coders looking for it or aspiring ones, is to keep it fresh and to always seek Something new, even if you’ve forgotten some ways you’ve built before. It may lead you to a door you haven’t traveled through before. To me that’s what this is all about. Starting somewhere and keep seeking doors.')
