Eg:<p><pre><code>    $ (. &#x2F;etc&#x2F;os-release; echo &quot;$NAME:$VERSION_ID&quot;)\n\n    openSUSE Tumbleweed:20210121\n\n    $ command -v ls\n\n    alias ls=&#x27;_ls&#x27;\n\n    $ grep -A6 -B1 &#x27;_ls ()&#x27; &#x2F;etc&#x2F;profile.d&#x2F;ls.bash\n\n    bash|dash|ash)\n        _ls ()\n        {\n            local IFS=&#x27; &#x27;\n            command ls $LS_OPTIONS ${1+&quot;$@&quot;}\n        }\n        alias ls=_ls\n        ;;</code></pre>')
