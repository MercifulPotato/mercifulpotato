Item(by='semi-extrinsic', descendants=None, kids=[25148090], score=None, time=1605764114, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25144708, text='Come now, this is only an enhanced form of prisoners dilemma, where the prison guards do a side hustle helping prisoners rat each other out, in exchange for a &quot;very small&quot; commission. Everyone spending money on advertising is a strong Nash equilibrium, but it is not the optimal solution.<p>If everyone spends money on ads, a single actor choosing to not spend money on ads would (probably) see a loss. And in this case, you have a centralized actor (ad companies) spending a lot of resources telling everyone this fact.<p>But if everyone decided to not spend money on ads, everyone (except ad companies of course) would see a win.')
