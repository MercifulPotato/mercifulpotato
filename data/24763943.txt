Item(by='lokedhs', descendants=None, kids=[24764558], score=None, time=1602582971, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24763010, text='Correct me if I misunderstood, but that sounds insecure.<p>I imagine an attack where someone attempts to log in to your Internet bank account, and at the same time calls you and tells you that they are the bank and that they have sent you a notification on the phone to confirm this.<p>You accept the notification on the phone and all of a sudden they&#x27;re not just logged in to your Internet bank, but also on the phone with you, with you trusting that they are indeed the bank.<p>The only way I see that this could be avoided is if they provide you with a verification code that will appear on your phone.')
