Item(by='cratermoon', descendants=None, kids=[25306840], score=None, time=1607098881, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25302082, text='It&#x27;s rent-seeking if, instead of selling you the font once under a mutually agreeable license, they charge you an ongoing fee to &quot;use&quot; the font under conditions that the foundry controls and can change without re-negotiating the contract. This is especially true if the condition are &quot;you can use for purposes x, y, and z, but if you want to use it for a, b, or c you have to pay a higher rate&quot;.')
