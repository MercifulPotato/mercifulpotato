Item(by='dredmorbius', descendants=None, kids=[24979964], score=None, time=1604413323, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979366, text='Inflation is a tax on interest-bearing, interest-indexed, or financial instruments;  loans, bonds, debt, and insurance claims (policies are priced in uninflated currency, claims are paid in inflated currency, see the 1980s liability insurance crisis triggered by high inflation, though blamed on much else).<p>Wage-earners, and fixed-income households <i>if inflation indexed</i> are largely untouched by inflation.')
