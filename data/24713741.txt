Item(by='scythe', descendants=None, kids=[24714139], score=None, time=1602111119, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24713432, text='&gt;With infinite time will order spontaneously emerge from heat death?<p>The answer may be yes or no in any particular axiomatization (model) of the physical laws, but depends strongly on the choice of model. Unlike a normal situation, when we talk about very-low-probability effects we are subject to any influence of &quot;quantum gravity&quot; and other &quot;new&#x2F;unknown physics&quot; that may have a finger, however light, on the scale.<p>In the Standard Model I think the answer is yes, but we are already aware of some deficiencies (neutrino transitions).')
