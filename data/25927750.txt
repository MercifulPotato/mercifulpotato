Item(by='weeboid', descendants=None, kids=[25929191], score=None, time=1611752954, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927561, text='Classification systems (&quot;ontologies&quot;) often represent a particular path through a graph, rather than an absolute tree.<p>In your example, the store adds &quot;Shoes&quot;. Then later, it adds &quot;Sale Items&quot;. Now you want some shoes, trousers, and shirts to be on sale.<p>So each item now has two edges, its &quot;primary_category&quot;, and &quot;sale_items&quot;. A little bit later, the store turns into a Sale Items at Massive Discounts! Store, and the primary ontology becomes price tiers.<p>etc')
