Item(by='1vuio0pswjnm7', descendants=None, kids=[25819181], score=None, time=1610941207, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25815862, text='It&#x27;s really easy to scrape Instagram from outside the browser as a non-user.  No API or Access Token needed.  Two steps: 1. Get the profile ID, 2. submit query via GraphQL.<p>For example, if the username is &quot;fish&quot; and we want the first 36 entries.<p><pre><code>   1. x=$(wget -O- -q https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.instagram.com&#x2F;fish&#x2F;?__a=1|grep -o &quot;profilePage_[^\\&quot;]*&quot; |sed &#x27;s&#x2F;profilePage_&#x2F;&#x2F;&#x27;);\n\n   2. wget -O- -q &quot;https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.instagram.com&#x2F;graphql&#x2F;query&#x2F;?query_hash=e769aa130647d2354c40ea6a439bfc08&amp;variables={\\&quot;id\\&quot;:\\&quot;$x\\&quot;,\\&quot;first\\&quot;:36,\\&quot;after\\&quot;:\\&quot;\\&quot;}&quot;</code></pre>')
