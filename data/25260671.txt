Item(by='adamjb', descendants=None, kids=[25261055], score=None, time=1606781055, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25260009, text='The decibel is defined as a tenth of the log10 of power, so power doubles every ~3dB.  Amplitude and pressure are proportional to the root of power, so amplitude doubles every ~6db. Loudness is roughly proportional to the root of amplitude so it doubles every ~9dB.<p>This leads to confusion when people don&#x27;t specify which quantity they are comparing.')
