Also, you can&#x27;t jut make the filesystem transactional, in the end you also need an entire transactional OS built around it. Some years ago I set out to eliminate the accidental complexity and kept removing parts of the system that caused any specific problem. In the end, the filesystem + OS turned out to be the biggest culprit.')
