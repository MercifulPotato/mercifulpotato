Item(by='marcosdumay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610128102, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25685973, text='&gt; The only positive effect that the NDA may have for a producer is that it might prevent the already existing customers to redesign their products, because they might not bother to obtain similar NDA from other vendors.<p>That&#x27;s a positive from their competitors requiring an NDA, not for they requiring one.<p>Anyway, large software sellers benefit a lot from stupid procedures from their clients that create a huge cost to start buying from a new supplier (like passing all suppliers through legal or having a management vote for them). If all hardware manufacturers require NDAs, they can force their buyers into creating similar procedures, closing the market for new entrants.')
