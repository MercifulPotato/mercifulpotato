Item(by='hakfoo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606432209, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25203687, text='How about a progress bar that you can expand into a more itemized status presentation-- either a list of tasks or even a log of exactly the last task started and when it was done.<p>Itemizing individual steps allows users to reason about their performance characteristics, if well explained.  These might cause jerkiness in the overall bar.<p>If I had a progress bar stuck at 27%, it would be valuable to click a little &quot;view more&quot; link and see &quot;11:34 AM: begin downloading file 27&#x2F;100: foo.tgz (62.3Gb)&quot;  I&#x27;d be more willing to expect to wait on it advancing than if it was a 30k file.')
