Item(by='goblin89', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611771844, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927252, text='Using telephoto lenses and angles that preclude the audience from understanding distances between people in a crowd without knowing the specifications of photography equipment is absolutely fine for artistic effect, but in documentary shots IMO it treads the line next to bad (regardless of the times) journalism, especially if another composition that allows the viewer to approximate crowd density objectively is possible.')
