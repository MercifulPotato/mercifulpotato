Item(by='ptero', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609990521, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25666179, text='There are different models claiming free stuff.<p>One is a google-style lock into an ecosystem of free apps that a company can monetize at any time. Stay away if possible: the users will be milked sooner or later.<p>The other is openstreetmap-style set of free data that anyone can download anytime, plus some apps (maybe free, maybe not) using it for some function. I see no problem with it as the lock-in is highly unlikely because the main feature (say, map data) is always available. My 2c.')
