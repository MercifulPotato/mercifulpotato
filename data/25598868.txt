Item(by='darawk', descendants=None, kids=[25599038, 25599472], score=None, time=1609459628, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25598031, text='This isn&#x27;t a very good criticism. Lots of new things recapitulate the structures they are replacing in many ways, while improving on them in just one or a few areas. The fact that ground is being retread is not itself a criticism or a sign of a lack of innovation.<p>Yes, Bitcoin requires many of the same structures as traditional finance. That does not make them equivalent. Traditional finance does not <i>allow</i> you sovereignty over your  money. Bitcoin has central custody, sure. But it&#x27;s opt in. That&#x27;s what makes it different. You may or may not care about this property, but its undeniable that it&#x27;s novel in the digital realm, and empirically many people do care about it.')
