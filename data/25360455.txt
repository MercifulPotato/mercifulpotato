Item(by='hn_throwaway_99', descendants=None, kids=[25360669, 25361689], score=None, time=1607531800, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25360310, text='&gt; Less Cal in than out is the way to go.<p>That mantra always drives me nuts. Not because it&#x27;s false (on the contrary, by the laws of thermodynamics it&#x27;s trivially true), but because it&#x27;s not helpful for people trying to lose weight as it ignores the dynamic homeostasis systems of the human body. I.e. as reported by this article, if you try to increase your &quot;out&quot; energy expenditure, your body releases hormones specifically attempting to increase your &quot;in&quot; by making you hungrier. Similarly, if you try to reduce your &quot;in&quot;, your body will lower its metabolism to try to reduce your &quot;out&quot;.')
