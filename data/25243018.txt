Item(by='emcq', descendants=None, kids=[25246836], score=None, time=1606625909, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25240692, text='A better rule of thumb is miles because hours don&#x27;t reflect the amount of energy put into your drive train.<p>Typical recommendations are every 500-750miles for a mountain bike. For the average mountain biker I think that would be more like every 125 hours at 6 mph. Mountain bike chains see much more abuse than road, and you can go further too.<p>You can be more scientific by using a chain ware tool to measure how much stretch there is.')
