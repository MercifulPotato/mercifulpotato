Item(by='nikau', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603676082, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24891862, text='Best way I found to reduce pump noise is to insulate it.<p>In my case I have a DDC style pump, and its very quiet after I got a car wash sponge and cut a square hole in it and put the pump inside.  It looks ghetto but its inside a case so who cares.<p>At first I was worried about heat, but its lasted 10 years so far.')
