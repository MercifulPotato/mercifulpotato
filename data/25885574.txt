Item(by='coldcog', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611432384, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25885019, text='Although developments have slowed down due to the pandemic and now the collapse of the dutch cabinet, the Privacy by Design Foundation and SIDN, who jointly develop and run IRMA, have been in talks and developing pilots with the Ministry of the Interior of the Netherlands to become an accepted party (&quot;toegelaten partij&quot;) under the WDO. That would mean that when the WDO comes into effect, the ministry issues basic personal data to IRMA apps, and IRMA becomes one of the ways in which citizens can authenticate to services alongside DigiD.')
