Item(by='KMag', descendants=None, kids=[25356425], score=None, time=1607497624, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25355757, text='&gt; The &quot;ellipse&quot; function should only take 2 arguments - a point and a size (or 2 points if that&#x27;s your thing).<p>How do you handle ellipses with different eccentricities?<p>There are several equivalent representations of ellipses, one of which is the two foci and the sum of the distances from the edge to the two foci... so two points and a distance (or you could use two points and the major axis, etc., etc.)  A pair of points don&#x27;t uniquely define one ellipse.  Likewise, a center and a &quot;size&quot; doesn&#x27;t uniquely define an ellipse.')
