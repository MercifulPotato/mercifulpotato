Item(by='Cerium', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604196986, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24956696, text='One way I have seen the integration of black box models in situations where incorrect decisions are unacceptable (in this case a medical environment) is to implement a good enough solution to the problem using conventional programming techniques, and then give the black box model free reign to provide more accurate answers bounded by the conventional system. In the case I saw, the black box model was able to improve the accuracy by about 10x over the existing system for 90% of the use cases. The other 10% fall back to the existing system and do no worse.')
