Item(by='implicit', descendants=None, kids=[25570012, 25568203, 25566817], score=None, time=1609208488, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564619, text='I did a little bit of GBA homebrew development back in its heyday.  It is a really fun little bit of hardware to hack on.<p>It&#x27;s powerful enough that you can write your whole game in C++, but not so powerful that you should rely on the system allocator for anything.<p>It&#x27;s in a lovely sweet spot for a particular dialect of zero-overhead C++ that&#x27;s really fun to write.<p>As a simple example, I had a little class library of data types that were crafted to exactly map to the hardware registers of the GBA.  The whole thing inlined away to nothing.')
