Item(by='gamblor956', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603394452, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24861024, text='And Hawaii. See the general excise tax. Fun fact: it&#x27;s a recursive tax on business <i>revenue</i> (not profit), so if you gross up prices to pass along the tax to customers on a transactional basis, you also need to pay GET on the gross up, ad infinitum. For example, on Oahu this results in a final grossup of approximately 4.7120% (starting from a base GET of 4.5%).')
