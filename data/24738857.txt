Item(by='u801e', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602336489, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24732168, text='&gt; Up until around 1990 or so 55MPH was the maximum speed limit<p>The NMSL (National Maximum Speed Limit) was enacted in 1973, modified in 1987 to allow 65 mph speed limits on rural interstates, and repealed in 1995.<p>Many states in the southeast, midwest and west raised their posted speed limits very soon after the NMSL was repealed.  States in the northeast took a bit longer to update their laws to remove their state maximum speed limit.')
