Item(by='asenna', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605819114, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25153998, text='On Firefox you can open up the console (right click -&gt; Inspect Element), head to the &quot;Network&quot; tab and check the very bottom of that panel. It should give you details on how much time it took and the size of your request. You can refresh your page and it refreshes the data at the bottom as well.<p>I believe it should be similar on Chrome and Safari as well, just locate the Network tab in the browser console.')
