Item(by='jen729w', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604781633, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25014499, text='&gt; Create a dedicated address everytime you subscribe to a service<p>I tried this and don’t recommend it.<p>Yeah, you get the advantage of knowing who leaked your address. But I don’t care. If the ‘spam and lists’ address gets too spammy, I’ll just create another.<p>The disadvantage is that you have to configure a mail client to send from this address if you ever want to do that, and I found myself doing it far more often than I imagined. Need to write back to a store regarding a transaction? Frequently enough. To my health insurance company for some reason? Of course.<p>No, the idea is to <i>remove as much friction as possible</i>. This adds friction. Keep it simple.')
