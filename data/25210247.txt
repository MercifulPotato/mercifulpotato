Item(by='codingdave', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606318339, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25210095, text='I&#x27;ve always tried to adjust the &quot;At {Company X}, we...&quot; statements because it distracts from the idea being presented and instead brings focus to which company was mentioned. By definition, that was a different company with different needs. The idea may still be a great one, but it needs to be evaluated in the current context.<p>I encourage my teams to bring in those insights, but we tend to settle on the phrase, &quot;In a prior life, we...&quot;, as that phrasing lets us know that it has worked for other companies, but keeps the focus on the idea itself.')
