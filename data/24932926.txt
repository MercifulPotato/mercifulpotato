Item(by='ozten', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603992772, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24916016, text='Take opportunities one at a time and study what they are asking for. What does this particular hiring manager need? Highlight your strengths in only the areas they are focused on.<p>I had a very challenging time getting a job offer the last time I did the interview gauntlet run (had 17 years experience at the time), because I kept talking about how many different skills I could bring to the table. I should have focused on listening to what they want.<p>Once you are inside the company, you can employ your jack-of-all-trades skills.')
