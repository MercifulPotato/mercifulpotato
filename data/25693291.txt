Item(by='elliekelly', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610152046, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25692948, text='Section 230 specifically <i>does not</i> apply to federal criminal liability and the circuits are currently (sort of) split about whether a platform could be held responsible in this situation.<p>The 9th Circuit ruled Sec. 230 <i>did not</i> shield a platform from civil liability arising from their “failure to warn” a potential victim of a violent crime. Meanwhile, the 2nd Circuit found Sec. 230 <i>did</i> protect Facebook from civil liability for hosting terrorism-related content.<p>And both of those were civil cases. In the context of potential criminal liability Sec. 230 couldn’t even be raised.')
