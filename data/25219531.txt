Item(by='Kim_Bruning', descendants=None, kids=[25221005, 25220872], score=None, time=1606394776, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25218684, text='I wish people <i>were</i> treated more like machines.<p>Big expensive machines, which require training to operate, and have big manuals full of pages and pages of &quot;Danger: failure to follow correct operating procedures may lead to injury or death.&quot;<p>I would really love to have a custom manual showing correct min&#x2F;max&#x2F;optimal operating parameters for sleep, warmth, hugs, food, etc. Especially if people would have to keep to it.<p>And then instead of getting angry when someone doesn&#x27;t do what they want (or getting angry at themselves for failing) , people would have to accept that they violated the commonly accepted operating procedures -thereby exceeding the tolerances of the system- and should do better next time.<p>Wouldn&#x27;t that be a huge improvement over how things work now?')
