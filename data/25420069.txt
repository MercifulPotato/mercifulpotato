Item(by='ludocode', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607966094, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25418586, text='alloca() is not a standard C primitive. It&#x27;s only an extension, and its purpose is to dynamically allocate memory that is automatically freed. Using a dynamic amount of stack space <i>is</i> dynamic allocation, it&#x27;s just not on the heap.<p>This still makes it really easy to overflow your stack, especially if for example your loop count above can come from user input. This is dangerous for all the same reasons that variable-length arrays are dangerous.')
