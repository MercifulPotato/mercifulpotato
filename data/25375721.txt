Item(by='CuriouslyC', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607619639, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25374140, text='If you are being demoralized by the initial hump of building things that you&#x27;re enthusiastic about, you just need to change your mindset and the way you approach building them.<p>Don&#x27;t be passionate about building something for other people, be passionate about something for yourself.  Let your projects be extensions of that passion, with you as the intended customer.  Not everything needs a fancy interface or a dozen use cases out of the box.  Don&#x27;t worry about scalability.  Just try to find the joy in building things to help pursue your passion.')
