Latest is determined by a clock.  Let&#x27;s say you wrote to 2 out of 3 nodes, and read from 2 out of 3 nodes, one of those 2 you read from is going to have the latest data.  This is how consistency works in Cassandra e.g.<p>There are other models, for example by using a consensus protocol.')
