Item(by='chousuke', descendants=None, kids=[25447785, 25447774], score=None, time=1608148218, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446915, text='It&#x27;s not really about running <i>servers</i> for 10 years. It&#x27;s about having a platform to build a product on that you can support for 10 years. RHEL software gets old over time, but it&#x27;s still maintained and compatible with what you started on.<p>Consider an appliance that will be shipped to a literal cave for some mining operation. Do you want to build that on something that you would have to keep refreshing every year, so that every appliance you ship ends up running on a different foundation?')
