Item(by='mastazi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610403447, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25736237, text='I would like to offer a different angle<p>&gt; Firefox and safari<p>For a long time Firefox called home... not to Mozilla but to Google upon installation (hence, before you could install any privacy extensions that would have prevented Google tracking). This was only rectified after a number of tweets about this problem became viral. The fact that Mozilla’s survival depends on its relationship with Google also created some other issues over time so after being a user for many years, I decided that Firefox was no longer a viable option for me.<p>Safari is a non starter since I can’t even use it outside of Apple’s walled garden (no Linux or Windows version is available).<p>All of the above in my personal opinion outweighs the issues that Brave had, which I don’t negate but in my eyes are minor.<p>So for now I stay with Brave.<p>Edit: I guess what I’m trying to say is that there is no perfect solution out there and you have to evaluate pros and cons based on your personal circumstances or goals.')
