Item(by='dfabulich', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609635183, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25615864, text='For IF newbies, your best bet is probably to just pick great IF games and use hints as needed. (Prefer gradual hints over step-by-step walkthroughs.)<p>The IF community maintains IFDB, and a list of the top 100 games. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ifdb.tads.org&#x2F;viewlist?id=k7rrytlz3wihmx2o" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;ifdb.tads.org&#x2F;viewlist?id=k7rrytlz3wihmx2o</a> (It can take a full minute to load; be patient.)<p>All of the games on the list are delightful, but a newbie can&#x27;t really be expected to finish any of them without hints. Luckily, almost all of them offer gradual hints, either linked from IFDB itself or in-game with a HINT command.')
