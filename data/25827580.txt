Item(by='dfgdghdf', descendants=None, kids=[25827711, 25827787, 25828084, 25828104], score=None, time=1611009646, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25822718, text='Here&#x27;s how I might open a debate with right-leaning people about freedom of speech.<p>If you sell a product, but make false claims about that product, then you are committing fraud. You have harmed someone (financially) with your speech, and contract laws rightly overrule freedom of speech here. So, if you believe in contract law, which is essential for free markets, then you must believe in limitations on speech... the question then is simply where to draw the line.')
