Item(by='stevesearer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602275946, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24733800, text='We initially used Google Ads, but phased them out 6-7 years ago because the ads were lower quality than we wanted and missing a big chunk of the ad sale due to Google&#x27;s fees.<p>If I remember correctly, we used Google and self-hosted for a while, but eventually it made financial sense to replace the Google Ad slots with self-hosted so we were able to capture the full ad sale.<p>Several years on, I am really happy with the decision and it is great to not be subject to the whims of an ad marketplace we have no control over.')
