I&#x27;m glad it existed, and glad I got to implement a few projects using it.<p>But, I&#x27;m not surprised. K8s is easier to work with if you&#x27;re a startup and really working from a greenfield perspective, and most enterprises still have a lot &quot;Pet&quot; style servers around. It seems like they never found the great fit they would need to survive in the face of stiff competition and a quickly evolving marketplace.<p>I wish them the best of luck with this shift and anything else they might pursue in the future. Focusing where they did at that point in time was a gamble, and the type of gamble that pushes innovation in tech forward.')
