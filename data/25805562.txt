Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=[25807187], score=None, time=1610831621, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25804100, text='Ordinary handprints are a natural idea. Stenciled handprints are an absolutely unnatural idea. That they appear in practically every cave-painting site indicates cultural transmission spanning tens of thousands of years and miles.<p>Similarly, &quot;seven sisters&quot; interpretations of the Pleiades are found from Africa to Australia and everywhere in between. The last time the Pleiades were recognizably seven stars was 40,000 years ago. &quot;Seven sisters&quot; legends everywhere include an accounting of the missing sister.')
