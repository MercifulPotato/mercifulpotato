Item(by='dissidents', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607535875, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25360752, text='Let me just add to this. Discovering new information (or new &quot;axioms&quot;) will not change the truth of your previous conclusions if you did everything correctly, but you may find that the information you believed before was incorrect. In general, I believe it will be better to use a probabilistic model for most real-world cases since it is very difficult to find &quot;axioms&quot; for almost anything.')
