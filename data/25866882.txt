Item(by='lifeisstillgood', descendants=0, kids=None, score=1, time=1611276946, title='Ask HN: Has WFH meant you have spent all your social capital yet?', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='First off, I recognise what a privileged position I have in the Covid-world - a nice middle class job that can be done sitting down in front of  a PC at home.  Compared to pretty much everyone else on the planet I have it just fine.<p>I just have noticed however, that I, and my coworkers are ... getting on less well.  Maybe it&#x27;s me, or maybe it&#x27;s that the chats over cups of coffee, the walls in the fresh air chatting about projects or gossip or nothing much actually play a really strong role in maintaining team cohesion and trust.<p>It&#x27;s hard to pinpoint a problem   but things just are getting <i>missed</i>.<p>Maybe it was always this way.  or maybe we have spent the good capital accrued over those cups of coffee and are now starting to creep into overdraft.<p>Anyone else?')
