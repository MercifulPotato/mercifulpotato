Item(by='noisy_boy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611758205, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25924566, text='I think it is relative. Compared to developing a car, a wordpress website can be completed in a  relatively short time; an ERP system on the other hand will take substantially longer. It depends on what is being asked to be delivered when at what cost.<p>Putting that aside, there is a time creep factor because people don&#x27;t place limits on the demands - ask and the developer can do it. So it is developed and now you need to ensure that the custom piece works reliably and doesn&#x27;t break and so on. Compared to that, a house needs to be carefully designed upfront and there are limits to what modifications you can reasonably ask for down the line - you don&#x27;t ask the builders to drag and drop the kitchen to the other side because you don&#x27;t like how it looks on mobile.')
