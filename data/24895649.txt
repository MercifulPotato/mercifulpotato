Item(by='TonyTrapp', descendants=None, kids=[24895805, 24895712], score=None, time=1603717108, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24895585, text='It doesn&#x27;t help your website if your own server has issues while the CDN has the best exports ensuring its 99.999% uptime. The probability of a failure is still a multiplicative factor of both uptimes, so it&#x27;s strictly worse. Your user won&#x27;t care if the CDN serving JS files is up while the website itself is unreachable. The only uptime that is relevant is that of your own server.')
