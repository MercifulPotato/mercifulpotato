Item(by='gfodor', descendants=None, kids=[25853009], score=None, time=1611178642, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25849491, text='Paradoxes are paradoxes precisely because they fail to disprove two contradictory claims.<p>For the Fermi paradox, the paradox is that the immensity of the universe leading to life seems obvious, yet the complete absence of that life around us seems to show it&#x27;s not there. So it disproves nothing, but actually highlights the difficulty of disproving either claim given the other.')
