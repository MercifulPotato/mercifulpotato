Item(by='stouset', descendants=None, kids=[25717020, 25720281, 25716454, 25718285], score=None, time=1610295830, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25716146, text='Also, without washing, you end up with rough spots of non-sticky partially-polymerized bits. Wash it with soap and a scrub brush. Nothing short of steel wool is going to affect the patina.<p>For proof of this, check out your aluminum baking sheets. If they’ve been used, they’re almost certainly covered in a shiny black substance that’s a complete pain in the ass to remove, even with abrasives. That’s essentially what’s on your cast iron.')
