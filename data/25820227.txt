If you assume you can, you may not succeed; you may be blocked by some external cause, but not by something you could have controlled.<p>In the case of helping others, you may play a role in clearing an obstacle that, not withstanding their own effort and belief, would have blocked them.<p>In both cases you are assuming you have power. Power to help yourself and power to reduce inequality by empowering others.<p>This isn’t a contradiction.')
