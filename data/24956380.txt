Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604190383, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24954987, text='&gt; Incidentally, if you pick up a US dollar bill, you&#x27;ll see the inscription: &quot;this note is legal tender, for all debts public and private.&quot; Therefore, that note derives its value from the implicit debt underlying it.<p>There&#x27;s no such thing. That it can be used to pay debts doesn&#x27;t mean there are debts underlying it, implicit or explicit. It means that if you incur a debt, and you have one, you can use it to resolve the debt.')
