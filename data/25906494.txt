Item(by='jandrewrogers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611597999, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25900312, text='Not necessarily. If you have indexing structures for data types that do not have a total order, only a partial order, you can store and do an indexed range search on data types that do have a total order. The primary implication is that the output of the range search will not reflect the total order in the way it would for a traditional B+Tree.')
