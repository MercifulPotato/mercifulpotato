Item(by='dheera', descendants=None, kids=[25799545], score=None, time=1610764850, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25799368, text='A lot of times literal translations eventually become a part of the destination language as well.<p>Examples include &quot;long time no see&quot; which is now common in English but has roots in either Chinese or Native American languages.<p>Likewise, the phrasing &quot;chicken-egg problem&quot; originated in Western culture but is now often used literally in modern Chinese, e.g. &quot;雞和蛋的問題&quot;.')
