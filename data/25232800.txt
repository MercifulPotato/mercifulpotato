Item(by='beagle3', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606513171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25231835, text='The body learns associations. There are reproducible experiments with rats who develop a liking for chalk water after sugar is added to it; and it takes them a couple of weeks to stop liking it when sugar is out.<p>There are some experiments showing the same is true with humans, with a time constant of 3-6 weeks.<p>From memory, relevant papers are from Michel cabanac. Seth Robert’s “what makes food fattening” is a great overview of related results, whether or nit you agree with his take.')
