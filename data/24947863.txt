Item(by='nwallin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604100846, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24947764, text='I agree that Prop 22 is not a perfect solution. But I think Prop 22 is a better solution than AB5. If independent contractor work is insufficiently protected, then independent contractors should be given better protections.<p>Gig workers need the flexibility to open up the Lyft, Uber, Doordash, and Instacart apps all at the same time. They need the flexibility to refuse a fare for any reason. They need the flexibility to work whatever hours they want. I don&#x27;t see how to build that flexibility into the rigid structure enforced by the employer-employee relationship.<p>Trying to 9-to-5-ify gig workers doesn&#x27;t make any sense to me.')
