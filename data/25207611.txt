Item(by='thinkingemote', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606298738, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25206505, text='Conservation and biodiversity is about humans manipulating the environment away from a natural equilibrium to something better.<p>A desert is at equilibrium, if we plant stuff and water it, it&#x27;s more diverse and ecologically better.<p>Often conservation requires active maintenance to keep the benefits. Usually this maintenance occured as a side effect of historical human activity (resource extraction, grazing, hunting, fire).<p>As humans do less interaction with the environment as they progress in cities they make nature less bio diverse paradoxically.')
