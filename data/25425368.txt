As in, something that might look nice but obviously smells of shit. Less crudely, you might describe milk that way: spoiled milk looks like good milk but it definitely does not smell like good milk. It doesn&#x27;t really have anything to do with a person in particular, unless historic usage was different from modern usage.<p>I don&#x27;t know about other languages, but I don&#x27;t think there&#x27;s any connection in English.')
