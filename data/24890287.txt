Item(by='Natsu', descendants=None, kids=[24892778, 24891526], score=None, time=1603663023, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24888829, text='Currently a handful of billionaire oligarchs own a majority of the sites used for public discourse by ordinary people.  I don&#x27;t see how that can remain the case and democracy can survive when you need only a handful of sites to censor some topic.<p>It seems to me that the online monopolies, natural or otherwise, should have to give way to something akin to common carrier status given their outsized importance on public discourse.<p>This isn&#x27;t exactly new.  Back in the days of the telegraph, those were abused and tapped and eventually the phone company became a common carrier.')
