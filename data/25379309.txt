And, serious Constitutional issues have been raised about the application of 2257 to “secondary producers” like individual users.<p>And, even under 2257, covered “secondary producers” aren’t required to provide <i>their</i> ID, they are required to have specific information about the maintenance of records related to the <i>perfomers</i> age affixed to the content.')
