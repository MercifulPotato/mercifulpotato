They were by any measure charismatic.  They leveraged that to further their visions to an end.<p>But, not all charismatic people use their charisma for the bad.  Some use it to form friendships and to climb social ladders, some to be productive in their role (salespeople), etc.<p>For some people it comes naturally.  For some it’s forced (practiced).
