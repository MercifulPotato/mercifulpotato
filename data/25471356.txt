Item(by='silicon2401', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608322449, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25470253, text='This is what I would do. Treat it like an engineering problem: break it down into smaller, simpler parts, and start with small steps.<p>You value practicality as well as personal interest&#x2F;fulfillment. Either pick one and start there or pick both and consider the options there. If you want to be practical, learn the most marketable skill that you&#x27;re at least somewhat interested in: Java, AWS, Docker, Javascript are all incredibly marketable. If you want to go for interest, just pick the thing you&#x27;re most interested in. You could also try and juggle two deep dives, at the cost of more time and less depth, but maybe that works for you.<p>Don&#x27;t worry about becoming a senior dev in a semester. Pick something and focus on it. That could be an AWS certification, building something in Java, etc. Once you get through that, then decide your next step.')
