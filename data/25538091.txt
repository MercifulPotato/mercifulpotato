Item(by='carlmr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608920838, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25532748, text='I often think that stopping people from writing code might be the best way to avoid bad code.<p>Stricter languages like Rust, ADA, or even F# (quite strongly typed), will not let you compile until you reach a certain level of explicitness&#x2F;correctness. Thereby teaching you some basic things until you can compile.<p>Languages like Perl and Python allow you to do a lot of things, which ends up unmaintainable because it&#x27;s too easy. The easy version just breaks during runtime then.')
