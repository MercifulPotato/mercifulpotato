Item(by='sbergot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607421368, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25343301, text='In my world a company probes the market continuously to see what the market wants. The business analysts are getting a stream of requirements and then design new features. Then devs implement those features. Development stops when market growth slows down or when the investors cash in.<p>The only way to predict what all requirement will be in a product lifetime would be to invent a time machine. This is not what I call &quot;a real solution&quot;.<p>The only real solution is to communicate a lot with the business analyst and make informed decisions on flexibility vs simplicity.')
