Item(by='dash2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604864822, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25028243, text='I&#x27;ll give you my favourite quote:<p><i>Ich höre Geräusche, die Andere nicht hören, und sie stören mir die Musik der Sphären - die Andere auch nicht hören.</i><p><i>I hear noises which others don&#x27;t hear, and they distract me from the music of the spheres - which others also don&#x27;t hear.</i><p>That is Kraus&#x27; alter ego speaking, in the context of the Great War and jingoism in Austria-Hungary, which the play satirizes.<p>If you want an intro, Clive James has an essay in <i>Cultural Amnesia</i>, which is a great book in its own right.')
