Item(by='sneak', descendants=None, kids=[25339117, 25339114], score=None, time=1607377930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25337426, text='The concept of data ownership is questionable. When you do business with me, and I observe you, are not my own observations <i>my</i> data?<p>I am all for privacy, but I don&#x27;t think that we get to dictate what other people are allowed to do with &quot;our&quot; data, legitimately obtained through the course of normal business. Observations are our own data, including observations about others.<p>You don&#x27;t own your reputation, for instance.')
