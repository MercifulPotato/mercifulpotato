Item(by='svara', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606142025, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25186138, text='Whether viruses are alive or not is purely a matter of definition.<p>Alive in this context means that it can infect host cells and replicate.<p>Contrary to what the sibling posts say, that doesn&#x27;t just require intact viral RNA, which by itself is not infectious, but an intact viral assembly, i.e in this case a lipid hull with various accessory proteins, such as the nucleocapsid that packs the RNA and the spike protein that binds to host cells.')
