Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602636596, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24771706, text='When Usenet originated, you needed to have an Internet-connected computer. In the late 80s, this was largely limited to university campuses, which meant that the process of educating new members on the social norms already in place largely only needed to happen for a few weeks in September. In September 1993, AOL offered Usenet access to any AOL subscriber. This gave rise to the term Eternal September.')
