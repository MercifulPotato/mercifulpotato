Item(by='ldiracdelta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604767715, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25015797, text='My REST endpoints allow a `fields` parameter that specifies the requested fields of the endpoint.  Even if you do have a huge object, you can pare it down. To get only `id` and `first_name` on a `User` endpoint, you do `...&#x2F;rest&#x2F;User&#x2F;fields={&quot;id&quot;=true,&quot;first_name&quot;=true}` .  Non-standard, but effective.  The backend doesn&#x27;t retrieve or send the un-needed fields.')
