Item(by='freeone3000', descendants=None, kids=[25160842, 25160759, 25160907], score=None, time=1605882542, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25160650, text='Garbage collected languages (Java, JS) have significant overhead, and unpredictable latency spikes.<p>Scope-based memory management (C++) has copying overhead and destruction overhead, and a whole lot of complexity to avoid those.<p>Ownership-based memory management removes most of the overhead associated with copying and destruction in a less-complex fashion.')
