Item(by='pdonis', descendants=None, kids=[24822552], score=None, time=1603069188, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821921, text='<i>&gt; if you are inside of the sphere, at least some of the mass will be pulling you away from the point at its center</i><p>If the mass is spherically symmetric, this will not be the case; all of the Newtonian forces from the masses further away from the center than you are will cancel out. This is called the &quot;shell theorem&quot;, and it turns out to hold even in General Relativity.')
