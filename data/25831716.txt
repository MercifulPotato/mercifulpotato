You have to flatten the dependency graph between the facts in a way that helps understanding, select what to include or omit, find metaphors sometimes, etc.\nSome of these require finding new insights about the facts.<p>You cannot just &quot;list&quot; facts if you want to help the reader to understand. You also don&#x27;t need dramatic stories, though memorable examples help.')
