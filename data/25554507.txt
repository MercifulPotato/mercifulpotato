Item(by='majewsky', descendants=None, kids=[25554803], score=None, time=1609105302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25554226, text='I think this is what Walter gets at with &quot;only becomes apparent after years&quot;. Right now, you are aware that a[i] is not exactly (only practically) identical to *(a + i). The problem only arises when, years later, someone who reads the code does not realize that. Writing getoffset(a, i) instead of a[i] is uglier, but it will correctly prompt the reader to check out the definition (or documentation) for getoffset().')
