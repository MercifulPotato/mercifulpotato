Item(by='jandrese', descendants=None, kids=[25866150, 25865699], score=None, time=1611265956, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25864392, text='Electric motors don&#x27;t need a transmission.  Adding a stick shift is just adding complexity for no benefit.  It&#x27;s more stuff to maintain and more parts to break and is going to make the car slower due to parasitic losses and inefficiencies.<p>If you&#x27;re shooting for 200kph+ speeds there might be a time where a simple high&#x2F;low transmission may be helpful, but that&#x27;s supercar territory.<p>Interestingly, Formula E cars have a 5 speed gearbox, but mostly because the rules says they have to.')
