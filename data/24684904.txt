This makes sense, both because regulatory capture is a real problem, and because smaller businesses have far less legal and economic leverage.<p>Modulo EBITDA per employee.<p>Of course, those regulations should step in gradually -- sharp cliffs are pretty much always antipatterns when it comes to regulation, law, and government services.<p>Yes, I know in France that companies try and get around this by splitting into lots of smaller firms, but that&#x27;s an issue for the regulators and the specific legal framework.<p>Nobody ever said these things are easy.')
