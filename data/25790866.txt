Item(by='chme', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610718395, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25789466, text='&gt; As a general rule, I consider all &quot;don&#x27;t be lazy&quot; arguments wrong.<p>I don&#x27;t agree nor disagree, but I just wanted to point out the hypocrisy of this argument:<p>If someone is too lazy to learn how something works, would it not be hypocritical for them to complain about the laziness of other people, that fail to make it easier for them?<p>And if this lazy person writes their own stuff, would they be diligent enough to make it easier for others?<p>It would be something different if that person was actually not lazy and, after understanding something, could point out or help implement ways to make the introduction for the next person easier.')
