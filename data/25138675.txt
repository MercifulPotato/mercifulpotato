Item(by='xtracto', descendants=None, kids=[25140003, 25139490, 25142445, 25140990, 25145223, 25142740], score=None, time=1605716876, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137443, text='&gt; Use the “hamburger method”<p>If you are a manager or someone in a position of &quot;power&quot; no, please don&#x27;t.  This has to be one of the most overrated and misused feedback methods.<p>The problem with giving sandwich&#x2F;hamburguer whatever feedback, is that you are undervaluing positive feedback. After you do this a lot of times, every time you want to give positive feedback to the person, she will be expecting the negative part, she will grow weary of your feedback and wont value the positive feedback.<p>Better learn how to give effective feedback ( <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.manager-tools.com&#x2F;2005&#x2F;07&#x2F;giving-effective-feedback" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.manager-tools.com&#x2F;2005&#x2F;07&#x2F;giving-effective-feedb...</a> highly recommended) whether it is positive or negative. And teach your collaborators to receive the feedback as what it is: just feedback.')
