Item(by='asyncawaaait', descendants=None, kids=[25050515], score=None, time=1605036133, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25049655, text='Awaiting a storage operation (ie. look up user) in C# will allow other calls to proceed until the I&#x2F;O is available to read.<p>Synchronous code locks you into the model of handling one request per thread.<p>Given the minor overhead (both cognitive and runtime) of a sync&#x2F;await, I can’t see why you wouldn’t want to do it here.')
