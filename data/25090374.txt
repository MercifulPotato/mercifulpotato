Item(by='rootusrootus', descendants=None, kids=[25090426], score=None, time=1605329267, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25087634, text='I think this is a good point to keep in mind.  Some things sound good but can hurt people&#x27;s perception of you if used too much, or incorrectly.  Apologize too quickly, and without really making sure it was actually a mistake worth apologizing for, and people start to get used to the idea of you being constantly wrong.<p>I&#x27;ve run into something that is kinda-sorta-similar with regards to praising other people.  I really took it to heart a long time ago that I should focus on building other people up and giving credit, always keeping positive, etc.  That&#x27;s not terrible advice, but I&#x27;ve found that if you give everyone else credit too often it shifts people&#x27;s perception of you.  So be nice, give credit where it&#x27;s due, but don&#x27;t turn away praise, and accept with good grace credit that is given to you.  You want people to remember that you are skilled.')
