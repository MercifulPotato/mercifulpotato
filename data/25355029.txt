Item(by='quadrangle', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607485044, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25353520, text='I agree with your sentiment overall (if you don&#x27;t want obstacles to merging back downstream code, maybe consider requiring that it keep compatible licensing, i.e. use the GPL, use copyleft).<p>But this sort of contribute-back-upstream is <i></i>not<i></i> what the GPL was written for and not how it behaves. The GPL is specifically a pass-it-forward <i>downstream</i> focused license. It is about requiring others to pass on the freedoms. The idea that it has any send-back-upstream requirements is a common misunderstanding that shouldn&#x27;t be encouraged.')
