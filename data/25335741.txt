Item(by='runarberg', descendants=None, kids=[25336149], score=None, time=1607367645, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25335037, text='&gt; The purpose of copyright is to encourage authors to create more works.<p>Is this true? I know in many cases copyright does the complete opposite as it hinders artists from creating derivative work. Then there is a whole genre of art—folk art; which is arguably <i>the most popular</i> genre—where artists continue to do art where the copyright is hardly ever perused, enforced, or if present almost always ignored.<p>You could just as easily make the case that in the art genre where copyright is the most loose, the amount of art created is the greatest.')
