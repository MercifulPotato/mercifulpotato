Item(by='gpderetta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601561379, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24648830, text='RAII is great for transactions. In a proper robust design, commits should always be explicit and destructors only rollback uncommitted transactions. If your rollback can fail, well, then you have bigger issues.<p>There is now enough introspection in the language to actually implement implicit commit in a non exceptional path, but I think it would be a mistake.')
