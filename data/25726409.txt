Item(by='red_admiral', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610355419, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25717390, text='There is a second kind of leverage that startups in particular can get out of their developers: expecting them to live for the job.<p>The choice between a company where (a) you work on assigned tickets, but standard business hours and weekends off and X days of guaranteed holiday per year versus (b) a high autonomy, high pay but if the company needs you to pull a 90-hour week then you do it without asking - it&#x27;s a tradeoff. Whether you get to write your own JIRA tickets is just one of many dimensions here.<p>One of those two options is not compatible, for example, with having responsibilities outside of work such as childcare.')
