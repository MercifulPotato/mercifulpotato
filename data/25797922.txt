Item(by='layoutIfNeeded', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610752720, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25797308, text='A digital pin will most likely output a signal using a zero-order hold, which is the simplest type of reconstruction filter.<p>A zero-order hold is just <i>one</i> possible way of turning a digital signal into an analog one which you can then measure with your oscilloscope. But the output of the zero-order hold is not <i>the</i> digital signal, because the digital signal is only defined at discrete sample points.')
