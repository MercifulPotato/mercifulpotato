Item(by='Bud', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603521099, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24874759, text='It&#x27;s not &quot;cheating&quot;, but it&#x27;s also not absolute pitch, by definition.<p>Absolute pitch (or perfect pitch) is the ability to identify a note <i>without</i> having any memory aid or reference tone to compare it to.<p>So if you have a constantly-present reference tone, due to tinnitus, and you figure everything out based on the interval from that tone, that&#x27;s simple relative pitch and the ability to figure out intervals. Not perfect pitch.')
