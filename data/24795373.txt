Item(by='dheera', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602803025, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24795206, text='Self-driving cars is not just about not <i>wanting</i> to drive.<p>It&#x27;s also about enabling Uber-style intelligent carpooling so that you can save energy while still maintaining similar times to driving, especially in places with poor or non-existent public transportation.<p>It&#x27;s also about reducing the number of cars in the world. You probably use your car for less than 5% of your life.  Most of the time it just sits there taking up space. Why do you need to own it? Imagine a world where you didn&#x27;t need to own, maintain, or park. We can reclaim parking lots and plant trees, build housing, or other things. We can also reclaim a lot of our time spent in traffic jams or looking for parking and do something more useful with it.<p>It&#x27;s also about enabling people who are unfit to drive to have a better quality of life. What if you&#x27;ve had a little to drink? What if you&#x27;re down with a fever and a headache and can&#x27;t concentrate on the road? What if you&#x27;re sleepy? What if you are prone to seizures? What if you&#x27;re blind, deaf, have poor motor skills? What if you&#x27;re a visitor to an area without a public transportation system and not confident renting a car and driving?')
