Item(by='athms', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603957234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24926717, text='I suspect the problem is the counter notice. You must agree to be held to US law and the maintainer resides in Germany.<p>According to the DMCA, the entity that files a counter notice consents to jurisdiction of the US district court of their residence. If not a resident of the US, jurisdiction is the district where the service provider is located. Once a counter notice is filed, one cannot argue the law doesn&#x27;t apply because of lack of jurisdiction.')
