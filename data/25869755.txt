Item(by='taneq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611305723, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25867231, text='Having maximum torque from 0RPM removes the need for a <i>clutch</i>, not a transmission. You still need to keep the motor within its effective RPM range, which is certainly easier for electric motors than ICE engines but is still a consideration. Many single-speed electric cars have much lower top speeds than their power output would otherwise allow.')
