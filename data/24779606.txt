Its a very loose multicause syndrome, not a disease.<p>&gt; Why isn&#x27;t every ninety year old automatically enrolled in an experimental program to reduce senescence?<p>Because consent, among other reasons.<p>&gt; We should be desperate and taking desperate measures to fight aging tooth and nail.<p>On a social level I think this is wrong for the same reason it is often wrong to desperately scrap at extending life on an individual level: the expected return in terms of life extension does not warrant the expected cost in terms of immediate quality of life.')
