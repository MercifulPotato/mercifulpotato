Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=[24740740], score=None, time=1602352185, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24740364, text='The Chinese Room is an argument that a Turing-like test is inadequate to prove understanding of a language because a combination of two things, neither of which have understanding, could conceivably pass it for every test scenario.<p>The fact that one of those two things is impossible (or, alternatively, is equivalent to a device encoding a full understanding of the language) negates the argument.')
