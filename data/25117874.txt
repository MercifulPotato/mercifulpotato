Item(by='modeless', descendants=None, kids=[25118868], score=None, time=1605565616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25117635, text='It&#x27;s more the other way around. The MIDI has extra information that obfuscates the original sheet music. Most commonly, timing variation which can be intentional and quite large, but also other kinds of variation and even mistakes.<p>Additionally, the system described in the article works on scanned paper sheet music, not electronic sheet music, so you also have to factor in OCR issues. All things considered it would be very difficult to manually write an algorithm to do this reliably.')
