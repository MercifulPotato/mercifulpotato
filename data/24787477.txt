He discusses it briefly towards the end of his (VERY strongly recommended!) talk &quot;Still Logged In: What AR and VR Can Learn from MMOs&quot;[1]. His essay &quot;How to build the scary future today&quot;[2] goes into more detail.<p>The overly simplistic TL;DR is: the people currently trying to track everything you do online would <i>really</i> love a future where all your <i>offline</i> physical stuff had a unique serial number and regularly updated room&#x2F;gps&#x2F;etc location data. An indoor map of logical features would be a useful component in that future.<p>I&#x27;m sure there are other, more beneficial uses as well. All technology is dual use.
