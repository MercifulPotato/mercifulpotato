Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=[25568553], score=None, time=1609233973, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25565308, text='The density of flash memory is competitive with magnetic tapes, but the retention time is too low, making flash memory completely unusable for archival storage, even if it would have been as cheap as magnetic tape.<p>In theory, write-once memory cards, using some kind of antifuses, could be designed to have a lifetime good enough for archival storage, but nobody has attempted to develop such a technology, because it is not clear if there would be a market for them.<p>Most people do not think far ahead in the future, so they do not care much about archival data storage, until it is too late and the information had already been lost.')
