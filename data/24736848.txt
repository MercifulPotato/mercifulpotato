Performance marketing (e.g. Facebook and Google ads)</i><p>&gt; <i>2. Virality (e.g. word-of-mouth, referrals, invites)</i><p>&gt; <i>3. Content (e.g. SEO, YouTube)</i><p>&gt; <i>There are two additional lanes (sales and partnerships) which we won&#x27;t cover in this post because they are rarely effective in consumer businesses.
