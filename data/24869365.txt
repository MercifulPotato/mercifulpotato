My only advice that has actually worked is network, network, network. I have a degree in Russian language, and now I&#x27;m a javascript dev. It only happened because 1) I can teach myself&#x2F;back to school for programming and 2) I met and made friends with someone who knew someone who was hiring a entry-level dev.<p>2 was really the hard part, can&#x27;t lie.
