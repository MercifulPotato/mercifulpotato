Item(by='reportgunner', descendants=None, kids=[24696606], score=None, time=1601974599, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24694375, text='The right way to open CSVs in Excel is:\nData -&gt; From Text -&gt; Browse to your csv -&gt; Delimited -&gt; Comma -&gt; Next -&gt; Select Text formatting for columns with leading zeroes -&gt; Finish<p>You can either send xlsx with format or csv without format. If this would be disabled then we&#x27;d have another group of people complaining that their dates from CSV are not parsed.')
