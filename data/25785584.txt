Item(by='hkarthik', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610672895, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25782039, text='When I was a VPE at a mid stage startup, I tended to ignore 99.9% of these pitches.<p>Except for one. It came through a trusted contact, and he specifically wanted to intro me to a founder who had built a data security solution that he truly believed in. Out of respect for him, I took the video chat with the founder, and included my head of security on the call.<p>The founder&#x27;s pitch was amazing, and I knew I wanted to use her product. She identified a problem that we definitely had, had built an elegant solution for it, and could demonstrate value very quickly in a 30 minute video call.<p>So if you want to get a VPE&#x27;s attention, build something great but leverage someone in their network to warm up the intro for you. It works wonders.<p>We purchased her product within a month and implemented it within 2 weeks. It&#x27;s now a core part of how we secured data internally.')
