Item(by='tsm', descendants=None, kids=[25194263], score=None, time=1606157729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25181256, text='&gt;  I write for about three hours in the morning—from about 9:30 till 12:30—and I do another hour’s work between 6 and 7 in the evening. […] I never correct anything and I never go back to what I have written, except to the foot of the last page to see where I have got to. If you once look back, you are lost.<p>Another example of creators using a schedule to force creativity (not waiting &quot;for the mood to strike&quot;) and <i>creating</i> without worrying about quality.<p>If you&#x27;ve been procrastinating writing that novel or playing that guitar or whatever, the best way to deal with it is to put half an hour on your calendar to just do it.')
