Item(by='klmadfejno', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607527881, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25358948, text='My gut intuition says you don&#x27;t need to check the entire space of possible lines, just the lines between any two dots (rotated by an trivially small degree  to separate the two), because a &quot;correct&quot; cut should always be defined by the bounds between which some dot on the border is included or excluded in a given cut.<p>You also don&#x27;t need to check each sequence of legal cuts. Most positions can be deduced to be unwinnable given n-remaining moves.')
