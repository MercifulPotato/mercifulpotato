Losing focus does <i>not</i> mean you should start exploring other options because you haven&#x27;t found the <i>best fit</i> yet; other projects that seem shiny now will inevitably go through their own difficult periods.<p>If you look for greener pastures whenever your organic focus starts to wane, you&#x27;ll never make it through the challenges that create real value.')
