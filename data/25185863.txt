I have plenty of wall switches (say - 20) that emit a signal AAAAAABBBBBB, I am only interested in the BBBBB part, and then I need to match this with an action.<p>Sometimes (usually) this is a toggle, sometimes a on, sometimes an off, sometimes something else.<p>This is easy to do in Python (the list if kept as a dict with actions, there are three lines, one for each action), but gets very verbose with YAML.')
