Item(by='souprock', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609412250, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25587232, text='What counts as zoonotic? What counts as engineered?<p>The lab was publishing research for many years. We know they grew bat viruses in HeLa cells that had been modified to have bat features. One would expect, as a simple matter of evolution, that the viruses would adapt to replicate without reliance on the bat features. It&#x27;s breeding.<p>Now, is that zoonotic or engineered? Reasonable people could argue either way. Does the term we use matter so much? William Shakespeare wrote that &quot;A rose by any other name would smell as sweet.&quot;<p>Either way, that is some seriously hazardous research with an obvious potential for permanent worldwide consequences. Somebody needed to say &quot;NO&quot;.')
