Item(by='jbrot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609366701, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25586731, text='Could you clarify why you think placing crypto rigs in space is a good idea? To run a crypto rig, you need to provide a lot of power and have a mechanism to keep the processors cool. Being in space seems to make both of these substantially more difficult (especially cooling the electronics). It seems highly implausible to me that crypto will be the “killer app” of space.')
