Item(by='gaogao', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609900530, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25653897, text='FAANG design interviewer gang represent.<p>In my personal opinion the biggest trap I see is other interviewers falling into a rut: they&#x27;ve asked their question hundreds of time and already know what they want, robbing it of that vital collaborative aspect.<p>Make sure to cycle what you ask, so that you can continue to be pleasantly surprised in interviews and remember that it&#x27;s a 2-way street.<p>Design interviews are so much more pleasant (and imo higher signal) than coding interviews. I remember the sobering moment I interviewed with another company and was asked a question I myself had asked before 60 times before, having believed it falsely to be unique and clever. It&#x27;s turtles all the way down.')
