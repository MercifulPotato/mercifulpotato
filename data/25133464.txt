Item(by='Akababa', descendants=None, kids=[25133940], score=None, time=1605676818, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25132080, text='If you look at it in terms of risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe ratio), a fixed transaction fee costs more Sharpe on a low-variance bet than it does on a high-variance bet. The variance of balanced contracts is higher than the variance of unbalanced ones (p(1-p) is concave with maximum at p=0.5), so after adjusting for risk the transaction fee is more significant for the unbalanced contract.')
