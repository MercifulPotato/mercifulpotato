Item(by='wtallis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609892239, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25652211, text='Another good way of looking at the problem is that you need to test <i>latency under load</i>, not latency on an otherwise-idle network. Ping tests on an idle network only tell you the best-case latency. If you try doing that a while couple of devices are running video streams (especially if it&#x27;s two-way from videoconferencing rather than Netflix), you get a <i>very</i> different picture of your network&#x27;s performance.')
