Those who see hope for fission look to get economies of scale with a modular approach, mass-producing simple, small, reactors, shipping them from the factory to the site on trucks, and attaching them to the grid.<p>I am not qualified to assess whether that hope is well-founded, but there are real differences in the approach.')
