And how does it compare to the billions spent on dedicated networking, radio gimmickry, and specialized hardware and software magic, plus the opportunity cost of pulling all that talent and capacity from other industries?<p>If every trade settled at, say, the rolling average price of the last 60 seconds-- or 60 minutes-- it would dramatically quiet the &quot;noise&quot; in the market.
