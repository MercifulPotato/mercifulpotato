Item(by='an_opabinia', descendants=None, kids=[25192086, 25191474], score=None, time=1606162593, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25190404, text='Many AMD enthusiast motherboards ship with Thunderbolt.<p>It&#x27;s a straightforward technology to implement and I believe it is now royalty free. The only implementations are Intel&#x27;s though and I&#x27;m sure they&#x27;re expensive.<p>Expansion is a complicated business model. Apple doesn&#x27;t even ship headphone ports. The AirPods are smash hit, not least because when you sell a $1,100 device a $100 accessory suddenly seems really well priced. PCs are similarly pretty high ticket items, that market supports a situation where spending $100 on custom internal PSU cabling is not that bad.<p>The Raspberry Pi is tough. Accessories cost more than 2x the device. It&#x27;s a model that doesn&#x27;t really make sense. You could say that people are buying these accessories, but really its greatest limiting factor is that it&#x27;s too cheap, it&#x27;s hard to psychologically deliver on the big ticket item + accessories shopping cart for it.')
