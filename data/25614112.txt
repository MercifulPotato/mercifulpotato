For us it has been a major way to communicate all the new features and fixes as they come available to users. It’s also a good way for the team to celebrate their accomplishments. I was surprised to learn it also had helped with investors and generally marketing.<p>Usually the person who was a major contributor to the highlighted feature of the week writes the changelog and then we look through our PRs to add anything that could be interesting.
