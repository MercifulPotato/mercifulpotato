Item(by='redvenom', descendants=None, kids=[25095761, 25094009], score=None, time=1605374513, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25093697, text='I switched away from Gmail about twelve years ago. It was not hard. I did get my own domain. What I did was I started forwarding my email there. I sat down, made a list of all services that I used Gmail with and changed my address. Every time I wrote an email I made sure that Reply-to: was set to my new address.<p>It actually was surprisingly quick and painless.')
