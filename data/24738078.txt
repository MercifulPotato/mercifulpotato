Item(by='rikroots', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602327381, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24736576, text='I&#x27;ve never built anything beyond Lego, Meccano and the occasional flatpacked piece of furniture. However, when it comes to &quot;granny pads&quot; the most important thing to take into account is what the specified granny needs, and would like, in her new pad. When my brother added a ground floor extension to the family home specifically for my mother, the two key requirements were for: an en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower and support rails by the toilet; and to remodel the kitchen with a lower level working surface (because she shrinks as she ages) and accessible (reachable) cupboards&#x2F;drawers.<p>tl;dr - consult the granny before you even think of laying a brick for her new pad!')
