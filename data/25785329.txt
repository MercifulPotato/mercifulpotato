Item(by='wyclif', descendants=None, kids=[25786279], score=None, time=1610671270, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25782798, text='When I came to the Philippines I was shocked by the large number of obese people here. I thought it would be like China because that was my only reference point in SE Asia. Relatively speaking, it&#x27;s not as bad as the States or the UK but it&#x27;s getting there. If you look back only a few generations, you&#x27;ll see what happened here: they were colonized by the US and adopted Western-style processed food and soft drinks. Also, car ownership is a new thing for most families. Before WW2 this was not the case, and thus there was far less obesity.')
