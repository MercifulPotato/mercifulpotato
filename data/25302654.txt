Item(by='jnwatson', descendants=None, kids=[25319512, 25305015], score=None, time=1607092747, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25302464, text='Async, and cooperative multitasking in general, requires all members participate in the contract:  you shall not block and you shall not go too long until yielding.  Once a piece of code violates that, all bets are off.  Python has explicit debugging mechanisms to help a developer detect the latter.<p>Cancel safety is less about killing an out-of-control task, and more about making sure the state after cancelling a task is consistent.')
