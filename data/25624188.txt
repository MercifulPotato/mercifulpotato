Item(by='core-questions', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609701176, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25623964, text='It&#x27;s well past time for an Internet Bill of Rights that re-implements the vision of freedoms given to us by the Founding Fathers and later architects of the Constitution, and applies it in a way that is contextually aware of the current lay of the land.<p>Anyone who has actually spent time online understands that private companies control all of the spaces that are akin to the &quot;public square&quot;. Sure, you can start your own site, but by doing so you&#x27;re effectively shutting yourself out from where the vast majority of people are spending their time. We cannot cede control of all dialogue to a bunch of American corporations who care far more about their own bottom line than they do about freedom or personal rights.')
