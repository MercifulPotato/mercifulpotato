I find this valuable for myself when I am downvoted but I did not expect to be.<p>Alternatively sometimes I make a comment that I think is worthwhile saying, even though I know it will be downvoted (for reasons I may disagree with).<p>Making comments just to farm karma is very easy but I don’t find it satisfying (and certainly doesn’t help create good discussion).')
