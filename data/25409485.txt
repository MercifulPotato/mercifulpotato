You want the perfect balance between investing and saving.<p>Savings allow you to preserve your value into the future till you detect a worthy investing opportunity.<p>Sadly enough, the present world has lost most important tools to save and preserve value: money and government bonds. Thus obliging all agents with value into risky investing instead of safely save for the future.<p>The situation explains why gold and Bitcoin soared last decade: economic agents need some store of value to park their savings.')
