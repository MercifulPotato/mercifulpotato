Item(by='Kliment', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603869751, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24915536, text='Cats can count to much more than two. If there are treats on a shelf and our older cat knocks them down, she&#x27;ll remember how many hit the floor (from the sound) and then not stop looking for them until she&#x27;s found that many. I suspect being able to keep track of distinct objects is useful when hunting if multiple small prey animals disperse in order to confuse the predator.')
