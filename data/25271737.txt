Item(by='giovannibonetti', descendants=None, kids=[25273199, 25272237], score=None, time=1606867466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25270107, text='&gt; Make it easy to make a nullable field non-nullable, not requiring a problematic and immediate full table scan.<p>It is not easy, but at least it is very viable to add not null constraints to Postgres 12+ with minimal locking:\n- First you add a not null check NOT VALIDATED\n- Then you add a not null constraint (it will require a full table scan, but with minimal locking)\n- Afterwards you can remove the unnecessary not null check')
