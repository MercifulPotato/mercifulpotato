Item(by='bigger_cheese', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606793312, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25260525, text='My Engineering Thesis had an oral component it was legitimately of the most nerve-wracking things I ever went through. I had to spend about half an hour in a room with 3 professors getting absolutely grilled over everything from basic theoretical knowledge to detailed technical nuances specific to my experiment. At times it felt like they were deliberately trying to trip me up by asking leading questions and trying to steer me in the wrong direction.<p>It would be very, very hard to bluff your way through something like this with Charisma alone. You have to be damn sure you know your topic in and out. For once off exams before graduation I think it is acceptable but I&#x27;d pity students who had to go through a grilling like this every couple of months.')
