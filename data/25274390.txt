When I was in my early 20s, he had a huge influence on me.<p>A random piece (yes, many, or even most, are like that):<p>---<p>Blue Notebook #10<p><i>Once there lived a red-haired man who lacked eyes and ears.<p>He was also lacking all hair, so he was called red-haired only with a large degree of generalization.<p>He couldn&#x27;t speak, as he was lacking a mouth. The same with his nose.<p>Even arms and legs, he just didn&#x27;t have any. Nor stomach, nor backside, nor spine. And no intestine. He didn&#x27;t have anything! Therefore it is totally unclear who is being discussed.<p>In fact, let&#x27;s not talk about him anymore.</i><p>---<p>More examples:<p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.scn.org&#x2F;realpoetik&#x2F;Daniil-Kharms03.htm" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.scn.org&#x2F;realpoetik&#x2F;Daniil-Kharms03.htm</a><p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;polyhymnion.org&#x2F;lit&#x2F;harms&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;polyhymnion.org&#x2F;lit&#x2F;harms&#x2F;</a><p>And his Wikipedia page:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Daniil_Kharms" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Daniil_Kharms</a>')
