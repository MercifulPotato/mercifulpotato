Item(by='leephillips', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604616922, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25002217, text='It may be that we are using the word in different senses, but I would say that Pluto has no hidden state whatsoever. The comments for cell ordering control what you see, so they are the <i>opposite</i> of hidden state. The execution order is determined by a static analysis of the dependencies among your code cells, and is determined <i>only</i> by what you see in those cells. There is no hidden state anywhere.')
