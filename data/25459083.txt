Item(by='asimops', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608232193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25458954, text='In fact, a trade-off has to happen. A cookie - or in terms of the GDPR, storing data on the end device - does not necessarily have to be technically required for it to be stored without consensus. A shopping cart, for example, can technically be coded as a GET parameter in the URL. However, since a cookie is the technically more sensible way to persist the shopping cart, a cookie can be used. This only needs to be explained - ideally in simple language - in the data processing statement.')
