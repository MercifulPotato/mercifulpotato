Item(by='ARandumGuy', descendants=None, kids=[25448115], score=None, time=1608144127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25446251, text='This is one of my big issues with Bitcoin, and other cryptocurrencies. It&#x27;s not a democratic system, it&#x27;s controlled by whoever has the most investment in the currency (either through mining power, or amount of currency).<p>This means that the people who get the most decision making power are incentivized to only make changes that help their investment. Any change that would help a majority of users, but harm these top users, is basically a non-starter.')
