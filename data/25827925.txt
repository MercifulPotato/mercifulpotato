Some fields like software engineering do not have such bodies and it&#x27;s wrong to attribute to software engineering rules that are used for some other fields.<p>Fields where this is used are highly regulated with usually law mandated sanctioning process that is designed to reduce direct risk to humans.<p>Do fields where there is no such risk have this type of sanctioning? For example mechanical (machine) engineers or chemical engineers.<p>Being an engineer doesn&#x27;t mean you need to be sanctioned by some body. If you get sanctioned, you might be Chartered Engineer or something similar. Interestingly, UK has Chartered IT Professional.')
