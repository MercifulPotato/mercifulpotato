Item(by='w0mbat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611344073, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25870686, text='A car stereo is supposed to have two +12V input wires and one ground wire.<p>The yellow wire is named &quot;constant&quot; or &quot;battery&quot;.\nThe red wire is named &quot;accessory&quot; or &quot;switched&quot;.\nThe black wire is ground.<p>When the car is turned off, the red wire drops to 0V and the radio turns off. The yellow wire is supposed to remain at 12V to maintain memory state.<p>The yellow wire draws a very low amperage, and would take years to drain the battery.')
