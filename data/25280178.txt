Item(by='tasuki', descendants=None, kids=[25280457, 25284598, 25289568], score=None, time=1606936381, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25279092, text='About a year ago, I discovered it was very helpful for me to have git branches ordered by &quot;recently modified first&quot;:<p>From my `~&#x2F;.gitconfig`:<p><pre><code>    [alias]\n        brt = &quot;!git for-each-ref refs&#x2F;heads --color=always --sort -committerdate --format=&#x27;%(HEAD)%(color:reset);%(color:yellow)%(refname:short)%(color:reset);%(contents:subject);%(color:green)(%(committerdate:relative))%(color:blue);&lt;%(authorname)&gt;&#x27; | column -t -s &#x27;;&#x27;&quot;\n</code></pre>\nI always spent a lot of time being confused about branches, and never realised how easy the solution was.')
