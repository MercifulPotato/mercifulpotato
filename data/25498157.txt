Item(by='andreareina', descendants=None, kids=[25498320, 25498189], score=None, time=1608577270, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25497773, text='You could explain anything with dark matter, but it&#x27;s a lot harder to explain multiple things with the <i>same</i> dark matter. The different observations (galaxy rotation curves, the Bullet Cluster collision, CMB power spectrum) all put different constraints on what the explanation has to be, and a particle that feels gravity but not electromagnetism is consistent with all of that.')
