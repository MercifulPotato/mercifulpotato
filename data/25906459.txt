Item(by='khawkins', descendants=None, kids=[25906730, 25907096, 25909167], score=None, time=1611597837, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25903576, text='The gig economy exploits the efficiencies of a trusting society masterfully. It would have been unthinkable 20 years ago that we&#x27;d let total strangers drive us home from bars and use our houses like hotels. But it turns out that with only a little oversight, we can trust our fellow citizens just as well as an employee of a professional company.<p>Systems of maintaining trust are very important, but having a trustworthy culture and populace is just as important. In the words of William Easterly, these systems can either be virtuous cycles or vicious cycles. Either trust flourishes or mistrust flourishes and going from one to the other is challenging because of its game-theoretical properties.')
