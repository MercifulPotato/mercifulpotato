Item(by='_Microft', descendants=None, kids=[25068314], score=None, time=1605167641, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25063914, text='I think the offense that people (including myself) took with it is that it conveys the idea that technical reasons are the only reasons that limit the operation of a power plant and that other, in this case environmental, reasons are second-class reasons that can or can not be taken into account.<p>Environmental impacts are externalities  (i.e. positive or negative effects on others (or something, in the case of nature) who are not directly involved and have no influence over them) and basically all environmental problems are negative externalities. Ignoring externalities is how we got our current environmental problems in first place which is why the argument rubbed me the wrong way.')
