Try explaining the concept of &quot;stack vs heap&quot; to someone who&#x27;s only done python programming, for example.<p>My brother went through a coding bootcamp that focused on Python and JS and then he started to get into Golang on his own and one day he asked me what the <i>point</i> of pointers is.
