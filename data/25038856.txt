Item(by='samatman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604950570, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25038007, text='The main problem which global state introduces, is making the bad assumption that there will only be one example of a given sort of data.<p>In games, that&#x27;s a safe assumption for many parts of the system, since you&#x27;re definitely only going to be running one instance of the game world at a time. So making that state private just means you have to pass around references to it on the stack, and keep track of what you&#x27;re doing.')
