Item(by='goatinaboat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606137590, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25182712, text='<i>What&#x27;s a NATO setup?</i><p>A conventional strap is in two pieces, so if one pin attaching it to the watch body fails you lose the watch. A NATO strap loops under both pins as a single piece, so if one pin fails, the watch is still attached by the other.<p>99% of people you see wearing a NATO strap will never be in a situation where they might have this problem mind you!')
