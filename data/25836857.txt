Item(by='stragies', descendants=None, kids=[25851306], score=None, time=1611081268, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25827803, text='For Linux, X11, USB-connection to Android-Device(s), and the TV over Network:<p>- Make another screen - 3 options, depending on driver&#x2F;available_outputs:<p><pre><code>  1: make a mode with `cvt`, add it with `xrandr --newmode`, bind it to unused output with `--addmode`\n  2: Use a dummy plug on a free output\n  3: Use `evdi` and the `example` application in evdipp to create a virtual screen, attach the provider with xrandr\n</code></pre>\n- Export the new screen, or a portion of it with `x11vnc`, using the `-clip` option with coordinates or screennumber.<p><pre><code>  Optional: Use `NoVNC` to make VNC available to any HTML5 browser\n</code></pre>\n- use adb port forwarding commands to forward localhost:whatever1 on the Android device to connect to the X11VNC port.<p>- Use ADB, or your finger to start VNC session on tablet to localhost:whatever1, and UPNP&#x2F;whatever to start browser on TV for NoVNC url.<p>That should cut down your research time ;)<p>Be warned, Evdi&amp;EvdiPP are currently still glitchy, but under active development.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;mlukaszek&#x2F;evdipp" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;mlukaszek&#x2F;evdipp</a>')
