Item(by='socialdemocrat', descendants=None, kids=[25901466], score=None, time=1611564773, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25900181, text='Being a homeowner does not mean you live in single family house. Yes, I own my home in Oslo but I live in an apartment. We have shared garage areas which is indeed common in Norway even for  people with single family homes. Garages are often clustered in Norway to keep neighborhoods car free.<p>We also have problems all across Norway with electrical capacity not being enough to install chargers in these garages. So upgrades to the grid has been necessary lots of places.<p>We only recently got a grid upgrade in my apartment complex to allow installation of chargers.')
