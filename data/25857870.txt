Item(by='Xixi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611229078, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25854615, text='The way I was taught is that ao (青) covers all shades of blue and green, although depending on context its meaning can be limited to blue.<p>So for instance if both ao (青) and midori (緑) are used to designate sports teams, then the ao (青) team will be the blue team.<p>Due to this ambiguity with the word ao (青) it&#x27;s in general better to use a more precise word to specify the shade of blue&#x2F;green, like koniro (紺色) for deep blue, mizuiro (水色) for light blue, midori (緑) for green, etc.')
