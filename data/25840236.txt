Item(by='SAI_Peregrinus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611097893, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25839310, text='For most operating systems, threads each have their own stack space, and (if supported) thread-local storage, but share heap, constants (.bss), and static variables (.data). It&#x27;s impossible to say exactly what resources a given thread is using from those shared resources, so htop just includes them all, for every thread.')
