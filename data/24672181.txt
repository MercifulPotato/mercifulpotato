On one hand, I believe the government should take measures to nurture the local startups and app, to give them a fair chance at growing. On the other hand, I don&#x27;t think an app store created and managed by the government will have the right incentive to be competitive against foreign players and may just end up  spiraling into a hot bureaucratic mess, with more and more hoops to jump through just to get an app on that platform.')
