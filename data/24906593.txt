Examples are necessary for both motivation, and for showing how the formalisms work, at this level. One good undergraduate textbook that has plenty of examples is Dummit &amp; Foote. However, that doesn&#x27;t mean that you don&#x27;t define anything properly, and solely use examples to show how to crunch certain computations.
