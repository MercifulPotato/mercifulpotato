Item(by='dotancohen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607623601, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25368854, text='To clarify this point. The Raptor uses two turbopumps. One turbopump hangs off the side and runs fuel rich, and this fuel-rich exhaust is the fuel input for the combustion chamber.<p>The second turbopump, to which tdy721 is referring, sits right above the injectors and runs oxygen rich. This oxygen rich exhaust is the oxidizer input for the combustion chamber.')
