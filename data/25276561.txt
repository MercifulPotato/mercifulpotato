So you can make 5500 ListObject rps for prefix=&#x2F;a&#x2F;b and simultaneously make 5500 GetObject rps for key=&#x2F;a&#x2F;b&#x2F;file1 and not be ratelimited.<p>In other word ratelimiting key is `${HTTP_METHOD}(${QURERY_PARAM_KEY}|${QUERY_PARAM_PREFIX})`')
