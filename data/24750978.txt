Our local power company deployed one carefully, and was very successful despite repeated suits by the cable-internet provider.  Eventually the ISP fought back at the state government level which limited the fiber&#x27;s expansion, but lots of rural areas launched their own using similar models.  Apparently the cable monopolies didn&#x27;t feel it was worth competing for rural areas, meaning a number of farmhouses, hours away from cities, have better service for cheaper.')
