The ability to upload 3D models on poly.google.com will be shut down on April 30, 2021.<p>What does this mean for you?<p>Your assets will be available at poly.google.com until June 30, 2021. You can download your entire library at takeout.google.com&#x2F;settings&#x2F;takeout&#x2F;custom&#x2F;poly, or you can download individual assets by viewing each asset’s page. After June 30, poly.google.com and associated APIs will no longer be accessible. You will not be able to upload new 3D models on poly.google.com after April 30, 2021.<p>We want to thank you for joining us on this journey. We appreciate you trusting us to host your assets and provide a space where they can shine. The amazing work that our users have uploaded to Poly every day has surprised, humbled, and delighted us, and we have you to thank.<p>For more information, view this Help Center article.<p>Thanks,\nThe Poly Team\nPoly')
