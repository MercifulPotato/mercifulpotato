Item(by='dyeje', descendants=None, kids=[25443303], score=None, time=1608102590, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25438719, text='Yes, the advantage of a GraphQL endpoint is you can ask for a variety of things and the tradeoff is potential performance issues for unforeseen queries doing N+1s or something.<p>If you control the API and know all the use cases for a REST endpoint, the advantage is predictable performance characteristics and the tradeoff is flexibility.<p>It all depends on what you need (and maybe what tools you&#x27;re using to mitigate GraphQL resolver problems).')
