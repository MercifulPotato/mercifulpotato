Visualizing a neural net is a fun problem by itself; neural nets are essentially DAGs, but somehow recording the flow of data has lots of quirks (especially when dealing with RNNs or skip connections). There&#x27;s a top-level view where you&#x27;re just discussing about the layers as they are connected, and a low-level view where the output of each op matters.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;pytorchrec.readthedocs.io" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;pytorchrec.readthedocs.io</a>')
