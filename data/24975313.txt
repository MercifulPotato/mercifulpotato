Item(by='xyzzyz', descendants=None, kids=[24976274], score=None, time=1604365790, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24974965, text='Population is not very relevant metric here. What matters is not so much how many people actually <i>live</i> in Manhattan, but rather how many people are commuting into it every day. In fact, commuters double the island population every day, and exceed the number of residents in 1920 by 50%.<p>What happened with Manhattan was that tenements were replaced with office buildings, and these use <i>more</i>, not <i>less</i> commuting.')
