But most things we call this are not really permanently broken (and we might be surprised to find some of them to be trivially recovered).<p>I don&#x27;t really hear &quot;bricked&quot; in use for things damaged mechanically or by water.  It&#x27;s usually in conjunction with a failed upgrade, etc.  Almost none of these things are really permanently broken, though some of them for practical purposes may be.')
