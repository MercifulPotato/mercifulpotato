Item(by='defnotanai', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608206604, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25439878, text='I am truly looking forward for CockroachDB to become the next PostgreSQL for planet-spanning database workloads. In our ecosystem we get more and more requests for CRDB integration.<p>Generally though, I would not say that full compatibility should even be desired. A k&#x2F;v database simply works differently from a strictly relational database. There are things like shard IDs, avoiding hot spots, deciding on how to paginate data.<p>Going too much into &quot;PostgreSQL&quot; replacement will eventually hurt CRDB because too much focus will go into making legacy enterprise SQL (along the lines of <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25454635" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=25454635</a>) work on this system. It&#x27;s the forklift approach of moving to the cloud. This becomes clear when skimming through the docs:<p>- <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;how-to-choose-db-index-keys&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;blog&#x2F;how-to-choose-db-index-ke...</a><p>- <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;stable&#x2F;performance-best-practices-overview.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;stable&#x2F;performance-best-p...</a><p>- <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;stable&#x2F;limit-offset.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;stable&#x2F;limit-offset.html</a><p>- <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;v20.2&#x2F;selection-queries#pagination-example" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.cockroachlabs.com&#x2F;docs&#x2F;v20.2&#x2F;selection-queries#p...</a><p>I think many of the &quot;light SQL&quot; patterns are really great - things like SELECT or WHERE, which e.g. DynamoDB can simply not solve without ElasticSearch. But all in all, I am very excited for CRDB to get more industry acceptance, and I think their cloud offering could also become very interesting - a competitor to Google BigTable &#x2F; CloudSpanner, AWS DynamoDB or Cloud SQL.')
