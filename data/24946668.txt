Item(by='cperciva', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604090356, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24945751, text='<i>&quot;Online&quot; used to mean &quot;not batch-processed&quot;.</i><p>Computer scientists still refer to &quot;online algorithms&quot; as meaning &quot;algorithms which process each input as it comes in&quot;.  The obvious algorithm for &quot;find the minimum element&quot; is online for example, since you can stop at any point and say what the smallest you&#x27;ve seen so far is; however finding the <i>median</i> takes more work (you need to maintain two heaps, vs. simply collecting all the data and sorting it at the end).')
