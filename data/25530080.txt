Item(by='jMyles', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608835016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25530003, text='Ransom usually means, &quot;I have some(one|thing) of yours, and if you <i>want it back</i>, you need to pay me.&quot;<p>Calling this &quot;randomware&quot; subtly blurs the line between copying and stealing.  The attackers here didn&#x27;t remove access to the data (clearly stealing), they made a copy (clearly a crime other than stealing, at least in my view).<p>It&#x27;s more like blackmail than kidnapping.')
