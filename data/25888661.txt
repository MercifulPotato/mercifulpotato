Item(by='surprisetalk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611457037, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25888504, text='In my experience, pen-and-paper helps me resist the urge to “just get started already”.<p>I don’t know why, but when I’m in the design-phase with a keyboard in my hands, I think I get too excited about building things and go straight to the text-editor to “hammer out the details” in code.<p>Away from the computer, I’m much more likely to complete a thorough design. And pen-and-paper remains my favorite tool for working on ideas that are too big to fit in working-memory.')
