Item(by='tpoacher', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609185906, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25545543, text='I don&#x27;t want to trivialize this article, but I feel like the whole thing can be reduced to the statement &quot;define &#x27;smaller&#x27;&quot;.<p>Instead it reads like a &quot;this is what &#x27;smaller&#x27; means for intervals&quot; at which point I just want to comment &quot;why?&quot; and add a &quot;[citation needed]&quot; tag.<p>It all depends on what your interval represents, or how you can agree that it should be treated. Does it relate to confidence intervals? Does it summarise a distribution? Should it relate to stochastic dominance? Should it represent conflicting evidence between true and false? Should it be treated as a fuzzy membership function?\nAll these interpretations would lead to different, equally valid interpretations for the term &#x27;smaller&#x27;.')
