It&#x27;s a slam dunk anywhere where it&#x27;s paired with natural gas due to fast throttling on open cycle and combined cycle turbines, and a little less so where coal is used. Nigeria, for example, is primarily natural gas for fossil electrical generation (~16% of their electrical generation capacity), so any kwh&#x27;s you&#x27;re generating with renewables is natural gas burned only when renewables can&#x27;t meet load demand.<p>Check out South Australia on OpenNEM [1].
