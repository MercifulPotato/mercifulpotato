Item(by='himinlomax', descendants=None, kids=[25158861, 25159959], score=None, time=1605864171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25155892, text='I hate these pointless analogies in popular science reporting. &quot;droplets of fluid will move like performers in a dance choreographed&quot; -- it may sound good to the writer but it just comes across as pretentious and does not help explain anything. In general those facile analogies do in fact make things muddier. The only exceptions is when they are stated along with how they are different from the phenomenon being described. Here, no point, except to pad the word count I guess.')
