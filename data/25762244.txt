Item(by='xpe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610547769, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25761181, text='<i>Who</i> benefits and <i>who</i> is hurt when reasonable people don&#x27;t find ways to speak their minds and advocate for their values?<p>This question is particularly apropos in modern times -- take the last week for example.<p>To ask the question in a different way: There may be a tradeoff between maintaining the <i>status quo</i> versus speaking about our <i>values</i>.<p>To me, it is really important to strive to speak and act according to our values _and_ to reflect on who (and what ideas) benefit from the status quo. It is entirely reasonable to be strategic about what you say, but I don&#x27;t pretend to think the status quo is <i>correct</i> just because it exists.')
