Item(by='nine_k', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608309726, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25467530, text='Another anecdote: in 1990s, Sun servers had relatively &quot;weak&quot; Sparq CPUs, contemporary Intels would wipe the floor with them in integer performance tests. But they had superior interconnect and I&#x2F;O channels, so e.g. in 4-core configurations Sparq-based servers showed much better application performance (say, running Oracle RDBMS) than the beefier Intel 4-CPU machines.<p>The connection fabric is utterly important, and expensive.')
