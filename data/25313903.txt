Item(by='jpkotyla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607169510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25313397, text='Perhaps some non-binary classification could be of use. You could imagine one or two additional categories of acceptance&#x2F;rejection, something like plausible and implausible. Journals could publish articles falling into these classifications making sure that it is abundantly clear that they have not been fully approved&#x2F; disapproved.<p>Having such options should allow for promotion&#x2F;demotion of ideas as they garner more&#x2F;less support.<p>The concept is similar to that of working papers and preprints, in the belief that getting more eyes (both expert and amateur) on a paper is better for truth discovery than if only a few experts evaluate.')
