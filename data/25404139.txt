Item(by='rayiner', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607831254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403245, text='Citizens United was based on the Court interpreting the statute as imposing restrictions on political speech (prosecuting a non-profit for distribution of a movie about Hilary Clinton) that are invalid under the first amendment. That’s not a situation where the court is saying what the law should be; they’re saying what kinds of laws are allowed under the constitution and which aren’t.<p>Here, the argument isn’t about the constitution. It’s about what “appropriate relief” means in the statute.')
