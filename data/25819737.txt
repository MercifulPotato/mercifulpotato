Item(by='mkl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610960570, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25818482, text='It was <i>What Do You Care What Other People Think?</i>.<p>He had been experimenting with what he could do while counting, and found he couldn&#x27;t count while talking, but could count while reading.  John Tukey didn&#x27;t believe him, and thought it should be the other way around.  It turned out Tukey and Feynman counted completely differently, Tukey by visualising and Feynman using internal speech, and so Tukey could count while talking, but not while reading.  So, not the same person doing both methods.')
