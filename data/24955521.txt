Item(by='pjmlp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604181428, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24955305, text='Modern Android versions have multiple tiers.<p>Interpreter written in Assembly for fast startup, if the application was never executed.<p>Followed by JIT compilation to native code and when the device is idle, the PGO data collected by the JIT is used to produce a binary for direct native execution.<p>As of Android 10 those PGO files are uploaded to the stores and then if a similar device installs the application, they will get the PGO data as well and thus achieve a relatively fast result for their initial compilation.')
