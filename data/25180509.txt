Item(by='jacobwilliamroy', descendants=None, kids=[25180804, 25180596], score=None, time=1606075077, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25177200, text='Always tip (in the United States) because your server often makes depression-era wages and sometimes, in some establishments, your server also has to split that tip with the kitchen staff. The people who create your dining experience will be underpaid for their irreplaceable time if you do not tip. Consider tipping 30% for breakfast because customers tend to sit for longer, resulting in lower customer turnover and fewer tips than other times of day.')
