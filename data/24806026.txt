Item(by='ravi-delia', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602891100, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24802168, text='What do you imagine a standards board for English doing? Publishing an official standard and then everyone rejoices that <i>finally</i> someone has told them how to write the language they already write in? How is it any different from the myriad of &#x27;rules&#x27; made up by well-intentioned people through history, many of which are now responsible for English&#x27;s lack of well defined spelling rules?<p>In the end such a body would wind up either as descriptive, which is interesting academically but also useless from a standardization perspective, or prescriptive, which would just lead to it being happily ignored.')
