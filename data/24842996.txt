Item(by='fanf2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603233203, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24842670, text='The minimum IPv4 allocation is a &#x2F;24 because that is the smallest network that can be globally routed. Smaller networks are filtered out of BGP to stop routing tables getting too big too fast. Anything smaller than &#x2F;24 has to be rented from a network provider who will advertise an aggregate route covering many customers.')
