Item(by='tannhaeuser', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604923090, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25032105, text='I guess a distinction has to be made wrt what kind of sw gets what license. In the forming years of F&#x2F;OSS, it was mostly re-implementing Unix, compilers, and POSIX tools, guided by strong specs and portability requirements. Now with Linux almost the only server OS standing, questionable innovations such as containers, one-of-a-kind software being released as F&#x2F;OSS, and the abundance of F&#x2F;OSS directly responsible for the &quot;cloud&quot; (in the sense that selling services and integration is the only area left where to make a buck), the copyleft&#x2F;non-copyleft taxonomy doesn&#x27;t seem to work anymore.')
