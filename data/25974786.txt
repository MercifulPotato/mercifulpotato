Item(by='fb03', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612055968, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25967914, text='It was so easy to draw simple graphics in pascal, right?<p>I have this snippet burned in my head and I used it all the time in MS-DOS&#x27; on Turbo Pascal 6 or 7 to get to the tasty 320x200 palleted graphics mode:<p><pre><code>    asm\n      mov ax,13h\n      int 10h\n    end\n</code></pre>\niirc, after that you could simply directly access video memory using something like &quot;mem[$A000:x+200*y] = color;&quot; and that was it.<p>Fun times.')
