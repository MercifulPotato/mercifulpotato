Item(by='ethbr0', descendants=None, kids=[25604625], score=None, time=1609442950, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25595375, text='The Shopify features you outlined are the &quot;how&quot;, but not the &quot;why&quot;. As in, why would I buy from a Shopify store, if an equivalent product were offered by a bundler?<p>1,000,000 stores using Shopify are effectively the same as 1,000,000 products on Amazon, only less convenient.<p>I think the overall takeaway is that trust doesn&#x27;t scale to Amazon-<i>now</i>-size: i.e. one of the biggest retail companies on the planet.<p>What it did scale to (and very well) was Amazon-1995-2010-size (eg Etsy or eBay), and there&#x27;s a lot of size between D2C and those variants of Amazon.<p>I&#x27;m open for convincing, but I don&#x27;t see a path to &quot;D2C eats the world,&quot; when for relatively modest amounts of capital one could create a smaller, right-size Amazon that could bundle and dominate.')
