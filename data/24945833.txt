Item(by='wtallis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604085523, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24945596, text='The Supreme Court is not prohibited from overturning existing precedent. They just generally choose to operate under a doctrine that says they should try to avoid overturning precedent. Lower courts are more formally bound by precedent from higher courts, but their rulings as constrained by precedent can be appealed up to the level(s) that are empowered to overturn the relevant precedent.')
