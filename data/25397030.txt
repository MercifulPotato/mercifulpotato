Item(by='ninjin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607765656, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25385137, text='A salute to Lynx, I rely on it every day to cope with `text&#x2F;html` e-mail in Mutt by virtue of the following line in my `mailcap` that has worked for anything hitting my inbox for about five years now:<p><pre><code>    text&#x2F;html; lynx -width 72 -assume_charset=%{charset} -display_charset=utf-8 -dump %s | sed &#x27;s|^   ||&#x27;; nametemplate=%s.html; copiousoutput\n</code></pre>\nTrying out the alternatives after reading the other comments here, perhaps I should give `w3m` a shot some day as it renders white space rather pleasantly?')
