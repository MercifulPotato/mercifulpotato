Item(by='knorker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603438844, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24865676, text='GPL yes, AGPL no.<p>AGPL triggers not only when you distribute the binary, but also when the AGPL (or derivative work, or &quot;corresponding source&quot;) is part of a <i>service</i> offered, even if it&#x27;s not distributed.<p>This is the so-called &quot;hole&quot; that AGPL is intended to plug, and is unique among well knows licenses.<p>IOW if you use mongodb as a backend database for your photo website, then it gets triggered.')
