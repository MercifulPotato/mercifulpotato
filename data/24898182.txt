Item(by='naringas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603730883, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24897894, text='I have a suggestion, it involves realizing that any job involves a variety of diverse activities, naturally some will be more enjoyable than others, but all of them will have to be done.<p>This realization allowed me to focus and enjoy the fun stuff while also being able to put up with the annoying bits.<p>A simplistic example with cooking (a favorite hobby) involved realizing that the activity of cooking as a whole involves a whole lot of cleaning and doing dishes.<p>Once I re-framed &#x27;cleaning&#x27; as &#x27;an indispensable part of cooking&#x27; then doing dishes (which is annoying) became less annoying somehow.')
