Item(by='KoftaBob', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605717719, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25134220, text='One detail that&#x27;s often neglected in these discussions: while the remote work trend opens up companies to a larger supply of workers, it simultaneously opens up the supply of jobs to the average applicant.<p>Google has more applicants applying because they don&#x27;t need to live in California, but the average applicant is also no longer limited to applying to jobs in the area they can live.<p>The companies have a bigger pool of applicants, but the typical applicant now has more leverage because they&#x27;re no longer geographically limited. In theory, this should lead to an equilibrium.')
