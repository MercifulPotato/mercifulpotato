Item(by='tylerhou', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607325054, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25325799, text='I think we agree.<p>Slippery slope arguments are valid if 1) there is no demarcated line (&quot;extingent circumstances&quot;) or 2) there is a demarcated line, and there is good justification for why that line might change.<p>Legalizing gay marriage is a clearly demarcated line. There is no justification for why gay marriage being legalized will lead to bestiality being legalized, so it is an invalid slippery slope argument.')
