Item(by='whatshisface', descendants=None, kids=[25036764], score=None, time=1604939111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25036364, text='IoT is embedded plus an internet connection. The internet connection is sometimes a big deal that changes a lot. There have been &quot;run the wire from A to B&quot; networks of controllers since forever, but with an internet connection the number of links grows as N instead of N^2, and you can connect to nodes around the world with no additional cost. For some applications, that&#x27;s hugely significant.<p>Internet protocol is huge and unweildy, but since you can buy standard components and install free software to handle it, it gains another huge advantage over the &quot;100 serial protocols&quot; world of yesteryear.')
