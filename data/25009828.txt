Item(by='yourabstraction', descendants=None, kids=[25010499], score=None, time=1604686702, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25009448, text='This is exactly how I look at technical debt, yet it seems so many people miss the appropriate financial metaphor, which seems obvious given debt is in the name. It seems popular to conflate technical debt with bad engineering, but appropriately pushing work off to the future is actually very thoughtful engineering. Technical debt is a tool that can be applied (just like financial debt) to work towards an end goal when you otherwise don&#x27;t have the means to pay the price upfront.<p>Getting a startup off the ground requires an excellent understanding of how much technical debt you can incur and realistic estimates about your ability to pay it off as your team grows.')
