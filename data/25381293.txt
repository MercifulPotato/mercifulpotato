Item(by='dannyw', descendants=None, kids=[25381567], score=None, time=1607651193, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25380140, text='Back in the days of pre-computing, we had the “first sale doctrine”.<p>This meant that as soon as you bought something, you had the right to do whatever you wanted with that physical item.<p>Including selling it. Including renting it out. Including bundling it with five other books from other publishers and selling it together for whatever price. Without any of their consent.<p>And it certainly includes tearing a page out. Or writing your own notes over the pages.')
