Item(by='titanomachy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610330640, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25721785, text='Good advice. Also: don&#x27;t blame the <i>people</i> around you for why you couldn&#x27;t fix something. Even if it&#x27;s because your manager was a short-sighted idiot, or the senior engineer on your team was jealously guarding his territory, you don&#x27;t have to say that in the interview. See if you can reframe it in a more blame-neutral way.<p>For example, if you couldn&#x27;t remove a bad pattern because it was too deeply entrenched for you to handle it yourself, and you couldn&#x27;t get anyone more senior interested: that&#x27;s expected for a junior developer and probably wouldn&#x27;t be held against you.')
