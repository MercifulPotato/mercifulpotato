Item(by='core-questions', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605825100, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25153841, text='Many paid SSO&#x2F;IDP solutions offer this, e.g. IDaptive has an App Gateway that has worked well for me, and Azure SSO has a more limited one as well. You run some agent behind the firewall, it talks outgoing to the cloud sso provider, and your end users get proxied through with the benefit of authenticating to the IDP before they even hit your service at all. Great way to slap 2FA on a lot of things without having to worry about VPNs.')
