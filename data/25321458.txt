Most of the volume of the packaged LSI-11 systems was I&#x2F;O and disk storage - there were several models of different sizes, I recall the 11&#x2F;23 was the largest.<p>The 11&#x2F;23 was not a consumer-grade product.  I don&#x27;t think it was intended to compete with PCs.')
