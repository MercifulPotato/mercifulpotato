Item(by='analog31', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608343827, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25474263, text='The terminology gets even stranger. ;-) A &quot;chair&quot; is a professorship that is funded by an endowment, that gets naming rights. So for instance, you might see someone like: &quot;Bob Smith, Alice Q Hammond professor of situational metaphysics.&quot; What it means is that Alice Q Hammond gave a bunch of money to endow a &quot;chaired&quot; professorship, that Bob Smith occupies.<p>There are &quot;chairs&quot; like this in professional orchestras too.<p>The taxonomy of academic titles could fill a book.')
