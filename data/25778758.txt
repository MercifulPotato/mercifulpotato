Item(by='modeless', descendants=None, kids=[25779513, 25779289, 25779248, 25779317, 25779357], score=None, time=1610643429, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25777207, text='It&#x27;s also important to realize that the backup includes your encrypted iMessage messages, <i>and</i> the key required to decrypt them. Meaning that if you have backups enabled, all the &quot;end-to-end&quot; encryption in iMessage is defeated. Apple and by extension the FBI can read your messages. This is documented by Apple here: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.apple.com&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;HT202303" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.apple.com&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;HT202303</a><p>Even if you disable backups, whenever you correspond with someone that has backups enabled those messages are still accessible to Apple.')
