Item(by='vram22', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604569118, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24996445, text='Another well-known long-running (20+ years) successful permaculture &#x2F; regen ag farm in India is Krishna McKenzie&#x27;s Solitude Farm, Auroville, Tamil Nadu. Again, mixed crops, herbs, fruit and other trees, multi-layer. On-site farm cafe with raw &#x2F; local &#x2F; vegan food. They also know well about and use local edible weeds as food, further reducing work and increasing efficiency - no need to even plant them, let alone cultivate! He has a video or two about that too, along with Nina Sengupta,  who wrote the book Edible Weeds of the Auroville Region. He also does talks, local outreach and education, and has a good video channel on YouTube about it all.<p>Mentioned it before too, in that same recent thread:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=24876047" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;item?id=24876047</a>')
