Item(by='GuB-42', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604251971, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24956821, text='In fact the French number system used to be base-20.<p>It ancient times we had spellings like &quot;deux vingt et dix&quot;: 2x20+10=50, or &quot;six vingt&quot;: 6x20=120. Now, that spelling only remains for 70 to 90. And not in all French speaking regions. Switzerland and Belgium use &quot;septante&quot;, &quot;octante&quot; and &quot;nonante&quot; instead of &quot;soixante-dix&quot; (60+10=70), &quot;quatre-vingt&quot; (4x20=80) and &quot;quatre-vingt-dix&quot; (4x20+10=90).')
