Item(by='8fhdkjw039hd', descendants=None, kids=[25419718, 25419536], score=None, time=1607962579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25419166, text='The strange thing about super-stimuli is it looks very ridiculous when animals fall for it; there is the funny example of a turkey trying to mate with a red balloon on a stick. But it is the nature of super-stimuli that they will not seem ridiculous when we fall for them, because the very features we use to gauge authenticity are what will be simulated. And so we will get sort of hollow simulacrum that emphasize these features and lack everything else.')
