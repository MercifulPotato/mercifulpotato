Item(by='spthorn60', descendants=None, kids=[25455182], score=None, time=1608155059, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25442097, text='As most home woodworkers could confirm, a filter-on-a-box-fan works wonders in a (saw)dusty garage. Even after sanding or sawing MDF, the air is clean after about 10-15 minutes. From personal observation, I&#x27;d suggest that non-taped is a good idea, as any non-filtered air is routed through the fan to help overall circulation; ideally you want to displace all the air in the room quickly.')
