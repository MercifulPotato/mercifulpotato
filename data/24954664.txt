Item(by='johndoe0815', descendants=None, kids=[24955426, 24958036], score=None, time=1604174215, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24953892, text='An interesting related fact is that the 48k Sinclair Spectrum also used 8 32kBit DRAMs (in addition to 8 16kBit chips).<p>However, these were not the special modules used in the Apple 3, but 64kBit DRAMs which had a fault in one half of the matrix, such as TI TMS4532-20 or OKI M3732.\nThe defective half of the chip was simply disabled.')
