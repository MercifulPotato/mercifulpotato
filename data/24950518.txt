Item(by='Kim_Bruning', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604143182, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24935332, text='Imagine a world where no one would want to take the night shift.<p>I always thought Early Bird&#x2F;Night Owl were just two different modes, like right or left handedness.<p>But while right and left handedness appear to be +&#x2F;- selectively neutral (these days at least), a percentage of night owls in society  would appear to confer a selective advantage.<p>Having a potential oral medication would give night owls a new superpower: An ability to switch shifts at the drop of the hat; or at least much more easily than the early birds.<p>In industrial programming you often need to take the customer&#x27;s shifts into account; so this could be useful to a few colleagues of mine!')
