Item(by='acdha', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603221976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24840962, text='That very much depends on what distribution you use. The Fedora&#x2F;CentOS&#x2F;RHEL world has had SELinux enabled by default for years. The Debian world has not but AppArmor is pretty popular there and while that&#x27;s a fairly different system it hits many of the same sandboxing points. Beyond the default configuration, anyone who is following a hardening standard like CIS is going to have SELinux enabled, too.')
