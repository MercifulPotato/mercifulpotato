Item(by='qayxc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602543381, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24760121, text='Drivers are major pain with Unikernels - you basically need to rewrite all the drivers for your Unikernel; this gets especially painful with application accelerators and GPUs, since those cannot easily be replaced by virtual devices (like storage or networking).<p>More importantly, app development is way harder since you don&#x27;t have all OS libraries available to you and cannot easily benefit from previous R&amp;D and workflows.<p>You basically need to start from scratch or rewrite your app, which simply isn&#x27;t always an option. There&#x27;s currently no mature tooling that I&#x27;m aware of that would allow you to simply recompile existing apps to turn them into Unikernels (I just discovered Unikraft [1], but I cannot comment on whether it&#x27;s any good).\nEdit: [1] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.unikraft.org" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.unikraft.org</a><p>Debugging is a pain as well compared to more standard development.')
