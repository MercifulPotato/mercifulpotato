Item(by='runawaybottle', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604892159, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25029285, text='Motivation is not a discipline. If you are constantly motivated, then it’s like, I guess ... not motivation. It’s hitting the boost in a racing game, if you are just boosting the whole game, that’s your baseline speed.<p>If you’re out of boost (motivation), just wait for it to recharge. But, certainly stay disciplined (maintain baseline speed), don’t stagnate, don’t miss your turns. When the boost shows up, it’ll be effective.<p>If you fuck up, and fall behind, the only thing the boost will be good for is bringing you up to speed since you languished wondering where it was this whole time.')
