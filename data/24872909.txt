Let someone else do the work, I agree.<p>But if the goal is to have a human-editable file that loads a few values into a map, and it often is, TOML is pretty nice.<p>I think we&#x27;ve all been burned by &#x27;configuration languages&#x27; which take on the complexity of a full programming language, in a worst-of-both-worlds sort of way.
