Item(by='Traubenfuchs', descendants=None, kids=[25045111], score=None, time=1605010248, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25024901, text='Modern, sane and productive languages usually come with strong abstractions for concurrent usage, e.g. atomic reference wrappers, thread safe wrappers (AtomicInteger) or special methods (Interlocked.increment) for numbers and concurrent collections.<p>With them and a basic understanding of single system concurrency, single system concurrency is and always will be trivial.')
