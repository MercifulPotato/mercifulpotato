Item(by='skohan', descendants=None, kids=[24912757, 24914565, 24912169], score=None, time=1603832649, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24911785, text='I have heard a lot of speculation that the move to remove the charger was motivated by Apple&#x27;s desire to sell more chargers, but I suspect that they could care less about moving chargers.<p>I believe it <i>is</i> profit motivated, but the real reason is the massive savings they will get on logistics by fitting almost 50% more iPhones in a shipping container by removing the charger.<p>If they pass some of that savings onto the consumer, it seems like it&#x27;s win-win.')
