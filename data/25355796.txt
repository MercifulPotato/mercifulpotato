Item(by='lmm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607493631, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25354597, text='&gt;  If you want a more user-friendly guarantee, you can reserve it when it&#x27;s added to the cart.<p>If you open an ACID transaction when the user adds something to the cart and don&#x27;t close it until they check out, you&#x27;ll find your database gets locked up pretty quickly. So you can&#x27;t actually use the ACID transactions to implement the behaviour you want - you have to implement some kind of reserve&#x2F;commit semantics in userspace, whether you&#x27;re using an ACID database or not.')
