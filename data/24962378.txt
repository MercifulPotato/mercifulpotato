Item(by='iguy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604263684, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24961998, text='I think Hindi&#x2F;Urdu is quite a bit like Serbian&#x2F;Croatian. The preferred script followed the religious divide, but (if I understand right) both sort-of standardised quite similar dialects out of a range of possibilities. And then later they were politically divided, and there were some efforts to move the official vocabulary away from each other&#x27;s.<p>IIRC Sanskrit has also been written in dozens of scripts, there&#x27;s a perfect one-to-one transliteration into most (maybe all) the north-indian scripts, although they have extra characters for their own sounds.')
