Item(by='bitexploder', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608602003, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25501885, text='There is a point where fitting a little more code on one screen actually helps. Usually not though. Our brains can only see a screen at a time. There is some optimal mix of terseness, especially when you know your reader (probably you in a few months!) will grok it, vs verbosity. If I find myself untangling a single line down the road in my brain it was too complex. Python is already so expressive! We all find our style, but generally I know I did it right if I look back at code at think “wow that’s easy to understand” vs “hmmn, what was I thinking there?” Heh.')
