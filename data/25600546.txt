Item(by='porknubbins', descendants=None, kids=[25600868, 25601351], score=None, time=1609471472, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25600339, text='Prompts is a piece of jargon, unfortunately confusing but crucial to anyone trying to memorize anything using an SRS. A prompt could take the form “What is a X” or be a blank “the dog ____ over the log” or just be a foreign word like 中国. So in some ways its a more general concept than a “question” but calling it the front side of a flashcard is probably clearer.')
