Item(by='umvi', descendants=None, kids=[24822507, 24823981, 24823529], score=None, time=1603061168, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821644, text='The difference is that light travels through space, but not time (similar to how a vertical line does not travel the x axis, only the y axis). The ball travels through both space and time.<p>The faster you go, the less you travel through time. Thus, if the ball were travelling at the speed of light, it would not travel through time either and would follow the same path as light.')
