There is, he held, a reciprocity or “recognition” that ties charismatic leaders to their followers<p>I haven&#x27;t really thought of it in this way, but it gave me an epiphany that my struggle with being charismatic, which is that I can be very, but it takes effort and is exhausting sometimes, is at least partly due to the context switching required to &quot;know your audience&quot;.
