Item(by='rgoulter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606993130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25287224, text='It&#x27;s a joke, not about an actual tragedy.<p>A good reason to put a glass of water on bedside table is because you might be thirsty in the middle of the night. You might accidentally knock the glass over and cause a huge spill instead. So &quot;in case he causes an accident&quot; is silly.<p>If in real life someone knocked over a glass of water that they put on their bedside table, it&#x27;s just like the joke, and &quot;tragic&quot;. (And an example of life imitating art).')
