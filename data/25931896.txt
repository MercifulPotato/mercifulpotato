Item(by='bitwize', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611772205, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25929399, text='Bob Altemeyer would disagree. He formulated the idea of right-wing authoritarianism (RWA) as a psychological condition. His research discovered that there is no corresponding left-wing authoritarianism -- that even the authoritarianism exhibited by followers of Stalin or Mao was right-wing.<p>Kinda hard to be authoritarian if one of your central beliefs is in the fraternity and equality of all. It&#x27;s more useful to describe Stalinist USSR or Maoist China as right-wing regimes cloaked in leftist rhetoric.')
