Browsers don&#x27;t make it easy to get ahold of a copy of the file that you can share, youtube-dl does. Add that to a bad README and that&#x27;s something that a court might treat very differently from a web browser.<p>This is not far off from how it&#x27;s legal to carry lockpicks but it&#x27;s often not legal to carry lockpicks around with the intention of using them to commit a crime.
