Item(by='GartzenDeHaes', descendants=None, kids=[25390538], score=None, time=1607702355, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25373353, text='One of the most useful programmer metrics that I&#x27;ve found is code churn: (new lines + deleted lines) &#x2F; total changed lines.  Instead of telling you how much work your programmers are doing, this metric tells you what kind of work your programmers are doing.  Small numbers mean bug fixing (end of project and maintenance) and large numbers mean new development and features.')
