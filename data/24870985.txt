Item(by='CydeWeys', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603469397, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24862806, text='One of my huge pet peeves is when sites have really idiosyncratic password requirements, like they require the use of at least 1 punctuation characters (but it&#x27;s from a limited subset of available punctuation characters), or uncommon requirements on length (I&#x27;ve seen both can&#x27;t be longer than 10 characters and must be longer than 12).<p>And yet, none of these requirements are visible on the login page!  So I have no freaking clue what my password might actually be, and thus my typical login flow for these lesser used accounts is always going through the password reset flow.  It&#x27;s a joke.')
