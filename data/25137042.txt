Item(by='wil421', descendants=None, kids=[25137085], score=None, time=1605709444, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25136974, text='Pilots have been relying on software to fly commercial planes for ages.<p>When a pilot is given clearance to land they punch numbers into the flight computer corresponding to the airport and approach. The plane will automatically line up with the correct runway.  The glideslope will also tell the plane where it needs to be as it is descending.<p>The planes are also able to land themselves. Pilots are there to make people feel comfortable and handle rare edge cases. They will be monitoring the autopilot at all times.')
