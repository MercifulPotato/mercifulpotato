Item(by='com2kid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610141723, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25686318, text='&gt; Many vegetables and some fruits can still be consumed (though not necessarily much fruit) and maintain a low-carb diet.<p>The other thing people don&#x27;t always realize is that the longer you&#x27;ve been on a low carb diet, the more carbs you can eat and stay ketosis.<p>After 6 months or so, even an entire medium sized apple (19g of sugar) isn&#x27;t likely to be a problem.<p>This is even more true for people who work out, heck 40g of sugar from fruit followed by 1 hour of high intensity workout, probably not an issue.<p>Starting out strict is needed to get on the keto train, but, but a low sugar ice cream with 10g a serving twice a week isn&#x27;t going to kick most people out of Ketosis if they&#x27;ve been there for awhile.')
