Item(by='mdorazio', descendants=None, kids=[25000180, 24999922], score=None, time=1604592030, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24998846, text='Be careful not to confuse consumer hardware with business hardware.  There are plenty of business hardware use cases where customers are definitely willing to pay money for something that amounts to a few hundred dollars worth of off the shelf components, custom PCBs, well-written control logic, and fanatical customer support.  You just probably don&#x27;t encounter this kind of hardware because it&#x27;s out of the public eye in manufacturing, logistics, etc. operations and getting to those customers requires knowing the target industry (or knowing people who do).')
