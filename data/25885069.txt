It would solve two problems: compensating people for building open-source software, and replacing the notion of IP for software with something more sustainable. More people should stand up and say, &quot;I made this, I want to make the world better, and I will add this to the commons that everyone can benefit from after I extract $X of value from it.&quot;<p>A social norm of requiring people to &quot;call their shot&quot; in advance feels like a powerful counter-force to greed.')
