Item(by='qsort', descendants=None, kids=[24765002], score=None, time=1602591482, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24764773, text='The difference is that we have neither the scientific knowledge nor the computational power to make an exact model of the human brain (nor we are anywhere close to either requirement, AFAIK).<p>In other words, we cannot pinpoint the exact sequence of operations and events that produce a certain behavior, while we absolutely can do that with ML models (you could in principle run machine learning models with pen and paper, obviously).<p>If you could show me a program that emulates my brain to a certain degree of precision (it&#x27;s unlikely that such a program would be a deterministic one, by the way), I would have no problem accepting that my brain is not meaningfully different from that program.')
