It&#x27;s possible to provide a ton of value on very complex frontends without doing much in the way of CSS, for example. I&#x27;ve been part of projects where the UI layer was handled in a dedicated library, and unless you wanted to be part of that team, you didn&#x27;t have to understand much more than which React components to use when.
