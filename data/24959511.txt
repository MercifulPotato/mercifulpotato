Item(by='andromeduck', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604238941, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24959290, text='I think moving to a 3-2-1 voting system instead of FPTP, IRV or Proportional would resolve most of the polarization issues in a few cycles. I think it&#x27;s the easiest way to force deliberation and moderation at the single district&#x2F;office level without conceding local representation or complexity for the voter. The key is not having a one dimensional optimal strategies or suboptimal equilibrium that result in plurality rule or tyranny of the majority.<p>I&#x27;d modify it to allow a super yes&#x2F;no with double the value per voter to force people to be a bit more selective.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;electowiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;3-2-1_voting" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;electowiki.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;3-2-1_voting</a>')
