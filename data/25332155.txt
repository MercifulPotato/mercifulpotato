Sharing that with three other vendors (think three other AMDs all competing with Intel) would catastrophically undermine their CPU business.<p>In any event it&#x27;s too late. They maybe had an chance of fending off Arm a few years ago - by the time any licensing now kicks in Arm will have established itself in the server &#x2F; PC market.<p>So the only sensible strategy for Intel is to make the most of x86 whilst they can.')
