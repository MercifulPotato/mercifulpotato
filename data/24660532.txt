Item(by='flohofwoe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601628653, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24654998, text='I think the problem boils down to the difference between a &quot;pattern-matching&quot; AI filling in details by &quot;inventing&quot; them, or a human restorator doing essentially the same.<p>A human restorator most likely has more background knowledge from other sources, while the AI might fill in completely unrelated details it&#x27;s seen in completely unrelated training material.<p>As long as there&#x27;s a disclaimer about the type of restoration that&#x27;s been used, both approaches are fine IMHO. The audience needs to understand that both are just &quot;projections&quot;. Similar to how we thought for a long time that ancient greece was &quot;marble white&quot;, when in reality it was full of colors.')
