Item(by='afiori', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603464493, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24853043, text='The second point is not about whitespace, but rather about how you can write your document out of order.<p>specifically TOML supports a syntax that allows to write documents like: (not actual syntax)<p><pre><code>    a.b=1\n    a.c=2\n    d=3\n    a.e =4\n</code></pre>\nthat would translate to {a:{b:1,c:2,e:4},d:3}.<p>So the criticism is that (in various cases) you need to read the entire document to be sure to have fully parsed one subtree of the document.')
