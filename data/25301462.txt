The point of using an executor service is not to reuse the threads. But instead to control their termination. If you read Nathaniel J. Smith&#x27;s primer [1] on structured concurrency. The ExecutorService in the examples act as the nursery. Loom is just being &quot;lazy&quot; and reusing ExecutorService for something that it wasn&#x27;t originally intended to do. Earlier versions had a specific class called FiberScope [2].
