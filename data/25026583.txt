Item(by='tikhonj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604851302, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25026201, text='Deliberate and repeated practice is a great idea, but in my experience trying to use &quot;willpower&quot; to focus is actively counter-productive. Instead, I&#x27;ve found that practicing mindfulness meditation and &quot;letting go&quot; of thoughts when I notice my mind wandering works far better.<p>I&#x27;ve been using Headspace[1] as an introduction to meditation and it only took a few weeks of regular meditation, working up from 5 minutes a day to 20, for me to feel a real difference. I didn&#x27;t know what to expect going in—I was a bit skeptical of the whole idea, to be honest—but I&#x27;m glad I stuck with it.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.headspace.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.headspace.com&#x2F;</a>')
