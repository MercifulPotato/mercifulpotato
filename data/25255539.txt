Item(by='gpderetta', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606753879, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25254809, text='The number of architectural registers are still relevant for register allocation because of course overlapping and independent  code sequences cannot share the same architectural register name. This is not very important for integer loads, but still relevant for FP where optimal scheduling requires having multiple computations in flight at the same time. In some cases 16 FP registers are not enough and Intel had to add 16 more FP registers with AVX512.')
