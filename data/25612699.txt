Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=[25612918, 25612964, 25612768], score=None, time=1609594059, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25612592, text='Variables can be varied... you can increment, decrement, do whatever you want with them. They keep track of things.<p>Pointers are used reference things allocated from the heap, and never anything else, unless you&#x27;re insane.  Pointers get \n directly handled in linked lists, trees, etc.<p>If at all possible, pointers should be avoided otherwise.<p>Values passed to a procedure can be done by value (the default in Pascal), or by reference (VAR parameters).<p>I don&#x27;t see why anyone wouldn&#x27;t copy values by default... it is the only sane way to do things.')
