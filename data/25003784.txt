Item(by='mshook', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604624080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25002926, text='Regarding your pay question, here in France it&#x27;s random people, you and me, who are election workers (I explained the process in another reply): it&#x27;s definitely not paid.<p>And the counting is done in polling stations after the voting process: just more hands and eyes that way.<p>And because it&#x27;s that distributed, it&#x27;s pretty much parallelized as much as you could. Another quality is you&#x27;d have to corrupt a fair amount of polling stations to make a substantial difference if you wanted to cheat.<p>Results are then sent to the next level (usually city hall which aggregates that and then sends that to the next level).')
