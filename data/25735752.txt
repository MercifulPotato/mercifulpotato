Item(by='jayra', descendants=1, kids=[25735758], score=1, time=1610395826, title='Key Insights from Deep Work (Cal Newport)', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='Key Ideas:<p>- Deep work means those activities performed in a state of near distraction-free concentration that pushes your cognitive capabilities to their limit. This creates new value, improves your skill, and is often hard to replicate.<p>- In contrast to deep work is Shallow work, which means tasks that do not create much new value and are easy to replicate, often being performed while one is distracted.<p>- A distraction free work session allows you to learn a new skill faster and leverage your current skills to produce more valuable work. This enables you to create more quality work by focusing on activities that matter.<p>- Newport calls deep work, “the superpower of the 21st century.”<p>The Deep Work Hypothesis:<p>- The ability to perform deep work is becoming increasingly rare at exactly the same time it is becoming increasingly valuable in our economy. As a result, the few who manage to cultivate this skill, and then make it the core of their working life, will thrive.<p>How To Deep Work:<p>1. Create routines. Habits take time and effort to stick. By creating a routine, you’re able to ritualise your deep work.<p>2. Eliminate distractions. You’ll be able to reap the full benefit of your deep work sessions.<p>3. Focus on the widely important. These are a small set of goals that maximise your deep work efforts.<p>4. Taking time off from work helps your mind to recharge and work better during deep work.<p>5. You should schedule blocks of time when the internet is allowed. The internet is full of distractions which can get in the way of deep work.<p>6. Commit yourself to a hard deadline.This artificial constraint will motivate you to deep work.<p>7. You need to meditate to sharpen your concentration skills and help you resist distractions.<p>Original Link: https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.readinshort.co&#x2F;deep-work-cal-newport-book-summary')
