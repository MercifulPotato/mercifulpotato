So for N people you have N connections carrying your traffic M, so the total bandwidth is N<i>M.<p>For torrent P2P you get clusters of peers sharing data, you don&#x27;t have to send data to every peer, the usual outcome is logarithmic, so you connect to log(N) peers for N participants with total bandwidth being log(N)</i>M.')
