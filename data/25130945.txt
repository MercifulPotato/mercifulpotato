Item(by='dathinab', descendants=None, kids=[25131879], score=None, time=1605653160, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25130644, text='Be aware that cranelift is <i>not</i> a LLVM replacement (and isn&#x27;t meant to be one).<p>It&#x27;s a fast but not-much-optimizing code gen which is complementing LLVM for the use-case where you want to fastly spit out code but don&#x27;t care much about optimizing it, beyond &quot;simple&quot;&#x2F;&quot;basic&quot; optimizations.<p>Besides that why do you think there should be  LLVM IR to cranelift coversions?
