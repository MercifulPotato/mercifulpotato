By definition, there isn&#x27;t anything readily apparent from the outside. You haven&#x27;t shown that to be absurd, you only showed that if you can see enough of the interior of a black box, it is no longer a black box.<p>You complain that the author&#x27;s logic will lead to creating complicated hidden variable theories but that&#x27;s exactly what the author is advocating.
