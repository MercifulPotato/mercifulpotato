Item(by='redis_mlc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610109746, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25679197, text='All things being equal, sure.<p>But I&#x27;ve seen older controllers that were tested well with RAID5, and not tested with other RAID levels.<p>Also, slamming in a &quot;new&quot; disk (but is it really new, or already stale?) might not work if the firmware version is different, or has a different default setting than the others, or what the controller expects. (For example, a different timeout or retry setting.)<p>You may also want to develop internal processes like reducing traffic to the rebuilding volume to avoid many of the possible rebuild problems under load.<p>I&#x27;m actually a fairly big fan of the cloud because of the above problems in managing storage. Most smaller companies are not up to the task.')
