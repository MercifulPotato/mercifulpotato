Datatypes is a general purpose framework for creating a hierarchy of parsers for file formats.<p>It can be images or sound, but also text, or anything else. E.g. here&#x27;s a small sample of datatypes from Aminet, excluding anything sound and graphics related:<p>* Datatypes for transparent decompression<p>* Datatypes for syntax highlighting<p>* Datatypes for parsing document formats (word etc.)<p>* Assorted hypertext datatypes<p>* Assorted datatypes for showing debug information and structure of various binary formats, like executables.')
