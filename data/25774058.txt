Item(by='simonh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610616149, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25772498, text='I think the dump and pump argument is more theoretical than real in a case like this. Pump and dump only works on short time horizons.<p>A bigger worry for me is stagnation. Suppose Google Docs gets &#x27;good enough&#x27; and prices out all the competition, but then Google loses interest because it&#x27;s not exciting anymore.<p>This happened with Internet Explorer. We lost years of advancement in browser technology because they dominated, wiped out the competition but then lost interest. Yes competitors came along again after a few years, but there was a significant opportunity cost to all of us.')
