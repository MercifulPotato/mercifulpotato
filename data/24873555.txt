Item(by='cbsmith', descendants=None, kids=[24874081], score=None, time=1603484208, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24873395, text='&quot;simplicity of http&#x2F;json&quot; always elicits snickering from me.<p>HTTP, by itself, is one of the more complex layer-7 protocols known to man. UTF-8 is not simple. Converting a decimal string representation of a floating point value into IEEE 754 is not simple (particularly compared to... memcpy). In a bizarre example of the tail wagging the dog, the ARM ISA has added an instruction specifically for parsing JSON...<p>There&#x27;s really not a lot of value in having different serialization formats, so the &quot;even if it doesn&#x27;t make sense&quot; requires a specific context for determining what makes sense.')
