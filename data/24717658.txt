Item(by='higerordermap', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602151697, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24715647, text='Indian definition of academic achievement is very rote learning focused. The academically very successful person is not likely to be very intelligent, they might just happen to be hard working because indian education system is mostly rote learning based.<p>In the above-average-but-not-best part of academic achievements spectrum is where most of the actually intelligent people are found. They are not as studious as their hardworking &quot;topper&quot; counterparts, but in general, actually understand the subjects and can think creatively.<p>In my experience here, I have seen lot of &quot;academically succesful&quot; people and lot of intelligent people, there are very few people who are in both of these sets.')
