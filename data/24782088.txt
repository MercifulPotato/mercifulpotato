Item(by='janellehmtam', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602712400, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24781530, text='Analogies can be helpful if they&#x27;re straightforward and accurate, and the company you&#x27;re comparing yourself to is well-known and has a $1B+ market cap (at minimum). Beware of using analogies that are either overly convoluted or inaccurate. As I suggest in this guide, you can test out whether or not the analogy works by trying it out on a couple people that know nothing about your business and asking them to explain back to you what your company does.')
