Item(by='0x1F8B', descendants=None, kids=[25156586, 25156579], score=None, time=1605835910, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25155203, text='What I would do is find a way to decouple your test running system from the lifecycle tooling. Develop a custom solution that run CI pipelines in k8s and collates results and passes them upstream to the lifecycle tooling whether it&#x27;s GitHub Actions or Jenkins or whatever. By doing this you free your pipelines from lock-in and can utilize cheap compute (DigitalOcean, GKE pre-emptibles, etc).<p>Take a look at Tekton CI&#x2F;CD for a good primitive that you can build on.')
