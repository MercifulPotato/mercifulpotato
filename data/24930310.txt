Item(by='yabones', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603978670, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24929973, text='That&#x27;s one part of it, but the real innovation in remote care is RPM devices (Remote Patient Monitoring). These can be anything from blood-glucose sensors, dialysis machines, blood pressure sensors, etc, that have an internet connection and send data live to a physician or nurse.<p>The struggle with these devices is that they&#x27;re often cheap embedded systems that never receive firmware updates, so they do present a security concern. However, they&#x27;re also immensely useful and have without a doubt saved lives.')
