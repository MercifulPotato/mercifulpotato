Item(by='dash2', descendants=None, kids=[25094883], score=None, time=1605379056, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25094010, text='I think scale might matter too. Companies are partly public goods problems. Each individual&#x27;s hard work benefits the whole company. As a result they will undersupply hard work. If the company is size N, they only share 1&#x2F;N of the total benefit they provide. So, the public goods problem bites harder when companies get big - even holding the level of competition in the market constant.')
