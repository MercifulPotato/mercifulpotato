This is also exactly the window of time where otherwise unacceptable laws will be accepted (like the AUMF after 9&#x2F;11) It&#x27;s potentially a dangerous time with long lasting effects that will be hard to claw back and we should tread lightly and carefully knowing these tendencies.<p>We still haven&#x27;t been able to spin down the military expansionism from 80 years ago after pearl harbor for instance. These systems can take on a life of their own and become really hard to undo. Careful careful.')
