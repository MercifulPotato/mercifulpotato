Item(by='snuxoll', descendants=None, kids=[24829913], score=None, time=1603133433, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24828264, text='Do you know what edition of D&amp;D you were playing? 4e was heavily focused around gridded combat and it turned many players off, as tactics and math overshadowed the role playing.<p>If you haven’t given 5e a try yet, do so - it’s substantially less crunchy that previous releases and frees combat from the tyranny of the grid if the group wishes to play it without.<p>As far as balance between combat and story, that’s all up to content. Published adventures tend to be very heavy on dungeon crawls, while many homebrew adventures lean more towards RP.')
