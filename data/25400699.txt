Some people are born marketers and are very good at persuasion, hyperbole, and other tools to sell a product&#x2F;service.<p>Others are natural consumers: they develop tastes and grow to be rabid shoppers. It&#x27;s good to sit in the middle of these two opposites, but often better to decide which extreme you want.<p>I know I&#x27;ve made the decision too late in life, when I wasted a vast portion of it trying to sell some product&#x2F;service&#x2F;idea when really I just wanted the latest Porsche!')
