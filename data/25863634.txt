Item(by='mathiasrw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611260156, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25859656, text='This is genius.<p>Data encoded in base1024 (here using the 1024 safe chars represented by emojis) gives much more efficient storage usage.<p>16 kB raw data encoded in base64<p><pre><code>    ceil(16*1024&#x2F;3)*4 = 21848 bytes long ~= 21.8kB.\n</code></pre>\n16 kB raw data encoded in base1024<p><pre><code>    ceil(16*1024&#x2F;9)*10 = 18210 bytes long ~= 18.21kB.\n</code></pre>\nSo base64 needs about 19.7% more data storage than base1024 and both can be used anywhere utf8 is supported.<p>Let the baseEmoji revolution begin...')
