Item(by='abathur', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611183067, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25851097, text='When I was working on my MFA thesis, I came to a realization that feels like it might overlap with what you&#x27;re describing.<p>It&#x27;s a little hard to put a bow on the idea (and why it isn&#x27;t trite...) at this length, but basically: I felt a connection between the experience of trying to parse some intentionally difficult&#x2F;impenetrable ~modernist texts, and the experience of trying to parse older texts where the phrasing is dated.<p>They&#x27;re too unfamiliar to parse with <i>fluent</i> ease, and the experience of fluent reading is just fundamentally different. In one case, the text is like a Rubik&#x27;s cube, and in the other it&#x27;s hard to even realize it exists <i>separately</i> from your instantaneous understanding of it.')
