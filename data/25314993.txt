We at VictoriaMetrics clearly see this - cluster version of VictoriaMetrics needs up to 2x more CPU and RAM resources comparing to a single-node version for achieving the same performance.<p>Single-node VictoriaMetrics provides almost perfect vertical scalability though. Its&#x27; performance scales almost linearly from rPI to a monster with hundreds of CPU cores and terabytes of RAM.
