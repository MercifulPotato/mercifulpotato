Many more people in the bike lanes than a few months ago. It’s getting to the point that the volume of bikes is beginning to exceed the system’s ability to rebalance itself (or be rebalanced by people whose job it is to do so); popular destination bike stations have no empty bike docks and popular source locations are nearly entirely empty.')
