Item(by='_davide_', descendants=0, kids=None, score=2, time=1606984227, title='Micro-services application on premise without Kubernetes?', item_type='story', url=None, parent=None, text='I are migrating a big application from multi-server monolith to micro-services, this software is not a SaaS but on-premise.<p>The current architecture leverage a database for each server, and many requests need to be processed by multiple nodes to create one cohesive answer, so far this architecture was fast,low-resources, but really painful and time-consuming to maintain and most important without HA.<p>Installations scales from hundreds of servers to a simple single server setup.\nAsking every customer to setup a kubernetes it&#x27;s not feasible, only a limited subset of customers have the skills to deploy and maintain it.<p>I&#x27;ve been searching for a simpler way for customers to deploy and maintain the infrastructure for months, seems like everyone jumped from on-premise to SaaS leaving a hole in-between.')
