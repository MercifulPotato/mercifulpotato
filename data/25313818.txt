Item(by='bloak', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607168708, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25288303, text='Where do you draw the line between &quot;content&quot; and &quot;mark-up&quot;?<p>* Word break nearly always gets treated as content, denoted by the space character.<p>* Line break can be either: &#x27;\\n&#x27; or &lt;br&gt;.<p>* Paragraph break is usually treated as mark-up: &lt;p&gt;.<p>Is there a good reason for that, or is it just historical accident?')
