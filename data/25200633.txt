Item(by='drewg123', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606238813, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25199930, text='One thing to watch out for is that FreeBSD ports&#x2F;pkg is a &quot;rolling release&quot;, where most 3rd party applications &amp; libraries are updated frequently, as opposed to every 2 years for Ubuntu.    The upshot is that you&#x27;ll want to just update firefox, and it will want to remove and re-install 57 packages.<p>I personally run the quarterly packages to avoid the churn as much as I can.  This is somewhat similar to Ubuntu, except &quot;major&quot; updates happen every 3 months.')
