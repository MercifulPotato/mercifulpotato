Item(by='harperlee', descendants=None, kids=[25447244], score=None, time=1608134772, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25443221, text='If you are thinking on doing web apps, I think Clojure is the best option, as it enables you to write clojure to target both server (either in clojure or clojurescript) and browser (with clojurescript, that compiles to javascript), and share code between those seamlessly (for example, protocols, specs, business logic, etc. that you may need in both sides).<p>That&#x27;s the most extended option by far, but I&#x27;m sure that you will find other lisps that compile to javascript (not that I know them).')
