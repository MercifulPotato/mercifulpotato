Item(by='kamaal', descendants=None, kids=[24710382], score=None, time=1602086377, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24709146, text='This is a nice use case to study how human behavior works when there&#x27;s no quotas&#x2F;limits.<p>I often say that unlimited calls means people talk less, not more. On the similar lines offering unlimited learning and self improvement budgets to your employees means people will likely spend them less, but more relevant training and development would happen.<p>A very similar argument can be made about unlimited sick leaves too.<p>When something is free people don&#x27;t feel the need to rush and fill the quotas&#x2F;limits. They use&#x2F;spend per relevant needs and scenarios now that they know they always have an option to use the thing when they need it.')
