Someone important to your beloved tries to convince your beloved of X.<p>2. Because <i>trust</i> extends in <i>both</i> directions between your beloved and you, your beloved tells you, &quot;Someone important to me said to do X.&quot;<p>3. Because your beloved has <i>agency</i>, they follow that statement with something like, &quot;I told them that this is what I think of X.&quot;<p>4. A conversation among two mature adults ensues, with the full range of human emotions in play.<p>You can do high-tech problem solving with 20&#x2F;20 hindsight until you drive yourself crazy. But if you didn&#x27;t have two-way trust, <i>or</i> at least one party didn&#x27;t realize have adult-level agency, it wasn&#x27;t a healthy relationship and you should move on to one that is')
