Item(by='cm2187', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603528460, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24875008, text='You can hint the video player with metadata.<p>To make the first subtitle track default:<p><pre><code>  -disposition:s:0 default\n</code></pre>\nOr you can make it forced:<p><pre><code>  -disposition:s:0 forced\n</code></pre>\nAnd to clear the disposition of the second subtitle track (if it was default in the source stream)<p><pre><code>  -disposition:s:1 \n</code></pre>\nThe problem is that video players are inconsistent in term of how they honour these parameters.')
