So from there - their role pretty much boils down to:<p>&quot;OK, so you&#x27;ve decided to start pushing this person out of the company&#x27;s bowels.  Let&#x27;s make sure we do with with proper legal CYA and minimal side effects, otherwise.  In fact we have system in place already - it&#x27;s called PIP:&quot;<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Performance_improvement" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Performance_improvement</a><p>&quot;Ya see, you just gotta make up some quote-unquote &#x27;goals&#x27; for the problem employee to &#x27;agree to&#x27;.
