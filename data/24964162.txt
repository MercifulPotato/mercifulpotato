Item(by='chrisfinazzo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604281022, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24963407, text='To continue the analogies, think of silverware and related instruments.<p>Notepad and TextEdit are like plastic utensils. They do the job (albeit not very well) and you can&#x27;t really mess up.<p>Word, Pages and WordPerfect are like regular forks and knives. Not too much harder to use although they do require a bit more discipline.<p>TeX (and its derivatives&#x2F;predecessors - Troff, Eqn, Grap) are like scalpels. Extremely powerful, but if you mess up, it can be hard to recover.<p>This doesn&#x27;t even get into the fact that the last category must be compiled into a document, and making sure that everything gets into the funnel can be a real challenge for the uninitiated.')
