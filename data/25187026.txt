The larger and more diverse a network is, the more likely a statement is to offend someone.<p>Many of the social issues we see are caused by the overton window narrowing so quickly and fiercely that it&#x27;s causing backlash. I&#x27;d bet that by slowing this restriction, we&#x27;d reduce the chance of social and governmental conflicts arising from swift social change. Banning facebook would do this.')
