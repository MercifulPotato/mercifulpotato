Item(by='realusername', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605895093, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25162567, text='&gt;  How do you check the security of _any_ device (e.g. Android, laptop, TV, watch, etc. basically anything that contains software that communicates with external servers) that you purchase?<p>I generally try first to buy hardware I can install myself the OS, that removes the temptation of the manufacturer to use the product as a data gathering tool.<p>&gt; Even with open source projects, it&#x27;d be a Herculean task to confirm that your purchased devices actually contain the same source code that&#x27;s published (e.g. imagine trying to confirm that the firmware on your wifi card is actually what it says it is).<p>When it&#x27;s open source, there are always more eyeballs on the system, it&#x27;s not perfect but it&#x27;s always a better guarantee of security and privacy.')
