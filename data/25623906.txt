It was hard to find out what to do with it, but finally found out that SF will take styrofoam waste for free and compact it with a densifier.  Their site says &quot;Our densifier converts Styrofoam into ingots, which can be re-manufactured into door and crown moldings, picture frames, and side and deck board.&quot;')
