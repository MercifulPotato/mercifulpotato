Item(by='Igelau', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608662459, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25509349, text='&quot;Duck tape&quot; originally referred to tape made from duck cloth. They started using it for duct work, and began to call it &quot;duct tape&quot;, to the point where &quot;duck&quot; fell out of common use and was able to be trademarked. They&#x27;ve also stopped using it for ducts.<p>So call it either one and people will know what you&#x27;re talking about.')
