(ie, there must have been a cover-up!)<p>This is definitely true, but another aspect which doesn&#x27;t get enough attention is an argument which relies totally on psychoanalysis. &quot;Group X would <i>surely</i> want to do x, and therefore they probably did!&quot;<p>The truth is that you generally don&#x27;t understand people&#x27;s incentives as well as you might guess.
