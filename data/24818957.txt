I’m not convinced we’ve reached consensus that we all need to be wearing masks but most are obliging.<p>The key factor here is that there are very good and obvious reasons to wear a mask, while the trade-off is a minor inconvenience. It&#x27;s arguably less effort than shovelling snow from the front of your house.<p>If the vast majority of us are obliging due to the hope that it has an effect, with little to no negative effects, why are people making this a hill they want to die on? Worse still, why are people making this a hill for others to die on?')
