Item(by='dmos62', descendants=None, kids=[25465685, 25467088], score=None, time=1608285083, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25465024, text='I&#x27;d interpret Bukowski&#x27;s famous &quot;don&#x27;t try&quot; not as &quot;don&#x27;t do what you don&#x27;t want to&quot;, but rather as &quot;don&#x27;t foster conflicts&quot;. To try is to do something when you half-believe that you can do it and half-believe that you can&#x27;t. It&#x27;s half-commitment. Bukowski is instructing, I believe (wink wink), to drop all that believing and just do things, without mental baggage.')
