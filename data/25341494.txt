They are more likely to work outside.<p>Poor communities are almost always on the east side of town because (in the Northern Hemisphere) prevailing west winds usually blow the town&#x27;s pollution east. This is also how the &quot;right&quot; and &quot;wrong&quot; side of the tracks came about.<p>It bears similarities to heat waves, which mainly kill the poor. You never see headlines about how the last heat wave killed twenty-three investment bankers in their penthouses.')
