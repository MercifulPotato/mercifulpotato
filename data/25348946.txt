What is it worth being able to text my wife while riding my bike?<p>Once you&#x27;ve dialed in what all these performance metrics are worth, we can compare it to other headphones on a level playing field.<p>What is entirely <i>trite</i> is people assuming that they are smarter&#x2F; better&#x2F; more knowledgeable about a product than the people who actually buy and use it.
