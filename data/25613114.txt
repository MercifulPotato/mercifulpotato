Item(by='jbotz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609598823, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25611609, text='I really don&#x27;t buy Pulumi&#x27;s primary selling point: That using a &quot;familiar (general-purpose programming) language&quot; for cloud configuration makes us more productive.<p>I&#x27;m not a huge fan of Hashicorp&#x27;s HCL---I&#x27;d prefer something more flexible and powerful like Jsonnet or Dhall---but I&#x27;m a believer in DSLs in general and that the right DSL is the best solution here.  Cloud configuration is really a very different problem domain from general-purpose programming and it seems to me pretty self-evident that a well-designed DSL can make us more productive in this space and produce more readable configurations than a general-purpose programming language.')
