Item(by='swagasaurus-rex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603927824, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24924997, text='Most of these SPAs you speak about have loading spinners, grayed-out placeholders, all sorts of tricks to make it feel like you&#x27;re in control while the fetch() roundtrip to populate the page data completes. The borders of your screen may not change, but the page filling with elements after a delay can be just as jarring.<p>If a non-SPA page loads near instantaneously because the client-side rendering isn&#x27;t bloated, it can feel just as good as an SPA with spinners.<p>You can also use tools like turbolinks the author recommended, to turn your non-SPA into an SPA.')
