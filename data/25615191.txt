Item(by='runlevel1', descendants=None, kids=[25615376, 25616078], score=None, time=1609613013, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25614881, text='In the case of Confluence specifically, it makes the cost of experimentation a lot steeper.<p>Confluence&#x27;s WYSIWYG editor will often make changes that can&#x27;t be reversed with &quot;undo&quot; -- especially those involving indentation. Copy-paste frequently screws up its formatting as well.<p>So if you don&#x27;t want to risk losing lots of work, you have to make many smaller changes. With each change taking a few seconds, it adds up quickly.<p>If it was markdown or some extended set of it, that wouldn&#x27;t be a problem.')
