Using a shared database for many different functions&#x2F;applications can be a disaster that eventually seizes up. Everyone is afraid to change the data structures because they don&#x27;t know what it will break. It&#x27;s a huge piece of global state with no isolation. Enter encapsulation and service orientation and bounded contexts.<p>So the parent is correct. It&#x27;s not enough to say data orientation is a good thing. It needs to be compared with previous approaches (encapsulation) and then explain what the tradeoffs are.')
