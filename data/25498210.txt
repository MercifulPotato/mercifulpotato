You are warned not to use the conflicting parts in code meant to be portable.<p>strstream has not been removed, yet, because spanstream is not in yet. Until that is in, there are certain things <i>only</i> strstream can do: specifically, attach to an existing buffer you need to parse things out of, or to format things into.')
