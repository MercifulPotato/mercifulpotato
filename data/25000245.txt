That&#x27;s ok. The bigger problem is that world wide two-sided marketplaces do not start as world wide two-sided marketplaces. Instead they grow from local marketplaces.<p>To grow, a two sided marketplace needs to be &quot;liquid.&quot; This means a high probability people looking for someone to do work will find someone <i>and</i> a high probability that people seeking to do work will find buyers.<p>My advice, build the project for the place where you live. Learn how to find workers. Learn how to find people needing workers. Learn how to connect them.<p>Then add the next town over. When that works, all the towns in the region. And so on. Good luck.')
