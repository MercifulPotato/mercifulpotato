Item(by='LocalH', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604286250, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24951706, text='A file copy of the contents is not a preservation of the original media in its entirety. There might not even be any &quot;files&quot; to copy, depending on the platform (many Amiga games would bypass having a filesystem entirely and access data in a raw, direct manner).<p>Working with disk images allows recovering &quot;deleted&quot; contents, or otherwise recovering data from &quot;slack space&quot; (say, if a file was edited and shortened, the data after the new end of the file in the final sector is likely still present).')
