Delayed gratification and everything.<p>So a more realistic scenario is that first she won&#x27;t study in a library because she doesn&#x27;t have a chair. Then, having a loan on the chair she won&#x27;t study because the old phone is too slow. Then, having a loan on both chair and the new iPhone, she won&#x27;t study it because it&#x27;s just too hard and going to the pub with the friends is more fun. So she will end up with another loan for a liberal arts degree, and will then wonder how to pay it off from a Starbucks salary.<p>Sorry, like it or not, people appreciate hard-earned things more than something that comes seemingly for free.
