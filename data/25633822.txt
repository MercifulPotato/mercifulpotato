Item(by='masklinn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609778732, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25633159, text='&gt; at least at the end of the day gold has actual uses<p>TBF gold doesn&#x27;t really have <i>uses</i> outside of industrial societies.<p>Sure it&#x27;s shiny so you can make pretty out of it (jewelry and other decorations), but for the most part it has <i>properties</i>: it&#x27;s ridiculously stable, it&#x27;s easy to work with, and it&#x27;s rare enough that the supply is rather limited but not so rare that it&#x27;s essentially nonexistent (unlike the platinum-group metals).<p>In economies which are static or very slow to grow (aka preindustrial) this makes it a convenient store of value, because it&#x27;s a slowly but ever-increasing (to the approximation of your supply sinking with a ship) supply.')
