Item(by='tunesmith', descendants=None, kids=[25808432, 25807159], score=None, time=1610834609, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25805112, text='In scala-land, a lot of people like to scoff at Akka because they prefer other pure fp concepts, but I don&#x27;t think they&#x27;ve found a replacement for Akka Cluster - where you need objects that have both state and behavior, meaning they need to exist in memory, and where there are too many to exist on one server.<p>If you don&#x27;t need behavior, you can use things like distributed databases or caches, and if you don&#x27;t need to scale out, there are other pure fp solutions. But for this kind of distributed behavior, it still seems to me that Akka Cluster is the killer app.')
