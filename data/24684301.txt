Item(by='umvi', descendants=None, kids=[24685492, 24687274], score=None, time=1601868418, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24683896, text='It&#x27;s generally something like:<p>1. Configure webserver (apache, lighttpd, etc) to forward certain routes to a particular unix socket (i.e. `&#x2F;tmp&#x2F;fcgi.sock`)<p>2. Create and launch a C&#x2F;C++ process that links in libfcgi.so (or similar) and has an event loop reading from the same socket (i.e. `&#x2F;tmp&#x2F;fcgi.sock`)<p>After that the webserver will forward incoming requests that match to the C&#x2F;C++ process over socket.')
