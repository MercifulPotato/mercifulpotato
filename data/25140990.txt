Item(by='gowld', descendants=None, kids=[25141892], score=None, time=1605726585, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25138675, text='A better alternative to &quot;hamburger&quot; feedback is:<p>1. Express appreciation for effort already investment, to show that the effort was well-spent.<p>2. Critique the work.<p>3. Express encouragement for future effort, to show that the recipient can solve these problems and succeed.<p>Spamming 3 unrelated forced comments in a weird order just confused the recipient, making them not understand which of the comments is more important.<p>That&#x27;s only superficially like &quot;positive comment, negative comment, positive comment&quot;, but below the surface similarity is where the important difference is.')
