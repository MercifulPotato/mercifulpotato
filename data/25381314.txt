Item(by='antisthenes', descendants=None, kids=[25381455], score=None, time=1607651392, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25381075, text='People always underestimate how hard indoor air quality is.<p>Vast majority only concern themselves with the temperature, whereas you want to at least consider CO2 levels, humidity and particle quantity (dustiness).<p>You also want to consider the direction in which the air moves when it gets returned to the HVAC unit, and where it comes back in later, and the level of filtration vs air flow.<p>And you might have to retrofit all of that into an old shoddy building.')
