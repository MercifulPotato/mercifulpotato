I think much of the benefit of volunteering etc. as spoke about in the article is from the feeling of &quot;connectedness&quot;. It&#x27;s not that being generous is in-and-of-itself beneficial, but that it helps connect to other humans. From the article:<p>&quot;Humans are extremely social, we have better health when we are interconnected, and part of being interconnected is giving&quot;<p>Being generous and resenting it or moving away from people would probably be antithetical to being connected. Being taken advantage of makes one feel <i>less</i> connected, which might be what you&#x27;re experiencing.<p>For a good, short read related to this I&#x27;d suggest Sebastian Junger&#x27;s book <i>Tribe</i>')
