Item(by='danfang', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611456495, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25888350, text='The reality is that companies at an early stage don&#x27;t have validation of their idea yet. That is priority #1. The scaling part comes only after you know your idea has merit. It&#x27;s also &quot;the easy part&quot;. With the right allocation of resources and talent, most scaling challenges can be solved. The same cannot be said of creating valuable &#x2F; interesting products.<p>The right approach is to optimize for speed and flexibility. Make it as easy as possible to validate your idea. Make it easy to tear down and rebuild in a &quot;scalable&quot; way if you&#x27;re lucky enough to make it past step #1.')
