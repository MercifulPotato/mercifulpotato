Item(by='shard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602556165, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24761569, text='I think it goes beyond the relationship, as a marriage is a commingling of finances and legal rights. A marriage is a common way to achieve pooling of resources and division of labor, with a long-term outlook for the benefit of all parties. Not the most romantic way of looking at it, but this must be taken into account as unaligned goals for the above are the most common reasons that marriages fail.')
