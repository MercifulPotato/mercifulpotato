Item(by='vnorilo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608366462, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25464354, text='This paper is worth reading; an implementation detail that may be useful to many is their use of reference counting: negative refcount is used to indicate thread-shared objects. Releasing objects goes fast path with a single branch when refcount &gt; 1, performing a non-atomic decrement. The slow path handles freeing memory, atomic counter mutation, and a sticky object flag encoded as overflow-by-one.')
