Item(by='foepys', descendants=None, kids=[25404349], score=None, time=1607833537, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403752, text='There are some smart home devices that use DECT Ultra Low Energy to communicate with their base station. It&#x27;s a pretty cool tech because DECT uses its own frequency band and doesn&#x27;t interfere with bluetooth or wifi in the often highly congested 2.4 and 5 GHz bands. Even firmware updates are possible because the bandwidth is large enough to push some megabytes through.')
