Item(by='dafoex', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610153852, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25690873, text='I&#x27;ve had strangely a lot of experience with stickers and sticky tape, and the material the sticker is made of can aid removal even if the glue is sturdy: paper stickers tear, but thicker plastic stickers will hold together as you force them away from the glue.  Alternatively, the glue could be water soluble, or perhaps soften with heat.')
