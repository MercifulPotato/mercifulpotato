Item(by='souprock', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602752429, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24761793, text='That stuff is minor. For about $10,000 you can change it out, and you certainly will over the years. Budget about $1000 per year for a normal system, with replacement every 10 years.<p>The serious concern is ductwork. Replacement means ripping open walls, unless you go for the weird industrial look. Fixing your walls is not cheap.<p>Normal ductwork grows a thick layer of toxic black mold. Normal ductwork is impossible to scrub clean.<p>Good ductwork has a hard and perfectly smooth inner surface. It can withstand vigorous scrubbing. It is made of bare uncoated metal that is corrosion-resistant and mold-suppressing. Nickel and copper are great.')
