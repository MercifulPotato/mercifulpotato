Not sure the extent that has been tested in court, though. Regulations on exporting encryption still exist in the US to some degree, though <i>Bernstein v. United States</i> was a turning point in that regard (holding that publishing encryption algorithm source code, including electronically, cannot be restricted by the Government).')
