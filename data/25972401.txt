Item(by='zlynx', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612036668, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25971923, text='That is your default Linux configuration. The distros tend to err on the side of functional systems but higher power use.<p>Start tweaking things like the stuff found in powertop. If your hardware is pretty standard Intel stuff you can get very low. For example, my Dell laptop will idle at 0.5 W with the display off, the SSD, USB, WiFi and PCIe bus all at idle.<p>That&#x27;s about 5 days of power on battery. About equal to what any version of Windows can do with Modern Standby settings.')
