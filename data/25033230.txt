I have no knowledge about the situation in Sweden, but in Germany there are known statistical problems like immigrants both having a higher chance of becoming suspects (&quot;Tatverdachteffekt&quot;) and crimes where an immigrant is suspected are more likely to be reported (&quot;Anzeigeeffekt&quot;).<p>Stastistics about <i>suspects</i> are most commonly used  in studies like these because the police, due to the seperation of powers, usually has no or at least less statistics of the actual results of charges.')
