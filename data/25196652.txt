Item(by='Gravityloss', descendants=None, kids=[25196823, 25196715, 25197079], score=None, time=1606208818, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25196132, text='The whole &quot;liters per 100 km&quot; or &quot;g CO2 per km&quot; approach doesn&#x27;t work with plugin hybrids because it depends much more on the use case than with regular ICE cars.<p>Someone can commute completely with electric power and do a few longer trips per year, getting the average emissions way way down.<p>And someone else who can&#x27;t charge at home or at work and who drives long distances daily might be using just as much gasoline as a regular car.<p>What are some good metrics instead of these emissions? Pure electric range? Charging speed? Required charging frequency with some typical usage profiles?')
