We care about proving the efficacy of our approaches and constantly improving.<p>We&#x27;ve been putting in a lot of effort over the past few years specifically to help teachers help their students achieve mastery in their subjects. One piece of that has been standards alignment for our content. I don&#x27;t know about the UK, but I do know that our content has been aligned with the US Common Core[3].<p>Since Khan is a non-profit, we&#x27;re often best known for offering all of our content for free so that anyone can learn (and our mission _is_ a &quot;free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere&quot;), but we know that a lot of learning happens alongside teachers in classrooms and are providing tools to help with that.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.khanacademy.org&#x2F;hc&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;articles&#x2F;360030753412-Why-Mastery-Learning-by-Sal-Khan" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.khanacademy.org&#x2F;hc&#x2F;en-us&#x2F;articles&#x2F;3600307534...</a><p>[2]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.khanacademy.org&#x2F;khan-for-educators&#x2F;k4e-us-demo&#x2F;xb78db74671c953a7:khan-for-educators&#x2F;xb78db74671c953a7:introduction-to-mastery-learning&#x2F;a&#x2F;introducing-mastery-learning-to-students" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.khanacademy.org&#x2F;khan-for-educators&#x2F;k4e-us-demo&#x2F;x...</a><p>[3]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.khanacademy.org&#x2F;commoncore" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.khanacademy.org&#x2F;commoncore</a>')
