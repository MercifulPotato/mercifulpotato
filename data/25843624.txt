AI systems are currently being used for bail eligibility, likelihood of recurrence, mortgage approval, and hiring. These are things that literally change people&#x27;s lives on an individual level, and the systems being used are currently indecipherable black boxes that return a result with no possibility of meaningful appeal.<p>There&#x27;s a sense of starting small - redlining is illegal. No company wants to redline - it cuts into their profits and dings their compliance scores. So they&#x27;re willing to work with us. Once we get that right, then, maybe we can start dealing with the cases where there aren&#x27;t millions of dollars of funding assisting with making the tech more ethical -- and then, cases where it may actually be working against the money.')
