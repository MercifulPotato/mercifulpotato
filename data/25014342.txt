Item(by='Thorentis', descendants=None, kids=[25015084, 25014478, 25014483], score=None, time=1604746075, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25014199, text='I think heirarchy is a natural human inclination. It&#x27;s observed in every part of the animal kingdom. But it is possible to simultaneously have hierarchy <i>and</i> autonomy at the lowest level.
