Item(by='leetcrew', descendants=None, kids=[24790388], score=None, time=1602778394, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24789999, text='I&#x27;m not saying you shouldn&#x27;t share anything with anyone, but there are some things where the upside of sharing isn&#x27;t worth the potential downside. when dealing with someone who is responsible for evaluating your job performance, it&#x27;s unwise to volunteer reasons for them to doubt that. it&#x27;s hard to judge productivity, especially for engineers. if you go through a rough patch but say nothing, people might never notice that you were less productive for a month or two. if you tell your boss you&#x27;re having an issue with alcohol&#x2F;drugs, they might perceive a drop in productivity that didn&#x27;t even exist! unless your issue is so severe that it can&#x27;t possibly escape notice, and&#x2F;or you have a good plan to fix it very soon, there&#x27;s just no reason to share this kind of stuff.<p>also, I would say work is quite a bit different than a gym. it&#x27;s a lot easier to find a new gym than a new job, and it&#x27;s much less disruptive to your life if you have to do so.')
