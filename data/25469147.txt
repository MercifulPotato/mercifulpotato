They pack down really small and are extremely visible. Similarly little IR and retroreflective glint squares are handy for spotting people at night.<p>That said if you&#x27;re doing anything serious away from people carrying a GPS and EPIRB&#x2F;SPOT is way more effective. The latter working even in the most remote locations. It&#x27;s no good being able to signal if no one is even looking for you.')
