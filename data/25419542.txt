Item(by='eb0la', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607963926, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25417965, text='Yes and no: Lambdas (and cloud functions) are to the cloud what Triggers are to Databases.<p>They&#x27;re very useful to react to events; but they also impose a lock-in.<p>For (very) light usage I feel they&#x27;re ok. But this is an opinionated feeling.<p>If you want to process a lot of stuff, you need one (or several) instances.<p>If you take a close look at other &#x27;serverless&#x27; offerings like KNative in K8s. They do something similar to launching daemon processes <i>but</i> they do launch pods and containers. If you already have K8s, it could be very useful. If not, use a message queue and instances.')
