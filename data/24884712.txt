Item(by='yaantc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603614401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24882936, text='In Ubuntu and Debian the dependency from pandoc to texlive is of the &quot;suggests&quot; type, not &quot;required&quot;. So you do not have to install texlive to use pandoc. You may use an interactive front-end like aptitude and simply deselect all the suggested dependencies you don&#x27;t care about (or configure aptitude not to install suggested deps by default).')
