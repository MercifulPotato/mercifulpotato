Item(by='jes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611258378, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25861998, text='We need effective regulation of these platforms.<p>The regulation must be forward-looking. Trying to respond after a social media company has harmed the public, say by hosting illegal content, is not good enough. Social media companies cannot be allowed to say &quot;Oh, sorry Congress, we made a mistake. We have now fixed it and it won&#x27;t happen again.&quot;<p>It might be that we need an agency like the FCC to regulate the internal operations of these companies and bring about true shared decision making.<p>Attempts to exclude regulatory authorities from meetings should result in criminal charges and prison time for repeat offenders.')
