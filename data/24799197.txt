Item(by='CallMeJim', descendants=None, kids=[24799305], score=None, time=1602841621, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24799021, text='Convenience&#x2F;security tradeoff.<p>If you only care about convenience, you shouldn&#x27;t be self-hosting - what if your server goes down? Much better to abstract this away and let someone else take care of it.<p>If you only care about security, you don&#x27;t want anyone else&#x27;s prying eyes anywhere near your recursively-encrypted secrets; self-host on a server that is airgapped from any publicly accessible networks.<p>Which of these do you care about more?')
