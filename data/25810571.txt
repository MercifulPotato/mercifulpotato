Item(by='chadash', descendants=None, kids=[25810777, 25810599], score=None, time=1610891127, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25809852, text='A piece of advice I once got from an old boss and very successful entrepreneur was to focus on increasing revenue, not on decreasing costs. I said don’t both ways increase profits? He responded that your costs could only go down 1x but your revenue can go up almost infinitely.<p>Not to say that frivolity is good and frugality is always bad. But it shouldn’t be an obsession.<p>As an employee I would much rather work for a company with soaring revenues than one with quickly decreasing costs :)')
