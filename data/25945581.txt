Item(by='BitwiseFool', descendants=None, kids=[25948712], score=None, time=1611856018, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25945428, text='Consider searching .edu sites for syllabi - every syllabus I have ever received had the required textbooks on it.<p>You could also try searching for ISBN&#x27;s on university domains, but I imagine this will be less consistent.<p>My university partnered with Barnes and Noble and published a list of required books per course as a bundle option. If a course catalog is available you can further refine your search based on course numbers (like FIN 4401 or CSE 4096, etc). Look for any partnerships a university has with a bookseller.<p>Those are some thoughts... good luck!')
