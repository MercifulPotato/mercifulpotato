Item(by='Bakary', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610639076, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25775006, text='War kills off the healthy, the young, the brave, the stress-resistant and so on. All of these are qualities that can make society better in a context of peace, but are a liability in the context of war from the perspective of the individual.
