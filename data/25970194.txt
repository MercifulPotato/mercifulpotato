Item(by='DennisP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612023111, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25956128, text='When I was in high school, I wrote lots of stuff in BASICA and spent long hours with printouts spread over the floor, tracing through GOTOs to figure out what was going wrong. I&#x27;d seen articles about &quot;structured programming&quot; and felt it would stifle my creativity.<p>One dad Dad brought home Turbo Pascal. To try it out I implemented Conway&#x27;s Game of Life. Once I got past my syntax errors, I was shocked when it worked correctly the very first time. I never wrote another line of BASICA.')
