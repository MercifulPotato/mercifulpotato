Item(by='bleepblorp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603854583, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24912385, text='The US (and Canada) have much weaker consumer protections for debit than credit.<p>If a merchant posts a fraudulent transaction against a credit card, you&#x27;ll most likely get your money back. If a merchant posts a fraudulent transaction against a debit card, you might not get your money back.<p><i>More dangerously, if your debit card credentials are skimmed and used to withdraw money from your account by any means other than the retail debit-card payment system, you won&#x27;t get your money back.</i>')
