Why is it necessary to pontificate about everything?<p>In any case, if you want a point, whenever technical people bring up the technical superiority of X protocol or tool in a discussion, I like to ask myself this question. How many people actually use the thing that someone is telling me is so much better for the world? If it&#x27;s so good, why is nobody using it for practical things? Have they used it before and there&#x27;s a decline, or is it a new up and comer and it just hasn&#x27;t caught on?<p>I think just asking the question sparks a valuable train of thought.')
