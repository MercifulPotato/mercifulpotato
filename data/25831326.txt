It won&#x27;t fit in my drill press that&#x27;s for sure.<p>Welcome to the wonderful world of jig-making. &quot;Making&quot; jigs has about the same amount of &quot;making&quot; like &quot;coding&quot; in programming. You have to invent or search for a jig which will make that task doable. I suggest clamping lightly two planks to sides of your plate, make a thick block with your desired hole, then screw that block onto planks so that your hole is centered between them. Now you have a guide for drill.')
