Item(by='robbiep', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609845018, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25644222, text='A ‘heart attack’ is an episode of myocardial ischaemia (restricted blood supply to the heart) usually through a blockage of an artery. Angina is a pain associated with myocardial ischaemia of a usually temporary nature. A cardiac arrest is the stoppage of the heart.<p>Lots of people survive the first (apart for the 250,000 Americans for whom the first presentation of heart disease is sudden cardiac death), not so many survive the latter')
