Item(by='Seirdy', descendants=None, kids=[25525422], score=None, time=1608765702, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25522353, text='Most sites should just need one low-res (32px) and one high-res (192px) icon; re-use the high-res icon as an apple-touch-icon (it&#x27;s supposed to be 180px, but 192px is close enough) and you&#x27;re good to go.<p>It&#x27;s important to remember that you&#x27;re not just targeting browsers; lots of other software needs to parse site icons. Fancy messengers (Signal, Element, Discord, iMessage, WhatsApp), bookmarking clients, search engines, social media widgets, etc. all parse and display icons, and not all of them support SVG. Some RSS readers show thumbnails; they will only use `&lt;link rel=&quot;icon&quot;&gt;`, and select the higher-res icon if it&#x27;s available.<p>Chromium DevTools has an &quot;Application&quot; pane that lets you preview your icon in the minimal safe clipping area; if it looks too crowded, you can specify an alternate maskable icon (unrelated to mask-icon) in your application&#x27;s webmanifest with `purpose: maskable`.<p>I recently gave this a spin on my own site. Relevant 80-line commit message:\n<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~seirdy&#x2F;seirdy.one&#x2F;commit&#x2F;b9a307a8c16d7d20f79764541353ed6a0035560a" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~seirdy&#x2F;seirdy.one&#x2F;commit&#x2F;b9a307a8c16d7d20...</a><p>Since that commit, I added a monochrome icon (same as mask-icon) and 512px icon (since not all programs handle svg well). Although it isn&#x27;t exactly an icon, I also added an Open Graph image for all the programs that use it.<p>My current webmanifest is generated from <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~seirdy&#x2F;seirdy.one&#x2F;tree&#x2F;master&#x2F;item&#x2F;assets&#x2F;manifest.webmanifest" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;git.sr.ht&#x2F;~seirdy&#x2F;seirdy.one&#x2F;tree&#x2F;master&#x2F;item&#x2F;assets...</a> by Hugo.')
