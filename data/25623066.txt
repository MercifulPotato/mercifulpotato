Item(by='wging', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609693809, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622919, text='Minifiers also reduce variable and function names to a minimal size, one character wherever possible. They do other things too... such as remove semicolons that are not needed (not in general possible, especially if you&#x27;ve gotten rid of line breaks, but can be done in the last statement of a block or function, for example.) The idea is to optimize for bits sent over the wire.<p>function a(){var b=&quot;<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;google.com" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;google.com</a>&quot;}<p>That&#x27;s why it&#x27;s difficult to read minified code even if it&#x27;s not obfuscated to the further extent that you sometimes see in attack payloads.')
