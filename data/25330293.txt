Item(by='diarrhea', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607329217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25330085, text='You are not having any issues because TeXLive <i>is</i> everything. The `full` scheme on `install-sh` or `texlive-full` on Debian takes up around 7 GB last time I looked, 2 to 3 of which are PDFs for package documentation.<p>That is, by a long shot, the largest install a lot of people have on their system. Nowadays, no biggie, but systems like Miktex allow to install-on-demand and of course, it allows you to shrink your installation significantly.<p>Full TexLive contains a number of packages average &quot;Western hemisphere scientific authors&quot;, arguably the largest LaTeX demographic, will never need: Eastern and other languages, music, humanities.')
