Item(by='formerly_proven', descendants=None, kids=[25623248], score=None, time=1609692205, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622564, text='RAM is not as reliable as you think. Some ECC memory hardly ever finds an error, some machines see them at a very consistent rate, e.g. 50 errors per TB-day. That would translate to 1-2 errors per day in a 32 GB PC. Without ECC you cannot know in which bucket you are.')
