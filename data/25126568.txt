Item(by='stared', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605634138, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25126208, text='I find &quot;On Children&quot; by Kahlil Gibran (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;poets.org&#x2F;poem&#x2F;children-1" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;poets.org&#x2F;poem&#x2F;children-1</a>) a wise guidance:<p>Your children are not your children.<p>They are the sons and daughters of Life&#x27;s longing for itself.<p>They come through you but not from you,<p>And though they are with you yet they belong not to you.<p>You may give them your love but not your thoughts,<p>For they have their own thoughts.<p>You may house their bodies but not their souls,<p>For their souls dwell in the house of tomorrow,<p>Which you cannot visit, not even in your dreams.<p>You may strive to be like them,<p>But seek not to make them like you.<p>For life goes not backward nor tarries with yesterday.')
