Item(by='invaliduser', descendants=None, kids=[25535252], score=None, time=1608886694, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25534778, text='Not OP, but as the name suggests, a VPN is a virtual private network: you can use it to create a private subnet from where you can securely access your other computers&#x2F;resources, even from a remote location.<p>For instance, at work we are mostly remote, and use a VPN  (OpenVPN here) to access the local network at the office with our on-premise build servers, and it also allows developers to work together sometimes (one running a debugging server on their dev laptop, another debugging the client from their own laptop as if they were sharing a local network, when actually they are hundreds of miles apart)')
