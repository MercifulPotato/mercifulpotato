Item(by='BenoitP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610824100, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803709, text='What we want could be a complexity metric corresponding to a set of lines.<p>I&#x27;d say that&#x27;s something that could be ML-learnable with the right dataset.<p>Given an answer from stackoverflow, ask an average developer to identify what was the question from a set of say 5. The average time taken to provide a right answer would be the complexity score.<p>You could use a transformer trained on code, and make it transfer-learn a function approximating that score.')
