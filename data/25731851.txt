Item(by='vharuck', descendants=None, kids=[25735122], score=None, time=1610384654, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25730527, text='We have to distinguish between those who are untouchable because of circumstances beyond their control (lineage, birthplace, disabilities) and those who are pariahs because of their behavior (criminals, militant anarchists, that mean old guy who sits on his porch with a shotgun).<p>I have sympathy for the former, but not the latter.  While it would be kind to reach out to the latter group and help them mesh with society, there&#x27;s no requirement anyone has to nor a guarantee the pariahs would want to mesh.')
