Item(by='jusssi', descendants=None, kids=[25623836, 25622090, 25621767], score=None, time=1609681001, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25621317, text='Many dev environments come in virtual machine images. On a good day they&#x27;re throwaway Vagrant builds, but sometimes there&#x27;s a significant manual setup required and you want to include them in a backup.<p>Incidentally, some backup utilities run into unexplainable performance issues trying to delta diff very large files such as VM images. Rsync in particular sometimes stalls completely when trying to sync a 30GB VM image to a remote server.')
