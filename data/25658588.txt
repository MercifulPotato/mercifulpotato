Leetcode, while being pretty useless in the real world, does get your brain into &quot;answer hard questions under pressure&quot; mode. Systems design questions prompt you to architect things which may be considerably different than you deal with during your day-to-day.<p>Preparing for an interview, for more senior developers, is mostly mental - even if you know the information required, can you retrieve it quickly? Do you get flustered under time pressure? Are you in hour 4 and getting tired&#x2F;sloppy? A bit of studying can help make some answers automatic, and help you present your best self.')
