Which doesn&#x27;t always mean 50K posts or pages, but those plus all your taxonomy pages plus all your pagination pages. A 10K post or page site can give you 50K URLs pretty easily if it uses taxonomies and pagination.<p>My case might be unique where the site is being built at the end of a pipeline that sorts through millions of rows of data and gives you the content you want based on your filters, but it&#x27;s honestly not difficult to get to 50K URLs for many sites that have some age on them.')
