When running a business, you need to think of the <i>bigger purpose</i> your potential customers try to fulfill by spending money.<p>Say you&#x27;re a high-class italian restaurant, you&#x27;ll find that you compete<p>- not only with high-class italian restaurants<p>- not only with <i>all</i> high-class restaurants in general<p>But in fact with all businesses that allow the customer to spend the same order of magnitude of money in return for the same high-class treatment, leisure, and relaxation.<p>It may sound daunting, but it in fact also means that your potential market is <i>much</i> bigger than you might initially believe.')
