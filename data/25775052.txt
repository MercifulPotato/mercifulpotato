Item(by='imtringued', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610623347, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25774635, text='It can be difficult for shareholders to earn money if there is high CPI inflation.<p>If the previous year over year ROI is 7% and inflation grows by 3% then you have to increase your ROI to 10% just to catch up with inflation. This means only the most productive companies will actually maintain their existing growth.<p>Compare that to a deflationary environment where every warm body with a big bank account can get 7% ROI without even trying or even do annoying things like employ workers.')
