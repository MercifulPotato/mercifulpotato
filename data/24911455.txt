Item(by='a1369209993', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603830371, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24908171, text='Put another way: every electric motor is also a generator and vice-versa. If you induce the motor to absorb energy from the power supply rather than emit it, that energy has to go somewhere. As usual, when not specified, &quot;somewhere&quot; tends to mean heat, sound, and inelastic deformation of anything in relatively close proximity to the excess energy.')
