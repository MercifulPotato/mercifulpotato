They said &quot;customer will be signed up for enterprise support&quot;, which we read as &quot;customer gets enterprise support for free&quot;.  Then we started getting bills for enterprise support ($1K&#x2F;month).  The contract said that, but was a bit vague; it was totally on us to ask for clarification up front.')
