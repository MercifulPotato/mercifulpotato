They are understood to be companies that operate in a very different business environment from the majority of other businesses. That is what also gives them the ability to place their equipment on other people&#x27;s private property.<p>And remember that a power company <i>can</i> cut off power to people if they are intentionally attempting to disrupt the power system, or simply not paying.')
