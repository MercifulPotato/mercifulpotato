Item(by='pembrook', descendants=None, kids=[25120421], score=None, time=1605576139, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25119159, text='All companies are &quot;tech-dependent companies.&quot; This is an intellectual dead end.<p>Traditionally, the business world has qualified tech companies as those which primarily function via zero marginal cost distribution provided by software and the internet.<p>If Airbnb and Ebay aren&#x27;t tech companies, then neither are Google and Facebook. All four are marketplaces (Google and Facebook are advertising marketplaces at their core).<p>Saas and marketplaces are the most solidly &quot;tech&quot; businesses that exist by the textbook definition. They fully take advantage of zero marginal cost distribution.')
