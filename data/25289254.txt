We&#x27;re building the features of Firebase using enterprise-grade open source tools. We&#x27;re particularly focused on scalability. We take proven tools like Postgres, and we make them as easy to use as Firebase.<p>Supabase had an Alpha launch [1] six months ago. This time we&#x27;re more stable and feature-full. For the past 2 months we&#x27;ve run benchmarks [2] and security audits and we&#x27;re confident to take on production workloads.<p>Our current feature-set includes a realtime engine [3] on top of Postgres, auto-generated APIs using PostgREST [4], and Auth with Row Level Security, OAuth, and magic links [5] .
