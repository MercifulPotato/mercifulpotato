Item(by='whiddershins', descendants=None, kids=[25290640], score=None, time=1607012709, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25290417, text='I think this is overblown. The earth is already ‘full’ of oil and gas and minerals and lots of toxic things. We are just transforming one type of substance into another - more useful to us - type of substance.<p>The issue is when the waste gets in to places where it wreaks havoc, like especially waterways.<p>Currently, afaik, this problem is primarily driven by a few countries in Asia, so I think the effort that would have the most impact is figuring out how to convince those particular Asian countries to stop throwing plastic in to rivers.<p>And anyone else who gets it in their head to throw plastic in to rivers.<p>As long as waste is contained properly it doesn’t seem to make so much of a net change in the earth.')
