Item(by='dimitar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605221411, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25075527, text='There are more factors in a realistic nuclear war:<p>- Quite a few nuclear weapons will be spent on military sites, which are often in low-population density areas.<p>- Quite a few nuclear weapons will be used to destroy other nuclear weapons.<p>- Nuclear fratricide, or nukes from the same side damaging each other in ill-coordinated explosions<p>- Air defence destroying at least some missiles and especially planes.<p>- Some bombs will be duds and fail to detonate due to bad maintenance.')
