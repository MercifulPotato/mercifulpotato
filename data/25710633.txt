Item(by='zozbot234', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610256004, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25710453, text='Agenda-pushing is quite common on Wiki, especially around controversial areas. You can read all about it on the Talk page for each article. The hope is that it all balances out in the end, but this does become harder to guarantee as high-quality, reliable sources for some points of view are getting increasingly thin and hard to find, both online and offline.  (I&#x27;m aware that Larry Sanger among others has complained about this development, but it <i>is</i> a genuinely hard problem to solve as we can&#x27;t just get rid of all sourcing standards in the service of less-represented viewpoints.)')
