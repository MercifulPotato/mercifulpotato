Item(by='acqq', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607768045, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25396609, text='No, according to the majority of experts, there&#x27;s still no sound basis for claiming that the strains have different infectiousness  and there&#x27;s even less evidence for different severity.<p>The only thing known is that we can decode the genome across the world and that the virus, as it spreads, slowly gets some small changes which could be followed.<p>There&#x27;s no sign that anything people did (especially &quot;lockdowns&quot;) up to now had any evolutionary effect on the virus.
