Item(by='mrob', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605489887, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25106615, text='&gt;1. Swap thrashing can bring the system into a completely unresponsive state.<p>This can be prevented using earlyoom (which is packaged in most distros):<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;rfjakob&#x2F;earlyoom" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;rfjakob&#x2F;earlyoom</a><p>It&#x27;s probably too difficult to fix the underlying design errors, e.g. fork() duplicating the process&#x27;s entire address space, thus requiring overcommit and copy-on-write, but losing only one process beats losing all of them. earlyoom should be enabled by default.')
