Item(by='toyg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610456889, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25745386, text='You say business is about <i>building products</i>, but it really isn&#x27;t.<p>Business is about making money. Building products is only useful insofar as it enables <i>selling</i> products, which is one way to make money (possibly one of the hardest).<p>So the question becomes, &quot;how do you position an organization to make money?&quot; - and that has a bunch of different answers depending on many factors. Some of these answers are generic cross-sectors and some are not, and recognising the difference is where the much-maligned MBA types can fall short.')
