Item(by='0xB31B1B', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603503966, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24862817, text='I do not care if you choose to live in a single family home or a unit in a multi family building. The issue that we have is that &quot;single family zoning&quot; where the maximum density allowed on a plot of land is a single house is a land use pattern that it is a government restriction that subsidizes homes for the well off at the expense of everyone else. In a world in which central city land that is currently zoned SFHs is rezoned for multi family dwellings, the price of SFHs would rise to meet the true demand, while the price of a &quot;living unit&quot; in the area would fall due to the land required per dwelling unit decreasing.')
