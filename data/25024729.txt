Item(by='steventhedev', descendants=None, kids=[25025119, 25024972, 25025015, 25025070], score=None, time=1604831630, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25024619, text='If you&#x27;re seeing retransmission errors on a wired lan, then you should replace cables and any network equipment until they go away.<p>A basic assumption of TCP is that retransmission only happens due to congestion. It fundamentally assumes a perfect channel, which is why wireless connectivity doesn&#x27;t play well with it. There have been many attempts to fix this but they require changes in both the AP and the client, so I don&#x27;t think anyone has really bothered to implement in common hardware yet.')
