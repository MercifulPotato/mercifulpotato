Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607373898, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25336974, text='HBM&#x27;s latency characteristics are about the same as any other DRAM however (including DDR4 or LPDDR4x).<p>So you have a high-bandwidth, but no better latency, L4 chip. And that&#x27;s kind of hard to deal with and optimized against.<p>Xeon Phi had a DDR4 + HMC (where HMC was a early competitor to HBM), and getting good performance out of that was unusual. DDR4 ended up having slightly better latency, so the HMC cache would hurt some workloads.')
