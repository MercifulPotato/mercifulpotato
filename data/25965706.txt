You might not like it if you are a BASH wizard, but it has a large number of modules which ease administration.    Even if you manage to spend most of your time in WSL2 and VisualStudio&#x2F;IntelliJIDEA you will still need to administer and interrogate your host OS.  Powershell is the sanest way to do that.<p>You should probably see what you can do with Chocolatey&#x2F;Scoop depending on security policy.  Package management in Windows is severely deficient compared to GNU&#x2F;Linux distros but those are possibilities.')
