Item(by='wildpeaks', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604061184, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24934928, text='Personally I&#x27;m very fond of three screens, it lets me split information by level of attention required: the center one is for what I&#x27;m focused on, left screen is additional information I might need (like documentation or console output), right screen for things I don&#x27;t really need to pay attention to (given my peripheral vision isn&#x27;t as strong on the right).<p>The positions are set in such a way that I can glance at the left without turning my head, whereas the right side requires me to switch focus &#x2F; turn my head to give it my full attention.')
