It works _perfectly_ (almost too perfectly)<p><pre><code>  Echo &quot;Keep-alive with Scroll Lock...&quot;\n\n  $WShell = New-Object -com &quot;Wscript.Shell&quot;\n\n  while ($true)\n  {\n    $WShell.sendkeys(&quot;{SCROLLLOCK}&quot;)\n    Start-Sleep -Milliseconds 300\n    $WShell.sendkeys(&quot;{SCROLLLOCK}&quot;)\n    Start-Sleep -Seconds 240\n  }</code></pre>')
