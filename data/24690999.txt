Item(by='jononor', descendants=None, kids=[24692072, 24691652], score=None, time=1601925366, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24689247, text='Always use checked pre- and post-conditions in a data pipeline.<p>This simple post-condition would have caught this issue:\nThe sheet after merge operation must have a number of rows equal to the sum of number of rows for all merged sheets.<p>Assuming this is a merge of a standardized input, then another post-condition might be:\nThe number of columns in output shall equal the number of columns in the input.\nMight want to check header names, and order as well.<p>Thinking in terms of universal properties, and putting the checks into production, is better than unit-testing.')
