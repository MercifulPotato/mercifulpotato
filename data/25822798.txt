Item(by='yarcob', descendants=None, kids=[25825604, 25823397], score=None, time=1610984543, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25822429, text='There is a huge variance with LED lights in my experience.<p>LED bulbs from Ikea are cheap garbage and die quickly.<p>220V 10W E27 Osram bulbs are pricey but reliable (but I don&#x27;t have as many of them, so maybe I was just lucky).<p>I&#x27;ve never had a lamp with non-replaceable, built-in LEDs die. Probably because the power supply is not directly next to the LED, and the LEDs are typically attached to a big heat sink.<p>The tiny GU10 LED spots are the worst and die about as quickly as the halogen spots they replaced.')
