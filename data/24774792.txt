Item(by='alelos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602668083, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24770286, text='You should look into Logitech Media Server (open source with community forks&#x2F;support).\nYou can install it on your NAS, I have it on a synology.<p>It also supports syncing music with multiple devices at a fraction, if any, of sonos or similar costs.<p>There is a plethora of plugins available including an AirPlay bridge to stream to your Apple tv.<p>You can use the excellent iPeng app (paid but recommended) as a phone remote (and even a source with an IAP).<p>I use the LMS ecosystem to stream from my NAS to my linux workstation (using squeezelite), to my tv&#x2F;speakers on an nvidia shield, to my phone via iPeng and to another chromecast.')
