Item(by='eyelidlessness', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603728112, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24896134, text='If you’re having heat issues on the 16” MBP (or, IIRC any of the USB-C models) try plugging the power in on the right side. I know it sounds crazy but it makes a difference.<p>Also, since we’re on the topic, if you get weird wake from sleep issues with external monitors, I’ve found the solution (at least for my setup) is to <i>always</i> wake the computer from the Touch ID button.<p>With all of that said, obviously these are not perfect machines. But in truth my 16” (BTO, max RAM, lowish specs on basically everything else) is the best computer I’ve ever owned.')
