Item(by='mannykannot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607523525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25357727, text='While this technique allows an attacker to avoid revealing the exploit if you simply redirect the curl output to a file, it will contain tell-tale information (in this case, bufferloads of zero bytes) allowing one to discern that it is up to no good.<p>The author hints at other techniques for detecting curl|bash (http or dns callbacks), which would obfuscate but not completely mask the attacker&#x27;s intentions.<p>Note that I&#x27;m not advocating for using curl|bash: it&#x27;s a technique for gathering low-hanging fruit, and there&#x27;s no point in putting yourself in that position.')
