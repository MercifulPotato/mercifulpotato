Item(by='hinkley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602025528, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24703293, text='I think it goes well beyond &#x27;writing too much code&#x27;.<p>If you&#x27;re coding at &gt;40wpm sustained, you&#x27;re basically vomiting out code without a care in the world.<p>I could see people getting into trouble if they can&#x27;t manage 30 wpm typing speed, which seems to disagree with what I just said, but coding often comes in little microbursts, and if it takes you too long to get an idea down, you might get distracted by something else and forget what the next step is.
