Item(by='Shicholas', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611556088, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25899532, text='Our answer to the author&#x27;s question, &quot;is there a way to unify the interface across systems?&quot; is to double-down on Postgres and in particular Foreign Data Wrappers for Elastic, Redis, and Neo4j and Postgres triggers&#x2F;functions to keep the data in sync.<p>As the author pointed out, Postgres is a battle-tested and great option, and for us it&#x27;s our company&#x27;s source of truth for all data. However, there are definitely use-cases where using elastic or neo4j to look at our data in a different light are very helpful.<p>IMO Postgres is one of the top-5 software projects of all time, I don&#x27;t feel like I&#x27;m making a bad choice by further grokking it.')
