Item(by='underdeserver', descendants=None, kids=[25009396, 25009476], score=None, time=1604682683, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25008587, text='To me tech debt is the result of callous introduction of invariants.<p>An inexperienced engineer keys your users table by their email. Suddenly a user changes their email address, and now you either refactor to use an autogenerated user ID (as you should have in the first place), or you have a huge brittle process for migrating a user between emails, or your entire codebase starts getting littered with statements looking for previous email addresses and copying data on the fly.<p>The mistake was that email=user identity is not a true invariant.')
