Item(by='codingdave', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604507520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24980710, text='I&#x27;d love to help. I&#x27;ve been writing software for the K-12 market for 8 years now. But if you are asking people to pay for the service, it isn&#x27;t a public education system, it is a private education system. And while that is interesting in its own right, it doesn&#x27;t fix the problems for the majority of students.<p>To do this as a public system, you have to get into the public funding. Which means you have to meet curriculum and testing requirements to qualify for that funding. You also need to accept that the public school system provides more than education. It provides meals to low income students. It provides time when the kids are not at home to allow parents to work. It provides clubs, sports, and social activities.<p>For a new system to succeed is disrupting public education, it needs to resolve all the resources the system offers, not just a new curriculum and teaching methods.<p>That being said, yes, I&#x27;m interested - but only if people are taking on the big picture.')
