Item(by='justin66', descendants=None, kids=[25588799, 25588669, 25587893, 25587614], score=None, time=1609369882, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25585225, text='That might be a good strategy for dealing with youtube videos but it&#x27;s not an equally good strategy for dealing with print media. The thing to remember about magazine or newspaper articles is that the author does not typically have any input on what the headline will be. A great article by a great author will quite often get a ridiculous headline from an editor, for all the bad reasons an editor might have: baiting the audience, putting their own spin on the story, or whatever.<p>You&#x27;ll miss some things if you just skip a story because of a bad headline, and I think the cited article is just another indicator that we ought to deliberately not give headlines <i>too much</i> thought.')
