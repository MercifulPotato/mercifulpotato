Item(by='dalbasal', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604943142, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25035499, text='There&#x27;s infinite nuance and meta game to referendums, but that aside...<p>(1) Referendums seem able to produce highly decisive vote counts on otherwise controversial political issues. Interesting, in itself.<p>(2) Issues that parties are at odds about aren&#x27;t necessarily 50-50 issues.<p>(3) Referendums can (sometimes) produce democratic legitimacy in ways that representative politics cannot.<p>All interesting, in terms of enabling decision making. Referendums seem to be gaining a little momentum. I think one hidden advantage is the fact that referendums structurally enforce a separation of idea and execution.')
