Obergefell v. Hodges was decided in 2015.<p>Support for gay marriage was at the 25% level nationally in ... well, per <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;web.archive.org&#x2F;web&#x2F;20050517033538&#x2F;http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.aei.org&#x2F;docLib&#x2F;20050121_HOMOSEXUALITY.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;web.archive.org&#x2F;web&#x2F;20050517033538&#x2F;http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.aei.or...</a> page 21 I would put it in the mid-1990s, 20 years before full nationwide legalization.
