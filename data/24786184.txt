Item(by='KozmoNau7', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602750479, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24785635, text='A major advantage of air tools over electrical tools is that the air flow and expansion causes cooling, which means you can run air tools harder for longer than equivalently-powered electrical tools, which can heat up dramatically when used hard.<p>Of course you have to mitigate moisture, but that is trivially solved with a moisture trap on the compressor&#x27;s outlet or at the tool if you&#x27;re using long air lines.')
