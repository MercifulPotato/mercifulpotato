Item(by='escape_goat', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603292369, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846026, text='It&#x27;s worth noting, for the sake of everyone here in the comments on a tl;dr tanget, that the organ is absolutely not new or &#x27;mysterious&#x27; in the least. There are a pair of salivary glands at the back of the nose in a place no-one thought to look at them. They were discovered because they accumulated radioactive glucose, like the other salivary glands, to an extent that was visible when a fairly new and sophisticated scanning technique was used. They revealed macroscopic structure upon examination in two cadavers.')
