Item(by='roca', descendants=None, kids=[25119527, 25116958, 25118772], score=None, time=1605558922, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25116362, text='If you insist that the difference between &quot;lockdown&quot; and &quot;no lockdown&quot; is whether the authorities back their restrictions with legal authority, it makes the term &quot;lockdown&quot; meaningless and you should stop talking about it.<p>What matters to me and most other responsible citizens is: what are the rules I need to follow? Can I have social gatherings? Can I go out for dinner? If I can&#x27;t, I&#x27;m calling that a lockdown. This is what determines the effects on the economy, mental health, personal freedom, etc. What is technically legal is irrelevant.')
