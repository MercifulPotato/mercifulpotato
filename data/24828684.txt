Item(by='monadic2', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603128834, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827965, text='Speaking from personal experience working with these reputation warehouses and with handling a good deal of incoming and outgoing email volume, they aren&#x27;t any more effective than other IP-based spam heuristics and if everyone configured their DKIM correctly these warehouses would be largely obsolete.
