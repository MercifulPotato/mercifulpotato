Item(by='whateveracct', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602779966, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24789648, text='I&#x27;ve started using NixOS as my primary OS. Lots of rough edges - both due to Linux and then NixOS on top. But the immutable nature of it has made it so easy for me to fearlessly hack around. And once I solve something, I immortalize it in code that I check into git.<p>Upgrades (both NixOS and kernel) are also fearless and trivial to roll back.<p>It&#x27;s really nice to declaratively manage stuff like NVIDIA drivers (w&#x2F;Prime), udev, printer drivers, etc. Much nicer than running commands and getting my computer in a stable state that I hopefully never mess up.')
