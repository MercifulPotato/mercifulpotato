Item(by='tschwimmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601998121, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24698587, text='VCs don’t rely too heavily on this. In fact, taking a early buy out is one of the outcomes that can be very good for a founder but terrible for a VC. VCs need outsize returns to boost the market overall portfolio, which has many zero outcomes. Getting acquired for 110% of your series A valuation doesn’t do anything for them.<p>That said, getting acquired as a strategic acquisition is a good outcome for VCs. However that’s much more rare and isn’t specific to big tech companies.')
