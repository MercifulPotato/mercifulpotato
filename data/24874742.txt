Item(by='ineedasername', descendants=None, kids=[24874783], score=None, time=1603490446, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24874567, text='At this point we have some proven designs that make things a little easier to get right because we&#x27;re not building from scratch. But a big part of the problem now is lack of expertise. We don&#x27;t have enough trained hands to build these sorts of specialized systems, or keep them running once built.<p>We need to bootstrap our way out of this problem. Appropriate education programs should be created and subsidized. The aging workforce that still exists should be paid well to put off retirement or hired out of retirement to help implement things like apprenticeships. In five years we could have a new generation of workers with an established pipeline to continue training more people.')
