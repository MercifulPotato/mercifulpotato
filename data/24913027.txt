But the qualia of red is the experience you have when that 700nm light hits your retina.<p>It&#x27;s conceivable that when 700nm light hits my retina, I have an experience which you would call &quot;blue.&quot; That&#x27;s why the qualia and the physical property of the light are two different things.')
