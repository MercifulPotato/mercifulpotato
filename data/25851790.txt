Listen to what they say, think about it and build it on next time you talk with them.  If they see you showing interest and learning about their domain, you&#x27;ll get a direct line to them.  And don&#x27;t always bring them problems, be sure to stroke their ego too by asking what they&#x27;re working on.<p>It doesn&#x27;t happen overnight, it requires perserverence and a dollop of luck. You won&#x27;t walk into a job like that, it takes years of building a reputation for yourself.')
