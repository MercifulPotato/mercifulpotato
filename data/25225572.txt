Item(by='didibus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606451780, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25224580, text='Python&#x27;s for isn&#x27;t a for-loop either, it&#x27;s a for-each loop also sometimes called a for-in loop.<p>In that sense, neither Python nor Clojure have real for-loops. But Python gets much closer, because Clojure&#x27;s doseq is similar to its while, it is actually syntax sugar for a recursive loop, and not an imperative loop. While Python&#x27;s for is an imperative for-each loop, which supports the statements break, continue and return inside it.')
