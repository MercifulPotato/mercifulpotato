Item(by='admax88q', descendants=None, kids=[25349545, 25348840], score=None, time=1607448758, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25346727, text='Pi-hole is still very much niche, and outside of that and enterprise network admins, the majority of users are stuck using the trash resolver of their operating system directed to the trash DNS servers of their ISP.<p>If DNS level blocking ever becomes popular enough, malware authors will change their systems to not use the system&#x27;s DNS, or to use hard coded DNS over TLS&#x2F;HTTP servers that they know will serve them the data they want.<p>Preventing proprietary applications from doing malicious things is a constant cat and mouse game.  Pi-hole works well for now, I would argue, because it&#x27;s not popular enough to put a big enough dent in the success rate of malware.')
