Item(by='sigjuice', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606517017, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25232945, text='By default, QEMU uses emulation which will be slow.  Qemu can also use an &quot;accelerator&quot; such as KVM.  See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.qemu.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;master&#x2F;system&#x2F;invocation.html#hxtool-0" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.qemu.org&#x2F;docs&#x2F;master&#x2F;system&#x2F;invocation.html#hxto...</a><p>e.g. I can do this on my Raspberry Pi 4 running 64-bit Raspberry Pi OS.<p><pre><code>  qemu-system-aarch64 \\\n      -M virt,accel=kvm -cpu host -m 2G -smp 2 -nographic \\\n      -bios &#x2F;usr&#x2F;share&#x2F;qemu-efi-aarch64&#x2F;QEMU_EFI.fd \\\n      -cdrom alpine-virt-3.12.0-aarch64.iso</code></pre>')
