Item(by='jesuscyborg', descendants=None, kids=[25129224], score=None, time=1605642739, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25127509, text='Use wcspbrk.<p>UTF-8 continuation characters are limited to the range \\200 through \\300 so there&#x27;s basically zero chance that if you choose something like comma as your delimiter that it&#x27;s going to tokenize the middle of a multibyte sequence.<p>Also take into consideration that, under the hood, functions like strpbrk() are typically accelerated by CPU instructions such as PCMPISTRI which doesn&#x27;t support UTF-8 natively but it does support UCS-2.')
