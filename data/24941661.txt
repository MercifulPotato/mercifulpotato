The incentive is not permissive licenses. The incentive structure confess from the IP regime itself. People treat patents, trade secrets and so on as things of value in their own right. Copyleft could force the publication of code that companies want to otherwise keep private. So they are incentivized to use permissive licenses to avoid that scenario.')
