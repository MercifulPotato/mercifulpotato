Item(by='saalweachter', descendants=None, kids=[24814221], score=None, time=1602954377, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24811020, text='Some of my favorite test queries for shopping sites (from back when I used to work on a comparison shopping engine:<p>[apples to apples] -&gt; frequently returns random &quot;Apple&quot; products, because when you dedupe, stem and remove stopwords from that query, you&#x27;re left with &quot;apple&quot;, and Apple products are big ticket items.<p>[shirt dress] -&gt; frequently returns dress shirts, because many sites essentially ignore word order or the notion of compound words.<p>[fish swim vest] -&gt; frequently returns fishing vests, partially because of overstemming (many POS stemmers will stem &quot;fishing&quot; -&gt; &quot;fish&quot;, but in a product search setting, it&#x27;s usually the wrong thing to do).')
