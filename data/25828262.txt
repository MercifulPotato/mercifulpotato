Item(by='contravariant', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611014413, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25826641, text='If you stick to multiplication and addition then the beauty of modular arithmetic ensures that over and underflow are only a problem if your final result under&#x2F;overflows.<p>In particular you can solve the problem in this article by just subtracting the numbers from 1+2+...+n-1+n. The remainder will be your missing number (though be careful with the 1+2+...+n-1+n = n(n-1)&#x2F;2 formula; division by 2 is very much <i>not</i> compatible with arithmetic modulo a power of 2, so divide first <i>then</i> multiply, or keep a running total if you want to be flexible).')
