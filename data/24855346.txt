Item(by='lmm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603346846, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24854951, text='If the government auctions off spectrum at a flat price then it gets certainty, which normally comes at a premium; doing revenue share the government retains more of the risk, so orthodox economics would say that it will get a better price on average.<p>There&#x27;s also an argument that the government doesn&#x27;t actually realise any advantage from selling off the risk: if the telecom companies fail, it&#x27;s still the government&#x27;s problem. So the government ends up paying extra for something it can&#x27;t actually use.')
