Item(by='tadfisher', descendants=None, kids=[25737696], score=None, time=1610396527, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25735617, text='If they don&#x27;t use it, the kernel does in order to initialize the entropy pool, which will also result in broken boots. To work around historically flaky kernel implementations, systemd calls RDRAND directly if it detects it needs to with a heuristic. This is why the fix for the current issue is to set both &#x27;nordrand&#x27; and &#x27;SYSTEMD_RDRAND=0&#x27;.')
