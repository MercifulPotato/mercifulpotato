Item(by='eecc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606553155, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25232989, text='It’s maybe something about having tools that are intrinsically more powerful.<p>To pick on an example given in the article:<p>File managers that “seamlessly” (air quotes intentional) support multiple network protocols and archive formats have a supposedly flat learning curve; so flat that no mental effort is expected.<p>Indeed there is plenty and very confusing for anyone not prepared to it upfront.<p>Firing up a dedicated - specific purpose - application primes the users’ attention and makes eventual struggles expected and even gratifying to solve.<p>It’s a hunch, no hard proof for this; maybe some behavioral scientist can chime in')
