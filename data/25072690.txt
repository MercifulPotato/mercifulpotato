If environmental improvements would decrease net income, they generally won&#x27;t do it on their own as it would be inefficient.<p>Similarly, companies won&#x27;t generally elect to weaken their bargaining position with employees, such as paying higher wages, reducing working hours, providing PTO or fair hiring&#x2F;firing practices.<p>One of the main purposes of government is to represent the will and interests of the general person, whether that&#x27;s internationally or in collaboration with the companies that provide jobs.')
