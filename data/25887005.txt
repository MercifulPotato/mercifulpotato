And in that case, appreciation and inflation are completely different things.<p>But when talking about housing&#x2F;asset prices in aggregate, a 10% appreciation means you need 10% more money to buy the same asset, thus losing 10% purchasing power. Whether you call that appreciation or inflation doesn&#x27;t make a difference.')
