This particular coast is going to be flooded, this particular mountain won&#x27;t have snow anymore, these particular crops won&#x27;t be viable anymore, this particular mode of living won&#x27;t work.<p>But given the chaotic nature of the climate and complex feedback loops these local predictions seem to be hard to make. You can say that some catastrophic events are going to become more common, but you can&#x27;t say &quot;the 12th of November 2038 a huge storm is going to ravage this country and cause countless deaths and billions of $ in damages, so let&#x27;s invest this money now to prevent it from happening&quot;.')
