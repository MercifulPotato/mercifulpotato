Item(by='jokethrowaway', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610675937, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25784739, text='One idea I started building and never finished was &quot;pseudocode as code&quot;.<p>The idea was to have a compiler for pseudocode used in CS books in place of real code.<p>There are a few small differences (eg. using = vs :=) but most books and interview-preparing materials use a C-like notation.<p>Then I started reading papers written by language researchers (which use haskell-like pseudocode) and realised the size of the task at hand.<p>Probably trying to please only CS students &#x2F; people doing interviews would still make for a fun hack.')
