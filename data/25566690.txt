Item(by='cortesoft', descendants=None, kids=[25566910], score=None, time=1609215799, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25564754, text='I do feel like anger and the vengeance it drives (in particular irrational vengeance that goes beyond restitution) has some evolutionary advantages.<p>In many ways it is like the doomsday device in dr strangelove... the fact that it is not rational, and will be carried out even if it is not in the continuing best interest of the person enacting revenge, acts as a stronger deterrent than a proportional response.<p>It changes the game theory to know a person will enact revenge forever.')
