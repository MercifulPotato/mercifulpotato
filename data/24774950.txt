Item(by='t0mas88', descendants=None, kids=[24775007], score=None, time=1602669783, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24774714, text='From practical experience before we moved everything to &quot;the cloud&quot; and emptied the office server rack: These small businesses have a Windows server in a closet somewhere with huge amounts of files on it, typically a users complete documents folder etc.<p>When the user is in the office, they have a 1gbit LAN connection to that server and everything is great. Now 20 people work remote and all those files are behind a shared 20mbit uplink from the office to the internet.<p>So the problem isn&#x27;t only people watching YouTube or the fact that they VPN through the office to get online, it&#x27;s also that all the files are still in the office.')
