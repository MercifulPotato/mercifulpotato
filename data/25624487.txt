Getting the binary to be smaller means taking more compile time, because compilers have to do work to reduce the size. It is much faster to produce larger binaries.<p>Additionally, most compilers produce something that&#x27;s useful for development and debugging by default, and that means including <i>extra</i> stuff for that purpose.')
