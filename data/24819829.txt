Item(by='munfred', descendants=None, kids=[24820426], score=None, time=1603044362, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24819744, text='If you look at section 2 of bill S.2658[0], you will see that the bill only applies to &quot;Large Communications Platform Providers&quot;. You bank is not that. Spotify and Netflix are not that. The bill doesn&#x27;t apply to them. And note that the current threshold is 100M monthly active users <i>in the United States</i>*. A third of the population using the communications platform monthly. Relevant passage of section 2:<p><pre><code>    (7) LARGE COMMUNICATIONS PLATFORM.—The term “large communications platform” means a product or service provided by a communications provider that—\n    \n    (A) generates income, directly or indirectly, from the collection, processing, sale, or sharing of user data; and\n    \n    (B) has more than 100,000,000 monthly active users in the United States.\n</code></pre>\n---<p>Original bill text:\n[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.congress.gov&#x2F;bill&#x2F;116th-congress&#x2F;senate-bill&#x2F;2658&#x2F;text" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.congress.gov&#x2F;bill&#x2F;116th-congress&#x2F;senate-bill&#x2F;265...</a>')
