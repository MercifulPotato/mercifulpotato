Item(by='thomasbacklund', descendants=None, kids=[25430795, 25429596, 25430489], score=None, time=1608033226, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25428621, text='If serious about creating shell script &quot;apps&quot;, I think standard POSIX shell is the way to go. The Bashism is seldom needed, even though it is powerful.<p>Why? Because shell scripting is often used in DevOps, and containers very often do not come with Bash, but with (busybox) ash or dash instead.<p>Say you are going <i>FROM nginx</i> to <i>FROM nginx:alpine</i> (to get that smaller image size), woha! your bash script is not working anymore.')
