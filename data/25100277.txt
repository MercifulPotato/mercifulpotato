Item(by='loopz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605439263, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25100046, text='A good bugreport and good followup on questions, is also collaboration. Those will only strengthen the code or documentation in some way.<p>When people ask you to fix their problem, without providing any useful information or relevance to the software, it&#x27;s hoping for free support. For companies offering free support, it sometimes makes sense to dive that deep into userland.<p>For individuals you need a clear model what you&#x27;re willing to do and how, as something that at first may seem fun and engaging, can quickly become too much and draining over time.')
