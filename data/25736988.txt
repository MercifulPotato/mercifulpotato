Item(by='Veedrac', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610399279, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25735086, text='Obviously it&#x27;s impossible to prove definitive statements about every possible <i>potential</i> action, as as per the halting problem some of those actions are unprovable.<p>It is as ridiculous to suggest that this means you can&#x27;t contain a superintelligence as it is to suggest it means you can&#x27;t, I don&#x27;t know, go buy bread. In both cases you <i>could</i> analyze running a program that doesn&#x27;t halt but you can&#x27;t prove doesn&#x27;t halt, and lock up your reasoning algorithm. The sensible thing is to not do that.')
