Item(by='mdifrgechd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601836653, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24680855, text='Yeah, that is one facet of the overall institutional approach to procurement that treats everything like a commodity that can be perfectly substituted and comes down just to cost (and maybe a few other checkbox categories). A flexible approach that procures for outcomes but doesn&#x27;t prescribe how to get there is much better at admitting creative delivery solutions, including those from new entrants.<p>Of course past experience is still a logical thing to consider when judging the overall risks of a project. But if you look for creative solutions you can get away from &quot;look, this entrenched legacy business is the only one meeting out experience requirements&quot;.')
