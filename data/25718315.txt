Item(by='pembrook', descendants=None, kids=[25720477], score=None, time=1610305686, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25716588, text='No.<p>The simple fact that you can create value for others without stealing that value from someone else means the economy is positive sum.<p>If all economic transactions were zero sum, then growth would be impossible.<p>Things like GDP, the stock market, etc grow every single year on average because they are <i>positive</i> sum.<p>That’s why it’s most smart to just buy-and-hold boring index funds. You’re taking advantage of the positive sum nature of all business in aggregate, instead of playing the zero-sum game of short term trading against other market participants.')
