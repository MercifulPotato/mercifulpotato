It&#x27;s freedom.<p>It&#x27;s the freedom to get things wrong, iterate and try again.\nIt&#x27;s the freedom to move resources around.\nIt&#x27;s the freedom of not having to wait for your procurement process and approval from a purchasing department.\nIt&#x27;s the freedom of not having to wait for your operations team to plug in and configure bare metal. And all of these freedoms have compounding interest.<p>For most organizations, what I just talked about equates to man years of work wasted and I think a lot of people nitpicking about cloud being expensive have lost sight of this.<p>Sure it costs more, but it&#x27;s from money you&#x27;ve already saved. You&#x27;ll have loads of saved money left to spend on more developers too.')
