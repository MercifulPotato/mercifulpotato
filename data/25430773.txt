Item(by='jforman', descendants=None, kids=[25430842, 25431054], score=None, time=1608043722, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25430577, text='Let&#x27;s say you&#x27;re a startup trying to advertise on Google, and somebody has paid a bot network to fraudulently click on your ads.  Now Google can&#x27;t detect that those clicks are fraudulent, so you&#x27;re billed for them.<p>Your channel efficiency unavoidably goes down, which increases your cost of customer acquisition because your other channels cannot pick up all of the slack.<p>Increasing the cost of customer acquisition is going to be bad for your business.  You will either need to reduce costs (by hiring less, for example), or increase your prices.')
