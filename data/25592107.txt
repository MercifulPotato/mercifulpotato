Item(by='harimau777', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609418461, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25591437, text='Suppose that the following are true:<p>- Someone is not able to make enough money to have a reasonable quality of life despite contributing to society.<p>- Society&#x27;s rules do not provide an alternative (for example, because natural resources and land have already been claimed or because someone doesn&#x27;t have access to capital).<p>It would not make sense for someone to willingly go along with a system that does not allow them to live a decent life. Therefore they must be going along with the system due to some form of coercion.<p>A situation where someone is forced via threat to go along with a system that does not meet their needs is subjugation.')
