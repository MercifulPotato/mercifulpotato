A lot of real advances are made by a largish collection of people plugging away and sharing ideas.  We really love the narrative around the archetype of a lone inventor, but it&#x27;s rarely that clear cut.  This is exacerbated by the incentive structures - we want to pick &quot;winners&quot;.<p>This isn&#x27;t to say that there aren&#x27;t people who really stand out, far from it.  But it&#x27;s probably a mistake to account too much progress to them.')
