Item(by='pritovido', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603544281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24878592, text='PS: You also need to understand that everybody is different.<p>I am incredible good remembering faces and facial expressions, very good remembering places. I see a place on a picture, I know where it is if I have traveled there.<p>But I am very bad remembering sounds. So I never try to remember using sounds like other people do.<p>You should learn about yourself, what are your strengths and use them. Buy different memory books and just try them like I did. Some techniques will work for you, some not.')
