If you have &quot;we saw x where we also saw y&quot; then it can mean anything from &quot;x causes y&quot; to &quot;y cause x&quot;. And also &quot;unknown factor z causes both x and y&quot;, &quot;it&#x27;s a random coincidence&quot;, &quot;they looked into the data long enough with enough different methods to find something&quot; or a combination of all of those.')
