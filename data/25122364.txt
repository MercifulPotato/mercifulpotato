Item(by='pritovido', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605611460, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25121501, text='You need a hardware USB logger. The expensive ones makes things much easier.<p>Cutter is a program for the disassembly of programs. In order to use it you need knowledge of assembly, and that usually takes at least a year of training.<p>Reversing the USB protocol by software is very hard for someone without experience. USB protocol is very complex and has lots of layers.<p>I recommend that you start watching videos on internet like &quot;reversing usb devices&quot; and looking at real projects that do it.<p>Also, last time I used wireshark it gave me a very low level representation of things. It is a very good idea also knowledge of programming like python or lisp that can transform the low level representation into high level information automatically.')
