Item(by='kibwen', descendants=None, kids=[25930796], score=None, time=1611763579, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25929465, text='Active and available parenting, ideally starting long before the teen years where they no longer want to take advice from their parents.<p>Saying that it&#x27;s 100% impossible to stop kids from using the internet is as true as saying that it&#x27;s 100% impossible to stop kids from trying alcohol. But there&#x27;s a difference between a kid getting drunk once at a party and a kid being a habitual, daily alcoholic.<p>As a parent you want to guide them towards healthy ways to engage with things that could otherwise destroy them in excess. Social media in moderation is fine. Social media in excess isn&#x27;t, and that&#x27;s where a lot of parents have left their kids (because even <i>they</i> don&#x27;t know how to use it in moderation; society hasn&#x27;t caught up yet).')
