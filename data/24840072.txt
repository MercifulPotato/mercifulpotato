Item(by='ballenf', descendants=None, kids=[24840210], score=None, time=1603214842, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24839416, text='The now-trite &quot;if you&#x27;re not the customer, you&#x27;re the product&quot; distinction just isn&#x27;t helpful anymore.<p>Youtube is a great example where creators, subscribers (both Premium and ad-watching) <i>and</i> advertisers are all Google&#x27;s customers.
