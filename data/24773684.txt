Item(by='formalsystems', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602656358, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24773266, text='I agree with the sentiment of working for oneself, some people are just wired with that kind of independence. However I wouldn&#x27;t go so far as to say startup founders are just at another level of motivation - the successful ones that I know and have worked for are at another level of maturity in their thinking and approach to product development.<p>Ask an engineer to build something and they will think of the twenty features which will make a compelling product, and the great ones will work day and night to deliver them. A great startup CEO will identify the three features that get the first customer, then roadmap the remaining features in conjunction with sales, marketing, and hiring.<p>Great CEOs know how to build teams and solve hard problems, usually from years of experience and failures. It&#x27;s hardly ever as simple as reading a book or overcoming some mental hurdles.')
