Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=[25867769, 25866811], score=None, time=1611253961, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25862160, text='For context: These are ultra-efficient LED light bulbs that are currently exclusive to the UAE.<p>LED efficiency is non-linear with increasing power input. LEDs are most efficient at lower currents. The harder an LED is driven, the less marginal increase in light output.<p>The Dubai Lamp gets higher efficiency by using more LEDs and driving them in the more efficient part of their operating curve. More parts cost, but lower energy costs long-term.')
