When a climber begins up a route the standard exchange with their belayer (the person managing the rope and keeping them alive in a fall) goes something like<p>A: &quot;Belay on?&quot;<p>B: &quot;Belay on&quot;<p>A: &quot;Climbing&quot;<p>B: &quot;Climb on&quot;<p><i>Then</i> the climber begins.<p>It&#x27;s interesting to me that highly regulated and totally unregulated activities have evolved extremely similar processes.
