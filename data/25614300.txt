There&#x27;s a border between the territorial waters of a given country and these international waters along the coast.<p>There&#x27;s also noun&#x2F;verb confusion. A state has borders (noun). A state can border another (verb). State A has a border, state B has a border, and A borders B.<p>California has a border with International Waters. Hawaii is directly surrounded by International Waters. California does not border Hawaii, though both have borders and border International Waters.')
