Item(by='mgerdts', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604756394, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013858, text='NVMe-oF is a transport protocol between an initiator (e.g. Linux nvmf module) and a target (e.g. spdk, Linux nvmf module, etc.) much as iSCSI is a transport protocol. The storage behind the target does not need to be NVMe for NVMe-oF nor does it need to be SCSI for iSCSI.')
