Item(by='dragontamer', descendants=None, kids=[25037400, 25037490], score=None, time=1604943562, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25037060, text='I&#x27;m basically going down the opposite path, and am learning how to more effectively use my printer for a variety of tasks.<p>You can Doxygen your code into a .pdf, and then print off a reference copy of your API. Use a 2-hole punch with standard prong fasteners: and you&#x27;ve got a decent binding system (far superior to staples).<p>Just $15 for a 2-hole punch, $5 for 100x prong fasteners. From there, double-sided print from a laser-printer is pretty efficient.<p>----------<p>A Booklet of 100 pages can hold a surprising amount of information, and practically works as a 2nd (or 3rd) monitor for your productivity.<p>Learning to make __books__ out of your printer, by using a basic binding system (comb binders, 2-hole punch, or 3-hole binders) is a good skill to have. It really does improve your productivity.')
