Item(by='seanwilson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606647190, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25242305, text='Sometimes the design space is so limited though (I&#x27;m talking in general here), multiple people will inevitably arrive at similar designs. I&#x27;ve felt this myself with mobile design which can be really constrictive. Once you enumerate obvious interface variations, weigh up pros&#x2F;cons, discard the obviously bad ones, iterate a little etc. you can come up with something you think is unique but has been done many times before.<p>I was designing a mobile paint app for example and had to make choices like how you bring up the colour picker, move layers, change tools, preview brush settings etc. There&#x27;s only so many ways to do certain things on a tiny screen.')
