Item(by='jacksonkmarley', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607880934, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407175, text='The argument against needing to learn vocabulary seems to ignore the problem of understanding native speakers who are talking to you. I can certainly say a lot of things with some creative use of a small vocab, but I won&#x27;t have a clue what people are saying to me.<p>More generally, I think language learning can be a bit like dieting, in that there are a lot of complicated individual factors, and the method that works for a given person is the one that motivates them and that they actually persist with.')
