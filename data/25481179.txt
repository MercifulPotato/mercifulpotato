Item(by='fortran77', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608411823, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25478282, text='I had a cousin with Pick&#x27;s disease, which is a rare form of early-onset dementia which has similar symptoms to Alzheimer&#x27;s.<p>(See <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dementia.org&#x2F;picks-disease-and-dementia" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dementia.org&#x2F;picks-disease-and-dementia</a> )<p>His sister (my other cousin) devoted a decade of her life taking care of him. (Neither cousin had married). Extremely sad and the effects traumatized the entire family. It is unimaginably sad to see a younger person succumb to dementia.<p>At no time did anyone even think of assisted suicide. I can&#x27;t imagine how this can be done fairly unless someone is of sound mind.')
