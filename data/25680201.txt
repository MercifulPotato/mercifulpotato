That way you&#x27;d have something like:<p>- Checking\n  - Food\n  - Housing\n  - Entertainment\n  - Emergency Reserve<p>Your paycheck would go into checking and then you&#x27;d &quot;fund&quot; those subaccounts from the top level account. Then, when you reconcile your statements (I did it every day by going online), you &quot;spend&quot; out of the subaccounts.')
