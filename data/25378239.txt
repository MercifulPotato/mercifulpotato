Item(by='wisty', descendants=None, kids=[25379275], score=None, time=1607632464, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25378149, text='&gt; What are Stories?<p>&gt; Web Stories are a web-based version of the popular “Stories” format that blend video, audio, images, animation and text to create a dynamic, less formal consumption experience that goes beyond a simple play button. This visual format lets you explore content at your own pace by tapping through it, or swiping from one piece of content to the next.<p>It looks like a format to let Google put small web pages with mixed media into search carousels.')
