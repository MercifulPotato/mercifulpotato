We often are trying to figure out what the code is supposed to do, trying to crystalize our half-baked understanding into something solid enough to code.  While in that state, it&#x27;s very hard to name things, not just because names are hard (though they can be), but even more because our understanding is fuzzy.  We don&#x27;t really have a solid understanding of what the thing is, and our fumbling attempts at giving it a name are part of the process of trying to figure out what it <i>is</i>.')
