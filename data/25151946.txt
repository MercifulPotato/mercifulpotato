Item(by='wbhart', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605807234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25151771, text='If I were a business, the answer is no, the regulations are very strict.<p>But I am not a business, I&#x27;m a hobbyist. I derive no income from my channel.<p>By my estimation you would need at least five employees in Germany to run an online business properly, as the law that governs businesses is voluminous and bureaucratic. I could not afford to run a business.<p>For example, if I were a business in Germany, other YouTubers could sue me simply because I didn&#x27;t follow packaging or E-waste laws in one of my videos. And that is to say nothing of surprise laws that you couldn&#x27;t even predict would be laws.<p>Also, under German law I&#x27;d need permission from my existing employer to run a business, which they would not likely give.')
