Item(by='vannevar', descendants=None, kids=[25963885], score=None, time=1611952255, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25961455, text='In the US, socialism is a pejorative term arbitrarily applied to some government services, but not to others. For instance, if the government pays doctors or social workers, that is socialism, but if it pays police or soldiers, that is not socialism. And if the government pays corporations when they encounter financial difficulty, that is not socialism, but if it pays individuals, that is socialism.')
