Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=[25730715, 25728659], score=None, time=1610363446, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25723161, text='Corrections welcome, but I think Europeans don’t count floors, they count additional storeys, 1-based.<p>I think that’s influenced from French, which has <i>parterre</i> (“on the ground”) for the ground floor, and <i>étage</i>, derived from Latin <i>stare</i>, “to stand”, for higher (and lower) storeys (in the end, both may come from Latin)<p>If Europeans use 0-based counting for floors, I would expect at least some language to say “zeroth floor”. I’m not aware of any.')
