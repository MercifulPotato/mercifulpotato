Item(by='twunde', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607271389, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25323973, text='It was almost certainly the state public utilities commission PUC, which depending on the state may have a different acronym (PSC is also common). These regulate telecom, water, and energy companies including ESCOs (companies that in theory reduce your energy bill by charging you a different rate than the local monopoly).  For companies regulated this way, PSC complaints are extremely effective.')
