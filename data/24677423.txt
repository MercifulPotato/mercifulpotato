Item(by='oriolid', descendants=None, kids=[24677548], score=None, time=1601802484, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24676601, text='Often you can get pretty good results from just knowing that cache misses are expensive and working from that point: Store together what is accessed together, use linear arrays to help prefetcher, prefetch manually if really needed (it usually isn&#x27;t and the prefetch instruction isn&#x27;t free), if your data is accessed effectively randomly try packing used parts into smaller amount of cache lines, etc...<p>The latency numbers are still useful for making back of the envelope calculations about how much it is possible to gain and whether it&#x27;s worth the trouble.')
