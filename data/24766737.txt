Item(by='goosyloosy', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602604285, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24758772, text='In my view, the serverless revolution is stalled because of lack of interoperability with other more complex application architectures. Serverless is IMO best suited as something available in-addition-to other more complex application architectures for a limited but growing set of use-cases. The current architectures I&#x27;ve seen make the &quot;we &lt;3 serverless&quot; folks go all-in on it and then regret their decision and walk back to using services &#x2F; docker &#x2F; Kubernetes, or even a Heroku style PaaS for more power and flexibility.')
