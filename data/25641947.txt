Item(by='Kalium', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609819495, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25639912, text='Information security is not a matter of secure or not-secure. Throughout history, it has always been a matter of available resources and risk management.<p>Failing to consider security in terms of resources and risk leads to moments like this. The crushing fear that you&#x27;re not doing enough, never doing enough, never sure enough...<p>It&#x27;s a bottomless pit of anxiety. You can always add on more protections, more tools, and more layers. Your anxiety is never really soothed because you can never actually fully banish risk.<p>As long as you conceive of information security as deterministic or binary, you&#x27;re going to struggle.')
