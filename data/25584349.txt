Any of your extra time you choose to spend on education will pay dividends in the future. I&#x27;ve managed to pull this off on occasion.<p>I&#x27;m a freelancer and not an employee, so you&#x27;d think it would be easy to negotiate such. However, I keep running into the same issue: few people need just a <i>little</i> software engineering. If someone needs some software engineering, they usually need a <i>lot</i> of it. Several times, I&#x27;ve been hired by customers with the promise of &quot;we just need 10 hours a week&quot;, but somehow get talked into working full time pretty quickly.')
