Spotify artist payments are not based on play counts directly. They are actually based on Spotify&#x27;s total revenue and the proportion of global Spotify streams that the artist received.<p>As a result of these extra plays, Vulpeck&#x27;s stream rate increases, but Spotify gets virtually no extra revenue (especially since a user would have to be a premium subscriber to get a fully quiet experience listening to the album). Vulpeck gets paid a bit more, and everyone else gets paid a minuscule amount less.<p>For reference, here&#x27;s one article that mentions this model of how Spotify pays its artists: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;qz.com&#x2F;1660465&#x2F;the-way-spotify-and-apple-music-pays-artists-isnt-fair&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;qz.com&#x2F;1660465&#x2F;the-way-spotify-and-apple-music-pays-...</a>')
