Item(by='DennisP', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608209938, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25454142, text='Go too far down that road, and you&#x27;re applying it to all altruistic behaviors, claiming there&#x27;s really no such thing as altruism because it&#x27;s all in service to the ego, and concluding you might as well act as selfishly as possible.<p>Personally I think it&#x27;s ok to do altruistic things, and conclude from the evidence that you&#x27;re an altruistic person. It is the most simple and straightforward explanation, and Occam&#x27;s Razor is as sharp as ever. And given the way cognitive dissonance works, even a person who starts out as ego-driven will likely become more altruistic over time, if they do altruistic things with no concrete reward.')
