We can scale to billions of records (see reddit comment demo in the news article).<p>Also we are not limited to common representations such as circles. In fact you can create your own in D3.<p>Here&#x27;s a live demo of 1.88 million historical wildfires: <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;wildfire2.kyrixdemo.live&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;wildfire2.kyrixdemo.live&#x2F;</a>')
