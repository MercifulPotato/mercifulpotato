Item(by='jacques_chester', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609880850, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25650177, text='I worked on buildpacks for a while. Those had to track &quot;only&quot; a few hundred upstream dependencies and build them from source. It took a fulltime team to build and maintain the infrastructure.<p>The problem here is economic. Because there is no body of information about software assets acting as public good, each individual is forced to internalise the costs. Either you internalise the cost of building everything yourself, or you internalise the risk of accepting incoming assets on faith. But you <i>bear a cost</i>.')
