Item(by='gregoriol', descendants=None, kids=[25406876, 25413109], score=None, time=1607870129, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25406644, text='Storing the second factor in the same password manager as the password is a lot like having a post-it with the password on your desk: it&#x27;s practical, but defeats the purpose.<p>But to add to this, I wouldn&#x27;t ever set up a 2FA on a service that doesn&#x27;t allow multiple second factors, and mostly static backup codes. Having only one (ie. device) is a much higher risk, of being locked out, than having the password stolen.')
