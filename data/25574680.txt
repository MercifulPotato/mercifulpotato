Item(by='brundolf', descendants=None, kids=[25575148, 25575123, 25575064, 25575059], score=None, time=1609274541, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25574427, text='Something I&#x27;ve realized in recent months is that restaurant portions are <i>insane</i>.<p>Even if you&#x27;re not eating a cheeseburger - heck, even if you&#x27;re eating a salad (assuming it has more than just lettuce) - the sheer amount of food you get in a single entree at most restaurants means that even a reasonable <i>type</i> of meal will end up being two or three times the calories it would be if you made it at home.
