Item(by='tremon', descendants=None, kids=[25006186], score=None, time=1604653341, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25006032, text='Not sure if it&#x27;s common across all tar implementations, but you can use -C to let GNU tar change directories before performing any operation. So this command is equivalent:<p><pre><code>  $ tar -cC &#x2F;source&#x2F;dir . | ssh rhost tar -xvC &#x2F;dest&#x2F;dir\n</code></pre>\n(I also left out -f - because it&#x27;s the default)')
