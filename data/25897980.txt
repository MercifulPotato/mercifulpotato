Alice&#x27;s client could wrap the message to Bob as an encrypted payload to a message addressed to switchboard@server, so that her provider doesn&#x27;t learn Bob&#x27;s address, and her provider could replace her metadata with switchboard@example before sending it to Bob&#x27;s, so that it doesn&#x27;t learn Alice&#x27;s address.')
