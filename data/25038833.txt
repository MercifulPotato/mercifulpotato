Item(by='vntok', descendants=None, kids=[25039040], score=None, time=1604950490, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25038806, text='The Schengen Area ( &#x2F;ˈʃɛŋən&#x2F;) is an area comprising 26 European countries that have officially abolished all passport and all other types of border control at their mutual borders. The area mostly functions as a single jurisdiction for international travel purposes, with a common visa policy. The area is named after the 1985 Schengen Agreement signed in Schengen, Luxembourg.\nSource: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Schengen_Area" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Schengen_Area</a>')
