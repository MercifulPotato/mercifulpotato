Item(by='nneonneo', descendants=None, kids=[25786872], score=None, time=1610682529, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25786701, text='Yeah, there&#x27;s a bunch of stuff in AArch64 that takes getting used to - for example, fixed-length instructions (everything is a 32-bit instruction!), tons of sanely-named registers (X0~X30, W0~W30), shorter literals (all literals need to fit in a 32-bit instruction), and so much more. Plus, it&#x27;s a total break from AArch32 - I&#x27;m pretty comfortable with 32-bit ARM code, and yet 64-bit ARM code still looks pretty unusual to me.')
