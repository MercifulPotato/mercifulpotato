Put that in a safe place, away from direct light and dampness.<p>Save the rest on two of Google Drive, OneDrive, iCloud, some other cloud storage, a backup service or copy to a computer in your home. Make your selection based on things that you will &quot;touch&quot; in some way at least every 12-24 months. Everything else will fail in a few years.<p>Don’t save crap you don’t need. Don&#x27;t futz around with optical media, tape or other nonsense. Don&#x27;t buy safes or safe deposit boxes unless that&#x27;s going to be part of your routine in some way.')
