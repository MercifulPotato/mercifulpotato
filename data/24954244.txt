Item(by='googthrowaway42', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604170923, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24954101, text='I think I&#x27;m less pessimistic than you are but I get where you&#x27;re coming from.<p>According to Pareto (yes, that Pareto), there is a natural ebb and flow of this sort of national vs self interest that exists within the ruling class. In a healthy society there is more circulation in and out of the ruling class which allows those with more of a national interest to dominate when that is necessary. If there is too much self interest within the ruling class and not enough circulation it ends up &quot;killing the host&quot;.<p>I would definitely recommend the book &quot;The Machiavellians&quot; by James Burnham for a formal survey of these sort of ideas. Pareto is one of these Machiavellians and so his work is analyzed.')
