And VDEV Removal gives some flexibility (at the cost of indirection on the read path). But yeah, ZFS is not super flexible for adding and removing random drives.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;openzfs&#x2F;zfs&#x2F;pull&#x2F;8853" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;openzfs&#x2F;zfs&#x2F;pull&#x2F;8853</a>')
