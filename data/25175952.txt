Item(by='beagle3', descendants=None, kids=[25175987, 25176140, 25176411], score=None, time=1606034296, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25148840, text='COBOL has a SEP field on it (and for good reasons - it <i>is</i> someone else&#x27;s problem), but when discussing APL, I&#x27;ve often reached for it as an example that everything is just preference.<p>When I show an APL or K gem such as &quot;|&#x2F;0(0|+)\\&quot; (which is a complete and rather efficient implementation of the maximum-subarray-sum problem), people usually complain that &quot;it&#x27;s unreadable&quot;.<p>But then &quot;ADD ONE TO X GIVING X&quot; compared to &quot;++x&quot; or &quot;x=x+1&quot; shows that explicit is only better than implicit when you&#x27;re not familiar with the notation.<p>Readability is all about your expectation and familiarity.')
