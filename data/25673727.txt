If we require more than this, then we will incur in damage to the scientific process for two reasons: (1) companies are not willing to make available software developed by their researchers, therefore they will publish even less; and (2) universities don&#x27;t have money and staff to produce and maintain software at these standards, so professors will be required to publish less papers.')
