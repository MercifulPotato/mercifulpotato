Item(by='softwaredoug', descendants=None, kids=[25597674], score=None, time=1609389898, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25569725, text='Who is your audience?<p>Having migrated from WP to Jekyll only to go back in the last ten years, I learned Wordpress (and Drupal, etc ) exist for a reason. They’re well understood by a large community of non technical marketing, PR, and other content professionals. So in the end if it’s more than for your own blog or a handful of devs, “modern tech stack” isn’t the main consideration.<p>Not to dissuade you—I’m curious myself if there are more modern WP competitors out there to help the non technical audience.')
