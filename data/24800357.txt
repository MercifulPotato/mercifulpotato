What conservatism fears is people looking for meaning in themselves, finding it and then withdrawing from the social institutions - leading to a general collapse. It links in very neatly with a world-view where meaning is to be found externally to the self.<p>It isn&#x27;t anything to do with the fixedness or flexibility of moral truths. Conservativism as a philosophy can handle extreme morally flexible if the institutions require it to survive.')
