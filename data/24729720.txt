Item(by='3np', descendants=None, kids=[24731114], score=None, time=1602250298, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24716319, text='Some differentiators that aren&#x27;t immediately obvious in the comparison:<p>&gt; SeaweedFS Filer metadata store can be any well-known and proven data stores, e.g., Cassandra, Mongodb, Redis, Elastic Search, MySql, Postgres, MemSql, TiDB, CockroachDB, Etcd etc, and is easy to customized.<p>I&#x27;m not very familiar with other DFS&#x27;s but at the very least glusterfs stores metadata as xattrs on an underlying filesystem and so has no need of an external data store.<p>Also, SeaweedFS has a &quot;master&quot; server (single centralized with failover to secondary) and &quot;volume servers&quot; (responsible for data).')
