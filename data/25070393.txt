Item(by='iguy', descendants=None, kids=[25071990], score=None, time=1605192275, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25070252, text='But regardless of whether the outcome fits your priors or not, you may wish to know whether the study has decent evidence, or is just noise.<p>And GP has a good point. The authors have some automated scheme for grading how well-qualified people are. The recruiters <i>also</i> have some such scheme. If these two schemes differ, and the distributions aren&#x27;t identical between the groups being compared, then you will detect group differences like what they see. To label these differences bias in the recruiter&#x27;s process, you must be confident that you have less bias in your process. The details of this correction are going to be crucial.')
