As technology progresses, the power to destroy, create, or maintain increases, but do they increase at the same rate? Case in point, our nuclear bombs could kill most of us within the hour, yet science is barely learning how to stop the great killer, aging. Will we get to a point where any individual can order a few parts and kill millions? It seems inevitable if we continue along our current trajectory.
