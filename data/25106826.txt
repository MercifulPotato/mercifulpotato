Item(by='scythe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605489161, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25106151, text='You can&#x27;t generally see two countries next to each other on a globe. The globe itself is accurate, but a human looking at a globe sees an orthographic projection up to binocular perspective, which is only accurate near the center. So you can&#x27;t compare India to Brazil for example.')
