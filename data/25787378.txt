Item(by='dlubarov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610688812, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25787120, text='Layer 2 protocols are just those that operate &quot;on top of&quot; a base protocol, such as Lightning Network (on top of Bitcoin), roll_up (on top of Ethereum), or POA Network (also Ethereum).<p>(I would argue that the terms are imprecise though -- some protocols are described as operating &quot;on top of Ethereum&quot;, but have their own consensus etc. which can function without Eth, so they could also be viewed as layer 1s with a bridge.)')
