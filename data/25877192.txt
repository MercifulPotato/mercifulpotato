Item(by='lrossi', descendants=None, kids=[25880926], score=None, time=1611355205, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25877026, text='I worked on some Linux apps in another life. I switched to Qt at some point. It was a breath of fresh air! Qt is very reliable and well documented. One can be very productive with it.<p>I think a lot of people have a bad opinion about it as they confuse it with KDE. While KDE does use Qt, the two projects are otherwise independent.<p>I’ve also looked inside the Qt code base a few times. It’s very tidy and quite easy to read. You can tell that their team is very experienced. I used to read their blog as well, they had a lot of good articles.<p>I really hope that they can continue to survive financially. Selling a mostly open source library is not very profitable.')
