Item(by='Tijdreiziger', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612195155, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25989029, text='Personally I would love to switch to OSS tomorrow, but I still see many showstoppers:<p>- Desktop: Linux vs. Windows - worse support for gaming (e.g. anti-cheat), no support for major creative software like Photoshop&#x2F;Affinity or Ableton&#x2F;FL Studio<p>- Mobile: FOSS Android distro vs. Google Android - no guarantee that apps I currently use will work without Gapps, no paid apps or in-app purchases<p>I have really been thinking a lot about this in the past months, because I would like to improve my privacy. The only real play seems to be moving to Apple&#x27;s ecosystem, and buying a console for the games (and paying a lot more and losing customizability and repairability in the process).')
