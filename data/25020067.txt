Item(by='evgen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604787132, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25018550, text='If a weather forecaster can predict the temperature next week to within a few degrees and can tell you when they are sure it will rain, when they are sure it will be sunny, and provide a good guess as to rain or shine on most of the other days you would consider that person a good weather forecaster.<p>Do you expect the weather report for the next week to nail the temperature of every day next week to within 1 degree?<p>The pollsters can tell you if a hurricane is coming, they can tell you when the weather might shift significantly (albeit not be absolutely certain about which direction it is shifting) and they can give you a very good idea of what to expect even if they do not get the exact temp for each day correct.')
