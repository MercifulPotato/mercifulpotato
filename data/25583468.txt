Item(by='pm90', descendants=None, kids=[25583925, 25586191], score=None, time=1609348282, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25582017, text='Deep thinking isn’t something “inherently good”. If you’re not inclined to it, I think that’s fine. Most humans may not be.<p>What I take away from reading profiles of the very intelligent is that for many of them the thing they’re known for also happens to be the thing they like doing and are inclined to it despite themselves. Some enjoy the pleasure that comes from deep thinking. Others enjoy understanding what others have thought up. It’s fine. There’s no competition.')
