Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=[25963239, 25963243, 25963205], score=None, time=1611955291, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25961053, text='Each time the issue of action to force Apple to open the App Store  comes up, I usually mention that Facebook will be the first to open a store.<p>If Facebook prevails, at the very least every developer and every user will have to deal with both Apple’s store <i>and</i> Facebook’s store.<p>We know that Facebook will permit apps which do tracking without consent.<p>This situation is objectively worse for both consumers <i>and</i> developers than what we have now.<p>It’s also worth pointing out that Facebook would be unaffected, or indeed May even benefit if the overall app marketplace contracted due to erosion of user trust.')
