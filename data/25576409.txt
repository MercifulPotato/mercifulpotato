Item(by='jedberg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609284853, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25576250, text='Japan has zoning laws at the country level, and they are much more sane than local zoning laws.  Basically, local zoning says &quot;this piece of land can be used for one thing&quot;.  Japan&#x27;s laws say &quot;this piece of land can cause a maximum nuisance of X, but it can have all these different things on it&quot;.  And then it&#x27;s up to the developers to decide if building a multi-unit building would more profitable than a single family home.')
