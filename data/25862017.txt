Item(by='chasil', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611252655, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25858510, text='When running &quot;yum update&quot; the packages are pulled from the registered repository, found in the &#x2F;etc&#x2F;yum.repos.d directory.<p>When switching the provider of package updates, the old repository must be disabled, and a new one substituted, among other changes that the new provider requires to switch support.<p>Oracle makes (by default) modest changes for such a switch. Red Hat essentially rewrites your whole operating system, and has added a warning that consultants should be retained for such conversions in case of trouble.')
