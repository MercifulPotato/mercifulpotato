Item(by='neurostimulant', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604689452, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25009653, text='I bought a new ink absorber when I got the error message on my Epson printer. Turns out the absorber is just a dumb piece of plastic and foam and the printer actually simply keeping a counter of how many pages has been printed and pop up the error when the counter exceeds certain threshold (the old absorber was still in a good shape). Simply resetting the counter with some apps clear the warning.')
