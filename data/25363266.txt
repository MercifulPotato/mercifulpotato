Item(by='abecedarius', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607542166, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25359780, text='Generalize BDDs to multiway decision diagrams: instead of binary trees on 0 and 1, make n-ary trees on a bigger alphabet. (You probably want to represent the nodes sparsely, not |alphabet| pointers in every node.)<p>This can directly represent a regex with | operators but not Kleene star. The DD canonicalization automatically makes this structure share suffixes, but not prefixes.')
