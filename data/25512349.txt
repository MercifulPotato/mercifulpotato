Item(by='gumby', descendants=None, kids=[25514057], score=None, time=1608677443, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25511884, text='Breed affects this.  We have large dogs (Irish Wolfhound and Great Dane) and it’s recommended not to neuter until the bone plates have fused — about 23 months.  So I waited and did it pretty much as soon as possible.  Smaller dogs can supposedly be done sooner.<p>Vets will neuter cats at six weeks which seems incredibly young, but “it’s fine”.<p>As far as I can tell there isn’t much actual science on this.<p>In my experience the best effect is on male cats — they become very sweet and friendly.')
