Item(by='dleslie', descendants=None, kids=[25306140], score=None, time=1607105036, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25286608, text='Doesn&#x27;t the &quot;spooky action at a distance&quot; become moot if we set aside the notion of a single forward direction of time for information? That is, when you observe one half of the entangled pair there is not a super-luminal speed transfer of information because the information was already available to the other half of the pair at the time that they were entangled; it will become the necessary state upon observation of its pair because that is what is necessary in the future.')
