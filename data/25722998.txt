Item(by='samatman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610328478, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25722917, text='Nope, what you&#x27;re describing is a mistake in focus-follows-mouse, one which has been made more than once.<p>In real, XWin-style focus-follows-mouse, the window you mouse over gets the focus, <i>but</i>, isn&#x27;t pulled to the front. If you click, it gets the front and the focus.<p>I use Moom, a semi-tile snap-style window wrangler, and a widescreen with three panels. What I want to do is just slide the cursor over and start typing, but I have to remember to click, and often enough I just don&#x27;t.<p>The One True Wiki has a nice discussion of the subject: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.c2.com&#x2F;?FocusFollowsMouse" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.c2.com&#x2F;?FocusFollowsMouse</a>')
