Item(by='titzer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602976729, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24812250, text='&gt; The problem is JS&#x27;s double-&gt;int conversion was effectively defined as &quot;what wintel does by default&quot;,<p>No, JavaScript &quot;double to int conversion&quot; (which only happens implicitly in bitwise operations such as |, &amp;, etc) is not like any hardware instruction at all. It is defined to be the selection of the low-order 32-bits of a floating point number <i>as-if</i> it were expanded to its full-width integer representation, dropping any fractional part.')
