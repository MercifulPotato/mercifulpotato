Item(by='AgentME', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607568306, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25364893, text='Firefox has no incentive to prevent people from blocking ads, and DNS over HTTPS isn&#x27;t a tool to block users from reconfiguring their own machines. All browsers let you configure your DNS settings still. The thing that DNS over HTTPS prevents is letting whoever is supplying your internet (including your ISP, whatever hotspot you&#x27;re using on the go, etc) manipulate your DNS. It&#x27;s not about removing control from the person who owns the machine.')
