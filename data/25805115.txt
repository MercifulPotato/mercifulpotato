Item(by='taurath', descendants=None, kids=[25806186], score=None, time=1610828264, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25803568, text='Toxic positivity often comes from the idea that seeking happiness is the right goal always. If you believe that then you believe anyone who is not happy is somehow failing and you can give them a hand up. The right word I think should be contentment. Happiness is impossible to have all the time. You can have contentment pretty much all the time.')
