I have always intercepted 53 and 853 and route them to my Unbound DNS recursive server.  Unbound caches requests, blocks known malicious domains, then forwards the requests over a vpn mesh to other unbound servers that talk to the root name servers.  The weakest link of course is that the root servers do not talk TLS yet.  DNSSEC can be useful for zones that are signed.')
