Projects were successful, but it folded a few years later due to other reasons.<p>One problem is that clients hate it, even when success was very measurable. Note that usually the projects were limited to ones where there was a directly measurable outcome to drive the metrics for the payments.<p>Wierdly enough you would think that such a business could pivot to developing its own product.
