If they transition to ARM, they are on a level playing field with Qualcomm, Nvidia, AMD, and Apple.<p>Intel has a massive incentive to keep Microsoft reliant on x86. If Windows (or Linux even) shifts over to primarily ARM, their ability to extract outsized profits from the industry vanishes over-night.<p>Microsoft on the other hand has massive incentives to get <i>anyone</i> creating high end ARM CPUs to compete with Apple.')
