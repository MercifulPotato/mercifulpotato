Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=[25381266], score=None, time=1607644140, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25375711, text='The biggest flaw with the complaint seems to be the claim that Apple locked down the iPhone as a response to the success of Cydia.<p>That seems hard to rationalize, given that the iPhone <i>was already</i> locked down before Cydia, and Cydia could only be installed by exploiting a security vulnerability.<p>It’s true that Apple made jailbreaking harder by fixing vulnerabilities, but you’d expect them to do that for security reasons.<p>If the iPhone had supported the installation of 3rd party stores, and then Apple had withdrawn this support after launching their own, I think the case would be stronger.')
