Item(by='lmkg', descendants=None, kids=[25458909], score=None, time=1608229791, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25457813, text='The cookie banner is <i>NOT</i> regulated by the GDPR.<p>It&#x27;s related to the ePrivacy Directive, which is deeply intertwined with GDPR but a separate piece of legislation.
