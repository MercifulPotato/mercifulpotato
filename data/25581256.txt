Item(by='forinti', descendants=None, kids=[25581453, 25581840], score=None, time=1609333027, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25569146, text='I love how you could mix 6502 assembly and also create macros.<p>For example, you could declare something like this to move two bytes from F% to T%:<p><pre><code>  870 DEF FNMOVW(F%,T%)\n  880 [\n  890 OPT pass%\n  900 LDA F%\n  910 STA T%\n  920 LDA F%+&amp;1\n  930 STA T%+&amp;1\n  940 ]\n  950 =pass%\n</code></pre>\nAnd then reference it from somewhere else like so:<p><pre><code>  220 OPT FNMOVW(&amp;52,&amp;4B)\n</code></pre>\nAnd the resulting code would be inlined into your assembly. It made for shorter and more readable assembly.')
