Item(by='ddek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605088176, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25049901, text='Never, ever, ever do this in production. This will block at least two threads, which <i>will</i> cause premature thread pool starvation at spike loads.<p>This pattern, &#x27;sync-over-async&#x27;, is to be avoided at all costs, at all times.<p>You&#x27;re not going to like it, but the MS recommended way of dealing with this is to make your callstack async.<p>In all honest - if you&#x27;re making an ASP net core web app, go all in on async from the start. If you have to mix and match, never go sync-over-async inside an action.')
