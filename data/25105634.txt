All participants in the study had previously been on statins and had stopped taking statins because they experienced side effects.  Any results obtained from this study are not generalizable.<p>2. They did not separate the participants into groups.  There was no control.  A better design might be to have 1&#x2F;3 of the group take actual statins for 12 months, 1&#x2F;3 take a placebo, and 1&#x2F;3 take nothing.')
