Those are both fairly innocuous actions within a regulated market.<p>Rights often exist along a zero-sum spectrum; e.g., Google&#x27;s right to surveil me on every site that uses Google Analytics impedes on my right to use the web privately. Telling people what they can and cannot do is not a hypothetical. It just comes down to where you draw the line.')
