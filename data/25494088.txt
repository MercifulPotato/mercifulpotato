Item(by='patrec', descendants=None, kids=[25495877], score=None, time=1608551882, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25493812, text='Unless you have an encyclopedic memory and copious leisure time, you&#x27;d probably be <i>much</i> better off writing perl scripts instead if you care about portability. There is an enormous amount of sometimes subtle divergence between behaviors of core tools you need for shell scripting (sed, tr, mktemp, readlink all behave very differently between different versions of unix,for example) and even if you manage to memorize exactly what&#x27;s in posix (and accept the fairly severe limitiations in expressiveness this entails), few things are actually 100% posix conforming.')
