Item(by='jandrewrogers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603041447, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24816745, text='They are leveraging their privileged hardware positions to enter adjacent high-value data markets that have little to do with their core business, from which they can generate considerable margin. Essentially, the hardware business becomes a loss leader for a sensor platform and data business. Many hardware companies are in a position to capture data models that would be difficult to acquire any other way, if they can stop thinking of themselves as hardware companies.<p>In the short-term, big tech companies can&#x27;t replicate what is possible for these hardware companies. Longer term, I would expect these data sources to be commoditized as well.')
