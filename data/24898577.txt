It&#x27;s more like 99% contextual.<p>If I ask for recent wildfire news and I&#x27;m in a state that doesn&#x27;t experience wildfires often, are you going to return 15 news articles about wildfires spread out over the 200 year history of the state? I almost certainly want 15 news articles about the current wildfires in some other parts of the country. Your algorithm doesn&#x27;t really say what to do here.<p>If I ask for &quot;recent relatively rare astronomical event&quot; recent might mean hundreds of years or more. If I ask for &quot;recent PC game releases&quot; it might mean a month or the current year. If I ask for &quot;recent public events in my town&quot; it might mean over the last week.<p>In many cases, &quot;there are no recent events&quot; is a better answer than &quot;here are the last 15 events.&quot;')
