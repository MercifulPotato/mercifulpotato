Most packaging should be glass, paper, or metal. Fewer things should be so disposable.<p>One thing I wish we could find a solution to is our oil subsidy problem. I get that the US gov wants to subsidize oil for the sake of national security but those subsidies have all sorts of crazy unintended consequences.
