With security, the rule is to be conservative with what you accept; anything unusual should be rejected.<p>In cases where backwards compatibility is needed, just use inet_pton() and let the libc maintainers deal with the bug reports (I <i>believe</i> inet_pton() dropped octal and hex support for ipv4 addresses)')
