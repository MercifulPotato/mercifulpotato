Item(by='kuschku', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604939043, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25036227, text='With counterfeit cartridges the printing behaviour may change, leading to the almost-invisible tracking dots on each page that encode the printers serial number, the cartridges serial number, time of day, etc to either become too visible or too invisible.<p>Also, with different ink your printer might print content with a different color profile, so it won&#x27;t look the same, and it might need to clean the printing heads more often (and the cleaning tech for that is often only designed for a limited amount of uses).<p>But while all these are legitimate reasons, projects like Instant Ink show the true reason is to just turn the printer into a subscription product: buying a printer at a loss, and making a profit with every page printed.')
