Item(by='duskwuff', descendants=None, kids=[25062238], score=None, time=1605124403, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061987, text='Allowing email addresses to be reused is a <i>huge</i> security and privacy problem. It means that any email which was still being sent to the old address -- including account recovery emails -- becomes accessible to the new owner of the email address.<p>Deleting data from &quot;dead&quot; accounts is fine and good. But they need to stay dead, not come back to life.')
