Item(by='lxe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610063969, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25679354, text='I think SMRs are very different from RTG. SMR is an essentially a small and fairly complex reactor design, like a molten salt reactor. It heats water or other liquid, turns it into steam, steam spins a turbine, just like a regular power plant.<p>An RTG is just a hot piece of radioactive material surrounded by thermocouples that directly convert the heat into electricity.')
