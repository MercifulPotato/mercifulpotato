Item(by='torbital', descendants=None, kids=[25565740], score=None, time=1609200873, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25563646, text='Speaking of academic papers, I&#x27;d love to see a focus on readability.<p>So many papers are just too dense for readers outside of the specific field. This can be because of the complexity of the subject matter, but I often notice unnecessary specific vocabulary and over-complicating concepts.<p>Because of this, it&#x27;s impractical for the layman to go read academic papers outside of their field. This leads to reading some news article that attempts to summarize it, and we all know that majority of the time it&#x27;s just the headline that gets read.')
