Item(by='kmeisthax', descendants=None, kids=[24801829], score=None, time=1602856739, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24795069, text='Alternative headline: What if your political or religious beliefs were rooted in a mental disorder?<p>I don&#x27;t mean to repeat the canard of &quot;conservatism is a mental illness&quot; - as a whole, it&#x27;s not. But there is a growing body of evidence suggesting a minor correlation between certain subsets of conservative thought and certain phobias. For example, people who tend to be conservative also tend to be more readily disgusted. If you make a pill that treats those phobias, then you are by definition also &quot;treating&quot; their politics, too; even if this is just by accident.<p>Nixon was smarter than we took credit for. Banning the drugs liberals and black people used wasn&#x27;t just a way to get a bunch of quick and easy felony convictions on his political opponents, it was a way to arguably ban the promulgation of competing political views altogether. Certainly, his paranoia is something that could have been treated by the drugs he was banning.')
