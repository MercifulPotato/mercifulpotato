Item(by='epaga', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603273445, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846162, text='Posted this quite a while back and thought I&#x27;d try again since I&#x27;ve added Android and non-TrueDepth iOS support.<p>Happy to answer any questions - here are a few typical ones:<p>&quot;How does it work?&quot; - It uses your phone&#x27;s AR capabilities to track your head in 3D then sends this data to the open source app OpenTrack which then emulates the TrackIR protocol for games to work with.<p>&quot;If you&#x27;re moving your head, how do you see the monitor?&quot; - you keep your eyes on the screen, plus your movement is magnified, so a slight nudge will move your head a lot further.<p>&quot;What devices does this work for?&quot; - any iOS 13 device and any Android device that supports AR Services.')
