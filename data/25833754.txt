Item(by='lotsofpulp', descendants=None, kids=[25834415], score=None, time=1611067039, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25833663, text='There’s placement, and then there’s altering the storyline and photography of a scene to achieve the goal of advertising a product.<p>For example, there’s a difference in someone drinking a can of Coke, versus someone spending 30 seconds talking about how they love Coke, and adjusting the camera angles so that the Coke logo is always front and center.<p>It’s possible to do product placement that doesn’t take you out of the story.  But recent stuff beats you over the head with it in ways that are inconsistent with normal behavior and it’s jarring, takes you out of the moment, and I have no idea how people tolerate it.')
