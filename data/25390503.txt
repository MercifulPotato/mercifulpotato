Item(by='perlgeek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607718379, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25372437, text='Your eyes cannot see orange directly, but as an overlap of different intensities of the stimulation of different receptors in the base colors.<p>So as long as the filters in the camera have roughly the same transmission curve as the sensitivity curve of the color receptors, all is good.<p>However, to animals (or hypothetical aliens) with other color receptors, the images produced by photo prints and screens would look quite weird, with colors all wrong.')
