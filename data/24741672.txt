Item(by='cweagans', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602361895, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24740520, text='pacman is for the official repos. There are a number of AUR helpers out there. Personally, I enjoy yay (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Jguer&#x2F;yay" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Jguer&#x2F;yay</a>). It&#x27;s very simple, it&#x27;s been consistently maintained over a number of years, and it works for most commands if you just replace &quot;pacman&quot; with &quot;yay&quot;. For instance, `yay -Syu` is the same as `pacman -Syu`, but it&#x27;ll handle your AUR packages as well.')
