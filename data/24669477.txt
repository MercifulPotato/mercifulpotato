So if you have a good team and a relatively good idea you will fairly easily raise a seed round from someone.<p>And it&#x27;s generally an awful idea for founders: (1) because most assume they will raise a Series-A when statistically they won&#x27;t and (2) you don&#x27;t learn to hustle when you have millions in the bank.<p>The best way is to follow companies like Buildkite.
