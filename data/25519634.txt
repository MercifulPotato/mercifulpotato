Item(by='laumars', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608743118, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25519268, text='This is exactly my experience as well.<p>Btrfs is the C++ of file systems: it’s powerful and works for a great many people. But the tooling is intimidating to new comers and unless you know exactly what you’re doing, it’s a ticking time bomb due to the plethora of foot guns and hidden traps.<p>This is why some people claim to have success with it while a great many other people, rightfully, claim it’s not yet ready for prime time.<p>ZFS, on the other hand, has not only protected me against failing hardware but it also has sane defaults and easy to use tooling thus protecting me against my own stupidity.')
