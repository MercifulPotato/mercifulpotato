Item(by='dugmartin', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602579618, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24759886, text='Another option that my kids have always liked is “Apple Pie Oatmeal”.  Just normal old fashioned oats (not quick) cooked with a skinned and cut up apple and a dash of cinnamon.  Make sure to use a good baking apple like a Granny Smith so the chunks don’t dissolve to mush.  Serve with milk and brown sugar - it tastes just like apple pie.')
