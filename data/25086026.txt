Item(by='jusssi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605294024, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25082233, text='Stalled as in, sender has data to send, receiver is ready to receive, but the transfer makes no progress or proceeds very slowly.<p>I can&#x27;t quote a number for the drop percentage, honestly I&#x27;ve forgot. Discovering the limit was a side effect, we were simply looking to test how a piece of software would behave over a bad connection. I just remember being surprised that everything just stopped when I put in packet loss that wasn&#x27;t anywhere near 100%.<p>My since-then-adjusted expectations would put any double digit percentage (yes, starting from 10%) of random packet loss as unusable conditions for TCP.')
