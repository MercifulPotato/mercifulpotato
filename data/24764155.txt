Item(by='nicexe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602585672, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24758772, text='I&#x27;m working with a different type of serverless approach.<p>For a few hackathons now my team tries to push a client-side frontend + smart-contracts on a blockchain as a backend solution. This has a UX hit (some downsides of it could be mitigated though) but it made our business logic easier to implement. Depending on the project it has additional benefits to go with this stack. This cannot be applied to every project though and usually the UX hit or the lack of private transactions is enough to favor more traditional stacks.')
