Item(by='ddingus', descendants=None, kids=[25256523], score=None, time=1606754548, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25255414, text='On the other hand, they do exist as a separate case, and have instructions that treat them as an individual register.<p>There is precedent for all this too.<p>Take the 6809, having 8 bit A and B accumulators.  These can be addressed as D, a 16 bit register.<p>D is not generally counted as it&#x27;s own register because the D addressing does not point to anything new.<p>A and B are counted as two registers.<p>If somehow D brought new bits, say it was 24 bits long, then it would need to be counted.')
