Item(by='throwaway2245', descendants=None, kids=[25272204], score=None, time=1606865646, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25270156, text='I know it&#x27;s somewhat taboo to give cash, but surely cash is better than a gift card (with a note to spend it on their hobby, if you like)?<p>If the gift card value is too low to get the treat that I wanted, then I feel obligated to spend more money than I would have; if the gift card value is too high, then I have to buy things I don&#x27;t want. Or I wait until I actually need the gift card (as I suspect most people do), and risk the gift card expiring or being lost.<p>Cash creates none of these issues. I have always felt much more loved when I receive cash with a personal note, no matter how much cash it is.')
