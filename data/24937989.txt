Item(by='VoodooJuJu', descendants=None, kids=[24938124, 24938120], score=None, time=1604019390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24932466, text='The subject of climate change always sparks some interesting and complex technological solutions. But what if the best solution isn&#x27;t more technology, but less? What if, instead of <i>more</i> civilization, the solution is <i>less</i> civilization? What if the solution to surviving climate change isn&#x27;t &quot;consume <i>other product</i>&quot; or &quot;consume <i>other fuel source</i>&quot;, it&#x27;s &quot;don&#x27;t consume&quot;? Imagine if we are the last generation to experience the benefits of electricity and abundantly-stocked grocery stores. Imagine returning to a Bronze Age or Medieval European way of life. Is that disturbing? What if it&#x27;s the answer?')
