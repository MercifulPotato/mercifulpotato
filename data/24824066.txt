Item(by='WClayFerguson', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603092978, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24823827, text='Any change in direction is an acceleration (by definition). Even an object moving in a perfect circle at constant radians per second is nonetheless undergoing a constant non-zero acceleration just due to change in direction. Acceleration is any change in a velocity vector, including simply a change in direction, and requires a force (if the object has mass)')
