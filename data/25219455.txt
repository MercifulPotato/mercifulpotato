Item(by='knuthsat', descendants=None, kids=[25222217], score=None, time=1606394216, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25217499, text='From my experience, JVM can&#x27;t do much when there&#x27;s a deep stack.<p>I remember just refactoring all of foreach-loops&#x2F;iterators into for-loops and got insane speedups.<p>Functions with inefficient iterations would get optimized if called directly but deep in the call stack and nothing happens.<p>These kinds of things are impossible with C++.<p>It&#x27;s possible to write efficient Java but you have to not use some of the language features to do so.')
