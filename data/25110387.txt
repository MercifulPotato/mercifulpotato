He also was severely obese with a BMI around 37. Last year his SGPT and SGOT levels really spiked up drastically and doctor suggested it was the beginning of Fibrosis and asked him to reduce weight.<p>He started intermittent fasting 18:6 and religious intake of ~3 litres (250 ml per kg).
