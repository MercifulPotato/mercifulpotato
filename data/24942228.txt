Item(by='coldpie', descendants=None, kids=[24942322, 24943194, 24942394], score=None, time=1604066300, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24942190, text='This is a deep and complicated question, but the top-line answer is it&#x27;s the best way to prevent cheating (note I didn&#x27;t say perfect). A big part of these classic games is how the random events are generated. It would be fairly easy to modify the game in an emulator to more often generate ideal random events, giving you an edge over people playing on the real hardware. So the community agreed that for the purposes of the leaderboards in question here, the game must be played on real hardware.')
