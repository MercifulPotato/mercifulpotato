Item(by='playpause', descendants=None, kids=[24721737, 24721570], score=None, time=1602171308, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24716901, text='We have different definitions for “feeling instant”. Most clicks on HN take well over 2s for me on a 4g connection, and often about half a second even on fast broadband. Sure that feels snappy compared to most websites, but it is still a completely different experience to using an app, or indeed a website that has been designed to eliminate perceptible delays as opposed to making them shorter. These are separate (and sometimes even conflicting) design goals.')
