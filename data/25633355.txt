Item(by='derin', descendants=None, kids=[25633517], score=None, time=1609777254, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25633299, text='An incognito browser would ignore all client-side cookies, so the Slack web client would not try to - say - resume a previous user&#x27;s session or re-use any previously saved data.<p>Likewise, incognito mode will also ignore most cached web content, meaning all assets on the Slack web app will get loaded again from scratch. This &quot;clean state&quot; start could, theoretically, get around issues with old - potentially incorrect&#x2F;outdated - assets being loaded, even though that really shouldn&#x27;t happen under most circumstances.')
