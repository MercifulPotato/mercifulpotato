In fact, I didn&#x27;t even notice that it was a typo until you pointed it out, since my brain autocorrected it as I was reading.<p><i>To put forth</i> means &quot;to offer an opinion; to argue for something&quot;. <i>Codswallop</i> is a rare, somewhat archaic slang term meaning, basically, &quot;bullshit&quot;. Thus &quot;to put forth codswallop&quot; means &quot;to argue for bullshit&quot;.')
