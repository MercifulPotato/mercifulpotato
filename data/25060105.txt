Item(by='rsweeney21', descendants=None, kids=[25060165, 25061070], score=None, time=1605113954, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25059502, text='Marriage is correlated with economic security, but economic security does not necessarily cause more marriage. I think it&#x27;s more likely the reverse. Those who get married have a better shot at earning more.<p>There are a lot of benefits to marriage, especially if you have a dual income marriage. For example, if one of you loses their job, you go to 50% of your normal income, rather than 0%. This gives you more time to wait for a higher paying job, rather than taking the first thing that comes along.<p>The benefits of support and encouragement from a spouse are also real.')
