Item(by='anuila', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604696135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25010212, text='In reasonable jurisdictions, the lifetime of the product will be defined <i>reasonably.</i> The lifetime of a car will never be 3 months nor 3 years.<p>For the average cheap-ish product, the lifetime is defined as &quot;at least 2 years&quot; in the EU, at least for consumers.<p>If someone announces EOL too soon and the product is popular enough, someone <i>will</i> complain formally. This combination however rarely happens.')
