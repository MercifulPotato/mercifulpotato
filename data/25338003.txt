Item(by='falcolas', descendants=None, kids=[25338214], score=None, time=1607377458, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25337375, text='&#x27;Resigned&#x27; indicates an action on her part, and typically considered to be an amicable parting. &#x27;Fired&#x27; means an action on Google&#x27;s part, typically because of a perceived misdeed.<p>By calling it a resignation, Google is making it sound as if it was an amicable choice she made, not their judgement call about her actions.<p>Words matter. Word choices always matter. Especailly single words which come with a lot of built-in context.')
