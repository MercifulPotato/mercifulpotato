Whether you start counting a list at 0 or 1, if your language of choice supports [x,x) notation, that syntax will effect an empty interval, and [x,x+1) will effect a single cell, no matter whether your list indexes the first element as 0, or 1. The only difference is <i>which cell</i> [x,x+1) refers to, which is the part that keeps sparking the controversy.')
