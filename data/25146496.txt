Item(by='emmanueloga_', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605763311, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25146352, text='Encyclopedic knowledge can be a great tool, no question about it! I think of it as memoization for the brain.<p>It may well turn out to be that some problem you are attacking could be simplified if you only knew that piece of &quot;trivia&quot;, but if you don&#x27;t know it you need to spend some brainpower re-discovering the thing instead of attacking the main problem.<p>The way I think about the learning &#x2F; performance is a bit like an RPG game. Encyclopedic knowledge is just another bar to fill in, with the corresponding trade offs.')
