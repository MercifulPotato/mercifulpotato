Item(by='Hizonner', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610717940, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25790690, text='All apps get sandboxed storage without even asking. On Android at least, permission for &quot;storage&quot; means permission for an area shared between all apps. And a lot of apps dump stuff into it that you do not necessarily want every other app to have access to.<p>It&#x27;s always been that way.<p>What it really needs is some kind of compartments, so that you can share storage between X and Y, and between Z and W, but not between X and W.')
