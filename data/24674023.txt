Item(by='btschaegg', descendants=None, kids=[24675114], score=None, time=1601752241, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24673815, text='Addendum: Since I&#x27;m somewhat bored, anyway :)<p>I whipped up a simple shell script for the polling thing.<p>Usage: .&#x2F;traffic.sh &lt;pid&gt;<p>Copy-pastable for your terminal (dollar signs signify your prompt, obviously):<p><pre><code>  $ cat &lt;&lt;&#x27;EOF&#x27; &gt; traffic.sh\n  #!&#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin&#x2F;env bash\n  \n  pid=$1\n  set -euo pipefail\n  \n  while true; do\n      date +&#x27;Time: %H:%M:%S&#x27;\n      sed &#x27;&#x2F;:&#x2F;!d&#x27; &#x2F;proc&#x2F;$pid&#x2F;net&#x2F;dev\n      echo\n      sleep 1\n  done \\\n  | awk &#x27;\n      BEGIN { MB = 1024*1024.0; }\n  \n      &#x2F;^Time&#x2F; || &#x2F;^$&#x2F; { print $0; next; }\n  \n      seen[$1] {\n          rx_mbps = ($2  - rx[$1]) &#x2F; MB;\n          tx_mbps = ($11 - tx[$1]) &#x2F; MB;\n  \n          printf(&quot;%10s Rx: %8.1f MiB&#x2F;s, Tx: %8.1f MiB&#x2F;s\\n&quot;,\n              $1, rx_mbps, tx_mbps);\n      }\n  \n      { seen[$1] = 1; rx[$1] = $2; tx[$1] = $11; }\n  &#x27;\n  EOF\n  \n  $ chmod +x traffic.sh</code></pre>')
