Item(by='edko', descendants=None, kids=[24798902], score=None, time=1602832049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24797980, text='It depends on the application. I did a couple of projects with Rust&#x27;s web-view crate (which uses native Safari on MacOS, and native Edge on Windows). This gave me access to the full JS and the full Rust ecosystem. I think the possibilities were richer than if I had used Electron alone. For example, I was able to use the Tantivy crate, for very fast and powerful full-text search. All of this with a fraction of the executable size.')
