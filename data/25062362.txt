Item(by='semi-extrinsic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605125166, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25060312, text='Have you tried PETSc?\nIt does sparse (and dense) LU and Cholesky, plus a wide variety of Krylov methods with preconditioners.<p>It can be compiled to use MKL, MUMPS, or SuiteSparse if available, but also has its own implementations. So you could easily use it as a wrapper to give you freedom to write code that you could compile on many targets with varying degree of library support.')
