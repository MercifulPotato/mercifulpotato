Item(by='veddox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609544756, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25607779, text='I would argue that the role of a leader is to provide the coordination that effective cooperation requires - in a sense, to foster interdependence.<p>I believe that it is impossible for any group of people to work together without some form of leadership taking place. I also believe that the bigger the task, the more important it becomes to have good coordination and leadership.<p>Of course, that‘s not to say that there aren‘t plenty of „leaders“ out there who‘s idea of their job is to „get other people to do things for you“.')
