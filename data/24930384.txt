Item(by='doodpants', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603979107, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24929726, text='&quot;The successive ratios of the terms, i.e. 1&#x2F;1, 2&#x2F;1, 3&#x2F;2, 5&#x2F;3 ... tend to a number called the Golden Ratio by the Greeks.&quot;<p>Fun fact: take <i>any</i> two numbers (e.g. chosen randomly), and use them as the seeds for a Fibonacci-like sequence by summing the last two terms to generate the next term. The ratio of any two consecutive terms in that series will tend towards the golden ratio.')
