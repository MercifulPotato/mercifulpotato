Item(by='kortex', descendants=None, kids=[25350640], score=None, time=1607436602, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25344266, text='The trick is to find &quot;fault lines&quot; throughout your monolith - places where it&#x27;s easy to create separation of concerns and narrow interfaces. Maintain and document these like they are external APIs. Only pass simple data through it (serialized or anything easy to serialize).<p>When the time comes, cleave these chunks off and wrap them in RPC&#x2F;REST&#x2F;graphql.<p>It&#x27;s good form even for monoliths since it makes for an easy interface to test.')
