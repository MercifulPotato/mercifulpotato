Item(by='bhickey', descendants=None, kids=[24942818, 24943675], score=None, time=1604068415, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24942486, text='Hi, I wrote the linked post.<p>The most important thing to keep in mind is the rendering artifacts demonstrate that the video in generated using double buffering. In single buffering, the CPU writes to VRAM which is ultimately read and displayed on the CRT. The circuitry writing to VRAM is racing the circuitry that reads it.<p>With double buffering you have two video buffers in memory. The CPU writes to one while the other one is being read. While aging hardware can experience glitches, rewriting a game to use double buffering simply isn&#x27;t something that will occur by happenstance.')
