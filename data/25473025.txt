Item(by='tunesmith', descendants=None, kids=[25473613, 25473082], score=None, time=1608331662, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25455696, text='For music scores, an alternative to buying a wide-format laser printer (which would print 11x17) is to instead print to Legal size (8.5 x 14) and then go to Kinko&#x27;s and blow it up to 11x17.  It sounds a little funny because the dimensions aren&#x27;t quite right, but we did this in a film scoring class when preparing conductor scores for a full orchestra, and it worked out well.')
