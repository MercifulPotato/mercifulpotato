Item(by='dsr_', descendants=None, kids=[25988482], score=None, time=1612185606, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25988288, text='As a household backup device, rather than a NAS, this is actually pretty good.<p>Use mdadm RAID-10 across four spinning disks, or RAID-1 across a pair and have two slots free. If the board fails, you can plug them into any other Linux box with SATA ports available.<p>Most people have no better than 1Gb&#x2F;s ethernet available in their house anyway. One expects a backup or restore to take a while, but also not to be interactive.<p>It&#x27;s probably cheaper to repurpose an old desktop, but this will probably use less power, even when both are asleep.')
