Item(by='owenversteeg', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607665605, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25382210, text='Putting a few concrete examples here to help people really get this:<p>- Single adult earning $52k a year in NYC makes an extra $1k. -Only- considering taxes, that extra $1000 is actually an extra $603.<p>- Single adult earning $52k a year in Idaho makes an extra $1000, after taxes that&#x27;s an extra $634.<p>- Single adult working in NYC making 220k a year makes an extra $1k, that becomes $523 after taxes.<p>- Now let&#x27;s factor in benefits: Single parent of 2 children in America, earning nearly anywhere between $20k and $40k: earning an extra $1000 will earn them somewhere between $100 and -$500 after taxes and benefits. Yes, that&#x27;s right, you can earn $1000 extra and end up with _less_ money in your pocket! If you&#x27;re wondering how, take a look at the Atlanta Fed&#x27;s page on the benefits cliff.')
