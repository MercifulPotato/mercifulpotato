Item(by='antognini', descendants=None, kids=[25448720, 25454224], score=None, time=1608149773, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25447680, text='&gt; Study endgames before openings.<p>Growing up we had a copy of <i>The Art of Chess</i> by James Mason.  The book starts out with the endgame, goes into the midgame, and ends with openings.  The philosophy was that if you don&#x27;t understand how to play when there are four pieces on the board, how will you be able to play when there are 32?')
