Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=[24804524], score=None, time=1602878499, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24804340, text='Note that the test actually proposed (not the press release blurb) says:<p>&gt; The moderation practices of a provider of interactive computer services are politically biased if the provider moderates information provided by information content providers in a manner that [...] disproportionately restricts or promotes access to, or the availability of, information from a political party, political candidate, or political viewpoint<p>That means that any service that chooses to do something like suppress known conspiracy theories is going to fall afoul of the proposed changes.')
