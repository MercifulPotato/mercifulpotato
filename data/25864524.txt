I started doing this with two or three close friends a few years ago, and started ramping it up to more friends some time last year.<p>Not everyone is willing to commit to monthly, but it&#x27;s not as awkward as you might think.  You could do one or two manually scheduled, and then after the meeting say &quot;hey, want to make this a recurring get-together every [second thursday]?&quot;<p>Pro-tip: you may have better luck arranging a hangout time immediately after the end of the workday (depending on the geography between you and them), with you both parting ways to eat a late supper afterwards.  In my experience folks are more willing to commit to that schedule rather than give up their evening to meet up after supper.')
