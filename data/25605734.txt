I&#x27;ve read too many papers with grand new theories that hinged on an experiment testing the tiniest of tiny facets. All of these have been forgotten now, even by their creators.<p>Once you have enough experience, know the field inside out, and have gotten a bit of a name, you can write a book or edit a special edition of a journal in which you may set out your ideas.')
