Item(by='granra', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603729554, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24896965, text='You can trace log iptables and see first if the wireguard traffic is being blocked by some rule<p>`iptables -t raw -I PREROUTING -p udp --dport 51820 -j TRACE` (assuming default 51820 port) and look at dmesg or `journalctl -f`<p>If that is not being blocked then you can look at `iptables -t raw -I PREROUTING -s &lt;vps&#x27; wireguard ip&gt; -j TRACE` and try pinging or reaching a service on your home network and look at dmesg again.<p>Just don&#x27;t forget to remove these rules again as they will continue to log.<p>Beyond that, I&#x27;m not sure.')
