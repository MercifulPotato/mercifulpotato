Item(by='fastball', descendants=None, kids=[25767024], score=None, time=1610564248, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25766259, text='The problem is that the act of computation is generating heat, so you can&#x27;t just insulate your superconducting CPU, you need a way to dissipate the heat it <i>must</i> generate (this applies to all irreversible computations). This is difficult, because the only way to dissipate heat in space is  radiation (with conduction usually acting as a middleman), which is constrained by the surface area of your radiator.<p>So no, it probably wouldn&#x27;t be any easier in space.')
