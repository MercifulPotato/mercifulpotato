Item(by='js8', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607356545, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25332472, text='To add to the other answer, Shannon&#x27;s information is also sometimes called &quot;surprisal&quot;. And it doesn&#x27;t describe only what is not known, but rather what can be predicted. So if you can predict new information, it has low surprisal, even if it previously wasn&#x27;t known (with certainty).')
