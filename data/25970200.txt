Item(by='kevincox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612023139, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25970133, text='All (good) monospaced fonts are uniwidth, not all uniwidth fonts are monospaced.<p>Monospaced is important if you want multiple sequences of the same length to line up, uniwidth is less restrictive of the font (because &quot;l&quot; and &quot;w&quot; can be different widths) but allows you to bold without changing the visual length.')
