Item(by='brudgers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604340840, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24966727, text='The best feedback on business viability is to charge money from the beginning. Until people pay for the product, everything they say is just noise as it relates to the viability of the product.<p>The hard part of asking for money is that most people will say &quot;no.&quot; The easy part of &quot;freemimum&quot; is that you can pretend people will buy it later and rationalize continuing to work on the project.<p>Or to put it another way, the simplest thing that might work from a business perspective is charging money. And right now, you are thrashing around blindly with freemium which is harder to get to right, less informative, and produces less revenue.<p>If you don&#x27;t know what people <i>might</i> pay for, then you don&#x27;t know what problem you are solving. If you know what problem you are solving then you know what people <i>might</i> pay for and charging money gets you direct conversion of the <i>might</i> to a <i>will</i> or <i>won&#x27;t</i>.')
