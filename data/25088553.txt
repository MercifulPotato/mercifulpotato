This put Sky in an interesting position, as they only recently added 1080P support to their <i>NowTV</i> streaming subscription service, as an optional upgrade to their usual 720P. Having already long promoted their service as delivering <i>HD</i>, [0] they were clearly at a bit of a loss as to how to describe 1080P. They ended up calling it <i>full HD</i>.
