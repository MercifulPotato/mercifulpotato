Item(by='onecommentman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607163156, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25312447, text='The only proven approach to successful superforecasting.<p>1. Choose 2^N people.<p>2. Develop M binary forecasts, M &lt; N.<p>3. Send half the sample a forecast of True, the other half the sample a forecast of False.<p>3. Whatever occurred, True or False, select the sub population to which you sent the correct forecast for further forecasting.  Discard the other half.<p>4. Repeat the forecasting and culling until you have 2^(N-M) people remaining.<p>5. Announce to the 2^(N-M) that you are a superforecaster and milk them for all they are worth.')
