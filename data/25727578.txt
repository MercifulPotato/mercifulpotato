Item(by='souprock', descendants=None, kids=[25727670], score=None, time=1610364844, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25721292, text='Just what is gained by failing to loop forever? It seems like a really low-value optimization. Nearly always, the optimization would violate the programmer&#x27;s intent or it would change an ordinary bug into a confusing bug. An infinite loop is not very many bytes on any processor.<p>The standard may allow the generation of insane code, but a decent-quality compiler does not do so. The standard ought to be fixed.')
