Item(by='regalaxyLogic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604534568, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24994338, text='Suppose I have an `Order` object with a `paymentDetails` property.<p>Since I accept payments from Paypal, Bitcoin and Credit Cards , the `paymentDetails` object may need to have the following shape:<p><pre><code>  Order {\n    paymentDetails: payPalTransactionId | bitcoinWalletAddress | creditCardPayment\n  }\n</code></pre>\nAnd we can keep going further, where `creditCardPayment` is:<p><pre><code>  CreditCardPayment {\n    number: string\n    expiry_month: string\n    expiry_year: string\n    cvv: string\n  }\n</code></pre>\nSo having a type system that can say &quot;this property could be of this type OR this type OR this type&quot; is useful when you can&#x27;t model all your data into one type like int or string')
