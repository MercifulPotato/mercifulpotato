Item(by='quotemstr', descendants=None, kids=[25971218], score=None, time=1612027647, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25969886, text='Anything other than a pipenet will be fingerprintable at some level. A pipenet is a network in which all links run at constant utilization, with dummy traffic (indistinguishable from useful traffic) sent over the links during idle periods. Pipenets are of course inefficient, but everything else is going to reveal <i>some</i> kind of signal distinguishable from noise at <i>some</i> level.')
