Item(by='agf', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608176231, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25452056, text='I see two ways to get out of this. One is to learn to think of those things and doing them well, even though they&#x27;re not original, as part of the form. Like meter and rhyming might be part of a poem&#x27;s form; it&#x27;s about how you do them, not that you do them, since they&#x27;re always there.<p>The other is to consciously decide to ratchet down your consumption of this type of entertainment and put more effort into finding things that are more &quot;for you&quot; -- they don&#x27;t rely on the tropes you&#x27;re used to. Look for foreign content, other types &#x2F; genres of shows, and reduce your consumption so by the time you&#x27;ve watched as much as you used to in a year, several years have passed and the style has morphed a bit.')
