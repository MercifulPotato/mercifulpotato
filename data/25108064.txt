Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605501413, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25106232, text='I don&#x27;t know if the discovery and use of the Roaring Forties predates or postdates Australian settlement, but in the Age of Sail, you need to sail south of Cape of Good Hope, which puts you in a strong westward wind belt known as the Roaring Forties, which will spit you out on the southern coast of Australia, with the southeast being the easiest to reach (because it&#x27;s the most southern one). So in terms of sailing time, it&#x27;s easier to get to southern than northern Australia.')
