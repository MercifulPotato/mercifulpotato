Item(by='SilasX', descendants=None, kids=[24682880], score=None, time=1601835095, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24674454, text='In order for it to be anything like an ideal hash function that evenly distributes the keys across buckets, it must be a &quot;avalanche effect&quot; hash function that mixes each byte with all the others. If you limit it to O(1) elementary operations, you can no longer have it operate that way, and are back to assuming significant collisions and iteration over them.')
