Item(by='akerl_', descendants=None, kids=[25450754], score=None, time=1608161173, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25450061, text='The way to accommodate a new key size or algorithm is to say “v2 of the protocol will use $key_size and $cipher, and will not use $old_key_size or $old_cipher. Upgrade to this version due to $reason_old_setup_is_obsolete”. A casual observation of history will find that attempting to build negotiation of security features into a protocol is akin to opting in to a fun, optional class of major security vulnerability. For reference: SSL&#x2F;TLS, GPG.')
