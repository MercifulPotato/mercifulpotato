Item(by='bumby', descendants=None, kids=[25138251, 25137399, 25137434, 25137474], score=None, time=1605710937, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137205, text='There is a pervasive misconception that you are paid for how much value you bring. In a market economy, you are paid commensurate to how much you can negotiate. The value you bring (real or imagined) is <i>part</i> of that negotiation, but so are other factors.<p>It pains me to see people get frustrated by this fact.')
