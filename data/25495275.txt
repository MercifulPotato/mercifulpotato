Item(by='rwmj', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608561375, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25491665, text='Keep warm settings on rice cookers keep the rice above 60C to avoid bacterial or fungal growth.<p>I use the keep warm setting on a rice cooker for another reason: To make black garlic.  Take 20 bulbs of good quality garlic, put it in the rice cooker, set it on &quot;keep warm&quot; (NOT cook!) for two weeks without opening the lid.  Note the rice cooker will be useless for anything else after this because you&#x27;ll never get rid of the ingrained garlic smell.')
