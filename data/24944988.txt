Item(by='gusgordon', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604080525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24944279, text='The primary goal of RCV in the US is to address the hostile partisan divide. Making more parties viable is secondary.<p>When you have a ranked choice system (even if it&#x27;s only in a fraction of states), it incentivizes the candidates to not disparage each other. If candidate A attacks B, it means the B supporters will rank A lower. RCV draws candidates closer together.')
