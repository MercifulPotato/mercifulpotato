Item(by='LeviIsaac', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607610665, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25373869, text='Listen better.\nI mentioned how talkers tend to be more likable, and that’s true. Sometimes, over-communicating puts people at ease. But it’s also important to pause once in a while and listen. Good communicators take a breath once in a while! Likable people are always listeners who are curious to (genuinely) learn new things. The best communicators talk and talk–and then listen for a response. That makes them an office favorite.')
