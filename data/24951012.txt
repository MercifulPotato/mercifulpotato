Item(by='II2II', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604148614, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24950780, text='Are people willing to line up, or are they being pressured to line up?<p>In many cases, a person&#x27;s principles are clear from what they say and do.  That being said, a refusal to align one&#x27;s self with a movement is often construed as supporting the other side.  There are many reasons for refusing to align: a preference for independent thought, a realization that ideological movements change, an understanding that one&#x27;s own principles shift (while labels often stick), or simply not buying into some fundamental points.  None of that seems to matter when issues are polarized.')
