Item(by='elcritch', descendants=None, kids=[25594944], score=None, time=1609427749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25592821, text='Nim fits great on microcontrollers! Benefit is that it compiles to C and therefore can run anywhere C can. It also lets you reuse all the rest of the embedded C environment and build tools, which given the nature of embedded toolchains is handy for hitting the ground running. Rust&#x27;s standard library&#x2F;runtime binaries are really heavy compared to Nim&#x27;s.')
