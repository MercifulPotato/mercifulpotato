Item(by='clbecker', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607022672, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25293129, text='Toolfinder is just a simple way to endorse software for what it&#x27;s good at and to find tools based on what you need.<p>I always found it a slog to search for new software to do something I needed-- most recommendation sites just give you generic star ratings, or you have to dig through a whole article or comments to figure out if it does what you want.<p>I thought this idea would be a super simple way to rate tools for specific features or jobs they do rather than just rating the tool as a whole.')
