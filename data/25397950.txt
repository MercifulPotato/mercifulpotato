Item(by='BerislavLopac', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607777706, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25389681, text='Take advantage of the fact that, right now, all (software engineering) jobs are remote, for (pretty much) all companies. Even when the pandemic is over, many companies will keep the remote model as they had the chance to see its advantages -- or at the very least they will be open to be flexible when hiring remote engineers.<p>In other words, keep applying using the traditional methods (LinkedIn, standard job sites), and during interviews try to explore what are the company&#x27;s long-term views on remote work.')
