Item(by='sjansen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611203079, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25855224, text='In my opinion you&#x27;ve hit on Ubuntu&#x27;s biggest contribution to Free and Open Source software.<p>When I started using Linux a couple decades ago, &quot;we ship when it&#x27;s ready&quot; was the dominant FOSS approach, and I thought it was the most sensible approach.<p>Then I got my first job and experienced the realities of enterprise support and planning. Today I wish more projects were as predictable as Ubuntu, and I&#x27;m gratefully for every one that is.<p>Ubuntu is far from my favorite distro technically, but the predictability of its LTS releases makes it my recommendation for most uses.')
