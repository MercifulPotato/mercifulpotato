Item(by='mnahkies', descendants=None, kids=[25620880], score=None, time=1609666013, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25620592, text='I don&#x27;t know that this is true for all technical debt.<p>Generally there should be a motivation behind &quot;fixing&quot; technical debt that is expressible in terms of business value, whether that means faster page loads, more uptime, or ability to deliver X,y,z faster (or at all in extreme cases)<p>If you can&#x27;t express the trade-off of solving the debt in terms of business value then it&#x27;s going to be low priority by definition.<p>Another strategy for things that as engineers we know will make things better in the long run but show little immediate value can be to ask for a small amount of time per sprint&#x2F;week to invest - ideally you should still be able to articulate why something is worth changing though')
