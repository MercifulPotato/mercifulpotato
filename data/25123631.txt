Item(by='user-the-name', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605620736, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25122290, text='The IDFA is intentionally limited to prevent many malicious use cases. For instance, each vendor will see a different IDFA for the same device, so you can not collect IDFAs to track users across applications.<p>It is for tracking, but not all tracking is malicious. It is used for things like attributing an ad impression to an app install, to measure how effective an ad campaign is at getting people to install an app. This information doesn&#x27;t really tell you anything about individual users, but is still useful when aggregated.')
