Apparently, popular, long dead authors are supposed to support their great grandkids from those royalty checks.<p>I wrote a school book report on The Great Gatsby from the text hosted on Project Gutenberg. And when I had an interest in looking at the text again imagine my surprise to find it no longer listed.')
