Item(by='valuearb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605999261, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25169761, text='I’m a contractor so I use Apple notes, a new note for each build. My client has a project management system but they are a small team and not interested in keep it up to date.<p>On my personal projects I use clubhouse.io, it’s really low friction project management system to setup and use. I write detailed notes in each task on what is broken or needs to change and why, and when I go weeks without being able to touch the code it gives me a great snapshot of its state to get me back up to speed. Plus when I add a contributor it should help them get started far easier.')
