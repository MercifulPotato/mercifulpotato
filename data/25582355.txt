Item(by='dceddia', descendants=None, kids=[25582853], score=None, time=1609341541, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25581300, text='SQLite does have an in-memory option but you don’t want to use that here, otherwise it won’t actually be persistent. It’d get destroyed when the app restarts. You want SQLite to persist to a file.<p>I think OP meant stop as in “unsubscribe”. Ability to unsubscribe is required by law in a lot of places, I’d definitely want to have that in place.<p>This kind of use case falls under “transactional email” and there are lots of providers you can use for that. Mailgun, AWS SES, Postmark, Sendgrid, and a ton of others. They’re purpose-built for sending one-off emails like this.')
