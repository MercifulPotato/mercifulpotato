Identified resources in 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020 were 41, 47, 54, 62 and <i>80 million tonnes,</i> respectively.”  <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Lithium#Reserves" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Lithium#Reserves</a><p>There is little to suggest this trend will suddenly stop unless we simply stop looking.
