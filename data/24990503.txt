There are usually special methods for those cases when it is important to get this right - such as, you need to observe for a set amount of time, and you need to report also the species that you know about but that you don&#x27;t observe. For the popular &quot;backyard counts&quot;, the scientists working with the data knows that it has been collected using a method where these details are not as accurate.
