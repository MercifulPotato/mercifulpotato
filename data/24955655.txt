Some act as parents and good neighbours. Others do more than just throw shade – they’re brutal bullies to rival species. It’s a hard-knock life.</i><p><i>A book called The Hidden Life of Trees is not an obvious bestseller but it’s easy to see the popular appeal of German forester Peter Wohlleben’s claims – they are so anthropomorphic.
