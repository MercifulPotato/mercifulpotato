Parker Lewis explains[0]:<p>&gt; [Bitcoin] is backed by the only thing that backs any form of money: the credibility of its monetary properties. Money is not a collective hallucination nor merely a belief system.<p>An interpretation:<p>Money is an emergent property of human cooperation to allocate capital, labour, and limited resources. (i.e. economics). Productivity is the use of time and energy in the satisfaction of human preferences.<p>What are the properties a money, be it gold, cowrie shells, baseball cards, nudes, exploits, fiat currency (legal tender), must strive to meet for humans to recognise it as &quot;a money&quot;: durability, portability, divisibility, uniformity, limited supply (scarcity), and acceptability.
