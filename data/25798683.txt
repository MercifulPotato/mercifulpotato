Item(by='danudey', descendants=None, kids=[25799211], score=None, time=1610757779, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25796384, text='On the other hand, DBs call fsync() very often, which would presumably trigger this frequent &quot;stop the world, write the data&quot; behaviour vastly more than otherwise, which could make your I&#x2F;O very stuttery.<p>My solution has always been to just add a RAID controller with a battery-backed write cache, and then to disable barriers on ext4 and switch to journal=writeback. The data gets written to the cache &quot;instantly&quot;, meaning less risk of data corruption (either at DB or filesystem level) from crashes or power outages. Saves having to worry about any of this sort of stuff.')
