Item(by='eyelidlessness', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608663588, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25503750, text='I think this is where I’ve been most at odds with common OOP approaches (apart from the common practice of widespread mutability). An interface should be an abstraction defining what a given operation (function, module) needs from input to operate on it and produce output, and nothing more. Mirroring concrete types with an interface isn’t abstraction, it’s just putting an IPrefix on concrete types to check a design pattern box.')
