Item(by='ricw', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610907279, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25813091, text='I couldn’t emphasise this more. Stick to the YC mantra: always talk to users. Ask them what they want or have problems with, quantitatively (questionnaire style) and qualitatively (talk to them in person, real user testing).<p>Added a new feature? Don’t assume it’s well designed. Assume something is wrong with it and that it can and should be further optimised and sweat the details. Details matter.<p>Unless you sweat the details, users will notice.')
