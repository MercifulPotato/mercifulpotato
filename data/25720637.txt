Markets and users are stochastic with no one really knowing what users are going to ask for, nor what competitors release and cause users to demand, well designed or not.<p>What a lot of these conversations boil down to is finding the sweet spot between over&#x2F;under engineering which is subjective. Are we wasting time solving problems that dont matter or are we shipping with a &#x27;just enough&#x27; type of mentality and risk exposing our users to buggy software?<p>No one likes to work with bad code but if its siloing itself into inconsequential CRUD apps or frivilous games&#x2F;social media it&#x27;s just annoying to experience&#x2F;work with.
