Item(by='adamkl', descendants=None, kids=[25471470], score=None, time=1608313789, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25469657, text='Agree.<p>I’m a huge fan of this approach when developing complex UIs, but you can definitely run into “state explosion”.<p>When you rigorously enumerate all the implicit states in your system you quickly realize that your system can have a <i>lot</i> of states.<p>It’s a balancing act. How much rigour do you need vs. how much code are you willing to deal with.<p>The implicit states will always be there whether you make them explicit via a state chart or not. But for simple things, it may not be worth the overhead.')
