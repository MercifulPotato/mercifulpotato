Item(by='jacquesm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609749229, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25628110, text='The problem with specialized databases is that people use them as a starting point at the beginning instead of realizing that that is a premature optimization which they will likely come to regret later on. Initially you are <i>much</i> better off by choosing a standard relational database (Postgres,MySQL,MSSQL, pick your favorite flavor) and then, when you <i>really</i> have to you may have to add something that is specialized. So unless there are hard technical constraints right off the bat (which is almost never the case) stick to simple.')
