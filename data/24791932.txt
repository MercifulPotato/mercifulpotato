He probably has some sort of plan, but it isn&#x27;t as fine tuned as Sanderson&#x27;s I suspect. Sanderson does multiple drafts as well, which works with how I write. My first draft may be complete trash, but getting it on paper helps me a lot in at least getting the ideas out and then I can fine tune or start over if I&#x27;m not happy at all.<p>But yes, I think Sanderson is very much a &quot;I write regardless and I&#x27;ll refine it over time&quot; type of person and doesn&#x27;t agonize over the little details until it&#x27;s time to actually do that.')
