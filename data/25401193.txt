Roughly, an object with __slots__ is a mutable counterpart of namedtuple. Both need to map a name into a field&#x2F;slot index. Of course, there&#x27;re ways to optimize that lookup (which primitive Python implementations, like CPython, may not do, but other implementations definitely do).')
