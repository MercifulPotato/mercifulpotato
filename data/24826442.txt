Item(by='adamnemecek', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603116281, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24825065, text='It makes more sense when you use tau as tau is a full rotation, not half rotation. e^tau=1.<p>Also I think that i is not interesting because i * i=-1, but because i * conjugate(i)=1. I never saw the -1 in the context of complex numbers but if you say it&#x27;s 1, it&#x27;s easy to see the unit circle. Similarly, with split-complex numbers, you have j * conjugate(j)=-1. Split complex numbers are about mirror reflection so the -1 makes sense.')
