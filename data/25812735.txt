The misspelling may signal the writer has not read much on the subject. Russian literature is influential enough in world literature that the mistake is a big tell, feel free to consult any lit department.<p>One of the common actions taken when analyzing a work of literature is writing out and mapping the main characters. Anyone that has done that has wondered about the &quot;Russian females often get an a at the surname end&quot; thing. It&#x27;s standard to address it in Russian lit studies, because it confuses non-native speakers.')
