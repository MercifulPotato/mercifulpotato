Item(by='faitswulff', descendants=None, kids=[25014940], score=None, time=1604751485, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25013959, text='I just set up a wildcard email forwarder on my domain and set up an email address for every domain or website I sign up for (e.g. ycombinator.com@mydomain.net). Keeps everything nicely separated, logins are predictable, and I can track who got my email from where.<p>It makes certain things more complicated, like authenticating against certain services, or if someone sends and invitation to one of your domain emails, but it works just fine 95% of the time.')
