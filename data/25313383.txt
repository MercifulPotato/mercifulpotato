Item(by='cloudhead', descendants=None, kids=[25314394, 25313757], score=None, time=1607164049, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25313184, text='The design of GitHub means that your canonical upstream is only accessible through the internet, and so are your issues, PRs etc. If you&#x27;re offline, you can&#x27;t access it, and if GitHub is down, same story.<p>Radicle, on the other hand, keeps all of this replicated locally, so push&#x2F;pull works offline, and the code&#x2F;social artifacts are replicated asynchronously, when you are online, but you don&#x27;t need to be online to work with your project(s).')
