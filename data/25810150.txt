Item(by='md_', descendants=None, kids=[25810302, 25810586], score=None, time=1610886228, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25809604, text='PGP is incompatible with both forward secrecy and metadata secrecy.<p>If you only want to keep your message body confidential (or authenticated), PGP is reasonable. But it provides a strictly lesser degree of secrecy than modern e2ee messaging applications.<p>In fact, depending on your threat model, modern SMTP-TLS (which is not e2ee, but does provide some metadata confidentiality and forward secrecy) may in fact be an improvement over PGP.')
