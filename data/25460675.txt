Item(by='EricE', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608240432, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25458098, text='Radial fans (also known as squirrel cage blower, or visualize a water wheel from an old fashioned water driven flour mill) will move air more quietly than an axial fan in a box fan.  Levoit on Amazon are some great, relatively inexpensive units and when the HEPA filter it comes with needs replacing I use use furnace filters cut down to size.  If you want it to last a bit longer, you can use quilt batting as a pre-filter before the furnace filter to catch the big stuff and extend the life of the furnace filter.')
