Item(by='ar-nelson', descendants=None, kids=[25784677], score=None, time=1610663359, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25783648, text='I have two recipes that use aquafaba: a grain-free biscuit recipe and a grain-free flatbread recipe. A lot of gluten-free breads use eggs because they need a binder to replace the gluten, but the eggs taste, well, eggy. The aquafaba makes it lighter, and not eggy, so it seems more like actual bread.')
