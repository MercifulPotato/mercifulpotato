Item(by='amai', descendants=None, kids=[25894218, 25894190], score=None, time=1611511132, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25887373, text='I haven&#x27;t done 100 coding interviews.<p>But here is a suggestion to fix the interviews: Let the candidate bring their own code!<p>First this gives you a unique inside in the programming style the candidate prefers. Second the candidate knows the code very well and is probably more relaxed when he needs to answer your questions about the code (given that she&#x2F;he didn&#x27;t copy-paste the code from somewhere).<p>It is similar to designers showing a portfolio of their work to a potential employer. So if this works for designers, why not for programmers also? It is called &quot;The Art of Computer Programming&quot; for a reason. ;-)')
