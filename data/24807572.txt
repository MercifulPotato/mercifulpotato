That&#x27;s why it is a paradox, kind of similar to the <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Card_paradox" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Card_paradox</a><p>Of course you can argue about the details in particular situation, just like you can explain that the card is not really a paradox, as the statements were written by different people, etc.<p>The main reason why I pointed out the story is that it is a certain answer to the question, why are not rational people (more) successful?
