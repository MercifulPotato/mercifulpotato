Item(by='berkes', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610801234, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25801037, text='But if you &#x27;have it in the database&#x27; it will still be smeared across your app too.<p>&#x27;Putting stuff in one place&#x27;, regardless of that place, is hard. Necessary, but hard. And it requires tradeoffs.<p>If you need a &#x27;sorry this username is taken&#x27; friendly error, your app needs to handle constraint errors from your DB. Even if only on the translation layer. At which point you&#x27;ll have it duplicated on multiple layers, add tight coupling between layers, or need to forego that message and e.g. settle with a generic exception instead.')
