Item(by='Twisol', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603563880, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24880698, text='Inheritance is a kind of implicit adapter pattern -- instead of implementing a (complex or low-level) interface directly, you can implement a slightly simpler or better-tuned interface, and the inheritance machinery takes care of adapting that interface to the one you ultimately need.<p>When used in that context, it&#x27;s truly excellent. I suspect your example from Django fits this pretty well. But it&#x27;s far too easy to abuse for general code reuse, which is a deep and alluring tar pit.')
