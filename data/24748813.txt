Item(by='cbkeller', descendants=None, kids=[24749843], score=None, time=1602448005, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24748685, text='Well, the promise of overhead-free multiple dispatch is that you don&#x27;t have to choose between a general solution with poor performance and a specific solution with good performance. You can have all the specific solutions you need in one place and automatically dispatch to the right one without any extra effort.<p>I know it sounds too good to be true, but my limited experience so far is that it <i>really is true</i>, with surprisingly few pitfalls (mostly, you have to think about <i>type instability</i>, which will be new to most, but is really not that hard to avoid).')
