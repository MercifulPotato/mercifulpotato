Item(by='amluto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608164976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25450185, text='Not necessarily. You can also tie algorithms to keys. For example, if you want to verify a signature, it’s perfectly reasonable for the public key to actually be (algorithm, private key as defined by that algorithm).  What you don’t want to do is put the algorithm in a message that is potentially controlled by an attacker.')
