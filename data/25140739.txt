Item(by='jw1224', descendants=None, kids=[25141078], score=None, time=1605725487, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25140618, text='Starting therapy earlier this year was possibly the best thing I’ve done for myself in years. It took about 3 months of weekly sessions to properly “notice” what a difference it was making, but now it’s blindingly obvious, and I’m so glad I did it.<p>I found my therapist through <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bacp.co.uk&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.bacp.co.uk&#x2F;</a> — I’m not sure which country you’re in, but there’s bound to be similar directories out there.<p>I set their filters to find therapists who deal with issues relevant to me, and within a practical distance. After that, I just systematically went through each of their profiles, read their bios, and narrowed it down to a shortlist of 10.<p>I then picked two based on gut instinct. I had an introductory session with them both, and immediately “clicked” with one of them, who I’ve been seeing ever since.')
