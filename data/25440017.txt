Item(by='bryanrasmussen', descendants=None, kids=[25440066], score=None, time=1608104018, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25432870, text='I think if you have a small team and are developing an API for your application&#x27;s needs probably things can be done as well or better in Rest, but if you have an API that needs to face to third parties or a very large organization with APIs that need to be exposed to multiple frontend teams or a product like a CMS that frontend teams that are not part of your organization then the benefits of GraphQL will often quickly outweigh Rest.<p>This is a feeling on the problem space, and not based on any studies though, as I&#x27;m unaware of any studies trying to determine this.')
