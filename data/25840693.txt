Item(by='chrononaut', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611100597, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25840204, text='The trouble in my opinion comes when these social networks begin to promote content to the user algorithmically. My Twitter feed is filled with content from users I do not follow. It makes up the majority of my feed. Likely, even if it was content that I was following, the most &quot;engaging&quot;, or oftentimes &quot;enraging&quot; content, would be the content shown first.<p>If social networks were largely just content crafted by the people I follow, it would then be more representative towards a digital representation of the in-person social structures that would or do exist.<p>They make much less money that way though. Your average user would probably also use it less.')
