The Caspian Sea Monster was not the endpoint of ekranoplane engineering.  It deals well with ~1m wave height.  For the Black Sea &#x2F; Mediterranean sea or the Baltic, a larger version would be required, and for the Atlantic or Pacific, larger still. You could plausibly even fly it over Arctic sea ice fields without bergs.<p>Boeing was throwing around proposals for a ground effect aircraft several times this large in the early 2000&#x27;s, with the largest bumping up against the weight range of naval destroyers at ~5000 tonnes.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Boeing_Pelican" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Boeing_Pelican</a>')
