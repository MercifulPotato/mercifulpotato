Item(by='martin__', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610753836, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25797914, text='The point of the example is that in Zig, that statement is exactly as simple as it looks.<p>What looks like a field reference (`c.d`) is just a field reference; there are no getters or @property functions that are doing more complicated things.<p>And similarly with the addition operator, that plus sign is just addition and doesn&#x27;t call a function somewhere else.<p>These abstractions are considered useful by the designers of other languages, but they are specifically excluded in Zig. The benefit of not having them being that it&#x27;s easier to follow the execution flow of the program.')
