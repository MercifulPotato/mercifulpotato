Item(by='MikeKusold', descendants=None, kids=[25064433, 25061760], score=None, time=1605119973, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061148, text='You’ve haven’t used those much then. Pumping water in the backcountry is a giant PITA. Even the clearest mountain stream ends up clogging the filters after a few liters and require you to back flush them. We despise using them and will often just boil unpurified water for our dinners.<p>Sawyer filters tend to be our favorite. Although if you only want to do a Nalgene amount of water and don’t mind some grit, then Steripens are a good option too.')
