Item(by='Animats', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601941437, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24692350, text='Life can&#x27;t be simpler yet because systems do not yet understand the consequences of their actions.<p>The first step to advanced simplicity is making it easy to do again what you did already. This is the &quot;reorder last shopping cart&quot; level.<p>The next step is to have a good understanding of what people are likely to re-order, and how often. Some marketing systems are starting to get this. If, last time, you ordered a hammer and some nails, next time you should be offered more nails, but not more hammers.<p>One obstacle to advanced simplicity is cross-vendor integration. This is why more than one remote is often necessary. There are entertainment systems that self-configure when plugged in, but only from one vendor.')
