Good people want to do a good job. An engineer designing a bridge doesn’t need to use it every day to be incentivised to do a good job; designing a bridge is an intellectual pursuit that is quite a bit beyond simple incentives.<p>The downside of this “skin in the game” approach is that you skip specifying things properly and end up with systems that <i>only</i> your devs can configure and upgrade.')
