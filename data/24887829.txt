Item(by='pedrosorio', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603645672, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24887700, text='If they were wrong, but “less wrong” than all others, you should pick their model (unless you have an oracle, because the alternative - flipping a coin or “relying on your intuition” is rarely better).<p>Also, it doesnt make sense to look at a single prediction to evaluate a model.<p>Out of all the predictions they have made (did you look at individual state predictions?), how many were correct (and how confident were they?) - how many were wrong (and how close to 50% were they?).<p>That is how you evaluate a model (aka cross entropy)')
