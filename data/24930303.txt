Later, I bought a programmable GBA cartridge and a tool that could write to it.  There were a Z80 emulators on the GBA to play the original gameboy games, and there were packagers available to make your Z80 programs, so I ported over my &quot;columns&quot; clone I made for zshell on the TI-85.')
