Item(by='jrockway', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603288271, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24847012, text='OpenTelemetry is just an API for instrumenting your software.  For actually collecting and viewing your traces, you&#x27;ll need an actual implementation -- Jaeger and Zipkin are some open source options.<p>If you develop an open source project and one of your goals is &quot;I sure wish my users could use Datadog instead of being forced to use an open source thing!&quot; then OpenTelemetry is a project you should keep an eye on.  The goal is to make the instrumentation agnostic to the underlying provider.')
