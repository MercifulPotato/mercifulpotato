Item(by='Pfhreak', descendants=None, kids=[25251398, 25251427], score=None, time=1606719635, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25250978, text='This policy is fine, easy to implement, and discourages the sociopathic behavior of ICEing charging stops.<p>That said, it&#x27;s not going to fix the problem. The root cause is cars -- both electric and petrol. Yes, EVs are an improvement over petrol cars. They decouple energy generation and transportation, so they will get cleaner with time.<p>But fundamentally we should be looking at ways to reduce the number of cars. Parking is a blight in urban areas, trains and public transport are massively better people movers, and even EVs produce significant pollution from their production and tire wear.')
