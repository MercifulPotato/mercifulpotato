Item(by='RcouF1uZ4gsC', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604942432, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25031483, text='For me, it is clay tablet and stylus to really get rid of distractions. Even with pen and paper, I can find myself doodling. However, with a clay tablet I am focused. As a benefit, the process of going out and collecting the clay and forming it into a tablet gives me time to organize my thoughts. Then when I do put my thoughts down, I can bake it it the oven and know that it will last a few thousand years. Pen and paper seems so ethereal in comparison.')
