Item(by='joshspankit', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610986367, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25823083, text='With sudden power loss, the difference is that any single write may be in the cache for longer than the time it would have taken to write it. In the case of no cache with a power loss there is only a single possible failure: power loss during write. With cache that expands to two possible failures: Power loss during write from cache, and power loss with unwritten data <i>in</i> the cache.')
