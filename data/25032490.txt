You communicate more if you are better at the technical parts. If you can solve most of the problem in your head and in that way predict most of the things you need before you start working you just go and ask for that information from everyone. However if you are worse on the technical side you will need more time working on the task before you have anything to talk about, and you might even miss some of the issues completely.<p>Forcing the second kind to communicate more wont make him better, it will make him worse.
