Item(by='walrus01', descendants=None, kids=[25378363, 25378537], score=None, time=1607630749, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25377124, text='One could also say that all of Gibson and Sterling&#x27;s early novels were warnings, describing in some detail near future cyberpunk dystopias.<p>When reading Neuromancer and Count Zero, the cumulative sum of the evocative description of &quot;the sprawl&quot; and the post war environment led me to form a fairly bleak mental picture of the place. And floating above all that an ultra elite class of the 1% of the 1% (sensenet stars, the tessier-ashpools, etc).<p>Islands in the Net also paints a picture of radically even more unequal wealth distribution, coupled with massive economic disruptions and civil wars in developing nations.')
