Item(by='simonblack', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611187481, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25848329, text='Having used a Pi4B 4GB for a few weeks while setting it up a webserver and UPS controller, I can honestly say that it was just as good, if not better, than several desktops and laptops that I have used in the recent past for everyday stuff.<p>Maybe it would fall down as a development machine which needs heavy compilation, or as a video editor, but I feel it would suit most people, most of the time.<p>(Many people use low-cost laptops, which probably have as innards something which is not much better than the innards of a Pi4B. They also have a keyboard and screen which is not supplied with the Pi4B so they seem &#x27;more complete&#x27; than a Pi.)')
