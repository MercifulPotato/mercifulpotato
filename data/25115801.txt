Item(by='Karunamon', descendants=None, kids=[25116806], score=None, time=1605554339, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25115397, text='As I understand it, DMCA claim fraud requires <i>legally proving bad faith</i>, which is a quite high bar to clear.<p>There are no provisions for negligence (which is what most of these claims probably amount to - you could even make a good argument for depraved indifference, but there are no provisions for that either) or mistakes, it has to be intentionally fraudulent. Even if it was a completely BS takedown, you&#x27;re left with proving malice rather than error.')
