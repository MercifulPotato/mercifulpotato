Item(by='briangray', descendants=None, kids=[25194460], score=None, time=1606178109, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25188542, text='I believe the key benefit of small games is not the minimalism or short duration, but the forced condensing of core gameplay. Essentially bundling the fun without the excess fluff. I&#x27;ve heard developers constantly note that features will be dropped and your favorite implementation will hit the chopping block when making games. In the case of small games, you&#x27;re turning this idea into a feature. Finding what can be stripped away to focus on engagement. Not what massive world can I develop to creature a robust experience, but what bits of the world can I shave to make a fulling experience.')
