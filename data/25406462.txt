Item(by='pansa2', descendants=None, kids=[25406586], score=None, time=1607866637, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25405645, text='&gt; <i>Please note that MicroPython is different from the Python that runs on a computer.</i><p>I once considered embedding MicroPython into a (software) product myself, but was dissuaded by the number of people who didn’t like being limited to a subset of Python.<p>Instead I used Lua, which is much less widely-known but is small enough to embed the whole language.<p>There’s an interesting trade-off between embedding MicroPython (“it’s the language you know, but you need to learn which bits are missing”) and Lua (“you need to learn the language, but it’s the same everywhere”).')
