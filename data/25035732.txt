Item(by='j45', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604935774, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25035156, text='It&#x27;s very true that everyone&#x27;s ideal formula and variables are different.<p>Narrative and anecdotal writing allows one to take from it what you like, as trying things out works better for some than understanding it all to begin with and it shouldn&#x27;t be discounted just because it may not work for all.<p>Conversely, it might be worth considering the importance of the reader to learn to extract what&#x27;s good for them out of any reading and not expect everything to be curated just for them.<p>Getting hung up on how a piece of writing is not perfectly catered for acceptance by the reader is likely going to leave a lot of nuggets, value and insights missed.<p>The comment above could be taken as merely a counteract of pushing another interpretation of it being imposing and presumptuous.  Still there&#x27;s many folks who don&#x27;t take this writing style as imposing and presumptuous.')
