Item(by='barrkel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602689058, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24777036, text='Don&#x27;t forget that it&#x27;s not a single instance game.<p>The more Netflix pulls shows, the more it develops a reputation for pulling shows.<p>That decreases viewer investment in new shows, as they&#x27;re incentivized to wait and see if the show lasts, or whether they&#x27;re going to be left with narrative tension and no payoff after a cancellation.<p>And with decreased viewer investment in new shows, more shows get cancelled because the numbers don&#x27;t look good. It&#x27;s pro-cyclical and it burns goodwill.')
