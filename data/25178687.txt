If people could live forever they may prohibit other younger and genetically different people from reproducing and introducing variety in the gene pool.<p>Certainly having wealthier people live forever now would be bad from a social perspective. Time is really the biggest factor in acquiring wealth and influence. If that goes unchecked then that power can become out of control. Realistically I dont think it was that different thousands of years ago.')
