I found it advantageous to do so, but I still don&#x27;t find this to be a good comparison.<p>The thing is, no matter which keyboard layout you use, there is the same amount of muscle memory required to be learned.<p>To contrast, a piano requires 12 times as much muscle memory as a Janko keyboard.<p>Learning to play a piano is <i>hard</i>, and it is made <i>significantly</i> easier by simply changing the physical keyboard layout.')
