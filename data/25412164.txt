I was glad to finally get a semblance of modern programming, but the old RPG veterans advised against using it, since the new fancy features often didn&#x27;t work so well, and anyway you can write anything you want without it.<p>After 14 months, my contract wasn&#x27;t extended. Maybe the complaint that I could be seen sleeping at my desk sometimes were a factor.<p>You young kids don&#x27;t know how good you have it...')
