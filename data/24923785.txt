There are four other endemic coronaviruses. Most people catch them as children and build up some immunity, so even if they&#x27;re reinfected later the symptoms are usually less severe. But they can be deadly to immunocompromised patients.<p>There is some strong circumstantial evidence that the 1889 &quot;Russian flu&quot; pandemic wasn&#x27;t caused by influenza at all but rather by the emergence of HCoV-OC43.
