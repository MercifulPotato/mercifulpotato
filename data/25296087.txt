Item(by='xupybd', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607034270, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25292255, text='I have seen large database heavy apps. They are the cleanest I&#x27;ve ever worked with. I&#x27;ve had no trouble composing SQL. I can&#x27;t think of one example where this has been an issue. You need to rely on views, functions and tables to store your business rules and look up the rules.<p>The big down side I see is the learning curve. Writing business logic in the database is not easy and not easy to maintain. It can take half a day to grow a large function. Even longer to debug sometimes. But you learn to structure things well.')
