There are benefits to both, but they are by no means required.<p>Don&#x27;t worry about premature optimization and how you are going to scale your business with or without investors. Build that MVP and see if you&#x27;re building something worthwhile. If you are and can demonstrate that people are actually interested you shouldn&#x27;t have a hard time finding someone interested in investing.<p>The most useful advice I can say is just start.
