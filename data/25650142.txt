Item(by='ako', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609876408, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25648934, text='Lock-in is not the only reason: on-prem&#x2F;on-pc deployments are more expensive as they have to consider more scenarios. How you install it, what other software is running, what software you want to combine it with (e.g., what database you want to run).<p>On-prem&#x2F;on-pc is also harder to debug and support, and harder to keep customers on the latest release, so you have to maintain more releases.<p>Overall cloud makes sense from a business perspective because it&#x27;s cheaper and takes less effort, so you can dedicate your developers to add valueable features to your software instead of dealing with customer specific situations.')
