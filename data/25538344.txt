Item(by='shalmanese', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608923483, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25535342, text='The average person has a really poor idea of what profit means because they analogize between their personal finances and a company&#x27;s finances.<p>What profit means for a company is essentially: &quot;Across my entire company, of all the things I could be doing, I couldn&#x27;t find a single initiative that I believe will yield me a risk adjusted ROI that is greater than just leaving the money in a bank account.&quot;<p>Growing companies shouldn&#x27;t be making a profit because they should be limited by their ability to execute on attractive growth opportunities fast enough. Mature companies can make profits because they&#x27;ve reached the limits of their attractive growth and now can be focused on harvesting the rewards.')
