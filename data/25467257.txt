Item(by='Dumblydorr', descendants=None, kids=[25467597], score=None, time=1608301016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25465932, text='It&#x27;s actually hard to practice concentrating with a game or active activity. If you truly want to meet your mind, sit down and do nothing, i.e. meditate. You&#x27;ll notice your mind racing, and you&#x27;ll see just why it&#x27;s hard to concentrate on anything. You can then practice letting go of intervening thoughts, without self hate, and just observe the breath, sounds around, but in a passive, calm manner, not reactionary or impulsive with the attention.')
