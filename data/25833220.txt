The Latin for &quot;above&quot; is <i>supra</i> or <i>super</i>.<p>&gt; <i>ultra-</i>, prefix:<p>&gt; 1. Signifying ‘lying spatially beyond or on the other side of’<p>&gt; 2a. With adjectives, signifying ‘going beyond, surpassing, or transcending the limits of’ (the specified concept).<p>&gt; Etymology: Latin <i>ultrā</i> beyond, employed as a prefix in the post-classical <i>ultrāmundānus</i> ultramundane, and the later <i>ultrāmarīnus</i> ultramarine, and <i>ultrāmontānus</i> ultramontane.<p>- Oxford English Dictionary Online')
