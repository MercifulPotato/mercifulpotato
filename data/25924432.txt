In the face of rising extremism and disorder Facebook effectively has to step in and <i>do something</i> to their best ability because in general the US has very few tools to deal with this at a democratic level.<p>When I see pictures of someone dressed as a shaman on the dais of the senate and lawmakers cowering behind benches it&#x27;s very weird to me to draw the conclusion that the pressing issue is too much authority and not enough freedom of expression.')
