Item(by='ponker', descendants=None, kids=[24723129], score=None, time=1602178610, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24721438, text='For me it’s just a hobby but plenty of businesses still use typewriters. Any time there is a international bureaucratic process like “Affidavit of intellectual property for imported electronics” when shipping between two countries that don’t have huge shipping volumes (say Ecuador to Senegal or whatever) you will see the typewriters come out because bureaucrats often only trust the true rubber stamped copies.<p>The IBM typewriters are rock solid enough that they’ll be a supply for the next several decades. There are mothballed Selecteics in attics that for $2-300 can be made good as new.')
