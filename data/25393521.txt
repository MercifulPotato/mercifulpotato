Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=[25397529, 25397420], score=None, time=1607731637, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25385333, text='Consider the use of capability tokens instead of username&#x2F;password pairs to grant access to features. This would allow someone to sponsor someone else without sharing any information.<p>Mojeek generates a signed token that IS authorization, you match it against a list of valid tokens, with no other information required.<p>This could be used to federate subscriptions, and hosting of front ends such that they could stand alone without having any knowledge of the users, while only serving valid users.<p>Flickr has something like this that grants access to an otherwise private portfolio, yet the user can revoke it if necessary, without ever sharing passwords, usernames, etc.')
