It runs on FreeBSD (x86, x86-64), Linux (x86, x86-64, PowerPC and ARM), Mac OS X (x86, x86-64, and PowerPC), NetBSD (x86 and PowerPC), OpenBSD (x86 and PowerPC), Dragonfly BSD (x86-64), and SunOS (x86-64 and Sparc).<p>Source:  <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;9fans.github.io&#x2F;plan9port&#x2F;man&#x2F;man1&#x2F;intro.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;9fans.github.io&#x2F;plan9port&#x2F;man&#x2F;man1&#x2F;intro.html</a>')
