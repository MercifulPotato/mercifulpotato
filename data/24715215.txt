Item(by='Matthias247', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602125930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24713318, text='Even if you can achieve comparable performance on N concurrent TLS connections (which I actually think you can), you have a high additional cost of establishing those N connections.<p>For a lot of systems the TLS handshake is the most expensive part of an operation. Reducing the amount of TLS handshakes by N can literally reduce cost by up to N in those systems.')
