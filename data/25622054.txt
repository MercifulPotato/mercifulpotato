If one&#x27;s domain name points to an untrusted server, a very fundamental mistake has been made and no amount of configuration can fix the issues associated with it.<p>Webmasters we have two things in control:<p>1. Their domain name. It should point to trusted servers only.<p>2. Their web server. It should be configured to return successful responses only for domain names they care about and no response&#x2F;error for everything else.')
