Item(by='Memosyne', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604552918, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24993123, text='If you have colorful icons you&#x27;ll need to duplicate the resources to account for light and dark variants unless you prefer sub-optimal legibility. If you emphasize color as a method of representing state you&#x27;ll need to adjust by shipping images for each state value. With monochrome icons the emphasis is placed on shape so you can efficiently render the whole icon in a different color without obfuscating its meaning.')
