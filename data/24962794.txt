Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604267126, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24961870, text='I’m fully in support of open hardware, and I’d support legislation to make unlocking mandatory.<p>However, I’m much less optimistic about the use cases you list:<p>“media players, portable terminals, IoT consoles, instrumentation interfaces, retrogaming consoles, in-vehicle dash screens”<p>As far as I can see, there is essentially zero demand for any of these beyond hobbyists, since they can all be done much better by one’s <i>current</i> phone.<p>For me, the big reason for open hardware is that Android and iOS are nowhere near meeting the potential for what personal computing could be and we need the freedom to develop alternatives.')
