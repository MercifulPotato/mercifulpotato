Item(by='Wowfunhappy', descendants=None, kids=[25719714], score=None, time=1610296500, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25715480, text='But I feel we also run into the reverse problem. While it&#x27;s true that neither Hurricane Irma nor Harvey nor Katrina, nor the Californian or Australian wildfires, nor the strangely warm arctic temperatures, can be <i>singularly</i> attributed to climate change, the <i>pattern</i> of all these events is clearly climate change at work.<p>And so we end up stuck in this stupid limbo, where because no one can be certain that climate change caused <i>this</i> disaster, nothing is ever done in response to <i>any</i> disaster. We should be viewing these events as a serious call to action.')
