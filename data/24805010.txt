Item(by='burlesona', descendants=None, kids=[24805195], score=None, time=1602881978, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24804692, text='There is absolutely zero chance of a housing glut in California. The regulatory environment is so violently opposed to housing that the vast majority of people who live there could never afford to move. When the end comes, I think it is more likely to be because some thing finally tips such that people leave the area and (because houses are priced at the margins) prices begin to actually drop. Once that happens there could easily be a rush for the exits, as nobody wants to be upside down on a $2 million loan.')
