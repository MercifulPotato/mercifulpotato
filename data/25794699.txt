Item(by='miki123211', descendants=None, kids=[25795953, 25801645], score=None, time=1610737097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25793163, text='Another similar service is Aira[1].<p>The main difference is that Aira is not free, but you&#x27;re helped by professional agents, who had to undergo various background checks and sign airtight NDAs. They have a 5-minutes-per day free plan, and businesses can also offer free service if you&#x27;re using Aira with one of their products.<p>My biggest frustration with Aira is that you need to own a phone number in one of the supported countries, which is a pain to set up and maintain.<p>All agents also have TeamViewer installed, so they can quickly and efficiently help with computer issues. I&#x27;ve been using it for over a year now (with the free plan), and I definitely recommend.')
