Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=[24845487], score=None, time=1603249114, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24843278, text='If low-risk groups engaged in more risky behavior and <i>no other factor changed</i>, you&#x27;d expect death rates of hospitalized patients to drop because a larger share of the hospitalized would be from groups at lower risk of death if hospitalized with COVID.<p>Also, if hospitals learned more about interventions (like that ventilation wasn&#x27;t appropriate as early or in as many cases as initially thought), and nothing else changed, same result.<p>Or if at-risk populations became more cautious, and&#x2F;or less exposed due to policy (like better approaches to nursing homes), same result.<p>So, this is not even a tiny bit surprising. Nor does it indicate any reduced need for control measures.')
