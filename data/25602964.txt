Item(by='bsenftner', descendants=None, kids=[25603299], score=None, time=1609506217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25601318, text='Virtually trying on clothing has significant social hurdles in the fashion industry, primarily due to a complete lack of trust within the industry. Design theft is one of the most major concerns, and any system attempting to enable consumers to virtually try on clothing requires the clothing designs to be released to 3rd parties before the items are in the market - and that early information release is exactly what all social lessons from past interactions in their industry tell them never to allow.')
