In the case of an OS, the &quot;big&quot; OS&#x27;s probably meet most people&#x27;s general computing needs. It&#x27;s not uncommon to write a custom &quot;OS&quot; for embedded devices. Some people do feel like making their own OS and there are plenty of examples out there of them and that&#x27;s perfectly fine.<p>Anything like this is also not written as law. Your text editor annoys you under these certain conditions? You don&#x27;t have to rewrite it, fix it, or switch to another editor.
