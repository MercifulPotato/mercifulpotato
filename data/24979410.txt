Item(by='sokoloff', descendants=None, kids=[24979526], score=None, time=1604410070, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24979010, text='Inflation is a tax on capital <i>held in monetary form</i>. It is not a tax on capital held as the earning power of an ongoing business. If I hold $1M of cash, I care about inflation eroding that over decades. If I hold $1M worth of DCF of Coca-Cola&#x27;s future profit streams, inflation does not concern me for that portion of my capital.')
