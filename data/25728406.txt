Item(by='alickz', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610370689, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25712467, text='I understand this logic but my worry is since smartphones have become the primary computing device for millions of people (&quot;What&#x27;s a computer?&quot;) treating them as closed systems like gaming consoles is a bad approach.<p>I think what&#x27;s better for the consumer, and society in general I suppose, is to treat them as general computing devices. Especially as we see them converge with PCs (e.g. tablets with keyboards replacing laptops).<p>Consider the following: If you had to pick only one, would you replace your PC with a gaming console, or a smartphone? I worry the vast majority of people would choose the smartphone, and as such we would have replaced the open PC culture we have now with a closed, proprietary culture.')
