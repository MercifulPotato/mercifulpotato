Item(by='zartar', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607284870, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25324534, text='A typical recruiting fee is 30 percent of an employees annual salary.<p>A typical onboarding process takes weeks or months of paid employee time.<p>Employees value to a company increases substantially over time due to knowledge of culture, process, etc.<p>Executives care a lot when company actions cause employees to leave. Human capital is one of the essential ingredients to a successful company.<p>These things are real money and execs care about the impact on bottom line.<p>This is typically the most important aspect of an exit interview. In my experience, the employee&#x27;s reason for leaving is thoroughly examined.<p>Does &quot;condemning an action&quot; have the same impact?')
