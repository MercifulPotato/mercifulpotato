Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603079541, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24821817, text='&gt; Every now and then I&#x27;ve been ranting here that there&#x27;s a missing task management product for software teams: one that would embrace that tasks form DAGs, not flat two-level trees<p>MS Project and many other task tools recognize that, considering all of start-to-start, start-to-finish, finish-to-finish, and finish-to-start dependencies (all of which can be simultaneous as well as happen-first dependencies), tasks can form directed graphs (not restricted to acyclic).<p>And many of those tools also have GANTT charts built in.')
