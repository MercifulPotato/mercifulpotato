Item(by='zepto', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602887257, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24803566, text='Genes, Environment, and Randomness covers a lot of causes!<p>Staying true to yourself is not a good idea if you have an inaccurate self-image.  It will cause great harm.<p>On the other hand you can’t in fact shape your identity around arbitrary desires about who you want to be.<p>There are constraints to what is possible for you become, especially if you care about being happy.<p>Knowing and respecting those constraints can be seen as a more positive interpretation of “being true to oneself”.  Interpreting it this way, there is no ‘one true identity’, but there is definitely a sense of who you are, and who you it’s better not to try to be.')
