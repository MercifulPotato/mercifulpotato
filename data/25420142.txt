Nice work Jabo and excited to see Typesense in action.<p>Open Library gladly &amp; thankfully tweeted its support of your hard work:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;twitter.com&#x2F;openlibrary&#x2F;status&#x2F;1338531822536310789" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;twitter.com&#x2F;openlibrary&#x2F;status&#x2F;1338531822536310789</a><p>In every intention of being additive to Jabo&#x27;s work, thought I&#x27;d share a few resources folks may not be aware of:<p>1. Open Library&#x27;s Full-text Search<p>Major hat tip to the Internet Archive&#x27;s Giovanni Damiola for this one: Folks may also appreciate the ability to full-text search across 4M of the Internet Archive&#x27;s books on Open Library: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.openlibrary.org&#x2F;2018&#x2F;07&#x2F;14&#x2F;search-full-text-within-4m-books" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.openlibrary.org&#x2F;2018&#x2F;07&#x2F;14&#x2F;search-full-text-wit...</a>. You can try it directly here: <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search&#x2F;inside?q=thanks%20for%20all%20the%20fish" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search&#x2F;inside?q=thanks%20for%20all%20...</a><p>As per usual, nearly all Open Library urls <i>are</i> themselves APIs, e.g.:\n<a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search&#x2F;inside.json?q=thanks%20for%20all%20the%20fish" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search&#x2F;inside.json?q=thanks%20for%20a...</a><p>2. Open Library&#x27;s search API<p>By adding .json to our search endpoint, you can use Open Library&#x27;s json search endpoint (which also contains bookcover links). You can also use this to fetch books by ISBN:<p><a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search.json?q=isbn:9781400067824" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;search.json?q=isbn:9781400067824</a><p>3. Book Cover Service (has issues)<p>We need to improve our cover service, it&#x27;s definitely been neglected, but the cover IDs from our data dump, solr search index, and book APIs can be plugged in to our cover store to point to or fetch book covers. We don&#x27;t yet have a current data bulk dump of covers available (it&#x27;s on the roadmap) but if you need &quot;all&quot; the covers, please email us openlibrary@archive.org instead of hitting covers 1-by-1. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;dev&#x2F;docs&#x2F;api&#x2F;covers" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;dev&#x2F;docs&#x2F;api&#x2F;covers</a><p>4. The new (unannounced) Open Library Explorer<p>I&#x27;m honestly kind of hoping no one reads this far into the comments because we haven&#x27;t announced this one yet and I&#x27;m sure there are major performance issues (it&#x27;s beta), but cdrini recently added dewey + library of congress classifications to our search index... Making this masterpiece possible:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;explore" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;openlibrary.org&#x2F;explore</a><p>The ability to browse Open Library digitally, with all the glory of a physical library.')
