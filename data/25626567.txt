Item(by='marcosdumay', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609719026, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25624014, text='My experience is that I agree that a standard punctuation is easier to write, but it&#x27;s harder to get a big picture from it than non-standard.<p>Anyway, it&#x27;s way harder to adapt to &quot;parenthesis before the function name vs parenthesis after the function name&quot; and &quot;semicolons after every statement vs. never use semicolons&quot; than &quot;punctuation is all parenthesis and commas (or newline and space as in normal Haskell) vs. punctuation uses the entire keyboard&quot;.<p>Meaningful vs meaningless indentation is also one of the things that don&#x27;t give me any problems at all to adapt.')
