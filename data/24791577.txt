Incidentally for a library that does one thing you might check out wasgen (which TFA is basically a GUI for). In short you give it a description of an arbitrary audio graph and parameters, and it builds all the nodes for you and then disposes them afterwards. So very suitable to build more complicated audio apps on top of.')
