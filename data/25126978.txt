Item(by='zachmu', descendants=None, kids=[25129155], score=None, time=1605635644, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25120256, text='No, that&#x27;s not possible. A panic must be recovered in the goroutine in which it occurs. An unrecovered panic in <i>any</i> goroutine kills the entire process, not just the offending goroutine. There is no way to top-down wrap an entire go process in a recover block to keep a panic from killing your process. You have to do it in each and every goroutine.')
