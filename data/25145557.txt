Item(by='flukus', descendants=None, kids=[25148058], score=None, time=1605753041, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25145197, text='It&#x27;s not &quot;this is the way it&#x27;s done&quot;, it&#x27;s &quot;this is how it should be done&quot;. Shipping information is much like the rest of the order information, you want a de-normalized price on the order and each line item in the database, not a normalized cost based on the price of things now.
