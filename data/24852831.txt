Declarative definitions of a grammar help identify those ambiguities... and if the grammar is defined separately from its interpretation, it opens the door to invisible ambiguities that can become another vector for vulnerabilities.<p>Unless you have provable implementations (great if you do!), declarative grammars meaningfully limit points of failure.')
