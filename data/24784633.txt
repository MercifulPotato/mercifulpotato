Do you believe that?<p>I believe a variation of that.<p>I believe that the first copy arose through <i>physical processes</i>.<p>Evoking &#x27;randomness&#x27; is unnecessary and misleading.<p>Do you <i>not</i> believe this?<p>To my knowledge, we don&#x27;t yet have a mechanism for how such a molecule came in to being (though there are ideas).<p>We also don&#x27;t have any reason to think that it must be some random single choice from a large possibility space, and we don&#x27;t have any evidence at all that it could have arisen from non-physical processes (what would that even look like?).')
