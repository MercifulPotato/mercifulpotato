Item(by='segfaultbuserr', descendants=None, kids=[25844522], score=None, time=1611136385, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25844441, text='KDE has a failsafe mechanism. If the screen locker has crashed, it shows a black screen of death with a huge error message.<p>&gt; The screen locker is broken and unlocking is not possible anymore. In order to unlock, switch to a virtual terminal (e.g. Ctrl+Alt+F2), log in and execute the command: &quot;loginctl unlock session c2&quot;. Afterwards switch back to the running session.<p>I think it&#x27;s a reasonable design.')
