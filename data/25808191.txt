Item(by='TylerE', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610858510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25806236, text='Also Beethoven was composing at a time when the piano was undergoing quite a bit of physical iteration.<p>A typical piano in Mozart&#x27;s day, just proceeding Beethoven, had a wooden frame, strings made of iron, and a range of perhaps 5 octaves.<p>The modern concert grand, the design of which was mostly settled on by about 1880, has a cast iron frame, strings of spring steel (&quot;piano wire&quot;), and a rage of just over 7 octaves.')
