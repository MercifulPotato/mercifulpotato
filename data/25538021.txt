Item(by='quesera', descendants=None, kids=[25538504], score=None, time=1608920095, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25537886, text='Received wisdom:<p>Don&#x27;t buy combination smoke &amp; CO detectors.<p>Smoke rises, so detectors are best placed on or near the ceiling. CO sinks, so detectors are best placed a few feet above the ground.<p>Considering the importance of early notification of either condition, these requirements appear irreconcilable.')
