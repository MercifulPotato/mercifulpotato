Item(by='lorenzhs', descendants=None, kids=[24721633, 24718384], score=None, time=1602159042, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24718338, text='NP-complete doesn&#x27;t mean unsolvable! It means that we don&#x27;t know any algorithms that run in subexponential time for <i>every input</i>. But of course we can use the algorithms with exponential worst case running time. We can also use algorithms that solve <i>most</i> instances fairly quickly but may take a very long time to solve <i>some</i> particularly hard instances.')
