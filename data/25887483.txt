Item(by='NickNameNick', descendants=None, kids=[25887620, 25887800], score=None, time=1611447595, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25887415, text='Depends where the plane is.<p>High altitude, away from any precision navigation areas, not a huge risk.<p>Flying a GPS approach to an airport - definite risk.<p>Bear in mind that a GPS satellite has a power budget of ~4kw, and it&#x27;s &gt;12,000 miles up.<p>A jammer may only have a watt or two of transmit power, but it can put all of that into a single channel, and it&#x27;s only going to be a few miles away from your receiver.')
