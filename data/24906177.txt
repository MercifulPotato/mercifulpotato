Item(by='viraptor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603796217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24905733, text='Even if you configure it carefully, you have the limit of: your db connection capacity &lt; app servers X max internal pool size.<p>Every time you add another instance, you&#x27;re not adding a &quot;happy path number&quot; of connections. You&#x27;re adding up to the internal max, because at some point you will get spammed with real or bot traffic.<p>It&#x27;s easier to aggregate the connections than carefully balance that setup every time you make infra changes or scale up. And you need some headroom for &quot;oh, this point release of the library spawns a thread now and gets its own connection pool... oops&quot;.')
