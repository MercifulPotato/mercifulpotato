Item(by='iso1631', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602750884, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24776505, text='I did some calculations on how much more renewable the UK would need based on demand over a given year, and how much storage.<p>With 20 times the current renewable, 1GWh of charged storage, and a perfect grid, starting on Sep 18th 2018, we&#x27;d be fine until September 30th at 18:15.<p>With 10 million EVs acting as a battery store, all topping at 50KWh and going as low down as say 25KWh, that would be 250GWh of storage, we&#x27;d last until September 30th at 19:30.<p>That doesn&#x27;t count the extra demand of the EVs in actually driving.<p>If we had 50 times current renewable and that 250GW of storage we&#x27;d be OK.')
