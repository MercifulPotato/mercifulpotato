Item(by='simonblack', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607834586, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403551, text='Adjuvants are the &#x27;extras&#x27; added to anything, not necessarily just vaccines, which help it in its action or make it more convenient in usage.<p>That could be thickeners or perhaps suspending agents to prevent the active ingredients settling too quickly on the bottom; that could be preservatives to stop it growing bugs; that could be ascorbic acid to prevent the active substance being oxidised and made inactive; that could be anti-freeze agents to prevent it freezing to become a solid.<p>A lot of the &#x27;extras&#x27; listed in the ingredients of prepackaged food could be considered to be adjuvants.')
