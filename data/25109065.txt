Item(by='saagarjha', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605511587, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25108829, text='I am very familiar with macOS&#x27;s security features. Preventing an application from having something maliciously command them into sending things on the network is not one of the threat models Apple considers (or at least, they have no specific mitigations against this). As a simple (but conspicuous) example, consider an app simply opening a customized HTTP link if Safari was exempt from the firewall to exfiltrate information. There are hundreds of ways to do effectively the same thing more surreptitiously.')
