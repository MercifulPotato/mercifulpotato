Item(by='ireflect', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602194980, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24716049, text='These are known as crowlers (can growlers) and are meant to replace the 1L and 1.89L glass growlers that have gone out of fashion somewhat over the last couple years.<p>Crowlers look like oversized beverage cans, and come with either a pull tab (like a normal beverage can) or a resealable metal cap.<p>Note that all beverage cans have a plastic liner (epoxy usually).')
