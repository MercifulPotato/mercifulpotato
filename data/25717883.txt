Item(by='mymythisisthis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610303901, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25715781, text='I never season my pans. Use soap, water, and aluminium foil (if food is baked on) to clean them. Dry in the oven to prevent rust.<p>A couple of times after I made eggs, I let the water sit for too long, and the pan got a bit rusty. No problem with cast iron! Just a bit of steel wool and it was as good as new.<p>A cast iron pan is the easiest pan you&#x27;ll ever use.')
