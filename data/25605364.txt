Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609525687, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25605239, text='So, you’re saying that it’s impractical to switch build systems, and that it’s inappropriate to suggest alternative build systems?<p>I think that if you’re reading a suggestion to “just use X” on Hacker News, the sensible thing to do is to evaluate the suggestion for your own. You don’t need to put a long list of caveats in front of every software recommendation. If your problem is, “My builds are slow, I want distributed builds,” then you should probably at least consider the two most popular solutions to that problem, which are Bazel and Distcc.
