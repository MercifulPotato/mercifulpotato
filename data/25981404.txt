The clearing firm, in order to make sure none of the Brokers becomes insolvent before settlement, requires every Broker offer up collateral - it&#x27;s collateral, it&#x27;s not spent if everything goes well.<p>It&#x27;s the SEC requiring brokers offer up insurance in case they explode.<p>Then, 2 days later, your actual money goes to the clearing firm and is exchanged for stock.')
