Item(by='Tomminn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606118093, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25184443, text='The key point is that if you lower the replication rate-- the number of people each person with the virus goes on to infect-- below 1, the number of active cases exponentially decays to zero.<p>The business-as-usual replication rate of covid appears to be somewhere around 4-6, meaning that if we have universal vaccination with something like 80% efficacy, the number of active cases will exponentially decay with <i>no other measures taken</i> (social distancing, masks, lockdowns, quarantines etc).')
