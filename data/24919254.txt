Item(by='edjrage', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603896146, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24917468, text='Well, it depends on what you mean by suffering (and some say suffering isn&#x27;t the right translation for what they really mean). For buddhists, pain, sadness and grief are not suffering, they are indeed an inescapable part of life and are to be fully embraced with awareness and compassion. Suffering is an unnecessary, clingy, ego-centric (hyphenated to emphasize the buddhist concept) reaction to them.')
