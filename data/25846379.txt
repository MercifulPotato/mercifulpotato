Item(by='linknoid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611150578, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25844279, text='12:00 should actually be 0:00 on the 12 hour clock, it belongs to the next 12 hour period.  11:59 PM is yesterday, 12:00 AM is one minute later, and falls on today.  Same with noon, 12:00 PM comes one minute after 11:59 AM.  When I see 12:00, I automatically convert that to 0:00, otherwise AM&#x2F;PM doesn&#x27;t make sense.')
