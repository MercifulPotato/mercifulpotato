Item(by='kazinator', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604542608, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24991698, text='ISO C 99, first sentence of Library clause:<p>&quot;A <i>string</i> is a contiguous sequence of characters terminated by and including the first null character.&quot;<p>later in the paragraph:<p>&quot;A <i>pointer to a string</i> is a pointer to its initial (lowest addressed) character.&quot;<p>The italics indicate that a normative definition is being given. So, the official C terminology is that a pointer to a null-terminated char array is a &quot;pointer to a string&quot;.')
