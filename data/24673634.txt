Item(by='cortesoft', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601748147, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24670920, text='A prisoner’s dilemma doesn’t require actual prisoners; it was the first example of this type of problem, which is why the name is used for all sorts of problems with the same characteristics; namely, that there is some optimal outcome for everyone that could be reached if everyone chose a particular decision, but their is always an individual incentive to ‘defect’ and choose the other option.')
