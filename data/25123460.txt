Item(by='kube-system', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605619651, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25120966, text='The US census is also intended to count people without an address.<p>Also, in the US it is state governments that are the authority for most things pertaining to where people live: property and rental law, identification cards, property taxes, voters registration, etc.  Most states <i>do</i> require people to change their voters registration and drivers license when they move between states.<p>It’s not that strange that you don’t register your address with the federal government, when in fact, the only interaction that many Americans have with the federal government at all is their yearly tax filing.')
