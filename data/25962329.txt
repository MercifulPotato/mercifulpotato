Because if you end up in front of a jury, that police officer is going to testify to that jury that you said &quot;you&#x27;d tell them anything when they granted you immunity&quot;, a truthful statement, and the jury is going to fill in whatever gaps the prosecutor suggests they fill in.<p>If you are in police custody you ask for <i>nothing</i> from the police.
