First is memorization of syntax and semantics of the language. Second is a kind of shadow learning, or &quot;street smarts&quot; for survival in the language; which parts of town to avoid and which paths avoid dark alleys, etc. These habits are hard to quantify, and beginner programmers especially tend to apply the &quot;street smarts&quot; from their first language to every future language, which creates problems.')
