Item(by='wsh', descendants=None, kids=[24909988], score=None, time=1603765267, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24899147, text='One place for an announcement would be “Show HN,” if your project fits within the guidelines [1], but I think <i>what</i> you announce is as important as where.<p>For example, how does your software compare, from a user’s perspective, to existing alternatives? What parts of it exist today, and how much work remains? Are you credibly in a position to deliver?<p>Your announcement needs to communicate your progress, competence, and realistic plans, or readers will tend to assume your project, like many, is so much vaporware.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;showhn.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;news.ycombinator.com&#x2F;showhn.html</a>')
