People are enthusiastic to use a new bridge, less so when they find out that they have to pay a toll to drive over it. Various charges can be used to manage congestion.<p>In a system where road use is &quot;free&quot; there would need to be significant government discipline to not heed demands of road users for more roads.<p>Heavy taxes on the purchase of new cars may be a good strategy to dissuade vehicle congestion, with lesser taxes on new EVs and lesser still on used vehicles.')
