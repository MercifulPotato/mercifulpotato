Item(by='ncmncm', descendants=None, kids=[25275090], score=None, time=1606843964, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25264023, text='You may presume so, but you would be wrong.<p>They are technically (also) lock-free, so require atomic operations when announcing additions, but being written by only one process, ownership of cache lines never changes, so hardware locks are not engaged.<p>Readers do poll, and sleep as long as the tolerated latency when nothing new is found.
