Item(by='29athrowaway', descendants=None, kids=[25806876], score=None, time=1610837455, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25805112, text='I used Akka cluster many years ago.<p>One of the problems I encountered was that to finding actors in remote actor systems. i.e.: you have a unique actor responsible for X, living somewhere in your cluster and you need to know its name as well as the IP address of the actor system where it is running.<p>A message queue solves this problem, but that was not the approach I took.<p>My solution was to implement an actor discovery system on top of Zookeeper. Using that, I could have a cluster-wide unique actors.')
