Item(by='colanderman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604636297, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24996089, text='On many systems,<p><pre><code>    grep &#x27;[A-Z]&#x27;\n</code></pre>\nwill match &#x27;y&#x27; but not &#x27;z&#x27; (note the case).  This is due to collation of the system&#x27;s locale, which intersperses upper- and lowercase letters.<p>Usually what you want instead is<p><pre><code>    LC_ALL=C grep &#x27;[A-Z]&#x27;\n</code></pre>\n(to match ASCII uppercase letters), or<p><pre><code>    grep &#x27;[[:upper:]]&#x27;\n</code></pre>\n(to match your locale&#x27;s uppercase letters).<p>FWIW I cannot reproduce this on my system any longer; it seems to vary by distribution.
