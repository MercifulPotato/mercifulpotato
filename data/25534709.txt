Any healthy person could manage it. Many would be better off for the conditioning and the experience.<p>But pushing on the edges of your design parameters is not going to be sunshine and rainbows. It&#x27;s supposed to hurt. You should be &quot;OK&quot; in the sense of &quot;stronger than the pain and will make it&quot; not &quot;feeling good.&quot;<p>And it&#x27;s probably going to hurt a lot more if it&#x27;s pushed on you from outside vs. a decision to challenge yourself.')
