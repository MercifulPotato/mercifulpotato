Putting the word computer back in doesn&#x27;t change that.<p>Look, for example, at Dennett&#x27;s seminal book, &quot;The Intentional Stance&quot; for a philosophical discussion of the expressive and reasoning power behind statements like &quot;The thermostat tries to keep the temperature at 60 degrees&quot;.')
