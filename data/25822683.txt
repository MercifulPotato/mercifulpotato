Item(by='sandworm101', descendants=None, kids=[25824090], score=None, time=1610983983, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25822618, text='A sun-synchronous orbit is a polar orbit where the satellite passes over each part of the earth at the same time every day.  That is very useful if you want to photograph things at regular intervals using cameras (stable light, no nighttime) or if your satellite uses solar panels as these orbits can be such that the satellite never falls into the earth&#x27;s shadow.')
