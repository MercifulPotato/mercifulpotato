Then go to a local metal supply place and ask if you can buy some scraps to practice welding---I ended up with a box of pieces of 1&#x2F;4&quot; steel plate for free. Technically, I should take them back to recycle when I am done, but I&#x27;m too ashamed of the horrible welds I did on them. I still can&#x27;t scratch-start worth a damn.<p>Just be sure to watch some videos or read up on on how to do it safely.')
