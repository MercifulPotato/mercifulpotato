Item(by='howlgarnish', descendants=None, kids=[25119160], score=None, time=1605539390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25111051, text='It makes sense <i>if</i> you&#x27;re aiming for a faithful transliteration of the original syllables, which is what Nihonshiki does.  However, if you want a phonemic transcription that can be read by non-native speakers, then it makes sense to do what Hepburn does and accurately represent the sounds (not the writing) of the language, where the consonant in ち is not the same as the one in た.')
