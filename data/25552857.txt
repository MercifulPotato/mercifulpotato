Item(by='somurzakov', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609092130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25550685, text='&gt;&gt;Safety is a characteristic of systems and not of their components\n&gt;&gt;Safety is an emergent property of systems; it does not reside in a person, device or department of an organization or system. Safety cannot be purchased or manufactured; it is not a feature that is separate from the other components of the system. This means that safety cannot be manipulated like a feedstock or raw material. The state of safety in any system is always dynamic; continuous systemic change insures that hazard and its management are constantly changing.<p>fits perfectly the description of Security of complex IT systems, nice way to explain why IT security marketplace is a wild wild west')
