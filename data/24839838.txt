Invalid UTF-8 is a good indication something went wrong. The earlier the failure occurs, the easier it&#x27;ll be to troubleshoot it.<p>Of course there are situations where you want to make do with your input and try to interpret it the best you can. Sometimes you need to be lenient in what you accept [0]. That&#x27;s how I&#x27;d expect, say, a web browser to operate.<p>Default to not accepting invalid input. Just fail fast.<p>[0]: Be liberal in what you accept, and conservative in what you send - Jon Postel')
