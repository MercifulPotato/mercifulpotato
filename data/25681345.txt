Item(by='charsi', descendants=None, kids=[25681706], score=None, time=1610078173, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25659272, text='My main takeaway from this article is that the correct way to support older systems is not by supporting older versions of SSL&#x2F;TLS. It is better to add support for plain HTTP delivery and only support modern SSL&#x2F;TLS variants. Since the author recommends upgrading anyone with &#x27;Upgrade-Insecure-Requests&#x27; header I don&#x27;t think there is a downside to doing this.<p>If you truly don&#x27;t have legacy clients trying to visit your website then 99%+ visitors won&#x27;t see any difference in behaviour. So no reason NOT to do this.')
