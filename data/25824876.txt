Item(by='Voloskaya', descendants=None, kids=[25826977, 25825474], score=None, time=1610994660, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25821101, text='SETI@home style distributed computation is not suitable for training something like GPT-3, unlike for SETI, the unit of work a node can do before needing to share it&#x27;s output with the next node is really small, so very fast interconnect between the nodes is needed (Infiniband and NVLink is used in clusters to train it). It would probably take a decade to train such a model over regular internet.')
