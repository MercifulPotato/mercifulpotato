Item(by='strogonoff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605247480, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25079248, text='Phoning home in this case is done to check whether an app’s signed with a valid certificate. Not checking that opens user’s machine to attacks where malware successfully pretends to be an authentic trusted app, likely gaining access privileges (Keychain, etc.) granted to that app by the user previously.<p>Linux distros can arguably get away without these checks since their users are typically more aware of what they are launching, but crucially because they are not as big a target due to smaller user bases.')
