But you will slip into flow, and find it easy to put this nonsense aside and get one of your good ideas moving.<p>(The apes are “evolved” by the Monolith by inspiring a tickle-fight. The Monolith has a creamy caramel center. The star-child takes one look at earth and yeets out.)')
