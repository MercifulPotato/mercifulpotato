Item(by='kome', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605713404, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25137719, text='Offshoring was a political decision. a decision that needed political consensus, action and supervision (to open capital markets, to set standards, to trade deals, to keep exchange rate stable, etc., etc.)<p>Offshoring doesn&#x27;t happen in a vacuum. And in some countries, offshoring hit harder than in others. For instance, Germany and Italy are still export powerhouses. This, again, was a political decision.<p>In the US the decision was to privilege financial and tech industry, more than production industry. It was a strategy, and like every strategy it created some winner and some losers.<p>Americans have to decide if keeping this strategy or trying something else.')
