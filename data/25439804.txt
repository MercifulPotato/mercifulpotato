Item(by='conk', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608101277, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25439704, text='PID can’t be used for control of any device that uses a relay. PWM will have the effect of just changing the coil voltage. Even if you could switch a relay on&#x2F;off quickly you’d just prematurely burn out the contacts.<p>PID is only effective with solid state switching devices, which are not really common for residential heating&#x2F;cooling.')
