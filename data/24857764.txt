The biggest change in the direction of tolerance might be the general abandonment of <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Supersessionism" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Supersessionism</a>, the doctrine that the covenant between God and the people of Israel had been superceded by Jesus.<p>You can still maintain the overall point, by arguing that prevailing attitudes drove the theology rather than the other way around. Supercessionism became drastically less popular after seeing the 20th century consequences of anti-semitism, moreso than pure reflection on scripture or anything like that.')
