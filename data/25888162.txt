Item(by='munificent', descendants=None, kids=[25888749, 25888410, 25888721], score=None, time=1611452945, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25887200, text='In order to keep woodworking finishes from drying in the can, many woodworkers displace air in the can by dumping marbles in. You could probably do the same thing with a wine bottle:<p>1. Drink as much as you want.<p>2. Pour small clean marbles into the bottle until the level of the wine is near the top again.<p>3. Put cork back in.')
