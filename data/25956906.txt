Item(by='DiogenesKynikos', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611926135, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25956552, text='The author&#x27;s proposal to start at a high-probability-density (HPD) point reduces the bias that you get from not doing burn-in, but it still creates a bias.<p>In order to get a reasonable answer from MCMC, you need to run for many correlation times. Burn-in should only last a few correlation times, so you don&#x27;t lose that large a fraction of your chain by throwing it away. And the part of the chain you&#x27;re using is biased, so you&#x27;re probably not gaining anything by keeping it.')
