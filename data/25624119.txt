Item(by='simias', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609700772, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25622811, text='It can be a concern, that&#x27;s true, but personally most of the stuff I edit end up checked into a git repository or something similar.<p>And I mean, we all spend all day editing test messages and comments and files on non-ECC hardware, yet bitflip-induced corruption is rare enough that I can&#x27;t say that I&#x27;ve witnessed a single instance of it in my life, despite spending a good chunk of it looking at screens.<p>It&#x27;s just not a problem that occurs in practice in my experience. If you&#x27;re compiling the release build of a critical piece of software, you probably want ECC. If you&#x27;re building the dev version of your webapp or writing an email to your boss, you&#x27;ll probably survive without it.')
