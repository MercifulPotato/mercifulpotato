Appreciation refers to an increase in the value of an asset over time. When an individual purchases a good as an investment, the intent is not to consume the good but rather to use it in the future to create wealth.\n<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.investopedia.com&#x2F;terms&#x2F;i&#x2F;investment.asp" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.investopedia.com&#x2F;terms&#x2F;i&#x2F;investment.asp</a><p>Buying Beanie Babies or tulip bulb futures or cryptocurrencies because you expect the value to increase over time is absolutely an &quot;investment&quot;. They might be good or bad ideas to invest in, but they&#x27;re without a doubt an investment.')
