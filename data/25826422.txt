Item(by='sethhochberg', descendants=None, kids=[25826592, 25826569], score=None, time=1611002725, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25826313, text='For anybody not super familiar with US government departments and confused by reading this after seeing other headlines coming out of the country in recent years, BLM in this context is &quot;Bureau of Land Management&quot; and not &quot;Black Lives Matter&quot;.<p>The BLM is the federal department that oversees most governmental land that isn&#x27;t managed by some other department. Broadly speaking, this is land which isn&#x27;t run by a local&#x2F;state&#x2F;tribal government, may not have any particular use designated, and is (usually) uninhabited - or at least intended to be.')
