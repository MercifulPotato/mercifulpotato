Item(by='numpad0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602410913, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24743779, text='Fractional inches notation is based on imaging tubes, not films.<p>There were of cylindrical fluorescent vacuum tubes that has rectangular imaging area at one end. Inch size notation like 1” or 1&#x2F;1.2” for CMOS sensors denote size of <i>equivalent imaging tube diameter</i> for its imaging area.<p>“135 full frame”, “APS-C”, “Super 35mm”, “Medium format” on the other hand refers to film sizes, and thus respective imaging area dimensions.')
