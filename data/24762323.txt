Item(by='klodolph', descendants=None, kids=[24762570], score=None, time=1602563833, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24761735, text='Once you get to 99.9% uptime you’re still less than 1 hour per month. I used to run apps for companies at work and it was standard operating procedure to have two app servers + load balancer for upgrades.<p>Drain a server, upgrade it, boot it, check that it works, let it accept traffic. Repeat once more. Done.<p>It may be true that most businesses don’t need the uptime, but I’d say it’s usually easier to give them high availability rather than field complaints when some random user tries to look something up during a maintenance window.')
