Item(by='johnwalkr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605016761, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25044470, text='I feel like there’s a threshold for fame that needs to be met for giving credit in the English-language, mainstream media. A famous name like Stephen Hawking would get directly credited for a result. A less famous researcher’s result would get credited to their school (eg MIT). Outside of well known western names and institutions, the result would get credited to the country (eg China or Japan).<p>The exception is underdog stories.')
