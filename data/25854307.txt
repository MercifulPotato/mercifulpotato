The main reason we don&#x27;t have them is because of Moore&#x27;s Law: wait a few years and a single CPU will be just as fast as multiple dataflow CPUs.<p>There are historical reasons why we&#x27;re programming in text in mostly imperative languages: graphics terminals were either not very good or very expensive, and computers had single CPUs, until quite recently.')
