Item(by='parliament32', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606935097, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25271029, text='&gt;Using [dig] might seem a little intimidating as its syntax is frankly plain stupid.<p>Dig has one of the least stupid syntaxes, IMO. It&#x27;ll happily take your query in any order and isn&#x27;t case sensitive. @ for the dns server you want to ask (or omit for resolv.conf lookup), + for options, otherwise specify name and type (and class if you want) in any order.. the following four queries will all get you the same info back without complaint:<p><pre><code>    $ dig TXT google.com. +short @8.8.8.8\n    $ dig +short @8.8.8.8 google.com. txt\n    $ dig google.com. @8.8.8.8 TXT +short\n    $ dig @8.8.8.8 +short google.com. txt</code></pre>')
