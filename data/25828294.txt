Item(by='PeterisP', descendants=None, kids=[25828918], score=None, time=1611014545, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25826300, text='The general legal approach is that dead people have no privacy rights, and their privacy tends to be protected only as much as it concerns the privacy and reputation of their surviving relatives. It&#x27;s fully legal to read and publish the private letters and intimate diaries of historical figures, as it is to make claims about their deeds and motivations that might constitute libel if they were alive.')
