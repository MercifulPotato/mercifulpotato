Item(by='jimkleiber', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608658557, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25508542, text='Ooo, solved my own problem.<p>MacOS has an option for &quot;mouse keys&quot; where one can toggle the keyboard to act as the directional control and click control for a mouse. So if I toggle it on, then I can use the &quot;i&quot; key to act as a mouse click and draw on the trackpad, and &quot;m&quot; key to hold a mouse click until I click &quot;.&quot; to release it. And then I can toggle the mouse keys on&#x2F;off by hitting the option key 5 times.<p>Woohoo!')
