Item(by='jph00', descendants=None, kids=[25164981, 25165577], score=None, time=1605901614, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25163811, text='There are two lead definitions for &quot;democratize&quot; in the Oxford English Dictionary. One of them is:<p>&quot;make (something) accessible to everyone&quot;<p>So the usage here is entirely consistent with standard English usage.
