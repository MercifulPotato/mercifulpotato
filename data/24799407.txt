But meat that is fresh and has no bad odour can be contaminated. You cannot use smell to tell if meat is contaminated or not.<p>We don&#x27;t eat raw chicken, even if it&#x27;s very fresh, because of the risk of food poisoning from things like campylobacter.<p>You need to be careful with cooked then chilled rice because of the risk of bacillus cereus.
