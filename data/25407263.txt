Item(by='mikejethi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607874103, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25407095, text='So if you want to find out what Facebook knows about you, you have to download your data from Facebook. It&#x27;s a JSON dump which does very little on its own to inform you what all of it actually means. We wanted to make a tool to easily analyse and understand the data.<p>You upload it to Ethi, but your data is encrypted in your browser before you upload, stored encrypted, and only decrypted in a serverless cloud instance to analyse and process it (there&#x27;s a lot of data analysis that happens) before being stored encrypted again.<p>So we can&#x27;t see your data when it&#x27;s stored, and it&#x27;s encrypted nearly all the time except when it&#x27;s being processed or you&#x27;re viewing it.')
