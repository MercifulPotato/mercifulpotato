Item(by='OldHand2018', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609722260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25624665, text='“The Machine that Changed the World” is an excellent book detailing differences between the Japanese style and American style of designing and building cars.<p>Near the end of the book is one of the most important parts, yet nobody seems to talk about it.
