Item(by='nocman', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602478345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24750809, text='I doubt you know much about welding then.<p>Like most professions you can become as skilled at it as you  wish, depending on your willingness to work to get better.  There are a <i>lot</i> of different types of welding, lots of different pieces of equipment to learn and master, and way more different materials to work with than most people realize.<p>Also, different situations present different challenges (like the fact that you have to be mindful of how your welds can warp the material).<p>I personally only know some the details second-hand (have professional welders in my family), but I know I&#x27;d want the guy with 20 years of experience over the one with 5, if I had something challenging I needed fixed.')
