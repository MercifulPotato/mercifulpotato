Item(by='izacus', descendants=None, kids=[24925457], score=None, time=1603915016, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24921031, text='One of the main bonuses of Flutter is also its underlying design - it really just needs a Canvas to render to and not much else. This makes it easy to port to other platforms (even web) and it allows you to have a very lightweight embedded system running a builtin app. This makes debugging easier as well, since you can just write the app on desktop, test in emulators and then deploy.<p>Our experience matches Sonys in that it works very well for embedded devices.')
