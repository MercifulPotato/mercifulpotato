Item(by='unishark', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605503496, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25108118, text='Certainly you need to consider both supply and demand, and how fast both are growing. Ignoring any of these variables turns your prediction into luck. You need a way to assess their relative size. Looking at how successful others in the market currently are is actually a pretty good way to assess this. Except if their high margins may be based in large part on factors you can&#x27;t benefit from, like network and monopoly (e.g. IP) advantages.<p>That&#x27;s kind of the case for all this kind of big-picture advice. There&#x27;s some truth but some missing variables that may be just as important. &quot;Bread crumbs&quot; was a good way to describe it, I think.')
