Item(by='thequux', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611308130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25865461, text='The difference between binary and text is deeper than just line endings and character set; PDP-10&#x27;s, for example, stored text 5 7-bit characters to a 36-bit word. In text mode, it would unpack that to ASCII, whereas binary mode would pack two words into 9 bytes. If you transferred in the wrong mode, you&#x27;d just get an amorphous blob of protoplasm and need to transfer again')
