Item(by='Retric', descendants=None, kids=[24925103], score=None, time=1603901162, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24914779, text='Having food shipped in still requires it to be created somewhere to be shipped out.<p>It’s very possible for some percentage of the population to starve even during a food surplus, but starving people will bid up the cost of food to the point where you can’t get a 40% of the population to have a surplus in a famine in any society that we know about.<p>Here is the official definition for the minimum to qualify as a famine:<p>“At least 20% of households in an area face extreme food shortages with a limited ability to cope\nThe prevalence of acute malnutrition in children exceeds 30%\n<i>The death rate exceeds two people per 10,000 people per day</i>“<p>That’s killing off over 7% of the population per year.')
