Item(by='ABCLAW', descendants=None, kids=[24815546], score=None, time=1602993835, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24815301, text='&gt;Lying to your partner is not a crime.<p>If you&#x27;re intending to obtain some benefit or deprive them of something, but fail to clinch it and &#x27;take their money&#x27; as you put it, the statement still triggers liability under most theories of fraud&#x2F;misrepresentation. Generally some injury needs to be suffered, though.<p>So no, it&#x27;s not the stealing. It&#x27;s the speech with bad intention.<p>As a sidenote, fraud and misrepresentation generally have some of the most interesting rules regarding evidence. The law really drilled down into what&#x27;s a sufficient record and what&#x27;s not in respect of attribution of liability in this area.')
