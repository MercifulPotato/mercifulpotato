Item(by='davidbanham', descendants=None, kids=[25106504, 25104840, 25108277], score=None, time=1605471923, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25104446, text='Yes, preventing a wildfire from starting on a 40 degree (celsius) day with no rain forecast for weeks and several others burning simultaneously is a desirable thing.<p>We try and manage the bushland with hazard reduction burns in the cooler months when weather patterns allow. These reduce the available fuel loads so that an uncontrolled fire in the area will be less intense.<p>There will always be fire in our landscape. Our job is to manage it as best we can to protect the trees, the wildlife and the homes that have been built near them.')
