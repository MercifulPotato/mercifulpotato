Item(by='chmod775', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610164599, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25679729, text='It&#x27;s a mistake to think of states and law as these neat, clearly defined things.<p>Nowadays law is generally a tool used by a group of people to make coexistence and societal processes smoother.<p>On the grander scale might makes right and whoever has the ability and will to make and enforce law will do so.<p>If however there are no laws, there can be no crime, by definition, and thus there is no crime going unpunished.<p>Does it matter right now? No. Will it in 50 years? Probably not still. Looking at our planet what we understand as modern law has generally only emerged in large groups of people. It&#x27;s debatable whether any small group truly needs it.')
