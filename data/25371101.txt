Item(by='erichocean', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607587608, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25362499, text='&gt; <i>Is there any evidence that removal of content one believes to be false actually achieves the remover&#x27;s goals of changing the opinions of the potential viewers?</i><p>Yes, recently in fact: Rasmussen polled Biden voters about the Hunter Biden laptop story that was suppressed on Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube (and legacy media), and 9.4% said they would not have voted for Biden if they had known prior to voting (IIRC ~50% of them had not heard about it).
