Item(by='odomojuli', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603170365, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24827295, text='For myself.<p>General chat: Proper punctuation. Keep sentences terse. Reply promptly. Leave no room for ambiguity.<p>Direct message: Often if a matter takes place outside of a public channel, it is because a duty or responsibility is being delegated to you or a sensitive subject is involved. One should take extra precaution to speak forthrightly to ease the recipient, but also take measures to be prompt and present.<p>Makes a world of difference.<p>The most important thing I&#x27;ve learned about online tone is that it&#x27;s less about what you say and more about how you tell others to speak to you.')
