Item(by='silvestrov', descendants=None, kids=[24678466, 24680002, 24678816], score=None, time=1601810769, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24678045, text='It can happend when towns grow quickly and the city sell the parcels for a significant amount of money.<p>In Denmark parcels are often sold for $50-100.000 in popular areas. This gives the town a significant amount of one-shot money.<p>If they use that one-shot money for running expenses rather than investments (in e.g. subways), then the town will go bankrupt when there are no new buyers of parcels.<p>It&#x27;s somewhat like a ponzi scheme.')
