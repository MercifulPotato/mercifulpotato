You can buy software all over the place, limiting it to iOS devices doesn&#x27;t make sense if your issue is competition from a consumer&#x27;s perspective. Sure there are exclusives but lots of platforms have those. Where the arguments start to stand up is competition from a seller&#x27;s perspective. If all your potential customers only shop at Target then you&#x27;re in a bind and Target suddenly has a lot of leverage over you.<p>These kinds of dynamics already exist in brick &amp; mortar retail with wholesale clubs. Very few people have memberships to more than one and are usually very loyal to the one they like so if you want to reach their customers you don&#x27;t have many options.')
