A compressor is usually a dumb dynamics processor that just looks at an envelope to compute gain reduction.<p>A loudness equalizer is a much smarter dynamics processor that can have gain reduction or addition and tracks perceptual loudness (not the envelope) while adapting parameters to hit some target loudness.<p>Its sort of like a really smart AGC, which is a general purpose dynamics processor.')
