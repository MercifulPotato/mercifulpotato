Item(by='joefourier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604252886, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24960798, text='I second this, using gst-launch, you can run a gstreamer pipeline in one line in the terminal that generates a hardware h264-encoded stream that you can tune to any bitrate. Something like this:<p><pre><code>  gst-launch-1.0 rtpbin name=rtpbin rpicamsrc preview=0 bitrate=4000000 do-timestamp=1 ! &#x27;video&#x2F;x-h264, width=1920, height=1080, framerate=30&#x2F;1,profile=high&#x27; ! h264parse ! rtph264pay config-interval=1 pt=96 ! rtpbin.send_rtp_sink_0 rtpbin.send_rtp_src_0 ! udpsink host=YOUR_IP port=YOUR_PORT rtpbin.send_rtcp_src_0 sync=false async=false\n</code></pre>\nGstreamer should include rpicamsrc directly nowadays for your convenience.')
