Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607211360, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25317987, text='Also, the Islamic Golden Age is not only about mathematics, but also about other sciences.<p>The chemistry progressed a lot during that time. Several preserved Arabic works contain a much improved classification of the known chemical substances, better than anything that existed before them.<p>That chemical classification was improved only in the 18th century, by several generations of Swedish chemists, leading eventually to the modern chemistry based on the notion of chemical elements, which was created by Lavoisier and his French colleagues, shortly before the French Revolution.<p>Attempting to deny the essential Arabic&#x2F;Islamic contributions to the evolution of the sciences shows just ignorance or bad intentions.')
