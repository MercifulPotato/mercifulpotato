Item(by='Someone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612045141, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25970583, text='“Customers rely on Accounts (I.e. have one or many)”<p>Alternative model: the Customer relation couples a Person to an Account.<p>I think that’s a better model, as it allows Person P to be a Customer at bank B but not at Bank C, Person Q to be a Customer at both B and C, etc.<p>It also allows you to model Persons before you model Accounts, breaking the circularity, allows Companies to become Customers, etc.')
