Item(by='mrtksn', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608011873, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25427051, text='I don’t think so, the look and feels is different.<p>Under the hood it will use some UIKit like UIScrollView but the whole architecture is different.<p>On UIKit, the objects you create stay retained as long as they are used and all you do is to change properties when using.<p>On SwiftUI, everything is released and retained every time there’s a change.<p>That’s why you need to address the life cycle differences when you use UIKit and SwiftUI together.')
