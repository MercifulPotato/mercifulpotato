I told my family not to buy me anything, and I wouldn&#x27;t buy them anything. My parents wrote my name on all the stuff given to nephews and nieces. My dad even offered to give me money and I refused it.<p>Since then, I&#x27;ve had the best winters ever. I don&#x27;t consume. I don&#x27;t fret about gifts. If I want to give something special to someone I love, I do it at another time of the year. It might seem grinchy, but I don&#x27;t feel that way.
