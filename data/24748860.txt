Item(by='CydeWeys', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602448421, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24748762, text='They also would have needed to have used different TLDs to be outside the reach of US law enforcement.  .com is run by Verisign, a US company, and if the FBI gives Verisign a court order to confiscate a given .com domain then Verisign can&#x27;t say no.<p>.com, .net, .org, .edu, and .gov are very much US-based and US-centric TLDs, which lots of people tend to forget.  Whereas other countries use their own ccTLDs, we have .us but barely use it, for historical reasons.')
