Item(by='fuzzfactor', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602430597, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24743245, text='Yes the input impedance of a good tube is much higher than the typical 1 Megohm resistor referencing it to ground.<p>That&#x27;s a million ohms so not much of your guitar signal is wasted passing through it to ground.<p>The input impedance of the tube reacts dynamically according to the signal coming in, and the often dynamic behavior of whatever is plugged directly into it.<p>When that&#x27;s just your guitar without any pedals or anything, that can be the most dynamic from a touch-sensitive point of view.')
