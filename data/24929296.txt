Item(by='tetris11', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603970532, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24928435, text='In bioinformatics, MCMC was used often linkage analysis to sample a &quot;genotype space&quot; of a true inheritance pattern of multiple related individuals to find the location and transmission pathway of disease causing variants.<p>Classical genetics and statistics were very tightly linked in this regard due to scarcity of data at the time (pre-2000).<p>Now that the field has exploded with big data, such statistics are no longer so neccesary to answer the same problems, and a lot of the early mathematical elegance associated with the field has given way to brute-force filtering methods.')
