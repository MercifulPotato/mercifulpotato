The cost of publishing papers online is very small. Sci-Hub is the proof of that.<p>Yet access to many papers costs about US$35 <i>per paper</i>. I understand that <i>none</i> of that income goes to the authors - they don&#x27;t benefit from their own work. That was the whole idea behind copyright.<p>The publishing house set-up is a dinosaur.')
