Item(by='ithkuil', descendants=None, kids=[25057247], score=None, time=1605084367, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25056429, text='What about all those men who cannot reach your ideal of what a &quot;real man&quot; should be?<p>Should we worry about them or dismiss them as unadapted to the society norm du jour? There will always be people who don&#x27;t fit. Nerdy types were losers a few years ago; now macho types are.<p>The word &quot;progress&quot; is quite insidius. It seems to imply there is a direction where &quot;better&quot; is located and we&#x27;re heading towards it.<p>Instead we&#x27;re relentlessly and half-blindly exploring the vast multidimensional space of possibility of the human condition. And this walk happens at different time-scales which are hard to fit in our everyday experience.')
