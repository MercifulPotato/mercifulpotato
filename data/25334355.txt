Item(by='ghaff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607361976, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25334201, text='I think even if you stipulate for purposes of discussion that a lot of companies in the tech space broadly and elsewhere are going to be more flexible in the future, that still leaves WFH&#x2F;Remote to cover a lot of territory:<p>- WFH or in-office 2-3 days per week<p>- Office every Wednesday<p>- Team get-together once a month<p>- Remote in the same&#x2F;adjacent time zone<p>- Remote anywhere you want that we can legally hire you (seems unlikely)<p>- Remote pay-adjusted (or not)<p>- Whether there is an office or not<p>In general, I expect the extreme cases will be fairly rare but pretty much every one of the scenarios (as well as whether they&#x27;re mandatory or optional) will drive different decisions relative to 5-days a week in the office.')
