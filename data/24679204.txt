Item(by='Xylakant', descendants=None, kids=[24680241], score=None, time=1601820992, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24679121, text='You don’t even need to force everyone to stop driving. Once density increases, distances get easier to cover by bike or by walking, adding policies that make people liable for the cost of space to put a car (ban or realistic fees for on-street parking) people will reconsider owning a car. Basically you can still drive - there’s still roads, but you need to stash your vehicle on your property. And then people will start considering whether they still want that garage or maybe a bigger flat would be cool, too.')
