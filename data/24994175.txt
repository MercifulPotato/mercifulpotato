Depression is characterized by many&#x2F;all feelings being severely diminished or completely absent. People who think everything&#x27;s going right in their life still get depressed, and it&#x27;s something of a trope for some depressed people to wonder how they can be depressed when they can&#x27;t point to anything in their life that should be depressing.')
