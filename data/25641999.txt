It is merely the least amount of money an employer can pay an employee without it being illegal, and has very little to do with the cost of living.<p>If I seem argumentative, it is because I am exasperated with the gymnastics that people will do in order to justify the conscription of more and more people into precarious existences under a gig economy, which largely benefits people at the top of the economic ladder at the expense of those at the bottom.')
