Item(by='qqqwerty', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605128930, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061010, text='To be fair to those people, at the beginning of the pandemic there was a lot of uncertainty around COVID-19 transmission rates. Buying a few weeks of food was a good way to hedge against the potential case where COVID-19 was so virulent that even going to a grocery store presented a high risk of transmission. Of course, we now know that even in a hotspot grocery shopping using basic precautions is fairly safe. But it was not clear at the time.')
