Item(by='boulos', descendants=None, kids=[24675970, 24676501], score=None, time=1601770828, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24675256, text='The title has been editorialized unfortunately. The page uses <i>Actively speaking two languages protects against cognitive impairment</i> which is an important summary of the paper: everyone in the study is basically bilingual, but the bilingual composite score (how bilingual you are &#x2F; how much you actively speak and switch languages) is suggested as a precursor of delayed cognitive decline.<p>The title of the original paper is <i>Active bilingualism delays the onset of mild cognitive impairment</i> which is also clear, if a little stilted as a headline.')
