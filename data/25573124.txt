Item(by='qualitypaper', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609266898, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25543360, text='I use a pair of bone conduction headphones and have some cheap earplugs. Better than any ANC headphone&#x2F;earbud I’ve ever had at cancelling noise. You also don’t get that negative ear pressure or buzzing and they can be quite comfortable. I can be in the middle of a construction site or on a plane and not hear a thing. The one drawback is the sound quality but I’m not exactly an audiophile and tbh the newer bone conduction headphones sound fine to me.')
