Item(by='wombatmobile', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603746187, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24900382, text='There are one or more grammatical or syntax peculiarities in the template answer proposed in TFA:<p>“I’m still trying to establish whether this role is a good fit for me. Why? What is this role worth to your company?”<p>The &quot;Why?&quot; is non-sequitously sandwiched in-between a statement of personal intent and a question that seeks to deflect back to the interviewer, reversing the question.<p>Leaving aside the issues of whether each of those pieces is strategically sound or likely to work, the syntax of the response requires (a) unscrambling, and (b) refinement.')
