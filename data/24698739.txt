Item(by='scubbo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601998150, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24694686, text='This highlights an interesting philosophical disparity. You (correctly) point out that a license is a &quot;permission to do something&quot; - but the perception among folks appears to be that the license is in fact what <i>restricts</i> your permissions. Thus, from their perspective, &quot;the smaller the license, the larger your freedoms&quot; - and, so, they conclude (incorrectly, but understandably) that the end-point of &quot;no license whatsoever&quot; would correspond with &quot;complete freedom&quot;.')
