Item(by='laughinghan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605308519, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25088391, text='To clarify, the important thing about &quot;reconciliation&quot; is not about reconciling with war criminals, but about reconciling with supporters of war criminals, which are usually a pretty significant portion (sometimes the overwhelming majority) of the civilian population of the region or community that the war criminal is from.<p>Often, those supporters have been on the receiving end of pretty serious hardships themselves. That doesn&#x27;t justify supporting war crimes, of course, but if you don&#x27;t acknowledge those hardships, you create resentment and the seeds of future strife, rather than healing.')
