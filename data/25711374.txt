Item(by='schoen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610259946, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25708099, text='I found this taxonomy (helping you decide &#x2F; helping you act) interesting, and I wonder whether there are other categories that it might naturally be extended with. Helping you deliberate, helping you discuss, helping you exercise command authority?<p>I was just watching some videos of mission control centers from space missions and they were interestingly different from any situations I&#x27;ve been in myself. They&#x27;re about helping people talk to each other in a very structured way, as well as looking at information together (also in a very structured way) -- with defined scopes of authority and responsibility, and responding to a sometimes quickly-evolving situation.')
