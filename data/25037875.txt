Item(by='hcarvalhoalves', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604946376, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25036663, text='If you follow Lean (where &quot;Minimum Viable Product&quot; originates from), there&#x27;s no hard split between design&#x2F;implementation phases, since the whole concept of MVP is avoiding waste in building something nobody wants.<p>The keyword is &quot;avoiding waste&quot;, not &quot;build something in 1 month&quot;. You can still waste 1mo work pretending to be fast and shipping crap, that&#x27;s not the point.<p>The time a Minimum <i>Viable</i> Product will take is still completely determined by the requirements (definition of &quot;viable&quot;, budget, experience, regulations, etc).')
