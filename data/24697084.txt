Garbage in Garbage out.  In consensus systems, however, any difference in behaviour can be a fatal vulnerability.<p>Some might find the motivation section of Bitcoin&#x27;s BIP340 informative: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;bitcoin&#x2F;bips&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;bip-0340.mediawiki#Motivation" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;bitcoin&#x2F;bips&#x2F;blob&#x2F;master&#x2F;bip-0340.mediawi...</a><p>Or this earlier publication: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;slowli.github.io&#x2F;ed25519-quirks&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;slowli.github.io&#x2F;ed25519-quirks&#x2F;</a> (which has more explanation for the S value related issues that the parent article mostly skips over-- but lacks the breadth and the amazing illustration of the incompatibility train-wreak).')
