Item(by='thamer', descendants=None, kids=[25051820, 25051849], score=None, time=1605039953, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25049253, text='I&#x27;m using Apache Arrow to store CSV-like data and it works amazingly well.<p>The datasets I work with contains a few billion records with 5-10 fields each, almost all 64-bit longs; I originally started with CSV and soon switched to a binary format with fixed-sized records (mmap&#x27;d) which gave great performance improvements, but the flexibility, size gains due to columnar compression and the much greater performance of Arrow for queries that span a single column or a small number of them won me over.<p>For anyone who has to process even a few million records locally, I would highly recommend it.')
