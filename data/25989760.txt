Item(by='keiferski', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612195260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25985361, text='FIRE is really only a good idea if you&#x27;re twenty-something, don&#x27;t mind being ultrafrugal, and pull $100K+ plus benefits from an industry with low entry requirements (i.e. programming and not law.)<p>In virtually all other situations, it&#x27;s a fool&#x27;s errand.
