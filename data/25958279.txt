Item(by='meibo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611934503, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25958029, text='Mind that the amount of QA you ought to have heavily depends on what game you&#x27;re making - e.g. with a story heavy game, you 100% want give or take 3 weeks on every update for localization checks, or for a MMO you probably want to run new clients&#x2F;servers with your testers first for about a week to make sure nothing catastrophic happens, which is especially important in online games(lots of bad PR and real money invested by players - you really don&#x27;t want to have to roll back &quot;saves&quot;).<p>All depends on budget and quantity of course, but this is what&#x27;s usually planned for from my personal experience working in that sector.')
