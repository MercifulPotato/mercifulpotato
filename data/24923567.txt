It is basically an advanced reference design tool. They do the work of making all the right component connections and getting everything on a board so that you can focus on software and basic functionality testing.<p>If your alpha design works as intended, and you realize you didn&#x27;t leave anything out or make other simple common mistakes you can get the $150 files that you can import into your design system and then nudge components around as desired. Or, you can send it off to a more boutique board house to layout, knowing that you are not going to have to come back later and make major disruptive changes.')
