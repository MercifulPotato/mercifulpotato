Item(by='tonyhb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611546547, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25898598, text='You want to use wf-recorder.  This is in my sway config:<p>&gt; set $screenrecord wf-recorder -g &quot;$(slurp)&quot; -f ~&#x2F;screenshots&#x2F;mov-$(date +&quot;%Y-%m-%d--%H-%M-%S.mp4&quot;)<p>&gt; bindsym Shift+Control+F6 exec $screenrecord<p>&gt; bindsym Ctrl+Shift+BackSpace exec killall -s SIGINT wf-recorder<p>Hitting shift-control-f6 will spawn slurp which lets you click and drag to set the area you want to record.  This will then launch wf-recorder and record the selected area, saving to ~&#x2F;screenshots&#x2F;mov-${date}.  You can use ctrl-shift-backspace to kill the recorder and end recording.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;ammen99&#x2F;wf-recorder" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;ammen99&#x2F;wf-recorder</a><p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;emersion&#x2F;slurp" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;emersion&#x2F;slurp</a>')
