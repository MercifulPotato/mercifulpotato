Item(by='hansvm', descendants=None, kids=[25317057, 25321099], score=None, time=1607191718, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25315808, text='Sidestepping your question, open-mindedness comes with its own costs. Properly analyzing an idea takes time away from other things you could be doing, and some ideas are so outlandish that your life will almost certainly be better by simply making the choice to spend as little time as possible flirting with such absurdities (except insofar as you might enjoy toying with &quot;what-if&quot; questions as an end unto itself). Are you practicing open-mindedness...everywhere?<p>It wasn&#x27;t that long ago that some crackpot tried to convince me that the modern atmosphere had damaged my lungs, so to replace the oxygen I was missing I needed to buy hydrogen peroxide injections. The claim as a whole either needs to have major structural flaws or overturn sizable portions of modern chemistry and physics to have a chance at plausibility, and my biases strongly suggest that instead somebody was trying to skirt legal oversight in order to con me.')
