Item(by='saagarjha', descendants=None, kids=[25404253], score=None, time=1607831888, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25403968, text='In every allocator there has to be a way to get “the memory” that is available for you to hand out. When a normal OS is present, system calls are used to get this in the form of pages. On embedded environments you don’t need to do this because “the memory” is just something like “addresses from 0x2000000 to 0x8000000” and you don’t have to ask for it, it’s just there for you to use.')
