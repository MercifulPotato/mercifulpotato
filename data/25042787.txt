Item(by='nicolas_t', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604982080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25040762, text='Another thing I do for restaurants on Tripadvisor is look at reviews by language (works better abroad than in the US). While it&#x27;s a bit stereotyping, I find that if a place is well reviewed by reviewers who write in Italian, French or Japanese, it&#x27;s generally more likely to be to my taste. I&#x27;ll add other languages depending on context , so if I&#x27;m looking for a Mexican, filtering by Spanish reviews is definitely helpful.<p>And helpfully when filtering by language, Tripadvisor will show the spread of reviews for that language making it easy.')
