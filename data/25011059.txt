Item(by='jmsad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604696236, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25007739, text='One thing to help fix this is for the VC community to become more comfortable with (and even encourage) funding rounds which allow founders and early employees to get some liquidity from their equity.  Taking a company from idea to IPO usually takes the better part of a decade (or longer), and it&#x27;s pretty unfair to ask people who have already risked a lot to keep living on peanuts and hoping that some unforeseen situation which may be largely beyond their control doesn&#x27;t send the value of their equity to zero before they get to IPO.')
