Item(by='opnitro', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607812289, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25402357, text='Not OP, but they mean the standard library, which is the set of functions, objects, and datatypes that come with langauge. That&#x27;s not really the best way to learn a language, since you&#x27;ll usually have the documentation at hand if you need it. It&#x27;s more important to understand the conceptual model the language works with, so that when you want to do something you&#x27;ll have an intuitive idea of where to look in the documentation and how to phrase your solution. Memorizing the standard library will simply come with usage, and isn&#x27;t something you need to focus on.')
