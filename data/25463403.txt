Item(by='steffan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608261401, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25463243, text='The 787 also pressurizes the cabin to a lower altitude.  Most aircraft pressurize to an altitude of 8,000 ft (~2,438m).  The 787 pressurizes to 6,000 feet (~1,828m), which is a noticeable difference - it&#x27;s like being in Denver vs. higher up on a mountain.  Your blood oxygen concentrations will be higher as a result.')
