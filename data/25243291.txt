Item(by='mycentstoo', descendants=None, kids=[25243312], score=None, time=1606630449, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25243037, text='Figure out what number you can live on and compare that to the expected growth of your investment. If you have $1 million and you assume a 6% growth rate, you can live on 60k and not work. If you want less risk, assume a lower growth rate. If you want a higher standard of living, work until your nest egg provides a greater income through investment.')
