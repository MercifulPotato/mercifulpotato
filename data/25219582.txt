For baselining, Nevada has ~3.09M people, making it roughly 1&#x2F;4th the population of Ontario (Canada). Sure, there are other (single payer) concerns at play - but the cost difference is crazy.<p>Let&#x27;s assume this was a &gt;10cm (the largest cateorgy) gash on the face or neck. The provider bills $417 (1). Assuming this is a nighttime emergency, and that the doctor traveled for this (and only this) event, the billing cost is $136.70 (2). Assuming the doctor needed an assistant, for an hour to complete this (extremely unlikely) there is a further billed cost of $60.04 (3). The cost of an overnight bed in Ontario is ~$320-500 (4). Finally, the cost of the ambulance (the only thing one would pay for) is $45 (5). This ignores costs such as gas reimburrsal, parking, etc; they can occur, but are rather low.<p>If one is Canadian, the total cost is $45, the cost of the ambulance co-pay. Visitors to Canada would pay an SUBSIDIZED maximum of $646.70, outside of the cost of the ambulance. For fun, let&#x27;s inflate these five times - just in case all of the billing codes are wrong, that&#x27;s still under half (prior to currency conversion) of the cost in the theoretically more efficient &#x2F; for profit system.<p>Sources<p>1. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;ohip_billing_codes&#x2F;66&#x2F;integumentary-system-surgical-procedures" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;ohip_billing_codes&#x2F;66&#x2F;integumentary-s...</a><p>2. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;blog&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;anaesthesia-ohip-billing-codes-cheat-sheet&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;blog&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;anaesthesia-ohip-billing-co...</a><p>3. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;blog&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;anaesthesia-ohip-billing-codes-cheat-sheet&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.dr-bill.ca&#x2F;blog&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;anaesthesia-ohip-billing-co...</a><p>4. <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.grhosp.on.ca&#x2F;care&#x2F;visitors&#x2F;billing&#x2F;room-options" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.grhosp.on.ca&#x2F;care&#x2F;visitors&#x2F;billing&#x2F;room-options</a><p>5. <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.health.gov.on.ca&#x2F;en&#x2F;public&#x2F;publications&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;amb.aspx#:~:text=Ontario%20Residents,a%20resident%20of%20Ontario%2C%20and" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.health.gov.on.ca&#x2F;en&#x2F;public&#x2F;publications&#x2F;ohip&#x2F;amb....</a>')
