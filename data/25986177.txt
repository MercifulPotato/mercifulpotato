Which is one of the weaknesses of the ISC license that doesn&#x27;t appear in either the N-Clause BSD licenses or the MIT license.<p>Because of the poor wording, permission is granted, but the notice is only required on things considered to be copies, rather than all the &quot;children&quot; of the original work.<p>You really want something like:<p>&gt; The above copyright notice and this permission notice shall be included in all copies or substantial portions of the work.')
