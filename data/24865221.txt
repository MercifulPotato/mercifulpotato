Item(by='branweb', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603417047, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24864789, text='Interesting...I wrote this to display a countdown in the i3 status bar:<p><pre><code>  #!&#x2F;usr&#x2F;bin&#x2F;env sh\n\n  DIFF_DAYS=&quot;&quot;\n\n  death_clock () {\n    DEATH_DATE=$(date --date &#x27;YYYY-MM-DD -u&#x27; +%s)\n    TODAY=$(date +%s)\n    DIFF=$(expr &quot;$DEATH_DATE&quot; - &quot;$TODAY&quot;)\n    DIFF_DAYS=$(expr &quot;$DIFF&quot; &#x2F; $(expr 24 \\* 3600))\n  }\n\n  i3status | (read line &amp;&amp; echo &quot;$line&quot; &amp;&amp; read line &amp;&amp; echo &quot;$line&quot; &amp;&amp; read line &amp;&amp; echo &quot;$line&quot; &amp;&amp; while :\n  do\n    read line\n    death_clock\n    echo &quot;,[{\\&quot;full_text\\&quot;:\\&quot;days left: ${DIFF_DAYS}\\&quot;, \\&quot;color\\&quot;: \\&quot;#0000FF\\&quot; },${line#,\\[}&quot; || exit 1\n  done)\n</code></pre>\nDefinitely not polished but seems to work if you fill in the &quot;YYYY-MM-DD&quot; with your expected date of departure.')
