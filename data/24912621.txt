Item(by='Polylactic_acid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603836847, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24911882, text='The same argument was made against banning single use plastic bags in supermarkets in Australia. People said that everyone would just continue to buy them and nothing will change. Turns out that most people did not keep buying them but instead took their old ones back to the store to reuse. Some people still buy them but overall there was a huge reduction.<p>When you give someone something for free they will take that option every time, charge just 15c for the bag and people will decide that they will bring back their old bags because its a small savings for almost no effort.<p>I also never see stray plastic bags floating around the street like I used to.')
