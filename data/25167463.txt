Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605925846, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25167178, text='&gt; This is yet another bait and switch where a public corporation is presented as a private party.<p>No, <i>the above</i> is the fallacy of equivocation. “Private” in “private party” unambiguously refers to the “non-government” sense of “private”, where the “party” may be a corporate entity or natural person. It does not refer to the “private(ly held)” vs. “public(ly traded)” distinction regarding how corporate stocks are traded.')
