Item(by='aidenn0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607067355, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25299910, text='Amps * Volts = Watts<p>Watt-Hours are unambiguous units of energy, Amp hours are ambiguous without knowing the voltage.<p>since you said mAh (milliamp-hours), I&#x27;m thinking you are talking about phone batteries which are typically in the 3.3-3.6V range depending on the chemistry, then 1000mAh would be 3.3-3.6Wh.<p>As a comparison the Dell XPS 13 has a 52Wh battery, so it&#x27;s not a very big battery.  49Wh is what the new MacBook Air has, but the Air has a much lower power CPU.')
