Item(by='Rumudiez', descendants=None, kids=[25973196], score=None, time=1612034720, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25969843, text='Fonts designed in this manner have been around for awhile, so it’s not really a new concept, except in its application to every glyph in a type family. The term I’ve used with type and other designers is actually “tabular,” which contrasts with “proportional.” Look up tabular vs proportional numerals for a topic that’s been around since printing presses ruled publishing.')
