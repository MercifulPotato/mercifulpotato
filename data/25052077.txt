Item(by='arcticbull', descendants=None, kids=[25053404], score=None, time=1605043168, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25050640, text='Buy a pair of Vibrams. Walking in toe shoes that have little to no heel support will naturally change over your gait in a week or two. I used to run 3-5 miles most days on concrete in running shoes and had knee and hip pain afterwards each day. Switching to toe shoes and by extension forefoot running eliminated the pain completely.<p>The impact of walking and running is dissipated into your calves instead of your joints, and the nice thing about muscle is that it grows back. Take it slow as you ramp up, I promise your calves aren&#x27;t ready for it yet.')
