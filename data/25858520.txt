Item(by='pc86', descendants=None, kids=[25859207], score=None, time=1611235310, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25852650, text='I think a more important question is whether or not a layperson should be evaluating technical literature on their own at all. I&#x27;m wholly uneducated and unprepared to evaluate even simple medical literature, so I find a doctor who I trust and who can explain why they do the things they do.<p>There is an idea, especially here in the US, and especially over the last 10-15 years, that you can just &quot;do your own research&quot; and come to correct conclusions about things that have been studied for decades or centuries. You can&#x27;t. If you want to do it for your own edification, or because it&#x27;s interesting, by all means. But please not for making real decisions that have a real impact.')
