Item(by='sam0x17', descendants=None, kids=[24734069, 24733457], score=None, time=1602268851, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24732607, text='The problem with any model where ad performance is based on how much cash you can throw down is that scams are much more profitable typically than legitimate services. A scam page can afford $5&#x2F;click if it&#x27;s scamming 1% of its clicks for $5000. Bidding on clicks is just a race to the bottom in terms of quality.<p>We should instead be using user metrics to evaluate and punish ads that lead to pages users regret navigating to.')
