Item(by='randomsearch', descendants=None, kids=[25025137], score=None, time=1604832866, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25024689, text='I think there&#x27;s an important distinction between recommendation algorithms, which is how I&#x27;d describe what you&#x27;re referring to, and _algorithms designed to maximise engagement_.<p>Recommendation algorithms try to provide content that you&#x27;ll enjoy, like new movies to watch.<p>Engagement algorithms will try to find content that keeps you engaged, even if that means enraging you. They don&#x27;t just exploit biases, rather they exploit what triggers you.')
