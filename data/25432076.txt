Item(by='acdha', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608051310, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25431992, text='It’s an order of magnitude less work to set an IAM policy because that doesn’t require ongoing maintenance commitments. An IAM policy is a one-time setup cost and the limited duration keeps people honest about not accumulating unmanaged local state. It’s also handy for non-administrators to contain a compromise or error - if someone pops a shared system multiple users will be affected.')
