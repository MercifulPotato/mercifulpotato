Item(by='war1025', descendants=None, kids=[25687291], score=None, time=1610126466, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25686835, text='A well cooked pot roast is not that much different from the sous vide trend that&#x27;s becoming so popular.<p>You put the roast in a dutch oven coated in salt and pepper the same as you would with a steak and a bit of water, then you let it cook at ~200F for 4 - 8 hours.<p>Usually you also slow roast some onions, carrots, potatoes, etc. along with it the last little while.<p>Then make a gravy from the drippings.<p>The gravy is good on the meat, but it&#x27;s more for the vegetables. The meat is delicious in its own right.')
