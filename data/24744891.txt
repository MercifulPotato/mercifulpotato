Item(by='29athrowaway', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602413024, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24744815, text='Usually you give each constant a name and then operate on them symbolically.<p>If it&#x27;s a simple enough operation you can skip that and just add a comment.<p>In this example, there are 9 arithmetic operations and there are clearly more readable ways of doing this. This is the point I am trying to make.<p>For the author of this article, using roman numerals and using arabic numerals are the same because they represent the same quantities. However, in practice, if you have 2 accountants and tell one of them to use roman numerals, the one using roman numerals will be less productive, make more mistakes and will likely be frustrated.')
