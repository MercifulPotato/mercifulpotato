Item(by='londons_explore', descendants=None, kids=[24810029, 24810076], score=None, time=1602942765, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24808795, text='I assume that most militaries have satellites with radars that can penetrate most ceilings to see planes in hangars etc.<p>Most militaries also have access to some high orbit satellites that have 24x7 coverage of an area, and then some low orbit satellites which fly overhead every few hours and can grab high res shots.   The combination makes it very hard to sneak a secret plane from one building to another between orbits, since the 24&#x2F;7 coverage will see it (albeit at lower res).')
