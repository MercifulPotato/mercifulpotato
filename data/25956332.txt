Item(by='toyg', descendants=None, kids=[25957174, 25956384], score=None, time=1611921023, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927861, text='Sometimes I think researchers do not understand second-order effects of their pet theories.<p>The societal&#x2F;market result of stuff like this will inevitably be something like &quot;Let&#x27;s crowdsource more of these creative activities, so we can drive down the already rock-bottom compensation rates of the few jobs that cannot be automated - this research says we&#x27;re not going to lose anything on the quality side anyway&quot;. In the (laudable) quest to make amateurs respectable, they might well end up stripping even professionals of what little respectability they had accrued.<p>It&#x27;s likely that democratization of creativity is a fundamentally unstoppable force in itself (like we&#x27;ve seen in photography, music, video etc); there is no need to hasten its rise though.')
