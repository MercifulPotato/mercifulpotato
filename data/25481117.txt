Item(by='closeparen', descendants=None, kids=[25482383, 25482301, 25481916, 25482203], score=None, time=1608411439, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25480713, text='Encountering people who live on that level can be a trip. I went to lunch with some classmates after lecture. I had been fixated on the content. But the salient facts to one of my classmates were instead: who had spoken, with how much confidence, how often, for how long, how the professor seemed to feel about it.<p>It was eye opening. There was this whole other dimension to the room, which I could barely see, but to her it was front and center.<p>I don’t aspire to be like that primarily or exclusively, but sometimes it’s helpful to notice and pay attention to.')
