However, advertising in the same forum supposedly has value.  If a company is promoting stories <i>for</i> a certain candidate or issue, and banning all stories&#x2F;posts&#x2F;accounts <i>against</i>, why does that not count as an in-kind donation, worth as much as the equivalent ads would cost?<p>(Not arguing that this practice is illegal per se, but it seems like it should be acknowledged&#x2F;reported.)')
