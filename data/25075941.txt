It already happens.<p>This is a little different as the employer is making a trade off between employee experience and employee cost. Employers don&#x27;t have an incentive to replace an employee with an equally skilled employee at the same market rate, but when the salary is randomly chosen out of a range, employers do have an incentive to replace high rollers with new employees who are statistically likely to roll something lower.')
