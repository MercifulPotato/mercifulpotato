So the First Amendment limits not just the laws the federal government can pass and enforce, but the laws state governments can pass and enforce, too.<p>With some exceptions, the First Amendment also prohibits laws <i>compelling</i> speech. This is at the root of platforms&#x27; legal rights to set their own rules for content moderation, or to choose what to publish.
