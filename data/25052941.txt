Item(by='mdpm', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605047383, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25039045, text='Consciousness is a property we ascribe to our own awareness of our awareness. As we are part of the universe, if consciousness is a thing, it is then a property of the universe, not just the human mind.<p>The human mind is a plastic, adaptive system, which is shaped by senses that consume the universe and which attempt to model the universe. It is past and present interlocked - causality, bound up in tight knots of matter.<p>This is the universe, being aware of itself. It is perhaps not capable of being understood, just reveled in.')
