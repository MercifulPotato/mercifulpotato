Item(by='ghaff', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603979958, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24927553, text='This is almost certainly not the sort of thing that a different CoC would have made any different absent an absurd IMO rule such as &quot;Do not criticize individuals, projects, companies, etc.&quot;<p>Rather, this is a case of where do you draw the line when something&#x2F;anything makes someone uncomfortable enough to complain. Do you apply a reasonable person standard and perhaps tell that person &quot;We hear you but we can&#x27;t agree and we&#x27;re not going to do anything about it. Sorry.&quot;? Or do you say &quot;We understand that we can&#x27;t tell you how to feel so we accept your complaint.&quot;?<p>In practice, for many situations, especially community events tend towards the latter.')
