Item(by='Qwertious', descendants=None, kids=[25134243, 25125507], score=None, time=1605628101, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25123135, text='When you&#x27;re writing into a text file and then save it, you don&#x27;t have to manually select what lines or pages you&#x27;re saving - you&#x27;re saving <i>all</i> of it.<p>This is the mental model 99% of newbies will have for something that basically pledges to save and sync each version of your files.<p>Deviating from the user&#x27;s mental model will create roadbumps and should not be done without a very strong rationale. Especially not for a feature that&#x27;s fundamentally optional.')
