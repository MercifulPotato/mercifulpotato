Item(by='renewiltord', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610320412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25711362, text='I think you misunderstand the purpose of a summary of a person . It isn&#x27;t P(think of this subject | think of this person), it&#x27;s P(think of this person | think of this subject).<p>Likewise, in a summary of a subject, a person would show up if P(think of this subject | think of this person) shows up.<p>So, for instance, Nelson Mandela shows up in the intro to Apartheid and vice versa; Pi shows up in Fabrice Bellard, but Bellard doesn&#x27;t show up in Pi; Poor Richard&#x27;s Almanack links to Benjamin Franklin but Benjamin Franklin doesn&#x27;t mention PRA in its summary.')
