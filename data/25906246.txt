Your goal isn&#x27;t likely to be to subordinate your life entirely to what the company wants, in a cult-like manner, with no regard for any other priorities like family, friends or personal growth - thus at the very least the kernel of mistrust always remains.<p>Some companies just manage to mask it better or tone it down to a level that if felt to be acceptable.')
