Item(by='repsilat', descendants=None, kids=[25148081, 25147814], score=None, time=1605771130, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25146992, text='Actually the negative leap second is &quot;more monotonic&quot; than the regular one. Normally a leap second adds an extra second (58, 59, 60, 00), but a negative leap second skips one (58, 00).<p>Uniquely specifying the four times in the first sequence as UNIX timestamps isn&#x27;t strictly possible, but it is possible with negative leap seconds.')
