And even in the case of some carpal tunnel.<p>But I went over some Sarno-related forum, and the mainstream view seems to be that pretty much all chronic neuropathic pain (including all NCV and EMG-confirmed pain) and all chronic musculoskeletal pain (including structural issues on x-ray) are mind-body. And there&#x27;s a significant nutty minority who seems to think even some cancer and such is mind-body.<p>Also, how does it explain people who have chronic pain, then surgery, then they never experience pain again?<p>I&#x27;d like to buy into this, but that just seems vaccine conspiracy level crazy - overly simplistic for most cases to say the least.
