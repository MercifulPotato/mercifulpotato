T+2 is not because of technological limitations of traditional databases or trust issues. Instantaneous settlement is possible (for example in the UK we have instantaneous inter-bank transfers), but it is not desired. Having delayed settlement means you can net offsetting transactions, it means you can handle outages in the infrastructure, it means you can unwind and correct errors.')
