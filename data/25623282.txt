Item(by='dgb23', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609695378, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25623118, text='The interesting thing is that Lisp syntax editing mechanics scale. There are more “moves” that you can do with a Lisp, especially if most your code is also functional.<p>My approach: learn 2-4 navigational moves first for a while. Then add more nav and editing moves at a later point.<p>As with everything: deliberate practice leads to mastery. At some point it becomes apparent that the weird syntax is a feature that has huge upsides (another one being macros for example).')
