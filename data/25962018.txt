Item(by='bonyt', descendants=None, kids=[25962615, 25962737], score=None, time=1611952175, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25961443, text='macOS doesn&#x27;t have sudoedit, but you can make a symlink to it, as sudo is effectively a multi-call binary:<p><pre><code>   ln -s `which sudo` sudoedit\n</code></pre>\nand then call it:<p><pre><code>   .&#x2F;sudoedit\n</code></pre>\nSo don&#x27;t take the lack of a `sudoedit` command as evidence that a system isn&#x27;t vulnerable.  I don&#x27;t know if the sudo included with macOS has been exploited though.')
