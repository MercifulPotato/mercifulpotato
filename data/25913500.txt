Item(by='tammet', descendants=None, kids=[25916279, 25913905, 25913914], score=None, time=1611647420, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25907346, text='The whole field has been dominated by research, i.e. the wish to make simple things complicated (in order to publish papers) as opposed to engineering, i.e. making complicated things simple (in order to produce usable software efficiently). As a result the standards are horrendously - and needlessly - complicated. The few major practical outcomes like the schema.org, json-ld and the google annotation system, are results of engineering, not research. Alas, json-ld has also taken a turn towards hypercomplexities.')
