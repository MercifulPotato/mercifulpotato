Item(by='scraplab', descendants=None, kids=[25371243], score=None, time=1607584886, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25366301, text='I followed the Hifiberry&#x2F;Beocreate guide to install a Beocreate amplifier inside an old Beovox CX50 speaker.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.hifiberry.com&#x2F;beocreate&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.hifiberry.com&#x2F;beocreate&#x2F;</a><p>Inside it runs HifiberryOS on a RPi 4, which I use to connect to Spotify and serve Bluetooth.<p>It runs pretty well, but the Spotify connection sometimes needs a kick (reboot) after a long idle period.<p>The main advantage of HifiberryOS for my setup is that it can control the DAC on the amp directly, including installing profiles for my specific speaker and managing the EQ.<p>It’s a shame the Beocreate amp is so expensive (£150), otherwise I’d set up a few more of these.')
