When you have more time than money, prefer rolling your own. It&#x27;s a well-known principle.<p>For a VC-backed startup with millions galore and a pressure to beat competition, buying is natural.<p>For a bootstrapped company or a side project, building your own may be best, especially when the advanced features that make market-leading solutions more expensive does not add a lot of value at the company&#x27;s current scale, and the growth is not exponential.')
