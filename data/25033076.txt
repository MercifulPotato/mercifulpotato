Linux will not help if you execute it.<p>Rule #3 -- If you can&#x27;t track it but need to run anyway, jail the heck out of it. Create a VM and run it inside, disabling also its ability to use network for anything else than reaching the Internet. Make sure it can&#x27;t reach any other machine in your network or ports on your PC through loopback.')
