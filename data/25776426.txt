Item(by='1_player', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610633545, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25768078, text='I disagree. I think a common antipattern is piping only one thing.<p>This is good and readable:<p><pre><code>    nuclear_missile\n    |&gt; open_hatch()\n    |&gt; open_fuel_lines()\n    |&gt; fire_thrusters(:all)\n    |&gt; configure_gps(%Coordinates{...})\n</code></pre>\nThis is unnecessary:<p><pre><code>    word_count = \n      words\n      |&gt; Enum.count()\n</code></pre>\nThis is better:<p><pre><code>    word_count = Enum.count(words)\n</code></pre>\nPipes read like a list of bullet points. We can easily deal with up to a dozen bullet points, but a single bullet point is bad grammar style.')
