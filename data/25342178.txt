Item(by='fairity', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607404798, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25341839, text='If I&#x27;m not mistaken, your claim is that: In times of crisis, leaders need to clearly and succinctly convey direction.<p>This is generally a good rule to follow because we want to maximize compliance, and being anything but succinct defeats that goal.<p>However, this case is an exception to the rule because there&#x27;s no action that requires compliance.  The official is, in fact, asking for no compliance.  Therefore, there&#x27;s no need to be succinct.  In fact, it&#x27;s a great opportunity to build trust with the general public so that when compliance is required, you are able to achieve it.')
