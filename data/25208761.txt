Item(by='TeMPOraL', descendants=None, kids=[25216526], score=None, time=1606309444, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25204677, text='Which is a sane pattern if a language has proper destructors (a seemingly non-existent thing in GC-enabled languages, unfortunately).<p>In C++, you&#x27;d stack-allocate an object representing a spinner, that spawns said spinner in its constructor, and deletes it in its destructor. Then the spinner doesn&#x27;t outlive the processing code, no matter what path execution took to end the processing.<p>There are similar patterns in GC-enabled languages (&quot;with&quot; idiom). Async does throw a wrench into this a bit, though.')
