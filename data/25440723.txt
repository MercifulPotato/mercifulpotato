Item(by='skrause', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608112245, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25440605, text='You can self-host the receiving side of your mail server (with spam filtering etc.), but <i>send</i> all your mail through a mail provider with a good reputation. Configure your own SMTP server to use that other server as a relay, you can even do your own DKIM signing before sending off your mail. At least then you shouldn&#x27;t have a problem with IP reputation.')
