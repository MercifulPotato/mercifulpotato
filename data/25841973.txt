First is regular that runs on 8086 and other was Windows&#x2F;386 2.0.<p>But confusion continue with Windows 2.1. There was also two versions - Windows&#x2F;286 2.1 (that also can run on 8086) and Windows&#x2F;386 2.1.<p>Windows 3.0 was like One-Size-Fits-All because minimum was 8086.
