Great for tutorials and experimental results.<p>Add identifiers and semantic markup to any concept to put your prose in the Linked Open Data cloud.<p>Anyone can tweak, clone and remix your articles. Spread your ideas, facilitate knowledge access and reuse.<p>Let your readers choose a view that suits them. Design your own or use preset themes.<p>Open source and under ongoing development...dogfooding, feedback and contributions welcome.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;dokie.li&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;dokie.li&#x2F;</a><p>For a quick peak at what a dokie annotated page looks like:\n<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;gallery&#x2F;ffH01dh" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;imgur.com&#x2F;gallery&#x2F;ffH01dh</a><p>I switch back and forth between Dokie and\n<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;web.hypothes.is&#x2F;about&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;web.hypothes.is&#x2F;about&#x2F;</a> and hope these features become more widely used.')
