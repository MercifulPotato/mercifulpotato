Guess what: it worked.<p>Which OT1H is a valid reason not to go all &quot;spirit&quot; and &quot;ideology&quot; in business; OTOH, if what you&#x27;re all about <i>is</i> spirit and ideology, it&#x27;s a valid reason to stop thinking what you&#x27;re doing is &quot;Open Source&quot; and go back to the original &quot;Free Software&quot; terminology.<p>As I think someone else mentioned already in this discussion, the term &quot;FOSS&quot; does more harm than good by conflating these different things, and should be avoided.')
