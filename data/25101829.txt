Item(by='michaelt', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605453662, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25100146, text='DMX is a one-way single-master bus.<p>That means you can&#x27;t have multiple light switches on the same network without quite a bit of complexity, you can&#x27;t detect what&#x27;s on the network, you can&#x27;t update firmware or do device setup over the network, and so on.<p>In stage applications that&#x27;s not a problem because all the light switches are in one place - the lighting desk - and the lighting guy there can see all the lights at the same time.')
