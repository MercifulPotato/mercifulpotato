Item(by='maratc', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606984457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25284497, text='&gt; National net wealth, also known as national net worth, is the total sum of the value of a nation&#x27;s assets minus its liabilities.<p>For the little sense that comparing a public company to a nation state makes, one still might be inclined to compare Apples to apples (and not to Oranges).<p>In this case, one should compare National Wealth (country&#x27;s  assets minus its liabilities) against Apple&#x27;s assets minus its liabilities. That calculation gives us about 65B dollars, which puts Apple somewhere between Ghana (95th) and Turkmenistan (96th).')
