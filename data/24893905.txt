Item(by='eigenvector', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603700866, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24886461, text='From a security perspective, ChromeOS bothers me because it has a much shorter software support lifecycle than the hardware lifespan.<p>We have ChromeOS devices out there are perfectly able to run the latest ChromeOS in terms of specs but aren&#x27;t supported anymore by Google because of an arbitrary time-based EOL and therefore will either be browsing the web unpatched or thrown away.<p>Whereas any other x86 computer from the last 12+ years or so can have up-to-date Windows or Linux put on it.<p>Another step on our march to disposable everything.')
