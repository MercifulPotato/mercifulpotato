Item(by='autocorr', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605777613, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25146808, text='Just a note for anyone thinking about transitioning from macOS to a Linux desktop workstation, like the author here: I would advise against using &quot;as light a desktop environment as I can stomach&quot;. Full-featured desktops like KDE and Gnome are comparatively light on resources and also simplify your personal system administration considerably. If you want to customize everything, by all means! :) That&#x27;s the beauty of Free Software. But despite what you may remember from 10+ years ago, KDE today is definitely not &quot;heavy&quot;.')
