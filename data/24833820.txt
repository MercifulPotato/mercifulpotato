Item(by='Myrmornis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603166578, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24833599, text='(Delta author here.) You can disable syntax highlighting, either by customizing the {plus,minus}-*-style options to not use &#x27;syntax&#x27; as the foreground color, or by selecting the diff-highlight or diff-so-fancy emulation modes. That would allow you to still have some of the other features if they are attractive to you, such as side-by-side view, line numbers, restructuring and streamlining of the default diff format output, copyable code (no +&#x2F;- characters), etc.')
