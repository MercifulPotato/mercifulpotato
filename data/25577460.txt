Item(by='kelnos', descendants=None, kids=[25580167, 25578581], score=None, time=1609293381, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25576989, text='I adopted a then-8-week-old kitten less than two months ago, and it&#x27;s been an eye-opening delight watching him adapt just to things in my <i>home</i>.<p>During his first week he managed to hop onto a window sill, and then fell off when he tried to turn around to walk along it the other way.
