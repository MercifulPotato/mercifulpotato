Leafy greens are easy to grow hydroponically because they grow quickly. The problem with them is that they have nearly zero calories.<p>People invest money into growing them hydroponically because they don&#x27;t keep, or transport well, which makes them incredibly expensive out-of-season. The high margins make it economical. Nobody&#x27;s going to be growing rice hydroponically, for instance, because rice retails for 75 cents a pound.')
