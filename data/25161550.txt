Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605887483, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25161156, text='There is plenty of idea space to explore in programming languages.<p>ColorForth, for example... lets you do some of the work a parser would do, at edit time, by adding additional attributes to the source.<p>Verilog, Ladder Logic, and Register Transfer Language all allow the programmer to specify a flow of logic and expressions that is always running, instead of a flow of instructions that we&#x27;re all used to.  Mixing this paradigm with the traditional code we&#x27;re used to offers powerful possibilities.<p>Transforming spreadsheets to a set of statements that are always executed, and vice versa could be fruitful as well.')
