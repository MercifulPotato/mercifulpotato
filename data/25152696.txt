Item(by='timr', descendants=None, kids=[25152791], score=None, time=1605810830, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25152431, text='There&#x27;s no one way to estimate it, so any such tool is just going to turn into a reflection of the assumptions used to create it.<p>Setting aside the probability that someone could be <i>asymptomatic and contagious</i> after a negative test (which actually seems to be fairly low), you&#x27;d need to know the false-negative characteristics of the different tests that were used.
