Item(by='nfriedly', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608567655, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25495251, text='My last employer, Tanium, offered single-trigger RSU&#x27;s (vesting required time but no liquidity event) - they were taxed as they vested.<p>We had a couple of choices to pay the taxes: the default was that the employer would buy back some of the stock and use that cash to pay the taxes. Employees would get to keep 70-some percent of the stock. The other option was that employees could write the company a check for the taxes shortly before vesting, and then keep all of the stock.')
