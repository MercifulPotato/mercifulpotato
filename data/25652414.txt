Item(by='throwaway201103', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609887524, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25650224, text='I got into programming at around middle-school age, with no particular guidance from my parents beyond general encouragement.<p>I have three kids and none of them are interested in programming.<p>It&#x27;s fine to support and encourage something your kids are interested in, and to give them opportunities to try things and discover those interests. But be careful you&#x27;re not pushing them into something just because you like it. Your kids are independent human beings and may be interested in very different things than you are, or than their siblings are.')
