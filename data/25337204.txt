Item(by='Karawebnetwork', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607373761, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25336635, text='About<p>Teddit is a free and open source alternative Reddit front-end focused on privacy. Teddit doesn&#x27;t require you to have JavaScript enabled in your browser. The source is available on Codeberg at <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;codeberg.org&#x2F;teddit&#x2F;teddit" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;codeberg.org&#x2F;teddit&#x2F;teddit</a>.<p>No JavaScript or ads<p>All requests go through the backend, client never talks to Reddit<p>Prevents Reddit from tracking your IP or JavaScript fingerprint<p>Lightweight (teddit frontpage: ~30 HTTP requests with ~270 KB of data downloaded vs. Reddit frontpage: ~190 HTTP requests with ~24 MB)')
