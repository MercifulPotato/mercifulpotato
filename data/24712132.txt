Item(by='MereInterest', descendants=None, kids=[24717239], score=None, time=1602101137, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24711688, text='I&#x27;d rather have language features to cover those use cases than a preprocessor.<p>As an example of what I mean, in Java and C++, class properties are typically accessed by getters and setters, since you might want to change the internal structure of a class without changing the interface.  In Python and C#, you can get the same benefit by changing a member variable to a property.  As a result, the boilerplate of getters&#x2F;setters isn&#x27;t needed.')
