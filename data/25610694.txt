Item(by='KingPrad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609565891, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25604867, text='In the US, asbestos litigation has a special system where lots of companies are named in each suit, then they compete to eliminate themselves as the main contributors to the person&#x27;s ailments.<p>For example, if a person did a brake job in the \n50s once, Napa Auto Parts is in the lawsuit because they made brake pads with asbestos. But oh, they were boilermaker for decades, working around asbestos-lined systems? Well, Napa is probably off the hook or they&#x27;ll chuck a few thousand at the person to settle, leaving the larger source more on the hook.<p>It&#x27;s a game of subtraction and divvying up cost by contributing exposure mostly.<p>Source: spouse worked on these cases.')
