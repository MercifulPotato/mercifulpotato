Item(by='dkjaudyeqooe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611756730, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25927931, text='Like every company, there is some good stuff and some trash. The materials tend to be the key to which is which.<p>Anything made of solid wood or laminated wood is great because it will take knocks and bumps and ast basically forever. Things like chairs and tables.<p>Lots of their all-metal stuff is good. One of their desk lamps with springs is a design classic, very cheap and amazing value.<p>Their non-stick kitchen pans are very good value as is most of their kitchen stuff, including glass, ceramics and some plastic stuff.<p>Anything made of MDF or manufactured wood is just pure trash.')
