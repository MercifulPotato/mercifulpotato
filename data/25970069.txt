Item(by='zeroimpl', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612022373, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25965310, text='The 8-bit vs 10-bit aspect is entirely unrelated to the HDR vs SDR aspect.<p>10-bit is just added precision in your numbers, and ought to be interpreted as adding more decimal places (makes more sense when you imagine the values going from 0-1 instead of 0-255).<p>HDR is indicated as a bit somewhere in metadata to tell the downstream components how to interpret the pixel values.<p>To be extra pedantic, HDR is a bit to indicate how to interpret the brightness (luma) channel. There will be other bits for signaling how to interpret the color channels.<p>Often these things are all done together, which is generally referred to as HDR10.')
