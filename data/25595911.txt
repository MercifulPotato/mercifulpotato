Item(by='chrisa', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609441510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25595128, text='If anyone does this but finds their camera turns off after 30 minutes (and if you can&#x27;t fix it by updating the firmware or changing settings), try this:<p>connect your camera via hdmi AND usb, then:<p>&gt; brew install gphoto2<p>&gt; gphoto2 --summary<p>You&#x27;ll know it worked if you see your camera make&#x2F;model show up.<p>That one --summary call is all that&#x27;s needed for my Canon m50 to stay awake forever!  I&#x27;m not sure if it works for other models, but when I figured that out I was super happy :)')
