The Bitcoin Craze where awareness of bitcoin hit the general public and the techies who got in first exploited the rubes for everything they were worth(and cryptocurrency industry&#x27;s response to the criticism: they should have known better!?!? That tarnished <i>all</i> cryptocurrency in the eyes of the public and they learned to avoid <i>all</i> of it, not just Bitcoin.
