Item(by='PragmaticPulp', descendants=None, kids=[25980154], score=None, time=1612111507, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25976488, text='Misunderstandings like this are part of the problem.<p>Anyone can take out a short position. It&#x27;s only marginally more complicated than buying a stock. Plenty of retail investors are taking short positions, too.<p>More importantly, the idea that short interest only goes down is a mistake. Anyone, retail or institutional, can close out a short position and re-open a new short position at a higher price. Total short interest remains unchanged, even though earlier shorts have been closed out.')
