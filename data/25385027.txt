Item(by='user-the-name', descendants=None, kids=[25386093], score=None, time=1607690114, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25384707, text='Here&#x27;s the thing, though: Societies always have norms, and norms are enforced by making norm-breaking views shunned. Letting people speak their objectionable views with no repercussions normalises those views, and makes them more acceptable.<p>Objectionable views HAVE to be opposed, or they will fester and grow. Polite disagreement doesn&#x27;t help, because the person will just go on to post them again, and again, and again. The fact that I politely disagreed with one of them will not help with the other 99 times.<p>There needs to be visible consequences for breaking norms, or the norms themselves will break.')
