Item(by='jcranmer', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602028405, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24700276, text='&gt; - The default &#x27;latin1&#x27; character set is in fact cp1252, not ISO-8859-1, meaning it contains the extra characters in the Windows codepage.<p>Actually, it&#x27;s generally saner to assume that people mean Windows-1252 when they say ISO-8859-1. Charset labeling is frequently incorrect, and C1 characters are so infrequently used that seeing one pop up probably means you actually wanted Windows-1252 instead.')
