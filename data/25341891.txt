Item(by='tzs', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607402378, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25340278, text='It might not be enough to get an <i>arrest</i> warrant, but it could be enough for a <i>search</i> warrant.<p>Find out the ISP that owns the address, ask the ISP what customer it was assigned to at the time in question, and while that doesn&#x27;t tell you who was using it for the particular accesses you are investigating (it could be anyone in the house at the time, or someone outside using that house&#x27;s WiFi, or someone from anywhere in the world using malware installed on a computer in the house), it does tell you that that house has a decent chance of having relevant evidence, and that will probably be enough to get a warrant to go look for that evidence.')
