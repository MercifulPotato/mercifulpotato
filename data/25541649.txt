Item(by='ps1983', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608960816, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25541269, text='I had a similar realisation when I started my IT career. I handled it by keeping my work and ambition separate. But not distinctly apart. Started my career in 2007, I was assigned to a Java maintenance project, while I had interest in databases and data warehousing which was lucrative during that period. So I tried to gain knowledge in that space during my personal time. During a meet the topic came for discussion that&#x27;s when I volunteered to do a proof of concept.<p>It worked for me. Idea is to achieve the thing that you want to do, without losing what you already have. Career is a journey and not a destination, and never make money the driving light.<p>It may sound philosophical but this worked for me. Hopefully it will work for you as well.')
