Item(by='jabroni_salad', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604638299, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25003638, text='Pretty much every call center every day. It&#x27;s a numbers game between having enough agents to handle every call without the queue getting backed up, and having few enough agents that they aren&#x27;t sitting around with nothing to do. This is called the Erlang C formula and there are a variety of calculators on the net if you want to play with it.<p>I currently work at a place that prioritizes employee utilization over process speed, so my team has a foreverqueue to ensure that time on task (TOT on my metrics dashboard) is maximized to its fullest. I&#x27;m just glad it is tickets and not phone calls.')
