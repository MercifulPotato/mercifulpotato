A person can find their life ruined merely by saying the wrong thing <i>or in the wrong tone</i>, all while being told that the system is just and fair and seeking to protect the vulnerable. It is dishonest, it is gaslighting, and it is frankly Soviet.<p>I am a criminal defense attorney with experience in personnel law.
