Item(by='beagle3', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603795171, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24900795, text='I used to run a no-account feed reader on my laptop, but realized it&#x27;s a mistake -- because it can only update when it&#x27;s on, and if you&#x27;re e.g. away for a week without connectivity (highly recommended!) you can&#x27;t get the updates back -- some of the feeds I follow average 20 posts&#x2F;day, but only publish the recent 100 on RSS.<p>I switched to an always-on server app (NewsBlur paid service, which has a no-ad app for iOS, and which I highly recommend, and I assume the Android version is good as well) - but it does require an account (you can self host; I was too lazy)')
