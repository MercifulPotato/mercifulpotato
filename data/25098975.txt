Item(by='Lio', descendants=None, kids=[25099055, 25099080], score=None, time=1605423968, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25098910, text='I guess I’ve got used to working with both sets of shortcuts but I miss the Vim undo system in other apps.<p>It’s worth noting that u and ctrl-r don’t operate on an undo stack but on an undo tree.<p>So if you have a series of edits and undo them all then do something else you can still get back to where you were before.<p>Use g+ and g- to walk the tree and the Gundo plugin is gives you a nice visual diff interface.<p>So having a separate shortcut and “stack” is actually a great feature of Vim IMHO.')
