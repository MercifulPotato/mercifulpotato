Using any database requires building up an expertise and understanding how to use its capabilities properly. If you hit a wall with a non-distributed database and your solution is to replace it with a distributed one - you will have a bad time. The surface area of what you need to know to use it properly still includes your basic data modeling, indices, troubleshooting that was required before, with a whole lot of networking, consensus protocols and consistency models to worry about (just to name a few).')
