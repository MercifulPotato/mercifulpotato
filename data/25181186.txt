Item(by='trentnix', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606079757, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25181108, text='In the bike business (and many others), there are middlemen in between the middlemen! Between me and a bike brand would often be a distributor and in between me and the distributor was a sales rep! I found my sales reps (who took 1-4 points off the margin) to be generally likeable and altogether useless. But there they were, a relic of bike retail of old, still hanging on.<p>And that&#x27;s the view of more than a few consumers in terms of how they view their bike shop. Anything that can be sold direct, will be.')
