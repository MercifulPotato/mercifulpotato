Item(by='abduhl', descendants=None, kids=[25784667, 25784747], score=None, time=1610665201, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25783950, text='It&#x27;s important to note that sovereignty can mean different things.  Certainly the federal government and each state government are different sovereigns, but that doesn&#x27;t mean that the federal government can&#x27;t use eminent domain to seize land in a state.  The federal government is the supreme sovereign in the USA.<p>It is similar for tribes: they are recognized as sovereigns on the same level as states.  The federal government remains supreme and, in fact, can revoke their sovereignty at any time through Congress.')
