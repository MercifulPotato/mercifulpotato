In contrast, the article has a weird setup for an apartment.<p>There are adapters you can use to work around the lack of wires. But there is a missing &quot;C&quot; (common) wire. So, as you discovered, smart thermostats have no easy way to also power themselves.<p>I was lucky when I added my smart thermostat. It turned out that my 20 y&#x2F;o house already had an 8 wire cable preinstalled.<p>For people stuck with 4 wires, there are adapters available that will multiplex two control signals onto one existing wire. This lets you free up another of the existing wires to become the needed fifth C wire.<p>Here&#x27;s one setup I found with a quick search. There&#x27;s even a video.
