Item(by='eecc', descendants=None, kids=[25484741, 25487916], score=None, time=1608450283, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25483297, text='Well, to be honest I much prefer - and trust - fully automated test pipelines to manuality. It affords more frequent and thorough verification of the final artifacts.<p>The problem comes when the automation is only partial because added at a later stage, and the existing software does not lend itself to testing.<p>The risk there is that some short-sighted line manager will go “YOLO, I have a deadline” and skip the tests rather than flag the problem and dedicate budget and effort to redesign the existing for testability.<p>That’s where guidance and oversight are key, and more often than not, sorely lacking.')
