Item(by='AnimalMuppet', descendants=None, kids=[25063457, 25065072], score=None, time=1605124031, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061830, text='The tower of theorems may be a graph, but it is not a directed acyclic graph.  (You probably want it to be directed, though...)<p>That is, you want to get to Z?  Well, you start at A.  But you can get there by the sequence B, D, H, R, Z, or you can get there by B, C, F, H, R, Z, or by B, C, F, J, Q, S, U, Z.  There is more than one route to proving many important theorems.')
