Item(by='kube-system', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604971459, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25040879, text='I don’t think the engineering of the device necessarily has any relevance to this problem. At its core, this is a consumer law problem, not an engineering problem.<p>We have laws to protect consumer because they have a right not to be cheated out of their money.  We <i>don’t</i> make it the job of lawyers and legislators to make decisions about which engineering architectures are right or wrong.<p>If we decide that consumers should be able to reasonably expect their widgets to last X duration of time, then that right shouldn’t be predicated on an engineering decision the consumer knows nothing about or has no power to choose.')
