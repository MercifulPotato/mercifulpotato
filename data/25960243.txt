Item(by='wtallis', descendants=None, kids=[25960327], score=None, time=1611943167, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25959865, text='The PCIe is all on a single IO die, but internally it is organized into quadrants that can produce some NUMA effects. So it is probably worth trying out the motherboard firmware settings to expose your CPU as multiple NUMA nodes, and using the FIO options to allocate memory only on the local node, and restricting execution to the right cores.')
