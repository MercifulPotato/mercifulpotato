This would leave us with a slow but dynamic <i>high-level-variant</i> (CPython) and typed <i>lower-level-variant</i> (EPython, mypyc &amp; co) to compile performant extension modules, which you can easily import into your CPython code.<p>The most prominent of such projects I know of is mypyc [0], which is already used to improve performance for mypy itself and the <i>black</i> [1] code formatter.
