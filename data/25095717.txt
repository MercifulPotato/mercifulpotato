Item(by='pyrale', descendants=None, kids=[25096362], score=None, time=1605388701, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25095198, text='&gt; Turns out some other page is editing its state<p>The solution to this particular problem is to stop thinking in terms of page.<p>Which page you&#x27;re on is only a matter of view, and maybe a minimal amount of view-related model. Most of your model data should be related to your application logic, not to a specific page or component. Separating your model logic and your view logic helps a lot to avoid having this problem, because your model logic becomes agnostic to what page modifies it.')
