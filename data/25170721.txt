Item(by='barnacled', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605977151, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25170030, text='I think there&#x27;s something to this but Derek is falling into a couple traps here - first the trap of good, pithy writing, and secondly assuming everybody else has he has.<p>I absolutely accept that learning to live frugally and no longer make the same old mistakes with money is a huge advantage. It&#x27;s amazing how that can compound and no longer having to worry about money is very freeing.<p>But then there&#x27;s the whole &#x27;and never had a job again&#x27; bit and the implication that his further success was either immaterial or just an inevitable consequence of having saved some money or maintaining a surplus - obviously not the case for very many.<p>It&#x27;s very well written and pithy but, like much of PG&#x27;s writing, leaves you feeling like hands have been waved like windmills.')
