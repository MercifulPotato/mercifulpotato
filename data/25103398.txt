Item(by='DangitBobby', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605464633, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25100411, text='The sentences have different meanings.<p>&quot;I don&#x27;t think a lot of people know what they want.&quot; -&gt; I believe it is a rare trait in a person to know what they want.<p>&quot;I think a lot of people don&#x27;t know what they want.&quot; -&gt; I believe it is a common trait for a person not to know what they want.<p>These things are not mutually exclusive nor mutually inclusive.<p>Lots of similar sounding statements are subtly different like this in English. &quot;I am not happy&quot; and &quot;I am unhappy&quot; have slightly different meanings as well.')
