Now, it seems clear that society is poorly equipped and adjusting poorly to social media.<p>I think giving more control to centrist platforms is the least bad of all the bad options I see.  I&#x27;m not sure how to mitigate the long-term risks introduced, but the bifurcation of society appears to be a slow motion car crash in progress.<p>I&#x27;d love to be convinced that there are better options, especially if they allowed me to move closer to free speech maximalism.<p>I think it would help to change middle school and high school civics curricula by adding cognitive biases, logical fallacies (including some basic statistical fallacies), and other tools to better handle social media.')
