Item(by='mikewarot', descendants=None, kids=[25063498], score=None, time=1605124487, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25061964, text='This is a new class of devices that couples fast SSD storage directly with an FPGA.<p>The types of computing that can be done in an FPGA require a new style of programming, because in an FPGA, programs get mapped into hardware, such that data flows through the chip, with all of the code running at the same time, instead of in sequence.<p>There are large differences in the style of programming required to get efficient use out of an FPGA... there are some ways of translating C that work, but they are crude crutches at best.<p>This is like going back to the early days of computing, when the new machines were expensive, but very fast... and programmer time was relatively cheap. It&#x27;s going to take a special new breed of programmer to make this really work well. We&#x27;re at the beginning of a new era.<p>I hope that makes sense.')
