Item(by='formerly_proven', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602069285, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24706953, text='USB 3 is a pretty plain 8b10b scheme. 10GBASE-T uses a far more complex modulation scheme which uses forward error correction, then applies a PAM-16 modulation and then packs two PAM-16 symbols into a 2D constellation of 128 symbols called DSQ128 (curiously two PAM-16 symbols would encode 256 values, but DSQ128 only contains 128).<p>Four pairs vs one pair already quarters the bandwidth requirements on top of that.')
