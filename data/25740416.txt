The &quot;it&#x27;ll never happen&quot; attitude pretty much guarantees that at some point someone will purposely or accidentally benefit from it. Now that the cat is out of the bag people are very focused on the issue at hand, which is good.<p>To believe America will &quot;fall&quot; you have to believe that there is, or there soon could be, a majority of people who believe in dismantling democracy and abandoning the constitution.
