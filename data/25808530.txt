Item(by='AdamJacobMuller', descendants=None, kids=[25809602], score=None, time=1610862974, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25807673, text='You should really implement support for the If-Modified-Since header and if-none-match header.<p>Every* RSS client is going to send that header on refreshes and if you haven&#x27;t posted something new since the date-time in the header, you can simply return a 304 not modified response (which is tiny).<p>You can get the best of all worlds with a short TTL on the CDN edge you will have low bandwidth utilization and fast updates.')
