Item(by='wheresmycraisin', descendants=None, kids=[24957642], score=None, time=1604208735, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24957131, text='For questions like these, you&#x27;d need to look at the distribution (in the frequency&#x2F;probability sense), not just the average, of the kinds of web sites you&#x27;d like the SSG to make. If your SSG is for ecommerce, sample N sites and draw a histogram. If it&#x27;s portfolio sites, sample those and do the same.<p>The reason average would not make much sense is that the distribution of page count is definitely not going to be unimodal or symmetric.')
