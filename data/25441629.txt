Metal foil on solid insulation. Metal stucco mesh. Even metal films on the panes of newer insulating windows.<p>I suspect that this effect is also why cell phone reception is so bad in some houses. We are kind of working at cross purposes here...<p>&gt;...does it require sheets of thick metal?<p>No but things work better when the edges of the sheets are electrically connected.
