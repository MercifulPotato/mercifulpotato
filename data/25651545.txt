Item(by='ianai', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609883001, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25650347, text='People sleep in cycles. Everyone’s timing of a cycle is different. But the average is around 90 minutes. You’ll feel dramatically better keeping your sleep to obey the boundaries of those 90 minute cycles.<p>Also REM occurs in the last cycles of sleep. So sleeping 4 hours with little REM and sleeping 6.5 hours with REM is like night and day.')
