Item(by='stevekemp', descendants=None, kids=[25383204, 25383032], score=None, time=1607668217, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25382675, text='In Finland almost every single can, and drinks bottle, has a corresponding price printed on it, ranging from €0.10 to €0.25.  You can return them at almost any local store and receive cash.<p>There are hundreds of unemployed&#x2F;retired&#x2F;homeless people who scour parks, and the city centers, collecting these discarded containers and recycling them for the money.<p>The return rate is pretty high, even with the tiny amount of money per-item.')
