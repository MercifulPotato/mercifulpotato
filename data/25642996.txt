Item(by='circularfoyers', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609830954, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25642521, text='I wouldn&#x27;t call it helplessly outdated, at most it&#x27;s a version behind. But there&#x27;s a number of reasons for this. For starters Telegram themselves lag behind in releasing the source code for the app[1]. Then the volunteers who make the FOSS version[2] have to update their fork and make sure it works. Then finally it&#x27;s submitted to F-Droid, where it&#x27;s compiled by their build servers (of which the build process occasionally breaks and needs to be fixed).<p>I think the assurances that this offers (entirely FOSS, reproducible builds, better privacy and security[3]) outweighs being sometimes days or weeks behind the releases they upload to the Google Play Store.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;DrKLO&#x2F;Telegram" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;DrKLO&#x2F;Telegram</a>\n[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS-Team&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS-Team&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS</a>\n[3] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS-Team&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS#changes" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS-Team&#x2F;Telegram-FOSS#changes</a>')
