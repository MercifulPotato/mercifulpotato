Item(by='danpalmer', descendants=None, kids=[25836453], score=None, time=1611078637, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25836134, text='I don&#x27;t have a feeling for how big that is, but I work from home in a small flat. I have found that I can get this separation from only using my desk for work. I don&#x27;t sit in my office chair or at my desk in my own time, if I&#x27;m writing code in my free time I do it on my sofa or at my coffee table.<p>Alternatives I&#x27;ve heard from others are things like dressing up for work. I had a colleague that wore a suit to work every day (at a tech startup) so that when he got home he could change into something else. That helped him define a boundary.<p>Find what works for you, it doesn&#x27;t have to be physical space.')
