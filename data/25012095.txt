Item(by='rtp', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604705158, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25008587, text='Software development looks a lot like evolution. The market and business requirements are the environment that weeds out the unfit software. Adapt or die. Codebases that are slow to adapt to outside changes are like species that are slow to adapt to selection pressures. So like the vestigial organs bursting from infection, so are companies that are unable to ship because devs are slowed down by messy code.')
