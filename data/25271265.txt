Sure, the big players in the recording industry can treat artists badly. But a service is provided, which is doing all business and marking side of things that most artists don&#x27;t want to bother about.<p>The idea of recording companies will still exist. Maybe somehow the big incumbents get caught out by the change to a &quot;digital economy&quot;, but all that will happen is new ones will appear, and will end up doing exactly the same as the old ones.<p>Digital distribution was supposed to free artists from record companies.
