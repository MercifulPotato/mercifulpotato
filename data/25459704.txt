Item(by='auggierose', descendants=None, kids=[25462767, 25459776], score=None, time=1608235554, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25459278, text='The free software &#x2F; open source &#x2F; proprietary software discussion is an important one. For someone new to it, this article is certainly interesting.<p>For me the question is how to solve this in the context of this: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Maslow%27s_hierarchy_of_needs" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Maslow%27s_hierarchy_of_needs</a><p>We&#x27;ll see there that freedom is pretty much high up in Maslow&#x27;s hierarchy of needs (it&#x27;s in the esteem part). Which means, it is very important, but only if your other needs are already met.<p>Can I live from making free software? How well can I live from \nmaking free software? These are the really interesting questions in my opinion.<p>Can I be free by making free software?')
