Item(by='tnli', descendants=None, kids=[25025174], score=None, time=1604832299, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25024611, text='Whether the glass is half full or half empty is not a fact, it&#x27;s an opinion. The glass being, say, 45.67% from full capacity is a fact. The glass being emptied right now at 55 millilitres per second or being filled at 45 millilitres per second is a fact.<p>It might be a fact that two people can have greatly different kinds of opinions from the same facts, but they both still deserve to know the facts.<p>Whether you consider the glass to be half full or half empty is not a fact. It&#x27;s an opinion.')
