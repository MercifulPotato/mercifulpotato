Item(by='heymijo', descendants=None, kids=[25551117], score=None, time=1609075565, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25550791, text='In your head &gt; out loud &gt; on paper &gt; to someone else.<p>&quot;First you communicate with yourself.&quot; - Peter Drucker<p>I remember Drucker laying out this hierarchy for communication in his tome on management. Each step is progressively harder. Something that made sense in your head, you might not be able to articulate on paper. Something you just wrote may not communicate to someone else.<p>IME, it is spot on.')
