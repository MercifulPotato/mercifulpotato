Item(by='jdeibele', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1610739285, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25788889, text='Looking at the design of the iPads and iPhones starting with the 12 it seems clear that there&#x27;s been a change in design decisions at Apple.<p>There&#x27;d been a drive to remove ports and to make devices as thin as possible, even if it meant losing functionality (no SD slots, USB-A ports, shorter battery life) or increasing complexity (dongles, battery packs).<p>That seems to be reversing to some degree.  Whether this was all Jony Ive or not, it&#x27;s nice to see more of a balance.   It should be possible to have a functional device but not include every I&#x2F;O port under the sun.')
