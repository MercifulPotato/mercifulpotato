Item(by='ddingus', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609812132, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25632698, text='Automation may also improve consistency, or quality.<p>There are two basic outcomes regarding labor:<p>One is to reduce labor and ride on productivity &#x2F; efficiency.<p>The other is to work differently, better.  Head count may or may not change.<p>In terms of which is better, there are strong arguments either way.<p>Everything costs something.<p>The first scenario is easy.  Margins go up, labor costs go down.  However, cost of change, maintenance, quality, business expansion may carry much higher costs and risks too.<p>In the second scenario, margins likely increase, but not as dramatically.  People are free for other work, training, to innovate, etc...<p>Lots of ways this can all play out.')
