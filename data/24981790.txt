Last year I knew it would happen because I started hearing first hand from people who were increasingly using multicloud.<p>One frequent scenario is when companies hire new staff used to one cloud and has existing apps in another. If the app can run anywhere it will likely run where the staff can deploy and manage it easier and cheaper.<p>Multicloud vendors are needed because there&#x27;s value in being able to abstract certain functionality or parts of the stack across hyperscalers.')
