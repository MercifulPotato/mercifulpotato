Item(by='swalsh', descendants=None, kids=[25959993, 25961976], score=None, time=1611941821, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25959714, text='It&#x27;s not really &quot;Stamped&quot;.  It&#x27;s more of a photographic process.  My basic understanding is that they apply a photographic reactive chemical, expose it. From there they apply another chemical process to form an oxidation layer, and then use an acid process to &quot;etch&quot; away material creating channels of positively charged particles.  Then they can apply a mask, and throw tiny amounts of metal which will become the gate.')
