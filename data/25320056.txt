Item(by='leafmeal', descendants=None, kids=[25320386, 25320324], score=None, time=1607217638, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25319137, text='Generally you don&#x27;t want to get to close to cashew trees. The seed and other plant parts contain urishiol. This is the same compound in poison oak&#x2F;ivy that causes allergic reactions.<p>In general people who harvest cashew fruit wear lots of protective clothing.<p>Also, fun fact, the seeds need to be heated before being eaten. There are no <i>truly</i> &quot;raw&quot; cashews at your grocery.')
