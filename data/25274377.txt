Item(by='wcoenen', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606897852, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25273781, text='RNA is definitely not like an &quot;abstract syntax tree&quot; or &quot;intermediate representation&quot;. It is the final machine code that is executed by ribosomes to build proteins. Proteins aren&#x27;t really &quot;executable programs&quot;, they are either passive building blocks or simple tools that do the same thing over and over again.<p>There are &quot;programs&quot; implemented by proteins and other components at a higher level (even things like clocks), but trying to include everything in one metaphor will stretch it to the breaking point.')
