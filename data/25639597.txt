Item(by='flowerlad', descendants=None, kids=[25639869], score=None, time=1609801965, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25637598, text='Does it strike anyone as odd that SolarWinds has a Vice President of security? Note that this company develops network and infrastructure management software and their updates are automatically pulled and installed by their customers. <i>Every</i> VP at such a company needs to be a VP of security, and <i>every</i> engineer needs to be a security engineer. Not understanding that is problem #1. Having a Vice President of Security at a company like this is a bit like having a &quot;Vice President of Profit&quot;. Just as profit is everyone&#x27;s job, so should security, at a company like this. If Target or Costco has a VP of Security that&#x27;s understandable because security is not integral to their products, but for SolarWinds it should have been.')
