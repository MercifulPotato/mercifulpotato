But those UltraScale DSP48 units are competitive vs GPUs.<p>It&#x27;s still my opinion that GPUs win in most cases, due to being more software based and easier to understand. It is also cheaper to make a GPU. But I can see the argument for Xilinx FPGAs if the problem is just right...')
