Item(by='dsr_', descendants=None, kids=[25364208], score=None, time=1607544638, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25363824, text='The FTC could, potentially, require:<p>1. That FB split into N independent companies, whereupon each share of FB would become 1 share in each of N companies.<p>2. That the supermajor shareholders (say, more than 10% of any company) each pick at most one company to retain that interest in, and sell off shares in the other companies until they had less than 10% of each of those).<p>3. That the new companies avoid overlapping directors.<p>It would be a long time in court. See the breakup of AT&amp;T.')
