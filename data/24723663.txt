Item(by='StillBored', descendants=None, kids=[24724606], score=None, time=1602189409, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24721383, text='sshfs adds far to much latency to large compiles which tend to open a ton of little files, read a KB or two, and close them. If your on a local LAN its doable on NFS or other low latency network sharing, but for everything going over the internet ssh is going to be far to slow. In those cases its better to just git pull the changes and maintain a local copy on the compile target.')
