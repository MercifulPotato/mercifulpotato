Item(by='JamesBarney', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609743636, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25627844, text='To expand on something you hinted at.  I think it&#x27;s a very important insight to realize that your code base doesn&#x27;t have to be homogenous, and you can make different trade offs in different parts of the app.<p>If you have an accounting app for instance, if it crashes every once in a while users will accept that.  If it calculates your taxes incorrectly leading to an IRS audit, that is unacceptable and an existential threat to your company.<p>In that scenario UI could be messy but your tax crunching code has to be the cleanest and best tested code you can write.')
