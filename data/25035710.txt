Item(by='standardUser', descendants=None, kids=[25037889], score=None, time=1604935668, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25034486, text='We should be clear about the goalposts. Reducing the transmission rate to a manageable level will be very easy with even just some fraction of the population being vaccinated. What exact percentage is unclear, but it&#x27;s a lot less than 100%. No realistic number of anti-vaxxers can change that.<p>Eradicating the virus is a whole different goal, and there could be lots of different impediments to that. After all, we still get vaccinated for a large numbers of illnesses that we haven&#x27;t eradicated but are just very rare. That&#x27;s not a terrible endgame for COVID.')
