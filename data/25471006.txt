Item(by='maxbendick', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608320432, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25454175, text='I&#x27;ve found xstate invaluable for multi-step flows on the front end.<p>Redux and similar libraries are a bit hard to read when making these because representing sequential distinct states is implicit. I.e., have an enum for the &quot;page&quot; and some reducer cases to update the page.<p>With xstate, the order of things is often visually apparent. Not only are distinct states bread-and-butter for statecharts, the machine definition in the code visually jumps out at the reader and screams &quot;these are my states, and here&#x27;s how to move between them!&quot;')
