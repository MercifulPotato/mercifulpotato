Item(by='moduspol', descendants=None, kids=[25035325], score=None, time=1604931289, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25034810, text='Philadelphia received over 350k mail-in ballots [1]. Biden&#x27;s current winning margin for the whole state is under 46k [2].<p>Wayne county (Michigan) received over 430k [3]. He&#x27;s won Michigan by 148k [2].<p>In Wisconsin, the margin is just 20k [4], with the most populous county representing 459k votes.<p>In Georgia--same kind of thing.<p>Normally I agree with the dismissive &quot;it&#x27;s so decentralized, you couldn&#x27;t do it.&quot; But it&#x27;s different this year. Anyone could have predicted that the pro-Trump areas would finish counting faster, and then the pro-Biden areas would have multiple days and nights to count, all in staunchly pro-Biden areas capable of swinging the election. Of course we expected mail-in votes to lean Biden, but that doesn&#x27;t mean anywhere from 51-99% indicates &quot;no fraud.&quot;<p>I&#x27;m not saying any fraud happened. All I&#x27;m saying is that it&#x27;s not rational to presume it couldn&#x27;t have happened without being detected. And it&#x27;s worth some efforts to see how well we&#x27;d catch it if it were occurring.<p>[1] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;whyy.org&#x2F;articles&#x2F;pa-election-2020-results-thousands-of-philly-mail-ballots-still-left-to-count&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;whyy.org&#x2F;articles&#x2F;pa-election-2020-results-thousands...</a><p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;results-president.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;...</a><p>[3] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;results-michigan-president.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;...</a><p>[4] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;results-wisconsin-president.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.nytimes.com&#x2F;interactive&#x2F;2020&#x2F;11&#x2F;03&#x2F;us&#x2F;elections&#x2F;...</a>')
