Item(by='galuvian', descendants=None, kids=[24669840], score=None, time=1601692869, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24668956, text='Factorio only has very basic gates. slightly more advanced than plain redstone, but not anything close to command blocks.<p>There are three types of combinators: arithmetic, decider, and constant.<p>Signals have a channel name (one for each item type and a few dozen other options) and an integer value.<p>Arithmetic combinators can perform arithmetic operations on one or all signals with one signal and a constant integer, or two signals.<p>Decider combinators perform comparison operations.<p>Constant combinators emit constant signals on a given channel.<p>There are also abilities to detect state on some other items and cause behavior to change, the equivalent of observers, dispensers, pistons, etc.<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.factorio.com&#x2F;Circuit_network#Logic_signals" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;wiki.factorio.com&#x2F;Circuit_network#Logic_signals</a>')
