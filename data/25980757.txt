Item(by='twic', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612115251, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25980105, text='You continue to do whatever it is you do now.<p>This idea is just that the actual value of every secret token should be prefixed with a recognisable string.<p>So instead of your config file looking like:<p><pre><code>  payment.api-key=sF1VGYGsJVnnb23a\n  logging.api-key=yunHagrya\n</code></pre>\nIt looks like:<p><pre><code>  payment.api-key=secret-token:sF1VGYGsJVnnb23a\n  logging.api-key=secret-token:yunHagrya\n</code></pre>\nYour code doesn&#x27;t even need to change. It&#x27;s just a convention used by parties issuing secret tokens, to make them easier to spot.')
