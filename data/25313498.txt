Item(by='chiefalchemist', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607165361, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25305568, text='A better headline would be:<p>No One Wants _The Inconvenience of Shopping for_ Used Clothes Anymore<p>Put another way, selling used clothing online is difficult; the overhead significantly more than new clothes. For example, every used item is unique, a one-off. Therefore, all the work that goes into bringing it to market (e.g., photos, description, getting it on the website) must be baked into the margin of that single item.<p>In other words, it doesn&#x27;t scale. The key would be to find something that does scale, and pairs well with the used clothing.')
