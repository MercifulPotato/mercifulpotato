Just wanted to dump out some of what I found when looking deeper into this.<p>Apparently 10 major unions have called for a strike for somewhere between 11-13 points of demands [1][2][3], though all sources seem to say &quot;12 point charter of demands&quot;. Here are the 13, the first and the last are sometimes included and sometimes not:<p>0 (possibly just a preamble?): Urgent measures for containing price-rise through universalisation of public distribution system and banning speculative trade in commodity market<p>1 Containing unemployment through concrete measures for employment generation<p>2 Strict enforcement of all basic labour laws without any exception or exemption and stringent punitive measures for violation of labour laws.<p>3 Universal social security cover for all workers<p>4 Minimum wages of not less than Rs 15,000&#x2F;- per month with provisions of indexation<p>5 Assured enhanced pension not less than Rs.3,000&#x2F;- p.m. for the entire working population<p>6 Stoppage of disinvestment in Central&#x2F;State PSUs<p>7 Stoppage of contractorisation in permanent perennial work and payment of same wage and benefits for contract workers as regular workers for same and similar work<p>8 Removal of all ceilings on payment and eligibility of bonus, provident fund; increase the quantum of gratuity.<p>9 Compulsory registration of trade unions within a period of 45 days from the date of submitting application; and immediate ratification of ILO Conventions C 87 and C 98<p>10 Stoppage of Pro Employer Labour Law Amendments<p>11 Stoppage of FDI in Railways, Insurance and Defence<p>12 (some documents omit this one) In addition to these the central trade unions are also demanding the withdrawal of the Land Acquisition amendment bill&#x2F;ordinance.<p>It appears that there are 11 central unions [4], and all but one have called for a strike [5], at least as of January 2020, though it&#x27;s unclear if that is still the situation.<p>[1] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;citucentre.org&#x2F;389-12-point-charter-of-demands-of-joint-trade-union-movement" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;citucentre.org&#x2F;389-12-point-charter-of-demands-of-joi...</a> has 11 points, but maybe the introduction is a 12th?<p>[2] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.jornalet.com&#x2F;documents&#x2F;12-demandas.pdf" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.jornalet.com&#x2F;documents&#x2F;12-demandas.pdf</a> has 12<p>[3] <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;citucentre.org&#x2F;component&#x2F;k2&#x2F;75-12-point-charter-of-demands" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;citucentre.org&#x2F;component&#x2F;k2&#x2F;75-12-point-charter-of-de...</a> has 13 lines<p>[4] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Trade_unions_in_India" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Trade_unions_in_India</a><p>[5] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.business-standard.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;economy-policy&#x2F;why-labour-unions-have-called-for-a-nationwide-strike-on-wednesday-120010700459_1.html" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.business-standard.com&#x2F;article&#x2F;economy-policy&#x2F;why...</a>')
