Item(by='bluehex', descendants=None, kids=[25454408], score=None, time=1608198305, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25429940, text='They kind of miss some of the charm of this word in translation. Yes “tsumu” is pile up and “doku” is reading but “(v)de-oku” is to do that verb in preparation or “for later”. So tsunde-oku which you can contract to tsundoku also literally just means to pile up something for later. It’s a wonderful pun.')
