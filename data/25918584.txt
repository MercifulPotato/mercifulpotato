In theory there is. But in practice, they can really make your life harder. Some may say that Signal is an alternative for WhatsApp, but if people you communicate with don&#x27;t want to use anything but WhatsApp, then Signal is useless. I hate Facebook but when I want to plan an event, I found nothing better, simply because that&#x27;s the platform that reaches the most people. Network effects... But also, your favorite show may not be on &quot;alternative&quot; streaming platforms, sometimes your job, or worse, the government may require a specific website.<p>There are extremists who are ready to find alternative friends, shows or jobs just to avoid using some website. It is a good thing these people exist, that&#x27;s how progress is made. But for most people you have to make compromises.')
