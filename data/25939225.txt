It&#x27;s not part of any common OS. They had it at Xerox PARC, but Steve Jobs chose not to take that part.<p>On the other hand, the PARC focus on replicating paper was a step backwards from work by Engelbart and others.<p>The limitation of a single desktop was put in place to allow children to ease into the desktop metaphor... it wasn&#x27;t meant for adults to be stuck with the training wheels on.<p>I&#x27;ve been digging back, looking for the ideas we missed... and boy, there are some really powerful tools waiting to be reified in a modern context.')
