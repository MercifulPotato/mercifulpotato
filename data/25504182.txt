Item(by='john_alan', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608625525, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25504030, text='I’m not saying short runs of 1 or 0 is not suitable for cryptographic use. I’m saying we always strive to remove any non-uniformity. Ie. Output of good CSPRNG will not have runs of one or the other.<p>True hardware random generators regularly have runs or non-uniformity. That’s why we use them to seed a CSPRNG not use the output directly.')
