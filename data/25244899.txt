Item(by='cobbzilla', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606654856, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25244330, text='The thing that tolerates the garbage is the decoder. The format says what’s valid and how to read&#x2F;write it.<p>Depending on your platform and what codec is used during playback, the corruption may be handled differently. The player might crash.<p>Best to scrub the file and properly reformat to remove corruption.<p>Another crazy thing - if the corruption is indeed due to fragmentation, the garbage bytes may in fact be another customer’s data! Or your company’s data, or whatever, you just don’t know.<p>You’re handing out random blocks of your disk to a customer, embedded in a video.<p>Depending on the type of business you run, you might not want to do this.')
