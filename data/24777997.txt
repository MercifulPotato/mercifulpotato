Item(by='madhadron', descendants=None, kids=[24778141, 24778881, 24778828], score=None, time=1602690616, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24777333, text='The context of this EWD is somewhat lost to history, because Dijkstra&#x27;s point of view in many cases won so thoroughly that we cannot conceive of what he was describing.<p>The BASIC he is describing had no call stack, nothing that would resemble a function in today&#x27;s languages. FORTRAN at the time handed masses of state around in global variables and the latest version had just added subroutines and functions. This is after ALGOL 60 had established the model that we all take for granted today.<p>I think the bulk of practitioners today would absolutely agree with him.')
