Item(by='brmgb', descendants=None, kids=[24755627], score=None, time=1602416627, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24743269, text='The result in itself as presented in the article derives from a somewhat  easy to follow train of thoughts:<p>- sound is a longitudinal wave propagating through a medium;<p>- its speed of propagation depends on some properties of said medium;<p>- thus a physical uperbound on these properties imply an uperbound on the speed of sound.<p>The fact that an upper limit exists is in itself obvious (we already knew one: the speed of light in a vacuum). Properly deriving it is the interesting part.')
