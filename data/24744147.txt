It can be saved on disk so you can open it directly (memory map) but it&#x27;s not a great storage format. Parquet or ORC is a better choice, but they don&#x27;t have as much tooling for import&#x2F;export. CSV is just the simplest way to transfer data.<p>You might be interested in DuckDB though which trying to create a new standard for passing datasets: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;duckdb.org&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;duckdb.org&#x2F;</a>')
