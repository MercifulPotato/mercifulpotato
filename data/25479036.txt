Item(by='jka', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608398440, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25478872, text='Bicycle route mapping is an iterative process, and often relies upon local cyclists recommending the best routes that they know of through a given area.<p>Cycling communities often provide guidance and cautions about route sections, and if you become familiar with cities &amp; counties then you may be able to let them know about risk areas and possible improvements.<p>That said, if you want to cycle a given route and you know of better options than the existing roads, let the trail mappers know; it could lead to learning a bit more about their choices, and perhaps improvements to the maps.<p>Beyond that, let&#x27;s hope that more roads and infrastructure are constructed with cycling in mind so that trail routes can be improved.  It&#x27;s an inexpensive, healthy transit method that fits to your own schedule.')
