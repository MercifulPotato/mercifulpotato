Item(by='ajconway', descendants=None, kids=[24876347], score=None, time=1603478352, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24870568, text='Leaving aside the fact that p2p is certainly not feasible in mobile devices, something being p2p or federated does not improve the service&#x27;s ability to evade censorship.<p>How would you block a p2p network like TOR? Just ban all the entry points. You can use proxies to access those, but then you can use the same proxies to access a centralized service.<p>How do you ban a federated network like Matrix or e-mail? Ban all the servers you can find, then you&#x27;re back to proxies.')
