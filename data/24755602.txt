Item(by='bdowling', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602517457, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24753690, text='I agree with you. My earlier point was that a license is not a contract, and shouldn’t be confused with one. My note at the end was that there is also a contract when you acquire a license through a purchase. The contract is typically of the form “you pay us money, we give you license”. That contract too shouldn&#x27;t be confused with the license acquired.<p>As you correctly point out, one who sells his only license to a piece of software no longer has a license. If he kept a copy of the software and continues to use it, he is committing an act of infringement. That is the same whether the license is for a term (subscription) or perpetual.')
