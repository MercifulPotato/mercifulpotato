Item(by='0-_-0', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602495767, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24746567, text='It might be a much higher frequency which you&#x27;re not sensitive to. My Galaxy S5 had 240 Hz. You can figure it out using a digital camera. Set the shutter speed to e.g. 1&#x2F;10s, and take a picure of a bright spot displayed on a dark background while moving the camere so you get &quot;motion blur&quot;, and count the number of times the object repeats. Multiply by 10 and you have your refresh rate.')
