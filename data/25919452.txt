Item(by='samprotas', descendants=None, kids=[25921581, 25922070, 25919887], score=None, time=1611685947, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25918911, text='Having executed several &quot;no-downtime&quot; cutovers between systems via DNS updates, I will warn you that a surprising number of clients never re-resolve DNS, so the TTL is effectively &quot;forever&quot; from their point of view.<p>For the rare case of lift-and-shift-ing for a system upgrade I felt morally okay about eventually pulling the plug on them, but I&#x27;d hesitate to design a system that relied on well-behaved DNS clients if I had a reasonable alternative.')
