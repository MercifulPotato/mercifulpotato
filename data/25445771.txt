Item(by='jonathansampson', descendants=None, kids=[25445858], score=None, time=1608141260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25445525, text='This is incorrect, and based on misinformation from 2018. In 2018, Brave users could tip unverified content creators with BAT. Those tokens would go into a settlement wallet until claimed by the intended recipient. This was a bad design.<p>Within 48 hours we had a new build of Brave out which held the tips locally, in the browser. The tip would be attempted for up to 90 days; if the unverified publisher were to verify within that time, the tip would be sent to their wallet. If the 90 days passed, the user&#x27;s BAT would be released within the browser to be distributed elsewhere.<p>You can read more about these changes in our blog post from December of 2018: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;rewards-update&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;brave.com&#x2F;rewards-update&#x2F;</a>.')
