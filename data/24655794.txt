Item(by='ocdtrekkie', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601583842, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24650718, text='USB-C felt like it forgot the entire design intention of previous cable standards: That connectors had a direct relationship to functionality.<p>When you build a PC, generally parts and cables only fit in places that they will function. When plugging devices into a computer, historically if you plug them in where they fit, it works.<p>USB-C&#x27;s decision to buck this behavior made it more confusing for users, not less.')
