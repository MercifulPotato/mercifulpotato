Item(by='vidarh', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609795185, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25636585, text='I&#x27;ve long argued referendums ought to require supermajorities based on how easy the change voted on is to <i>undo</i> and an assessment of the extent of the change. It encourages the side that wants change to aim to find ways to minimise the change they ask for and maximise the viability of reversal to lower the bar for the vote.<p>Achieving a reasonably objective assessment of what the required majority should be would be very hard, though.')
