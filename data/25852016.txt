Those two properties are the major reasons they are the staples of our diets. There&#x27;s little sense in growing and processing them on site at food distribution centers. Growing and storing them within a reasonable distance of food distribution facilities is sufficient for sustainable reliance on grains.<p>I believe rice can be stored safely for years given the right conditions.
