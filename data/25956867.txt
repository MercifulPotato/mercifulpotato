Item(by='jariel', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611925825, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25956360, text='A company is a balance of power between:<p>Owners, Debtors, Executives, Employees, Customers and Suppliers - and the Externalizations (i.e. governments, regulations, treaties).<p>The purpose is whatever the owners want it to be, in the context of that balance of power.<p>Sometimes Execs or Employees have incredible power.<p>As a supplier to a monopsony like Apple, the &#x27;purpose&#x27; may largely be dictated by that relationship.<p>Company owners tend to want to make money, the do so typically by growing.<p>To the extent that externalizes are minimized, there&#x27;s nothing wrong with that.<p>Sometimes externalizations are unavoidable (i.e. imagine a Starbucks on every corner of every street), ergo, it can&#x27;t make sense, but it&#x27;s not always the case.')
