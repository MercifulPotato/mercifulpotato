Item(by='walkingolof', descendants=None, kids=[24905404], score=None, time=1603782510, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24904384, text='In Sweden at least, &quot;allemansrätten&quot; as a law, comes from a change made in the 40s, to resolve conflicts between city dwellers need for &quot;friluftsliv&quot; and landowners, in-favor of the former.<p>Since 1994 &quot;allemansrätten&quot; is part of the constitutional law, securing the right for everyone to go anywhere under certain circumstances (cant stay longer than a couple of nights in the same place, cant stay within eyesight of a permanent resident etc etc)')
