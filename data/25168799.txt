Item(by='tgv', descendants=None, kids=[25169119], score=None, time=1605953329, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25168282, text='I think (but I&#x27;m also someone who likes more densely packed interfaces) that it boils down to &quot;discoverability&quot; and friends: a sparse interface with a few well chosen controls can be easier to explore and navigate, precisely because there is less information. So on the one hand it eases the task of the user (cognitive load, if you want), and on the other it forces the builder to prioritize.<p>If that leads to difficulties for complex and infrequent tasks, it&#x27;s only a disadvantage for the more technically oriented user.')
