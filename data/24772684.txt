Perhaps AWS can achieve adoption of Lambda without needing to cater to customers who are cautious about getting locked in, but any challenger might find that it&#x27;s much easier to gain customers if they also provide some form of an escape hatch.<p>As Jeff Bezos would say about retail, &quot;your margin is my opportunity.&quot;')
