Item(by='andrewzah', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602436993, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24745564, text='CRTs are still heavily used in the smash bros. melee gaming community as the nintendo gamecube natively outputs to 480i.<p>Using a modern 720p+ display involves upscaling, which causes latency. This latency is wildly inconsistent across tvs, which makes competitive play untenable.<p>There are monitors with extremely low response time (~1ms), BenQ was the go-to for a while. It used to be that the cost was prohibitive, but now finding CRTs is more prohibitive... especially finding a good trinitron.')
