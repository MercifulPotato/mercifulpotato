Item(by='floatingatoll', descendants=None, kids=[24655758, 24652962], score=None, time=1601568260, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24652385, text='Nintendo’s Switch plug happens to physically and electrically <i>seem</i> to accept USB-C charger connectors, but it’s not a USB-C plug, and the Switch should not be considered compatible just because it uses a similar plug.<p>Nintendo should have taken more steps with their charger to restrict the Switch to authorized chargers only, rather than permitting unsupported chargers (such as standard USB-C ones) from being able to power it. Or even just outright used a proprietary connector!<p>None of this is any fault of the chargers. They’re the ones adhering to the standards correctly. The Switch is at fault.')
