Item(by='Balgair', descendants=None, kids=[24802457, 24802539], score=None, time=1602865713, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24801750, text='Fortunately, there already exists a secular church, so to speak: Unitarian Universalism[0].<p>The modern Unitarian Universalist (UU) church is very welcoming to people of all faiths, religions, and creeds. You do not need to be atheist to join, nor do you need to believe in anything. Most congregations are very welcoming of new members.<p>Four US Presidents, eleven Nobel prize winners, and Sir Tim Berners-Lee are Unitarians. There are many Unitarians out there, though they do not tend to proselytize much, which is maybe why you haven&#x27;t heard of them.<p>As always, finding the right congregation is very important. The UU church has a good site here to help:<p><a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.uua.org&#x2F;find" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.uua.org&#x2F;find</a><p>With the current Covid-19 crisis, local churches near you may be closed or only doing virtual services. Please check with the minister.<p>[0] <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Unitarianism" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Unitarianism</a>')
