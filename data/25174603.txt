Item(by='yokem55', descendants=None, kids=[25174836, 25175452], score=None, time=1606014388, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25174400, text='It isn&#x27;t even necessarily &#x27;residential&#x27; IP&#x27;s. It&#x27;s IP&#x27;s that don&#x27;t have a valid reverse dns, preferably one that is also forward resolvable. Most ISP&#x27;s won&#x27;t let you set your reverse DNS on &#x27;residential&#x27; connections, so it ends up being a blocker. Now you could set up a vpn tunnel to a vps provider that lets you set your reverse dns, and then things get a bit easier.<p>So prereqs -\n1) Valid reverse DNS on your sending IP. Preferably with a hostname that is also forward resolvable.\n2) SPF Records\n3) DKIM')
