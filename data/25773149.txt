Item(by='Groxx', descendants=None, kids=[25773501], score=None, time=1610608233, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25771692, text='ISO 8601 is pretty absurd when you actually read it.  `2021-W02` and `--01-14` are valid, as is `--1013` (quick!  guess what that means!  and beware that `-1013` is valid too!)<p>Please, everyone, use <i>a single format at all times</i> in your systems.  I don&#x27;t really care what it is, though I&#x27;m fond of `2021-01-14T06:28:08Z` because it&#x27;s unambiguous.  But don&#x27;t just say &quot;use ISO 8601&quot;, it&#x27;s far too vague and you&#x27;ll inevitably have variations.')
