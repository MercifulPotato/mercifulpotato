Item(by='fossuser', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602006345, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24699906, text='As someone who deals with pretty intense anxiety often, it’s worth just waiting for more general data.<p>Physical symptoms from anxiety are real and hard to separate from non-anxiety related symptoms. The current covid&#x2F;election&#x2F;general global craziness sets anxiety levels high.<p>I haven’t caught covid, but if I had I imagine I’d be similarly freaking out over every anxious feeling via hypervigilance.<p>None of this means that there aren’t real long term effects, but it’s worth being skeptical in context of anecdotes.<p>You can find anxious people going down rabbit holes of vague symptoms for basically every possible disease. People are not able to adequately assess themselves.')
