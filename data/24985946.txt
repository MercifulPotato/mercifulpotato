Item(by='wfleming', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604454175, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24985875, text='Amazon does not need to make a profit off of books, for a variety of reasons. It can therefore sell them at a much narrower margin, or no margin, or even a loss.<p>Other booksellers generally don&#x27;t have the many additional sources of funds that Amazon does. So if they compete with Amazon on price, they lose money and go out of business. And if they don&#x27;t compete with Amazon on price, customers go to Amazon and the bookseller still goes out of business. Bit of a rock and a hard place for the booksellers.')
