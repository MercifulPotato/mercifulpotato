Item(by='vinay_ys', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611330286, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25871316, text='Here&#x27;s one way to look at it:<p>AWS has a lot of closed source components behind the scene that makes Elasticsearch scale and run reliably in their cloud environment.<p>Elastic can develop similar closed source components to make things scale – in non-aws environments – in private clouds – in on-prem deployments.<p>Why does this matter?<p>There are a lot of those customers – banks etc – who will stay on-prem and medium size cloud customer for whom public cloud is more expensive than private cloud. These are the ideal customers for Elastic.<p>Among them, there will also be some who will do hybrid – onprem + AWS. Elastic can price their product to compete with AWS&#x27;s and use their service in both on-prem and in aws seamlessly.')
