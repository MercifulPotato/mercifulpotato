The planet would run out of literally everything else before we run out of water.<p>It takes water to make a human. You will pass the point where you can&#x27;t expand the population before you get to the point where you can&#x27;t maintain the population that already exists. If something does decrease the water supply, you will in a short period of time have a smaller population to support. Such is the nature of carrying capacity.')
