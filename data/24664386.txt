Item(by='jacurtis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1601656806, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24664310, text='I am one of those people who struggle to say no. Just this year, I consciously started saying &quot;no&quot; to people more frequently because I was getting overstretched with commitments and knew I needed to stop.<p>So far I have never regretted saying &quot;no&quot; to something (even though I suspected I might regret it at the time). But I have frequently regretted saying &quot;yes&quot; to something.<p>By saying no to more things, it allows you to have more time to do a good job at the few things you say &quot;yes&quot; to. Plus there is a power in taking your life by the reins and not letting other people dictate what you do because you blanket accept everything they request of you.')
