Item(by='packetlost', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1612028905, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25970121, text='This is the correct use of the word &quot;fallacy.&quot; The &quot;appeal to authority&quot; fallacy is the belief that because some piece of info comes from someone (it&#x27;s generally an <i>individual</i>) who is in a position of authority means that it&#x27;s automatically true. It has nothing to really do with whether the piece of info is accurate or not, and consensus is generally a way to mitigate the issues that arise from this fallacy.')
