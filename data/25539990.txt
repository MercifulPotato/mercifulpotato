There are very few games that are rich enough to come close to holding my interest as much as those; poker, Titan, and Diplomacy are the only ones that immediately come to mind. Then last year I finally delved into 18xx (I had vaguely known about the genre for decades) and it leaped into that list as well.<p>There have been a few projects to make these games playable on computers over the years, but <a href="http:&#x2F;&#x2F;18xx.games&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">http:&#x2F;&#x2F;18xx.games&#x2F;</a>, launched this year, is in my opinion clearly the best (disclaimer: I&#x27;m a contributor to the codebase). Ideally you can find a game with experienced players who are eager to help a newbie learn the ropes.')
