Item(by='justsomeuser', descendants=None, kids=[24998529], score=None, time=1604585390, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24997894, text='I agree as a developer, but I think the economics favour having many incompatible UI libraries:<p>A. It’s expensive to standardise and agree on what the UI primitives would be, and a standard would force  the lowest common denominator of features. If you want a new whizz bang button now you have    to create an RFC and cajole agreement.<p>B. It’s profitable to own the platform, which includes the UI, because then you can charge 30% taxes. If Apple spent money making a portable library they would be attacking their own income source.')
