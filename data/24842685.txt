Item(by='spacedome', descendants=None, kids=[24842967], score=None, time=1603230485, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24842080, text='Higher level BLAS operations, such as solving a linear system, or computing svd&#x2F;eigen, cannot be done in-place the way matrix multiplication can, and require additional memory of a predetermined, fixed size, called the work array. This cannot be pre-allocated in Julia, as there is no interface to do so in LinearAlgebra, so these BLAS calls will always allocate memory.')
