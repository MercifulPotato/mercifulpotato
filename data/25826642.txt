Item(by='MrRadar', descendants=None, kids=[25828048], score=None, time=1611004065, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25826318, text='Encrypting data <i>after</i> it has transited untrustworthy networks (which could have surveilled or modified it before it gets to you) is about as useful as closing the barn door after the horse has already escaped. The encryption (and authentication) needs to happen at the origin to get any security benefit.')
