Item(by='sgt101', descendants=None, kids=[24702386, 24702319], score=None, time=1602016380, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24701732, text='Don’t waste your time, Leuconoé, living in fear and hope\nof the imprevisable future; forget the horoscope.\nAccept whatever happens. Whether the gods allow\nus fifty winters more or drop us at this one now\nwhich flings the high Tyrrhenian waves on the stone piers,\ndecant your wine: the days are more fun than the years\nwhich pass us by while we discuss them. Act with zest\none day at a time, and never mind the rest.<p>–Derek Mahon, The Hunt by Night (1982)')
