It&#x27;s often an option, and there&#x27;s very few drawbacks for employee and employer.<p>The 20% paycut is pre-tax, so the real cut is a lot lower. It also lets employers hire a 5th person for every 4 80%s, so total productivity is likely higher due to people being happier, and there&#x27;s more jobs total.<p>It&#x27;s hard for me to imagine why tech companies wouldn&#x27;t want to offer this to all their employees.')
