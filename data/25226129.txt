The client certificate it uses is tied to the serial number of the device itself, when it registers for the push service.<p>Apple sees the client IP of the push connection, naturally.<p>Therefore, based on IP geolocation, Apple really does have coarse location history for every single iOS device by serial number.<p>Apple is indeed storing your (coarse) location history.')
