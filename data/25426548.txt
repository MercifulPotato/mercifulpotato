Item(by='scythe', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608002149, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25420221, text='The nonlocality can be eliminated by replacing the `guard {}` block with a `resolve;` statement to be placed at the end of a block containing multiple `defer`s. This also reduces nesting and solves the question of where the `defer` is executed and makes it easy to grep to the location where the `defer` statement will be executed. Of course, it would be a syntax error to use `defer` without a following `resolve;`.')
