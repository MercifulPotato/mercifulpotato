Item(by='jsnell', descendants=None, kids=[25213872, 25214027, 25213825], score=None, time=1606335158, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25213548, text='There&#x27;s a good reason for what you&#x27;re identifying as misuse.<p>If you show a captcha after a failed password, you need to show a one after a correct password as well. Otherwise you leak information. You can have other solutions, e.g. in a login flow that splits the username and password entry, it&#x27;s advantageous to put the captcha between those two steps. But even in those solutions the display of the captcha must be independent of password correctness.')
