Those jobs are created by entrepreneurs or government. Some entrepreneurs choose to do without employees. Software development and publishing would be a niche where this model applies.<p>Job markets are usually protected by work permits. Arguments about protectionism can go either way. I&#x27;m not advocating either position, only making a realist&#x27;s observation.<p>Job creators and people who have income are usually recognized as a benefit to an economy.
