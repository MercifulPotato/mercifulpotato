Item(by='sokoloff', descendants=None, kids=[24729775], score=None, time=1602249924, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24729639, text='I think there are reasons to make either choice (one global salary vs a localized market salary).<p>The former optimizes for fairness&#x2F;equality of continuing employees and takes the &quot;what is the value of the work?&quot; as the foremost question.<p>The latter puts the focus on &quot;what is the market price [the next-best-alternative] for this employee?&quot; and tries to  ensure that you don&#x27;t have unhappy employees staying longer than either side would prefer because they are &quot;stuck&quot; with a salary that they can&#x27;t match in any other opportunity available to them and so do just enough to not get fired.')
