Item(by='ravi-delia', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611530456, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25897095, text='There are very real situations where signaling <i>actually</i> explains something.  Mostly they involve people doing something that seems irrational or hypocritical on the surface; the hidden variable is of course social approval.  In other situations, however, &#x27;People do X to signal they do X&#x27; really is just a complicated way of saying &#x27;People like to do X&#x27;.  Once you have the action, it really doesn&#x27;t make any sense to bother figuring out why.')
