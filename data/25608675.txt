The average English word is 4.7 letters long. Let’s round up to 5 and add 1 for a space to make 6 characters. Novels are around 90,000 words long. So 800G of pure text represents 800G &#x2F; 6 char per word &#x2F; 90,000 words per book =~ 1.5M books. The Library of Congress has 39M books, not to mention all the text produced that’s exclusively online.')
