Item(by='jlokier', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602328760, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24738016, text='Because <i>whatever they do</i>, Apple&#x27;s actions or inactions will have an effect on people in the real world.<p>In the case of videos which might get people beaten or killed or other things, you can argue that Apple shouldn&#x27;t act to take those down due to anti-censorship principles, or you can argue that Apple should act due to anti-beating-and-killing-or-other principles.<p>What you can&#x27;t correctly argue is that Apple should avoid getting involved.<p>It&#x27;s literally impossible for them to not be involved.  Whatever they do or don&#x27;t do has the effect of supporting one outcome or the other.')
