Item(by='neolefty', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1606580412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25238251, text='The goals here are kind of sideways from heat dissipation.<p>* Different parts from different processes — for example DRAM and CPU need different processes, so it doesn&#x27;t work to put them on the same chip — this integration is a step closer than on-package.<p>* Yield — if you <i>really</i> want sixteen cores, and you&#x27;re pushing the edge of your process, your yield for perfect eight-core chiplets will be dramatically better than for a sixteen-core chip or chiplet.<p>But yeah concentrating all that into multi-chip chip will also concentrate the heat.')
