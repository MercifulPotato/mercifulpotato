Item(by='speed_spread', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1607526080, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25350794, text='I would draw the line at semantic indexing. If the data is only accessible through immediate metadata (filename, sequence number, datetime, etc) then it&#x27;s just a data_store_. If the engine also maintains indexes derived from the data being stored, then it&#x27;s a data_base_. In that regard, Kakfa would not be a database since it has no knowledge of what it is storing.')
