Item(by='misnome', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611909919, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25946055, text='I do something similar, I gave up on trying to get the bash history to work properly and so just have this:<p><pre><code>    echo &quot;$HOSTNAME $$ $(date &quot;+%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%S%z&quot;) $*&quot; &gt;&gt; ~&#x2F;.fullhistory\n</code></pre>\nIt&#x27;s attached to the preexec hook of <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;rcaloras&#x2F;bash-preexec" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;rcaloras&#x2F;bash-preexec</a>, so is run before every command. This means that everything goes into one easily-greppable file, but is still separable by PID&#x2F;host machine - since my work has me walking around a large facility, often I&#x27;ll remember where I was when I did something but not exactly when, so can narrow down by machine.')
