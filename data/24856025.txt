Item(by='taffer', descendants=None, kids=[24856044], score=None, time=1603356520, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24846540, text='Let&#x27;s assume there is a business requirement that an order belongs to a customer. By definition, this is business logic. If you implement this requirement by creating a foreign key constraint, then you have added business logic to the database. If there is a business requirement to calculate the sum of all wages in a department, and you do this via an aggregation in SQL, then you have processed business logic in the database.<p>If you are not allowed to do this kind of things, then what is left? Do you interact with your database as if it were just a bunch of VSAM files?')
