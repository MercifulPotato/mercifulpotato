See Progress and Poverty [0] by Henry George, named to capture the idea that poverty often follows progress. The core idea is that as a society&#x27;s productivity increases, this just gets captured by higher land values and rents, leaving labour having to pay even more rent to just survive.<p>[0] - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Progress_and_Poverty" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Progress_and_Poverty</a>')
