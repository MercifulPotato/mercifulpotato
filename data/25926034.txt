Item(by='duckerude', descendants=None, kids=[25926308, 25926260], score=None, time=1611739531, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25925491, text='The expected personal payoff of voting is extremely small. One vote only makes a small difference. The probability of your vote tipping an election is on the order of winning the lottery.<p>If you look at the payoff for society as a whole then voting is worth it, because it potentially affects an enormous number of people.<p>But your individual payoff of your individual vote can easily be a million times smaller, practically irrelevant.')
