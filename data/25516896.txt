Item(by='runawaybottle', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608726832, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25503809, text='Treat it like writing, there is such a thing as a first draft. It’s going to suck, but this is not the draft you put all your edits in. The draft is just a vision for what it should be.<p>Then, no matter the impulse to leave it alone and mark it as ‘working code’, go back and edit it.<p>This alleviates the pressure as it’s only your first draft, followed with a discipline to go back and fix your abstractions, organization, and elegance.')
