Item(by='Emendo', descendants=None, kids=[25383246, 25383528], score=None, time=1607662421, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25379680, text='The current state of having alternative options in a market could not be counted on.<p>Think back about a decade ago when there were a number Smartphone platforms. They were backed by (back then) industry heavy weights such as Palm, RIM, and Microsoft. If one didn&#x27;t like Apple or Google Android, there were plenty of options out there. As the market matures through, consolidation happens, and now we are left with two platforms.<p>What happens if, tomorrow, both those platforms decided to not play nice? Are the Chinese Android phones with no Google Play Service or the Pinephone really viable and reasonable alternatives?')
