Item(by='munchbunny', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608313366, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25468035, text='My personal and vicarious experiences with VC&#x27;s have been that they are very much a mixed bag, and choosing to work with them in general is a necessary evil depending on the type of company you want to build. For my future startup attempts, if I can help it I will not work with VC&#x27;s.<p>And if I have to work with VC&#x27;s, then I will have a strong preference for working with VC partners who are former operators. The investor having substantial experience in the trenches (such as former founders) makes a massive difference in the productivity of the founder&#x2F;investor working relationship.')
