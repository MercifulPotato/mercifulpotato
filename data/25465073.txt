Item(by='codetrotter', descendants=None, kids=[25465374], score=None, time=1608280467, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25464658, text='Yeah same in Norway.<p>For example if someone asks me if I want to go somewhere with them at a specific time, and I would like to go with them but I already plan to do something else at that time, then I would say “nja” and while I say that think out an alternative suggestion for when we might go.<p>Another example is if I talk with someone and they say something that I agree with but then say something I disagree with. Then I would say “nja”. In this case it would be similar to English where you might say “yes and no”. And then state what I agree with and what I don’t agree with and why.')
