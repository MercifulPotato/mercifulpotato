Some of them can even exchange genetic material with each other (!). This is a way for them to exchange &quot;knowledge&quot; (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Pilus" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;Pilus</a>).<p>I can guarantee you that if you observed amoebas under a microscope for a while, you would realize that these are more sophisticated little machines than you think.<p>&gt; &quot;largest genomes belongs to a very small creature, Amoeba dubia.
