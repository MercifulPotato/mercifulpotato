An index is used to prevent a full scan of the key space in order to find a record. You have indexes in both row and column oriented databases.<p>What a column-store database gets you is fewer I&#x2F;Os on reads since all the data for a column is stored together. This is ideal for some analytic queries on warehouse databases with wide rows.')
