Item(by='kps', descendants=None, kids=[25073578], score=None, time=1605192937, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25070312, text='A ‘trick’ for fancy prompts containing information is to give them the form,<p><pre><code>    : all the stuff;\n</code></pre>\nAs long as ‘all the stuff’ is parseable as shell words (which you can ensure by quoting with printf &#x27;%q&#x27; if there&#x27;s doubt), then you can copy and paste entire lines.<p>The trivial copyable prompt would be<p><pre><code>    :;</code></pre>')
