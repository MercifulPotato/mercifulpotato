Item(by='magicalhippo', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608061872, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25433093, text='There are a lot of really useful use-cases between not using regexp and using regexp to parse email addresses.<p>For example, our system needs to handle container numbers[1] as part of an official form, and we check them for correctness to avoid rejection of the form.<p>As far as we&#x27;re concerned the container number consist of four letters and seven digits. However the user is allowed to submit a container number with a hyphen or a space between the letters and the digits.<p>Writing a regex that checks for a properly formatted container number is quick, easy to understand and a lot less convoluted than most alternatives.<p>[1]: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;ISO_6346" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;ISO_6346</a>')
