Item(by='crispyporkbites', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603183567, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24834552, text='Chatbots suck, because AI just isn&#x27;t good enough or empowered enough to actually help in 99% of requests.<p>ChatBOXES on the other hand are amazing, a few reasons why:<p>- You can&#x27;t accidentally hang up and have to re-dial and wait on hold<p>- There&#x27;s an incentive for the company to respond while the customer is there, because they might disappear at any moment (unlike email)<p>- There&#x27;s a record in writing<p>- It works with people with poor language skills<p>- No hold music &amp; you can see where you are in the queue<p>- Support teams can scale by handling multiple conversations at once and using copy&#x2F;paste from scripts, terms etc.<p>- You can include hyperlinks, screenshots etc. to make things super clear or open up other tools (e.g. screen share)<p>- Contact details, email address etc. can&#x27;t be misheard<p>- No bad signal &#x2F; fuzzy line<p>- You can data mine the requests to figure out common requests and optimize for them<p>Long live the chatbox!')
