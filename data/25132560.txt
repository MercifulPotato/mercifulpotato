Git has strong network effects, and people are generally very conservative about tools like source control. Git also has the benefit that there are a lot of people used to its quirks, and that skill investment makes them feel fond of it.<p>The same was true with the switch to WYSIWYG OSes and word processing tools; they were generally liked by people new to computing, but people who were steeped in the old ways, many switched only reluctantly.')
