Item(by='nescioquid', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609345092, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25582402, text='&gt; The &quot;will be exploited by few against the many&quot; could be applied to any and all technologies.<p>Some technologies have a low barrier of entry and can be widely distributed. The long bow could pierce armor, and was cheaper (and I imagine easier to make) than armor. So technology can equalize, though more broadly, what sorts of technologies a society develops seems bounded by things like economy and warfare.<p>Penrose believes intelligence depends on understanding, and that understanding is essentially not computational, and I was persuaded by his writing. But that doesn&#x27;t mean that the techniques we label as AI can&#x27;t be pernicious or used in an exploitative way.')
