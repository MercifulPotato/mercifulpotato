Item(by='sz4kerto', descendants=None, kids=[25719639], score=None, time=1610307979, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25718499, text='It really all depends on the methodology. I recommend some reading about &#x27;p-hacking&#x27;. It is not that hard to find to find really shocking findings in large datasets -- the question is how hard you look. In this particular case, the question is how many hypotheses have been evaluated, i.e. has the author &#x27;mined the data&#x27; for correlations. If yes, then the acceptable p value is much smaller than normal.')
