Item(by='chrisandchips', descendants=None, kids=[25163587, 25162519, 25165915], score=None, time=1605891910, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25160204, text='I would recommend Joshua Foer &quot;Moonwalking with Einstein&quot; for those interested in memory. Spaced repition is effective to an extent, but I find the techniques explored in his book to be much more effective. The tl;dr is that you are much better off constructing &quot;memory palaces&quot; in your head. You pick a place you know extremely well (like your family home), and you imagine yourself navigating that space and &quot;placing&quot; reminders of the things you&#x27;d like to remember along the way.<p>It&#x27;s like inventing a very surreal dream to help you avoid forgetting things. I use variants of it for things like people&#x27;s names and ideas I&#x27;d like to explore all the time, it&#x27;s super helpful.')
