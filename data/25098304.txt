Unless you&#x27;re launching something in a completely saturated marketed it&#x27;s always better (cheaper, faster, easier) to find new customers than trying to take existing one from other business.<p>Bonus: Growing the market by 0.01% gets you more customers than taking 0.01% of the existing market.')
