Item(by='adrian_b', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1602697293, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24778739, text='In a superconducting coil there are similar repulsive magnetic forces, which can easily disintegrate the superconducting wire at very high currents.<p>Therefore the coil assembly must be designed to support the wire and resist to the highest forces expected.<p>So there are similar problems with flywheels and superconducting coils, in the case when the stored energy densities are also similar.')
