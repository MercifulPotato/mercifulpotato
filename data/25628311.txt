Item(by='millstone', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609740350, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25626819, text='A programming language idiom is a pattern or technique that you expect your readers to know, even if it isn&#x27;t obvious when encountered for the first time. Here&#x27;s an idiom:<p><pre><code>    for (int i=0; i &lt; 10; i++)\n</code></pre>\nWe all instantly know this means loop 10 times, from 0 through 9. But on first read it takes a little work to figure out what&#x27;s happening.')
