Item(by='peterwwillis', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604692099, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25007697, text='The simplest thing is make friends with your neighbors. Once you have a bond with your neighborhood, you can become educated about local issues, and spread this knowledge among your community. Then the community can work collectively toward its interests.<p>Even if you live in a rich &#x2F; sheltered community, you can still help your neighbors understand the needs of underprivileged communities and how they can help.<p>Finally, organize community engagement days. One day a week for you and your neighbors to clean up, attend a city council meeting, learn about current events, or vote in local elections. Or just have a mini block party for people to chat.')
