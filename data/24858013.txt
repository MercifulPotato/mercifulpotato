Item(by='tlamponi', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1603374837, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24857586, text='Socialized healthcare systems often have room for a private sector, so you can have both.<p>You can also go to other doctors in a socialized one, if their treatment (style) does not fit your cause.<p>Also, in my experience there&#x27;s lots of choice in most socialized health care systems.<p>I had a gastroscopy in Austria, the Doctor asked me if I&#x27;d like a general anesthesia or rather a local, nerve blocker - mostly to avoid the gag reflex.<p>He said that it may not be as pleasant without the anesthesia, but I still opted for it and am really glad I did. While it really was not pleasant, I was up and going again in about half an hour.')
