Item(by='nrmitchi', descendants=None, kids=[25196371], score=None, time=1606156892, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25189761, text='Availability of your applications, and availability of the kubernetes control plane, are not typically correlated. One of the nice things about how Kubernetes orchestrates is that the master is <i>not</i> in the path of requests. Ie, in many situations the Kubernetes master may be unavailable, but your applications will be unaffected and can still serve traffic.')
