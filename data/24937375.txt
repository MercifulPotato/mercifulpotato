Item(by='dragonwriter', descendants=None, kids=[24937970, 24937573, 24937446], score=None, time=1604014412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24937303, text='&gt; The whole reason they have this protection is so they CAN let anyone post without fear of litigation.<p>No, the reason they have it is so that they can <i>moderate</i> without fear of litigation; before 230 unmoderated content generally did not expose companies to liability unless they knew of unlawful content (distributor model), but any attempt at moderation made them liable for everything on the site, whether or not they had specific knowledge (publisher model); 230 specifically was passed to negate liability as a publisher for user submitted content even if the platform engage in moderation.')
