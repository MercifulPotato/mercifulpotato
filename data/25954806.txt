Item(by='tsimionescu', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1611903386, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25949228, text='I think a general case is concurrent mutation of a data structure. If you are adding some item to a list in parallel with others, you will usually need to return some token (e.g. index) that will allow your caller to retrieve that item later.<p>You MAY be able to get away with making this a pure function by returning a shallow copy, but I believe that depends on how central the sharing aspect is.')
