(Though who knows, there might be other relevant stuff somewhere.)<p>Note the use of the phrases &quot;primarily designed&quot;, &quot;has only limited commercially significant purpose&quot;, &quot;is marketed ... for use in circumventing&quot; -- I think the RIAA is on thin ice in this respect, but that&#x27;s not for me to decide of course. Grepping through my bash history I see some stuff that could perhaps be RIAA stuff, and other things that aren&#x27;t.<p>Anyway:<p>(a) Violations Regarding Circumvention of Technological Measures.—<p>(2) No person shall manufacture, import, offer to the public, provide, or otherwise traffic in any technology, product, service, device, component, or part thereof, that—<p>(A) is primarily designed or produced for the purpose of circumventing a technological measure that effectively controls access to a work protected under this title;<p>(B) has only limited commercially significant purpose or use other than to circumvent a technological measure that effectively controls access to a work protected under this title; or<p>(C) is marketed by that person or another acting in concert with that person with that person’s knowledge for use in circumventing a technological measure that effectively controls access to a work protected under this title.<p>(3) As used in this subsection—<p>(A) to “circumvent a technological measure” means to descramble a scrambled work, to decrypt an encrypted work, or otherwise to avoid, bypass, remove, deactivate, or impair a technological measure, without the authority of the copyright owner; and<p>(B) a technological measure “effectively controls access to a work” if the measure, in the ordinary course of its operation, requires the application of information, or a process or a treatment, with the authority of the copyright owner, to gain access to the work.<p>(b) Additional Violations.—<p>(1) No person shall manufacture, import, offer to the public, provide, or otherwise traffic in any technology, product, service, device, component, or part thereof, that—<p>(A) is primarily designed or produced for the purpose of circumventing protection afforded by a technological measure that effectively protects a right of a copyright owner under this title in a work or a portion thereof;<p>(B) has only limited commercially significant purpose or use other than to circumvent protection afforded by a technological measure that effectively protects a right of a copyright owner under this title in a work or a portion thereof; or<p>(C) is marketed by that person or another acting in concert with that person with that person’s knowledge for use in circumventing protection afforded by a technological measure that effectively protects a right of a copyright owner under this title in a work or a portion thereof.<p>(2) As used in this subsection—<p>(A) to “circumvent protection afforded by a technological measure” means avoiding, bypassing, removing, deactivating, or otherwise impairing a technological measure; and<p>(B) a technological measure “effectively protects a right of a copyright owner under this title” if the measure, in the ordinary course of its operation, prevents, restricts, or otherwise limits the exercise of a right of a copyright owner under this title.')
