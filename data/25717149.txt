Item(by='skohan', descendants=None, kids=[25717349], score=None, time=1610300515, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25714822, text='I have to say, at my current company we are using Serverless, and it really does feel like it reduces complexity.  No runtime&#x2F;framework to set up, no uptime monitoring or management required on the application layer, and scaling is essentially solved for us.  I mean you do pay for what you get, but it does feel like one of those technologies which really lowers the barrier to entry in terms of being able to release a production web application.  In terms of building an MVP, a single developer really can deploy an application without any dev-ops support, and it will serve 10 million users if you manage to get that much traffic.<p>I&#x27;m sure it&#x27;s not optimal for every case, but for an awful lot of cases it seems pretty darned good, and you can save on dev ops hiring.')
