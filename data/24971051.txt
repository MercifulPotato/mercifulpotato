Item(by='kevincox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1604340412, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=24970921, text='If I had to guess they probably had timeouts that were too aggressive. Client timeouts are a very hard problem because it is difficult to tell the difference between &quot;working, but slowly&quot; and &quot;something went wrong, the best bet is to try again&quot;.<p>Back in the day we used to have timeouts based on individual reads&#x2F;writes which will often better answer &quot;is this HTTP request making progress&quot;. However the problem with these sort of timeouts is they don&#x27;t compose well so most people end up having an end-to-end deadline.')
