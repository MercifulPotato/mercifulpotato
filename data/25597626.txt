Item(by='madrox', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1609451755, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25590846, text='When I got my first job reporting to a non-technical executive and had authority to pick the PM tool, I thought this was my chance to find the Tool That Didn&#x27;t Suck. Going down that rabbit hole led me to the sad truth that it&#x27;ll be impossible to make a project management tool that the engineer likes until the engineer stops perceiving project management data entry as busy work. Finding a new tool ameliorates this until the novelty of working in a new UI has worn off.<p>Engineers are an autonomous bunch, and the best coders among us despise doing anything that isn&#x27;t actually writing code. Unfortunately, like doc writing, there is more to being a good engineer than writing code.')
