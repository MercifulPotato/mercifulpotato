Item(by='vel0city', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1608335336, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25473490, text='The CPU on the Xbox was a slightly customized Pentium III. Microsoft didn&#x27;t necessarily design it, they worked with Intel to ensure it met the requirements they had in mind for the Xbox.<p>The CPU on the Xbox 360 was co-designed with IBM, working to integrate their PowerPC-based PPE cores with an AMD GPU.<p>As an outsider looking in, Microsoft definitely dabbles its toes into designing chips but they&#x27;ve always done it with a partner already big in the space of chip design. Even with that Pluton &quot;chip&quot;, its more about creating a standard secure enclave system between existing CPU manufacturers based on some Microsoft specifications rather than an entire chip design ready to get sent to a fab.')
