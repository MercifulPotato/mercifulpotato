Item(by='names_are_hard', descendants=None, kids=None, score=None, time=1605072597, title=None, item_type='comment', url=None, parent=25043481, text='I can&#x27;t speak for halal, but kosher is not about blessings. In the case of meat, there are a myriad of rules that regulate the production,  starting from animal selection (only a kosher species, and then only an animal that meets certain health criteria - a cow with a punctured lung may not be eaten), then the slaughter technique and procedure, then the removal of specific parts of the animal that may not be eaten, salting to remove the blood, and ultimately preparation (no mixing with dairy, etc).')
